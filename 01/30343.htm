'Out of memory' refers to your RAM.

memory = RAM

storage = hard drive

Out of memory means there's not enough RAM available for the programs you are running. It usually has one of two causes:

1- You have too many programs open at one time.

2- You are infected by malware, since malware writers typically don't care how much of your RAM they use.

If you close some of the programs you are running and still see the message, you are infected with malware.

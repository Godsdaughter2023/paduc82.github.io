a. user say:

Hi DKemper,

 

When you attach photo in Live Mail, you can do the following to avoid the files getting saved in Skydrive:

  1. Login to your Windows Live Mail using your prefered Hotmail Account.
  2. Compose a New Email
  3. Click on the Attach File Button
  4. Select two photo from the dialog box. Click the OPEN button. The two files will be attached not as an album.
  5. After Step 4, repeat Step 3, this time selecting multiple photos. These photos will not become photo album and will be saved/attached to your email as separate files.

Take note that when performing Step 4, select only two(2) photo's first. This is to make sure you don't make a photo album when attaching photos.

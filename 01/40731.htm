Hi Brian,

 

After further investigation, we found the following senarios.

1. When you downloaded Skype for Business Basic 2016 via https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=49440 on the RDS server, the server will refuse you to install it.

 

2. When you installed Skype for Business Basic 2016 deployed with shared computer activation via the Office 2016 Deployment Tool, Skype for Business Basic 2016 will be allowed to be installed. So to use the Shared Computer Activation feature in Skype for Business Basic 2016, users should still be assigned the Office 365 ProPlus licenses.

 

You may need to subscribe to Office 365 ProPlus licenses to use Skype for Business 2016 or Skype for Business Basic 2016 on the RDS server.

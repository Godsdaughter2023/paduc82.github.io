    wildattorney say:

    How to Make Cracked Stone Brick and Mossy Stone Brick

    I suspect many don't know this, but if you are playing on Survival Mode, there is a way to make mossy stone brick and cracked stone brick, which normally appear only in Strongholds.  First, craft some stone brick stairs.  Now, if you place the stairs on the ground and the stairs while facing either north or west, you will get stone brick.  No surprise there.  We've known since the the game came out on Xbox that if you mine stairs, you get a single resource block used to craft the stairs, not the stairs themselves.  However, if instead you place the stone brick stairs on the ground and mine the stairs while facing east, you get mossy stone brick.  Alternatively, if you place the stone stairs on the ground and mine the stairs while facing south, you get cracked stone brick.  The only other way to obtain mossy or cracked stone brick in Survival Mode is to to mine them from the walls of a Stronghold, but those bricks are naturally limited and you have to find a Stronghold on your seed--no easy task.  I hope this helps.

      Having the task bar on the bottom of the screen has nothing to do with

      it can be on any of the sides you prefer it on. To move it from any
      side to another, simply click on an unused part of it and drag it
      where you want it. Be sure to click within it, not on the edge;
      clicking on the edge and dragging will resize it, not move it.

      If it won't move, it may be locked. In that case, right click on it
      and uncheck "Lock the taskbar," then try again.

      real estate than having it at the bottom. And with my task bar on the
      left side of my screen, alt-tab continues to work (as does win-tab,
      which many people prefer).

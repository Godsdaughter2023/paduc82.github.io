I think it's a conflict between the thinkpad fingerprint management system and Win 10's own fingerprint system.

 1. Log on computer only by local account

 3. Record fingerprints ONLY in thinkpad fingerprints system

It seems the only way makes fingerprint logon works on my computer.

Moving contacts from one computer to another

¡firedog say:

The normal method of transferring contacts from one application to another is to export them from the source application as CSV, then import into the target application. In this case, use the Export button in the Windows Live Mail contacts window to export as CSV. Then in Outlook, follow the instructions on this page to import the CSV: https://support.office.com/en-us/article/import-contacts-to-outlook-bb796340-b58a-46c1-90c7-b549b8f3c5f8

NB: when you get to step 9, before selecting Finish, click the Map custom fields button. This will give you the opportunity to match Windows Live Mail's field names to Outlook's. Field names have to be precise: Email, Email address and E-mail address are all different, and unless you choose the right ones, data will end up in the wrong columns or may be not imported at all.

When version 10.3.181.14 of Adobe Flash Player was released it installed the Flash Player Settings Manager in Control Panel.  Local Storage Settings can be managed through this console. There are three options Allow, Ask before using local storage and Block. It would appear your computer is set for the second option. The data stored is referred to as a Flash cookie and like other cookies contains information about your preferences for viewing a particular site. Before version 10.3 these settings had to be made while online by visiting the Global Settings page directly or accessing it by right clicking on a flash video and selecting Settings(which you can still do).

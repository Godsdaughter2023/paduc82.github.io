adrianne buc say:

This is issue is known to caused by corrupted USB driver, missing important software and driver updates, and faulty USB controllers. You can try the methods below to resolve the issue:

Method 1: Uninstall USB driver and reconnect the USB flash drive:

  1. Right-click on the Start menu and select Device Manager.
  2. Click the drop-down menu for Disk Drives.
  3. Right-click offending USB flash drive, and click Properties.
  4. Select the Driver tab, and select Uninstall.
  5. Unplug the USB flash drive and restart your computer.
  6. Plug in the USB flash drive and the driver should automatically reinstall.

Method 2: Reinstall USB controllers

  1. Right-click on the Start menu and select Device Manager.
  2. Expand Universal Serial Bus controllers.
  3. Right-click the USB device, select Properties.
  4. Go to Driver tabclick uninstall.
  5. Repeat for each device under Universal Serial Bus controllers.
  6. Restart your computer to reload the drivers and connect your USB flash drive.

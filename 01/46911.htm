pink flamigno say:

Removing password on startup in windows 10

I have windows 10. I am the only user on this computer, so I am the administrator. I did not set up a password to log in at start up. After I logged in to the games app on my computer (using my Microsoft account) my computer started requiring me to use my Microsoft account password to log in at start up. I have tried going through run, netplwiz, and changing the requirement. It will not stop.

ruz san say:

Hi,

Windows 10 integrates your Microsoft account with your user account on the computer. This is why you see your Microsoft account as the available account when logging in to the system. This should be remedied by simply switching the sign-in option in Settings. Follow the steps below:

  1. Click on Start.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Select Accounts.
  4. Go to Your account.
  5. Click on Sign in with a local account instead.
  6. Enter the password for your Microsoft account.
  7. Recreate the local account.

You will be asked to sign out for the changes to be applied. This shall keep your Microsoft account from being connected to your local account, and you will be able to sign in to the computer with the local account.

Don't refrain from posting if you need more help.

      ralphiebaby say: I was in the same situation but was able to do a system restore to go back and find out exactly what it was I had removed. I found that the "Intel Control Center 1.2.1.1007" present on Dell Precision laptops is redundant. It doesn't have anything to do with performance tuning or anything else - it bears only one control, and that's for launching the Intel Rapid Storage Technology Tool. Which can also be started directly from its system tray icon, or from the start menu.

      So you can delete the Intel Control Centre on these machines with total impunity. You will lose nothing.

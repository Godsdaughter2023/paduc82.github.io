There's no such thing as Home Gold on the Xbox 360. If you wish to have gamertags play online on the Xbox 360 console after your gold family pack subscription expires each person will need their own gold membership.

The gold sharing only applies to Xbox One. If you get a Xbox One, one gold subscription will suffice for 1 console.  If you get multiple consoles then you'd need to get another gold subscription for that other console and everyone on that other console will be able to login and play and have a gold subscription.

To recap currently the way it's setup if you're going to purchase multiple Xbox One's you'll need 1 gold membership on each of those consoles for everyone to be able to play online on any console. If you get 3 Xbox One's you'd need 3 gold subscriptions (one for each box).

The Xbox 360 will only work if you continue to pay for singular gold subscriptions for each account you wish to have online access.

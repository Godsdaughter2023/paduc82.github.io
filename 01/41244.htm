joanna 777 say:

Hi,

Did you recently installed a Windows 7 update?

Most of the time, updating your device drivers after updating your operating system will resolve this Code 10 error. To do that, you need to uninstall all those four "WPD FileSystem Volume Driver" that gives an error.

  1. Go to Device Manager > Portable Devices.
  2. Right-click on each one of the WPD FileSystem Volume Driver > Properties.
  3. Click on Uninstall device in the very bottom.
  4. Restart your computer after uninstalling all four devices and Windows will automatically install the device drivers needed.

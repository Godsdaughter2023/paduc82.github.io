To delete a portion of video, you just need to make a Split before the undesired part and again at the end.  On the Video Tools | Edit tab there is a Split button, or you can just press 'M' on the keyboard.  Do that before and after the part you want to remove.  Then right-click on the clip in the middle and choose 'Remove'.

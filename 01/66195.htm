    NUM LOCK, Acer notebooks, startup windows 8/8.1

    for anyone who is annoyed and have NUM LOCK turned off on acer notebook and cant find it in BIOS (because its not there), here is the solution:

    1. run "regedit"
    2. go to \HKEY_USERS\.DEFAULT\Control Panel\Keyboard
    3. change value "InitialKeyboardIndicators" from "2147483648" to "80000002"
    4. restart and u will have NUM LOCK ON always on windows startup
    its possible its working on other notebooks and computers using win8/8.1. if not utfg :)

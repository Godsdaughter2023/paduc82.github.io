Getting hardware drivers (video,. audio, network) from MS Updates is generally never a good idea and poor advice.

Microsoft Update does not know what hardware drivers are best for your system, will often/usually send you the wrong outdated drivers and if MS Update ever offers you any HW drivers, I would decline them and tell MS Update to never offer them to you again (by checking the appropriate box) so you don't accidentally install them.

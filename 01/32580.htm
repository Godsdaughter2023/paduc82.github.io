david m. connell say:

You can snap both programs side-by-side on the desktop. Just drag Word partway off one side of the screen, and then Adobe off to the other side. Each program will snap in place so it takes up half your screen, making them side by side like you want.

If you have dual screens, you'll have to adjust one of the programs manually. Snapping doesn't work on the "shared edge" between the two monitors. It thinks you're just trying to drag the program between screens. But you can still resize the program manually so it's side-by side with the other.

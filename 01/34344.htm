big guy maxi say:

Xbox 360 Slim Random Reboot Loop Solution

Hey guys/girls

Was recently having an issue with my Xbox 360 slim 250 Gb rebooting randomly, sometimes within seconds of me turning it on, sometimes not at all, and everywhere in between. This was obviously getting very frustrating, so after a lot of searching, experimenting, trying power cords, cleaning the touch Xbox logo on the outside and the connecting pins on the inside etc, I realized it was the HDD. As I didn't want to fork over wads of cash to get one here in Australia, this is the solution I came up with:

  • I had a 1 Tb Seagate external drive laying around, format it, transfer save files, profiles etc.
  • Remove Xbox HDD drive (while off of course)
  • Find a 32 Gb usb stick, plug into to back.
  • Turn on, go to settings, storage and allocate unformated 32 Gb usb as the System drive. 
  • Sign into Xbox live
  • Download updates.
  • Problem solved and good to go.

While you can play games without the system drive, you can't update the system, or use your Xbox live account at all, so I recommend setting up the system drive.

Since your "Open with" button is working, what we can suggest is for you to check the Registry Editor on your computer.

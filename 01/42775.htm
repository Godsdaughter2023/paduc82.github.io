If there are no Administrator accounts on the system, you can boot to Safe Mode to access the Built-in Administrator account.

1. Press WinKey+I to display Settings.

2. Click the Power Options icon.

3. Hold down the Shift Key while you click the Restart option.

4. On the first screen, select Troubleshoot.

5. On the next screen, select Startup Settings. Click the Restart Button.

6. On the next screen, press the F4 Key.

The computer will start in Safe Mode. (Be patient)

Click the Arrow Icon.

Select the account that is named Administrator.

(This account does not have a password, by default) 

7. After Safe Mode starts, open Control Panel / User Accounts.

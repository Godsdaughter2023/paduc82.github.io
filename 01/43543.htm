None of those have anything to do with your connection to the internet. Your internet service provider provides your connection to the internet.

Your router affects the bandwidth of your LAN. It has no effect on the speed of your connection to the internet.

Using a different DNS provider may give you additional services, like filtering, and it may have superior uptime, but it doesn't make your connection to the internet any faster.

Your WiFi card driver may affect the bandwidth between your router and your WiFi card, but it doesn't make your internet speed any faster.

Your operating system doesn't provide your connection to the internet, so it has no effect on your internet speed.

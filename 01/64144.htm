Alright friends,

I have figured it out. The way to solve this problem is to access the folder with the pictures you want sorted through Libraries. Here is a step-by-step method of doing this:

  1. Find the folder with the pictures you want sorted by date.
  2. Right-click the folder and mouse over "Include in library" and select "Pictures".
  3. Go into the File Explorer and browse for the new, copied folder location.
  4. Right-click the folder and click "Arrange by" to arrange the pictures in the folder by the time increment that you want (either month or day.) The "Arrange by day" option doesn't work with one of my folders, but I guess you just can't have everything you know?

shiift n driift say:

Hello MachineStarfish,
Whilst it's currently impossible to merge two accounts together it is possible to change your email addresses around.

Preparation:
Firstly, you're going to ensure that if the account is a child account that you have the parental account details. 
Secondly, once that's done you require another LIVE email address, if you do not have one already you can do so via visiting live.com and selecting "Create Account".

Changing E-Mails:
Once you've got two emails (your current one and the one you've just made) you can begin changing emails around.
On the xbox visit the 'settings' tab, from here select 'account' then navigate to the section "Your Information".
Select "Account Security" then "Change Microsoft Account".
Click yes and enter the information asked by the console.
Once you've done this, upgrade your offline account to an online account and enter your previous credentials (for the email you would like).

Complete!
That's the process complete for changing emails.

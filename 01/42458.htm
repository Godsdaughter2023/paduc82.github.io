I cant zoom in movie maker with the zoom and pan controls. - Microsoft Community

brianhogger say:

I cant zoom in movie maker with the zoom and pan controls.

OT: Zoom.

I cant zoom in movie maker with the zoom and pan controls.

considerate_guy say:

Pan and Zoom effects are designed for photos. They do a very slight, gradual pan and/or zoom to a specific spot in the photo.

They are not static zoom effects that let you crop the focus down to a specific part of the clip. In other words, you can't zoom in directly to the middle and have it stay there throughout the clip.

You can apply the Pan and Zoom effects to video clips and it will do something. But since the effect is designed for a still image, I have a very hard time noticing that it is doing anything on a video clip. The motion in the clip hides the fact that the clip is slowly panning and/or zooming.

Further compounding the issue is that the effect happens over the complete duration of the clip. When you are doing it on a photo that is only 5-7 seconds long, you can see it happening. But if you apply it to something like a 3 minute clip it takes all that time to do the zoom. It was already slight to being with and at that speed you won't see it at all.

So yes, pan and zoom work. They are designed for photos. They can be applied to video but you won't see the results you expect.

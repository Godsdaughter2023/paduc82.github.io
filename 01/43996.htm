Copying old files to newly created User Profile

Hello Terry,

Here are two methods that you can follow to transfer the photos from your phone to your Windows 10 PC.

Method 1: Connect phone to PC via USB cable:

  1. Connect your phone to the PC with a USB cable.
  2. Change the USB setting to allow you to transfer media or photos and not just charge the phone.
  3. Open the Photos app on your PC.
  4. Select Import and follow the instructions. This will automatically select new items you haven't imported before, or you can choose what to import.

Method 2: Download the Microsoft Phone Companion app:

  1. Download the app from the Windows Store on your PC.
  2. After the app is installed, open Phone Companion by selecting the Start button, and then selecting Phone Companion.
  3. Follow the instructions to import your photos.

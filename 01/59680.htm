You are quite right that a video file is composed of thousand of frames, but videos use a compression algorithm to save memory and disk spaces. So a single video with hundreds of frames are much easier to load in Windows Live Movie Maker than a hundred photos.

[quote user=AniMater]simply having a file which is too large to upload to you-tube is what concerns me.[/quote]

Windows Movie Maker also uses compression to save your movies. By doing this, your video might be smaller depending on the saving settings or length of the video.

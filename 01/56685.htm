100% CPU usage does not necessarily mean that all your memory is used up.  When you use all of your 1 G RAM and need more, Windows will go to the hard disk and use Virtual Memory.  Disk accesses are quite slow (and your system will slow), but because your computer is waiting for the disk access (and not the CPU), your CPU usage will not be 100% but your hard drive access light may be on constantly.

Windows Camera App Permissions on Windows 10

If you get the following message when you access the Windows Camera app..  "This app needs permission to use your Camera in Settings"

And find there is no specific permission button for the native camera app, under camera permissions in settings, as there isn't, and then you come here to look for a solution, and read that you should uninstall and reinstall the camera drivers, reboot to factory settings or even buy a new laptop....

Stop - 

Go into microphone permissions and grant the camera app access to your microphone, as  there is a setting for it there, you will probably find that this will solve the error and you can access your camera app the way it was intended to be accessed.

I suspect that your new installation went astray somewhere and that parts of your old Windows folder are still used by the current OS. You can find out like so:

    ren  F:\Windows  Windows.unwanted

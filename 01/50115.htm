But i do these two ones and now my laptop shuts down completely:

1.  Power Options --> Require a password on wake up --> Uncheck the "Turn on Fast Start up".

2. Win + X --> Device Manager --> On System Devices find Intel(R) Management Engine Interface , Open that, On Power Management tab Uncheck Allow Computer to Turn off This Device to Save Power.

a. user say:

Hi,

Welcome and thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Community.

Please follow the below methods and check if it helps:

Method 1: 

Follow these steps to run the printer troubleshooter:

  1. Press “Windows Logo” + “W” keys from the keyboard.
  2. Type “Troubleshooting” in the search bar and press “Enter”.
  3. In the “Troubleshooting” window, click on “View All” on the left pane.
  4. Click on “Printer”.
  5. Click on “Advanced” and then click on “Run as Administrator”.
  6. Click “Next” and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the troubleshooting process.

Method 2:

Open Device Manager by pressing Windows key + X, click on Control Panel; select System and Maintenance.

  1. Click on Device Manager.
  2. In Device Manager, locate Printer and double-click to check if it’s working properly.
  3. Go to Driver tab, then click on Update Driver. And follow the instructions.

Let us know if this fixes your issue or not, we will be happy to help you.

 

Thank you.

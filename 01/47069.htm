A method to disable Cortana on Windows 10 Anniversary - Microsoft Community

isaaclow say:

A method to disable Cortana on Windows 10 Anniversary Update!!!

* Works on my Windows 10 PC running the anniversary update *

Step 1: Go to File Explorer and then to "This PC" (located in the menu at the left).

Step 2: Locate "Windows (C:)", open it.

Step 3: Go to the folder named "Windows" then find "SystemApps" and open it. 

Step 4: Look for this folder named "Microsoft.Windows.Cortana_cw5n1h2txyewy". Leave this window open.

Step 5: Go to "Task Manager" and end "Cortana" service until it does not restart.

Step 6: Go back to the window from Step 4. Right click and change the name of the folder to a totally different name (eg. XXYYZZ) and Cortana will not work on the taskbar (or anywhere else) !! 

Remember, if Cortana is not stopped in the Task Manager, a "Folder in use" message will be displayed and this will not work.

So you must go to "Task Manager" and keep ending the "Cortana" service every time it restarts (usually in a few seconds after you ended it) until it is completely stopped.

As you have mentioned that WI-FI network connection disconnects at 4:00 am daily. It means that the Hotel in which you are residing might have done some settings in the router so that it stops at 4:00 am in the morning.

Are you getting any error message when internet connection disconnects?

A wireless network router has a limited broadcast range. The further the computer is from the router, the weaker the broadcast signal. Solid objects, such as walls, metal furniture, and electrical appliances might interfere with the signal and decrease the usable range.

For testing purposes, you may check with the customers residing next to your room in the hotel about the issue. You can also check with the Hotel people that the router is situated on your floor or different floor. If it is on different floor then there might be a chance that connection is not strong.

Possibly, the wireless connection reset itself after a certain interval and in the process internet connection disconnects and reconnects.

Let’s try these methods below and check if that solves the problem.

A Belkin Modem-Router which is connected by wired connection to a Desktop computer(Homegroup creator). I then have a Wireless Laptop (not currently connected to said homegroup). I then have My Desktop computer connected wirelessly to aforementioned Belkin Modem-Router.

The Dekstop computer(Homegroup creator) runs AVG anti-virus and firewall. Windows firewall is turned off.

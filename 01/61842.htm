I figured out the solution almost by accident today, as I had the same issue with banner notifications no longer appearing after the November/v1511 update.

Here's how you get them back:

- go to Control Panel and select "All Control Panel Settings" (if it's not already your default) to display the full menu of items

- click "AutoPlay"

- check "Use AutoPlay for all media and devices" if not already checked

Now, to get the banners back: in every item in the listing below the checkbox that you want a banner for, change its setting to "Ask me every time".  The key one will be "Removable drives", which is the first item in the listing.   When done, click "Save" in the lower right of that window.  Test by inserting a removable drive (external HDD, SD card, etc.) - you should now see your black drive banner again in the lower right just above the taskbar.

As long as you do not alter the existing file and folder structure you can create additional folders, in general avoid putting individual files in the root folder.

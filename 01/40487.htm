    1. Reseat the AC cable into the Dock PSU.

    2. Unplug your Surface Dock from the power outlet, wait 10 seconds, and plug it back in.

    3. Remove the Surface Connector from the Surface device and have the customer look for metallic debris within the Surface Connector port on the device. A flashlight is sometimes needed to identify debris.

    4. Rotate the connector over 180 degrees and plug it in again.

    Make sure that the LED indicator light on the Surface Dock is lit.

  • Restart the Surface.

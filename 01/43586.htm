Hi Abhinav,

I would like to inform you that Bit locker will prompt to enter the password to unlock the encrypted drive only once after you login to the computer. Once you enter the password, Bit locker will not prompt again even if you sign out from your account. Other users logging to the computer with a different account will be able to access the encrypted drive without prompting for the password. You will need to restart the computer so that the encrypted drive gets locked again and other users cannot access the encrypted drive.

Does the encrypted drive prompt you to enter the password credential when you restart your computer and try to access the encrypted drive?

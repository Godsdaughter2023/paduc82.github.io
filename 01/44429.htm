bhuvanchandra say:

i have just format my computer before formatting i have take all backup of c drive . but  1 folder is encrypted on my backup now i am unable to decrypt this folder and also unable  copy and  open this folder. please help me regarding this issue.
wunders say:

I am assuming that you have XP Pro and that you have encrypted your files using the Encrypting File System (EFS).  When using EFS, the decryption key to these files is kept in an encrypted form with your Certificate Store.  When using EFS, you  *MUST* back up your certificates or risk permanently losing access to your encrypted files.  Details are contained in this article which is a mandatory read if you are using EFS:

"Best Practices for the Encrypting File System"
  < http://support.microsoft.com/kb/223316 >

Skip down to the paragraph "Why you must back up your Certificates"
Bottom line here is that you have two options to recover access to those files.  The first is to restore your certificate from the Certificate backup you created per the above article before reformatting your computer.  The second option is to completely restore your prior system from an image-style backup you made of your that system before reformatting and re-installing.  Otherwise, I'm afraid you've permanenty lost access to those files.

EFS is affectionately known as the "Delayed Recycle Bin" in this forum.

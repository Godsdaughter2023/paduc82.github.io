Thanks for coming back. It seems that you are having a problem setting up the Family Safety client itself.

 

First, Family Safety is part of Windows Essentials and this is different from Microsoft Security Essentials.

 

To properly set up Family Safety, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in first to the admin account on your computer.
  2. Run Family Safety by clicking on the Start button, typing Family Safety on the search box then press Enter.
  3. Once the Family Safety client is launched, you will be prompted to sign in to the Family Safety client using the parent account.
  4. Select the family members you want to monitor. Click Next.
  5. Match your existing Family Safety member to their Windows account. Click Save.

  6. Sign in to Family Safety website and change the settings as per your preference.

  7. Open the Family Safety client again, then click the Refresh button or the Get the latest settings from the website icon located at the upper right part of the window.

You can check this article for the detailed instructions. Let us know if you have questions regarding setting up so we can further assist you.

Since you did not ask any question I am not sure what reply you expect. If you wish to reset the file and folder permissions then you can do this:

  1. Log on under an admin account.
  2. Create a new desktop shortcut for cmd.exe.
  3. Right-click this shortcut, then left-click "Run as administrator".
  4. Accept the challenge.
  5. Type these two commands and press Enter after each:
    takeown  /f  "C:\Program Files"  /R
    cacls  "C:\Program Files"  /T  /C  /G everyone:R  system:F  "NT Service\TrustedInstaller":F

herm-8e84969e-50ad-4cb3-af0d-78ab28a61115 say:

Game saves are stored in the cloud, and will be synced to your new Xbox One whenever you go to play one of your games.

To move your games it's as simple as going to "Manage Game" and selecting "Move All". Once you have everything transferred and you want to prepare your old console for sale, just go to Settings > System > Restore Factory Defaults. All content and accounts will be deleted.

How do I change the cmos battery in my Dell Dimension 8300 - Microsoft Community

clark reed nichols say:

How do I change the cmos battery in my Dell Dimension 8300

How do I change the cmos battery in my Dell Dimension 8300?

 

Thanks,

Clark

erik zessler say:
Easy!  So long as you have a few minutes and the right replacement battery ready to go.  Just follow these simple steps.
  1. Unplug power cord.
  2. Lay tower on right side.
  3. Remove left-side panel - likely removing the screws in the back.
  4. Identify the CMOS battery on the motherboard.
  5. Press the metal tang to release the battery.
  6. Check the model number on the old battery, ensure that you have the same replacement.
  7. Install new battery, reinstall side panel, stand tower upright and plug in power cord.
  8. Start the system and immediately enter the BIOS (CMOS setup) and correct the date/time and other settings.
Source: mickliq at Computing.net - http://www.computing.net/answers/hardware/how-do-i-replace-cmos-battery-in-dell-desktop/73611.html

Firstly, let’s check if the sound card gets detected in Device manager.

2. Double-click Sound, video and game controllers to expand that category. If a sound card is listed, you have one installed. davem121 say:

If you can get into Windows, press the shift key whilst clicking Restart to get to the Windows Recovery Environment

If you cannot get into Windows, start your PC, then just as Windows attempts to load, press and hold Power Button for 5 - 10 seconds to perform a hard shut down

Do this twice

On the third start Windows will boot into the Recovery Environment and from there you can access System Repair, Safe Mode, Command Prompt . . . etc.

Go to Troubleshoot - Advanced Options - Reset this PC

You will then have 3 options:

  1. Keep my files and Apps
  2. Keep my files
  3. Keep nothing

Select the option that suits your needs and click Reset

For best results make sure your device is not connected to the Web and unplug all USB devices . . .

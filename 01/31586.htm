frederik long say:

I cannot connect to a wireless network. When I attempt to connect, either through the network center or by using the icon in the notification area, I get the message that there are "no wireless networks available", even if I am right next to a router.

This message is generated under these conditions:

  • There are no wireless networks available. This could mean that the router is defective. Take your machine to a friend and check if his WLAN is visible.
  • Your wireless adapter is deactivated.
  • Your wireless adapter is ain "Airplane Mode".
  • Your wireless adapter is defective.

There is no point in checking any DNS settings unless you have a live WLAN connection, same as there is no point in checking your TV's loudspeaker settings unless it is receiving an antenna signal.

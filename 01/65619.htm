There is no such thing as "DVD content" and "CD-ROM content" in this context.  There are just files of different types that are stored on an optical disc.  You can store both video files and photo files on a CD-R/RW, space permitting.   The fact that you're using a DVD has nothing to do with any of this.   You can download and use ISOBuster to see what's on the disc, and recover the files if necessary.  If ISOBuster doesn't see the photo files on the disc, it means they're not there.

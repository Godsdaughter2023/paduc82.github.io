-Using the registry editor open HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Control Panel

-Left click on "Desktop"

-Scroll down until you see a value known as "WallPaper". To the right of it you'll see a path.

-Type out the full path name in Windows Explorer.

-It is here you'll find your current desktop background.

-If you're looking to recover a whole album that was saved as your "Synced Theme", you'll have to right click on the desktop, switch to the next image, refresh Windows Explorer, copy the image that has appeared, and finally paste it to a folder. The desktop will do but it will be messy.

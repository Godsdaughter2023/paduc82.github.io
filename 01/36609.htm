File Explorer customized format - Microsoft Community

rzjunction say:

File Explorer customized format

I would like to always have my File Explorer window open with the This PC section expanded and everything else (such as Quick Access or One Drive) not expanded. I know it will open the way you leave it when you close it, but isn't there a registry change or group policy to customize the default?

Windows 10 v1809

keith a. miller say:

The limited testing I just did shows this works:

Open Explorer, expand This PC and collapse all other items in the Navigation Pane.

Open regedit and navigate to:

HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\Modules\NavPane

  1. Right-click NavPane and select Permissions...
  2. Click the Advanced button.
  3. Click the Add button.
  4. In the Permission Entry dialog, click Select a principal.
  5. Type your username & click Check Names, then click OK.
  6. Change the permission Type to Deny
  7. In the lower section, click Show advanced permissions ('link' in upper-right corner)
  8. Check the box for Set Value
  9. Click OK, accept the warning, and OK your way out.

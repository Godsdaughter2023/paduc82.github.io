You probably need to update the drivers for your video card. 

If you have a "brand name" computer (HP, Dell, Lenovo, etc.) go to the computer manufacturer's web site and find the video drivers for your particular computer there.  Many of the larger computer mfrs have an "auto detect" option to determine what hardware you have.  Otherwise, you'll have to enter a specific model number or a Dell "service tag."

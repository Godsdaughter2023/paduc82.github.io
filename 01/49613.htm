obsessive power say:

Hi,

Should I use a PIN to log into Windows 10?

A pin should be much easier for you to remember than your Microsoft Account password.  However, if other people use your computer, or you are frequently around other people (who could glance over your shoulder), it would be advisable to not use a PIN and stick with a password instead.

What are the rules of a PIN?

A PIN for Windows 10 can only be 4 numeric digits long, like the PIN on a Debit/Credit Card.

How to Add a PIN:

1. Open the Windows Search box.

2. Search for Sign-in options.

3. Under PIN click the Add Button

4. Enter your 4 desired 4 digit PIN.

How to Remove a PIN:

Same as above, but click the Remove button instead, then click Remove again just under the 'Are you sure you want to remove your PIN' message.

What if I know my Microsoft Account password, but I've forgot my PIN?

Easy.  From the Sign in options as above, click I forgot my PIN. You'll then be asked to enter your Microsoft password.  You can now reset your PIN.

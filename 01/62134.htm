xbf diego m say:

Hi Ivan,

Thank you for coming to the forums. Digital games cannot be transferred from one account to another, only from one console to another and only every 4 months. You cannot have licenses on two different consoles. Content licenses are stored in the Xbox 360 console rather than in the Xbox Live account.  When you purchase digital content on the Xbox 360, you receive a license to use that content. The license has two parts: the system license and the local license. The system license applies to the gamertag that purchases the content, and the local license applies to the console where you first downloaded the content. Once a game is downloaded, a local license is applied to that Xbox 360 and other profiles can play that content on the same console.

You would have to transfer the license and download the profile that owns the content to the other console if you want the other profile to play the content, but then the original console will lose the local license and you wouldn't be able to transfer back until 4 months go by.

So essentially, it is not possible to do that.

Here are some support articles for reference

  • https://support.xbox.com/xbox-360/store/download-content#395696d4f9e34835b5c97123eed4a5cb
  • https://support.xbox.com/en-WW/xbox-live/download-content

I hope that helps.

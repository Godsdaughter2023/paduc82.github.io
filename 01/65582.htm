Method 1

If you see the black screen or have a flickering screen before sign-in:

  1. Try connecting to different video outputs on the PC. Sometimes a change in driver can cause the video to be sent:

  2. To a different Adapter: from integrated graphics to a discrete card or vice versa.

  3. To a different output: from HDMI to DVI, DisplayPort to VGA, or any other combination.

Try to boot into Safe Mode with Networking or Enable low-resolution video.

Note: Safe mode is a troubleshooting option for Windows that starts your computer in a limited state. Only the basic files and drivers necessary to run Windows are started. Here is the link: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-in/windows-8/windows-startup-settings-safe-mode

When your computer is in safe mode, you'll see the words Safe Mode in the corners of your monitor. To exit safe mode, restart your computer and let Windows start normally. 

Method 2:

  1. Check Device Manager and look for Display Adapters
  2. If you see 1 item under Display Adapters, rollback the driver or uninstall the display adapter in Device Manager, and enable the option to delete driver software.

 Check the PC or display adapter manufacturer’s site for an updated driver.

I hope this post is helpful. Do get back to us if you have further queries and we'll be glad to assist you.

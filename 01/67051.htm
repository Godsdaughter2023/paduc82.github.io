a) To clean up the fan, you will need your trusty can of compressed air.

i. Start by powering down your PC, removing the case lid, and locating the various fans.

ii. Starting with the power supply, blow through the internal slits from inside the chassis, aiming so dust will exit the back.


b) i. If you need to use a vacuum to clean the inside of your computer, use a portable battery powered vacuum designed to do this job.

ii. When cleaning fans, especially the smaller fans within a portable computer or laptop it's suggested that you either hold the fan or place something in-between the fan blades to prevent it from spinning.

iii. Spraying compressed air into a fan or cleaning a fan with a vacuum may cause damage to some fans or in some cases cause back voltage.

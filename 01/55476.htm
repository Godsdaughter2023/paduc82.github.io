john rubdy say:

Hi Jeffy,

Synaptics and Cypress is the most popular touch-pad drivers. I will show you how to disable the zoom gesture using each. If you don't get the option visit the computer manufacturer website and install the touchpad drivers which are compatible for Windows version installed on computer.

 

First Synaptics, to disable pinch zoom:

 

  1. Press Windows key + X, select Control Panel.
  2. In the list of option select Mouse and then click Mouse from the top of the search results. This opens the Mouse Properties window.
  3. Next, click the right-most tab, labeled Device Settings, and click the Settings button.
  4. From the left-hand column, click Pinch Zoom and uncheck the box on the right labeled Enable Pinch Zoom.
  5. Click OK to save your preference.

 

For laptops with the Cypress touchpad driver:

 

  1. Open the Mouse Properties window as before.
  2. Next, click the Cypress TrackPad tab and click the link, "Click to Change Cypress TrackPad Settings."
  3. Then click on the 2-Finger Gestures tab and uncheck the box labeled Zoom In/Out.
  4. Click OK to save your preference

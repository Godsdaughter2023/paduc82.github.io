zefa feng say:

WHY must I wait 5 minutes until my device restarts after processing Network Reset?

Since Windows 10 Anniversary Update, if something goes wrong with my network, troubleshooting the issue by Network Reset, why must I wait 5 minutes until my PC restarts to complete the Network Reset after clicking 'Reset now'?

What will happen and what will it result in if I restart my PC manually as soon as the count-down dialog box 'You are about to be signed out' pops up.

lizette ags say:

Hi Sam,

Network reset removes any network adapters you have installed on your computer including the settings for them. This process involves uninstalling and reinstalling network adapters and 5 minutes is the allotted time to ensure that the whole process gets completed. Restarting the PC manually during this process might interrupt the current task the system is undergoing. We advise that you wait for your PC to restart automatically and see if it resolves the issue you're having.

For more information, visit this link (refer to Use network reset to reinstall network devices).

Feel free to post back if you have any other questions and we'll be happy to assist you. We are happy to help you out.

If it were my computer, I would replace the CD/DVD drive.  One of the most common failures is the DVD/CD drive.   

The problem is simple to solve and low cost.  About $20.  Remove the power cord,  Hold the power ON switch in for at least 7 seconds.  Lay it down on its side with the wires on the bottom.  Take the side off.  You may have to remove the front panel as well.  The DVD drive is held in place usually by 2 easily accessible screws.  You have to remove 2 cables from the back.  Pull the old one out.  Slide the new one in its place.  Attach the two wires, they only fit one way.  Put the 2 screws in.  Put the side and front back on.

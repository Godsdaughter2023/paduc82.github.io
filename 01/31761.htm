cmdrkeene say:
Don't worry they aren't gone.  There's a few underlying reasons about why this happened.  For one, the My Documents folder has a file in it (hidden system file) called desktop.ini that defines the "friendly name" of the folder.  This is a variable.  If another user had the permission and looked at your account folder, they'd see "Bob's Documents" instead of My Documents.  For the life of me I'll never understand why they went to all this trouble instead of just calling it "documents", and you'd understand if you were already in Bob's folder.  Oh well, I digress...

You can undo the rename manually, or turn on the display of hidden files and just delete that desktop.ini file.

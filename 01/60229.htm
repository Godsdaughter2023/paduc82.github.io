vijay b say: ​Hi Johan,

1. What is the make and model of the printer?
2. Is the printer connected to the network?

I would suggest you to try the following steps.

a. Click on Start.
b. Click on Devices and printers.
c. Select the printer you want to uninstall.
d. Click on print server properties.
e. Click on Driver tab.
f. Select the printer you want to uninstall and click on remove.
g. Select driver and driver package and click on Remove.

Do get back and let us know the status of the issue, I will be glad to help you further. We, at Microsoft strive towards excellence.

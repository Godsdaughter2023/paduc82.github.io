MeipoXu (MSFT CSG) has a method to reinstall the Photos app. Check out the link below.

Type:

Get-AppxPackage *photos* | Remove-AppxPackage

Type:

Get-AppXPackage | select name, packagefullname
Press {ENTER}

Underlined item is the package name we got from the 2nd command.

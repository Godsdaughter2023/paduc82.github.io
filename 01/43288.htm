mike--- say: ID Chuckie,
    Thank you for posting to the Microsoft Answers Forum.

There is not a way, from within the Operating System, to password protect programs.  This is the type of scenario for using a Standard user account.  You can use the Guest account or setup another standard account and then just logoff your computer before another person uses it.  Or lock your computer whenever you are not using it and that will keep anyone from being able to access it.

There are third party programs that you can purchase that perform this type of function.  If setting up another account or locking the PC is not a valid solution, then you may want to research a third party program protector.

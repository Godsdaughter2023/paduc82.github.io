don cuthbert say: Right-click on the speaker icon at bottom right of Windows Desktop.  Choose Recording Devices from the menu that pops up.  Right-click in the blank space in the Sound menu to check to display disabled and disconnected devices.  See if the mic shows up.  Then right-click it to make it the default communications device and ensure that it is enabled.

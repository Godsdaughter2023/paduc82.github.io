chiromark say:

My problem after the anniversary update was: Cortana was replaced by "Search Windows" bar at the taskbar.  Cortana app was still in the app list, but when I clicked onto it, it only had the Home icon that said, "Start typing to search for apps, files and settings".  The Note icon for Cortana was missing and so I could not enable it.  I tried everything from changing language and speech settings to enabling Location in Privacy settings , but to no avail.  One solution suggested to create another account with administrative privileges and see if Cortana was working there.  If so then it suggested to use that as the main account and delete the other account.  This was a stupid suggestion because I would have lost all my files.  I then found one solution that did work.

In the "Search Windows" type regedit and press Enter on your keyboard, and then click YES on the window that pops up.  Next, on left-hand side, under Computer, open HKEY_CURRENT_USER\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Search

Left-click Search and on the right side, you will see many files appear.  Double click BingSearchEnabled.  Here, you will see a Window named Edit DWORD (32-bit) Value.  In the Value data, change the 0 to a 1 and then click OK.  Close the regedit window.  Now left-click within that "Search Windows" bar at the taskbar.  It will change to Cortana.

Audio Connections

If you are using VGA or DVI to connect your computer to a TV, you’ll have to find another way to get your audio going.  You basically have two choices: you can use external speakers that you hook up to your computer (or use the speakers built into your laptop), or you can use a separate audio cable to output the sound from your computer to the TV.

DVI to HDMI

You’ll probably never see a laptop or television with a DVI port on it.  However, it’s pretty common to find them on desktop computers.  DVI uses digital signals to send video output, so it can be easily adapted to HDMI while still maintaining great quality.  The biggest drawback to using DVI instead of HDMI is that it doesn’t carry audio.

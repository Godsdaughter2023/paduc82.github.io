Where does Windows Media Player Auto Rating information store?

I got a new computer and copied all the songs from old computer to the new one. However the auto rating informatoin (blue stars) wasn't copied to the new computer. Where could I find those inforamtion? Please help, thanks!
mary- say:

Hello Peter the Skipper,

Thank you for your reply.  In the Windows Media Player library, the blue stars are auto ratings and are controlled by non-Microsoft data providers and some online stores.  You can override an auto rating by manually choosing a new user rating (yellow star.)

The blue stars may not be transferred although by default most items in the Windows Media Player Library are automatically given a rating (blue star).

New tracks are auto-rated with 3 stars. When you first play a track the auto rating increases to 4 stars and the rating goes up only if the track is played all the way through.

You may copy your user ratings to your new PC though.  You may view the link below which includes information on copying the user ratings for your files to your new computer.

Creating a New Library in Windows Media Player

I hope this helps.

Your NAT types are having problems because of what it says at the top of the page: "We have confirmed that this network device is unable to support more than one console with an Open NAT. "

Best plan?  Ditch this device, and get a regular cable modem instead.  For those who don't know the difference between a gateway (which this device is) and a modem, a gateway has 4 ethernet ports in the back and/or has wireless functionality.  Then get a router that can support multiple consoles. frederik long say:
How to get that option in the context menu?

You need to save the lines below as "Folder.reg", then double-click "Folder.reg" in order to import it into the registry. The result will look like this on a right-click:

Windows Registry Editor Version 5.00

[HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\Directory\Background\shell\DateFolder]
@="&DateFolder"

[HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\Directory\Background\shell\DateFolder\Command]
@="wscript.exe \"C:\\Tools\\DateFolder.vbs\""

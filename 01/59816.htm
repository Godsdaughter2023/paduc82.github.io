Hi Stanley - as you are on Windows 7 I'm assuming these are icons remaining in Windows Media Player - and that you deleted the videos from the explorer folder... is that correct?

For future reference... whenever you delete any media files do so in Media Player (click on the icon to select then right click and choose 'Delete from Library and My Computer'... the icons will go! (If you make an error the file can be restored from the recycle bin).

To remove the icons left behind you'll have to restore you WMP library by clearing and rebuilding the database.... Details of how to do this can be found in this thread from yesterday 7th November - my post beginning 'That Sounds Promising...' It's the second part and picture 2 that are relevant - Cheers, Ric.

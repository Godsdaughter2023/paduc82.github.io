There is no option to add another user's face to Windows Hello on a single account. However, you may have your family members register different accounts with their fingerprints, or facial recognition under the same account. This can be done under Settings.

  1. Click Start.
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Accounts.
  4. Select Family & other users.
  5. Add people using the Your family, or Other users options. The only difference between the two is with family, you get an access to child controls, for monitoring and blocking content.

  1. Click Start.
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Select Accounts.
  4. Select Sign-in options.
  5. Select Windows Hello.
  6. Choose Facial recognition or Fingerprint, then Set up.

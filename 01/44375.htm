icestorm iii say:

Licensed third party controllers are fine, including ones with turbo. They're all wired (far as I know there are no licensed third party wireless controller pads). Unlicensed third party devices (like 360 controllers that have been hacked up and repackaged) are not.

 

The only "grey area" is Datel. Datel's controllers are not licensed (and at least some are wireless). Microsoft sued Datel. Datel sued Microsoft. They settled out of court. Ever since then, MS permits the controllers on the console.

     md c:\\HWID

     Set-Location c:\\HWID

     Set-ExecutionPolicy -Scope Process -ExecutionPolicy Unrestricted

     Install-Script -Name Get-WindowsAutoPilotInfo

     Set-ExecutionPolicy RemoteSigned

     Get-WindowsAutoPilotInfo.ps1 -OutputFile AutoPilotHWID.csv

Please note that during the executing the command we change the execution policy to RemoteSigned by 'Set-ExecutionPolicy RemoteSigned' command, I suggest to you before executing this command Get-ExecutionPlicy first to see the current setting, then after finishing your job, changing back your configuration to the default.

Usually Photos app automatically creates albums for you, featuring some of your best shots from around the same time and place. Still you can manage your albums by creating new or by editing the existing one.

If you'd just like to see and edit the album from any device, select Upload. Then it'll be ready to go if you decide to share it later.

However currently there is no option available for deleting the multiple albums at same time, instead you can delete one at a time. Post your feedback on this using Photos app, which will help us to improve your experience.

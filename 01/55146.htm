"when on battery the Hibernate mode vanishes from shut down menu list"

Whilst video card drivers seem to be common culprits for hibernation problems, in this case you might not find any problems when you check Control Panel, Device manager to check & update your video card driver.  Do look at the other entries as other drivers can also have strange effects.

As it is only happening on battery I'd say you need to check your BIOS first to make sure it is not a setting there that disables hibernation from battery power and then check the settings in your Control panel, Power options, {active Power plan settings}, Advanced settings.  At the top of the Advanced settings dialog, there is a button for Show setttings that are not shown - click on this first to make sure that you can see & check all the settings or you can't be sure of what you might have missed.

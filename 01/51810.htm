considerate_guy say:

When you are seeing black bars on the side of your video, it means the original video (or photos) are in a different aspect ratio than your project.  This most often happens when using photos or video that are in a 4:3 aspect ratio (similar to the shape of old analog CRT TVs) rather than a 16:9 aspect ratio (widescreen like today's modern HDTVs).

 

One fix may be to just change your project settings to 4:3 to match the aspect ratio of your source files.  Go to the Project tab and change the Aspect Ratio to 4:3.

Always Open Windows Explorer in maximum view - Microsoft Community

arthur h say:

Always Open Windows Explorer in maximum view

When I goto Windows Explorer I would like it to always open with the maximum view.
At the moment it opens in smaller view and I have to click on the max view.
How can I always have it to open in the max view?

Arthur H

a. user say:

Stretch the window to fill the screen (make it max view) and then close it.  When you open it again, it will be max view - it saves the last way it was closed as the default for how to open the next time.  This is also true with Internet Explorer.  Keep in mind that if you minimize the screen and then shutdown or logoff, you'll be back to the smaller view again when you next open it (it doesn't open in absolutely minimal view - it uses the half-way size instead).  In internet Explorer this sometimes happens when a secondary screen opens and you just close it and then shutdown or go to sleep - then it uses that last closure as the default for new windows even though you didn't mean to do so.  There's no way I know to "lock" it at opening at max view all the time - you just need to be careful how you close it when done.

I hope this helps.

Hi Bob,

Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Community.

I understand your concern, I will be glad to answer your query.

A disk marked as active means that the computer will use the loader (an operating system tool) on that partition to start the operating system. This is the drive where the computer boots from. If having both the drive as active is not causing any issue, you can safely ignore it. However, if you would like to change the status to inactive, you can right click on the drive in disk management and select "inactive" on the drive where "boot" is not listed.

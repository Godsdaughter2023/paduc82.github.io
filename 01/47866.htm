srimadhwa b say:

Hi,

Thank you for posting your question in the Microsoft Community.

No, you cannot get a free upgrade to apply directly to the XP computer. Yes, you must install Windows 7 and then work through all its updates up & including SP1.  Only when SP1 is installed will you qualify for the free upgrade to Windows 10. Your XP to 7 upgrade will be a full, clean installation.  Your subsequent update from Windows 7 to Windows 10 will just be an update not a clean install.

You'd either need to upgrade to Windows 7 and then to Windows 10, or save your files to do a clean install of Windows booted from the DVD.

When they say one year. It means once every 365 days, this starts counting from the firs time you assign an xbox as a home xbox, not from the 1st of Jan each year. So if you changed first set a console as a home console. (Basically the first time you loaded up your account on an Xbox One) on the 3rd of March, you'd have to wait until the 3rd of March the following year.

    2. On the right side of the Mouse page, select Additional mouse option under Related settings.

    3. Go to Pointers tab.
    4. Under Scheme, select your preferred mouse pointer settings from the dropdown list.

    1. On the Start menu, type Control Panel and then hit Enter.
    2. Choose Ease of Access Center.
    3. Under Ease of Access Center, click Change how your mouse works link.

    4. Select your preferred mouse pointer settings under Change the color and size of your mouse pointers.

    5. Click Apply and then hit Enter.

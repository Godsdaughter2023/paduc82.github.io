Tmoney139, your original xbox live gamertag has a MAID (Microsoft Account ID) which is in the form of an email address.

 

You can find the email address on your console by tapping on the silver GUIDE button in the middle of the controller, which invokes the Xbox Guide menu.

 

Go two clicks to the right to Settings and choose Account Management, then go another two clicks to the right to Account Security then choose Change Windows Live ID.

 

The current MAID (email address) appears on the right side against a grey background.

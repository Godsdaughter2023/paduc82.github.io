Hello,

There is a chance that the webcam drivers were not properly installed or corrupted. To reinstall your webcam drivers, you may follow the steps below.

1. Close any applications that are currently using the webcam (this may include web pages). Save and close any applications that you are currently working on as the computer will need to restart.
2. In the Search box, type Device Manager.
3. Touch or click Device Manager (Control Panel).
4. Touch or click the arrow to the left of Imaging devices.
(When there is not a listing for Imaging Devices, Contact Manufacturer)
5. Touch and hold or right-click Integrated Webcam or USB camera.
6. Touch or click Uninstall.
7. Confirm the uninstall process by clicking OK.
8. After the process, restart the computer.

Note: If the issue persist, the webcam software should also be uninstalled and reinstalled after the drivers are installed. Please contact the manufacturer for assistance.

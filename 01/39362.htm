rick rogers say: Hi,

That driver controls a slot on your system (usually laptops) that accepts PCMCIA cards. See a description:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Pcmcia-type-ii-and-iii.jpg

Uninstalling it will not aid or increase performance. The driver only comes into play when the slot is in use, and if uninstalled any add-on card inserted into that slot will not be recognized or used.

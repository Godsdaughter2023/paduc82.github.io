You can give the following commands in Command Prompt (Admin) a try:

bcdedit.exe -set TESTSIGNING ON

That generally means there is an issue with your local profile folders.  This can be permissions or file corruption.  Browser to %appdata% and rename the Skype folder to something else like SkypeOld.  When you restart Skype a new Skype folder along with your Skype name subfolder will be recreated.  You can then attempt to edit your settings and see if they save.  If they do you can migrate your old history file (main.db) and another other personal files from your old folder to your new to restore your history and misc files.

davem121 say:
Hi jorly5002, this can be done but requires a Registry hack . . .

Click Your Start Button, type regedit and hit Enter, to open the Windows Registry Editor

Navigate to:
HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Control Panel\Desktop\WindowMetrics

On the right, you will see 2 keys:

BorderWidth & PaddedBorderWidth

The default is:

BorderWidth = -15

PaddedBorderWidth = -60



To change these values, double click each key

Increasing these values will make the Window borders thicker

Note: you must Restart your PC for the changes to take effect, and it takes a bit of adjusting to get the value right . . .

san_martin say: It is Greenwich Mean Time - The time at Longitude Zero. Technically UTC & GMT  are not the same. UTC is a time standard while GMT is a time zone.

a_user_here say:

Universal time (is Universal Time Coordinate UTC) is also known as Greenwich Mean Time(GMT).

It is the time zone where the "day begins".  All other time zones are "offsets" from it.  For instance Pacific Daylight Time PDT is UTC - 7 hours.

For whatever reason, let's just say you want to upgrade your version of Windows 10 Home to the Pro version to grab those features mentioned earlier. Microsoft has made it incredibly simple although it is not a free update. Here is how it works.

3. Pick Update & Security

4. Click Activation

5. Click Go to Store

You are now brought to the Store listing for Windows 10 Pro. Here you can buy the upgrade for a one-time cost of $99. The purchase is made through the Store just like buying a new, game, movie or music.

Once you complete the purchase, the OS simply unlocks those Pro features once the bits have been downloaded. Your Windows 10 license key is also automatically converted to a Windows 10 Pro one. Since your hardware ID is linked to your license in the Store clean installs should also be okay.

Hi,



To properly isolate your concern and provide you an appropriate solution, please clarify the information below:


1. Are you using a modem or a gateway?

2. Do you have a public or private IP address?

     a. Sign in to your router settings.

     b. Access either the status page or WAN settings page.

     c. Verify or record the WAN or Internet IP address.

     d. On your computer, go to the What Is My IP Address site or the IPmarker site to verify your public IP address.


If the IP addresses match, then your router is being assigned a public IP address and open NAT should be possible. If the IP addresses do not match, then your ISP is supplying you with a private IP address and open NAT will not be possible under the current configuration.


For information about how to how to access your router and view your WAN or IP address, visit our Networking Hardware Information forums.

themasterhoo say:

This is, at least, a temporary fix. I had been experiencing the same problems and it's apparently a server-side issue. This actually worked for me and should hopefully help you, as well.

1. Right-click Start (or Win key + X)

2. Click Command Prompt (Admin)

3. Type in Command Prompt "netsh int ipv6 set teredo client teredo.trex.fi" (without quotation marks) and hit Enter

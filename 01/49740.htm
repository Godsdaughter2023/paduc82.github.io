Hi,

 

You need to bridge the line out socket of the TV Tuner and the Line in socket of your sound card with a 3.5mm stereo cable.

You should have received one of these cables with your TV card.

So basically the cable runs from the GREEN SOCKET on your TV card to the BLUE SOCKET of your sound card.

Once this is done, make sure you set your TV cards software audio settings to your sound card.

  3. If you have an TV tuner device, click the device, click Set Default, then click OK.

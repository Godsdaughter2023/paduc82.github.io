    Your call probably initiated/maintained a higher resolution on the cell network. Since such negotiation is automatic, there is no way to directly control the quality. The only way this is affected is by making the network conditions worse, forcing a lower resolution call. Even if you are on a seemingly better connection, there could be other factors limiting the highest call quality or resolution maintained.

a. user say:

I don't know what "locked in there for good" means.

Some folders in your user profile (aka user account) can, if you wish, be moved to a different location ... and that can be a very good idea.

The folders that can be moved are located in C:\Users\ name  where name is the name of your user profile. Here they are:

Contacts

Desktop

Documents

Downloads

Favorites

Music

Pictures

Video

Some people, including me, like to move these folders to a different partition on their hard drive or, even better, to a different hard drive altogether. That way you can backup and restore your software and your personal data separately.

The other folders in your user profile are actually part of the operating system.

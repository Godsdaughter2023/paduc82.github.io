How to change boot priority in windows 8 - Microsoft Community

a. user say:

How to change boot priority in windows 8

I want to change the boot priority in windows 8 to cd and how do i do that? Also how do I make my Internal Hard Disk the boot priority again?

slance310 say:

Hi,

 

This is normally done in your BIOS that you can enter when you first Power On....

There should be  buttons or text that informs which keys to hit....

 

My computer enters BIOS by tapping Del or F6 at the first screen you see, but all manufacturers choose which buttons they will use....

 

Once in BIOS there should be a Tab for Boot Order, or something similar....

 

You don't need to keep changing your boot device. Select your CD\DVD drive as the 1st boot device, and your internal hard disk as the 2nd boot device...

 

Your computer will check, and if there is no disk in the CD\DVD drive, it will go to the next device on the list... In the example, it would be the hard drive...

 

It is possible that your BIOS will allow more than two boot devices.

When you delete a file, it is deleted.  You do not need to do anything else.

tryx3 say:

Jump lists only appear for shortcuts you've pinned to the Start menu or Taskbar so you can shorten your deletion task by not having any pinned shortcuts.  You can also set Recycle bin not to intervene & just to allow files to be completely deleted straightaway - right-click on Recycle bin & select properties.

It's normal for 'Host Services: Local Server' to waste so much internet. Maybe this can help you!

1. End task 'Host Services: Local Server' in 'Task manager' and then reboot your computer (if it doesn't reboot itself).

2. Check if there are any updates downloading or installing (If there are, try shutting down 'Windows Update' and 'BITS' service and reboot your computer).

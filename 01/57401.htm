Server Response: 452 #4.5.3 Too many recipients.

There may be two things in play here.

  1. The server response indicates that you have exceeded some limit on the number of mail messages you're allowed to send. Email Service Providers impose limits on the number of recipients per message, and often per hour or per day in an attempt to combat spam. These limits may not be made public for the same reason: if a spammer knows that there's a limit of 100 messages per hour, say, then he'd be careful only to send 99. 
     
  2. The limits may also vary depending on whether the server knows you or not: known users may be allowed to send more messages than anonymous ones.

To check point (2): right-click on the account name in the folder pane and select  

  • On the Servers tab 
     
    • Ensure that the is your full email address.
                   
    • Ensure that Log on using clear text authentication is selected.
       
    • Select My server requires authentication.
       
  • On the Advanced tab 
     
    • Change the server port for outgoing mail to 587
       
  • Click Apply and OK

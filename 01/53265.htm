Looks like the 1803 version installed a 64-bit version of WMP12.

Solution:  Go ahead and check "C:\Program Files\Windows Media Player" and look for "wmplayer" file. That should be it. Make a shortcut of it!

Any old shortcuts that you may have on your desktop for WMP is most likely still linked to the old 32-bit version in the "C:\Program Files (x86)\Windows Media Player" folder, which is the problem we're all having. Delete the old shortcut.

cmdrkeene say:

Really this comes down to "how would you like to get your pictures to your PC?"  Because there are a lot of options, and you might like some better than others.

The old way is to plugin a cable and transfer the picture files by drag-and-drop (just like if you had some files on a USB stick "thumb drive").

But a more modern way is to make it automatic.  If you install the OneDrive app on your phone, you can set it so all your pictures will automatically copy to your OneDrive pictures folder on your computer, hassle-free.  I have mine set so it doesn't wait until my phone is charging, that way the pictures I take are copied to my computer within a few seconds every time I take a picture. It's great -- plus if I drop my phone nothing is lost because it's already backed up.

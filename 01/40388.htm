lemp say:
What you're looking for is Windows Internet Connection Sharing (ICS).  This is built into Windows XP.  As you recognize, however, you do need the Ethernet adapter on the desktop to be working.

Start by looking in Device Manager (Start > Run > devmgmt.msc > OK)
  • Expand the "Network adapters" section by clicking the + to its left
  • You're looking for an item in the Network adapters section with "Ethernet" in its name.
  • Please list the names of the items in this section.
  • If you recognize one of them as an Ethernet adapter, does it have a warning icon next to it?
  • Double-click on the entry for the Ethernet adapter; what shows under "Device status" and "Device usage"?

If the Ethernet adapter needs a driver, you should get it from the computer manufacturer (if you have a "name brand" computer) or from the manufacturer of the adapter itself.  Do not rely on Windows Update or third-party "find a driver" websites.  You can download the driver using another computer, copy it to a USB flash drive or burn it to a CD, and get it to the computer that needs it that way.  If you need help finding a driver, please provide the make, model, and Service Tag (if a Dell) of the computer and the full name of the Ethernet adapter as shown in Device Manager.


Once you have a working Ethernet adapter, it will show as an icon in Network Connections in the "LAN or High Speed Internet Connection" section.  You can then use the directions HERE to set up ICS on your laptop.  In Step 5, you would right-click the icon for your wireless adapter.


After ICS is configured on your laptop, you can use the default configuration of the Ethernet adapter in the desktop (obtain an IP address automatically).  There is one catch, however:  you must use a special "crossover" cable to connect the Ethernet adapter on the laptop with the Ethernet adapter on the desktop.  A standard cable will not work.  You can get a crossover cable at any computer or electronics store or online.  For example:  http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16812119153

Hi Richard,

For the UK region, dates are usually written in "day month year" (DMY) order. This order is used in both the traditional all-numeric date and the expanded form..

To change the date format, follow the steps below:

1. Type Control Panel in Search.
2. Click Control Panel.
3. On the Control Panel, Select Clock, Language, and Region.
4. Under Clock, Language, and Region; click Change location and check if it is set correctly.
5. After making sure that we have the correct location, under Clock, Language, and Region; click Change date, time, or number formats.
6. Click on the drop-down buttons and see if the format you are looking for is available.
5. DD-MM-YYYY format may not included on the list.
6. Click on Additional settings, to input preferred format.
7. Under Date tab, change the Short date format to DD-MM-YYYY.
8. Click apply to save the settings.

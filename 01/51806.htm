gabriel_c_r say:

I was able to find sort of a workaround on this.

I had noticed the taskbar might have been affecting me positioning the picture password when the settings app was open, so I tried to make the set up as full screen as possible.

--

First, set your taskbar to "Automatically hide in desktop mode" inside the Settings App.

Then go and re-set your Picture Password. Even if it is the same picture, select "choose new picture" and select the picture you want.

After this, you can un-hide the taskbar again.

It should make the black bars go away, however, a much smaller one will appear vertically on the space between the picture and the big tab on the left. Much less noticeable in my opinion.

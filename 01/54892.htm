The email address that you are using in the Microsoft Store will be the email address that will appear as your administrator account.   1. Click on Start.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Select Accounts.
  4. In the Your Info tab, Under Your email and accounts, click on Sign in with a local account instead.
  5. Enter the password for the Microsoft account to confirm that you are authorized to make the change, and then click on Next.
  6. On the Switch to a Local Account page, enter a temporary local user name and password.
  7. Click on Next to sign out from the Microsoft account and sign back in using the new local account password.
  8. Go back to Accounts, and this time, click on Sign in a Microsoft account instead.
  9. Fill up the information needed.

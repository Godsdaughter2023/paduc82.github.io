Hi MarkFilipak.Windows,

Games that were developed prior from the 4K resolution era cannot go further on this resolution. You can only change the game resolution based on the available in-game options.

You can force to change the resolution by editing the text of a config.ini file or whatsoever of a game folder, but you will not be able to force an old game to be upscaled at higher resolution.

The start of a 4K resolution era is around 2013, even if you have a 4K monitor, this will not be possible since the game is too old to handle this resolution. There’s a hardware generation gap between your game and today’s batch of computer monitors/displays.

Compared to other games that were developed around 2016 to the present, they are highly capable to run in higher resolution due to technological advances on the hardware.

  • Force the device to shut down and restart. Press and hold the volume-up button and the power button at the same time for at least 15 seconds and then release both.

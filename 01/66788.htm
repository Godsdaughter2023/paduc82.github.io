xbf brad a say:

Hello Sized,

Here is a list of troubleshooting steps you can try.

Fix for 3rdparty headsets 

1.) power cycle console 

2.)Go to settings> Kinect and Devices> Devices and Accessories> [...]> Assign controller to account> Update control> unassign account> test headset/chat 

3.)Make sure mic is "ON" Press the home button> go to the Gear on the  menu> Audio> Turn Mic off and on. OR go to settings> Kinect and Devices> Devices and Accessories> [...]>Audio> Mic on> Adjust volume. 

4.)You can test your controller if it is broken by "Buzzing" the controller. The buzz feature is connected to the headset port, and if the controller does not buzz then the port may be damaged. [Turning on Vibration if it does not "Buzz"> Settings> Ease of Access> Controller> Vibration Settings> Turn on Vibration]. 

5.)Some mics are detachable and may be damaged so try testing the headset on another device to see if it works (Example: windows 10.   

 Test the Headset with Skype Test or Voice Recorder). If there is an issue with using the headset on an Xbox controller,  you can try using a headset adaptor. 

6.) Make sure there is no Enforcement on the account. 

7.) Make sure the controller is not full of dust in the headset port (you can use compressed air to clear the dust). 

8..) Make sure controller is fully charged/ change battery type. 

9.) Use a headset adapter or standard headphones (Main fix) **************************

10) Soft or full console reset. (may not work all the time) 

Hope this helps.

Brad

Please click on a Gmail account name in the folder pane and press Alt-S F. This should reveal the IMAP folders dialogue. If the folder All mail is visible, then you'll see duplicates of all messages, because All mail is just that - Gmail's label for every message. You'll see a received mail in both the Inbox and in All mail.

In your settings at mail.google.com, you can select which labels to show in IMAP. It's a good idea to hide All mail.

PS: Incidentally, re-installing the program will rarely have any effect at all on the ability to send and receive messages. This is governed by account settings, both in the client and at the server, and those won't be touched by a re-installation.

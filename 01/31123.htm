smlunatick say:

What is the capacity of the SD card?  Any SD cards greater or equal to 4GB are in reality, these are SDHC cards.   SDHC cards have the exact "physical" size as the SD cards but the internals "electronics" are different.  To read SDHC cards, the card reader must be SDHC compatible. 

You can not normally "convert" a SD card reader with any software update, firmware patch or Windows Updates.

However, one good thing is that all SDHC card readers can always read the older SD (2GB or less) cards.

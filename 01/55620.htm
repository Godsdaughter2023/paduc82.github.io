To work around this common bug you can try the following:

  2. Paste the following and press Enter: %userprofile%\AppData\Local\Microsoft\Windows\Shell\
  3. Double-click on LayoutModification.xml (you can open it with Notepad)
  4. Search for section <taskbar:TaskbarPinList>

  </DefaultLayoutOverride>

    <defaultlayout:TaskbarLayout>
      <taskbar:TaskbarPinList>

      </taskbar:TaskbarPinList>
    </defaultlayout:TaskbarLayout>
  </CustomTaskbarLayoutCollection>

a. user say:

Hello Gary,

We regret the inconvenience. Let me assist you.

Follow the below methods and check if it helps.

Method 1: Remove and reconfigure your account in Windows Live Mail.

Note: Before removing an account, check that the messages you want saved are available when you sign in to the web version. If some of your messages are available only in Windows Live Mail, export your messages before removing the account.

To remove and reconfigure your account, please follow these steps:

1. Right-click the account you want to remove, and then click Remove account.

2. Click Yes to confirm.

3. To reconfigure, open Windows Live Mail by clicking the Start button . In the search box, type Mail, and then, in the list of results, click Windows Live Mail.

4. In the lower-left corner, click Mail.

5. Click the Accounts tab, and then click Email.

6. Type your email address, password, and display name, and then click Next.

7. Follow any additional steps, and then click Next.

8. Click Add another email account if you want to add more accounts, or click Finish to start using Windows Live Mail.

For more information on reconfiguring your account, please refer to the link below:

How do I add an account to Windows Live Mail?

Method 2: Repair the Live Mail

  • Type Programs and Features in the search bar and click on the first selection.
  • Under Programs and Features, "Windows Live Essentials" is listed. Highlight and click "uninstall/change" at the top to get the repair dialog.
  • The repair actually applies to all the installed components of the Essentials package, including Mail.

Hope the above information is helpful. If you need further information, feel free to write to us and we would be happy to help you.

Thank you.

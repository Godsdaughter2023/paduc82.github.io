The sdbus.sys is the SecureDigital Bus driver and is part of the Operating System.

However the problem is most likely with another driver.

The same error was reported in the following link:

http://windows7forums.com/windows-7-support/39578-getting-bsod.html

In that link, which involved a Dell Vostro, there is a reference to  the O2 Micro chipset driver.

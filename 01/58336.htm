If you are attempting to reinstall and you are still able to boot into a vista environment you can recover the product key from the system files.

Method 1:

Method 2:
use your favorite search engine to find 3rd party key finder utilities. (you accept sole responsibility for any 3rd party software you choose to download and run on your computer, make sure you validate the security of any software you choose to run by researching its authenticity.)

If the previous methods do not work for you because you are unable to boot into a Vista environment you should be aware that all other relevant Microsoft Support lines require you to have your product key available.

Our only recommendation in this situation is to contact your OEM for repair disks that usually have the product key preconfigured in the installation environment and would not require you to input it separately. However if this is not the case then they will still be your next contact point for further support on this issue. Check with them to see if they can provide you with any licensing keys based on your computers serial numbers or service tags.

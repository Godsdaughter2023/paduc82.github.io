davem121 say:

This is a common enough error, you will need to get into Command Prompt to fix it . . .

Start your Laptop, then just after the Samsung logo disappears and Windows 10 tries to boot, press and hold the power button down for 5 - 10 seconds to perform a hard shut down

Do this Twice

On the third boot attempt, Windows will boot into the Windows 10 Repair Environment, from there you can access Startup Recovery, Safe Mode, Command Prompt . . . etc.

Go to Advanced Options - Command Prompt

Run this command (replacing C with the drive letter of the disk being checked), and hit enter:

chkntfs /x c:

Reboot your laptop and the disk checking loop should be gone, if not repeat the process . . .

geethu b say:

Hi Myers,


Internet service providers can charge by the amount of data used (the amount of data sent and received by your PC). That's called a metered Internet connection. These plans often have a data limit, and if you exceed the limit you might have to pay extra. In some cases, you aren't charged extra but your connection speed becomes slower until the billing cycle ends.

 

1.       What is the make and model of the laptop?

2.       How are you establishing connection between printer and laptop?

3.       Are you using wired or wireless Internet?

 

This issue may occur if the printer needs some updates that need to be installed to complete the setup and due to slow Internet connection the setup is unable to install the updates. I would suggest you to try using a wired LAN connection and check if the setup completes.

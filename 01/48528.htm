haibusa2005 say: So at last I resolved my issue! I removed the RasMan (Remote Access Connection Manager) dependency from the ICS service and thus probably broke the VPN and Dial-up support . Here is the solution for those, who have the same issue. Everything you do is at your own responsibility!

Press WinKey+R to open Run dialog. Type in Regedit . Go to HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\services\ then right click on SharedAccess and choose Export . Save the file so you can have a backup if you want to restore the settings in the easy way. Now open SharedAccess, double click DependOnService and remove the RasMan entry. Restart the computer and now ICS will work fine. Doing this "tweak" will probably break the VPN and Dial up support!

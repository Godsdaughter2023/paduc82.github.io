1. Go to "This PC"

2. Double click "OS (C:)"

3. Open "Users"

4. Open your username (The Profile you are currently working in)

5. Open "AppData"

6. Open "Local"

7. Open "Packages"

8. Open "Microsoft.WindowsStore_8wekyb3d8bbwe"

9. Open "LocalCache"

10. Delete the entire folder, with contents, you see there.

For those who didn't want/need a step by step:

C:\Users\Username\AppData\Local\Packages\Microsoft.WindowsStore_8wekyb3d8bbwe\LocalCache

Then delete the one folder in LocalCache.

And that's pretty much it. Opened store and no issues after. Also if anyone is having issues with the Mail App, if you have AVG, it's the firewall on that so just turn it off and mail will start syncing. Hope this helps everyone.

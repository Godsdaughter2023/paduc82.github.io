You can submit them by email.  Here are the rules below.  Also they said to send 1 picture per email.

File format and size:  JPEG if possible (or they will convert it). Image needs to be at least 1920 pixels wide by 1200 pixels tall, at 96 dots per inch (dpi). Images can be larger than this, but must not be smaller. JPEG format is preferred, but you can also send GIFs, BMPs, or PNGs.

Sending us your work:  Attach one image per email. If you send five pictures, send five emails.  Please limit your submission to ten or fewer, we'll let you know if we want to see more: but remember, only one image per email.

Don't zip or compress your art.

Include this info in each email:

•Your name (how you want to be credited)
•A brief description of your art or photo
•Your preferred contact email name
•The address of your website, if you have one

Send it to winpgal (at) microsoft.com

cmdrkeene say:

Two finger tap is for right-click, at least unless the manufacturer has customized their driver or implementation.  Hardware features like touchpads are things Microsoft can't control -- every PC vendor gets to design and pick how their models run.  Microsoft just runs the software system.

Unless the manufacturer decided to include options,  you are really out of luck on this front.  Microsoft's OS can't modify the hardware's defaults without a driver to do it.  Here is one other potential thing to try:

  1. use Windows Logo+R to open the Run box
  2. Run the command main.cpl
  3. In the mouse options that opens, look for a touchpad tab. Some manufactures integrate their options here, but they don't have to. Toshiba has a different utility for this.

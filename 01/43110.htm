The Xbox One doesn't have a graphics card like a desktop PC or dedicated graphics chip like some gaming laptops.

The main chip on the Xbox One is a combined CPU and GPU. So if you drop the console often enough it probably will stop working at all.

However, depending on how you drop it, other components that belong to the graphics output (mainly connections to the outside like the HDMI port), will break more easily, as the console might fall on a cable connection, and the cable might bend the connector out of shape or tear it off the mainboard.

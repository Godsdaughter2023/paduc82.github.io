johann eva say:

Hi Joe Clark GLP,

You're using NEC Multisync PA272W and an ASUS display monitor on the same computer.

Each brand and model of display monitors are completely different from each other. Manufacturers do not create a hardware or device that is exactly identical to their competitor brands.

If you connect multiple displays monitors with different brand and model, your computer will have a conflict with its drivers and will generate communication problem within these monitors. Having an output of the two (2) or more monitors will make output confusion on your one and only driver which is the graphics card.

One difference will make an impact to its display in terms of color, saturation, hue, brightness, and contrast.

We suggest that you attach or connect display monitors completely identical with each other (having the exact brand and model) only to avoid these hardware and output conflicts.

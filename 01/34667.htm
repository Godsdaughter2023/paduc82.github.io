wilfriedrijsemus say:

Had the same problem and found it weird.

So I found a youtube movie

and found the answer

  1. Search on your PC: "Bluetooth and Other Device settings"
  2. Click "Add bluetooth or other device"
  3. Select "Bluetooth"
  4. Turn on the Power button and wait for the signature JBL sound. The blue light is now permanently ON
  5. On the opposite side of the JBL USB connector you see a Bluetooth button. Click it. The blue light is now BLINKING
  6. Windows will now populate the list in 3. with JBL Clip 2
  7. Select and you are done

Especially step 5 is easily forgotten

mood history

danida_u say:

Hi there mutitaChampathin

Skype mood messages cannot be deleted from the application. However, you could try these steps below as a workaround. 

  • Sign out from the application. 
  • Open ~/Library/Application Support and open the Skype folder.
  • Locate a folder named after your Skype name.
  • Inside, look for a file named keyval and delete it.
  • Re-launch the Skype app and log back in.

Please feel free to post back if you need further assistance. 

Thank you.

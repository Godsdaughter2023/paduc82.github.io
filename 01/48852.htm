theplectrum say: Bad move !

PC makers sometimes "tie" in the drivers to their machines so that the only reliable way to update is directly through their websites. Drivermax will detect your audio device and go to Realtek to get the driver. Not the solution, unfortunately

MS Update for that matter is not as it says, up to date..

Go rather to the PC/Laptop maker's website and locate the support page for your machine. Download the audio drivers from there and install.

If its a machine you built yourself, go to the motherboard maker's website and do the same from there.

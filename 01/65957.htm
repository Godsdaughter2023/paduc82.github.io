cmdrkeene say:

We can't see any email address you type here, it hides it for privacy.

But it doesn't matter, what you ask is actually super easy.

Open Settings > Accounts and go to the "Family & other users" section.

At the bottom, click to add another user.

I recommend you ignore any prompts to provide an email address and click the "I don't have it" or "sign in without a Microsoft account" options at the bottom until you get to the plain user account options (create a local account).  I tend to call mine "LocalAdminUser" but you can call it anything.

When created, click the user in the list and you'll see the option to change the account type (to make it an administrator).

Then press Ctrl-Alt-Delete and sign-out of your current account and sign-in to the new admin account you just made.

Finally, go back to Settings > Accounts > Other people and switch your old account (the one that uses your Microsoft Account email) to be a standard user.

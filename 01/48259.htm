  • What is the format of your USB Flash drive?
  • Are you unplugging your USB Flash drive directly from the port or are you ejecting it by right clicking the USB device from My Computer?
  • Are you able to see the Safely Remove Hardware icon on your system tray?

In the meantime, we can try to manually bring back the Safely Remove Hardware Window. To do so, you can follow the steps provided:

  1. Press Windows button + R to show the Run dialog box.
  2. Copy this command and paste it in the Run dialog box. “ rundll32.exe shell32.dll,Control_RunDLL hotplug.dll “
  3. Press Enter.

That will bring up the Safely Remove Hardware dialog which you can also use to stop the USB device you want to unplug. If you will close this window, the icon will restore back to the system tray.

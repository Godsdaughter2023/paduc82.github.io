deebrownsp say:

The scroll bar being so narrow was also a huge aggravation to me so I figured out a "work around" fix.  Here are the instructions:

Scroll Bar Too Narrow Here’s a Fix        6-30-16

 

 1. Open a webpage 

 

2. Click the icon to the left of the “Close Page X”  in the upper right corner of your screen and your page will become smaller. 

 

3. Move the page to the far right edge of the screen and to the top edge of the screen by clicking and holding your cursor on the top blank portion of the page (the blank portion of the page that is in line with the closing X) and drag the page to the top edge & to the right edge of the screen simultaneously.  The webpage should now be in the upper right portion of your screen.

4.  Place your cursor on the left edge of the page so you see the  <->  arrow and while holding your clicker down slowly drag the edge of the page all the way to the left of the screen.  Then release your clicker.     

 

5.  Do the same with the bottom of the page by placing your cursor on the bottom edge of the page so you see the  <->  arrow and while holding your clicker down slowly drag the edge of the page all the way down to the bottom of the screen.  Then release your clicker.

 

6.  Your scroll bar should now be wider and easier to see and use.

 

NOTE:  The resizing of your browser webpage like this  will stay in place this way even if you close and reopen the browser or shut down your computer and re-boot it.  I tested it on my computer and it stays.

Hi svetlik1406,

Thank you for posting on the Xbox forums. I'd be happy to answer your question.

Available languages for games is up to the developers to program that into the game which would only apply to the regional market the game is purchased in. Either there is an in-game setting, or the license you purchase/download from the Game Pass (based on your region) has the language programmed into it. If the developers said that the Russian language is only available in those regions and not in the UK, then you cannot get the Russian version of that game unfortunately. The Xbox Game Pass for PC is available in Russia but you need to purchase it from a local retailer. Game Pass and Gold are no longer sold online in Russia, but they are still sold in partnered retailer stores.

Also nowhere in the product description of the PC version of this game in the UK Microsoft Store store states the available languages. If you saw something related to that, it was just saying which languages are available but you still need to be in the correct region to get a license with the same language.

Compatibility mode

Follow the steps below on how to install driver in compatibility mode:

1. Download the Network adapter from the manufacturer’s website and save it on your local disk.

2. Right click on the setup file of the driver and select “Properties”.

3. Select “Compatibility” Tab.

4. Place a check mark next to “Run this program in Compatibility mode” and select operating system from the drop down list.

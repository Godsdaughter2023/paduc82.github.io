lsweeting say:

I had precisely this problem and I've just managed to solve it.

My touchpad only showed u as "PS/2 Compatible mouse" in device manager, but I managed to manually install the windows 7 drivers through device manager.

I found them by searching my C drive for "Sentelic" and they were located in the following file:

C:\eSupport\eDriver\Software\Touchpad\Sentelic\PointingDevice_For_Clickpad\XP32_Win7_32_Win7_64_9.1.7.0

I pointed the device manager driver installer there and it installed. After a restart I could scroll with the touchpad, although it was a bit choppy. I then downloaded the newer drivers for the sentelic touchpad from the ASUS support site here:  http://www.asus.com/uk/supportonly/UX31E/HelpDesk_Download/

You might have the Elantech touchpad installed instead, but hopefully you can find the drivers in the same location or by searching like I did.

I still can't get smart gesture to install, but that's not a problem now that I can scroll happily.

Hope this helps you.

SOLUTION to 0x80a4001a Sign-in error on Xbox One - Microsoft Community

klenis say:

SOLUTION to 0x80a4001a Sign-in error on Xbox One

REMOVE PROFILE, power cycle console, re-download profile

Saved games and progress acquired online are backed up in the cloud.  Gamerscores, achievements, purchased games/DLCs, etc are tied up to the account. Just make sure you know the email and password of the account you have issues with.

1. Press the Xbox button  to open the guide.
2. Select System > Settings > Account > Remove accounts.
3. Select the account that needs to be removed, and then select Remove to confirm.
4. When you're finished, select Close.

After that, do a power cycle on the console. Don't add the profile yet.

1. Turn off the Xbox One console by pressing the Xbox button on the front of the console for about 10 seconds until it shuts down completely.
2. Wait 2 minutes before powering it back on.

Please re-download your profile by selecting the icon at the upper-left corner of the dashboard. Select ADD NEW, input your email and password.

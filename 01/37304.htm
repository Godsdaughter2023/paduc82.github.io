Typically programs inform Windows they are a media playback program and this is automatic, and the computer won't sleep while media is playing. However, from what you describe, it seems IE and YouTube don't play well.

When you want to watch videos, simply go into "Power Options" menu and click the radio button next to the "Presentation" profile. This will turn off sleep and makes the screen stay on. You have to create the profile yourself. You can start by "copying" the default one that is there and changing the settings. Remember to make "Turn off display" to "Never", and "Hibernate after" to "Never".

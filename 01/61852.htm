stef_88 say:

Hi there,

I had a similar problem. After the latest round of updates my Surface no longer recognised the camera. The way I fixed it is as follows:

- go to Device Manager

- Check Sound, video and game controllers as suggested before

- in my case, the Intel(R) AVStream Camera 2500 entry was missing as described by others

To get it back and working again:

- check for an entry that reads Unknown devices

- there should be an unknown device in the drop down menu

- if you click on it, you will see its location as  Intel(R) HD Graphics 520, everything else will probably say unknown

- Go to the Driver tab

- Click update Driver (Rollback Driver is probably greyed out and not accessible)

- Click Browse my computer for driver software

- Click Let me pick from a list of device drivers on my computer

- There should now be a list in the next window that has a couple of Intel(R) AVStream Camera 2500 drivers in it

- select the one previous to the one that stopped working (you probably want the one before your current one)

- click next

- Your driver software will now install

- Restart your computer

-> The camera should now be working again.

Hope this helps someone! And thanks for all the posts and advice in this thread, which helped a lot in getting to the root of this problem...

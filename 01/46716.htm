ronnie vernon say:

Hi

The BCCode: 116.

Bug Check 0x116: VIDEO_TDR_ERROR:
http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ff557263(v=VS.85).aspx

The VIDEO_TDR_ERROR means Timeout Detection and Recovery.

Usually, when a TDR occurs, the system tries to restart the display driver to recover, which normal only causes the display to appear to blink and then continue.

In this case, the display driver stopped responding and the system was not able to recover. If this happens more than 6 times within 1 minute the blue screen is displayed and the computer shuts down to avoid any damage or corruption.

There can be many issues that cause this error to occur.

1. An outdated display driver.

2. Any hardware components that have been overclocked.

3. Too many graphic intensive programs running at the same time.

4. Gaming programs that try to use more graphic resources than the installed device can supply.

etc.

Things you can do:

Check the graphic card manufacturers website and download/install the latest video card drivers.

Check the manufacturers support forums for any current, known issues with the graphics device.

 

NVIDIA TDR Troubleshooting - NVIDIA Forums: http://forums.nvidia.com/index.php?showtopic=215256

 

Let us know what you find.

Hi,

I know this thread is years old, but no one seems to have answered the original question.

You set up your media center using your handheld IR remote but when you hit this stage of the set up what the Media Center needs to establish is what hardware you have to allow the Media Center to change the channel on the Sky box.

Lacking an opposable thumb and fingers, it obviously can't use your IR remote control or the Sky remote.

Instead, it needs to use an IR transmitter to allow it to change the channel on your Sky box. This is an IR blaster and is usually in the form of a 3.5mm headphone plug at one end of a 1-2m cable that has a small IR bulb at the end of it. This bulb is usually encased in a plastic housing with adhesive on one side of it to allow you to stick it on the front of your Sky box. 

You should be able to buy one for a couple of pounds on eBay or a decent quality one for less than £10.

  • The only way to downgrade from Windows 10 Pro to Home is by uninstalling Windows 10 Pro first. So, before downgrading to Windows 10 Home, you may need to back up all your data to prevent data lost.

  • Once Windows 10 reaches its expiration date, your PC will reboot every 3 hours, causing some unsaved data you're working on to be lost.

You can zip (compress) a file so that it takes up less space. This makes it easier to email or move the file. You can also zip multiple files into a single compressed folder. If you’re trying to extract everything in a compressed folder and don’t see the Extract all command, select the compressed folder, tap or click the Home tab, and tap or click Properties.

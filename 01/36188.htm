After the recent updates, I found that Control Panel would crash, causing a brief blank screen whenever initiated. This occurred regardless of user, with or without admin privileges and from any initialization location. Even clicking on the executable directly.

However, it did not happen in Safe Mode or Diagnostic Mode, which told me there was a conflict with some service.

Your Skype number is used for your contacts to reach or call your Skype account through a mobile or phone number.

techfreak_ say:

To add clarity to why this happens:

There is no explicit block occurring. It is working as intended. Unlike the default operation of Skype numbers, Skype to go access numbers restrict incoming calls to those that are assigned. Even when calls are forwarded they will retain the identity of the original caller (Caller ID or anonymous), not the ID of the access number. Attempting to use the Skype to go Access number system to daisy chain a call will not modify this behavior. Incoming calls simply do not gain the identity of each access number they are routed through.

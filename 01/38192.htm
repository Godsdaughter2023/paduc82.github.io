 I want to create a video Loop that has about 10 minutes of dead black space in between video clips how can I do this in Movie Maker?

considerate_guy say:

One easy way is to add a title (black background) with no text and set its duration to 10 minutes. Put that at the end of your clip. An alternate approach is to add a black image instead, again with a duration of 10 minutes.

As for the looping, Movie Maker isn't in control of that. It just creates a movie with the content you give it. If you want to do any looping, that has to be done in your media player. For example, in Windows Media Player, you can press Ctrl+T to toggle the looping any video that plays.

I had been trying to download Windows 10 on an Asus laptop with Windows 8.1 and after a lot of frustration I found the solution:

3) Bring up the program.

4) Let it determine your partitions.

5) When you see a horizontal bar graph at the bottom, use your cursor to slide the boundary between the unallocated partition and the main C drive so that the unallocated partition has slightly more space, at least 100 MB.

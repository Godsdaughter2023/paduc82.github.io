First of all, is the Windows 7 copy you bought a retail copy or an OEM
copy. If it's a retail copy, you can do this. If it's an OEM copy, its
license ties it permanently to the first computer it's installed on
and it may never be moved to another.

If it's a retail copy, simply uninstall it from the Netbook; there is
no process called deactivation. Then install it on your main machine
and activate it. *Online* activation will fail, but just do the
telephone activation you are prompted to do; it's quick and easy.

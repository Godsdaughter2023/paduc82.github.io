Hi Alain,

Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Community. I understand your concern and I’ll be glad to assist you.

Go to Disk Management and make a note of the left to right layout of the partitions in the graphical layout as this is important when shrinking/extending partitions.

If your unallocated space is immediately to the right of the C drive, all you need to do is right-click the C drive graphic and select Extend Volume.

You can only do this if the unallocated space is contiguous with the original C drive.

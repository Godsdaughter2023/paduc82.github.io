If you are trying to defrag your computer's hard drive to minimize its slow performance, you may refer to the steps below:

  1. On the Search bar, type Defragment and Optimize Drives.
  2. Open the Optimize Drivers window.
  3. Once you have selected the hard drive, click Analyze. We always recommend that you click Analyze first before optimizing. If the result is less than 10% then most likely, optimization is no longer needed.
  4. Click Optimize button and wait for the process to complete.

Please take note that defrag may take some time depending on the number files and the size of your hard drive. You also have an option to set an optimization schedule at your most convenient time:

  1. On Optimize Drivers window, click Change settings.
  2. From the drop-down menu, you will have an option to choose Daily, Weekly, Monthly.
  3. Next to Drives, click Choose button and select the drives where you want to apply the schedule.
  4. Click OK to apply changes.
  5. Click OK again then Close.

Let us know if you need further assistance.

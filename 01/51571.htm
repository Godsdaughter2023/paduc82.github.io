xircal say:

Chkdsk corrects disk errors only if you specify the /f command-line option.

Chkdsk /r scans the whole drive for bad sectors and fixes any errors found within them. If it doesn't find any bad sectors it terminates. It doesn't go on to fix errors found outside of bad sectors. 

However, you can check the HD for errors by doing the following.

  1. Open File Explorer and right click your C: drive.

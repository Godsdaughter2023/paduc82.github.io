3. If you do not have a mapped network drive to the remote computer, click the "Map Network Drive" button to open the wizard.

8. Click the Windows "Start" button again and click "Computer."

9. Double-click the "C" drive and navigate to the location of the Windows 7 backup image.

10. Drag and drop the image from the opened "C Drive" window to the opened mapped network drive.

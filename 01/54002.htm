Computers today are capable to boot from numerous devices including CD/DVD drive and Flash Drive/USB devices apart from HDD. If your computer tries to boot from flash drive / disc without any master boot record, it will throw this error message.

Try to remove any USB devices and CD/DVD from your PC. Try to make sure the boot sequence point to your HDD first in BIOS setting.

TCP/IP Netbios Helper service helps make things work when you don't use Netbios on your network and you use SMB (file sharing, etc) connections to servers over IPv4.

kenneth_f say:

Hi

One possible reason why you are seeing two accounts under one email address is because you might have signed in to Skype using your account's associated email address instead of your Skype Name without merging them first, thus resulting to a new Skype account.

All Skype accounts were transitioned to basic Microsoft accounts last October 2016, although users with legacy accounts (Skype Name-created accounts) still need to fully update their accounts to enjoy the benefits of a full Microsoft account.

If you are not seeing the Skype credits on your account, there is a possibility that you have signed in to a different Skype account, probably your Microsoft account (email address). I suggest you to make sure that you have signed in the correct Skype name (username). If you are not sure of your Skype name, you can check the order confirmation from Skype, your Skype name is indicated in the order details.

Also, if the Skype account was already linked to your Microsoft account, it is not longer possible to unlink them. Here is the related FAQ link: Can I merge or unlink my Skype and Microsoft accounts?

To add a computer under your child’s Microsoft account, you can simply sign in with the Child’s Microsoft account on the computer and verify it.

Follow the steps:

Go to Settings>Accounts>Your info.

Click on Sign in with a Microsoft account instead option.

Follow the on-screen instructions.

Once done, verify the account.

Go to Settings>Account>Your info.

Click on Verify.

Follow the on-screen instructions.

 

The child’s Microsoft account is now setup on the computer and verified.

To check and confirm if the devices are added, log on to https://login.live.com/ with your child account, go to View Account (top right icon menu), and then on the top menu bar click Devices. You will see list of associated devices. Check if all the three devices appear in the list.

 

As you have mentioned the issue with “gets shut off”, if the computer is getting shutdown while the child is using, it could be possible that you have set a screen time limit for your Child’s Microsoft account.

Follow the steps to check and remove the screen time limit:

Go to https://login.live.com/ and sign-in with your Microsoft account.

Find your child’s name and select Screen time.

Check the option Use one screen time schedule and see if it is ON or OFF.

Switch OFF from ON, if the screen time limit is turned on.

After installing the Feature Pack for my Windows N version (1709) it worked perfectly well.

Hi,

One of the possible causes why you are having this issue is due to the settings that appear on the System Settings page are derived from the registry settings for the preferred power plan. A power plan is a collection of hardware settings and system settings that manages how your computer uses power. Power plans can help you save energy and maximize system performance.

For initial troubleshooting, we suggest that you run the Power troubleshooter. To do so, follow these steps:

  1. On your keyboard, Press Windows key + X, then select Control Panel.
  2. On the upper right part of Control Panel, type Troubleshooting.
  3. Click View all on the left navigation pane, then select Power.
  4. Click Advanced on the power troubleshooter dialog box, then click Run as administrator or enter the credentials if required. Click Next.
  5. Follow onscreen instruction complete the Power troubleshooter.
  6. Restart the device.

Keep us updated with the result.

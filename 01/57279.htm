If that doesn't work, one easy way to fix it is the "repair button" you mentioned.  In this case, System Restore.  You just choose a date/time before the problem happened, and all your system files are restored to that time.  It only takes 2 minutes, it won't touch any of your files, and the best part is you can undo it if you don't like the results.  It is by far the safest and easiest option.

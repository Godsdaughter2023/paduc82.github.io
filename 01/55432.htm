vinod archak say: Hi,
 
1) Go to your User Accounts and see if there are any unfamiliar accounts that are Administrator. If so, delete the accounts. Make sure you have an Administrator account that you have created and change the password so you are the only one who knows the password.
Then delete any accounts that you are not familiar with or don't want on your computer. Do not delete a Network Service account or Local Service account.
Do not delete Administrator, or System or Guest account. However, you can disable the Guest account.
 
2) Change your user account password every few days until you know that you are no longer being compromised.
 
3) Right click on the taskbar and open up Task Manager. Go to the users tab. There you can see if there are any other users connected to your computer. If there are users you are not familiar with, highlight the account and disconnect the user.
 
4) Get your computer secure before you go out on the internet. Only connect to internet when needed. You can turn off your router if you are not on your computer. Download the appropriate Windows 7 .ISO file (must match what your product key version is for)


Windows 7 Home Premium 32Bit: http://msft.digitalrivercontent.net/win/X17-58996.iso
Windows 7 Home Premium 64Bit: http://msft.digitalrivercontent.net/win/X17-58997.iso

Windows 7 Professional 64Bit: http://msft.digitalrivercontent.net/win/X17-59186.iso


Windows 7 Ultimate 32Bit: http://msft.digitalrivercontent.net/win/X17-59463.iso
Windows 7 Ultimate 64Bit: http://msft.digitalrivercontent.net/win/X17-59465.iso


How to find out if I have a 32 or 64Bit version of Windows installed on my computer:
http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows7/find-out-32-or-64-bit


After downloading the correct .iso file use ImgBurn or Gear ISO to create a bootable DVD.

This will need to be done from facebook itself.

1. Log-in to your facebook account from your computer or Internet Explorer on your Xbox 360 Console.

-

4. Under Apps you Use, locate Xbox Live, to the far right of the app you will see an "x". Select the X to remove the app.

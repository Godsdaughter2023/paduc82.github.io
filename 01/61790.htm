karen_[01] say:

Hello eiaserarafat,

Welcome to Skype Community forum!

To activate your voice messaging:

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. In the Manage Features section, select Call forwarding.
  3. Select the button to enable call forwardingand then select how many seconds to forward calls after.
  4. Select Voice messaging, then Confirm. 

It will be activated immediately, and once someone try to call you but you missed it or you're not logged in, the voicemail will appear on your Skype account.

To send someone a voice message:

  1. Sign in to Skype application.
  2. Click Contacts and select the person you want to leave a message and click the call button.
  3. If the call was not picked up, you will be asked to leave a voice message.

Please note that if the contact you want to leave a voice message did not enable the voice messaging in Skype, it would not be possible for you to leave one. Also, it will depend on the contact's settings how many seconds it will take before you were routed to his/her voicemail.

I hope this information helps, but if you have further questions and clarifications, please do not hesitate to reply to this post.

a. user say:

Hi, Jan,

 

What is your Windows Operating System?

Which email client are you using?

 

A tracking feature in not available in web email like Gmail, Yahoo! Mail or the new Windows Live Hotmail,

 

If you have Windows Live Mail installed on your computer, then access the Windows Live Mail options (Not Hotmail options)

If the Windows Mail options don’t appear,

1. Open Windows Live Mail and Press ‘Alt’ key on the key board, this will bring up the Menu bar.

2. Click on Tools and then click on options.

3. Click on the Receipt Tab.

4. Check the box next to ‘Request a read receipt for all sent messages’.

 

Using Outlook:

 

1) Create a new mail message like you would normally do.Before compiling the email click Options and then tick the un-ticked box in the tracking toolbar that says"Request a delivery receipt"

 

2) If you would like to add a read receipt for every email you send do the following:

Tools > Options

In the preference tab Under the heading email click on the "E-mail options"Button.

A new box will appear, click the "tracking options" button.
In tracking options tick the box that says"Read receipt"

Click OK then OK again and finally Apply.

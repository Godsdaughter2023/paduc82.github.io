glovebox22 say:

Babu, 

  Thanks for the response. The issue was actually caused by the computer being a member of the Homegroup. Apparently homegroup credentials for a machine supercede that of an installed credential. Moreover, they cannot be removed while the PC is a member of that Homegroup.

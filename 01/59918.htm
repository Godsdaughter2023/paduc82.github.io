  1) Open C:\Users\Default\AppData\Local\Microsoft\Windows Live
  2) This directory will have two subdirectories, with one file each. Go into each directory and apply the following:
    a) Right-click on the file, open Properties.
b) Click the Security tab.
c) It will give a message: "To continue, you must be an administrative user with permission to view this object's security properties. Do you want to continue?". Click the Continue button.
d) Click the Add... button.
e) Enter "Everyone" into the box at the bottom of the dialog. Click "Check Names"; it should underline the "Everyone". Click OK.
f) Ensure the new "Everyone" entry has "Read" and "Read & execute" checks under the "Allow" column.

   After doing this for both files, attempt to login to the account. It should go through.

 

 

That procedure (bascially) worked... although the various responses on my system were slightly different along the way (possibly owing to slightly different Win 7 versions? or because I was already logged in as admin?).

and

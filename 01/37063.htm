When doing the initial update of a new installation it often takes more than one round through windows update to get all of the patches.

It is obviously stuck on one.

  • Break out of the update, worst case, just turn off the power. 
  • Restart,
  • go into Windows Update,  
  • find Update history.
  • Find the last update that failed.
  • double click to see details. Trouble shoot the problem, there are often links to possible solutions
  • repeat windows update scan. This time limit the number of updates you apply. Start with just the one that failed,
  • once you get past the one casusing the trouble, pick a small subset of the ones avaialble
  • keep repeateing until you can do a couple of update scans and not find anything
  • after you get the updates all done do a full image copy backup of your computer so that you have a known good point to restore to that will not require applying all of these patches again.

Follow the below steps and check if it helps.

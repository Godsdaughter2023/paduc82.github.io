Have you tried manually changing the wifi channel in the hub?

The hubs automatic wifi channel selector isn't always reliable. So it's best to try changing the channel manually.

First switch off xbox then using the web browser on your computer type 192.168.1.254 in the address bar.

aaron zhu msft say:

Hi Dave,

 

I now understand that you’re using pure Skype for Business Online and the other organization is using Skype for Business on-premises server. I’d like to explain that Skype for Business external connectivity (federation) enables a Skype for Business user to connect with users in other organizations that use Skype for Business as well as those that host their own Skype for Business Server on-premises.

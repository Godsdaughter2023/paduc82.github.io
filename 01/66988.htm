There are two Skype version that you can use on Windows. andre da costa say:
There is no way to combine them. You can backup the files from one account, restore in the account you want to keep, then discard the account.

Step 1: http://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/wiki...

Step 2: Make sure the account you decide you want to keep is an Administrator.
open Start > Settings > Accounts > Family and other people
Select the account
Click Change account type
Choose Administrator then click Next

Step 3: delete the old account, log into the account - open Start > Settings > Accounts > Family and other people

Select the account
Then click Remove

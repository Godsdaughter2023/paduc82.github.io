vijay a. verma say:
Windows 10 license and Office 365 license are independent of each other. Existence or non-existence of one product doesn't matter for the other. Hence, you can buy Office 365 license without having Windows 10 or vice versa.

Q. If I need to buy windows 10 license first and subscribe to Microsoft 365
A. Not required. But ideally, you would like to have an OS first and then buy applications for that OS.

Q. If I only need to subscribe the Microsoft 365 plan to have the Windows 10 licensing
A. Buying Office 365 doesn't entitle you to Windows 10. Similarly buying Windows 10 doesn't entitle you to Office 365.

Q. When we stop the subscription, the W10 and Office licenses will still be valid on the computers or they are "virtual licenses", only valid for the subscription period.
A. W10 is one time payment and is not on the basis of subscription. Hence, no concept of stopping the subscription for W10.
Office 365 licenses are valid only during the period of the subscription. If you don't renew Office 365, the license will expire and Office 365 will go into Read only mode.
There is another version Office 2016 which is one time payment and not on subscription model. Hence, the license is perpetual.

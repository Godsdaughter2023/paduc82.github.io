By default Pressing “Alt + Shift” key combination switches input language when multiple languages are installed and pressing “Ctrl + Shift” switches the keyboard layout.

5. Click on Change key sequence button to assign whichever key combination you prefer.
You may also opt not to assign anything as per your convenience.

If you remove the key assignment, you will have to click on the language bar to change the input language.

elohmn say:

It's both complicated and a little "off" the way it was created. Just about any Rep (Avoid or Prefer) will raise Stars if the Player also has many other Players that left no Rep. I know, now you're probably saying ??!!!

It works like this. Think of a brand new Profile that has never seen any other Players via Xbox LIVE (Rep or not). Now, that Player goes into a MP Game and plays against (I'll just round it off) 30 other Players. One of those Players leaves behind an Avoid Rep against the Profile. This means 29 Players left no Rep and 1 Player left Avoid Rep. The Stars on that Profile will go up because it is built by Default to basically look at all the other 29 Players and say, they left no Rep so overall the Profile was more "positive" then "negative" while on Xbox LIVE. It's 29 vs 1, the Stars go up.

It's designed like this to keep out people from just lowering a Star Count on a Player by means of Trolling with Avoid. Out of all of the thousands upon thousands of other Players most of us have encountered by now (that left no Rep), there is no way a handful of sore losers will ever lower anyone's Stars.

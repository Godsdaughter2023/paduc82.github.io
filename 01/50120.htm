nycmoonstar say:

That is perhaps because the system clock is set wongly on the CMOS. When the system wakes up to record a TV show, it's going by system's internal clock. To fix this, get in the BIOS and adjust the time on CMOS.

Before your computer boots into Windows, you should see black screens with white letters on them. At the bottom of the screen should tell you what key on your keyobord to press in order to enter BIOS. It's the DELETE key on my GIGABYTE motherboard, and then I can set the clock on the CMOS.

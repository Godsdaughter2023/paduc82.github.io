considerate_guy say:

When you say the videos are imported directly from the camera, do you mean you are adding them directly into your project while the camera is connected to the computer?

If so, that's the issue. Movie Maker doesn't actually "import" your video. It instead just references the media wherever you tell it to find it. If you are telling it to retrieve the video directly off the memory/SD card of the camera, then every time it needs to preview that clip it will be doing it dynamically over the USB cable to the camera's memory.

You want to make sure you first import/copy all those videos onto your PC's hard disk. Then work from there instead of from the camera.

The next question is about the exact model number of the Canon IXUS. It's possible that the video format isn't 100% compatible with Movie Maker. I could tell you more if I knew the model number.

If they do turn out to be a problem, look for a video conversion program that can convert to .wmv to .mp4. I can't specifically recommend one here so you'll have to do some research to find one that works.

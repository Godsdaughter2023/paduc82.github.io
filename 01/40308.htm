Hi

First of all, welcome to Skype Community!

It is unusual that the application automatically shares your screen when on a video call.

In this case, I strongly suggest to follow the following troubleshooting steps:

1.    Make sure Skype is no longer open. Quit Skype, then in the menu bar click Go > Go to folder.
2.    Type ~/Library/Application Support/Skype/ into the box and click Go.
3.    Drag the file called shared.xml to the trash.
4.    Go to the folder that has your Skype Name, and locate config.xml and drag it to the trash.

5.    Open your Applications folder, and drag your copy of Skype to the trash.

6.   Empty the trash.

7.    Download and install the latest version of Skype.

I also advise you to update your camera driver for your device. You may check your manufacturer's website on how you can update your webcam driver.

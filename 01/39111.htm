At the same time, however, there is a setting in Windows that you can use to turn off your computers ability to be remoted into.

There is no way to specify one subscription over the other. When it comes to minute subscriptions, the smallest amount of minutes get consumed first. Since both of your subscriptions are unlimited, they basically have equal preference, so there is nothing to switch between. You will be covered in both scenarios.

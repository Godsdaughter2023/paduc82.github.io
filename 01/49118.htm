eschapin say:

how to add multiple addresses from email to contact?

It used to be easy back with Outlook but with Windows Live Mail, I can't find a way to add multiple email addresses from a single email to one group contact.  Is there a way to batch add that doesn't involve adding one at a time, and adding them to a group, and hope I didn't miss someone?

ralph_ys say:

Hi ESChapin,

 

Welcome to the Microsoft Community Forum.

 

Thank you for posting your concern here. As you mentioned, you wanted to add email addresses from an email to one group contact. For you to add these email addresses into a group contact, you need them to add first to your contact list. Once they are on your contact list, you can create a group and add them into your desired group, which in Windows Live Mail it is called Category. Here are the steps on how to do it:

 

1. Sign in to your Windows Live Mail.

2. Open the email that has the contacts you wanted to add into a group.

3. Right click or Double click their name or email addresses and add them to your contact list.

4. After adding them to your Contacts, go to Contacts and select Category.

5. Enter the Category name and add all the contacts you wanted on that group.

6. Click Save and you now have their contacts on your desired group.

 

Hope this helps. Let us know if you need further assistance regarding this.

When your computer doesn't have enough memory for all of the actions it's trying to perform, Windows and your programs can stop working. To help prevent information loss, Windows will notify you when your computer is low on memory. You can also learn to recognize the signs of low memory and take steps to prevent the problem.

Signs of low memory include poor performance, low-memory or out-of-memory notifications, and display problems. For example, if you try to open a menu in a program when your computer is low on memory, the program might respond slowly or appear to stop responding. If the menu appears, it might not respond when you try to click an item or it might not display all items. If you click a menu item, the menu might also disappear and leave a blank area on the screen instead of displaying the contents of the document or file you're working in.

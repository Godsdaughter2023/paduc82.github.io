a. user say:

Unless you are writing your own drivers for XP and using the Windows Driver Foundation Service to help you debug them, you should disable the WDF Service.  There is no need for this Service to be starting and running all day with nothing to do - it just slows your system down. 

There are plenty of XP Services you can do without and WDF is one of them (unless you are writing your own XP drivers).

Click Start, Run and in the box enter:

services.msc

Click OK to launch the Services Applet

Locate the Windows Driver Foundation - User-mode Driver Framework Service, right click the Service, select Properties and choose
Note that you may not be able to "Stop" the Service first, but that is okay.

If you try to keep WDF running, on startup you may start to see complaining messages (really from Windows Media Player) of this fashion:

Application Error : The instruction at "0x7c91b21a" referenced memory at "0x00000010". The memory could not be "written"
The instruction at "0x7c91b21a" referenced memory at 0x00000010"
application error 0x7c91b21a 0x00000010
The instruction at "0x7c928fea"referenced memory at "0x00000010". The memory could not be 'written' 

Disable the WDF Service, your system will reward you with better perfromance and be on your way.

afzal taher say:

Hi,

Windows Photo Gallery is designed to automatically display the pictures and videos stored in the Pictures folder on your computer. It is not a replacement for folders
on your computer. As a result, you should not delete the pictures in Photo Gallery or the Pictures folder unless you actually intend to delete the picture from your computer.
If you do, they will be deleted from your computer and no longer appear in either the Pictures folder or in Photo Gallery.

You can Import your Pictures to your external hard drive without using Photo Gallery.

Follow these steps:
a. Open Photo Gallery and Add the folder(s) of the pictures by clicking on file and then clicking on Add folder to Gallery.
b. Browse to the folder you want to add.

When you want to remove the Pictures, remove the folder(s) from Photo Gallery, not the pictures.

From the Gallery, right click on the folder and click on Remove from Gallery.

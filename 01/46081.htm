aileen alf say:

Hi Hannah,

It's possible that the Pinch Zoom feature is enabled on your device. To check, follow these steps:

  1. Type Mouse & Touchpad settings and select the best match.
  2. Click on Additional mouse option.
  3. Click on the Device Settings tab and click on the Settings button.
  4. From the left side panel, click Pinch Zoom option and uncheck the box next to Enable Pinch Zoom.
  5. Click Apply and OK.
  6. Close the window and check if the changes are effective.

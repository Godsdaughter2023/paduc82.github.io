How to back up and restore the registry in Windows

jomel_g say:

Hello

Welcome to Skype Community!

I can imagine the inconvenience this has been causing you. Let me help.

Please make sure that your Skype audio settings is set to the audio driver you are using. Please follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to Skype for Windows 10 (UWP).
  2. Click your profile icon.
  3. Scroll down to see the Skype settings.
  4. Scroll down and locate Audio settings.
  5. Select the playback device you are using.

If the problem persists, please update your audio driver by following the steps below:

1. Press Windows Key + X key from your keyboard, and click Device Manager.
2. Expand Audio input and output.
3. Right-click on the Speakers listed and click on Update Drive Software.
4. Restart your computer after the update.

I hope this helps.

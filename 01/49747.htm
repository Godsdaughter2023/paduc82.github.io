For £35, you should have received a reasonably large hard drive.  I don't know what UK prices are, but I'd suspect that amount should have bought something in the neighborhood of 250 or 320 GB.

Before you take it back, try the following.

Open Device Manager (right-click My Computer > Properties > Hardware > Device Manager) and see if there is an entry for "Floppy Disk Drives."  If so, expand the entry by clicking on the + and then double click on the "Floppy disk drive" (without the "s") entry.  Look at the "General" tab.  Ensure that the box named "Device usage" shows "Use this device (enable)".  If not, make it so.  See what is said under "Device status" and click the "Troubleshoot" button if necessary.

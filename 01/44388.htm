I've tried sending, then walking away for 20 minutes.  When I return they are still in outbox and message at bottom right says it's still publishing.  It's not even timing out on me. I have not changed anything on my computer and my last windows update was June 9.  I am using windows 7 64. Here is a list of how I do it and the fixes I have tried.

I go into my pictures, choose the pictures I want to send, then choose send to mail recipient.  When the box comes up, I choose attach, which then opens the live mail, and then I send. 

I am always logged into my live ID when I do this and my name is displayed at top right.  I have always done it this way without issue.  I tried sending from within, so choosing add an attachment from within live mail and it still causes a problem. 

I have tried sending different pictures with the same issue.  I have tried just sending one picture vs many and still it/they are stuck in the outbox.

I am able to send the pictures through one drive by choosing share then selecting the pictures.  It says that they have been sent.  Unfortunately, I really don't want to send them this way. 

I have tried to send a different attachment (word document) and it works.  Also, I have no problem sending or receiving text messages.  I am only having problems sending pictures all of a sudden

I checked my firewall and nothing seems to be getting blocked. 

I have tried doing a repair of windows essentials from add/remove and that didn't fix the problem.

Thanks in advance.

Jennifer

karen_[01] say:

Hello

Unfortunately, the option to change Skype name is not possible. If you wish to use a different username, yes, you'll need to create a new account.

To close your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to Sign in with a different Microsoft account. 
  2. Check that the page shows the correct Microsoft account, and then click Next.
  3. Read the list, and select the checkboxes to acknowledge you’ve read each item.
  4. In the Select a reason drop-down list, choose the reason you're closing the account.
  5. Select Mark account for closure.

Note: Your account will be closed after 60 days but if you change your mind anytime during the 60 day period, all you have to do is sign in to your account again to cancel the closure.

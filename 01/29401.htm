Firstly uncheck the option to "Lock the taskbar" because when the taskbar is locked, the sizing handles aren't visible.

To change the size of the taskbar, follow these steps:
 
a. Move the mouse pointer to the thin bar that appears along the top strip of the taskbar, until the mouse pointer turns to a two-headed arrow.
 
b. Hold down the left mouse button and drag that top edge up or down until the taskbar is the width you want, then release the mouse button.

    gangstu say:

    Go Settings > TV & OneGuide > Troubleshooting > Enter Device Remote Code > Enter cable or satellite box remote code. Enter C0877. This will give you all functions except for "Skip backward" and it won't pop up the details page.

    You can use code C1877 to get all features, including "Skip backward", but it will pop up the details page, but you can quickly say "Xbox, Play" after changing channels to close this page.

bruce hagen ~ mvp say:

Messages that were sent to Windows Mail via a POP3 account will remain even if you remove the account.

Tools | Accounts will give you a few options. You can either remove the account completely, or go to the properties of the account and under the General tab, just uncheck the box to "Include this account........". That will leave it set up, but dormant.

Another option is to go to Tools | Options | General and uncheck: Send & Receive Messages At Startup, and Check For Messages Every X Minutes. If you uncheck these options, you can open WinMail and use it if you need to, but it won't do anything unless you do a manual Send/Receive.

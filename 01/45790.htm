kerry_brown say:

You can do this by setting the scope for the Remote Desktop rules in the firewall.

Open the "Windows Firewall with Advanced Security" tool. Type firewall in the search box then click on it. Click on "Inbound Rules". Scroll down to the Remote Desktop rules. There may be more than one. You'll have to change all the active ones, i.e. those that are green. Change the scope to only allow the IP addresses you want.

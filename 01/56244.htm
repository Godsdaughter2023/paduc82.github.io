mark bu say:

Hello Eric,

 

IMAP is supported within the Mail app on Windows RT, you have already confirmed that by stating that you are able to receive mail. Sending Mail uses SMTP. You may be encountering an issue with the SSL/TLS settings when sending mail.

 

Here is the how SSL/TLS connections behave on Mail app.

 

Send with port 25 (Server requires SSL check box unchecked)

1.       If your email server supports STARTTLS, the Mail app will do unsecured connection followed with a TLS handshake.

2.       If your email server doesn’t support STARTTLS, the Mail app will send email with unsecured connection.

 

Send with port 465/587

1.       If Server requires SSL check box checked, Mail app will do direct SSL connection to the given port (usually port 465).

2.       If Server requires SSL check box unchecked, Mail app will do unsecured connection follow with TLS handshake. (usually port 587)

 

You may want to check with your email provider and try adjusting these settings as it may allow you to send your email.

 

Mark

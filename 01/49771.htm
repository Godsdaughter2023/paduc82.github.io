This usually happens on laptops depending on power adaptor connected or if your laptop is running on a battery. To save some power, it usually changes the desktop background in your computer automatically.
 
I'd suggest you do this:
 
a. Click start, Control Panel.

f. Set Slide show under Desktop background settings to Paused and then click OK to save the changes.

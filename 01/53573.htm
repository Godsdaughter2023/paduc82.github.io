Almost completed retired but the original solution was by modem over a standard telephone, although possible it is very hard to share this type of connection.

 

Far more common in domestic situations is ADSL on top of your phone service. This is normally indicated by the use of a splitter connected to your line, one output connected to a standard phone and the other to an adapter connected to the computer. In this case you need an ADSL router. Most ISPs can provide one or their website will have the settings required to use your own.

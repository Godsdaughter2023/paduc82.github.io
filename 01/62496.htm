walrusgriper say: I'm sorry, how do I disable the office task? 

kurogami87 say:

- Open the task scheduler as administrator

- In the top left of the window you should see the 'library'. double-click it

- Open the folder 'Microsoft', then 'Office'

- In the next window you should find the tasks OfficeBackgroundTaskHandlerLogon and  OfficeBackgroundTaskHandlerRegistration

- Check the time time of the ....Registration -task to see if it fits with the time your command prompt starts and closes.

- If it matches, then this task should be the culprit

- Right-click and deactivate it

There is no option for you to pin a group to your phone's home screen, but you can pin your group for it to be shown on top of your Chats tab inside the Skype application.

  1. From the Chats screen, tap and hold on the group you want to manage. 
  2. Select Pin Chat.

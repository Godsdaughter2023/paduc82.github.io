considerate_guy say:

Open up Movie Maker, go to the first menu (File / Alt+F) and look under Recent Projects.  You should see the project file used to create your movie. Click the project you want to open.

Now this time, when you go to Save Movie, instead of choosing the Burn to DVD option, choose to save it to your PC.  Pick the desired resolution (or let it pick what it thinks is best).  That will create a movie file.  You can then copy that movie file onto your USB flash drive.

Another option is to look into DVD copying options.  You may already have some software that came with your PC. Or you can try doing it with free software like ImgBurn. Do a search for "ImgBurn DVD Copy".

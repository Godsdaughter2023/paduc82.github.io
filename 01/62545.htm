Hi

 

Even on a 64bit computer the amount of available RAM will be less than the amount that is installed.

 

This is because the BIOS will reserve a certain amount of the installed RAM for use with the installed hardware (usually the graphics device).

 

You can view the details of the way that this memory is being used in the Resource Monitor.

 


Press the WinKey+R to display the Run dialog box.

 

Type  resmon  in the Open box and click OK.

 

In the Resource Monitor window, look at the Physical Memory section.

 

The amount of RAM noted in the Hardware Reserved section is the amount that is not available to programs and apps.

 

You can hover the Mouse Cursor over any item to see a popup description.

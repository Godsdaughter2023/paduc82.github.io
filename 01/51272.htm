a. user say:

 Hi
Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Community.

From the provided information, I understand that you face the issue with loading recorded video into movie maker, it shows black screen.

Ensure that you have the proper codecs to play your videos and audio files – audio and video require codecs to play the file and it’s important to have the right codecs installed or updated in your PC.
 
 
   - Try running the Microsoft FixIt tool for codecs: FixIT
   -  Media info will be able to identify any issue with missing of faulty codecs and whether it's already in your computer or not: Media Info
 

Another possibility is because of your video card drivers are not updated. Movie Maker makes extensive use of the video card GPU (graphics processing unit). The display driver affects the reliability, performance, and compatibility of the video card with different software.
To determine the version and video card driver date, run the DirectX Diagnostic Tool (dxdiag):
 
DirectX Diagnostic Tool
1. On your desktop, click Windows button + F on your keyboard.
2. Click Apps then type dxdiag then hit Ok.
3. Click on the Display tab and you will see the driver details including the driver version.
4. If your video card driver seems outdated please go to the video card driver manufacturer and see if a newer version is available.
Example:
http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us
http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/Pages/index.aspx
http://downloadcenter.intel.com/

jeeped say:

I'm sorry to say, but if you are working within a Windows 7 environment, you are using old tools. Windows 7 has the 'snipping tool' that greatly eases the task of a small cropped screen shot. If you navigate the cutting rectangle correctly, no cropping or other work is required. It can be directly saved as .PNG, .GIF, JPG or .MHT or sent directly as an email attachment with or without saving the image.

Click your Start button and type 'snip'. The Snipping Tool should be evident in the results above. Click on it and follow the supplied directions and basic Windows operations to create your pre-cropped screen shot.

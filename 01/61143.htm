mythicdawn say:

How to clear Windows 10 Sync Cloud Backup?

"When sync is turned on, Windows keeps track of the settings you care about and sets them for you on all your Windows 10 devices."

I am curious how to delete the cloud backup for Windows 10 on my Microsoft account so it won't sync a previous installation's settings.

a. user say:

Hi,

 

You cannot delete the backed up/previously stored sync settings from the cloud storage on the PC. However, you will be able to disable certain features to not sync on cloud storage from Settings window on the PC.

 

To turn off features not sync to the cloud storage, follow the steps below:

 

  1. Press Windows + I keys on the keyboard to open Settings window and click on Accounts.
  2. From the left side panel click on Sync your settings and slide the bar to Off for the features you do not want to sync under Individual sync settings.
  3. Close the window and check if the changes are effective.

 

Refer to the link below on About sync settings on Windows 10 devices:

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-in/windows-10/about-sync-settings-on-windows-10-devices

 

Write to us with the status of the issue on the same post for further assistance, we will be happy to help you.

There is one important aspect to bear in mind. If the license(s) you refer to have been supplied with either or both of these PCs then under the terms of the license agreement they are not transferable to another PC.

If however they are full boxed product licenses purchased separately from the PC in question (and not an OEM pack) then provided they are removed from the original hardware then the license can be re-applied to a new PC.

The activiation process will be reinitialised if the image has been transferred to another hardware platform and will happen every time. Even automatic reactivation will work at least once  but if it happens too often then you will be instructed to phone the activation centre. The fact is that you can only have it licensed on as may PCs as you own the licenses for.

The EULA makes no specific allowance for the same license to be installed on a "live" system and a mothballed one not in use.

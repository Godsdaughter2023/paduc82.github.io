In Timeline view...try dragging the Scrollbar at the bottom
of the Timeline to the position you are looking for. Then left
click on the Time track to advance the Playback Indicator.
To split the clip...type..Ctrl+L.

It's possible that your project is too complex for your
system resources. It's also possible that your source files
have compatibility issues with Movie Maker.

Many formats are reportedly compatible with
Movie Maker but the most reliable choices are:

Photos - bmp
Video -   wmv or dv-avi
Music -   wmv, wma, wav

It's usually the best bet to create (Publish) several smaller
.wmv movies and save them in a folder. Then when you
create a DVD each one will be a Scene (Chapter).

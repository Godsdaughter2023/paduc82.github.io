Q: What are some of the cool new game mechanics in Gears of War 4?

A: There is quite a few new gameplay mechanics and enhancements in Gears of War 4, here's a few key ones to mention.

 

1 – The Vault Maneuver (close cover combat)

  • The vault maneuver helps players move through the environment faster and results in exciting execution moves when done properly. 
  • Players can hold Up and press B while running towards cover to smoothly hop over and keep running.  This is great for closing gaps with enemies behind cover
  • If you Vault directly into an enemy, you will stun them temporarily.  Hold the Y button to perform a cool execution

 

2 – The Yank Maneuver (close cover combat)

  • The yank maneuver helps players engage with enemies on the opposite side of a piece of cover and results in exciting execution moves when done properly. 
  • Players can hold Up and press B while in cover across from an enemy who is also in cover.  If performed correctly, the player will pull the enemy over the cover and stun them temporarily, allowing you execute them by Holding Y

 

3 – Destructible Cover

  • In a number of levels, cover that you and the enemy hide behind can be destroyed, exposing them to easy take downs.  Swarm pods are a good example as there are large debris/wood piles that can be destroyed to help in getting the enemy out of cover and exposed to gunfire.

  • Soldier: Soldiers start with a Lancer, Gnasher, Snub pistol and grenades, and play like the classic Gears of War archetype. As an all-purpose class, Soldiers focus on killing enemies wherever they're found. Soldiers can improve things like explosive damage, ammo capacity, durability and reload speeds.

Some of the skills include increasing the damage of fortifications, their speed, durability, in addition to decreasing construction costs.

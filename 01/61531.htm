"We're sorry, but something went wrong.  Your Microsoft account wasn't changed to a local account....."

This has taken me almost 3 years to figure out.

I had to add my Microsoft Account to the Windows Activation setting (NOTE: Windows was already activated, I just had to link it to my Microsoft Account I use to sign-in to the computer with).

  1. Click on the Windows Logo Start
  2. Click on the Settings Gear icon
  3. Click on the last icon Update & Security
  4. Select Activation
  5. Click Add a Microsoft Account
  6. Sign-in with your Microsoft Account

Now try to switch to a local account.

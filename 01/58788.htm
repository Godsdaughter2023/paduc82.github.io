    Hi,

    Method 1:
    To learn the controls for the STB, go through the TV Signal setup again and follow these steps:

    a)       On the Automatic TV Signal Setup Screen, select "I will manually configure my TV signal" and click "Next".
    b)       On the Select Your TV Signal Screen, select "Cable" and then Next.
    c)       On the Do You Have a Set-top Box Screen, click "Yes" then next.
    d)       On the Prepare for Set-top Box Setup screen, click "Next".
    e)       On the Select a Working TV Signal, Select the one you have
    f)        On the Set-top Box Remote Screen, Select ""Yes" and then "Next".
    g)       On the Identify Set-top Box Remote, click "Next".
    h)       Follow the on screen instructions. Select "Have Media Center learn my remote from scratch" and then "Next".
    i)         On the Prepare to Identify Remote Screen, hit next.
    j)        On Channel Change Buttons, select the method used.
    k)       On the IR Learning Screen, Hold the remote away from the IR receiver. Hold down the number, raise the remote to the receiver until it says "Release button to continue" then move it away from the IR receiver. Keep the button held down.
    l)         Do this a minimum of two times until it is ready for the next button and repeat step 10. If it takes more than 4 times (one entry try and one confirm try), then make sure the remote is not in the line of site on the receiver until the button is held down for a second and that when held up to the receiver, it is no more than three inches from it.
    m)     On the Set-top Box Codes Learned Successfully Screen, click on "Next".
    n)       On the Select Number of Digits Screen, select the appropriate amount and then "Next.
    o)       On the How Do You Change Channels? Screen, Select the appropriate option and click "Next".
    p)       On the Test Channel Changing Screen, use the Media Center Remote and try changing the channel. Wait about 10 seconds and the channel should change (Give it a little time for the IR signal to travel down the wire to the Set-top Receiver and the TV signal to clear the video capture card buffer to the screen). If successful, select "The set-top box changed the channel correctly", then "Next".
    q)       On the Set IR Signal Speed screen, hit the channel up or down buttons at least six times. With a delay, the channels should change. It does go in order (1, 2, 3, 4,...), so if the channel does not exist, just hit up or down until it hits a channel that exists. You may need to adjust the signal speed. Select "The channel changed correctly" if it changed and then "Next".
    r)        You are done. Select "Return to TV Settings" and click "Finish"

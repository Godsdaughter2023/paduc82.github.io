For the ones that are showing with a duration of 14 seconds, can you double-click on them and go to the Video Tools | Edit tab?  See if it actually does show a duration of 14 seconds.  If so, just reduce it back to 7 seconds.

 

Also, for displaying your items on the storyboard, the "ghost" images as you call them, just represent the continued playing of your photo or video clip.  If you want to instead see each item as a single thumbnail, use the zoom slider in the lower right.  Set it all the way to the left so that you are fully zoomed out.  In this view you will see one thumbnail per item.  Alternatively, if you want to see each item indicating its duration by a larger strip, zoom in by moving to the right.  Play with the zoom slider to get a feeling of how it works.

The answer unfortunately for you is that it can't be done.  The HDMI connector on your HP-All-In-One is an HDMI out.  This means it can only send a video signal out, to another larger display for example.  It can't receive an incoming HDMI signal and display it on the monitor.

detectivemuffin say: I have finally fixed this mystery of a problem.

After trying 3 different USB adapters(Linksys WUSB54GC v1 & v3, Zonet) and 2 routers(Belkin N, Linksys G) and 2 OS (XP, Vista), I fixed it with none of these being the issue.

The problem was my mobo. I have gigabyte P55M-UD2. Gigabyte had released a new BIOS in Nov, 09. My BIOS was P55M-UD2.F3 and I bumped it to F5. And now zonet ZEW 2500P worked instantly with my old linksys router WRT54G on windows 7 64 bit.

Updating BIOS is a little risky, so be sure your new downloaded BIOS matches your mobo.

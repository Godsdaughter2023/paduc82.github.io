There's no way to convert a self-signed certificate into a certificate signed by a root CA.  Moreover, simply adding a certificate to a particular area of the crypto store does not change its abililties.  The Trusted Certificate Root Authorities certs must be issued by trusted CAs.  Adding your own cert to that store area doesn't make it trusted.

internet expert say:

You must have a display with at least HDMI 2.0 in order to drive 4K@60Hz.


HDMI 1.4 only drives up to 4K@30fps, but the Xbox One X is not designed to accommodate this, and will only display up to 1080p.


Your monitor does indeed have an HDMI 1.4 input. It's at least implied in the online manual through the "standard timing table", where 4K@60Hz is only achieved through the DisplayPort input. I do not believe there are any adapters available for this (if there were, they would be active adapters and be quite expensive).

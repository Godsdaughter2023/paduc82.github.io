xbf amber m say:

Hi ExTz xx!

Thanks for reaching out to us! Sorry to hear you are having difficulties in adding your account to your console. In order for the profiles/accounts to be added to the console, you would need a steady connection to your network. You may wish to check your network connection to see if you receive any error messages since the second error code you received (0x80a40008) usually occurs due to a connectivity issue.

Also, if you are connecting wirelessly (via wifi), you may experiences some connection issues if you have been connected to that network for some time. Wireless signals and devices can be thought of as performing a handshake of sorts when they connect and, over time, can loosen and either become spotty in delivering signal or cut out altogether. Should you be connected by wifi to your console, I recommend a physical power cycle of the console (do not plug back in until after the wifi is back up) while unplugging your router or network hardware for a minimum of 5 minutes simultaneously, then set your network up again on the console. This should refresh the connection and give you a stronger signal and connection, then you can attempt to sign back in. 

To perform a physical power cycle:

  1. Turn off the Xbox One console by pressing the Xbox button  on the front of the console for about 10 seconds until it shuts down completely.
  2. Unplug the console's power cable. Wait 10 seconds.

    Important Be sure to wait 10 seconds. This step resets the power supply.

  3. Plug the console power cable back in.
  4. Press the Xbox button  on your console to turn on the Xbox One.

If you have a wired connection, you may wish to check your Ethernet cable to ensure that it is performing well by switching it out with a different one to see if you get a different result. That being said, a hardwired Ethernet connection for your console is highly recommended since it greatly increases speeds and reduces lag, especially if you play multiplayer games online.

Please give these a try and let us know the results. I hope that this is helpful!

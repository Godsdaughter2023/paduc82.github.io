igor leyko say:
Hi,
in most countrys one cannot use OEM Windows separately from computer it was preinstalled, AFAIK only Germany and Russia allow to do this.
Activation does not mean a use rights, see EULA: Successful activation does not confirm that the software is genuine or properly licensed. (article 5).
System builder cannot use installed system itself or transfer OEM to other computer, it have to sell a computer after installing OEM. The same rules are active for other Windows versions since July, 1, 2007. There were different rules before this date but I do not think they have practical meaning now.

What seemed to be a problem that was not gonna go away soon (after having tried pretty much anything...) i have solved it. If someone else gets these problems too, i hope this will help:

**------------**

I loaded the game up, when it started to sync, i waited it out. It can take a long *** time and most likely will result in a error, but that's ok. When it gave the error:

We couldn't sync your data with the cloud just now. Try syncing again or use this game or app offline. (8083201d)

I noticed that when it has synced about 80 to 90 %, it will have all your local savedata. I chose to stop syncing and let the game load. Load my most recent save and after that made a new one or let it autosave. Quit game and took the Xbox One offline. I load the game again and this way avoid the cloud. I load the latest savefile and make a new one/autosave and quit game. went back online, booted game, this time the syncing was much quicker and it STILL gave an error around 70% but i chose to try syncing again and....it worked. This was for The Witcher 3. I then tried it for GTA V and this time i didn't even go offline and it worked just as well. So now i am not sure if you even need go offline, but try it anyway if the problem persists. Now when i boot up my games, i don't get that horrible nonsense anymore. I have to do this trick to all my affected games though, but that's ok.

No matter what console a profile is signed into it will always be able to play its digital games and use its subscriptions (gold, EA access, Game Pass)

Each profile can set ONE console at a time as its home console and any other profile on that console can then play its digital games and use its subscriptions. Each profile can change what console it has set as its home console only FIVE times a year.

Now when a profile has reached its FIVE times and tries to set a different console as its home console it will give an error message with something that says you have reached your five times and will give you a date on when you can change your console again.

So unless that is the error that your friend is getting it has nothing to do with how many times your home xbox has been changed and has nothing to do with your other friends having set their console as your home console.

Method 1:

Follow the steps.

 

a. Click Startb
b. Type: CMD, from the results, right click CMD->Click 'Run as Administrator'
c. At the Command Prompt, type: chkdsk /r

 

When you restart your system, your computer will be scanned for errors and attempts will be made to correct them.

 

Note: While performing chkdsk on the hard drive if any bad sectors are found on the hard drive when chkdsk tries to repair that sector if any data available on that might be lost.

 

Additionally you may refer the following link for more information:

Check a drive for errors

You may also perform a virus scan

www.microsoft.com/security/scanner

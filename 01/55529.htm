While BSODs can have multiple causes, in your case, I suspect bad RAM here since your BSODs are basically random.

Some signs of bad ram are:

- BSODs are more frequent after a "cold boot".
- BSODs happen more whenever something puts more stress on the CPU
- Sometimes, BSODs just happen without putting any stress on the CPU - as in, a bsod happens out of the blue
- Programs just abort/quit working randomly
- IE and Firefox crash really often
- distorted graphics once in a while

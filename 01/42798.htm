jeeped say:
The COM Surrogate is a process spun off to perform an action that the system has determined to be less than optimal. In this way, if the action crashes, it is the COM surrogate that suffers and not your base operating system. Thumbnails for images and videos come into this category. The most common reason behind numerous surrogate crashes is a flaky codec and your description of the problem coming up when navigating folders and files seems to confirm this.

Uninstall any codec packages that you have recently installed and look for more reliable codecs. I use and trust the K-Lite packages myself.

  1. While you're logged in, switch the input language to the one you desire.

  2. Type "region" into your Search box and choose the option for regional settings.
  3. On the Administrative tab, click the Copy button to copy the current settings over to the login screen.

A Cyclic Redundancy Check (CRC) is an error checking technique used by computers, specifically the hard drives or optical disks. A Cyclic Redundancy Check error happens when data on the hard-drive or DVD becomes corrupt.

  1. Were there any changes made on the computer prior to the issue?
  2. Did you try running the Chkdsk command as an Administrator?

  1. Swipe in from the right edge of the screen, and then tap Search.

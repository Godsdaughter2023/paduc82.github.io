Hi KamalaKanth,

Welcome to Skype Community Forum!

I regret to inform you but we do not have API or a program in Skype that lets you export your chat history that will include dates, timestamps etc. 

You can only export chat history conversation using CSV file with any spreadsheet program, such as Microsoft Excel. Some content of your chat conversation history will not be included, once exported. For example: media files, shared URLs and similar content is exported within XML tags; some messages may be split into separate rows due to formatting issues.

To export your chat history:

  1. Select Tools > Options.
  2. On the left, select Privacy.
  3. Select the Export Chat History button.

Important:

The last 30 days of your conversation history are stored in the cloud, so won't be exported. Only the history saved on your hard drive, according to your 'keep history for' setting, is exported.

Hello,

Thank you for posting your query on Microsoft Community forum.

Here are some reasons why you might be unable to find or install an app from the Windows Store:

. Your preferences might be hiding apps.

. Family Safety settings might be hiding apps because of age rating.

. The app isn’t available in your country or region.

. The payment method associated with your account isn’t from the country or region you’re in. 

. Have you made any recent hardware or software changes to your computer prior to the issue?

Try the steps mentioned below and see if it works for you.

Method 1:

Follow the steps from the link:

Why can't I find or install an app from the Windows Store?:

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-IN/windows-8/why-find-install-app-windows-store

Method 2:

Check if the date, time and time zone is set according to your time and time zone.

Follow the steps to set the date and time:

a. Press Windows key + C on your keyboard to show the Charms bar.

b. Type “Date and Time” in the charms bar search.

c. Click Change date and time button (To change the time zone click on time zone tab).

d. Click “Apply” and “OK” to save the date and time.

Method 3:

Clean Store Cache by following the steps:

a. Press the Windows+ R keys on your keyboard to open the Run dialog.

b. Type WSReset.exe, and click/tap on OK.

c. The Store will now open, and state the following: The cache for the store was cleared. You can now browse the store for apps.

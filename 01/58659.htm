  1. Press and hold the power button on the right hand side of the keyboard for ~3 seconds [or press "Connect"] until the blue LED starts to flash slowly.  It may alternate two flashes and a pause.
  2. On the Start Menu, click "Control Panel."
  3. Under "Hardware and Audio," click "Add a Device."
  4. As the Device scan proceeds, the Bluetooth keyboard should appear in the device list.
  5. Select the keyboard.  You should be presented with a passcode.

  7. Your keyboard is now ready for use.

  2. Select Command prompt admin.

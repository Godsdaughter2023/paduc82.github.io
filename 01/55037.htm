wunders say:

You do not need to have drivers to connect to the internet, but you do need drivers to control your Network Adapter on your computer.  Can you network-connect to other computers in your home?  You provide no details about what you've tried or the error messages or symptoms you are seeing but a good first step is to bring up the Device Manager (Start -> Run -> "devmgmt.msc") and see if it reports any problems in the Network Adapter section.  If so, you probably should connect to your computer manufacturer's website (or website of your network card marufacturer if it wasn't provided with your computer) using another computer and go to the Support / Download section and download the latest driver for your network card.  Move it to your machine and install.

a. user say:

Hi,

Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Community.

I understand your concern regarding the issue that you are facing.

I understand your concern about blocking sender and finding for junk folder on Mail app in Windows 10, we will help you with this issue.

Method 1: How to block the sender.

I would like to inform that, Mail app is by design does not have option to block the sender, instead you can block the sender from Outlook account and same applies to Mail app as well.

 If you want to add the sender of the certain email to your blocked senders list, kindly follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. Open or view the email you want to be blocked.
  3. Click Sweep on the toolbar.
  4. Select Delete all from…
  5. Tick also block future messages.
  6. Click Delete all.

This will also delete all messages from the sender.

 

Here’s how you can add the email address manually:

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. In the upper-right corner of the page, click wheel/cog icon, and then click on Options.
  3. Under preventing junk email, click Safe and blocked senders.
  4. Click Blocked senders.
  5. Under Blocked email address or domain: type the email address where these emails came from.
  6. Click Save.

Method 2: To find junk folder in Mail app

To find junk folder on Mail app follow: Log-in to Mail app, in the left-pane click on More and you will see Junk folder.

Hope it helps. Kindly update the status of this issue, we will be happy to help you.

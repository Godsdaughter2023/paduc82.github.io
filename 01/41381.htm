Hi,

There are laptops that will wake up only when you press a button or you move the mouse. The feature that will wake up the laptop from sleep when opening the lid is only specific to some laptops. You can find out if your laptop has this feature by following these steps:

  1. Right-click the Start button and select Device Manager.
  2. Expand System devices, and then right-click ACPI Lid.
  3. Choose Properties.

If you can see the Power Management tab in the Properties window of ACPI Lid, you will have the option to wake the computer when opening the lid. You just need to access the Power Management tab and enable it. If you do not see the Power Management tab, your computer will only wake up when moving the mouse or by pressing any keys.

There some laptops that may have this option in the BIOS menu.

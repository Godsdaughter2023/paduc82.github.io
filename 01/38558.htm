Facial recognition issues (can't sign in after update)

Every time when I start my laptop, when it comes to the log in page, the laptop does the facial recognition, and then should let me get in to the desktop after it's finished. However, after I updated the laptop about a month ago. After the facial recognition, it comes up with a hello :) symbol but doesn't sign in. Therefore I need to put in my pins every time I start my laptop. Is there any way to solve this issue? Thanks a mill!

a. user say:

Hi,

If you can't sign in to your laptop after the recent update, then this could be due to an outdated camera driver. Another factor is due to Group Policy settings. To determine if this is really the case, sign in to your PC using your PIN, next, go to Edit group policy > Policies > Administrative Templates > Windows Components > Biometrics > Facial Features > Use enhanced anti-spoofing when available" change 'Not Configured' to 'disabled'

Let us know if this goes for you.

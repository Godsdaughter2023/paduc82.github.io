malkeleah say:

I'm not sure what you mean by "the task bar menu". Do you mean that all the icons that were on the Desktop are now on the Taskbar at the very bottom-left of your screen? Right-click an empty area of the Taskbar and you will see an entry for Toolbars with a little arrow next to it. If you slide your mouse to the little arrow, you'll see what Toolbars are currently enabled. Post back with which ones have a checkmark next to them. In the meantime, you can right-click icons that are on Toolbars on the Taskbar (not the ones in the far lower-right of your screen) and drag each one to the Desktop. Let go of the right mouse button and then left-click on "Copy".

Also, right-click an empty area of the Desktop and make sure there is a checkmark next to "Show Desktop icons".

Hi Damien,

I understand the inconvenience caused to you regarding the installation of AutoCAD 2017.I will certainly assist you.

Follow the below steps and check if it helps.

I suggest you to download the ORCA from their website (program allows you to modify an MSI file) then follow the below steps.

If you have a thumb drive or DVD, you will need to copy the contents onto your hard drive.

  1. Navigate to the location of the installation files
  2. Navigate to the x64 folder
  3. Find the application folder:
  • INSTALLFILES\X64\Acade

Once Orca is opened, navigate to the “CustomAction” table

  • InstallPreJit
  • NgenCreate
  • NgenCreateRollback
  • NgenDelete
  • NgenDeleteRollback
  • UninstallPreJit

Go to File > Save. Than close the Orca application.

Install AutoCAD application.

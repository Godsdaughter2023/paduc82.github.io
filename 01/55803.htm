Anytime you need to reinstall Windows 10 on that machine, just proceed to reinstall Windows 10. It will automatically reactivate. You will prompted to enter a product key a couple times through the installation, click I don't have a key and Do this later.

miikka [skype & teams] say:

Hi,

in the latest version of Skype it's possible to disable the feature 'People you may know' and you can also fully control if you are shown in that feature for other users. This is possible via Settings - Contacts - Privacy.

The options for this in the settings are:

'Receive suggestions' - with this you can show and hide the feature

'Appear in suggestions' - with this you can control if you and your contacts are shown in these suggestions for other users

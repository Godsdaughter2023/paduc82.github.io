Hiya. I know this is an old thread but it can be done. Is a bit of pain but it only takes a few minutes.

Hello everyone. This is my first time posting on the forums so forgive me if come off with something that has already been said or I don't make sense lol.

I've managed to connect my Xbox 360 and Xbox One to the BT wifi hotspot where I live so if it's any help, here's how I managed it (Xbox One):

I have an iPhone so if you have an android then you'll know how to find the info on your phone that you need.

I have the BT wifi app downloaded to speed things up cause it automatically signs me in so I recommend it.

Once I'm connected to the wifi with both my phone and Xbox One if you tap on the info and copy all of the numbers from the phone into the info on the Xbox One your Xbox One will now recognise the wifi.

Next if you go to settings > general > about, copy your phone's wireless address into the Xbox One's alternate MAC address (this means when you sign into BT wifi your Xbox One will automatically sign in).

After that open the wifi app, let it sign in and then your Xbox will automatically sign into Xbox live :)

IMPORTANT NOTES - if your doing this for the first time you might need to turn your Xbox One off and on again from the mains to refresh everything.  I'm able to do everything except for play multiplayer (I'm still working on finding this part out lol). You'll need to sign into BTWifi every 2 hours as it logs you out of a hotspot after that given time.

For 360 users it's the same steps except you only need to copy the MAC address, nothing else! :) and with 360 you CAN play multiplayer :D

If anyone has any questions feel free to message me and if any one has figured out how to play Xbox One multiplayer using a wifi hotspot I'd be very thankful if you told me how. Sorry for the long post

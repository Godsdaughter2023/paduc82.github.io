Hello Andre,

At this time, "Own Templates" are not supported.  This is a feature that is only supported for Activity Packs that are published.

 

For mixing colors, the Custom Colors you mix are only saved if you use them somewhere in your painting.  Then, they are automatically saved in your palette.  Your custom palette is only applicable to the individual artwork that you created with it.  There is no need to delete colors as we provide a seemingly endless list you can cycle through directly beneath the mixing area.

 

You are able to reposition your custom palette by drag-and-dropping colors between wells on the palette.  This allows you to reposition some colors into your static well spots.  The custom well spots will repopulate based off most frequently used colors.

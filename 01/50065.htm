shekharbhadoria say:

Hi AilynApiag

I am shekhar bhadoria, I will suggest you a few important reasons for the delay in sending messages while at good internet speed. There may be some of the reasons for delaying of messages. 

Mobile Device Issues

Problems of a Mobile device may cause the delay of message transmissions. Mobile device issues like device turned off, a weak or uncharged battery may negatively affect the text message delays. Devices that have an adjustable antenna may delay the transmission process if the antenna is damaged or not fully raised.

Different Networks

The sender and receiver may have problems with the message transmission process if both are using a different network, because of communication between network prioritizing their own network.

Network Traffic

Texting during periods of heavy network use impacts text delivery speed. Periods of increased traffic may create congestion on the local network and delay the arrival of messages to the handset.

Delivery Receipts

It should also be noted that the arrival of delivery receipts (DLRs) is not always a good measure of when the SMS was delivered. Delivery receipts are often prioritized lower than other traffic (i.e. lower than the SMS traffic) on networks, and at busy times or when networks approach maximum capacity they will de-prioritize delivery of delivery receipts further in favor of SMS content, leading to potentially very delayed arrival of delivery receipts in your account.

These may be a few reasons behind delay messages with good internet.

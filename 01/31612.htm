2. Did you upgrade a Win7 SP1 computer or a Win8.1 computer to Win10?
3. Was the upgrade offered & installed via Windows Update or did you use the Media Creation Tool & ISO download?
4. What anti-virus application was installed before you upgraded to Win10, was your subscription still current & did you uninstall it before you upgraded to Win10?
5. When & why was Malwarebytes' Anti-Malware (MBAM) installed? Is it MBAM Premium or MBAM Free? Has MBAM ever detected & quarantined anything since it's been installed?
6. Has a(nother) Norton application or a McAfee application EVER been installed on the computer since you bought it?

I'm going to stab wildly in the dark here...
Right click on the speaker in the notification tray and select Sounds.
Scroll to notification click it and then click Test. is that the sound you are hearing?
You can change the sound and you can also select which notifications will make the sound:
Change notifications in action center
In Windows 10, action center is where to find your app notifications, as well as quick actions, which give you quick access to commonly used settings and apps. Change your action center settings at any time from the Settings app.
In the search box, enter settings and then select the Settings app.
Go to System > Notifications & actions.
Do any of the following:
Turn notifications on or off for some or all apps.
Choose whether to see notification banners when a notification arrives.
Choose the quick actions you'll see in action center.
Select each of the notifications and turn off sounds.

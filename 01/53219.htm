    into the paint program.

4. Sign-in into your Windows Live™ and click 
    More and click SkyDrive.

5. Select the Public folder, and click add Add files.

6. Then drop a image into it.


Figure A: Drop files.

7. Click Upload.


Figure B: Uploading file.

8. Select the image and then click it one more time,
    to open it inside a blank page.


Figure C: Select your image.

9. Press Ctrl+A and Ctrl+C.


Figure D: Select your image with Ctrl+alt.

10. Navigate to your post and press Ctrl+V.

11. That's it.

 http://www.virtualbox.org/

 

VMware
 http://www.vmware.com/

Hi Rodney

 

Suspending an app saves the app in it's current state.

 

Example:

 

1. Open the Windows Store app and select an app to view the details.

 

2. To Suspend the app, drag the app to the bottom of the screen and quickly release it. After 5 seconds, the app will be suspended.

 

3. Click the Windows Store tile to open the app again and you will see that it is still showing the details of the app that you selected in step 1.

 

If you want to completely Close the app, instead of suspending it, when you drag the window to the bottom of the screen, hold it there for about 2 seconds until you see the app "Flip" and then release it (or press Alt+F4).

 

Let us know if you have more questions.

First of all, go to https://account.live.com/Proofs/Manage and check that your security info is accurate and up to date. Remove or update outdated phone numbers or emails.

Ideally, if you want to make your account basically hacking proof, scroll down a bit and enable Two Step Verification, and set up a free Authenticator App on your smartphone. 

That way, whenever you or a hacker tries to sign into your account from a new device, a code has to be entered, that only your phone can provide. Unless the hacker manages to also physically steal your phone, they can't get into your account. 

One thing you need to know however is, that the Xbox 360 can't request the second authenticator token (yet), so you'll have to scroll down even further, and create a so called "App Password". 

After creating that app password, go to your console settings, and under storage clear the system cache by pressing Y on any storage device and choosing the clear system cache option. 

Then find your Gamertag Profile on the storage device, and delete it. 

WARNING!!! The console will ask if it should delete content associated with the profile. DO NOT let the console delete that associated content!!! Let it delete the PROFILE ONLY!!!

Then, after the profile is deleted, press the Guide button, and choose "Download Profile"

Provide your profile's Microsoft Account email, and the APP PASSWORD instead of your regular password. 

Let the console download your profile, and you're done!

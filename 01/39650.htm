¡firedog say:

Error 0x80004005 is the most generic message you'll find - it just means FAIL, with no further indication of what might have failed and why. However, it is quite common when using POP3 to access an NS mailbox.

The solution is normally to access the account in webmail (just click mail.fairgamesports.com or type the URL into your browser's address bar) and clear out the junk and trash folders, then move all messages from the inbox into a user-defined archive folder. Then try send/receive again in Windows Live Mail. You should see No new email messages on the status bar and no error message. Do you? 

Can you identify the messages that were in the webmail inbox that haven't been downloaded into Windows Live Mail? The oldest of these is probably the dodgy one. Deal with it in webmail, then move the undownloaded messages back into the inbox and try Send/Receive again in Windows Live Mail.

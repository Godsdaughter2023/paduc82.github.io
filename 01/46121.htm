nnnnnnick say:

Alright I think I've figured it out,

For whatever reason, Japanese toll free lines (lines starting with 0) don't work for international calls. Even though I have a skype number for Japan, it may still be registered as a non-local number because the number starts with 505, which is uncommon. 

So, to put it short, skype numbers in Japan don't seem to work with ANY number that starts with 0. 

If you need to call a toll free number, try to find the company's international number and call it from Skype. Or just call whatever number you can at the company and ask for a transfer to a non 0 number (this is what I had to do for my bank...).

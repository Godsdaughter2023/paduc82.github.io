a. user say:

I found my own solution to the problem.

 

It was not an Apple issue - they make pretty good PC hardware (just need to provide UEFI 2 updates).

 

The problem was:

 

Windows update KB2795944

 

This update blows away the ATI drivers and replaces them with the Win 8 generic driver. Reinstallation of the ATI drivers does not repair the damage. Before the update, graphics ran well, including Blu Ray, and Blu Ray 3D. After the update, graphics are like molasses, Blu Ray and Blu Ray 3D no longer work.

 

Solution:

1. Turn off all Microsoft automatic updates.

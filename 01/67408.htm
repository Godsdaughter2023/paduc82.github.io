Obviously deleting an installed programs folder is usually not the best way to uninstall the program - sometimes there are lots of things involved in uninstalling a program...

Most things when they get installed install the program and files and add some stuff in the registry so that when you use Add/Remove programs to uninstall, it looks in the registry for an uninstall string that is the command used to uninstall the program properly.  Uninstalling could be very simple or very complicated.  The program authors hopefully know what to do to uninstall their stuff cleanly.

The Windows 10 operating system provides many features that helps in the less consumption of battery and make its’ life little longer. But the different features are located in different areas of the Windows and so we are here to represent you those features and their importance. Well, it does not mean that you don’t require an outlet, it just means that you can make your laptop work for some more time without an outlet. But the main consumer of battery is your display screen.

How to Increase Your Windows 10 Laptop’s Battery Life.

Decrease Display Screen Brightness

The light of your display screen consumes the largest amount for your battery. And the only way is to lighten the brightness of your display screen. You can easily adjust the brightness of your screen Windows 10 whenever you seem it to be too high than the required amount. By reducing the screen brightness, you improve the battery life of your laptop.

1. Go to Windows 10 charms settings (Window button + I). Click Change PC Settings. Adjust the brightness as per your requirement.

2. The easiest way is to use the keyboard keys. All you will do is look for the function (Fn) key and the brightness keys. Keep hold the function (Fn) key and then press the brightness keys to adjust the brightness of your screen.

3. Another way to adjust the brightness setting is from the Windows Mobility center.  Navigate the following path: Control Panel -> Hardware and Sound -> Windows Mobility Center. The first option is of Brightness. Drag the brightness slider to the left and lower the screen brightness.

Battery Saver is a newly added feature on Windows 10, though it is not in a complete active mode, but still it will help you. As you open PC Settings on your screen, you will see Battery Saver listed in the bottom.

When you are into the Battery Saver you will notice its detail in the right pane. When you turn on the Battery Saver, the feature will limit the background activity of your laptops.

Windows 10 also provides you a Power Plan. By default, the power plan for Windows 10 is Balanced. But as you want to save power, you can select the Power Saver option.  You have also some advanced settings provided, but we recommend to access them only if you are aware of its functioning.

To select the power plans, let the Control Panel arrive on your screen. Click on Hardware and Sound option. Again click on Power Options. Now do your required settings here.

Hope this helps.

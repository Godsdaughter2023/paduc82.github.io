If you open a file on the volume chkdsk will ask you if you want to dismount the volume, if you answer no it will then ask you if you want to schedule a chkdsk when the computer restarts.  You cannot cancel a chkdsk in progress unless you press and hold the power button and doing so may damage the file system.

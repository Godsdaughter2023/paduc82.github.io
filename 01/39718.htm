'mike p say:

OneDrive is complicated for most people - Microsoft hasn't figured out how to keep it simple, but they try.

If you right click on your onedrive main folder, in the context menu you should see a onedrive section, one of the items there is 'always keep on this device'   Sounds like you want to add a checkmark there - which will keep copies on both your drive and the onedrive cloud.  

The free up space, if you click that... will keep files in the cloud but not on your computers hard drive.

You might also want to look in OneDrive settings and look through those, and under autosave tab will see checkboxes to or not to always include docs/picts in onedrive.

As far as not enough space I cant help you there, you need to figure out your own solution.  Buy more space could make life simple though... or perhaps add google drive or dropbox and learn how they work.

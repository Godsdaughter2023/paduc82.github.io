Usually there are 2 types of restore available, a destructive restore and a non-destructive type. The non-destructive type would re-install the operating system, drivers and programs that originally came with your computer but it would leave the files you put on the computer alone. You would only have to re-install any software that you added.


A destructive restore however would reformat your harddrive and reset your computer to the same state it was when it came out of the factory. After a destructive restore all the files and software you added to the computer yourself are gone forever.

"CPU" stands for "Central Processing Unit." In modern personal
computers, the CPU is a relatively small electronic chip, an

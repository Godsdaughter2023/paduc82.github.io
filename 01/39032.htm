I would suggest you to try the following steps to create a folder on the desktop.

  1. Right-click the taskbar at the bottom of your screen, then clicking Show the desktop.
  2. Right-click in any open space on your desktop, click New, then click Folder.
  3. Type a name for the folder, then press the Enter key to complete the creation of your new desktop folder.
  4. You can move the folder around on the desktop by clicking on the folder, then dragging it to your desired location.
  5. You can also rename a folder by right-clicking the folder, then clicking Rename.

Note: Once the folder is created, you can simply drag and drop the website shortcuts into that particular folder.

andrewbocanegra say:

You can play Xbox One games offline. You just need to make sure the game has completely installed the update. Because updates download automatically, Xbox still wanted you to know that the game had an update and therefore prompts that popup to "finish" the install.

As you said you work on a ship, I would recommend putting your console into energy-saving mode. This effectively makes it operate like the 360 did, where off is off, and on is on. As a result, updates are not automatically downloaded but you are instead prompted to install updates when you start up a game and are connected to the web. This way games won't download updates without your direct knowledge and you'll be ready for offline whenever.

Fix for Canon PIXMA MP Windows 7 64bit Scanning - Microsoft Community

simon egli say:

Fix for Canon PIXMA MP Windows 7 64bit Scanning

Hello,

I couldn't get my MP530 scanner with MP Navigator to work on Windows 7 64bit. Here is the solution, that should also work for other MP printers where ScanGear & MP Navigator is not working under 64bit Windows 7:


In 64bit Windows the registry entries for ScanGear are all under HKLM\Software\Wow6432Node\Canon\ScanGearMP\ScanGear\11.2\Devices\MP530 as opposed to  HKLM\Software\Canon\ScanGearMP\ScanGear\11.2\Devices\MP530 in 32bit Windows.

But in the file 

C:\Windows\twain_32\MP530\_SETSCN_.INI the entry is set to


[Registry]
Key=HKLM,Software\Canon\ScanGearMP\ScanGear\11.2\Devices\MP530


Simply change that entry to


[Registry]
Key=HKLM,Software\Wow6432Node\Canon\ScanGearMP\ScanGear\11.2\Devices\MP530


and MP Navigator will work.


If you're using a different printer than MP530, simply look in the folder MP??? under C:\Windows\twain_32 where ??? is your printer model, and replace of course the label MP530 in _SETSCN.INI with your printer model.


Greetings,


David Egli

andre da costa say:
To change your display name on the Skype website,

1. Log in to your account using to the Skype website: Skype website
http://www.skype.com/

2. Click on Sign in and choose My Account.

3 Scroll down and locate Edit profile under Account details.

4. Under Personal information, you will see your current display name, click on Edit profile on the right part of the page.

5. Enter your desired display name and click on Save.

Note: If this doesn't work on your current browser, you might want to try to use a different browser to change your name.


To change your display name using your Skype client, follow these:

1. Open up the Skype application and click on your profile picture.

2. On the right pane of the client, you will see your account details, above your Skype name will be your account's display name, click on it and the display name will be highlighted.

Note: To distinguish the Skype name and the display name, your Skype name will be after the word Accounts while your display name will be in a larger font size.

3. Change the display name to a name you prefer and click on the check icon on the right side of the box.

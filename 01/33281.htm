diana d say:

Hi Hoosier11,

• Who is the manufacturer of the printer?

This error can occur if when using a network or printing from a laptop. When the print job goes into a pending status, the printer is either in Work Offline or Pause Printing mode.

A) Restart the print job

1. Open the Printers folder:
◦Windows Vista: Click Start, click Control Panel , and then double-click Printers .
2. Double-click the Printer icon (not the fax icon if one is present) for the product. A window opens with a list of print tasks currently assigned to the printer.
3. Click the first document in the list, and then click Printer. If there is a check mark next to Pause Printing or Use Printer Offline, click to remove the check mark.
4. If the page does not print, click Document, and then click Restart.

B) Clear the print queue and restart the computer

1. Open the Printers folder:
◦Windows Vista: Click Start, click Control Panel, and then double-click Printers .
2. Double-click the product icon. The queue window opens.
3. If any documents are waiting to be printed, click Cancel or Purge print jobs/documents.
4. Restart the computer.

Set-WinUserLanguageList en-GB

cmdrkeene say:

If you reset your password online, your computer won't "know" the new password until the next time it connects to the Internet.  So you'll need to use your old password instead to login once first.

Did you try clicking the "eye ball" button on the right side of the password box to make sure it has it the way you intend?  That way you can see if there's anything wrong (like maybe a number got missed).

As for your question, a reset while keeping your data will also keep the password, so that won't help you here (otherwise someone could steal your computer, reset to clear the password, then get all your files!)  But as an alternative you may be able to boot to Safe Mode and use the built-in administrator account to force-reset your password.

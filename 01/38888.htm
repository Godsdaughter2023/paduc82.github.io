How to change from Tablet mode to Desktop mode in Windows 10

When your Windows 10 system is currently using tablet mode, there will be no tile available on the screen to quickly switch back to desktop mode. To switch from tablet mode back to desktop mode, tap or click the Action Center icon in the taskbar to bring up a list of quick settings for your system. On a tablet or a system with a touch-panel display, you can also access the Action Center settings by performing a quick swipe from the right edge of the screen.

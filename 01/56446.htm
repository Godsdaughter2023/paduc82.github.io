ocryzen say:

All games in the Optical Drive Buzz [even when the games are installed] & is to do with the composition of the discs + Disc copyright checks on the console &/or Xbox Live etc..

GRFS discs, could be slightly thinner & lighter than the older discs & the Optical Drive, will always try to spin at the same speed to read discs.. Thus, making the lighter disc, spin a bit faster than a slightly heavier disc & make more noise etc.. [Power-Weight Ratio of Drives motor etc..]

Nothing to worry about, if the games are being read &/or working fine..

If the drive stops reading discs, then send the console in for warranty repair...

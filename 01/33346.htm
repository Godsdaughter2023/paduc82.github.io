    I have the latest version of Movie Maker.

    considerate_guy say:

    Okay, I did a little research on Dxtory and it appears that it puts the gameplay audio in one audio stream and the added audio (microphone) in a second audio stream.  That's really cool, but not something that Movie Maker can support presently.  Movie Maker extracts the primary audio stream and uses it.  The second audio stream is ignored as if it doesn't exist.

     

    If you can find a way to convert the file and merge the audio streams into a single audio stream, that would work.  Alternatively, if there is a way to extract the second audio stream into its own audio file, you could use that in Movie Maker as your "narration" track.  For example, Freemake Video Converter can select one track and output as .mp3.

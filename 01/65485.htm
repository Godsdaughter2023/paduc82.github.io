bravoman21 say:
Hello Willmish,

Microsoft uses a new activation system for Windows 10 where a unique signature is created for each individual computer (called Digital Entitlement) and as long as your hardware remains the same, you're able to (re)install and activate Windows 10 on your computer without a product key.

With that in mind, you will not be able to transfer the Digital Entitlement from your desktop PC to your laptop since the signature was created specifically for your desktop PC. You will need to obtain a new Windows 10 license if you want to use Windows 10 on your laptop.

net stop cryptsvc 
ren %systemroot%\System32\Catroot2 oldcatroot2 
net start cryptsvc 
exit

3. Delete all tmp*.cat files from the following folder:  

%systemroot%\system32\CatRoot\{F750E6C3-38EE-11D1-85E5-00C04FC295EE}  

If no files that start with “tmp” exist in this folder, do not remove any other files. The .cat files in this folder are necessary for installing hotfixes and service packs.

Important: Do not rename the Catroot folder. The Catroot2 folder is automatically recreated by Windows, but the Catroot folder is not recreated if the Catroot folder is renamed.

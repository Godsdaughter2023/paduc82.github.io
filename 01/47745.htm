Hi,

 

Sorry for the confusion about this issue. Please follow the steps below in order to add your certificate in your Windows Live Mail so that you will not encounter the same dialogue box again:

 

  1. Sign in to Windows Live Mail.
  2. Press Alt + F + O + S simultaneously on your keyboard and you will be re-directed to Safety Options on your Windows Live Mail.
  3. Click on Security Tab.
  4. Under Secure Mail, click Digital ID.
  5. Click Import.
  6. A Certificate Import Wizard will appear and click Next.
  7. Click on Browse to locate the certicate the double click on it.
  8. Click Next.
  9. Select Place all certificates in the following store and make sure that you Personal is selected. If not, click Browse then select Personal then click OK.
  10. Click Finish.

If you encountered any errors or having difficulties following my instructions, let us know so we can continue our support.

I would check the status of the Print Spooler Service.

Click on the Start button and select Control Panel, Administrative Tools, Services. Locate Print Spooler in the list. Check that it has started and that the StartUp type is Automatic.

4. Right click on ImageIconCache.db and click on Delete.

7. Empty the Recycle Bin.
8. Restart the computer.
9. When you go back, you will notice the Size of the ImageIconCache.db file is smaller, and the Date Modified is now the current date. (See screenshot below)

[Guide] Spotify on Xbox One – FAQ - Microsoft Community

maxen say:

[Guide] Spotify on Xbox One – FAQ

If you've run into problems using the Spotify app on Xbox One, or simply have some questions about how it works, this FAQ guide could have you covered. If your question is not answered here, feel free to make a new thread about it detailing your issue and the steps you've tried so far. Details on where to create your thread can be found at the bottom of this post.


I need help getting started with the Spotify app on Xbox One.

For instance, downloading and installing the app, logging in, or seeing an overview of the app features.


Take a look at our Getting Started guide.


I’ve run into issues with the app! What should I do?

For instance, the app is not loading content, the app crashes when trying to open it, or your Xbox is behaving oddly after installing the app.

Though it may sound like a simple step, the first thing to try is to power cycle your console:

  • Press the power button for ten seconds until it beeps and switches off
  • Unplug the power cable from the wall outlet
  • Wait 5-10 minutes
  • Reconnect the cable and turn the console back

This step has a tendency to fix issues, especially ones that just crept up.


You should also try uninstalling and reinstalling the app. If neither of these steps work and you need more help, make a thread in this section and detail the steps you have already tried.

 

I’m only a Spotify Free subscriber. Can I use the app for Xbox One?

Yes.


I don’t currently have a Spotify account. Can I use the app for Xbox One?

Yes, you don't need to have had a Spotify account before downloading the app, but you will need to create an account before being able to log in. You can do this from the Xbox One app itself, any other device with a Spotify app, or Spotify.com.

You cannot use the app without creating a new Spotify account or logging into an existing one.


What is the difference between a Free and Premium Spotify subscriber’s experience with the Xbox One app?

Premium subscribers will not hear adverts.


Do I need an Xbox Live Gold subscription to use the Spotify app on Xbox One?

No.


I’m trying to log in but I don’t see the option to use my password.

Try scrolling to the far right of your screen; the option may be partially cut off by the instructions for logging in with Spotify Connect.


I'm trying to log in with password but it won't work.

If you created your Spotify account with Facebook, you won't be able to use your password to log in. You will have to use the Spotify Connect method.


I cannot use Spotify Connect to log in because my other device does not show my Xbox One in the list of devices for Spotify Connect.

Try closing and reopening the Spotify app on your other device to refresh the list.


When I’m browsing the app, where can I see more details about what’s playing?

Press down on the D-pad or move LS (the Left Stick) downwards to the bottom of the screen, where it should show the artist and song currently playing. Once you hit the bottom, album artwork will expand to fill the screen, showing more details about the current song; it will also show album artwork for recently-played and soon-to-play songs.


I started playing music in Spotify, but when I closed the app and started a game it stopped playing in the background.

Don’t close the app. Simply start up the game you wish to play and the music will continue streaming if the app is still running in the background. Quitting the app, or force-closing it, will stop you from enjoying background listening.


I'm having playback issues!

Take a look at these suggestions from Spotify for resolving common problems with not being able to hear or play your music.

Note: This list of suggestions is universal and not specific to the Xbox One app, so you may find some steps are not relevant to your platform, but most apply and are worth checking.


Can I download songs to my Xbox One’s hard drive for offline listening like I can on other devices?

No. At this time, the app only supports streaming.


Where can I suggest app features?

You can always discuss the app here in the Xbox forums (make sure you’re on the Discussions tab and not Support), but to make formalised feature requests, or upvote requests from others, take a look at Spotify’s own Ideas page.


Further Assistance with the App or Your Spotify Account

If you need more assistance using the Spotify app on Xbox One, create a post here in the Spotify section of the Xbox Forums. Make sure you're still toggled to "Support" so your thread is created in the most appropriate place for getting help.

If you need help using your Spotify account, take a look at Spotify's own Support section.

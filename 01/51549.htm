andre da costa say:

Windows 7 Enterprise is a volume license client product. It is used by large organizations that deploy Windows 7 in bulk to hundreds or thousands of computers. 

Also, the type of activation method being used is KMS licensing, which requires connecting to an organization server every 180 days (6 months) in order maintain activation. 

The key in use is actually a client KMS key from Microsoft's website. So, in short, you need a genuine license. 

Looking at the rest of the report, your system might have been preinstalled with either Windows 8.1 Pro or Windows 7 Professional. So, I would try reinstalling either of those editions.

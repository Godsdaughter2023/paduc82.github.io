Please refer to the steps below which shows how you can remove a contact/phone number from your contact list:

A. On Skype for Windows

  1. Sign into Skype.
  2. In your contact list, find the number you want to remove. Right-click the number and select Remove from Contacts. Then confirm by clicking Remove.

B. On Skype for Mac

  1. Sign in to Skype.
  2. Find the number in your contact list. Go to the sidebar and click Contacts, then to the right, click Skype. You may have to click >> to see all of your lists.
  3. Right-click the contact you’d like to delete and select Delete Contact....

C. On the new Skype

  1. Find the number you want to remove in the Chats screen.
  2. Tap and hold, and then tap View profile.
  3. Scroll down and select Delete contact.

D. On Skype for Linux

  1. In your contact list, click the number you want to remove.
  2. Click the down arrow next to the contact you wish to remove and select Remove from Contacts.
  3. Click Yes to confirm that you want to remove the contact/phone number contact.

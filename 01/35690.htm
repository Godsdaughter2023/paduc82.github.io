    a. user say:

    Hi,

    Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Community. We understand the inconvenience caused and will assist you.

    As per the description I understand that the Windows.old folder is unavailable in your PC. I would like to inform that the Windows.old folder will be created after an upgrade to Windows 10 is done. This is used to revert back to the previous version of Operating System and the option will be available for 30 days from the day of upgrade to Windows 10. After the 30 days grace period, you will not be able to revert back to the previous Operating System but the Windows.old folder will be available in the computer until a disk cleanup is performed. As you are unable to locate the folder in your system I suggest you to search for any third party software to recover the folder.  

    Hope the information is helpful. Keep us posted for further assistance.

Hi Andrew,

 

I understand that when your Computer boots up, if there is a DVD in the DVD drive, it spins. Your query is to know how to disable the DVD drive at the time of reboot.

 

I would like to inform you that when the computer reboots it checks all the hardware, to ensure that it is working or not. Hence, when your computer restarts it checks the DVD drive which makes noise.

 

It is recommended to do not enter the bootable DVD or CD in the drive at the time of reboot. You can take out the DVD and then shut down the computer, so that the next time when system reboots there should not be a DVD in the drive.

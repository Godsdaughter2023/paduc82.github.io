STLang.dll is a dynamic link library file used by Sigmatel software. This is essential for the audio playback capabilities of your PC. The errors caused by this error can prevent your PC from playing music and any other form of audio.

You can actually delete the driver that’s causing the problem and then allow Windows to load the default driver.

a. Click on Start, Click on Run, and type devmgmt.msc in the search box.

b. On the Device Manager window, scroll down the list and open up “Sound, Video and Game Controllers“

c. Click on the audio component and, right click and select “Uninstall”

d. Reboot the computer.

Hi _JediProgrammer

Most likely this is because the Fast Startup option in Windows 10 is actually the same as hibernate. When you shut down a Windows 10 PC, the state of RAM is written to the hard drive and when you startup again, that is just read back from the hard drive into RAM

Most users don't know that to truly shut Windows 10 down you need to use the restart option, then Windows is really shut down and re-started and this is a very good thing to do every now and then to flush out RAM and does in fact fix a lot of issues in Windows 10

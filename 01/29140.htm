jeeped say:
The size required for storage of a recorded show will depend upon the format used and the length of the movie. The term Hi-Def is simply not enough information to quantify an appropriate answer.

With regards to your 16Gb USB thunb drive, you would be much better off getting a 1TB or 2TB external hard drive with a USB hookup and external power source. If the Kogan STB has no settings to adjust for recording quality, just record a half hour program and use that as a guide for future recordings, multiplying the space required by the difference in recording time.

jetta48 say:

Hi

No - Photo Gallery and the Photos app are two separate apps.

Generally, your photos exist in one place on your hard drive when you import them. The two apps are just seeing these stored files. The apps don't store the photos within themselves - they just show you which photos are available and let you do things with them.

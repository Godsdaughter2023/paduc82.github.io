Flatout is getting a new game on PS4, Xbox One and PC

The Flatout series is always one we have been fond out and the good news is that there's another one in development, this time for PC, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.

Publisher Strategy First and developer Kylotonn Entertainment, will release the title in 2016, with director, Emanuel Wall saying, "FlatOut is our most important franchise. There is a strong vocal community that have numerous constructive ideas and suggestions on what they would like to experience in an all new FlatOut. We would like to invite anyone who is a fan of FlatOut to be part of the process. We are excited about our partnership with Kylotonn Entertainment."

The original FlatOut games were developed by Bugbear Entertainment, although Flatout 3 was developed by a different team and seemed to lose the appeal of the first two.

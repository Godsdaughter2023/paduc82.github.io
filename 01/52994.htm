nerdyrebel21 say:

Assign a new IP address with higher number to Xbox. I changed mine from xx.xx.xx.4 to xx.xx.xx.44 and haven't had a problem since. Think of IP address as intersections, .2-.10 as one .11-.20 another and so on. Your router/modem automatically assigns the lowest numbers in order as devises are added, wired and wireless. All traffic is trying to get through that first intersection to all your devices and clogging it up, hence your disconnects. There is little to no traffic at the higher IP addresses, or intersections, so your XBL connection speeds right through.

Skype doesn't recognize a video camera

I have the latest version of Skype installed on my Win 10 desktop computer.  I have a Logitech C270 webcam and software installed.  Windows recognizes my webcam, but Skype will not, so it is impossible for me to make video calls.  I want to know why Skype doesn't recognize my video cam and what I need to do to fix this.  Thanks.

davem121 say:
Hi WesternGuy,

Open the Settings App (gear icon on your Start Menu)

Go to Privacy - Camera

Make sure that Skype is turned on in the list of apps that can access your webcam . . .

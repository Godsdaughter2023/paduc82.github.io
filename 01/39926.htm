System Restore (at least what I was suggesting) should have no impact on your files in general.  It is, as the name implies, for SYSTEM files.  Your files (ones you created) are not "SYSTEM" files (or those belonging to the operating system itself.)  While there are unique exceptions to this rule, they are few and far between.  You may be thinking of "Restore Previous Versions" . . .?

Quoting Microsoft from here:
http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/system-restore-faq#1TC=windows-7

"System Restore affects Windows system files, programs, and registry settings.

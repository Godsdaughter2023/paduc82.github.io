Hi Rod,

The difference is very simple. When contacts are saved to the Mail and Calendar app on Windows 10, they are saved locally and only on your PC.

Richard,

I talked to the devs and they explained what is not working with your file. We only support a subset of WRL/VRML features including:

  • Mesh
    • Vertices
    • Faces
  • Materials
    • object color
    • texture
    • vertex color (the same vertex color is used in all polygons that share this vertex)
    • color indices (different color is used for each the polygon that share this vertex)
    • face color

All other features of VRML are ignored, including "Box".

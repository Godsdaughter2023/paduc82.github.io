davem121 say:
Hi Arun,

If you are not using Edge

In your Browser, at the top right click the 3 dots (ellipsis)

Choose Settings - Advanced Settings

Somewhere you will find the 'Hardware Acceleration' setting - turn that off

Restart your Browser

If you are using Edge:

Open Edge

Go to this website: https://helpx.adobe.com/flash-player/kb/flash-p...

Click the Test Flash Player button

Right-click on the image below the Test Flash Player button choose Settings

Uncheck Enable hardware acceleration

Click on Close

Restart Edge

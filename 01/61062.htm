Xbox One Elite Bundle

I see they're now offering the xbox one elite bundle which comes with a 1TB Hybrid SSD drive, and the $150 elite controller that everyone's been talking about.

For those who don't know, a hybrid SSD drive is a normal platter drive, with an additional SSD storage chip built into it. This way, the drive doesn't cost much more than a regular HDD, but still gives some SSD benefits. The drive will copy the files used most often into the SSD chip so that those files will load much faster. For the X1, this means much faster boot times, especially for "energy saver" mode. If you tend to play 1 game a lot, that game's loading times will likely become much faster as well. I don't suspect you will find much difference in OS performance post boot.

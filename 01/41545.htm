Hello,

Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Community.

I request you to activate built-in administration account from the Windows recovery environment.

Follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Settings menu and go to Update & security > Recovery > Advanced startup. Clicking Advanced startup will restart your PC in recovery mode.

  2. Click Troubleshoot to see your advanced options.

  3. In the Troubleshoot menu, click Advanced options. Click Command Prompt to change enter command prompt.

  4. Type “net user administrator /active:yes” and press Enter.

  5. Once you’re done, you can access your built-in administrator account by logging off your current account anytime.

  6. Type "exit" and press Enter. Click "Continue" to come out of Windows recovery environment.

I hope this helps you. If you have any further query in future, feel free to contact us. We will be happy to help you.

  4. Locate your network adapter once again, right click it and choose Update Driver Software.

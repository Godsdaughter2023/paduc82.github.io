How can I transfer files using a USB Flash Drive?

1. Insert the USB Flash Drive

2. Open Windows Explorer - click Start then click Computer.

3. Locate the pictures on the computer, highlight them, then select Copy.

4. Within the Windows Explorer window, open the USB flash drive.
5. You can now copy the pictures to the USB drive by selecting Paste. You can also create a separate folder for the pictures and paste them there.

6. Plug the USB flash drive into the other computer, then copy them to your Documents folder.


You can also burn the files to a CD or DVD to transfer them.

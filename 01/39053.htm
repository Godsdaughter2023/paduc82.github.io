Hi All,

I have listed some steps below for troubleshooting achievements and challenges that do not unlock. If none of these work let me know and I can post the rest of them.

1. If you have met the requirements for the achievement to unlock but the notification does not immediately display or your gamer score does not update, allow for 24 hours to past as there could be possibly a delay in the Xbox Live service that validates the unlocking of achievements and challenges, although this is rare. Your progression on the game will have been recorded, so don't worry.

2. If there still no notification of the achievement unlocking after 24 hours, open the achievement app and see if the achievement(s) shows unlocked there. Next try hard resetting your Xbox One so the System Cache is cleared.

This can be done by holding the power button for 10 seconds while the system is on. The system will then turn off. When you hit the button again, it will start and display the green start-up screen. Now reconnect to Xbox Live and wait one hour to see achievement(s).

3. If clearing the cache does not make a difference, try removing your gamer profile from your Xbox One, and then add the profile back to sync game data from the cloud. Again leave the Xbox One connected to Live for a minimum of 1 hour to let your profile sync with the achievement unlocking service.

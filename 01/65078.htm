Hi ladydi63,

 

If you power on your laptop when the battery is above 95%, you will get the message "plugged but not charging"

 

If you power on your laptop when the battery is less than 95%, you will see the message "Plugged and charging" until the battery charge reaches 100% (not 95%). That's why sometimes, even if you see that your battery is at 97% and charging also, that means before starting your laptop, the battery was less than 95% and reaches 97% since then.

[PID]

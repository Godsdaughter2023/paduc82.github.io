Move all your User Library folders to D:

Open the Settings App (gear icon on your Start Menu)

However, try these:

Method 1: Show Disabled devices and set speaker as default device.

Perform the task mentioned below to set speaker as default:

a.     Click on Start button and click on control Panel.

b.     Click on Sound icon.

c.     In the new window click on the "Playback" tab. Right click on the blank space and click on Show Disabled devices.

d.     Now check if Speaker is listed and right click on it and choose enable.

e.     Highlight that speaker and click on "Set as Default"

Method 2: Also update the sound card with the latest drivers from the manufacturer’s website.

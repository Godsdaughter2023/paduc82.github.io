Upon loading an installed game, your Xbox will read the game in the disc tray to make sure you still own the game so you can't install games then return them or rent them, install and then return them etc,. The main point of installing games is to protect from two things.

1. To protect your discs so if a disc were to suffer from minor damage and get a few scratches, it won't effect your gameplay.

2. To put less stress on your console as it reads the games and therefore giving your console and it's DVD drive a longer life expectancy.

6501ridge say:

USB Ports stopped working when I upgraded to windows 10

I upgraded to Windows 10 pro from Windows 7 pro and my computer will not detect my USB drives. I use them to transfer data from my Computer at work, to my lap top, and my desktop at home. They were all running windows 7 and they worked perfectly. I only upgraded one computer so far and the windows 10 computer is the only one they don't work on. What do I need to do to fix this or do I just need to go back to Windows 7. I have to be able to transfer data.

a. user say:

Hi Ridge,

Thank you for being part of Windows 10 family.

 
Sorry to know that you are facing issues with USB Ports on Windows 10.

Refer to the below Methods and check if it helps.

Method 1: I would suggest you to run the Hardware and Devices troubleshooter and check if it helps.

1. Press Windows Key, then click on Search bar.

2. Type “Troubleshooting” without the quotes and click on Troubleshooting.

3. Then click on View All on the left panel of the screen.

4. Click on Hardware and Devices.

5. Click on Next and follow the instructions.

If the issues still persist, refer to the below method.

Method 2: I suggest you to check the status of the external drive.

1. Press Windows Key + R Key.

2. Then type “devmgmt.msc” without the quotes and hit on Enter Key.

3. ExpandPorts(COM and LPT)

4. Then right click again on the driver and click on Update driver software.

5. Again Expand Universal Serail Bus Controllers.

6. Then right click again on the driver and click on Properties.

6. Check the status of the drivers.

7. Then right click on each drivers and click on Update driver software.

Hope it helps. Contact us if you need any further assistance, we will be glad to help.

  • mspaint.exe should be in C:\Windows\system32
  • You probably also will find a copy of mspaint.exe in C:\Windows\ServicePackFiles\i386
  • You probably also will find one or more copies (shown in blue type) in folders named C:\Windows\$Nt.... These are older versions that have been replaced

You can do some pre-requisite tasks to ensure a smooth migration from Windows 7 to Windows 8.

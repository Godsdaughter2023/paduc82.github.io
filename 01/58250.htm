therealmagaman say:

More then likely your router is designating the IP off the units MAC Address, however since your wired and wireless connection is two different network devices, they each have a differnet MAC Addresses.

An easier way to fix this is to go into your router settings through a browser and you should see a section for DHCP Reservations. You should then see each of your devices including the Xbox wired and wireless (again it will show twice with different Mac Addresses) you can then reserve a different IP address for each Xbox connection and then set-up port forwarding to each of those IPs.

I have the e-4200 model from Cisco, and the DHCP reservations are right on the Basic Settings Page.

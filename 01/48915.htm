Windows 10 on a Dell Inspiron PC; this is what works for me to fix ghost touch - change the screen refresh rate.

For some reason, the system's refresh rate changes on its own. Resetting this works every time for me.

Assuming your machine is using Intel Graphics, open the Intel Graphics Control Panel from the system tray (this can be tricky given your system is recording screen touches all over the place - what I do is run my fingers all over the screen to overload the inputs, this usually offers a very small window of opportunity to actually click on the tray and select the proper icon).

Select Advanced Mode.

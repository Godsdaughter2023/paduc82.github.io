bb.90 say: Just got mine to work. Here's what I did.
First, make sure the printer is on and plugged in.

1. Download the Windows 8 Canada drivers from http://canoncanada.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/39815/~/ip4500-series-printer-driver-ver.-2.11-(windows-8-x64%2Fvista-x64).

2. Install the driver.

3. After you click Complete, go to Control Panel > Hardware and Sound > View Devices and Printers.

4. Right click on your iP4500 (mine just said "Inkjet Printer") and click remove.

5. Unplug the printer USB and plug it back in (I used a different USB port & left the printer on).
*May need to plug into different USB port, turn on/off printer, or restart computer to get it to work.

6. Windows should be installing the driver, this time it will come up as Canon iP4500. 

Let me know if it works, you might have to play around with the drivers/steps to get it to work, but that is step by step what I did. Good Luck!

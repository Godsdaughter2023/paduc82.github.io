arminatorx say:

The Xbox console does not support USB sound devices or USB headsets. 

Only headsets that plug into the Xbox controller are supported. Be that 3.5mm jack headsets (CTIA standard) or the chat headsets with a USB-similar-looking plug that goes into the wide connector of the controller. 

Also there are specifically licensed headsets that connect wirelessly directly to the console like the Plantronics RIG 800LX

But the only headsets that have an USB connector use that connector to get USB power to their wireless transmitter, but no actual sound is transmitted over that USB cable.

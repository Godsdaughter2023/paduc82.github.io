    chris cupler say: Answer depends on the license type (Retail, Upgrade, OEM System Builder, or OEM-Preinstalled) that you're referring to.

    Retail and Upgrade can be transferred; OEM version cannot (they're forever tied to the hardware on which they're first activated.)
    Transferring the license simply consists of installing the OS on the new computer, and activating it.  If it won't auto-activate, you should be given the option to do a phone activation (this is, typically, a <5 minute phone call, which often doesn't even require you to talk to a human.)
    There is no need (nor method) to "de-activate" the old computer.  Just decommision (cease to use) it.

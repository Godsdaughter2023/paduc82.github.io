debleena s say:

Hi Rosa,

The window size for Hearts is actually by design in Windows XP.


However, there is a workaround. You may follow these steps:


Step 1

a. First, copy the game.

b. Click on Start, All Programs, Games.

c. Right click on the Hearts game.

d. Select Properties from the list.

e. Press the Find Target button on the next window.

f. Right Click on the highlighted file and select Copy.

g. Next paste the copy to your Desktop.

h. Right Click on your Desktop.

i. Select Paste from the list.


Step 2

Finally, we'll change the resolution setting for the game:

a. Right click on the game you just pasted to your Desktop.

b. Select Properties from the list and Click on the Compatibility Tab.

c. Put a check in the box marked "Run in 640 x 480 screen resolution"

d. Now whenever you want to play, click on that game on your Desktop and it will open in full screen mode using the largest Cards and Numbers possible.


Hope the information helps.

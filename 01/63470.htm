Sticky notes are kept in a single stack that you can browse. The number of the current note and the total number of notes appear near the upper-right corner of the note.For example, "8/14" indicates that a note is the eighth of 14 notes.

1. Open Sticky Notes by tapping the Start button, typing Sticky Notes in the search box, and then tapping Sticky Notes in the list of results.

2. Tap the Previous Note or Next Note arrow near the upper-right corner of the note, under the title bar.

You can export and import your sticky notes stack.

When you import a stack of sticky notes, you either add the imported notes to your current stack or replace your current stack with the imported one. You can import a stack that you previously exported

Follow these steps to import a stck of sticky notes:

1. Click on Tools and click Import.

2. Navigate to where you saved the Sticky Notes.
a. Highlight the .snt file.
b. Choose if you want to Merge with your current stack of Sticky Notes or Replace them.
c.  Click on Open.

When you export your stack of sticky notes, you save a copy of the stack by specifying a file name and location. You can do this to create a backup copy or to make the stack available for copying to a different computer. After you export the stack, it remains unchanged on your desktop.

1. Click on Tools and click Export.
2. Navigate to where you want to save the stack of Sticky Notes. 
3. Type in a name.
4. Click on Save.

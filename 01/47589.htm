a. user say:

Scanreg does not exist in Vista.  No registry cleaner, fixer, booster, optimizer, enhancer, repairer or anything like that is included orshould ever be used with Vista.  Few do anything at all that is helpful, some are conduits for the distribution of malware, and quite a few can cause a great deal of harm (to the point where a re-installation is the only option).  In general, they are mostly poison to your system.  Yes, some people do use them and don't have these problems, but the risk far outweighs the rewards.  Vista doesn't need these things - it takes care of the registry quite well on its own.  If you value your system and your registry, you won't use any such product (including Scanreg).  I don't want to see you back here with a serious problem we can't repair caused by one of these products (and I have seen quite a few).

If you have a specific problem that you suspect is caused by the registry, then document that problem and its symptoms and why you think the registry is responsible and we'll see if we can help you in some other way than the very bad one you are/were considering.

canadian tech say:

"sudden" shutdowns are usually done by Windows to protect the system from over-heating.

Do NOT use your laptop on any soft surface.  Only hard surfaces.  There are vents on the bottom side that are the source of air used by the fan to cool your system.

If that does not fix it, you may have to get some work done to clean out the fan and possibly replace it and possibly replace the thermal paste between the processor and the heat synch.

Games typically generate far more heat than other applications.  Laptops are a lousy choice for gamer PCs, simply because they can not provide sufficient cooling by basic design limitations.

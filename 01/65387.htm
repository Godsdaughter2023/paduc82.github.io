6. Open the Mail app and click the option to add account.  Enter your Hotmail email address and password.

7. After you click Sign in you'll be asked if you want to use the same account on all the apps.  For the calendar to work properly you must allow this; so DO NOT choose this app only BUT click next.

8. After a few moments you should be able to go to the inbox as normal.  However, when you open the Calendar you should no longer see a separate 'Microsoft account' but just your Hotmail one.

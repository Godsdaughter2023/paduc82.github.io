davem121 say:
When you first purchase your laptop, you should charge it fully and let it run right down, do this 3 times. Thereafter, it is best to keep the battery between 20% and 80% . . . Well that is the current best practice! In reality, you should do the 3 full charges and discharges. After that let the battery run down on a regular basis (2 - 3 times a week) to prolong battery life. If you use your laptop all the time and it is plugged in all the time, this will reduce battery life, so if it is plugged in all the time, pop out the battery and only use it when travelling . . .

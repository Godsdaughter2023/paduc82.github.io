Hi quilterfry,

What program are you using to export the photos to SD card reader?

Windows Photo Gallery does not have an option to export the photos to SD Card reader.

The better approach would be to copy the photos directly to SD card reader from the pictures folder.

Here are the steps you need to follow:

1. Put the SD card into the slot on your SD card reader. Plug it into the USB drive slot on your computer.

2. Click on Start and click on "Computer" menu for PCs. The SD card should appear as a new drive in the menu. Open the card's folder.

3. Open the folder on your computer's hard drive that contains the pictures you want to transfer to the SD card.

4. Highlight all of the files you want to transfer. You can highlight multiple files simultaneously, by clicking one file once. Then, hold the "Shift" key and use the directional arrows to highlight more files, or you can position the mouse over another file and hold the "Shift" key while clicking once on the file.

5. Right click the mouse and select "Copy" from the menu.

6. Open the SD card's folder again, and right click the mouse and select "Paste" from the menu to copy the files into the SD folder. You can also drag and drop the files from your hard drive's folder to the SD card's folder.

7. Try disconnecting and reconnecting the SD card reader to ensure the photos have transferred successfully.

Hope this information is helpful.

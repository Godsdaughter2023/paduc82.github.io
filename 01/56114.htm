smwutches say:

It's not a bad disc. It has to do with the region of the disc vs. the region of your console. US used NTSC region coding and Australia uses PAL. Generally NTSC discs won't work on a PAL console and vice versa unless the disc is region free.

So when you purchase games you'll either need to purchase a PAL version or make sure the disc is region free.

sciencedr. say:

most recent or common attacks or scams

recent methods to gain access to your computer
andre da costa say:

- Keyloggers (records your key strokes to steal your passwords when you try log onto a merchant site or bank website)

- Drive by downloads, you visit a certain web site that runs a script and attempts to download a piece of code to your computer compromising your system without you even knowing.

- Social engineering through email - receiving email from a stranger that sounds credible asking for certain personal information such as credit card, passwords... It can even come in the guise of an email from your ISP or Web Mail provider.

A new trend is using a friend or close family members compromised account to obtain valuable information, these are called zombie account (you have to be very careful about these types email this days.). Always confirm with the person if you weren't expecting an emergency email, call them first.

- Root kits, Trojans are becoming more stealth and clever, you have types that can now attack the BIOS level make it difficult to recover your system, in most cases requiring a clean install.

It is always to backup regularly, use a limited user account daily usage, avoid web sites that are questionable, becareful where you download software from or other types of media. Keep your computer update and always keep your Antivirus utility update and do regular scans and also becareful of thumb drives you are connecting to your computer.

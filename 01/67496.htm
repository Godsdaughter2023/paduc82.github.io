Hi Jordan,


While upgrading to Windows 10 and the progress bar slows down significantly and you are seeing a black screen with a blue circle at the 30-39% range, then Windows may be downloading all dynamic updates for Windows 10. At 96%, then Windows may be backing up your data. 

The length of time to download Windows 10 may vary, it will be dependent on the quality of the internet connection, the amount of data to move and the computer’s speed.  It may take as long as 24 hours. If your hard drive is still flashing, there’s a good chance that it is still making progress. You may try disconnecting any USB devices and extended display cables that may be connected.  This has been seen to speed up the download process significantly.

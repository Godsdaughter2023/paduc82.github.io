Head on over to Settings > Privacy > Camera, and you will see that new apps like 'Camera' have been added to the list.  (Note:  Non-Store apps will not appear on this list.)

If you use a third-party camera app that was not downloaded from the Store, and you find you no longer have access to your camera, try flipping the toggle under "Allow apps to access your camera" to On.  Flip the 'all apps' and individual app toggles to your preferred settings.

Similarly, if you have lost access to your microphone, go to Settings > Privacy > Microphone, and try flipping the toggle under "Allow apps to access your microphone" to On.

If you are admin on your device, you should see an additional button at the top of the page. Clicking this button will launch an admin toggle that controls webcam/microphone access for all users of your device. We hope you found this information useful in resolving any camera errors you came across after taking this update. As always, you are welcome to drop us a line in the Feedback Hub.

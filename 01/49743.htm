AHCI Port 0 Device Error when Boot - Microsoft Community

pere garcia i ricart say:

AHCI Port 0 Device Error when Boot

The message "AHCI Port 0 Device Error" appears when boot process in Windows 7.

Process finishes but PC runs very slowly.

How can fix this device error?

hp-margaretha-sorin say:

Hey mate,

Seems like you're experiencing a drive failure. This AHCI device error was generated from the BIOS' S.M.A.R.T. detection. It has absolutely nothing to do with the Windows 10 operating system. At the time you see this error message, the operating system has not been yet started. S.M.A.R.T. has detected that your hard drive at port 0 is faulty and the drive may or may not be accessible. Your hard drive may fail any time once this happened.

Try to press F1 and boot your operating system, back up your files and look for a new drive to replace the faulty one.

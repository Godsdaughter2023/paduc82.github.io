My computer is an MSI GT72 2QD.

There are no updates available for my computer, I have installed them all.

I tried installing a chipset driver from my computer manufacturer's website and restarted, but the issues still remain.

I also tried making another account on my computer and tried shutting down and entering sleep mode, but the issues still remained.

Help please.

travisgatlin say: Bumping this, still hoping for some suggestions.

randy a_ say:

Hi, I've had similar problems with sleep and found that disabling Away Mode and enabling Sleep Mode under the Power Options did the trick for me.

Go to:

  1. Control Panel\Hardware and Sound\Power Options\Edit Plan Settings
  2. Click on Change advanced power settings
  3. Make sure that your active power plan is selected in the dropdown box
  4. Scroll to multimedia settings (2nd from the bottom)
  5. Expand When sharing media

If you notice "Away mode" under either battery or plugged in, change it to "Allow the computer to sleep"

This prevents the default media sharing schemes from keeping your computer up all night.

Hope this helps :)

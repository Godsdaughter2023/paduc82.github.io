theasimmohammed say: Actually the problem persisted due to the fact that:

1. "Windows Font Cache Service" was running, so I disabled it.

2. "Windows Presentation Foundation Font Cache 3.0.0.0" was also running, so I disabled it too.

Click Start, enter "run" into the search box and click on Run and input "services.msc" and click ok. Once you have done that locate the two services, above, once located double click on the first one found and in the drop down menu next to "Startup Type" select "Disabled", and then click apply and then ok, do this for the second service also. Once that is completed restart your PC, and hopefully your issues should be resolved.

Install that and click "Full Scan" and after it has completed (It may take a while) click "Fix Errors".

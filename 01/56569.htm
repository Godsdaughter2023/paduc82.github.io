Code 24 or "This device is not present, is not working properly, or does not have all its drivers installed." occurs if the device is installed incorrectly. The problem could be a hardware failure, or a new driver might be needed. Devices stay in this state if they have been prepared for removal. The recommended resolution is to remove the device, however, we can't do that with the Standard PS/2 Keyboard and Microsoft PS/2 Mouse device drivers because they are part of the operating system (OS).

The Standard PS/2 Keyboard device driver is a driver needed by your PC to make the built-in keyboard work if you're using a laptop, and to make any external keyboard to work with your PC.

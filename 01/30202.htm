Does anyone know why I have a 2 instead of a 3 next to my name? If it updates like it's supposed to, I should have been a 3 last month.

dirty loco say:

It isn't the amount of time you've had Xbox Live account.  It's the amount of time you've had a GOLD subscription.  So any time you have had a silver account, that time will be paused until you go gold again.  Once you go back to gold, the time resumes.  Here's an example:
 

  • You start a gold account with a 3 month card on 18th January 2010
  • On the 18th April 2010 you can't afford to renew the account, so it turns silver
  • Two weeks later you buy a 12 month subscription, renewing it on the 2nd of May 2010


That this means is the 1 year tenure will NOT be on the 18th of January 2011.  It will be 2 weeks after that date, due to the 2 weeks of having a silver account.

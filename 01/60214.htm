geoffc1 say: Tags are one form of metadata that is used by WLPG. If your photos are in JPEG format, then metadata such as people/geotag/caption/ratings are stored in two places:

1) in the Pictures.pd6 database of Windows Live Photo Gallery (WLPG), and
2) in the individual JPEG photo files as XMP metadata.

So, you should be able to move your photos to another computer, and the WLPG on that new computer should read the photo files and import the metadata (including tags) automatically.

I say "should" because there have been instances where WLPG has not been writing out metadata into the photos as it is supposed to do. See:
http://windowslivehelp.com/thread.aspx?threadid=eb30158f-0a4f-4779-9212-e87e04e28cf0

It would be wise to test a few of your photos on another computer that is running WLPG if you can, before disposing of your old computer...

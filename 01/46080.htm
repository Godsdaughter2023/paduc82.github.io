This issue usually occur due to the fingerprint reader driver is out dated. Let’s try the simple troubleshooting steps and check if it helps in resolving the issue. Follow the below methods:

 Method 1.

I would suggest you to run the Hardware and devices troubleshooter to diagnose the exact issue.

  1. Press Windows + X keys on the keyboard and select Control Panel.

  2. Type Troubleshooting in the search bar on the Control Panel.

  3. Click on Hardware and sound under Troubleshoot computer problems.

  4. Click on Hardware and devices under Devices.

  5. Click on Advanced on the Hardware and devices dialog box and click on Run as administrator (Enter the credentials if required).

  6. Click on Next and follow onscreen instruction complete the Hardware and devices troubleshooter.             

If the issue still persist try the below method.

Method 2.

I would recommend you to update Upek fingerprint reader driver from Device Manager manually and check if this helps. Follow the below steps:

  1. Press (Windows key + X) and click on “Device Manager”.

  2. Click on “Biometric Devices” and expand it.

  3. Right click on the driver device and select update.

  4. Once update is done, restart the computer. 

Hope this helps. Post us with status we will be glad to help you.

    Details

    Why is Microsoft putting so much emphasis on cross-platform?

    The Windows 10 companion app helps further Microsoft’s cross-platform strategy to make high-value Microsoft services available to everyone on every device, regardless of OS, to help them do more.


    Does offering the Cortana companion app on other platforms mean you’re giving up on Windows Phone?

    No. Windows has always supported a wide range of devices, and this is just another opportunity to make devices work together through a Windows experience that’s available when and where you need it.

    How is the Cortana experience different on Windows vs. iOS and Android?

    Cortana is deeply integrated into Windows 10 to offer unique experiences you’ll only find on Windows devices. Some features require access to the system that aren’t currently possible with iOS or Android, so things like toggling settings or opening apps won’t initially be available in the Cortana companions for those platforms.  Similarly, the ability to invoke Cortana hands-free by saying “Hey Cortana” requires special integration with the device’s microphone, so that feature will be limited to Windows Phones, PCs and Android.

    When will the Cortana Companion app be available in the Google Play and Apple App Stores for Android and iOS users?

    The Cortana companion was launched on December 9th and is available now for both Android phones and iPhones!

    Are you sure Apple and Google will allow Cortana in their stores?

    We can’t speak for Google or Apple.

    On which versions of Android and iOS will the Cortana Companion app be available?

    The Cortana Companion app is available for Android versions 4.1.2 or later.

    For iPhone, it is available for iPhone 4 with 8.0 and later versions.

    Which markets are getting Cortana via the companion app on Android and iOS?

    Cortana’s availability on Android and iOS via the companion app will mirror Cortana’s availability on Windows 10 PCs over time -- namely in the US, UK, China, Germany, France, Italy and Spain.  For markets where Cortana is not available on the PC, Windows 10 still lets you search the web, search your personal content and control settings on your PC.

    Why limit Cortana’s availability to just these few markets? Why isn’t it available worldwide?

    Wherever you are, Windows 10 lets you search the web, search your personal content and control settings on your PC. In order to provide the high-quality experience of a digital personal assistant, Cortana requires extensive work on local voice models and the continuous availability of rich local data. It’s an incredibly high quality bar, but we think it’s absolutely the right one.

    Can the Cortana Companion app be installed on Apple’s iPad? If so, which versions?

    The app is not specially designed for iPad, so the experience in iPad is less optimal than in phone.

    What are the feature differences across Cortana on Android, iOS and Windows?

Film & TV folder - Microsoft Community

desmondcolebourne say:

Film & TV folder

can you please tell me how to stop "Film and TV" from automatically saving my videos. Can it be set to ask first?

a. user say:

Hi Desmond,

The Movies & TV app doesn't necessarily "save" the videos. Instead its set to find files in video format in a location you specify.

It will automatically look for videos in the location so it can't be set to "ask first". The most convenient action we can take is to remove the folder where it looks for videos.

While on the Videos section of the app, click on Change where we look and remove the folder by clicking on the "X" button.

Once done, there should be no videos in the app anymore.

Let us know if you need more help.

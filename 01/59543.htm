cmdrkeene say:

The clipboard doesn't hold files - only text (so just file names) for short-term reference.  You can clear the clipboard by copying something else, at which point the old list in the clipboard is gone forever.

The files that had not yet been moved should be in their original location.

The files that had been moved but then failed are no longer available., and you'll have to restore from your most recent backup.

If you were moving them to the CD burner, they might still be in the "waiting to burn" folder.  Look here:

C:\Users\your username\appdata\local\microsoft\windows\burn

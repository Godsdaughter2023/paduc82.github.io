Perform the steps mentioned below to add Zoom to taskbar.

a: Type "Magnifier" while viewing the Windows Start screen.

  • b: Right-click the "Magnifier" result and then select "Pin to Taskbar." The button instantly appears on your taskbar, but you might need to change the settings.

  • c: Return to Desktop mode and then click the Magnifier button on the taskbar.

  • d: Click the "+" or "-" icons to adjust the zoom level.

  • e: Click "View" and then select your preferred zoom mode. "Full Screen" zooms the entire screen, but "Lens" uses a small Zoom window that overlays the main screen. "Dock" places the zoom window in a separate panel, so your normal window isn't obstructed.

  • f: Click the gear icon to further adjust the Magnifier. You can try inverting the colors and changing the tracking method used to follow the mouse, keyboard or text insertion points.

  • Click/tap on the Application Tools Manage tab

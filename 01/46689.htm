ann marie scrimshaw say: Hi Kevin.  Microsoft did offer an unsupported registry cleaner (RegClean) back in the Win9x days when the size of the registry did affect performance but that is no longer the case and it was withdrawn a few years back.  I cant imagine that they would consider releasing a similar utility again for general use.  To do so, would require a vast amount of knowledge and continuous updating, not just of the registry entries written by Microsoft software but third party applications too.

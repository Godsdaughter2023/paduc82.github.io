eury lee say:

Windows 8.1 touchpad(scrolling and zooming) not working (Acer)

I downloaded Windows 8.1 today and when my computer was done reloading everything I went online only to find that scrolling and zooming did not work on my touch pad. I went through settings as well as looking online but I haven't been able to find a solution. Any suggestions?
babu v say:

Hi Eury,

 

What is the model of your Acer Laptop?

 

Let's run the hardware troubleshooter and check if it helps.

a. Swipe in from the right edge of the screen, and then tap Search.
(If you're using a mouse, point to the upper-right corner of the screen, move the mouse pointer down, and then click Search.)

b. Enter Troubleshooting in the search box, tap or click Troubleshooting, and then tap or click Hardware and Sound.

c. Tap or click Hardware and Devices, and follow the instructions on the screen. You might be asked for an admin password or to confirm your choice.

 

If the hardware troubleshooter fails to fix the issue then I would suggest you to visit the Acer website and install the updated touchpad drivers for the touchpad.

http://www.acer.co.in/ac/en/AU/content/drivers

 

Hope this helps and do let us know if you need any assistance.

samir muranjan say: Hi Eury,

I have the same model Acer as you and faced a similar problem. I downloaded two drivers from the Acer support website today (http://us.acer.com/ac/en/US/content/drivers), an ELAN touchpad driver and a Synaptics touchpad driver and my scrolling and zooming functionality has been restored. 

My OS is Windows 8.1 Pro 64-bit.

Hope this works for you as well.

It's Windows 10 Hybrid Shutdown so that the system can shut down and start really fast. You can turn it off manually:

  • Go to Search, search 'Power Options' (Or right click your battery icon, and select Power Options)
  • In Power Options, select "Choose what the power button does"
  • Select "Change settings that are currently unavailable" located under Define Power Buttons and turn on password protection.
  • The Shut Down settings at the bottom become available.
  • De-select "Turn on fast start up"

I've done this and it shuts down as normal without needing to select the power button. It might be a little slower in shut down and a start up but at least everything shuts down correctly and your battery doesn't drain.

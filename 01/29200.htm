lightyear013 say:

Amish, considering that your soundbar is designed for 7.1 surround sound you're going to want to connect your Xbox One to it using HDMI and then in your Xbox settings, under display and sound, set the HDMI audio to 7.1 uncompressed. This should get the best sound out of your system.

DTS is going to compress the audio signal before it sends it to the soundbar which will then have to decompress it before it plays it back. This can potentially lead to a loss in audio quality during playback, whereas with the 7.1 uncompressed it doesn't have to go through that extra step so it should give you a more accurate playback of the audio.

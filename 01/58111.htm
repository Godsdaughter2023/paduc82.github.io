thedarkdragon11 say:

I have a halo 4 xbox so when i press the power button it sounds.the fans spins.the lights around the power button do not light up.the power brick only lights the light orange.i try to sync the controler with my xbox after i prees the start button on the xbox and the contorler just blink continuously and yes i checked the cables are firmly in place

razullian say:

Is the orange light on the power supply solid?

When the XBox is off, the power supply light is orange. But when the XBox is turned on and working fine, the power supply is lit up with green. If the light is orange, it usually means a hardware problem with the XBox (like a broken disc drive, for example). Before sending it in for repairs, try step 2 and if you can, step 3 below.

Is the orange light on the power supply flashing?

Step 1: Try plugging the power cable into a different plug socket. If the orange light turns to any other color than green or flashing orange, there is a good chance that your power supply needs replacing.

If the color turns anything other than green, also try this:

Step 2: Turn the XBox off and then unplug all the cables from the power supply starting with the one leading from the plug socket. Let it rest for up to 10 minutes before plugging it back in, making sure that every cable is in place, and then turn the XBox back on. If still not green, try step 2.

Step 3: If you have access to a second power supply from the same version XBox (for example from a second XBox or a friend), try plugging it in and turning the XBox on. If the XBox comes on without a problem, you will need to buy a replacement power supply.

If none of this helps, and even the alternative power supply lights up in orange with your XBox but works with its original machine, then your best option is to send it in for repairs. If your XBox is still under warranty the repairs should be free.

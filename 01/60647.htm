[quote user="CrazyKraka"]Concerning the power supply if im to purchase a 240v power supply would the console run properly or is the system only designed to run off of one voltage.[/quote]The console runs off DC power. The power brick converts from the local AC power standard to the DC power required by the console. An Xbox One power supply designed to run in a 240v region will provide the correct DC power to the console.

Note that the Xbox One power supplies are not worldwide compatible from an AC input perspective. A US power brick will not work in a 240v region.

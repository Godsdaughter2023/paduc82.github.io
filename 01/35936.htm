how to install bluetooth driver? - Microsoft Community

billlyzenga say:

how to install bluetooth driver?

how to install a  bluetooth driver

a. user say:

Bluetooth actually isn't a single driver. It's a collection of drivers, each of which provides a specific function to a Bluetooth device. That's why Bluetooth is correctly referred to as a 'stack.' With that language lesson out of the way ...

The stack for any given Bluetooth device depends on what specific Bluetooth functions are available for that device. The Bluetooth stack for any given device is usually provided on a disk that comes with the device or is downloaded from the device manufacturer's website.

a. user say:

You should have received a disk with your Bluetooth device that has the manufacturer's Bluetooth stack for your device. If you don't have the disk, no worries. Just go to the device manufacturer's website and download the correct Bluetooth stack for your device. No one else, besides the manufacturer, has the correct Bluetooth stack for your specific device.

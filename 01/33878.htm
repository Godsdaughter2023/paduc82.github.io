a. user say:

Hi,

There are many different ways that ntdll.dll errors may be displayed on your computer. Ntdll.dll errors can be caused by a number of different things which result in many different error messages, but some of the most common are: a corrupt or damaged version of the ntdll.dll file itself, corrupt hardware drivers, or issues between Windows and other programs. Ntdll.dll error messages might appear before or after a program is used, while a program is running, when Windows is started or shutdown, or even during a Windows installation.

Ntdll.dll errors can sometimes mean that a piece of hardware in your computer is malfunctioning. Try to Completely uninstall and reinstall Skype app and other applications that you recently installed that you think causing this. Also, update drivers for any hardware in your computer where updated drivers are available. Outdated drivers sometimes cause ntdll.dll errors.

Lastly, repair your installation of Windows. If individual software reinstallations fail to resolve the problem, a repair installation of Windows will replace the ntdll.dll file.

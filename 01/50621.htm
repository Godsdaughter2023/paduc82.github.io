zacabeb say:

Xbox 360 games are typically targeted at a 1280x720 resolution, although there are exceptions where games run at a lower or higher resolutions than that. A few XBLA games run natively at 1920x1080.

.

Performance and memory restrictions keep the Xbox 360 from running typical games at 1920x1080.

.

The primary reason for setting the Xbox 360 to output at the display's native resolution is to take advantage of the Xbox 360s internal scaler. This can improve picture quality to an extent by providing scaling that's more optimized for game graphics, letting games be scaled directly to the display resolution, and minimizing the effect of any chroma subsampling done in the display.

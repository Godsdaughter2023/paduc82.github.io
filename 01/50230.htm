Unlike Xbox 360, the Xbox One does not have a menu option to clear cache on the console. Instead, follow these steps to clear cache on an Xbox One:

In addition to Basith's advice security encryption is setup initially on your wireless router. Use WPA2-Personal (aka WPA-PSK [AES]) or at a minimum WPA with a long random key.

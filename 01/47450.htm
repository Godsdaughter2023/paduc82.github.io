Whether XP mode will work for you depends on what you plan to do in it. Regular office programs will work fine. The one exception is starting an XP mode program up on the 7 desktop - that's slow, because you're booting the XP virtual machine as well as starting the program. When you close the program, the VM is hibernated, so you wouldn't have to wait when you reopen it until you reboot your computer. But since you want to simulate XP itself, that shouldn't be a problem.

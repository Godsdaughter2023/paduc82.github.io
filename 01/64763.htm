Hopefully Sumit Dhiman2 and SpiritX MS MVP will weigh in on the new dump files too but here is what I think:

You said the computer always crashes when you come out of standby or sleep mode so that is good that you have a repeatable failure case so that when you make an adjustment you can test against the known failure case.

If you start making adjustments (like updating your video driver) just do one thing at a time and test so that when the problem goes away you will have a good idea about what fixed it.  If you change too many things at once you might not know which adjustment did the trick.

https://support.norton.com/sp/en/us/home/current/solutions/v60392881_EndUserProfile_en_us

smwutches say:

If you have the console set for instant on mode, turning the console off will put it into standby mode. You would have to turn it completely off by holding the power button down on the console.

If it's not on instant on mode, then turning it off via the controller or settings would turn it completely off. 

xbf brad a say:

Hello Ashrafqadir,

Thanks for coming to the forums with your question. There are two ways you can power off the console completely.

  1. Power cycle the console--Hold the power button on the console itself until it shuts off.
  2. menu option turn off console---Hit the guide button on controller, go to far right icon(system), go to bottom to turn off console.
  3. You can also go to power and start up and select energy saving mode this will fully shut down the console every time you turn it off.

Thanks,

Brad

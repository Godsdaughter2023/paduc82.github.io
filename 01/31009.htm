nicka-oh say: I found out what the problem was.  It was the NVIDIA Com Object error.  Essentially, my computer was having an error and Windows was trying to report it without my approval.  So, I went into the action center and made sure that it asked me before sending information.  Then, I went into the NVIDIA Control panel > Help >preferences tab and unchecked the box for automatically checking for updates.  Now, the problem that has been haunting me for months is gone and I can finally type and work, uninterrupted.

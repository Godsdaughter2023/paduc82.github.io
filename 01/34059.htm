a. user say:

Hi Lego,

I realize your concern about installing the graphics drivers on your computer.

I would like to inform that, Microsoft has also released a troubleshooter that allows you to hide Windows updates and drivers updates. Thus prevent Windows from reinstalling them until a revised version becomes available.

Download the troubleshooter wushowhide.diagcab from Microsoft; it’s a standalone application, no installation required.

When you run the application, you will receive:

  1. Show or hide updates screen, click on Next.
  2. You may click on Hide updates to hide and on Show hidden updates to see which updates are hidden.
  3. For now, select Hide updates, check mark the updates for graphics drivers, click on Next, and follow onscreen instructions.

Hope it helps. Reply to the post for further assistance on installing the graphics drivers for further assistance.

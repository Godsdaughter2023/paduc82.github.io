Your issue is likely that you are using the same account on those computers. In recent Windows 10 update, it will automatically sync the active wallpapers to every Windows 10 devices you've logged in the same account. However, you can prevent it by:

If yes, go ahead and open with the viewer and make sure the checkbox at the bottom of the open with window is checked.

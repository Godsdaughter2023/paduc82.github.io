Viruses exploit the disk structures that your computer uses to start up by replacing, redirecting, or corrupting the code and data that start the operating system. It can also destroy the firmware of your hard disk. Virus infection can never destroy your disk physically.

To address your concern about a virus infected HDD, we suggest that you contact your Original Equipment Manufacturer of your hard disk. Inform them about the issue and the troubleshooting steps that you've performed. They can help you to install the latest firmware on your HDD and it may synchronize it on your device's motherboard.

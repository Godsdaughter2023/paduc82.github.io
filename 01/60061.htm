My fans on my laptop have stopped working - Microsoft Community

mistymeechan say:

My fans on my laptop have stopped working

My fans on my laptop have stopped working for some reason. I have been told to check to see if they have possibly be turned off somehow or to update my 'bios'. How do I do this?
petaratanasov_87 say:

Go on your laptop's manufacturer's website, go to support, select the exact model of your laptop and you should find the latest BIOS version. Most BIOS files come with a tool that overwrite the existing one with the new one. They should also come with a manual on how to do it.

BE EXTREMELY CAREFUL! If something goes wrong and you mess up the existing BIOS you could literally kill your laptop - fixing it will cost you! Make sure you follow instructions to the letter, especially the most important one - the laptop must be on AC power and also the battery must have at least some charge so that if the power goes out the laptop finishes the process (which takes just a couple of minutes tops).

In my experience the BIOS is the unlikely cause for the fans not working. Whats more likely is a hardware issue - either the fan (or fans) is faulty or the chip that handles it (reads the temperature data and tells the fan to blow and how hard to do it).

mike hall... always say:

If you delete the recovery partition, you will lose the primary source of recovery and also the primary source of recovery disks.

 

If you make the disks and then delete the partition, you will have to rely upon only the disks for recovery.

 

Computer manufacturers do not always supply or even have recovery disks available for sale.

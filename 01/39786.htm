louiscornell say:

Ok - I found the fix. It was not caused by a Windows Update.

For anyone who has Spotify installed with Windows 10 x64, it can interfere with keyboard media buttons.

To fix this you have to disable "SpotifyWebHelper" under startup programs.  Under search, type msconfig, then under the Startup tab, open task manager, then disable "SpotifyWebHelper" and reboot.  Problem solved.

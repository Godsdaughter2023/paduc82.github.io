You can find your router's IP address on the manufacturer's website, or on the internet, or it might even be on a sticker attached to your router. It might possibly be 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.0.1. Then press enter.

You'll be brought to the router's configuration screens, one of which let's you assign a username and password for your router. Typically in a section called Administration, or something like that.

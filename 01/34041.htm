The free 60 minutes from Office 365 will not be applied on your Skype account automatically. You will need to link your Skype and Office 365 account in order to activate the free minutes on your Skype account.

To Change Screen Resolution of Display in Control Panel:

1. Open the Control Panel, and click on Display icon.

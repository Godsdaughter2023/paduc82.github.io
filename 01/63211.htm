Screen display uses the most power.  Lower brightness to as low as you can accept.  Notice two different condition settings:  plugged in and on battery.

If your battery shows dead when it is plugged in, either the battery is worn out and/or the charging cord is defective.

Laptop batteries have a life expectancy of about 3 years.  You may need to replace yours.  They are not that expensive.  But, know well that laptop batteries are very specific to your make and model.  Type a search on Amazon of your make and model and the word battery and you will see lots of choices.

Windows is pretty good at managing your battery settings based on your PC's needs. You can click the Battery icon in the bottom right corner of your screen to change the slider to better performance or better battery life, depending on how fast you need your PC to perform.

There isn't really any better settings for battery life, as the best settings depend on what you're doing with your PC at the time.

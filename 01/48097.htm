Reset the network settings

Reset the network settings on your Xbox 360 console to the defaults. Here's how:

1.Press the Guide button on your controller.

2.Go to Settings, and then select System Settings.

3.Select Wired Network.

4.Select Configure Network.

5.Go to the Additional Settings tab, and then select Restore to Factory Defaults.

6.Select Yes, Restore to Factory Defaults. This resets the console's network settings only, and not other settings.

Now, restart your console and test your Xbox LIVE connection. Here's how:

1.Restart your console.

2.Press the Guide button on your controller, go to Settings, and then select System Settings.

3.Select Network Settings.

4.Select Wired Network.

5.Select Test Xbox LIVE Connection.

If this solution worked, your console will connect to Xbox LIVE.

Note If you are prompted to update your console software, select Yes

There a quite a few FAQs covering the majority of your questions on the Skype website.

1.  You can send SMS or IMs.  This has never been inbound SMS. 

2.  A Skype number is an inbound subscription similar to a landline tethered to an Internet connection.  Its sole purpose is for you to receive inbound phone calls at your computer.  Without a Skype number people would no be able to call you using their mobile or landline phone.  The one exception to that is using Skype Connect by someone creating a number for you or possibly your creating one for yourself..  A Skype number can be your Caller ID, a Skype name cannot.

Some phone numbers you will not be able to reach without Caller ID.  This is due to their spam settings blocking all anonymous calls.

4.  You go to the Skype number page or references to it.

5.  Custom voicemail is not longer an option.  A generic voicemail greeting is heard.  You receive voice messages in the client.

6.  You can enable call forwarding on your account to another number.  This single rule will apply to your users who dial your Skype name or your Skype number (if you have one).

7.  You can in the Skype for Windows and Mac Desktop clients.  That option is not available in other clients (Skype App, Skype for Web, etc.).  You can always verify this by installing the client and going through the available options, once you are signed in.

scotthay say:

The Sony DCR-TRV310 does not have a USB port on it.  If you plugged a standard USB cable into the port on the front of the camera that says DV IN/OUT then you plugged it into the firewire port, not a USB port.  You need an IEEE 1394 cable to hook this up to a computer (that has a firewire port).  Not sure if links work here, but this cable from Amazon will work;  https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00NH11SZA/ref=oh_aui_detailpage_o00_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1

Windows 7 should recognize it and install the driver automatically if you haven't already blown your firewire port jamming in that USB cable in the camera (they are easy to blow and are not hot swapable like USB, so don't unplug/plug them in when both the computer and camera are on or you could blow either the firewire port in the computer or camera or both).

If you are using some sort of firewire to USB converter cable then the company that made the converter should be supplying you their driver if it isn't built into Windows.

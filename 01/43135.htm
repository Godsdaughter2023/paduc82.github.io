I have got the same problem at the beginning and took me a while to realise that was actually a fake message. I have got "There are no handwriting option for this language" message at the Chinese language option.

Steps to use the handwriting:

1. Open the application when you want to use handwriting, e.g. a search engine or notepad

2. Switch to the language that you want to do handwriting, in my case Chinese

3. At the task bar, click at the keyboard symbol (if you don't see it, right click at the task bar and select "Show touch keyboard button)

4. At the touch keyboard, you should be able to see a keyboard layout on the screen, at the right bottom corner, click at the "change input method" icon. 

5. Select the symbol with a board and a pen. 

6. Now, the keyboard on the screen will change to handwriting pad on the screen. 

7. Start writing with the mouse or touch pad on your computer with pressing down the left button.

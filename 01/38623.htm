manjit kaur say:

Hi spud.9,

 

Thank you for using Microsoft Windows Vista Forums. 

 

You cannot add Titles and Credits in Windows DVD Maker, however you can use Windows Movie maker to do that.

 

If you know where you want the title or credit to appear in your movie, click that location on the storyboard/timeline.

 

1.      Click Tools, and then click Titles and Credits.

2.      Click the link that corresponds to where you want to add the title or credit.

3.      In the Enter text for title box, type the text that you want to appear as the title or credit.

4.      After you type the text, the monitor displays the default animation and format for the title or credit you want to add.

5.      To change the title animation, click Change the title animation, and select a title animation from the list.

6.      To change the font and color for your title, click Change the text font and color, and then choose the font, font color, formatting, background color, transparency, font size, and position of the title to your liking.

7.      Click Add Title.

 

Once you have done with that, you need to click on publish movie to burn CD/DVD.

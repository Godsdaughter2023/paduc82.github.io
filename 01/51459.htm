I don't bother with Microsoft's apps, but when you're in Photos, open Settings > Sources and see what folders are listed there. Those are the folders where the app is looking to find your pictures. The artwork for your music should be stored in your Music folder, and your Music folder should not be a source folder for the Photos app - unless that's the way you want it.

Individual applications will organize your pictures in their own way, which you can usually control at least somewhat. The thing to remember is that this is only the application's own internal organization. The images in your C:\Users\<your username>\Pictures folder remain the way you arranged them and nobody but you is going to change that.

To put a finer point on it: The images in my Pictures folder are presented completely different depending on which application I'm using.

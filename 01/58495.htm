"If you already have Vista installed, and you have a profile setup that you want every user to have, log on to the Vista PC with an account that has local admin access. It can't be the same user account whose profile you want everyone to share.
- One logged in, click Start, right click Computer, choose properties, click Advanced system settings on the left hand task pane, and on the Advanced tab, click the Settings button in the User Profiles section.
- Click the profile you want everyone to use, then click the Copy To button. Click the Browse button under the "Copy profile to" field and navigate to c:\users\default, click OK.
- Then click the Change button under "Permitted to use", and pick the group (domain or local) that you want to have access to the Default profile (usually Everyone or Authenticated Users).

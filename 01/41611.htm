Hi,   

Welcome to Microsoft Windows 7 Answers Forum! 

Conversion occurs only if your device requires files to be converted to a quality level and/or format that is optimized for playback on the device. Conversion does not affect the original file.

Hence conversion is device specific because the files will be converted to the format in which the device is designed to play.

In case if you wish to disable the conversion then you may follow the below provided steps.

1.      Click the arrow below the Sync tab, point to the device, and then click Advanced Options.

2.      The properties dialog box for your device is displayed. Click the Quality tab, and then clear the Convert music, pictures, videos, and TV shows as required check box.

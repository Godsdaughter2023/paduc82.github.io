a. user say:

There is no correct driver for Windows 10.

Only HP can write drivers for HP hardware, and the last Windows driver for your printer was written for Windows XP. After that, HP stopped supporting that printer on Windows, so HP hasn't tested it on Windows 10 and hasn't updated the driver to keep up with the huge changes in Windows since XP.

You can try one (or more) of the old HP drivers included in Windows 10, and hopefully it will work, but there's no guarantee. Without support from HP, you're left to fend for yourself.

There's nothing that Microsoft can do to help, since Microsoft can't write drivers for another company's hardware. At best, they can write a generic driver that may or may not work properly, or at all.

HP explains this on their website, here.

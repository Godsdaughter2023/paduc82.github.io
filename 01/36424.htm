spellweaver16 say:

Crunchy:

Xbox content licenses are bound to two things. The gamertag that purchases it, and the console it's purchased on. Transferring licenses just swaps the licenses over to the console you perform the transfer on. As long as one of the two is present - you are either on the console who currently has the licenses, or signed into the gamertag that purchased the content - you will have no problems playing it. If you do run into issues, call customer support to set up a manual content license transfer.

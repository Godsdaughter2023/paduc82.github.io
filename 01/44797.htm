I would recommend that you disable all of the third party items in that list to begin. Look in the Product Name and Company columns to identify these third party extensions. Select one and then hold down the Ctrl Key and select the others.

Log off/log on and test the results. If the problem is gone, then you know the culprit is in that group that you disabled.

 

Enable half of that group and test again. If the problem reappears, then you have narrowed it down to the group that you enabled.

Next, disabled half of the second group and test.

You may find that in many cases you do not even use those third party shell extensions and you can simply leave them disabled.

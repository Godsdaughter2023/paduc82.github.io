camicaziboss say:

palmettobling say:

With Home Console sharing, you don't even need a physical copy.  

Consider it like this.  You own two licenses of the game.  One that is always valid on your home console, no matter who is logged in, and a second copy that is valid with your profile, no matter where you are signed in.  

So you have console A and console B, and account A and account B.  

If Console A is set to Home, then account B (doesn't own the game) can play on Console A, meanwhile you can still play the game on Console B at the same time someone is playing on Console A.

No need to own a second copy of the game for that configuration.

Let me know if that doesn't cover what you're looking to accomplish!

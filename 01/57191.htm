rob koch say:

Unfortunately most consumers have no idea how the Windows installation process actually works, just like yourself, so there's a lot of confusion over what this message means.

In truth, the uninstall process is simply a reversed install, so what's being asked is whether you want to allow the uninstaller to do it's job and nothing more.  Obviously you need to allow this or the uninstall will simply stop.

The confusion here often comes about since some less reputable programs used to ask all sorts of questions about why you wanted to remove them or even create wrap-around situations where they tricked you into reinstalling or updating them instead.

The problem is that unless you're used to performing uninstalls, you've likely never seen this message, so it seems not to make sense to those who don't understand how a Windows uninstall works, which as you can see even some helpers here clearly don't.

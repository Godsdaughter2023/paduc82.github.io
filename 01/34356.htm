cmdrkeene say:

That message means the person who sent the email is trying to put "active content" (programming code) into the message.  They are adding event listeners so they can detect when the email has been opened so that they can track you and measure how many people read the message.

Unfortunately these practices that companies use are modern functions and don't necessarily work with the decades-and-decades old email programs like Vista Mail.   The company that sent the email is the only ones that can fix the error directly (you can't modify the code of emails you are receiving).

While you can't stop the errors, you could try suggestions like these to stop seeing the messages about the errors.

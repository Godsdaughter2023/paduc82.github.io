a. user say:

There are several ways you can do this. Here's one:

You need to know your printer's MAC address. That's a combination of numbers and letters in the format XX : XX : XX : XX : XX : XX : XX : XX : XX : XX

Then, open your router's administration console and go to the network map. Look for your printer's MAC address and you'll see its IP address.

Unless you assigned your printer a static IP address - in which case you wouldn't be posting - the IP address of your printer will change from time to time.

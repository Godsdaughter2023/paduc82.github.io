SYSTEM: Full

Administrators: Full

Users: Read, Execute and List contents

TrustedInstaller: Full

No. The laptops presumable came with Windows preinstalled, and if so,
they are OEM copies of Windows.  The biggest disadvantage of an OEM
version is that its license ties it permanently to the first computer
it's installed on. It can never legally be moved to another computer,
sold, or given away (except with the original computer). If the
computer dies or is stolen, your license dies with it.

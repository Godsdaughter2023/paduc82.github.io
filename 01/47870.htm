There's no tracking like you may think:  every individual program is responsible for "remembering" where their windows were last shown, and deciding if they will use that remembered position or just ignore it and show up somewhere else.  Some programs just continuously open and add some extra pixels to the coordinates (like if you just continuously open more Calculators, they will just keep opening 10-pixels over from the last one).  IE might be different.  In fact, every single program on your entire computer might be different, because every programmer that builds it gets to decide for themselves.

  2. Click on Devices.

  3. Click on Typing on the left pane.

  4. Now, scroll down and click on Advanced keyboard settings.

  5. Click on Input language hot keys.

  6. Now, in the Advanced key settings tab, click on Change Key Sequence and you can assign the keys as per your preference.

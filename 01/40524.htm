    Hi,

    Laptops these days tend to try and save on power requirements by including two graphics cards in laptops. The Intel HD graphics might sound impressive, but it's the low end display unit and is just a chip on the motherboard. It's good enough to run things like flash video on Youtube and minor animations in web pages, but anything requiring high end graphics will use the main graphics card which will be dedicated with its own memory.

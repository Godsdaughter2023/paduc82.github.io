bruce hagen ~ mvp say:
If they all come from a domain you don't get any other mail from, you could use a message rule for that. Message Rules is located under the Folders tab on top.


To block a domain, create a Message Rule:

Where the from line contains people
Delete it and Stop processing more rules (or Delete it from the server)
Click on Contains People and Add the domain. Ex: yahoo.com. 


You could also use an address book only rule.

Start with the Address Book only rule and as you get messages you want from people not in your Address Book, (E-mail from businesses, bills, your ISP, etc.), you can add them to the rule.

Tools | Message Rules | Mail | New.

Box 1 - Where the From line contains people {check it}

Box 2 - Check: Delete it & Stop processing more rules if you want messages not in the Address Book to go to Deleted Items or the Junk folder.

Box 3 - Click on the blue words Contains People

Click on the first address to highlight it. Scroll down to the end and hold down the Shift key while you click on the last address. This will highlight them all. Right click on them and click on From in the menu that appears.

Now click Options and Choose: Message *does not contain* the people below in the top Options box, and choose: Message matches any one of the people below in the bottom Options box.

Edit 05/08/2015 :

There is some solution for this problem (at least it works for me) :

1) Change your PC name and wait (one day maybe ...)

2) If nothing changed (after 2 days ...), there is some problem with account syncing

- Turn off sync settings in local PC. Tuto here : http://www.tenforums.com/tutorials/4077-sync-settings-turn-off-windows-10-a.html

- Wait a bit and turn it on

- Your PC will be sync again with online microsoft account => wait more (one day maybe ...) and you will see de new device name online.

3) The last solution (not the best for me) :

- Delete device from online microsoft account

- From local PC, disconnect microsoft account and change to local account

- Reconnect to microsoft account from PC

- Wait ... wait and wait => you will see it again online with new PC name

Please leave your comment which solution works for you !

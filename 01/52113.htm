A Skype name is the username you created when you first joined Skype, other than your email address or phone number. If you sign in with an email address instead, then you have a Live account, not a Skype Name.

  • Through the Skype website:
  1. Sign in to the Skype website.
  2. Scroll down and click Purchase history.
  3. The Skype Name of your account will be shown at the upper right side of the Purchase history page, before the Sign out button.
  • Through Skype for Web:
  1. Sign in to Skype for Web.
  2. Click your profile icon.
  3. Your Skype Name is displayed under your display name and profile picture.

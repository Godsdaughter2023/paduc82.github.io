Hi npgunner,

  

  1. What is the issue you are facing related to DHCP client\server?

Renew the client DHCP lease

The IP address for this computer is the same IP address used by another computer on the network. No user action is required. The DHCP client will attempt to get a new IP address from the DHCP server.

To perform these procedures, you must be a member of theAdministrators group, or you must have been delegated the appropriate authority.

If you do not want to wait for the client to make its automatic renewal attempt, you can manually release and renew the IP address of the client by following these steps:

1.    At the DHCP-enabled client computer, clickStart, in Start Search typecmd, and then press ENTER.

2.    Typeipconfig /release, and then press ENTER.

3.    Typeipconfig /renew, and then press ENTER.

Verify

To verify that address conflicts have been resolved:

1.    At the DHCP-enabled client computer, clickStart, in Start Search typecmd, and then press ENTER.

2.    To verify a DHCP client lease, typeipconfig /all to view lease-status information.

3.    The output of theipconfig command should show an IPv4 or IPv6 address marked "preferred"

danida_u say:

Hi Adrian,

Q: When I set up a Monthly plan does it include a phone number?  or do I have to pay for the Monthly plan plus a separate amount for the phone number? Do I need a phone number (for myself) to call a land line?

A: Calling subscriptions do not come bundled with a Skype Number. They are purchased separately and function differently.

Calling subscriptions are calling plans that allows you to make unlimited or fixed-minute calls to landlines and/or mobiles (if applicable) while a Skype Number is a phone number that allows you to be called by other people using their mobile or landline and you pick up the call on Skype.

You do not need a Skype Number if you will be making an outgoing call. You can initiate paid calls even without the presence of a Skype Number, as long as you have an active calling subscription on your account that covers the country you want to call.

For additional information regarding the different paid-features that Skype offers, refer to this FAQ: What are the different types of Skype subscriptions and pay-as-you-go options?

It kind of depends on your reasons for wanting a video DVD and whether this is for personal viewing or shared viewing.

rcoover say:

How do I change the settings in Mail in Windows 8?

I wish to place the oldest email messages at the top and can find no way to do this. How are the Mail settings changed?

dena b say:

Hi RCoover,

There is not an option to change the order the email is displayed. You can use the Feedback option in the app to submit your suggestion.

  1. Open the Mail app
  2. Press the Windows key + C
  3. Select Settings
  4. Select Feedback
  5. Select a feedback option
  6. Select Next
  7. Enter you suggestion
  8. Select Submit

bill smithers say:

Hi,

If your XP came preinstalled on the older computer (Dell, HP, Acer, etc), or you had an OEM System Builder's License, it is an OEM License.

An OEM License is non-transferable and is tied to the first computer that it is installed on and the License dies when the Computer dies.

No reinstall on another Computer or Activation is allowed.

 

If you had an XP Retail License which you bought and installed yourself on the older Computer, it is transferable.

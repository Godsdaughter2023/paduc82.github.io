Troubleshoot for app compatibility

  1. From Start, swipe in from the right edge of the screen and then tap Search (or if you're using a mouse, point to the upper-right corner of the screen, move the mouse pointer down, and then click Search).

If you know the compatibility mode that your app needs to run, here is how to apply it:

  1. From Start, enter the name of the app you want to run in compatibility mode, and in the search results list, swipe down or right-click to select the app, and choose Open file location.

Windows Update does its own sequencing quite automatically.  There is absolutely no need to do 50 at a time.  I've doing this on hundreds of PCs for years and have always simply allowed Windows Update to do its thing.

Mind you, I am quite selective about what updates I allow.  Usually only about 180 of those initial 235 actually get installed.

You may be interested to know that ALL Windows 7 development ended December 31, 2014.  Microsoft committed to provide SECURITY update for Windows 7 owners until January 2020.  However, they have chosen to mix in with those updates, "optional" updates.  You can bet that those optional updates are actually changes to your Windows 7 system that gradually get it ready for Windows 10 conversion (whether your system will run it or not) and to gradually change your Windows 7 system to one more like Windows 10.

Consequently, when I get that list of 235.  I carefully go through the ones NOT labeled security one at a time.  If the issue date of the update is after December 31, 2014, I right-click it and hide it (in other words refuse that update).

Also, as of October 2016, Microsoft became frustrated by the effort that I described above and decided to make that much more difficult.  They accomplished that by bundling all the updates for the month that they want you to install into ONE update, labeled deceptively as a security update.  You can read a lot more about this in the discussion at:  https://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/forum/windows_7-update/windows-update-how-will-it-change-your-system/6d2ee7f8-908f-4ddb-8fd9-1eab55773141?tm=1476461665170

Printers normally can normally power on independently of the computer. If its not powering on independently, then this is more likely a hardware issue, not a Windows 10 issue.

andre da costa say:
A UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) is not designed to provide the appropriate voltage to power a printer. Keeping one connected to a UPS can damage the motor inside the printer.

I have tried removing the user, but as I anticipated it blurted a few Warning messages and the user could not be removed. Is this because they sit at the top of a hierarchy?

Thanks people,
vatsan [msft] say:

Creator Owner is a placeholder that corresponds to whatever user is the owner of the object (file, folder) in question. Typically, whoever creates a file is also the owner of the file. If you created the file, 'Creator Owner' will be replaced with your user account's SID when doing authenticaiton.

This article explains Creator Owner - http://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/library/cc780850(WS.10).aspx

Close the settings app

Log out of that user account

That's It, your new Admin Account will replace the old account

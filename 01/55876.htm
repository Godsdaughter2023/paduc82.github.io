Windows Fax and Scan - media is write protected - Microsoft Community

truerock2 say:

Windows Fax and Scan - media is write protected

I had moved my D:\ drive to E:\

This caused Windows Fax and Scan to display this message:

"media is write protected"

when I tried to send a fax.

To fix the problem, I had to go into Tools, Fax Settings, Advanced, "Move Folder" and change the location of Inbox/Sent

I ended Windows Fax and Scan and re-booted my PC

Then I went to Tools, Fax Accounts and "Removed" my account and then did an "Add..." of a new account

I ended Windows Fax and Scan and re-booted my PC

The above fixed my issue with "media is write protected".  I have read in other posts that it was necessary to "turn the 'Windows Fax and Scan' 'Windows feature' off".  Then re-boot and and then turn it back on and re-set it up.  "Windows Fax and Scan" is not an application you can uninstall.  It is a "Windows feature" that you "turn off" in order to uninstall it.

Also, I've read where some individuals skip the re-booting of their PC between changes by stopping and re-starting the fax service.  Although that is faster than re-booting the PC - I find stopping and restarting Windows services to be more complicated.

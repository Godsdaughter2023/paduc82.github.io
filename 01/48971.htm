Metered connection

I recently got a limited number of GB's for my cell phone and my internet each month because it was less expensive for me (like a bundle) and I just bought a printer and was trying to get it installed to the computer and I get a message that say setup incomplete due to a metered connection.  What can I do about this I need my printer. Do I have to change my cell phone service?  Thanks

cmdrkeene say:

Depends how your Windows device connects to the internet.  Does your Windows computer connect using your phone's connection?  If so, data usage on your computer will cost you against your limited number of GB's for data on your cell phone.  That's not a "problem", you just have to be aware of what your limit is and make sure you don't use too much.

 

If your computer connects through an "unlimited" home internet connection that has unlimited data use, you can tell Windows that it's not really a metered connection and it will not try to save data any more.  To do that, just touch-and-hold (or right-click) on your wifi connection name (just like you were going there to connect for the first time) and you can unset it as metered.

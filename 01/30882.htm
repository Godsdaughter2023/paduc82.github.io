lethalplatypus say:

Based on how the TV is configured, and any receivers/etc. that are between the TV and the Xbox this can potentially happen if the XBox thinks that the HDMI needs to re-establish the handshake.

Basically, when you first connect up a device on HDMI, it sends encryption keys back and forth.  If your TV doesn't keep ahold of the connection to the 360, the 360 may think it needs to resend those codes.  If the 360 only sends those codes on startup, it may force it to reboot.

Some receivers will "hold open" the connection to HDMI so that issues like this (and other problems like sound fading out, etc.) don't happen.

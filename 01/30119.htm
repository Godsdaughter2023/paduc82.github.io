What model computer? That is important. Today's computers come with integrated video as well as added separate video cards. If you have a separate card, the integrated video is disabled and cannot be used. The integrated video is usually vga. If you want to use dual monitors, see if there is an available port on the separate card and use that--dvi or hdmi. The extra monitor has to have a matching port. Vga is old analog and not very sharp display. Use DVI and HDMI for digital.

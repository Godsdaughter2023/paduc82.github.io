tryx3 say:

Is the Lid close action one of those you are being denied access to? 

This can happen if there is a PC maker's power management utility running and it has been reported in some Samsung netbooks & others.

If so, find the "utility" Samsung Fast start in the Samsung program folders [in the Start menu, All programs folders].  Start it & select Disable.  If that option doesn't exist then just uninstall it at Control panel, Programs & features.  You will gain full access to your power options & will lose nothing as this utility is not doing a single thing that you cannot do for yourself.

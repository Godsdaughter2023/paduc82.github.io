considerate_guy say:

The Video Tools | Edit tab is used both for video and pictures.  When you have video, you have more options like the video volume, the start/end points, speed, etc.  If you are editing a picture, though, there is only one option (Duration).  So I suspect you selected a picture and that's what it is trying to edit.

Additionally, if you are trying to change the duration of an animation, you should do that from the Animation tab, not the Video Tools | Edit tab.  The duration option is to the right of the animation choices.

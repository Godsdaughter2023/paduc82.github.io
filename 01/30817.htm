To start troubleshooting your issue, we recommend the following:

  1. Start your device in Safe Mode.
  2. Set your mouse settings and restart in Safe Mode.
  3. Check if the issue still exist.
  4. If this resolves your issue, there is third party program interfering with your mouse's settings.
  5. If not, uninstall and then reinstall your mouse drivers. Follow the steps below:
    1. Open Control Panel > Hardware and Sound > Device Manager.
    2. Expand the node that represent the type of device that you want to uninstall, right-click the device entry, and click Uninstall.
    3. On the Confirm Device Removal dialog box, click OK to start the uninstall process.
    4. Remove your mouse and then reconnect. That should reinstall the driver of your mouse.

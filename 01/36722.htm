how do you unhide a folder on the c drive - Microsoft Community

carolterentiak say:

how do you unhide a folder on the c drive

A virus recently hid my user folder which is immediately under the c drive.  How do I unhide it?  I have removed the virus already.

wunders say: First, configure your system to show hidden folders
   Start -> settings -> Control Panel -> Folder Options
then in the "View" tab,
   Check the "Show Hidden Files & Folders" check-box
stubborn problems may also require that you uncheck the:
   "Hide Protected Operating System Files" check-box.
OK you way out.

Then navigate to your hidden folder, right-click on it, then select "Properties"
Then uncheck the "Hidden" box.
OK your way out.

If it works, you can change your Folder Settings back to the defaults.

HTH,
  JW

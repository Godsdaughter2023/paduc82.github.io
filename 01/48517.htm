4. Tap the N key (New task)
5. Type the word Explorer and press Enter

By the way, your assumption about the logged-on status of the problem machine is incorrect. Other PCs can access files and folders on a networked machine independently of any logged-on session. They get authenticated by means of their account name/password, for which they might get prompted.

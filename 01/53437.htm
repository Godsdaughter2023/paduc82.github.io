Can I go back 5 months on systems restore - Microsoft Community

gussie taylor say:

Can I go back 5 months on systems restore

How far is it permissible to go back in time on the systems restore.?

 

<IE8>

jetta48 say:

You will have a cetain amount (%) of hard disk space allocated to system restore points. When that space is used up, the oldest restore point gets deleted. To see how far back you can go now just look at the available restore points as it will vary according to your allocated % space and hard drive size. 

 

You can allocate more space and hence go back further in the future. Of course you cannot use this to create restore points in the past.

 

If you turn off System Protection then turn it back on again at any point all of the previous restore points will get deleted.

 

For more details please see:

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows-vista/System-Restore-frequently-asked-questions

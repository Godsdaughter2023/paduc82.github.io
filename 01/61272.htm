This is how I did this:

1. Open device manager.

2. Right click on device that problematic. In my case, both flash disk and external HDD stated as unknown device.

3. Choose Update driver

4. Choose Browse my computer for driver software.

5. Choose Let me pick from a list of device drivers on my computer

6. In my case, I choose USB mass storage. (But I bet, if you choose anything at all, windows will do auto detect the device so don't worry with what you choose.)

7. In the next screen, choose what ever that windows gives you. In my case USB mass storage.

8. Windows will come out with generic... Choose that one.

9. Go back to device manager. The problem has not yet solved yet. But now, the problematic device no longer unknown device. In my case, it changed from unknown device to disk drives.

10. Repeat step 2-8.

11. In step 6, I choose disk drives.

12. Problem not solved yet.

13. Repeat step 2-8 once again.

14. In step 6, I choose portable device.

15. And,... YES! Problem solved! My flash disk and external HDD can be detected and completely works.

I hope my post can give alternative solution to anyone who face the same problem since I saw many post about the same subject that has not yet been solved.

Good luck.

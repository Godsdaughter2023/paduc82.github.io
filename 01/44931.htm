Method 1:

  1. Restart your computer.
  2. From the Taskbar printer icon, click open the Printer > Printer Menu > Cancel all documents.

Method 2:

  1. Type services.msc in Windows search and hit Enter to open Services Manager.

All modern computers will slow, or even sometimes stop, the CPU (processor chip) inside the computer if the computer detects that the CPU is overheating.  It can overhead if the cooling fan is not working, not connected properly or installed upside down, or if there is dirt and dust inside the cooling fins, or if the heat sink is not properly attached to the processor.  If you are not skilled at hardware repair and troubleshooting I would suggest enlisting the help of someone who is, since the eventual most-likely result of this problem will be a meltdown inside your computer requiring very expensive repairs.

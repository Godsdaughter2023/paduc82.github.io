a. user say:

There are two entirely separate issues here.

Firstly, the Recovery Partition has NOTHING whatsoever to do with backing up data. You do NOT backup data to the recovery partition but to some other media - external HDD, DVD, flash drive etc.

Secondly if your Recovery partition really IS deleted (and someone would have had to do that physically, it just doesn't "happen") then the only thing you can do is to contact your computer Vendor and see if they'll provide you with a Recovery DVD.

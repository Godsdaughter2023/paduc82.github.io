tech-knight say: Hi,

It could be because the service required to perform the backup(Volume shadow copy) is stopped due to some reasons.
Check if this service is stopped. If so, start the service and backup should work fine.
Steps to strat the service:
*Click on start button.
*In the search box above that type in services.msc and hit enter.
*The above step would open up service(local) windows.
*In the services windows l;ocate the service by name VOLUME SHADOW COPY and check the status under startup type.
*If it is stopped, right click on it and click on start.

Hope this helps.

Do post back if need further assistance.

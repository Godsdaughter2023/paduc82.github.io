laxnigel say:

Hey There!


Gamerscore will update automatically, there's nothing you need to do.  Sometimes it might take a little bit, but you will get the credit. 


As for the achievements posting, playing Xbox 360 games on your Xbox One will not cause achievements to post to your Xbox One feed..  

For example, in a Xbox One game, you get a little achievement note in your player feed showing your unlocked achievement.  You will not get that achievement feed item in your Xbox One feed for getting an Xbox 360 achievement.  Your gamerscore will increase either way, because your gamerscore is not attached to 360 or One, it is attached to your Xbox Live profile.  As long as your console(s) is/are connected to the internet, your global gamerscore will increase with any achievement- One or 360.

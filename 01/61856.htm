darleen pen say:

Hi Suzzanne,

Windows 10 lets you modify and personalize the Start Screen. Follow these steps to successfully add an application to Start:

  1. Click on Windows key to trigger the Start Screen.
  2. On the app list, look for the application you want to add on Start.
  3. Right click on the application and then select Pin to Start.

Follow these steps to successfully add a folder to Start:

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Click on Personalization and then click on Start.
  3. Click on Choose which folders appear on Start.
  4. Toggle on the button beside the folders you want to add to Start.

Let us know if further assistance is needed.

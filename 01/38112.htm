ricw-nw say:

Hi - simplest explanation - wav. files were developed with MS involvement in the early days and were only intended for short sound files (alerts, start-up etc.) because of their (then) comparatively large size. Metadata handling was not designed in. There are programs that can add limited tagging to wav. files but as far as I know artwork cannot be added. I add a 'Folder' image to wav album folders and of course artwork will appear in WMP if pasted there. To paste art in WMP select the desired image (jpg format is best) click copy and then you can paste it to the art square (as below)

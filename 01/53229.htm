You can manually force a cleaning cycle as follows:  

  1. Turn on the printer and wait for it to become ready.
  2. Press and hold the power button
  3. While holding the power button press and release the X button twice.
  4. Release the power button.

Be advised that cleaning cycles are generally not needed even once during the life of a typical cartridge (at least for genuine HP cartridges) and excessive cleaning can shorten the life of the printer.

Change Lock screen Password on laptop

kapil arya [directly] say: Hello AndrewQF,

I'm Independent Advisor and Microsoft MVP, here to help you with your question.

For that, you need to change your password, try these steps:

1. Right click Start Button or press Windows + X keys and select Settings.

2. In the Settings app, navigate to Accounts > Sign-in options.

3. On the right pane of the window, under Password, click Change.

4. Then type in your current account password and click Next.

5. Now type your new password, confirm it and add a password hint to remember it. Click Next.

Your password is changed now.

Hope this helps!

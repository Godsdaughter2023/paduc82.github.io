How do i cancel/turn off the blue highlight "tool/feature" that keeps overwriting my text??? I think its something to do with CUT/COPY/PASTE "tool"??

See above in "Subject" box
cmdrkeene say:

There's no feature like this.  Usually when people say what you're saying, it's because their hand/arm/wrist is getting too close to the laptop's touchpad while they are typing, so it cause the mouse pointer to "drag" and highlight (blue highlight) the text, and then the next letter you touch overwrites the highlighted part.

One solution:   go to touchpad options in your computer Settings and turn down the sensitivity

Another solution:  tape a business card or some paper over the touchpad so it isn't so sensitive.

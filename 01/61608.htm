Here are the steps to switch from a Microsoft account to a local account:

  1. Swipe in from the right edge of the screen, tap Settings, and then tap Change PC settings.
    (If you're using a mouse, point to the lower-right corner of the screen, move the mouse pointer up, click Settings, and then click Change PC settings.)
  2. Tap or click Accounts, and then tap or click Your account.
  3. Tap or click Disconnect, and then follow the instructions.

Once you switch to a local account, then switch again to Microsoft Account using the correct URL.

Follow these steps to switch to Microsoft Account:

  1. Swipe in from the right edge of the screen, tap Settings, and then tap Change PC settings.
    (If you're using a mouse, point to the lower-right corner of the screen, move the mouse pointer up, click Settings, and then click Change PC settings.)
  2. Tap or click Accounts, and then tap or click Your account.
  3. Tap or click on Connect to a Microsoft Account.

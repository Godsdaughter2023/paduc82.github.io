The icon cache may have been corrupted:

The registry entries we need to remove are anything mentioning 'EldosIconOverlay' found under
 
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\ShellIconOverlayIdentifiers\

and also under
 
HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Wow6432Node\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\ShellIconOverlayIdentifiers\
 
for 64 bit Windows versions.
 
64 bit Windows has the entries in both locations.
 
Basically anything with 'EldosIconOverlay' in the name under those locations.

Reinstalling the application after removing these entries will put the correct entries in place and make the magic happen. You may need to reboot after doing this to get it to kick in.

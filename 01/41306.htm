Connecting legacy Plantronics headsets to a Windows 10 machine - Microsoft Community

jib halyard say:

Connecting legacy Plantronics headsets to a Windows 10 machine

Headset Tip.

This tip is for people with the following Starting Situation:

  • You have an older ("legacy") Plantronics headset, such as an "H-series" Plantronics SupraPlus HW251. 
  • You have a two-piece rig:  (1) the headset, which terminates in a Plantronics-style "quick disconnect" connection, and (2) an adapter cable with a quick-disconnect attachment at one end and (probably) a 2.5 mm jack at the other end.
  • Historically, you connected your headset rig directly into a desk phone.

Goal:

You desire to use this headset with your Win 10 machine (in my case it was a Surface 3 machine running Win 10) in order to make Skype calls.

Tempting, but incorrect resolution:

  • Plantronics makes a wide variety of adapters, one of which is a Quick Disconnect to 3.5 mm jack adapter.  It is tempting to purchase this (relatively) inexpensive adapter, swap in the new 3.5 mm jack adapter in lieu of the existing 2.5mm jack adapter, and just plug in your "upgraded" headset rig into the 3.5 mm jack on your machine. 
  • This approach will fail.

Correct resolution:

  • First, you must be willing and able to allocate 1 USB port on your machine and about $70 to solve this headset connection problem.
  • Second, determine through web research exactly which Plantronics headset model you have.
  • Third, call Plantronics customer support, which at this writing is 1-855-765-7878.  Be surprised that a real person answers the phone and is helpful.  Ask the Plantronics customer support person which "DA" adapter unit is compatible with the specific model of Plantronics headset that you have.  The DA adapter has a Quick Disconnect at one end, a USB at the other end, and some electronics in between.  Note that Plantronics developed more than 1 DA adapter unit, and the legacy headset to DA unit compatibility information is not on their website.  Plantronics customer service can figure it out, though.
  • Forth, order the DA unit recommended by the Plantronics rep.  You will order the DA adapter from a third party reseller.
  • Fifth, receive the part, connect it up, and test it out.  Win 10 recognizes the DA adapter, your headset works as it should, and problem solved.

If Windows Live Messenger is popping on Windows startup you can disable it as follows.

 

a.       Login to Windows Live Messenger.

b.      Press ALT on the keyboard. Click “Tools” and then “Options” from the menu bar.

c.       On the “Options” window click “Sign In”.

d.      On the right pane under “General” uncheck the option which says “Automatically run Windows Live Messenger when I log on to Windows”

e.       Click Apply and OK.

 

For Internet Explorer:

 

If you are referring to tabs not opening in Internet Explorer when trying to open links, then it most likely is due to any incompatible add-on.

 

Method 1:

Step 1:  Run Internet Explorer in No Add-Ons mode to check for the issue.

 

a.       Click Start, and then type Internet Explorer in the Start Search box.

b.      Click Internet Explorer (No Add-Ons). Internet Explorer opens without add-ons, toolbars, or plug-ins.

c.       Test to verify that it works correctly.

 

Step 2: If it works fine then use the Manage Add-ons tool to determine which add-on is causing the issue

 

a.       Open Internet Explorer.

b.      Click Tools, and then click Manage Add-ons.

c.       On the Show drop-down menu, select All add-ons to display all add-ons that are installed on the computer.

d.      For each item in this list, select the add-on, and then click Disable in the Information window.

e.       When you have disabled all the items in this list, click OK.

f.        Exit and then restart Internet Explorer.

g.      If issues do not reoccur, repeat steps 1 through 3.

h.      Click Enable for a single add-on.

 

Refer:Internet Explorer add-ons: frequently asked questions

 

Method 2:

If none of these methods work, you can try optimizing Internet Explorer and check for the issue.

Hi weiijii,

Shut your system Down

6. Save BIOS changes, restart your System and it should boot from the Installation Media


PLEASE NOTE once you have ran those commands in Command Prompt. When you restart your System, you need to change the boot order back so your system boots from your hard drive . . .

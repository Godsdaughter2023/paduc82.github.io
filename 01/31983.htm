Hi Ahamed,

When you copy music, pictures, and videos from your PC to a blank CD or DVD, it's called "burning." When you copy music, pictures, and videos from a CD or DVD to your PC, it's called "ripping." You can use Windows Media Player to do both. May we know what type of file are you copying?

To copy a CD with only one DVD drive, kindly follow these steps:

  2. Create a location folder and copy the contents of the CD to the folder.
  3. Remove the CD from the DVD drive.
  4. Insert the blank CD, and then burn the contents of the folder into the new CD.

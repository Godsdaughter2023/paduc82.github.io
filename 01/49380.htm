whats_the_answer say:

combine 2 photos on one jpeg file

I simply want to print two photos on one photo.

I use Windows 7.

ludwig j say:

If you want to print from Photo Gallery, then you can print two photos on one sheet. Click the File tab, move the pointer to Print, click Print in the right menu. Note the options on the right side in the dialog. There are two for printing two photos per sheet.

 

If you need to prepare a file with two photos - so you can send it away for printing - you can do it in Paint. It is not too troublesome. Open one photo in Paint. Reduce the view. Drag either the right border (for vertical photos) or the bottom border (for horizontal photos) so the area is bigger than twice the size of the photo. Note that this resizes the "canvas" not the photo, the photo will remain the same. Then use "Paste from" and load the second photo. Position it next to the first photo. You can leave some white space between them. Then bring the outside of the "canvas" (drag border) to just touch the second photo. It is really a faster operation than reading these instructions, you will get the hang of it.

 

Now for the most important detail: Use SAVE AS and give the combo a new name. Do not use SAVE as that will overwrite the first photo!

 

Good luck! If you need additional help concact me at askLudwig.com

tim de baets say:

Are you burning the CD as an audio CD, or as a data CD containing MP3 files? You can check by clicking the Burn options 'checkmark' button in the top right corner.

Album art *might* be transferred to a data CD, but certainly not to an audio CD. Audio CDs simply don't support album art, so it's not possible.

Burning album information to an audio CD is a feature called  CD-Text . WMP doesn't have built-in support for CD-Text but you can install the WMPCDText plug-in to allow WMP to read it.

To create a CD with CD-Text, you will need to use a different burning program, such as Nero Burning Rom or Burrrn .

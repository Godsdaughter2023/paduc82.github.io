schief say:

Hello @cambojnr.  I'm sorry you are having trouble connecting Xbox One SmartGlass to your console.  We have recently updated the FAQ at the top of this forum to include some additional trouble shooting suggestions for connection issues.  Please give these a try and let us know if you can get connected:

  • Make sure Xbox SmartGlass Apps is “On” in Settings/Preferences/SmartGlass Connections. 
  • Your SmartGlass device needs to be on the same network as your Xbox One Console:
    1. Make sure you’re not on your cellular/3G/4G network or on a separate wifi/switch.  Temporarily disable cellular data if you device has it to ensure you are connecting to your wifi network.
    2. Make sure you are not connected to your router’s Guest Network
    3. Make sure you disable Access Point/WiFi Isolation or related feature on your router
    4. If your Xbox One Console is connected via wired cable, temporarily disconnect the cable and connect the console to the same wifi network that your SmartGlass device is connected to
  • Turn off any VPN connection as this may prevent discovery/connection from functioning
  • Make sure you are running the Xbox One SmartGlass app and not the Xbox 360 SmartGlass app.
  • If none of these steps work, turn off your Xbox One console and unplug it for 30 seconds and restart the console.  
  • If you are still unable to connect, please post your situation including your network configuration (inc. router model), and SmartGlass device in this forum here.

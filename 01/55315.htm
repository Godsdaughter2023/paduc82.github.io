auggy say:

You can try disabling NetBIOS over TCP/IP:

Open the Network and Sharing Center on the taskbar > Change adapter settings > right-click the Local Area Connection > Properties > click on "Internet Protocol Version 4 (TCP/IPv4) > Properties > Advanced > WINS > then select "Disable NetBIOS over TCP/IP" > OK

If disabling NetBIOS over TCP/IP does not solve the BSODs or creates any other issue re-enable NetBIOS over TCP/IP.

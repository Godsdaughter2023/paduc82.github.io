  1. Tap twice
  2. Hold down on the second tap
  3. Move around and you're dragging
  4. Release finger to stop dragging

  2. Hold down on the second tap
  3. Move around and you're dragging
  4. Release finger
  5. Reposition finger and continue dragging by flicking the touchpad some more

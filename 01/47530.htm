    Richard,

    You need to "relocate" the Documents "user folder" at C:\Users\Whatever\Documents to your D:\Documents folder.

    Right-click on the folder C:\Users\Whatever\Documents, select Properties, select the Location tab.

    1  If there is no Location tab you can just move all your files & subfolders out of C:\Users\Whatever\Documents then delete the folder.

    2  In the Location tab, enter the path to your D:\Documents folder then click Apply / OK  -Windows will ask if you want to move all its contents and you can say Yes.  Personally, I always move all the  files & subfolders out of C:\Users\Whatever\Documents myself first just to simplify the operation but I still say Yes so that Windows will take care of deleting the original folder for me.

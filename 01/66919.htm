To uninstall any Windows updates do the following:

i. Click start->type in Programs and features and hit enter.
ii. In the Programs and Features window, click on the View installed updates link.
iii. After a few seconds, Windows Vista will load the list of installed updates. Scroll down until you find the update you want to remove, select it with the mouse and then click on Uninstall.
iv. Windows Vista will ask you to confirm that you want to uninstall the update you selected. Click on Yes and the uninstall procedure will start.
v. Depending on the size of the update, the uninstall procedure can take from a few seconds up to several minutes.
vi. Wait until the above window disappears and then, if needed, reboot your PC. The update is now removed.

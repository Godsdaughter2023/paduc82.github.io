For the best results a pagefile on a secondary drive needs to be in the first partition on the drive and I would dedicate the partition solely to the pagefile. With regard to setting a pagefile are you failing to click on Apply after clicking on Set? Also you need to restart your computer for the changes to take effect.

Open the Control Panel, then type language into the Search window in the upper right. In the results on the left, under the green Region and Language, select Change keyboards or other input methods.

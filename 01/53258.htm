As for Gimp, follow these steps:

1. With Gimp up and running, go to 'Edit -> Preferences' at the top of your main Gimp window.
2. Toward the bottom of the menu on the left, look for Window Management and click on it.
3. Click on one of your other Gimp windows, such as the toolbar.
4. Now hit the green '+' circle to maximize this window.

1. Press Alt + Space bar when Windows is maximized.
2. Click on Size and drag the edge of the window to the desired size.

nirmal s say:

In Windows 8.1, the Camera app sometimes lags or is slow .

In Windows 8.1, when I start the Camera app, it will lag or work slowly.

ravinath p say:

Hi,

 

The Camera app may lag or come up slowly if you have just started up Windows, or have just resumed the PC from sleep. If the PC is performing a high amount of background processes, as it does when starting up or resuming from sleep, this will affect the performance of the Camera app. The app should smooth out and improve performance in a short amount of time, when there are fewer background processes running.

xbf diego m say:

Hey Godpuncher,

It's not recommended plugging into power strips/surge protectors simply because some of them may not be able to handle the electrical current draw the Xbox requires, especially during certain actions that require a lot of power, like starting up a game that was "paused" from start-up with Instant-On Mode. The more devices you share with the Xbox on that same power strip can affect the power strip further and over time can cause corruption to the power supply. The power supply itself should have the current draw on there and you'd have to confirm with the power strip that it can handle all of the power requirements it has to deal with.

Our full article on the Xbox One Power supply can be found here.

The original Xbox has an external power unit so you may be able to replace it. Unfortunately they are not produced anymore, so you may need to look online for one or at your local game store.

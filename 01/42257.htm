If your pc is getting stuck at 22% then giving you the "Something Happened" and you have tried all the fix's the web has to offer and you are still stuck try this.

1. Open System Recovery Options when you start your computer(Most computers press F8 as soon as your pc starts. If you have HP like me press Esc)

3. If it says 0 windows installation, type bcdedit /createstore bcd

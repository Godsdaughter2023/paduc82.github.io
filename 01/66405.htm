mouneshwar r say:

Hi George,

 

The option “CD audio” input is called CD Player option in Windows Vista and Windows 7 which is available under Speaker properties.

 

But before you change the level of the CD Player, you need to enable Stereo Mix under the Sound option.

 

Step 1: To enable Stereo mix and make it as default audio device

a.    Right click on Speaker icon and click on Properties.

b.    Switch to recording tab.

c.    Right click on Microphone and choose “Show disabled devices”

d.    If you see CD Player option, select it as the default audio device and save the changes by clicking on apply.

e.    Else, select Stereo mix and click enable.

f.     Set Stereo mix as default audio device.

 

Step 2: Set the level for CD Player under Speaker properties

a.    Right click on Speaker icon and click on Properties.

b.    Switch to Playback tab.

c.    Right click on Speakers and click on properties.

d.    Switch to level tab and enable only CD Player.

e.    Click on apply to save changes.

 

Now you should be able to play CD directly using Paltalk.

 

Hope this information is helpful and let me know if you need any further assistance.

The Audio Settings in the Game DVR can be changed so you can disable the Audio when recording game clips. To do that, follow the steps below:

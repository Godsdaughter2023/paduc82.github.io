considerate_guy say:

Movie Maker will just take your photo dimensions and shrink them so it fits vertically (or horizontally) within the project area. If the aspect ratio of the photo doesn't match the aspect ratio of your movie, you'll end up with black space around it.

Skier-hughes has given a possible workaround which is to edit your photo and add your own color/frame/etc. around the outside of the picture so that it matches exactly with the aspect ratio of your movie. Another option is to actually crop your image (in an image editing program or in Photo Gallery) so it has the correct 4:3 or 16:9 aspect ratio. Then there will be no space around the photo at all.

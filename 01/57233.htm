Logitech MX5000 combo issues after Windows 10 Upgrade

Today I installed the Windows 10 upgrade you sent me.  Since the install, my Logitech MX 5000 Keyboard Mouse Combo does not work.  I notice that the driver for the BT Mini Receiver shows an error for in 'Settings/Devices/Connected Devices'.  I have uninstalled & re-installed but it comes back to the same error.

Please help me resolve this

zaklajoie say:

Here's what I did to fix mine.

1. While the BT receiver is plugged in, open Device Manager and delete the Bluetooth adapter that has the yellow exclamation point beside it.  I chose to delete the drivers too, but that is entirely up to you if you don't want to do that.

2. After you delete it, unplug the BT receiver and then plug in again. 

3. Device manager will detect that it was plugged in and will go through the detection process.  It should be detected under the Human Interface Devices as a Bluetooth HID device.  My Logitech software immediately started after the BT adapter was detect by the Device Manager and completed the configuration process.

Hopefully this helps a few of you.

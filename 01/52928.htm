Hi Alex

Most likely this is a permissions issue on your Webcam . . .

1
If you are using Skype on Windows 10:

Open the Settings App

Go to Privacy - Camera

Make sure 'Allow apps to access your camera' is turned on and make sure the Skype app is also set to On


2.
If you are using Skype on a Mac with OS Mojave:

Select System Preferences from the Apple menu

Choose the Privacy tab

On the left side, click Camera to manage app permissions.

Tick the box next to Skype to grant access to the camera

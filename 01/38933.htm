How to Find SSID (Network Name)

Login Information

  • Gateway - 192.168.0.1
  • Username - admin
  • Password - password

How to Find SSID(Network Name)

  1. Open web browser.
  2. Login to router by entering http://routerlogin.net  in to the address bar or by enter the default gateway IP address.
  3. The router will then prompt you to enter the user name and password. The default user name and password are: "admin", "password".
  4. From the menu on the left under the Setup section choose Wireless Settings.
  5. The SSID can be found on this page labeled Name(SSID).

davem121 say:
Hi Mia,

You will find the best device drivers for those devices on the support page for your device on the manufacturers website . . .

Utilities like Advanced Care will always report drivers are out of date, when a lot of time, there is nothing wrong with your system at all . . .


With regards to your Audio problem:

Right click Your Sound Icon

Choose 'Playback Devices'

Clikc your Playback Device in the resulting Dialog and click Properties

On the second dialog, click on the Enhancements

Disable all enhancements

Save and close those dialogs



Open the Settings App

Go to Update & Security - Troubleshoot - Playing Audio

Run that troubleshooter to see if it resolves this issue . . .

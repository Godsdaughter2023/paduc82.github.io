Set the frequency between 10 minutes and 24 hours - once an hour is the default. Hi RIck, there does seem to be an issue with the shutdown mechanism in the latest Windows 10 update . ..

It is not really good for your system to be using the Power Button to shut down on a regular basis . . .

Here is a workaround, until Microsoft rolls out a patch:

Right click your Desktop, choose New - Shortcut

Set Target to: %WINDIR%\system32\shutdown.exe /s /hybrid /t 0

Name it: Shutdown

Use that shortcut to shut your system down

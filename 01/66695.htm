Go to the storage settings, and press the button combination LB RB X LB RB X.

Let the console perform maintenance, and restart. 

Now insert your USB drive

Hey there,

I had the same problem after a clean new installation of Windows 10.

Because I didn't want to fomat all my drives I reset their NTFS permission.

  1. Open an cmd with administrative rights
  2. Use the following command: icacls Y:\ /T /C /RESET
  3. Instead of Y:\ you should use your drive letter.
  4. Wait. It'll take a bit.

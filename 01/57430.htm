There are three important things that need to be stated clearly, because just adding a Flash Drive with 8 GB or more of free space (or other external drive etc) to the PC concerned will not work.  A more powerful PC needs to be used for half of the operation:

  1. One must use another PC with space available.  At first, the Flash Drive (or other storage device) should be added to to this more powerful PC, and its drive letter noted in File Explorer.
  2. One then must download the Media Creation Tool to the same more powerful PC.
  3. When the the "Download Windows 10" page appears, here is where one can take a longtime to discover what to do!   Don't get worried that this heading appears to suggest that this is to download Windows 10 to someone who has not got it.   Alternatives are given, the first one being "Update Now" - which one would think is what needs to be used.

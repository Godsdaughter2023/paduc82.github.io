the larch say:

Try this special syntax:

content:~="00-00-00"

Use name:~="00-00-00" if it is parts of filenames that you are searching.

The hyphen symbol, like most special symbols, is not searchable without use of syntax such as ~=, which means "match anywhere".

[Update:  apparently special search symbols such as ~= don't work with the content property when searching in indexed locations, but do work when searching in non-indexed locations.]

The Realtek settings you are using only apply to the speaker and headphone 3.5mm jack outputs on your machine.

- 'Virtual' 5.1\7.1 is just that. Virtual surround via Stereo speakers.

Similarly 'Sonic for Headphones' would not help with a 5.1 speaker setup. This is only used

by the machines headphone output socket.

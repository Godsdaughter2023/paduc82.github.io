freddrick pal say:

Hi,

 

 

Digital Rights Management is the technology used to protect files from unauthorized distribution or playback. There are instances that the DRM components are encountering problems and needs to be reset. You can follow the steps below in resetting the DRM on your computer:

 

1. Make sure that your computer is configured to show hidden files.

                a. Click on Start button.

                b. In the Search box, type Control Panel and press Enter.

                c. Select Small icons under View by and go to File Explorer Options.

                d. Under the View tab, click on Show hidden files, folders, and drives.

                e. Click on Apply and OK.

2. Locate and open the DRM folder.

                a. Press Windows+R.

                b. Type regedit and press Enter.

                c. Expand the HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\Software\Microsoft\DRM key.

                d. On the right pane, double-click DataPath.

                e. In the Edit Binary Value dialog box, the location of the DRM folder is at the column on the right.

3. Select all the files in the DRM folder and press the Delete button.

 

Once you delete the files inside the DRM folder, you will need to download the licenses again for your Zune application.

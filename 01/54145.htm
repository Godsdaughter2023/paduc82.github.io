    You left out a few steps.  

    1.  Download the Expression 1680 driver for WIndows 10 / 64 bit.  Link is here.
    2.  Unzip the compressed file to a directory on your hard drive.  
    3.  Make sure the scanner is connected by USB and turned on.

    4.  Go to the Control Panel > Device Manager and look for your Perfection 1660 scanner.  It will be listed under "Imaging Devices" or "Unknown Devices."  

    5.  Right click on the scanner entry, then click on "Update Driver Software." 

    6.  Click on "Browse my computer for driver software."

    7.  Click on "Let me pick from a list of device drivers on my computer."

    8.  Click on "Have disk."

    9.  In the Install from Disk popup screen, click on browse and navigate to the directory containing the uncompressed Epson 1680 driver files.

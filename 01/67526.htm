Hello AimeeY and mgcamp84. 

The problem could be due to an outdated driver. 

1. Go to Device Manager (WinKey + X and then press M)
2. Click on the > symbol next to "Network adapters" 
3. Right click on the wireless network adapter and select "Uninstall"
4. Check the box that says, "Delete the driver software for this device" and then click "OK"
5. Right click on "Network adapters" 
6. Select "Scan for hardware changes" and wait a moment
7. Go to your taskbar and click on the Wi-Fi icon
8. Select the connection, check the box for "Connect automatically" and then click "Connect"
9. Enter the network security key and then click "Next" 
10. You should now be connected.

In the event that you still encounter the same connectivity issue,

1. Go to Network Connections (WinKey + X and then press W)
2. Right click on your Wi-Fi and select "diagnose"
3. Wait a moment until the troubleshooting has completed and click "Close" 

or

1. Go to Device Manager (WinKey + X and then press M)
2. Click on the > symbol next to "Network adapters" 
3. Right click on the wireless network adapter, select "Disable" and wait for a few moments
4. Right click again on the wireless network adapter, select "Enable" 

This issue may be due to a compatibility problem that arises when the hardware (network adapter) is given a new driver by Windows 10.  A driver is software that connects the hardware to the operating system or to computer programs.  When upgrading to a new operating system, the network adapter may encounter changes that will affect how efficiently it can communicate with the system.  To counter this, Microsoft provides a generic driver.  However, ultimately, it the job of the manufacturer to provide a suitable update for their device, not Microsoft.  As of October 2015, many network adapter manufacturers do not have drivers that have been updated to correct this issue.  Buying a replacement (e.g. external USB network adapter) may be futile as they were likely shelved before Windows 10 was even released and will encounter the same problem.

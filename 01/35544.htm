Get-AppxPackage | Select Name, PackageFullName

Get-AppxPackage PackageFullName | Remove-AppxPackage

sickbaomei say: Hi all,
Just to feedback that after extensive search on the internet for solutions, I finally manage to solve the problem... It has been bugging me for many days without a clue how to resolve this and I am glad I did last night. For more information, go to this site :-
http://idoneitmyself.wordpress.com/2011/03/09/troubleshooting-0x800f081f-error-on-windows-2008-r2-sp1-installation/      (It works for Windows 7 too).

Just run this command at the Administrator command prompt :-
dism /online /remove-package /packagename:Package_for_KB976932~31bf3856ad364e35~amd64~~6.1.1.17514

After its completed, re-run the Service Pack installation again and it should work.

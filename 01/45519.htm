Hi,
 
Thank you for contacting Microsoft Community.
 

I understand that you are unable to enable notifications from being displayed on the lock screen.

I would suggest you to perform mentioned method and see if it works.
 
Remove an App from the lock screen and then re-add it again.
 
Step 1: Remove the App from the list to be shown on the Lock Screen.
 
 
a) Press “Windows Logo” + “C” to display the charms bar.
 
b) Click on “Settings” and then on “Change PC Settings”.
 
c) In the Personalize tab, under “Lock Screen Apps” click on the App and then click on “Don’t show quick status here”.
 
d) This will remove an App from the Lock Screen App list.
 
 
 
Step 2: Turn on notifications in PC Settings. (This should be on by default.)
 
a) Open the Settings Charm.
 
b) Tap or click Change PC settings.
 
c) Tap or click Notifications.
 
d) Slide the Show app notifications and Show app notifications on the lock screen switches to On.
 

Step 3: Add an App to lock screen notifications
 
a) Open the Settings charm.
 
b) Tap or click Change PC settings.
 
c) Tap or click Personalize.
 
d) In the right pane, with the Lock screen tab selected, under Choose apps to run in the background tap or click one of the “plus” symbols and select an App.
 
e) Then under Choose an app to display detailed status, tap or click the icon beneath it and select Weather.

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-IN/windows-8/how-manage-notifications

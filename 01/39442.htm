Copy and Paste events in the Windows 10 calendar

As there is No way to copy and paste events in the windows 10 calendar, I have a workaround.

I synchronize my windows 10 calendar with Google calendar and a business calendar on my android phone. You can copy and paste on either the Google online calendar  or a calendar app on your mobile device. 

On the Google calendar that is synchronized to your Windows 10 calendar, 

-single left click on event to be copied (a small box opens up with limited details of the event)

-click on the 3 vertical dots, this opens several options (on the upper right between the delete can and the close X) 

      You can also double click the event to open a full page will all the details of the event, Click "More Actions", then "Duplicate"

-From that drop down, click "Duplicate" This opens the duplicated event. 

-Change the date and save or don't change the date and save. 

-if you didn't change the date there will be two duplicate events on the same day. You simply drop and drag one of the events to a new date. 

-if I need multiple copies I duplicate multiple events without changing the date and save each of them. I go back to the calendar view and drop and drag them individually to each new date.

Hello pc loopy,

This is a solution that has worked for some others with the writer experienced transient error, 0x800423F3. It is contributed to: WAW8

Try the following:
1) Start the computer in Safe Mode without networking.
2) Open a command prompt as administrator
3) Type "net stop winmgmt" without quotes and press enter to make certain the WindowsManagementInstrumentation (WMI) service is not running.
4) Go to the windows\system32\WBEM\ and rename the Repository folder.
5) Reboot and restart as normal
6) Open a command prompt as administrator.
7) Type "net stop winmgmt" without quotes and press enter to stop the wmi service.
8) Type winmgmt /resetRepository and restart the system.

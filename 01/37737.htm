Does the outline appear near/at the same location of a program window that was previously in that space?  There's an issue that goes back to Windows 7 (maybe earlier) where you click a menu item (like right-click and choose "refresh") but the fadeout effect of the "refresh" would remain stuck visible on the screen, typically due to graphics card issues.  In fact, it may be a good idea to update your graphics card with the latest version available at the computer manufacturer's website.

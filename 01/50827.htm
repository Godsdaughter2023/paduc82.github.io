If formatted with NTFS, it's a permissions thing.  The users on computer 2 are not the same as the users on computer 1 and they cannot be MADE so.  Nothing overly complicated - you simply have to go to the root of the drive on a system trhat DOES see everything and add the "Everyone" user to have FULL access, inherited.

Follow these steps:

Step 1: Click on Start, select "Settings" and then click on "Control Panel".

Step 2: Double click on the icon called "Mouse".

Step 3: Select the properties tab of the touch panel. The name of this tab varies depending on the manufacturer of your computer, your version of the operating system and the drivers installed on the machine.

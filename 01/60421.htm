"How to Refresh Windows 8 and Windows 8.1"

  • The PC boots into Windows RE (Recovery Environment).
  • Windows RE scans the hard drive for your data, settings, and apps, and puts them aside (on the same drive).
  • Windows RE installs a fresh copy of Windows.
  • Windows RE restores the data, settings, and apps it has set aside into the newly installed copy of Windows.
  • The PC restarts into the newly installed copy of Windows.

"How to Reset Windows 8 and Windows 8.1"

"When you reset your PC, the drive will be completely reset, and all your apps and files will be removed from it. can i use the original product key for windows vista home premium OEMAct, which is printed underneath of my laptop, for a new installation of windows vista home premium sp2. Please i need reply ASAP. Many thanks

a. user say:

 

You can use the Product Key on the bottom of your Laptop to reinstall Vista on that Laptop only.

The Operating Systems content are the same, whether OEM or Retail Licence.

Read this about Licences:

If Vista came preinstalled on your Computer, it is classified as an OEM licence, and is non-transferable.


The OEM Vista Licence is tied to the original Computer it is installed on, and dies with the Computer.
You can NOT transfer it to another Computer/Laptop

If a Retail Vista Licence, you may transfer it to another Computer/Laptop.
But, it may only be installed on one Computer at a time.

 

One Licence = One Installation.

 

SP is short for Service Pack, which is for Performance and Security enhancement of all the Vista OperatingSystems.

 

There have been 2 Service Packs released, up to this date, for Vista.

Please use Powercfg command to check this issue. To do that, refer to the steps below:

a) Open "cmd" and "Run as administrator".

Powercfg -devicedisablewake "devicename"
Note: "devicename" is a device name listed as a result of running the first command.

Powercfg -devicedisablewake "Broadcom NetXtreme Gigabit Ethernet" without quotes

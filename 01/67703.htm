Combining movies together

How do I combine multiple videos; example part 1, part 2 and part 3 together using windows live movie maker?

considerate_guy say:

First, I assume you have each of your parts as actual videos (part 1.wmv, part 2.wmv, etc.).  If you have multiple projects (.wlmp files), you would first need to save each as a movie (File > Save movie).

 

If you have your individual movie files, it is very easy.

  1. Open Movie Maker
  2. Click Add videos and photos
  3. Choose all your movie files (you should see them added to your storyboard)
  4. Click Play button and confirm they play in the desired order.
  5. Save your project (File > Save project).  This is just in case you want to go back and make changes to order, add titles, etc. later.
  6. Save your final movie (File > Save movie).

Hi,

They maybe valid U.S addresses for receiving mail and what-not, but U.S bases overseas in Europe and Asia are generally not legal U.S territory.  My quick research suggests you should Look at your SOFA (Status of Forces Agreement), either that or speak to your legal representative or Embassy.

There is of course a secondary issue which can not be overridden by the above, that being Internet access.  Xbox Live sets your physical location based on your I.P Address.  For Example, if you were in the U.S Air Force based at RAF Lakenheath and had access to a Xbox console, you would get your Internet access from the host nation, in this instance from a UK ISP.

mark ole say:

Hi Morgan,

If volume rockers are not working, running the Surface Diagnostic Toolkit might help resolve the issue. The toolkit runs a series of tests to diagnose your Surface device. The diagnosis and repair time averages 15 minutes or less, but can take an hour or longer. This will also download the latest updates for your Surface. Click here to learn how to run the toolkit.

If running the toolkit doesn't work, force shutdown your device.

  1. Press and hold the power button until the screen turns off (about 10 seconds), then release the power button.
  2. Press and release the power button to turn your Surface back on. You should see the Microsoft logo.

If that doesn't work, use this shutdown process to make sure your Surface completely turns off. Press and hold down the power button until your Surface restarts and you see the Windows logo screen (this takes about 20 seconds), then release the power button.

Also, if the volume controls on the keyboard do not work, hold down the Fn key until the light turns off.

If you have additional questions, or if the issue persists after the troubleshooting steps above, please let me know by replying to this thread. This way, I'll get notified and respond back to you as soon as I can.

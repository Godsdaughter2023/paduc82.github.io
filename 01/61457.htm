basith m say:

Hi,

 

To revert back to Windows 8, you will have to reset your RT device. If you perform Reset, all of your personal files will be deleted and your settings will be reset. All apps that you installed will be removed. Only apps that came with your PC will be reinstalled. So before you perform Reset, I would suggest you to take backup of your personal and other data files.

 

For further reference, you may refer to the following Microsoft Help article.

How to refresh, reset, or restore your PC:

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-8/restore-refresh-reset-pc

 

Please feel free to visit our website for any help with Windows Operating System.

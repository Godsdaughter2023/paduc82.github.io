Go to the website for your laptop's manufacturer and look for SUPPORT and\or DRIVERS...

Somewhere there will be an area to enter your model number or your serial number....

Once that is done, you will be asked to choose an operating system, unless it is auto detected....

Select Win 8.1 and see what comes up... there may be drivers of multiple types, and maybe not...

That depends on what the manufacturer has provided for Win 8.1...

If you can't find what you need, then look for Win 8 or Win 7 drivers....

Without knowing anything about your laptop, I can't be more specific...

A lot of laptops require you to use THEIR Graphics drivers because they have modified either the driver or the hardware...

As a result, the standard drivers from the graphics chip manufacturer won't work...

Hi BC. I'm Greg, an installation specialist and 9 year Windows MVP, here to help you.

It does not cost $99 to change out of S Mode: https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/445606...

Please take note of the Status at Settings > Update & Security > Activation at the top. It should say that you have a Digital License for Pro version. If you've signed in with your MS account then it should say it's linked to the MS Account.

If for some reason (like it has an embedded WIndows 8, 8.1 or 10 Home key) it does not give you the choice during install for Pro and installs Home instead, you can apply the Pro Upgrade from your MS Account, or you can go offline to insert the Pro Generic Version key from here: http://winaero.com/blog/generic-key-to-install-... then after it changes to Pro version it will activate when you go online with the Pro Digital License.

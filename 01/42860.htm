There are some other threads about this.  I tried some things with no success.  But finally got the following to fix my problem:

  1. Go to Settings -> System
  2. In the left column, click on Apps & features.
  3. Scroll down through the list of apps till you get to Photos. Click on that.

Now you should see two buttons.

If the buttons are Reset and Uninstall, press the Reset button.  That should fix your problem.

If the buttons are Move and Uninstall, you have a different problem from what I had and I don't have a solution.  But there is a Reset button under Advanced options that might do something.

Hope that works.

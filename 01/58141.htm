a. user say:

According to the Casio website, Casio stopped supporting that keyboard after Windows 8. That means Casio has not updated the driver to keep up with the changes in Windows, and has not tested the keyboard on Windows 10 to see if it will work properly.

Without support from the manufacturer, it's only a matter of time before hardware stops working properly, or totally.

Casio doesn't have its own community forum, but there are at least two unofficial community forums that try to provide help with Casio keyboards. One is the Casio Music Forums and the other is the Casio section of Keyboardforums.com. Either one may have information that might help your keyboard soldier on for a while longer.

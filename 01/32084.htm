mike--- say: Sunny3456,
    Here is a list of the scoring in Purble Pairs.

The points you earn during regular gameplay are as follows:
* Making a match with a tile the first time you turn it over: +30
* Making a match with a tile the second time you turn it over: +20
* Making a match with a tile any other time: +10
* Earn a Sneak Peek Token: +20 [additional to regular pts for match]

For Purble Jokers, which “matches” as a 3rd tile, the points given should be the “match” points for the pair that matches with the Joker, and an additional 10 points for the Joker itself.

Note that most power-ups and blockers do not give you additional points, just the points for matching them. However, power-ups do have a beneficial impact on your score because they save you time (and the reverse is true of blockers).

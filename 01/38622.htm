drlg say: I fixed it.
Just in case someone has the same problem.  I followed the previous advice and chance another of the files.

Here's the solution:

To prevent any more 'disappearances', change this registry key:

In McAfee, First disable access protection in navigation /general settings

1) Open McAfee

2) Click on Navigation

3) Click on General Settings and Alerts

4) Click on Access Protection

5) Un-Check Use Access Protection

6) Click on Apply

Edit the registry:

1) Open the Registry Editor (regedit.exe) and browse to this 'folder'

1b) type regedit.exe in Windows search (windows start button)

1c) click yes if asked for permission.

[HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\McAfee\SystemCore\VSCore\On Access Scanner\McShield\Configuration\Default]
2) Locate the entry ExcludedItem_28 in the list 
3) double-click on ExcludedItem_28 and
4) change the value 3|3|*.TMP to 3|3|*.LOG
5) Close the Registry Editor, save your work and reboot the computer.

Reenable access protection by following the McAfee steps above.

a.  Go to your C:\Users\<Username>\Documents\BioWare\Mass Effect\Config directory and rename the BIOEngine.ini file to something else.
b.  Launch Mass Effect without running the Config utility. The game will recreate BIOEngine.ini using default values, and should correctly identify your audio hardware.
c.  In the in-game settings Hardware Audio should already be selected - do not change it back to software audio or make any other changes to the Sound settings.

 Re-install audio drivers

Uninstall the sound drivers completely from Device Manager and then install the latest audio drivers for your computer.

1.    Open Device Manager.

2.    Expand Sound, Video and Game Controllers

3.    Right click on the Audio component and click uninstall.

Once the drivers are completely uninstalled, download the latest drivers for your sound card from the computer manufacturer.

 

Now try plugging in your headset. If you’ve any drivers that came along with your headset, install them and then check if they work.

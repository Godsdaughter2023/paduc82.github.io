a. user say:

Two things to know:

Switching from a 32-bit architecture to a 64-bit architecture won't give your computer 'extra power.' The advantage of x64 is the ability to make use of more than 4 GB of RAM. That will certainly be an advantage for Adobe Creative Cloud but it won't improve your computer's ability to perform data-intensive tasks. You'll need an upgraded graphics card and/or CPU for that.

You can't upgrade from a 32-bit architecture to 64-bit; you need to do a clean install.

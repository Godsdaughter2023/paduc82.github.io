Hi,

You do not use the Vista System Restore feature to put your Computer back to Factory Settings.

System Restore only puts your Computer's settings back to an earlier date.

 

Ask your Computer Manufacturer how to do a Recovery to Factory Settings.

You would either look for a way to start the process under Programs > then the Computer Manufacturer's Software > look for it there > or you would press a certain Key or Keys at Boot.

That would be either F10, F11, Alt + F10, etc depending on the Manufacturer.

Ask them which way to do it as it is their Recovery process.

 

Or ask them to send you a set of Vista Recovery disks to reinstall the Operating System.

They should do that for a small cost.

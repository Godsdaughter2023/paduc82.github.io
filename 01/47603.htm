darleen pen say:

Hi,

The pre-installed Alarms & Clock application in Windows 10 works like other traditional alarm apps. If you'll set an alarm using the pre-installed alarm app, a small message box will appear at the lower left part of your screen and the computer will play the alarm sound.

However, you can modify how Alarms & Clock application notifies you. You can disable the sound when the alarm arrives and enable the notification message. To do so, follow these steps:

  1. Using Search, type alarm.
  2. On the search results, select Alarms & Clock (Trusted Windows Store app).
  3. Set an alarm by clicking the + icon.
  4. Once done, click on the three dots icon and then select Notification settings.
  5. In Notifications & actions window under Get notifications from these senders, look for Alarms & Clock and then click on it.
  6. In Alarms & Clock window, make sure that the options for Notifications, Show notification banners and Show notifications in action center are enabled.
  7. Once the alarm arrives, it will not play a sound and you'll get a notification message like this, regardless of what activity you are doing on your computer.

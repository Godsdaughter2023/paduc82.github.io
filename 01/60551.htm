internet expert say:

The Xbox One supports the following resolutions:


-480p (non-recommended, bare minimum)

-720p

-1080i

-1080p

-4K UHD (only for Xbox One S)


"2K" is not exactly a standard TV resolution, but maybe you're attempting to use a monitor with a resolution of 2560x1440, or 2560x1600? Something along those lines? Whatever the native resolution of your 2K display happens to be, the Xbox will likely attempt to output at 720p or 1080p, and you'll either see stretching and/or black bars to accommodate for the 16:9 aspect ratio it tries to force. The Xbox One does not support resolutions other than the ones listed above.

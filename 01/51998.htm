Hey guys,

If we detected your TV and volume controls don't work, this means that your TV doesn't support discrete POWER ON commands, just POWER TOGGLE.

If you want this to work, there's something counter-intuitive you can do to enable this.

Go into TV & OneGuide settings and power options and enable "Xbox Turns off my TV". This will enable sending POWER TOGGLE for Xbox On.

However, be aware of 2 things:

1) This will toggle your device power state on Xbox on and off. This means if your device is On when you power on the Xbox, it can turn it off.

2) If you change inputs to use an Xbox 360 or other device on your TV, this may not be the best experience.

Lif3Chang3s Hey there :)

 

Here is how you get a new app password.

 

1.Sign in to your Microsoft account. (www.outlook.com)

2.Under Password and security info, tap or click Edit security info.

If you're prompted for a security proof, enter it and tap or click Submit.

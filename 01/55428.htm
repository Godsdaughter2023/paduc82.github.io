Setting Up Telstra Tbox (2) Voice Controls Through Xbox One

Hi Everyone, 

I have been having a play today with the OneGuide, Telstra Tbox and my Xbox One. I have had the TV and Amp turning on with the Xbox One for quite a quite a while, but have always had trouble with the Telstra T-Box. Below is how I setup the Tbox to work correctly with Xbox one.

3. Go to Cable or Satellite Box then, Cable or Satellite Box Setup

4. Enter "Netbox" in the Brand Box and Go Next

8. Go Back to the TV and One Guide Settings Screen (Back twice from the Device and Power Options screen)

9. Go into One Guide and Under HDMI set TV line up to your local area.

10. Go to TV and Access OneGuide, add the channels you watch to the favorites list (channel must be in list for voice control to change to that channel)

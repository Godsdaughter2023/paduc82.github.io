Hi Z999,

If the child account is not receiving emails from senders that are not on the child contact list, it is possible that Family Safety is set up wherein the parent manages the contact list of the child account. If the contact list is being managed by the parent account, the child account cannot receive emails from non-contacts or other sources and add contacts. Even though you mentioned that for initial setup the contacts have been set to be controlled by the child, we need to make sure that the settings are correct. To do this, please perform the following steps:

3. On the child’s settings page, in the navigation pane, click Contact management.

4. To allow the child to receive emails from non-contacts and add or remove their own contacts, click Child manages their own contact list.

5. Click Save.

After changing the settings, please refresh the Family Safety filter so that the changes will be reflected on the computer. To do this, please refer to the article below:

Remember to Refresh the Family Safety Filter

  • Disable (preferrably uninstall) your Antivirus utility before attempting the upgrade.

  • Disable General USB Devices (example - Smart Card Reader).

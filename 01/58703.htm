But as Mike suggested, it could still cause permissions issues for a user, or users, particularly if your machine is on a network.

However, when you see  a lot of errors in Event Viewer, unless your machine has had problems or significant loss of performance and you are troubleshooting that issue, the best fix often is, don't worry about it.

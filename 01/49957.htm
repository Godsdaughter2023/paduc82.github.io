Let's simplify this...

I asked what the BIOS shows in terms of amount of memory installed and you said 1GB Installed.  The BIOS has nothing to do with whatever operating system you have installed.  It is at the hardware level - what it sees is unaffected by your operating system.  If it says you have 1GB, then you have 1GB of working memory.

First check that you actually physically have 3GB installed - put your hands on the memory.  Second - make sure your BIOS isn't configured in some 'safe mode' that limits it to 1GB.  Third - if the BIOS don't see it - Windows is not going to see it - so until you get the BIOS to see your 3GB of memory (if it physically exists and is non-defective) - Windows is the least of your concerns.

Skype subscription/credit selection - Microsoft Community

shaheenpallikalakath say:

Skype subscription/credit selection

How can I choose in between Skype subscription and Skype credit when I make a call?
julie_s1 say:

Hello

There is no option to choose between Skype subscription and Skype credit when making a call.

If the country you are calling is covered by your subscription, then when you make a call it will be the subscription that will be used.

But if you already used up the minutes of your subscription, or the country you're calling is not covered by the subscription, then that's the time your Skype Credit will be used.

If you have further questions, please do not hesitate to reply to this post.

a. user say:

Hi,

Windows Briefcase has its feature to automatically sync multiple copy of files. Briefcase updates the files on your computer to match the modified versions from other users when you connect it. Users only need to use Windows Explorer and then drag or copy their files into the Briefcase once.

Here are the steps on how you can sync Briefcase over a network:

  1. Move the Briefcase over the network to other user.
  2. Right-click the Briefcase, and then click Update All.

NOTE: Any further changes to either the files on other computers or those in the Briefcase are synchronized whenever the user right-clicks on the briefcase and selects Update All.

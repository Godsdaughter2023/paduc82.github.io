Hi,

 

Error 651 is caused if your modem (or other connecting device) has reported an error.

 

Try these steps and check if it makes any difference.

 

Step 1

Run the network troubleshooter and check.

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows7/Using-the-Network-troubleshooter-in-Windows-7

 

Step 2

The file “raspppoe.sys” found under c:\windows\system32\drivers folder seems to be the root cause of the issue.

Rename raspppoe.sys to raspppoe.old and then check.

 

Step 3

Try to re-register the file and check if it works fine.

a) Click on Start.

b) In search window type cmd and press enter. Right click and select Run as administrator.

c) In the command prompt window typeregsvr32 raspppoe.sys and press Enter.

 

Step 4

Try power cycling the Modem and Router and check if it works fine.

a)      Save all your current work, close all open applications, then shut down your computer. Wait for a minute.

b)      First, turn your Router off and wait for a couple of minutes.

c)      Turn off your modem.

d)      Disconnect all the Ethernet cables that are connected to the router, modem and the PC.

e)      Reconnect them and ensure that there are no loose connections in between them.

f)       Now, switch on your PC and let it boot.

g)      Switch on the modem first and after it gets initialized, switch on the router.

Old battery? Unplug power to computer - remove battery and clean the contacts with a pencil
eraser (do not use this if your battery has thin slots - just clean the knife edges that fit into

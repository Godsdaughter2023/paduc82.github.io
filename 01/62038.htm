natearnold say:

Usually compatibility settings will be most useful. 

1.  Right click on the shortcut or app executable file (use the shortcut...it is a ton easier). 

2.  Click on "Properties"

3.  Go to the "Compatibility" tab and you will see a few options.

The most useful options for AoE II's ACT is to make sure that the "Override high DPI settings" (or in the case of the Windows Creators Edition update, "Override high DPI scaling behavior.") box is checked (For Windows 10 Creators Edition Update, make sure "Scaling is performed by:" "Application" is selected).

You will also notice a check box with "Reduced color mode" with a context menu below it.  Check that box as well and in your case you will want to select "16-bit (65536) color".

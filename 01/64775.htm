kitty rickardo say:

I have had this same problem.  I have an HP Deskjet F380 printer and changed from colour to grayscale some time ago (to save on ink consumption) - and like you I forgot how to get the colour back.  I have no idea whether the system, printer etc. makes a difference to what will work and what will not.  However, in my case, the answer was simple (although it took a long time to find it!).

This is what I did:

Start>Control Panel>Hardware & Sound>Printers>Right click on your default printer>Printing Properties>Preferences towards the bottom of the page>Page/Quality at top of page>Colour at top of page.  You will see a box on the left of the pane (Print in Grayscale) and to the right of this there is the image of a page.  If you click in the "Print in Grayscale" box the page to the right will be in black/plain text.  If you un-tick the "Print in Grayscale" box the page to the right will show coloured text.  Click OK>OK and it is done.  It is simply to switch from one to the other - just follow the instructions above to print in grayscale or colour.  It works for me every time.

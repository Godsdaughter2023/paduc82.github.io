leo invictus say:

You can adjust the Y-axis by choosing "Controller" at the title screen, choose your save slot, then select Options, then Options again (not Controls), and either scroll left or right to invert the Y-axis gameplay or Y-axis camera to your liking.   I don't see an X-axis option.

I wanted to add that the above instructions apply when using a standard game controller. 


The game may also be played using a Kinect, but in that situation there is no option to alter axis control.

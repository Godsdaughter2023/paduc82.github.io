Administrator permission required If you're prompted for an administrator password or confirmation, type the password or provide confirmation.

2. Click the Boot tab, and then click the listing for ?Windows 7???. (The one you plan to deleted)

3. Click Delete, set the Time out to '0', click 'Apply' and 'OK', and then click Restart.

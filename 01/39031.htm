If your USB headset mic stops working, windows 10 especially after - Microsoft Community

jaredfio say:

If your USB headset mic stops working, windows 10 especially after update

My USB headset Mic stopped working. Sounds was fine mic didn't work. I tried everything in all the forums, Different port, drivers, set as default, enable, check mute etc... everything.

I even contacted windows support and as I was talking to them I discovered the answer. This issue may happen after an update which sets your settings to default. 

If you have this issue and nothing else works try going to: 

Settings > Privacy Settings  > All Apps to use Mic  > (Enable) 

**Windows 10 **

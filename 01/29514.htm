There is a lot of mis-information about the names of the different Aero functions in Windows 7.

Aero Peek: When you hover the cursor over the small, rectangular, Show Desktop button at the right end of the Taskbar, all windows will disappear and you will see the Desktop, until you move the cursor away from that button. You can also press the Windows logo key+Spacebar to peek at the Desktop. While peeking at the Desktop, you will see transparent lines running at an angle from top/left to bottom/right.

When you have a program open and you hover the cursor over that programs Taskbar Icon, you will see a small Thumbnail of the open window, this is called the Thumbnail Preview.

When you hover the cursor over the Taskbar Thumbnail of the open window, the full Window that the Thumbnail represents will be displayed, this is called the Preview Window.

I managed to resolve the problem.

What I did was opening PowerShell and issuing the following command:

Get-AppxPackage -Name Microsoft.WindowsStore

This told me what I needed to do next. I issued the following commands:

Get-AppxPackage -Name Microsoft.WindowsStore | Remove-AppxPackage
Add-AppxPackage -Path -Register

And that's it. Problem solved. Things that I had tried before and failed:

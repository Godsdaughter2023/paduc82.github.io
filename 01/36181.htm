RB3 Achievement Guide - Microsoft Community

ride the piggy say:

RB3 Achievement Guide

There are 62 achievements with a total of 1250 points.
1000 points are On-Disc achievements. 250 are considered DLC Pack 1 achievements.

Most of your achievements can be tracked in Career > My Goals. I listed where each achievement is tracked in case you're missing one or feel like you've achieved it and haven't received credit.

Tune Up 4
Calibrate your audio/video setup for the optimal Rock Band 3 experience.

Career > My Goals > Rock Band > Rock Band Basics
Use auto-calibration with the RB2 Wireless (or other compatible controllers) or manually set your calibration.

Self-Made Dude or Lady 5
Create a Character.

Career > My Goals > Rock Band > Rock Band Basics
Hop in to the Customize menu and create a new character.

Best. Name. Ever. 5
Rename your band.

Career > My Goals > Rock Band > Rock Band Basics
In the Customize menu, rename your band.

Well Connected 6
Connect your Rock Band 3 Band with rockband.com at http://www.rockband.com.

Career > My Goals > Rock Band > Rock Band Basics
Unavailable at the moment, link your RB3 band with rockband.com using the Account Linking code found in the RB3 pause menu shell Options > Extras > Account Linking

You Ain't Seen Nothing Yet 20
Maintain overdrive for 60 seconds.

Career > My Goals > Rock Band > Overdrive
Easiest done with a band that is coordinating full overdrive bars. Suggested songs: The Kill by 30 Seconds to Mars, Handlebars by Flo-bots, Are You Gonna Be My Girl. It can also be done in solo play using Guns 'n' Roses' "Sorry" on expert guitar. YouTube has some great videos to follow on that.

Millionaire Club 25
Get 1,000,000 on one song.

Career > My Goals > Rock Band > Score
Possible with 2 players on Hard/Expert depending on the song or 3 or more with players on medium or easy. Find a song you enjoy that has a good portion of triple chords or a lot of notes in general. Use overdrive together and keep your multiplier up! Dire Straits' Walk of Life or The Flaming Lips' Yoshimi both have a ton of triple chords and paired with a good bassist and drummer you can easily snag a million points.

Rock Band Master 25
5 Star on Medium (or 3 Star on a higher difficulty) any 50 Rock Band 3 songs.

Rock Band Legend 30
5 Star every song in Rock Band 3 on Hard.

Rock Band Immortal 50
5 Star every song in Rock Band 3 on Expert.

Career > My Goals > Rock Band > Epic Rock Band 3
These three work in conjunction. Use your career "My Goals" section to track what songs remain. 5 starring all songs on expert up to 50 songs will net Rock Band Master then completing the set list will get Legend and Immortal as well. I recommend going for all these AND Endless Set list 3 at the same time. The ES3 is unlocked from the start so you can hop right into it immediately. Anyone who is platinum in RB2 can easily achieve all these at once.

Alex's Luggage Combination 30
Beat a Rock Band 3 score of 12,345,678.

Career > My Goals > Rock Band > Epic Rock Band 3
Get a combined career score on all instruments totaling over 12,345,678. Finishing ES3 or playing 80-100 songs will grab this pretty quickly.

Hometown Threwdown 20
Complete the "Hometown Throwdown" Road Challenge.

Real Nor'easter 20
Complete "The Wicked Awesome Tour".

Hell Defrosted 25
Win all rewards on the "Hell Freezes Over" Road Challenge.

Wilderness Survival 20
Complete the "Through the Wilderness, Eh?" Road Challenge.

Major Mileage 25
Get 90 or more spades on "The Long Drive South" Road Challenge.

Party Animal 20
Completed the "Total Debauchery" Road Challenge.

The Connoisseur's Connoisseur 25
Get 90 or more spades on "The European Connoisseur" Road Challenge.

Mile High Club 25
Complete the "Really Frequent Flyers" Road Challenge.

Ultimate Road Warrior 30
Win all awards on the "Really Frequent Flyers" Road Challenge.

Career > My Goals > Road Challenges
Complete all Road Challenges with at least enough spades to get all 3 awards will grab you all of these Road Challenge achievements.

HOPO-cidal Maniac 25
Kill 53,596 Hammer-ons and Pull-offs.

Career > My Goals > Guitar Goals > Epic Goals
One of the most time consuming achievements in RB3. Takes about 1.5-2 weeks of solid GUITAR (must be done on guitar) playing to net 53,596 HO/POs WITHOUT strumming them. If you have the habit of strumming your HO/POs you will not get credit for them. Stick to songs with a lot of HOPOs such as Antibodies, Green Grass/High Tides and many DLC songs. Keep track of your progress in MY GOALS.

Bleeding Fingers 30
Get 85% on all Guitar Solos in Rock Band 3 on Hard or Expert.

Career > My Goals > Guitar Goals > Guitar Solo Goals
Hit 85% or higher on all guitars solo sections on RB3 career songs on Hard or Expert difficulty. Using the My Goals screen can help you keep track and just play the songs until you're good enough to hit the solo sections. Drop to Hard if you're struggling on tough solos like Dio's Rainbow in the Dark or Ozzy's Crazy Train.

Guitar Perfectionist 25
Get 100% accuracy on Expert Guitar.

Career > My Goals > Guitar Goals > Epic Goals
100% a song on Expert Guitar. Does not have to be a full combo. Best song is Charlene (free DLC) since it's short and super easy.

Guitar Apprentice 20
5 Star on Easy Guitar (or 3 Star on a higher difficulty) any 25 Rock Band 3 songs.

Career > My Goals > Guitar Goals > Easy Song Progress (or any difficulty)
Complete 25 RB3 on-disc songs with a 5* rating on Easy Guitar or 3* or more stars rating on Medium or higher. Just play and this one will be yours in no time. You can also sort your track list by difficulty while playing solo and work your way through the 25 easiest songs.

Bass Streaker 10
Get a streak of 500 notes on Bass.

Career > My Goals > Bass > General Bass Goals
Complete a 500 note streak on bass. If you have any Judas Priest songs (like You Got Another Thing Comin') those are great. Otherwise, try The Look by Roxette. Difficulty does not matter.

Most Authentic Strummer 10
Hit 100% of the notes, only strumming up, on Hard Bass.

Career > My Goals > Bass > Authentic Bass Goals
Many people have issues using the GH series guitars so try this with a RB1 or RB2 guitar if you have one. Pick an easy song (like Charlene) and only up-strum songs. Can be done on Hard or Expert.

Bass Apprentice 20
5 Starred on Easy Bass (or 3 Starred on a higher difficulty) 25 Rock Band 3 songs.

Career > My Goals > Bass > Easy (or higher) Song Progress
Complete 25 RB3 on-disc songs with a 5* rating on Easy Bass or 3* or more stars rating on Medium or higher. Just play and this one will be yours in no time. You can also sort your track list by difficulty while playing solo and work your way through the 25 easiest songs.

Drum Roll, Please! 10
Nail a drum roll.

Career > My Goals > Drums > General Drum Goals
Drum roll sections are highlighted like a overdrive fill section except only on one note. Def Leppard's Foolin' has several throughout the song. Difficulty does not matter. In order to "nail" it you have to hit the notes as fast or faster than the section shows in game.

Fastest Feet 15
Hit 90% of the Kick notes in a song on Hard Drums.

Career > My Goals > Drums > General Drum Goals
Hit 90% of the orange kick notes in a song on Hard or Expert drums. If it comes to it, pick an easy song like Charlene.

Drums Apprentice 20
5 Star on Easy Drums (or 3 Star on a higher difficulty) any 25 Rock Band 3 songs.

Career > My Goals > Drums > Easy (or higher) Song Progress
Complete 25 RB3 on-disc songs with a 5* rating on Easy Drums or 3* or more stars rating on Medium or higher. Just play and this one will be yours in no time. You can also sort your track list by difficulty while playing solo and work your way through the 25 easiest songs.

Keys Streaker 15
Get a streak of 350 notes on Keys.

Career > My Goals > Keys > General Keyboard Goals
Complete a 350 note streak on regular 5-button keys. Difficulty does not matter. If you're playing on a lower difficulty shoot for a longer song that has a high tier on keys, such as 25 or 6 to 4 or China Grove.

Keys Apprentice 20
5 Star on Easy Keys (or 3 Star on a higher difficulty) any 25 Rock Band 3 songs.

Career > My Goals > Keys > Easy (or higher) Song Progress
Complete 25 RB3 on-disc songs with a 5* rating on Easy 5-button Keys or 3* or more stars rating on Medium or higher. Just play and this one will be yours in no time. You can also sort your track list by difficulty while playing solo and work your way through the 25 easiest songs.

This does not stack with Pro Keys Apprentice.

Pro Bass Apprentice 20
5 Star on Easy Pro Bass (or 3 Star on a higher difficulty) any 25 Rock Band 3 songs

Career > My Goals > Pro Bass > Easy (or higher) Song Progress
Complete 25 RB3 on-disc songs using the Squier Pro or Mustang Pro Controllers with a 5* rating on Easy Pro Bass or 3* or more stars rating on Medium or higher. Just play and this one will be yours in no time.

You can also sort your track list by difficulty while playing solo and work your way through the 25 easiest songs.

Drum Trainer Initiate 15
Complete the introductory Pro Drum Trainer lessons.

Career > My Goals > Pro Drums > Pro Drum Trainer Goals
Finish the whole 1st section of the Pro Drum Trainer. You don't need a full pro set. I completed this with a yellow and green cymbal activated. The game will ask you to select which cymbals and pedals are active when you start the trainer.

Drum Trainer Graduate 30
Complete the final Pro Drum Trainer lessons.

Career > My Goals > Pro Drums > Pro Drum Trainer Goals
Finish the pro drum trainer in it's entirety. The final lessons will be impossible for most people. There are tips online but skill level is a majority of the requirement for this one.

Play a Real Guitar Already! 15
Play "The Hardest Button to Button" on Pro Guitar.

Career > My Goals > Pro Guitar > Introducing Pro Guitar (First Song Lessons)
Play the song "The Hardest Button to Button" by The White Stripes using the Squier Pro or Mustang Pro guitar controllers. Difficulty does not matter.

Pro Guitar to the Max 15
Max out your Score Multiplier meter on Pro Guitar.

Career > My Goals > Pro Guitar > General Pro Guitar Goals
Reach a 4x multiplier while using a Squier Pro or Mustang Pro controller then activate your overdrive. Once it shows a 8x multiplier you will have the achievement.

Power Chords 15
Complete the "Power Chords" lessons in the Pro Guitar trainer.

Career > My Goals > Pro Guitar > Introducing Pro Guitar Part 2 (Power Chords)
Finish the "Power Chords" lesson in the Pro Guitar trainer with one of the Pro Controllers.

Complex Chords 25
Complete the "More Chord Holding and Arpeggiation" Pro Guitar lessons.

Career > My Goals > Pro Guitar > Mastering Pro Guitar
Finish one of the final lessons "More Chord Holding and Arpeggiation" in the Pro Guitar trainer with one of the Pro Controllers.

Pro Guitar Apprentice 20
5 Star on Easy Pro Guitar (or 3 Star on a higher difficulty) any 25 Rock Band 3 songs.

Career > My Goals > Pro Guitar > Easy (or higher) Song Progress
Complete 25 RB3 on-disc songs using a Pro Guitar with a 5* rating on Easy Pro Guitar or 3* or more stars rating on Medium or higher. Just play and this one will be yours in no time. You can also sort your track list by difficulty while playing solo and work your way through the 25 easiest songs.

Hit at least 90% of the notes in 3 songs on Expert Pro Keys.

Career > My Goals > Pro Keys > General Pro Keys Goals
Complete 3 songs on EXPERT Pro Keys with the keyboard without missing too many notes. Sort by difficulty and work your way up the set list. Whip It, Before I Forget and Need You Tonight are all very easy songs.

Complete the final Pro Keys trainers.

Career > My Goals > Pro Keys > Lesson And Trainer Goals
Finish the final Pro Keys lesson in its entirety. The final few lessons require lots of 4-note chord switching, arpeggiation and keyboard solos. They are extremely tough and good luck to you!

Pro Keys to the Max 15
Max out your Score Multiplier meter on Pro Keys.

Career > My Goals > Pro Keys > General Pro Keys Goals
Reach a 4x multiplier then activate overdrive on the pro keys controller to get an 8x multiplier for the achievement.

Play a song with triple harmonies and get a triple awesome (hit all 3 harmony parts in a section). Rock Lobster can easily be done with one person on one microphone. Can be done on Easy.

Complete 6 songs on Hard or Expert vocals and hit at least 90% of the song. You can use songs with lots of talky parts for an easy achievement.

Best to perform a Custom /Clean install of XP. Never can tell what unwanted (like viruses) may exist.

1) Jot down the make and model of your computer.

2) Verify the 25 character product key is still affixed to the case or bottom of a laptop.

3) Go to the manufacture's support web site and find out which "F" key you need to press when booting the computer to initiate a reinstallation of XP (or use the Recovery Media if you have it).

4) Reinstall XP and create a new user account. Note: If using the "Recover" CD media you will need to reenter the product key to activate Windows.

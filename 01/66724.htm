Just to add to WPAK's suggestion on using the USB cable that came with a/your smartphone:

Some phone makers save costs by not connecting the data lines in their charging cables. So if you use such a charging cable, the controller will power on, but will not connect to the console, as there are no data lines to transmit the controller signals.

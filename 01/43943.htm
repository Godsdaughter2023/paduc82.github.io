Anyone with XBOX One X not getting 4K resolution from your 4K TV via Onkyo AVR that has two HDMI Out ports? The screen keep getting back to 1080p regardless what you do, then do this:-


1. Connect another HDMI OUT port (SUB) Non ARC to another HDMI IN (non ARC) port of your 4K TV.

2. On Onkyo remote, press Quick Menu and change HDMI Out from (MAIN+SUB) or MAIN to just SUB

Method 1: Enable Network Discovery and Configure Sharing Options in Windows 10

 

Step 1. Open Settings >Click Network and Internet.

Netsh winsock reset 

Netsh int ip reset

Ipconfig /release

Ipconfig /renew

Ipconfig /flushdns

 

Restart your computer once after executing the above commands.

 

Method 3: Verify Function Discovery Resource Publication serviceThis step has to be performed on all other devices.

 

1. Press Win + R to open the Run window and type the command “services.msc”. Press Enter to open the Windows Services window.

2. Locate the Function Discovery Resource Publication Service, right-click on the Service and select Properties.

3. Ensure that the Startup type of Function Discovery Resource Publication service is Automatic and that is started.

 

Method 4: Ensure that SMB 1.0 is manually enabled

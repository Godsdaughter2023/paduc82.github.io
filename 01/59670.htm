I suspect the problem has to do with the EXIF "orientation" tag.  In the brief research I've done, I've seen comments that complain that the native Windows 7 image viewers don't properly handle EXIF rotation information (and possibly corrupt it).  Perhaps Photo Viewer is interpreting the orientation tag one way and the pdf printers are interpreting it another way.

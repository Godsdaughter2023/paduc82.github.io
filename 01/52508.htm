    Hi VengefulOrange,

    I'm Stefano an Independent Advisor here to help you.
    Consolidation is the process that move files and folders to the top of the memory disk and defragment the files by filling the empty spaces without performing any optimization such as zoning.
    That's the definition of "Consolidation".
    Basically, the consolidation makes the folders and files physically close to disk, so that they are easier to read and write.

As what Patricia Yu said, if the computer was not shut down cleanly, a Kernel Power Event 41 message is generated. It may, or may not necessarily cause blue screen errors. Blue screen error is usually caused by hardware related concerns. Improper shutdown may not only affect the software, but also the other features in your laptop. The hard drive may be affected negatively by an improper shutdown. This is because the read and write heads will have been in the middle of a process, meaning that they will be rotated at high speeds when the computer shut down. Any hardware in use will also be affected. So a continuous instance of unexpected shutdown may cause your hardware to fail, thus, resulting in a blue screen error. Please make sure that you always turn off your laptop properly.

  • Add a new contact called "My Group" (or whatever)
  • In the Personal email box, list all the email addresses you want in the group separated by a comma (no spaces!!) - eg "*** Email address is removed for privacy ***,*** Email address is removed for privacy ***"
  • Save

 

To send an email to that group:

  • Start the People app, highlight My Group then click "Email Personal" in the right pane and choose "Mail", then OK.
  • The Mail App will open and (if you only have one account) a new email will appear.  If you have multiple accounts you need to select the account.

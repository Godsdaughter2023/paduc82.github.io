You can try toggling your ClearType settings in Windows.  From the run box (Windows Key + R) you can execute: cttune.exe to bring up this control.  Beyond that the issue is more than likely due to a graphics/hardware acceleration setting that is affecting the fonts that are displayed in the new Skype for Windows Desktop.

miguelonky say:
Since Skype joined to Microsoft time ago, you must has to close your microsoft account attached to Skype:

Go to the Microsoft account closure page (http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkId=523898). You may be prompted to sign in or verify your password.

In the Select a reason drop-down list, choose the reason you're closing the account.

Select Mark account for closure.

Your Microsoft account will be closed in 60 days. It wait 60 days before permanently deleting accounts in case you change your mind or need to access something before it's gone. You can change your mind any time during the 60 days, just sign in to cancel the closure.

* Reference:
https://support.skype.com/en/faq/FA142/can-i-us...

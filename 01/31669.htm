In essense:

1. Uninstall the current bluetooth driver (Add or remove programs > Lenovo Bluetooth with Enhanced Data Rate Software).

2. Install the latest Lenovo bluetooth driver from http://support.lenovo.com/us/en/downloads/ds031759

3. Reboot. (I also re-initiated pairing, by pressing the button on the back of the computer as well as the keyboard and mouse, just to be safe). After a short while (~20 seconds) up popped a window (still on the login screen) reconnecting the keyboard and mouse.

    Hi,

    As you have lost the Product Key from the Laptop, it is a preinstalled operating system and you will have to contact your Computer Manufacturer.

    They will not give you another Product Key.

     

    All you can now do is ask them to send you a set of Recovery disks for your Model Computer to reinstall the operating system back to how it was when new.

    Also while you are in contact with them, if you never receviced a reinstallation Disk at purchase, ask them how to make Recovery disks to do the same thing, if you still have access to the Recovery partition.

    Also ask them how to use particular Keys to reinstall.

First, finding a clandestine cargo job can be difficult as they are somewhat rare. To find them click on any airport from the main menu map, when the window opens to show you airport details, click on the "Jobs Board" button at the upper right.

Take off, and now is the hard part. You must fly below 1000 feet AGL (above Ground Level) to stay away from the cops, if you are detected, mission fails and you start over.

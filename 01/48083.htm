techfreak_ say:
When you take a snapshot in the latest versions of Skype (8.x/14.x), the snapshot is automatically saved to the cloud in the conversation. You do not have to share the screen for the participant to see it. Once the conversation ends, the snapshot still remains so that you or your participant can save the snapshot. The only person who can delete the snapshot from the conversation in the cloud, is the person who took it. If you go back to the snapshot you took during the conversation, you will be able to delete it. If the other participant took the snapshot, only that participant would be able to delete it. Since they cannot, this reaffirms you took the snapshot. Once you remove the snapshot, it will disappear from the conversation. You and your participant will no longer have the snapshot, unless either of you decided to save a copy outside of the application.

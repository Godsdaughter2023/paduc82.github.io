I have the same problem in an old Outlook 6.00.2900.5512 under a virtual Windows XP SP3.

When you move cursor up, one line at a time to figure out at what point does it start flicker again, it happens only when you leave body of original message and enter the reply area. At this point the scrollbar becomes bigger covering almost whole side of a message window and the flickering comes back again and CPU skyrockets and what's worse, if you keep clicking the Outlook becomes irresponsive and has to be closed by Ctrl+Alt+Del and Task Manager.

A workaround to that problem would be selecting the whole message after you hit reply and cutting it out. When you done replying, just paste it back.

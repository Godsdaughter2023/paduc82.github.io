Hi Cornstalker86,

Xbox One doesn't have notifications for friends signing in. With the friends list expanding to 1000, we chose not to turn these on because with large friends lists you'd have popups all the time. I'm on the console team and we're looking at the best way to add these in without it being overwhelming or annoying.

Right now the best way to keep tabs on what people you care about are are doing and when they go online/offline is to mark them as Favorites, then use the Activity app. It's a snap-only app that has two views:

1. Feed - its a mini version of the activity feed that shows up on the front page of the Friends app

2. Favorites - a real-time updated view of what all your favorite friends are doing *right now*. as people go on and offline, or change their game or app the list updates.

When I'm playing games I often say "Xbox, snap Activity" ...look at the favorites list or feed quick, then "Xbox, unsnap". It's really fast with your voice. Depending on the game, i'll also just leave Activity snapped, too.

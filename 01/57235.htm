Solution for no sound after win 10 upgrade. - Microsoft Community

ossiemac say:

Solution for no sound after win 10 upgrade.

There are many posts referring to no sound after win 10 upgrade. Thought that I should share this solution because it worked for me.

  1. Right click the windows Start icon (bottom left of screen)
  2. Select Device manager
  3. Expand the sound device (Might also include video and game controller and perhaps others)

Now the following procedure for each of the sound drivers in the expanded list

  1. Right click the driver
  2. Select Uninstall
  3. Check the box against "Delete the driver software for this device" (this is important to force the download of a new win 10 drivers during restart of computer because it did not work by just uninstalling.)
  4. Click OK

Restart the computer (ensure the computer is connected to the internet for the restart)

New sound drivers for win 10 should automatically download and install.

If the sound still does not work then go back to device manager and check to see if sound drivers have an icon indicating not working and if so then go to the manufactures site and down load the win 10 sound drivers and install.

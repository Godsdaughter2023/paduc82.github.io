malkeleah say: Your reply is ambiguous - I can't tell if you've tested other USB *devices* in the ports or if you've just tried the WD in other USB ports on the same computer. If the latter, I would say that the WD draws too much power for your computer to supply. This is especially common with laptops. If this is a WD which gives you the option to use an electrical adapter, try that. If not, then you should get a self-powered external hard drive. If no other USB devices work in your computer, then there is probably something wrong with the USB ports on the motherboard.

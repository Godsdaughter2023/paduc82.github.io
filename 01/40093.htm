Please go to https://account.microsoft.com/services/ and check your subscriptions. 

If the Gold membership shows a due amount of money, and the card for the Gold membership is cancelled, you need to add a new payment option first. 

Click on "Payment & Billing" and click on "Payment Options". There add your working credit card. 

Back on the Services page, you need to manage your Gold subscription. Change the payment method to the new working card, and pay the due amount if necessary. 

After the due amount is payed, you can cancel your Gold subscription. 

You can choose to cancel immediately, or cancel at the next renewal (so you are still golden for the paid amount of time). 

After the Gold membership has run out (either by cancelling immediately, or waiting after the cancelled renewal date), you can then remove your payment option.

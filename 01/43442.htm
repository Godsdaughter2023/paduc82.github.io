They are stored in separate places according to their type, all in folders under your username folder (just like always).  If you pick "This PC (C:)" from that drop down for Videos, the actual location used will be C:\Users\YourUsername\Videos (that's just too long to show in the drop down).

Pictures, Videos, Music, Documents, and much more are all stored separately.  You can open your username folder to see them all.

None of these are actually stored in the "roots" of the drives: it works the same as it's worked for decades.

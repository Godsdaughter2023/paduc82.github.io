cindymarier say:

Microsoft pen shortcuts not working. FIXED IT!!!

I could not get my pen shortcuts to work.  I tried everything and so reached out to Geek Squad.  After 5 HOURS of chatting with two different agents, the second agent finally fixed what should have taken a minute of time.  I'm going to tell you what she said to do.

Go to your Cortana search bar at the bottom left of your screen.  Type the word:  surface.  Press search.  The surface page will pop up.  Go to the tool bar on the left side of the screen.  Tap the "pen" icon.  Drag the slider bar to the right as far as possible toward "Heavy".  Then go back to your search bar and enter bluetooth.  Disconnect the pen.  Then click on Blue tooth.  While it is looking for things to connect, click and hold down your pen till the light on the pen comes on.  About 5 seconds.  Once your computer finds the pen, click on it to reconnect it.  Once it is reconnected, try to use the shortcuts.

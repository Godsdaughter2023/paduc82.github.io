    They are probably scrambled because Word can't read a PDF file. You need a PDF Reader such as Adobe or Foxit. Foxit is my personal preference.

    Adobe Reader
    http://get.adobe.com/reader/

    Foxit Reader
    http://www.foxitsoftware.com/pdf/reader/

    Once you have such a reader, you may need to create a file association.

      1.     Download and save the attachment on the desktop.
      2.     Right click on the saved file.
      3.     Select: Open With from the pop-up menu.
      4.     Click: Choose [default] Program.
      5.     Select: Adobe or Foxit Reader.
      6.     Check: Always use the selected program to open this kind of file.
      7.     Click OK.

    This should enable you to open these attachments directly from the e-mail now. If you still have problems, see this.

    Unable to open .PDF attachments from Windows Mail
    http://www.winhelponline.com/articles/236/1/Unable-to-open-PDF-attachments-from-Windows-Mail.html

First, the My Home Xbox feature is meant for use in one household where there's multiple people using the same Xbox or to share within a family in one house. Accessing an account you don't own and transferring my home Xbox licenses to your Xbox from an account you don't own are against the Xbox Code of Conduct and Terms of Use.

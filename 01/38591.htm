using Advanced Startup Options means using one of the available repair or recovery tools.

Continue

Continue is available on the main Choose an option screen and says Exit and continue to Windows 10... (or Windows 8.1/8).

When you choose Continue, Advanced Startup Options will close, your computer will restart, and Windows 10 or 8 will start in normal mode.

Obviously, if Windows isn't starting properly, the very fact that brought you to Advanced Startup Options, heading right back into Windows probably won't be helpful.

However, if you found yourself on the ASO menu some other way, or are done with some other repair or diagnostic process, Continue is the quickest way out of Advanced Startup Options and back into Windows.

canadian tech say:

That means it is over-heating and shutting itself down to protect it.  You need to find and fix the source of that heat.  Some possibilities:

  1. Fan not working
  2. Fan stuffed with dust
  3. Thermal paste between the CPU chip itself and the heat sink on top of it has dried out and needs to be replaced
  4. Using it on a soft surface and thereby blocking the vents on the bottom

Continuing use of the computer in this state will eventually cause catastrophic failure

It's because of a technical issue that was introduced when Microsoft changed payments from Microsoft Points to "real world currency". 

Since several years ago, you are unable to get Xbox 360 games on the Website (and later Xbox One, as the Xbox One uses the store website as backend) with prepaid money on your account.

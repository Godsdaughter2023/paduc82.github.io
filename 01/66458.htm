hairyfool say:

Having thought further on this there is another possible solution called Internet Connection Sharing. which  may be simpler. I haven't used this option for years (since home routers were introduced) but is still active.

 

Disable DHCP on the Dovado or use a crossover cable between the PC and the laptop.

 

Enable the Wireless on the PC and check that you have an internet connection. Disable Wireless on the Laptop. Connect the LAN ports of both the PC and Laptop (via router or crossover cable).

 

IN the PCs Network and Sharing Centre next to Connections you should have 2 options Ethernet and WiFi. Double click on WiFi then on the next screen click Properties.

 

The second tab on the next screen is Sharing, tick Allow other network users to to connect.... then OK.

 

Set the IP Adresses for the PC and Laptop as in my previous post but this time on the laptop set the default gateway to the IP Address of the Wired port of the PC, set the 1st DNS to the address of the Huawei and set the secondary to that suggested for your ISP.

 

This sets up your PC to have access to the internet via Wireless and allows the laptop to access the internet through the PC

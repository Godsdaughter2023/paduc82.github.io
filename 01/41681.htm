Well, it could be a couple of issues.

First of all, when did this start to happen?
Did you change/add or remove anything to your computer?

Is this a wired mouse, a wireless mouse, or a Bluetooth mouse?
Does the mouse work with a USB stick which acts as the transmitter?

I have had this issues happen to me as well!
The first thing I would do is to re-seat the battery.
Take it out and place it back again.
Try the mouse. If it still gives issues, try replacing the battery with a new one. Even if you replaced it already 5 minutes ago, replace it again with a new one. Gotta rule out the battery. Battery issues are responsible for 90% of mouse pointer issues.

Hope this helps somewhat. Remember, just swapping out the battery solves 90% of mice problems.

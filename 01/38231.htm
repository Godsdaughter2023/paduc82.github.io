I certainly understand your concern.

I would suggest you to try the following steps and check if that helps.

  1. Click the power icon on the system tray on the taskbar, and then click "More Power Options." A new window will launch.
  2. Click the "High Performance" radio button under "Hide additional plans," and then click the "Change Plan Settings" link. A new window will launch.
  3. Click "Never" in each of the categories next to the following drop-down boxes -- "Turn off display" and "Put the computer to sleep."
  4. Click "Save changes." Sleep mode is disabled.

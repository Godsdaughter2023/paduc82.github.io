frederik long say:

Wordpad behaves much like MS Word:

  • A paragraph is defined as a collection of words terminated by a carriage return (=Enter).
  • Line spacing applies on a per-paragraph basis. You cannot change it for part of a paragraph.
  • Carriage returns are entered by the user. Wordpad does not enter any carriage returns by itself.
  • In Word you can make your carriage returns visible. In Wordpad you can't. This makes it more difficult to determine the end of each paragraph.

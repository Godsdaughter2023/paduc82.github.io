Windows 10 login/password on start or wake

Microsoft pushed some updates a  couple days ago.  Since the update I have to log in with user ID and enter a password on restart and wake up.  I have gone to "netplwiz" and verified my account settings do not require this.  I tried to enabling the  login required(rebooted) then Disabled it (rebooted).  It works once and then I'm back to needing to login with user name and password.  How do I fix this issue.
tomdziura say:

Solution discovered.  It appears that since I do not shut down my PC very often I describe the situation incorrectly.   I was only getting prompted when waking up the system after it had gone sleep. 

To correct this:

Click on the windows icon lower left of screen.  Select Settings, then user accounts on the right.  On the left side of the window that appears select sign in options.  At the top of the page your sign in option is available.  I selected never as opposed to when PC wakes from sleep.

don cuthbert say:

Boot the computer from Windows 7 install media on USB or DVD.  There is usually a key to tap during power-up, which will allow you to choose what to boot from, or to enter BIOS/firmware to switch the boot order to DVD or USB before the hard disk.  What key to tap depends on the make of computer.

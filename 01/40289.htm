lemp say:

Method 1

  • Resize your browser so you can see the browser and your desktop in the same screen.
  • Left click the icon located to the left side of the address bar.
  • Continue to hold down the mouse button and drag the icon to your desktop.

Method 2

  • Right-click an empty spot on the Desktop and select New > Shortcut
  • Select and copy the complete address in the address bar of your browser.
  • Paste the web address in the "location" box of the new shortcut
  • Click Next

kathleencollins say:

Go to People window :

Open hotmail > under your name, click Options > More options > left side, click Contacts > here is your People window.

 

How to Edit contacts within a group

On the People window, top left, click the “ All v “ > you group is on the drop down list > click the group name > now you have all the contacts within that group listed > to edit, click at the contact’s name ( not its check box ) > click Edit at the toolbar to do editing......

Note : Edit option only appears at the toolbar after you have selected a contact.

¡firedog say:
Depends what you mean by Edit.
  • To add a contact to a group or to remove a contact from a group, select the contact in the left-hand column and click Groups on the toolbar. Tick the box for the group you want to add the contact to, or untick the box to remove the contact from a group.
    Use the checkboxes next to contact names to select multiple contacts at once to add to a group.
  • To Rename a group, click Manage > Manage groups. Click the group name to rename it.
The commands for managing contacts are not many. The functions available are explained in this post: Contacts are now People: What's new and different?

taidara say:

I suggest that you ask your roommate, politely, for permission, to access the Ubee and apply those changes.

An application on your console, can only diagnose and discover your NAT's behavior with the help of STUN (Session Traversal Utilities for NAT), which is implemented through the Xbox Live diagnostic test (Test Xbox Live Connection), or diagnosed through different game servers.

Note that the process to test the behavior of your NAT, makes no attempt to test every relevant facet. So, even if through a diagnose and discovery test, which shows your NAT's behavior as Open, it can drift from this state, to moderate.

deepw say:

Ok I have the RV511 and had the same issue.  The problem is the WiFi adapter.  if you look at your system settings you will find you have a Broadcom wifi card.  YOu have to take the back off the laptop and remove this card.  Some sites say to disable the card but that does not work you have to physically remove it.  FYI if you replace the card after upgrading from windows 7 to windows 10 your system will not work.  Buy another compatible WiFi card or get on to Broadcom or windows for an up to date driver but until then its WiFi or windows 10....

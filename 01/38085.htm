93 people found this helpful

svenm.se say:

Now I have found the problem. When I installed the app "HEVC Video Extension" from Microsoft Store, the other app “HEVC Video Extensions from Device Manufacturers” already was installed. So, I got two apps for the same purpose. When I found this I uninstalled the latter one and then all problems started. Installing/uninstalling the two apps i different combinations did not solve the problem.

Today I was desperate, took ownership of the folder C:\Program Files\WindowsApps and looked inside. There were two folders for HEVC Video Extensions. I uninstalled "HEVC Video Extension" the normal way, from Settings->Apps. After that there was still a folder "C:\Program Files\WindowsApps\Microsoft.HEVCVideoExtension . . . ". I renamed this folder, restarted the system and installed the app “HEVC Video Extensions from Device Manufacturers” from Microsoft Store. It is pure magic, now it works again without any problems!

HEVC Video Extensions from Manufacturers

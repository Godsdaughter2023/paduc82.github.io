a. user say:

Hi Paul,

 

Welcome to Microsoft Community and thank you for posting.

 

I understand the inconvenience that your computer installs Windows 10 update every day. We will help you with this issue.

 

I would like to inform that the update 3035583 is responsible to install Windows every day on your computer.

 

I suggest you to search for this update and uninstall it.

Steps:

  1. Type Control Panel in the Search box from the desktop and select Control Panel.

  2. Click on Programs and then, under Programs and Features, clicking View installed updates.

  3. Click the update (3035583) to remove, and then click Uninstall.

Note: Administrator permission required if you're prompted for an administrator password or confirmation, type the password or provide confirmation.

 

Hope this information helps. Reply to the post with updated status of the issue to assist you further.

Just an overview, there are two types of Skype names, the first one is what we called legacy. These accounts with legacy Skype names were created before Skype migrated to Microsoft.

In the earlier days of Skype, when you sign up for an account, you will have the option to choose your username or create your own. But due to the Microsoft migration and as an additional account security, this option has been removed.

Currently, if will sign up an account, the system will provide you a pre-define username which we call Live user ID. These ID's cannot be used to sign in to Skype unlike the Legacy ID's. But you can still use your Live ID as a reference if someone wants to add you in Skype.

Now, to check what is your username, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to our Problems signing in page.
  2. Enter your email address or phone number and select Continue.

    Important note: When entering your phone number, be sure to enter it with no spaces or dashes (example: 12223334444).
  3. We'll show you all the Skype accounts associated with that email address or phone number.

If you're signing in to Skype using your Microsoft account, please refer to these steps:

  • Sign in to Skype website.
  • Scroll down and click Purchase history.
  • In the upper left corner of the page, you will see your Live ID.

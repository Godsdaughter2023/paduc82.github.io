For those interested in DIY with Network Adaptor shortcuts, it's this simple.

1. Open Network and Sharing Centre.

2. Open 'Change adapter settings'

3. Right click the Ethernet icon and choose create shortcut from the dropdown menu.

4. The shortcut cannot be created there and you will be offered to create it on the desktop instead.

5. Once the shortcut is on the desktop; right clicking on it will allow you to Enable or Disable

    alternately. Et voila.

I hope this helps anyone seeking a solution to matters of this nature. It just helps with the workflow by allowing you to Enable and Disable you Ethernet Adapter without having to go through the Network and Sharing Centre each and every time. Alternatively ... Just switch off you modem; that works too!

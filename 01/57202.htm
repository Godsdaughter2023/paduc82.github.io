Which is the best way to align to faces or edges on 3D Builder... I noticed that this tool is not available, at least out of the box. What can be activated are the collisions, but it doesn't align the object faces, just help to move them around . There is any other way?

cbgrant [msft] say:

Snapping is your easiest way to align faces or edges. Once you have launched 3D Builder and open an object click on the hamburger menu in the top left. Then at the bottom of the menu that appears click on "Settings". Under settings there should be a toggle titled "Snapping". Make sure it is turned on.

Snapping allows objects when moved slowly close to other objects to snap to each other. This should help with your aligning.

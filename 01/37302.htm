Get GParted.

Hi,

You would have to download Ymir Entertainment Metin2 or Metin2 File Extractor to open the file.  However, this does not make sense as the extension is for a gaming file.

According to OpenTheFile,

“EPK is a data package format used by Metin2, an eastern fantasy-based Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game.  It may contain multiple types of data, including 3D objects, textures, sounds, scripts, and level data loaded by the game.”Source: http://www.openthefile.net/extension/epk

  1. Go to where the .EPK file is downloaded and right-click.
  2. Press copy.
  3. Now, connect your USB if it isn't already connected.
  4. Go to your USB (Start> Computer> USB NAME).
  5. Right-click and press paste.
  6. It is now on your USB.
  7. Disconnect your USB (Start> Computer> Right click on USB Name> Eject).

If you are still unable to open the file, you could try to contact them and explain that you cannot open the file.

This may be caused from the Power Save feature on most Laptops.

The Processor operates at the maximum rated speed with the AC Power supply plugged in; once you disconnect the power supply the processor reduces its speed to approximately half the rated speed.

 

You set your computer for Hibernation and it is on the power saver option; it will likely come out of hibernation when the power supply is reestablished - the hibernation senses the change and wakes up the computer for use.

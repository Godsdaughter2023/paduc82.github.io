You're most likely specifying the drive and/or partition numbers incorrectly. For -rawdisk use \\.\PhysicalDrive# where # can be found via Disk Management in Windows. For -partitions section 9.9.1.2 of the manual states:

The numbers for the list of partitions can be taken from the output of

VBoxManage internalcommands listpartitions -rawdisk /dev/sda

Of course you'll need to use \\.\PhysicalDrive# instead of /dev/sda in the command above.

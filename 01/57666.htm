slance310 say:

Hi,

You are getting that message because of either your selected drive is formatted incorrectly, or you are installing from the wrong type of media...

It is saying your machine uses UEFI Bios which requires that the OS drive be formatted as a GPT Disk... You may have reformatted it as a MBR...

The most likely reason is the installation media... Most UEFI machines won't install into UEFI Bios with a DVD, or a NTFS Formatted Flash Drive, like the one Win 7 USB\DVD Tool would make...

It is possible that the flash drive created with the "Create Media" website won't create the proper one either... At one time it would not, but that may have been corrected now...

You can create a proper bootable Flash Drive with a 3rd party tool called RUFUS and a Win 8.1 ISO Image... You would want your options to look like this, with the exception that your device\file name will be different...

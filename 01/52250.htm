Initially when you create a Microsoft account, that account will be used to sign in to your computer. So, that account will be set as a primary account. By signing in to the primary account on your computer, you will not be able to remove it. You need to sign in to a new account and then try to remove the primary account.

 

To remove the primary account and add a new account as a primary one. Open Mail app (go to search window and type mail app) and click on Settings option within mail app > select Manage your accounts > you will be able to view all your user accounts that are created on your computer > double click on the primary account and select Delete this account. By doing that, it will remove the account from the application.

 

Also, the Settings under Email & app accounts will contain all your accounts which were created which would not affect your new account.

 

Hope it helps.

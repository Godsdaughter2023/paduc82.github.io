I just dialed a non-Microsoft toll-free number and was able to go through the prompts of an IVR without issue. There is not a singular solution for such problems as they are based off many variables and can occur using any service, especially VoIP. Using a non VoIP service increases the chances of the problem not occurring as the audio is not compressed, is in a more original format, and you generally pay a higher premium which may have a positive effect on the overall line quality. Variables that can randomly create this issue:

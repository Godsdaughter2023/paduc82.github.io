t-richards say: I can't tell you whether or not these drivers allow digital output to work correctly, but here's what worked for me (mostly, anyway):

Since the Montego DDL uses the CMedia CMI8738 chipset, download this set of drivers: CMedia CMI8738-MX Audio Driver 1.04 for Windows 8 - Softpedia.
I used the external mirror. The Softpedia secure download mirror appeared to be corrupted.

  1. Once the download finishes, unzip it.
  2. Skip the setup.exe and head straight to Device Manager (Win+Pause/Break -> Device Manager).
  3. Right click your audio device and choose Update Driver Software.
  4. Select Browse my computer for driver software, then Browse... and then navigate to the directory where you unzipped the files. (You might even care to navigate all the way down to WIN8\SoftwareDriver\driver)
  5. Click next, accept the manufacturer and you're done!

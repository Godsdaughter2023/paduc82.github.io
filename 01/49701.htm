After launching Windows 7 service pack 1 installation (KB976932) I got an error message "Access is denied" with "Error" in the window title.
Usually on C: (that is my case, too, but the drive used is the boot drive or the system drive depending on the update) there is a new directory created. Installation is interrupted with a meaningless message.

Use an account that does NOT belong to the "guests" group (ex. remove your account from that group, logout and login again as it is required for the permissions change to take effect) and restart the SP1 installation.

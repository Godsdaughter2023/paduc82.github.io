Does Surface Pro support USB through DisplayPort?

james vinyard say:

Well, I checked everywhere since you asked about using a video port as a USB port and the answer to that would be "no".  A USB port, however, can.  Reason being is the port has to have bidirectional signal capability and video ports are unidirectional.

 

If you do a complete web search for "HDMI to USB" you will find vice versa (USB to HDMI).

 

The version of your port number is in your device driver settings in the Control Panel under "Device Manager".  The technical specifications on the Pro model are sparse on the Microsoft web site and do not list version number.  Other people have mentioned that they have used an Apple mini-port display adapter with theirs and worked so you may not be limited to just the Microsoft version of the mini-port adapter.

 

In either case, a docking station is what you need for what you want to do.

a. Go to the Language control panel again, click "Advanced settings" there.
b. In Advanced settings, under "Switching input methods", check the box next to "Use the desktop language bar when it's available", and then click the Save button.

Back on the desktop Taskbar, at the right end of the language area you'll find the Tool Menu. On the top, you’ll see the IME Pad. Select that, and the floating IME Pad will appear for your use in handwriting, stroke, radical, or symbols input.

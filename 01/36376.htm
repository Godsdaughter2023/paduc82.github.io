Hello Dan,

Thank you for further concerns on the issue.

Activation helps verify that Windows on your computer is genuine and hasn't been used on more computers than the Microsoft Software License Terms allow. In this way, activation helps prevent software counterfeiting. Thus, there are many system files that are responsible for carrying out the activation operation.

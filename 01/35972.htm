Removing the Xbox App from Windows 10 - Microsoft Community

nathan roberts (sunknath) say:

Removing the Xbox App from Windows 10

Article applies to: Windows 10

Introduction

The Xbox App comes installed with Windows 10. Although this is good for gamers like me. Other folk who use their computers for work or personal use aren't interested in Xbox or the App. Some even see this as a waste of space. While, with other apps, you can right click and select uninstall. The Xbox App is embedded into the Windows files, it can't be uninstalled easily. 

This article will guide you though how to uninstall the Xbox App from Windows 10. 

Main Content

You will first need to right click on the start icon on your Taskbar. This will bring a menu up that you can use. 

Select 'Windows PowerShell (Admin)' from the list. You may need to enter your username and password before continuing. Once you have entered the username and password - if you needed to - you will get the following window.

You will then need to write the following in the window:

Get-AppxPackage Microsoft.XboxApp | Remove-AppxPackage

This will remove all packages for the Xbox App. Thus, it will not be on your system.

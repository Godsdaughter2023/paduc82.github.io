can i install programs on the secondary drive? - Microsoft Community

kenkwk say:

can i install programs on the secondary drive?

can i install programs on the secondary drive?
andre da costa say:

Yes, you can, but its really not recommended for desktop programs, but nothing preventing you from doing so. Its just that, when a future revision of Windows 10 comes out, you might need to remove them in order to facilitate the upgrade.

As for modern, universal applications downloaded from the Windows Store, you can move those to a difference drive.

Click Start > Settings > System > Apps & Features > select the app then click Move then choose the other partition.

If you are running the Windows 10 Creators Update click Start > Settings > Apps > Apps & Features > select the app then click Move then choose the other partition.

My earlier posting is still correct.  The fact that your computer finds your network, tells me that the network is there and the wireless device in the computer can see it. 

The fact that there is no Internet connection provided means that the wireless device in your computer is unable to receive an IP address assignment from your wireless network. An IP address assignment is required to gain access to the Internet.

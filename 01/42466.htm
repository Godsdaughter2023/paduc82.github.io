davem121 say:
Hi Andre, Windows is meant to run StartComponentCleanup to keep that folder under control . . .

You can manually run that process . . .

Click your Start Button, type cmd, then from the resulting list, right click Command Prompt and choose 'Run as Administrator'

schtasks.exe /Run /TN "\Microsoft\Windows\Servicing\StartComponentCleanup"

Please note: this command can take time to complete . . .

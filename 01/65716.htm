    rwb1012 say:

    I'm guessing it has changed to support WSL.

    Here's how to check...

    Regedit

    HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\services\Tcpip\Parameters\Interfaces\guid_of_your_network_interface\DhcpInterfaceOptions

    Read through the bytes until you find 119 (hex 77) followed by seven zeroes. The next byte is the length of the option 119 information.

    If it's not there then the option isn't used.

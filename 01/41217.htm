Hi Jerly,

 

If Windows Media player stops playing after the screen turns off, then most probably the computer has entered sleep mode or hibernation mode. To avoid this, I would suggest you to change the idle time after which Windows enters sleep/ hibernation or turn off sleep/hibernation. Refer to these steps:

c)       Click on change advanced power settings.

d)      In the advanced settings window, expand the sleep section and set the timer for the ‘sleep after’ and the ‘hibernate after’ options as desired.

e)      Save changes.

 

Try these steps and let us know the results. We’d be happy to help if you need any further assistance.

Not sure if you found an answer to your question.  I just picked up a Surface Book yesterday and have started surfing the web looking for a car adapter.

Surface Book power connector is compatible with the Surface Pro 3 and Surface Pro 4.  There are numerous adapters available for the Pro 3.  Mostly it look like sellers haven't updated their descriptions to include the newer Pro 4 and Book.  However I did find one seller who did include Pro 3, Pro 4, and Book in his description.

What I've been doing is looking at Pro 3 car adapters, then reading customers comments to see if anyone has used it with the Book.  I found one adapter where a custom was talking about having success using it with the Pro 4.

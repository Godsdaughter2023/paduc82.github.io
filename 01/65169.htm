Hello,

The Untrusted Certificates section acts as a reference blacklist so as not to allow these certificates if you come across them during browsing. This serves as a list of harmful certificates that could, later on, affect your system.

The certificate listing is a list Windows uses to prevent potential harm to your computer. This list is sometimes updated in one of our periodic Windows updates called Root Certificates, as it is important to manage root certificates to avoid MITM attacks.

Without something like the Untrusted Certificates, all safe or harmful certificates would be classed as trusted and would sail through your system which could cause a lot of issues in the long run.

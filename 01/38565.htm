Somebody may be interested in this. I finally got skypeforlinux working as user.

user). I looked at the permissions (and content) of:

~/.config/skypeforlinux/logs/skype-startup.log

/usr/share/skypeforlinux

Everything was owned by root, apparently because of previous runs of

skypeforlinux as superuser. I did:

sudo chown  XXX:XXX .config/skypeforlinux/logs/*
sudo chown -R  XXX:jXXX  /usr/share/skypeforlinux/

where XXX is my username. I am not sure if this is the right and safe way

but skypeforlinux works for me now without sudo.

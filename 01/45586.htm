  1. Practice with Bitlocker on a USB flash disk until your are completely at ease with the concept.
  2. Back up your hard disk.
  3. Perform a few spot checks on a different machine to ensure that you can read the backed up data.
  4. Follow all the recommendations relating to backing up your certificates

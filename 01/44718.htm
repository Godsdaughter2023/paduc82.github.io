In Windows 10, Sleep mode is usually defined as low-power state in which system display is turned off and running programs are saved into memory. Usually, by default, your Windows 10 will go to sleep mode after 5 minutes of inactivity when it on battery. The same thing happens in 10 minutes, when your system is on AC power.

Hi Starmann,

1/ Click the download option on the file/application you want to download

2/ A yellow security warning appears at the top of Internet explorer telling you that the download is blocked and to click on the link for further options.

3/ Click on the yellow security link and select 'Download File'

4/ A file download security warning window opens.

5/ Click the Save button

6/ A 'Save As' window now opens.

7/ In the 'Save As' window click the 'desktop' icon on the left to save your file/application to your desktop. Alternatively, click the 'down arrow' to the right of the 'save as' dialogue box at the top of the window and select 'desktop'.

Unless you change it the next time you download a file/application you will find that 'all' downloads are downloaded to your desktop now instead of the default My Documents or Downloads folder.

Error code 43 denotes that one of the drivers controlling the device notified the Operating System that the device failed in some manner.

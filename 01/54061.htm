A follow up on this report.  Heat was an issue, but it was not causing the PC's to freeze.  The random freezes were caused by conflicts between two firewalls.  I was using PC Tools Firewall Plus, and then installed ESET's Smart Security.  At random intervals, the two firewalls conflicted and locked the system.  A reboot restored it until the next conflict.   This only happened on my Win7 PC's, not on my WXP SP2 PC. 

Uninstalling Firewall Plus solved the problem.

  • Details: The built-in administrator account that comes with your computer should be disabled at all times, and only used for emergency situations, like restoring your computer or resetting some other user's password.

  5. Exit Control Panel and Restart the computer.
  6. Log on with the account you changed to an Administrator account.  It should now have full access to the computer.

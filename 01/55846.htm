Moving your SSD to completely new hardware is possible, but may be riddled with issues along the way.

Firstly, your depending on your Windows 10 license type, your Windows may not activate on the new hardware. OEM licenses are tied to the motherboard and a motherboard change means a new license is needed. If it's a retail license, entering the key that came with your purchase should work. I definitely recommend backing up your files and reinstalling Windows onto the SSD in the new PC. This will ensure your hardware drivers are all correct and you won't have any issues.

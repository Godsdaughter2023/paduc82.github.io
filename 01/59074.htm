keith a. miller say:

In the registry, you need to delete the value named:

{F42EE2D3-909F-4907-8871-4C22FC0BF756}

Found under:

HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\User Shell Folders

You can do this manually by running regedit, navigating to the above key, and then deleting the above value.

Or you can launch PowerShell (WinKey+X) and copy and paste the following command:

Remove-ItemProperty -Path 'HKCU:\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\User Shell Folders' -Name '{F42EE2D3-909F-4907-8871-4C22FC0BF756}' 

Sign out & sign back in for the change to take effect.

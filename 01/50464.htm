martmcd say:
Hi Muskiet,
unintentional waking from sleep is more often caused by the NIC responding to an "are you still there" request from the router or other network device.
right click on start and select device manager
expand network adapters
right click on your network adapter and select properties
click on the power management tab
if "all this device to wake the computer" is ticked, either untick it or tick "only allow a magic packet to wake the computer"

also disable wake timers

settings -> system -> power & Sleep
click "additional power settings"
click on change plan settings
click on change advanced power settings
expand sleep
expand wake timers and select disable
click ok

if it still wakes up
open a command prompt and type powercfg /lastwake press enter
let us know the response

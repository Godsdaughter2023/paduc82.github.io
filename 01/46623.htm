arya s asok say: Hi,

1. Did you make any changes on the computer prior to the issue?
2. What happens when you try to use Lifechat LX-3000 headset?
3. Do you receive any error message?
4. What is the make and model of the laptop?

Method 1:
You may plug-in the USB headset to any other USB port and try the steps in the link and check.
Enable sounds in LifeChat headset
http://support.microsoft.com/kb/935749


Method 2:
If you face any issue furthur then you may also remove and reinstall Lifechat device and check:
a. Go to Start and go to Control Panel (Classic View)  and select System icon, and click Device Manager on the left.
b. Expand the Universal Serial Bus controllers
c. See if the first or second entry is "Lifechat Lx-3000 Microphone ".
d. Right-click on Lifechat Lx-3000 Microphone and select Uninstall.
e. Unplug and plug the Lifechat Lx-3000 Microphone onto the computer.
f. In the Device Manager, click the Scan for hardware changes button on the toolbar and let it scan your computer.
g. When the system finds the new device, it will request to install the right driver.
You may also update the audio drivers and check..
http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows7/Update-a-driver-for-hardware-that-isnt-working-properly

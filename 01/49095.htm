james vinyard say:

No, no limit.  The controller can handle a drive up to 2.3TB (without a BIOS patch) and with a patch, you can go far beyond that.

Both Apple and Microsoft are slow in getting the boot kernel up-to-speed to support higher capacity drives but the onboard SATA controller can handle it.

Before doing anything, though, go to http://www.ifixit.com and do a search for "Surface Pro 2" and look for the teardown guide.  They also sell toolkits and a few spare parts as well.

You may find that taking the machine apart will not be an easy task!

I have not seen any SSD drives beyond the 1TB capacity and even that one is extremely rare to find, so the current models should have no problem.

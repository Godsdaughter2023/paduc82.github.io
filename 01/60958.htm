Disable energy saving mode in Windows 10

  1. Click Start
  2. Type Power Option and press enter to open it
  3. In Power Option window, on right panel under "Choose or Customize a power pan" --> Selected Plan there is a Balanced.
  4. Then click on Change plan settings
  6. This will open Edit plan Setting window
  7. Make Turn off the display to NEVER in both Battery & Plugged In
  8. Make Put the computer to sleep to NEVER in both Battery & Plugged In
    10. <

    Note:

    1. Battery: It will do accordingly when system is on Battery Mode(Charger is not Plugged)

    2. Plugged In: It will do accordingly when system is on Plugged In to wall(Charger is Plugged In  )

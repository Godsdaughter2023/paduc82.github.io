It looks like your laptop cannot do a recovery, because there is too much memory in your disk to handle. From here it looks like all you need to do is clean up some disk space.

To clean up some disk space go to your charms at the right side of the screen (drag your mouse onto the right of the screen) click "Search" type in "Disk Clean-up" click "Disk Cleanup by deleting unnecessary files" then you will come to the window "Disk Clean-up for (disk name)" then check all the files you don't want by checking the squares next to the file names. As we did before go to the right of the screen and press "Search" and then type in "Defrag" now click on "Defragment and Optimise Drives" next it should open the window "Optimise Drives" now click on your main drive, click "Analyse" wait for it to analyse, now when the analyse is done click "Optimise" then it should take a few minutes from there.

Additonal Step:

Again go to the left of the screen and click "Search" and type in "Add remove programs" and click "Add or remove programs" now from there just uninstall any programs that you don't need by right clicking on a program and clicking "Uninstall"

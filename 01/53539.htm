You can definitely import photos and videos from your Samsung S2 phone to your Windows 10 device. Here's how to do it:

If you have a Microsoft account you have (at least) 5 GB of storage space on OneDrive. If you have a Google account you have (at least) 15 GB of storage in your Google Drive. If you have an iPhone you have an amount - I don't know how much - of free storage space in iCloud

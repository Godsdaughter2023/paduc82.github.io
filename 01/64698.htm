ran mo msft support say:

Hi JP_984,

 

Thanks so much for the information. In the fiddler log, I did not find the correct autodiscover queries performed. So the issue could possibly be related to the Autodiscover service settings as Lync client use Autodiscover to locate the EWS endpoint then get the information via the EWS endpoint.

 

Meanwhile, according to the test result in “Detect Autodiscover.html”, the autodiscover test was indeed failed. I can see that your SSL certificate include the incorrect name. It now contains xxx.net namespace. But in required Hybrid certificate situation, the SSL certificate should include the namespace “autodiscover.xxx.net” or “*.xxx.net”. Then, the migrated mailboxes will be redirected to autodiscover.outlook.com namespace successfully. This will allow Lync or Skype for business client to locate the Office 365’s EWS endpoint “outlook.office365.com/ews/exchange/wssecurity” which allow them to get conversation history from their mailboxes.

 

So, please update your SSL certificate subject name like what I mentioned above and re-run HCW. After that, check if Autodiscover works. If so, the EWS should work for Lync or Skype for business clients.

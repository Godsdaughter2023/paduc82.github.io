    andre da costa say:
    This is normal. AMD developed the x86-x64 instruction set which was launched in 2003 as the AMD64 processor. Microsoft actually worked with AMD in the early days to create the AMD64 processor. With this in mind, the labeling of cumulative updates that target 64 bit processors will carry the AMD designation/copyright. Intel actually licensed AMD's 64 bit technology to use in their Intel 64 processors introduced in 2005.

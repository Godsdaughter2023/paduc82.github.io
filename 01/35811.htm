Windows 10 free upgrade does not activate by product key and will have no unique product key after activating.  It activates by upgrade from a qualifying, activated Windows 7 or 8 OS.

  • you have to choose to keep Windows settings and files

robotsquid say:

Fixed this. Turns out the WLAN AutoConfig service was stopped.

For anyone with the same problem:

  1. Press Windows Key+R, type "services.msc" and press Enter
  2. Find the service "WLAN AutoConfig"
  3. Right click, go to Properties and change Startup Type to Automatic

Problem should be solved

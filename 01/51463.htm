wunders say:

You really need to find out what is taking up all of your disk space and deal with that.  Adding more disk space will simply allow whatever potential malware program you may have been infected with more space to use up.

Try downloading a disk space analyzer and find out exactly where your disk space is being used and free it back up.  Two freeware programs that I use are "SpaceSniffer" and "WinDirStat".

SpaceSniffer:  http://www.uderzo.it/main_products/space_sniffer/

WinDirStat:    https://windirstat.info/

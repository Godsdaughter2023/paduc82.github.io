Hi

In general (I am not deeply familiar with iPad)

UltraVNC is the richest in features and the most flexible Remote Control applications.

However, it is little harder to set than Windows Remote Desktop (RDT).

If your version of Windows does not support Hosting RDT, or you need a feature that is not provide by RDT, then UltraVNC is the answer.


RDT and UltraVNC are both private by using very strong Encryption and connecting directly computer to computer (locally, and over the Internet) with No in between 3rd party Server.

Hi,

Please provide the information as to which updates are being prompted to your Windows under Windows Updates. Then you may check if those updates are ment for your Windows or not from http://support.microsoft.com.

For the moment you may try the following steps:

  1. Open the services Windows from the control panel (Control Panerl-->Administrative Tools)
  2. Stop the Background Intelligent Transfer Service, Cryptographic Service, Windows Module Installer and the Windows Updates services.
  3. Rename the downloader to downloader.old from c:\programs Data\microsoft\network
  4. Rename the SoftwareDistribution to SoftwareDistribution.old from c:\Windows
  5. Rename the Catroot2 to Catroot2.old from c:\Windows\System32
  6. Restart the Background Intelligent Transfer Service, Cryptographic Service, Windows Module Installer and the Windows Updates services again.
  7. Check for the updates.

You may also try to run the FixIt from http://support.microsoft.com/kb/971058/en-us.

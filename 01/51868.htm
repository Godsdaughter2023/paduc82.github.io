Basic setup with an audio/video receiver

With an audio/video (A/V) receiver, the best and most direct option is to connect the devices sequentially:

1.Connect your set-top box HDMI output to the Xbox One HDMI input.

To access it, you will need to boot into when you start your computer by pressing a function key. This can be either F1, F2, F9, F10, F11, F12 or even DEL or Tab key. - Samsung uses ESC on most of its systems

- HP uses F9 on most of its systems

- Fujitsu uses F12 on most of its systems

- Acer uses F12 on most of its systems, but you often need to go into the BIOS to enable it

- Dell uses F12

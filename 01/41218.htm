Hi,

Having an incompatible mouse driver could be a possible cause why you are experiencing an issue with the performance of your mouse. To address this concern, we suggest running the automated troubleshooter for devices and drivers. This tool can scan and repair damaged drivers of the connected peripherals on your computer. Visit this link, click Find and fix problems with devices and hardware and then follow the prompts to successfully run the tool.

If the same issue persists, we suggest adjusting the speed of your mouse pointer. To do so, follow these steps:

  1. Using Search, type Mouse settings.
  2. From the search results, click on Mouse settings (System settings).
  3. Click on Additional mouse options.
  4. Click on the Pointer Options tab.
  5. Under Motion, move the slider to Fast.
  6. Under Visibility, make sure that the checkbox beside the Display pointer trails option is unchecked.
  7. Click Apply and then click OK.

Update us about the result.

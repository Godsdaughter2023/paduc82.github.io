considerate_guy say:

You can save your movie in a format more conducive to being emailed.

From the first File menu (Alt+F), choose Save movie

In the list of output formats, choose For email

The email format saves your video in very low-resolution as 426 x 240 with a bitrate of 1.6 Mb/s, to try and reduce the file size. That's because most email systems limit how big a file you can send. You can then attach the resulting .mp4 file to an email for your recipient.

A better way to send video (since it is so huge) is to instead upload it on a sharing site (OneDrive, YouTube, Vimeo, Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) and share a *link* to the file, for your recipient to play and/or download.

    wayneconfidential say: Actually, that isn't the only fix nor is it the best option in my opinion.
    Try this:
    Open PAINT, then click the BLUE drop down box in the top left hand corner of the screen (this is the box that gives the drop down options for saving a file), now click SAVE AS and a selection of save as formats including JPEG will appear to the right, move your cursor over the JPEG option and RIGHT CLICK (not left click), three options will be presented, click (select) the top option which says ADD TO QUICK ACCESS FOLDER.

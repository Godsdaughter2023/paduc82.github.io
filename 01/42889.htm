Xbox Live purchasing in India - Microsoft Community

suman maradani say:

Xbox Live purchasing in India

Xbox Live purchasing just doesn't work in India.

The only payment options you can add in India are VISA and MasterCard credit cards.  Since August 1, 2009, the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), which is the regulatory body for all banks in India mandated that all credit cards issued by Indian banks use additional authentication for online transactions and not just information available on the card like CVV, expiry date etc.  All banks in India since then have either adopted 'Verified by Visa' for VISA or 'SecureCode' for MasterCard credit cards.  So, in summary, since that date, you cannot use a credit card issued by an Indian bank without getting redirected to the issuing bank to provide additional authentication for _any_ online transaction.  (You'll see information regarding this change on all major Indian e-commerce portals that accept credit cards)

Xbox Live in India doesn't support both 'Verified by Visa' and 'SecureCode'.  You'd see arcane messages like the following when you go through the Xbox Live portal:

"We can't authorize the payment method. Please make sure the information is correct, or use another payment method. If you continue to get this message, please contact your financial institution."

"Your payment instrument could not be authorized. Please contact your provider, or try a different payment option."

Few additional points to note:

  • Purchasing Microsoft Points at a retail store isn't really a viable option - most stores here just don't sell these cards.  Some sell the Xbox Live GOLD membership cards, but Points - very rare
  • Microsoft Points are region locked - so it is not like you can buy them on Amazon or other places and use them here

Question to Microsoft: Do you folks have plans to fix this anytime soon?  It's been over 3+ years.

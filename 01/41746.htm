Adobe Flash videos play OK.

wiscanoer say:

Joy:

The article on setting up HTML5 videos didn't help, but after much searching I did find a solution.   The problem was caused by the IDT High Definition Audio Driver.    Windows 10 says this driver is the best for my device, but that is not the case.   On the HP forums, many people have mentioned problems with this driver, although most of the reported problems are sound related rather than repeating video.   I'm not clear why the audio driver would cause video to repeat, but I'm not going to argue with success.

Steps to fix where:

1. In Device Manager, select Sound, Video, and Game Controller, then right click IDT High Definition Audio CODEC.

2. Select Update Driver

3. Select  -> Browse my computer for driver software.

4. Select -> Let me pickup a list of device drivers on my computer.

5. Select  High Definition Audio Device (Not  IDT High Def...  this is important.) and click Next.

    Appears Warning message, but reply Yes.

6. ! Windows has successfully update your driver software.

Key is step 5.

To answer your first question, there is a sensor in the console that detects it being laid flat or upright. Sometimes, I have found, if you unplug the console from everything and remove any discs inside, rotate the console and give it a gentle shake. This has worked for me in the past.

 

 

Secondly, try clearing the memory device cache by selecting it under system, storage and hover over your device the FIFA game save is on and press Y to clear cache. If you're still having issues, delete your profile and recover it back to your storage device. Hope this helps!

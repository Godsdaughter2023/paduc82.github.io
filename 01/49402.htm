dantesparda504 say:

It's a linear third person shooter that relies heavily on bullet time. It's definitely not a sand box game.

.

The biggest thing about the game is the story. All three games tell a fantastic story about a man that's not exactly your ordinary hero. He is a pill addict and an alcoholic, he is constantly depressed because of the death of his wife and daughter. It has fantastic narrative and it's a fantastic story telling experience.

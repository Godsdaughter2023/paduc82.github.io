Burn speed changes how fast your disc is burned and will not affect the end product. Burning speed depends on how fast your burner is. I'd take a look at the power settings. In my case it was trying to save power.

  1. Go to “control panel”.
  2. Click on “device and printers”.
  3. Right click on the Xbox Controller and click on “properties”.
  4. Under “hardware tab” click on the keyboard device and go to “properties”.
  5. Then under “general” tab click on “change settings”.
  6. Click on “power management” tab and uncheck the box “allow the computer to turn off this device to save power”.
  7. Then click on “ok” and restart the computer.

If that doesn't work...

  1. Go to “control panel”.
  2. Open "Power Options"
  3. Clink on "change plan settings" for the plan you're currently using
  4. Change advanced settings
  5. Look for your controller and adjust settings as described above.

Hope this helps!

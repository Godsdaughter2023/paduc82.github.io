The partitioning shouldn't matter because you could do a complete backup of only one partition at a time so the complete backup doesn't really know about any other partitions than the one it did a complete backup for (presumably your C:\ drive).  Even if some of the boot records are wrong because there were multiple partitions before the restore process is not going to recognize this - it's just going to restore what was backed up whether it is currently right or wrong (as long as the partition you're restoring to is at least as big as the one the backup came from and they are both formatted the same way which I assume is NTFS).

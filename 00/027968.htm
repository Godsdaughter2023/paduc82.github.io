vijay a. verma say:

1. Let's say that F1 has you values R,Y,A,G,B or whatever is your case.

2. Let's say that your circle is in A1.

3. Put following formula in A1

=IF($F$1="R","n",IF($F$1="Y"," "&"n"&" ",IF($F$1="A","  "&"n"&"  ",IF($F$1="G","   "&"n"&"   ",IF($F$1="B","    "&"n"&"    ",)))))

Depending upon your numbers, you can increase/reduce your IF conditions (same thing you can achieve through LOOKUPS / CHOOSE also)

4. Choose Webdings Font (Don't choose Wingdings, it is Webdings).

5. Middle Align this cell A1.

6. Put CF formula for R by choosing "Format only cells that contain" and format the Font in Bold and Red Colour. (Don't format by Fill but by Font)

Do, the same for Y, A, G and B also by putting in following formulas

Y: =" "&"n"&" "

A: ="  "&"n"&"  "

G: ="   "&"n"&"   "

B: ="    "&"n"&"    "

Now, you have what you want.

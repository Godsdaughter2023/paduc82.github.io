Hello janbailey:

 

Thank you for posting to the forums regarding the issue you are having syncing through the Outlook Hotmail connector.  I just wanted to chime in with a couple of things. 

 

First, you will not see a sync from your phone to your local Outlook account.  The Outlook Hotmail connector is a one way sync.  Content from Outlook will be synced to your Microsoft account and subsequently synced to the phone.  The connector does not take information added to the phone and route that through the Microsoft account to your local Outlook account. 

 

I am not sure it has been specified where you receive this error code and message.  If you are receiving this in Outlook you will certainly want to seek additional support for the Office support via the forums or http://support.microsoft.com/contactus/?ws=support.

Add logo to Outlook.com signature - Microsoft Community

chrisjones_757 say:

Add logo to Outlook.com signature

Hi, I'm trying to add a logo to my signature on outlook.com.  Does anyone know how I do this?  Thanx

ron sommer mvp 2009-2017 say:

I have edited this for an outlook.com signature.

From Windows Live Celeste G

To create a personal e-mail signature with image in your account, follow the steps below:

1. Sign in to your account at https://outlook.com
2. In the upper-right corner of your account, click the Gear icon and then click “More mail settings.”
3. Under “Customize your mail,” click “Message font and signature.”
4. Ensure that “Rich text” is selected on the dropdown menu displayed beside the “Insert Emoticon” icon.
5. Open a new browser, then go to the website where the image that you’d like to include in your e-mail signature is located.
6. Right click the image then "Copy."
7. Go back to the browser where you've opened your account and paste the image in the “Personal e-mail signature” box by pressing "Ctrl" and "v." Create your e-mail signature.
8. Click “Save.”

FYI: You can only add an image/ picture in your e-mail signature that’s Web based (picture that is available in existing websites or stored in an online storage). This is the reason why on step 5, I’ve asked you to open a new browser where the image is located.

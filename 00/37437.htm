MBPrakash - If that other computer's screen is set to a different resolution (number of dots that make up the screen), then the picture will fill a different portion of the screen.

  1. Change the resolution to match that of the picture.
    This will cause your screen image quality to be reduced, blurry, out of proportion (circles won't appear round) and you won't be able to fit as much stuff on-screen.

    or


  2. Use the fill option to increase the picture's size.

There's no alternative.

No. Regardless of what your computer is, an upgrade from Windows XP to
7 is not possible. You can change from XP to 7, but you have to do it
by a clean installation, not by an upgrade; that means that after
installation you have to restore all your data from backups to
external media, and reinstall all your programs from their original
media.

And leaving aside the difference between upgrading and clean
installing, you should be aware of the following two important points:

1. An XP-era computer is usually way underpowered to run Windows 7
with acceptable performance. Your computer can probably run it, but it
will probably be very slow, and you should probably upgrade several
hardware components. For example, 1GB of RAM is very little

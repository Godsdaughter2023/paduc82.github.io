vijay a. verma say:
1. One way is the way which Ugo Lopez has pointed.

2. Another way is to have a helper column and insert sequential number 1, 2, 3.....But once again, it has to be part of your sorted range. Now, you can reference to this.
Let's say that this helper column is column G and values 1,2 etc are filled from G1.

Hence, in your case the formula
=(H24+H26)*-1
Becomes
=INDEX(H:H,MATCH(24,G:G,0))+INDEX(H:H,MATCH(26,G:G,0))*-1

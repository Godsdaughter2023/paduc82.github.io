jay freedman say:
First, if you insert the screenshot (or other graphic) as In Line with Text, then you must either change its text wrapping to one of the other types or else insert it on a drawing canvas (Insert > Shapes > New Drawing Canvas).

Then insert the shape(s) (circle, etc.) and position it where you want it on the other picture.

Hold Ctrl while you click each of the objects (could be many of them) so they're all selected at the same time.

On the Drawing Tools ribbon, in the Arrange group, click the Group button and choose Group from the menu. That combines all the selected shapes into one, which you can resize or move as you like. You could also cut the combined shape, delete the drawing canvas, and insert the combined shape -- the canvas only serves to allow both in-line and floating objects to be selected simultaneously.

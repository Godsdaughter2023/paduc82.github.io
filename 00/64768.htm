davem121 say:
Hi yellowhoody

In the following, I am presuming the HDD is Drive D, if it is not, please replace where necessary

Open Windows File Explorer

On the top right, right click your Documents library folder and choose Properties

Choose the Location tab

In the Location Box, type D:\Documents

Then click Apply and OK and confirm that move

That will move your Documents folder to the HDD

Do the same for Pictures, Music and Videos, each time change the folder location, for example Pictures will go to D:\Pictures

When that is done all your personal folders will be moved to your HDD and all files copied into them will automatically go to your HDD, not your SSD. And you will have a lot more free space on your SSD

That's It!

Chapters are a feature of the DVD burning, not of the movie creation.  But if you are asking how you can get Windows DVD Maker to create scenes using your desired chapter points, rather than picking them randomly, here's a trick.

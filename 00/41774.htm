Really good motherboards (particularly servers) will maintain their own event logs but they are few and far between.

There are two forms of interrupts within PC architecture. The normal one allows a device to flag up that it needs attention but waits until it is serviced. An NMI is compulsory, it has no wait condition. For a total crash to occur I think even that is not being processed.

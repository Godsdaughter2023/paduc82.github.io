Hi QUINNIFER,

You may try changing the file format to default program which is required.

1.      Right click on any of the XPS document.
2.      Click Properties.
3.      Click on Change under General tab.
4.      Select the program with which you want to open with.
5.      Click OK.

Now check if you are able to open the document in MDI format.

MDI files can only be opened or edited in Office Document Imaging

Refer the article for more information: About Microsoft Document Imaging Format (MDI): http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/help/HP062193601033.aspx

To edit a cover page with Windows Fax and Scan:

1. Click the Start, click All Programs, and then click Windows Fax and Scan.
2. Make sure you're in Fax view by clicking Fax at the bottom of the left pane.
3. Click the Tools menu, and then click Cover Pages.
4. Select a cover page to edit, and then click Open.
5. Make changes to the cover page as needed.
6. Once you've edited the page, see how it will look by clicking the File menu, and then clicking Print Preview. (When you're done with the preview, click Close.)
7. To save your changes, click the File menu, and then click Save.

Refer the article for more information: Create or edit a fax cover page: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows7/Create-or-edit-a-fax-cover-page

serds_p say:

Hi,

If you have marked your Skype account for closure, but still has an active Skype number subscription, you will still be billed on the next billing cycle as your Skype account has 60 days before it is completely deleted from the time you marked it for closure.

You can cancel your Skype number subscription so that you will no longer be charged on the next billing period.

Details on how to cancel a Skype number is found in this support article: https://support.skype.com/en/faq/FA12164/

You can log in to your account again to cancel your Skype number subscription so that you can close your Skype account without getting billed within the 60 day waiting period for your account closure.

In case you need details on how to close your Skype account again, follow steps provided in this article: https://support.skype.com/en/faq/FA142/

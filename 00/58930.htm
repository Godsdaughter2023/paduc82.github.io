eambo say:

Hi sleekdrol,

I'll keep looking for further confirmation, but unfortunately from what I can gather the Xbox cannot authenticate/redirect to an authentication page.

In order to get online, you'll need to either:

- If possible, get your university to set your port up for direct connection without authentication.

- If not possible, you can share a desktop or laptops connection with your Xbox

The latter is possibly easier, if you have a laptop. Essentially you connect your laptop wirelessly, share the connection through the ethernet port, then plug that into the Xbox. Your laptop becomes the "authenticator" in this scenario.

More information on that particular process can be found here: support.xbox.com/.../win-computer

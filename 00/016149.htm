    brian tillman [outlook mvp 2007-2019] say:

    Yes, you can access both accounts.  Tools>E-mail Accounts>Add a new e-mail account>Next.  Supply all the information for that account, finish the wizard, and Outlook will pull messages from the mailboxes of both accounts, storing them in the same Inbox.  When you reply, Outlook will automatically choose the account that received the message or you can choose the one you want manually.  If you want messages to stay on the server as well so you can reference them online, don't forget to check the "Leave a copy of messages on the server" box on the Advanced tab of the More Settings dialogue.

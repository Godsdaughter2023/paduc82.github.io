I have had similar problems which were solved by setting the 'Autocenter' property of the form to true.  One thing to watch out for if you don't do this is, if your colleague uses 2 monitors and then reverts to 1, he may find that when he opens a form it doesn't appear.  It is, in fact open on the 'phantom' monitor which is where he had it open last time.  I have spent many hours of frustration on this one, only to find that 'autocenter' is the answer!

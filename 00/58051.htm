Hi,

The TWAIN working group does not make, provide, or distribute any scanners or drivers.

They come up with a specification that helps those scanner or camera vendors to write the drivers for their devices.

When the manufacturer write a driver following the TWAIN Specification, it is often referred to as a "TWAIN Driver" or a "TWAIN-compatible Driver."

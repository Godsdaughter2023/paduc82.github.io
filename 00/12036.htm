Problems sending SMS from Nokia PC Suite - Microsoft Community

yossefyaakov say:

Problems sending SMS from Nokia PC Suite

Hi, I had a problem sending SMS from my computer via the Nokia PC Suite, got message in the PC Suite "Message Sent", but on my Nokia phone there was error message "message not sent" or "could not send message to...".  

I solved it for myself, and when I tried to "Reply" to other people in this forum who had the same problem it showed a message "This Thread is Locked", so for the benefit of users who still have problem sending SMS's from Nokia PC Suite I provide here the solution that worked for me: I simply deleted old SMS messages from my Nokia phone (from the Inbox and Sent box) - make sure that you delete it from the Phone - it can be done through the Nokia Suite or directly from your Nokia phone.  After deleting old SMS's it is a good idea to restart your phone.  Apparently there is a limit to the number of SMS messages that can be stored on the phone, and when you exceed that number you are no longer able to send SMS's.  After deleting a bunch of old SMS messages now it works well for me. I hope that this is helpful!

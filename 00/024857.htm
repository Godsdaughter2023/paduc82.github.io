That's possible, too :-)

The trick is to delve a bit more deeply into the "Insert Table of Contents" dialog box. If you click on "Options" you'll get a list of styles that Word currently "sees" as valid for building a TOC. By default, these will be the styles Heading 1, Heading 2 and Heading 3, which I assume you used.

Calculating Employee Turnover Rate from Historical Data - Microsoft Community

thubbard say:

Calculating Employee Turnover Rate from Historical Data

I have a list of employees with start & end dates for the last 3+ years and need to present a monthly turnover rate & Average Turnover rate for the time stated for a report.

What formula can I use to do this? I would assume to begin with either a Vlookup or more likely a Sumif(s), right?

Figured it out on my own, probably crudely.

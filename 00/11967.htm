Is there a setting to change to keep Outlook 2010 Calendar - Microsoft Community

hawkstl say:

Is there a setting to change to keep Outlook 2010 Calendar appointments longer than 30 days or less? There was a setting in 2003 and I believe in 2007.

brian tillman [outlook mvp 2007-2019] say:

Outlook never deletes calendar entries by default.  Click File>Options>Advanced>AutoArchive Settings.  Do you have autoarchiving enabled?  If so, you can either disable it completely or if you wish to leave it enabled in general but you want the Calendar excluded, right-click your Calendar folder, choose Properties, select the AutoArchive tab, and select "Do not archive items in this folder".

Your archived items will be in the Calendar folder in your Archive PST.  The AutoArchive settings in Options will have the path to that PST.  Select the option to show the archive folders in the Navigation Pane and you'll be able to open that calendar and move the items back to your main Calendar.

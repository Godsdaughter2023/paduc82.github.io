You can give the permission of the Inbox folder to people you want.  Right clicking on the Inbox folder > Properties > Permissions.

If you have subfolders, create a new folder under Inbox folder. Give this new folder correct permission and then move all the other folders to this new folder.

Administrator can also grant permission via Exchange Online PowerShell:

https://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/library/dd298062%28v=exchg.160%29.aspx?f=255&MSPPError=-2147217396

How to connect to Exchange Online PowerShell:

Alison

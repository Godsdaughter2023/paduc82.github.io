brian tillman [outlook mvp 2007-2019] say:

You should never specify anything between "https://" and "mail.live.com/". Always allow the connection to choose the appropriate node. Nodes come and go. Leave the node out and you'll always get a valid node.

If your account has been moved to the Office 365 Exchange backend, DeltaSync no longer works. You must add Exchange ActiveSync to Windows Live Mail by installing this update: Update for Windows Live Essentials Mail 2012. You know this is necessary when you see "Outlook Mail" or "Outlook Email" at the left end of the menu bar when using the web interface, instead of seeing "Outlook.com".

Alternatively, you can reconfigure your account as POP or IMAP. With POP you'll lose all two-way sync. With IMAP, mail will sync, but calendar and contacts won't.

While your iPhone will support EAS, it will also support full Exchange, so add your account into the phone through Settings>Mail, Calendar, Contacts as an Exchange account. Allow the phone to automatically configure the account settings.

To create Groups

To create a group, first go to the People page. Then click on Manage on the command bar and select Manage Groups. Click on a group name to change the name. Click on the group name and then click on the garbage can icon to delete a group. Click on the plus sign to create a new group.

 

Maintain Groups

To edit the group you need to be on the People page. You just need to click on a contact and then click on Groups. This opens a list of all of your groups and you can add or remove this contact by checking or not checking the check box beside the group name and then clicking on Apply.

See who is in a Group

You can see who is in a group by clicking on the All at the top of your contact list and selecting the group name. This filters the list to show only the members of the group.

 

Send email to a Group

Once you have the group created you just need to type the group name in the To box to be able to send to the group. Once the group name is in the To box you can click on the plus sign to the left of the group name and the group name will be replaced by the individual contacts.

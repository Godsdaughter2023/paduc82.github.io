    I hope that I can help you solving your problem with the following little "manual" showing how I proceeded. By the way, my mother language is German, and I use the German version of Windows 10, so, hopefully, I translate the menus and steps correctly.

    1) On every single device in the network, open the "Services" section in the Control Panel, set the following service to "Automatic" and start them:

       - HomeGroup Provider

       - HomeGroup Listener

       - Peer Networking Grouping

    2) In the Windows Explorer, open the "Options" menu, and change the settings to make sure that hidden and system files are visible.

    3) Shut down ALL the other computers in your network except the one on which you want to create a new homegroup. This step is essential!

    4) Open the folder c:\Windows\ServiceProfiles\LocalService\AppData\Roaming\Peernetworking. Delete all the files in the folder.

    5) In Windows Explorer, tap or click on "HomeGroup" (in the navigation area of Windows Explorer, almost at the end). Tap or click the button "Create new homegroup" and wait until it is created. I recommend that you copy the shown key to save it.

    6) The computer now automatically enters the new homegroup. The first part is completed.

    7) Start another PC in the network. Wait until it has fully started. In Windows Explorer, tap or click "HomeGroup", and then tap or click the button "Enter homegroup". You probably get the message that your key is being checked; don't worry! After a few (more) seconds, Windows asks you for the key of the new homegroup. Enter it, tap or click "OK" or "Continue", and wait for about 1 minute (depending on the speed of your PC). You should then get the message that your device is a member of the new homegroup. Success!

    8) Do the same with your other devices.

    9) Finally, check the settings of the shared folders on each device, and make sure that they are shared with the members of the homegroup.

    This solution worked perfectly for me, and I hope that it helps solving your problem with the homegroup, too! Let me know if it worked for you! Good luck!

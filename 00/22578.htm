a. user say:

Hello StellaChow:

Thank you for posting to the forum regarding the questions you have about emails received on your phone.  At this point I am unsure if you are stating that multiple different accounts you have added to your phone are syncing emails to the phone or if the issue is that all the emails associated with your Gmail account are syncing to the email section of your phone. 

If you are noticing that emails are being received on the phone for accounts you do not want synced you can adjust the syncing of emails to the phone.  Go to settings > emails + accounts.  Tap on each account and you will find syncing options.  There should be a check box for email.  Unchecking this would prevent all emails to this particular account from synching to the phone.

If the issue is only with the one Gmail account and the fact that you are getting advertisement emails along with your work/personal emails, this is an issue with the filters on the Gmail side of things.  You might want to sign into the Gmail account on your PC and look over your account settings.  Look for option related to SPAM or junk mail and see if you can specify specific senders or increase the likelihood that something sent to you is recognized as SPAM.  This should force at least some of the emails you do not want to see into a SPAM or junk folder.  You can check these folders separately from your primary inbox. 

If you are having some issue other then what has been discussed above we would need much more detail regarding what is happening on your phone and what you would like to happen.

Please let us know if you have any other questions or concerns we can assist you with.

With the selection in the Header of the second Section of the document, you need to check the Different First Page box in the Options section of the Header & Footer Tools>Design tab of the ribbon

kingsmoss say: Sorry, I'm still not getting it. Let's say page 10 is the last page of Section 1, page 11 is Chapter 1 - where I don't want a Header. doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

You need a section break between pages 10 and 11 so that the first page of Chapter 1 is page 11.

As long as you are in the second section of the document, it does not matter what page you are on when you check the Different First Page box.  The effect of checking that box will be to remove the header from the first page of the section.  The balance of the pages in the section will retain the header from the first section of the document.

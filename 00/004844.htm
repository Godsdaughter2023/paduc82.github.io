=IFERROR(INDEX($A:$A,SMALL(IF(AND($C$1:$C$100=M$1,$B$1:$B$100=N$1),ROW($1:$100)),ROWS($2:2))),"")

This is an array formula

You cannot use AND and OR in array formula because they process the entire array parameter.  Two alternatives:

=IFERROR(INDEX($A:$A,SMALL(IF(($C$1:$C$100=M$1)*($B$1:$B$100=N$1),ROW($1:$100)),ROWS($2:2))),"")

or

=IFERROR(INDEX($A:$A,SMALL(IF($C$1:$C$100=M$1,IF($B$1:$B$100=N$1,ROW($1:$100))),ROWS($2:2))),"")

Note:  I have not vetted your logic to see if it is reasonable or if it can be improved, perhaps avoiding the AND operation altogether.

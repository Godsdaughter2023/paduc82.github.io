hansv mvp say:

Make sure that you have field names (column headers) above your data in columns A, B and C.

You can create a pivot chart from the data:

  • Click anywhere in the data.
  • Activate the Insert tab of the ribbon.
  • Click the lower half of the PivotTable button and select PivotChart.
  • Specify where you want the pivottable and pivotchart, then click OK.
  • Drag the name of the x-values to the Axis Fields box.
  • Drag the name of the y-values to the Legend Fields box.
  • Drag the name of the z-values to the Values box.
  • Change the chart type to Surface.

What you are seeing are the MIME quoted-printable characters of the message.  The message format isn't being properly rendered as it arrives.  The usual cause of this is your antivirus program and AV programs from Symantec are among the worst offenders.  Completely uninstall Norton Antivirus with the NAV Removal Tool (http://us.norton.com/support/kb/web_view.jsp?wv_type=public_web&docurl=20080710133834EN&ln=en_US) and reinstall it without the mail scanning feature.  You don't need it anyway, particularly because you are smart enough not to click on attachments in mail messages.

Hello Steve,

When creating a new presentation in PowerPoint, mostly you will have 96 dots per inch (dpi) on your screen settings. When you run a smaller PowerPoint slideshow on a larger television or computer screen, then PowerPoint will always enlarge the presentation so that it will fit on your screen.

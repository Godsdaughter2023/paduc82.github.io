    The first cell (call it A1) could have data entered into it at any time in the following formats:

    ##

    ##/##

    ##/##/##

    ##/##/##/##

    then change each of those numbers by a single value in another cell (call this A2), and display the result in another cell (call this one A3)

    So if for example those numbers displayed in cell A1 as 30/20/10/5 and then in cell A2 i had the number 4, i would want cell A3 to display 34/24/14/9.

    Sometimes data may be entered into the cell with only 1 to 3 of the numbers like 30/10 or maybe 30/20/10 and the formula would need to account for that too so those examples if entered into cell A1 and increased by the number 4 in A2 would display in A3 as 34/14 or 34/24/14 respectively.

    SOLVED!!! Big thanks to Rajesh C. for his formula:

    =IF((LEN(A1)-LEN(SUBSTITUTE(A1,"/","")))=1,((MID(A1,1,(FIND("/",A1)-1))*1)+A2&"/"&(MID(A1,(FIND("/",A1)+1),2)*1)+A2),IF((LEN(A1)-LEN(SUBSTITUTE(A1,"/","")))=2,((MID(A1,1,(FIND("/",A1)-1))*1)+A2&"/"&(MID(A1,((FIND("/",A1)+1)),(FIND("/",SUBSTITUTE(A1,",",""),FIND("/",A1)+1)-(FIND("/",A1)+1)))*1)+A2&"/"&(MID(A1,(FIND("/",A1,FIND("/",A1)+2))+1,2)*1)+A2),IF((LEN(A1)-LEN(SUBSTITUTE(A1,"/","")))=3,((MID(A1,1,(FIND("/",A1)-1))*1)+A2&"/"&(MID(A1,((FIND("/",A1)+1)),(FIND("/",SUBSTITUTE(A1,",",""),FIND("/",A1)+1)-(FIND("/",A1)+1)))*1)+A2&"/"&(MID(A1,(FIND(",",SUBSTITUTE(A1,"/",",",2))+1),(FIND(",",SUBSTITUTE(A1,"/",",",3)))-(FIND(",",SUBSTITUTE(A1,"/",",",2))+1))*1)+A2&"/"&(MID(A1,((FIND(",",SUBSTITUTE(A1,"/",",",3))+1)),(LEN(A1)-(FIND(",",SUBSTITUTE(A1,"/",",",3)))))*1)+A2),(A1+A2))))

    If anyone is trying something similar, this worked perfectly as intended :)

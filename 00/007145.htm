You might do a hard reset as Eric suggested or edit the registry in order to re-enable the checkbox. However, be careful as you might render your device inoperable.

 

Modify the following reg key:

 

Hkey_Local_Machine\Security\Policies\Policies\00001023.

 

Change the value from 0 to 1

 

Click OK to save changes.

 

Download a free registry editor from http://www.phm.lu/Products/PocketPC/RegEdit/

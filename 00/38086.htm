Hello,

Thank you for visiting Microsoft Community.

As per the description, you want to know whether you can safely trash the contents of the Microsoft Antimalware Scans directory as it is consuming a lot of space on the SSD hard drive on the system.

I certainly understand your concern.

I would like to provide you with the information that there is no way to limit the size of the folders. Though you can delete the logs. In order to do that, refer to the following steps. 

  1. Open the “Run” dialogue, type “services.msc”, and click “OK”.
  2. Scroll to the Microsoft Antimalware Service and double-click to open its Properties window.
  3. Stop the service, and then delete the log.
  4. Then restart the service and restart the computer.

I hope this information is helpful.

Please do let us know if you need any further assistance, we will be glad to assist you.

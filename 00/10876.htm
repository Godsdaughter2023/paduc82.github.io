How am I going to maintain the format after paste it to Excel work sheet without changing the data from cell to cell.

alexamktg say: What probably is happening is that Excel thinks you are pasting dates, so it auto-formats the cells in the way it thinks you want it.  So performing an import is a good way of telling it to preserve your formatting.

Another option is to do a Paste Special instead of just a regular Paste.  To do this you can either right click or use the Paste Special feature from the File menu.  Then you have the option to maintain the original format for the cells you are pasting.

Now if it is still auto-formatting, than select the cells giving you trouble, right click, go to Format Cells.  Under the Numbers tab, choose Texts so that it will stop trying to convert your numbers into a date.

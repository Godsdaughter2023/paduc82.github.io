Hello John:

Thank you for posting to the forum regarding the issues you are having with Facebook chat.  When you get a cannot connect error this tends to indicate there is either an issue with the phones connection or an issue with the accounts being used on the phone.

When you use Facebook chat on the Windows Phone 7 this is something you should be doing through the integrated messaging application and not through any downloaded Facebook application.  Please confirm for us the following.

  • Are you trying to use Facebook chat through the messaging app or through the Facebook application?
  • What is the exact word for word error message you are receiving?  Please include the text portion of the error and the error code.
  • When did you first start noticing issues with Facebook chat on your phone?

When it comes to troubleshooting your issues with Facebook chat there are a couple things I would suggest you try.

  • Soft reset the phone.  To do this go to settings > applications > Internet Explorer > delete history.  Once the history is deleted press and hold the volume keys, power button and camera together for 3-5 seconds.
  • If the issue continues the next thing to do is uninstall the Facebook application from the phone.  Then go to settings > emails + accounts > tap and hold the Facebook account > select the delete option.  After the Facebook account is removed from the phone unlink your Microsoft account and Facebook account.  You can do that by signing into your account at http://account.live.com .  Once you are signed in click on the manage related accounts link and then select the connected accounts link on the next page.  Here you should see your Facebook account with an edit option below it, click this.  Finally, click the remove this connection completely link.
  • Finally, go back to your phone and reboot the phone.  Once it is rebooted, re-add the Facebook account to your phone and then re-install the Facebook application.  Try to use the chat function.

Please let us know If you have any other questions or concerns we can assist you with.

Mark W.

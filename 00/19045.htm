john w. vinson say:

It may just be a formatting problem. A Date/Time value always has both a date and time component - it's stored as a Double, with the integer part being the number of days since midnight, December 30, 1899 and the fractional part being the time.

 

Try setting the Format property of your combo box to

 

"mm/dd/yy"

 

to see what's actually there.

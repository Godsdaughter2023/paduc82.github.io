I understand that you're having difficulties using your Microsoft (Outlook) Account on your Windows Phone.

In the situation you're describing, it’s possible that your Microsoft Account profile could be incomplete, causing the sync issue.

In order to take care of that,

Please Sign In with that Microsoft Account to the web site :

https://account.live.com/SummaryPage.aspx

And update all the information there.

If all the fields are already full, please make some small change in order to force Account Information Update.

After that is done, Sign In to the : https://account.xbox.com/en-US/Profile

This should Create your Microsoft Account's Xbox Live Profile, (needed for Windows Phone Store)

Alternatively you can use the link below to Accept the Xbox Terms of use.
https://live.xbox.com/en-US/Account/NewTermsOfUse

Next web site to visit and again Update the profile information is :
https://commerce.microsoft.com/PaymentHub/Profile

Just like before, If all the fields are already full, please make some small change in order to force Account Information Update.

And a last one : http://g.live.com/0fsenus4/nexusmigrate

This link should take you to outlook.com web site, in some situations it will present you with a terms of use to accept, if so please do that.

Now that you have your Microsoft Account's Profile updated.

Soft Reset the Phone (Volume Down + Power Buttons, hold together for about 15 seconds)

When the phone turns back on go to Settings > Date + Time, disable automatic and manually set the correct Date and Time.

Next Settings > Find My Phone - Enable all options there Please,

Settings > Backup - Enable all options there,

Settings > Applications (Swipe the whole Settings List to the Side (left or right) to access Appications) > Internet Explorer - Delete browsing history.

Last thing to do would be in Settings > Email + Accounts, over there tap and hold your finger on your Microsoft Account and Select Sync.

After following those steps, your phone should have full functionality back again.

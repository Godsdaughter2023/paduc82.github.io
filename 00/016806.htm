Welcome to Microsoft Community. We are glad to help you with your concern.

According to you post, you would like to know how you will cancel your domain account, because you don't want your old emails still be accessible since you already moved to a new service.

If you still have access to your "Admin control panel" you still can cancel the service for your domain. To cancel the service for a domain, you need to perform the following steps:

1. Sign in to Windows Live Admin Center by using your administrator Windows Live ID.

2. Under Your domains, click the appropriate domain.

3. In the navigation pane, click Domain Settings.

4. Click Cancel Service, and then click “Yes.”

Notes: When a service is canceled, Microsoft accounts are maintained so that users can still sign in to all other Microsoft services. However, Outlook.com subscriptions will be canceled. This includes any existing Ad-free Outlook.com subscriptions.

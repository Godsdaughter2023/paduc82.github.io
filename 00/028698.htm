srb_ppt say: Why does the same custom bullet graphic work (align center to line) in 2003, 2007 but not 2010?

Should any graphic for a custom bullet have a square canvas size? 

What I've found is, if you make a graphic that is taller than wide, PPT2010 "squares" the image, actually shrinking it down to fit as a bullet.  If you make the canvas square, nothing helps.  If you make the canvas extra wide, it gets shrunk as well-- whatever you import, it needs to be square for PPT2010 to leave it alone in scale.

The only work-around I can find is to create a square canvas in Photoshop, place the bullet image in the lowest, center position of this square canvas, then paint a single pixel dot in the upper most top of the canvas, then save as a trans png.

When I do this, at these the image is forced to sit lower, as PPT recognizes that pixel, and top aligns it.

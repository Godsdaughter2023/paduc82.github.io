Here are some tips on how you can play the music in your car. (Assuming that you're going to use your car's music player.)

A. If your car's music player has a built-in USB port.

If this is the case, best thing to do is copy the musics on a portable USB instead on a external hard drive. To copy the music on a USB device, follow these steps:

  1. Insert the USB on the USB port of your laptop.
  2. Locate the folder where the musics are downloaded, highlight the files you want to copy to the USB.
  3. On your keyboard, press Ctrl + C. (This is the shortcut to copy the files.)
  4. Locate the USB drive folder, double-click the folder.
  5. On your keyboard, press Ctrl + V. (This is the shortcut to paste the files on the USB folder.)
  6. Remove the USB from the laptop, and try it on your car's music player.

B. If your car's music player is Bluetooth capable.

This is the most convenient way to listen music in your car. All you need to do is download music in your phone. Connect your phone to your car's music player via Bluetooth. Music will play through your car's speaker instead on your phone.

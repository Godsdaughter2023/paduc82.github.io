 Dim oCC As ContentControl
 Dim oTmp As Template

How do I convert PDF into work. - Microsoft Community

lee n deefoote say:

How do I convert PDF into work.

I need to convert a book done in PDF into Word format.  I have text, charts, graphs, photos etc.  I need to do this with as much accuracy as humanly possible. The less errors...the better, after all, time is money! Can anyone help me? 

macropod say:

You will need to use some third-party software, such as Adobe Acrobat Pro, that can convert PDFs to one of Word's formats.

The quality of the output will depend on the converter used and, unless you have the same fonts as the original and use the same page layouts, printer driver and justification algorithm as the original (the latter two being quite unlikely unless the original was created in Word), you're unlikely to get an exact match. Moreover, any header/footers or footnotes/endnotes in the source are liable to become body text when converted to Word - I don't know of any converters that convert such content correctly. If you're scanning a book, you will likely have OCR errors and other formatting errors (e.g. line breaks at the end of every line and/or content in frames) to correct as well.

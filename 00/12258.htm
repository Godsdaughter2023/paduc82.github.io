suzanne s. barnhill say:

To create that type of shading, you must use paragraph shading (and a border), not field shading. Select the paragraph you want to shade, then click the arrow beside the Shading button in the Paragraph group on the Home tab and select the desired shading color. To add a border, use the Borders button next to the Shading button.

Note that in your example, it's entirely possible that the creator used a table cell and applied a border and shading to it. The three-column section of Core Competencies is definitely a table. FWIW, I don't consider that a particularly attractive résumé.

rohn007 say:
You've spotted the problem.

Office Pro Plus 2013 is an older version, different "Edition" from Office 365 (2016). My guess is that it is a "free trial" that was installed on your computer at some time. The trial expired long ago. Because it was installed before Windows directs activation attempts to it. But, a 365/2016 activation won't work on it so the activation feels. You keep getting a message to activate 2013 Pro Plus, but it does not explicitly say so.

The fix for this problem is simple enough. Right click on the entry for 2013 Pro plus. Select "Uninstall". When the uninstall is finished, right click on the Office 365 entry and select "repair".

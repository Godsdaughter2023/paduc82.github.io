Obviously the easy solution is to tell the students to go to the Format menu, but why won't it show up on the quick menu on the ribbon?  I've checked in Font Book to make sure there's no duplicates, checked the font files in the Library, restarted the computer, etc.
john mcghie say:
Fints will show up only if they are correctly-encoded, Unicode, Open Type True Type.  Type 1 fonts and OpenType PostScript fonts will not show up, and will not work.  Fonts that are not encoded according to the Unicode standard may play up, also.

If your students were to modify one of the styles to contain Navajo (say: Body Text) and add that to the ribbon, it would always show up.

Basically, the Font menu is not a "good" way to use Word: it's really designed to be used with styles, and you will find that a lot faster and easier.

a. user say:

Factory reset is actually a procedure developed by the computer manufacturer, so detailed instructions are on the manufacturer's website. Just be sure that you're following the procedures for your model of computer.

The advantages of a factory reset are: 1) it's over quickly; 2) it was designed for your computer, so you know it's going to work; and 3) you'll get all the programs and drivers that were installed at the factory. In addition, you won't have any worries about activation.

The computer will be exactly as it was when it left the factory, which is not the case when you reset the computer according to Microsoft's procedure.

All will be well provided that no one has monkeyed with the manufacturer's recovery partition.

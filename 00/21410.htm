why is my word document transparent?

[private message is removed by forum moderator]

john korchok say:

In that case, you're importing a PDF, not a Word file. Dragging it onto PowerPoint imports it as a picture and it is translated to EMF format for internal storage. I can confirm that a PDF saved from a Word file has a transparent background, even if you set the page color to white.

To fix this:

  1. While the PDF is selected, choose Picture Format>Format Pane. The Format Pane opens at the side of the program window.
  2. Click on the Fill & Line tab (looks like a bucket of paint).
  3. Expand the Fill section and give the PDF a white fill.

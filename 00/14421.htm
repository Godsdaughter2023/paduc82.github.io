Office 365 subscriptions do not use product keys.
Once you renew the subscription, there is nothing else you need to do, just launch any of the apps in the suite and use them.

And no, you don't need to download Office 365 again. If your subscription runs out, all that happens is Office becomes an expensive viewer that can only open and view documents; you can't edit them.

To view the status of your subscription:



Go to your Services & subscriptions page.
https://account.microsoft.com/services?ref=offi...

If prompted, choose Sign in and enter the Microsoft account email and password associated with your Office 365 subscription.

If you see a circle arrow, then your subscription is set up for recurring billing, unless you turn recurring billing off or cancel it beforehand. The next charge info shows you when you’ll be charged next, and for how much.

If you see Expires on [date], then your subscription will expire on that date, unless you renew.

john w. vinson say:

Try this (if you want to exclude any transaction after and including a SERVICING transaction):

SELECT DSIT, CLID, STATUS, DATE_TIME
FROM tablename
WHERE DSID NOT IN
  (SELECT DSID FROM tablename AS XCL
    WHERE XCL.CLID = tablename.CLID
    AND DATE_TIME >=
      (SELECT DATE_TIME FROM tablename AS XCL2
        WHERE XCL2.CLID = tablename.CLID
         AND XCL2.STATUS = "Servicing"))

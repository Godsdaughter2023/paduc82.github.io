So this message is displayed in the e-mail you get back when someone accepted an invite or declined an invite, also when you go to the date on the calendar where you made the event/appointment/meeting etc, it shows no one has accepted it, yet they did, hence the e-mail.

One of the main reasons because of this is because of the Calendar the entries (meetings/events/appointments etc.) are being made on is not the right one.

FIX: When you click on your Calendar Icon (Usually on left bottom side next to your Email icon), it will open your Calendar. Now You will see on the left pane a 'MY CALENDARS' box filled, with two Calendars under it with also boxes.

One Calendar will have a '(This Computer Only) next to it and the other one (might have more) does not have this next to them. ALL ENTRIES SHOULD BE MADE IN 'THIS COMPUTER ONLY' Calendar. There might be 'Other Calendars' with that also next to it.

Note: If you don't have a left pane showing the different calendars, Check at the right bottom corner next to Connected/Offline where there is also a zoom adjustment there should be a small book and a small box next to the book. Click on the Small box, this should open the pane for the different Calendars. It should be the Normal View. (The Calendars will have different colors to help you see which one you are currently on.)

I hope this helps in the future for anyone experiencing this problem.

Good Luck

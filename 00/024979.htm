Hi

Skype Numbers are used to receive calls from mobile and/or landline numbers or non-Skype users. If you are not expecting calls from mobile and landline numbers then there is no need to purchase a Skype Number.

Skype purchases are tied to the account. You will find your Skype Number if:

  • You are signed in to the account you used to purchase the Skype Number.
  • The Skype Number hasn't expired yet. If expired, you can still reactivate it within 90 days from the day of expiration.
  • The payment method attached to the Skype Number is active and have sufficient funds.
  • If the Skype Number not be acquired by a different user. If a Skype Number expired and wasn't reactivated during the 90-day period, it will go back to the pool of available Skype Number for purchase.

You'll find your Skype Number by logging in to your account on the Skype website. Scroll down to Manage features and click Skype Number.

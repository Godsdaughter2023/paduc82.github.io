jeffgoodman say: Hi Bryan

The first step is to create a Named Range which will change based on the selection in Cell B1 as follows:  
  1. On the Formula ribbon click on Name Manager
  2. Click on New
  3. Enter MyRange as the name
  4. In the "Refers to" box enter =INDIRECT("Sheet1!$C$2:"&ADDRESS(ROW(Sheet1!A2),MATCH(Sheet1!B1,Sheet1!C1:N1,FALSE)+2))
  5. Click on OK & Close.
In your cell B2 enter the formula =SUM(MyRange) to calculate a sum.  It will also work with AVERAGE, COUNT etc, or any other calculation you want to do on the range.

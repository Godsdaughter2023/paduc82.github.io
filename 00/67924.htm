Skype Crashes on Windows Mobile 10

There are reports where Skype will crash on Windows Mobile 10, the behavior is as follow:

You click on Skype icon on Windows Mobile

A screen open with blue Skype icon on it and after few second it will close (without showing login menu) and you return to home screen.

If you see above issue, do the following steps:

1) Open Settings->Update & security-> Phone update and Check for updates and if there is any update install them and restart your phone

2) Open Store and click to open menus and click on Download and updates and Get updates and if there is any update for Skype install them

3) Turn off your phone and wait for 10 minutes and turn it on again.

4) Click on Skype and then click uninstall and turn off your phone, turn it on again and open store and search for Skype and install it.

5) Open Settings->Apps->Apps & features and find Skype in list and click on Advanced options and click Reset there (note this will remove your chat history too.)

6) If none of above steps fix the issue, then open Feedback Hub app and report this issue and make sure explain everything there and also search and if problem already reported upvote it and add additional comments.

teylyn say:

Try this formula

=COUNTA(C18:C22)-SUM(COUNTIF(C18:C22,{"x","pal","hol"}))

In words: count all the text values in the range and then subtract the total count where the range has either x, pal or hol.

amit tandon say:

Formula in cell B7 to be copied to the right:

=COUNTIFS(B3:B5,"<>pal",B3:B5,"<>x",B3:B5,"<>hol",B3:B5,"<>")

Another option:

Formula in cell B9 to be copied to the right - you may add multiple values to exclude here by just updating the range H2:H4:

=SUMPRODUCT(NOT(ISNUMBER(MATCH(B3:B5,$H$2:$H$4,0)))*(B3:B5<>""))

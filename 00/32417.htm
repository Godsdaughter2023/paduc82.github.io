I just got mine in today and they are spectacular. PX5 + Xbox + Droid = awesome. I'm streaming music from my droid's Pandora to my headset while playing and talking to friends, great sound quality.

I messed a bit with the presets. It seems as if the guide on the website is outdated, mostly referring to the Preset Manager rather than the new Advance Sound Editor (which replaced PM). I found some of the presets to work (namely the master presets, but game/chat/mic ones do not even appear, if you know something I don't please tell me!). I started with Bass+Treble boost, then brought the mids up a bit and even out the blast cap and tweaked the thresholds a bit. Really you have to just fiddle with it to see what each knob does. I found this to be best for my games (mainly Gears of War, and other shooters). You may prefer higher trebles, or mids, or basses. Just mess with it.

The mic mods are fun, but my friends all say its too hard to hear me. I didn't want to bother messing with it too much while playing, but I'm sure you can come in crystal clear if you adjust thresholds as you adjust pitch.

My chat is default with some volumes and threshold turned up a bit so I can always hear people when the game gets loud. Start with game sound then do chat. Mic I would always leave as standard unless you want to disguise your voice.

Hi Tschnetzler!

Your achievements will remain on your Microsoft account regardless of whether you`ve reset your Xbox or not. Once you`ve unlocked a achievement, you`ll always have it. For example, if you`ve completed a mission, got the achievement for it, and then reset the console, and lost the game data, when you load up you`ll find it`s still there unlocked as a achievement.

Now as far as the data goes, I`d try doing a re-sync of your game data. Normally, this should occur when you boot up the game and are connected to the internet.  It should then allow you to get into your saved game and see everything you know you should have.

Should it not appear after syncing, then it`d imply to me that before re-setting the console, the data wasnt syncing over to your cloud storage, and was rather singlely saving to your HDD. Which if that is the case, then I`m afraid any progressive data over what you are seeing now would be lost.

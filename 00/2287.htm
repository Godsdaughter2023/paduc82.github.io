Hi Robert,

You can use PowerShell to get all the members of a distribution group.

In Office 365, we can use the message trace to search the sender address, recipient address and subject of all emails sent to or from a specific user. Here are the steps:
1. Sign in to the Exchange admin center (https://outlook.office365.com/ecp) with an admin account.
2. Navigate to mail flow—message trace, specify the time, sender and recipient. Click Search.

Or you can use the cmdlet Get-MessageTrace to retrieve the result for a single user in PowerShell, then use the export command “>” to export it.
E.g. Get-MessageTrace -SenderAddress *** Email address is removed for privacy *** -StartDate 01/13/2016 -EndDate 02/13/2016 | FL > D:\report.csv
To trace the emails sent to a specific user, replace SenderAddress with RecipientAddress.

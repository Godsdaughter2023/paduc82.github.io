mccbleue say: Whilst you are in any feature that uses predictive text (such as writing an SMS message), the # key can be used to cycle through Caps lock, Caps off etc. This also switched the prediction on and off - look at the pencil logo in the top right next to the ABC indicator - if it has two lines under the tip of the pencil the prediction is switched on - just keep pressing the # key until you have the desired setting (Abc, ABC or abc for your 'Caps' mode) without the lines under the pencil and prediction will be off so you can type as normal.

joël séguin say:

There are two main differences:

- Instead of a summary bar, you get a rollup-bar, which is a different barstyle, showing the reported gantt bars instead of a start-to-end black bar.

- When assigning a recurring summary task, the subtasks are actually assigned, not the summary. Never assign a resource on a true summary task... (unless it's a recurring...)

That's about it I think.

julie sheets say:

Hi Rolando,

Recurring are usually scheduled based upon a specific set schedules -- for example every Tuesday, the second Monday of every month, every 5 days.  They are usually the same definition -- same task duration, frequently the same resources.  Each occurance is constrained with a Start No Earlier Than constraint and are frequently do not have predecessors.

I personally rarely use recurring tasks -- while there may be similar processes in projects -- usually in my world they are dependent upon other tasks and have specific defined deliverables which are different.

I hope this helps.

linley meslier say:
Hi,

It is not possible to sync Contacts sub-folders. This feature is not supported. However, it does work for the Inbox. Assuming you use a topuchscreen device, open the email account and click Menu > Tools > Manage folders. You should see a small + before your Inbox, so click to expand. Your subfolders should be listed. Just select the checkbox for the subfolder that you want to sync.

simba. say:

Escape from white "settings setup" screen after feature update

If you are stuck with the screen and unable to access your desktop, turn on Narrator (highlight the name to do this, when the option is offered) then follow the voice prompts. Choose Yes to every option offered and you will get the greyed out Accept buttons activated, and finally get out.

Other "escape routes" have been aired on the forum but I found this the easiest way. Steps to perform SFC scan

I use 6 different accounts in Mac Outlook and have seen the same behavior.  

I think your issue may be caused by Outlook Contacts.  It seems that Outlook is auto-recognizing you based on a Contact (the "me" contact) and replacing the email address with the Contact display name.  So it may look like it's being sent by you, or from your personal address, but may not truly be.  The true test would be to send yourself an email from another account, then reply to it and check that account to see if your name (or the company name in your Account settings) is being sent.

  • Contacts, make sure that the contact with your name (the "me" contact) has ONLY your personal email address; Outlook will often add email addresses to your contact if it sees them in your Accounts

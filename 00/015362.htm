Hi,

This can be done using VLOOKUP.

Assuming your table of rooms and capacities is in N1:O9. In L2, put the formula =VLOOKUP([Room],$N$1:$O$9,2,0)

    How do I play my Zune in my car? - Microsoft Community

    brooklyn2007 say:

    How do I play my Zune in my car?

    My car's USB port doesn't recognize my Zune device. How do I play my music in my car?
    joel ivory johnson say:

    In general a vehicles's USB will only recognize USB mass storage devices such as Flash drives and portable hard drives. Some cars are equipped with systems that can interact with devices that are not using USB mass storage mode such as the Zune and the iPod . 

    Since your car isn't able to play music off of the Zune over USB you will need to connect the headphone jack on the Zune to the aux input on your audio system. You can find the cable you need to do this at any electronic store.

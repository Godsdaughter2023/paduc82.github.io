rohn007 say:
should be simple enough.

Easiest case, if all of the paragraphs you manually applied right justification to are supposed to use the new style:
. * go into Replace dialog, with nothing entered in "Find what". Make sure there is no formatting displayed under the "Find what" box.
. * click on the More button
. * click on format drop down button
. * click on Paragraph
. * in alignment, select Right from drop down
. * add any other formatting that will uniquely identify rquired paragraphs
. * click OK to close the Formatting drop down
. * you will now see format attributes displayed under the "find what" box
. * move to replace with box
. * again go to the Format dropdown
. * go to styles,
. * select your new custom style
. * OK
from here you can use find next / replace to do change in controlled manner, or sleelct "replace all" to do a shotgun change. If the change does not go as expected, you can use <CTL><Z> to undo it.

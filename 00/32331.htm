Hi Brett,

Are you using a Local Account or Microsoft Account?

I would like to inform you that if you are using a Microsoft Account to login to the computer than you cannot change the email address associated with Windows Store. You can change the email address associated with the Microsoft Account and then you can login to the Windows Store with that email address.

Method 1:

If you need to change the email address associated with the user account, first you have to switch the login account to Local user account. Then switch back to Microsoft account and provide the desired Email ID to that user account.

Follow these steps:

Step 1: Switch to local user account:
Follow these steps to create a “Local User account”.

a. Press “Windows key +W”.

b. Type “users” without quotes in the search box.

c. Then select “Users” from the left panel.

d. You will get an option “Switch to a Local account” right below your user account name.

e. Select “Switch to a local account” option.

f. Enter the required details.

g. Then login to the local account and check how it works.

Step 2: Switch back to Microsoft account

Follow these steps to create a “Microsoft account”.

a. Press “Windows key +W”.

b. Type “users” without quotes in the search box.

c. Then select “Users” from the left panel.

d. Select “Switch to Microsoft account” right below your user account name.

e. Enter the required details (Primary Email ID).

f. Restart the computer and then try to login using new Primary account.

Method 2:

You can also try to remove this Microsoft Account from Windows Store and then add the desired account you want if you need to change the Microsoft account associated with Windows Store. You can refer to the steps mentioned in the following Microsoft article to do that.

To remove a PC from your account

a. On the Start screen, tap or click Storeto open the Windows Store.

b. Swipe down from the top edge of the screen, and then tap Your account. (If you're using a mouse, right-click, and then click Your account.)

c. Tap or click Your account.

d. Under Your PCs, tap or click Remove next to the PC you want to remove from your account.

Once you remove the account you can add the desired account you want.

Hope this helps.

Sometimes, servers push the sent email - rather copies of it - multiple times to the mail client. I have noticed this happen in Gmail. In the case of Gmail, there is an option to disable this 'sent items copying'. Perhaps something similar is available with Cox IMAP. Perhaps under some kind of a web console or something?

In addition to this, I would also suggest another solution. Link the Cox email with your main windowsphone account at OUTLOOK.COM.

christopher miller say:
What is probably happening is that the Mail app on your phone is putting a copy of the message in the Sent items on your phone.  When email is sent through Cox's email server, they are copying it to the sent folder on their side.  The next time your phone syncs with the Cox email server, it pulls down the latest messages in the Cox set folder, creating the multiple messages that you are seeing.  I'm not sure why you would see 3 copies of the message.

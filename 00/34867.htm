a. user say:

You can backup to OneDrive, but I wouldn't recommend it.

In the first place, most people's upload speed is much slower than their download speed. (Verizon FIOS is a notable exception.) And OneDrive imposes it's own speed limitations.

It would take many hours, even days, to backup your computer to OneDrive. In addition, unless you subscribe to Office365, or have purchased additional space, your backup will quickly outstrip your free space on OneDrive, which i think is 5 GB.

ganesh kumar n say:

Outlook can print the whole item or just certain pages but not a selected part of the text.

To print a selected part of an email, follow the steps below.

1. Click to open the Email in Outlook.

2. Click Actions > View in browser to open the email in browser.

Note: If you are unable to see the View in browser option, please first change the format of the mail to HTML, click the “Format Text” tab, then, click HTML.

3. In the web browser, select the part of the text you want to print, click the print label on the toolbar, then, and click Print

4. In the Print window, select the option 'selection'. Then, click the print label or press Alt + P to print.

roady [mvp] say:

Which mail account did you configure in Outlook Desktop?
File-> Account Settings-> Account Settings...-> tab E-mail

You'll only see emails on the Outlook.com website when you also configured Outlook with an @outlook.com address and keep the email within that mailbox (which is the default). To also check emails from other providers such as Gmail, you must add the account as a connected account in Outlook.com on the web.

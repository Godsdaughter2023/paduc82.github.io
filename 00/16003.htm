john w. vinson say:

TABLES have Fields, which store data.

 

FORMS and REPORTS do not have Fields: they have Controls, tools which display data stored in fields.

 

It's easy to confuse them, but a FIELD is not a CONTROL, and a CONTROL is not a FIELD. They're different entities and they work differently!

 

Your form doesn't have any fields, stacked vertically or any other way. It has Controls, probably Textbox Controls; these Controls are (probably) bound to Fields in your form's recordsource.

 

Each Control has a "Can Shrink" property. If you set that property to Yes, and if there are no labels or other data-containing controls to the left or right of your control, the textbox will shrink vertically to nothing if it doesn't contain any data.

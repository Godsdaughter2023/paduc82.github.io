jonathan - support engineer say:

Hello Chazer,

Thank you for posting to the Microsoft Answers Community Forum.

It appears that somehow you have made those "Faded" files classified as hidden.  That is how hidden folders and files appear, faded.  The fix is easy just right click on the files and folders that are hidden and you do not want them to be and from the menu that appears select properties.  Once in that window just make sure that you are in the "General" tab and uncheck the little check box at the bottom next to the word "Hidden".  Then click on "OK" and you should be all set.

Thank you,

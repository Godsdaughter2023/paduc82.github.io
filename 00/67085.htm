a) Insert the installation DVD or USB and boot Windows 8 from it.
b) In the ‘Windows setup’ page, select the ‘language to install’, ‘Time and currency format’ and the

BOOTREC /FIXBOOT, and then press ENTER.
BOOTREC /REBUILDBCD, and then press ENTER.
Restart the computer and check.

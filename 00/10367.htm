    harryzhanghz say:

    Thanks Lennart for providing such detailed information! The boot perf issue only occurs on HDD where loading fonts are slow, especially in the case that there are a lot of fonts to load. Here is the major differences between Mac Office 2011 and Mac Office 2016 that contribute to the issue: 2011 is not sandboxed application, and all the Office fonts are installed in /Library/Fonts/Microsoft that are accessible system wide. But 2016 turned to sandboxed, that such fonts locates inside application bundle that system has to load on every session, and HDD is not efficient at that.

    We are proactively working on this issue now, and will keep you updated when it is fixed.

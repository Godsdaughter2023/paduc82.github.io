Hotmail has a feature that prevents losing email messages if you accidentally set up POP3 to delete them after reading them on the server. Here’s how it works:

I'm guessing you have and SSD drive with limited room.

Probably the best solution is to buy a used laptop and install on that.

Office 365 is a subscription product and requires regular, although not constant, internet connection. Your Home subscription package allows connection on multiple platforms.

Also, check with your school. Some schools pay for students to have downloaded applications on their desktops; most pay for a less-expensive version that only uses the online apps. Do you know which your school is using?

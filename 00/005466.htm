doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

Use the following code:

Dim i As Long
With ActiveDocument
    For i = 1 To .Fields.Count
        If InStr(.Fields(i).Code.Text, "docVarName") > 0 Then
            .Fields(i).Code.Text = Replace(.Fields(i).Code.Text, "docVarName", "newdocVarName")
            Exit For
        End If
    Next i
    .Variables("newdocVarName").Value = "docvarvalue"
    .Range.Fields.Update
End With

Unless a license for Office 365 was included with your purchase, you need to actually subscribe. Many new computers include a trial version of Office 365 but not an actual permanent license.

Some include installation on up to five different computers and other devices.

https://setup.office.com/

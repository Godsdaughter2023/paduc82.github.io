metallikahn say:

I've solved the problem. It is a Yahoo problem and they are just terrible about letting you know how to get it working.

1.) Log into your Yahoo mail account in your Web Browser

2.) Cursor over your account name on the Upper Right part of the screen and select account info.

3.) On the new page that comes up select "Account Security" and then "Manage app Passwords"

4.) In the "App Passwords" pop  up window select "Other App" from the Pulldown and then name it whatever you want. (I suggest Windows 10 Mail)

5.) Press the Generate button and then Copy the password it creates.

6.) Create your Windows 10 Mail Yahoo account as normal, except be sure to use the new password you just created on the web. (Note: This password will ONLY be used for your Windows 10 Mail account and will not effect any other Yahoo passwords you have. ie... you will still use your old password when logging in via a web browser.)

This Should completely solve the issue and get your Yahoo account working perfectly on your Windows 10 app!

Good luck! Hope this helps.  :-)

Integrated calling option has been removed on new Skype 8 for iPhone. You need to manually call your contacts from your iPhone using the dial pad or make them as your phone number contacts in Skype by following the below steps:

  1. Click the "calls" tab to open the dial pad and call the number you want to be added as a Skype phone number contact. (The call doesn't need to be answered.)

  4. Click the edit button to edit the name of your phone number contact.

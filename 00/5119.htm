Export costs by month by task to Excel - Microsoft Community

philip greenberg say:

Export costs by month by task to Excel

How can I export a table of costs by month and by task to Excel?

Thank-you,

Philip

 
julie sheets say:

Hi Philip,

I'm not sure how far you have gotten - so I'll detail the steps from the beginning:

  • Open the project file and click the Project ribbon to show the reports.
  • Click Visual reports to open the Visual Reports dialog.  Click the All tab and select Cash Flow Report.
  • In the "Select level of usage data to include in the report", click the drop-down and select Months.
  • Click the View button to create the report and export the data to Excel.
  • In Excel, click the sheet tab named "Task Usage" to view the Pivot Table sheet.
  • Click in the Pivot table to show the Pivot table field list on the right side.  (If you can't see the Pivot Table fields, click the Pivot Table Tools Analyze ribbon and click "Field List".)
  • Scroll the list to the bottom and select Monthly Calendar to add the Monthly Calendar data to the Columns.
  • Drag Tasks from the Filter section to the Rows.

  • In the Pivot table, click the [+] to expand the tasks and/or date data.

This can happen with presentations created in earlier versions of PPT when you move them into PPT 2007 and onward.

Unfortunately, you cannot restart the computer or pause the Windows 8.1 upgrade from Windows Store. It's best to stay connected until the update is done. If you don't, you’ll need to connect again to finish setting up later, and setup will take longer.

K guys i seem to have fixed my problem. Basically there were 2 things i tried.

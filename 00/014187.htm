jay freedman say:
The reason the TOC runs off to the right is that there's a tab stop set at 6.49" (just to the left of the right margin for a full-size page), and when you switch to bookfold half-size pages that tab stop doesn't change. You'll have to do it manually.

Select all of the TOC entries. Press Shift+F1 to open the Reveal Formatting pane. In the Paragraphs section of the pane, click the Tabs link to open the Tabs dialog.

Select the 6.49" entry and click the Clear button.

Type 3.49" in the top box, click the option for the dotted leader (option 2), make sure the Right alignment is still selected, and click the Set button. Then click OK.

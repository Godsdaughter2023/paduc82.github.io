Holiday Calendar - Microsoft Community

wes poynter say:

Holiday Calendar

I am trying to create an email that would include a list of "holidays" on it to send out to all of our staff to add to their calendars. I know how to do this for single days but am unable to figure out how to do it for multiple days.
iry chen msft say:

Hi Wes Poynter,

 

It seems that your Holiday is noncontinuous, you cannot send directly in an email. However, you can try to create a calendar on your side and share the calendar to your employees as a workaround:

 

  1. On Calendar Page, choose Folder, New Calendar, choose where to place the calendar.
  2. After create a calendar, create some events you want to be out of office in the calendar(such as Thanksgiving Day…).
  3. Select the calendar and click Share Calendar under Folder.
  4. Send the email to the employees and then they can know your “holidays” via the calendar you shared.

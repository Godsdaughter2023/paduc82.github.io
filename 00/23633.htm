susan_s. say:

Hello

Your conversations are saved on your hard drive for a period of time, which you can specify in your history settings. The last 30 days of your conversation history are also stored in the cloud, so you can access it when you're signed in to Skype on any device. History older than 30 days is saved on your hard drive, according to your history settings. 

If you're using the new Skype for Desktop for Windows, Mac and Linux (v. 8.13), your conversation history is stored in the cloud for two (2) years.

However, it is not possible to import the local chat history of Skype for Windows desktop (classic Skype - 7.40) so it will take two (2 years) of using Skype before you will have 2 years of chat history under the new Skype for Desktop application.

Hello Funky Chipster,


Your deposit is being prevented due to a mismatch between your Irish Rewards Account region and your British Microsoft Account region. Since your Rewards Account is a separate entity from your Microsoft Account, migrating your Microsoft Account region will not also change your Rewards Account region. Therefore, when your Rewards Account attempts a deposit in one regional currency, it cannot complete when your Microsoft Account is of a differing currency. Also, since your Rewards Account is Irish, it is only tracking purchases in Euro. Purchases in a different regional currency will not be eligible for Rewards.


However, this issue can be fixed by heading over to the Microsoft Commerce page at (https://commerce.microsoft.com) to update your Microsoft account to match your Xbox Live Rewards location. Another option if you wish to continue with Rewards in your current Xbox Live region is to opt out of the program at ( https://rewards.xbox.com/optout/ ) , wait 48 hours and then sign back up again, which can also take 48 hours.  


While you will lose your progress by starting a brand new account, you can start earning Rewards Credits that will successfully reward your newly registered regional currency. To confirm that you have re-registered correctly, you can check your join date from the MyRewards page at (https://rewards.xbox.com/myrewards/). If your join date shows a specific date and not a date of "???", then your account has successfully been re-registered. If you open a new post after being able to log in to your new Rewards account, you can request  any pending deposits from your old account to your new one.

To share screen using your Skype version, you may follow these steps:

  1. Start a voice or video call with a single person or a group. 
  2. During the call, tap or click the more button ( ... ) ellipsis icon in the call bar, then choose Share screen. 
  3. If you're using more than one screen, select the screen you want to share.

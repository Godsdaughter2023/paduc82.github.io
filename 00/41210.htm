The only thing you need to remember is, that everything you buy digitally, is tied to your Account only. 

And your account can roam to whatever console you want, and as long as you are connected online, you can use your Gold membership and play your games. 

If other people should be able to benefit from your games and memberships, you can designate ONE console to be your account's Home Console. 

Your account will then share your games and Gold membership to that console, and anyone playing on that "Home Console" will be able to play your games. 

If however you have two kids, only one kid can play your games on the Home Console, the other kid needs your account to be signed online on the second console to play your games, and benefit from your Gold membership. 

If you don't want your account to be accessible while your kid plays on the "Not-Home-Console", or if you are not playing yourself, you might want to consider to let one kid buy the games and Gold membership and share it to the other console.

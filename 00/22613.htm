adultage say:

How to create a Microsoft Account or a primary Windows Live for your Windows Phone.

 

Have you bought a Windows Phone and you don't know how to use it? First of all you will need to create a new email address. 

 1.      Go to this web page http://account.live.com/ and click on Sign Up Now.

2.   It will appear a form where you need to insert all the required information. It is really important to insert a mobile phone number and an alternative email address. They will be really useful in the future for recovering the password or getting secure codes in order to access to your security or billing info.

In order to create a new email address click on the blue link “Or Get a New Email Address”, as it is shown on the picture below. I would suggest you to create an outlook account. 

Insert all the required data, included a mobile phone and a secondary email address. These information will make your life easier in the future to manage your Microsoft Account (Windows Phone 8) or primary Windows Live (Windows Phone 7). 

Note: basically there is not difference between Microsoft Account and primary Windows Live. Both of them are respectively the main email address of Windows Phone 8 and Windows Phone 7. 

3.  In the lower part of the same page, when you finish to full fill the form, there is a security procedure to create the email account for your Windows Phone. You must insert the blue letters in the space below. If they are not written clearly, or you can't read them well, you can click on the blue links New, in order to make the system change them; or Audio, so a voice will read the letters for you. 

Click on Create Account when you finish. 

4.  Once you finish go to http://www.xbox.com and click on Sign In. 

5. Sign In using the just created email account and related password. If you are asked for accepting the new terms of conditions and create a Xbox Live account, please do it; it is free and necessary.

6. Click on My Account, at the upper right corner of the web page. 

If you can't see My Account at the upper right corner of the web page, you can click on My Account below the picture of your Avatar (the human being image). 

7. Click on the green written Payment & Billing, below the icon of the credit card. 

8.  It will appear another web page which title is “Help Us Protect your Account”, see the emage below. Clicking on the arrow you will be able to select the way how to receive the secure secret temporary code. In the second space you have to confirm the last four digits of the selected phone number (if you have decided to receive the code by phone text message), or the email address you selected (if you have decided to receive the code by email). 

Click on Next then. 

9. Insert the received code in the proper space and click on Submit. 

10. Click on Billing Info. 

11. A window ,with two options at its lower side, will be opened. Click on the option which is at the right side, it could be Add Billing Info or Change Billing Address (if the billing info were already inserted). 

12. Full fill the form with all the required information. I would suggest to insert your mobile phone number, it will make managing your account easier in the future. 

Click on Next when you finish. 

13. Click on Next below the Billing Info resume. 

14. Close the window when you finish. 


Your new email account is ready to be used. 

Now is time to work on your Windows Phone. 

  1. Switch on the phone. 

  2. Take your phone and check the following data are correct: date, time, time zone, region. 

  3. On Date + Time disable Automatic, if the option is not present,it means it is set up on Manually already. 

  4. Connect your phone to Internet. 

  5. Flick the screen from the right to the left.

  6. Tap Emails + Accounts.

  7. Tap Microsoft Account (Windows Live if you have a Windows Phone 7).

  8. Insert your new email account and related password. 

  9. Tap on Sign In. 

Note: Don't set up more than one Windows Live account in your Windows Phone 7. 

If you find this post useful please click mark it. 

Bye.

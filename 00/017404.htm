The number of rows & the number of columns in an Excel worksheet are fixed at 1,048,576 x 16,384 respectively. Those you don't use cannot be removed.

dgdc---01 say:

As others have noted the Windows Phone 8007000E error is because of an error with Windows Phone not being able to handle Gmail contacts. Contact photos have specifically been reported to trigger the error. International characters may cause problems as well.

 

I was able to resolve my instance of the error by taking a rather drastic action. I exported all of my Gmail contacts to a file, deleted all contacts from Gmail, then imported the contacts file. The exported file, being a CSV file, did not include the offending contact photo(s). A less drastic action would be to identify large contact photos and delete/replace the photos.

 

If you choose this option be sure you select all of your contact upon export and verify you have all contacts in the exported file before deleting Gmail contacts.

 

My export included all of my Labels and after the import Gmail continued to use linked contact photos (e.g. contact's Google profile photos).

 

Exporting Gmail contacts, https://support.google.com/mail/answer/24911?hl=en
Import contacts as a CSV file, https://support.google.com/mail/answer/14024?hl=en

Office 2013 license is now transferable, you may refer to the following link for more information about the same:

Since you had already activated Office Professional Plus 2013 on the current computer, you can just uninstall it from Programs and Features on the current computer. To access Programs and Features menu follow these steps:  

  1. Press the Windows logo key + X to see a list of commands and options. (Basically a quick way to get to Programs and Features in Control Panel).
  2. Click Programs and Features. 

Once uninstalled you can reinstall Office Pro Plus 2013 on the new computer. In order to be able to install Office 2013 on your new Windows 8.1 computer, you will have to login to your Microsoft Account with your Microsoft Account credentials and then download and install Office 2013. Microsoft Account would be your Outlook.com or Hotmail.com or Live.com email account or any other email account which you have used for the Office 2013 purchase registration.

You may download and install Office 2013 from the My Account page by clicking on Install and following the onscreen instructions. After installation close the installation confirmation box and make sure to restart your computer.

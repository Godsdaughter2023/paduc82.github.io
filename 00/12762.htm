Right now, there is no button for a force to receive emails from your other mail servers. The time interval of the emails is based on the following reason:

  • The connection of the sender.
  • The connection of the sender's server to the Web.
  • The connection of the receiver's sender to the Web.
  • The connection of the receiver.

1. Sign in to Outlook.com.

2. At the upper-right, click the gear icon and select More mail settings.

3. Under "Managing your account", select Your email accounts.

4. Under "Add an email account", you can now check if your accounts are listed in send-and-receive accounts.

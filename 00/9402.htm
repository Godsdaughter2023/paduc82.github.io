Hi Julie,

Thank you for posting your concern in Microsoft community and we are happy to assist you regarding your issue in Outlook. For instructions about archived emails, please refer to the steps below on how to create (archived folder) and save (archived email):

 

Creating Archived folder:

  1. Log in to Outlook.com
  2. Click on the email you want to save as an archived  email.
  3. At the top, beside Delete choose Archive.
  4. Two options will appear: Click on Create a new folder, Called Archive and Archive message in selected folder.

Saving archived emails:

  1. Log in to Outlook.
  2. Click on the email you want to save as an archived      email.
  3. At the top, beside Delete choose Archive.
  4. If you choose the second option, Archive message in selected folder. You can locate your archived folder on your chosen folder e.g. New folder.

If you're still having issue finding your archived folder or emails, we suggest that you use the search email options at the upper right section to find your archived emails and then locate on what folder it was save. After finding the emails, remove the folder and then try again to save an archived email, you'll see now an option to create an archived folder.

If you need further assistance with this issue or any other Outlook related issues, get back to us and we will be glad to assist you.

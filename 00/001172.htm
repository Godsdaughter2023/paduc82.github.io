gloria gong msft say:

Hi Robertatkinson,

According to your description, only Outlook client cannot be opened after you update to Mojave OS and you can use other Office applications correctly. The troubleshooting step you take is to update all Office applications and after the update, only Outlook asks you to activate it and other Office applications are still in activated status.

To check your account, please click your profile picture and you will see the account address:

Then please reset the password of your account and save it to a safe place so that you can use the account to activate your Office applications next time if it is needed.

After you reset the password, please try to clear the stored Office keychains and resign in Office application to see if Outlook can be activated. (Please make sure that you have reset the password successfully after you clear the keychains, you can try to access Outlook Online to see the result)

Clear keychains: KeyChain Access in Applications> Search for Office> Delete all Office related keychains.

If the suggestion doesn't work for you, you can try to create a new user profile to launch Outlook to see the result:

macOS Sierra: Set up users, guests, and groups on your Mac

To better understand your situation and provide help for you, please help confirm some information:

1. What is the version of your Office applications?

Please check it by clicking top Outlook menu>About Microsoft Outlook> Version number.

2. Please capture a screenshot of the message that Outlook asks you to activate it.

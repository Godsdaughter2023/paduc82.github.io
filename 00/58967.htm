johnujlaky say:

Magicjack Contacts Not Downloading Problem

This is a fix for the Magicjack Contacts Not Downloading Problem, which occurred after the Anniversary Update.  I don't know where the original thread went, but I could not locate it. Anyway, the way to fix the no downloading of your Magicjack contacts is as follows:

1.) Right Click on Windows (Start Button)

2.) Choose Run

3.) Enter  INETCPL.CPL

4.) Click on the Advanced Tab of the resulting Window

5.) Scroll Down to The Security Section and make sure to check the box for SSL 3.0

6.) Click Apply, and then OK

7.) Restart your Magicjack and Contacts should Download

This worked for me. I can't take the credit for this solution however. It is the result of someone posting to my original post on the Magicjack Forum. They got lucky and actually talked to a tech at Magicjack who knew what they were doing. Just wanted to pass this along so maybe it will help someone else.

frederik long say:

A Windows 8.1 Command Prompt is fine.

You can copy most of your files in one fell swoop.

Your first task is to identify the drive letter used by Windows. It is usually C: but it could be any other letter but not drive X:. These commands will tell you:

dir C:\win*

dir D:\win*

dir E:\win*

etc.

I shall call this S: (Source). On this drive there will be a folder S:\Users\Jarp (or similar).

These commands tell you the letter for the external drive:

dir D:\
dir E:\
etc.
I shall call this T: (Target)

This long command will copy your files to the external disk:

for  %a  in (Desktop Downloads Music Pictures Videos)  do robocopy "S:\Users\Jarp\%a"  T:\%a  /e  /r:1  /w:1  /xj

The command will not copy your favorites. Their location is browser-sensitive.

The command may or may not copy your E-Mail files. Their location is mail client sensitive.

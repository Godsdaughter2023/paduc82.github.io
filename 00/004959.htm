There are 60 minutes in an hour so in A3 use,

=MOD(MOD(A1,1440),60)

That is strange, but I don't think there's any way to fix it from within Word -- it's strictly a question of the "Hide extensions" setting in Windows Explorer (actually, the value in the registry that
represents that setting). If you take a document from one of the misbehaving computers and open it on a normally behaving computer, click on the field in the footer, and press F9 to update the field,

On the misbehaving computers, it may help to check the "Hide extensions" box, click the Apply button, then uncheck it and click Apply again. That should write the correct value into the registry,
after which Word should behave normally.

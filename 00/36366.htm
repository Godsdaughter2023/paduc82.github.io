Sorry about your problem there.  Can you elaborate on the above text I have quoted please.  

Textures going crazy sounds like a hardware issue with the console's APU (Accelerated Processing Unit).  These graphics issues can manifest in different ways depending on what is being run.  Early failings can appear as dots or squares on textures, or the whole screen showing wrong colours. later or more faulty units can show massive textures appearing on the sides or emanating from the middle of the screen.  These anomalous graphics can come and go depending on what's happening on screen.  This is a graphics hardware problem, changing HDMI cables will have no effect.

This is caused either by heat build up in the console, or failing hardware. 

Do you have your console in a place with good ventilation? 

Could you provide us with some screenshots of what you are seeing please?

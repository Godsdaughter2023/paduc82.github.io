hansv mvp say:

With a binary number of up to 16 bits in A1:

 

=IF(LEN(A1)>8 , BIN2HEX(LEFT(A1, LEN(A1)-8)), "") & RIGHT("00" & BIN2HEX(RIGHT(A1, 8)), 2)

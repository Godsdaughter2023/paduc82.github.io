Changing font colors was removed in Windows 8 and up.

If you know the RGB number color codes which you can get at: http://cloford.com/resources/colours/500col.htm you can change them in the registry.

  • On left pane navigate to HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Control Panel\Colors

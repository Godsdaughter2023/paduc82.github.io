luke moraga say:

Let's say the list of customer IDs are in col A, and invoices are in col B. The ID you want to look up is in C2.

In D2:

=IFERROR(INDEX(B:B,SMALL(IF(A$1:A$3000=$C$2,ROW(A$1:A$3000)),ROWS(D$2:D2))),"")

Confirm this formula as an array using Ctrl+Shift+Enter, not just Enter. Copy the formula as far down as you think you'll need. Each cell with this formula will return the 1st, 2nd, etc. matching value.

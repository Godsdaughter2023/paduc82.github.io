DisplayPort or DigitalPort is different from Digital Visual Interface (DVI). DisplayPort is newer than HDMI and available to the latest model of laptop, while Digital Visual Interface (DVI) is the oldest standard port, however, still present on some monitors, laptops, and desktop graphics cards.

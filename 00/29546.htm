How do I Disable Touch Screen on Windows 10?

I have a Dell pc/ tablet, but most of my touch screen has broken (due to a crack) but often it spazzes out in one area, so I need help on how to turn off the touch screen for windows 10, please and thanks!

a. user say:

Hi, 

Welcome to Microsoft Community. Your interest in Windows 10 is much appreciated. 

Method 1:

To disable touch screen you may try the steps and check if it helps.

a. Press Windows key + x.

b. Type Control Panel.

c. Go to Hardware and Sound.

d. Click onPen and touch.

e. Click thetouch tab.

f. Disable use your finger as an input.

Method 2:
If the issue persists, I would suggest you to try the steps and check if it helps.

a)    Press the Windows logo key + X.

b)    Select “Device Manager” from the list.

c)    Click the little arrow next to “Human Interface Devices” to expand the list.

d)    Click the touch screen driver.

e)    Right-click, and select “Disable” from the list.

f)     Click yes on the dialog box that asks if you are sure you want to disable the touch screen driver.

Note: When you want enable touch screen follow the same process and select Enable, when right-clicking the touch screen driver.

Refer to the link for additional information.

Touch: swipe, tap, and beyond
http://windows.microsoft.com/en-IN/windows-8/touch-swipe-tap-beyond

Keep us posted if you face any issues on windows in future. We will be glad to help you.

jomel_g say:

Hello

Question 1: with the new Skype the audio does not sync well with video: lipsynchronity is poor. What to do?

Answer: This can happen when either your Internet connection or your contact's is not meeting the bandwidth required by Skype. Please visit this support page for more information: How much bandwidth does Skype need?

Question 2: how to remove / delete conversation history?

Answer: There's no option to delete your conversation history in this version at once. You need to manually delete them by contact on your Chats list. The steps on how you can remove your conversations per contact are as follows:

  1. Right click the conversation you want to remove from your Chats list.
  2. Select Delete Chat.

Question 3: the contact list used to show icons PLUS name. Now only icons are shown on the left. Does an option exist to have both again in the contact list?

Answer: There should be names as well aside from the profile icon of your contact on the Chats list (left hand panel of the app) when the application is maximized. Please adjust the application's size for the names of your contacts to appear.

Question 4: In the conversation layout I used to have messages from sender and receiver UNDER each other. Now one part (sender) is left, the other(receiver) is right. Is this the new an unchangeable way to display the conversation?

Answer: Conversation messages should appear under each other depending on which came first. The messages you are sending will show on the right hand side of the message box, while your contacts' will show on the left hand side of it.

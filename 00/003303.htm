=IF(ISNA(MATCH($C3,Empty.xlsx.!$B$2:$B$150,0)),"",INDEX(Empty.xlsx!$B$2:$B$150,MATCH($C3,Empty.xlsx!$B$2:$B$150,0)))

If Empty is a worksheet, you don't need .xlsx after it.

 

You can replace your formula with

 

=IFERROR(VLOOKUP($C3,Empty!$B$2:$C$150,1,FALSE),"")

 

And to retrieve the corresponding date:

 

=IFERROR(VLOOKUP($C3,Empty!$B$2:$C$150,2,FALSE),"")

steven_b say: Greetings 鹏军杨,

The Surface Pro and Surface China Edition offered in China do not implement a TPM Security Device Chip. However, you can use Software encryption:

To encrypt the Windows 8 Pro Chinese operating system:

1. Go to the Start Screen

2. Type: Group Policy

3. Choose Settings

4. Touch or click on Edit Group Policy

5. Navigate to Computer Configuration/Administrative Templates/Windows Components/BitLocker Drive Encryption/Operating System Drives

6. Once there, double-click the option “require additional authentication at startup”. 

7. Choose the option Enabled and in the options pane select the checkbox Allow BitLocker without a compatible TPM.

8. Click OK

9. You can now open BitLocker and encrypt the OS drive. 

Note: that the user (you) will be required to have a USB drive on-hand to store the BitLocker key and will be required to enter the key every time Windows boots. (Either by inserting the drive with the key file or manually entering the key.)

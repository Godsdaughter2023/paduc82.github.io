Yes. Unfortunately that does occur with quite a lot of operations in Excel. Where the link is a simple equals then no problems.

  • Add a sheet to the workbook containing the formula.
  • Copy the Data from the linked workbook.
  • On the added worksheet in the workbook containing the formula Paste Special -> Paste Link (Paste Link button is at bottom left of the Paste special dialog)

You can try the following steps to see the outcome.

You need to configure your Outlook.com (live.com) account using IMAP (no need to delete your existing EAS account. Once done, you can easily move whatever info to the IMAP folders which will reflect the exact same data as seen in the EAS folder set. Once done copying/moving your data, you can simply remove the IMAP account.

Strongly suggest that you suppress auto send/receive for the IMAP account before moving/copying your data so as to avoid the sync from going on while data is being moved to the folder. Once the process is completed, do a manual S/R on the Imap account and everything will then get uploaded - your EAS account will show everything that was uploaded.

Hey there, Mottley. Like Mrs. Joust said, mobile hotspots are not a recommended (or supported) connection type for your Xbox. This is for two reasons. How do I allocate unused disk space to my existing C: drive to - Microsoft Community

paddydelaney say:

How do I allocate unused disk space to my existing C: drive to leave me with one HDD?

I have an existing hard drive (C:) to which I would like to add the 120GB of unallocated space on my netbook. I'm using Windows 7 starter and so far all the answers I've found are related to partitions. I already have the small drive labelled "System" and another small drive labelled "Healthy recovery partition". I would like to have all my other available memory allocated to the C: drive but I'm having great difficulty achieving this. I should point out that I'm pretty technically inept and so I would really appreciate any help you could give me. Thanks.
hairyfool say:

You can only do this if the unallocated space is contiguous with the original C drive.

 

Press the Windows key +R to open the Run box and enter diskmgmt.msc to open Disk Manager.

 

In the lower box check the layout of the drive. If the "Recovery" partition lies between the C "System" drive and your unallocated space then the drive cannot be extended into it.

 

Your alternative is to create another partition in the unallocated space and use the Location tab in many of the user profile directories to relocate them to the new partition to free up space on the C drive.

This quest will be given when you first enter Michael Angelo's Workshop, located in the southwest corner of the New Vegas Strip, opposite the NCR Embassy. You'll be instructed to talk to Michael Angelo, or Sheldon Weintraub, brother of the chick who hangs out at Vault 21. He'll seem pretty stressed, which the player can make worse by passing a few skill checks. The player can continue to bully him for Xp and Caps through a 50 Speech skill check, or use a 55 Medicine check to start the quest. You don't have to pass the skill check, just select that option.

It appears that Michael (*Cough* Sheldon) is running out of inspiration, and will enlist the player to go find him some, as his isloated time in the Vault has made him "Scared" of the outside world. He wants the player to take pictures of signs around the Mojave to give him inspiration. To do so, he'll gove the player 24 Camera Film and a very special Codac R9000. You'll need to take pictures of these five landmarks:

  • The Dinosaur Thermometer in Novac
  • The Bison sign on the roof of the Bison Steve Hotel in Primm
  • The sign above the entrance of Camp McCarren
  • The sign above the main doors of HELIOS One
  • The big bottle at the Sunset Sarsaparilla Headquarters

When you have taken a picture of one landmark, you can return to recieve upto 200 Caps. After all pictures have been taken you can return and recieve 500 - 5000 caps, based on quality of photo.

Successful photos earn 100, 125, 150, 175, and 200 Xp immediately. Completion of this quest results in an additional 200 Xp, giving the player a total of 950 Xp.


Unmarked Quest: Suites You, Sarah

Unmarked Quest: Suites You, Sarah

To start this quest, the player will have to talk to Sarah Weintraub at Vault 21, and after following through the right dialougue tree, you can offer to fetch Vault jumpsuits for her. Vault 11 suits can be found in the Vault 11 Living Quarters, around 20 or so can be found in there. Vault 3 suits can be purchased from Mick & Ralph, they'll have around 1-4, but they don't restock them. For a full list of locations, follow this link.

After bringing her the 30 Vault Suits, upon revisting she'll mention "missing" the player charcter. You will be then able to, you know, woohoo, with her as you both "Catch Up" with one another. Doing this will give the player a "Well-Rested" bonus, which is no longer surprisingly absent from the beds she usually offers. Also, after passing two speech checks, 50 and 75 respectively, she will agree to such acts after recieving suits, inclusing Boomer suits.

You have to go to your room (which is the room left to Sarah's room) after work hours and she teleports to your room immediately. If you also put a sexy sleepwear in her inventory, she wears it for you. If you remove her vault 21 jumpsuit while she is still sleeping she will put on any other form of clothing you put in her inventory but won't remove it again.

Microsoft is aware of the problem affecting a number of users running a full scan with MSE.

Most of the lockups are occurring in two folders:

\Windows\Winsxs\backup and/or \windows

Installer\$PatchCache$\Manager\

At this time, there are two workarounds while we await a solution from Microsoft which will probably come in the form of updated signatures or an update to the scanning engine.

  1. Exclude the above folders from being scanned in MSE settings
  2. Change your scheduled scan to a quick scan

As long as real time protection is enabled, a full scan on a frequent basis is usually overkill. A quick scan looks for malware in the most common locations. A full scan has to touch every file. This is a known issue for Fusion Drive Macs.

Hi Kathy,

Welcome to Microsoft community and thank you for asking the question.

As per the description provided, it looks like you are unable to send emails using Outlook 2011. Also as I understand the problem started after changing your ISP from Tiscali to BT Internet.

If that is correct, these issues occur as your current ISP, that is BT Internet, may not allow sending of emails from the Tiscali email server. It is not possible for BT Internet to monitor the SMTP traffic of Tiscali.

As a work around you may change the Outgoing server for the Tiscali email account to use the BT Internet services SMTP server, and then authenticate the same using the BT Internet email account.

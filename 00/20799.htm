marites_p say:

Hi All,

Q:  Some posts say it IS possible to delete/merge accounts, others say it's not.    

A:  First, it is not possible to merge two Skype accounts. You can only merge one Skype account and Microsoft account. Also, there is a change in the process in merging a Skype account to a Microsoft account. To merge accounts, the email address or phone number cannot be an existing Microsoft, but can be a newly created Microsoft during the workflow.

Q:  There are some that say in removing a Skype account, I'll also delete all of the associated Microsoft accounts. Since the Skype is linked to her Hotmail account, I don't want to risk deleting her email account.

A: If your Skype account is linked to a Microsoft account, then it is considered a unified sign in credentials across all Microsoft products. With that, once you delete your Skype account by default the associated Microsoft email will be deleted and you will lose access to all Microsoft services such as Outlook.com, Office Online, One Drive, Xbox Live, or Windows.

Should you have further questions, do not hesitate to contact us back.

You could insert an empty column to the right of the column with the data.

=LEFT(A2,FIND("-",A2))

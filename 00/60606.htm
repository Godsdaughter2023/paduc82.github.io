I use this to watch TV on my monitor.   Sometimes the picture screen is square when I open the player- which I prefer and sometimes it is rectangular.  All the settings I modify don't seem to change this, and it appears random.  Can anyone help?  Thanks.
chris cupler say:

Media Center will do some on-the-fly switching of its Zoom setting, depending on what you're watching.  Example:  watching a program that's marked as 'HD' will (should, but nearly always does) switch you to Zoom 1 (normal), which is really no zoom at all.  An HD program is assumed (generally correct) to be widescreen; therefore, no zoom needed.

If a show is in standard-def (and, 4:3 aspect ratio, as opposed to widescreen's 16:9 or 16:10), it'll be 'square' (centered, black bars on both sides) when in Zoom 1.  The other three do some stretching (and, Zoom 4 does a pretty good job of filling the screen from a 4:3 source.)

Either way, you can change what you see:  hit the i (or info) button on your remote (or, with your mouse:  right-click) to bring up the quick menu, and scroll over tozoom.  Change zoom setting as desired.

HTH,
Chris

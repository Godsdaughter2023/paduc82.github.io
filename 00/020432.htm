john korchok say:

No, there is no such feature in Office for Mac. But many customizations are saved in files that you can carry from one installation to another.

  • Macros, AutoText and styles are kept in Users/YourUserName/Library/Group Containers/UBF8T346G9.Office/User Content/Templates/Normal.dotm.
  • AutoCorrect entries are in Users/YourUserName/Library/Group Containers/UBF8T346G9.Office/Microsoft Office ACL [YourLanguageName].
  • Custom dictionaries are stored in Users/YourUserName/Library/Group Containers/UBF8T346G9.Office/Custom Dictionary.

To open your user Library folder, hold down the Alt key while clicking on the Go menu and choosing Library.

Delete

Edit:

Got it. I tried all of the recommended methods, but what finally worked was going into regedit and doing the following:

  1. Press CTRL + F and enter 0006F03A-0000-0000-C000-000000000046 into the search box, then click Find Next.
  2. Delete the key by right-clicking on the entry and click Delete.
  3. Press F3 to repeat the search until no more registry keys are found.

chris de herrera say: Hi,
With ActiveSync 4.5, Microsoft has removed the option to specify which devices wins when there is a conflict.   There is no option to go back and allow a device to win.

As for the notes, you can lose them for a variety of reasons:
1. The text is too long and the will be truncated.  Notes are limited to approximately 16k in length.
2. The notes contain images or other files that are too large.
3. The notes are corrupted.  Run scanpast on your PC to fix them.

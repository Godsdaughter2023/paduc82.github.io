john korchok say:

Modifier keys constrain the drawing tools, making it easier to draw exact angles than the freehand technique. For lines and arrows, hold down the Shift key to force lines to vertical, horizontal or 45 degree angles. The Shift key also draws symmetrical shapes, so it allows you to draw a perfect square using the rectangle shape.

The Control key (if you have one) draws shapes outward from a center point and lets you resize shapes around the center point.

Entering shapes sizes and positions using the numeric fields in the Format Shape pane give you precise, repeatable results. The Shape Format Align tool is very useful for exact alignment and distribution. The Selection Pane is excellent for changing object visibility and stacking order.

PowerPoint diagrams can be as accurate as those from any other drawing program, as long as 1/100" measurement increments are sufficient.

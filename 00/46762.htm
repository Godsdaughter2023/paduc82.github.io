andyczuk say:

After trawling the internet and Asus forums, a resolution...

For those who have an ASUS laptop (ROG Strix 502VS in this case) with a Samsung nVME SSD drive (this bit is important, this issue doesnt occur on the same laptop model with standard SATA drives), go into the BIOS Advanced settings and disable SMART disk monitoring. I have no idea why this worked but it did. For some reason it seems SMART monitoring is interfering with the FIRST_BOOT phase on the Windows install.

After turning off SMART the 1st attempt at installing the update worked perfectly.

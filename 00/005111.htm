Hello

Thank you for reaching out to the community!

Yes it is possible to make a conference audio call up to 25 contacts (24 + yourself) in one go. It is advisable that you add the contacts to one group first before initiating the video conference or initiate the video call first then add the participants. Here are the steps:

  1. From your Calls  list select the new call button. 
  2. Select all the participants you want to call. 
  3. Select the Call button. 
    Note: All group calls will always start as audio calls, but you can turn your video on during the call by selecting the video      button. 

However the maximum number of video streams (video feed that you can see on your window) would vary depending on the platform you are using. If you are using a Windows device, preferably a desktop. This should support 5 video streams that you can view on the conference and you can have the option to choose on whose video feed you are going to view.

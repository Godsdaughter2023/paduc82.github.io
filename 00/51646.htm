Hi Samar,

 

 

We are glad to know that you have chosen Windows 8 and thank you so much for posting your query in Microsoft Community.

 

You would like to know on how to purchase an App from the Windows 8 store for your kids’ accounts without associating a credit card with their account.

 

You can definitely do this and get your kids their favorite apps or games in their account.

 

When you purchase an App from the Windows store from your account, it can be installed on 5 different machines. You can also install that app for any other users on those 5 machines. The pain is that you need to install the app for each user that you want to use that app.

 

 

For this, you can purchase the App from the Windows Store from your account and then login to your child’s account and go to the Windows Store and follow these steps to install the App that you have purchased, on your child’s account.

 

A.       From the Windows Store, press “Windows Logo” + “C” and click on “Settings”.

 

B.       In Settings, click on “My Account”.

 

C.       Click on “Sign Out” to sign out from your child’s account and then re-login to your account.

 

D.       You will see all the apps you have purchased from your account and you can install it on your child’s account.

 

E.        Repeat this for each account on the PC that you want to install the app for.

 

 

As a reference, you can check the answer posted by “TheOldWiseMan” in the below thread:

 

http://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/forum/windows_8-windows_store/how-do-i-buy-apps-for-kids-accounts/4fbff2e7-50f4-4e35-969f-de0efab3be72

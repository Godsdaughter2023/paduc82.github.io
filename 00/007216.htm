I suggest that you set your junk filter settings to Standard if your settings is currently set to Exclusive. Follow the steps below and check if it helps:

  1. Sign in to preventing junk email.
  2. Under preventing junk email, select filters and reporting.
  3. Choose a junk email should be in standard.
  4. Delete junk email should be in ask the sender to remove you from mailing lists and automatically block spammers.
  5. Block content from unknown senders should be in Show attachments, pictures, and links for senders with good reputations.
  6. Click on save.

The best way to make sure that emails from your contacts are directed to your inbox is to add them to your contacts list. To do this, please find a message from your contacts, open it and click the Add to contacts link that you'll find in the message header beside the sender’s name.

In addition, please remove and re-add their email addresses to your Safe senders list to prevent their messages from getting into your Junk folder by following these steps:

  1. From your Inbox, click the gear icon on the upper right corner beside your name.
  2. Select options.
  3. Under preventing junk email, click Safe and blocked senders.
  4. Click Safe senders.
  5. Enter the email address or domain in the box labeled Sender or domain to mark as safe: and click Add to list.
  6. Make sure that you enter the correct email address/domain.

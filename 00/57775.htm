i) To Disable the Volume Icon - Select (dot) Enabled and click on OK.
ii) To Enable the Volume Icon - Select (dot) Disabled or Not Configured and click on OK.

i) To Disable the Network Icon - Select (dot) Enabled and click on OK.
ii) To Enable the Netwok Icon - Select (dot) Disabled or Not Configured and click on OK.

 

 

    g. For the Power (battery) Icon - In the right pane, right click on Remove the battery meter and click on Edit.
NOTE: This will remain grayed out, even when enabled, unless your computer is a laptop or has a UPS battery backup connected to it.

i) To Disable the Power Icon - Select (dot) Enabled and click on OK.
ii) To Enable the Power Icon - Select (dot) Disabled or Not Configured and click on OK.

 

     h. For the Action Center Icon - In the right pane, right click on Remove the Action Center icon and click on Edit.

i) To Disable the Action Center Icon - Select (dot) Enabled and click on OK.
ii) To Enable the Action Center Icon - Select (dot) Disabled or Not Configured and click on OK.

 

i.      When done, close the Local Group Policy Editor.

j.     Log off and log on, or restart the computer to fully apply.

myall_blues say:

You can also do it without having a formula in the cell.

Select from C5 to wherever your cells to be coloured go to.

Conditional Formatting->New Rule->Use a formula to determine which cells to format

In the 'Format values where this formula is true' box enter the following formula:

=AND(YEAR($B5)=C$3,MONTH($B5)>=RIGHT(C$4,1)*3-2,MONTH($B5)<=RIGHT(C$4,1)*3)

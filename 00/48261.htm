Hi Rayne,

The "Go back to an earlier build" feature can be used if you upgrade your operating system from Windows 7/8/8.1 to Windows 10. You may restore your computer to factory settings. To do so, please follow the steps under Reset your PC on this link.

The Windows.old folder is automatically created when you upgrade your operating system. You can retrieve the files from your old operating system within 30days in Windows 10 1511 and 10 days in Windows 10 1607. This folder will be automatically deleted after the said periods.

Get back to us if you need further assistance.

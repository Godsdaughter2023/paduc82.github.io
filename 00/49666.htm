rgharper911 say:

This is a change in behaviour since Windows 7.  Devices are logically disconnected but power to the device is not turned off as it was in earlier versions of Windows.  Why?  I don't know for certain but a good guess might be that this is more friendly towards external hard drives - the logical disconnect lets the drive flush buffers and such while power is still applied to the drive.  Or it may be a charging issue for devices - disconnect the device but it can still charge while connected.

suzanne s. barnhill say:

Is the user with the problem using the exact same printer driver as the others? If the right side is the trailing edge, this may well be an issue with the unprintable area, which may vary from one driver to another but are often close to half an inch for the trailing edge (this is required for paper handling).

You can ascertain the unprintable area in a blank document by setting all four margins to 0". When you click OK, you'll get an offer to "fix" the settings. Accept the offer and note what margins Word sets; these are the minimum margins defined by the printer driver. You can then Cancel out of Page Setup without saving.

The printer may have a "borderless" setting; if so, this should be tried (though I believe it often requires a special carrier or applies only to smaller paper sizes).

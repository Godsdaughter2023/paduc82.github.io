Please be informed that recovering deleted emails should be done through Hotmail (web), please refer to the instruction below on how to retrieve your deleted messages:

 

In the left pane of your Hotmail window, click the Deleted folder, scroll to the bottom of the window, and then click Recover deleted messages.

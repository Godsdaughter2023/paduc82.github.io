If your handset is working fine without the display & if you are able to make/receive calls from the handset & if you want to copy the contacts from the Phone to Sim Card, you can follow these steps:

 

1.) Switch Off the handset & Switch it On (So that it would be in the standby screen when Switched On)

2.) Press the left selection key (Menu)

3.) When you press the Menu, the 1st option on the Screen would be the Messages,

4.) Scroll Down once, so that the Contacts would be the highlighted.

5.) Then press the Left selection key (Select), so that the Contacts would open.

6.) Once Contacts is opened, the options available would be: 1) Search, 2)Add Contact, 3)Delete, 4) Copy 5)Settings, 6)Speed Dials, 7)No Screening

7.) Scroll down according to the options & press the Left Selection key (Select), So that the Copy option is opened,

8.) Now the options available when you press the Left Selection key (Select) is "From Phone to Sim Card"

9.) Now press the Left Selection Key again & the 1st option would be "One by One" & "All",

10.) Scroll down once, so that it would highlight the "All" option & press the Left Selection key, it would highlight on "Keep Original" & the 2nd option is "Delete Original",

11.) Select any of the option & the contacts is copied to the Sim Card.

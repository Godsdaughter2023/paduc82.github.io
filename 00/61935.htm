auggy say:

The minidump files show DRIVER_IRQL_NOT_LESS_OR_EQUAL and IRQL_NOT_LESS_OR_EQUAL stop errors all being caused by the e1k6232.sys.

The e1k6232.sys is the Intel Gigabit Adapter driver.

The same errors have been reported elsewhere such as in the following link:

http://www.sevenforums.com/crashes-debugging/98926-bsod-during-video-playback.html

You appear to be running the latest e1k6232.sys driver (dated Apr 6, 2010) available from Dell.

You also appear to have the Cisco VPN client installed and that may be contributing to the issue.

Has the Cisco VPN client always been installed?

One thing you could try is to roll back to the original Windows driver for the Intel NIC.

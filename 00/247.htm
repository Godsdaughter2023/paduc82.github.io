Hi Stephane,

 

For your problem, do you mean that you want to detect language automatically in Outlook? If so, you can refer to the steps below to enable this feature. As far as I know, it is enabled by default.

2.       On the Review tab, in the Language group, click Language.

3.       Click Set Proofing Language.

c) After you figure out how to create it manually, turn on the macro recorder and do it again, then turn off the macro recorder when finished.

  • ListStyle set to 1-fmLitStyleOption
  • RowSource set to the  table created in d.

g) Add OK and Exit buttons: (name then cmdOK and cmdExit)

h) Copy the recorded macro to your UserForm's cmdOK_Click routine.

I) Modify cmdOK_Click to populate the Advanced Filter's Criteria Range with items selected in ListBox with * on either side as shown in Example a) . Then perform the filter.  The advance filter's VBA will look something like this:

[MyDataRange].AdvancedFilter Action:=xlFilterInPlace, CriteriaRange:=[MyCriteriaRange]

bernie deitrick say:

The value in A2 is a number, formatted as that time.

The best thing is to keep the value in A2 as a formatted number and use

=DAY(A2)&" days, "&HOUR(A2)&" hours, "&MINUTE(A2)&" minutes, "&SECOND(A2)&" seconds"

If you wanted total whole hours, instead of days and then hours, you would use

=DAY(A2)*24+HOUR(A2)&" hours, "&MINUTE(A2)&" minutes, "&SECOND(A2)&" seconds"

Or just

=INT(A2*24) &" hours, "&MINUTE(A2)&" minutes, "&SECOND(A2)&" seconds"

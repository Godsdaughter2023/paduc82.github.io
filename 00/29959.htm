You should not get disk errors, as it indicates a hardware problem with your hard disk.  It does sound like your hard disk is failing or has some bad spots that ought to be marked by chkdsk as unreadable.  You could do a surface scan of it with chkdsk (will take a long time to complete).  You could also check in your BIOS settings to ensure that SMART monitoring is enabled for your drive if that is an option.

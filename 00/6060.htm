macropod say:
Word provides no means for formatting the page #s in a different colour from the heading text. To do so, you would have to convert the TOC to text (e.g. by deleting it and pressing Ctrl-Shift-F9, or Ctrl-6), then apply the colouring to the page #s. Do note that doing so means the TOC will no longer update and you won't be able to click on the entries and have them act as hyperlinks. If you really need that functionality, you can replace the converted TOC entries with coloured cross-reference and page reference fields.

I don't see anything wrong in this screenshot. Looks like there's just a simple misunderstanding at your end regarding the exact meaning of the Date column in Explorer. There are columns named Date taken and Date created/modified/accessed - I hope you know that? Date taken is the EXIF date stored by your camera, scanner etc. in the image, whereas the other 3 are file system attributes. Date is actually a combo deal that by default shows the EXIF date i.e. Date taken if that tag exists in a file, otherwise it shows Date modified.

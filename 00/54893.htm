    I suspect that the reason that you are seeing the warning message is that the Surface Pro 3 does NOT support Connected Standby (now renamed by Microsoft to "InstantGo"). CS is needed so that the Alarms app continues to run in a low power mode. PCs without CS can only go into traditional Sleep or Hibernation modes - and in these modes, the Alarms app doesn't run.

    - the warning...

    Of course, Microsoft still hasn't got the design right. So you effectively can't set an alarm to wake you up in the morning and have more than 4 hours of sleep...

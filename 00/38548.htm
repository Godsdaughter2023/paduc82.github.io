This happens when your Skype and Office 365 accounts were not successfully updated. That's why when you access Skype using your Office 365 account, it created a new Skype profile under that email.

A possible reason why you're not seeing your Skype Credits on the account under your Office 365 email is because those Skype Credits are under your original Skype account. Having said that, you will need to recover that original Skype account of yours by resetting your password.

Since there is no option to merge Skype to existing Microsoft accounts anymore, I'm afraid you can only use the 60 minutes of calling on the Skype account connected to your Office 365 account and not with your original account.

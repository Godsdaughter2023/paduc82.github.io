thomas peel say:

Problem Solved:

When I installed Windows, I disabled every option in the privacy panel in the settings, including the microphone app thus not allowing any app to access my microphone, once disabled, everything works properly.

So to do it (on Windows 10):

 - Click on the windows icon, or press it on your keyboard, it opens the start menu

 - Go into settings through there (the little crank icon)

 - Click on Privacy

 - Then Microphone

 - And in this tab, allow the access for the apps (you can also choose which apps has access)

nlabran say: I was able to finally resolve this. I found out through testing that this problem only existed while using Chrome. Didn't have the video issue in other browsers. So, after doing a little research, I tried turning off the hardware accelerator in Chrome and the problem went away. Go figure.

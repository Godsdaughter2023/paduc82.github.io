What does it mean to “archive” something?

Archiving is a way to free up space on your phone. An “archived” item remains in the archive section of your My Phone account, but is removed from the phone. We saw a similar problem with a client deck where pasted text came out bold and subscript even though this was not the default text setting.

Your problem may of course be entirely different but in this case saving as HTML and then reopening and saving as pptx worked. In 2010 this can only be done with code and in 2013 it doesn't work at all.

If you have 2010 available you can try this code ON A COPY. You might find that some new features are lost or text sizes change in some cases.

Sub fixme()
Dim opres As Presentation
Dim opresFix As Presentation
Set opres = ActivePresentation
opres.SaveCopyAs Environ("TEMP") & "\fix.htm", ppSaveAsHTML
Set opresFix = Presentations.Open(Environ("TEMP") & "\fix.htm")
opresFix.SaveAs opres.Path & "\Fixed.pptx"
End Sub

Thanks for the reply. In regard to pre-orders made using a credit/debit card, your card will not be charged until approximately 10 days prior to the release of the game, as our Ambassador Njal the Blue stated, but a hold may be put on the amount of money required. When you pre-order, there will be a place marker put onto your games/apps until release of the game but the content won't be available for use until the payment goes through and the release officially takes place. 

The only time you would be charged immediately for a pre-order would be when you use Microsoft account credit. As you chose to pre-order using a credit/debit card (based on the image you provided), and the release has not yet happened, you would likely be denied a refund as Microsoft has yet to withdraw the money from that account. As previously suggested by my fellow XBF, you can request a call back from Xbox Support and request that the pre-order be cancelled prior to its release, which would then tell the system not to charge your card. From there, it would be up to your financial institution backing the card to release the hold, dependent on the time frame according to their terms.

One usual way is to look up the unique key, eg Stock Code using a vlookup,
then extract the rest of the data associated with it

Assume your source table as posted in Sheet1's cols A to D
In Sheet2,
you have the DV for Stock Codes in A2 down
Place in B2:
=IF($A2="","",VLOOKUP($A2,Sheet1!$A:$D,COLUMNS($A:A)+1,0))
Copy across to D2, fill down as far as required

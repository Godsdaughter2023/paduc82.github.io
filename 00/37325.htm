To change the language of your PC, please follow the steps below:

  • Press Windows key+i to open the Settings.
  • Click Time & language.
  • Click Region & language.
  • Under Languages, click Add a language.
  • Choose the language you want to add.
  • Select the new language, then click Set as default.

To change the language of welcome screen and New User Account, please follow the steps below:

  • Check Welcome screen and system accounts and New user accounts.
  • Click OK.
  • Click OK again.
  • Reboot your computer.

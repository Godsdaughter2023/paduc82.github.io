ben m. schorr -bschorr say:

I think you may have the wrong idea on what "Open Backups" does. That doesn't create a backup - that takes you to the backup folders. The folders you're subsequently seeing ARE your backups.  Don't go in there unless you need to restore something from your backups.

What you should do is go to File | Options | Save & Backup and set the Backup folder to be on your external hard drive.

Then just go back to using OneNote as usual.  If you ever have to restore something from backup THAT'S the time to go to File | Open Backups.  Otherwise...just stay in your primary folders.

... And there are no Unicode characters for subscript numbers, so you need to apply subscript formatting to the ordinary characters.

Actually there are true Unicode characters (not high ASCII) in the range of U+2080 to U+2089 for subscript (denominator) numbers; e.g. H₂O₂.

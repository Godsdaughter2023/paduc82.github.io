Hi Denmarfl,

This happens because the web site icons are stored with your web surfing history in the Internet Explorer’s History folders.

If you have set Internet Explorer set to delete your history on exit or only maintain a few days history the icons on the desktop will be deleted when the history that contains the icon is deleted.

Refer to these steps and check.

  1. Right click on the offending icon (blue and green globe, etc.)
  2. When the window opens left click on "Properties"
  3. Left click on "Change Icon"
  4. At the top right hand corner of the window left click on "Browse"
  5. Scroll down until you see "url.dll"
  6. Left double-click on "url.dll," then...
  7. Left click "Apply" then left click "Okay"

Try this work around to create a shortcut:

  1. Open the website from the web browser and copy the web address (Ctrl + C – Copy)
  2. Right click on the blank desktop, click onnew and then on Shortcut.
  3. Type the location or pasted the copied the web address (Ctrl + V – paste)
  4. Click on Next, type the name for shortcut.
  5. Click on Finish.

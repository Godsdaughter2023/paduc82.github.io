1) Open device manager
2) Right click on the USB to serial adapter
3) Select properties
4) Select the details tab
5) Look at the hardware IDs

For example, if you see USB\VID_0409&PID_005A&REV_0100, you would search online for USB\VID_0409&PID_005A.  You may be able to find the drivers for a similar device that will work with yours.

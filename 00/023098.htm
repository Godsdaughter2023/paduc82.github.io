1. Might or might not work, but...

    Go to the sign-in page and type the first letter of the username into the name field. If a drop-down AutoComplete list appears, put the pointer on the username and hit the Delete key.

2. Also might or might not work...

    Control Panel> User accounts> your account> Manage your credentials> check all categories. If the site is listed, remove it.

3. There were easier, less destructive ways to do this in earlier versions of IE,

    but those methods don't work in IE11...

    Internet Options> Browsing history> Delete> delete Passwords and Cookies.

    If you do not have passwords backed up, you can import them into Chrome

and you will have a list you can view. You can't import them back into IE, 

but there will be a viewable list.

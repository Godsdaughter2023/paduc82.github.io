Sacre Bleu!

Yes, you are able to change the language on your Surface and input method of the keyboard. You can select another language at anytime through your settings option. Try this (hopefully you can translate this from the French on your Surface): 

  1. Swipe in from the right edge of the screen, and then tap Settings. (If you're using a mouse, point to the lower-right corner of the screen, and then click Settings).
  2. In the lower right hand corner tap or click Change PC settings.
  3. On the left side tap or click General.
  4. Scroll down to Language
  5. Tap or click Language Preferences, click or tap Add a language, find and add English (United States).  

NOTE: In order to see the changes made, you will need to restart your Surface.

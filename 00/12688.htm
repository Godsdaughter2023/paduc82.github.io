Mail Merge record loss

When creating labels the mail merge drops the record at the page break before continuing on the next page

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:
In the mail merge main document, there should be a «NextRecord» field before the first merge field in each of the labels on the sheet EXCEPT for the first label.  If you are missing a record, you may have a «NextRecord» field before the first merge field on the sheet in which case, you would also be missing the label for the first record in the data source, or you may have the «NextRecord» fields after the merge fields on all of the labels on the sheet.  If that is what you have the «NextRecord» fields after the merge fields, delete that field from the last label on the sheet.  If you have it before the merge fields, delete it from the first label on the sheet.

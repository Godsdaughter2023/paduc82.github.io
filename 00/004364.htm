    Step 1: Visit zune.net/KIN to download the Zune software and sign up for a Zune Pass

    Connect your phone to your computer using a USB cable. The Zune software will take you through the set-up process.

    Step 3: Accept the update

    You'll be prompted to download an update to guarantee that your phone has all the latest features. When the update is complete, your phone will restart by itself.

    Step 4: Name your KIN phone as it will appear in the Zune software

    Personalize your phone by giving it a name that you'll see in the Zune software.

    Step 5: Link your KIN phone to your Zune Tag

    On the naming screen, you have the option to link your Zune to a Zune Tag. If you created a Zune account at any point before or during installation, you'll already have a Zune Tag. If you don't have one, you'll have a chance to create one if you want.

    Step 6: Link your phone with the Zune software on your computer

    When you link your phone with your computer, every time you connect, your music, pictures, and videos are automatically synced to your phone, according to the preferences you set.

    You can use your phone with as many computers as you want, but it can only be linked with one at a time. Linking with another computer clears the sync relationship, or sync groups, that you established on the original computer.

    If you don't want to link your phone, you can connect to a computer as a guest. With a guest connection you can still sync, just not automatically. Items must be added and deleted individually from your phone when you want to make changes.

    Step 7: Choose what to sync

    Decide what gets added to your KIN phone by the Zune software and what gets skipped every time you connect to your computer. You have different options depending on the media: sync everything; Smart sync; or sync only the individual items you select. For details, see Sync Media to Your Phone.

    Step 8: Fill up your phone with your media collection

    The last step is syncing your media collection to your KIN phone. Depending on the amount of stuff you have, this could take a while. If you just want to add your music and worry about the other stuff later, choose All for your music sync option and leave everything else in either Items I choose or Manual sync mode.

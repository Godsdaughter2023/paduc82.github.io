Thanks for reaching out to Xbox forums support. 

We apologize for the inconvenience and frustration this has caused but rest assured we are here to help. We would like to troubleshoot the console itself a bit more in an effort to clear this up we suggest a full reset of the console on more time from the Troubleshoot screen by using these steps :

  1. Unplug the network cable if you're using a wired network connection.
  2. Power off your console, and then unplug the power cord to ensure that the console is completely powered off.
  3. Wait 30 seconds, and then plug the power cord back in.
  4. Press and hold the BIND button (located on the left side of the console) and the EJECT button (located on the front of the console), and then press the Xbox button on the console.
  5. Continue holding the BIND and EJECT buttons for 10-15 seconds.
  6. Listen for two “power-up” tones a couple of seconds apart. You can release the BIND and EJECT buttons after the second power-up tone.

Once the troubleshooting screen comes up select reset console and then select reset and remove everything. Give it a shot and let us know how it goes.

[Guides] Forza Horizon 3 Fast Level Up and Skill Point Guide

If you are looking to level up faster in Forza Horizon 3, our guide below will help you achieve this by showing a method that will help you earn around 90.000 XP points in 10 minutes.

Without further ado, here is what you need to do to get easy Forza Horizon 3 XP and obviously Wheelspins.

In our video guide, we have used the Jaguar F-Type Project 7 Horizon Edition model, but you can use whatever car you want as long as it is a Horizon Edition and grants XP boost.

If you don’t know how to identify such a car, take a look at the image above and notice the pink mark on the right side, which specifies that the car is a Horizon Edition.

Assuming you don’t have such a car, try the auction house, although these cars can also be won at Wheelspins.

Next, upgrade your car to increase its top speed. If you don’t know how to do that, simply load an upgrade created by other players.

The next thing you have to do is to upgrade the Byron Bay Horizon Festival to level 5. For this you will need fans; therefore, complete more Races, Championships, Danger Signs, Speed Zones, Speed Traps, or Drift Zones.

Now, check your map and find the Goliath Circuit Race which is located on the beach, north from Byron Bay Horizon Festival.

Once you get to the location, you can opt to play a single Exhibition Race, or you can use a Blueprint to play six laps. It depends on how much time you have.

Before starting the race, modify the difficulty to fit your driving skills. Try to maximize the payout by changing the difficulty of Drivatars, the damage, or other aspects, but keep in mind that you need to win the race so don’t go all in if you don’t have the required skills.

Now, all you have to do is to play and win the race.

Since the Goliath Circuit Race is rather a long event, it has the best payout, but you will need around 10 minutes to complete it, or 10 minutes/lap if you have multiple laps.

The tweaks made to the difficulty will affect the number of credits you earn, while the Horizon Edition car will double the XP points.

In the video below you can see that our car increased the number of points by 100% and with the added bonus from the lineup and the skills we have earned 90.000 XP on a 10 minutes race with one lap.

This means that if you pick 6 laps with the same settings, you will get 540.000 XP points in one hour.

However, there is a trick which you can use to further maximize the amount of XP points received at the end of the race. Go to the Skill Shop and buy the perk named “The XP Bump”. This will give you triple Skills XP for the next 3 campaign races, meaning you get more XP from the skills performed during the race.

The Perk costs 10 Skill points, but if you are a good driver you can get 3 or more in a single lap from the Goliath Circuit. Therefore, if you are a good driver you can pay for the perk every 3 races, which is how long the perk lasts anyway. If you play more than one lap during a single race you can profit even more.

If you don’t want to spend so much time in a race you can opt to find all XP Boards to level up faster.

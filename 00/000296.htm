stefan blom say:

Holding the FN key will be required if your keyboard is designed that way, yes.

Since updating caption numbers doesn't work, your caption (SEQ) fields may be unlinked (converted to static text) or they may be locked (which prevents them from updating).

To find out if the fields are still "live," press Alt+F9; you should see field codes similar to { SEQ Figure }. If the fields turn out to be unlinked, Alt+F9 won't reveal any field codes, and you'll have to adjust the numbers manually or reinsert the captions (both options would be equally time-consuming).

To ensure that a field hasn't been locked, right-click the field (or the field code when it is displaying). If the Update command is greyed out, the field is locked. You can unlock all fields in the selection by pressing Ctrl+Shift+F11, after which updating should work again.

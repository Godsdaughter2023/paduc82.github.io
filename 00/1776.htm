On Mon, 22 Nov 2010 02:34:57 +0000, techiegz wrote:

As I said, WP7 or any email client do not "delete" emails from POP3 servers, POP3 is designed by default to "download" your actual messages to your email client, not copies. If you want otherwise you can set the retention option on the server. Whether your email client also provides you the option is up to whoever the software company is but email servers are the central portal to enable it and by default this is not a?POP3 feature. Sorry but you might want to read the relevant RFCs on POP. Deleting a

server-side operation. The client needs to issue an explicit DELE after a
RETR command in order to remove an email from a POP server.

To answer your last question first: I'm not sure where you're finding these instructional videos but regardless of the source I've found that those who post them rarely mention whether they are using Word for Mac or Word for Windows, let alone which specific release of the program they're working with. There can be significant differences based on those factors.

As for removing Section Breaks, it isn't necessary to select the markup. Place the insertion point at the beginning of the break then press the forward delete [del] key in the navigation key group if using a full keyboard. On a laptop or condensed keyboard press fn+delete.

As mentioned above, it isn't necessary to select the break, but if you prefer to do so it can't be done easily in your release using a dragging method... you'd need to drag to the beginning of the content in the following section. Instead, position the insertion point at the beginning of the break, press Shift+→ to select the break, then press the delete key.

harry stevens say:

Hi Richard,

You will NOT lose access to your emails while the update is ongoing, but you can still update the phone number directly to the Account Settings without waiting 30 days. Here's how you do it:

1. Login your account at https://account.live.com.

2. Click on Security & Privacy on top of the page.

3. Click on More Security Settings or Manage Advanced Security.

4. At this point you'll be asked to get a code, please get one from your current phone number. I recommend checking the "I use this device frequently, don't ask me for a code" option. I'll explain this later.

5. You'll now be at the Security Settings page. Here, you can add in your new phone number.

If the new phone number will only be available as soon as you lose the old one, you will normally undergo the 30 day waiting period. But, if you checked the option that I provided above, what would happen is that when you access the Security Settings page again, you will not be prompted to get a code, so you can immediately add in the new phone number.

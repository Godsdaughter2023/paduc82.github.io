jt kimbell [msft] say:

Hi,

The Motorola QIP 7216 has an HDCP limitation that only allows for 1 device to sit in between it and the TV. Do you happen to have an AV Receiver/Sound bar in the chain along with the Xbox One.

If you do, you have 2 options:

1) Call Frontier and explain you need a different STB that doesn't have this HDCP issue

2) Use optical audio to take your AVR out of the HDMI chain.

You can create a room mailbox to manage a meeting with an user account. Please refer to this KB article - Create and manage room mailboxes.

After you've created a room mailbox, you can change how it handles meeting requests (including whether it responds automatically or someone needs to decide what to do). By default, it'll automatically accept or decline requests depending on whether the requests conflict with any existing meetings on its calendar.

There are two options to handle meeting requests.

ron sommer mvp 2009-2017 say:

Emails are sent and received by an email server.

You can access the emails on the server using a browser, email program or email app.

When you use a browser to access your emails, the emails stay on the server.  You have to get online to read, compose, and send emails.

When using a program or app, emails can stay on the server and download to your computer or copies of your folders and emails can sync to your computer.  You can read and compose emails without being connected to the Internet.  The program or app connects to send messages and to download new messages.

Microsoft did not include an email program with Windows 7.  Some computer manufactures included Windows Live Mail and other software.

Windows Live Mail is one of the programs in Windows Essentials.

To make a complete backup of emails on the server, you need for all folders and messages to sync to your computer.  That is what adding an account to Windows Live Mail will do.

To save the emails in case of a hard drive crash or a loss of emails from the server, export the emails from Windows Live Mail.

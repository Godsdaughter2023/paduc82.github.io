a. user say:

Windows 10 Mobile upgrade should not remove any files on your phone. To make sure that you will not lose any data or files, you can copy or move the files or photos from your phone to OneDrive or  connect it to a PC. You can also use the contacts+message backup app to make a backup of all your contacts (in VCF format) and messages (SMS & MMS including attachments) to SD card (in XML format) for later restore. The backup is stored in backup+restore folder on the SD card.

If you upgrade your phone to Windows 10 Mobile, there are lot of supported features, phone updates and applications that you will get. Visit this link for more information about Windows 10 Mobile Specifications and System Requirements.

Let us know if you have any questions.

You cannot use a construction like

it would have to be

    ob6.Enabled = True

Private Sub ob3_Click()

Hello Kathryn,

 

There really isn't an order so to stop the algorithm for Chronologically arrange the topics.

 

Feeds are based on algorithms which prioritizes active stream content and will be different for every user AND will be different on the mobile app than from the desktop.

 

These are the conversations that are most relevant to you, based on what you subscribe to and your interactions with Yammer network content.

 

It finds any result that contains at least one of the search terms and then orders the results using an algorithm that gives priority to (a) results containing more of the search terms than others (ex. search for the brown fox, (b) results that are more recent, and (c) results that are more likely to be relevant to you (i.e.result is in a group you're a member of). We still support exact phrase search (i.e. only match "the brown fox", not "the fox brown") if you use quotation marks around the search terms.

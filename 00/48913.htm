HDMI cabling, by design, is compatible with any HDMI device. While there are a few variations (Ethernet, High Speed, Standard Speed), if it's  High Speed HDMI cable it will support everything on the 360. It does not have to be a Microsoft-specific HDMI cable.

 

Far as audio is concerned, HDMI on the 360 supports two channel LPCM and Dolby Digital 5.1. It isn't limited to a two-channel output. There are several other lossless multi channel formats that have been added to the HDMI standard over the years. Those do not work with the 360.

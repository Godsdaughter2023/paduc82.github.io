One of the more aggravating changes is that the boundaries of text boxes and other design objects are not visible unless you mouseover them. jomel_g say:

Hi

To answer your question, it's not possible to change your Skype Name. Since the migration of Skype accounts to Microsoft accounts, the option to create a unique Skype Name has been removed. This is because we want to focus on allowing you to login-in with a single sign-in to Microsoft services with just one password. The new account creation process will only require a combination of an email and password or a phone number and password as minimum requirements when signing up.

If you created your account with a phone number, our system will create a Skype Name that starts with "live:" and a mixture of random alphanumeric characters. And if you create an account with an email, your Skype Name will start with the word "live:" plus your email less its domain.

I hope this answers your query.

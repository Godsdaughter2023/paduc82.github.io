Based on your description, your Office 365 email address is spoofed and you received lots of emails that indicating that you were sending emails failed. The spammer uses your mail address in the 'From:' line of their messages. If the spammer's message can't be delivered for any reason, the receiving host will send back a bounce or non-delivery report to the address in the 'From:' line.

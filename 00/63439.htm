Bernard,

The Product key is on the Certificate of authenticity label.  If your Windows 7 was preinstalled by a major computer maker then the label will be fixed to the outside of your computer or, for many laptops, inside the battery compartment.  In all other cases, the label will have been provided with the installation disk[s].

If your Windows 7 was preinstalled & the Product key on the Certificate of authenticity was never used then there is no other way to retrieve your Product key**.  If you have ever activated your Windows 7 using the Product key on the Certificate of authenticity then that key can be extracted by several utilities including Belarc.

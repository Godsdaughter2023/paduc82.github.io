The other possibility is that the broken links are not hyperlinks, but text or graphics that were inserted (using Insert > Object) from other files and "linked" to their source files. To fix those, go to the File > Info page and scroll to the bottom of the right-hand column. Under the Related Documents heading, click "Edit Links to Files".

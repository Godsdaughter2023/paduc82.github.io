pete_uk say:

Use a helper column on the master sheet (column AB ??) with this formula in AB2 (assuming you have a header row):

 

=IF(AA2="","",AA2&"_"&COUNTIF(AA$2:AA2,AA2))

 

then copy this down, even beyond your data.

 

Then in the other sheet you could have a formula like this in A2:

 

=IFERROR(INDEX(Master!A:A,MATCH("1_"&ROWS($1:1),Master!AB:AB,0)),"")

 

Then copy this down as far as you need to.

 

Hope this helps.

 

Pete

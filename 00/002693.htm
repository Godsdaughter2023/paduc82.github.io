easona say:

Hi, KeriFox1980. There are several conditions in where double tapping the screen will be ignored. These are designed so you don't accidentally wake up your device. It will wake up when you double tap the screen on the condition that:

• the double tap is not attempted near the four edges of the display
• the first and second tap is less than 3 millimeters apart
• the first and second tap is less than 0.6 seconds apart
• the first tap occurs at least one second after the screen goes blank

The following conditions will force the double tap feature to be temporarily disabled, and you will have to wait a few seconds before trying again:

• three consecutive failed double tap attempts have occurred in succession
• the screen is touched for at least three consecutive seconds

You can also adjusting the display sensitivity by going to Settings > display+touch, swipe to touch, and switch Touch sensitivity to High. If the issue persists, try checking if you have the latest software version by going to Settings > phone update > check for updates. When you combine multiple projects in a master and uncheck the Link to Project checkbox in the Insert Project dialog, as long as the resource information matches, Project will collapse the identical resource together.  If you view the Resource Sheet in the master project, does the resource name appear once or multiple times?

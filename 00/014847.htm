I believe the cells you are trying to put a formula into may be formatted as Text.

Format them as General and the formulas should process as expected. If this is the case, start with specific cells you wish to place formulas in.

If you are using the worksheets primarily to collect raw data for an import to Access, then formatting within the entire worksheet should not be of any real concern. With A1 selected, press CTRL+A to Select All. Change the cell number format to General.

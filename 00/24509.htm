Email to OneNote - me at OneNote.com FAQ

What is *** Email address is removed for privacy ***?

It’s a simple way to remember things.  You can forward receipts, travel plans, quick to-dos or anything else from you inbox to *** Email address is removed for privacy ***, and it will save the contents of your email into your OneNote notebook.

How does *** Email address is removed for privacy *** work?

OneNote looks at the sender of the mails we receive and use that to determine which notebook to save the email into.

How can I start using *** Email address is removed for privacy ***?

Go to http://www.onenote.com/emailsettings and select the email addresses you’ll send to *** Email address is removed for privacy *** from. After that, just start sending mails.

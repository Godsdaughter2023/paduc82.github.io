The asterisk wildcard will count formula blanks but is only good for text and will not work on numbers.

A1: ="" (a formula blank)

A2: #NUM!

A3: 10

A4: X

A5: <space> (A char 32 space character)

A6:             (nothing, an empty cell)

=COUNTIF(A1:A6,"<>")

=5

DirtyEstrbuny, I recently had a similar issue and I learned to resolve it by the following steps:

1. Go to your dashboard -> System settings -> Storage Devices -> Choose a Hard-drive or Memory Unit (Depending on which you have the game downloading on), DELETE the game file (in your case it would be Dead Rising 2).

2. Go back to Storage Devices and press (y) on the HDD/Memory Unit you have chosen to download the game on.

select CLEAR GAME CACHE. (Do this 2 times).

3. Once you have cleared the game cache (2 times), Go back to storage devices -> select your HDD/Memory unit, Scroll down to and select "System Items",  delete the 2 files marketplace updates and Rights files.

4. Once done that, Go back to your HDD/Memory unit, and select "Gamer Profiles", Select your gamer profile and press delete (CHOOSE TO JUST DELETE THE GAMERTAG NOT CONTENTS!!)

5. Once done the above steps, Go back to your dashboard, Scroll right to the "Social" panel, and select "Sign in or Out". Then scroll all the way to the right until you come across "Download Profile"

Enter in your e-mail address associated with your XBL account and then your password.

6. Once gamer profile has been recovered, Head back to the marketplace -> search for your game, and download again and your done ! it should work.

If it continues to not download properly, it is either a regional issue, or a connection issue.

Hopefully these tips help you out, They really helped me out when nothing else worked.

( You do not lose ANYTHING when deleting a profile and selecting ONLY DELETE PROFILE)

Best of luck, Let me know how this turns out for you.

In addition to what DH65 suggested, fonts also are embedded into PDFs generated by using the File> Save As - PDF method & selecting the Best for printing option. Of course, both methods create significantly larger PDF files because they embed all fonts used in the source document in their entirety.

The Best for electronic distribution... option provided by the Save As method in Word embeds only subsets of the fonts used in the source document in order to render a smaller PDF file.

In neither case is there any further action to be taken by the user.

Aside from adding the email address of the sender on your blocked sender's list and creating email rules, please be informed that you can also configure your Junk mail filter settings to Exclusive. This feature will let you receive emails from your contact list and safe senders list.

 

To set up a junk mail filter to Exclusive, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in here.
  2. Click the gear icon located on the upper right panel of your mailbox, just beside your username.
  3. Select More mail settings options from the drop down list. 
  4. Under Preventing Junk email, click the Filters and reporting option.
  5. Under Choose a junk email filter, select the Exclusive option.
  6. Click on Save.

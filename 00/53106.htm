a. user say:

Hi Bob,

Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Community.

You'll get this prompt if you have do not have admin privileges. You can remove this by following methods.

Method 1:

  1. Login with an admin account and right click on the game file.
  2. Click on properties and click on compatibility.
  3. At the bottom of the new window that pops up, uncheck the option Run this program as administrator
  4. Click Apply and OK to save changes.

Method 2:

  1. Login with an admin account and right click on the game file.
  2. Select Properties and click Security.
  3. Click on Edit the select the user you would like to allow to use the program.
  4. Place a check mark on Full Control under “Allow” in “Permissions for”.
  5. Click Apply and OK.

Hope this will help you. Keep us posted with the status of the issue to assist you further.

The way to backup Office Outlook is to export your data. You can export your entire data store (.pst) or individual components of your data store, such as your Address Book.

If you want, you can store the exported archive(s) to OneDrive (and that's a good idea.)

Then, in the new computer, you download the archive(s) from OneDrive to the desktop, and import them into Office Outlook on the new computer.

That's the way to transfer Office Outlook data to a new computer. If that's not what you did I don't know how you're going to bring your data back into Outlook.

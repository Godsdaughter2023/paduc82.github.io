danida_u say:

Hi Anna Drozhinski and welcome to the Skype Community! 

Q: how do i know my contact name (the one that ather peple use to make a contact)& how can i change that..

A: We term this as Skype Name, although it could also be called as Skype username, Skype ID, Skype handle, etc. Skype names are unique usernames you created when users first joined Skype. However, since Skype migrated to Microsoft October of 2016, the option to create a custom Skype Name is no longer possible. Thus, new users who would be creating their accounts using an email address or a phone number, you would have a Microsoft account. 

If you used an email address to create the account, your Skype username would be live:anythingthatcomesbefore@. If you use a phone number to create the account, you would have a Skype username with live:randomlettersandnumbers. These Skype usernames are automatically generated by the system and cannot be changed.

To locate the Skype name of your account, you can try these steps below. 

  • Sign in to My Account on the Skype website. 
  • Scroll down to Account details and click Purchase History. 
  • Your Skype username would show on the upper right corner of the webpage beside Sign out. 

Hope this answers your questions! Let us know if you have further concerns.

Hi John,

If you find that the apps are installing even if these are already installed, it means that the apps are updating. App publishers sometimes update their apps to add new features and fix problems, and these updates are automatically installed through the Microsoft Store. However, you may turn this setting off by following the steps below:

  1. On the Start screen, select Microsoft Store to open the Store.
  2. Select the account menu (next to the search box), then select Settings.
  3. Under App updates, set Update apps automatically to Off.

Regarding your question, the suggested solution will work with your edition of Windows 10. However, modifying the registry can cause serious problems to your computer if done incorrectly.

stefan blom say:

As long as there are tracked changes or comments in a Word document, Word will show them for you, as you (re-)open the document. This is a privacy option. To permanently get rid of the markup, accept (or reject) all changes and delete comments.

Okay I seem to have found the problem with my previous formula. So simple but I didn't know the syntax. Here is the finished result: =IF(COUNT(C7,D7,F7,H7)=0,"",SUMPRODUCT(C7*-1+D7*-1+F7+H7+E6)).

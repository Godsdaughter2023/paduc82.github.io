The SIM lock has nothing to do with contract duration, even many pre-pay phones are locked.

A SIM lock is the consequence for obtaining a phone at a substantially reduced price or even free of charge. The network provides the phone and each individual network has its own policy on when a restriction code will be given - most will unlock a phone after six or twelve months, even if a contract is still ongoing.

The charge is is nothing more than a (very expensive) administration fee for the effort of locating the code and sending it to you. Networks are not actually under any legal obligation to provide unlocking codes.

Yes, do the following:

6) Restart the computer.

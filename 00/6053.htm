a. Now, navigate to the location and delete CREDHIST file.

C:\Users\<username>\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Protect\Credhist and delete Credhist.

b.Open Internet Explorer to check if the issue persists.


Once the issue is fixed, you can uncheck the option 'Show hidden files and folders'.

How can I make a cylinder and have four different colors in that - Microsoft Community

sshuler say:

How can I make a cylinder and have four different colors in that cylinder?

I need to have a picture of a cylinder that contains (4) different colors.  Is there a way to achieve this?  Thanks.
jay freedman say:

Are you trying for something like this?

To make this in Word, I started by going to Insert > Shapes and selecting the cylinder shape in the Flowchart group. By dragging a rectangle on the document surface, I made one of the four pieces (each of which looks like the top quarter of the stack). I copied that piece to the clipboard.

I clicked again in the text paragraph where the cursor was when I started (which is where the anchor of the shape is). Then I pasted a second copy of the shape, and dragged it so it appeared to sit on top of the first shape. By holding the Ctrl key while tapping the arrow keys, I aligned them so there is no gap or doubled edges.

I repeated the paste-and-align step twice more.

To change the colors, I selected one of the shapes and clicked the Shape Fill button on the Drawing Tools tab, then chose a color. I repeated that for two more shapes, leaving the fourth shape with its original fill color.

Finally, to keep the shapes together, I held the Ctrl key while clicking each of the shapes in turn; when they were all selected, I clicked the Group button on the Drawing Tools tab and selected Group from the dropdown.

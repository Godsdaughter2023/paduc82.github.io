If the offline installer does not work for you, then you can try switching to a wired connection.  I assume that you have a wired connection into the house, and then go wireless from there. 

The way the connection generally goes is a wire in from phone/cable company (unless you have satellite phone/internet/TV).  It connects to a "Modem" (typically provided by the service provider).  A few modems have Wireless connections, but in MOST cases there is a wired connection from the Modem to a "Router".  The router typically is where the wireless connection is made from, it will probably have between 1 and 6 antennas.  The router will also have 4 ports labeled "LAN 1 to 4".   To go to a "wired" connection,  simply plug in an "ethernet" cable from the router to your computer ethernet port.

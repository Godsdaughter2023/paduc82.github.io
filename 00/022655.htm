Hello,

One of the possible reasons why thumbnail previews for your images are not showing is due to the configuration of the settings. To troubleshoot your concern, follow the steps below:

  1. Go to File Explorer > Choose the drive for your device.
  2. Click on View > Under Layout select Extra large icons, Large icons, or Medium icons.
  3. Check if the thumbnails are now appearing.

If the thumbnails are still not showing,

  1. Go to File Explorer > Choose the drive for your device.
  2. Click on View > Select Option > Hit Change folder and search options.
  3. Go to View tab > Under Files and Folders, make sure Always show icons, never thumbnails is uncheck.
  4. Hit Apply then OK.
  5. Check if the thumbnails are now appearing.

Keep us posted with the status of the issue.

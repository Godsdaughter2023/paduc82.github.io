suzanne s. barnhill say:

To clarify, do you want to just accept the current change or do you want to accept the current change and advance to the next? You can add a keyboard shortcut for either one:

  1. Right-click on the Ribbon and choose Customize the Ribbon.
  2. In the "Customize the Ribbon and keyboard shortcuts" dialog, click on Keyboard shortcuts: Customize (lower left corner).
  3. Select the All Commands category.
  4. Select either AcceptChangesAndAdvance or AcceptChangesSelected and press the shortcut key you want to assign to this function.

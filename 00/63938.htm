To rename the file, follow these steps:

a: Click Start, clickRun, type %windir%\inf in the Open box, and then clickOK.

c: Rename Bth.inf to Bth.bak, and then press ENTER.

b: Locate the device.

c: Right-click the device, and then clickUpdate Driver.

d:  Follow the steps that appear on the screen in the Hardware Update Wizard to specify your own driver for the Bluetooth device.

roady [mvp] say:

The connection tab is gone since you are no longer connected via RPC over HTTP but via MAPI over HTTP. The RPC over HTTP dialog was also not to be used for Exchange 2007 and higher connectivity; It was for compatibility with Exchange 2003. Exchange 2007 and later accounts are to be configured via Autodiscover.

Anyway, instead of recreating the account, try the Repair... button instead in;
File-> Account Settings-> Account Settings...-> select your Exchange account

This will force Outlook to look for the Autodiscover service again and reapply the settings.

Also, accepting the alert once might actually reapply the correct settings or do you still get it the next time that Outlook is started?

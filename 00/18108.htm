marshall barton say:

First, you need to figure out which character is causing the little boxes.  It's probably a line feed, Chr(10), or carriage return, Chr(13).

Then you can change the report text boxes that display the problematic fields to use an expression something like:
   =Replace(fieldname, Chr(10), "  ")
to put a couple of spaces in place of the line feed (or carriage return).  If you want a new line instead of a couple of spaces, use:
   =Replace(fieldname, Chr(10), Chr(13) & Chr(10))

    Select (for example) C1:C1000.

    On the Home tab of the ribbon, select Conditional Formatting > New Rule...

    Select 'Use a formula to detemine which cells to format'.

    Enter the formula

    =ISNUMBER(MATCH(C1,$A$1:$A$1000,0))

    Adjust the range if needed.

    Click Format...

    Specify the desired formatting.

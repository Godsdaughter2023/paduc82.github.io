vijay a. verma say:

You can generate random numbers between 1 and 30 by following formula -

=RANDBETWEEN(1,30)

(Replace 1 with 050XXX and 30 wiith 3000XXX)

If you want to generate unique random numbers put following in A2 and drag down -

=AGGREGATE(14,6,ROW($1:$30)*NOT(COUNTIF(A$1:$A1, ROW($1:$30))), RANDBETWEEN(1,30-ROW($A1)+1))

(Replace 1 with 050XXX and 30 wiith 3000XXX. If you are starting your cell from somewhere other than Say B3, replace A1 with B2)

For removing duplicates - Data tab > Remove Duplicates

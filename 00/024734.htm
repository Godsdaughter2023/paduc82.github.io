daniel pineault say:

The issue isn't your IT, it's runtime.  Runtime is a striped down version of access made to run an existing database, but not to do development work.  For all development you need the full version of access.

In your scenario, you mention 2010 and 365.  This you must do all your development in the oldest version, so 2010, to ensure it runs for all your users.  So you need to do all your work on PC1

In such a scenario, you want to place the back end in a shared folder, accessible by all your users/PCs, and then distribute a personal copy of the front end to each user (never share a copy of the front end otherwise you'll be in front problems).

As a side note, office 365 had been horribly unstable do to bugs in almost every single update.  If highly recommend you disable updates and manually update things in a knowledgeable and controlled manner.

OneNote link shared throguh OneDrive - how to open in OneNote - Microsoft Community

Åke malmgren say:

OneNote link shared throguh OneDrive - how to open in OneNote 2016?

I got a link to a OneNote notebook from a colleague. When I open the link, I see the notebook in OneNote Online. I can choose to edit it in the browser, but I want to edit it in OneNote 2016 on the PC. This option is not available for OneNote. It is available for Word or Excel documents in Office Online.

How can I edit the notebook shared with me in OneNote 2016?

john sarc say:

Hi,

To edit the shared OneNote file in OneNote app, you need to open it in Onedrive. See the steps to do so.

  1. Open the shared link and it will prompt you to OneNote online.
  2. Once opened, click on "Edit Notebook" option beside  "Print" located on the top right of the page.
  3. Once clicked, you would be given two options: Edit in Microsoft OneNote and  Edit in Browser (choose Edit in Microsoft OneNote).
  4. Click on Continue.
  5. You will be the prompted to OneDrive with the shared note. Also, a pop-up message will show up saying "Did you mean to switch apps?"
  6. Click on "Yes" and it will prompt you to OneNote app with your shared note.
  7. You can now edit the file in OneNote app.

Let us know if this helps you.

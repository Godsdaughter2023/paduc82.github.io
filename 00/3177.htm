How do you record macros in powerpoint 2007 or 2010? - Microsoft Community

laura ianson say:

How do you record macros in powerpoint 2007 or 2010?

I am attempting to create a presentation, where there are three pictures each picture leads to a different page, I can do this with hyperlinks but I also want to record the information about which picture was clicked on. Any suggestions?

shyam pillai say:
There are no macro recorders available in PPT 2007/2010. The recorder could not keep with the feature changes introduced in newer versions so it was depracated. You will to refer the VBA help to create the macros.

david marcovitz say: There are a couple of ways to do this. You could easily have three separate macros, something like:

Sub Pic1()
   MsgBox "The first picture was clicked."
   'Replace the above with whatever you want to keep track of the picture being clicked

   ActivePresentation.SlideShowWindow.View.GotoSlide 7
End Sub
Sub Pic2()
   MsgBox "The first picture was clicked."
   'Replace the above with whatever you want to keep track of the picture being clicked

   ActivePresentation.SlideShowWindow.View.GotoSlide 8
End Sub
Sub Pic3()
   MsgBox "The first picture was clicked."
   'Replace the above with whatever you want to keep track of the picture being clicked

   ActivePresentation.SlideShowWindow.View.GotoSlide 9
End Sub

Or it can be done with one macro

Sub WhichPic(oShp as Shape)
   MsgBox "The following shape was clicked: " & oShp.Name
   'Again replace the above with what you really want
   If oShp.Name = "Picture 1" Then
      ActivePresentation.SlideShowWindow.View.GotoSlide 7
   Else If oShp.Name = "Picture 2" Then
      ActivePresentation.SlideShowWindow.View.GotoSlide 8
   Else If oShp.Name = "Picture 3" Then
      ActivePresentation.SlideShowWindow.View.GotoSlide 9
   EndIf
End Sub

Of course, a lot of this really depends on what you mean by "record the information about which picture was clicked on." You might want to have a look at my site (http://www.PowerfulPowerPoint.com) and Example 9.10, which has a quiz that keeps track of which buttons were pressed and reports the results on a slide at the end.

Sub recorder(oshp As Shape)
Dim iFileNum As Integer
Dim strPath As String

Dim osld As Slide

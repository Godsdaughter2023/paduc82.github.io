With

B1: text to find....red

A1 having text that may or may not contain the B1 text

 

This regular formula returns 1 if the text is contained, otherwise zero:

C1: =MAX(COUNTIF(A1,{"";"* "}&B1&{"",", *","; *"," *",".*"}))

 

Examples:

0......fred

1.....a red rose

0.....fred and dave

1.....blue, red, and green

1.....red and red and red

1.....it was red; or maybe not

1.....it was not red.

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

Go to the Developer tab of the ribbon and with the selection in the Content Control, click on the Properties item in the Controls section of the ribbon and in the Content Controls Properties dialog that opens, uncheck the box for "Remove content control when contents are edited".

Note however, that there will still be a change in the layout unless you insert exactly the same amount of text.

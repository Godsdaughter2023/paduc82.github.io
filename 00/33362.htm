Skype Numbers cannot receive SMS (text message) replies.

I sent an SMS text message to my friend but they can’t reply. When you send an SMS text message from Skype, the recipient will see the first 11 characters of your Skype Name and they won’t be able to reply to your SMS text message.

To resolve this, you need to change the sender ID so it displays your mobile number instead of your Skype Name. The recipient will then be able to reply to your SMS text message and their reply will be sent to your mobile (not to Skype).

ebaltazar-I got it fixed. It's the Samsung Fast Start program that causes the disabled options.

To fix it:

Go to Start/All Programs/Samsung/Fast Start, which will open and you will see the default setting is 'On'. Select 'Off'. 

Go back to the power option controls and the option to 'Do Nothing' for Lid Close Action will be there.

Pretty quick fix. Surprised Samsung didn't know about it when I spoke to them.

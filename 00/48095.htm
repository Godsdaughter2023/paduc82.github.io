A computer supplies little power to every USB inserted to it. If there are too many USBs getting inserted simultaneously, it will summed up and surpassed the required power from the USB port. Hence, it will give up and trigger the blue screen error to shut down your computer to avoid further electrical damage to your hardware via USB port.

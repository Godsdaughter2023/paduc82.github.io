Hi gs,

I'm sorry that you're so frustrated.

The camera "stack" consists of the camera driver, camera APIs that run on Windows Media Foundation, and camera applications, such as Windows Camera, Skype and third party camera apps.

The apps and APIs are identical on all versions of Windows. (Unless you have the N or KN version.)

The part that varies from one machine to another is the camera driver, and the only way that any camera app will work is if the camera driver is installed and working properly.

It sounds like you know all this, but I want to reiterate it because it's why we need to focus on the camera driver.

The good news is that your Windows 8.x camera driver should still work on Win10--there haven't been any changes that would cause an 8.x driver to stop working. I am not aware of any such bug with your machine, which is why I want to find out the state of your driver.

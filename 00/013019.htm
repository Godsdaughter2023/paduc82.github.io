john korchok say:

When you paste the charts, click on the bottom half of the Home>Paste button and choose Paste Special. Select Paste link and click on OK. 

After updating the Excel charts, when you re-open the presentation, PowerPoint will ask if you want to update the links. Word is reserving an area for Track Changes and Comment balloons on the right side, and compressing the document to allow for that.

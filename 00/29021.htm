a. user say:

Hi,

Yes, you can perform this option by including Khan Academy to your Allow list and changing the Web filtering level to only allow the sites that are in your Allow list.  
 
 1. Sign in to https://fss.live.com using your Parent account.
 2. Click on Edit Settings under the username of your child.
 3. Under Web filtering, tick the Turn off web filtering and then Save.
 4. Tick on Turn on web filtering adjust the slider to Allow list only.
 5. Save.
 6. Click Web filtering lists.
 7. Type the web address of Khan Academy in the allow http:// text box and click Allow. 
 8. Save.
 9. Refresh your Family Safety settings: How to refresh Family Safety Filter settings
 10. Make sure that Youtube is not in the blocked list.

Note: After changing this option, use your child's user account and check if the YouTube site will be blocked but the videos that are in the Khan Academy site will be allowed.

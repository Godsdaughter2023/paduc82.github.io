Broken components on the system files or any misconfigured settings related to Cortana reminders can be one of the factors why this issue occurs. In line with this, here’s some methods that you can follow to have this straighten out:

Method 1: Turn the Calendar on.

If you are not receiving notifications based on the date and time, it is a possibility that your reminders calendar is off. Go to Calendar, search for the settings. In the display, go to Remind me, and tick the checkbox beside Reminders.




Method 2: Check your Account Settings.

In a case of person-based reminders, you might have accidentally deleted the contact or use an account without the contact’s name. Having said that, check it and modify it. To check the accounts, just follow the steps below:

  1. In the Search box, type Settings and press Enter.
  2. Click on the Accounts icon.



  3. Open the Family & Other Users settings and then click on Add someone else to this PC.



  4. This will help you in adding a new user to your PC with their Outlook account.

Method 3: Fix problems with your Microsoft account.

Logging out and logging back on may resolve this issue. To see if your Microsoft Account is causing the problem, follow these steps:

  1. Click the Start button.
  2. Click the user icon.
  3. Click Sign Out.
  4. Sign in again, using your Microsoft account.
  5. If that doesn’t resolve the issue, restart your device

Method 4: Restart the Cortana Process.

You can stop and restart the Cortana Windows process if the previous options haven’t resolved your problem. To restart the service:

  1. Press Windows + X keys on your keyboard and select Task Manager.
  2. If applicable, click More Details.
  3. From the Processes tab, scroll to locate Cortana and click it one time.
  4. Click on the End Task.
  5. Once done, restart your device to apply the changes and check if the issue persists.

If you continue to have this issue even after performing the above steps, please reply to this thread. The system will notify me so I can get back to you and provide you more answers.

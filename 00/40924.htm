Removing that panel is just a matter of loosening 4 Phillips-head screws after you shut down the laptop, unplug it, and remove the battery.  For directions, click on the link for the Service Manual that dax1792 provided and go to pdf page 47 (manual page 4-10); stop at step 2.  Blow out any dust with a can of compressed air (get at any computer or office products store).

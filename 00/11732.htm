Hi anonigo,

Try the below steps:

Step 1:
You might have sound turned off in Internet Explorer's advanced settings.

To check:

1. Open Internet Explorer.

2. Go to Tools> Internet Options.

3. Select the "Advanced" Tab.

3. Scroll down to the "Multimedia" sub-heading.

4. Make sure the box next to "Play sounds in web pages" is checked. Click "ok".

5. Restart Internet Explorer. Test to see if sounds will now play.

Step 2:
It is also possible that Internet Explorer has been selectively muted in the Volume Mixer.

To check this:

1. Open Internet Explorer, preferably to a page that should be playing some sound.

2. Click on the volume icon in the windows system tray (in the right-hand lower corner of the taskbar), and then click on the link labeled "Mixer".

3. A panel will open showing the individual volume levels of all running apps that play sounds (you may need to scroll to the right to see all of them).

4. Make sure that the volume level belonging to Internet Explorer is not set to mute.

Hope this helps.

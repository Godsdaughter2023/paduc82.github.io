Please follow the steps below to achieve your requirement-

Get-MsolUser | Where-Object { $_.isLicensed -eq “TRUE” } | Select-Object UserPrincipalName, DisplayName, Country, Department | Export-Csv c:\NEW\LicensedUsers.csv

Import-Csv C:\NEW\LicensedUsers.csv | ForEach { Set-MsolUserPrincipalName -UserPrincipalName $_.UserPrincipalName -NewUserPrincipalName ($_.UserPrincipalName.Split(“@”)[0] + “@abc.com”) }

It means "not equal to" but I find it easier to read by interpreting it to mean "is not".

A1<>"yes" = A1 not equal to yes

A1<>"yes" = A1 is not yes

PowerPoint will convert the whole slide to WMF, so resize the grouped object as required.

I never use WMF within a Word document, mainly because 99% of my documents are delivered to external contacts and JPG gives me several benefits, 1) the customer cannot edit the image easily; 2) the image file size is often much reduced compared to WMF.

WMF is not an option from Paint, and most other bitmap applications, as WMF is a raster format.

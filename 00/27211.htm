Function GaussRandom(Optional stdabw As Double = 3) As Double
   Do
      GaussRandom = (Rnd * 2 - 1) * stdabw
   Loop While Rnd > Exp(-(GaussRandom^2)/2)/Sqr(2*3.141592654)/0.4
End Function

Sub Test()
   Const ANZAHL = 1000000

   Dim arr() As Double
   Dim i As Long

   ReDim arr(1 To ANZAHL)
   Randomize

   For i = 1 To ANZAHL
      arr(i) = GaussRandom()
   Next
End Sub

betty gui msft say:

Hi Aung,

It is also available for admin to change the email address of distribution group (The email address only can use internal domain), following are the detailed operation steps:
1. Login to https://admin.microsoft.com/Adminportal/Home?source=applauncher#/groups.
2. Expand Groups section to Groups, click the specific distribution group to its profile.
3. Click Edit next to its name.

4. Type the new email address you want to add to the distribution group.

Besides, you can also choose the powershell cmdlet, just need to change the cmdlet I mentioned in the last reply to “Set-DistributionGroup –Identity <Group name> –PrimarySmtpAddress <primary email address>”.

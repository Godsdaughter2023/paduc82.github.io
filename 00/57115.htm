For outlook.com your emails and contacts are stored "in the cloud" (i.e. on servers owned by Microsoft). Your emails and contacts are not stored on your PC. If you PC crashes you just use another PC to log in to Outlook.com and there you will see your emails and contacts.

 

Microsoft servers are backed up, and in many cases the data is replicated over several servers.

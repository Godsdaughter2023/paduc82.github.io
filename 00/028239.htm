excelaid say:

Formatting Cell into month(MMM) issue

Hello,

 

I'm running into a small problem when extracting month from a coulmn of date (ex. 1/15/2010 gives me 1 using =month() ). When I convert the cell into mmm after I got the month in number format its returning only Jan for all the months 2-12. I'm wondering why.

 

Thanks,

mike h.. say:

Hi,

 

Do it this way. Say your date is in a1 in another cell anter the formula

 

=A1

 

Now format that cell as MMM

 

As an alternative use =TEXT(A1,"MMM") which needs no formatting.

 

 

EDIT.. Youu wanted to know why it happens using =month(a1)

 

As you've established that returns a number in the range 1 to 12. Now if you format that as MMM excel treats that number not as a month but as a date. In Excel dates are numbers and the number 1 is 1 jan1900 and the number 12 is 12 jan 1900 so formatted as month each of those numbers is in the month of january hence it returns Jan for each.

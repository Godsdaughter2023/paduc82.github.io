Use INCLUDETEXT fields to bring the contents of the individual documents into the destination document. Although the article https://wordmvp.com/FAQs/TblsFldsFms/includetextfields.htm is old, nothing in this feature has changed except the locations of the commands. The command that was Insert > Field is now at Insert > Quick Parts > Field.

The INCLUDETEXT field defaults to using the explicit path to the source file. If you need relative paths (not likely when the sources are on SharePoint and accessible to all users), see http://windowssecrets.com/forums/showthread.php/154379-Word-Fields-and-Relative-Paths-to-External-Files.

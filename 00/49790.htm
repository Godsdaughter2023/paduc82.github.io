ahousie say: I fixed my problem...

To temporarily disable the shutdown kicking in , after the warning popup displays, I went to the command prompt and typed in "shutdown -a" so I could troubleshoot.

Then I went into the Event Viewer under Application and saw that a couple weird services that I don't need anyway were failing to start because they had missing registry information/dll info.  This was causing the svchost.exe service to fail (and the default recovery for that is to reboot)

I stopped the weird service (in this case it was Bonjour) because I didn't need it anyway and went into Properties for that service and changed it to "Manual" start from "Automatic"

Thank you for posting to Microsoft Community. I understand that you want to create a video that shows your performance with multiple angles.

  2. On the Edit tab, click on the Video volume then move the slider to the farthest left. Do the same to all videos to mute them.
  3. On the Home tab, click the Add music icon.
  4. On the lower right part where it shows Audio and Music, change it to Videos then locate the video file which you will be using the audio as the music.

You can now arrange the videos according to your preference.

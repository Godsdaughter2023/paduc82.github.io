how do i burn an mp4 video to a playable dvd

have a recorded mp4 video. need to make a dvd playable on a set top box. how do i go about it?

considerate_guy say:

You need to purchase DVD authoring software. Note: The video DVD standard was created in the late 1990s and doesn't support HD video. You will be "down-rezzing" the video to a maximum of 720 x 480p (NTSC).

Microsoft used to provide a program called Windows DVD Maker in Windows 7, but they stopped including it in later versions of Windows, considering it a "legacy" format, much like Apple did when they stopped shipping iDVD with their iLife suite.

Do you have a Blu-ray player or a modern game console? If so, you should be able to burn the .mp4 file onto a disk and have it play as a data DVD. And it will be in HD format.

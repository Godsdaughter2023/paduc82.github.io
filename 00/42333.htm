muralidhar_g say:

Hi Fred,

 

Thank you for your reply and sharing the required information.

 

Method 1

In this scenario try disabling the Windows Defender SmartScreen and check the status of the issue. To do so,

 

  1. Launch Windows Defender Security Center from your Start menu, desktop, or taskbar.

  2. Click the App and browser control button on the left side of the window.

  3. Click Off in the Check apps and files section.

  4. Click Off in the SmartScreen for Microsoft Edge section.

  5. Click Off in the SmartScreen for Windows Store apps section.

 

Method 2

Please try the below suggested steps from the administrator account.

 

  1. Press Win+R to bring up Run dialog, then type in gpedit.msc, hit Enter.

  2. In the left pane of Local Group Policy Editor, go to: Computer Configuration > Windows Settings > Security Settings > Local Policies > Security Options.

  3. In the right pane, double-click on User Account Control: Run all administrators in Admin Approval Mode.

  4. Change the security setting to Disabled and click OK.

  5. When finished, close Local Group Policy Editor and then restart your computer.

 

Hope it helps.

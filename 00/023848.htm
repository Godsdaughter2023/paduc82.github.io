Hi Ayham,

Please be informed that Skype Names cannot be changed. Additionally, when Skype accounts were migrated to basic Microsoft accounts last October 2016, the option to create a custom Skype Name is also no longer possible.

As a workaround, you can either change your display name or create another account using an email address. Remember, accounts created using an email address will have a system-generated Skype Name which will show as live:whatevercomesbefore@.

Also, since your Skype account is already a Microsoft account, it is not possible to merge or unlink your Skype and Microsoft account. With your account you will have access to sign in to all your Microsoft services - including Skype, Office Online, Outlook, OneNote, OneDrive, Xbox, and more.  When you sign into one of the Microsoft services you may be prompted to add an email.  Adding an email simply makes it easy to recover your account while adding additional security.

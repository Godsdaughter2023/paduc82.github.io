Even when you choose to delete your out-of-date security info, MS don't fully remove it for 30 days.

russ valentine say:

I have no idea what you mean by "Windows Outlook." There is no such thing. Almost all smart phones will synchronize with Microsoft Office Outlook if Outlook is running a standard POP/SMTP Internet Mail account or Exchange Server. Other mail account types in Outlook (IMAP and HTTP) are far more uncertain.

Since Outlook does not participate in any sync operations, this is not a good place to ask your question. Your question must be directed to the vendors of the phone software. Just recognize that any syncing you do with Outlook stands a good chance of altering your data in way you won't want and will do so permanently. So never run a sync without backing up your Outlook data first.

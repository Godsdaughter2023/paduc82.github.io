Check your assumption by using right-click on the Taskbar icon and examining its menu.   If the window is really "minimized"  the menu would confirm that by showing  Minimize as being unusable and only  Restore and Close  as being usable.   Usually your symptom description indicates that the window is really in its normal mode but just off-screen or hidden in some other manner.   Then you could use the Taskbar icon's other right-click menu items, such as Move and Size to reposition or resize the window to make it more apparent.

apollo p say:

In Word 2003, Click on Insert/Symbol.  This opens the Symbol dialog box.  From here, click on the Character code box and type in: 221E

This is the infinity symbol.  If you use it often, it may be a good idea to add it to your AutoCorrect list.  You can do this by clicking on the AutoCorrect button and adding a custom key selection, such as (8).

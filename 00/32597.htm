alex_tech09 say: I don't think you can password protect individual files without third party software, however I do recall something to do with encryption.

But here's an idea. Make an administrator account and password protect it. Put the pictures in your documents for that account. Make them hidden files. Add a password to the account.

Then make normal user accounts without administrative priveleges for the other computer users. That way they won't be able to view your images at all and they won't have access to your files. For added security you login your admin account in safe mode, right click the folder they are in.
Click properties, navigate to the security tab, edit permissions for the users and deny full control for all of the users except for you the administrator.

you can even deny full control for the administrator so that no one will have access. then you can go back and allow access for yourself when you want to view them.

-Alex

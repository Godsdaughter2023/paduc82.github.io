    [Moved from: Windows / Windows 10 / Network & internet]

    john vil say:
    Hi Tom,

    Depending on your email configuration, your emails may or may not be deleted from the server if you delete it from the Windows 10 Mail app. If you're using a POP3 account, this will let you download messages from your server to your email client. Deleting emails on Windows 10 Mail should not reflect on your server with this account type.

    IMAP accounts sync your email client with the server. If a message is deleted from the Windows 10 Mail app and starts to sync with your server, that same message will be deleted from the server.

    Should you need further assistance, don't hesitate to post back.

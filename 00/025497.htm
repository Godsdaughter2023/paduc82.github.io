rohn007 say:

If you are doing any math, especially multipication and division, you can have tiny decimal fractions that normally do not show up in 2 decimal displays. But the result is that 0=SUM(....) could be false.

This is a known issue in Excel and computers in general. The short excuse is that people count in groups of 10, decimal. Computers count in 2's, binary.  Binary cannot always exactly represent Decimal values. Rather than rely on Excel to do implicit rounding, one fix is to explicitly round calculations to 2 decimal places.

You want to use the free upgrade to 10 you got on the machine with 8.1 on a different machine.

You can't install the free upgrade to another computer by itself.

The Windows Product Key/License for the Qualifying Operating System,  Windows 8.1 was absorbed into the Windows 10 Upgrade during the installation process and becomes part of the Activated final install of Windows 10.

You have gone back to 8.1 and removed the free upgrade to 10.

Your choices are to either:

It depends on how Windows 10 was originally configured on your computer.

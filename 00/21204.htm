If the Title field is blank for any given record it will not occupy any room in the merged result. The only <space> that needs to be avoided is the one between Title & Firstname. Another way to look at it is that a <space> should be inserted there only if the Title field is not blank, so the easiest approach is to not insert a "hard" <space> to begin with :-) That can be done by using an If...Then...Else... field.

  1. When you insert the Fields do not type the <space> between those two fields, just insert them as:
  2. {Title}{Firstname}<space>{Lastname}
  3. In the main document use Option+F9 to reveal the Field Codes (depending on your keyboard type you may need to use fn+Option+F9)
  4. Click between {Title] & {Firstname}
  5. Click the Rules button on the Ribbon & choose If...Then...Else...
  6. In the Insert Word Field dialog,
    1. select Title from the Field name list
    2. Select Is Not Blank from the Comparison list
    3. In the Insert this text area press the Space Bar once
    4. Click OK
  7. Click the Update Labels button on the Ribbon. The result will be displayed as:
  8. Press Option+F9 again to toggle off the display of Field Codes (Note that the Rules field will not be visible unless Field Codes are displayed)

If you are looking for a check box form field (which requires that you protect the document for forms), then you need to use the Developer tab.

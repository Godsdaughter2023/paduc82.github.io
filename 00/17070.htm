Word sometimes makes the text in the last row of a page disappear when the cell is middle- or bottom-aligned vertically. Try:

  • Setting the cell's vertical alignment to Top.
  • Moving around any pictures in the header or at the top of the page (the bug seems related to floating graphic elements present on the same page)

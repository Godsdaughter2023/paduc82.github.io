chrmit say:

I have just encountered the same/similar problem and spent an age trying to find a solution both here and through Google with no success. The good news is that after a bit of trial & error I have managed to resolve the issue with a very simple workaround :smileyvery-happy:

 

  • When installing NSU, continue through the installation until presented with the error message.
  • Here, grab a pen and paper and note down the full path of the .DLL causing the problem.
  • Now continue to install (ignoring the error) by clicking on the right arrow.
  • Once insallation is complete, open the start menu and select 'Run'
  • Here you need to type "regsvr32" (without quotation marks) followed by a space then the full path of the file you have noted from the error message. (Note - the path should be in quotation marks). As an example, below is exactly what I typed to resolve my issue

regsvr32 "C:\Program Files\Common Files\Nokia\Tss\ProductApiLoader\ta_productapiloader.dll"

 

  • Now open NSU and update your handset.

 Good luck and remember to feed back your results on this thread for the benefit of others.

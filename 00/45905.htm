onyx is here say:

You can certainly download the DLC on one console and then access it on another at a later date.  However, be aware that when you download to the second console, only you will be able to access the content on the second console.  This is because DLC is licensed to both the gamertag and the first console it was downloaded to.  If you do a License Transfer to the second console then the DLC will be available to everyone on the second console and only you on the first.

You can access your download history on the console by going to System>Accounts>Download History.

Monthly subscription not renewal.

techfreak_ say:

All subscriptions attempt to bill 3 days prior to the subscription cycle ends. If the Skype.com site states your subscription is billed on the 19th and renews on the 22nd, it should renew on the 22nd. However, those minutes will renew based on the anniversary date and time. If the time of day does not match the time of day you signed up, those minutes would not be released yet.

If you ran out of minutes of an existing subscription and purchased the exact same subscription over again, before the old subscription expired, you would not get any minutes sooner. Minutes would only be available after the old subscription expired, even if the old subscription was cancelled.

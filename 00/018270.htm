Excel can't but you can. :-)

The basic problem is to calculate the first day of the given week in the given year

=MAX(DATE(B1,1,1),DATE(B1,1,1)-WEEKDAY(DATE(B1,1,1),2)+(B2-1)*7+B3)

Where B1 contains the year and B2 the week number and B3 contains the nth daynumber.
That means you have to split your string into 3 parts first. You can do that using TextToColumns or formulas.

hpholm say:

The stop restart app was a tool to confirm a critical software update was applied on some Lumia phone models. The app itself does not resolve restart issues.

All you have to do software-wise is confirm the phone and its apps are fully updated.

Random restarts can be caused by, for example:

- Battery not seated correctly in the phone, damage or corrosion on the battery contact points, or a weak or worn out battery.

- SIM card not seated correctly in the phone, SIM card damaged or worn out, or internal damage or corrosion on the SIM connector in the phone.

- SD memory card not seated correctly, SD damaged or worn out, or internal damage or corrosion on the SD connector in the phone.

Please ask a skilled friend for help if in doubt how to check the above.

If the SIM card is old or cut to size, then I suggest request a replacement SIM from your mobile operator as a precaution. They should provide a replacement SIM for free when the restart issue is explained to them. This way you keep the same number and credit.

The SD memory card should also be replaced if there is any doubt about its health.

Master Pages in Publisher are intended to contain content that is seen in each page of the publication. You can also create multiple master pages and apply them to different pages. In a Purchasing Department they had to download the list of active POs every day, and add comments if there were new POs, but keep the comments for the other POs active.

 

As the query will change the position of the rows, and some will come and go, a solution was needed.

 

They had six columns updated from a query.

PO

Vendor

Buyer

Item

Required Quantity

Due date

 

And in column G they would enter their comment.

 

The solution was to:

 

a) Copy the sheet as it was, with all the comments, and paste it as valued to another sheet called "old comments".

b) Comments would be retrieved by using a VLOOKUP in column G. The VLOOKUP looked for codes in "old comments".

c) Upon refreshing the data, buyers would update column G in the refreshed sheet.

garym123 say:

You can reset your phone again and get your settings and SMS messages restored if you backed them up. Your apps will be downloaded as part ot the restore.

 

If you don't want to reset again you can install all the apps you had by going to windowsphone.com. On your phone account is 'purchase history'. From there is a list of your apps with a reinstall link.

garym123 say:

Before you do a reset did you backup you phone in settings, backup? You pics and music will not be backed up so save that to the PC.

 

When the phone starts up after the reset you have one chance to do a restore during phone setup. That is when it asks for a MS account and password.  If you select 'later' you can not restore.  This is over your data connection, not WiFi.

Your backup will be downloaded along with your list of installed apps.  The phone will start downloading apps and go to the start screen.

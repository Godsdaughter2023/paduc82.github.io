doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

If you press ALT+F9 to toggle on the display of the field codes in the document, what do you see where the Table of Contents should be, particularly in the case where you see "No Table of Contents Entries Found"?

You should see { TOC \o "1-3" \h \z \u }

In that code, the meanings of the arguments are as follows

\o Builds a table of contents by using outline levels instead of TC entries

"1-3" Builds a table of contents with three levels (if corresponding outline levels are present in the body of the document)

\h Hyperlinks the entries and page numbers within the table of contents

\z Hides the page numbers in the table of contents when the document is in Web Layout View

\u Builds a table of contents by using the applied paragraph outline level

If you cannot sort it out, send me a copy of the document, referencing this thread in the covering email message, I will investigate the issue.

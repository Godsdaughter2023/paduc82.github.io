A fix, no, but a workaround might be to insert a duplicate of each slide that includes animation, edit the slide to move the animated shapes to their final position (or to wherever you want them to appear in the printed/PDF'd version).

Set these slides to be hidden so they don't appear when you show the presentation.

Create a custom show that includes any un-animated slides plus these edited versions of the animated slides.

That gives you a printout or PDF that'll do what you're after.

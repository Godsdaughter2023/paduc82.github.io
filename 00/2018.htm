janeencasebolt say:

how do i get a template for letters in office word 2010

Original title: templates

how do i get a template for letters in office word 2010

suzanne s. barnhill say:
  1. Click on the File tab.
  2. Click New.
  3. In the top row, click Sample Templates. The samples include a number of letter templates in the Office styles (Adjacency, Apothecary, etc.)
  4. If none of these is quite what you want, click the Back button to get back to the Available Templates window, where you should see Office.com Templates.
  5. Click Letters (or Letterhead). There will be more than you know what to do with!

jannine_p say:

Hello

Adding contacts using the new Skype isn't the same as the classic version of Skype. To find contact on the new Skype application:

  1. Select Search Skype.
  2. Type the name, Skype name or email of the person you want to chat with.
  3. Select your friend from the search results and you'll be taken to a conversation window.
  4. Type a message to your contact and select the Send button.
  5. Once the message has been sent, a contact request will be automatically sent to the contacts you want to add.
  6. Once they respond, it means that they were able to accept your contact request.

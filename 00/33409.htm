Does it start to ring after you login, and is the ringing sound like a telephone? If yes,and you have a fax/modem installed, go to Control Panel > Device Manager, look for a fax modem in the configuration and disable it. Does the ringing stop? 

 

If the answer is no, look to see if the fax console is trying to call out, assuming that you have fax console installed.

 

Still ringing?

 

Open up Task Manager - CTRL + Shift + Esc. What programs are running? Nothing should be there after a fresh boot.

 

Is there anything unusual in the Notification area (the bit by the clock)? Maybe something has made its way into 'autostart'

 

Is one of your partitions full to bursting? Have you been saving stuff to your recovery partition?

 

What is the status of the Action Center icon in the notification area? Just a plain white flag?

 

Report back here with your answers to the above. It may take a while to think of another possible cause. Hopefully, somebody else can add their two cents.. :-)

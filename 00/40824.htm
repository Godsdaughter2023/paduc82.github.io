Port forwarding for Xbox One is done on your router or other network hardware, not on the Xbox console itself.

Xbox Live requires the following ports to be open:

Port 53 (UDP and TCP)

Port 80 (TCP)

Port 500 (UDP)

Port 3544 (UDP)

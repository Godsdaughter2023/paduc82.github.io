Activision/Inifinity Ward and 2K/Gearbox are aware of that issue and are working on a patch that fixes that issue.

miss diggz say:

Hey again everyone,

I'm truly sorry to hear about the freezing issues being experienced. As I previously posted in this thread, we suggest, if you haven't already, trying out these troubleshooting steps below.

  • Uninstall MotoGP 15 from your Xbox One console.
  • Power Cycle your Xbox One console by holding the Xbox button on the front of the console for approximately 10 seconds. The console will power down.
  • Once your console is completely off, unplug the console from the power source and wait about three minutes.
  • After three minutes has passed, plug all the cords back in and turn on your console.
  • Sign into your account and download/install MotoGP 15 to your console and try playing it again.

If the troubleshooting steps above don't seem to make a difference and you're still getting the freezing issues, I would then strongly recommend contacting MotoGP 15's developer, Milestone srl. Those are the proper folks to get in touch with for further assistance regarding MotoGP 15. Hope this helps, thanks in advance!

  1. Press Windows key + X, select “Command promt (Admin)”.
  1. In the command prompt window, type “chkdsk/r” and press Enter.
  2. Press “Y” when it asks if you want to check disk to run when the computer boots the next time.
  3. Restart the computer for Check disk to detect and repair any corrupted sectors in your hard drive.

 

Important: While performing Error-checking or ChkDsk on the hard drive, if any bad sectors are found and when attempting to repair that sector, the data available on that sector might be lost.

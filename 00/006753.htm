1. Ran winword.exe /a from the search bar of the start menu (this verified it was an add on causing the problem because it started with no issues)

2. Restarted Word and clicked on the File > Options > Add-Ins.

3.  This screen shows you which add-ins you have installed, and which are active and inactive.

4.  At the bottom of the screen, you'll see a selection box next to the word Manage.  Choose COM Add-ins, and click Go.

5.  You can deselect all of the add-ins at once, or eliminate them one by one (restart Word after each selection) until you find which one is the issue.

For me, it was the Acrobat PDFMaker add-in, so I disabled that one and haven't have a problem since.

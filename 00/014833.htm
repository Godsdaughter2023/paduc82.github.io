Office 365 Team Site with upload only permission - Microsoft Community

linhnguyen nhu say:

Office 365 Team Site with upload only permission

Dear all,

I need to create a Site with upload only permission, users can upload file but cannot edit or delete the file. Is this possible with Office 365 Team Sites?

Thanks a lot. 

sheenali_msft say:

hi linh,

yes, it is possible. please follow the guide below on how to create and assign a group with upload and view only permissions.

1.    create a new permission level.
navigate to the team site and go to the site settings. under users and permissions, click site permissions.

on the site permissions ribbon, click permission levels.

 

in the permission levels page, click the “add a permission level” button.

insert the name and description for your new permission level. here you will need to select the permissions for your document library (which is also a list).

for uploading and viewing only, you will need to choose “add items”, “view items”, “view application pages”, “view pages”, “use remote interfaces”, “use client integration features” and “open”.

note: you can further customize your permissions as you like.

 



2.    create a new group that uses this new permission level.
navigate to the team site and go to the site settings. under users and permissions, click people and groups. this will bring you to the team site members group by default. navigate to team site groups by clicking on “groups” at the quick launch menu on the left of the page.

 

inside the people and groups page, click new > create group.

insert the name of the group, and configure the settings as you like.

under “give group permission to this site”, select the name of the permission level that you just created. in this case, the name of my permission level is upload & view.


3.    add users to the group
the last step is to add users that you wish to give the upload & view only permission to the group that you just created. to do this, go to the group that you just created, and click on new > add users.

Spell check & Proofing issue with - English (U.K) Language in Microsoft Office Products

We have noticed an issue with Spell check & Proofing  when language set as default to  English (U.K) . Microsoft Spell check not able to identify certain misspelt words . (Eg : Organization, Prioritize)  .

 

Where as when we set the Language as default to English (U.S) microsoft Spell check works fine.

 

It is very important for us to identify all the misspelt words with English (U.K) language. But Microsoft spell check ignores certain words without detecting as Misspelt.

 

Any solution for the above very much appreciated

 

 

graham mayor say:
Where - as in the case of certain words with ise or ize -  both are correct alternatives in UK English, then the spell checker will not flag either spelling as incorrect - because they are not incorrect. If you prefer to flag one or other as incorrect, then you need to add the 'incorrect' spelling to an Exclude dictionary.

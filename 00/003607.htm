    tina zhou msft say:

    Hi Rachel,

    Thanks for the query. 

    As a student to get Office 365 for free, you need your school email address. You must be a student at an academic institution and:

    1. Have a school-specific email address provided by the school (for example, contoso.edu) that can receive external email.

    3. Have Internet access.

    jack cook - aka help_line say:

    Update #2

     

    Got it!!  What happened was that I was able to switch account (which is what you want to do).  So of course as I was playing , did I write down what I was doing?  NO!

     

    Fortunately, yesterday I set up a new Live account and the Touch I was using was originally set uo with a Hotmail account so it was similar to your setup ... I wanted to change form one to the other and since I could not delete, that posed a problem.

     

    So I went to Messaging

    Then I clicked on New Email Account

    I entered my new Live account and pressed next.  It auto setup finding the settings then gave me the instruction to click NEXT to complete it

    So when I did that it actually brought up my old account.  I clicked on account options and I was given 4 choices on that page of which one was to SWITCH ACCOUNT

    I pressed SWITCH ACCOUNT, it gave me a message telling me my setting were about to be changed....I clicked YES

    It went out to the net, had me agree to a terms of use, I had to sign into the new account and it happened after a few seconds (well, more than a few actually ...but it did it).  It then gave me an option on what I wanted on the Today screen (I accepted that) and then it synced my email and contacts and it was done....

     

    So, SUCCESS in changing the account ...it worked abbeit just a little slow but it may well be the time of the day.  All in all, 3-5 minutes, I was not really timing it but the important thing was that it changed.

     

    So I did it again, switching back ...that worked too.

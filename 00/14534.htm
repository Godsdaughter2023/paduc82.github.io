moranyan say:

Hi Jeroen,

Q. Why is the attachment hidden?

A. You can grant permission to others to see your calendar. By default, others can only see free/busy times. This means when proposing a meeting and using the Scheduling Assistant, everyone can see blocks of time when you are busy, but cannot see other details, such as subject, location, participants, notes, or attachments.

Q. How can I make the attachment visible?

A. You can grant Reviewer permissions to others to see your calendar. With Reviewer permission, people can read items, but cannot create, edit, or delete items.

Q. Is there even a way to provide attachments to read-only users through the calendar?

A. No, since read-only users cannot see the attachments in the calendar, it’s not feasible to provide attachments to read-only users through the calendar events.

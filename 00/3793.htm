However, I want to add a simple addition to it and it's not working.  This is what I'm trying to do:  in the formula where it sums E7:E502, I want it to subtract from E7:E502 the total of F7:F502, so that the total sum for the product "F2" will be Col. =SUMPRODUCT(E7:E502-F7:F502,--(A7:A502="F2"))

Note that the first double negative (--) was superfluous in your original formula.  The purpose of using double negative is to convert the logic values TRUE and FALSE resulting from comparisons (A7:A502="F2") to 1 and 0, which SUMPRODUCT requires.

But any arithmetic operation will have the same effect.  So you could write:

=SUMPRODUCT((E7:E502-F7:F502)*(A7:A502="F2"))

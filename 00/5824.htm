Hi,

1.     Have you made any changes to your computer prior to this issue?

2.     What is the exact error message?

3.     Is this an issue specific with downloading iTunes?

You may follow the steps and then check.

a.Open Internet Explorer by clicking the Start button , and then clicking Internet Explorer.   b.Click the Tools button, and then click Internet Options.

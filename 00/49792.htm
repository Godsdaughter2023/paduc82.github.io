Accessing Windows 8 firmware settings - Microsoft Community

rebecca_aa1 say:

Accessing Windows 8 firmware settings

I want to get to the firmware settings menu, and be able to boot Surface from a USB device if I need to.

How do I get into the BIOS on Surface?
steven_b say: Greetings Rebecca,

Surface Pro has the latest firmware interface, called Unified Extensible Firmware Interface (UEFI), instead of BIOS.

 

You can use Windows Advanced startup to restart Surface Pro to the UEFI firmware settings menu. You can also start Surface Pro from a bootable USB device. The steps are:

 

1.       Swipe in from the right edge of the screen, and then tap Settings.
(If you're using a mouse, point to the upper-right corner of the screen, move the mouse pointer down, and then click Settings.)

2.       Tap or click Change PC Settings.

3.       Tap or click General on the left side of the screen.

4.       Scroll down to Advanced Startup on the right side of the screen and tap Restart now.

5.       At the Choose an option screen:

 ·         For the UEFI firmware settings menu, tap or click Troubleshoot, then Advanced Options, then  UEFI Firmware Settings, then Restart.

 ·         To start Surface from a USB device, tap or click Use a device, then tap or click the name of the USB device.

     

Note  To start from a USB device, first attach the a bootable USB device to Surface Pro. 

 

For more info, see What is UEFI?

patc---01 say:

If you mean you want to view the contents - eg: a phone number or diary entry - the answer is, no not easily. 

 

You could try looking at it using a text editor like notepad or textpad which will probably show some of the data in readable text, but possibly not what you're after and besides, most of it's not going to be easy to read.

 

The only real option would be to:

 

- backup your phone

 

- restore the old backup you're interested in onto your phone

 

- do whatever checks you need to and WRITE DOWN anything you want to keep

 

- restore the latest backup onto your phone

 

Of course, the data - and any changes you make - on the phone from the old backup will be lost when you do the second restore, hence the writing down anything you want to keep.

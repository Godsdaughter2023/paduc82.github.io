=MATCH(A2,Sheet2!$A$2:$A$10,0)

=MATCH(B2,Sheet2!$B$2:$B$10,0)

Sub MoveMEtoFront()

mala_s say:

Hello DanielFPineau,

Thank you for writing to Microsoft Community Forums.

The issue could be occurring due to direct import of files to your PC. So, I suggest you to import your files to hard disk and then from the hard disk to your PC. Also, before we start with the troubleshooting steps, kindly disconnect all the external devices (except keyboard and mouse) that is connected to your PC and check with the steps listed below and see if it helps.

Method 1: Disable Parallel Preview Generation setting:

I would recommend you to disable Parallel Preview option. To do that, go to Lightroom Classic Settings > Preferences > Performance > disable Parallel Preview setting.

Method 2: Give permissions to the imported files.

I would also suggest you to give permission to the files that you are trying to import. To do that,

  • Right click on the folder (folder which you want to import) and click on Properties at the bottom of the context menu.

  • Click the Security tab, and click on Advanced. If you are prompted for an administrator password or for confirmation, type the password or provide confirmation.

  • Click on Change beside owner.

  • Type Everyone, click on Check Names and click on OK.

  • Click on Add.

  • Click on Select a Principal.

  • Type Everyone, click on Check Names and click on OK.

  • Click on Full Control.

  • Click on OK.

  • Now, click on Apply and then clickOK.

Also, please include the dump files in your next response for further clarification of the issue. To upload the mini dump files from the location here, which will help us determine the exact cause of the issue.

c:/windows /minidump

Copy the files and zip it and upload it here.

To enable mini dump file:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click System.

3. Click the Advanced tab, and then click Settings under Startup and Recovery.

4. In the Write debugging information list, click Small memory dump (64k).

Regards,

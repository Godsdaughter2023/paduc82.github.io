EDIT (solution)

I have found a solution to this. Thank you very much to Arshad' Khan and Bernd P. for trying to help.

1. Take a screen clipping (Windows+S) and OneNote will automatically open.

2. Click Misplaced Sections (icon at bottom left corner) then right click on Unfiled Notes then click Merge into Another Section... and Merge Section window will open.

3. Click '+' (plus) sign beside Unfiled Notes and select Unfiled Notes (not the one with the paper image) then click Merge.

4. Alert windows will appear. Click Merge Sections then Delete.

5. Close OneNote.

6. Take a screen clipping (Windows+S) and OneNote will automatically open with an alert window. Click OK.

7. Close OneNote again.

8. Take a screen clipping (Windows+S) again and OneNote will automatically open with Select Location in OneNote window opened.

9. Select Unfiled Notes (not the one with the paper image) then check 'Don't ask me again and always do the following:' then click Send to Selected Location.

To check whether you did it right, take a screen clipping and see if the error is still there or not. I hope this helps :)

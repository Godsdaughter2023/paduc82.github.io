bob jones aka: cybertaz say:
Phillip is correct that a PDF can be inserted only as a graphic object but you're not limited to 1 page of the PDF. What you're limited to is inserting 1 page of the PDF at a time.

I'd suggest that you first go to Word> Preferences> Edit to confirm that Insert/Paste pictures as: is set to In Line with Text.

At the end of p.6 just go to Insert> Photo - Picture from File. When you select the PDF you'll be provided with a dialog. Select the 1st page to be inserted. Once it's inserted, click outside the bottom right corner to deselect the image of the PDF object, then repeat the process for each additional page.

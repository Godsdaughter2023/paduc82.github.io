Hi,

 

We understand your concern about deleting Facebook friends from your contact list.  Unfortunately, there is no option to delete Facebook contacts in your contact list. Once you've connected your Facebook account to your Hotmail, your Facebook friends are automatically added to your contacts.

 

If you want to remove your Facebook contacts from your Hotmail account, you will need to disconnect from Facebook services. To do this, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your account here. It will direct you to your profile.
  2. Under you profile picture, you will see the services that you are connected with.
  3. Click Manage.
  4. You will now see the Facebook logo. Click on the Edit link.
  5. Scroll down the page and you will be able to view the Remove this connection completely link.
  6. Click the link and a pop up box will appear. Choose Remove on the confirmation message and Facebook will now be disconnected/unlinked on your Microsoft account.

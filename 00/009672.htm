Uncheck "Send immediately when connected" as follows:
File-> Options-> Advanced-> option group: Send and Receive-> option: Send immediately when connected

Then "Disable Scheduled Send/Receive" as follows:

Send/Receive > Send Receive Groups > Disable Scheduled Send/Receive

This is not 'my' program. I do not work for nor speak for Microsoft. This is a user forum.

As for your formatting issue, Word is not a page layout application so there are no 'pages' in a Word document. Documents comprise of text flowed between margins and around objects. The displayed pages are a result of that text flow.

The number of lines that will fit on a 'page' is determined by various factors including the defined paper size, the top and bottom margin size, the font size, the line spacing and the paragraph spacing. These values may also be affected by the printer driver used to format the document. See http://word.mvps.org/FAQs/Formatting/TextReflow.htm

Hi & welcome too,

The 30 day waiting period can not be waived.

What should I know about the 30-day waiting period?

Even when you choose to delete your out-of-date security info, MS don't fully remove it for 30 days.

This waiting period helps to deter hackers from updating your security info and gaining access to your account without your knowledge.

After 30 days, the user of the account will be able to access the security settings and update his/her preferred information.

But the waiting period doesn't mean you're locked out of your Microsoft account.

Check to see if you can still access your emails via https://mail.live.com.

Hi,

 

Try it like this

 

Sub DeleteRowsOfEmptyBCells()
    Dim LastRow As Long
    Dim r As Long
    Dim Counter As Long
 
    Application.ScreenUpdating = False
 
    LastRow = ActiveSheet.UsedRange.Rows.Count + _
      ActiveSheet.UsedRange.Rows(1).Row - 1
 
    For r = LastRow To 1 Step -1
        If Len(Range("B" & r)) = 0 Then
            ActiveSheet.Rows(r).Delete
            Counter = Counter + 1
 
        End If
 
    Next r
 
    Application.ScreenUpdating = True
 
    MsgBox Counter & " Empty rows were deleted."
 
End Sub

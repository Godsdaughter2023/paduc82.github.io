braintrove.com say: It could be that InfoPath 2003 doesn't support this. I know it works in InfoPath 2013--Filler only, not the designer.

If you have Win7 or Win8 you can use the Magnifier app in Windows. To access this you can go to the control panel or you can use hold down the Windows key on your keyboard and press the + and - keys to zoom in out, and Windows+ESC to cancel zoom.

A Form does not have a Height value like a Control. A SECTION of a Form does, however, so you need to refer to a Section of your form for the Width:

Me.Width = 1 * 1440

Me.Detail.Height = 20 * 1440

If you set the Detail section to a specific width, the other sections will be sized along with the Detail section.

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

Create an Excel data source with a column of the numbers that you want to appear on the tickets with the first row containing the word "Number"

 

Then with your document open, expand the Start Mail Merge item on the Mailings tab of the ribbon and select Letters.  Then use the Select Recipients facility to select the Excel workbook as the data source.  Then use the Insert Merge Field facility in the Write & Insert Fields section of the ribbon to insert the Number mergefield in each place on the document where you want the ticket number to appear.

 

Then, before the first «Number» field on the second and third tickets, insert a Next Record field from the Rules dropdown in the Write & Insert Fields section.

 

You should then have the following on your document

 

«Number»                                            «Number»

 

«Next Record»«Number»                    «Number»

 

«Next Record»«Number»                    «Number»

 

Then, use the Finish & Merge facility in the Finish section of the ribbon to execute the merge. 

 

Edit Individual Documents, will produce a document containing all of your tickets that will allow you to check that everything is in order and you can either print that document, or execute the merge again directly to the printer.

jeeped say:
... I have tried to UNHIDE the column, but that doesn't work, any ideas as to what I did wrong?

It would seem that you have hidden column A or inadvertently set the width to 0, essentially the same thing.
Method 1 - Click and hold the mouse's left click on the B in the column headers to select the entire column B. With the mouse's left click held down , move the mouse to the left over to the row labels until you see a 2C in a small hot-tip box. Release, then right click the selected area and select Unhide.
Method 2 - click the intersection of the row and column header labels or tap Ctrl+A to select the entire worksheet. From the Home tab's Cells group, select Format, Column Width and assign a value of 8.43 which is the default.

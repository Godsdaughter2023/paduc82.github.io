I had the same issue with the new Outlook.com - my images never loaded regardless of browser or machine.  I solved this by correcting the content type of the images.

 

In my case the images were being served out of an Azure storage account and they were not uploaded with the correct content type.  The ImageProxy in Outlook.com must be checking for valid content types such as image/jpeg, image/png, image/gif etc.  Once I fixed the upload of the images on Azure storage everything works perfectly.

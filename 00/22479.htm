stephenpsa say:

Hi Max,

The big question is what of kind of license you have. MS sells two types of licenses:

a) Full Licenses and

b) OEM Licenses

A OEM license if tied to a specific type of MB and cannot be transferred to any other. So if your license came with your machine and you did not replace the MB with the exact same Make/Model, then you are out of luck and will have to buy a new license.

Have a look at your system's product key (Control Panel->System and Sec->System), if it contains the letters OEM than you have an OEM license, but, alas, if not, well, I cannot say for sure that it's not an OEM license anyway.

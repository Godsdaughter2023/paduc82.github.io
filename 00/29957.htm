This issue could occur if the USB devices are not entering the suspended mode. in other words, the USB devices connected to the computer are responsible to prevent the computer from going into sleep mode.

I would suggest you to uninstall and reinstall the USB devices and check if it fixes the issue. Follow the methods:

Method 1:

Follow the steps mentioned below:

  • Exit command prompt.

Method 3:

Follow the steps to enable suspended mode under power options:

  • Type power plan on the Start screen and click Choose a power plan.
  • Next to your currently selected plan, click Change Plan Settings.
  • Click Change advanced power settings. Click on Change settings that are currently unavailable.
  • Click the box to expand USB Settings, then expand USB selective suspend settings.
  • Select disabled. Check for the same steps for hard disk tab as well.

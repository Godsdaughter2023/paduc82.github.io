yoskopolite say: Ok I finally found the issue. In my case, here are the exact conditions that cause the controls to flash :
  1. Using Excel 2010 (mine is a 32bit version)
  2. Having Freezed Panes
  3. Having "floating objects" (ActiveX controls, shapes, etc...) over the Freezed Pane
  4. Having the "Print_area" name defined for one (or more) entire row/column.
  5. Starting a Print Preview or a Print.
My solution was : creating the "Print_area" name juste before the print preview, and deleting it just after that.

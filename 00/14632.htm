Soft Reset in Windows 10 Mobile is not that complicated. It can be done by keeping volume down button and power button pressed together for almost 15 second.  After few seconds, you should feel the phone vibrating and see the boot screen once again. Once the phone restarts, you can release the button and you are good to go.

A soft reset will only clear memory, terminate all apps and reload the OS. It won’t erase or access any user data. If you have a phone with a removable battery, just remove the battery and reboot after replacing the battery. That will also clear the memory, and terminate the apps and reload. There will be nothing erased.

hansv mvp say:

Start by recording a small macro to your personal macro workbook. It doesn't need to do anything useful. Once you have done that, Personal (PERSONAL.XLSB) will be visible in the Visual Basic Editor.

Now open the workbook with the macros. In the Project Explorer on the left hand side of the Visual Basic Editor, you can drag some or all modules under the Modules heading from your workbook and drop them on Personal. This will copy the modules.

Alternatively, you can open those modules by double-clicking them, and copy/paste individual macros into modules in Personal.

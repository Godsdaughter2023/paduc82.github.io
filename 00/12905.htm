The page ruler uses the lower left hand corner of the page as 0,0.  You can reset the 0,0 point to a different location, such as the upper left corner, however, that will result in a ruler that shows the numbers going down from 0 to minus 11, for an 8.5 x 11" page, for instance.

Here is a short macro you can run:

Sub InsertCells()
    Dim r As Long
    Dim m As Long
    Application.ScreenUpdating = False
    m = Range("A" & Rows.Count).End(xlUp).Row
    For r = m - 1 To 1 Step -1
        Range("A" & r).Offset(1).Resize(2).Insert Shift:=xlShiftDown
    Next r
    Application.ScreenUpdating = False
End Sub

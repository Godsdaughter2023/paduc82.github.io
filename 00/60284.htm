Microsoft standard answers!!!!! HA HA.

Here is the real answer. No drivers needed.

This adapter is only fully compatible with any controller as long as it has SI (silicon image) chip on it.
It could be Addonics own or third party one but it has to have SI chip on it.
If you don't have SI chipped controller it will work with some cards and will not with others.

On the other hand it sometimes automatically assigns drive letter to the card and sometimes it will not depending on the OS upgrades. If it does not do it automatically you need to open Disk Manager and assign it and as long as you don't format it in the camera it will stay assigned. So learn to delete all pictures in the camera instead of formatting in the camera. If you reformat the card you need to go through Disk Manager again.

Mine used to assign the same drive letter to any card but after a month (maybe after some automatic OS (win7 64) upgrade) it just stopped. That is why I am here to find the answer to this.

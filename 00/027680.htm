jennifer shi msft say:

Hi Iksan,

Thanks for posting your question here. This feature to "Get email address" of a channel in Microsoft Teams needs to be enabled by the IT admin. 

If you are admin of your organization, please follow below steps to see if this feature has been enabled for your Teams:

1. Sign in Microsoft Teams Admin Center: https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/company-wide-settings/client-settings.

2. Negative to Org-wide settings > Teams settings.

3. Under Email integration, enable "Allow users to send a channel email addree" > Save. For your reference: 

Meanwhile, if team members still cannot get email address after you enable this feature in Teams admin center or this feature has been enabled for your Teams, please check if you can get email address for this channel. If so, as a workaround, you can share this email address to team memberms. 

Please feel free to share any update with us. Also, we need to confirm following questions if you need further assistance about this issue:

Regards,

Jennifer

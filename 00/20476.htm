To hide rows then put a button on your worksheet and attach the code below. To unhide the rows attach another button and change the TRUE in bold to FALSE and attach that code.

Dim LastRow As Long, x As Long
LastRow = Cells(Cells.Rows.Count, "D").End(xlUp).Row
For x = 1 To LastRow
If UCase(Cells(x, "D")) = "NOT APPLICABLE" Then
    Rows(x).Hidden = True
End If
Next

stargazer2255 say:

WOW! I think this is the first time I've ever known a Lumia 720 user having call quality problems.

 

First for the unnecessary vibrations: Tap Settings, tap ringtone and swipe Vibrate to Off. But as for call dropping there's only these steps:

 

1. Soft reset: press and hold the volume down and power keys for 10 seconds and switch it on again.

 

2. Hard reset: tap Settings, tap about and tap reset your phone. (Back up all data of your phone before attempting this)

 

3. If all else fails: A. Change your SIM card if it's cut down from a mini to micro size or if you're given a defective micro SIM card. B. Send the phone back to its point of purchase for a replacement phone. If the point of purchase refuses your request, take the phone to the best place: your nearest Nokia Care point for the replacement phone.

Please always give the make and model of your device when posting an issue.  Help us help you.

Your issue has nothing to do with an Insider preview build.  Your issue is that the licensing manager in Windows is reading your license as based on a volume license key.  Volume license keys are only available from an organization which has a contract with Microsoft for licensing a fixed number of seats for one price.  The organization owns the volume license key and a user granted use of the VLK by the org must renew activation sometime during a six-month period in order for his Windows to remain activated.  This renewal is done by connecting to the orgs network.  The org's licensing server then renews the activation for a further six months.  If you are not a member of an org's volume licensing plan and unable to connect to that org's server then your Windows license is not valid.  How you obtained your Windows license then becomes an important question.

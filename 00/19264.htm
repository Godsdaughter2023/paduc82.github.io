Hi Brenda,

Generally, the maximum number of distribution groups that a user can configure as contacts can be set to any integer value between 0 and 64. The default value is 10.

Set-CsClientPolicy -Identity Global -MaximumDGsAllowedInContactList 64

davem121 say:
Hi MollieLyons, this setting is well hidden and not very intuitive, but it can be found in Options . . .

Click File - Options

On the left click Proofing

On the right click 'Autocorrect Options'

In the resulting dialog, click on the 'Autoformat as you type' tab

Uncheck 'Border lines' in the 'Apply as you type' section

Clock OK and close all dialogs

Done, that's It!

bitshifter---01 say:

Ever sense I purchased my Nokia 5800 XpressMusic I was not able to browse the web through wifi.  Like everyone else I received the sickening "No Gateway Reply" message.  I called the Nokia helpdesk several times and they were unable to figure out the problem.

 

For background information - I was able to connect laptops, iPhones, iTouches, and a wireless printer to my wifi without any problems.  I just could not get the 5800 to browse.

 

Solution - Go into your router and set WEP to 128/104 bit and set the key type to hex.  Hex for those who don't know consists of the characters 0-9 and A-F (ie. 0af91de).  I then deleted and reinstalled my connection and am now able to surf the web.  Be sure to update the WEP key for the rest of your wireless devices.  Cheers.

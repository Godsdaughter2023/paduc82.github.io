Assuming your table is in columns E and F.  Put the route # you want to find a count for into cell G2 and enter this formula in another cell, such as H2:

=SUMPRODUCT(--($E:$E>=5),--($F:$F=$G2))

=COUNTIFS($E:$E,">=5",$F:$F,$G2)

Outlook on the desktop cannot sync via ActiveSync. That is a lightweight protocol exclusively for mobile devices. It is usually what the device is doing when you tell it you have a "Microsoft Exchange" account. This is why it works on your iPad or any iOS device, as well as Android, but not MacOS or Windows.  Those clients support the full Exchange experience.

So, if you are connecting to a work account or one with hosted Exchange, simply fill in the Exchange info provided by your admin.

shane devenshire say:

Hi,

I think you are going to need to give us more info.  If you are using Office 2010 why are you in a Works spreadsheet?  Are you in Excel 2010 or Works?

When you execute a Fill Right command in Excel the command is automatically over when you release the mouse.  You can do Fill Right in Excel by:

1.  Highlighting the cell you want copied and all the cells to the right that you want filled - then press Ctrl+R

2. You can drag the Fill Handle from the cell you want to copy as far to the right as you want filled and then release it.  The Fill Handle is the small square at the bottom right corner of the active cell. 

3.  You can highlight the cell you want to copy and the cell you want to copy it to and choose Home, Fill, Right.

Regardless of the method the command is over automatically after you complete the step.

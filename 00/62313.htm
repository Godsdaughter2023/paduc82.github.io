    Automatic logout in Windows 8 laptop

    a. user say:

    Hello,

    I will certainly help you with the request you have for Windows 8.1 computer.

    Windows 8.1 can automatically shutdown at specific time, please go through the following steps to schedule task.

    Follow below steps to schedule task:

    a.     Press Windows + C and search for Schedule Task and Push Enter.

    b.     From the Action menu in Task Scheduler, click "Create Task..."

    c.      In the "Create Task" windows that pops up, Type in a Name and a description. Something basic like "PC Shutdown."

    d.     Click "OK."

    e.     On the "Trigger" screen, click on New and Begin the Task at Idle.

    f.      On the "Action" screen, choose "Start a program" and click "Next."

    g.    On the "Start a Program" screen, type C:\Windows\System32\shutdown.exe in the "Program/script" text field.

    i.       In the Add arguments text field type /s.

    j.       Click "Next."

    k.      Confirm your settings on the Summary screen and click "Finish."

    You may refer the below link:

    Schedule a task

    Note: Holds good for Windows 8.1 as well.

    Hope the information helps. Let us know if you need further assistance with Windows related issues, we’ll be glad to assist you.

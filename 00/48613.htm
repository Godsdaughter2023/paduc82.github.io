Yes, it's free. No, you will not have to buy it a new key when it's released. And yes you can get the upgrade for free when it is released.

 

Unless you have a spare computer to install it on, I strongly recommend against installing previews of new Windows versions. That's for two reasons:

 

1. The reason it's a preview instead of a released version is that it hasn't been fully tested yet and probably still has many bugs--maybe even very bad bugs. You put everything on your computer at risk if you run a test version.

 

2. When the actual 8.1 is released, you will have to do a clean installation of it. An upgrade from the preview will not be possible.

 

Read here: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-8/preview-faq , and especially note what it says in the second and third items.

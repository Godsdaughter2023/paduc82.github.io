jay freedman say:
The Proof folder is used by Office 2003 and earlier. The UProof folder is used by Office 2007 and later.

The technical difference is that the dictionary file(s) in the Proof folder uses the older ASCII character encoding, and the dictionary file(s) in the UProof folder uses Unicode encoding (hence the 'U').

Hello,

  please use this function:

Sub Clearcontents()
    Dim iCurrentRow, iCurrentCol As Integer
   
    iCurrentRow = 1
    While ActiveSheet.Cells(iCurrentRow, 1) <> ""
        iCurrentCol = 1
        While ActiveSheet.Cells(iCurrentRow, iCurrentCol) <> ""
            szFrom = ActiveSheet.Cells(iCurrentRow, iCurrentCol)
            szUntil = ActiveSheet.Cells(iCurrentRow, iCurrentCol + 1)
            If szFrom < Date And szUntil < Date Then
                ActiveSheet.Cells(iCurrentRow, iCurrentCol) = ""
                ActiveSheet.Cells(iCurrentRow, iCurrentCol + 1) = ""
            End If
            iCurrentCol = iCurrentCol + 2
        Wend
        iCurrentRow = iCurrentRow + 1
    Wend
    MsgBox ("End of procedure")
End Sub

How to add Custom Ringtones on Windows Phone 8 - Microsoft Community

johan van mierlo say:

How to add Custom Ringtones on Windows Phone 8

Summary
Level: beginners This article will summarize and will provide easy steps how to add custom ringtones to your Windows Phone 8.
Details
Windows Phone 8 (not Windows Phone 7) will now allow you to connect your phone with a micro usb cable to your computer and let you have access to certain folders for you to add files. One of these folders is the "Ringtones" folder.  By simply copy and paste you can get ringtone files from your computer to your Windows Phone 8.

Steps to add a ringtone to Windows Phone 8

To use a music track as a ringtone you have to be sure that the track meets the following requirements:

  • The track is in MP3, M4R or WMA format
  • The track is less than 30 MB
  • The track is not protected with digital rights management (DRM)
  • There is no longer any limit on track length
  1. Connect your Windows Phone 8 to your computer with a micro usb cable.
  2. Browse using your explorer on your computer to the following folder:
    "My Computer" -> "Name of your Windows Phone" -> "Phone" -> "Ringtones"



  3. Paste the ringtone you want into the Ringtones folder.
  4. Now disconnect your Windows Phone 8 from the micro usb cable and browse on your phone to:
    "Settings" -> "ringtones+sounds" and tap on the box "Ringtone"



  5. You will able to see your copied in Ringtone and be able to select it.

If a custom ringtone does not appear, one of the following problems could be the case:

  • The Custom section of the ringtone picker appears only after at least one valid ringtone is added.
  • Verify that the format, size, and length of the ringtone meet the requirements for custom ringtones.
  • Make sure that the custom ringtone is being copied from the computer to the correct folder on the phone. It cannot be copied from the SD card on the phone.
  • Try a different method to copy the ringtone. For example, if the customer is using Windows Explorer, have them try the Windows Phone 8 sync client or use one of the Ringtone applications in the Windows Phone Store.
Can you use an entire song as a ringtone?
Yes, as long as the song does not use digital rights management (DRM). In addition, you must make sure that the song is in MP3, M4R or WMA format, and is less than 30 MB. There is no longer a limit on ringtone duration.

Currently Windows Phone 8 does not allow custom sounds to be added for other notification like Text/SMS.

Tip!
You can add different ringtones to certain people in the contact list of your People Hub.
  1. Go to your contact list in the People Hub and find the person you want to assign a specific ringtone.
  2. Tap on the bottom right pencil icon to enable editing of your contact.
  3. Now tap on the "+ringtone" to assign a specific ringtone.
  4. The Ringtone will be added to your contacted automatically. Now tap the back button on the bottom of your phone to finish the editing process.

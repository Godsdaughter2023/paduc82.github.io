a. user say:

Hi Louise,

Thank you for posting your query on Microsoft Community and sorry for the inconvenience caused.

It is possible that your Account has been migrated to Outlook.com Preview hence your contacts and calendar will not sync any more. If you're using the migrated account in Windows Live Mail, it won't sync either, because the Delta Sync protocol that these functions depend upon isn't supported by Outlook Mail anymore. Follow the steps below to confirm whether your Account has been migrated to Outlook.com Preview:

  1. After signing in to your Microsoft Account and the account Hello! page opens, click View inbox in the left-hand column. 
  2. Your Outlook.com page should open. In the top left-hand corner, some of you may see this:

               

If your Account is migrated to Outlook.com preview and if you're facing the issue then I suggest you to remove and re-add your account in Windows Live Mail. Refer to the link below for more information:

How do I add an account to Windows Live Mail

For firewall issue, you can try to disable the security software/firewall to isolate this. You may also read on the post below for more troubleshooting steps that you may perform:

How can I resolve machine configuration issues in Windows Live Mail?

Please get back to us with the updated status of the issue so that we can work on it to help you better.

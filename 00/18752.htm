hal hostetler - mvp-outlook say:
Which is why I asked about TrendMicro.  Local email scanners are, by virtue of how they must operate, highly trouble prone and should not be used. Disappearing email is only one of many problems they all share. Anti-Virus on client machines is certainly a find thing to do, just not the mail scanner parts. If these machines are running local email scanning try removing the email scanners (leaving the rest of the suite) and see if the problem persists. Note that you may have to uninstall the entire suite, then re-install without the email scanner to fully remove email scanning (this is the only way to get rid of Norton's and McAfee's scanners).

wunders say: You will need to go to the user control panel and create a new user with your name (give it administrative privilege).  Then log into your computer with your new user name.  When your are logged in as your new user, you will then be able to delete the previous user.  Note that the "owner" as appears in certain screens may need to be changed via another procedure:

"How to Change Name and Company Information After You Install Windows XP"

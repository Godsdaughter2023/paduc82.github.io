Autofiltering on the Task Mode column should show you two choices:  Auto Scheduled and Manually Scheduled.  Are those the two choices you see when you click the AutoFilter pick list in the Task Mode column.  If you select Manually Scheduled on the pick list, Microsoft Project will display all Manually Scheduled tasks, along with their respective summary tasks, even if the summary tasks are not Manually Scheduled.  Could that be why you think the AutoFilter is not working?

a. user say:

Ralph

Open the main Skype window, then click on Tools in the menu bar and select Options.

Click on IM & SMS in left-hand menu. In the area that loads, click Show Advanced Settings near the bottom right-hand corner.

Next to the "Keep history for" line, there is a button to Clear History, click this.

Individual delete is possible only for alerts sent by you where you can right click > remove chat

If you want to delete the received alerts. It will not be possible to delete received alerts individually through Skype application. But you can delete the entire conversation by right clicking on left pane > Delete conversations.

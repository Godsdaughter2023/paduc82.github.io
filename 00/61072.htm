tony_du say:

Hi FredCaffrey,

 

I am sorry to hear about your issue with the backlight on your Touch Cover 2. To adjust the backlight, Please use the FN+F2 to make it brighter and the FN+F1 for lower light. If this does not work, please check to make sure it is detecting properly. To do this, follow these instructions:

 

1. Swipe from right side of screen to bring up charms and select Settings.

2. Select Change PC Settings.

3. Select PC and Devices.

4. Select Devices.

5. Verify that your touch cover is showing under Other Devices.

6. Select your cover and select Remove Device.

7. Physically remove the touch cover and restart your Surface.

8. Once restarted, attach your cover and let Windows detect. Restart once more.

 

If this does not work, you may want to return the touch cover to point of purchase or contact Surface Technical Support.

 

Let us know if you need any further assistance.

You can check on your normal template by renaming it, with Word closed, and re-opening Word.

How to Find the Normal Template

If the problem goes away, your normal template was the problem. If not rename it back and continue on to Add-Ins.

Add-Ins in Microsoft Word

That switch will start Word without any Add-Ins. If the problem goes away with that switch, you need to identify the Add-In causing the problem. This involves switching them all off and then turning them back on, one at a time, until the problem recurs. Then you write the author of the Add-In and ask that author to fix it.

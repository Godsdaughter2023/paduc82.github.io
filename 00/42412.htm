The point that so many users of Windows Live Mail fail to appreciate is that the sign-in is not a security feature - you don't have to sign in to be able to use the program. Another thing to realize is that an Outlook (.com) account is not necessarily an @outlook.com account. The email address involved could be @msn, @hotmail, @live or @outlook, to name a few free Microsoft email account providers. It could also be @gmail, @yahoo, @comcast or @myownpersonalcustomdomain.co.au, just so long as it's registered as a Microsoft Account at signup.live.com.

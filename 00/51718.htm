a. user say:

Each time Windows Update installs a major upgrade (like the recent Creators Update) a windows.old folder is created. This gives you an opportunity to roll back in case the upgrade is not working properly on your computer. If you leave it alone, the windows.old folder will be automatically erased after 10 days have passed from the day your computer was upgraded.

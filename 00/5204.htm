FWIW, this works (here, PPT 2010, Win7-64, Saturday, 5:10pm, while holding tongue slightly in cheek and holding breath ... YMMV of course):

Sub DueTheGroupItem()

Dim oGroup As Shape
Dim oSh As Shape

Set oGroup = ActiveWindow.Selection.ShapeRange(1)

Set oSh = oGroup.GroupItems(1)

oSh.ZOrder msoBringForward ' bringtofront works too


End Sub

With a proper file browser you can navigate to C:\data\communities-log\ on your device. There is a urpTrace.txt. Here you should see that the "Social" app tries to contact to:

 

https://sm.ovi.messaging.nokia.com/cds

 

But there is no answer. Even if you put this URL in your Browser there is no answer. As this request is made by "curl" on the N8-00 you can also try the same on a Unix machine:

 

freebsdsrv01# curl https://sm.ovi.messaging.nokia.com/cds
curl: (52) SSL read: error:00000000:lib(0):func(0):reason(0), errno 54

 

So, this URL does not work. But this one is available:

 

https://sm.premium.ovi.messaging.nokia.com/sn/facebook

 

For me it looks like they made a huge failure in the distribution of this device. Looks like on the original delivered devices is the correct "Social" app which connects to 

 

https://sm.premium.ovi.messaging.nokia.com/sn/facebook

 

But on the factory reseted devices the old "Social" client from the discontinued Beta will be loaded. Or something similar is happening.

 

At this time the only thing we can do is to hammer on Nokias doors and hope that they deliver the correct client ...

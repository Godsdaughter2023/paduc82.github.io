=============

Symptom:

Office 365 Planner does not appear in App Launcher nor View My apps for any users.

Root cause:

The Planner licenses are not assigned to users correctly.

Solution:

Follow the steps below to re-assign the Office 365 Planner license to the affected users.

1. Sign into the Office portal with the global admin account, click Users > Active users.

2. Double click the affected user > Edit next to the Product licenses.

3.  Tick off the Office Online and Microsoft Planner license as below > click Save. Please make sure any other license status is not edited.

4.  Tick on the Office Online and Microsoft Planner license > click Save.

5.  Log in the user’s account again and check the status of App Launcher.

karl donaubauer say:

Hi,

add a new text box "txtSumTimeUsed" to your subform with this control source:

=Sum([TimeUsed])

where "TimeUsed" would be the field name (= control source) of your "Time Used" text box. If the new text box works, i.e. shows the expected sum, you can set its Visible property to No.

The Expression for you calculated control "Hours Remaining" would be:

=[HoursAllocated] - [SubFormName].Form![txtSumTimeUsed]

where "HoursAllocated" - well you get it... and "SubFormName" has to be replaced by the Name of your subform i.e. the name you see in the Name property of the subform control in the parent form.

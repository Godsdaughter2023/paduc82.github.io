why do back side pages in booklet print upside down? - Microsoft Community

arrowood say:

why do back side pages in booklet print upside down?

I use Office Publisher 2007 on a Windows 7 system with a Lexmark 543dn printer with duplex capability.  I have created an 8-page 1/2 letter-size booklet, which I previously printed out with no problems.  Now for some reason the "back side" pages are printing upside down.  I ran the Two-sided Printing Wizard successfully, but the problem persists.  Is there something else I need to do to correct this?
arrowood say: I'm replying to my own question here, because I solved my problem.  Here's what I found:
  • When printing two-sided full-page documents, "page" and "sheet" both mean the same thing.  On my Lexmark C543dn printer, this calls for "Flip on the Long Side".
  • On a half-page 5.5" x 8.5" booklet that is not the case --- flipping the page on the short side flips the sheet on the long side.
  • It was necessary to change the "Two-sided Printing Options" to "Two-sided, flip short side".  My booklet then printed just fine.

chris.brandsma say: OK, here is what I had to do, after struggling with this all day.

Open Device Center, Go to Mobile Device Settings,
Change all the values.
Click OK.
Check them again.
Click OK.
Chang them back to what they were supposed to be (DMA).

Now it works again.

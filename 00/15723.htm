Transfer an Installation, Phone Activate

What version of Office did you buy: Home and Student, Home and Business, Office Pro?

How did you buy it: factory installed, online download, retail boxed CD, Product Key Card?

If you bought a “retail” (“FPP”) license then you have no problem. Retail licenses allow you to activate on 2 (3 for Home and Student only) of your computers at the same time AND to transfer those installations to new computers in the future. With a retail license, you don’t “use up” installations.  All you have to do is run the installation file on another computer and use the same Product Key

If you bought an “OEM” or “PKC” or 2007 “MLK” license you are out of luck. Those licenses cost less initially because of this specific license limitation. The new computer is replaced under warranty, but you have to pay for a whole new license. M$ thanks you.

Uninstall it from the “old” computer (a dead computer qualifies as “uninstalled”) then simply install and activate on the new computer.

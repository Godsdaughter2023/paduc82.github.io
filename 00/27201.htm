adrianhughesnsd say: no worries mate :). well here's how it goes: If you have an unbranded phone, then it is not tied to a network carrier. there are two types of unbranded phone, either a generic regional one, or a Country Variant one. in the case of a generic regional one, e,g. Australia, Nokia will provide the update to those devices. if the device is a Country Variant, then Nokia will provide the update to the local branch of the Nokia, and it is up to say Nokia Australia to APPROVE and release the update with their own customisations (certain apps, country regulations and law settings for things such as camera shutter sounds etc).

if your phone is a branded variant, then Nokia provides the update to the network carrier, and it is up to the network carrier to APPROVE the update or not, with their own custom wallpapers and random shishkebab that they choose to add.

davem121 say:

Hi ReidThePeep,

This macro should work for you, it will save the current document to your Desktop with the current documents name as a PDF

Sub Save_To_PDF()
Dim strPath As String
Dim strName As String
strPath = Environ("USERPROFILE") & "\Desktop\"
strName = Replace(ActiveDocument.Name, ".docx", ".pdf")
ActiveDocument.ExportAsFixedFormat _

Range:=wdExportAllDocument, _ 
Item:=wdExportDocumentContent, _ 
IncludeDocProps:=False, _ 
KeepIRM:=True, _ 
CreateBookmarks:=wdExportCreateNoBookmarks, _ 
DocStructureTags:=True, _ 
BitmapMissingFonts:=True, _  OutputFileName:=strPath & strName, _
ExportFormat:=wdExportFormatPDF, _
OpenAfterExport:=False, _
OptimizeFor:=wdExportOptimizeForPrint, _
UseISO19005_1:=False
End Sub

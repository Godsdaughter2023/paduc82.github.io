john - project say:

djjjblawson,

Yeah I admit I saw you post when it first appeared a couple weeks ago but "things" happened and I couldn't respond. I hoped someone else would step in but they didn't, so here we are now.

The bad news is sorry, but Project doesn't have the "intelligence" to make the kind of resource assignments you want. You can certainly achieve the first part by assigning resource 1 at 75% to  task 1. That will spread resource 1's assignment level at 6 hrs on day 1 and 4 hrs on day 2. However if you then assign resource 1 to Task 2, Project will simply repeat that asignment with a resulting overallocation. However, what you can do at that point, assuming Task 1 has a higher priority than Task 2, is to level the resource. Project will then move resource 1's assignment to give 2 hrs on day 2 and 6 hrs on day 3.

Resource 2's assignment on task 3 will spread as desired simply by setting the assignment level to 75%.

On the second part of your "need", what exactly do you mean by automatically adjusting the dates? When you assign multiple resources to a task Project automatically adjusts the duration, and subsequently the finish date, assuming the task is not of fixed duration type. Are you looking for /expecting something else?

Hope this helps.

John

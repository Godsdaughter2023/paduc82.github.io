scjr say:

The sequence you probably want is as follows:

1. Create your master merge file (before you have actually merged anything)

2. Merge your master merge file with the Excel file to produce your letters (this will produce one big file)

3. Run the Document Splitter from the Add-in tab in Word

Assuming that you have separated each letter with a section break, running the Document Splitter should create a copy of each separate letter for each contact in your list.  So, if you have 3 letters in your master merge file and 10 contacts in your contact list, you will end up with 30 separate letters, which is what it sounds like you are looking for.

 

To get the add-in set up properly, I would assume that you have:

1. Unzipped the Document_Splitter.zip file in your template folder

2. Added the file as a Word Add-in (Office button > Options > Add-ins, under "Manage" have selected "Word add-ins" and clicked Go, in the Templates and Add-ins dialog box click on Add and selected Document Splitter.dot to add).

a. user say:

Hello AntzieAnnie:

Thank you for posting to the forum regarding the issue you are having with notifications on your lock screen.  The notifications should certainly go away when you check the messages you are being notified of.  We understand how this could get confusing and hope some of the suggestions below help you get rid of these notifications.

The first thing I would suggest you try is removing the Facebook account from your phone.  You should be able to do this by going to settings > emails + accounts > tap and hold the Facebook account > select delete.  Once you have done this, go to settings > applications > Internet Explorer > delete history.  Upon completion of this step remove the battery from your phone, replace and reboot.  If the phone does not have a removable battery, simply reboot your device.

If the issue continues, try disassociating your Facebook account from your Microsoft account.  You can do that by signing into your Microsoft account at http://accounts.live.com.  Once signed in go to permissions > manage other accounts > edit > remove this connection completely.  Upon doing this, go back to the phone and remove the Facebook account if listed here.  If it is not listed on the phone, go to the phone and tap settings > emails + accounts > tap and hold the Microsoft account > select sync.  Upon the completion of your sync, reboot the phone and check your lock screen notifications one more time.

The final thing to consider is a factory reset of the phone.  Keep in mind that this will set the phone back to it's factory settings.  Media can be backed up to your PC, http://support.xbox.com/en-US/zune/software/syncing-media.  Contacts and calendar events should be backed up to the cloud.  You can verify this by going to http://contacts.live.com and http://calendars.live.com.  The information on your phone should be listed here.  Text message history and game progress will be reset.  To reset the phone, tap settings > about > reset your phone.

Please let us know if you have any other questions or concerns we can assist you with.

mike middleton say:
If I have data with average and standard deviation, how do I calculate tolerance limits of 99%?

If you want to assume the normal distribution is appropriate, maybe you could use the        =NORMINV(probability,mean,standard_dev)
worksheet function with probability values of 0.005 and 0.995.

-  Mike Middleton, www.MikeMiddleton.com

mike middleton say:
Thank you Mike for replying back to my question. I have the following question connected to the same question. What is the Probability? How do you calculate if you have mean and STDEV values?
The Probability argument of NORMINV is a cumulative probability for the normal distribution. For more information, choose Help, type norminv into the Search box, select NORMINV function from the list (not NORMSINV function), and refer to the example.

Or, if the mean is 50 and the standard deviation is 10, and you want a symmetric interval, enter into cells
=NORMINV(0.005,50,10)
=NORMINV(0.995,50,10)

The only thing this indicates is that the problem is not the battery. Please take the time and have a look at the following link for a graphic of a typical laptop charging diagram.

 

Laptop Battery Charging Diagram:

You will see that the power cord, adapter, and plugs are simply the visible components. There are around 6 different computer chips, on a circuit board, inside the laptop case that are involved in the charging circuit. In that diagram, note that the only thing Windows does is to provide the information that the charging circuit is reporting, to the user. In that diagram, you can click any of the chips to get a description of what it does.

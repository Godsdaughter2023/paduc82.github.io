Not just one Serialnumber...but the most recent transaction for each unique serial number which could be 2000 separate items.

Probably the most efficient method is a JOIN:

SELECT SH1.*
FROM StockHistory as SH1 INNER JOIN
    (SELECT SerialNumber,MAX(Dated) AS LatestDated
     FROM StockHistory
     GROUP BY SerialNumber) AS SH2
ON SH2.SerialNumber = SH1.SerialNumber
AND SH2.LatestDated = SH1.Dated;

ken sheridan say:
After the update to three tables, sbfrmPropertyManagement works on its own, but still adds new record in tableContacts.

That is inherent in the model.  When you have a many-to-many relationship type modelled by three tables like this:

Properties----<PropertyContacts>----Contacts

the PropertyContacts table is modelling the relationship type between properties and contacts.  It does this by resolving the many-to-many relationship type in to two one-to-many relationship types, by having two foreign key columns, e.g. PropertyID and ContactID, which reference the primary keys of the two referenced tables.  The contact data must be stored somewhere and the only place for this is in a row in the contacts table.

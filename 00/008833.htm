Private Sub Worksheet_Change(ByVal Target As Range)

Dim Cell As Range

With Cell

diane poremsky mvp (slipstick.com) say:

While you can hide the folders from your sight, Outlook can still 'see' them and will use them. I don't think it will be a problem with these accounts, because Outlook will default to the default account folders for appointments and contacts, but you do run a risk of having items added to the hidden folders.  Hiding is better than deleting as Outlook will eventually recreate deleted folders and is less likely to unhide folders.

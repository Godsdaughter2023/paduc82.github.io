cmdrkeene say:

Any stylus won't work. 

Some styluses just mimic your finger and have no real pressure-sensitivity or fine point control.  That is, you can't draw small lines and shapes easily.  These kinds of stylus will work on any touchscreen phone or PC.

Other styluses are "active" and communicate with your touch-screen in a finer detailed way, allowing you to use pressure sensitivity and draw fine details.  These kinds of pens only work on screens designed for them, like the Surface Pro 4.

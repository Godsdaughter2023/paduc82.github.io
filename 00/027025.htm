I have no trouble turning one of these paragraphs from a heading level to normal. It then does not show up in a revised Table of Contents.

Method 1: Select entire paragraph including the paragraph mark at the end of the paragraph. In the styles pane select "Clear all." Paragraph reverts to normal style.

Method 2: Place cursor in paragraph, anywhere in paragraph, and select normal style from Quickstyles or from the Styles pane.

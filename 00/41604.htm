Hi Cheryl,

A guest account is an account for users who don't have a permanent account on your PC or domain. It allows people (guest) to use your PC without having access to your personal files. Users signed in to the guest account can't install apps or hardware, change PC settings, or create a password.

Because the guest account does allow a user to log on to a network, browse the Internet, and shut down the PC, you should turn off the guest account when it isn't being used.

Since the Guest account doesn't (can't) have a password, it will be automatically signed in to it when you click on it's user icon on the sign in screen, and if the Guest account was the last one that was signed in when you restart or start up the PC next.

To avoid this, you could do any option below:

• Make sure the administrator account was the last one that was signed in.

• (recommended) Turn off the guest account, and use a password protected standard user account instead.

Cut will remove the selected clip, title, song, photo, etc. and put it in your clipboard.  It's similar to selecting an item in a Word document and choosing 'Cut'.

However, I have a feeling you are looking for a way to split your clip into a couple parts.  This is done by going to the Video Tools | Edit tab.  Set your playback marker somewhere in the middle of a clip (video) and then click on the Split icon.  The keyboard shortcut is M.  That will split the clip into two parts.  You can continue making splits as needed.  Then if you want to remove a section, just right-click on it and choose Remove.

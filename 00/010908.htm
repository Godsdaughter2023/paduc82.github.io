May I know whether you are referring to the Office 365 Distribution Group (DG) or Office 365 Unified Group (OWA group)?

For the Office 365 DG, all the members in your organization can join it by default. If you have set it to be “approval for joining”, you can change the original setting back via editing the DG in Exchange Admin Center (EAC)> Recipients> Groups> choose the DG> membership approval.

a. user say:

Hi Ahmed,

 

I would like to inform you that Brian Tillman [MVP-Outlook]'s post is correct. In addition to that, make sure that the setting is properly configured in Outlook.com. Follow these steps to do so:

  1. Login to your Outlook.com account.
  2. Click the cog wheel icon next to your name and select More mail settings.
  3. From the Options page, click Connect devices and apps with POP.
  4. Once on the Connect devices and apps with POP page, tick the radio button next to Enable.
  5. Another set of options will appear below which is Deleting downloaded messages.
  6. Tick the radio button next to Do what the device or app says—if it's set to delete messages, delete them.
  7. Click Save.

 

This setting will remove the emails on your device once you delete it from your online account, through a mail client, or vice versa.

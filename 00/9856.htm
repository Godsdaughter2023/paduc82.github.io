bob jones aka: cybertaz say:

Ensure? The only honest answer is no.

There is no guarantee that Word documents will display identically even if opened on 2 seemingly identical computers running the same OS & same version of the software. There are precautions that can be taken to minimize the disparity but there is no surefire method, especially when graphic objects are involved. Fonts, printer metrics & other system/software settings determine how text flows & influence how a Word document displays from one device to another.

The key question is whether the documents you're producing are in final form or are intended to be edited further by the client. If the former, I'd suggest generating PDFs from the Word documents & providing the client with them instead or or at least in addition to the actual Word document files.

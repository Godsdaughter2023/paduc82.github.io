The "issue" you are referring to has nothing to do with Outlook or Microsoft and everything to do with how email protocols (in this case suspect you're using POP3 from your description) work regardless of the <Email Client> in use (in this case Microsoft Outlook). 

Using another server (ergo - another email address) using the same email protocol isn't going to make the slightest bit of difference.

<Email Clients> connect/sync with <email accounts> - they do NOT connect/sync with other <email clients> so since you don't want to buy a 3rd party product to sync horizontally between devices, you're going to have to understand the world of email protocols (how ANY <email client> connects with an <email account>)

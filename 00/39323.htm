Is there an official Microsoft password manager that allows me to - Microsoft Community

tuespetre say:

Is there an official Microsoft password manager that allows me to retrieve passwords?

I am tired of using different third party things like Lastpass and passpack. I would like something that integrates with my Microsoft account and allows me to save and view account login information along with any arbitrary extra information. Is there something like this out there? Or should I just keep a locked-down document in SkyDr... I mean, OneDrive?
cmdrkeene say:

Absolutely. It's actually existed since Windows 2000, but no one knows about it.  It got a facelift in Windows Vista and 7, and then in Windows 8 it gets (finally!) enhanced to work with your Microsoft Account.

When you use Internet Explorer and choose to let it remember a password, they are encrypted and stored on your computer.  You can view these in the Control Panel, but a quick way to get to them is search from your Start menu (or in Windows 8 press +W to do a search specifically for settings.)  Type "vault" into the search box and then choose "Manage web credentials" in the search results list.

In the Vault, you can see all your saved passwords (if you're an administrator, you can even view the stored password by unencrypting it).

And as I said:  Windows 8 syncs these over your Microsoft Account, so your saved passwords are remembered and used on every Windows 8 PC, tablet, phone, and device you use.  It's awesome.

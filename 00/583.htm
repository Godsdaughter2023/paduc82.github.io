alzaeem80 say:

i just want to know what does M and K stands for?? in TFT screen colors

psychomania---01 say:

Google could have quickly answered that question for you. It's simple a way of shortening numbers.

16M = 16,000,000 colours so M is a way of shortening the word million.
65K = 65,000 colours, K is a way of shortening the word thousand that been used for 100's of years, it's unclear why a K is used but it's a well known term.

Many people cannot tell the difference between the two colour depths. The difference is most visible in areas where colours are blended together. You can find more info about colour depths and screen technologies on google or wikipedia.

tanz1987 say: M comes from 'mega' meaning million, and K (originally lowercase) comes from 'kilo' meaning thousand. These are Greek terms used throughout science and technology.

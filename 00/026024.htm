This is difficult to picture without seeing the process flow first hand, but could you have a cell (hypothetically Z1 for instance) that summed a column of numbers within the import? If so, then a 'trigger' could be built based on the Worksheet_Change function. Something like:

Private Sub Worksheet_Change(ByVal Target As Range)

    End If
End Sub

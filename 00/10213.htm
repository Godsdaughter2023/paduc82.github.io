Depending on the type of Office (subscription or retail), you might find that file in one of these locations:

C:\Program Files\Microsoft Office 15\root\vfs\ProgramFilesCommonX86\Microsoft Shared\VBA\VBA7.1\1033\VBE7INTL.DLL

C:\Program Files (x86)\Common Files\microsoft shared\VBA\VBA7.1\1033\VBE7INTL.DLL

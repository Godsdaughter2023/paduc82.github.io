How do I delete the Database for Outlook v15 - Microsoft Community

malafaci say:

How do I delete the Database for Outlook v15

I tried running V15 of Outlook and the Database has become corrupt with hundreds of duplicated records.

I have deleted the duplicated records in the cloud/office365 database (sent there by Outlook V15 on my mac.

How do I delete the database on my mac and refresh it with the content from the cloud?

I hope that makes sense. 

talking moose say:

This is not officially supported but should work to rebuild your database.

  1. Quit Outlook.
  2. In the Finder, hold the Option key as you choose Go menu > Library.
  3. From Library go to Group Containers > UBF8T346G9.Office > Outlook > Outlook 15 Profiles > Main Profile (or the folder with the most recent date) Data.
  4. Move all the sqlite files to the trash (you should see about nine of them).
  5. Launch Outlook and it should begin rebuilding your database.

Allow time for Outlook to complete the rebuild process. Time depends on how much data you have stored locally. I’ve estimated about 10 minutes per 1 GB of mail data but that’s very rough.

Hope this helps!

***Post moved by moderator to appropriate forum category***

bernie deitrick say:

Array-enter (enter using Ctrl-Shift-Enter) a formula like

=IFERROR(INDEX(B:B,SMALL(IF($A$1:$A$100=$E$2,ROW($A$1:$A$100)),ROW(A1))),"")

where your VLOOKUP was

=VLOOKUP(E2,A:B,2,False)

I.E., B has your values of interest, A holds the values you want to match against, and E2 has the key value (Pancakes).

Copy that formula down until it returns blanks.

¡firedog say:

It looks like you've done all the right things except, perhaps, disabling McAfee's anti-virus mail scan. However, the error message indicates both that you're not using authenticated SMTP and that you ought to be ("Authentication is required before sending"). The standard port for authenticated SMTP is 587, and that's the one RR uses. 

Select My server requires authentication on the Servers tab and, for good measure, click the Settings button and enter your full RR email address and password. On the Advanced tab, change the port to 587. Close down WLMail and wait a few minutes before re-launching.

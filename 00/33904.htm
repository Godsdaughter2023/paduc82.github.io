3. Unplug any USB devices plugged into USB port on PSU.

4. Reseat cord from wall to PSU.

5. Plug PSU back into wall outlet. If possible, try different wall outlet.

6. Plug power connector back into side of device.

Hi,

Error 777 – This means the connection attempt failed because the modem (or other connecting device) on the remote computer is out of order.

 

This error can occur if you have installed your modem with an incorrect driver.

Try to update the hardware drivers.
For more information refer to the link below:

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows7/Update-a-driver-for-hardware-that-isnt-working-properly

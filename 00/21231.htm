blu3b3ard say:

I found this on this page: Upload Failed: We're sorry, someone updated the server copy and it's not possible to upload your changes now. How do I retrieve changes

By AlexanderJosephson:

"Hi Everyone!

I also received this error: "Upload failed: we're sorry someone updated the server copy " with those two seemingly innocuous messages "keep local copy" or "keep server copy."

I picked "keep local copy"...and lost 9 pages (aka 6 hours) worth of work. And of course, my homework is due tomorrow morning, 8 am. 

AHHHHH!!!!!

After much screaming, crying, and frantic googling, I managed to find the back up of the server version. Since I couldn't find any guidance on how to do this and only managed to stumble on it by accident and (of course) through my mad computer skills, I thought I might give a how-to-guide for everyone else who has this problem.

HOW TO RECOVER YOUR LOST SERVER FILE AFTER THE "UPLOAD FAILED" MESSAGE:

 1) Log into your "online" version of your sky drive: https://onedrive.live.com

 2) Find the appropriate document that didn't save correctly, and open it in the browser.

 3) Click "File" then "Info" then "Previous Versions" 

 4) For me at least, I was given the current version, and 6 older versions...one of which was the actual document I needed. 

 5) Click "Restore" (which I didn't dare do) or "Download" and then open and save your file.

Note: my Microsoft word is set to back up automatically every 10 minutes. I've never seen it do this. However, looking at the "older versions" on my web browser, it looks like it automatically backs up my word document every time I save. So hopefully, even if you don't have the autosave selected, your file will still be there. 

Thank goodness. Let me know if this helps everyone!"

So if you tried to find recent copies of the document in your files, look again on Onedrive.com.

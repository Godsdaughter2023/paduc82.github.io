The IT Techs in our office have discovered how to fix this flaw in Excel 2013. (They discovered it accidentally but it still works!) First, you need to close all of your Microsoft applications (Word, Outlook, etc.). Then:

(1) Click on Start\Control Panel\All Control Panel Items\Programs and Features.
(2) Click once on Microsoft Office 365 ProPlus - en-us.
(3) Click on Change.
(4) Click on Online Repair, then click Repair.
(5) Click Repair again. Repair runs, then notifies you that it's finished.
(6) Close the Office window, close the Programs and Features window.

Now, go find the Excel spreadsheet you were trying to open before and double-click on it. It will open.

Depending on the type of email account, your Default message store may not be called Personal Folders - it might say Mailbox - Your_Name or your email address. When you click on it, Outlook Today comes up on the right.

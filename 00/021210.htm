rohn007 say:
Immediately after you paste the url, enter a space char. Autoformat needs the space to recognize the end of the "word" before it can apply autoformatting.

suzanne s. barnhill say:
Make sure you've enabled "Internet and network paths with hyperlinks" on the
correct tab(s) of AutoCorrect Options. To be effective when you type URLs,
it must be enabled on the AutoFormat As You Type tab. To be effective for
pasted URLs, it must be enabled on the AutoFormat tab AND you must manually
run AutoFormat after pasting since there is nothing that causes this
AutoFormat to fire when you paste as it does when you type.

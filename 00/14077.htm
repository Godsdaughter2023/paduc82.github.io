At this time there does not appear to be a built-in setting to disable spell checking in Skype 8.x. In regards to a workaround you can perform the following to permanently break spell check:

Browse to %appdata%\Microsoft\Skype for Desktop\dictionaries. Delete the dictionary file located there (en-US.bdic).  Set the permissions of the dictionaries folder (in its properties and your account permissions) to deny writing. The only way to disable wikimarkup, that I am aware of, is per message. You can append @@ or !! at the start of your message.  This will disable formatting for that message.

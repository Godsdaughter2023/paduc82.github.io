  4. Right click on the Graphic adapter and select Uninstall
  5. Restart the computer

After restarting the computer run the Windows Update and the latest update will be installed.

hansv mvp say:

If you select an entire paragraph, click the format painter, then apply it to an entire paragraph or paragraphs, you'll apply the paragraph style. However, if you apply it to part of a paragraph, the copied style will be applied as character formatting.

You can use the keyboard to copy and paste paragraph style:

  • Click anywhere in the "source" paragraph.
  • Press Ctrl+Shift+C to copy the paragraph style to the clipboard.
  • Click anywhere in the "target" paragraph.
  • Press Ctrl+Shift+V to paste the paragraph style.

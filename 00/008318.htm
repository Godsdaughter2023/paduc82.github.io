bob jones aka: cybertaz say:
What Rich suggests is spot on for changing the Width [i.e., Thickness or Weight] of the Border, but just in case you're actually referring to the Length... There is no setting for that in the Borders & Shading options. The Borders run from Left to Right Margin. If you want them to be shorter go to Left & Right Indentation to the bordered paragraph.

Bob J.

rich michaels - mvp (greatcirclelearning.com) say:
From your Menu Bar select Format > Borders and Shading.  There you will see the Width control.  

The Borders pull-down button menu from the Home tab in the Paragraph group doesn't provide that option and you have to use the Format > Boarder and Shading command.

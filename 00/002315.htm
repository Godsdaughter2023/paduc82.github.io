Hi Black books,

 

There are no official articles about it. If you want to move one Exchange Online reseller tenant to another tenant from Microsoft, you need to purchase a new tenant and move data manually. After that, you can cancel the old subscription. For my understanding, generally, if the users purchase Office 365 from reseller, they should get the product keys and manage the tenants by yourself. Of course, you can also let the partner help you manage tenants. It is depending on your environment.

 

When you mentioned that they just moved to Exchange Online, may I know whether their accounts have no any data? If so, you just need to purchase a new tenant with related licenses. If anything is unclear, please feel free to let me know.

 

Best Regards,

Erick

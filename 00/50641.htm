a. user say:

If that's the only thing that takes long to download it's not your fault and it's not Windows' fault. Blame it on the internet. The installer for your game could easily visit a dozen or more computers on its way from the download server to your computer. If any one of those intermediate computers experiences a problem, you will too.

Then again, the download server may have a problem or maybe it's just undergoing routine maintenance.

Data travels through he internet in pieces called packets. It may also be that most of the packets needed to install your game have arrived, but the few remaining packets got lost or corrupted en route.

In each case the solution is the same: Cancel the present download and download again later. Or maybe tomorrow.

reveriex say:

Hi Glen! ^_^

So you are in trouble coz you are not able to choose secondary USB camera to function as primary one... Don't worry , I got you covered :)

Just follow given steps! :D

Method 1: If the Webcam is listed under Devices and printers, please follow the steps.

 

a.       Press Windows key + X.

b.      Select Control Panel.

c.       Click on Devices and Printers.

d.      Check if the Logitech webcam is listed.

e.       Right click on (Brand Name) webcam.

f.        Click on Set this device as default.

Follow method 2 if webcam is not listed.

 

 

Method 2: What best we can do is we can disable the Internal webcam. You will be able to enable the webcam anytime you want.

 

Please follow the steps to disable HP Envy Webcam.

a.       Press Windows + X, click Device Manager.

b.      Click Imaging devices.

c.       Right click on  webcam.

d.      Click Disable

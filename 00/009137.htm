Select the cells you want to format.

Press Ctrl+1.

Select Custom in the Category list.

Is there a limit to the number of Quick Parts you can enter in Word - Microsoft Community

tinalandry say:

Is there a limit to the number of Quick Parts you can enter in Word 2007?

Can Quick Parts handle over 100 entries?
jay freedman say:
Yes, QuickParts can handle a very large number (much larger than 100) of entries. The entries themselves are stored in template files, including Building Blocks.dotx and Normal.dotm and others, which are individually limited to 32 megabytes of text plus many megabytes of graphics per file.

The downside is that very large numbers of entries and/or overloaded template files will make Word take a long time to load the QuickParts. To make startup of Word faster, the QuickParts templates (other than Normal.dotm) aren't loaded until you click the QuickParts button or one of the other buttons that show galleries of building blocks (Header, Footer, Page Numbers, etc.).

If you're planning a project that uses a lot of QuickParts, pay attention to the Gallery and Category boxes in the Alt+F3 dialog box. Using these classifications properly will make it much easier to find the specific entry you're looking for.

Speaker Wont Connect to Device, Here is a possible reason - Microsoft Community

kdeborah say:

Speaker Wont Connect to Device, Here is a possible reason

I have had this ongoing problem with my various Bluetooth speakers and various devices.  When I was recently told what the problem was I had to bang my head on the wall.  Of course.  Maybe this will help solve your problem too. 

First, think about the device as a "parent" and the speaker as a "child".  The "child" cannot pair with another "parent" until the first "parent" it is paired with unpairs it.  In other words "sets the child free".  Do not just close Bluetooth but go into the device that is the "parent" to the speaker "child" and unpair it.  Then the new device will find it and you can pair it up.

I hope this helps someone else struggling with the concept of paring speakers to devices.

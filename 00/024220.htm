Reset Protection is a hardware feature, built into the phone during manufacture. It cannot be added by an update to the phone operating system or firmware later, if the phone does not have it built in.

If the option is not seen on the 'find my phone' screen, then the phone is either built without this feature - or has not yet received the OS version to enable it (8.10.15127.138 and later) - or the service is not (yet) available in the country or area where the particular phone variant is sold.

The Xbox One has a LARGE number of TCP and UDP ports which need to be forwarded to make sure all forms of communication are not being blocked or throttled. And, all of the ports have to be forwarded, because different games may use different ports. The ports you will need to forward for the Xbox One are:

Port 88 (UDP)

Port 3074 (UDP and TCP)

Port 53 (UDP and TCP)

Port 80 (TCP)

Port 500 (UDP)

UDP Port 3544 (UDP)

UDP Port 4500 (UDP)

Also, if you play Ghosts, you will likely also need to forward ports 3075 and 3076, as many gamers have issues if those ports are still closed. The details on Xbox One network ports can be found here:

support.xbox.com/.../network-ports-used-xbox-live

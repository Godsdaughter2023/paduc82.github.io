Hi LachlanSimpson,

 

Thank you for providing the requested information.

 

Please try to add/connect your Twitter account again to your Microsoft account and then remove it afterwards. After doing so, kindly verify if your Twitter contacts are still in your People page. To do this, please follow these steps:

 

To add:

  1. Sign in to your account here. It will direct you to your profile.
  2. Click Connect under your profile picture.
  3. Click on Share on Twitter.
  4. Click Connect
  5. Follow the on screen instructions. When it's done, contacts and updates from Facebook will now synchronize on your Microsoft account.

To disconnect/remove:

  1. Sign in to your account here. It will direct you to your profile.
  2. Under you profile picture, you will see the services that you are connected with.
  3. Click Manage.
  4. You will now see the Twitter logo. Click on the Edit link.
  5. Scroll down the page and you will be able to view the Remove this connection completely link.
  6. Click the link and a pop up box will appear. Choose Remove on the confirmation message and Facebook will now be disconnected/unlinked on your Microsoft account.

aileen alf say:

Hi,

To remove Twitter contacts, we suggest that you follow these steps:

  1. Open the app containing the accounts you’d like to delete, either Mail or People.
  2. Click on the Windows icon and select Settings.
  3. Click on Accounts. When the Accounts pane appears, click the name of the account you want to delete. The Accounts pane changes to show the settings of your chosen account. (You can’t delete your Windows account, but others are fair game.)
  4. When the pane displays the account’s settings, click the Remove Account button at the pane’s bottom edge.


For some accounts, Step 4 takes you to another settings area. When removing Facebook from the People app, for example, Step 4 takes you to a site online where you can choose exactly what types of Facebook information you want to share with the People app.

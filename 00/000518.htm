bernie deitrick, excel mvp 2000-2010 say:

If there is a specific column that you want to match, you are better off using that column than finding the last cell of the sheet, since that can result in too many cells being filled. For example, to match the formulas in column  N to column M:

Range("N2:N" & Range("M" & Rows.Count).End(xlUp).Row).Formula = "=3*M2"

where the "=3*M2" is the formula as entered into cell N2 - Excel will update the formula the same as if you used Ctrl-Enter after selecting the range.

BErnie

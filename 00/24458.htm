Just about the only usable material about Word Bibliography was produced by Yves Dhondt, who posted a number of utilities at http://bibword.codeplex.com/ including a facility for creating a new type. AFAICR it's all quite techie stuff, but you may find what you need there. He explains that there are in any case constraints on the "types".

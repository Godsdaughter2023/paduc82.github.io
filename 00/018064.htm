Hi Lewis,

Sorry for my late reply since I was out of office. In fact, Outlook contacts are independent with Skype for Business contacts. Because the contacts you added may not have Office 365 account while they have email accounts hosted by other email providers (Gmail, Yahoo), and they couldn't use Skype for Business. Or some contacts you added in Skype for Business are Skype users, who don't have Office 365 account. Considering of this, it's not convenience to create the contact in one side and automatically display in the other side. So Office 365 for Business doesn't sync the Outlook contacts directly with Skype for Business contacts.

In Office 365 for Business subscription, there's an address book in your organization called Global Address List (GAL). All contacts in GAL will appear in everyone's address book, and only admins have the permission to create or remove contacts in Admin Center. If you have contacts in GAL, then you can just select the contact and click OK to add them in Outlook. Moreover, you can add the Skype for Business address in IM address field because IM (instant message) is the feature of Skype for Business. 

In the meantime, if your business contacts use Office 365 business accounts, what you need to do is just adding them as contacts in both applications. Because their email addresses are Skype for Business addresses. For those who don't have Office 365 business accounts, they can't use Skype for Business. Also, for the people who has multiple addresses, they can only login Skype for Business with Office 365 business account.

john korchok say:

Try this:

  1. Draw a line, then click on the Drawing Tools>Format tab.
  2. Click on Shape Outline>Weight>More Lines... The Format Shape pane opens at the side of your screen.
  3. In the Width field, set the width you would like to use. You can set any number here. Leave the pane open.
  4. To keep that wight for future lines, right-click on the line segment and choose Set as Default Line. Now you can draw several line segments at the same weight without having to set the width for every line.
  5. When you're done, set the default line weight back to a useful value for normal line drawing.

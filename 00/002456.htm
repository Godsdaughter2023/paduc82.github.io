stefan blom say:

"Style" has a specific meaning in Word; it refers to a named collection of formatting instructions. Resetting formatting using Ctrl+SpaceBar (for font formatting) and/or Ctrl+Q (for paragraph formatting) is only efficient after you have applied styles systematically to text. If you haven't made "active" use of styles, you are in fact using the built-in Normal style in most cases (because it is applied to the blank paragraph in Normal.dotm, the blank document template).

You will have to use different paragraph styles for different text paragraphs, and you will have to modify those styles to suit the needs of your document.

For more on styles in general, take a look at http://shaunakelly.com/topic/word/styles.

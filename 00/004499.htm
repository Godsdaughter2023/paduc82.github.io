I am suspecting that you are confusing the Find function with the general Find functionality.

The Find function is for finding a matching character (or string of characters) within a specific string. Normally used to test if a specific character or group of characters exist in a specific cell.

The Find functionality is for finding something by searching the entire worksheet. For the find functionality, select the Home ribbon and then the Find & Select (far right of the Home ribbon) and then select Find and enter the character or characters to be found in the Find what field. You can restrict the Find functionality to search only a specific range of the worksheet by selecting the range to be searched before selecting the Find & Select.

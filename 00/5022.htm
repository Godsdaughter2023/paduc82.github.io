hansv mvp say:

While scrolling with the scrollbar, Excel keeps the top of the work area aligned with the top edge of a row. There is no way to avoid that.

If you have a mouse with a mouse wheel/middle button, you can click and hold down the mouse wheel/middle button anywhere in the worksheet, then move the mouse slightly up or down. As long as you hold down the mouse wheel/middle button, the window will scroll continuously. The moment you release the mouse button, Excel will snap the top of a row to the top of the work area again.

If you boot from the ISO to install Windows, it will replace the current OS and offer to keep the existing files under Windows.old.

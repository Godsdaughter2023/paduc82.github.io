How do I convert a cell formatted in [hh:mm] to a decimal format [hh.h] ?

How do I convert a cell formatted in [hh:mm] to a decimal format [hh.h] ?  i.e., (3:15) three hours and 15 minutes = 3.25 hrs

tasos_k say:

Hi,

in cell A1 is 3:15

in cell A2 write

=HOUR(A1)+MINUTE(A1)/60

number format (2 decimal places)

example

if A1 is 3:10, in A2 is 3.17

if A1 is 3:30, in A2 is 3.50

if A1 is 3:50, in A2 is 3.83

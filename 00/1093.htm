glenprouty say:

Hi OwlCroft,

Controlled Folder Access has been very user unfriendly. Determining the complete

path for an App that has been blocked, is somewhat of a pain.

But there is good news! In W10 v1809 this procedure has been improved tremendously.

When you are on v1809, and get a blocked App, you can now allow it to pass, easily.

Click on the Notification, and you will be taken directly to the "Add an App routine".

Click on the + sign, and you are presented with the option to see a "List of all recently

blocked Apps". Select the option, and then review the list. A single click on any entry,

allows that App to pass. Just be careful not to select any that you don't trust.

Of course, any that you allow to pass, are now on your PCs WhiteList.

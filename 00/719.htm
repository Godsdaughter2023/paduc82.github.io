julie fka profjulie say:

If your phone hard reset itself so that all your user data was removed and you had to go out and set everything up again, then all you need to do iscreate a My Phone partnership again by opening up the My Phone client on the phone, logging in with your Windows Live ID and Password, selecting the items you want to sync and syncing the data to your phone.  In this scenario, My Phone should recognize your phone as a new device and sync the data that is currently stored on My Phone to your phone.

But if all that happened is that somehow all your contacts were accidentally deleted from your phone, but all your other data is still there, then you'll need tostop the My Phone Service on your and create the partnership again.  Terminating the partnership will not remove any data from your My Phone web page.  Then when you set up the My Phone partnership again, My Phone will recognize the phone as a new device and sync the data to your phone.

Once in VBA open the Project Explorer (it's not already open). View / Project Explorer.

In the Project Explorer go to:-

Forms

To turn on the Properties window:-

View tab

Properties window.

You can now edit the Form's Properties.

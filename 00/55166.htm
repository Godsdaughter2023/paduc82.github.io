laudablepus say: Sounds like your HDD is set to power down after a time. To change that setting go to >Settings >System >Power&Sleep >under Related settings choose Additional power settings > Change plan settings (of your current power plan) >Change advanced power settings > under Power Options expand Hard Disk, turn off hard disk after >change the time to a longer period, 120 min.?, 240 min.?, 720 min.?, whatever you think is useful. Having the hard drive running does use more power and dumps more heat. Some are of the opinion it's better for the drive's lifespan to keep it spun up and not continually stopping and starting.

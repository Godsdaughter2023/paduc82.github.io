jeremycheng say:

The reason for the error is very simple.

There are too many emails in my account and searching through those emails takes around 60 seconds for any given keyword.

Yet on Outlook.com the search AJAX is set to timeout at 30 seconds so that the search WILL NEVER COMPLETE before the AJAX request times out and terminates the connection.

Interesting enough, the Outlook.com android app does not have the 30 second time limit built into the search. Hence the search function works perfectly fine on the android app even though it takes around a minute for results to come up.

To solve the problem Microsoft simply needs to increase the timeout for outlook.com ajax from 30 seconds to perhaps 120 seconds.

Here Microsoft, I have given you the solution. However, given the lackluster out-sourced technical support, I doubt the solution will be implemented anytime soon.

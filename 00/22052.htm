Hi William,

 

Thank you for contacting Microsoft Community.

I understand that Internet Explorer gets launch when you start your system. This issue could be caused if the registry files are corrupt.

To get a clear understanding of the issue, I want to know:

  1. What is the make and model of your computer?
  2. Did you make any changes on your PC prior to this issue?
  3. When modern Internet Explorer gets launch automatically, does it displays any website? If yes which website is displayed?

 

As you have already tried some troubleshooting steps from your end, and the issue is still not resolved .I suggest you to follow the methods below and check if it helps.

Method 1: Delete scheduled Task.

1) Press CTRL+SHIFT+ DELETE Key Together.

2) Open Taskbar, then click on Performance and Maintenance.

3)

4) Task Scheduler to see if they are set to launch on startup there.

5) If you are able to see any task created to launch Internet Explorer on startup,

6) Delete the task.

 

After trying the steps of method 1 if the issue still persists try method 2.

 

Method 2: Perform clean boot.

 

Check the issue in clean boot, there are possibilities that any third party application is triggering Internet Explorer to open after you restart the system.

Follow the article mentioned to perform a clean boot.

Refer: http://support.microsoft.com/kb/929135

Note: After performing the troubleshooting steps for clean boot refer the section “how to reset the computer to start normally after clean boot troubleshooting”.

 

After trying the method 1 and 2 if still the issue is not fixed provide us the Event
Viewer Log .This will help us to know what exactly event occurred when Internet Explorer opens automatically.

 

Follow the steps below to collect the logs under Event Viewer:

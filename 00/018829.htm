The operative part of your message is "imported my Outlook contacts from my Palm database".

The issue with the FileAs (or Name) fields, is that if these fields are not updated via Outlook (either via the UI in manual mode or via the Outlook Object Model when done programmatically) - the Address Book display settings get messed up for the contact items imported (not anything unique to the Palm import process).

Your options are as follows:

- open each contact affected in Outlook and re-save it (a seriously tedious process especially if a lot of contacts are involved)

- use the code snip as shown in a related thread
http://social.answers.microsoft.com/Forums/en-US/outlookcontact/thread/b37e25da-aa94-483c-a3af-881ac54dde02

- use the FileAs standardization function in ContactGenie Toolkit - http://www.contactgenie.com/toolkit_4_outlook.htm which allos you to standardize the FileAs to any of the supported CTK formats without impacting your AB display settings

a. user say:

Hi beckystorniolo,


Welcome to Microsoft Community! Let me help you with your concern.

 

I understand that you would like to block a specific sender from sending you emails. To add a specific sender on your block sender's list, refer to the steps below:

 

1. Sign in to youl account.

2. Click gear icon  located at the upper-right side of the page.

3. Select More more settings.

4. Under Preventing junk email, click Safe and blocked senders.

5. Click Blocked senders. 

6. Enter the email address or domain on the box labeled Blocked email address or domain: and click Add to list >>.

 

You can also create a Custom Filter or Rules to avoid receiving emails from a specific contact. Please refer to the steps below on how to do this:

 

1. Sign in your account.

2. Move your cursor over the Folders link on the left pane.

3. Click on the Gear icon, and then click Manage Rules.

4. Click New.

5. For this step you will be asked to setup your rule.

6. Under Step 1: Which messages do you want this rule to apply to?

 

Choose the condition that you want to apply, 3 fields are required for this step. On the first field when you click the drop down button you have,

 

 - Sender's  address, Sender's name,To /cc address, Subject and Messages has attachments.

 

 - On the second field you have is, contains, contains word, does not contain, begins with, ends with.

 

 - On the third field you will enter the word that can conclude the action of the 2 fields (for example Subject, is, SPAM).


7. Under Step 2: What action do you want to apply?

8. Click the button beside Delete these messages.

9. Click Save.

 

Note: Please make sure that the emails that you have blocked are not included on your Safe sender's list or Contacts lists so that the rules and blocked senders will work properly.

 

Hope this helps. Let us know if you need additional assistance.

Figured it out. The BitLocker features of ADUC are now dependent on a separate Windows 10 RSAT feature: "RSAT: BitLocker Drive Encryption Administration Utilities". This mimics the way its handled in Server 2016+ as well.

Add BitLocker features to ADUC:

  • Go to "Apps & features"
  • Go to "Manage optional features"
  • Go to "Add a feature"
  • Go to "RSAT: BitLocker Drive Encryption Administration Utilities"
  • Click "Install"

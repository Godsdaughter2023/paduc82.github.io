rick rothstein, mvp - excel say:

Put this normally entered formula in B1 and copy it down...

=IF(COUNTIF(E:E,A1),"YES","")

You can replace the "Yes" with "X" if you actually wanted an X in the cell (as you asked for initially) rather than the word YES that you asked for later. This E:E used in this formula works for XL2003 as well as XL2007 or above (I'm not sure about versions prior to XL2003 because I do not have them available to me).

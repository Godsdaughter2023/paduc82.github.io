Hi,

The Recent activity family settings for your child allows you to turn on activity reporting for your child so that you can see what they've been searching for online. You can also get activity reports emailed to adults in the family weekly. To prevent them from receiving the activity report follow the steps below:

  1. From a web browser, go to http://account.microsoft.com/family and sign in with the Microsoft account of an adult in the family.
  2. Open Settings in Windows 10, click on the Accounts icon, click on Family & other users on the left side, click on the Manage family settings online link.

  4. Click on Recent activity on the left side, and turn off Activity reporting on the right side.

Let us know if the solution above works for you.

Please try these options one by one and check after each option. These options are independent and can be executed in any sequence. Do let me know if these don't work for you. If not, I will provide further troubleshooting steps to you to solve this problem.

1. Clear of all your browsing / history data.

2. Reset Edge
Type Apps in Windows Start Search box > Click Apps & features > Click Default Apps > Go down, Select Microsoft Edge and click Reset > Reboot your Computer

3. Uninstall Edge and Reinstall Edge
https://www.wintips.org/uninstall-reinstall-mic...
(There may be ads on the page for products. Please thoroughly research any product advertised on the page before you decide to download and install it. Your discretion is advised)

4. Run Built-in & Guided Walk through Windows Update troubleshooter
Built-in : Type troubleshoot in Windows Start Search box > Click Troubleshoot > Click Windows update > Troubleshoot

Guided Walk though - https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/10164/...

5. If nothing works, perform a Windows 10 repair upgrade. Repair upgrade fixes all Windows errors and retains all files, applications and settings. Below is a good guide to perform repair upgrade by Andre Da Costa, MVP
http://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/insider/wiki...

Source - https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/402356...

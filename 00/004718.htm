jennifer shi msft say:

Hi Nicole,

Thank you for posting your query in our forum. According to this article, if your account is an Office 365 Business Premium account, then the answer to your frist question is yes, it is supported to collaborate with people outside your organization in Microsoft Teams by granting them guest access. 

However, guest access is turned off by default and if your account has global admin premission, you can enable guest access in following simply steps: 

1. Sign in following website with your global admin account: https://admin.microsoft.com/AdminPortal/Home  

2. Navigate to Settings > Services and add-ins > Microsoft Teams, under the drop-down list next to "Select the user/license type you want to configure", select Guste. 

3. Make sure the status of "Turn Microsoft Teams on or off for all users of this type" is On and then click Save, then you can add peoples outside of your organization into Microsoft Teams as a guest.

You can find detailed steps in the video provided by this article: Guest access in Microsoft Teams.

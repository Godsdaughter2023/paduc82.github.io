Hi Paul,

Personalization settings can only be changed when Windows 10 is activated. This feature is created by design. Also, if you have already activated Windows 10 on a computer and you used that image on another computer, the new computer's Windows 10 will be deactivated. What happened is the hardware (previous PC) will get a digital entitlement, where a unique signature of the computer will be stored on Microsoft Activation Servers.

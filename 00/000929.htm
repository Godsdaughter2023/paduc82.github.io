Expired subscription

yang zheng msft support say: Hi Absinthium1000,

Here I would answer your questions:
1. What happens to our data if we stop paying for a specific subscription and then maybe decide to move to another within the same tenant (and the same family of products): from E1 to E3 for example (or vice-versa).
The data will not be affected and you can still access the data after switching to the new license.

2. Do we still need to take out our data or will they be shared with the new subscription?
No.

3. And if I have 5 users and renew only 4, what happens to the date of the person whose seat I don't renew?
Firstly, I’d like to let you know that we cannot reduce license number when renewing a subscription. You can only renew all the licenses and then manually cancel the licenses you don’t need.
And after you remove the license of the user, the data will be kept in Office 365 for 30 days. After that, the data will be removed and can’t be found. However, if the user is originally assigned an E3 license, you can enable in-place hold or litigation hold for the user mailbox and then change the user to an inactive mailbox. Then the data of the mailbox will be kept and not be removed.

Regards,
Yang

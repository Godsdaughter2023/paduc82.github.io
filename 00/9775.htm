hale001 say:

Hi AbhijeetKhopade,

Here are the answers:

1. For this requirement what would be ideal suggestion for licensing of Office 365?
I would suggest you use Office 365 Business Premium

The link below shows the comparisons between different Office 365 plans.
technet.microsoft.com/.../office-365-plan-options.aspx

2. One Drive or SharePoint Document Center?
If the "One Drive" you mentioned refers to OneDrive for Business, it is our personal document library—the place to keep our work documents and other files and the default document library in a user's My Sites in SharePoint Online.  To collaborate with other users in the same organization, you can use a team site.

Please refer to the article for detailed information:
support.office.com/.../Start-using-your-team-site-OneDrive-for-Business-and-Newsfeed-to-share-documents-and-ideas-abeace23-ffb2-4638-944c-860a2484b4bb

3. Need to share documents with external users but security is main constraint.
After external users accept the file invitation, others you don't share with cannot access it. Any invitations you send to external users will expire in 7 days, and you can also revoke user access to the sharing.

For details:
support.office.com/.../Share-sites-or-documents-with-people-outside-your-organization-80e49744-e30f-44db-8d51-16661b1d4232

4. IRM is one of the requirement too.
We can turn on IRM service using SharePoint admin center.

support.office.com/.../Set-up-Information-Rights-Management-IRM-in-SharePoint-admin-center-239ce6eb-4e81-42db-bf86-a01362fed65c

5. What bandwidth we should focus on?
Because there are many variables to consider for a user when estimating network traffic and they are unique to each customer environment, we are not able to provide the accurate official bandwidth requirement.

Here is an article about network planning for your reference:
support.office.com/.../Network-planning-and-performance-tuning-for-Office-365-e5f1228c-da3c-4654-bf16-d163daee8848

6. Document should work in offline mode, once in network changes should be merged into existing Document Library.
You can sync OneDrive for Business or site libraries to your computer and then access your files from File Explorer instead of from a web browser. All updates will sync to OneDrive for Business or site libraries whenever you’re online.

support.office.com/.../Sync-OneDrive-for-Business-or-site-libraries-to-your-computer-23e1f12b-d896-4cb1-a238-f91d19827a16

7. What will be the approximate cost to implement all above?
It depends on which subscription of Office 365 you choose.

Thanks,
Hale

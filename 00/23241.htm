If I were doing this, I'd export to Outlook Express (for XP) or Windows Mail (for Vista) on the old PC and transport the result to the new PC for importing into Windows Live Mail.  In Outlook, choose File>Import and Export>Export to another program or file.  Choose Outlook Express for messages or Comma Separated Values (Windows) for Contacts and Calendar.

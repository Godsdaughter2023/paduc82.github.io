rohn007 say:

So if I interpret correctly, your mouse is "accidentally" moving while you are typing. 

Actually, the solution may be even cheaper.  Try using a different Mouse PAD. 

About 10 years back I had a parallel problem with a new computer I bought then. It came with a fancy "new" (at the time) mouse with a laser "ball" and free mousepad.  As I moved the mouse, at random times the mouse pointer would zoom off to the edge of the screen. I was ready to return the computer when I accidentally switched the new mouse pad for my old one. The problem went away. Turned out that the problem was that the free mouse pad had a shiny plastic pebbled surface and every now and then that teeny-tiny laser pointer would hit a pebble "the wrong way", causing the on screen mouse pointer to zoom away.

Your problem may be similar with the wireless mouse. Try mouse pads with different surfaces, ones that have more "drag", like a cloth surface.  Maybe that will be enough to keep wireless mouse from slipping when you are typing. 

A replacement mouse pad is cheaper than an new mouse ...

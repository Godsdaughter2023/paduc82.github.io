If you select some text, then click the highlight tool, it will be a one-time action - the tool won't remain active.

If you select some text, then double-click the highlight tool, the tool will remain active until you click it again or you press Esc.

If you don't select text, i.e. you see the blinking insertion bar, then click the highlight tool, it will remain active until you click it again or press Esc.

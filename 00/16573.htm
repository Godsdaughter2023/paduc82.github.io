Moving pictures in Word doc

Can someone tell me why I am having difficulty moving picture clips on my Word doc (on my Mac).  I can move the picture using the arrow keys, but it jumps the picture too far.  So I try to hold down the Option key while tapping the arrow key and nothing moves.  This will work on other pictures and text boxes, but not on certain pictures.  Why?  How can I make it work?
rich michaels - mvp (greatcirclelearning.com) say:
By your description it appears that these pictures are "floating" meaning they have a text wrapping/placement attribute assigned to them and are not inserted as inline images.

These type of images, just like Text boxes, must be anchored to a paragraph mark. The behavior you are describing indicates that when you attempt to move them a slight distance either the distance isn't enough to change which paragraph mark they are anchored to or the text wrapping setting is set to Square and it makes this fine tuning of position more difficult.

First try changing the text wrapping attribute to "Tight" and see if that helps.

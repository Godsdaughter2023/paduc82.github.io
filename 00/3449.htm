  1. Go to Word> Preferences - Ribbon & Toolbar
  2. On the Ribbon page
    1. Click the ▶︎ adjacent to Home in the right list.
    2. Select Paragraph in that list to put the Styles Pane back in its original position [a command is added directly below the one currently selected].

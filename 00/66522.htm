save this to a batch file onto your desktop, it will remove the event errors in windows 7. open NOTEPAD copy and paste this below into and save as clearevent.bat

@echo off
FOR /F "tokens=1,2*" %%V IN ('bcdedit') DO SET adminTest=%%V
IF (%adminTest%)==(Access) goto noAdmin
for /F "tokens=*" %%G in ('wevtutil.exe el') DO (call :do_clear "%%G")

:do_clear
echo clearing %1

goto :eof
:noAdmin
exit

O ventilador continuará a funcionar mesmo com o console desligado no modo instant-on por vários motivos, incluindo: resfriamento do console e atualização de itens, enquanto essa opção estiver ativada para "manter meu console e jogos atualizados" em configurações> power definições. Isso também fará com que o console demore mais para ligar.

The fan will continue to run even while the console is turned off in instant-on mode for various reasons including: cooling the console and updating items while you have that option enabled to "keep my console and games up to date" in settings > power settings. To solve this problem please turn the console into power savings mode in Settings > power, but this will prevent games and your console from updating while it's turned off. It will also make the console take longer to turn on.

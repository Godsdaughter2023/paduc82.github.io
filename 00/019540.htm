You can copy them, but you can sync your playlist to your thumbdrive.


Click the Windows "Start" Menu and select "Windows Media Player" from the "Programs" list.
Step

Click the "Copy to CD or Device" tab on the left side. Click the down-arrow under "Items to copy" and select the playlist you wish to copy.

a. user say:

Hello Levi:

 

Thank you for posting to the forum regarding the issue you are having viewing pictures you are tagged in.  From research I have done, this tends to be related to settings on the Facebook account.  This may be related to your account settings or the settings of those that posted and tagged the pictures.

 

The first thing we would like to suggest is you go to Facebook.com and sign into your account.  Go to your account settings and review all privacy settings.  Make sure that there are no restrictions regarding what can be viewed and especially no restrictions related to mobile.

 

The next thing we would suggest is to check with the person that tagged the pictures.  We have see reports that this may be related to their settings when it comes to privacy settings for third party applications. 

 

The final thing we can suggest you look into is the linking of your Microsoft account and Facebook accounts.  Please go to accounts.live.com and sign into your account.  Once you are signed in, go to account settings > preferences > if your Facebook and Microsoft account are linked, unlink them.  Once the accounts are unlinked, go to your phone and remove your Facebook account.  You can do this by tapping settings > emails + accounts > tap and hold the Facebook account > select delete.  After the account is deleted, uninstall the Facebook application.  After the application is uninstalled, add the Facebook account back to the emails + accounts section.  Finally, go to the marketplace and reinstall the Facebook application.

 

Please let us know if you have any other questions or concerns we can assist you with.

 

Mark W.

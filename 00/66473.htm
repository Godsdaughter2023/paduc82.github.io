vibroplex say:

Hughesnet Gen4 has a minimum lag of 240 ms in each direction because the signal must leave your house, go to the satellite in Clarke orbit http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geostationary_orbit, 22,306 miles above at the speed of 186,282 miles per second, then down to the earth station, and only then does it start trying to get onto the Internet to reach the Xbox servers. When the signal returns, there's another 240 ms delay. This makes for slow gaming, which is made worse by the bufferbloat caused by the Hughesnet caching.

 

The hotspot, on the other hand, doesn't have that delay, so when it comes to gaming, it's less worse.

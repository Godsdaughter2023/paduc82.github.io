xircal say:

Hi,

Assuming you can get to the lock screen, hold down Shift while you click the power button and click Restart. This should take you to the boot menu.

Click Troubleshoot ---> Advanced options ---> System Restore.

In the System Restore menu select a Restore point from a time prior to changing the resolution and click Next. If you can't see enough Restore points, checkmark the box to show more.

Click Next and then follow the prompts from there.

When System Restore runs the screen will turn black and it might appear as if nothing is happening. It can take up to 15 mins as well but eventually, the familiar lock screen will appear and you should be able to login and boot normally.

Privacy of shared Notebooks on OneNote

Are the Notebooks shared with company team members private to other members of the company? Does the Office 365 Administrator have access to the Notebook contents?

What is the difference between OneNote and OneDrive in Office 365?

jerryduan_msft8021 say:

Hi ACUE,

For your personal site’s OneNote notebooks are created and stored in the OneDrive for Business library. As for the person who have the view, edit, or management permission of the notebooks, it depends on whether and what permission the notebook is shared with the user. The global administrator as well does not have access to your notebooks if the notebook is not shared with the admin. Owners of the notebook can check and manage the list of the permission information of a notebook via the menu dialog for the item (ellipsis besides the item) SHARE > Shared with > ADVANCED.

For a TeamSite’s notebook, same as the personal site, the person who have the view, edit, or management permission of the notebooks depends on whether and what permission the notebook is shared with the user. The site collection administrator has the full control permission for the whole site collection by default, so the TeamSite notebook is accessible for the site collection administrator.

OneDrive for Business is a special library in your personal site where you can store, sync and share your work files. For the details, please check this article: What is OneDrive for Business?

Jerry

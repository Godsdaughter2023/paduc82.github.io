How can I use the datedif function to project future dates?

 For example: someone enters a program today and will require follow up in 6 months, then at the 1 year mark, and every year after that.
gary's student say: Use DATE() rather than DATDIF()

Enter the date in A1.

In A2 thru A4 enter:

=DATE(YEAR(A1),MONTH(A1)+6,DAY(A1))
=DATE(YEAR(A1)+1,MONTH(A1),DAY(A1))
=DATE(YEAR(A3)+1,MONTH(A3),DAY(A3))

Then copy A4 down as far as needed.

You will be able to disable right click option in Windows, but you will not be able to disable on any particular program like Internet Explorer and other Browsers.

 

Follow these steps to disable mouse right click option on Windows.

a.       Click “Start” button.

b.      Type “regedit” without quotes in the search box and hit enter.

c.       Navigate to the following registry value.

Computer\HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Policies\Explorer

d.      Right click on the right pane.

e.      Select “New” and then select “DWORD (32 bit) value.

f.        Name it as “NoViewContextMenu”.

g.       Double left click on “NoViewContextMenu”.

h.      Change the value from “0” to “1”.

i.         Close the Registry Window.

j.        Restart the computer for changes to take effect.

k.       To enable the right click option, you have to change the “NoViewContextMenu” value from “1” to “0”.

l.         Then restart your computer.

I have had the same problem.  On Windows Phone 7.5, this scenario worked without any issues.

Here are the steps to add a second Office 365 account to your Phone:

 

Step 1

- Log on to Portal.MicrosoftOnline.com

- Navigate over to Outlook

- Write down the name of the server from the address bar:

E.g.  hkxprd3456.outlook.com

 

Step 2

- Navigate to email+accounts

- Click "add an account"

- Choose "advanced setup"

- Type your Office 365 email address and password, then click next

- Select "Exchange ActiveSync"

- Scroll down to Server and type the server name from step 1

- Click Sign In

 

I know these steps are a little cumbersome, but it works.

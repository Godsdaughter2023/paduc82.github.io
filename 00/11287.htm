kuttusram say:
  1. How do I remove funmoods from newly opened tabs on my browser?

    In MS Internet Explorer 9 - to remove funmoods search from new tabs, go to
    Tools -> Internet options -> general -> tabs settings, and set the "when a new tab is opened, open" drop down, and choose "The new tab page".

    In MS Internet Explorer 7 or 8 - to remove from funmoods new tabs, the program has to be uninstalled completely. Please find instructions here.

    In Firefox- Go to Tools and then addons, click Options, then select Advanced and uncheck the first option "Use funmoods as enhanced search for new tab".

  2. How do I remove the funmoods toolbar?

    You can hide the toolbar by right clicking on the toolbar area, then find funmoods Toolbar and uncheck it. To remove the toolbar altogether, you will have to uninstall funmoods.

  3. How do I remove funmoods from my browser's default search?
    (this is the search located on top right in Explorer/Firefox and in the address bar of Chrome.)

    In Explorer- Go to Tools and select Internet Options. In the first tab labeled General, click Settings then select your preferred search provider and click Set as Default.

    In Firefox- Click the downward arrow button next to the search box. Then select Manage Search Engines. Select funmoods Search and click Remove.

    In Chrome- click the wrench icon and select Options. Make sure you are on the first tab "Basics" and then look for "Search" option to select your preferred provider.

You can set up a table of period numbers and dates as so:

Period Date
1 09/01/2010
2 23/01/2010
3 06/02/2010
4 20/02/2010
5 06/03/2010

If period 1 is in F1 and the first saturday of the year is in F1. Fill Column F with periods up 27.

In G3 use the formula G3+14 and fill down. If you then give the name of the table say, Periods, it will not matter in which sheet the table lies. You can then use a lookup formula in the form. If the period is in B2 use:

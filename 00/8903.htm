How can I back up windows 10 on an external drive?

how to back up windows 10,

a. user say:

Hi,

Thank you for posting your query in Windows 10 Technical Preview forums.

I understand that you want to know how you can take a backup of data on Windows 10 on an external drive.

To take a backup of Windows 10 on an external drive follow the steps below:

  1. On the search of start menu type PC settings.
  2. Scroll down and go to Update and Recovery.
  3. From the available options of Update and Recovery, select the File History.
  4. The right pane of the File History is the place where you will carry out the work. You will see a couple of options being mentioned there such as turn on and off File history etc.
  5. The File History option will be greyed out until you insert an external drive to your computer. Attaching the external drive in the supportable port, the File History option will allow you to turn it on or off.
  6. Slide the switch and turn it on and then your files of Desktop, Documents, Contacts, Libraries, Music and Videos will be backed up in the inserted drive.
  7. In case you wish to have the backups on another drive, insert that drive and click on select a different drive link. Attached drives will be scanned, choose the desired one.
  8. To carry out a manual backup, click on Backup now button. The date and time details of the last backup will be displayed on top of that option.

Hope the above information helps. Please let us know if you need any further assistance using your Windows.

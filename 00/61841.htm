I nad exactly same problems with my new / fully patched Surface Pro 3: miniSD Card not working / not recognized. Solution mentioned ealier worked.

So, the exact steps below:

1) Open "Device Manager" and find non recognized device in "Other devices"

2) Righht-Click and use option "Update Driver Software..."

3) "Browse my computer...."

4) "Let me pick from a list ...."

5) from the list fine and click on "SD host adapters"

6) Choose the last one: "USB Mass Storage Device" - do not choose any "Related USB Card Reader devices"

... and SD Card should work from now.

I downloaded the latest surface drivers from Microsoft and it came with the Realtek drivers. I hope this helps everyone else having problems, for me the Realtek driver was the issue.

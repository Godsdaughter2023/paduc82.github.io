Hello,

To clear the recent files straight from the Word app, they're all need to be cleared from OneDrive's settings. To do this:

  1. Click on the Menu button on the top left and click on OneDrive.
  2. Click on Recent Docs then select all the files that you want to remove on the list then click Manage > Remove.

If you still see the files listed, close then re-open the app and see if the files are gone. You can also find these links useful:

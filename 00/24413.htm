- change to this directory

- delete this message or move it to another folder

- switch off your phone

- delete all subdirectories on the "Phone Browser" directory on your pc

- power on the phone

- reconnect with your pc

- restart PC Nokia Suite

- at this time PC Nokia should be able to complete successfully

 

if the problem still exists, check if another file with a wrong filesize exists in the messages directory.

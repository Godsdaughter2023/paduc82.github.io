You are using the Windows 10 Mail app and posted the instructions for that app.

Your Title bar probably shows Inbox - Outlook - Mail.

The first word shows what folder is being viewed.

The second word shows the account being viewed.

The third word is the program name.

 (If someone can explain why settings don't work across multiple devices I would appreciate that as well.) 

You want to know why conversation settings don't apply across multiple devices.

Emails are sent and received by an email server.

When you access your emails, an email protocol is used to access the server.

Pop3 protocol only downloads emails to and from the server.

IMAP protocol syncs with the server.  The read/unread status of the email syncs.  A deleted email will be deleted from the server and on your device.  When you reply to an email, the email shows there has been a reply.

The server does not use a conversation setting.  It is not a setting that is handled by the email protocol.  Your app or device is also not setup to receive settings from the server.

Do you mean underline spaces but not tabs? As far as I know the only way to do that is to underline all text, then remove underlining from the tabs.

Click Replace All.

benishiryo say: hi mimkora.  when you are using SUMPRODUCT, do not range up the whole column.  that slows down the workbook.  so use a range you are unlikely to reach; say 1000:
=IF(SUMPRODUCT((PO!$A$2:$A$1000=A2)*(PO!$C$2:$C$1000=B2)*PO!$H$2:$H$1000),SUMPRODUCT((PO!$A$2:$A$1000=A2)*(PO!$C$2:$C$1000=B2)*PO!$H$2:$H$1000),"No Change")

the above is assuming your data has headers & start in row 2.  if you have Excel 2007 & above, you can range the whole columns & even calculates faster with SUMIFS:
=IF(SUMIFS(PO!H:H,PO!A:A,A2,PO!C:C,B2),SUMIFS(PO!H:H,PO!A:A,A2,PO!C:C,B2),"No Change")

the 2 solutions will not work though, if you have Cust PO & Parts repeated in PO sheet.  if that's the case, let us know & we'll provide an INDEX array formula

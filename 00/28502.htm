webbrewers3 say:

Sadly, there's no out of the box way to resolve this. Msft deceptively named what is in reality a hard limit, a "list view threshold" which  implies it only affects list views. But it actually prevents most operations like adjusting permissions.

The only workaround is to connect your library to an Access database and then remove chunks of it to other lists until you get below the 5k limit. 

All the fixes Msft suggests are only applicable BEFORE you get to 5k items.  It's a classic catch-22.  Once you go over 5k, you're stuck.

  • In the main menu bar, change to View > Draft
  • Now select View > Footnotes, also in the main menu bar

    • If you have a larger-than-usual space below the separator line, select Format > Paragraph and set Space After to zero
  • In the dropdown menu, select All endnotes
  • Click again to select Endnote separator and repeat the last two steps in the hollow bullets above
  • Click on Close next to the dropdown menu
  • Change back to your preferred View

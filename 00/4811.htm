john mcghie say:
In Outlook for Mac, you must first show the Local folders.

First go to Outlook>Preferences and ensure that "Hide On My Computer folders" is NOT checked.  If it is, you won't be able to see the local folders, even if you create them.

Now, in the folder list, you will see an "On My Computer" entry at the bottom of the folder list, with a green arrow on it.  If you have "Group similar folders" set, this entry may not be at the bottom of the list, but it will be below any IMAP account(s) you have connected.

Right-click "On My Computer" entry and you can create a new folder within this structure.

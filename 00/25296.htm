There are two types of rule sets for two types of sheets: CSV sheets and Web (accessed from online documents) sheets both having the months of the year in the one workbook.

paulri say:

Calibri look-alike serif font?

I like the smooth, rounded shape of Calibri, but also like a serif font, so the letters "I" (as in eye) and 'l' (as in bELL) don't look so much alike. Is there a serif font that looks just like Calibri? 
jay freedman say:
Try the Ebrima font that comes with Windows 7. It's a sans serif font with sections for a number of African languages, but the Basic Latin section (what you'll see if you apply it to your document) looks a lot like Calibri except for serifs on the capital I. It's also larger than Calibri, so you would probably want to reduce the size from 11 pt to 10 pt for an approximate match of layout.

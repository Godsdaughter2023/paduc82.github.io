Hi,

The minutes of your calling subscription will be used to cover the forwarded calls if you were unable to answer the call to your Skype Number on the application.

The call is still directed to your Skype Number, however if call forwarding does kick in the caller, will not be notified that the call has been forwarded to a mobile/landline number.

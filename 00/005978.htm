This can normally be fixed by deleting the History and Temporary Internet files. You can do this by following these steps:

a)      Open Internet Explorer (only one Internet Explorer window open)

b)      Press Alt + F on your keyboard

c)       Click Work Offline

d)      Click Safety

e)      Click Delete Browsing History…

f)       Uncheck the Preserve Favorites website data box

g)      Check the Temporary Internet files and History boxes

h)      Click Delete

i)        Wait for the process bars to finish

j)        Close Internet Explorer

k)      Open Internet Explorer

l)        Press Alt + F on your keyboard

m)    Click Work Offline to disable it

Now as you visit your Favorites websites the Favorite icon (the icon on the Favorites) will be downloaded again

Some dates have volume, others don't (there may not have been production on the dates or it is in the future). What I want to do is get the average volume for each day of the week: Mon, Tue, Wed, ..., Sun.

I can't seem to get the average. The problem seemingly is those pesky zero days. I tried to get a count of production Mondays with:

joeu2004 say: gebobs wrote:
I seem to be able to get the total volume by day with:
----- for Monday and modified for the other days

I can't seem to get the average. The problem seemingly is those pesky zero days. I tried to get a count of production Mondays with:

Your second formula is close.  You can use either of the following, whichever feels better to you.

=SUMPRODUCT((WEEKDAY(Data2011[Date])=2)*(Data2011[Volume]>0))

or

=SUMPRODUCT(--(WEEKDAY(Data2011[Date])=2),--(Data2011[Volume]>0))

Note the change in the placement of the right parenthesis in the second condition.

In the second form, the double-negative (--) is needed to convert TRUE and FALSE to the numeric values 1 and 0, which SUMPRODUCT requires in this context.  Any arithmetic operation has the same effect, which is why the double-negative is not needed with multipication (*).

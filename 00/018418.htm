hansv mvp say:

Let's say you have a date in cell A1.

To extract the month name as a string, you can use the formula

=TEXT(A1,"mmm")

for the abbreviated name ("Aug"), or

=TEXT(A1,"mmmm")

for the full name ("August")

The YEAR function extracts the year as a 4 digit number:

=YEAR(A1)

I don't know what else you'd want.

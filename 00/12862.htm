Archiving is a way to free up space on your phone. An “archived” item remains in the archive section of your My Phone account, but is removed from the phone. Items deleted from the Archive are permanently deleted.

pamcaswell say: I am guessing that Table-Text is a paragraph style.  What do you mean when you say Helvetica Neue 11 pt is your default font? 

If it is not part of the MS Word definition of the defaults, go to the Set Defaults tab (Styles pane > Manage Styles button) and enter all the settings you want for your regular text paragraphs there.  Then modify the Normal paragraph style to match the defaults.  Barring any other oddities, your Table-Text style should rule. 

When working in post W2007 documents,  never just change the normal paragraph style, or worse, ignore it.  Always change document fonts by changing Style Sets or by directly changing the settings in the Set Defaults tab and either leaving the normal paragraph style unchanged or (if you are revising a document) changing its settings to match the defaults.

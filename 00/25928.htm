a. user say:

Hi everyone,


Thank you for posting here in Microsoft Community.


I'm happy to help you with your concern.
 

If you wish to copy and paste emails from Microsoft Excel to Outlook.com, please follow these steps:
 

1. Make sure all the email addresses ends in a semi colon (;).
2. Copy all the email addresses in the column.
3. Hover your mouse to an empty cell.
4. Press right-click then hover your mouse to Paste special.
5. Click Transpose.
6. Copy the selected cell.
7. Paste it on the Bcc field in Outlook.com.

 

Should you have any other questions or concern, please let us know.


Thank you.

a. user say:

Hi,

 

If you want to copy and paste email addresses from Microsoft Excel to Bcc field of Outlook.com, you need to separate the email addresses using comma (e.g. abc @hotmail.com, zxc @hotmail.com) or else it will be detected by our system as a single email address only.

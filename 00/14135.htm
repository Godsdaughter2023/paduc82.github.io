jay freedman say:

Open the second dialog on that button (Review > Language > Language Preferences).

In the list under "Choose Editing Languages", click on the language you want to remove, and click the Remove button at the right side of the list. Click the OK button, and then shut down and restart Word.

In the "Set proofing language" dialog, the language you chose will appear below the double line, and will not appear above the double line.

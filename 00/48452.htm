Hey there Aries!

I'm sorry to hear you're having issues getting Borderlands 2 to play properly for you. Let's see what we can do to get things working properly.

First off, just to explain:

When content is purchased from the Xbox Live Marketplace, ownership of that content is given to the profile that purchased it as well as the console that it was purchased from.

So, since you purchased the content on your home console, it is accessible while offline. However, when you want to play it on your mobile console, you have to be logged into your account and online.

Now then, it sounds like you did a license transfer from your home console to your mobile console. That should enable you to play Borderlands 2 in offline mode on your mobile console, but (since ownership now lies with the mobile console instead of the home console) you'll need to be logged into your account and online on your home console to play the game now.

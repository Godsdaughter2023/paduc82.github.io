The official answer is "No", since Office 2007 is no longer supported.

In reality, you currently probably won't run into any compatibility issues but future updates to Windows 10 might cause it to break since Office 2007 is no longer taken into consideration.

It is best to upgrade to a currently still supported version of Office such as Office 2010, Office 2013 or Office 2016/Office 365.

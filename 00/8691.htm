Microsoft Office Word - Word Wrap - Microsoft Community

fernando. say:

Microsoft Office Word - Word Wrap

How can I change the word wrap in Micrsoft Office Word to the middle of the page like WordPad ?
macropod say:

Hi Fernando,

In WordPad, you have three wrapping options: none; to ruler; and to window. The second of these gives a panel that looks like a page in the middle of the screen. In Word, you can get a similar appearance by using the Print Layout view. In all Word versions, you get this via View|Print Layout. Changing the zoom level affects how much of the screen this takes up - make it too small and you'll have two pages side-by-side on screen.

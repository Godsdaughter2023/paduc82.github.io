Hi David+22,

How did you share your calendar and how did you open it? By "Outlook 365", do you mean OWA(https://outlook.office.com/)? If so, I'd like to deliver that it’s not feasible to share a calendar to a DG (Distribution group) or Office 365 modern group.

 

However, we can share a calendar to a SG (Security group). Please see the steps below:

1. Create a SG in EAC (Exchange admin center) > recipients > groups > click the add icon (+) > Security group.

Note: Add all the staffs to the SG when creating it.

2. Open the Office 365 calendar in OWA (Outlook Web App).

3. Click Share > type the SG’s email address into the Share with box > Send.

Then all staffs in the SG should receive the share invitation.

By using the SG, you can send emails to it and users in SG can send emails from the SG with the Send As/Send on Behalf permission assigned. For the details, you can refer to this article.

You can add existing DGs to the membership of the SG, the members in DGs can receive the emails sent to the SG and calendars shared to the SG.

The difference between SGs and DGs is that you can’t assign permissions to DGs. You can use SGs to assign permissions to shared resources but DGs are only used to create e-mail distribution lists.

Feel free to let us know if you encounter any problem during the procedure, we will assist you further.

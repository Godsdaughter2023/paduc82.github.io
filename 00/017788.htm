deleting contacts - Microsoft Community

deleting contacts

a. user say:

Hi Willyhank,            

Welcome to the Skype Community forum.

I'm sure, this could be annoying. Don't worry, I'll look into this for you.

If you're referring to your address book, technically, by performing a complete uninstall and reinstall of the application should delete your Mac address book contacts from your Skype account. However, the risk of doing this is that your Skype conversation might get deleted too. If you're okay with that, you may follow the instructions below:

  1. Quit Skype by selecting Skype > Quit Skype.
  2. Open your Applications folder and drag your copy of Skype to the trash.
  3. Then open ~/Library/Application Support * and drag the Skype folder to the trash. The ~ sign represents your home folder. To find your home folder, open Finder and, from the menu bar, select Go > Home.
  4. Open ~/Library/Preferences and drag com.skype.skype.plist to the trash.
  5. Open Finder and search for “Skype” using the search feature in Mac OS X. Move all of the results returned to the trash.
  6. Click Ctrl and the trash icon, and select Empty Trash.
  7. Restart your computer.
  8. Download and install the latest version of Skype.

*By default, the Library folder is hidden in Mac OS X v10.7 “Lion” and higher. To find your Library folder, open Finder and, from the menu bar, select Go, press and hold the option key, and then select Library.

If you wish to keep your history, deleting your Mac contacts one by one is another option. Please let us know if you have questions.

marites_p say:

Hi, 

Screen sharing is a free feature of Skype. To share your screen, kindly follow the steps below: 

  • Sign in to Skype. 
  • Start a voice or video call with a single person or a group.
  • During a call, tap or click the plus icon or ... icon in the call bar, then choose Share screen. 
  • Use the Share your screen drop-down to choose what to share - either your full screen or a specific window, then choose Start. 

Take note that you can only share your screen if the call is answered.

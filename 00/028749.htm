kdbbarrett say:

The answer to your second question is.  You have to create a template and save it as your default template. 

  1. Open a new page in OneNote and select the Insert tab.

  2. Click or tap the Page Templates button.

    The Templates pane appears at the right side of the OneNote window.

    1. With the note you want to turn into a template open, select the Save Current Page as Template link on the Templates pane.  Note: In your case create a blank note and remove the date and timestamp and then go to step 3 below.

I don't know how to explain this but I'll try my best.If you plug the pin slowly(that is first only inserting the pin halfway or more and then snapping)the buttons don't work.

I don't know if anyone has noticed it, but even if you plug in loosely the headset icon on your n8 screen will show up.so even if you plug it properly after that(till it snaps), the headset buttons will not work....in short, if the headset icon shows up before the snap sound, the headset doesn't work.this is NOT how you want to plug in...

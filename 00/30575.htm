Usually the HP install disks take care of connecting to a printer, particularly a Multi-function printer.  However you can also connect using the Add Printer Wizard.

The first place you went wrong was when you selected the apparently obvious choice of a "Network Printer".  Instead, select "Local Printer" and leave "Automatically Detect" unchecked.  On the next screen, select "Create a New Port" and change the "Type of Port" to "Standard TCP/IP port".  This will bring up yet another wizard which will create a port.  Enter the IP address of your printer in the following screen.  The rest of the choices from here on out are fairly obvious.

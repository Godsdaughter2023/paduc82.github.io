gsprock say:

Average by rank

I am trying to make an average based on rankings of certain data.

joeu2004 say: GSprock wrote:

I am trying to make an average based on rankings of certain data.
I have a list of data that I wish to ignore the top 10% and bottom 10% of the results.  Then average the bottom 25% of the middle 80% and average the top 75% of the middle 80%.  Any suggestions?


Assume your data are in column A.

Average of lower 25% of middle 80%:
=AVERAGEIFS(A:A,A:A,">"&PERCENTILE(A:A,10%),A:A,"<="&PERCENTILE(A:A,30%))

Average of upper 75% of middle 80%:
=AVERAGEIFS(A:A,A:A,">"&PERCENTILE(A:A,30%),A:A,"<="&PERCENTILE(A:A,90%))

If you want formulas that are compatible with XL2003, assuming the data are in A1:A10000, use the following
Average of lower 25% of middle 80%:
=AVERAGE(IF(A1:A10000>PERCENTILE(A:A,10%),
IF(A1:A10000<=PERCENTILE(A:A,30%),A1:A10000)))

Average of upper 75% of middle 80%:
=AVERAGE(IF(A1:A10000>PERCENTILE(A:A,30%),
IF(A1:A10000<=PERCENTILE(A:A,90%),A1:A10000)))

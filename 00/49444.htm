The drive will make kind of a buzzing noise, sounding like the drive is spinning out of control. This usually happens sporadically and I believe doesn't effect every disc, only discs with certain DRMs. It will normally happen after you start a game, whether you install it or not, when you shut down the console, often preventing shutdown, or very rarely on the dashboard after inserting a disc or exiting a game. I have the problem I just described and it has been reported by many other users as well. Usually exiting the game and ejecting and reinserting the disc will fix the in game problem, ejecting the disc (don't have to remove it) and shutting down will fix the shutdown problem, and ejecting an reinserting the disc will fix the dashboard problem.

It might be a shot in the dark but it's worth a try. Good luck.

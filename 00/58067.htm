Do you see the Windows logo and then a black screen?

If that's the case, then we suggest that you try starting the computer in safe mode through Window recovery. Once in safe mode, uninstall the video driver and restart the computer.  To start in safe mode, follow these steps:

  1. Boot from the Installation media. (If you don't have one,  click on this link.)
  2. On the initial setup screen, enter your language and other preferences, and then select Next. If you're not seeing the setup screen, your PC might not be set up to boot from a drive. Check your PC manufacturer's website for info on how to change your PC's boot order, and then try again.

  4. Choose Enable Safe Mode.

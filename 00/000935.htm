Give this formula a try...

=TRIM(RIGHT(SUBSTITUTE(A1,"-",REPT(" ",99)),99))

Note that the 99's mean the formula will only work correctly if the text being parsed is less than 100 characters long. If the text could be longer, then change the two 99's to a number equal to or greater than the longest text string that it will ever have to process.

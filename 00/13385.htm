chrispeng_msft6626 say:

hi jannis,

we cannot create a rule with a condition “the recipient is a distribution group”.
as it’s not feasible to create a transport rule for the emails sent to the distribution group, i suggest you create a shared mailbox with the same address as a workaround. you can try the steps below:
1. delete the distribution group.
2. create a shared mailbox using the same address with the one of the deleted distribution group (eg. *** Email address is removed for privacy ***). grant yourself full access permission of it.
 

3. navigate to mail flow > rules, then click the plus icon to create a new rule.
4. select the recipient is under apply this rule if, in the pop-up window, choose *** Email address is removed for privacy *** > add > ok.


5. select reject the message with the explanation under do the following, in the pop-up window, write the information you want the senders to receive.


6. ok > save
7. then if others send emails to *** Email address is removed for privacy ***, they will receive an ndr with the information you filled above.

if you encounter any error message when performing the steps below, please provide a screenshot of it.

  • Type services.msc into the start menu search window.
  • Right click on the found icon,
  • select "Run As Administrator".
  • In the Services dialog, click on the Name column header to sort alphabetically.
  • Scroll through the list to find the Application Virtualization client service AND the "Application Virtualization Service Agent"

  • try clicking on the Start button

  • Note the "this service depends on ..." list, make sure they are all started.

joe men say:

Hello Andy,

The most likely reasons as to why your Lumia 735 not charging on your 15000 mAh power bank are as follows:

  • The phone is charging slowly due to software issues.
  • The output of your power bank is insufficient. You might need to use a power bank that has an output of 2.1A.
  • The USB type Micro-B charging cable is not connecting properly on your Lumia 735's micro-B charging port. The pins might need to be adjusted/lifted.
  • The USB type Micro-B port on your Lumia 735 needs to be replaced or cleaned.

Here are the possible solutions that you can try:

  • Soft reset your phone by pressing and holding the Volume down + Power buttons until it vibrates while connected to the power bank.
  • Try using a power bank that is equipped with a 2.1A power output.
  • Use a different charging cable. It is recommended that you use the charging cable that came with your device.
  • Take your phone to a service center and have the charging port inspected. If the issue is with the charging port, it can be replaced.

Let us know what happens after doing the mentioned solutions.

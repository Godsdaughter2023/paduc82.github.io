jay freedman say:

What happened to it? No idea -- anything from a botched update to gremlins.

How to get it back:

  • Right-click the ribbon and click Customize the Ribbon. The Options dialog opens.
  • Open the "Choose commands from" dropdown and click "Main Tabs".
  • Click the plus sign next to Home, to expand it.
  • Click the Editing item to select it.
  • Click the Add button in the center of the dialog. "Editing" should appear at the end of the list of groups under Home on the right side.
  • Click OK.

If any part of this procedure doesn't work as described, repair Office.

Public Sub txtLookup_AfterUpdate()

On Error GoTo Err:

    Dim rst As DAO.Recordset

    End If
    
Exit_Err:
    Exit Sub

Err:
    MsgBox Err.Description, , "Sorry"
    Resume Exit_Err
End Sub

hansv mvp say:

Change

    rst.FindFirst "txtNickname Like '" & Me.txtLookup & "*'"

to

    rst.FindFirst "txtNickname Like " & Chr(34) & Me.txtLookup & "*" & Chr(34)

Chr(34) is the double quote character ". This should handle O'Brien OK, but it'll fail if there are nicknames that contain a double quote ".

Hide Background Pages in Microsoft Visio 2013 - Microsoft Community

nick steblay say:

Hide Background Pages in Microsoft Visio 2013

Microsoft Visio 2013 has a great feature that allows you to hide background pages.

1) Enable the Developer Tab in the Ribbon

2) On the Developer Tab check Drawing Explorer to open 

3) In the Drawing Explorer right click on your Visio file name and select Protect Document

4) Check Backgrounds to protect hide background pages

Pages won't hide until you click on a page tab.  This is preferred way as opposed to using the ShapeSheet properties.

If you or other players hear an echo or other noise during gameplay, try the following:

  • Turn the volume down on your TV or stereo.

    • Port Forwarding

rl_1015 say:

How To Fix The Windows 8.1 Problem With Printer Off-line

1- Print a page - If an indication of PRINTER OFF LINE Then ONLY do the following;

2- Click on SETTINGS

3- Click on CONTROL PANEL

4- Right-click on the printer-icon that is off-line.

5- Select SEE WHAT'S PRINTING

6- Remove the CHECK MARK at USE PRINTER OFFLINE by clicking on the CHECK MARK there.

7- Printer should be now ready to resume-printing.

Why Microsoft has the capability of making the printer OFF LINE at the USE PRINTER OFFLINE option, by somehow inserting a CHECK-MARK at that option without the user directly doing that is a mystery to me?

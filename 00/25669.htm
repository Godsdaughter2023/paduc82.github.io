When you open a SharePoint document from within the Document Library connected to an Outlook folder, a copy should be stored in your My Documents\SharePoint Drafts folder for offline editing.  When you close the document, it should prompt you to update the server with your offline changes.  If you later open that document from your Drafts folder, you should see Check In/Out commands from the Server menu under the Office button.

If you require a better offline editing experience, you may want to consider SharePoint Workspace or Colligo Contributor.

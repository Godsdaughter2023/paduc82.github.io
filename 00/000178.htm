In your case, the Address Book is nothing more than a different representation of your Contacts folder. Change the default address list of the Outlook Address Book

Restoring a pst-file of a POP3 account on a new computer

2. Click on the Options icon located at the upper right part of the screen.

3. Choose More mail settings.

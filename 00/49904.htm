onyx is here say:

Sounds like you've created an offline profile.  The settings will not be available on an offline profile as it does not connect to Live at all.

What you should do is create a new email address specifically for that account then, on the console, log in to it.  On the main Home screen you should see an option to Join Live.  Select it and follow the instructions adding the email when it prompts you to do so.  It will give the account a new gamertag which you can change once immediately for free.

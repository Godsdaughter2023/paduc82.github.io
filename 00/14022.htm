I see you have been given some advice from people trying to help, and that’s good, but let’s look at your error log. It says:

Crashed Module Name: merp

 “merp” stands for Microsoft Error Reporting. So this bit of of your crash log says that Microsoft Error Reporting had trouble setting up the user interface. This kind of problem might be related to a bad font. So, before going to the trouble of completely removing and then reinstalling Office, check your fonts. Another posibility is that permissions are incorrectly set. After each system update, be sure to run Disk Utility to verify the startup volume and repair disk permissions. Running Repair disc Permissions just once after an OS update is sufficient.


Please consider trying this approach before trying the slash and burn approach. If a bad font or permissions problems are at the root of this crash, then reinstalling probably won’t change anything.

vaidya darpan msft say:

Hello AkikoS- [O365],

Thanks for your post in forum. Regarding your description, we cannot move blocked domain list from transport rule to spam filter. We can only manually add an email address and domain name in spam filter block list under Protection in Office 365 Admin Center. For your reference https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/office365/securitycompliance/create-organization-wide-safe-sender-or-blocked-sender-lists-in-office-365

Also, if the block domain transport rule has not work properly, we need to add the domain name and email address in block list under spam filter. Because spam filter is the second option for block domain list in Office 365 Admin Center. But the simple and easy way is add email address and entire domain name in block list under spam filter.

On the other hand, regarding move block domain and email address to spam filter from transport rule, it cannot meet your requirement. Your time, understanding and cooperation will be highly appreciated.

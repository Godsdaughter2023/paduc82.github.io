Sub Insert_Row()

In addition, I would like to explain the information that as the admin who deploys Office 365 ProPlus to the users in your organization, he might want to have more control over how often your users get these new features. For the Office 2016 version of Office 365 ProPlus, Microsoft provides you three options, called update channels, to control how often Office 365 ProPlus is updated with new features. Current Channel provide users with the newest features of Office as soon as they're available. Deferred Channel provide users with new features of Office only a few times a year.
 
If you would like to get the latest update, you can keep your Office 2016 in Current Channel. You can find more details about update channels in this article: Overview of update channels for Office 365 ProPlus

Deleting registry key is one choice, there are a couple of others you may want to try first. As Yves pointed out, the application window may not be minimized, but somehow have been relocated off screen.

When the task bar indicates that Word is the "active" application, hit <ALT Spacebar> <M> and the one of the arrow keys once. Then use the mouse and drag it around in large moves until you see the application window. Click again to drop it in place.

The other way is to change your screen resolution. The specific details of how you do this may vary, (I have Vista)

  • right click on desktop,
  • select Personalize option,
  • select Display Settings option (or something with similar name),
  • change the screen Resolution from current to lowest possible setting (ie 640x480),
  • click on Apply button, everything will look huge
  • a dialog will display asking if you want to keep this setting, click on NO (you don't need to make the change permanent to get the effect you need)
  • everything will return to normal, except that now all of your windows will be stacked in the upper left corner of the window in small sized application windows
  • just drag the other windows out of the way until you find Word.

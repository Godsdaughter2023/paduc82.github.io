bob jones aka: cybertaz say:

There is no keyboard shortcut for that, but there really is no need for one... It's handled as a built-in AutoText Entry. Just begin typing the date & press return when the AutoComplete Tip Box appears. It typically takes no more than 4 characters to enter the current date. BTW: The keystroke you're using is assigned to insertion of a DATE Field, not a literal date.

=SUMIF($A$2:$A$100,"<0",$A$2:$A$100)

Enter below in cell B2 - sum of positives:

=SUMIF($A$2:$A$100,">0",$A$2:$A$100)

Hi Johan,

You can change the default search to Current folder through the Outlook Options. Please follow the steps below:

  1. Open Outlook 2016.
  2. Select File.
  3. Choose Options > Search.
  4. Under Results > Include results only from, select Current folder.
  5. Click OK to finish.
  6. Restart Outlook 2016.

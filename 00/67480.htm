...Long story short tho, assuming you have tried to sync the connection properly by pressing both the controller and console sync buttons for 5 seconds (which it sounds like you have) then it is likely to be EITHER a problem with the console or the controller. It is very unlikely to be both.

There are a number of things you can do to work out which it is:

  1.  if the controller is wireless then get hold of a cable for it (the ones that come with 'plug and play' kits). Hopefully a friend will have one you can borrow.   3. borrow a wireless controller from a friend. See if it will sync with your console (using the normal sync process).
  4. ....Then see if your controller will sync with his console. 

If your controller will sync with his console, then your controller is fine, but your console may have a wireless problem.

If his controller will sync with your machine, the problem is likely to be with your controller.

If your controller wont sync with his console AND his controller wont sync with your console then there is likely to be a problem with BOTH your controller and your console.

petrib say: Enter the number in your Contacts using any name, or perhaps just a single chracter like 'z'.

Then in Messaging when you are in the To: -field, do a long press of the '#' key to switch the text entry mode from numeric (123) to text (abc).

Now you can just enter the 'z' character and hit the green/send key.

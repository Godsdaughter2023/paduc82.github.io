when you get a email how do you put it into a file that you have made

how do you put emails into folders that you have made in windows 7. files and folders i have made folders and now i want to put files into the folders
cmdrkeene say:
Just drag-and-drop.  Note that emails should usually remain inside the email program.  Emails are stored as records in a database, and the email program is most efficient and capable of reading that database.  If you pull the email out of the program, the email becomes orphaned and can be corrupted.  It also cannot be searched or sorted for finding later.

You can create folders inside the email program, usually by right-clicking the Inbox and choosing "New Folder".

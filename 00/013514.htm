The easiest away to get two windows side by side is:

  1. Open the two windows you want side by side.
  2. Drag one of these to one side by its titlebar using a mouse, touchpad or finger. Once it reaches the snap zone it will show a ghost window of where it will fit. (aim for the middle of that side or it will snap to a quarter screen on each corner instead of a half screen on each side)

Back on the desktop Taskbar, at the right end of the language area you'll find the Tool Menu. On the top, in Windows 8 Pro, you'll see the IME Pad. Select that, and the floating IME Pad will appear for your use in handwriting, stroke, radical, or symbols input.

 

Hope this helps

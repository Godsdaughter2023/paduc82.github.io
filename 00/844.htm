Hey there, Chad here from the OneNote team.  Thanks for the debugging info!  That helped a lot.

Here's what's going on.  At least one of your notebooks has a lot of sections.  A LOT of sections (like, thousands).  This is stretching the limits of the system in that we can process the request, but it takes a little while.  In the web clipper, right now we set a timeout of 30 seconds and if the request hasn't come back by then, then it fails.  In your case, the query to get your list of notebooks and sections is taking about a minute and a half, so by the time the request comes back the client is long gone.

It's actually quite interesting and we're talking right now about a couple of different ways we can optimize for this situation.  There are some things that we might be able to do in the short term to get you unblocked, but the longer-term solution might take a little work.  I'll update this thread as we are able to at least get you unblocked.  Of course, the other alternative is to reduce the number of sections in your notebook.  Doing this will help the query speed up and allow the request to come back in a reasonable time.

Thanks again for reaching out to us,

-Chad

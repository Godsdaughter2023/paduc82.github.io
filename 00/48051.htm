Ran into the same problem. Solved the problem by:

1. Go into Device Manager

2. Expand Universal Serial Bus Controllers

3. Right click on both Intel(R) 5 Series USB devices and uninstall them. As a result, all other USB devices are gone under the list.

Beware you would loss the use of your USB keyboard and mouse for the next step, restarting. Hopefully you have a keyboard or mouse with PS/2 connection.

4. Restart computer if still has use of keyboard or mouse, or use the reset button on computer front panel.

After restarting, windows 10 reinstalled the drivers for the 2 Intel(R) and all USB ports are working again.

TRY AT YOUR OWN RISK. GOOD LUCK.

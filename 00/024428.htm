ryan2529 say:

Thanks for the advise, but I figured it out. Apparently the Snap to Shapes was on, but not an option in the Grid and Guides window like it was in PowerPoint 2007. It was the mention of QAT that lead me to do some research on what it was. After some research, it led me to do the following in order to add the Snap to Shape option to my Ribbon;

- Right Click the Ribbon --> Customize the Ribbon

- Click the "Choose Commands From" drop down and select "Commands Not in the Ribbon.

- Add a new group to whichever tab you want the Snap to Shape option under the "Customize the Ribbon" section (I added it to my "View" tab since that's where the other snap options are under.

- Scroll down to "Snap to Shape" command, select it, and add it to the group just added.

- Click "OK" and go to the tab where you've added the command.

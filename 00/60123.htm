The recovery partition will be intact after upgrade to Win 10.

0 people found this helpful

internet expert say:

If your console is set to "Instant-On", you're likely hearing your fan and/or your hard drive still spinning. This is how the console keeps itself ready whenever you decide to get back into the game -- it's normal, and nothing to be concerned about. The system continues to run while in this state, and it stays active in case it needs to update something too.


If you want to disable this, just set your console to "Energy-Saving Mode" instead of "Instant-On". That setting should completely power off the console when you decide to turn it off. Please note that your console will need to a minute or two to boot up every time you power it on, and you'll have to sit through any needed updates.

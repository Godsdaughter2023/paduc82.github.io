If AutoRecover feature is ‘ON’ on your mac, then you may find the document. AutoRecovery files are temporary and only exist if it thinks that Word shut down unexpectedly. a. user say:

Hello pineapple penny,

 

Welcome to Microsoft Community. We are happy to help you with your concern.

 

We understand that you keep getting spam/junk emails and we're sorry to hear that. You said that there's no way for you to unsubscribe from it. You also mentioned that you cannot block the sender because the sender keeps changing the email address.


In order to prevent receiving spam/junk emails we advise you to set your Junk mail filter to selecting Exclusive option. This feature will let you receive emails from your contact list and safe senders list.

 

To set up a junk mail filter to Exclusive, please follow these steps:

 

1. Sign in to your Microsoft account.
2. Click the gear icon located on the upper right panel of your mailbox, just beside your username.
3. Select More mail settings.
4. Under Preventing junk email, click on Filters and reporting option.
5. Under Choose a junk email filter, select Exclusive.
6. Click the Save button.

 

Also, to report junk email sent to or coming from Outlook mail accounts please refer to the article below.

 

Report abuse, phishing, or spam in Outlook.com

 

If you have additional questions don't hesitate to ask. We love to hear from you again.

Let me give you some more insight.  

The Reputation system is a complex beast that factors many different things. Examples : It's not just about simply leaving a rating. If you ban another player or vice versa from your server on Battlefield 4 for Team Killing to get a vehicle, or many other  types of unsporting behavior and so on it will effect their reputation.  But if you were banned for no good reason, it does NOT effect your reputation. It's not just about collecting data, but how the system uses and weighs the data collected. If a dozen people suddenly report a single user, the system looks at a variety of factors before lowering ones reputation.

Example: Did each of those players actually play in an online game with the person they reported? If not, all of their complaints will not equal that of a single person who spent 15 minutes playing Forza online with the reported user.

The system also looks at the reputation of both the person reporting and the alleged offender, if a user sends a barrage of reports against a single user will not continue to hurt your reputation.  Each and every Xbox One game with an online service can include the ability to recognize negative in game behavior and react to them accordingly. Even if no one reports a user for team killing in Call of Duty: Ghosts or Battlefield 4 or say driving the wrong way in Forza, the games will know that you have not been playing the game the right way, and you can get lowered because of it.

It is simple, play the game clean and fair the way it was meant to be played and all will be good.  This reputation system is not being exploited or abused.  Seeing as each and every one of these reports are reviewed.  KCCO, and your reputation will raise.

You can set OneDrive to no longer launch when you log in to Windows in its settings.

To temporarily disable WIndows Updates, do the following:

1. Manually check for, download and install any updates for your system.

2. Use Cortana/Search, or "Winkey+R" to open "services.msc".

3. In the list of services, select "Windows Update" and go to Properties.

Change Startup type to "Disabled", click "Stop" and confirm with "OK".

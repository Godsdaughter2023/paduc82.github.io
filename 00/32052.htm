For some reason when you check the "keep my device from sleeping to say hey cortana" option, in the power & sleep settings, the sleep option is set for "sleeping when pc is plugged in" to "Never", so this option prevents the  "keep my device from sleeping to say hey cortana" option from being unchecked.

abi99 (2) say:

Situation A

Nokia » Menu » Settings » Connectivity » Internet telephone » Accounts » (do not Select but just highlight you account ») Options » Details » 3G internet call support:On

If you use a Nokia Series 40 without Wi-Fi and this is Off, you get the error message ‘Mobile data not allowed’ and ‘Connection failed: Mobile data not available’. Consequently, the Nokia 207, Nokia 208, and Nokia 515 are affected because they support VoIP over 3G only.

Solution A

You have to edit the XML file and add 

<parm name="ALLOWVOIPOVERWCDMA" value="1" />

under the application w9013.

Situation B

Nokia » Menu » Settings » Phone » Network mode » Dual mode

Solution B

Your phone needs reception of a 3G network (HSPA, HSDPA or UMTS). Therefore, please, enable dual mode and move to a 3G reception.

Situation C

If the above two situations do not apply in your case, please, reply here and we have a look at additional causes. Please, tell us Nokia » Menu » Settings » Configuration » Preferred access point » Options » Details » Mobile data access point.

  1. Sign in to your Microsoft account via a computer browser.
  2. Choose Settings  next to your name in the upper-right corner of the page, then click Options.
  3. In the left navigation bar, under General, chooseMobile Devices.
  4. Select your phone in the list, and then select Remove to delete the device partnership.
  5. Select Yes in the confirmation box.
  6. Soft reset your phone by pressing and holding the Volume down + Power keys until it vibrates.
  7. After your phone restarts, sync your email again.

Let us know what happens after doing the steps above.

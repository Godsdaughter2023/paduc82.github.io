Outlook.com is new and changes to it are being made.
Since it a new and a different system the fastest way to learn where things are is to look around a bit.
Check all the menu pull downs, see what is where.
Most of the Hotmail functionality is still there just in different places.
The menus in Outlook are context driven.
That simply means they change to give you the options you need at that moment.
When you first open Outlook.com there are only 2 things you can do.
You can click "new" and the menu will change, or you can click on an email, and again the menu will change to help.
Some things are not very obvious like clicking the "to" button once gives favorites, twice gives all contacts.  That also applies to CC & BCC
Also if you click the "#" in the list it displays the letters of the alphabet to choose from.
 Some things are more obvious like forwarding is under reply.
You can also right click the message then click forward

If you have specific questions we are happy to help but you should probably look around a bit as you will probably find where everything is

babdi say: Hantms
Chargers are designed specifically to suit certain type phones keeping in view of charging rate. The voltage also plays an important part ie.,9V or 3V output. Using a charger of 9V into a phone which is designed for 3V will burn the circuits. Again the charging rate, mAH of the battery also matters. If a battery is designed for 950 mAH and if used with a charger meant for a 1500 mAH ( with correct voltages )the battery will charge quicker but will also reduce the battery life. Battery can also get very hot which is undesirable.

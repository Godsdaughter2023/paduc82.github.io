robert aldwinckle on forums say:
mouse wont click and drag letters

That could be a symptom of a problem with keyboard modifiers.   E.g. sometimes Shift- and Ctrl- (especially) get "stuck" and the only way that you become aware of this is seeing strange symptoms such as you are noticing.   In that case, it helps to have a keyboard diagnostic tool such as mskey.exe (e.g. if you have a Microsoft keyboard with IntelliType or Microsoft Mouse and Keyboard Center installed).   In any case, pressing those keys tends to release them and fix the problem symptom being caused by them being "stuck".

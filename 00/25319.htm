Make sure that "Straight quotes with smart quotes" is enabled on the AutoFormat tab of AutoCorrect Options (not just AutoFormat As You Type), and then run AutoFormat on your document to correct them.

Alternatively, with this option selected on the AutoFormat As You Type tab, use Replace (Ctrl+H) to replace " with ". Word will automatically perform the correction.

Access both tabs with Office Button | Word Options | Proofing | AutoCorrection Options.

Running AutoFormat in Word 2007 and 2010 is not as simple as it was in previous versions, but you can add the AutoFormat command to your Quick Access Toolbar or use the keyboard shortcut Alt+Ctrl+K.

In most computers, when the computer is powered up, the fan runs continuously.  Most also have multi-speed fans that turn faster when the temperatures in key places gets past a programmed point.  So what you are hearing is likely a higher speed fan than usual.

If you are hearing the high speed fan running a lot that means you are generating too much heat and that can be fatal to electronics.  You do need to address this problem rather urgently.

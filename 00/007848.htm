suzanne s. barnhill say:
  1. Press WindowsKey+R to open the Run dialog.
  2. In that dialog, paste the complete file path for your Word 2007 executable, enclosed in quotation marks; the default location is "C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Office\Office12\WINWORD.EXE."
  3. Add \r to the path: "C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Office\Office12\WINWORD.EXE" \r (note the space before the switch).
  4. Press Enter.

This will "reregister" Word 2007, that is, reestablish its association with Word files in the Windows Registry. For Excel and other Office apps, you'll have to do something similar. The switch for Excel is \regserver. I don't know what it is for other Office applications.

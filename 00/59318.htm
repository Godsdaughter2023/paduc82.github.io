It all depends, on what error code has come up, when the RROD occurred & what lights on the ring, light up... It could be easily fixable..

1. Bottom Right Light [4th Player Light Red] = Hardware Failure.. (E74 etc)

2. Top & Bottom Left Lights [1st & 3rd Lights Red] = Overheating Issue..

(Should stop flashing, after it cools down.. Turn off immediately)

3. Top Left & Bottom Left & Right Lights [1st, 3rd & 4th Lights Red] = General Hardware Failure..

(no Error code, will be displayed & it should work after restart)

4. All Four Lights Red = AV Cable Error.. (Change AV Cable)

..

About the HDD... You can't use the old Pro/Elite HDD in the new 360 Slims etc

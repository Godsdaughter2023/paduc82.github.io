toddallcock say:

Sir I had downloaded video from youtube via uc browser. I can play downloaded video only in uc browser but video is not visible in video+music. What will I do.


Third-party apps in Windows Phone are "sandboxed" meaning data they control is theirs exclusively, and not viewable by other apps. (There are limited exceptions, like pictures, Office Docs, and in some cases, music.)

 

Any video you download in a third-party app belongs to that app and can only be viewed inside that app.

 

As a workaround, some apps allow you to reupload your downloads to your PC or to a cloud service like Skydrive or Dropbox, so they can then be accessed by other apps, but that function has to be included by the app developer.

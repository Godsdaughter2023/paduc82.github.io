keesnederlof say:

I had the same trouble with an old version (6.0) of Photoshop Elements and my new 4K monitor. Indeed, the program looked as made for ants!

Don't bother with settings, it won't work.

This worked, at least for me in Windows 10 Home, build 1803:

1. Open Explorer and search for the application (exe) file of the program that causes problems, e.g. in my case: Photoshop Elements 6.0.exe

2. Right click and choose Properties

3. Select the tab "Properties" and click on "Compatibility" button

4. Click on button "Change high DPI settings"

5. Tick the two boxes and choose "[when] I open this program" and "System (Enhanced)".

6. Click OK

Now my program showed normal (readable) fonts and icons! Hope that will work for you too.

lordalukard say:
Dear New,
That is related to that particular printer.
1. Test the Printer
Make sure your printer is working properly. Access the printer's settings by clicking Start and choosing "Devices and Printers." Right-click the printer you are using and choose "Printer Properties." Click the "Print Test Page" button on the General tab. If a test page prints, the device is working properly. If not, right-click the printer again and choose "Troubleshoot." Window will scan for any problems and offer solutions for issues it finds.
2. Printer Settings
Printer settings may also cause problems, particularly with sheet sizes, color printing and duplex printing. Some printers have the option to print a publication correctly, but the settings need to be enabled in Publisher first. Select the "File" tab and click "Print" or press "Ctrl-P." Click "Printer Properties" and select the "Effects" tab, if available. Choose "Print Document On" in the Resize Options section and select the "Scale to Fit" check box. Click "OK" to apply the changes.
3. Incorrect Size
As Publisher offers the ability to print a wide variety of document sizes, publications can sometimes print too small or too large. To resolve this problem, you need to modify the paper size in Publisher, which is different from changing the page size when you create the document. Select the "File" tab and click "Print" or press "Ctrl-P." Click the "Usable Paper Sizes" drop-down in the Settings section, which displays the current paper size, and choose the correct size.
4. Printing Multiples
Some Publisher documents print multiple publications on a single page, such as business cards or labels, but when you make the changes, your printer dispenses several pages with one publication per page. Other times, you may wish to print multiple copies of a publication. To change the number of items per sheet, select the "File" tab and click "Print" or press "Ctrl-P." Click the "Pages per Sheet" drop-down and select the number of publications you want per page. To print multiple copies of a single publication, change the number in the "Copies per Print Job" box.

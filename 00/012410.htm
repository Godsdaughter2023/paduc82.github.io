Delete your existing My Phone phone connection on the web and delete all the data that is currently on My Phone.
Uninstall My Phone from your windows mobile device
Delete or rename the PIM.vol file on the windows mobile device - you'll find it in the root directory, but if you are using file explorer you won't see the extension.
Delete your existing ActiveSync partnership between your windows mobile device and your computer
Attach your device to your computer and create a new ActiveSync partnership, sync the contact, appointment and tasks data to your device.
Reinstall My Phone to your mobile device and go to My Phone on the web, tap through the initial start up prompts and sync the data.

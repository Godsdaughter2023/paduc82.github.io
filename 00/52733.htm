If the account is not a child account, you would turn it into an online account following the instructions here However, it is not possible to turn an offline account into an online child account. Since you've already created an online account that is used on Windows it would already have an Xbox account attached, you would not be able to use that to connect to an offline account either since it's already an account (hence the information about transferring and merging accounts). You can download her online account to the console so she can use it there as well, but her progress cannot be moved over from her offline account.

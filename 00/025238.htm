How can I access shared notebooks on OneNote for iPhone and iPad? - Microsoft Community

tony mac. say:

How can I access shared notebooks on OneNote for iPhone and iPad?

I created a OneNote notebook on SkyDrive and I want to be able to share it with a friend. My friend can access the shared notebook on SkyDrive, but it doesn’t appear on his iPhone or iPad. 

What can I do to fix this?

cody c. say:
There are a few key requirements before OneNote for iOS will recognize shared notebooks:
  • The SkyDrive folder that the notebook is located in must be shared. This is different from selecting the notebook individually and only sharing that individual file.
  • The recipient must have edit privileges for the entire folder. Read only privileges will prevent the notebook from syncing. 
  • Sharing requests must be sent via email. Only providing a hyperlink to a shared folder will result in the notebook never appearing on the app.

The steps below explain how to properly create and share a OneNote notebook for access on an iPad or iPhone:
  1. Visit www.skydrive.com and log in with your Microsoft account.
  2. In the top-left corner, click Create > Folder.



  3. Name your new folder and press Enter.
  4. Ensure the checkbox next to your new folder is checked and click the Share button at the top of the page.



  5. Choose the Send email tab on the left and enter the email address for the recipient. When you’re finished composing your message, ensure the Recipients can edit checkbox is checked. Checking the second box for Require everyone who accesses this to sign in is optional.



  6. Click Share to send your invite.
  7. Open your new folder by clicking on its name in the list.
  8. To upload an existing OneNote notebook, choose the  button and select your OneNote 2010 notebook. 


    To create a new notebook, click the Create button and then choose OneNote notebook.


Once you have properly shared a folder and added a OneNote notebook to the folder, the recipent will need to choose to sync that notebook to their iOS device. Follow the steps below to add a shared notebook to OneNote for iOS:
  1. Open OneNote on your iPad or iPhone.
  2. On the Home screen of the app, press the Sync Settings button.



  3. Choose Notebook Settings.



  4. Choose On next to the notebooks you’d like to sync. Shared notebooks will be labeled “Shared by…” under the name of the notebook. 



    Note: It can take up to 30 minutes for a shared notebook to appear in the OneNote for iOS app after it has been shared. 

  5. Return to the Home screen of the app to see your synced notebooks.

bob jones aka: cybertaz say:

The ability to assign keyboard shortcuts in Excel has been eliminated as of Office 2016. Likewise, the built-in keyboard shortcuts provided in the program can neither be modified, reassigned nor removed.

The recommended alternative of using the macOS method is usable only for explicit menu commands. Unfortunately, Merge Cells is not explicitly listed.

The only means of using a keyboard shortcut would involve creating a macro with a keyboard shortcut assigned to it.

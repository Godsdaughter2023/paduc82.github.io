The command Set-SPOTenantSyncClientRestriction is used to control tenant-wide options. If we add the domain GUIDs to the safe recipient list and enable this command, users in the home(not joined in the domain ) will be unable to sync files with OneDrive for Business sync client.

However, after enabling the command, it’s not feasible to achieve your second requirement to allow some VIP users to sync the documents with OneDrive for Business sync client at home (not joined in the domain), only users in the safe domain list will be allowed to use the OneDrive for Business sync client. Set-SPOTenantSyncClientRestriction

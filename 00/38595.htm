mimori 775 say:

You can try a few things: 

  • Check Xbox Live Status
    Always a good first step is to make sure the signal disruption isn't just you. You can check the xbox live status: here. 
  • Test other Xbox Live Games
    Is it only one game that's causing the connection issues? You may need to check the servers for that particular game if so. 
  • Disconnect any third-party headsets / wireless keyboards
    Third-party headsets / keyboards can sometimes run on the same frequency as the wireless router which causes the disruption in signal...I would also recommend turning off other blue tooth enabled devices one at a time to find out which one might be causing the interference. 
  • Wireless signal degrades over distance 
    How close are you to the router? Are there any objects/walls/piping/etc between the location of your xbox and the router? Sometimes these objects can cause the signal to be weakened. 

    A helpful tip is to limit the about of objects between the router and your console. 
  • Power cycle your console and router 
    As mentioned in other post, power cycle your console, but I would also unplug/reset your router.
  • Try a wired connection 
    If your still having issues with your connection, purchase an Ethernet cable and try a wired connection. If you still end up with signal disruption the cause may be your internet service provider not the xbox.

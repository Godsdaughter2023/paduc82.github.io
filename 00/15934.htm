Outlook + Paragraph symbol + New messages - Microsoft Community

lisalane say:

Outlook + Paragraph symbol + New messages

How do I stop Outlook from adding the Paragraph symbol to all new messages?
jeeped say:
How do I stop Outlook from adding the Paragraph symbol to all new messages?

The paragraph or pilcrow (e.g. ¶) symbol is a formatting mark and it is displayed only if an individual computer has that option turned on, e.g. it may be displayed on yours when you create the email but not on the recipient's computer when they read it. Likewise, it does not print irrespective of its display status.
Due to Outlook's close relationship with Word, the toggle for turning the display of formatting marks is pretty much the same. You can use the keystroke combination Ctrl+Shift+8 or click the ¶ button in the Format Text tab's Paragraph group.

shyamal debnath msft support say:

Hi David,

For this scenario currently there is no direct way to migrate mailboxes from one Office 365 tenant to another one smoothly. You can consider, PST migration (exporting and importing .PST files).

For your reference: Use Office 365 Import Service to migrate PST-files

However, the built-in migration tools in Exchange Admin Center, only IMAP migration is supported to migrate mailboxes between two Office 365 tenants. When you migrate the user's email by using IMAP migration, only the items in the users' inbox or other mail folders are migrated. Contacts, calendar items, and tasks can't be migrated with IMAP, but they can be by a user.

If types of migrations described above you don’t want, you may also consider a third-party email migration tool or working with a partner to migrate email to Office 365. For more detailed information, please refer to the article: Work with a partner to migrate email

Your understanding will be highly appreciated.

Shyamal

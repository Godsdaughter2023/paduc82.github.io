I'm not sure the references in the Vlookup are correct. You may want to remove the IfError to see what error reason gets returned. 

VLOOKUP([Site ID],‘P:\Dropbox\Data Control Systems\[PROPERTY DETAIL SHEET MASTER.xlsm]Main_Frame_Table,COLUMN(Main_Frame_Table[CLIENT STATUS ACTIVE INACTIVE]),FALSE)

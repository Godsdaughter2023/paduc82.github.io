Hi Derek,

 

Unfortunately, you cannot transfer or merge Gold and Silver Xbox LIVE accounts.

However, you may be able to take the primary email address associated with your Xbox LIVE Silver account and attach it to your Xbox LIVE Gold account.

This would mean that you would have to either switch the email addresses associated with both the Silver and Gold accounts, or you may attach a new email address to the Silver account.

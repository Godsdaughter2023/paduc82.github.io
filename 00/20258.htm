  • Comment Replies / Mark Comments “Done” – Comments are a powerful tool when working on a document with others, and we’ve added functionality to address the two common requests: you can now reply to a comment, to have a discussion about a specific item, and you can mark a comment “done”, so you don’t have to delete it to communicate that you’ve taken action on its contents.

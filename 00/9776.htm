Yes, that may help. The interesting part is "Waiting on Application Name: spoolsv.exe:spoolss". john korchok say:

You can do this in Paint:

  1. Open the image in Paint.
  2. Choose Image>Resize/Skew.
  3. In both the Resize Horizontal and Resize Vertical fields, type in 75 (the ratio of 72:96). Click on OK.
  4. Save the image.
  5. In Word, right-click on the image and choose Change Picture. Select your resized version. Word replaces the image but maintains the same physical size. Your image is now 72 d.p.i. instead of 96.

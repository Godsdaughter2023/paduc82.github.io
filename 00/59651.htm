bella2010 say:

Windows Explorer not maximising from taskbar when clicked

When I position the mouse over the Windows Explorer program icon on the taskbar the small preview window/s will appear but will not will not maximise into a large window.  When I click on it or right click 'maximise' on the preview nothing happens. 

a. user say:

In fact, it likely is maximizing or restoring, but off-screen where you cannot see it.  This has happened to me occasionally as well.

Try this:

1.  Hold the shift key down and right-click the icon on the Taskbar (or right-click on the preview as you have been doing).
2.  Choose the Restore entry if it is not grayed out.
3.  Hold the shift key down and right-click on the icon on the Taskbar again.
4.  Now choose the Move entry.
5.  Without doing anything else, press one of the arrow keys (e.g., the left arrow) on the keyboard.
6.  Without clicking anything, move the mouse.  Your window should appear on-screen.
7.  Click the mouse to drop the window, then you can resize it or whatever to be where you want it.

I suspect this is a glitch in the multi-monitor support of Windows 7, and that the window actually believes it's off on another monitor even if you have no other monitor active.

-Noel

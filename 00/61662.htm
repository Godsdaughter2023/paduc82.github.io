Hi, Jon.
Not so much as a "workaround", as different hardware.
What you read is, generally, correct:  with a 'normal' tuner card, any HD content that the STB tunes will be downgraded to SD-quality.  It's the limitation of S-video + analog audio that kills you there.  All you'll get is 480i + stereo audio.

But!  There is an alternative.  Rather than a 'normal' tuner card, you can get a Hauppauge HD-PVR.  This will get you a component video + digital audio input, that MC can use as a "tuner."  Not the cheapest thing in the world, true; but, it does get you a native HD-input "tuner" in Media Center that you can connect your HD sat box to.

Chris

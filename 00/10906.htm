2.1) Open File Explorer, browse to C:\Windows\SoftwareDistribution\Download folder. It contains several subfolders named with a long hexadecimal string.

Footnote Text is the style that Word applies to text in footnotes. If someone has applied different styles, you can do the following: On the View tab, click Draft. On the References tab, click Show Notes. Word displays the footnotes pane at the bottom of the page. Click inside the pane and press Ctrl+A to select all notes. Then apply Footnote Text via the Apply Styles pane (Ctrl+Shift+S).

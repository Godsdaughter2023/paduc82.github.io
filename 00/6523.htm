john mcghie say:
I am guessing here, but it is likely that the section break is within (or immediately adjacent to...) the Table of Contents.

Anything in that position will be replaced whenever the TOC updates. If you add a paragraph mark between the TOC and the Section Break, that may resolve the issue.

You are on the right track: you do need to control the "side" of the section break.

You do need to be aware that the section break that controls the text is the break that FOLLOWS the text, not the one that begins it :-)  This is typical of Word: the formatting container that marks the end of the text contains the formatting for it.  This applies to section breaks and paragraphs.

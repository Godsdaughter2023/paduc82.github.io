Hi VuLe,

 

It seems that you configured the Mail Exchange (MX) record on the Domain Name Service (DNS) incorrectly.

 

To confirm that you own the domain, follow the steps below, depending on how you configured your domain:

 

1. Sign in to http://domains.live.com by using your domain administrator if you have not already done this. Then, select your domain in the list of domains that you control.
2. On the Domain settings tab, locate the MC server , and then copy the entry that appears there in bold letters. The entry has the format of xxxxxxx.pamx1.hotmail.com . The x’s represent numbers.
3.

Visit your domain registrar, and then create a new MX record. To do this, use the information that is provided in the MX record section and the MX server information that you copied.

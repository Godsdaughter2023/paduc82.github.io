A DVD may not play properly in some DVD players or DVD-ROM drives for any of the following reasons:

·         The disc format may not be compatible. If you burn a DVD using the PAL (Phase Alternating Line) format, you will not be able to play that DVD using a player that only supports the NTSC (National Television System Committee ) format, and vice versa.

1. Open Computer by clicking the Start button, and then clicking Computer.

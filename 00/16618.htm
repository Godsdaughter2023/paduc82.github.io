=DATEDIF(A2,TODAY(),"m")>=6

2. Disable add-ins.

b. Select COM Add-ins after Manage section and then click Go.

c. Click to clear all the check boxes in the list and then click OK.

d. Restart Outlook, but not in safe mode.

e. Check add-ins one by one to see if any one caused the high CPU usage.

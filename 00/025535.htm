Hi Anna,

 

I’m sorry for the delayed response. As I understand, you were unable to change you primary email address to the old one and incoming emails to your primary are going to a sub-folder instead of inbox. Don’t worry. We’re here to help you on this.

 

When you try to change your primary alias, do you receive any error messages? If yes, please provide this to us.

 

As for your mailbox, incoming messages are going to the sub-folder because of the email rule that was created by the time you change your primary alias. To stop this, you need to delete the said rule. Here are the steps:

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. In the upper-right corner of the page, click wheel/cog icon, and then click More mail settings.
  3. Under Customizing Outlook, click Rules for sorting new messages.

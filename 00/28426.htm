a. user say:

VinnySimeone2015 -

Thank you for posting.

You are correct that resetting the phone is the only way to change the Microsoft Account on it.

Some stuff can be transferred from one account to another.  Media that's been stored on OneDrive you can just download onto your PC and then upload it to the correct account.

Contacts you can export and then import to get them moved.  To do that, first you'd need to sign in with the account that's on the phone right now here:
http://people.live.com

If you click on Manage, you'll see an option to Export.  That will export the contacts as a spreadsheet file that you can save on your PC.  Then log out, and back in again with the correct account.  If you click the option to "Start import" you'll then see "outlook.com" as an option.  The next step will let you choose that file you just saved to upload.

Calendars can't be exported, but you could share important calendar events with the other account as one way to get them onto the right one.

App installation / purchase history can't be transferred from one account to another, neither can backups or text messages.

Once you've gotten everything switched around, you would go to Settings -> About -> Reset My Phone to start the reset process, and set the phone up with the correct account.

Regards,
Kevin J S

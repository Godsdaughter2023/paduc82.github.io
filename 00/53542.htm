a. user say:

One reason why you can’t access the shared folders in your network is that the network administrator might did some changes with the network settings in the office. You may try updating the group policy on your office computer.

Here are the steps on how to update the group policy:

  1. Click Start then choose Run.
  2. On the search field, type “cmd”.
  3. On the command prompt, type gpupdate, then press Enter.

If the issue still persists, we suggest contacting your network administrator for assistance.

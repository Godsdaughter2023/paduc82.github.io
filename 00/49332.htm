To get the store back you can:

1. Digit "Powershell" in Windows search bar
2. Right click on "Powershell"
3. Click on "Run as Administrator"
4. Digit "Get-AppxPackage -name *store*" without quote and press ENTER
5. Select the content from the InstallationLocation field and right-click on it to copy the content to the clipboard.
6. Open Notepad, paste the output, and save the file, as you will need this information later on.
7. Type the following command on PowerShell and press Enter:
Get-AppxPackage Microsoft.WindowsStore | Remove-AppxPackage
8. Type the following command, replace YourStorePacakgeName with the package name you copied to Notepad, and press Enter:
Add-AppxPackage -register "C:\Program Files\WindowsApps\YourStorePakageName\AppxManifest.xml" -DisableDevelopmentMode
9. Reboot your PC.

The most common causes are:

  1. Using incompatible file types. Look around the 10% mark in your project. Look for some clip in a different file type. Try temporarily removing that clip and then see if you can save the movie. If so, the clip is the problem. Convert that clip to .wmv or another common type and try again.

Hi CW.CW.

Greetings from Skype!

Just a heads up, once you open your mobile data and Skype is running at the background of your Android app, Skype will automatically use your data plan and may use  more than 2 times the data for background activities as it does in foreground. Hence, it makes perfect sense to Restrict background data usage for Skype app, in order to prevent it from using expensive cellular data.

You may follow the steps below on how to restrict background data usage:

1. Tap on Phone settings (Gear icon).

2. Tap on Data Usage.

3. On the Data Usage Screen, make sure you are on the Mobile tab and scroll down to take a look at Apps listed under section App Usage.

4. Now, tap on the App (Skype) that is using more mobile data to find out more details about the Cellular data used by this App.

5. On the App Data Usage Screen details view, you will see that the Cellular data used by the Skype app is split into two sections – Foreground and Background data usage.

6. Then, enable the option "Restrict background data".

Note: Apps that are restricted from using Cellular Data for background activities will not be refreshing or updating in the background while you are connected to a cellular network. However, once you move to an area covered by WiFi, these apps will start their compulsive background activities and update using the less expensive (or Free) WiFi data.

Hope that this information helps.

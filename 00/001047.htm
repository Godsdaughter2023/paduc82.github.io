format tab

shane devenshire say:

There is no Format tab per se.  You have formatting options on the Home tab.  And you have a Font dialog box by clicking the quick launch button in the bottom right corner of the Font group on the Home tab. 

The Paragraph dialog box can be opened by clicking the quick launch button in the bottom right corner of the Paragraph group on the Home tab.

There are various other formatting locations depending on what you want to format, for example if you are working with a table you have the formatting options on the Home tab and those found under Table Tools, Design and Table Tools, Layout (these are only visible if you are in the table).  There are quick launch buttons on some of these tabs.

You can also customize the QAT to allow quick access to frequently used formatting commands.

 

If this answer solves your problem, please check Mark as Answered. If this answer helps, please click the Vote as Helpful button. Cheers, Shane Devenshire

stefan blom say:

In the AutoCorrect dialog box, make sure that "Internet and network paths with hyperlinks" is selected under "Replace as you type" (you'll find the option on the AutoFormat As You Type tab).

To display the dialog box: Click the Office button, and then click Word Options. In the Proofing category, click the AutoCorrect Options button.

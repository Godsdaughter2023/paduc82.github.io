When you attempt to utilize WiFi, your Server must be within the same domain network that your Exchange Server is in.  If you are at a coffee-shop hotspot, you'll need to establish a WiFi connection, then after you are connected to the internet, you will also need to start a VPN connection to your Server's Domain network.

 

3.) When you attempt to bridge a connection via BT to your phone, you have to clear two hurdles: Primarily, the relationship between your phone and PDA must be pre-setup by way of pairing.  Both have to readily recognize each other.  Secondarily, your service provider has to allow you to bridge your BT connection over to their data service. This is typically referred to as tethering.  You need to ensure that your provider allows this, or else this particular equation will be dead in the water.   Once you have a clear connection from the PDA to the internet via the phone (and the BT pairing), you will once again need to setup a VPN connection to your Exchange Server's domain.

Part of the information is derived from the user information data. The easiest thing to do is this:

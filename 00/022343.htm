  1. Click on one of the folders that shows to be empty.

  2. Click the "VIEW" tab at the top
  3. Click "Change View", then select "Save Current View As a New View..."
  4. Name the view whatever you want, Make sure "ALL IMAP folders" is selected and click OK.
  5. Now Click "Change View" again. Select the new View you just created. And then click "Apply Current View to Other Mail Folders...".
  6. Make sure the top Level box is checked, then check the box at the bottom "Apply view to subfolders. Click OK.

This should fix the view for all folders and sub-folders that you have all at once. Hope this helps, worked for me.

The camera module in Nokia C7 is a full focus camera, using EDOF technology.  This is very different from the AF module in Nokia Nseries devices such as Nokia N78 and Nokia N79.

There are multiple ways to do it:

on Windows desktop: Right-click the message and select Remove Message.

on Skype for Windows 10: Right-click the message and select Remove.

When you delete a conversation, all the messages in the conversation are deleted, and it's removed from the sidebar and your history. The conversation will be deleted from Skype on that specific device, but will still be visible on other devices unless you delete it from there too.

Find the conversation you want to delete...

Deleting a conversation is not currently supported on Skype for Windows desktop, or Skype for Windows 10.

Deleting a conversation will remove the entire message history from your device, but it's still visible on other devices unless you delete it there too.

Hiding a conversation only removes it from your recent list but keeps the history on your device if you want to reference it later.

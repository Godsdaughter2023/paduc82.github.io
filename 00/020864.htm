"DcSettingIndex"=dword:00000000

 

Registry Key 2

[HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Power\PowerSettings\8619B916-E004-4dd8-9B66-DAE86F806698\C763EE92-71E8-4127-84EB-F6ED043A3E3D\DefaultPowerSchemeValues\381b4222-f694-41f0-9685-ff5bb260df2e]

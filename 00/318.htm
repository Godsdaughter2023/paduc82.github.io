grschinon say: The N97 is compatible with a standard, not with any particular capacity of card.

The standard that the N97 observes is microSDHC. Any microSDHC card (and, by extension, earlier microSD card) will be compatible with the phone: 4GB, 6GB, 8GB, 16GB. As of now, the largest microSDHC card available is a 16GB card. When 32GB cards become available there's no reason why they shouldn't be compatible. 32GB is, however, the largest capacity admissible by the standard. Anything greater will be part of the SDXC standard with which the N97 is not compatible.

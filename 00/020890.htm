Over all I'm VERY excited about where the Win 10 Tech is headed, although I do feel it needs some refinement...  I was running it on a lower end (now only $50 from Microsoft's store!) device - Nokia Lumia 635, but I had NO slow, laggy, choppy issues running this build on an older device.

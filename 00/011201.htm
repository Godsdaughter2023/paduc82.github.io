I have checked that your domain C is verified in Office 365 but the MX record is pointed to your own email server.

Hi Catherine,

 

I tried to double-click the emails in the navigate pane's email list, and the email pop-up in a new window. I did further research and found that this feature is related to the settings of the browser. For example, if you are using IE, you can change the settings as the following steps show to open email in separate window:

 

  • Open Internet Options either from within Internet Explorer.
  • On the General tab press the Tabs button.
  • In the “When a pop-up is encountered” section select the option:  Always open pop-ups in a new tab.

 

For different kind of browser, the steps to change the settings could be different. If you failed to change it, please contact the support of the provider of the browser for further assistance.

sockatume---01 say:

A "shortcode" is a short phone number used for forwarding text messages to/from a service, while the "premium" part refers to it being a charged-for service like ringtones, sports updates, or something similar. It sounds like you are being billed for a subscription service by SMS.

 

The 84000 range belongs to Vodafone, so something's seriously odd here. I think one code can forward to several different services depending on what you put in the subscriber message so I can't be any more specific.

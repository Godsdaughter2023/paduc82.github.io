hansv mvp say:

Here is a formula-only solution that needs an auxiliary column (which you can hide, if you like).

Enter the following formula in E1:

=IF(A1="",OFFSET(E1,-1,0),A1)

and fill down to somewhere past the range you need, say E100. This will provide a filled out list of names.

Enter the following array formula in D1 (confirm with Ctrl+Shift+Enter instead of just Enter):

=IF(A1="","",INDEX(B1:B$100,MATCH(IF(A1="","",MAX(IF($E$1:$E$100=A1,$C$1:$C$100))),C1:C$100,0)))

Fill down to D100.

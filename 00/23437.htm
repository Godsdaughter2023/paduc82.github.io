hansv mvp say:

Small rounding errors are unavoidable. Excel is only accurate up to 15 significant digits. In 2108955.41829907, the 7 is the 15th digit counting from left to right.

To avoid such problems, round the sum to the required precision. For example, if all your numbers have 2 decimal places, use a formula such as

=ROUND(SUM(M2:M1410),2)

jeffhubb_ say: The OneNote Recycle Bin is a new feature in 2010 - you can access it from the Share tab.  Deleted sections go here as individual sections, and deleted pages go into the Deleted Pages section.  You can empty it from within OneNote (Share-->Notebook Recycle Bin-->Empty Recycle Bin).  It's safe to delete this folder via Explorer, but OneNote 2010 will re-create it the next time you delete something unless you disable the feature altogether (Share-->Notebook Recycle Bin-->Disable History for This Notebook).  Because 2007 doesn't know about this new feature, it will show up as a section group (folder) in 2007 named OneNote_RecycleBin.

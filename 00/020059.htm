uniquelydistant say:

Looked deep onto the questions and found out how.  It has to do with account sync settings.  What I found additional on my own was a little silly.

So basically, have to go to settings, then account, to sync your settings.  Just disable internet explorer settings.  The silly part is the settings window has to close to register alterations.  So while the window is closed, delete the favorites you want deleted, then turn back on sync, and it's saved.  Not closing the settings window doesn't allow the account to remember what was deleted, so it will bring back the links.  Small coding issue, but pretty frustrating just the same.

Mind if I make a suggestion?  First, allow a master account for a computer that acts like the administrator.  Other devices link to the account as slave accounts.  Master account rules above the others so all alterations made by master is passed to slaves.  Any alterations made by slaves will stay with the slave computer, with an exception rule if you want it added to the master as well.  This will resolve all this headache, plus possibly others.

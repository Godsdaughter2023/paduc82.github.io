    You can change any hardware in your computer except for the motherboard and/or CPU and Windows 10 will remain activated. So a new WiFi card is no problem.

    Microsoft considers a new motherboard and/or CPU to be a new computer - in that case you would need to buy a license to remain activated.

    The beautiful thing about Windows 10 activation is that once Windows 10 is activated on a computer, unless you change the m/board or CPU, it's activated on that computer forever. That means you can reinstall or clean install Windows 10 on the same computer as many times as you like until the end of time, without ever needing to enter a license. Whomever gets your computer after you can do the same.

    Even space aliens can install Windows 10 on that computer, provided they can obtain the download on their planet.

HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Internet Explorer\TypedURLs
and 
HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Internet Explorer\TypedURLsTime

Delete everything except the (Default) value, located at the top.



To enable Internet Explorer delete browsing history, go to the following location:
HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Policies\Microsoft\Internet Explorer\Control Panel

HKEY_Local_Machine\Software\Policies\Microsoft\Internet Explorer\Control Panel
if more than one user account is affected.

HasModule is a property of the Form or Report object that
you don't get directly with AllForms.
In addition you have to open the form or report to check the property.
e.g.
 
[vbnet]
 
Dim obj As AccessObject
 
For Each obj In CurrentProject.AllForms
   DoCmd.OpenForm obj.Name, acDesign, , , , acHidden
       Debug.Print obj.Name, Forms(obj.Name).HasModule
   DoCmd.Close acForm, obj.Name
Next obj
 
[/vbnet]

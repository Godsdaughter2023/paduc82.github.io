techfreak_ say:

Call and subscription rates are based on where you are calling to, and never where you are calling from. The only time rates differ slightly based on location is when different taxes are involved (VAT, GST, etc.). Taxes are applied based on the primary residence/home of record on the account, not where you travel to.

If you need unlimited calls to the US (regardless of location), you purchase/sign-up for a US subscription. If you need unlimited calls to Italy (regardless of location), you purchase/sign-up for an Italy subscription. If you require both, you purchase/sign-up for both or find a subscripton that combines both. Currently there is no singular subscription that cover US and Italy for both landlines AND mobiles.

The 6.99 you are referring to is probably the NORTH AMERICA Mobiles and landlines Unlimited mins subscription which covers calling the following countries:

American Samoa
Canada
Guam
Mexico
Northern Mariana Islands
Puerto Rico
United States
U.S. Virgin Islands

There is one for 2.99 that is the UNITED STATES Mobiles and landlines Unlimited mins subscription which covers calling the following countries:

Guam
Puerto Rico
United States

All subscriptions attempt to auto renew 3 days befoe the subscription cycle ends, to avoid last minute billing issues. If you requires the subscription for  a singular cycle (for example a month), you would cancel the subscription ahead of these 3 days do avoid the auto billing a renewal for the next cycle. Cancelling a subscripton still allows the current cycle of coverage to complete but halts a renewal.

Office 2010 Bootleg features Described

To address one issue at a time.

 

The only Genuine and Authentic Microsoft Office Professional 2010 are made in Puerto Rico. The COA will say 64bit,32 bit English dvd for distribution in the USA only.

 

Anytime you see the yellow Ingram Micro label, be aware it is definitely a counterfeit copy. There is a real company named Ingram Micro however the labels that they use are blue not yellow.

 

Anytime you see an International COA it is a fake.

 

Once opened you will not see the white magnetic anti theft strip placed directly above the disc.

 

Also the activation key placard will be written in Chinese only.The Genuine Microsoft activation key placard is written in multiple languages none of which are Chinese.

 

The counterfeits will use an activation key that is a Volume key that is used for developers only.The key may or may not work right out of the box, however if it does work it will certainly be blocked shortly afterwards by Microsoft servers.The exact same key has been packaged and sold to many different customers purchasing the same software.

 

Other things to look for, the plastic shrink wrap quality is of far less quality and if you observe carefully the shrink wrap will not look as professional.

 

Most times the COA will appear to have a been opened and will show a very fine tear in the COA barely noticeable but a fine hairline tear is generally present.

 

Lastly any version that says Made in the USA is a counterfeit.  No matter how Authentic it may appear, it is not Genuine.

 

There are very few sellers on eBay and Amazon that are selling the Genuine article. If any seller fails to show clear photos of the COA ask yourself WHY. That aspect alone should be enough to trigger a pause response.

 

Genuine Microsoft discs are laser etched. The fake/ counterfeits will have a label that is carefully attached to the disc. The way to tell is there will be a ring all the way around the circumference of the disc. You will be able to see the ring. Look carefully.

 

DO NOT PURCHASE FROM THAT SELLER AND BUYER BEWARE!!!IF THE DEAL SEEMS TOO GOOD TO BE TRUE THEN GO WITH YOUR GUT INSTINCTS BECAUSE IT LITERALLY IS TOO GOOD TO BE TRUE.

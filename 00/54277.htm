I'm glad everything worked out for both of you.

Neither Microsoft nor any legitimate organization will suddenly, out of nowhere, pop up a message warning about computer problems and providing a telephone number to call. That is never legitimate.

The safest thing to do, when that happens, is to kill the browser from Task Manager. If you click anywhere in the browser window, even on the 'X' (which may be fake) you can initiate the download of malware.

These fake warnings happen when website owners allow their websites to be hacked, either because they don't know how to protect them or because they abandoned or gave up their URL. Those warnings also appear on websites that are illegitimate to begin with, but we're not dealing with that here.

One way to deal with this problem, which affects the entire internet, is to use a third-party DNS service that filters out problematic websites. There are many good choices, mostly free. I use OpenDNS.

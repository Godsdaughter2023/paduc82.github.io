steve winograd say: Your plan should work fine.  Specify a subnet mask of 255.255.255.0 on both NICs to make 192.168.0.x and 192.168.1.x different subnets.

You don't need to specify GW and DNS on the secondary NIC, and you don't need to create a static route.

To access the FreeNAS box on the secondary NIC, use its IP address (192.168.1.100), not its NetBIOS name.

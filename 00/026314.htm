jerryduan_msft8021 say:

hi peter,

in sharepoint online, if you are the global administrator, you can manage to turn off sharing for non-owners on all sites in the site collection in the sharepoint admin center.

for a personal onedrive for business library, the owner still be able to do that by either sharing the user with not higher than the view only permission level or customizing a permission level to prevent users from sharing the documents and folders to another external users. to do that, please see the steps below:
1. in the onedrive for business library, click the gear button in the top right corner > site settings > site permissions > permission levels.
2. click edit permission level > scroll down to the bottom of the page and click copy permission level.
3. create a new permission level based on the edit permission level with a name such as ‚Äúdisable sharing‚Äù and uncheck open items, view versions and delete versions under the list permissions.
4. save the customized ‚Äúdisable sharing‚Äù permission level.
5. then every time you share with the users, you need to share with the ‚Äúdisable sharing‚Äù permission level via the steps: click share button of the item to go to the share interface > shared with > advanced > grant permissions > show options.
     

note: if the user already has the permission level higher than the view only permission level, you need to firstly remove the user's permission by clicking remove user permissions in the permission management page.

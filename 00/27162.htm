Is there a way to select all of the footnote numbers and give them a different style than Normal?  Or is there some other solution that would preserve the small font of the footnote numbers?

Hope I am being clear.

Thank you.

suzanne s. barnhill say:

You can search for Superscript formatting and apply the Footnote Reference style, which is superscript by default.

  1. Press Ctrl+H to open the Replace dialog.
  2. Click More>> to expand the dialog.
  3. With the insertion point in the "Find what" box, click Format and choose Font...
  4. In the Font dialog, check the box for "Superscript" and click OK.
  5. With the insertion point in the "Replace with" box, click Format and choose Style...
  6. In the Find Style dialog, select "Footnote Reference" and click OK.
  7. In the Replace dialog, click Replace All.

Applying a different paragraph style will not affect the Footnote Reference formatting. To be honest, though, applying a different paragraph style won't affect superscript formatting, either, unless it is applied to more than 50% of the paragraph.

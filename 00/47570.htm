thelmaforrest say:

disable sign in options

Please, Please help me disable the password requirement on my computer.  I am nearly 70 years old and am frazzled when I can't remember the password and log on to my computer.  I promise to never leave it in the hands of my grandchildren.  I have tried checking the box and unchecking the box that says Users must us a pin or password but it always requires one.

davem121 say:
Hi Thelma, here you go . . .

Open the Settings App
Go to Accounts - Sign-in Options
Choose to sign in with a local account
(in newer versions of Windows 10, this might be on the Your Info Settings Page)
Enter your old password and leave the new password box blank
Close the Settings App

Click your Start Button, type netplwiz and hit Enter
In the resulting dialog, uncheck the option 'Users must enter a user name and password to use this computer box'
Click OK

That's It!

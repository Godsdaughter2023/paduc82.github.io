Hi,

Thank you for writing to Microsoft Community Forums.

I understand how difficult it is, when you lose the spot of the desktop items every time and you'll have to rearrange them. Auto arrange icons and Align icons to grid are the only default options to align the desktop items in Windows 10.

However, you can prevent the users from saving certain changes to the desktop. If you enable this setting, users can change the desktop, but some changes, such as the position of open windows, or the size and position of the taskbar, are not saved when the user logs off. You may check the steps below and see if it is useful to you.

1. Press Windows key + R to open Run.

2. Type gpedit.msc and press Enter.

3. Click on User Configuration > Administrative Templates > Desktop.

4. Double click on Don't save settings at exit.

5. Make a check on Enabled.

6. Click on Apply and OK.

If the issue persists, as your systems are within an education establishment, I'd suggest you to post your query in IT Pro TechNet forums, where we have support professionals who are well equipped with the knowledge on the options within systems connected to the domain network and can provide you with details in regards to your query.

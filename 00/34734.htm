You can set up an account for a child under 13, however each account that you set up requires  a unique email address.  (An email address is required to sign up for Xbox LIVE.)

This means that your children will each need their own email address to set up their account.

You would not want to use your birth date and email address, since this would prevent you from managing the safety settings for your children.

When the system detects that your child is under 18, you will then be asked to provide your email address.  This will enable you to manage your child's safety settings.

john - project say:
E. taylor,
First of all, which version of Project are you using? Second, is it updated with the latest service pack?

When you say "Baseline" drop down, what exactly are you referring to? The Bar Styles window has 6 columns. The closest thing to a "baseline" drop down is under the From and To columns. Is that what you mean? If not, then that's probably why it's not working. The Baseline1 Start and Baseline Finish1 fields need to be selected in those two columns.

One other thing, Project uses what I would refer to as reverse order in the Bar Styles. What that means is that if a bar style criteria lower in the list conflicts with a bar style near the top of the list the bar style toward the bottom will prevail. So, if you added you new bar style to the top of the list and something below it "trumps" it, then you won't see your bar style. And if you are using Project 2010 or later, the number of default bar styles has greatly expanded, so it's a little hard to figure out what may be in conflict. My advice is to create your own custom view with a minimum set of bar styles that meet your needs.

John

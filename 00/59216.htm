If you want to use Skype for Business, you need purchase Office 365 for business, and you can check this reference, then you need to register an Office 365 account if you don't have.

About moving your Skype contacts to Skype for Business, there is infeasible. But you can import your Skype contacts to the Outlook's contacts. About how to import Skype contacts to Outlook:

1. Launch the Skype application on your PC and sign in to your account.

2. Open the "Contacts" menu at the top of the window, go to the "Advanced" submenu and select the "Backup Contacts to File" option.

3. Navigate to your desktop and click "Save" to export your Skype contact data as a vCard file.

4. Launch Microsoft Outlook and go to the "Contacts" tab in the lower-left corner of the window.

5. Click on the "File" button in the upper-left corner and select "Import And Export."

6. Highlight "Import a vCard file (*.vcf)" from the list of actions and press "Next."

7. Select the vCard file you just exported to your desktop and click "Open" to import it into your Outlook contacts.

Note: you just can import Skype contacts to the Outlook other calendars folders not Skype for Business contacts folder.

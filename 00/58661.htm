You seem determined, so I won't argue with you.

Backing up is critically important. Everyone should be making an image backup every day. More often than that (every X hours) can also be a good idea, depending on how the computer is used.

Anything that makes backing up easy, fast and automatic is a great thing. Unfortunately, most people who make wild, raving posts here don't backup or don't think they should have to.

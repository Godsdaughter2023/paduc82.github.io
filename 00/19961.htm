hansv mvp say:

The characters aren't ordinary dashes, but non-breaking hyphens (Unicode character decimal 8209).

Here is a macro to remove the rows:

Sub DeleteRowsWithDashes()
    Dim r As Long
    Dim m As Long
    Application.ScreenUpdating = False
    m = Range("A" & Rows.Count).End(xlUp).Row
    For r = m To 1 Step -1
        If InStr(Range("A" & r).Value, ChrW(8209)) Then
            Range("A" & r).EntireRow.Delete
        End If
    Next r
    Application.ScreenUpdating = True
End Sub

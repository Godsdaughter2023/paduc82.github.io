jeepeenl say:

Problem (finally) solved!

The solution is nowhere to be found on the internet so I'll post it here and hope it will help others.

The Google account on the phone that couldn't login to the Microsoft Apps had the option 'Allow less secure apps to access accounts' enabled. After I disabled that, I could login to the MS Apps.

You can enable/disable it here: https://www.google.com/settings/security/lesssecureapps

This option isn't available if you use Google 2-factor authentication, and I think this whole problem doesn't exist if you use 2-factor, because that's what the Google account on the phone without issue uses.

Unbelievable this isn't documented and that even Microsoft support doesn't have this information.

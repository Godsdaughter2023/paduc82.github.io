With that date/time in cell A1, you can use this formula:

 

=OR(MOD(A1,1)>TIME(22,0,0),MOD(A1,1)<TIME(7,0,0))

 

By using TIME you can easily see how you can modify it for other ranges which span midnight. Note that this is strictly BETWEEN, so if you want it to include those outer values you should use >= and <=

rob koch say:

Your current problem has nothing to do with the computer, it's now a simple case of phone abuse, related to a scam.

What you need is to block the caller's number or at least stop answering their calls, since they'll keep trying until you don't answer anymore due to the fact that you did what they asked in the past.

If your phone has caller ID, then you should either use that to block the number(s) they use to call you or if possible don't answer any number you don't recognize or isn't from your local area.  If this isn't possible then try calling your phone service provider to see whether they have any options to block the number, though these are typically more difficult to use and again may not work due to spoofed numbers from an overseas VoIP phone.

You can also try simply hanging up as soon as you're aware it's the scammers calling, since the more they can get you talking, the more likely they'll continue to call.  If you stop communicating with them, then they'll usually give up more quickly.

The point is that these are bad people with no consideration for you, so don't provide any to them in return.  However, try to avoid making them angry, since if you do then they might do something even meaner like using your phone number for future fake calls to others, which often results in your receiving angry calls from others they've tried to scam.

The last resort is to change your phone number, but along with the obvious negatives, this only stops the current caller and does nothing to protect from future scam calls by others.  So generally the best method is to get a phone with Caller ID and actually pay attention to it, never answering any call from a someone you don't know.

Rob

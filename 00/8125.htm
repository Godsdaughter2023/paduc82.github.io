1. Login your account using this link "https://account.live.com".

2. Click “Notifications” located at the left side of the page.

3. Click “Manage email preferences”.

4. If you find the email address, please hit the “Remove” link under the email address.

RENAMING PROCEDURE:

 

1. Sign in to https://account.live.com

2. Click "Update email address".

3. Select "Use a different e-mail address".

4. Enter an e-mail address "you want to use".

5. The system will check for its availability.

6. Hit "Save".

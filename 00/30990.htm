This issue occurs when all the following conditions are all true:

 

- You have a removable drive configured as drive letter C.
- You do not have a removable disk in the removable disk drive.

 

Here are few methods that you may try in order to fix the issue.

 

Method 1: Change the drive letter assignment for the removable drive to a letter other than C.

 

To change the drive letter assignments in Windows 7, follow these steps:

 

a. Log on to the computer as Administrator.
b. Insert a disk in the removable drive.
c. Click Start, right-click on Computer, and then click Manage.
d. Click Disk Management.
e. Right-click the partition, logical drive, or volume that you want to change, and then click Change Drive Letter and Paths.
f. Click the removable drive, click it, click Change, click the drive letter that you want to use, and then click OK.

stefan blom say:

The problem is that "Mirror indents" has been selected for the List Paragraph style. Click in a numbered paragraph. In the Apply Styles pane (Ctrl+Shift+S), verify that you see "List Paragraph" as the style name and then click Modify. In the dialog box, click the Format drop down and click Paragraph. Clear the "Mirror indents" option and click OK twice. 

That should fix the immediate format problem.

However, I really do recommend that you set up a numbered paragraph style and, specifically, that you do not make use of List Paragraph (which is the built-in style that Word applies to all numbered and bulleted items unless you have chosen a specific style).

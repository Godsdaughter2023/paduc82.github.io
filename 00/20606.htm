Somebody made a mistake when creating this template, by including an extra paragraph mark in the footnote separator. To remove it (you only have to do this once to fix all the footnotes in the document):
  • Click View > Draft, and turn on nonprinting characters if they aren't already showing.
  • Click References > Show Notes.
  • In the top bar of the Footnotes pane, set the dropdown to Footnote Separator.
  • Click the Reset button.
  • Change the dropdown to Footnote Continuation Separator, and click the Reset button again. (This would affect only footnotes that are so long that they get split onto a second page.)
  • Switch back to Print Layout view.

If you plan to use the template again, you'll have to open the template itself as a document, and make the same changes there. That will prevent the problem from appearing in new documents based on the template.

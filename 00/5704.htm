WACC- Weighted Average Cost of Capital - Microsoft Community

tannerherman say:

WACC- Weighted Average Cost of Capital

VVV Corporation currently has bonds outstanding with coupon rate of 10% and a yield of 12%. The shareholders of this company require a rate of 14%. The company's tax rate is 30%.

Company is trying to raise new capital of $4 million. 40% will be from issuing new bonds and 60% from issuing stock.

What is the weighted average cost of capital for this new capital?

vijay a. verma say:

I have never been required to calculated WACC in the past, hence this is my first attempt on this. The formula for WACC (as per google) -

WACC = (Equity / Total Capital) * Cost of Equity + (Debt / Total Capital) * Cost of Debt * (1 - Tax Rate)

Hence, your calculation would be -

=(0.6*0.14)+((0.4*0.1)*(1-0.3))

= 11.2%

0.6 - Equity proportion

0.14 - Equity Cost

0.4 - Debt proportion

0.1 - Cost of Debt

0.3 = Tax Rate

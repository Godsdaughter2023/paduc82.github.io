What you are describing is basically a table of contents. The easiest way to create a TOC is based on headings in the document, but that's not the only way. Using this method, you can have your TOC display any text you desire. If it is text that already appears in the document, so much the easier for creating your TC entry.

A dropdown box with 150 entries would also require some scrolling. You could format the TOC with a small font and tight line spacing and accomplish about the same thing. But I take your point.

Thank you for posting to the forum regarding your issue with roaming being enabled on your phone after an update to 8.1.  From my understanding the only roaming setting you can control on the phone is related to data usage.  When it comes to roaming for cellular calls, this would be something you would want to address with your cellular provider. 

Some things to try to get rid of they symbol displayed on the phone in the meantime.

  • Go to settings > cellular + SIM > turn data connection OFF.  Leave this off and check to see if the icon has disappeared.  If it has, go back and turn this back on and see if the icon has disappeared.  If the icon never disappears, this is further evidence that the icon you see is likely related to calling settings.

gerald vit say:

Hi Lianna,

To address your concern, Outlook has the option to automatically delete emails in the Deleted Items folder upon signing out.

To enable this feature, follow these steps:

  1. Click the gear icon.
  2. Click Options.
  3. Under Mail > Automatic processing click on Message Options.
  4. Put a check on the tick box beside Empty the Deleted Items folder when I sign out option.
  5. Click Save.

Let us know if this answered your query.

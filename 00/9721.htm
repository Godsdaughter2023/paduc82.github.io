gary zhu msft support say:

Hi Marc,

From my experience, the two CNAME records you added should be wrong. You need to remove your custom domain name after “selector1” and “selector2” you added in your DNS hoster.

Please use the following format for the CNAME records:

Host name:                                         selector1._domainkey.<domain>

Points to address or value:           selector1-<domainGUID>._domainkey.<initialDomain>

TTL:                                                        3600

Host name:                                         selector2._domainkey.<domain>

Points to address or value:           selector2-<domainGUID>._domainkey.<initialDomain>

TTL:                                                        3600

Here is an example:

Host name:                                         selector1._domainkey.cohovineyard.com 

Points to address or value:           selector1-cohovineyard-com._domainkey.cohovineyardandwinery.onmicrosoft.com

TTL:                                                        3600

Host name:                                         selector2._domainkey.cohovineyard.com 

Points to address or value:           selector2-cohovineyard-com._domainkey.cohovineyardandwinery.onmicrosoft.com

TTL:                                                        3600

For more information, please refer to this article:

https://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/library/mt695945(v=exchg.150).aspx#SetUpDKIMO365

2.) Disable the sub-option "Enable Faster Data Synchronization" is unchecked.

Restart your desktop computer, and perform a soft-reset on your Omnia.   When both are up & working again, connect them with again with your USB cord and let us know what happens...

Hopefully your system will be able to sync now.

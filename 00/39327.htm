As I mentioned before, most upgrades to Windows 10 go reasonably well. ¡firedog say:

[IN-USE] account is locked by another session or for maintenance, try again.


There can only be one active connection to a POP3 mailbox. If you are connecting to this account from another device, e.g. a mobile phone, then that device may be monopolizing the connection, so that WLMail can't get through. Try switching the other device off for an hour or two to see whether you can connect from WLMail.

To find your program version, in WLMail press Alt-H A. If this doesn't show you the version and build number, press Escape twice, then Alt-F B.

=SUMPRODUCT(($F$2:$F$11="SIT")*(Q2:Q11="NA"))


There is nothing wrong with your formula , although it returns 2 for the above sample data set. I'm assuming that you expected 3 before you changed Q11 to Completed from NA. I would guess that you have leading and/or trailing spaces or non-breaking spaces that are fouling up the returns.

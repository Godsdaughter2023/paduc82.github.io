Many people cite the lack of a "Start" button in their need to find something to dislike about a system that confused them and remained an enigma to many.  Others claim the Frankenstein nature of moving from the "Start" screen to the "Desktop" was a jarring experience and wasn't fluid.  I haven't read or seen any articles or comments that redress what I believe was the fundamental underlying flaw in Windows 8 and as far as can been seen still exist in the current Technical Preview, that of consistency.  However in all fairness to Microsoft and by their own assertion this is an early build, so may be the things I'm going to discuss now may well be redressed at a later stage.  I certainly hope so.

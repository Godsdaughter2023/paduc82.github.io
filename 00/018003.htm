The tabloid size is 11" x 17" (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paper_size#Loose_sizes). Most inkjet and laser printers in US homes, schools, and offices can't handle any paper wider than 8.5". That means you'll have to select a software-only "printer" such as PDF output, and set the page size.

On the Page Layout tab of the ribbon, click Size and then click More Paper Sizes. In the dialog, click the paper size list and choose Custom Size at the bottom. Then enter the width and height as 11" and 17". If you get a warning that margins are outside the printable area, click Ignore.

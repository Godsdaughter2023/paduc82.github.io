1.  Exit all Office programs.

2.  Go to Your folder:
[home]/library/preferences

3.  Trash the file:
com.microsoft.autoupdate.plist
(and any other microsoft.autoupdate files you find there)

and also trash the current version of Auto Update APPLICATION (probably in your Office 2011 folder in your Applications folder).

Launch an Office application (word, outlook, etc.), and then Quit.

In your browser, do a google search for Microsoft Auto Update 2.3.6 (or whatever version is current that you want to install), and download the app/installer directly from the Microsoft website, and install.

Launch an Office application.  From the HELP Menu, select "Check for Updates"

karl timmermans [outlook mvp 2012-2018] say:

If you still have access to your old OL'2007 computer - then you may find the following of interest

How to Find & Copy the Right Outlook PST Files
http://www.contactgenie.info/how-to-find-right-outlook-pst-files

As the other responses stated - export/import is not the approach to use in this case and which files you copy depends on the "type" of email account involved.

Note: two things to also keep in mind especially if you're email account(s) in OL'2007 have been configured as IMAP accounts

#1 - IMAP account data uses an .OST file to store it's data in OL'2013

#2 - Whether Contact/Calendar data is stored in an IMAP data file depends on whether or not it was the first email account configured for the profile etc. If they are stored in an .OST file - the contact/calendar folders will have the additional description of "This Computer Only" in the folder description.

For additional background info on what data files get created/used based on the sequence of your email account creation you may also find the following of interest

Understanding Outlook Migration - Part 3 - Email Account Creation
http://www.contactgenie.info/understanding-outlook-migration-email-account-creation

Additional Details
How do I divide a movie into 15 minute segments, save each as part 1 part 2 etc to upload to youtube

considerate_guy say:

If you are putting the clips, photos, titles together in WLMM, you just need to make sure you only put 15 minutes (or less) into your project file.  So you would have a separate project for Part 1, Part 2, etc.

If instead you have a really long video clip that you wanted to split into 15 minute parts, I'd suggest doing the following:
1) Add the long clip into WLMM.
2) Split it into parts that are 15 minutes or under (using the 'M' keyboard shortcut, or the menus)
3) Save your project at this point in case you make a mistake.
4) Now delete everything except the first clip and choose to save it to your PC or upload to YouTube.
5) After that is done, either use Undo (Ctrl + Z), or reload the project.
6) Now delete everything except the second clip, upload, reload...
7) Repeat the steps until done.

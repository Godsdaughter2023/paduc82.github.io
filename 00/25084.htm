internet explorer 9 tabs detach - How do I reattach a tab or stop the - Microsoft Community

sirius96 say:

internet explorer 9 tabs detach - How do I reattach a tab or stop the detach from occurring?

Sometimes when I click on a tab in a tab group it gets detached. How do I reattach it or stop this from happenening? I find this very annoying.
kosh vorlon say:

Hi Sirius,

 

That's intentional - in a way.  The reason it is detaching is because not only are you clicking on it but you are ever so slightly (at least - perhaps even more overtly) moving the mouse at the same time.  That detaches the Tab to make it into a separate Window - a feature of IE9.  You can't disable the feature, but you can stop it for the most part by using more care to remain still when clicking the tab and just click quickly - the longer you hold down the button, the higher the chances are that this will occur.

 

To return things as they were, open the other IE Window where the Tab came from and then open the new IE Window with the tab that shouldn't be separated (so the new Window with the single Tab is technically on top of the original Window where you want to return the tab).  Click on the separated tab and hold the button down and scroll down.  You'll now see the window move below the original window and be able to see the original window with its Tabs.  While still holding down the mouse button, scroll the Tab to where you want it in the original Window between two other Tabs or at the beginning or at the end (give it a moment and it will make a place wherever you choose) and once it shows in the old Window, release the mouse button and Tab will be restored to the original Window wherever you elected to place it.  The new Window will close automatically once this is done (assuming it has no additional tabs in it).

 

I hope this helps.

 

Good luck!

You could try turning on the Built-in Administrator account in Command Prompt in order to be able to backup your files

Start your PC, then just after the Manufacturers logo disappears and Windows 10 tries to boot, press and hold the power button down for 5 - 10 seconds to perform a hard shut down

Do this Twice

net user administrator /active:yes

Reboot and try to log into the Administrator Account, if it looks for a password leave it blank, log in and backup your files.

net user administrator /active:no

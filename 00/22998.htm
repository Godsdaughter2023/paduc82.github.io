doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

It is a Next Page Section Break that you would need to insert between Pages 3 and 4

At the bottom left of the Word window, do you see PAGE: #    SECTION:  # as shown below?

If so, when you are on Page 4, it would need to show SECTION: 2 for the Link to Previous button to be enabled.

If you do not see the SECTION: # on the status bar, right click on the Status Bar and check the box for Section in the Customize Status Bar dialog that will appear.

stefan blom say:

Note that you need a section break, not a page break, for an actual section to be created.

As Doug wrote, you can verify that the insertion point is in fact in section 2 by looking at the section number on the status bar. That number isn't displayed by default, so you may have to right-click the status bar and click to select "Section" on the context menu.

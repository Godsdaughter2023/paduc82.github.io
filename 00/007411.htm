OneNote App (17.xxx) IS pre-installed in Windows 10. You maybe just updated it using the Microsoft store.

Why would you uninstall it anyway? If you do not want to use it just remove the tile from the start menu and ignore it after that.

david biddulph say:

Hi,

The first date, you have used [Jun/18/2010] was a "pure" date - meaning, it was:Jun/18/2010 00:00

The "problem": 

The new starting time [C2] is not Midnight [00:00] of Jun/21/2010 BUT:Jun/21/2010, 1:30 PM.

Add 12 hrs to Jun/19/2010 13:30 and you'll get: Jun/20/2010 01:30

Add to that another 13:30 hrs and you'll end up with: Jun/21/2010 15:00

If you'll display the dates & times as Decimals, you will notice the cause of all the misunderstanding.

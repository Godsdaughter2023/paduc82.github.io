The path to the Normal template is this: ~/Library/Application Support/Microsoft/Office/User Templates/Normal.dotm

The path to any templates you create is: ~/Library/Application Support/Microsoft/Office/User Templates/My Templates

The Startup path for Global templates is: ~/Applications/Microsoft Office 2011/Office/Startup/Word

Hi,
 
Calculated fields and items work with a column of numbers only.  I assume that you have a column in your source data which I Y or N.  Since these are text values, calculated fields and items will not give you % ages the way you want them.
 
You could try this.  In your source data, insert a dummy column with all 1 values.  In the data area of the pivot, drag the dummy column.  Now in the calculated item, your formula will N/(Y+N).
 
This will give you the no show ratio.  Format these are % ages.  However, you grand totals would be incorrect now so remove the grand total and insert a calculated item with grand total of Y+N.
 
Hope this helps.

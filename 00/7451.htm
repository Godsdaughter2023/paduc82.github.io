No, unfortunately OneNote doesn't have that sort of capability presently.  Closest you can come is if you have a page with the title of the product code already you could enclose the product code in double brackets ([[ ]]) and OneNote will automatically build a hyperlink to that page.

You can manually link to the Excel spreadsheet if you like, but that would mean you click the link in OneNote and it opens the Excel spreadsheet for you in Excel.

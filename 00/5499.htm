You shouldn't need too.. plus there is some confusion as to whether or not it affects the warranty..

 

There are no additional features you will get or lose by changing firmware so I honestly wouldn't bother..

 

Re-flashing your device using the current firmware is fine, but installing a different country/region firmware could technically affect your warranty, though no one from Nokia has said either way..

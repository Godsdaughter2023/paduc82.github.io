denislepoivre say:

I'm not entirely sure, but I think the photos will only be set to sync automatically once after they were taken.

If they haven't had the chance to be synced because of insufficient data connection, the sync will be put in a pending state until you connect to a Wifi network or get sufficient signal strength.

However, when you turn off the phone or restart it, you will reset the background tasks such as photo sync or downloads.

As I said, I'm not 100% sure that completely accurate but there is some truth there.

To put it simply, you'll need to wait till the photos have connected before you turn off your phone.

dirk goldgar say:

I don't know how you "lost the wizard", but you can certainly do this without it.

In order to do it, the form's recordsource must include the columns to which you want to bind the option groups.  So that's the first thing to make sure of.  Also make sure that those columns are numeric, so that they are able to hold the range of values that the option groups will have.

After that, you should be able to open the form in design view, click on the option group you want to bind -- not one of its options, but the option group frame itself -- and open its property sheet.  Go to the Data tab of the property sheet, and click on the property line for "Control Source".  A dropdown arrow should appear at the right of the line.  Click on it to drop down the list of fields (columns) in the recordsource, and select the field to which you want to bind this option group.

That should be it.  If something about this doesn't work, let us know.

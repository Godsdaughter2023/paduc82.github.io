If the computer goes back to sleep on its own after a few minutes, then that's actually normal, since putting a PC to sleep only places it in a standby mode where it can wake itself to perform things like Windows Update or other maintenance tasks in the middle of the night when you aren't using it.

If it wakes up and stays that way even if you don't touch it or returns to a login screen, but then never goes back to sleep then something may be wrong.

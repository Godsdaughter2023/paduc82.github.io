The ellipsis next to the delivery status is a keyword diagnosis tool which allow you to test whether a keyword is showing ads in real-time, if not what is the root cause and suggested actions to fix. You can think it as an instant status which is very time sensitive due to marketplace dynamics, and can keep changing overtime, e.g. low ad rank at this moment, but turn to showing ads next time due to competitor changes even though you haven't make any change to your account. This is why it is not showing as a "static" status by default in the delivery column, and requires you to trigger the test.

It is more statistically meaningful for optimization purpose, as it aggregates the results based on a selected time range, instead of reporting the instant delivery status for any specific time snapshot.

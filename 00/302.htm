To help fix anything we need to know if your PC is running in UEFI or in Legacy BIOS mode. UEFI uses a GPT-initialized disk and requires a FAT32-formatted System/EFI partition, which also makes it more simple to rebuild if there is a problem. If you could run diskpart, use list volume to show the various volumes. See if one is a FAT32 System/EFI volume. Also, if you list disk, it will show an asterisk under GPT if your disk 0 is a GPT disk.

jomel_g say:

Hello,

Simply log in using your Skype Name and Skype password, and not your Microsoft email and password. If you cannot recall your Skype password, you need to reset it:

  1. Go to this link: https://a.login.skype.com/diagnostics
  2. Type the Skype name (not your email address) of the account you are trying to recover, and hit Continue.
  3. Look for Reset password button and click it.
  4. You will be routed to Microsoft's website for password reset.
  5. Click any radio button, and hit Next.
  6. On the "Recover your account" page, please make sure that the Skype name of the account you are trying to recover is listed on the first box.
  7. Type the characters (CAPTCHA) shown into the second textbox and click Next.
  8. Verify the account.

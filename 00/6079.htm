Unfortunately, there is no such switch. You cannot make a reference to a paragraph number include the trailing punctuation.

After you insert the REF field with the \n or \r (or \w), click in the text (the Cross-Reference dialog box will remain open), manually type a period and a space, and then insert the REF field for the paragraph text.

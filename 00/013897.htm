How do I add animation one by one

What i want more is a slide which has some pictures in background and animated object will appear in sequence but the problem is they just get the animation effect and they are right there at the beginning of the slide show on same slide , what should I do ?

david marcovitz say: I don't completely understand your question. An entrance animation will start off not being on the slide and then when the animation is initiated be on the slide. An exit animation will start off on the slide and then when the animation is initiated leave the slide. An emphasis animation will always be on the slide but change in nature (grow/shrink, change color, spin, etc.). You can apply any of these animations to any object on your slide and even apply more than one to the same object. For example, you can have an entrance animation followed by an exit animation so something appear and then disappears. The exit animation can happen at the same time as the next object's entrance animation (if the timing is set to With Previous).

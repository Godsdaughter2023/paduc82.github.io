Hi Bill,

To clarify the situation, I’d like to confirm how you configure the account in Apple Mail.

If you configure the account via Exchange, the items in trash folder will be synced to the server as well, and if you add the account in Outlook for Mac via Exchange, all these items will be in the Deleted Items folder.

neo zhu- [o365] say:

hi bill,

here are the detailed steps:

1. confirm the account in apple mail.

a. open apple mail.

b. click mail > accounts.

c. check if the account is configured via exchange.

2. if the account is configured via exchange, you can add the account in outlook for mac and then the items in trash will be downloaded to the deleted items automatically.

a. open outlook for mac.

b. click outlook > preferences.

c. click exchange or office 365.

d. type the account information to add it.

3. if the account is not configured via exchange, you need to add an exchange account (such as an office 365 email account) in apple mail and move the items in trash to that account.

a. open apple mail.

b. click mail > accounts.

c. click exchange and type the account information to add.

d. move the items in trash to this account.

e. repeat the step 2 to add the exchange account and the original account .

f. move the items between these two accounts.

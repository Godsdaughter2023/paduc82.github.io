    1. On the search bar, type Device Manager and hit Enter. 

    2. Click View and choose Show hidden devices. 

    3. Locate Kindle Fire in your Device Manager, right-click and choose Update driver. 

    4. A popup window will appear and choose "Browse my computer for driver software". 

    5. Click on "Let me pick from a list of available drivers on my computer". 

    6. Select Portable Devices and click Next. 

    7. Uncheck the "Show compatible hardware" checkbox, select MTP USB Device and click Next. 

    8. Restart PC.

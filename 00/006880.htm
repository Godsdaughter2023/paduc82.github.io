There's a few steps we can try off the bat to help fix your Windows Live ID and Facebook issues:

  • Navigate to Applications > Settings > Applications > Internet Explorer. Once here, tap Delete History. You can also open Internet Explorer from your start page, go to the ... and then go to Options/Settings and do it there.
  • On a PC (preferably), go to http://xbox.com > Sign in > My Account and go to Billing Contact Information. Make sure this is up to date. Also go to http://account.live.com and then ensure that all of your information is filled out under "Edit personal info" (work address is not needed) and that you have an email address, a phone number, or two of each of those set up in "Edit security info".
  • We'll want to refresh your email account, the primary one, on the phone. Please navigate to Applications > Settings > Email + accounts. You'll want to tap and hold on the account and then Sync. The main account is usually at the top of the list, and says Windows Live on top of it.
  • Delete and then readd your Facebook account in Settings > Email + accounts. Try syncing it by tap and holding on it, then pressing Sync.
  • Please shut down your phone entirely by holding down the power button for 10 seconds. While it's off, if possible (as not all phones can do this), take out your battery for a few seconds and then put it back in. Then turn on your phone again.
  • Toggle your connection from wifi only to data only and try each one, then try them together.

After you try these steps, please let us know if they work for you. If not, can you gather the following information for us?

  • What type of phone you have:
    The version of Windows Phone 7 you are running (7.0, 7.5, or 7.8. This can be found in Settings > About and Software):
  • Has this been happening for a while? Did it start to happen after a certain change took place on your phone?

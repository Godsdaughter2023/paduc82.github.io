Word makes no assumptions about print order. If you have "Reverse print order" selected in the Print Options, then it will print in that order. Unless your printer duplexes automatically, you will need to opt for "Manual duplex," which can get dicey. By and large, the assumption (I think) is that you'll be printing camera-ready pages (that is, just one side of the sheet, for photocopying or quick printing) or printing to PDF. Front: 8 and 1
Back: 2 and 7

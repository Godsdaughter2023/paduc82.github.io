jimmclachlan- [o365] say:

Hey Mouran,

I think I have discovered why I have experienced this behaviour. Regardless of the fact that I imported the calendar entries, when a calendar item is created, at the time of creation, the calendar entry gets the reminder duration set as the default. There are certain exceptions based on the properties of the calendar item. For example, "All Day" events get a reminder duration of 0.5 days and recurring events get a reminder duration of 18 hours (so says docs). That aside, whatever reminder duration is in effect (either the default or some other duration if you change is), it remains with the event, even if the reminder is turned off. Then, in the future, if you decide to turn the reminder on, the event does not use the default reminder duration, it uses the reminder duration that was in effect at the time the event was created, or the last saved reminder duration for the event. It never goes back to use the default duration. The default reminder duration is only used when the event is initially created and never after that. I just learned soemthing new. I hope that helps some other folks.

a. user say:

Office 2010 opens Office 2016 files

Hi,

I have for a long time Office 2016 in my PC. It could open all of the files in File Explorer in each Office 2016 apps.

One day when repairing the installation of Office 2010, it could not open the apps in Office 2016, but in Office 2010.

I chacked the default apps settings, the files can be opened with Office 2016 apps, but they open in Office 2010 apps.

graham mayor say:

Only one Office application can have control of the registry with regard to file associations. As you have recently repaired Office 2010, that now has control and will continue to have control until the next Office 2016 update which will return control to 2016. You can short-circuit that process by repairing Office 2016 now.

See http://www.gmayor.com/Toolbars_in_word_2007.htm   which explains how multiple Word versions can be made to coexist more comfortably (though no matter what you set, Office 2016 will always grab the registry after an update).

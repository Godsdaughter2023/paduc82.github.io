megatron63 say:

Assuming everyone installed an SD card to their phone, this is what I came up with:

My wife upgraded from an HTC One M8 (Windows Phone 8.1) to a Lumia 950 and also had a "low storage" warning. After checking out her Lumia 950, I discovered it was automatically installing apps from her prior phone to the main phone memory. When she originally installed an SD card into the Lumia 950, she selected the option to install apps onto the card. But, her phone ignored that option and installed it on the main memory anyway. The solution is to go to settings and type "storage" - you will go to an area which shows all your apps. By pressing an app on the list, you can choose between moving or uninstalling the app - choose move and send it to the SD card. It was a LONG process for my wife, but she eventually moved the bulk of her apps to the SD card and the "low storage" warning went away. Her phone was also able to update after that. Hope this helps!

There is not a background music feature in Sway yet.  You can add audio, video, and embedded content that contain sound and if you put it at the beginning of your Sway, your reader can play it when they start viewing the Sway, but that is the extent of the support right now.  If this is a feature you'd like to see in Sway, please add your support for it here:  https://sway.uservoice.com/forums/264674-sway

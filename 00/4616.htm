resourceful say: Hello. Well, the info deleted when you delete a partnership will not affect SMS as a hard reset would. Just Outlook stuff contacts/calendar/notes/tasks. If you are getting any weird behavior from the PIMBackup program, definitely delete a few items and try a restore for a test just to make sure it works correctly prior to proceeding.

It is unknown what breaks the partnership. I usually find it occurs when I haven''t synced in a while. So it could be updates to Windows or AV/firewall or Office/Outlook. Problems with Outlook, e.g. if you have add-ins from your AV or connectors, or your .pst file becomes corrupted are causes. I suppose it could be a problem with the phone as well with installed programs. Not real easy to troubleshoot because the error reporting is not that great.

Just for your own info, uninstalling/reinstalling WMDC is not enough to recreate a new partnership. You have to remove the partnership from WMDC AND the phone at the same time. Don't know why it has to delete info from the phone, though. It is said that it is a security feature for business so you don't take company info with you when you leave the company.

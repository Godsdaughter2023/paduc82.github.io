steve rindsberg say:

Chandra may be correct, but I think it's more of a long-known problem.  It's probably not the number of columns but the size of the data as displayed in Excel that's causing the cut-off.   To make this clearer, try this:

Select the data you want to copy/paste.

Change the font size so it's much smaller, say half the current size.

Select all of the columns you want to copy/paste and doubleclick exactly on the rightmost edge of the rightmost column in the area at the top where the A, B, C etc are.  That'll resize each column so it's just wide enough to hold your data.

Now select your data, copy it and paste it into PPT.  Did more of it (all of it, even) show up?

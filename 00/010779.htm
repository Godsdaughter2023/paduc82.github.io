steve rindsberg say:
This can happen when you bring slides from one presentation into another.  If the masters/layouts have "user created" content on them, PPT tends to duplicate it.  It's got no way of knowing that the image on one master is the same logo as an image on the incoming master, so rather than risk tossing out something you might want to retain, it keeps it.  

Best way I know of avoiding this is not to bring slides/masters from other presentations into the one you're working on.  Instead, add a new slide/master/layout then copy/paste the content you want onto it.

The correct answer is that there is no limit. You can do it as often as you need to or want to. That's true of both the Full and Upgrade version.

 

You can also move the Full or Upgrade version to a different computer as often as you need to or want to, as long as one copy is never on two computers at once. But the license for an OEM version ties it permanently to the first computer it's installed on. Although even there you can reinstall it on that computer as often as you need to or want to, you may *not* move it to another computer.

 

And by the way, formatting and reinstalling because you are infected with a virus is almost always way overkill. There are several good anti-virus programs available, and they will successfully and easily remove most viruses. Formatting and reinstalling is much more trouble and cause problems of its own.

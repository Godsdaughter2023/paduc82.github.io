timoteo iv ofr say:

Hello,

It seems that you're having issues with your Multimedia Messaging Service (MMS). Try restarting your phone with a SIM card inserted, and by doing this your phone will load the settings from the SIM card. Then, try sending a multimedia message to your own mobile phone number. This will test the sending and receiving capabilities of your phone. Note that Multimedia Messaging Service requires cellular data connectivity.

If you can connect to the internet but you can't send multimedia messages, you can try entering the MMS APN settings manually. Search online to find the multimedia settings for your mobile operator. To add your settings manually on a Windows 10 Mobile, go to Settings > Network & wireless > Cellular & SIM > SIM settings. At the bottom you should see MMS APN and tap +. (For Windows Phone 8 depending on the phone model: go to Settings > access point, and tap + or go to Settings > cellular+SIM > SIM settings > SIM, and tap add MMS APN.)

If you're unable to connect to the internet using your cellular data connection, you can follow the steps provided in our Internet Connections FAQ article.

a. user say:

Aha! This sounds like a familiar problem.

When you go to print, and the printer dialog asks where you want to "save" the output, that often means that your default printer is a PDF printer or possibly an XPS printer. PDF and XPS are file formats, so you're actually 'saving' the printed output to a file.

Another possibility is that you, or whomever else printed last, saved the output to a PDF or XPS file, and never changed the setting back to printing on a piece of paper. Fortunately, it's easy to make that change.

To change your default printer, go to Control Panel > Devices and Printers. Right-click on the printer you want to be the default and select Set as default printer.

To switch your printer back to using a piece of paper, in the printer dialog hit the Change button and choose the printer you want.

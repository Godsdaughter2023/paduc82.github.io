deepak b say:

Hi James,

 

Welcome to Microsoft Community!

 

You want to share internet connection using laptop.

 

To share internet connection, make settings accordingly and connect the tablet to computer using USB connection.

 

To make settings:

 

a. Right click left bottom corner of the desktop.

b. Select Control Panel.

c. Select Network and sharing center.

d. Click on Set up a new connection or network.

e. Click connect to Internet.

f. Click Setup a new connection anyway.

g. Choose how you want to connect.

h. Put check mark for Allow other people to use this connection.

i. Click connect to complete.

 

Now connect the tablet to computer using USB connection to share the internet connection.

Yup, its a non-genuine Windows 10 Pro Volume License KMS client.

Whoever installed it, might have installed a volume license (organization key) on your computer. 

Windows 10 Pro volume license clients are used by large organizations that deploy Windows 10 in bulk to hundreds or thousands of computers. Sometimes, users will copy these organization keys off the Internet and use them to install Windows 10 Pro, but they are blocked from activating because it can't reach appropriate KMS host (Server computer) which handles the activation request. 

You need to purchase a new full license.

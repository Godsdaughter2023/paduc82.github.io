a)  The first time that Hotmail receives a POP3 instruction to delete emails after reading from a mail client, Hotmail will not delete the messages.  Instead, Hotmail will move the emails that were successfully downloaded by the client into a POP folder and out of the Inbox.  The POP folder will be available in the Folders list.

b)  Hotmail will keep the emails in the POP folder until the user logs into the Hotmail Web interface and confirms whether Hotmail should respect or ignore the POP3 DELETE command.  

Note: This setting is not mail program specific and would apply to all the mail clients/programs that the customer may use. By default this setting is set not to respect the delete command from the mail programs.

Follow these steps to confirm your POP3 client settings:

1.  Go to Options - More options.

2.  Under Managing Your Account select Pop and deleting downloaded messages

3.  Confirm the POP3 setting option that works for you.  Click Save.  Hotmail will respect your choice.

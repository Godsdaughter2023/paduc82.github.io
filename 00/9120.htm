sheen valentine ali msft say:

Hi All,

Just wanted to share what Renate shared with us in our private conversation in case anyone else have this issue.

====================================

We use English and Norwegian in Sharepoint, often randomly but we only write Norwegian on our tenant.

When I edited the SharePoint page I used an English version of SharePoint.

There is a setting under language that was checked for us which do not overwrite translations.

In effect, since I was editing the page in "English", the "Norwegian" SharePoint users did not get the update, hence the variation of users that were "affected".

We changed the setting to "Yes" overwrite translations, and now everything is back to normal.

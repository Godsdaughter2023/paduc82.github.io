hansv mvp say:

After copying the worksheet, the formulas will look like

='[OriginalWorkbook.xlsx]SheetName'!A1

To correct this:

  • Make sure the copied sheet is the active sheet.
  • Press Ctrl+H to activate the Replace dialog.
  • Enter the name of the original workbook, enclosed in square brackets, in the 'Find what' box. In the example above: [OriginalWorkbook.xlsx]
  • Leave the 'Replace with' box blank.
  • Click 'Options >>'.
  • Make sure that 'Look in' is set to Formulas and that 'Match entire cell contents' is clear (not ticked).
  • Click 'Replace All'.

  • Restart your computer.

  • Check to see if your computer’s date and time are set correctly.

  • Set your browser to accept third-party cookies.

Try signing in to other Microsoft services

See if you can sign in to other Microsoft services, such as SkyDrive or MSN. If you can sign in to one of these sites, the issue is likely with the site you were trying to sign in to, not with your account.

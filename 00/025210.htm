Excel keeps details of cells it considers to be in use.  It of course uses a rectangular area to do define this.  That is what you see when you goto the last cell - it is the lower right corner of this rectangle.

Hi Peter EU,

By default when Exchange Online in Office 365 is setup for your organization, the postmaster address, e.g. *** Email address is removed for privacy *** is set to *** Email address is removed for privacy ***.

Open a form with date descending order (DoCmd.OpenForm) - Microsoft Community

melhzy say:

Open a form with date descending order (DoCmd.OpenForm)

Hi Cmmunity,

 

For the DoCmd.OpenForm command-

Can I open a form with date descending order? How can I do that? Thanks.

 

The form is created based on a table.

hansv mvp say:

You could use something like this:

 

    DoCmd,OpenForm FormName:="MyForm"
    With Forms!MyForm
        OrderBy = "MyDateField DESC"
        OrderByOn = True
    End With

 

where MyForm is the name of the form and MyDateField is the name of the date field on which you want to sort.

    Your configuration doesn't matter in this case as it is the main mail server of the domain librisprijs.nl who isn't accepting the emails and falls back to the other mail servers configured in their MX records. This could be for a simple reason such as that the main mail server was down for maintenance at the time but there are many other reasons for this to happen as well (none are caused on your side).

    However, these "fallback" MX servers apparently can't relay it back to the main mail server either and thus delivery will still fail.

    The issue is completely up to librisprijs.nl to solve.

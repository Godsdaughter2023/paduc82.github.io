Hi Ashley,

You can transfer your history from one computer to another – for example, from a Mac to another Mac, or a Mac to a Windows machine – by copying your Skype Name folder from one computer to another.

To transfer your conversation history:

Open Finder and navigate to ~/Library/Application Support/Skype/.

On your new computer, install and sign in to Skype so that the necessary folder is created, then sign out of Skype again.
Replace the Skype Name folder with the one that you saved in step 2.
Sign in to Skype. Your chat history will be there when you browse through your conversations.

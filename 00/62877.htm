Sounds like the PhotoStory may be too large and complex. Does this
same behavior occur on small projects? PhotoStory 3 has a photo
limit of 300 but it can be much less if many transitions and motion
effects are used. Also...if you are using .MP3 music...try using .WMA.

FWIW...you could create several small PhotoStorys and save in the
WMV format...then import the WMV files into Movie Maker for combining
into a final movie.

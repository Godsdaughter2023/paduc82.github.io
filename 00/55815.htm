    Greg,

    De-bloatifying my PC is a worthy project for another day.

    The PC had started the upgrade process Thursday night about 10:30 pm.  It was still executing (at about 30%) early Friday morning.  Sometime between 7:00 am and 5:30 pm Friday, it got into the state I showed above in the screenshot.

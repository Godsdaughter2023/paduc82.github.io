lemp say:

In Windows 7, you'll find "Change when the computer sleeps" under "Power Options" in the Control Panel or more quickly by clicking Start, typing sleeps into the Search box, and pressing Enter.

If you have a laptop, you'll see two different settings, one for when the computer is plugged in and one for when it's on battery power only.

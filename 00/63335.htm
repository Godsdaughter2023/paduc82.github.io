frederik long say:

This is by design.

"Moving" a folder means that you move the whole structure from the source folder to the destination folder, complete with its permission settings. The individual files are not touched. It's like sending a parcel by post: The post office gets no access to the parcel's contents.

"Copying" a folder means that you copy each file to the destination folder. During the copy process it acquires the permission settings of the destination folder. It's like being screen at the airport: Security will examine each item in your bag and discard items deemed dangerous.

You must therefore do the move in two steps: First "copy", then "delete".

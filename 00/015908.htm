First

(a) remove the battery for 5 minutes;
(b) reinsert the battery;
(c) connect to your charger;
(d) connect the phone to your pc;
(e) run the updater again.

Leave it for a few minutes.


If that doesn’t work, then try a “deep reset”
(or a “hard reset” as it is sometimes known).
Note that you will probably lose all your data.

(a) ensure the phone is off;
(b) hold down the following three keys:
'call answer', ‘*’, and '3'; then
(c) still holding those keys, press the ‘power on’.

Give it a chance,
it may take several seconds to work;
release the three keys
when the phone some signs of turning on.

This manoeuvre may take several tries.

If that doesn’t work take the device to a Nokia care centre to see what they can do.

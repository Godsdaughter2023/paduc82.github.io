    arvind m. [microsoft] say:

    Here's a set of steps that work for me on 950/950XL:

    Install Microsoft Health from Store on your Phone.

    Delete an existing entry (“pairing”) for your Band if it exists on the Phone.

    • Go to the Settings app on Phone.  Select Devices, and then select Bluetooth.
    • If you see an entry for your Band, touch and hold on the entry to bring up a Delete option.  Tap Delete to “unpair” this entry

    Set up your Phone for pairing.

    • In the Bluetooth settings page above, check and if necessary change Bluetooth Status to On
    • Stay on the Bluetooth settings page on your Phone - this sets it up for the upcoming pairing step.

    Set up your Band for pairing.

    • On Band, tap on the Settings tile (the one that looks like a gear wheel), and then the Bluetooth icon.
    • Tap on the current status (e.g. On) to bring up a list of options.  Swipe right/left until you see Pairing, and tap on Pairing. The Band should now display Pairing as the current Bluetooth status.

    Pair your Phone and Band

    • Watch the Bluetooth settings page on Phone – the Band should eventually show up here after several seconds.   If you are in a location that has many Bluetooth devices in your vicinity, you may have to scroll down a list and/or wait for generic "Accessory" names to be resolved to proper names. Tap on the Band name when it appears.  This initiates the pairing process.
    • You should now see prompts on both Phone and Band asking you to check that the other is displaying a numeric code.  Tap on Accept if the codes displayed on the two are identical (this is a security check).
    • On the Phone, the Band should now show as Paired.
    • This successfully concludes pairing.

    Set up the Microsoft Health app on Phone to work with your Band

    • Start Microsoft Health on your Phone
    • It should prompt you to accept a few, in particular, you should see a prompt asking you to allow the app to access your Band – make sure you accept this.
    • Microsoft Health will attempt to sync data from your Band to your Microsoft Health account (in the cloud).  If you see a “timed out” error on the first attempt, wait for a few seconds and let it try again.

    Hope this helps

efraín morales say:

HDMI sound

To transfer the PC sound to your Sony TV (or any other):

1.    Install the latest drivers for your sound card

2.    Connect the HDMI cable from PC to the assigned TV HDMI PC output

3.    With the TV off, restart your PC

4.    Control Panel > Sound > Playback and the HDMI should be there

5.    Right click the HDMI output and select Test; the soundbar will move

6.    Classify the HDMI as Default Device

7.    While there change the icon to a TV and rename as Sony HDMI Output

8.    Turn on the TV and select the assigned HDMI PC jack for the input

9.    Test again; the sound will come from the TV

10.Any reconnection will need the TV turned off

11.On: PC then TV; Off: TV then PC. Always remember the TV HDMI PC position.

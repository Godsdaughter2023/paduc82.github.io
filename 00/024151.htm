bernie deitrick say: Array enter (enter using Ctrl-Shift-Enter) the formula

=SUM(IF(LEFT('[Contract Summary Info.xlsx]Contract Summary'!$A$2:$A$10000,4)=$E45,'[Contract Summary Info.xlsx]Contract Summary'!$E$2:$E$10000))

=SUM(IF(VALUE(LEFT('[Contract Summary Info.xlsx]Contract Summary'!$A$2:$A$10000,4))=$E45,'[Contract Summary Info.xlsx]Contract Summary'!$E$2:$E$10000))

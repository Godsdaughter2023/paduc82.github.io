graham mandeno say:

Hi Chris

Does your app create temporary tables, say, for creating complex reports?  This would certainly explain the bloating.  I always create temporary tables in a temporary database, and link them to the front-end.

Also, have you tried decompiling the front-end?  (More info here: http://www.granite.ab.ca/access/decompile.htm)

A front-end will grow naturally as you make changes to the code and (either implicitly or explicitly) recompile.  It is good for the health of your front-end to decompile/compact/repair/compile regularly.

jay freedman say:

Times New Roman is distributed with Windows under license from The Monotype Corporation. The version in Windows 8 is 6.80, and the version in Windows 10 is 6.90.

Times is a font created by Linotype Corp. (which has since merged with Monotype). That was the basis for the Adobe PostScript font named Times, which is an Apple core font. What you see as Times may be that PostScript version, or it may have been installed with a printer.

For more details, see https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Times_New_Roman, in the section headed "Times Roman and Times New Roman".

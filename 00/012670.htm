Hello metalkitsune

Rather then just tell you to ignore this message as being fake since MS wouldn't send out a message like this, you can easily confirm that by

#1 - Hover (not click on) over any link in the message and look at where that link is actually pointing to. This should be done EVEN IF the URL "appears" to be legitimate since what you see may not be where it's actually pointing to
OR
#2 - Right-click on the link , select <Copy> and then paste it into any text editor (i.e. Notepad)

If ANY of the links do not point to a valid MS website, then it's a fake email. The best way to check you're account is to log into it normally

The steps regarding URL's listed above would apply to any email received from any major vendor such as PayPal, your bank/credit card company, Amazon etc etc.

    It’s really unfortunate that you are facing this issue. But don't worry we will try to help you out.

    The issue could be caused dues to several reasons like network proxy or Firewall device does not support range requests. Corruptions with BITS service might even cause the issue. Error 0x80200013 is defined by download job failed due to insufficient range support.

    Please provide us certain information to narrow down the issue:

    1. How is the computer connected to the internet? Is it wired or wireless?

    6. Click on OK.

    1. Press Windows key + letter ‘R.’
    2. Type Services.msc and hit OK.
    3. Look for the service named BITS.
    4. Right click and select properties.
    5. Change the startup type to Automatic.
    6. Start the service.
    7. Apply changes and hit OK.
    8. Restart the computer.

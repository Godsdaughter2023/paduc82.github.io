The final thing I will suggest at this time is to do a soft reset of the phone.  You can do this by going to settings > storage sense > phone > temporary files > delete > Internet Explorer settings > delete history.  Once this is done, press and hold the down volume button + the power button until the phone vibrates and reboots.

petrib say:

The N8 does not support DVB-H broadcasting at all.

 

As far as I know, most, if not all, broadcast networks have shut down their trial or the one or two production DVB-H networks that there in us, or are at least in the process of shutting them down (probably also in Latvia, but...).

 

DVB-H as a mobile TV technology is, essentially a failure. (DVB-T2, which is something that Nokia is not support in phones, seems to be the replacement.)

 

The N96 uses DVB-H for movile TV, but the N8 doesn't.

 

The N8 problem is probably something else than with the N96.

 

Call your service provider (your network operator, or the TV service provider), if it is a broadcast TV service. If it is a TV app that streams video from the net using packet data or WLAN, check that your packet data or WLAN works, and if it does (e.g., the web browser works, or email), contact the application developer.

You should be OK if you do this: -


1. Power it off and unplug the power lead for 30 seconds or so.


2. Plug the power lead back in.


3. Press and hold both the controller sync button and the eject button on the console itself. Tap the power button on the console while continuing to hold the other 2 buttons.


4. You should hear 2 power up tones. Once you've heard the second tone, you can let go of the 2 buttons and the console will boot into the startup troubleshooter. From there you can choose the offline system update option and hopefully you can get back up and running via that.

Hello Derse,

 

As Gmail uses different mail server from Exchange server, so after you created rules for Gmail accounts in Outlook client, the rules are stored locally in the Outlook client. And they are not stored in the server. So, when you configure your Gmail account in a new Outlook client on another PC, the rules don’t exist. Your need to re-create the rules manually on the new PC.

 

If you set up the rules for your Office 365 for Business accounts, then rules are stored on the cloud. You can check the instructions in this link to manage your rules via Outlook Web App(OWA): Inbox rules in Outlook.

 

Hope the information helps.

Rick

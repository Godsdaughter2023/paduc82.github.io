Hi Zac,

You may access the Windows Recovery Environment by making your computer think that the startup is failing. To do so, follow the steps below:

  1. Turn on your computer.
  2. When you see the loading dots, immediately turn off your computer.
  3. Repeat step one and two. Twice will be enough for the computer to think that you are having issues with start up.
  4. Once the computer boots up for the third time, you will see the loading dots on the screen and the message "Preparing automatic repair".

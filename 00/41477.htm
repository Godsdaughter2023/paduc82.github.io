Login Information

MAC Filtering

  1. Open web browser.
  2. Login to router by entering http://192.168.0.1 into the address bar
  3. The router will then prompt you to enter the user name and password. The default user name and password are: "admin", "password".
  4. Select Wireless Settings from the menu on the left.
  5. In the main screen click the "Setup Access List" button to the right.
  6. Place a check in the "Turn Access Control On" box to activate the filter.
  7. If you're currently connected to the network, you can select the device from the Available Wireless Stations list.
  8. Click the radio button next to the chosen device.
  9. Click Add to add the device to the Trusted Wireless Stations list.
  10. If you are not currently connected, you can enter the device address manually. [Enter the MAC address & name of the device in the Add Manually section]
  11. Repeat this to configure each wireless device to obtain a wireless link to the router. [*Note: If you select Trusted Wireless Stations Only when configuring on a wireless computer, you will lose your wireless connection when you click Apply*]
  12. When complete Click Apply.
  13. The selected devices should then appear in the "Attached Devices" menu.

Error 80153021 occurs when making a purchase on Xbox LIVE

You see the following error code and message when you try to make a purchase through Xbox Live on your Xbox One or Xbox 360 console:

Purchase pending.

Status code: 80153021.

This may mean one of the following:

The Xbox Live service is temporarily unavailable.

Your Xbox Live Gold membership or Xbox Music Pass has an outstanding balance and new purchases are being declined.

Your PayPal account country does not match your Xbox Live account country.

Your account has incorrect or incomplete billing information.

Solutions

To resolve this problem, try the following solutions.

Solution 1: Check the Xbox Live service status

Go to the Xbox Live status page to see whether the service is up and running. If there are any service alerts, wait a while and then try again.

Solution 2: Update the payment option for your membership

If you have an Xbox Live Gold membership or an Xbox Music Pass and you receive this error code when trying to make a purchase, it means you have an outstanding balance and need to pay this amount before making any further purchases. Usually you have an outstanding balance because there's a problem with the payment option that you use for your membership. See How to change your Xbox Live payment option and transfer your balance to resolve this issue.

Solution 3: Use another payment option

If you use your PayPal account as a payment option on Xbox Live, that account's country must match your Xbox Live account country. You'll need to use another payment option to complete the purchase. See Add a payment option to your Xbox Live account in order to resolve this issue.

Solution 4: Check your billing information

Verify that your billing information is correct. For example, make sure that the billing address for your payment option matches the billing address used by your credit card company, and that your payment option expiry date has not passed.

To change the billing address for your payment option, see How to change or update Xbox Live account billing information.

Solution 5: Contact Xbox Support

If you keep experiencing the problem, contact Xbox Support.

This means your Xbox account is a child account or you have a suspension preventing you from joining clubs.


Xbox account age can not be changed as it is permanently connected to the first date of birth entered when creating your Microsoft account. This can't be changed even by Xbox support. Your account will be promoted to an adult account when that original birth date reaches 18 (19 in Korea). You can change your Microsoft account age but that doesn't change your Xbox account age.

https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/12417/microsoft-account-add-members-to-family

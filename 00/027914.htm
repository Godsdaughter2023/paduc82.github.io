hansv mvp say:

The Short Time format is a clock time format (00:00 - 23:59 or 12:00 AM - 11:59 PM), not an elapsed time format. Unlike Excel, Access does not have any built-in elapsed time format.

One alternative is to show the elapsed time as decimal hours (in your example, 76 hours and 20 minutes would be displayed as 76.33):

([Date_Time_NOC_Notified]-[Event_Date_Time])*24

Another option would be to compute hours and minutes separately:

Hours: DateDiff("n",[Event_Date_Time],[Date_Time_NOC_Notified]) \ 60

Minutes: DateDiff("n",[Event_Date_Time],[Date_Time_NOC_Notified]) Mod 60

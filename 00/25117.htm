Importing from an .mbox file is not the same thing as importing from Apple Mail.

According to the Office for Mac Team Blog Outlook currently cannot import Apple Mail on Lion. The format of Apple Mail changed after Outlook was released by Microsoft. Microsoft has made no announcements yet about its plans to support importing from Mail on Lion. I would imagine this will be addressed in a major update in the future.

For now, you can drag individual messages from Mail to your Desktop and then drag those into Outlook.

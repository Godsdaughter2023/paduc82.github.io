AFAIK, the only restriction on the type of content that can be included in the conditional output is that you can't output a table; mergefields, text & graphics are all supported. The only other limitation that might be a bother in your context is that the numeric picture switch length is limited to 63 characters, meaning there's only 61 characters available for the display (including whatever a mergefield might output). Graphics count as a single character, so they're not a problem, but text & mergefield output may be. kathleencollins say:
How do I move contacts into a group I've created?

Open outlook.com > top left, place your cursor over the logo Outlook > a down arrow will appear > click at the arrow > click People > here is your People window.
 

How to add contacts to the group you have created :

Still in People window > click a contact you want to be added to the group > if you have multiple contacts to add, click the box at top left of the contact > continue clicking the top left box of each contact you want to add to the group until you are done> at the toolbar, click Groups > check the box in front of the group name > click Apply > all the contacts you have selected are now in the group.

Note : make sure you click squarely at the check box. If you click outside the box, all previous boxes you have checked will be nullified, and you’ll have to start all over.

merge fields - Microsoft Community

alice1970 say:

merge fields

I have inserted a merge field with text after it and now its showing in red instead of black. Do you know what would cause it to turn the text red?

jay freedman say:

Unexpected red text often indicates that you had Track Changes turned on when you inserted the text. That could happen if you accidentally pressed the shortcut Ctrl+Shift+E.

Click the Review tab of the ribbon. Click the bottom half of the Accept button and choose "Accept All Changes and Stop Tracking". If the red text changes to black, then Track Changes was the cause.

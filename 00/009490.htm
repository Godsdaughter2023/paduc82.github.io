Private Sub Worksheet_Change(ByVal Target As Range)

Hi,
 
Please follow the steps below for speech settings and check.
 
1)  Go to the Windows Start Button.
2) Type Speech settings in the Search box.
3) Choose the Change text to speech settings listed.
4) Under Voice selection use the drop down arrow to change the voice.  
5) Under Voice speed, use the slider bar to change the rate. Move the slider bar to the left for a slower rate or to the right for a faster rate or halfway for a normal rate of speed.
 
Also I suggest you to refer to the steps in the article to add speak command in the Word:
 
https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Using-the-Speak-text-to-speech-feature-459E7704-A76D-4FE2-AB48-189D6B83333C#__toc282684835
 
Note: It applies it 2016.

https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Using-the-Speak-feature-with-Multilingual-TTS-E522A4F2-37CB-492B-BE6A-8997D23DFE70

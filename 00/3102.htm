fraser xu msft say: Hi Gero,

It’s not feasible to save files to Shared with me “folder”. Let me explain the reason below:

Shared with me is not a real library in OneDrive for Business. This means, actually, it is not a folder. It’s similar as a view rather than a folder.

Consider this scenario: Use the Search function in a Windows computer and you will get a list of the search result. However, those files are not store in this list. This is what a Shared with me folder do. It is just a list to show the result of the files which are shared with you by other people.


This means: Files in your Shared with me “folder” are not really store in your OneDrive for Business library. They are just a search result and the real locations of those files are other people’s OneDrive for Business libraries.

In a short, “Shared with me” folder don’t have any storage quota and we cannot save files to it. Your understanding is highly appreciated.

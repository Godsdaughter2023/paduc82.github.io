bill sproule - msft say:

The remote sources you see in the Photos apps (Flickr, Facebook, Skydrive, other PCs) are associated with the Microsoft Account you are logged in with (or have attached to your Windows logon).  The only reason I can think that one Windows 8 machine would show some options but another does not would be if the Microsoft Accounts logged into the different machines are different.

 

You can also hide sources in the Photos app.  To unhide them, in the Photos app, go to Settings (Win-i), chose options, check the options you want to appear.

 

Flickr and Facebook require your Microsoft Account to be provisioned with those services.  You can elect to revoke access to your Microsoft Account from those services via their web sites.  To check on your permissions, go to Settings, chose options, click the Options link next to Flickr or Facebook.

 

Hope that helps.

1. Press Windows Key + R

2. Type netplwiz

3. And select the account and uncheck the option "user must enter user name and password to use this computer".

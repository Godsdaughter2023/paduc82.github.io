a. user say:

Hardware drivers are created by hardware manufacturers, not Microsoft.

Manufacturers provide drivers free of charge.

To obtain drivers for your hardware:

1- Make a list of all the hardware attached to and inside your computer.

2- Obtain the model name and number for each of the above.

3- Go to the manufacturers' websites and find the section where drivers are shown.

4- Download the Windows 10 driver for each of your hardware devices.

If there is no driver specifically for Windows 10, usually the driver for Windows 8 or Windows 7 will also work.

5- Follow the manufacturers' installation instructions very carefully.

Note: Since the hardware manufacturer supplies the driver, it's entirely up to the hardware manufacturer to choose which of their devices will have a Windows 10 driver. For older hardware, you may not find a compatible driver.

If you are not good with computers, you might want to ask someone more knowledgeable to do this for you. A computer is a very complicated device, and this cannot be avoided.

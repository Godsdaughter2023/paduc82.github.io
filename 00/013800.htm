karl timmermans [outlook mvp 2012-2018] say:

No you cannot have 2 (or more) email addresses within one distribution list entry. Any contact that has more than one valid email address (be it multiple email addresses and/or fax numbers) will appear in the AddressBook mutliple times. You would need to add each entry separately.

The names that appear in the DL are not governed by the settings of your AB. The name that gets displayed in the DL is the Email DisplayAs field which is not configurable within the DL. Whether the email address appears in the Email DisplayAs field makes no difference in terms of someone being able to determine what the email address for that recipient is.

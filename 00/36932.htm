hazmat radio say:

The "family" feature has nothing to do with sharing Xbox Live Gold.


The account that has Xbox Live Gold needs to be set as the Home Console on the Xbox console where you want the subscription to share out.

Sign into your account on your Xbox console and go to - 

Settings > Personalization > My Home Xbox > See if it says Make this My home Xbox (meaning this profile is not set as the home console ) or This isn't my home Xbox (meaning that this profile is set as the home console on your Xbox)


You cannot set your profile as the Home Console on two or more Xbox consoles at the same time.

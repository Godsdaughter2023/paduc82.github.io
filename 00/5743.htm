There's no elegant way to conditionally sum across worksheets.

You'd think you could do something like this:
=SUMPRODUCT(IF(Sheet4:Sheet1!A1=1,Sheet4:Sheet1!B1))

But...no.
This is one approach:
=(Sheet4!A1=1)*Sheet4!B1+(Sheet3!A1=1)*Sheet3!B1+(Sheet2!A1=1)*Sheet2!B1+(Sheet1!A1=1)*Sheet1!B1

Like I said...inelegant.

I hope that helps.

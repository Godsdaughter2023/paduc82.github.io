I've done some research but it's not trivial to connect an external microphone.

a. user say:

Hi RS13,

 

Thank you for posting your question here in Microsoft Community. We understand that you wish to setup your account connection from POP to IMAP. However, it is currently not supported by Outlook.com as what ZigZag3143 had mentioned.

 

The reason why emails are kept in sync on your iPhone is because it is on Exchange Active Sync. Your mobile device supports EAS connection and that is why the sync process between iPhone and Outlook.com server is real-time.

 

If your mail application on Mac supports EAS connection, then you may use the following configuration:

 

·         Server address: s.outlook.com

·         Domain: <leave empty>

·         User name: Your email address

·         Password: Your password

·         TLS/SSL encryption required: Yes

 

You will need to contact Apple support for the steps on how to manually configure EAS connection using the mail application in your Mac computer.

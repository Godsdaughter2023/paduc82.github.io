Hi some head sets don't work in the Xbox controller. Its because the jack has the connections in the wrong spot. The left, right, mic, and ground are in different spots on the jack. for example the apple ear buds wont work in the controller.

They make little connectors that can convert it to the correct connection.

If you have a mic you need the 3 line converter. They have names for the 2 they are both TRRS. 

  

The difference is the little metal connections are in different spots. The ground and the mic are switched. The ground being in a different spot causes the whole thing not to work, because its a common ground.

 The Xbox uses CTIA/AHJ ......Left, Right, Ground, Mic. (Green Turtle Beach)

The other one is OMTP ...... Left, Right, Mic, Ground  (Blue Turtle Beach)

If your headset is blue you need a converter.

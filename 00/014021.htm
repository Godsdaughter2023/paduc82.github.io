1. Select entire sheet by pressing CTRL+A two times OR select the range where you want to apply Conditional Formatting.

rick rothstein, mvp - excel say:

Hmm, I missed that... thanks for noticing it. Here are two corrected array-entered** formula that ClintonElmer can use depending on what he wants to display when Column A has a blank cell between the R's as one of his responses to me indicated was a possibility...

Repeat previous cells total for blank cell in Column A
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
=(B1<>"R")*SUM(INDIRECT("A"&(MAX((B$1:B1="R")*ROW(A$1:A1))+1)&":A"&ROW(A1)))

Display a 0 for blank cell in Column A but continue summing afterwards

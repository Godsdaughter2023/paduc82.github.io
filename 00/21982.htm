hansv mvp say:

Move the mouse pointer to the left hand side of the first cell that you want to format, until the mouse pointer becomes a solid black north-east pointing arrow, then click.

Move the mouse pointer to the left hand side of the next cell that you want to format, until the mouse pointer becomes a solid black north-east pointing arrow, then hold down the Ctrl key while you click.

Repeat for the other cells that you want to select.

martin birrane say: Hi Mark,
  It sounds like the Readyboost service "Sysmain" has been switched off or is disabled

Click on Start and type Services in the Run box. 

When the services icon appears right click on it and select "Run as administrator"

When the services dialog appears double click on "Superfetch" and change the Startup Type to "Automatic" and restart your computer

Since you have AAD Connect enabled, to ensure both the on-premises and Exchange Online can use the DG (Distribution Group) for mail delivery, we suggest you create these DGs in the on-premises directly, then sync them to Exchange Online via AAD Connect, so that both side can view the DG in the GAL.

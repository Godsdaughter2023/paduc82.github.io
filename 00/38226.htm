    armaan gagarin say:

    This is how I got my Bluetooth Microphone to work..

    On a Windows 8.1 system..

    1.) Press Windows key + X. Select COMPUTER MANAGEMENT.

     

    2.) Expand SERVICES and APPLICATIONS and select SERVICES.

    3.) Look for the BLUETOOTH SERVICES and click on " RESTART the Service " on the left. Do this for all the Bluetooth services, one by one. Additionally, you can also RESTART the WINDOWS AUDIO Service, just to be sure.

    4.) Then go to Control Panel > Hardware and Sound > Manage Audio Devices. Your HEADPHONE will now be accompanied by a HEADSET in the Playback Menu and a HEADSET will also show up in the RECORDING Menu, which is essentially your Microphone Device.

    I hope that helps..!!! Good day..

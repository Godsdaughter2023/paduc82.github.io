You should be able to scan any document or photo using your Kodak ESP All-in-one Printer; however you will not be able to print as you have ran out of ink.

 

This issue could be caused by cables that aren't connected properly, corrupted drivers, incompatible drivers, printer settings, missing updates, and problems with your printer.

 

1. Do you receive any error code or message?

2. Have you made any recent hardware or software changes to your computer prior to the issue?

stefan blom say:

Note that you can update cross-references in a slightly easier way: switch to Print Preview (Ctrl+F2) and then back to your previous view (ESC).

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

With linked textboxes, the text "flows" from one to the next.  It is not replicated in the next which from your message appears to be what you are after - "the text in the primary text box does not appear in the linked text box."

 

To achieve that, insert one textbox, inline with text and with a paragraph mark after it and select the textbox and the paragraph mark and insert a bookmark.  Then at the place in the document where you want the textbox and its contents replicated, insert a cross reference to the text of the bookmark.

 

Note, the cross reference will need to be manually updated (select and press F9) to get it to display the text.

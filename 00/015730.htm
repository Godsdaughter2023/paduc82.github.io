mikek703 say:

To use multiple headers and footers you need section page breaks and not simply page breaks.  First, make sure you have a section page break after the body text and before the end matter pages (delete the page break and from the Page Layout menu, click on Breaks and select Section Break Next Page.  Then go into the header of the end matter pages and it should say "Same as Previous."  While in the header, go to Header and Footer tools menu and click on Link to Previous.  This will unlink the end matter header from the body text header.  Then you can delete the text that is there and type in your new end matter text. Do the same procedure for the footer.

macropod say:

It appears you are working with a mailmerge, in which case your:

Statement One field should be constructed along the lines of:

{IF{MERGEFIELD SFL5_PROJECTS_NOT_TO_EXCEED_CLAUSE}= "True" "Customer has purchased Services from Red8 that shall not exceed {MERGEFIELD SFL5_PROJECTS_WORK_PRODUCT_TOTALS \# "$,0.00"} and Customer will pay Red8 as each Service described herein is performed." "Customer has purchased the Services on a Firm-Fixed Price basis from Red8 and will pay to Red8 {MERGEFIELD SFL5_PROJECTS_WORK_PRODUCT_TOTALS \# "$,0.00"} for the performance of the Services set forth in this SOW (plus taxes if applicable)."}

Statement Two field should be constructed along the lines of:

  

{IF{MERGEFIELD SFL5_PROJECTS_NOT_TO_EXCEED_CLAUSE}= "True" "Customer has purchased Services described in this document on a Time and Materials basis at the rate(s) indicated below. Actual hours worked will be billed monthly.

Any change to the terms of this SOW or the scope of the Services may result in a modification to the SOW Fees.

Fee Schedule

Resource Type Estimated Hours Rate Total
Sr. Engineering Consultant 80 $190.00 $15,200.00
Project Management 12 $160.00 $1,920.00
Travel     $3,125.00
Total (estimated)     $20,245.00

" "Customer has purchased the Services on a Firm-Fixed Price basis from Red8 and will pay to Red8 {MERGEFIELD SFL5_PROJECTS_WORK_PRODUCT_TOTALS \# "$,0.00"} for the performance of the Services set forth in this SOW (plus taxes if applicable)."}

Note the reduced number of field braces (i.e. { }) and the numeric picture switch ( \# "$,0.00") for the currency format. Note also that the field brace pairs (i.e. '{ }') for the above examples are all created in the document itself, via Ctrl-F9 (Cmd-F9 on a Mac); you can't simply type them or copy & paste them from this message. Although you could insert the mergefields via the standard Word dialogues, you would need to have Word's field code display toggled 'on' (e.g. via Alt-F9) in order to add the switches. The spaces represented in the field constructions are also all required.

If its just the games closing and returning you to the dashboard the Luckily you can actually fix that issue....

Goto Settings, then power options and change the power mode from Instant-On to Energy Saving and then turn off the system.  

Reason why it works is when Instant-On is on its basically sends the system into a standby mode when you turn it off instead of fully shutting it down your console never fully gets to shut down and the xbox one never gets to boot up fresh.  Energy Saving mode will let the system fully turn off.  When using energy saving mode the startup of the system can take like a min or so.  Once your issue is fixed simply re-enable instant on and it should work like normal.  Just repeat if you have the issue again.  Or you could just leace it on energy saving if you can stand the long startup screen.  Its weird that the system has this issue,  hope its fixable threw a update. =/

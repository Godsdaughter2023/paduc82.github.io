They are genuine Microsoft product keys for retail licenses, but not for Retail sale. Create an Outlook Calendar to Share and Sync with Recipients Calendar

Hello,

Wondering if there is a way to create a calendar in Outlook and share it with someone so when they open it they can sync those dates from the calendar I'm sharing into their personal calendar.

marvin ma msft say:

Hi Jade,

Thank you for posting your concerns in Microsoft Community. For this issue, it's feasible for Outlook to create a new blank calendar and share it with someone, while it's not feasible to sync the events of this shared calendar to their own personal calendar in real time. 

To create a new blank calendar, you can switch to Calendar mode and click Add Calendar button in the ribbon.

And then click Share Calendar button to set up who can view this calendar.

After this, they can view this calendar in their calendar list. But it's infeasible to sync the events to their own personal calendar. So if you prefer to sync the events between calendars, we suggest you feedback in Outlook UserVoice website to share your ideas. Microsoft puts a great value on the feedback of customers. It's highly appreciated for your understanding.

Regards,

Marvin

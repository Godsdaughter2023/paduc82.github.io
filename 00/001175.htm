Delete one drive folder on second computer

if i delete the one drive folder on my laptop will it also delete one drive files from my desktop?

don cuthbert say:

Unless you uninstall OneDrive, you might find it difficult to delete that folder.  If you only delete the files in it, while OneDrive is active, it may interpret that as wanting to delete the files stored online also, which will eventually synchronize with your Desktop and could delete those files too.

Normally, local copies of files deleted from the online storage are moved to the Recycle Bin.

If what you actually want is to remove the local copies of files from the laptop, while leaving them on OneDrive (cloud storage) go to OneDrive settings (on the laptop). On the Account tab, Choose Folders and deselect the option at the top of that page. This should eliminate the local copies of files on the laptop, while preserving them online and on the Desktop. You could also chose to keep local copies of only some OneDrive folders on the laptop.

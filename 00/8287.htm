a. user say:

Hi jackholden,

 

Welcome to Microsoft Community. I understand that you are unable to locate your emails on your desktop. This issue is possibly caused by your POP3 settings. POP3 is a mail protocol that is used when accessing account in mobile devices or email client. This type of protocol downloads your messages and automatically deletes it on the server.

 

To prevent this from happening, please enable the option to leave a copy of the message on the server when using POP. You may refer to these steps:

  1. Sign in your account and go to your inbox.
  2. Click the cog icon located at the upper right of the page and select More mail settings.
  3. Under Managing your account, click POP and deleting downloaded messages.
  4. Click the button beside Don't let another program delete messages from Outlook.
  5. Select Save.

If the issue still persists, please let us know.

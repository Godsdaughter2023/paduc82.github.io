hansv mvp say:

I'd create helper columns. Let's say the company names are in column A, starting in A2. If necessary, insert a blank column in column B. In B2, enter the formula =LEFT(A2,4) and fill down as needed. Do the same on the other sheet.

You can now match the sheets on column B.

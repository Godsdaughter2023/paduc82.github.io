a. user say:

Hi mostafa,mostafa,mostafa,

 

Let me assist you with your concern. We understand that you want to reduce the font size of an email when printing in Outlook.com. I would like to inform you that it is by design that when you preview an email, there is no option to reduce its font size. link.

 

As a workaround, you can follow the steps below on how to edit an email and print it with your preferred font settings.

  1. Select an email that you want to print.
  2. Click ... and select Print.
  3. From the print preview page, copy the content of the email.
  4. Open a Microsoft Word (or any Microsoft Office application).
  5. Paste the content of the email.
  6. Edit it to your preferred font size and style.

I hope I was able to help you. If you have any other questions, feel free to post back.

Hi Ziid,

1. Yes, we can use the Configuration.xml file to configure all installation options for Click-to-Run for Office 365 products when using the ODT.

2. It is doable. Please refer to Deploy Office 2013 by using Group Policy computer startup scripts, which is also applied to Office 365 ProPlus.

3. You may deploy Office through the ODT and further Configure update settings for Office 365 ProPlus by using Group Policy setting.

4. Group Policy settings can be used regardless of whether you deploy Office 365 ProPlus to your users by using the Office Deployment Tool.

6. If we decide to make a change after we configure the GPO, we can update the Group Policy setting by using the Group Policy Management Console. The updated policy settings are automatically applied to Office 365 ProPlus, through the normal Group Policy update process.

7. We can take using ODT and GPO as the first option to manage Office if we have a large users in our organization. You can further refer Determine the deployment method to use for Office 365 ProPlus.

In response to your and the original poster's issue:

Skype numbers are landlines and are part of the telephone system. Due to this, you will be open to the same issues you would run into on a traditional phone line. Until the global telephone infrastructure makes some changes or new standards where controls or filters are added by every telco, unverified Caller ID spoofing and robo-dialing will still be a reality. Disabling discoverability and only allowing calls from contacts are methods to help eliminate calls to your account, not Skype number.

aashish g say:

Hi Backwithabang,

 

Thank you for posting your question on Windows Vista Forums.

 

Do you get indication or some error message including an application or device when you receive the DEP error? (Please Specify).

 

Since you have already formatted the hard drive and reinstalled Windows Vista on the machine, it seems that the issue is because of a device attached to the machine.

 

You can try to unplug any external devices and check if the issue persists. You can also try to disable certain internal devices to check if one of them is causing the issue. Please follow the given steps to verify if some hardware is causing the issue:

 

Follow the steps below to perform hardware clean boot:

1.       Click Start and then right click on Computer.

2.       Click on Manage.

3.       Click Device Manager and the left hand side.

4.       Right Click on Sound Card, Video Adapter, and Network Adapter one by one and click disable.

5.       Reboot the computer. 

If the problem is fixed then you can enable the hardware devices one by one to find the exact device that causes the problem.

 

If you are receiving information about the application or device throwing the DEP error, you can also try to disable DEP for the same. You can follow the given steps:

1.       Click Start and then right click on Computer.

2.       Click on Properties.

3.       On the right hand side, click on Advanced System Settings.

4.       Click on Settings under the Performance group.

5.       Click on Data Execution Prevention tab.

6.       Choose the 2 option “Turn on DEP for all programs and services except the one I select.

7.       Select the application or device name suggested under the list or you may the same if required.

8.       Restart the computer.

 

Hope the above information helps. Please post back and let us know.

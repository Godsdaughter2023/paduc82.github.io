That's a VBA error. Here's how to fix it:

  1. Open Tools>Templates and Add-ins.
  2. Check the Global Templates and Add-ins panel for the Adobe Acrobat add-in.
  3. Below the window, check Full path to see the path to the add-in file. Make a note of it.
  4. Close Word.

  6. Navigate to the path you noted and drag the file to your desktop.
  7. Restart Word to check that the error no longer occurs.

Thanks Jay,

I always do what you requested, have for years. 

I did more investigation in my Outlook account and folder settings on all three devices [iPad, iPhone, & MAC], here is something for all to consider.

Turns out the solution is more subtle when it comes to IMAP accounts; such as Compuserve that I use.

In CS and the Outlook apps; Messages end up in Sent folder or in the Sent Messages folder on IMAP accounts; depending how you set up the Outlook app for the IMAP email on the iPhone & iPad.

In Outlook 2016 for MAC you should choose The "Sent (Server)" folder in Advanced Settings: Folders.

{there is no option to choose "Sent Messages (Server)"}

Therefore you need to insure that in the iPad & iPhone Outlook settings for the IMAP account; you make "Sent" the main folder [not "Sent Messages"].

I copied/moved the emails in Sent Messages folder that I wanted into "Sent", and all three devices & apps have all emails sent in sync.

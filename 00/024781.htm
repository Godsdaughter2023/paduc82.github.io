glenprouty say:

Hi Haydnguy,

The CFA feature functions, by blocking APPS from altering the contents of certain

protected folders. Apps that are not recognized by W10 and its "White List".

You can see the folders, that are involved in this procedure, by scrolling down to

"Ransomware protection", on the "Virus & threat protection" screen in WD, and

selecting "Manage ransomware protection". Then select "Protected folders", below

the CFA "ON" switch. You are not altering these

pass through CFA. You are adding the App that was blocked, to the "White List".

Ransomware intends to encrypt or alter the contents of these protected folders.

CFA only allows apps on the "White List" to do that. If you only add apps to the

"White List, that you want, you prevent ransomware from attacking your PC.

Anything from the internet, that attempts to alter the protected folders, is also

subject to passing the White List.

Before v1809 Build 17763.55, it's a little "user unfriendly", to add apps to the list.

Not hard, but inconvenient. When you are at that build, it is straight forward and

simple. One click on the app, from the recently blocked apps list, and it's done.

Regards,  Glen

how to delete a page in a middle of document in office starter 2010 - Microsoft Community

suman6saha say:

how to delete a page in a middle of document in office starter 2010

i want to delete a paga in middle of a document in office starter 2010
jay freedman say:
Click at the top of the page. Use the vertical scroll bar to bring the bottom of the page onto the screen. Hold the Shift key while you click at the end of the page, which will select all the text on that page. Press the Delete key.

If the next paragraph is still on the next page, then one of two things is true: Either there is a manual page break there, or the next paragraph is formatted to start a new page.

You can click the ¶ button on the ribbon to show the nonprinting marks, and if you see a dotted line with the words "Page Break" in the middle of it, select it and delete it. Click the ¶ button again to turn off the marks.

If there is no page break, right-click in the paragraph that starts the new page and choose Paragraph from the menu. In the Paragraph dialog, click the Line & Page Breaks tab. If there is a check mark in the box for "Page break before", click to remove the check mark, and click OK.

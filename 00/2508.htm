a. user say:

Hi,

To create a birthday and anniversary reminders in outlook contacts, please follow the below steps.

1. Open outlook, click on contacts tab from Mail tab.

2. Now create a new contact or open an existing contact.

3. Now follow the steps mentioned in the below article to add a birthday and anniversary reminders to outlook contacts.

https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Add-a-birthday-or-anniversary-for-a-contact-333CCB47-1D57-405A-8D61-3CC39B80912E

Note: The above article applies to Office 2016 as well.

After updating the contacts with birthday and anniversary reminders, please follow the below steps.

1. Click on calendar tab, now in the search option, type birthday.

2. It will list all the birthdays events that are present in the calendar.

3. Now you can select all the events , right click and copy the events.

4. Paste in excel/word and send the file as attachment.

5. The other way to send, select all the events , right click and click on quick print.

6. Give the file name for the .PDF and send the .PDF file as attachment.

7. Now you may follow the same steps for anniversary events.

Hope the information helps. Let us know if you need further assistance.

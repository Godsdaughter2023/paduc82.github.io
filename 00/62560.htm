32 GBs would eventually fill up if you have to store the operating system

- Windows 10 (which uses around 8 to 10 Gbs of space),

- operating system cumulative updates, which can be anywhere 500 MB to 1 GBs

- App updates from the Windows Store 

- Your personal files

Your computer is not safe with Updates disabled. Updating your computer is a healthy part of maintaining it security. Its a hallmark of Windows 10 to install updates; not doing so could put your system at risk.

How to Redirect Your Personal Folders in Windows 7 - Notebooks.com

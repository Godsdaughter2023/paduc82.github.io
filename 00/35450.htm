sandra hayward say:

Select the picture or pictures you want to add a tag to.

Right click.

Select Properties.

Click the “Details” tab.

Under the Description section you will see Tags listed. To the right of it you will see a Value column. Hover your mouse in that column where Tags is listed. You will see that area framed by a border. Click your mouse in that field. You can now paste or enter the information that you want. When done, click OK.

If you have to go back into edit, just click on that area again to get in. You will have to double click to see all the information. Edit as needed and click OK to save.

When you hover your mouse over a picture, it will show the following information: Item Type, Date Taken, Tags (if information is entered), Rating, Dimensions and Size. If it doesn't, make sure you click somewhere in the window that your picture is in.

If you still can’t call up your pictures by your tags, I think you may have to have your files indexed? Not exactly sure about that. I had that problem before and I had my Tech Support remote in to fix.

Hope this helps. :-)

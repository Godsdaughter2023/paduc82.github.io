young24601 say:

After searching for a long time, I was able to fix this problem of working fine in Skype but not in the Camera app.

1) Go to device manager by right clicking This PC -> Manage and going to "Device Manager"

2) Find the QuickCam 9000 in "Imaging Devices" and right click and "Update Driver Software"

3) Select "Browse my computer for driver software"

4) "Let me pick from a list of device drivers on my computer"

5) Pick "USB Video Device" and NOT "Logitech QuickCam Pro 9000".

This enabled my QuickCam 9000 to work in Skype and also the Windows 10 Camera App.

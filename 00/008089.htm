Open a new workbook.  Customize as you wish.  File>Save As Type:  scroll down
to Excel Template(*.XLTX) and select.  Name your workbook "BOOK"(no quotes).
Excel will add the .XLTX to save as BOOK.XLTX.

Store this workbook in the XLSTART folder.

This will be the default workbook for File>New>Workbook.

You can also open a new workbook and delete all but one sheet.  Customize as

    swischner say:

    • Create an additional free Microsoft account
    • Use that new account to sign in your OneNote app on Android instead of your original one
    • Login to another OneNote client (PC, Mac) or OneNote Online with your old account
    • Share all notebooks with your new account one by one. Set access rights to read only for those you want to prevent from accidentally being edited on your phone.

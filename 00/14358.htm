christine delaurentis say:

Theme Colors are specific to Themes and Templates whereas Standard Colors stand alone, apart from the Theme.

 

If you choose a Theme Color for anything (font, shape, etc) and then switch from one Theme to another the color will change to the same exact color slot in the new Theme (ie. Accent 2, Lighter 80%).

 

Conversely, if you choose a Standard Color for anything (font, shape, etc) that color will remain unchanged when switching from Theme to Theme.

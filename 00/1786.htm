PClem,

Well actually with a 24 hour clock 16.5 hours after 0800 is 0030, not 1230.

Yes there is an easier formula now that we know you want to add continuous time. Jan's answer is on the money but there's a little bit more to it than meets the eye. If the interval is in hours, the DateAdd function only works with whole hours, so 16.5 won't work. However you can convert it all to minutes and your all set

Finish1=DateAdd("n",990,[Start])

"How do I create a set of backup recovery disks?"

Your Acer computer includes Acer eRecovery Management software that allows you to perform restore operations directly from your hard disk drive, allowing for a faster recovery process and no need for discs. Acer eRecovery Management does, however, allow you to create recovery discs. The recovery discs will allow you to restore your computer should something happen to your hard disk drive.

To create your backup recovery disks follow these steps:

  3. Windows User Account Control may ask for your permission to run this program, click Yes.

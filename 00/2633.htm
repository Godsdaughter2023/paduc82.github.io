amit tandon say:

Presuming cell A2 = Salary, A3 = %contribution, the you may use either of the below formulas:

=(MIN(A3,3%)+MIN(MAX(A3-3%,0),2%)/2)*A2

or

=(MIN(A3,3%)/2+MIN(A3,5%)/2)*A2

These will calculate the 401k company match - 100% for the first 3%, then 50% for the next 2%.

Justify Tab in Text Box

rich007a say:

Hi,

Can you see the ruler at the top?  (If not, View tab > Show group > Ruler).

Can you see the small square just to the left of the ruler?  It probably has an L shape in it.

Click it and it will cycle through each type of tab stop.  Click it twice and you will get a mirror image of an "L".  That is a righthand tab stop.

 

Click on the ruler where you want that tabstop.  One press of the tab key should jump the cursor to that point and the text will be right aligned.

 

Alternatively, you can right-click on the text > Paragraph > Tabs > then set the Right tabstop manually.

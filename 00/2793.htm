Uncheck the boxes "Ignore colors specified on webpages" and "Ignore font styles specified on webpages" in the Formatting section of the Accessibility window if you want to change the colors back to the default settings.

sanjaymehta---01 say:

First of all, don't listen to people who recommend reformatting the phone for every simple thing.

 

SMS and call notifications get hidden if you press "delete" on the notification. Switching them back on is buried here:

 

Go to Tools - Settings - General - Personalization - Home Screen -  Home Screen Applications - Enabled Applications

 

Scroll through  the list to turn specific notifications etc on or off. There should be no need to reformat.

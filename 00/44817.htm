A VGA splitter is not a true dual monitor solution.  It is merely a way to DUPLICATE a single VGA signal out to multiple VGA output devices.  In other words - you can mirror, not extend.
 
You want/need a video card that natively supports dual monitor setups in order to extend your screens across multiple monitors instead of having a mirror setup.

For more detailed information on Services go to  -
Each Service has a brief description of what it does. Only disable or stop services you are confident your system does not use.

Using Clean Boot is one way of troubleshooting background processes that may be slowing down your machine or conflicting with games/software.
How to troubleshoot a problem by performing a clean boot in Windows Vista or in Windows 7

Also be sure to keep your HDD  defragmented,  and run Disk Cleanup frequently to keep the Temp folder from getting too large.

My understanding is World of Tanks is a cpu intensive game, so you need a good cpu if you are going to turn up some of the in-game settings.
Of course your graphics card also has to be up to the task of running the game with whatever settings you are using.
If you get mouse lag and have V-sync enabled be sure to enable  Triple Buffering  in your garphics cards profile for that game, or just disable V-sync in the games settings.

A big possibility for lag could be the games online servers are not up to the traffic they get, or your own internet connection is not fast enough or the 'ping' to the server you are playing on is too high.

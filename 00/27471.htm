windows phone remote lock not working

duanekucinich say: I figured out how to fix this.  You go to windowsphone.com, and account settings (upper right corner, put cursor on your name).  Then below your phone picture (middle right) you should see "remove this phone".  Click remove (dont worry, you dont lose your skydrive, or apps).  Then go to your actual phone "Settings\email+accounts". Tap "Windows Live" (the first one if you have more than one).  Re-enter your password.  After about 15 minutes I got a welcome to windows phone email at that email address, when I opened Windowsphone.com it looked like it did before.  The padlock icon next to "Lock" was gone, this is how it looked before I remote locked my phone the first time and could not lock it again.  I am scared to try remote lock again, so I haven't yet.  I may try that soon, because I really need to verify that this works.  Please post if this fix works out for you.

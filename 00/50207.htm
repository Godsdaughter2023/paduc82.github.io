  5. On the Startup tab in Task Manager, for each startup item, select the item and then click Disable.
  6. Close Task Manager.
  7. On the Startup tab of the System Configuration dialog box, tap or click OK, and then restart the computer.

My name is Angel. I am an Independent Advisor. Thank you for posting in Microsoft Community.

Try this:
-Disable (preferrably uninstall) your Antivirus utility before attempting the upgrade.
-Disable General USB Devices (example - Smart Card Reader).
-Do a clean boot and try again.
-Disconnect from the Internet during setup, if you are connected by LAN (Ethernet) or Wi-Fi, disable both and attempt setup again.

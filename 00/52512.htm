Method 1:

Initially, I suggest you to update the display driver from the device manager and check if it helps.

Follow the below steps.

a) Press Windows +X keys on the keyboard.

b) Select Device Manager.

c) Find and display drivers and expand it.

d) Right-click the display drivers, and click Update.

e) Follow the onscreen instructions to update display drivers driver software.

f) Reboot the computer and check.

Method 2:

Uninstall sound adapter driver from the Device Manager, restart the computer and check if that helps you.

  1. Press Windows and X keys together.

  4. Restart the computer after the successful uninstallation of sound Device.

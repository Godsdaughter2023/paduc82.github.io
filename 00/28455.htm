We understand you'd like to have a walk through about the 30 day lock-out period. First, let me inform you about the policy bar. However, you can still access your mailboxes through Outlook.com. Other Microsoft services that require security code upon sign in such as Xbox Live & Billing, 30-day lock out process will apply.

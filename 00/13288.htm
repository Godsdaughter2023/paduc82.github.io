Hello & welcome,

What should I know about the 30-day waiting period?

Even when you choose to delete your out-of-date security info, MS don't fully remove it for 30 days. This waiting period helps to deter hackers from updating your security info and gaining access to your account without your knowledge.

After 30 days, the user of the account will be able to access the security settings and update his/her preferred information.

But the waiting period doesn't mean you're locked out of your Microsoft account.

So you need the content of the body... the copy and paste method doesn't work well with bodies.

You have two options:

Create a task folder for exporting the tasks then select all and copy (NOT Move) the tasks you need to export to the new task folder. Export the folder then delete the copied tasks (to avoid confusion).

The other option is using a macro to export them to a spreadsheet.

If you need to do this frequently the macro will be faster in the long run, but for occasional use, copy and paste to a temporary folder should suffice.

If you just need to get the details from the body into separate columns in Excel - VENDOR, HIRED, COMPANY, etc - you'll need to use a macro.

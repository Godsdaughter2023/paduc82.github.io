hansv mvp say:
  1. Select L4.
  2. On the Home tab of the ribbon, click Conditional Formatting > Manage Rules.
  3. Click New Rule...
  4. Select 'Use a formula to determine which cells to format'.
  5. Enter the formula =K4<0
  6. Click Format...
  7. Specify orange as font color or fill color.
  8. Click OK twice.
  9. Repeat steps 3 ... 8, but with =K4>0 and green.
  10. Finally click OK.
  11. Use the Format Painter to apply the same formatting to the cells below L4.

Alternatively, you could have selected L4:L300 in step 1. Excel will automatically adjust the formula for L5, L6 etc.

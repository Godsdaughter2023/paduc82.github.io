Sub CheckPivotData()
Dim varPCol As Variant, varCell As Variant
Dim varPRow As Variant, varGRow As Variant, lngRow As Long
    For Each varPCol In Me.PivotTables("MyPivot").PivotFields("Company_Property").PivotItems
        For Each varGRow In Me.PivotTables("MyPivot").PivotFields("P&L Group").PivotItems

amit tandon say:

Couple of options:

Option 1: Enter below formula as an array formula (Ctrl+Shift+Enter) in cell B2 & copy down:

=SUM(IF(FREQUENCY(IF((Sheet1!$B$2:$B$100>=EOMONTH($A2,-1)+1)*(Sheet1!$B$2:$B$100<=EOMONTH($A2,0)),IF(Sheet1!$A$2:$A$100<>"",MATCH(Sheet1!$A$2:$A$100,Sheet1!$A$2:$A$100,0))),ROW(Sheet1!$A$2:$A$100)-ROW(Sheet1!$A$2)+1),1))

Option 2: Enter below formula as an array formula (Ctrl+Shift+Enter) in cell C2 & copy down:

=SUM(IF(Sheet1!$A$2:$A$100<>"",IF((Sheet1!$B$2:$B$100>=EOMONTH($A2,-1)+1)*(Sheet1!$B$2:$B$100<=EOMONTH($A2,0)),1/(COUNTIFS(Sheet1!$B$2:$B$100,">="&EOMONTH($A2,-1)+1,Sheet1!$B$2:$B$100,"<="&EOMONTH($A2,0),Sheet1!$A$2:$A$100,Sheet1!$A$2:$A$100))),0))

Note: Column A should be a valid date, any date of the month (ex. cell A2 can be any date between March 01, 2018 to March 31, 2018). The formulas match both the month and year.

daniel pineault say:

Whenever you are performing development (ie editing queries ...) you should use a local copy of the BE.  This will drastically improve performance and also avoid any accidental mistakes to the production data.  Once you are done your development, you relink it to the production BE and place the new version on the server for all to use.

As for the query being slow to run, there could be a number of factors influencing this.  Things like, first and foremost your network itself, do you use persistent connections at the startup of your database, ...

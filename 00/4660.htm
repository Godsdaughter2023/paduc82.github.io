bob jones aka: cybertaz say:
Protection features in Mac Word are not as extensive as what's provided in the Win Office version. On the Mac version there is no Restrict Editing pane - a Section is either Protected or it's not.

You can create Section Breaks then specify which Sections should be protected... when you go to Tools> Protect Document the Forms option will be active along with the Sections... button.

Even though Headers & Footers can differ from one section to another all H/F are a part of the same 'container' in the document. I can't think of any way in Mac Word to enable protection of some but not others.

There could be something wrong with the computer, the MagicJack or both. Not all of your USB ports
are powered, and the MagicJack pulls a lot of power, so it is important that you are using 1 of the 2
(some have 4) powered ports on the computer. Besides the 2 (some 4) powered ports all the rest of
the USB ports are not powered and are only suitable for devices that do not need much power. Actually
it is best to use a Powered Hub or 2 which are not expensive.

To find out which of your USB ports are powered refer to the documentation both on disk and on the
System maker's website about your model, the System maker's Support and/or their forums if any.

sfc /scannow

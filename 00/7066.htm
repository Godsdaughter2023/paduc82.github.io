How are the headings in the document numbered?

If you press Alt+F9 to toggle on the display of the field codes in the document, what do you see in place of the table of contents?

For a table of contents that is created from Outline Levels (Heading 1, Heading 2, Heading 3) you would generally see:

{ TOC \o "1-3" \h \z \u }

{ TOC \h \z \t "Heading 1,2,Schedule L1,1,Schedule L2, 2,Schedule L3, 3,Chapter heading,1,Title,1,Annexure,1" }

Style                             TOC level

Heading 1                          2

Schedule L1                        1

Schedule L2                        2

Schedule L3                        3

Chapter heading                1

Title                                     1

Annexure                             1

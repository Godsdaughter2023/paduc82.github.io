What you're expecting is referred to as forced justification. I honestly don't know of any word processing program that uses it as a default. Many modern word processing programs don't even offer it as an option due to the detrimental effect on readability & appearance... Word dropped it eons ago :-) It's typically available in page layout software & graphic design programs which offer a more comprehensive array of typesetting & copyfitting features.

This is a feature called double-tap and drag.  It is quite sensitive, but what you do is touch the Touchpad quickly twice in a row and that locks it in select mode.  Remove your finger from the Touchpad and it should release.

If you don't want to use the feature, you can turn it off in the Settings app.

gary coombs say:

I use the double-tap and drag a lot. On the second tap you do NOT lift your finger but while keeping it solidly on the pad, you then start dragging. While dragging in a line of characters you then see the line being progressively marked until you lift.

Now with the line nicely marked, tap the marked line simultaneously  with two fingers (this becomes a right-click) and then you will get the right click menu where you can decide what to do with the marked line.

You can also double tap and hold with a single finger and drag what you have marked in the same way you marked it to move the marked line around.

Until you practice this a bit, it can be a bit confusing to do. After practicing it becomes second nature to do.

Hi,

Method 1:

I would suggest you to follow these steps and check if allow this device to wake the computer is checked in Device Manager.

  1. Click Start, type Device manager in the Start search box and hit Enter.
  2. Select keyboard, it will display your keyboard, right click and select ‘Properties’.

  4. Check the “Allow this device to wake the computer” check box.

van_m say:

Hi ScottRamsay_wombat,

 

Since Skype is now one of the product services of Microsoft. Skype accounts needs to be updated or migrated to a Microsoft account.

 

https://support.skype.com/en/faq/FA34652/bringing-skype-and-microsoft-accounts-together

 

If your Skype account is not yet migrated and you want to change your password, you will still be prompted to go through the updating process of the Skype account. As all recovery process for Skype accounts need to go through Microsoft. Also, you will need to verify your identify before you can change your password by getting a security code to any of your security proofs or recovery options.

 

You can use a different Microsoft account (Email address) for your other account. Here’s a link below for more information on how to update your Skype account

https://support.skype.com/en/faq/FA34657/a-single-sign-in-for-skype-and-your-other-microsoft-services

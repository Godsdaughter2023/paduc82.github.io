Yes, sure.  If you view the Resource Sheet, you will see a column (field) for initials.  This needs filling in - I suggest for example Fred Blogs as Blogs for the Resource Name and FB in the Initials column.  Then, from the Gantt Chart view, Format Ribbon, in the Bar Styles tab select Format and choose Bar Styles..  In the dialog, you should see Task highlighted in the Name column.  At the bottom select the Text tab, and you will see all 5 positions where text can be shown. a. user say:

Hi MartaIQ,

 

Did you set up your account in such a way that emails will instead go to your gmail account even though it's addressed to your MSN account?

 

If so, it's  possible that you won't receive emails in your MSN account if you didn't choose to leave a copy to your MSN account. All messages are instead forwarded to the email address that you choose to send emails which are addressed to your MSN account.

 

It's great to know that Hotmail can keep a copy of the forwarded emails into your MSN account. In effect, you will have a copy of an email both in your MSN account and another account (including 3rd party yet a registered Microsoft email address). For this to take effect, please follow the steps below:

 

1. Sign in to your MSN account.

2. Go to inbox  and proceed to Managing your account.

3. Look for  Email Forwarding.Tick the circle before Forward your mail to another email account.

4. Provide the email address under Where do you want your messages to be sent?

5. Tick the box before Keep a copy of forwarded messages in your Hotmail inbox.

 

You must have forgotten to do step 5, that's why.

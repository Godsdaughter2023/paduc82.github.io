word 2010 apostrophes appear as equal sign to my recipients - Microsoft Community

dirtylittlerabbits say:

word 2010 apostrophes appear as equal sign to my recipients

 

 hi..

i sent a word 2010 docs to some contacts . when they opened it, all apostrophe contents appear as equal sign on their end..

why is that??

 

 they have office 2007 on their comps.

 

 

suzanne s. barnhill say:
This sort of thing usually results from legacy documents that began life in WordPerfect and have used the WP Typographic Symbols font for single and double quotation marks, dashes, and the like. When opened on a computer where that font is not installed, another character is substituted, often a capital A, @ sign, or the like. Given that the apostrophe in the WP Typographic Symbols font is character 61, and that this is also the ASCII number for the equals sign in most non-symbol fonts, I'd say this is a strong possibility.

simpelecjess say: With the above being said about the document being started in WordPerfect; is there a way that the person that is receiving the file on the new computer without WordPerfect get this formatted to show correctly or do they need to have WordPerfect installed on that computer as well.

suzanne s. barnhill say:
The best approach would be to correct the error at your end, but that's more difficult when you can't see the errant characters (unless you uninstall the font). On either end, the only real recourse is to search for the characters and replace them. This is not too difficult with @ and =, more difficult for, say, A (unless it's an A with some sort of accent, which I don't remember, as I haven't dealt with this problem in a number of years and don't currently have the font installed).

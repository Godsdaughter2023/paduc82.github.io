The arroba or @ symbol is on different keys, depending on the keyboard layout you use. I have a fix for this problem on the iPAQ 210.    The (HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\Software\Microsoft\Inbox\Svc\SMS) registry key needs to be deleted after Garmin XT is installed.

This is the process I ran through:   I compared the registry before Garmin XT was installed to the registry after Garmin XT was removed from my iPAQ.  I did this because the problem still existed after Garmin XT was uninstalled.

Of the registry keys left after Garmin XT was uninstall the following key : (HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\Software\Microsoft\Inbox\Svc\SMS)

was the key that seemed to be the most logical suspect key as it was under the INBOX key and the value of its subkey pointed to one of the CLSIDs installed by Garmin XT.

I deleted this key and the problem was corrected.

I did another Hard Reset, installed Garmin XT, and the create email problem was present. I deleted this key and the problem was corrected.  I did another Hard Reset, installed Garmin, uninstalled Garmin, and the problem was present. I deleted this key and the problem was corrected.  I ran some small tests on Garmin XT and email account creation, and everything seems OK.

This has only been tested on the iPAQ 210, and I would recommend you save the registry before deleting the key.  I will also send this fix to Garmin under the ticket I opened with them.

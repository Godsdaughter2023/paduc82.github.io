If the part of your worksheet that will be printed is too wide or too tall for one page, they only way to print it on one page is to reduce the scaling to less than 100%. You cannot fit it on one page AND keep the scaling at 100% - that is contradictory.

jiaxing bian msft say: Hi ltheONEl,

SharePoint Online will try to send email notifications to the address that’s same with your Office 365 login account. Since no mailbox is associated with this account, you will not receive any emails.

As a workaround, you can replace the Work email in User Profile with steps below:
1. Go to Office 365 admin center > SharePoint > user profiles > People > Manage User Profiles.
2. Search for the user and click Find, then click the account name and select Edit My Profile.
3. Find Work email, and replace the original email address with your personal email address. Then save the change.

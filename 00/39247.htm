    Hmm - the unfortunate fact is you do get faulty batches of discs....these Memorex discs (usually a reliable brand) may have been old stock and started to deteriorate.     godliek say:

    Wow I finally managed to solve the problem.

    Here's what I did:

    The 0x80070003 error means Path not found, but what path?

    I used Process Monitor utility to check what path the .net installer is trying to access and found this:

    C:\Users\USERNAME\ntuser.dat

    The thing is that I DELETED this username and I'm using another one now!!

    So why was the installer trying to access this file in a user profile for a deleted user???

    I searched the registry for USERNAME and found it under HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows NT\CurrentVersion\ProfileList

    then i deleted this user's key.

    and Viola!


    success!!

Its normally hidden or located in C:\Boot

200 MB recovery partition
This is for the Windows Recovery Environment (WinRE), which also shipped with Windows Vista only it was available from the Windows Vista installation disc only, although some OEMs made it available on a separate partition on their factory installations. Given all the allegations surrounding Microsoft’s supposed cooperation with the United States government on implementing the PRISM surveillance program, it’s not too far-fetched to worry that the National Security Agency could one day ask Microsoft for access to video feeds of users’ living rooms through the Xbox One Kinect sensor. The big reason is that, unlike with telephone metadata, there’s no way for the government to plausibly argue that watching you in your home without a warrant is anything but an unlawful search.

Turn off conversation history at the tenant - Microsoft Community

eric cutcher say:

Turn off conversation history at the tenant

Our legal group doesn't want Skype for Business messages to be discoverable and thus has requested conversation history be disabled on all users or purged daily. I found reference to Set-CsArchivingPolicy, but that doesn't appear to be a valid powershell command for Skype for Business online. Any ideas out there to accomplish this for 1000+ users?

vasil l. michev say:

You can control this via the Client policies. To get a list of all the policies and the corresponding IM settings, use something like:

Get-CsClientPolicy | ft Identity,DisableSavingIM,EnableIMAutoArchiving

To get only the policies with disabled conversation history, use:

Get-CsClientPolicy | ? {$_.DisableSavingIM -eq $true} | ft Identity,DisableSavingIM,EnableIMAutoArchiving

If you only want to disable conversaton history and no other settings, assign the users the Tag:ClientPolicyNoSaveIMNoArchiving policy.

aaron zhu msft say: Hi Eric,

Have you referred to the information Vasil mentioned? Do you still need assistance? Moreover, you can also use the following command to configure the global client policy to achieve your goal for all users in your tenant.

Set-Csclientpolicy –Identity Global -DisableSavingIM $True -EnableIMAutoArchiving $False

Aaron

One thing that may cause this is if your dates are not actually dates as Excel expects them.

If you use the formula =TYPE(A1) where A1 is a cell containing a date a date would return a 1, a 2 indicates text.  It is also possible you can tell simply by looking at the alignment within the column left for text right for number, but formatting may alter this.

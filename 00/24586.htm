Well you are going to have to be a bit more specific on the sync issue.  The process of having a calendar entry go from your computer to device and visa versa is the same in Vista as it was in XP.

 

Of course the difference is XP used active Sync and Vista uses WMDC (Window Mobile Device Center). 

 

So you need to give a few more specifics so I can understand what is happening?

 

 

Ring tones are pretty easy to put on a device.  For yours, do the following:

 

Connect your Smartphone to your desktop computer with the USB cable.

Using ActiveSync or WMDC on your desktop computer, copy the ringtone file to the sounds folder of your device.

(The Sounds folder is located on the device in the Application Data folder).

 

Disconnect your device then press Start.

Select Settings, Select Sounds.

Then scroll through the ringtone choices and select the ringtone you just copied into the device.

Select Done and your NEW ringtone will work!

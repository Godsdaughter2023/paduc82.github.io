If you want the DVD content to be copied to your laptop, follow these steps.

b.      Right click on the DVD drive and select Open.

c.       Select any file you want to copy, right click and select Copy. (For Multiple files you can hold the CTRL key and click on each file you want. For all the files press the key combination CTRL+A).

a.       Open the email.

b.      Click New to create a new email message.

c.       To attach a file\picture, click “Attach” and then browse to the location where your file is located.

d.      Click on the file\picture and then click OK\Open\Insert, whichever option is available.

e.       The file\picture will now be attached to the email message.

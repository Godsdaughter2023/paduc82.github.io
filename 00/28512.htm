unable to edit converted pdf - Microsoft Community

dla700 say:

unable to edit converted pdf

After scanning a  statement on my Epson WF-3540 I saved as a PDF file.

Opened PDF with Word 2013 then converted to a Word Document.

I am not able to edit it.

Please help.

Dave

jay freedman say:
There are two kinds of PDF files -- those that contain formatted text, and those that contain only a picture of text. Your scanned statement is a picture of text.

When you use Word to open a PDF file that contains text, the text will be "reflowed" into the Word document, just as if you had copied the text from another Word document.

When you use Word to open a PDF that contains pictures, they stay pictures -- whether they're pictures of your cat, or pictures of text.

The necessary step that you're missing is Optical Character Recognition (OCR), which "reads" the picture and outputs text. Word doesn't do OCR at all. Your scanner might have come bundled with some OCR software, or you can buy software to do it.

If you have OneNote as part of your Office installation, you can paste the picture into it, right-click it, and click Copy Text from Picture. The text will be placed on the clipboard, and then you can paste it into Word. Then you'll have to reapply any formatting, and be prepared to do a lot of fixing of errors.

joel in vic say:

Group Texting should be available on the Lumia 640, if it is running Windows phone 8.1.   If you have upgraded the phone to Windows 10 Mobile I believe it hasn't been fully and properly implemented yet.

There are factors that will effect whether or not you can send/receive Group Texts on WP8.1.

  • Group Texting on WP8.1 might require that your service agreement with your carrier has MMS (Multimedia Messaging Service) enabled.
  • Group Texting on WP8.1 might require that you have Data service with your carrier turned on.  Although, if you do not want to actually USE data there is a work around for this.  Ask if this applies to you.

The first thing to check is that Group Texting is turned "ON".  Group Texting requires that the proper settings are enabled on your phone.  To check this, open Messaging --> tap the "..." in the bottom right --> tap "settings" --> toggle Group text to "on".

If it doesn't, post again, and let us know if you have a Data plan with your carrier.

gokul t say:

Hi,

 

Xerox/nwwia folder is part of the Windows Image Acquisition service. It’s not a virus/malware of any kind.

 

If you wish to delete the folder, follow these steps:

 

a.     Click “Start” and click “Run.”

b.     Type “services.msc” and hit “Enter.”

c.     Right-click “Windows Image Acquisition” service and click “Disable.”

d.     Restart your computer and delete the “\XEROX\NWWIA” folder.

 

Note:

If you use a scanner or transfer files from a digital camera to your computer, it’s best to leave the folder as it is.

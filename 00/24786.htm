steve rindsberg say:

PowerPoint only plays animations when you move to the slide from a previous slide.

You can fool it by adding a slide just ahead of the slide you're interested in and setting the slide to automatically advance to the next slide after 0 seconds, with no transition effects.

For all intents and purposes, that makes the newly added slide invisible during playback, but your animations (and sounds are a form of animation) will play on the subsequent slide.

Hi Celestine,

 

If you want to move the files in the OneDrive for Business library from one Office 365 business subscription to another Office 365 business subscription, you can follow the steps below.

a. Install and set up the new OneDrive sync client.

b. Sync the files from the OneDrive for Business library in the first tenant with it. For notebooks, you may create new notebooks in the OneDrive for Business library in the second tenant and then copy the data from the notebooks in the OneDrive for Business library in the first tenant to the new ones to avoid sync issues.

c. Right-click the client>Settings>Unlink OneDrive to stop sync.

d. Access the OneDrive for Business library and drag multiple files from the local sync folder to it.

 

For the mailbox migration, please use either the PST import or the IMAP migration.

a. Export or backup emails, contacts, and calendars to an Outlook .pst file.

b. Import emails, contacts, and calendars from an Outlook .pst file.

For your reference: Migrate other types of IMAP mailboxes to Office 365

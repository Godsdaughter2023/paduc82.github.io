ralph san say:

Error 0x800705b4 is related with Windows Defender. Temporarily disable all third party anti virus programs on your computer (if there's any). Then, make sure to turn on Windows Defender. Here's how:

  1. On your keyboard, press Windows + I (to open Settings).
  2. Select Update & Security.
  3. On the left pane, choose Windows Defender.
  4. Click Turn on Windows Defender Antivirus.
  5. Restart your computer for the changes to take effect.

Afterwards, try to install the latest update, and check if the issue will persist.

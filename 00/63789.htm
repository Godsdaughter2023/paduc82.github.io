    Battery plugged in not charging - Microsoft Community

    bobvinton say:

    Battery plugged in not charging

    I just solved my problem. I have a fairly new HP Laptop which came with WIN 8.1. No battery problem then. But then I did the 'so-called' upgrade to WIN 10, when the battery problem started. Because of this & other problems in WIN 10 I 'Rolled Back' to WIN 8.1. I found that while other problems had gone away, the battery problem Rolled Back into WIN 8.1.

    Today when the battery charge was showing as 48 %, purely on a whim, I tried this -

    Shut down the computer. Pulled the AC plug. Started the computer in battery mode. Used battery mode for 10 minutes or so on various operations.

    Shut down the computer again, replaced the AC plug, started the computer in AC.

    Then I found the battery charging rapidly to 80 % then 90 % & finally 'back to normal' - 'Fully charged (100%)'.

    I emphasize that this was in WIN 8.1 after the 'Roll Back'. I didn't try it while in WIN 10. So I can't guarantee that this simple solution will work for everybody. But it does seem to be worth a try. It worked for me.

    Don't ask me any technical stuff. I don't know anything about that. Just keep it simple.

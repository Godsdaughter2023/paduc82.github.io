rick rothstein, mvp - excel say:
What is the proper way to multiply Feet and inches in this format  3' 4", also for yards and inches in this format 1-24". Any help would be appreciated. thanks


You can use this formula to convert your first value into a real number of feet...

=LEFT(A1,FIND("'",A1)-1)+MID(LEFT(A1,LEN(A1)-1),FIND(" ",A1)+1,2)/12

and you can use this formula to convert your second value into a real number of feet...

=3*LEFT(A1,FIND("-",A1)-1)+MID(LEFT(A1,LEN(A1)-1),FIND("-",A1)+1,2)/12

You can then multiply, divide, or do whatever to them as needed.

=IFERROR(INDEX($D$12:$D$19,MATCH(C2,$C$12:$C$19,0)),"")

A B C D
1 Name Team Hours Company

4 Mandy 11 20 INS

I'd like to get a result like this:

Hours Per Team
A B C
Company Team 11 Team12
1 USA (Sum of Hours) (Sum of Hours)
2 INS (Sum of Hours) (Sum of Hours)

Is this possible using a sumproduct formula? 

Thanks for your help! 


mike h.. say:

Hi,

=SUMPRODUCT(--($D$3:$D$1159="USA"),--($B$3:$B$1159=11),$C$3:$C$1159)

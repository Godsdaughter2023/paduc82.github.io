Fill in color in picture

I drew a picture in Power point and want to color it in how do I do that
david marcovitz say: PowerPoint is a drawing tool, not a painting tool. Generally, it creates a bunch of shapes, rather than a bunch of dots. That means you can change the attributes of a shape easily (such as filling in an oval), but you can't add something easily based on the way the lines happen to intersect (such as the paint bucket tool in a painting program). You can try selecting all the pieces of your shape (possibly but not necessarily grouping them) and then copying them (ctrl-c). Then you can go into a painting program and paste them (ctrl-v) and see if you can get what you want.

1. Switch Battery saver mode off, or connect a charger to your phone: Tap Settings > battery saver > Off.

2. Move closer to a Wi-Fi access point to get a better signal.

3. If possible, try to create a connection with some other device that supports Wi-Fi (to see that the access point is really working).

4. If you are using a home Wi-Fi network:

  1. Unplug the power from your Wi-Fi router, and switch off your phone. Plug your router back in first, then wait for 1 minute and then switch on your phone.
  2. In the router settings, remove the WLAN security temporarily, and try to create a WLAN connection from the phone without any security settings first. Refer to your router's user manual to find detailed instructions.
  3. If you keep getting an Incorrect password error, log in to your wireless router, and change the operating mode 802.11b/g/n to 802.11g only, and then restart/reset the router, and try to create a connection again.
  4. Disable other active network services such as video streaming and online games.
  5. Check on the router's support page that it has the latest firmware installed.

5. On your phone:

  1. Tap Settings > Bluetooth and switch the status temporarily to Off.
  2. Delete the non-functioning Wi-Fi access point and connect it again: Go to Settings > Wi-Fi, under Manage known networks, tap and hold on the access point name and select Delete.

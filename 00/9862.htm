That sort of thing no longer exists out of the box.  There may be 3rd-party alternatives.

Hello KimLebech and Thor-MartinStrand,

 

We apologize for the delayed response on your concern about merging accounts on Outlook.com. Please be informed that merging of accounts is not possible.  Instead, you can Auto-forward your Hotmail account to your new Outlook account. You’ll have all of your mail in one place, and can reply to it using your new address.

 

To auto-forward your email:

 

1.       Log in to your Inbox.

2.       Click the gear icon located at the upper right part of the screen, then click More mail settings.

3.       Under Managing your account, click Email forwarding. You might have to sign in.

4.       Select the radio button for Forward your mail to another email account.

5.       Enter the email address for your new Outlook account.

6.       If you want, check the box for Keep a copy of forwarded messages in your Inbox.

7.       Click Save.

 

Note: Free Outlook.com account users need to log into the account they choose to auto-forward at least once every 365 days to avoid having the system remove the mailbox for inactivity.

 

Also, you can also link the two accounts together and set up your other email account to send and receive emails.

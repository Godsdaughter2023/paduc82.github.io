Original Title:HP4500 FAX

lemp say:
Original Title:HP4500 FAX

I have set up the All in One HP4500 Officejet wireless but the fax does not work. When doing the test, it says that I have the phone cord connected to 2-EXT and it should be in 1-LINE but that's where is already. Any idea what I'm doing wrong?

1.  Do you have an analog phone system ("plain old telephone service" or POTS)?  Answer should be Yes.

2.  Are you using the thephone cord that was supplied with the printer?  Answer should be Yes.

3.  If you connect a standard telephone to the wall jack instead of the printer, do you hear a dial tone when you pick up the phone? 
4.  Is there anything else connected between the printer and the wall jack (e.g., are you using a DSL filter or a splitter)?
5.  Is there anything (other than a blank plug that may have been supplied with the printer) in 2-EXT?
If your answers are not as indicated in bold, call HP support.  Also note that if you are in certain non-U.S. countries, your telephone system may not support anything connected to the 2-EXT connector.

With respect to Q. 1, the alternatives to an analog phone system are VoIP telephone service (e.g., through your cable company or other ISP) or a digital phone system (commonly found in businesses, e.g., a PBX).  If you have a digital system, you can't use the fax.  If you have VoIP you may (or may not) be able to use the fax, depending on your ISP.

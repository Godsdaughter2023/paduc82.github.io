cliff gu msft say:

Hi Matthew,

 

What’s the requirement of resetting the saving location for notebooks? Could you give us some detailed examples?

 

Generally, if you want to change the saving folder in the OneDrive for Business library, you can navigate to the OneDrive for Business library and then click Move to to change it. Then you can close the notebook and delete the parts that aren’t synced.

 

If you want to save the notebook to a site library in SharePoint Online, you may create a new notebook there and copy the content in the previous notebook from the OneDrive for Business library to the new one in the site library in SharePoint Online.

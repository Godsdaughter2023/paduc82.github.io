How do I save a word 2010 doc to a flash drive? The D: drive - Microsoft Community

williamweldy say:

How do I save a word 2010 doc to a flash drive? The D: drive does not come up as an option in "save as"

When I try to save a word 2010 doc to removable flash drive, it does not show up an an option. How do I do it?
jay freedman say:
I can't say why the removable drive doesn't appear -- on this Windows 7 installation, my flash drive does appear -- but I will tell you that it's probably for the best.

Documents saved directly from Word to any kind of removable drive, or opened and edited directly from a removable drive, have a high probability of becoming corrupted. There are a number of reasons for this, ranging from the way Word uses temporary files in the document's folder, to people's habit of pulling the drive out before all in-memory data has been written to the drive. The bottom line is that it's best not to do it, ever.

Always save new or modified documents to the hard drive. Only after the document is closed, use Windows Explorer to copy the file to the flash drive. When opening a document, copy it from the flash drive to the hard drive and work on it there. Don't think of it as an annoyance, but as insurance against losing the document.

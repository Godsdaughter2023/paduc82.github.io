Here are the answers for your questions:

1. So in reality will the first customer that associates a vanity domain to an Office 365 subscription own it forever or not?

The custom domain will stay associated with the first tenant forever, as long as he doesn’t remove it manually and the tenant is still in use.

First, the owner of the domain reports the conflict and we will ask him prove his ownership. If we verify he owns the domain, we will disassociate the domain from the holding tenant. Then the rightful owner can add it to his tenant.

g. Follow the instructions.

Update the Display Driver and check if that helps in fixing the issue.

a. Hold power button to shut down the system.

b. Press power button to start system.

c. Repeat the steps multiple times (twice or thrice until you see the recovery page).

d. After that, select "advanced repair option" in Recovery page

f. Select "Advanced options" in Troubleshoot page.

m. Choose, "Browse my computer for driver software”.

n. Choose, "Let me pick from a list of device drivers on my computer".

o. Select the graphics card driver, and click the next button.

p. Follow the same steps for the additional graphics card as well.

phillip m jones say:

If it is not too long, do the following and see what happens.

 

  1. Open Document and in home tab of Ribbon click on the "Show Hidden Characters" button shown with the symbol ¶ (looks like backward P).

  2. Click at the beginning of the Document.

  3. Scroll down to the end of the document.

  4. Look at the end of the document you will see two ¶'s
    ¶
    ¶

  5. Now shift-click on right side of the first ¶.

  6. Now hit ⌘ - C (Copy).

  7. Now open a new blank document click at the top and do plain Paste. 

If this works the while background should disappear.  Believe it or not the very last ¶ mark contains all of the formatting information for the entire document.  By copying all of the paragraph marks except the last you eliminate the formatting issue and remove any possible corruption. 

Now  Format if necessary the document in the new document save under a different name and try to see you have the background.

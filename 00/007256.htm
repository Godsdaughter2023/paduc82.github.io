Clear just the value showing in the merged cells. . . . .

Private Sub Worksheet_Deactivate()

Delete the DV dropdown and clear any value from the merged cells.

Private Sub Worksheet_Deactivate()
    With Range("E1:H1")
        With .Validation

            .IgnoreBlank = True

        .ClearContents

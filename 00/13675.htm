Hello,

With Skype group chat, you can chat to two or more people at the same time following below steps:

1. Sign in to Skype.

2. Click on the plus sign (+).

3. Select New Chat to open New conversation window.

4. Check the box beside the name of the group chat participants and select the Start button.

You cannot share a power point with them while in a Skype group chat. You need to start a voice or video call in order to share power point with a group. Below are the steps on how:

1. Start a voice or video call with a single person or a group.

2. During a call, tap or click the plus icon in the call bar, then choose Share screen.

3. If you're using more than one screen, select the screen you want to share.

4. Use the Share your screen drop-down to choose what to share - either full screen or a specific window, then choose Start.

5. Select Stop sharing when you're finished.

Hope this helps! Let us know if you need further assistance.

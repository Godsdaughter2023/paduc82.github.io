Depends on the type of processor you have. Open Start, type type msinfo32 in the search box then hit Enter, click System Information from the top of the results list. Scroll to the very end and look for the four items that begin with Hyper-V. If you see a Yes next to each one, you're ready to enable Hyper-V.

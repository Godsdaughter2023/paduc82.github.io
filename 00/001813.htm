confiture---01 say:

If your CK-7W was produced before July 2007 then the short answer is sadly no.

 

The software in the CK-7W will only send a charging supply when it receives the data signal from the CA-27.

 

Later kits were supplied with a CA-116 cable which has the Nokia charging pin instead of the now discontinued pop-port connector (as on the N80). These later CK-7Ws also had different software to enable the new cable. There are then adaptors from the Nokia pin connector to micro USB (I'm not aware of any to mini USB though).

 

Older units cannot be upgraded though, and this lack of upgrade potential was one of the reasons for the CK-7W being replaced in 2008 by the CK-100. another issue with the CK-7W on newer phones is echo for the other caller.

 

If you have a newer CK-7W then it would have been supplied with this CA-116. If not, it' could be time to upgrade...

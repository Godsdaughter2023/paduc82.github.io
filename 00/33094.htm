For Display issues with Windows Updates one fix that is working is to Roll back or Uninstall the Driver on the Display Device > Driver tab, restart PC to reinstall driver.

You can also try older drivers in Device Manager > Display device > Driver tab > Update Driver > Browse > Let Me Pick.

Then you will know you've tried everything in addition to Windows Update drivers.


See these steps which almost always work to get Sleep timing out properly. http://www.makeuseof.com/tag/fix-windows-10-sle...

To test this I open Advanced Power Options, set Display and Sleep to 1 minute, and Hibernate to 2 minutes, then after each step sit back and wait to see if they all three work.

I hope this helps. Feel free to ask back any questions and let us know how it goes. I will keep working with you until it's resolved.

tyler durden_883 say:

I have a Lenovo s10e idea pad. When I went from xp to 8.1 I had the "plugged in not charging" error. Solution- 1. power off pc. 2. unplug power cord. 3. Take out battery. 4. put the battery back in 5. plug the power cord in 6. power up pc. 7. open device manager 8. disable Microsoft ac adaptor 9. disable Microsoft acpi compliant control method battery. 10. Power off pc. 11. repeat 1-6. 12 open device manager and enable both the Microsoft ac adaptor and the Microsoft acpi compliant control method battery.

Note some have uninstalled at steps 8 and 9 and reinstalled at step 12

I just went from Windows 8.1 to Windows 10 and I only got as far as step 6 and Windows 10 already had a 12 min estimate to Full Charge.

Good Luck

jt kimbell [msft] say:

Hi,

The IR extension port always receive the exact same IR signal we're sending to the Kinect. (they both receive it from a chip inside the Xbox)

You have the right idea to use the extension cables, and I'm sorry it's not working. If you have a camera phone or digital camera that doesn't have an IR filter, can you look at the Kinect and see 3 real bright lights shining. If you can see those, then your phone can see IR.

Try doing some IR blasting with your extension cable and see if you can detect IR with your phone by looking at the extension cable's LED.

If that DOESN'T work, then you may have a bad cable. If it is blasting IR, we may have the wrong codeset for your devices.

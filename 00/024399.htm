bill manville say:

Freeze panes freezes the columns to the left of the active cell and the rows above the active cell.

If you just want to freeze rows 1 to 4, put the cursor in A5 first
If you just want to freeze columns A to C, put the cursor in D1 first
If you want to freeze rows 1 to 4 and columns A to C, put the cursor in D5 first.

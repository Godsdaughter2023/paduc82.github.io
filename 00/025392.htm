2. Click "+" to create a new rule.

5. Click Save button to finish the configuration.

After the steps above, the specific mailbox will receive the same message as the one in public folder automatically.

It took a while to decipher your description, but I think I have a handle on your requirements. Make a copy of your project workbook and try this code on it,

Sub mcrGatherData()
    Dim aSheets As Variant
    aSheets = Split("CY,PY,Budget,Fcst1,Fcst2,Fcst3", ",")
    Application.ScreenUpdating = False
    For s = 0 To 5
        With Sheets(aSheets(s))
            For r = 7 To 43 Step 12
                Union(.Range("A3:F" & .Cells(Rows.Count, r).End(xlUp).Row), _
                  .Range("A3:L" & .Cells(Rows.Count, r).End(xlUp).Row).Offset(0, r - 1)).Copy
                Sheets("SumData").Cells(Rows.Count, "A").End(xlUp).Offset(1, 0).PasteSpecial _
                  Paste:=xlPasteValues
            Next r
        End With
    Next s
    Application.ScreenUpdating = True
End Sub

It would seem that Union was the VBA function you were looking for. Tap Alt+Q when you are done to return to the worksheet.

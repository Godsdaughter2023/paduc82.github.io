jllatham say:

Same questions that HansV MVP asked: what is the problem with the formula/results of the formula?

As far as using merged cells, I'll be a bit more outspoken: Merged cells for pretty much anything except labels/unchanging text on a worksheet is a bad idea.  Causes many problems both on worksheets and even more if the cells need to be referenced in VBA code (a macro).

You can often achieve the same appearance as 'Merge and Center' for cells on a single row by entering your text/formula/whatever in the leftmost cell you would have chosen to do the merge with and then selecting all of the cells you would have merged and then use Format --> Cells --> and in the Horizontal Alignment list, choose 'Center Across Selection'.  Then your reference to the cell is very clear - it is the one in the leftmost cell of the group.

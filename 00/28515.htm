Use this formula,

=(NETWORKDAYS(IF(WEEKDAY(A2,2)>5,WORKDAY(A2,1)+1/3,A2),B2)-1+MOD(B2,1)-MOD(IF(WEEKDAY(A2,2)>5,WORKDAY(A2,1)+1/3,A2),1))*24

In case call is received on weekend (Sat or Sun) then the above formula will consider call coming as on Monday at 8 AM

You will see the Bold one part of my formula will check whether call came on Sat or Sun and if Yes, then it will take the next work day (Monday) and +1/3 represents time which is 8 AM.

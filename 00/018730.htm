As you add people/contact to a message, we'll suggest possible matches. You can choose which suggestions appear. You can set it to give suggestions for anyone you've communicated with (contacts and non-contacts) and suggest only people/contact in yourcontact list. Please select the latter of two options. Below are the steps.

 

1. Log in to your account.

2. Once your Inbox contents are shown, click the gear icon right beside your account name found on the upper right hand corner.

3. Select More Mail Settings options in the drop-down menu.

4. Right under "Customizing Outlook", choose Auto-complete suggestions.

5. Choose the option Only suggest people in my contact list.

6. Hit on Save.

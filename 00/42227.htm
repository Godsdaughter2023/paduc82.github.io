jack mvp say: Hi

Look at the options of your email application, and check the Request Receipt option (if there is one).

Do not be disappointed if you spend time in configuring you email application and never see a receipt.

Email does not go from your computer to the addressee computer.

It goes from your computer to your email’s account server (somewhere in the USA), and from there to the addressee’s email server (somewhere in the USA).

The addressee fetches the email from his email server.

Many email server are set not send back a receipt regardless of the request.

Even if the email server can send a receipt, a user can set his email Not to give one.

In other words you want to make sure that a message is received, you have to go to the post office and send the mail with request of  Signature Confirmation and send back receipt.

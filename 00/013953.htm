Get the value of a dropdown from VBA

edwin tam say:

I guess your "dropdown" is a Form control in your worksheet:

Assuming your drop down is called "Drop Down 1"

Sub macro1()
Dim tmp As String
tmp = ActiveSheet.DropDowns("Drop Down 1").List(ActiveSheet.DropDowns("Drop Down 1").ListIndex)
End Sub


Or, you can refer to each dropdown by their index number (but not preferred):

ActiveSheet.DropDowns(1).List (ActiveSheet.DropDowns(1).ListIndex)

Regards,

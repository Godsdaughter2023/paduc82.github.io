I suspect you mean "Design mode" on the Developer Tab. The Design Tab has no form controls.

Everything on the left except the little suitcase menu are content controls. They should be what you are using. Specifically, unless you have a specific reason to use something else, you should use a Plain Text content control. That is the second Aa button.

You need to turn off Design Mode if you have it turned on.

Excel allows users to protect the structure (the arrangement of sheets) of all sheets in a workbook. If you can remember the workbook structure password, you can easily remove the protection in Excel.

devlin sco say:

Hello Pat,

One benefit of having your Microsoft account as the sign in information of your PC is that you'll have the ability to sync setting between your Windows 10 devices. You're still free to go back and use a local account if you wish to do so. Follow these steps:

  1. Select the Start button.
  2. Go to Settings > Account > Your info.
  3. After confirming that the PC is set up with your Microsoft account, click Sign in with a local account instead.
  4. Enter the password for your Microsoft account to confirm that you're authorized to make the change, and then click Next.
  5. On the Switch to a local account page, enter your new local user name and password.
  6. Click Next to sign out from your Microsoft account and sign back in using the new local account.

You may need to sign in again to some Windows Store apps and other Microsoft services after this procedure.

Let us know if you need further assistance.

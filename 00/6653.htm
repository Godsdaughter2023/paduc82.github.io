john w. vinson say:

The subform would be based on just one table - tblProjectAssignments.

For example, if you wanted to see information about a Project and see (or enter or update) which Employees are assigned to that project, your mainform would be based on tblProjects; the subform on tblProjectAssignments, using the ProjectID as the master/child link field.

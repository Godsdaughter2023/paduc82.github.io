    Another workaround this problem is to first save your theme as a custom theme:

    1. Go to the Design Tab
    2. Click the down arrow on the main themes gallery
    3. Click "Save Current Theme"

    This action will dissociate the theme from any in-box theme that you may have started with to create your custom theme.  Next, apply your custom theme to your current presentation:

    1. Go to Design Tab
    2. Click the down arrow on the main themes gallery
    3. Select the theme you just saved, or if it's not showing up click "Browse for Themes..."

    Now, when you switch between 16:9 and 4:3 (or any other slide size), your custom formatting will be preserved.

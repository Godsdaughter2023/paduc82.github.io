I assume the mail you are calling Outlook is your outlook.com or hotmail account. That one is an Exchange account and should work as you want, marking read, deleting and synchronizing that across devices; phone, PC, etc.  Syncing is not always instantaneous. If you have the account set up to sync as items arrive, you should get e-mails fairly immediately, and other things sync at that time. Reading an e-mail on the phone doesn't always trigger a right-now sync so you could see that if you are sitting looking at your PC at the same time. PoP3 accounts, like Comcast, just don't do that. The client just downloads a copy off the server and typically it is deleted at that time.

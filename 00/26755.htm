You cannot change the numbering you get when you click the Numbering or Bullets button. There is no record a macro option in Access like the one in Excel.

There is also a language differentiation to make.  What Excel refers to as a Macro is actually VBA.  So in Access it is properly referred to as a VBA procedure.  In Access Macro are similar in the sense they are for automating certain processes, but it is its own language (not VBA).

That said, you can create either a Macro or custom VBA procedure to automate pretty much anything you wish in a Desktop database.  On the other hand, if you are dealing with a Web App, then it does not support any VBA, so you are limited to Macros.

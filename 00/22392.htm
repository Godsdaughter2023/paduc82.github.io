diane poremsky mvp say:
The admin can use  powershell to turn it off -
Set-CalendarProcessing -Identity username -RemoveForwardMeetingNotification $True

or the notifications can be deleted - in owa, go to gear > options and under calendar invitations, tick the box to delete notifications about forwarded events.

  2. Type in Settings and press Enter.

  6. Under Mouse Properties, select the last tab option for touchpad, then click Settings.
  7. Select the Click tab.
  8. Under Taps, uncheck the box for Three-Finger Tap and Four-Finger Tap.
  9. Under Clicks, uncheck the box for Three-Finger Tap and Four-Finger Tap.
  10. Click Ok.

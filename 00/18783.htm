Hi Steve,

Since Office 365 Home and Office 365 ProPlus are applied with the same installation technology and the same version, they cannot co-exist on the same computer. Due to they have overlapping Office applications, either of them will be replaced by another one.

Given the situation, you could switch your Office 365 Home account to sign in Office 365 ProPlus. It can also work fine.

For more details, you could refer to Supported scenarios for installing different versions of Office, Project, and Visio on the same computer.

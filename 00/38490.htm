I'm using windows 10 education. Will the product key of windows 10 education be expired when I was not a student anymore?

It depends, if you are currently authenticating on your college network through what is called a Windows Server domain, it might be using a type of product activation called KMS. KMS requires that you reconnect to the campus network every 180 days (6 months) to keep it activated.

If you got a product key with your Windows 10 Education when you downloaded from DreamSpark or got it from the Campus bookstore, your faculty, then it likely uses a MAK license (Multiple Activation Key) which does not expire. So even when you graduate, you can keep using it. If you lose it though, you will have to purchase a new license or reinstall the original version of Windows 10 that came with your computer.

To check if your Windows 10 Education will expire, click Start > Settings > Update & security > Activation, it will indicate when it will expire.

arminatorx say:

Your PC would have to have a WiFi hardware that is compatible of being turned into an access point. 

If you have Windows 10 installed on your PC, simply open the settings, by clicking the start menu, and then clicking on the gear/cogwheel symbol on the lower left. 

In the settings window, go to the network settings. 

On the left choose the "Mobile Hotspot" category and enable the mobile hotspot. 

You can also set a specific name and Passphrase for your Hotspot. Then you can try if your console sees that hotspot, and you can then use your PC's LAN connection to provide Internet to the console via WiFi. 

Alternatively, open up a command prompt as administrator.

Enter the following command: 

netsh wlan set hostednetwork mode=allow ssid=NAME key=PASSWORD

Replace NAME with the desired name for your WiFi Hotspot and PASSWORD for the Passphrase you want to use to encrypt your WiFi. 

Be aware, that you can not use your WiFi to connect to the internet at the same time. 

Your PC must have a LAN connection to the internet to be able to provide WiFi Hotspot functions. 

If you want the Xbox to provide Internet to a PC, then this won't work. The Xbox can not provide WiFi Hotspot functions.

Change Wireless Channel - Microsoft Community

xbr dionysus say:

Change Wireless Channel

Login Information

  • Gateway - 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.0.1
  • Username - admin
  • Password - password/password1/admin


 

MAC Filtering

 

  1. Open a web browser.
  2. Log into the gateway by entering “192.168.1.1” into the address bar of the internet browser.
  3. Once logged in, click on “Wireless Setup” on the top of the page.
  4. When prompted to enter the username and password. Type in the current username and password (see login information).
  5. Click on “Wireless advance settings” on the left hand side.
  6. On the main page, check the “Enable Access List”
  7. Select “Accept all devices listed below”.
  8. Enter in a MAC address from the device you would like to add.
  9. After adding the devices, click on Apply.
  10. You can also remove the MAC addresses by selecting them in the “List” box and selecting the “Remove” button.

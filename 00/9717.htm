=SUMPRODUCT(($B$2:$B$10)*(FREQUENCY(IF($A$2:$A$9=$D2,MATCH($B$2:$B$9,$B$2:$B$9,0)),ROW($B$2:$B$9)-ROW($B$1))>0))

Refer below image of sheet RAW DATA:

Two Options are listed:

OPTION 1: Column B: Cell B1 matches job1 in column A (of sheet RAW DATA) & totals column L values that exist multiple times ONLY once - ex. Job1 total is 11+ 8 + 16 = 35. Enter below formula in cell B1 as an array formula (CTRL+SHIFT+ENTER) & copy down:

=SUM(IF(FREQUENCY(IF('RAW DATA'!$A$1:$A$10=$A1,MATCH('RAW DATA'!$L$1:$L$10,'RAW DATA'!$L$1:$L$10,0)),ROW('RAW DATA'!$L$1:$L$10)-ROW('RAW DATA'!$L$1)+1),'RAW DATA'!$L$1:$L$10))

=SUM(IF('RAW DATA'!$A$1:$A$10=$A1,IF(COUNTIF('RAW DATA'!$L$1:$L$10,'RAW DATA'!$L$1:$L$10)=1,'RAW DATA'!$L$1:$L$10, 0)))

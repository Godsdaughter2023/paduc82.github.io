princess mat say:

Hi LDCA,

Yes, you can connect your journal entries from Office 2007 to Office 2016. You just need to change the journal entries file type to .pst. And use the Import/ Export for it to be imported in the Outlook 2016.

Make sure the journals entries that you want to move were already in .pst then follow the steps below to import your Outlook 2007 journal entries to Outlook 2016:

  1. Open Outlook 2016.
  2. Go to File.
  3. Click on Open & Export.
  4. Select Import/ Export.
  5. Locate Import from another program or file and click Next.
  6. Choose Export to a file, then Next.
  7. Select Outlook Data File (.pst) > Next.
  8. Choose Journal, click Next.
  9. Choose if you want to save the duplicate items or not then hit Finish.
  10. Check if the journal entries were successfully imported.

    max, singapore say:

    One way is index/match

    Eg Assume your source table as posted is like this
    Roles headers run in A2:A10, ie: Self, Peer, etc
    and responses headers, ie: A,B,C,D are in C1:F1
     
    Assume that your desired pernutations are listed in I2:J2 down,
    viz in I2:  Self,  in J2: A
    Put this in K2: =INDEX($C$2:$F$10,MATCH(I2,$A$2:$A$10,0),MATCH(J2,$C$1:$F$1,0))
    Copy down. Modify to suit the extents of your source data

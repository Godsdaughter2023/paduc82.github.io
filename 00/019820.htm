hansv mvp say:

On the View tab of the ribbon, in the Views group, click Draft.

Double-click any endnote number to activate the Endnotes pane.

Select 'Endnote Separator' from the dropdown at the top of this pane.

You can now delete the separator line.

You will probably also want to select 'Endnote Continuation Separator' from the dropdown and delete the line there.

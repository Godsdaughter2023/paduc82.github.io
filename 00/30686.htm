    HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Authentication\LogonUI\UserSwitch.
  3. Right-click on Enabled, then change the value from 0 to 1.
  4. Right click on the registry key "Userswitch", then choose Permissions.
  5. Click on Advanced, then hit Disable inheritance at the bottom part of the dialog box.
  6. Choose "Convert inherited permissions into explicit ones for this object".
  7. At the top of the dialog box, change the owner from SYSTEM to the group of Administrators.
  8. Double click the group Administrator, then put a check on Full control > Allow.
  9. Double-click on SYSTEM, then select "Deny" for "Type" on top, then click Show Advanced permissions.
  10. Click on Clear all, then put a check on Set value.
  11. Click  OK > Apply > OK.
  12. Close the Windows Registry, then restart your computer.

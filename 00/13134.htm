Here are a few things that can cause this:

  • If track changes are not displayed then the Reviewing pane will appear when you insert comments. On the Review tab, make sure you're seeing "Final showing markup".
  • On the Review tab, under Show Markup that Comments is selected.
  • How many comments are in your document and how long are they? I've found if you have a document that has so many comments that if there isn't enough room to edit/add your comment the Review pane will open. If more than one person has added comments/revisions then you might be able to turn off revisions for other authors to make more room. For this, on the Review tab, click Show Markup, point at Reviewers and then turn off the display of other reviewers.

Also, by default the Reviewing pane should display to the left of the document, rather than below. On the Review tab, click the arrow next to Reviewing Pane and then click "Reviewing Pane Vertical". I realize this won't remove it from view but showing it on the left does make it a little less obtrusive.

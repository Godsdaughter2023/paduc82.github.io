clint gubich say:

The formula you pasted has a bunch of nonsense characters and is difficult to decipher.

If the result of the 2nd VLOOKUP is in fact zero, then that would return a zero.

ISNA returns a TRUE or a FALSE, so depending on how you have the cells formatted you might get a 0 for "FALSE".

Either way, it would be easier to just do this formula (I am guessing based on the non-numeric characters in your formula above, but adapt to your needs):

=ISNA(VLOOKUP($A23,sheet!$A$1:$AP$38,4,0),"")

There is really no need for the IF as it looks like your two VLOOKUPS are the same.

The button to empty the deleted folder works, but is very slow, especially if you have a lot of mail to delete.

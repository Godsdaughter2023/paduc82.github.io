*Note: To complete the following steps you must have Control Panel Home selected within Control Panel.

 

1.   Click Start, and then click Control Panel

2.   Click System and Maintenance

3.   Underneath Device Manager, click View hardware and devices

If you are prompted for an administrator password or confirmation, type your password or click Continue

4.   Expand DVD/CD-ROM drives

  To update your DVD/CD-ROM driver:

  

1.   Click Start, and then click Control Panel

2.   Click System and Maintenance

3.   Underneath Device Manager, click View hardware and devices

If you are prompted for an administrator password or confirmation, type your password or click Continue

4.   Expand DVD/CD-ROM

5.   Right-click your DVD/CD-ROM drive, and click Properties

6.   Select the Driver tab

7.   Click the Update Driver button.

8.   Select Search automatically for updated driver software

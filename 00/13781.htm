Try to open Outlook in safe mode and verify the status.

1.    Open Outlook

3.    Click on ‘GO’ beside Manage – COM Add-ins and uncheck the add-ins and click ok.

4.    Close and open Outlook and check.

 

If you still face issues, repair Office following the steps below and check.

1.       Click on Start> Computer> Uninstall or Change programs

2.       Right click on Microsoft Office 2010 and click on change

3.       Select Repair and click continue

4.       Close when it is completed.

 

If the above does not help, restart the computer in clean boot and check.

canon1013 say:

Replace 0 with dash

If I enter 0.00 in my cells, I would like it to show up as a dash -

If I do this, will a formula still work?

hansv mvp say:

You could apply a custom format such as

0.00;-0.00;-

or

#,##0.00;-#,##0.00;-

to the cells; this will display 0 values as a dash without affecting the underlying value, so formulas will still work as intended.

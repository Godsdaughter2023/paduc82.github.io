  • Adjust your browser’s spell check settings (this can be found under your browser’s Settings/Tools/Options menu).

how to change a written page A4into A5? - Microsoft Community

katylagey say:

how to change a written page A4into A5?

I made a tet in A4, but now  i want to put it in A5, How do i do that?

 

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

I made a tet in A4, but now  i want to put it in A5, How do i do that?

 


The simplest way, if you want to retain the same layout is to print the document 2-pages to a sheet on A4 paper and then cut each page in half.

If that is not acceptable, and you do want to retain the same layout, you will need to change the paper size to A5 and then change the size of the margins and font sizes by a factor of one divided by the square root of 2 i.e by 0.70710678

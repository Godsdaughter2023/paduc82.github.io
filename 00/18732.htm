Select the rows that you want to format conditionally.

Let's say that the active cell within the selection is in row 2. (You can look at the box on the left hand side of the formula bar to see the address of the active cell).

Select Format | Conditional Formatting...

Select Formula Is from the first dropdown, then enter the following formula:

 

=$C2<TODAY()

 

Click Format... and specify the desired formatting in the Patterns tab.

2G = EDGE (shows as 2G)

3G = GPRS (Shows as 3G)

3.5G = HSDPA (shows as H)

4G = HSDPA+ (Shows as H+)

4G LTE = LTE (Sows as LTE)

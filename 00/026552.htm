anthony.b say:

In Excel 2016, do as follows:

[1]  Open any existing Excel workbook, or start a new blank Excel workbook.

[2]  Click on File -> Options

[3]  On the General tab, look in the "Personalize your copy of Microsoft Office" group.

[4]  Change the Office Theme to "White".

[5]  Click OK.

Your Quick Access Toolbar icons will now have color.

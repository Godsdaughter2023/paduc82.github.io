mouneshwar r say: Hi Talisman,

This problem occurs because your HDTV or high resolution monitor does not support the High-bandwidth Digital Content Protection (HDCP) protocol. The HDCP protocol is required to play a DVD, an HD-DVD, or a downloaded movie in high definition. But not all HDTVs or monitors support this protocol.

The port on the monitor to which you connect the HDMI cable must support HDCP to work correctly with the cable. If it does not, you can't see a movie as it plays, or the movie plays at a greatly reduced resolution.

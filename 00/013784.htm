iceslick 21 say:

Just a heads up: the Stop Restart app is only for Windows Phone 8.1, not Win10Mobile. This link and info is utterly useless for the Lumia 950 or 950 XL.

A lot of people have found that most reboot issues are stemming from the SD card, and how Win10Mobile interacts with the cards. If the size is too large (i.e., greater than 32 GB) or if you have your phone set to install ALL apps on the SD card, then when the phone tries to access the card or update info for those apps, it crashes the phone, forcing the reboot.

Here's an older article explaining what some people found:

https://forums.windowscentral.com/microsoft-lumia-950-xl/397342-lumia-950-xl-random-reboots.html

Try moving different apps, especially if they periodically go online for updates (like news, multiplayer games, other auto-updating apps), and move them from your SD card to your phone's hard drive. This has helped many people fix the reboot issue.

Caption labels are stored in the Normal template, that is, they do not travel with the document (unfortunately).

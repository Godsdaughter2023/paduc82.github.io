9926 - Why does Cotrana take focus when nothing is displayed?

If you are working on something and ask Cortana a question which only requires audio feedback (weather, simple math if it ever works, and the like) - why does Cortana take system focus?  Once the answer has been spoken, the user needs to either click on the window or Alt+Tab back to the window to continue working.  Basic functions like this should never detract from a user's primary focus.

And... to head off questions as to why this matters - I am looking at Windows 10 from an accessibility standpoint using JAWS.  Unless there is something to be displayed, Cortana should not take focus away like it does.  This just limits the usability of Cortana and adds steps during work.

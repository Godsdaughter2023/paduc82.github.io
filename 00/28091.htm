bmuy say:

Can I undo the favorites organize after it has alphabetized (read "screwed up") my favorites list?

Can I undo the favorites organize" once it has occurred?  I accidentally hit it every year or two, and it redoes my favorites alphabetically, which seems ridiculous.  I had them listed from oldest to most recent, which works better for me, now it is a jumble.  Alphabetical does me no good.  I would also like to disable this so-called "feature".
don varnau say:

Can I undo the favorites organize" once it has occurred?  I accidentally hit it every year or two, and it redoes my favorites alphabetically, which seems ridiculous.  I had them listed from oldest to most recent, which works better for me, now it is a jumble.  Alphabetical does me no good.  I would also like to disable this so-called "feature".

Even if you don't accidentally "Sort by name" Internet Explorer is capable of reverting to alphabetical order occasionally for no obvious reason.

If you don't want to rename Favorites so that alphabetical order works for you  (aYahoo or 1Yahoo will come before bing or msn, for example) you can export a registry key (maybe once per week) to preserve your custom sort order.

[Windows key + r]> type in REGEDIT and hit Enter.

Navigate to HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\MenuOrder\Favorites

Right-click Favorites and export it to the folder/location of your choice.

To restore your custom sort order in the future, double-click or right-click> Merge that REG file.

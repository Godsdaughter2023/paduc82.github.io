    What you are really asking is why isn't 1.05*.95 = 1

     

    If you know one of your numbers is 1.05 and you want the result to be 1

     

    1.05*x=1

    x =1/1.05

     

    x =.95238095....

     

    .95 is close to x - that is why your numbers are close but not exactly the same.

     

    Another way to look at this

     

    5% of 100 is 5

    However

    5% of 105 is 5.25

     

    When you grow your number by 5% - then reducing by 5 percent - your starting number is different - which means the amount of reduction will be different...

Presence of incompatible, faulty or corrupted device drivers will result in irregular performance of the device on the PC. If the BSOD error are caused due to incompatible, faulty or corrupted device drivers installed, it can be rectified by booting the computer in Safe mode.

 

Method 1: Boot the computer in Safe mode by following the steps from the link below and check if the issue persists.

 

Safe mode is a diagnostic mode that starts your computer with limited drivers necessary to run Windows. The words Safe Mode appear at the corners of your monitor to identify which Windows mode you are using.

marcoman86 say:

Hey I know this is super old but maybe my suggestions will help someone anyways. What I found is that when you try to export a sound clip or prioritize a sound clip Sony Vegas will automatically assume that the entire project is for sound only; even if you add your video clips and effects in afterwards. This makes it super annoying when you try to render your video project and you only can see the audio rendering options. After you have clicked on "Render As", you might find that the options you have are all audio templates. To change that simply click on the little blue link underneath the "Browse...." button that says "More Filter Options". You then should see a bunch of options appear underneath Output Format:

Tick the box next to "Video templates only" and voila! All of your video options are back! Worked Great For me hope I could help you because this really frustrated me after working on my project all day LOL.

Greetings ENV

From my contact at a 3rd party company that makes a dialer program: "...incoming call dialog takes the caller ID from the registry and then it shows the contact that is the first in the call history list with this ID".   So the icon/label that comes-up depends most on whom you've called most recently with that same telephone number.

Now, bear in mind this is a 3rd Party application, so HTC's dialer (which is incorporated into the Sense GUI [previously called TouchFlo 3D] also created by HTC) may operate differently...

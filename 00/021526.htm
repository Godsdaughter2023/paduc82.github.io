    suzanne s. barnhill say:
    Yes, it's a style issue. The tab stop in the TOC 2 style has been either deleted or moved. You might try viewing the document in Draft view. In that view, you should be able to see the ruler extending beyond the right margin, and if you see a right-aligned tab stop there, you can drag it back to the right margin. In either case, doing this in any Level 2 entry will fix the entire TOC because all TOC styles are set to update automatically.

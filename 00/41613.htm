This is just a side effect of a security system... there the web site's content doesn't appear to be coming from the URL that is entered into the address bar.  It gives the appearance that the site has been hijacked...

The prompt is for folks to "vote" on whether or not they think this is a legitimate site's contents or not.  You cannot "game the system" by voting multiple times yourself.  Instead, you have to wait until enough people have voted that it's OK.

So, you have two choices...

A) Fix the underlying problem with the web site's content (which might be difficult for the "average Joe" to do)

B) Wait until there are enough votes for the prompt to go away.

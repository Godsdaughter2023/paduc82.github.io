Device Installation Settings - a third option is needed

In "Device Installation Settings", you only have the choice to install, or not.  You never have a choice, or see which drivers are going to be installed.

Some times, you may want to choose which drivers get updated.  For example, a generic video driver may be pushed to your machine when you already have a specific driver from the manufacturer.  The collision results in non-functioning video.  Or, you may be testing a specific driver with the manufacturer, and windows will fight you during the testing.

In the area between Always and Never, in yellow, put in a third option "Let me decide".

Add the third option, making "Device Installation Settings" more like the options in Windows Update.

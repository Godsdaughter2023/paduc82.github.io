2-Sided Printer with Each Section starting on different page

I have a very large (750+ pages) document with fifty different sections.  I need to print double-sided, but each section needs to stand along.  For example, pages 1 - 11 are section 1 and pages 12 - 13 are section 2.  Pages 1 - 11 need to print on 6 sheets with the back of sheet #6 blank.  Section 2 would then start with page 12 on a new sheet #7. Any suggestions?
suzanne s. barnhill say:
Use Odd Page section breaks to separate your sections. But note that page 12 cannot be an odd page. When you use an Odd Page break, Word will automatically insert a totally blank page 12, so your next page will be 13. If you're not numbering the pages, this is not an issue, and if you're restarting at 1 in each section, Word will convert a Next Page break to Odd Page, anyway, because it will not print page 1 on an even page.

Hibernation writes your current session to the harddrive and then essentially shuts down so the computer draws very little power. Hi,

The video output of your laptop to a secondary monitor will maintain it's screen resolution and aspect ratio settings. This could be the cause as to why your TV zooms in on the video output when you choose the option to output only on this device. Since both devices are capable of 1080p resolution, one factor that can contribute as to why the video output looks like it's zoomed in is the size of the TV screen.

Usually when devices with different screen sizes are used, the device with the bigger screen will scale the output to fit the entire screen. You can adjust the screen resolution for each of your displays to get the best output. Take note that the steps that are available was for a previous version of Windows but still applies in windows 10.

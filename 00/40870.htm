To boot into UEFI - with Surface Pro turned off:

  1. Hold the volume up button. (This is the side closest to the power button)
  2. Press and release the power button
  3. When the Surface logo appears, release the volume up button.
  4. The UEFI menu should appear.
I believe there are 4 or 5 hardware device there with simple on off switches. For normal use they should all be set to ON. If the camera switch is somehow OFF - that was your likely problem.

Opening Spreadsheet on Startup - Microsoft Community

timmiandi say:

Opening Spreadsheet on Startup

How do I open a spreadsheet automatically every time I start my PC?
TIA

ronnie vernon say:

How do I open a spreadsheet automatically every time I start my PC?
TIA


Hi

 

1. Create a Shortcut for the spreadsheet file.

 

2. If you want the spreadsheet to open at startup for your user account only, move the shortcut to the

C:\Users\your name\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Startup folder.

 

If you want the spreadsheet to open at startup for any account on the system, move the shortcut to the  C:\ProgramData\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Startup folder.

 

Note: In step 2 the AppData folder is hidden by default.

To unhide this folder open Windows Explorer, click Organize/Folder and Search Options/View Tab. Select the option Show Hidden files, Folders, and Drives.

Click Apply / OK and close/restart Windows Explorer.

  1. Launch the People app.
  2. Tap the ellipsis icon located on the lower right corner of the screen.
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Tap Filter contact list.
  5. Tap the check boxes of the contacts that you want to show in the said app under the Show contacts from.
  6. Tap on the Done button.

If your contacts are still not appearing after making changes in the filter settings of the mentioned app, let us know.

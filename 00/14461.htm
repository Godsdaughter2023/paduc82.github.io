a. user say:

FWIW I just tried this with a .doc I just created in Word 2000, and the spell checking was OK.

So my best guess is that on your system, the text in your Word 2000 documents could have been set to "Do not check spelling or grammar" in Tools->Language. (Although that sounds rather unlikely to me if you're using the Spell checker as a matter of course).

It is probably worth trying the following on Word 2011 on a copy of your document if you have not already done so:
 a. Select all the text 
 b. Go to Tools->Language
 c. Ensure that "Do not check spelling or grammar" is unchecked
 d. Select the language you want
 e. click OK.

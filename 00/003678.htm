IMAP account is a mirror image of web-mail and some of the folders that do not come under IMAP account like Drafts; Contacts etc. will be associated with that OST file and will be named as on this computer only after the folder name. These folders will not be synchronized with web-mail.

Before removing any IMAP account from Outlook it is recommended to take a backup of those folders. tom_bpc say:

Michael,

It's not clear what you expected to happen, and the only obvious problem in the screenshot is the resource over-allocation.

You've highlighted the duration variance of Task ID1 - a top-level summary task.  This value indicates that the duration of Task ID1 - as currently forecast - is 14.5d less than its Baseline duration.  Based on the screenshot, this most likely indicates that the project is currently forecast to finish 14.5 days before the Baseline Finish.  The value has NOTHING to do with any measures of progress (i.e. percent completes, actual durations, or earned value metrics.)  It may appear to you that it is "no longer updating" simply because your recent progress updates have not affected the forecast completion date of the project.

It's possible that the inactivation of some tasks led to a shortening of the overall project schedule (compared to the Baseline), that's not obvious from the screenshot.

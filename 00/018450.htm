hampshire82 say:

Annoying Problems with new PowerPoint 2016 for Mac

Several things that were fine in 2011 have become problematic in 2016:

1. The Wheel animation on a group does all individual items in the group instead of on the group. This has been discussed in another post and apparently nothing can be done about it. The only real solution is to save the group as a picture and reimport as one object, adding the animation to the picture.

2. The Wipe animation on a table creates a humorous reverse Wipe effect. If you have, let's say numbers in the table cells and color backgrounds in each of the cells. The Wipe animation will animate the colors in the direction you want (From Top) while the numbers (or text) in the cells wipes in the opposite direction. There is no real solution or way to alter it. To get numbers in the table to look like they wipes properly you have to use From Bottom to get the numbers to wipe from the top but then the color wipes in the other direction.

3. Many windows are no longer previewed on the slide. If, for example, you have a couple of paragraphs of text and want to change font size, leading or space between paragraphs through the Format > Font or Format > Paragraph window, there is no live preview on the slide. You have to guess at what will work, hit OK and hope, or rinse and repeat.

4. To Reorder Objects you have to have something on the slide selected. This was not the case in 2011. I got into the habit of just going to Reorder Objects when I wanted to use that, without anything selected. If you have nothing selected in 2016, you can still select the dropdown Reorder Objects and reorder items. When you click OK, nothing moves when you go back to the slide. It took a two-hour, 9-person call to Microsoft to discover that you have to select something, not even what you want to reorder, to use it. Why change it? Why not have Reorder Object greyed out if you can't use it?

Thoughts or solutions on any of these would be appreciated.

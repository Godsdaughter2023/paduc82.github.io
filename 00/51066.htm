You can identify and stop the process that is killing your drive. There are two methods and tools that you can use to identify the process, and once you have identified the bad process, you can kill it in Task Manager.

Method 1:

  1. Click the Start button.
  2. Type perfmon.exe /res and press Enter.
  3. On the Overview screen, click the Disk section down arrow to see a list of all processes and files they are writing or reading from your drives.
  4. Click on the Read, Write or Total headings to sort all disk operations to identify which process and file is currently using the disk the most.

Method 2:

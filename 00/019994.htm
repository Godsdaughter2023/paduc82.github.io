In your original message you do refer to viewing the document in Preview but you also state "When he opens the document with Word...". The Word document content is what it is regardless of what program is used to view it.

  • You can have text formatted as Hidden in a Word document. Such content would not be visible in Preview, but the user would have to know how to display it when opening the document in Word.
  • Perhaps the document could have a 'device' usable in Preview to cause the document to open in Word, but only if the device exists in the document itself [and is supported in Preview].

IOW, you can have content in the document that isn't seen when viewed in Preview, but you can't have Preview display content that isn't in the document being viewed.

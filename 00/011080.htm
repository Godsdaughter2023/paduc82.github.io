    Word will often do this with images above 24-bit colour depth.

    It is looking for 24-bit (RGB) images.  Sometimes you can get away with higher, sometimes not.

    You will have better luck in .docx format.  Whether you succeed with PDF depends a bit on what the PDF contains.  The ones containing EPS (vector) are generally fine, but PDFs containing raster graphics can be a challenge.

The confusion typically stems from the fact that the print order can be controlled from 2 different locations :-)

Word has an internal setting in Word> Options> Print. That same setting can also be accessed while in the Copies & Pages menu, selecting Microsoft Word, then clicking the
Most printer drivers, however, also offer a setting in the Copies & Pages but select Paper Handling & choose Page Order menu. [Wording may vary depending on the printer driver.] Some printers are set by default to reverse the page order depending on how the printed pages are delivered.

If Reverse is selected in both locations they essentially cancel one another.

Did you consider consult a competent local workshop (or a skilled friend)?

- A mobile phone should not restart on its own, or heat up during shut-down, and it must not become hot after it is shut down. We need to consider a mechanical defect, which is best handled by a qualified technician.

Backup all personal files to a PC via USB, or to OneDrive
Perform a fresh system backup via the options in Settings
In Storage Sense, select the SD card (if any) and tap 'Remove'
Shut down the phone and remove the SIM card and the SD memory card

Check if the issue persists?

If the issue persists:
Perform a factory 'hard' reset from Settings > About
Start up the phone without SIM or SD and without adding your Microsoft account

If the issue still persists:

Demonstrate the issue at the place of purchase, then claim the warranty

* Notice that mechanical damage and water damage voids the warranty

If the issue is gone after removing the SIM and SD card or after a hard reset, then the SIM or the SD card may have become faulty. Replace and try set up the phone again.

rohn007 say:
BootMGR is the part of the "boot" (startup) drive that is the very first thing read from the HD when starting your computer. It contains the very basic information about the way your drives are setup for booting, like telling the BIOS which is the C:\ boot drive.

There is a small chance that your BIOS / EUFI is set to read your DVD or USB BEFORE reading the HD. That would cause the message and unplugging devices would fix it.

Hi Anne B,

 

In addition to JMH3143. MS - MVP. response, in email signature there is no Insert button, the only toolbar provided is Font toolbar. You can also use the given steps below on how you can upload your Picasa picture in your Outlook.com email signature:

 

1. In your Outlook.com Inbox page, click the gear icon beside your Profile name.

2. Click Mail more settings.

3. Under Writing email, click Message font and signature.
4. Under Personal signature, type the content of your signature and set the signature mode type to HTML and enter the image in this format:

<br><img style="border: 1px solid black; top: 20px; width: 86px; height: 82px;" src="replace this with the new URL"> <br>

Note: Under src="replace this with the new URL", you should enter the URL link you copied in your Picasa album. Since the image is too big, you need to resize it to be able to use it as email signature.

5. Click Save.

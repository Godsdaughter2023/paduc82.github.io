The camera drivers for the Surface Studio can be found in two locations within Device Manager.  The first camera driver is in the Imaging Devices and is listed as the “Microsoft LifeCam Front.”  The second camera driver is in the System Devices section and is listed as the “Surface IR Camera.”  Below are the steps to uninstall and reinstall the camera drivers for your Surface Studio. 

To uninstall the drivers:

  1. Double click on the driver to open the Driver Properties window. 
  2. In the tab labeled Driver near the bottom of the window you will find a button labeled Uninstall.  Please click the Uninstall button. 
  3. You will then see a Confirm Device Uninstall window appear.  After ensuring that the check box next to “Delete the driver software for this device” is empty, please click the OK button to uninstall the driver. 
  4. Once you have uninstalled both drivers you will need to open the Action menu at the top of the Device Manager screen and select “Scan for hardware changes.”  The cameras should reappear in the Device Manager under their respective sections. 
  5. Please restart the Surface Studio to finish the reinstallation process. 

Hope this helps,

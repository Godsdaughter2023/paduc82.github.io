Now, open the start menu (I hit the Windows key). Click on the icon with the three horizontal lines to reveal your list of applications. (I don't have the screen in front of me - pardon me if I get the naming wrong here...) You should see an icon for the site under the list of apps, probably quite obvious - perhaps under a heading called Recent.

You can right-click on the one with the unwieldy name, choose Rename, and call the link whatever you like. This will change the name that appears under Apps, and on the Start Screen as well.

Now you can just drag if from the Apps list, over to the start screen, to create a tile. (I'm not certain, but you may be able to right-click and choose "Pin to Start", too.)

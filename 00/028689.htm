All open workbooks are displayed within the Excel application window. If you maximize the workbook window within Excel, you can see only one workbook at a time, but you can easily switch between open workbooks by pressing Ctrl+F6 to activate the next one (or Shift+Ctrl+F6 to activate the previous one).

  • Click Arrange All.

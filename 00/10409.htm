On the History Pane (pain), at the top of the pane there is a drop down selection box that allows you to filter the History view. The options are

View by order visited today, By most visited, By Site and By Date (you may already have more than zero days of History selected, and just did not see the drop down options on the History pane).

Tip: Turn on the Address bar Autocomplete (Tools>Internet Options - Content tab, Autocomplete, Settings button, check Address bar, Favorites and History options....

As you start to type in a web address in the IE Address bar a drop down list of matching web addresses from your Favorites and History lists will be offered. Pressing Shift+Enter will automatically open the most likely match. This makes it very easy to quickly navigate to your most visited sites.

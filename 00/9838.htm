How do I change the setting on a document from South African English to US English?

I have a document that was written in South African English.  I need to change it to US English so I made the corrections as I went through the document.  However, all of my corrections reverted to South African English.  How can I reset the document to accept US English?  I do not want to change my default settings.
Thank you.
jay freedman say:
  1. Select the entire document (Ctrl+A).
  2. Click Tools > Language > Set Language.
  3. Click English (U.S.).
  4. Click OK.
  5. Save the document.
You may need to repeat these steps for each section's headers and footers, text boxes, and footnotes/endnotes if the spell checker highlights "errors" there (the Ctrl+A selects only the body of the document).

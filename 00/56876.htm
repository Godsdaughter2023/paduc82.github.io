barb bowman say:

Your screen shots showed some pretty high temps, which would cause throttling. If this was a recent issue after that June 27 update, I'd be inclined to recommend system restore. But since this started months ago, you may want to consider a complete wipe and reimage. 

Go to https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/surfacerecoveryimage  

(with a different Windows computer if yours is not working properly) 

and download a recovery image for your Surface. You will need a 16GB USB stick formatted FAT32 (use a Windows computer). Use FULL format and not quick format and give the volume a name like surfboot.. There are full instructions and a video at https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/4023512/surface-creating-and-using-a-usb-recovery-drive#download

The above would not remove firmware updates as they are not reversible.

FWIW...large complex projects tend to cause issues
with Movie Maker...it might be a good idea to create
several smaller projects....save them individually in the
.wmv format and then reimport the collected .wmv files
to be combined into a final movie. Or...you could import
the collected .wmv files into your DVD Authoring software
and each clip could be a Scene (chapter).

Try converting the VOB to WMV...

The following freeware can do the conversion:

(FWIW...it's always a good idea to create a System
Restore point before installing software or updates)

Format Factory
http://www.pcfreetime.com/
(FWIW...you may want to uncheck
all the boxes on the final screen)

After downloading and installing Format Factory...
open the program and choose an Output Folder...
(this is where you will find your files after they are
converted)

Drag/drop your VOB clips onto the main screen...

Select 'All to WMV' / OK...

Click...Start...on the toolbar...

That should do it...

Login Information

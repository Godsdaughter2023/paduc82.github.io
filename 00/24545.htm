The "Do not check spelling and grammar" setting is a kind of formatting applied to specific text, much like bold or italic. Before you uncheck that box, you must select the text (usually the entire document) from which you want to remove the setting.

The other setting you mentioned is not relevant. The Proofing options page always shows the heading "Exceptions for <document name>", but there are no exceptions unless there are check marks in the boxes below it.

bofra say:

If the mailbox can be moved back to on-premise, then you have an easier option. Once off-boarded, Archive mailbox exported and Imported into primary mailbox, you can re-onboard the mailbox to O365.

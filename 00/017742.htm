john korchok say:

Theme>Slide Master>Slide Layouts>Slides describes the workflow that works. Start by creating font and color themes. Then apply those to the Slide Master (the larger slide at the top that never actually appears in any presentation) and format the master to include all the formatting you'll need in the average presentation slide. Next, create Slide Layouts that either reuse the Slide Master, or, for cover and section divider slides, omit the background graphics and create a new design. After all the above is complete, create the slides and apply layouts to them.

In your message, you mention that you can't edit text or images. This is because text or images entered on a Master or Slide Layout are not editable on the final slides. Instead, insert a placeholder on the Master or Slide Layout. Then users can click on the placeholder and enter text or images.

jack cook - aka help_line say:
I'm assuming you want to determine the actual 10 digit number used for dialing the phone.  If that is the case then there are a couple of ways to determine the phone number. 

Using the phone, call a phone that has caller ID and read the number that is listed.

On my Touch Pro 6.1 device, if I go into Settings, there is a phone icon under the Personal Tab.  If I click on that icon, the phone number is listed at the top of the screen under the first tab called Phone.

If that is not what you want and it is the IMEI number you are looking for, there are generally a couple of ways to find that as well.  Often times it is found byy removing the battery and there is a strip attached with that number.  Also, if you open Settings and click on device information, it will be listed under the Identity Tab.

I hope that helps you out.

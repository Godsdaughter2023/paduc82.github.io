In order to copy and paste an sms content into a webpage(ie. google.com):

 

1. open the keyboard

2. open sms message and choose 'Forward' in the menu. (for forwarding the sms)

3. hold down the shift key, and select the text you want using the arrows keys(up, left,right,down joystick)

4. hold down the shift key again and press copy (you will see copy, in the bottom-right side of the screen (instead of options)  

5. now close the keyboard!

6. goto the wanted webpage's text area

7. from the options menu, you'll miraculously find the editing option, then choose paste. and thats it!

suzanne s. barnhill say:

If you want Word to handle this for you, go to Mailings | Create | Labels, click Options..., and, in the Label Options dialog, with Microsoft selected as the label vendor, choose one of the 1/4 Letter options (one is portrait, one landscape). Click OK, then, back in the Labels dialog, click New Document. You'll get a page with a 2x2 table.

Actually, that's what you should get (and what you'd get in Word 2010); in Word 2007, confusingly, you get one 1/4 page table cell and the rest small pages that are connected.

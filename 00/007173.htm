Technical Level : Basic

Summary

If you are trying to purchase a Video in Xbox Music & Xbox Video, but keep on getting this error: Can't download. The item is missing or we can't get to it." 0xc00d001a (0x80070035), then please read this article for help fixing this problem.

Although the steps are shown in Xbox Video, the same steps still work in Xbox Music.


Details

If you encounter this error message, you will need to go into the Xbox Video Application on your device.

You will then need to swipe your finger from the right hand side edge of your device (if touch screen), or put your mouse pointer at the very bottom of the right hand corner until your settings charm appears. Once the charm shows on the right hand side of your screen, please click onto the "Settings" option.

Once you click onto this option. The App settings page should then load up on the right hand side of your screen. Once it does, you will then need to click onto the "Preferences" option for the App.

Once you click onto this option, you will then be able to see the "Change where we look" option at the top of the menu.

Once you click onto this option, you are then able to remove the locations that are not available, or you don't want the app to look into. Then add the ones you do want the app to look in for any videos. Once you have added/removed location(s), please click done. The app will then refresh its self and it should load up any videos in the locations you have added.

This should also resolve the problem with the error.

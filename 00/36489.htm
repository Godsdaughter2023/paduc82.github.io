DOS could not do this. It was not suffiently advanced. Fortunately for you, the last we saw of DOS was 15 years ago under Windows ME. We now have the Windows Console. @echo off

for  /F  "delims="  %a  in  ('dir  /s  /ad  /b')  do  @dir  / b "%a\*.txt"  2>0

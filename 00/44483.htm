It appears that you do not have a valid license or product key.  The product key shown is "generic," and not a unique key for your device.   The report also says, "OEM Activation 2.0 Data-->
BIOS valid for OA 2.0: yes, but no SLIC table."  The "SLIC" table is data in the BIOS that contains (or should contain) the OEM product key but in your case there's nothing there. 

You should contact the person or entity that sold you the computer.  You will probably end up having to purchase a license.

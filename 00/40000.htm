Step 7: Don't find yourself typing your password a billion times a day just because you do something other than constantly work on the computer.

You obtain Drivers either from your Computer/laptop or Hardware Manufacturer.

If they do not have the Drivers that you are looking for, the machine is not supported by them for that particular Operating System and the particular Operating System you are looking for Drivers for was not designed to run on that Computer/laptop or Hardware.

Microsoft only offers Drivers through Windows Update that have been passed onto them by the Computer/laptop or Hardware Manufacturer.

It would appear that you are out of luck.

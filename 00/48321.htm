I have a Macbook and the Pyle PT-659DU Tape deck and have the same problem as you.After a lot of research,I found that only older Macs with Power PC processors will work with this turntable.When Apple switched to using Intel processors,the USB audio Codec was dropped,as they had a new codec for the new macs.

The Windows version of the USB audio codec should work for you.

So,the only workaround for you,if the above video won't help,is to use RCA audio cables from the line out of the tape deck to the audio line in of your PC.It works OK for me.

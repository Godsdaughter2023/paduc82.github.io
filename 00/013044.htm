It is possible. Like much in Word, it is not necessarily simple nor intuitive.

In Word 2013 and 16, If you use a style for your formatting of the cell, rather than direct formatting, that will repeat in a repeating section content control.

This behavior is anomalous in that font-level direct formatting is repeated but paragraph-level formatting requires a style.

Understanding Styles in Microsoft Word

I use styles as a matter of course in formatting templates so had not noticed this anomaly.

Also, if you apply the decimal tab or right alignment directly to the entire column (rather than the cell or content control) the setting will repeat (or at least be used).

jetta48 say:

If it is a stanndard external HDD (not SDD) then I would defrag it periodically, but it probably won't need to be done as freqently as a boot drive.

judith_c. say:

Hi TimRoss,

 

I am happy to assist you on how to show all images in your email messages in Windows Live Mail. There are settings in your Mail that you need to modify in order for all images to show in your email messages without the need to always click on "Show images" especially when the sender's address is not added in your Safe senders list. To do this,  follow the steps below:

 

1. Open your Windows Live Mail.

2. Click on the Menu button then click Options.

3. Select Safety Options.

4. Go to Security, under Download Images, the following should be these settings:

 4.1. Block images and other external content in HTML email should be unchecked.

 4.2. Show images and external content sent from email addresses in my Safe senders list should be checked.

5. Click Apply then OK.

 

If "Block images and other external content in HTML email" is checked, all images from senders that are not added to your safe senders list or a fist time sender will prompt you to click on "Show images" and add the sender to your "Safe senders list".

 

Hope this helps.

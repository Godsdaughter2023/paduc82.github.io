You're not dumb, we all had to learn it and it's very complicated. If it wasn't for cpitchford being here there'd be a lot of dead phones!

 

Flashing and re-flashing are basically the same thing so don't worry. 

You don't flash OTA. The OTA service is different with the N900 to other phones because you don't manually check. When an update is available you get a flashing orange symbol in the status bar, tap it and it tells you what update is available. When new firmware is available you get notified like that.

 

You can also check on NSU like previous phones. 

 

You really don't need to worry about flashing, it isn't necessary to use or update the phone. The only time to use it is if you want to wipe the device completely  (which can also be done by reinstalling firmware through NSU) or if you 'brick' the phone i.e. it won't turn on.

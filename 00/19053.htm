doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

For that purpose, in a blank document, I would create a textbox into which you insert your signature and set the Layout Options for the text box so that it is Behind the Text, and then position it at the desired position at the bottom of a page, and then with the textbox selected, create a Formatted text AutoCorrect entry (File>Options>Proofing>AutoCorrect Options>AutoCorrect) that would replace say sig with that text box.

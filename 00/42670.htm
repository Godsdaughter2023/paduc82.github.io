To break a mirrored set, follow these steps:

  1. In Disk Management, right-click one of the drives in the mirrored set, and then choose Break Mirrored Volume.
  2. Confirm that you want to break the mirrored set by clicking Yes.
  3. If the volume is currently in use, you'll see another warning dialog box. Confirm that it's okay to continue by clicking Yes.

To remove a mirror, follow these steps:

  1. In Disk Management, right-click one of the volumes in the mirrored set, and then choose Remove Mirror
  2. In the Remove Mirror dialog, select the disk from which to remove the mirror.
  3. If the mirror contains a boot or system volume, you should remove the mirror from the secondary drive rather than the primary. For example, if Drive 0 and Drive 1 are mirrored, remove Drive 1 rather than Drive 0
  4. Confirm the action when prompted. All data on the removed mirror is deleted.

As per the description, I understand that you would like to change the network from public to private.

  1. To change the network profile, we have to go into the PC Settings screen. To do that, open the Charms bar and click on Change PC Settings at the bottom.

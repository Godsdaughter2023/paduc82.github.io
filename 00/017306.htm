dianeoforegon say: Office 2008 did not have activation. You do need to enter your CD Key, but this is not activation like Office 2011 has.

Make sure you've typed the correct characters. The following characters are frequently mistaken for one another:

B and 8
G and 6
U and V
VV and W
Q and O
The letter O and the number 0 (zero)
The letter L and the number 1

What is the CD Key?

The Product Key also called CD Key is on a yellow or bright orange sticker on the CD package. It will look like this: ABC4Z-Y9ABC-Y6MNO-K2ZZZ-P23EF 

The Product ID is given during the Setup Assistant process. The Product ID will look like this: 11111-130-0001111-12345).

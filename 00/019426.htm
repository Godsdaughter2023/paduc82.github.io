jane 777 say:

help all my phone numbers have disappeared

I successfully copied all my phone numbers from my old phone to my nokia lumia 930. Everything was ok for a day or two, today I find that all my contact phone numbers have disappeared. I haven't done anything to my new phone however my old phone has now been wiped.

my new phone now looks like it did before I copied the contacts across. what do I do?

julie fka profjulie say:

Did you create a Microsoft account on the phone?  If so, your contacts will be synced to your Microsoft account.  If you lose the phone or wipe the phone, the contacts will still be stored in your Microsoft account.

If you copied all these contacts over to the phone BEFORE you created a Microsoft account, then the contacts would not be backed up or synced anywhere.  Adding a Microsoft Account after the contacts have been stored on the phone, leaves them "orphaned" in that they are not synced or backed up anywhere because they are not and never will be associated with the Microsoft Account.]

Once your contacts are properly linked to your Microsoft Account, you can verify that they are stored there by logging into your Microsoft account from a computer.  Then go to https://people.live.com/, input the same username and password you used when you created the Microsoft account on your phone.

Audio Buzzing - Microsoft Community

maria_970 say:

Audio Buzzing

After windows update sometime at the end of July 2018, my speakers started to buzz.   I am  sure it has to do with Cortana update.     I have tried changing my Realtek High Definition to Default settings.  I also updated driver.  Then I went to Realtek website to get the most recent driver installed, all has failed.   I cant be the only person having this problem,  please advise on my options to resolve this issue.      Thank you

aileen alf say:

Hi Maria_970,

If updating your audio driver does not fix the issue, try reinstalling your audio device instead. Right-click on the Start menu button, click on Device Manager to open it. In the Device Manager, double-click on the Sound, video and game controllers section to expand it. Right-click on your audio device., click on Uninstall, and then restart your computer. As soon as your computer reboots, it should detect and then reinstall your audio device. 

Another suggestion is switching to the generic Windows audio driver. To do so, you will need to:

In the Device Manager, double-click on the Sound, video and game controllers section to expand it. Right-click on your audio device and click on Update Driver Software. Click on Browse my computer for driver software. Click on Let me pick from a list of device drivers on my computer, select High Definition Audio Device, and click on Next. Follow the onscreen instructions to replace the computer’s default audio driver with the general Windows High Definition Audio Device.

If you continue to have this issue even after performing the above steps, please reply to this thread. The system will notify me so I can get back to you and provide you more answers.

Best regards.

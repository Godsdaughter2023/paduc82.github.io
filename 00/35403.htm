Hi Ron,

To answer your question, kindly follow the steps below:

To create a local user or administrator account:

Method 1:

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Click on Accounts.
  3. Select Family & other people.
  4. Click on Add someone else to this PC.
  5. Enter a user name, password, password hint, and then select Next.

Method 2:

  • Follow the steps given on this Wiki article create by Andre Da Costa: How to: create a Local Account in Windows 10.

To remove a user account or administrator account:

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Click on Accounts.
  3. Select Family & other people.
  4. Select the user you want to delete under Other users and click on Remove.
  5. Accept the UAC (User Account Control) prompt.
  6. Select Delete account and data if you wish to delete account and the data and follow the onscreen instructions.

Note: Only administrator user can delete a user account. Log on as the new administrator and delete your account.

    charles kenyon say:

    1) Turn on display of formatting characters. Showing non-printing formatting marks in Microsoft Word

    2) Select the paragraph following the page break and press Shift+F1. Word's Reveal Formatting Codes

    • "Keep with next" or
    • "Page break before."

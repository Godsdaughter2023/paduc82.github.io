Can anyone help?

 

Sub TEM_Sort2()
'
' TEM_Sort2 Macro
'
    Dim Lastrow As Long
    
    Lastrow = Cells.Find("*", SearchOrder:=xlByRows, SearchDirection:=xlPrevious).Row

'
    Range("A6").Select
    Selection.CurrentRegion.Select

    ActiveWorkbook.Worksheets("Rpt").Sort.SortFields.Clear
    ActiveWorkbook.Worksheets("Rpt").Sort.SortFields.Add(Range("A6:A" & Lastrow), _
        xlSortOnCellColor, xlAscending, , xlSortNormal).SortOnValue.Color = RGB(204, _
        192, 218)
    ActiveWorkbook.Worksheets("Rpt").Sort.SortFields.Add(Range("B6:B" & Lastrow), _
        xlSortOnCellColor, xlAscending, , xlSortNormal).SortOnValue.Color = RGB(255, _
        255, 0)
    ActiveWorkbook.Worksheets("Rpt").Sort.SortFields.Add Key:=Range("G6:G" & Lastrow), _
        SortOn:=xlSortOnValues, Order:=xlAscending, DataOption:=xlSortNormal
    ActiveWorkbook.Worksheets("Rpt").Sort.SortFields.Add Key:=Range("B6:B" & Lastrow), _
        SortOn:=xlSortOnValues, Order:=xlAscending, DataOption:=xlSortNormal
    ActiveWorkbook.Worksheets("Rpt").Sort.SortFields.Add Key:=Range("D6:" & Lastrow), _
        SortOn:=xlSortOnValues, Order:=xlAscending, DataOption:=xlSortNormal
    With ActiveWorkbook.Worksheets("Rpt").Sort
        .SetRange Range("A5:AE" & Lastrow)
        .Header = xlYes
        .MatchCase = False
        .Orientation = xlTopToBottom
        .SortMethod = xlPinYin
        .Apply
    End With
    Range("E13").Select
End Sub

Hi MyCloud,

Here is what happens when you migrate mailbox data to Exchange Online using an IMAP migration.

1.Mailbox items in the Gmail server are copied to the corresponding mailboxes in Exchange Online. This process is called initial synchronization.
2.After initial synchronization, the mailboxes are synchronized every 24 hours, so that new email messages sent to the Google apps are copied to the corresponding Exchange Online mailboxes. This process is called incremental synchronization and continues until you delete the IMAP migration batch.

So the mailboxes for your old domain are synchronized every 24 hours. If you want to receive the emails to old and new domain name in Office 365 at the same time, I suggest you can turn on the Gmail Forward feature or consider a third-party email migration tool.

There is a relatively full featured driver available for the Deskjet 6540 written by HP and delivered by Microsoft through Windows Update.  If the automatic isntallation is not working do the following:  Connect the printer by USB.  Click on Start, Devices and Printers, Add a Printer, select the proper port and when the list of printers comes up click on Windows Update in the lower right part of the dialog box.  It will take a few minutes for the update to complete, once it does choose the HP (not Hewlett-Packard) Deskjet 6500 series.

When your computer is upgraded to Window 10, it automatically replaces the former sound driver to a new one. This replacement won't be compatible with the old system of your computer. Many people have made further mistakes by updating newer & more advanced driver that will make problem even worse.

We must re-install back the old driver for the old chap.

Right click on the sound icon on the bottom task bar or from device manager to bring up sound tap. In the sound tab, click on properties to bring up another tab that has sub menu tab "drivers" (second tab from the left.) Click on this driver and you will see:

driver details

update driver

roll back driver

You should choose this roll back driver and re-configure your speakers again, it will work as it used to be. When you click on roll back driver it will take few minutes to re-install the old driver. Be patient, let it be and it will run smoothly again.

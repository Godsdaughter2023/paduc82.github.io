al moorerms say:

Outlook 2016, Editor options for "Display Email Content" missing after changes made

Recently installed office 2016, while customizing outlook 2016, I went into options under Mail==>Editor Options==>

Advanced ==>Display Content, and changed the setting for "show Image Placeholder from "off" to "on".   realizing that is not what I wanted I went back to turn the setting back off.  However, the section called "Display Content" is completely missing, so I cannot change the option to not show image placeholder.  The only Advanced option sections available now are "Editor Options", "Cut,Copy and Paste", And "Display".  "Display Content" is gone.

I did a "quick repair" of Office 2016 to no avail.

Any ideas on a next step?  I cannot get any downloads of images on my emails.

P.S. the actual Name of the missing section is "Display Email Content"  

al moorerms say:

Problem Solved, I stumbled upon the fix.  Even though level 1 support told me that "Display email Content" options did not exist in outlook 2016.....they were wrong....it does exist.  there are 2 set of "File==>Options"

one set is on the initial panel when outlook is started the second set of options appear when you double click on a message and open the message.  in this panel you have "File=>Options=>Mail=>Editor options=>Advanced"

under"advanced" you DO have an options segment called "Display email content"  and there is where I turned on "show image placeholder" and caused all my problems. 

I now have images downloading with my messages. 

This is resolved.

Time Scale in office calendar - Microsoft Community

eldergnome51 say:

Time Scale in office calendar

I have the time scales for daily and weekly views set at 15.  Despite this I only see 30 minute intervals in setting appointments.  Additionally I cannot adjust the time scale for monthly views at all as the are greyed out.  Any ideas?
diane poremsky mvp say:

Monthly view does not use time scales. When you click the New appointment button in Monthly view (or from another folder), it defaults to 30 min.

You'll need to change the time yourself - you can type it in one of two ways:
In the end time field, type 15m, 30m or 45m and tab out.  It picks up the hour from the Start time and uses the value entered as the minutes. Example: at 3:30 start, 45m as the end converts to 3:45 PM. 

Or type it in as hour dot min and tab out. Example: type 2.15 (with a period in place of the colon) and tab out and it changes to 2:15 PM. 2.45 = 2:45 PM

how to I keep skype off my hotmail account? - Microsoft Community

sick of change say:

how to I keep skype off my hotmail account?

how to I remove skype from my hotmail account?
danida_u say:

It is not possible to separate the connection of the accounts especially if you previously merged your Skype account to your Hotmail account. Additionally, if you previously signed in to Skype using your Microsoft account without updating them into one, this will result to a newly-created account. Unlinking the accounts is currently not possible. See this support link for additional details. Can I unlink my Skype and Microsoft accounts?

Skype is also an integrated feature of Outlook.com, thus it is not possible to remove the Skype icon on your Ourlook.com page. If you do not want to receive any notifications when signed in to your Hotmail account, then you can customize your notifications settings for incoming Skype calls and/or chat notifications. Please refer to this FAQ for additional information. How do I turn off Skype in Outlook.com?

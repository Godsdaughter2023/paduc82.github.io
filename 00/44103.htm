Back in December news surfaced that Xbox Fitness will stay free for Xbox Live Gold members in 2015 but "some of the content available will change in January". Clearly the subscription model was off the table.

Because the subscription didn't happen Microsoft had to renegotiate licensing deals and the content providers can demand any price they want. Either way, the free workouts were never supposed to be free forever. The problem isn't paid workouts, the problem is excessively high priced workouts.

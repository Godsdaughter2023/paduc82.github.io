j w stuart say:

Start and enter: Task Scheduler

Click on 'Task Schedualer (Local)'

Under the 'Active Tasks' list locate 'Windows Backup Monitior' and double click.

In the 'Name' column at the top of the display single click on 'AutomaticBackup'.

On the extreem right under the 'Actions' / 'Selected Item'  catagory, click 'Export' and rename the export file name from 'AutomaticBackup' to 'MonthlyBackup.xml'.

Now select 'Import Task' and scroll down to the MonthlyBackup.xml file and click 'Open'.

In the 'Create Task' Window click on the 'Triggers' tab and the 'Edit' button and change the 'Settings' from 'Weekly' to 'Monthly'.

Now change the 'Start' time so it will not collide with your weekly backup.

Finally click the 'OK' button twice.

Now you have two scheduled backups.

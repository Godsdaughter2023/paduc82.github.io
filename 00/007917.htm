How do you make tent notices (for wedding tables, etc.) - Microsoft Community

teresadiewert say:

How do you make tent notices (for wedding tables, etc.)

I am trying to make tent labels for identifying tables etc. I know it is in the word program but can't find it. 
jim gordon mvp say:
If you want to make them one at a time, then from Word's Tools menu choose Labels. In the Labels sections choose Options. Choose Avery 5305 - Tent Card, and then click the OK button. Click into the document, which will have 4 areas in which you can type. Enter a name in the 2nd and 4th areas, and then print (card stock works well for this).

If you have more than one or two to make, then use the Mail Merge feature. From the Tools menu choose Mail Merge Manager. In these steps:
1. Create New - choose Labels
This opens the Labels Options dialog. Choose Avery 5305 - Tent Card then click the OK button
2. Get List
Choose New Data Source to enter the names you want for the tent cards -or-
Choose any of the other options if you want to import the names from a database
3. Insert Placeholders
Drag the appropriate placeholders into the document. Put them into the 2nd and 4th areas in the document
4. Filter recipients - use if necessary to filter records from the database
5. Preview Results - click the ABC button and then click through the records to make sure your data is being displayed properly. Use File Print and print the first page on plain paper as a test. When you're satisfied the cards are being printed right, move to the next step
6. Complete the merge. Click the Merge to Printer button.

Some printers will want you to use the Manual Feed tray to accommodate card stock or the heavy stock Avery uses if you buy that brand of 5305 tent cards. If your printer does this, you can choose the tray in the Printer Options in the Print dialog. The Avery 5305 tent cards are pretty nice. I just used some yesterday.

If you want to set a specific storage for a site collection, do this:

Go to your SharePoint Admin Center(https://contoso-admin.sharepoint.com )-> Settings->on the Site Collection Storage Management section, choose Manual-> Then, go to the Site collections section, tick a site collection, click Storage Quota, then set the storage.

Regarding when you will receive the update.  The latest update has been released to mobile operators and manufacturers for review and roll out.  The current update will be 7.10.8862.144, http://www.windowsphone.com/en-us/how-to/wp7/basics/update-history.  As stated the final release of this update to the end customer is being performed by the OEM and the carriers.  It will be released in a staggered fashion.  At this point it may be a simple matter of waiting for the phone to notify you of an update.  To ensure you get this notification, go to the phone > tap settings> phone update > turn on push notifications.

 

Regarding how the update will be performed, this is quite easy.  Once you are notified by the phone that the update is available do the following:

  • Connect your phone to your PC running the Zune software.
  • Click on settings.
  • Go to the Phone header.
  • Select update.
  • Perfrom the update.

Before moving forward with the update you should perform a few steps in preparation.  Those are outlined here, http://www.windowsphone.com/en-US/How-to/wp7/basics/how-do-i-update-my-phone-software.

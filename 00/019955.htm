Hi,

To sum for YES use

=SUMPRODUCT((C1:C7="Yes")*(A1:A7))

to sum for CASH and YES use

=SUMPRODUCT((B1:B7="Cash")*(C1:C7="Yes")*(A1:A7))

if my info looks like this:

9                 check                yes

16               cash                  no

22                                       no

9                 cash                 yes

10               cash                 yes

16               check               no

22               cash                 no

how can it to report the total paid by cash and total paid by check and the total if the item has been delivered, without selecting each field by itself. I would like it to update if a no changes to a yes.

 

basically sum(a1:a7) if(c1:c7=yes) etc. many thanks


To sum with one condition:

=SUMIF(C1:C7,"yes",A1:A7)

To sum with multiple conditions:

Sum A if B = check and C = Yes:

=SUMIFS(A1:A7,B1:B7,"check",C1:C7,"yes")

Better to use cells to hold the criteria:

  • E1 = check
  • F1 = Yes

=SUMIF(C1:C7,F1,A1:A7)

=SUMIFS(A1:A7,B1:B7,E1,C1:C7,F1)

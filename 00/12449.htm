At the same time, we are now working on a solution for this problem and aim to release an update of the map data already during January 2013. We will inform you via this thread in the Nokia Discussion Forum once the update has been deployed. You can also check regularly for map data updates via the On-device Map Loader in the Maps application or via Nokia Suite.

We are very sorry for the inconvenience caused.

charles kenyon say:
Hi,

Please do not shoot the messenger.

Most versions of Excel do not include PowerPivot; it is only distributed with certain Office products and I'm afraid yours is not one of those. See https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Where-...

Versions that include PowerPivot are:
Office 365 Pro Plus
Office 365 Enterprise E3
Office 365 Enterprise E5
Office Professional 2016
Office 2016 Professional Plus (available via volume licensing only)
Excel 2016 standalone

PowerPivot is NOT included with any of the following:

- Subscriptions that do not include PowerPivot
Office 365 Education
Office 365 University
Office 365 Home
﻿Office 365 Personal
﻿Office 365 Small Business Premium
Office 365 Business Essentials
Office 365 Business
Office 365 Business Premium
Office 365 Enterprise E1

- Products with a one-time purchase (perpetual license) That do NOT include PowerPivot
Office Home & Student 2013
Office Home & Student 2016
Office Home & Business 2013
Office Home & Business 2016
﻿Office for Mac
﻿Office for Android
﻿Office RT 2013
Office Standard 2013
Office Professional 2013
﻿All Office versions older than 2013

Excel 2010 did not come with PowerPivot but it was available for all versions as a download. That download, though, does not work with later versions of Excel. If you have a licensed copy of Excel 2010, you can download it from here: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/detail...

This response is inspired by information provided by E for Excel in this previous response: https://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/msoffice/fo...

I hope this information helps. Please let me know if you have any more questions or require further help.

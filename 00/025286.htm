nj15 say:

nuggety,

 

Welcome to Nokia Discussions

 

Nokia 108 will be able to read cards ranging from 64mb up to the maximum of 32GB as per recommended by Nokia.

 

SD (Standard Capacity) Variant comes 64mb to 2GB.

 

SDHC refers to High Capacity based on the SDA 2.00 specs. SDHC cards carry a capacity ranging from 4GB to 32GB. These cards are rated with speeds of 2, 4, 6 and 10.

 

I'd recommend a 16GB or 32GB Class 4 for your Nokia 108. The heavily favoured brands are namely Sandisk, Lexar, Transcend and Kingston.

    Duplicate emails display in Sent Items folder for IMAP accounts

    This occurs when both Outlook and the email server are adding copies of the messages to the Sent Items folder after they are sent. To fix this, go to Outlook > Preferences > Accounts > select the IMAP account > Advanced > Folders and select Don't store a copy of sent messages.

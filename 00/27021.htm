  1. Launch any Office 2010 application.

  3. Click on 'Help' in the navigation bar on the left.
  4. Click on 'Change Product Key' link on the right side of the window.

  1. Click on the Windows 7 Start Button.
  2. In the Search box, type 'CMD' (without the quotes).
  3. In the Search results, cmd.exe will be listed.  Select with your mouse, and then click on the right mouse button.
  4. Choose 'Run as Administrator' from the menu, and click on it using the left mouse button.
  5. This will open the Command Prompt window.

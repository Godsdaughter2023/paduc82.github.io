Hi Charles,

Thank you for writing to Microsoft Community Forums.

I understand your concern. If you open a link in Outlook within a virtual desktop, your default browser will open the link in a new window within the virtual desktop. If the Internet Explorer’s tab settings are set to open links from other programs in the current tab or window, then the same window or tab will be reused whenever you click any other link in Outlook or in File Explorer within the virtual desktop.

Part of your drive is reserved for Restore Points and possible backups and even a possible
Recovery partition (hidden or not). Add to that there is overhead so a 320 GB drive does not

System Restore usually reserves 15 % of drive space and updates that are needed to run computer

format to their max capacity.

remove letter=L


Where L is the drive letter you want to remove.

assign letter=L


Where L is the drive letter you want to assign or change.

If you want to assign the drive letter E, type assign letter=E and press Enter.

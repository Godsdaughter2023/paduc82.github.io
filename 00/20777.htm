stefan blom say:
There seem to be at least two things discussed in this thread: Read Mode (Full Screen Reading in some versions of Word) and Protected view.

To prevent documents from opening in Read Mode, clear the option to "Open e-mail attachments and other uneditable files in reading view" at File tab | Options | General.

To prevent Word from opening files in Protected view, you have to clear the options at File tab | Options | Trust Center | Trust Center Settings | Protected View. However, to completely get rid of Protected view, you must also change the File Block settings. See http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/infopath-help/what-is-file-block-HA010355927.aspx?CTT=1.

Of course, it should be noted (as Jay already did) that changing these options decreases security and may expose your computer to viruses.

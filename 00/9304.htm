Pagination in Office Word for Home - 2016 - Microsoft Community

guygosselin say:

Pagination in Office Word for Home - 2016

I have carefully followed instructions several times to begin pagination partway through a document.  I am able to accomplish all of this including getting the software to display a "1" in the proper place; however, the software does not automatically paginate the rest of the document.  What can I do?

suzanne s. barnhill say:

To start pagination partway through a document, you must have a section break between the first and second parts. In the header or footer of the second part (which must be unlinked from the corresponding header/footer in the first part), you insert a PAGE field (Alt+Shift+P). This will repeat on each page in the section provided you have inserted it in the primary Header/Footer. If you have enabled Different First Page, then you have to insert a PAGE field in both the First Page Header/Footer and the primary Header/Footer. You'll also, of course, have to use Format Page Numbers to start the numbering at 1 in this section.

To better understand page numbering, see http://word.mvps.org/FAQs/Numbering/PageNumbering.htm. Although the article was written for (much) earlier versions, the principles remain the same.

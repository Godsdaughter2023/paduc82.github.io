steve winograd say:

First, create a Category in Windows Live Mail's contacts:

  1. Press CTRL+SHIFT+C to open Contacts . 
  2. Click the arrow next to New and select Category .
  3. Select the contacts for that category and click Save .

When you want to send a message:

  1. Open Contacts .
  2. Click the category.
  3. Hold down the CTRL key and click the people who should receive the message.
  4. Right-click one of them and click Send Email .

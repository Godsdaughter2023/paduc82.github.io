jay freedman say:
In addition to what Doug said:

  • The green line is an alignment guide, which is a new feature in Word 2013. It shows you when a picture you're dragging is aligned with a margin, with the center of the page, with the top of a paragraph, or with another picture. It really doesn't have anything to do with the inability to move the picture up beyond the bottom of the header area. If you don't want to see alignment guides, select a picture; on the Picture Tools ribbon tab, click the Align button, and click "Use Alignment Guides" to uncheck it.
  • If you save the document in Word 97-2003 (*.doc) format, you can drag a picture past the bottom of the header (or the top of the footer) as in previous versions. However, if you then use File > Convert to change the document to Word 2013 format, the picture will be forced out of the header/footer area.
  • The best way to handle this, assuming you want the picture only on the first page of the document, is to open the header area; on the Header & Footer Tools tab, check the box for Different First Page; and insert the picture into the first-page header.

            Set oDoc = Documents.Add(Template:=ThisDocument.FullName)
            oDoc.Variables("varApt").Value = xlSheet.Range("C" & i).Text
            oDoc.Variables("varRoom").Value = xlSheet.Range("D" & i).Text
            oDoc.Variables("varLast").Value = xlSheet.Range("E" & i).Text
            oDoc.Variables("varFirst").Value = xlSheet.Range("F" & i).Text
            oDoc.Variables("varType").Value = xlSheet.Range("A" & i).Text
            oDoc.Variables("varExp").Value = xlSheet.Range("B" & i).Text
            k = i - 1
            strMates = ""
            Do While xlSheet.Range("C" & k) = xlSheet.Range("C" & i)
                If Not xlSheet.Range("E" & k).Text = vbNullString Then
                    strMates = xlSheet.Range("F" & k).Text & _
                           Chr(32) & xlSheet.Range("E" & k).Text & " (Room " & _
                           xlSheet.Range("D" & k).Text & ")" & vbCr & strMates
                End If
                k = k - 1
            Loop
            m = i + 1
            Do While xlSheet.Range("C" & m) = xlSheet.Range("C" & i)
                If Not xlSheet.Range("E" & m).Text = vbNullString Then
                    strMates = strMates & xlSheet.Range("F" & m).Text & _
                               Chr(32) & xlSheet.Range("E" & m).Text & " (Room " & _
                               xlSheet.Range("D" & m).Text & ")" & vbCr
                End If
                m = m + 1
            Loop
            If strMates = vbNullString Then
                oDoc.Variables("varMates").Value = "Not yet allocated"
            Else
                oDoc.Variables("varMates").Value = strMates
            End If
            oDoc.Fields.Update
            oDoc.SaveAs2 _
                    FileName:=strPath & "Docs\" & _
                              xlSheet.Range("C" & i).Text & "-" & _
                              xlSheet.Range("D" & i).Text & ".docx", _
                              AddToRecentFiles:=False
            oDoc.Close 0
        End If
    Next i
End Sub

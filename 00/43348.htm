a.    Click on Start; type “Programs and Features” without quotes in the Start search box and press Enter.

Method 2: To open Programs and Features wizard from Windows Explorer.

a.    Press Windows Button + E

b.    Click on the option “Uninstall or change a program” from the toolbar.

 

Method 3: To open Programs and Features wizard from Control Panel Classic View

a.    Click on Start; Click on Control Panel.

b.    Select Small Icons from the drop down menu by clicking on View by.

c.    Click on Programs and Features.

 

Method 4: To open Programs and Features wizard from Control Panel while in Safe mode

a.    Use F8 while restarting the computer.

c.    Once you login to Safe mode, follow method 3 to open Programs and Features wizard.

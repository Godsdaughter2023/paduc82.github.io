arminatorx say:

Also I thought region issue comes only when you put the disc right? Is it the same for game bought via xbox marketplace? 

Yes, as the download games are basically "images" of an actual disc, the console is tricked into playing the game from the hard drive, but "thinks" it is an actual disc in the drive (that's why you also get unreadable disc errors, even though it's a download game that became corrupted). 

So if you bought a console from a different region (e.g. USA or Japan), and you have an account from India, and download a game from the marketplace in India, your console will show you the region error. 

Also, changing the language region of the console won't change the region the disc checks. The console region (PAL, NTSC US and NTSC Japan) is based on the hardware and can't be changed.

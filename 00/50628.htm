brian tillman [outlook mvp 2007-2019] say:

Your PC is expecting a wired network connection.  In the side or rear of the PC is a, RJ-45 jack.  It is in this jack that you should plug in a twisted-pair ethernet cable with RJ-45 plugs on each end.  Then plug the other end of the cable into your network router.

rajithr say:

Hi Al,

To view the items downloaded to your device, go to the Books view and select the On Device tab.
To view items in your Kindle Library that are not downloaded to your device, go to the Books view and select the Cloud tab.
Books with a checkmark next to their title have been downloaded to your device. Books without a checkmark have not yet been downloaded to your device

Hope this information helps. If the issue persists, we will be happy to help you further.

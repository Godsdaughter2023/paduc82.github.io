Lennie

lennie hamilton say:

I think I may have answered my own question.  Turns out that outlook 2010 defaults to imap settings if you let it figure out the email servers on its own, whereas for these two email address I used pop3.  IMAP effectively mirrors the server, whereas pop3, unless you tell it otherwise, downloads the email to the device for you to deal with as you wish, hence the additional pst files.

I just copied the emails over to the inbox, removed the email accounts then recreated them using pop3 and bobs you uncle, it all works as previously.

I will leave this here incase anyone else comes across something similar.

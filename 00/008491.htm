ken sheridan say:

Try this:

SELECT [Calendar equipment Query].VEHICLE, [Calendar equipment Query].Mth, [Calendar equipment Query].Yr, CCur(Nz([Calendar Query and Equipment Activity Table].[Part Cost],0)) AS [Part Cost]
FROM [Calendar equipment Query] LEFT JOIN [Calendar Query and Equipment Activity Table] ON [Calendar equipment Query].VEHICLE = [Calendar Query and Equipment Activity Table].VEHICLE;

The CCur function simply ensures that the amounts are returned as currency values.

PS:  The reason your final query didn't work is that in the flavour of SQL used by Access you can't restrict a query on the right side of a LEFT OUTER JOIN or vice versa, other than testing for Null.  It turns it back into an INNER JOIN in effect.  Some other SQL products work differently in this respect.

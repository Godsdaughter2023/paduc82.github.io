marysully say:

Yes you can manipulate graphics and move them around in a page.

 To move graphics and shapes freely, you may want to set the wrap style to “tight.” Once set you should be able to move the graphic to any position on page.

To overlap the letter “F” onto the letter “O.” and then onto a photo you will first have to create textboxes for each of the letters. Borders and fill can be removed if needed. Once you position the “F” over the “O” you will need to “Group” the text boxed letters together.  Once “Grouped” you can move the grouped letters onto the photo. The layering technique can be tricky.  Work slowly with patience to complete.

To insert a picture into a Shape: Insert shape, select and right click shape -> Format shape -> choose “fill” -> click on “picture or texture fill” radio button and then “Insert from: “ click on “File” then insert picture into shape. Picture will take on form of the shape. You should then be able to place grouped letters over picture.

Lots of luck with it!

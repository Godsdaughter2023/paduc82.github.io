albert d. kallal say:

I find this happens rather often especially with machines having more than one version of access.

Open Access, file->options->Add-ins\\On the bottom, you see Manage (with a combo box), it should be showing com add-ins, and then click on Go button

You then get a list of options to re-enable, and one of them should be:

[x]   Microsoft Access Package Solution Wizard 2010 COM Addin

Check the above, and the package wizard should re-appear under the file->save and publish option.

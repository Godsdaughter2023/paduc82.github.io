3-Click the program list entry for the version of Microsoft Office that is installed and select the "Change" button.

4-Select "Add or Remove Features," and choose click "Next" or "Continue."

5-Click the plus sign next to "Office Shared Features" to expand the category, and choose expand the "International Support" category.

6-Click "Hindi Font" or "Universal Font" depending on the Office version, and click "Run from My Computer".

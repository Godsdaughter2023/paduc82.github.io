I am trying to build a journal entry for booking amounts to a financial system.  I would like the formula to pull from the correct date column based on the date indicated on the journal entry.  I started to use index, then thought maybe hlookup but haven't figured out exactly what imbedded formulas to use.  The data to be pulled has the dates horizontally across it.  Any ideas would be great.  Thank you in advance for the help I will receive.

 

 

e.j. gun say:

The formula assumes that your main data on Sheet1 (or whatever) is as you show - starting in Cell B2.  Change $B$3:$B$9 to $B$3:$B$1000 or however many rows of data you have.  Same with the $C and $D references.  $B is assumed to holde Vendor Name, $C is assumed to hold Invoice #, $D is assumed to be the starting of the columns with date-based data in them.

 

If your data on the main sheet is actually starting in Cell C2, then shift B, C and D to C, D and E, respectively.

 

Eric

e.j. gun say:

I assume that your table starts in cell A1 on sheet "Sheet1".  I also assume you have a list of unique Vendor names on another sheet, in Column A, starting in Row 2.  Enter the following formula in cell B1 and drag down for as many Vendor names as you have on the second sheet.  I assumed you have 1000 rows of data on "Sheet1".  I also assumed that your dates go from Column B to Column E.  Adjust those assumptions to your needs:

 

=SUMIF(Sheet1!$A$2:$A$1000,$A2,OFFSET(Sheet1!$B$2:$B$1000,0,MATCH($G$2,Sheet1!$B$1:$E$1,0)-1))

 

where:

 

Sheet1!$A$2:$A$1000 is your list of Vendors on "Sheet1"

$A2 is the particular vendor you are trying to add to the report

$G$2 holds the specific Date that you want for the report

Sheet1!$B$1:$E$1 holds the dates as in your data above

 

HTH,

 

Eric

Sling App Not Accepting American Express as Form of Payment

Even though the app features AMEX as a selectable form of payment, when you enter your AMEX card number, it reads it as an invalid card.

The problem arises, because:

  1. all AMEX card numbers are only 15 digits in length
  2. the AMEX security code is 4-digits in length size

The Sling app itself, only recognizes cards numbers that are

  1. that are 16-digits in length
  2. and only recognizes security codes that are only three digits 

Thus making impossible for Gold members who pay with AMEX, to use their favorite method of pay

It's completely safe, and there is no reason to suspect a problem. The

think it's a much better solution than Toggle Keys, which makes a beep
that is almost inaudible on many computers.

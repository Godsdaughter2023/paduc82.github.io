    rohn007 say:
    Is the whole space on the external drive showing under Q:\?

    The Q:\ drive is the default assignment for MS Click-to-run installations of Office 2010 online downloads or factory installed Office 2010 Starter. But it represents only a small portion of the HD.  The CTR drive does NOT contain your personal data file, only the program code for Office.  The Q:\ drive is defined to be inaccessible to end users.

    Your personal files should be in another partition on the HD. They should be accessible.

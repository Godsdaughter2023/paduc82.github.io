xenonee say:

It appears I've answered my own question after looking around some options in Acrobat. I'm still not sure why checkboxes in Word render as black boxes when checked as a fillable form in Acrobat, but to fix this, all you need to do is this:

  1. While in Edit view of the “Prepare Form” section of Acrobat, right-click on the check box and select Properties.
  2. Go to Options in the window that appears.
  3. In the Button Style dropdown menu, choose Check.

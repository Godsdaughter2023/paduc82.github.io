MAC Filtering

Login Information

  • Gateway - 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.0.1
  • Username - admin
  • Password - password

MAC Filtering

  1. Open a web browser.
  2. Enter http://192.168.1.1 or http://192.168.0.1 into your address bar. (Or click either link)

  3. If prompted, enter "admin" for the user name and "password" for the password.

  4. Click on Wireless Setup from the menu at the top.

  5. Click on Advanced Settings from the menu on the left.

  6. Click on Wireless MAC Authentication.

  7. Disable the MAC Filter and click Apply.

a. user say:

HDMI is superior to component for a few reasosns (the most important being)...

 

1: It's digital. There is no need for digital to analog, then analog back to digital conversion, which can cause a loss of PQ.

 

2: It's one cable. With component, you have to run three seperate connections for the video, plus whatever audio you'll be running.

 

3: With digital, you don't have to worry so much about quality. Since HDMI sends a series of 1s & 0s, all they have to do is get where they're going. Quality is not much of a factor beyond actual defects. With component cables, you will see a lower quality picture with lower quality cables. Where with HDMI, you should see no difference between a $5, $100 or $1,000 cable, so long as they are all working.

 

 

And do not buy Monster. While Monster is by no means a top of the line brand, it is overpriced for what it is (basically a mass market brand that on the surface appears better than cheap, no-name brands). Monster is one of, if not the biggest joke in the A/V world.

This is of importance because when working with government accredited workstations, there is a requirement to install all applicable updates within a certain timeframe.   For some levels of accreditation, that's 30 days.   At the highest levels, in some cases, that's 7 days.

When the images are late, it means that those responsible for the accredited information systems must look up the appropriate updates via the monthly Technet bulliten, thenmanually download each update for that system.  When you have multiple systems, some of which are using Windows 7, some Windows 8, and some using Windows 10, there's a risk that an update might be missed.

Up to date iso images are vital to keeping such workstations updated and secure.  Such workstations are inspected regularly by the government and non-compliance is a serious issue.

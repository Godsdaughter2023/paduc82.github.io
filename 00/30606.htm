Moving settings ... not possible.

Moving your own files (documents, pictures, etc.) ... easy:

1- Attach an external USB drive or a USB flash drive to your old computer.

2- Copy the files and old folders to the external/flash drive.

3- Attach the external/flash drive to the new computer.

4- Paste the files and folders to the corresponding locations in the new computer.

Trick: There's always a trick, right? For security, every Windows user account must be unique, which means that 'you' on the new computer is different from 'you' on the old computer. Makes sense? Of course!

When 'you' on the new computer wants to open files from 'you' on the old computers, Windows might say back off - those files don't belong to you. In the unlikely event that this happens, post again. There's an easy enough way around that.

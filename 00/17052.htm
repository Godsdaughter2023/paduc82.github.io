I am unsure what you are requesting so I have attempted to answer your question with the following answers.

Adding the number to your Skype profile

You can place a phone number in your Skype public profile, but that will simply allow others to use Skype to place phone calls to your landline using Skype credit. You cannot receive incoming telephone calls.

Expecting people to dial a phone number to reach you over Skype

If people dial your landline, they will reach your landline. There is no number portability to import your phone number to Skype. You could purchase a Skype number to receive inbound phone calls, but that will be different than your landline number. You could forward your calls from your Skype number to your landline number or vice-versa but that would probably require Skype or provider credit.

Adding the number as your Caller ID for outbound calls you make.

You can only verify phone numbers that can received SMS short codes. This generally limits the verification of phone numbers to mobile providers or virtual landlines that can receive these SMS messages.

You need to put the files in a Team Site and then all users that have access to that team site can sync that library down to their machine.

If you have only one Office 365 account and want to sync the OneDrive for Business library to each PC in different offices, you can let each user login to the OneDrive for Business Online with this account on their own pcs, then sync the OneDrive for Business library with the next generation sync client:

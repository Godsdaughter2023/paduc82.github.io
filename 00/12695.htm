How can I change it so it calculates time?

From that I would like it to work out the average time that I run how could I then do that?

HELLLPPP please

jeeped say:

... I enter the values as 18.45 for 18 minutes 45 seconds, but if i did 2 runs that distance it would show it as 36.90 rather than, 37.30 ...


You are attempting to record the 45 seconds as a Base10 decimal of a minute (0.45) rather than ¾ of a minute. Excel records a day (24 hours) as 1 with time being a decimal portion of 1. 12 hours is 0.5 and 00:18:45 is 18 minutes and 45 seconds or 0.013121. You need to change your data to reflect that by inputting in the format 00:00:00 or compensate mathematically.

Edit: you might want to try a formula like the following to convert your decimal minutes/seconds to an Excel time value,
=TIME(0,INT(A1),MOD(A1,1)*100)
... where A1 contains the 18.45 value. Format the cell with the formula as one of the Time format masks or use a custom number format of,
[h]:mm:ss;@
... or possibly,
[m]:ss;@

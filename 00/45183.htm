After some digging through network communication happening during outlook.com's skype login, I managed to actually solve this. So for anyone else, you just need to add two new lines to your "hosts" file:

0.0.0.0 contacts.skype.com

  1. Click on the Start button to open your Start Menu. The Start button looks like this:
  2. Click on the Control Panel menu option.

Hi - there are a number of variables here that depend on the spec of your car player. Many older car players will only play audio CD. Many recent models will play mp3 format files but may not play other formats. Audio CDs will only hold between (roughly) 15 and 30 songs depending on the duration (maximum playing time 79 minutes approx.) so your reference to 50 songs probably would not work.

- I think you'll have to try and find out the limitations of the car system and probably stick with burning audio CDs making sure you keep the run time for each disc around 78 minutes.

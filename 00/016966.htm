sylvie liang msft say:

Hello HainesGB,

Thanks for coming back to us. Generally, an email is deleted only if it reaches its retention age. As @ Shyamal mentioned before, the retention age is associated to different types of items. Items in the Deleted Items folder are handled differently. For example, when the user deletes such an item, and the MFA processes it for the first time in the Deleted Items folder, it stamps the current date as the start date.

Furthermore, MFA processes a mailbox once in seven days, this might result in items being expired up to seven days after expiration date stamped on the item.

Given this situation, I would suggest you wait for seven days to check the result.

By the way, the items in mailboxes placed on Retention Hold aren't processed, please double check whether Retention Hold is removed.

Have you tried charging your Lumia 822 using another compatible USB cable? When you say, 'power/volume thing', are you referring to rebooting the phone by simultaneously pressing and holding the volume down and power keys until it vibrates and restarts? If yes, try doing the following steps and see if the phone will respond :

 

  1. Switch off your phone, and disconnect the charger.
  2. Press and hold the volume down key, and connect the charger. If an exclamation mark (!) is shown on the screen, release the volume down key.
  3. Press the volume and power keys in the following order: volume up, volume down, power, and volume down.
  4. Wait for your phone to reset. During the reset, you will see gears rotating on the screen for up to 5 minutes, after which the screen goes blank for ~30 seconds. And then, your phone will restart.
  5. Go through the initial phone setup. Make sure that you have the date and time set correctly on your phone (Settings > Date + Time).
  6. Disconnect the charger.

Note that this step will wipe out all personal data in your phone. 

 

Let us know how it will go. :cathappy:

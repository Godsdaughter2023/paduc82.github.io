  • The first thing we would like you to do is clear your cache. Go to settings > applications > Internet Explorer > delete history.
  • Once you have done this we would like you to try and sync your Facebook account via both Wi-Fi and Data separately. Go to settings > Wi-Fi > ON and settings > data/mobile > OFF.   • Next, we would like you to set the date and time on your phone manually. Go to settings > date + time > set the automatic setting to OFF > set the current date, time and time zone. Once you have done this, go to settings and sync your Facebook account again.
  • Next, we would like you to reset the password for your Facebook account, make sure the password is 13 characters or less.  Once you have changed the password for this account, remove the account from your phone and add it back with the new password.
  • The final thing we would suggest is to delete the Facebook account from the phone and the Facebook app from the phone. After you have done this, sign into accounts.live.com with the Microsoft account on your phone. From here, go to account settings and click on preferences. Here you will have the option to link and unlink accounts. Make sure that Facebook is not currently linked. Once you have done this, go back to the phone and sync your Microsoft account and then add the Facebook account to the phone.

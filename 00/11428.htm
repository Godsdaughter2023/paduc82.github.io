omne---01 say:

In Windows Vista, when you try to pair your laptop with your mobile, Windows will give you the pairing code.


  1. First enable both your laptop's and phone's Bluetooth, Set your phone's Bluetooth to "Show to all'"
  2. On your PC go to: Control Panel\Bluetooth Devices
  3. Click on "Add Wireless Device"
  4. In "Pair with a wireless device" window, you should be able to see your mobile. Select your mobile and click next.
  5. Then Windows will give you the "pairing code", same time a pop-up will open in your phone asking you to enter the code, enter the code and it's done!

Also you can set both your devices as authorised so they don't ask you for permission every time.

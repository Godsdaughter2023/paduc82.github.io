Hi bubbaet,

 

We regret to hear that Clean Up and Exclusive feature aren't working for you. We'd like to provide a workaround for this instead.

 

There's a feature called Email Rules in Outlook.com which can e tailor fit to automatically segragate or delete incoming messages to/from your inbox. To do this, please see below:

  1. Go to Outlook.com.
  2. Click on the gear icon at the top right.
  3. Choose "More mail settings".
  4. Under Customizing Outlook, choose "Rules for sorting new messages".
  5. Click "New".
  6. Create your rule/s for incoming messages.
  7. Click Save at the bottom.

susan_s. say:

Hello,

Your Skype name is the username you used to create your account in Skype. If you sign in with an email address, then its a Microsoft account, not a Skype Name. You can also edit your Skype Profile such as your location, email, or phone number to make it easier for your friends to find you.

Below are the steps on how:

  1. Sign in to Skype.

  2. Scroll down to Account details > Settings and preferences > Edit profile.

  3. On the next page, scroll down to Profile settings > Discoverability. Make sure the box is mark check. This will help people to find and add you on Skype.

To find your Skype Name, please check below article:

https://support.skype.com/en/faq/FA10858/what-s-my-skype-name

NOTE: You cannot search for your own Skype Name as you log in to the account on the same device.

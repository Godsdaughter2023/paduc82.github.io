Hi,

 

Welcome to Microsoft Community.

 

I understand that you want to merge the two email accounts that you have created so that you have one inbox to view the email messages in your account and I'm here to assist you. I would like to inform you that the change from Hotmail to Outlook interface is purely in appearance but with different features from Hotmail. Meaning, you can still sign in with your @hotmail.com account in Outlook.com.

 

Currently, we don't have a feature to merge two email accounts to have one inbox. I suggest that you try to add it on your other account as a send only account, be informed that you will be unable to receive messages from the send only account as you can only send with it. This is where auto forwarding comes in.

 

Let's say that you want the @outlook.com account as a send only account and the @hotmail your main account, you need to forward the emails that you receive from @outlook.com account to your @hotmail.com account so that you can monitor the in and outgoing emails. Be informed that you can only have the latest emails from the @outlook.com account.

 

Here are the steps to add a send only account:

 

1. Sign in to https://outlook.com

2. Click the gear icon on the upper right part of the screen and click More mail settings

3. Under Managing your account, click Your email accounts

4. Then click Add a send-only account

5. Fill out the form of the account you want to add as send-only

 

After adding your @outlook.com account as a send-only in your @hotmail.com account, add your @hotmail.com account as the email that you will forward to when setting the auto forwarding feature. Follow the steps below:

 

1. Sign in to https://www.outlook.com/, In the upper-right corner of the Outlook home page, click the gear icon.
2. Then click More mail settings.
3. Under Managing account, click Email forwarding.
4. On the Forwarding page, click Forward your mail to another e-mail account.
5. In the address field, enter the e-mail address where you want to forward your e-mail to, and then click Save. You may also check the box Keep a copy of forwarded messages in your Microsoft account inbox.
6. After you specify an address where you want to forward your e-mail to, you must reply to an e-mail that asks you to validate your account.

 

Feel free to ask if you have more questions.

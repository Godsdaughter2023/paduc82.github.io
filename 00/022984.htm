As I see it, you have two issues. One, the problem of importing the .docx file and the second is linking.

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

If the cells below A6 are empty, ActiveSheet.Range("A6").End(xlDown).Selected causes cell A1048576 (the last row) of the worksheet to be selected and then your next line with the Offset(1, 0) is trying to select the cell in the next row and of course you cannot do that because you are already at the bottom of the worksheet.

Use the following instead to select the first empty cell in column A after cell A6

ActiveSheet.Range("A6").Offset(1, 0).Select
Do While Not IsEmpty(ActiveCell)
    ActiveCell.Offset(1, 0).Select
Loop

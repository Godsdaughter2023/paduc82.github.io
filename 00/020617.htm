hansv mvp say:

Change italic to non-italic enclosed in underscores:

  • Press Ctrl+Home to move to the start of the document.
  • Press Ctrl+H to activate the Replace dialog.
  • Click More >> to expand the dialog.
  • Make sure that 'Use wildcards' is not ticked.
  • Click in the 'Find what' box.
  • Leave it blank.
  • Select Format > Font...
  • Select Italic in the 'Font Style' list box, then click OK.
  • Click in the 'Replace with' box.
  • Enter   _^&_
  • Select Format > Font...
  • Select 'Not Italic' in the 'Font Style' list box (you have to scroll down to see it) and click OK.
  • Click 'Replace All'.

Change text enclosed in underscores to italic:

  • Press Ctrl+Home to move to the start of the document.
  • Press Ctrl+H to activate the Replace dialog.
  • Click More >> to expand the dialog.
  • Tick the 'Use wildcards' check box.
  • Click in the 'Find what' box.
  • Enter   _(*)_
  • Select Format > Font...
  • Clear the 'Font Style' text box, then click OK.
  • Click in the 'Replace with' box.
  • Enter   \1
  • Select Format > Font...
  • Select 'Italic' in the 'Font Style' list box and click OK.
  • Click 'Replace All'.

suzanne s. barnhill say:
When you create the merge, section breaks are inserted. It is these that
will have to be changed. One way to do this is as follows:
 
1. Click the Select Browse Object button (the round button between the
double arrows at the bottom of the vertical scroll bar) and choose Browse by
Section.
 
2. Starting at the top of the document, click the double down arrows (Next
Section) to place the insertion point in Section 2.
 
3. Go to the Layout tab of Page Setup and choose "Odd page" for the section
start type. Click OK.
 
4. Click the Next Section button.
 
5. Press F4.
 
6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 to the end of the document.
 
It should be possible to do this with Replace, selecting an Odd Page section
break and copying it to the Clipboard, then replacing ^b with ^c, but
unfortunately this does not work; although you can theoretically copy and
paste section breaks to transfer other section formatting, the section start
type is not included.

haiyusky say:

Hi Martenlastnamell

Please perform the following steps to completely remove the credentials first:
1. Right click the OneDrive for Business icon on the taskbar and stop syncing OneDrive for Business folders.
2. Close all Office applications including OneDrive for Business.
3. Reset IE settings and delete personal settings: https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/kb/923737#bookmark-manual%20steps. 
4. Remove all the stored credentials in the credentials manager (Control Panel > User Accounts > Credential Manager > Windows Credentials).
5. Run regedit.
6. From Registry Editor, browse to:
HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Office\15.0\Common\Identity
7. Delete the Identity folder.
8. Open a browser and sign into the SharePoint site.
9. Click Sync and sync the library again.
10. If the issue remains, exit the OneDrive for Business sync client and re-run it as administrator to check the outcome.

If the issue persists and it is convenient, clear OneDrive for Business cache using fix it tool and try again.
Note: You may resync all your libraries after clearing OneDrive for Business cache.

Let me know the updates when you have time, so we can help you further.

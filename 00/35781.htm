Before using the Connect App, you should first configure your computer's Projecting to this PC settings. A Miracast adapter should be installed on your computer computer to enable this setting. To do this, you can follow the steps below:

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Click on System.
  3. Click on Projecting to this PC.
  4. From the drop-down menu, select Available everywhere.

  5. Turn On This PC can be discovered for projection only when it is plugged in.

Once you have configured all your settings, open the Connect app on the device you're trying to project a remote screen. Then on the remote device, your Windows 10 PC, do the following:

  1. Open the Action Center.
  2. Click Expand.
  3. Click the Connect button.
  4. Click the name of the device you're trying to project.
  5. Check the Allow input from a keyboard or mouse connected to this display option as necessary.

To disconnect the Connect app, you can follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Action Center.
  2. Click Expand.
  3. Click the Connect button.
  4. Click Disconnect.

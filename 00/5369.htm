ed ferrero say:

Once you use Find, you can click one of the Fill Colors to colour the background of the selected cell.

If you use Find All, click Ctrl-A whilst you are still in the Find dialog to select all the cells that have been found, then click a Fill Color. You can always use Find All again and set the Fill Color back to 'No Fill' once you are done.

rdrdrd---01 say:

Setting Up E-Mail

How do I set up my email address on the N95 8GB? I'm having trouble with it.

mccbleue say: What sort of trouble?

Here are the basics:

Menu>Messaging>Options>Settings>E-mail>Mailboxes>Options>New mailbox

(1) Choose what kind of incoming mail server you ISP uses (POP/IMAP)
(2) Enter the e-mail address
(3)Enter the name of your ISP's incoming server
(4) Enter the outgoing server (this could be your ISP's smtp or your network operator's, some companies only allow you to connect to their own SMTP when connected to their network)
(5) Set access point in use (if using anything but WLAN, it should be whicever access point you use for accessing the web on the internet - not WAP/GPRS, the full internet access point)
(6) Choose a name for the mailbox (this is just what will be shown in your Messaging menu, it doesn't have any effect on any other settings)

Once finished there, select your new mailbox from the Mailboxes menu and press Options.

(1) Go to Connection settings>Incoming mail settings and enter your username and password, and any security settings if your ISP has them
(2) If your ISP (or network operator) uses security settings on outgoing mail, fill them in Connection settings>Outgoing mail settings

These are the only necessary steps, you can check other settings in the Mailbox if they apply to you. Most of the info you need you can find in the account settings in your mail client on the computer.

Otherwise, let us know how far you have got and what error messages you are seeing.

shirley wang msft say:

Hi Kcshocker,

We can log into OneNote 2016 desktop client with both Work/school account and Microsoft account. To add the second account, we can click File > Account > Switch account > Add Account. We can switch between them via the steps above. 


If you would like to use two accounts at the same time instead of switching between them, we can click File > Account > Connected Services > Add a service > Storage > OneDrive. Then log into the second account. Both the OneDrive for Business site and OneDrive personal site appear: 

If we log into OneNote with the Work/school account, we can save the notebooks to the OneDrive for Business site. To check it, we need to log into Office 365 portal and access OneDrive for Business library in your organization. If we log into OneNote with a personal account, we can save notebooks to personal OneDrive site and can access them via logging into personal Microsoft services.  

If you leave your company and the work/school account is removed, you can’t access the notebooks in OneDrive for Business library. We can back up the notebooks in OneDrive for Business to OneDrive. To back up the files in OneDrive for Business library, the OneDrive for Business sync client or Open with Explorer function in SharePoint Online is a good choice.

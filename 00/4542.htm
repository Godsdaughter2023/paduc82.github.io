The analogous registry keys for .dotx and .dotm templates (for both Word 2010 and Word 2007) are HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\Word.Template.12 and HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\Word.TemplateMacroEnabled.12, respectively. The key for Word 97-2003 .dot templates is HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\Word.Template.8.

"C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Office\Office14\WINWORD.EXE" /n /f "%1"

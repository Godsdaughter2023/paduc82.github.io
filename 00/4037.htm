john mcghie say:

Just make sure you have the latest updates applied, to Word, your operating system, and your printer driver.  This sounds suspiciously like a PostScript error.

When you look in the print preview, the latest version of Word actually sends the document out to the Printer Driver, then displays the output of the printer driver instead of sending it to the printer.  So normally, if it is correct in the preview, there's nothing wrong with the Word output, and we need to look further down the chain.

You may have to rake your printer driver out and force it to update.

Hope this helps

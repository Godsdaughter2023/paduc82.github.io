  • We have unassigned the E3 without ProPlus licenses from users two months ago, but they are still showing as having a mailbox in the O365 Exchange Admin Center.
  • We have also disable the mailbox in the ECP (On Premise Exchange Admin Center.) The mailbox still appears. Any help would be greatly appreciated. Thanks!
smoothsax say:

I have finally figured out the resolution. 

The email addresses in the O365 EAC for these users was showing as *** Email address is removed for privacy ***. They never had a license, but somehow had an mailbox after we implemented this Hybrid setup earlier this year. So these are the steps I took:

1. Add an SMTP for *** Email address is removed for privacy *** in our on-premise AD under the Proxy Attribute.

2. Perform a DirSync.

3. I then assigned a basic license to the user. In my case, E3 w/o Pro Plus. This prompted O365 to attempted to create a mailbox. 

4. Once the mailbox was created, I verified that the O365 EAC was now showing *** Email address is removed for privacy ***, instead of *** Email address is removed for privacy ***.

5. I was then able to remove the license from the user. Which in turn removed the mailbox. 

6. I then went back into our on-premise AD and remove the SMTP address.

*Note - not sure if this had anything do with it, but I disabled the mailbox in out on-premise Exchange. It is also called the Exchange Admin Exchange, but also known as ECP. This was done as part of an unsuccessful troubleshooting step last week.

Thanks for all of the assistance. The two posts from Lance and Edward was very beneficial, and have  helped me resolve other issues. I believe the problem in this thread was from a problem with our initial Hybrid setup that should be resolved now.

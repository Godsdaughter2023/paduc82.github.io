daveteerlink say:

How do you resolve Error occurred while launching issue with Outlook for Mac 2016?

I  made the mistake of updating MacOS after updating Office for Mac 2016. When I brought Outlook up I got the following error:

At that point, Outlook will not come up at all.

It used to be that there was a Outlook/Office db tool that could fix this.  Is that still true?

bryan cheng msft say:

Hi DaveTeerlink,

Actually, when creating a new profile, there is no need to open outlook client.

  1. From Finder, open the Applications folder.
  2. Ctrl+click or right-click Microsoft Outlook, and then select Show Package Contents.
  3. Open Contents > SharedSupport, and then launch Outlook Profile Manager.

Given your situation, your old mac has saved your email a year ago, you can also try to sign in your account in your mail provider’s website and check whether you have some emails missing. In normal circumstance, your emails will sync to email sever, when your sign in your account in client, your email will sync to your outlook client. If your outlook works properly after creating a new profile, your emails in the past year will sync to your client, and you can move those emails a year ago to your new mac refer to this link: https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Export-items-to-an-archive-file-in-Outlook-for-Mac-281A62BF-CC42-46B1-9AD5-6BDA80CA3106 , your will have your data entirely in this way.

you can also request further assistance in Outlook for Mac build-in support in your old mac. Build-in support is a dedicated support channel focus on Outlook for Mac issue.

Thanks,

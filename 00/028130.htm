rmiller02 say: i found a fix!

well, i just picked up this phone the other day, and i absolutely love it. simple and sturdy. except it started making an annoying rattling noise. it scared me a bit at first because i thought it was something important internal.

i decided i'd explore to find out after i read some posts about this problem. after doing a bit of trial and error, i pin pointed the problem down to those little clear blue light covers on the sides. there are little metal tabs that stick out by the clear lenses that seem to support the back cover. the rattling only happened when i had the cover on the phone. i took the phone completely apart and realized that when those tabs are pushed in by the back cover, the lenses just sit there and rattle with no pressure on them from the metal tabs.

so what i did was, using a small screw driver and tweezers, i wedged a very small piece of paper in between the lens and the plastic frame to support them from rattling when the cover was on. i do not believe you have to take the phone completely apart to do this. if one of your lenses pops out, simply place the small piece of paper where you want it then the lens will pop right back in from the outside.

be careful doing this and not to break any pieces. i used a very small square piece of paper folded in half and made sure i did not cover the part of the lens that the blue light shines through with the paper.

i hope this helps you all. it's a rather simple fix, but it took my rattling away. :)

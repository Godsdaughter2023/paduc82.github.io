It may be that your printer driver isn't compatible with Windows 10.

As a test, leave PowerPoint out of the picture.  Start Control Panel, open Devices and Printers, right-click the printer's icon, choose Printer Properties from the pop-up menu and then on the General Tab of the Properties dialog box that appears, click Print Test Page.

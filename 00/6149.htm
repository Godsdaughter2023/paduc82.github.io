miker.riley say: After posting this I finally found another post that seemed to have a similar problem and a solution.  I tried this and it worked.

Basically you create a new line above the bad Header 1 entry.  I did this by hitting <Return> at the end of the previous line.  You then enter the text as you want it to look at change the Style of that line to be Header 1.  Now remove the bad line.

When you update your TOC with <F9> it should have fixed the bad entry.

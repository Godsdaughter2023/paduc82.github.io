For backwards compatibility to Excel 97-2003 you can use IF() together with ISNA() like this,

=IF(ISNA(VLOOKUP(...)),"",VLOOKUP(...))

... but IFERROR() was introduced in Excel 2007 and tightens up the formula a good deal,

=IFERROR(VLOOKUP(...),"")

IFERROR function

Where C1 is the lookup value, and A:A the table from where to lookup C1

joeu2004 say:

WDPgh wrote:

IF(AB4 >1.0, <1.25, "17.25", IF(AB4 >1.24, <1.50, "14.50", IF (AB4 >1.49, <1.75, "12.75")))

The answer I got was "FALSE", it should have been "12.75"? Is there an error in my input?

First and foremost, do not put quotes around values that you intend to be numbers. Type 17.25, not "17.25".

Second, you are over-specifying the requirements. That might get you into trouble, especially if AB4 contains a calculation.

Third, you neglect to cover the case where AB4<=1 and AB4>=1.75.

Finally, the formula that you show above cannot be the formula that returns FALSE (not "FALSE" <wink>). What you write above is syntactically incorrect. Excel does not permit you to enter it.

In the future, copy the formula from the Formula Bar (next to "fx" in the toolbars) and paste it into your postings.

To remedy all of the above, you might write:

=IF(AND(AB4 > 1, AB4 < 1.25), 17.25, IF(AB4 < 1.5, 14.50, IF(AB4 < 1.75, 12.75, "out of range") ) )

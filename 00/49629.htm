Hey Denise

In regards to the E202 error code, the article that was included above was just to refer you to the steps required to bring up the troubleshoot menu. Just as a reference I will also be placing those here:

  1. Power off your console, and then unplug the power cord to ensure that the console is completely powered off.
  2. Wait 30 seconds, and then plug the power cord back in.
  3. Press and hold the BIND button (located below the Xbox button on the console) and the EJECT button (located on the front of the console), and then press the Xbox button on the console.
  4. Continue holding the BIND and EJECT buttons for 10-15 seconds.
  5. Listen for two “power-up” tones a couple of seconds apart. You can release the BIND and EJECT buttons after the second power-up tone.
  6. The console should power up and take you directly to the Xbox Startup Troubleshooter.
  7. From here select Reset this Xbox and choose the option to Reset and keep games and apps.

If this does not work, I recommend following steps 1-7 and selecting the option to Reset and remove everything.

If the issue persists on the device, I would recommend to contact Customer Supportfor further assistance or visit the Device service and Repair site for any warranty information on the console.

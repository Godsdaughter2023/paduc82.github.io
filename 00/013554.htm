a. user say:

Hi balvis4,

 

I'm pleased to assist you with your concern on recovering your lost/deleted emails.

These are the possible reasons why your emails were missing or deleted from your mailbox:

  1. Messages were manually deleted either due to accidental deletion or were moved or deleted due to your account filter settings.
  2. Messages were deleted due to account compromise issues where a hacker might have gained access to your account and deleted the emails.
  3. You are using an e-mail client or a third party spam filtering software to access your account. This often happens when using thePOP3 protocol to retrieve your mail and the option “Always leave a copy of the message on the server” is not selected. There are also security programs that include e-mail filtering systems. There are instances when the software configuration causes false positives causing valid messages to be either sent to the junk folder or deleted altogether.

    Note: If you have configured your emails on your phones using POP3, you might lose your emails too if you haven't chosen to leave a copy on the server. We suggest that you use Exchange ActiveSync (EAS) to access your emails on your phone instead of POP3. EAS keeps everything in sync between your phone and Hotmail, so whatever you do on your phone, like delete and email, add an appointment, or update one of your contacts with a new number, will also be reflected on the web, and vice-versa.
  4. The account was marked as “Inactive." Microsoft accounts become inactive if you don’t sign in for more than 365 days or within the first 10 days after signing up for an account. After an account becomes inactive, all messages, folders, and contacts are deleted. Incoming messages will be sent back to the sender as undeliverable. Your account name is still reserved.

To resolve the issue, you can recover the emails that were accidentally deleted or missing from your mailbox by doing the self-recovery process. Please visit the help articles below for more information:

 

Recover deleted email messages in Hotmail

 

What should I do if email messages are missing from my Hotmail account?

 

However, if you're using third party email client to access your Microsoft account, please make sure that you have selected“Always leave a copy of the message on the server” option to prevent this incident in the future.

 

Thank you.

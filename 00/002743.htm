a. user say:

Hi Daisy,

Thank you for posting your query on Microsoft Community.

Kindly login to https://outlook.com/ through your outlook.com account.

 

If you have been upgraded to Outlook.com (preview) interface, then follow the steps below to block email accounts. 

1. Click on the Gear (settings) icon and select Options.

2. On the left pane, expand Mail > Junk Mail and select Blocked Senders.

3. Now add the email Id of the sender you wish to block.

If you are still on Outlook.com interface, then follow the steps below to block email accounts. 

1. Click on the Gear (settings) icon and select Options.

2. Under Preventing Junk email, click on Safe and blocked senders.

3. Now, click on Blocked Senders. 

4. Type the email address and select  Add to list >>

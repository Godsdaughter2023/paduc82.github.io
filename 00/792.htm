bill manville say:

A custom number format of 0*0 will put the number in the cell and fill it to the right with zeros.
You can't control the number of digits except by varying the column width (!).

Better might be to use a formula to convert the values to 9-character strings.

=A2&REPT("0",9-LEN(A2))

Having got the 9-character strings you could copy and paste as values and delete the originals.

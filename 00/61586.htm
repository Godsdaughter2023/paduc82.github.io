Thanks for reaching out to us! If you would like to wipe your console prior to selling it, you'll want to format the storage device.

To format a storage device

  1. Press the Guide button  on your controller, go to Settings, and select System Settings.
  2. Select Storage or Memory.
  3. Select the storage device that you want to format, and then press Y  on your controller.
  4. Select Format. You will see the following message:
    This will delete all content on this device. Do you wish to continue?

The console serial number is located on the back of the console, and behind the oval door on the front of the console.

The automatic cycling of wallpapers can be done in Windows 8 as well. All you need to do is to place your wallpapers in one folder and place it somewhere safe so that you may not rename it or delete it by accident.

To make wallpapers change automatically in Windows 8, follow these steps.

a. Right click on your desktop an choose Personalize
b. In the personalization window, click on Desktop Background.
c. In the desktop background window, click on Browse button to locate the directory where your wallpapers are located. After that, click on Select All button and all the wallpapers in that directory will be selected.
d. At the bottom of the window, you will find option to shuffle wallpapers. Check the Shuffle option and set the time after which you want to change the wallpaper.
e. One drawback with this method is that you have to place all your wallpapers in one directory i.e. if your root directory has sub-folders with other wallpapers in them, windows will not index those wallpapers. So you have to place all your images in one folder to cycle them all.

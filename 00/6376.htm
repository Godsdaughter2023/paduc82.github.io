What you're referring to is a feature of the Windows OS defined as Access Keys, not specific to Office apps. (I believe it was once referred to as Active Menus but I'm not sure I'm remembering the name correctly.) At any rate, it has been carried forward to the Win Office Ribbon.

OS X doesn't offer a comparable service for anything other than the main Menu Bar [which is what Daniel described] & the primary [Standard] toolbar. One of the OS X options is to use Option+F10 to access the Standard Toolbar. The Tab key can then be used to navigate from one button to the next & the Arrows can be used to open lists provided by certain buttons. That capability doesn't extend to other toolbars or to the Mac Office Ribbon.

Hi,

Carry out the following steps:

1. Once your Area chart is created, click on the DESIGN under the CHART TOOLS MENU

   (see picture)

  And then click on "Select Data"

2. When the "Select Data Source" box pops up, click on  "Hidden and Empty Cells" Button

   (see picture)

3. Now when "Hidden and Empty Cells Settings" Box pops up,

    you will see that the radio button marked "zero" is pre-selected.

   You have to select the "Gaps" radio button and click OK and come out.

 

4.  Your work will be done.     (see picture)

  

When you have Stacked Area Chart type selected and if you follow these steps then,

some how the "Gaps" radio button remains GREYED OUT and you are not able toi select it.

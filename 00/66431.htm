Honestly, using Geth Shield Boost - or Barriers or Fortification - on a Soldier is kind of an unnecessary overkill. Most of the time you should be firing from Adreneline Rush, which gives you more than enough of a damage boost - and if you pick Hardened you take 50% less damage anyway.

 

Squad Cryo is really useful, even on Insanity, as long as you use it correctly. You a going to want to equip it to your squadmates weapon with the heighest rate-of-fire to increase the chances of them freezing an enemy.

If you equip either Zaeed or Thane - with the 50% weapon damage boost from their passive ability -  with the Incisor sniper rifle and Squad Cryo ammo they can normally freeze any standard trooper in two 3 burst rounds.

cody c. say: Hi italoz,

My understanding is that Entourage does no have any recipient limits built into the software. Instead, these limits are enforced by your mail provider. This is usually to prevent spammers from using their servers.

To work around this, you'll need to send multiple emails with a smaller group of recipients. Alternatively, you can contact your mail provider and ask for an increase in their recipient limit.

Hope this helps!

macropod say:
There is no mergefield limit, per se, although there is an overall limit of 2,147,483,647 fields in a document. The more pertinent issue is whether Word can access as many data fields as your source contains. Depending on the data source, the OLE DB driver used for mailmerges may not be able to access more than 255 fields (a limitation that applies to Excel data sources, for example).

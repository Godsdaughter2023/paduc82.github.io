I'm not clear on what you intend to do with the percentages... especially since your expressed concern pertains only to the negative percentages.

If they aren't intended to be used in calculations there really is nothing to be gained by 'converting' them to values. For appearance sake you could just select the entire column & apply Right Alignment as well as any font color or other font formatting you want.

  2. Press return
  3. Go to Edit> Fill> Down [Command+D or Control+D]
  4. Apply Percentage number formatting

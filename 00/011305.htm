If you're asking "how do I search the Outlook AddressBook" by Company then the answer is "you can't"

For accuracy, the Outlook AddressBook is a specialized view of the contact folders that are set to be a member of the AB. Ergo, you can search the applicable contact folder by company by
1) opening the applicable Contact folder,
2) click on the Search box
3) which will result in the <Search> ribbon appearing and from there,
4) click on "Search Tools" --> Advanced Find
- in the case of searching the Company field
5) enter the company name you want to search in the "Search for the words" box
6) select the "Company field only" from the list in the <In> drop-down

The standby time in the specification is a theoratical figure based on ideal conditions that can only be replicated in a laboratory. In practice, you be laughing if you got half that. This figure is also the standby time which means everytime you use the phone for anything such as have the backlight on, listen to music, make a call, this figure is reduced.

Selection.PrintOut Copies:=1, Collate:=True

Selection.PrintOut Copies:=1, Collate:=True ', IgnorePrintAreas:=False

As I ever say: Never use Select, Selection, ActiveCell in your macros.

E.g. if you select cell A1 and the cell is merged with other cells, then all those cells are selected. So every macro that requires a single cell fails. Use the Range object directly to avoid such errors.

Look in your sheet for merged cells or whatever is selected and try to insert the rows manually to discover the problem.

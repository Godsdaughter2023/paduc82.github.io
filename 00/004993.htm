john korchok say:

I'm not sure where you're seeing More Styles. Style Sets are displayed on the DESIGN tab and any style sets you have saved are kept in C:\Users\YourUserName\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\QuickStyles. Style sets are actually just ordinary Word templates with a .dotx ending.

You can open this folder to check of the template files are still there by editing YourUserName to your actual user name, then pasting the path into the address bar of any Windows file window. An example of what could have happened is if you have recently switched user profiles, that could cause your custom style set templates to "disappear", but they are still in your old user profile.

If you are referring to the default style sets that come with Word (i.e. Elegant, Minimalist, etc.), then your copy of Office may need a repair:

  1. In Windows, click on the Start button, then on Control Panel.
  2. Choose Programs, then Programs and Features.
  3. Right-click on Microsoft Office 2013 and choose Change.
  4. Select Online Repair, then click on Repair. Allow the process to finish, then restart Windows and check Word to see if the default style sets have been restored. Word may ask for your 25-character product key when you restart.

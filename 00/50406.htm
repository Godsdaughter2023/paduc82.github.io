2016 12 19- SURFACE BOOK WITH SURFACE BOOK DOCK AND DUAL 4K MONITORS NOT WORKING. – POSSIBLE FIX
https://answers.microsoft.com/thread/2389a507-e...
<snip>
I had the exact same setup and got it working today. I have a 512GB i7 Surface Book with Performance Base and two Dell P2715Q monitors. The monitors were delivered today and I got them working with the Dock.
The short story is this - I saw this post on MSFT’s website: https://www.microsoft.com/surface/en-us/support... shoot-docking-station-surface-dock.
Paying particular attention to two key points:
1. This monitor is specifically listed as approved (though I have no idea how to check the firmware or update it).
2. The key phrase on this page is:
You can fix some DisplayPort video issues by disabling “MST mode” or “DP1.2 mode” on your monitor. Refer to the owner’s manual that came with your monitor to learn how. Disabling “MST mode” or “DP1.2 mode” on your monitor will cause display daisy-chaining to stop working. If you’re using more than one monitor, we recommend that you connect both monitors directly to your Surface Dock, one to each Mini DisplayPort on your dock.
Now, to the amateur this would mean that you should do what it says, specifically turn off MST, do not daisy chain and connect both monitors directly in to the Surface Dock. This is their trick - to try to throw off the less experienced trouble shooters out there.

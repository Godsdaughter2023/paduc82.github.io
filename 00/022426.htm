How do I delete the current music off my Zune mp3 player?

I purchased a used Zune model 1091 mp3 player with music on it that I want to delete so that my disc space is 100% free. How do I do it?

a. user say:

Hi StevenSims,

 

There a couple of way to delete media from your Zune device.  You can delete content from the Zune itself or with the Zune software on a PC.

 

Outlined in the following steps (from http://www.microsoft.com/windowsphone/en-us/howto/wp7/music/delete-music-videos-and-podcasts.aspx) are how to accomplish this:

 

To delete music, videos, and podcasts (Zune player method)


1.) On Start, tap Music + Videos.
2.) Flick to Zune, tap Music, Videos, or Podcasts.
3.) Tap and hold the item that you want to remove (for example, a song or album), and then tap Delete.

 

To delete music, videos, and podcasts (PC method, requires Zune sofware installed)

1.) Connect your phone to your PC using the USB cable that came with your phone.

**If the Zune software is installed on your computer, the program will start automatically. If it isn't installed on your computer, see Install the Zune software.**

2.) In the Zune software, click Phone, and then click Music, Videos, or Podcasts.

3.) Right-click the item or items that you want to remove (for example, a song, album, or artist), and then click Delete from (phone name).

 

 

Note
Deleting an item from your phone doesn't delete it from your computer. Items that you delete from your phone are automatically skipped during future syncs with the Zune software.

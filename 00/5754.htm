julie fka profjulie say: You can use an option in WMP to set a key so that when it is pressed the screen will be turned off.  The key you designate will act as a toggle.  When you press it the screen will be turned off, pressing it again will turn the screen back on.  To set this option:

Open up Windows Media Player on your Pocket PC
Tap Menu > Options > tap the right arrow to toggle over to the Buttons tab
Tap the Buttons tab and scroll down the page to the end until you see the "Toggle Screen" option.  Tap it to highlight it, then tap the Assign button and then press the button you 'll press to goggle the screen on or off.
Tap OK at the top of the screen and you're done.

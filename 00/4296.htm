ashish mathur say:

Hi,

 

Try this

 

1. Suppose the smaller dataset is in range A2:A50 and the larger one is in D2:D100

2. IN cell B2, enter this formula and copy down

 

=COUNTIF($D$2:$D$100,A2)

 

3. If all records in the small dataset are available in the large dataset, then all numbers in column B should be >0

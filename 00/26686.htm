Filter by a month and year

diane poremsky mvp (slipstick.com) say:

Oops. I missed the Excel part in the metadata. I thought it was Outlook VBA. :( Sorry about that.

You need to use DateSerial to get the values for 6/1/2014 and 6/30/2014 then filter for everything in between.  

 Dim dStart As Long
 Dim dEnd As Long

'replace [year] & [month] with the year and month or the cell reference where it picks up the date to use

 dStart = DateSerial([Year], [Month], 1)

' day 0 of next month = last day of previous month

 dEnd = DateSerial([Year], [Month] + 1, 0)

 Criteria1:=">=" & dStart, Operator:=xlAnd, Criteria2:="<=" & dEnd

ETA: this assumes the values are for any day of the month. If all dates are the same, you only need criteria1.

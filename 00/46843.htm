Hello Pawlybeans,

In the Windows 10 environment, there are two ways on how to disable the touchpad when a mouse is connected. Here are the steps:

Method 1: Settings

  1. Press Windows + I on your keyboard to launch Settings.
  2. Click Devices.
  3. click Touchpad.
  4. Uncheck the option "Leave touchpad on when a mouse is connected."

Method 2: Control Panel

  1. Launch Control Panel by searching via Cortana search box.
  2. Click Hardware and Sound.
  3. Under Devices and Printers, click Mouse.
  4. Click the Device Settings tab.
  5. Uncheck the Disable internal pointing device when external USB pointing device is attached option.

In some laptop, the option to disable the touchpad is dependent on the support software. If the above steps fails, it means that this option can only be performed by the software Sony has provided.

I too have suffered from the problems of partial recordings or no recordings at all, which seemed to have no obvious solution.  As Windows Media Center was preinstalled I had no way of re-installing it properly after uninstalling it.

(I tried to uninstall and had to do a complete recovery of my C: drive to get it back!)

So, before trying any of the above complex solutions, I would strongly urge you to try the simplest fix first... http://www.thegreenbutton.tv/forums/viewtopic.php?f=5&t=6075.

I believe that the problem started when I was faced with a completely unresponsive machine, showing just a blank screen.  I 'hard-booted' and all appeared OK, but then I found that WMC scheduling wouldn't work properly. 

This was most likely because I had bounced the box while WMC had the "StartRecording" file in "C:\Windows\System32\Tasks\Microsoft\Windows\Media Center" open, thus corrupting it. 

Deleting this file (with WMC closed) seems to fix the problem.

I know this seems too good to be true, especially if, like me, you've been trying to find a fix for several days, but hey, what've you got to lose?

This could happen if your Normal.dotm template was saved with Tracked Changes turned on. To open your Normal.dotm template:

  4. Open Word, then choose File>Open.

  6. Open Normal.dotm, turn off Track Changes and save the file.
  7. Close Word, reopen and test a new document.

entegy say:
Microsoft doesn't maintain one, but I found this website:

What you'll want to do is grab the web link of the iCal version (not the Microsoft Outlook version!) of the calendar you want and subscribe to it in your Outlook.com calendar.

To subscribe to the calendar:

  1. Right-click the iCal version and choose "Copy shortcut" or "Copy link location" or whatever the equivalent is for the browser you're using.
  2. Go to your Outlook.com calendar.
  3. Click "Import" at the top of the screen.
  4. Click "Subscribe" on the left side of the screen.
  5. Paste the URL into the URL box.
  6. Give the calendar a name, colour and charm of your choosing.
  7. Click "Subscribe"


That's it!

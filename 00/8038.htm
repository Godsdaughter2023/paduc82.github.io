The best way to have all of your mails in one place is to Auto-forward them from your Hotmail account to the new one. You can also use your new address in replying to your emails. Here are the steps to set it up:

 

1. Sign in to your Microsoft account at http://mail.live.com

2. On the upper-right corner of the page, click Options then More options...

3. Under Managing your account, click Email forwarding. You might have to sign in.
4. Select the radio button for Forward your mail to another email account.
5. Enter the email address for your new Outlook account.
6. If you want, check the box for Keep a copy of forwarded messages in your Hotmail inbox.
7. Click Save.

 

Note: Please be reminded that free account users need to sign in to the account they choose to auto-forward at least once every 270 days to avoid having the system remove the mailbox due to inactivity.

HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Office\16.0\Common\ExperimentEcs

Once these registry keys have been deleted uninstall MS Office and reboot your PC.

Reinstall Office and you should find the issue resolved.

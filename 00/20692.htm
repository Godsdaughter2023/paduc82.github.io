BTW, many OEM manufacturers provide trial versions of anti-virus programs preinstalled on computers as part of their software package. It is not uncommon for a computer manufacturer to partner or enter into an agreement with a well known anti-virus vendor (Norton, McAfee, etc) to include their product as part of a marketing strategy for generating revenue. 5. Click Safe senders.

7. Click Add to list>>.

1. Sign in to your account.

2. In the upper-right of the window, click the Options icon (gear icon).

3. Click More mail settings.

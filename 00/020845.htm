joshua_969 say:

Hello, we were able to resolve this issue.  For anyone interested the following steps were taken:

1. Switch back to the old admin portal to review errors not found in the newer admin portal. In our case this resulted in an error about being unable to determine the country or region.

2. We used ADSIedit to change the users "c" value, in our case from "EU" to "US". We sync one way from AD to office365.

3.  We forced replication, waited a few minutes, and things started working again.  We did have a couple of users take an extra minute or so, but even they were resolved quickly.

The big issue was that the new admin portal gave no indication or hint of this root cause error. Hope this helps someone else.

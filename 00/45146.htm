8) Then add your account back to your Xbox One and try to send a message again

With windows 10, to receive Bluetooth files from a paired device to the computer.

1.  In settings click on 'devices',

2.  click on 'Bluetooth',

3.  click 'more Bluetooth options',

4.  in 'Bluetooth settings' click on 'com ports' tab,

5.  then click 'add', select 'incoming (device initiates the connection)'.
The computer sets an incoming com port with the name of your device.
From the Bluetooth menu on your computer,

1.  After you click 'receive files' on your computer,

2.  on your mobile device you click the 'share link' from the file or picture,

3.  click 'Bluetooth', then click the device(computer name) you are sending to.

  Don't wait for your mobile device to search for connected devices. It will still say, "no nearby devices found."

  The computer will give you a 'save the received file' dialog box, you can choose where you want to store the file.

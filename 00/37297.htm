You can move all your documents without a problem, but not your

registry and elsewhere in \Windows, to where the programs are.

them is likely to create problems with some programs.

So it's best to reinstall the programs.

Hello,
 
Welcome to Microsoft Community and thank you for posting your query.
 
Sorry for the inconvenience caused, I will certainly help you with this issue.
 
Did you check in the Windows.old folder?
 
If not, go to the below location and check, In this location all files docs, pictures etc., would be there, copy them and paste to your desired location.

C:/Windows.old/users/<Your Username>
 
Windows.old folder will be created when you upgrade from previous Windows version to Windows 10.
It will be automatically removed by performing a disk clean-up after 28 days from the date of upgrade.
 
Please refer to the link below for steps to manually retrieve your old files.

How to retrieve files from the Windows. Old folder:
http://windows.microsoft.com/en-IN/windows-8/restore-files-upgrade-windows-old

(Also applies to Windows 10).

You may also check if the files are present in the recycle bin.
If found, Right click on the files and select restore.

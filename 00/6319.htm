Hi Sandro,

if you need an Exchange on-premises for recipient management purposes only and also managing your on-premises Active Directory objects for use with password sync, you don't need to establish a hybrid connection.

If you want to onboard and offboard mailboxes between your local Exchange and Exchange Online, a hybrid (or minimal hybrid) connection is required.

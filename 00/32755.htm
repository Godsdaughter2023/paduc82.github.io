I'm sure you've checked this but ways that I've seen this happen are...
 
1. One of the email services is set to auto-forward all messages to the
other
2. One of the email services is set to query the other for new messages, and
download all new messages
3. Your mail app has a rule set to forward incoming messages.
4. Your mail app has a "reply-to" address set which is built in to its
outgoing messages.
 
Then there's the issue of duplicate Gmail sent messages using their IMAP
servers.
 
I'm guessing that sender to your Zoomtown email are also sending to your
EarthLink address because they haven't deleted it from their mail list.

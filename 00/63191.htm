The driver \Driver\WudfRd failed to load for the device ROOT\WPD\0000 error might occur if a service driver fails to load properly. You may try the methods listed below and check if it works:

Method 1

Turn off Fast Startup:

A service driver that couldn’t load properly might result in such issues. You may disable Fast Startup and check if it works.

  • Open Control Panel and categorize by Power Options.

  • Click on Choose what the power buttons do and choose Change settings that are currently unavailable.

  • Disable Turn on fast startup (recommended) and Save the changes.

Method 2

Adjust Power Management for NVidia:

  • Open Control Panel and categorize by small icons.

  • Click on NVidia Control Panel and choose 3D Settings.

  • Select Manage 3D settings and set it to High Performance instead of Adaptive under Power Management mode.

  • Restart the machine to see if it works.

You may also want to go through Tips to improve PC performance in Windows 10 to optimize the performance of your PC.

Note:

Reset will result in the loss of data and the applications installed. You may take a backup of the data before performing Reset.

The attachment files are actually created on the target system on the day they are saved to the local HDD: this is what happens when you open attachments in Word, even if they are open as temporary files. When you go into Customize Toolbars & Menus, click on the name of the Menu Bar toolbar, then click the Reset button & confirm.

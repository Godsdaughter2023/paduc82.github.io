    (rearranging files so they are contiguous, which makes for quicker access ) may not be finished.

    You could try running the Windows defrag tool instead, but this one is also known for getting stuck, or

    appearing to get stuck, and is not very popular.

    will be more or less starting from the beginning, but it should still take no more than a couple of hours

    at the most.

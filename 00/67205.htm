Ok I do not see much on here about actual strategy with this game. So I will start the ball.

1. When you see an allied parked, occupied tank in the middle of the battlefield check before you hop in and start madly driving towards the enemy. Before you act like a hero and waste the tank in a stupid suicide run,check the situation! Not hard to read!

2. In case you did not know, the UAV only can use it's secondary machine gun fire if you have VALT mod active on your toon. I didn't know that till recently.

3. Tired of committing suicide after jumping off a building when you get shot? Did you know if you double tap the jump button you auto deploy your chute? No magic. Just the controls.

4. Engineers can use their fix tool to take apart the enemy tanks as well! Use it! And did you notice the Kill Tool achievement? Yup. You can drill someone in the head just like the medic can actually shock someone to death! TASTY!

5. Are you playing for fun or to win the round? I understand sometimes you want to just snipe in the shadows. You want to be the soldier covered in the most guts. The highest point rank. We all have been there. But if you want to win, work with your team. Support them as you move up the map. Team up when attacking. When you have 5k points and the team still loses this should be saying something to you! Yup your a star. But not a team player.

6.The team with the most snipers usually loses. Snipe, scout, cap! Sprinkle Mortar for flavor.  Don't just sit on the hill and snipe. You kill one target great. Two even better. But for certain someone is b-lining to snipe you back since we all get to see your ingenious hiding spot when we die. Tunnel vision is the fastest way to get a knife lodged in your noggin. C4 is great but you can't push the button on the trigger if someone already rolled over you.

7. Holy helicopters. Yup, I hate them because they are the weakest part of my game. I need to learn them because the team that has the best helicopter support will win hands down every time (unless that pilot doesn't follow point 5). I find the controls for this machine difficult and unruly. But I have seen surgeons in the air and I swear one day I will figure this out!

8. RPG vs mines. Ok mines are great. I love them because so many people just miss the audio cue they are in their path and then BOOM, double or triple kill. Of course when I am playing against experienced players that know team work I make diddle with mines. RPG's are the best tool to take out tanks and sometimes even other units and buildings. Only recently have I been practicing with them and I can see their value. The best part is you are mobile with a RPG and don't have to wait for someone to jack in the box your devious plan. One side note, two mines behind a tank and a grenade works awfully well.

9. Medics are amazing when you get good with them. And boy can you rack up points fast if you have a team that works as a team! Drop the heals  and res your mates. Try not to die and check the area. There is probably a reason three of your team just keeled over. Might want to deal with that before you rush out with the blood packs!

10. Saving Ryans privates with grenade spam. If you are a **** class drop a resupply kit behind cover and then start hucking grenades all over the attack zone. The nice thing about team damage off is you don't have to worry about killing your own. But boy does it sow great chaos in the enemies plans. Note: you should behave like a scout class in this case and keep moving each time you kill someone. Bet your donuts someone is coming to knife you when they die this way!

This is just a start to what I have found over the course of my play. Now I want to hear your tips to newbs and experienced alike. Just because you think it is common sense does not mean others think the same way! Show us your brilliance! 

Enjoy!

Norton 360 v2 adds a feature called "Backup Status Indicators" which clearly identifies which files have been backed up, which files are pending backup and which are excluded from backup. This feature is responsible for the Tick mark (green box) and double arrow (blue box) overlay icons.

    Disable icon overlays in Windows Explorer

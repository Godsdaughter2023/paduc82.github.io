You can do that via the Insert Table of Contents facility and then click on the Modify button on the Table of Contents tab of the Table of Contents dialog and then select each of the TOC # Styles in turn and click on the Modify button in the Style dialog and then in the Modify Style dialog, click on Format and then on Tabs and set a right aligned tab stop at the desired location.

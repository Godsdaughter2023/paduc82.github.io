I got it to work!

I had tried the Realtek drivers previously and the installation reported that it couldn't find the matching adapter. I checked the DVD that came with the motherboard and discovered that the original drivers were Realtek. Also, the ASUS support site only had Realtek drivers. Big clue here!!! This is why I decided to give it another go and ignore the error message about not finding the adapter.

During my search for a solution, I perused some BIOS version history on the ASUS support site and found several versions later than the version I had installed that mentioned the WiFi adapter. This clued me in that maybe I should update the BIOS.

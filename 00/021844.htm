susan burkholder say:

I had the same problem and it was driving me nuts but I finally figured it out today how to change the internal shape margin.

 

If you haven’t already done so enable the Developer Tab under Options – customize Ribbon and under Advance I selected “Run in developer mode”.

 

  • Click on the developer tab

  • Double click inside the text box of the shape (title or name)

  • Click on Show ShapeSheet and select Shape

  • Scroll down until you see “Text Transform”

  • Click inside the “TxtWidth” box and adjust the width of the margin per your needs and hit enter to accept your change.

  • Close the Shapesheet and you’re done.

 

Note:  You will have to do this for each individual shape’s margins.  I don’t know a way to change the actual templates so the margins are larger each time you create a new org chart.

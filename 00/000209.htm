Image in icon will not show on Facebook post of SWAY...only the green - Microsoft Community

jonathand1950 say:

Image in icon will not show on Facebook post of SWAY...only the green SWAY icon


 

 I create SWAYs to post to facebook...usually no problem.  But occasionally ...and now for sure...the background image I use does not show on facebook, just the green SWAY icon.  I have done everything...created a new sway and copied the text; duplicated it; selected different images...NOTHING works.  I can see the image on SWAY but when I share it..no image until you open it.  Then who cares..I need the image to draw people to open the sway..not the green icon.  HELP

jason (office engineer) say:

What is shown on Facebook is decided by Facebook.  When you share from Sway, you are redirected out of the Sway site to Facebook.  When you load the Facebook share UI, they load the link from your post and grab content out of it to show.  Like search engines, they don't grab this information live.  It is grabbed once when the initial post is made and, depending on the site, it could be as much as a week or two before they update the stored information for a URL.  That is why you sometimes see stale data in web search results, for instance.

There are two reason why the Sway image will show instead of one of your images:

  1. The Sway has no image content in it - clearly, your Sway has an image in it and it is a size that works.  Is it possible that the first time you opened the Facebook post UI that you didn't yet have an image in your Sway (or your image hadn't finished processing yet)?  If so, Facebook would have just stored the Sway image.
  2. Facebook doesn't have permission to display the image - depending on the type of account you use the access Sway, it might not.  If you're using an Office365 organizational account (like from a school or an employer), the default permissions (in the Sway Share dropdown) are set to be only for your organization and not for public consumption.  If that was your setting when you initially shared the Sway, Facebook would have only been able to access the Sway image.

In both of these cases, if you want to change what is shown, it is tied to the URL you use on Facebook's side of things and I know of no way to get it to update.  From our side, the only way to do that is to change the URL of your Sway.  There are two ways to do this:

  1. In the bottom of the Share dropdown in Sway, in the More Options section, is a button that says Reset the Share Settings.  Doing this will reset all your permissions to their default state, but it will also change the URL on your Sway to a new one.
  2. You can duplicate this Sway to create a new one (from the Sway ... menu) and then share again.  Be careful not to mix up which Sway is which though and delete the wrong one after trying this.

Please let me know if this doesn't solve your problem.

Jason

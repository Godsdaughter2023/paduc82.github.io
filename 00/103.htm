From what I've read, the upload bandwidth to O365 is ~500mb/hr.  We've seen as high as ~800mb/hr uploads when moving folders from a local PST to the Cloud.

Make sure you move the PST file itself off your network server or share to the local drive (C:\) and upload it from there.  Also make sure your computer is setup to not go to "Sleep" after a certain time of inactivity, otherwise the upload will fail.

With a 25GB PST, you're looking at a very long time to upload that PST based on your bandwidth speeds.  I would recommend breaking that PST up into 1GB chucks if possible and upload them separately.

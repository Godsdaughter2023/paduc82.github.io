First, sign in here with the email you set up the phone with.  You can doublecheck what account you used by going to Settings -> Email+Accounts and look for the first account listed as Windows Live.  Once signed in, click on Edit Personal Info, and make sure the date of birth, country, and time zone are filled out fully and accurately.  Then click on Security Info on the left.  If you previously set up Two Step Verification, you'll need to make sure you've created an app password on this page, and entered that on your phone as the password for your account.  Also, make sure that any secondary security proofs like phone numbers and alternate email addresses have been verified.

https://live.xbox.com/en-US/AccountMigration

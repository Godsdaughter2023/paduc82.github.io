williambryce say:

This issue seems to be caused by the settings in power management.  I created an advanced power management profile and when the brightness setting is lower on power than when on battery and you have the power adapter plugged into the Surface (Surface Pro in my case) and the battery is fully charged then the surface goes from 'slight charging - realizing the battery is charged then stops charging and switches to battery for a second' and repeats this over and over.  This is probably because the default logic for handling a fully charged battery is to 'stop charging' then 'test again to see if it needs charged'

Anyhow I got it to stop doing this by making sure the brightness was higher on power than battery and enabling the adaptive brightness when on power.

Then it went away.... you may have to mess with the advanced power settings to get it to stop cycling on your surface.

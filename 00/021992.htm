john w. vinson say:

The maximum value of an autonumber is 2147483647, after which it will roll over to -2147483648 and start counting up to 0. So you're nowhere CLOSE.

This is a symptom of corruption in your database, which can usually be cured by simply compacting and repairing; thereafter the autonumber should start up one higher than the current max value. It might be necessary to run an Append query inserting a new record with a Long Integer being inserted into the autonumber field, one less than the desired new start point; this dummy record can then be deleted.

ALTER TABLE MyTable ALTER COLUMN MyAutonumberField COUNTER(250800, 1)

I understand that you would like to receive email notifications from Facebook on your Outlook.com account and that you would like to have these emails forwarded to your Gmail account. You can do this by following these steps:

Integrating Facebook to Outlook.com:
1. Sign in to your account on https://account.live.com
2. Under Permissions, click on Add accounts.
3. Click on Facebook and sign in when prompted.

Note that you have to enter your Outlook.com account in your Facebook account settings to receive notifications. Integrating Facebook to your account would only allow you to chat with your Facebook contacts on Outlook.com and be able to see your Facebook contacts in your Outlook.com People page.

Once you've set-up your Outlook.com account in Facebook, we can create a rule to forward these notifications to your Gmail account. Here are the steps:

1. Sign in to your account in https://outlook.com
2. Click on the gear icon next to the account name on the upper right side of your mailbox page.
3. Select More mail settings.
4. Under Customizing Outlook, click on Rules for sorting new messages.
5. Click on New, then under Step 1., change the 3 fields to the following:

1st Box: Sender's name
2nd Box: contains
3rd Box: Facebook

6. On Step 2., select Forward to and enter your Gmail account.
7. Click on Save.

I hope this helps. Let us know if you need further assistance.

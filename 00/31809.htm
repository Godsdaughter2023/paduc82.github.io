Step 3: Then, turn off your Bluetooth Speakers completely and restart the computer once.

Step 4: Then, follow the steps mentioned in Connect a Bluetooth device to again connect your Bluetooth Speakers to your computer and check if you are able to hear from your Bluetooth Speakers.

Method 3: Change the Playback Device.

Note: If you have plugged in your headphones and then trying to hear the audio from the Bluetooth Speakers, then you may need to change the playback device or remove the headphones from your computer and see if you are able to hear the audio from the Bluetooth Speakers.

Step 1: Right-click on the Sound icon from your taskbar and click on Sounds.

Step 2: Then, go to Playback tab and click on your Bluetooth Speakers > Click on Set Default and see if you are able to hear from your Bluetooth Speakers.

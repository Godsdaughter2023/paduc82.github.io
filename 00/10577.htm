satscool say:

This worked for me

  1.  If your phone is currently stuck on the manufacturer's logo, try removing the battery to turn it off.
  2. Disconnect your phone from the USB cable.
  3. Hold down the camera button.
  1. Continue holding down the camera button and connect the USB cable between your phone and computer.
  2. Continue holding down the camera button until you see the connect-to-computer screen.
  1. Release the camera button.
  1. Leave the phone restart alone, will take around 4/5 minutes

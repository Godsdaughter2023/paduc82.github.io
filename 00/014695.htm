Try inserting them as linked pictures instead.

Outlook.com automatically checks your other account for new messages every 30 minutes, and can receive up to 60 messages at a time.   5. A pop-up box will appear, click Remove.

  8. Click Next.
  9. Then, follow the next step.

a. user say:

Hello j.h.g.

 

Thank you for posting in the community.

 

I understand that your phone is recognized as a camera on your Win XP pc.

 

Please try next steps:

1. Make sure you have at least Windows XP SP2 (Service Pack2). You can check it by right clicking on My Computer & properties.

2. Make sure you have Windows Media Player 10 or later. if you have Windows XP 64 bit you need Windows Media Player 11.

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/download-windows-media-player

3. These latest version of WMP have MTP capabilities. Your Windows Phone will show up under MTP devices. When you connect your phone via USB cable it will try to install drivers. When drivers are installed properly you will be able to see phone folders.

 

4. If drivers were not installed correctly:

Start -> right click on My Computer -> Properties -> Hardware ->Device manager. You might see your phone as Unknown device with exclamation mark.

 

Right click on the Windows Phone device (Unknown device) -> Properties -> Driver tab -> Update Driver. Click on browse My Computer for driver software. Browse C:\Windows\Inf folder on your PC and click OK.  It will find and install driver.

You might need to restart phone and PC.

 

Please let us know if it helped or you have another quiestions.

 

Regards,

Maria

Step 1: Check your Windows version by typing "winver" to Run command. If your version is not 1511 (10586.xxx) or newer and you haven't received this through Windows Update, you need to upgrade your Windows manually.

Step 2: (Optional but recommended) Make a backup of your system and important files on the system disk just in case. Your files should remain intact through the upgrade process, but you never know.

Having a synchronized clock is a great idea. You can refer to the steps below on how to synchronize with the local standard clock.

  1. Open Control Panel.
  2. Choose Clock, Language, and Region.
  3. Select Date and Time.

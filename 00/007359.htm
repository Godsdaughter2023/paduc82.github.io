You misunderstand the bug.   The Windows 10 start menu has a limitation of 512 "shortcuts" (not apps).  Most apps will have more than one shortcut (such as "readme" and "uninstall").   The average seems to be about 4 or 5 shortcuts per app.  Additionally, windows settings count toward your 512 limitation (control panel, display settings, networking, etc).   

Hopefully I have helped you understand that the 512 shortcut limit is a serious flaw, and will eventually affect every Windows 10 user unless it is fixed.

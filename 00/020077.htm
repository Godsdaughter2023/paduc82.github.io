joeu2004 say: gabus1m wrote:
I have a column of numbers with sometimes a letter after the last digit.  Example :
12.20
13,40
15,12A
15,90C
I need to REMOVE the last letter from the cell, in order to leave just the digits (for later summing them (the column) up).

Suppose your data are in A1:A100.  Put the formula into B1 and copy down through B100:

=IF(ISNUMBER(--RIGHT(A1,1)),--A1,--LEFT(A1,LEN(A1)-1))

You can then copy B1:B100 and paste-special-value into A1:A100, then delete B1:B100.

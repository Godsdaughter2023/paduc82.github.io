Protected mode is turned on by default in the Internet and Restricted sites zones and an icon appears on the status bar to let you know that it's running.

Protected mode icon

To manage you contacts in Skype, kindly follow the steps below:

  1. Go to Contacts.
  2. Select the add contact icon or click directly in the search text box and type the name, Skype name or email of the person you want to add. Click  Search Skype.

Business Card sync in Office for Mac 2011 - Microsoft Community

ekenne say:

Business Card sync in Office for Mac 2011

I'm trying to make business cards using the templates in Office-Word for Mac 2011 but I would like to know how to synchronize the changes made to the first card to all ten cards on the page without making the changes individually to each of the ten cards.

Thanks for any help!

john korchok say:

I create most of my business card templates to sync to the card in the upper left. You can start from a Microsoft template-produced page, but you will be revising it substantially. Here's how:

  1. Create the master card in the upper left corner. Make sure each line of text is separated by a carriage return.
  2. Select each line in turn and create a Style from it. It doesn't matter that the styles all look the same, but each line of the original must have a different style name i.e. "Name", "Title", "Address1", etc.
  3. On the next card over, choose Insert>Field...
  4. In the Categories: list, choose Lists and References.
  5. In the Field names: list, choose StyleRef.
  6. Click on the Options button, then select the Styles pane.
  7. Select the style for the first line (probably "Name"), then click on OK.
  8. Enter a carriage return.
  9. Repeat steps 4-8 for each line of information.
  10. Format the second card to match the first, but do not use any of the specially named styles. Those styles must be used only on the master card.
  11. Copy and paste the second card to all the rest of the cards.
  12. Test your document by changing the name on the master card. Then use File>Print to preview the cards. This forces an update of the fields. All the cards should show the revised name spelling.

Please post again if you need help creating the styles.

3. Select More mail settings.

4. Under Managing your account, click POP and deleting downloaded messages.

5. Check Do what my other program says-if it says to delete messages, then delete them

6. Click Save.

Your Windows 10 products are only associated to your Microsoft Account, they are tied to the devices they are installed on. Each will have a digital entitlement to run Windows 10

Activation will not be an issue if and when the time comes to reinstall Windows

A lot of users think that the activation is tied to their Microsoft Account, but this is not the case. If you were to sell one of your devices, you could remove it from your Microsoft Account and it would still be activated.

If you purchased the Windows 10 products separately from the devices, you can install that onto another PC at a later date as long as you de-activate the previous device . . .

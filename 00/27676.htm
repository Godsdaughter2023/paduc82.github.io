memht say: I may have figured out what is going on and a way around this problem:

Because I don't use this account anymore, it does not connect to the server (imap). So when I was deleting these messages, I was actually "marking for deletion." They were still there in the inbox, but Outlook was hiding them until they are really deleted (when connected to the server).

What I did to fix my specific situation, I sorted the emails in the archive by date modified. This brought up all the emails I had attempted to delete to the top. I deleted those from the archive (which actually moves them out because the archive does not need to connect to the server). I then went back into the account and manually drag and dropped whatever was remaining into the correct archive folders.

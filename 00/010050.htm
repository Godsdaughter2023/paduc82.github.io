hansv mvp say:

You don't have to set it up for every individual cell; you can do it for the whole new range in one go.

Select the range of cells to which you want to apply conditional formatting.

Note carefully which cell is the active cell within the selection - you'll see its address on the left hand side of the formula bar.

Select Format | Conditional Formatting...

Specify the rules as if they are for the active cell, and take care to use relative cell references, e.g. greater than G2, not $G$2.

When you click OK, the conditional formatting rules will automatically be adjusted for each cell in the selected range.

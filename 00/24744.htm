3) Drag the item to the desired destination folder

4) highlight the first contact in the sequence
5) hold down the shift key

6) highlight the last contact in the sequence

7) release the shift key - hold down the CTRL key

8) drag the selected items to the desired destination folder

9) hold down the CTRL key

10) highlight each desired contact (keeping the CTRL key down)

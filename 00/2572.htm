How to delete a lot of blank slides out of the document , out of around 200 + slides there  are 2 blank slides  after  every 1 or 2 .

kindly guide.

steve rindsberg say:

This will depend somewhat on what exactly you mean by blank slide.  But if we assume that a slide with no shapes on it is blank, then:

Sub BlankTheBlanketyBlankBlanks()

Dim x as long

With ActivePresentation

For x = .Slides.Count to 1 step -1

  If .Slides(x).Shapes.Count = 0 Then

    .Slides(x).Delete

  End if

Next

End With

End Sub

The reason why beach ball icon appears because you have insufficient RAM. This means more paging and swapping causing fewer CPU cycles became available to application. If an application can't get the CPU time they need, the beach ball icon appears.

To help you with your concern, follow these step by step procedure:

  1.  Manually uninstall Office 2016 through the steps given on this link. (Note: You need to check your Trash folder if there are important files, back up them, then delete unnecessary files to empty your Trash folder.   2. Reinstall Office 2016.
  3. Once done reinstalling, go to file location of the three folders that you back up. Since you reinstall the Office, it will create new folders same as what you have back up from your desktop. Move those three new folders to Trash.
  4. Once done, copy the three files and paste it to the location where you delete it before.
  5. Restart your PC.

If these steps won't work, we suggest you to contact your device manufacturer for further help.

m3tal daz3 say:

If your console was lost or stolen, change your password immediately.

  1. Sign in to https://account.microsoft.com with your Microsoft account.
  2. Go to Security.
  3. Select Change Password to reset the password for your Microsoft account.
  4. You may be asked to verify that you’re the account owner by receiving a security code.
  5. Enter your current password.
  6. Enter and confirm your new password. 

We recommend you report your stolen console to your local law enforcement as well as your insurance company. You’ll need to provide the serial number for the missing console. To find it:

  1. Sign in to https://account.microsoft.com with your Microsoft account, and select Devices.
  2. Scroll down to the console that was stolen.
  3. The serial number will be listed under the device’s name.

You may want to review your billing activity to make sure the person who has your console hasn’t made any unauthorized charges.

  1. On account.microsoft.com, go to Payment and billing > Order history.
  2. If you don’t recognize a charge, contact us.
  3. Keep an eye on your account to make sure that any possible unauthorized charges have time to be posted to your account.

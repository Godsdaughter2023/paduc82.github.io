lemp say:

A "PCI modem" is an add-on card inside a computer that enables communication over telephone lines (the way we used to connect to the Internet).  It may also include fax capability depending on the model.  Other than possibly for fax capability, most people don't use or need modems these days and they are no longer included unless you specifically order them when buying the computer.

You apparently deleted something necessary for this hardware to function.  Exactly where and how did you "uninstall" this item?  Depending on what you did, you may or may not be able to restore functionality simply by responding to the Found New Hardware Wizard.

For more help, please identify the make and model of the computer and, if available, any "Service Tag" or similar identification label on the computer.

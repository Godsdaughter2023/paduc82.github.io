charles kenyon say:

I'm sorry, this is not how Templates in Word work. You have a lot of work ahead of you. Automatically update styles does not bring in document structure but only the styles, which will be unpredictable for you with direct formatting.

Direct formatting will always remain unless you copy and paste as plain text. My suggestions:

  1. Create a new document based on the template.
  2. Copy and paste into the new document as plain text.
  3. Use Styles to match formatting from the original.
  4. If there are Tables, Text Boxes, graphics in the original, copy them one by one and to the extent possible remove direct formatting from them (Ctrl+Spacebar & Ctrl+Q). Then use styles to format them.

That is what I would do.

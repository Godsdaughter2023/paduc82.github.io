jay freedman say:

On the View tab, choose Outline view. On the Outline ribbon (which appears only in Outline view), click the Show Document button to display the rest of the buttons in the Master Document group.

In the past, Master Documents have been associated with irrecoverable document corruption. This seems to occur mostly in cases of editing subdocument content from the master document. I don't know whether Microsoft has done any work to reduce those problems, but it's advisable to use the master only for assembly and printing of the whole project, or else keeping scrupulous backups at frequent intervals.

An alternative is to use INCLUDETEXT and RD fields to create a complete assembly with TOC and index. That older technology is much more stable.

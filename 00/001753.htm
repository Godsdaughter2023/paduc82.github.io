If you cared about only one previous cell (e.g. A1), you could simply write:

=if(A1="", "", yourFormula)

=if(count(A1:A10)=0, "", yourFormula)

Zune HD and Xbox Music

Ive had a Zune HD for a long time. Once the Zune pass ends and I strictly get my music from the Xbox music app will I still be able to sync the music to my Zune?

a. user say:

Hello All:

 

Thank you for the posts and the great wealth of feedback and discussion.  It looks to me there is still quite a bit of unclarity when it comes to the changes on the horizon.  I hope the following details help easy your minds.  If anything is still unclear, let us know and we will continue to work on getting this matter cleared up.

 

The Zune marketplace is being retired but this will have a limited impact on those that use Zune for a Music Pass and for the syncing of content to their Zune players.  The number one thing I want to clarify is that you will still be able to use your Music Pass to download songs from the marketplace.  Once you download these songs you will still be able to sync those songs to the Zune player.  It will not be necessary to use the Xbox Music application to download new WMA content with your Pass. 

 

For those on the original Music Pass with the 10 song credits, you will still be able to use those credits via the Zune software.  The only effect to those buying music is for those that use Microsoft points to buy from the marketplace.  The retirement of the Microsoft points system is at the heart of this change.  As points are going away, so is the ability to buy content with points via the software.  Any content previously purchased via the Zune software, download with a Music Pass or ripped from your private collection will still be able to be synced to your Zune players and Windows phone 7's via the software.

 

Another impact the retirement of this marketplace will have is in the world of videos.  Videos will no longer be able to be purchased or redownloaded via the Zune software.  You will be able to continue to enjoy your previously downloaded videos and sync those to your supported devices.  There will be a website released in the near future that will allow for the purchasing of new content.  Stay tuned for more specific information on this site. 

 

Please let us know if you have any other questions or concerns.

 

Mark W.

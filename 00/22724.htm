What do the rectangles in the calendar mean? - Microsoft Community

entegy say:

What do the rectangles in the calendar mean?

Been trying to figure this out, but I can't. On the agenda view, my events have rectangles next to them. Some are empty rectangles, some are filled rectangles, and one event seems to have a faded fill.

What the heck do they mean?

eeewing say:

Those rectangles indicate your status for the event:

Filled rectangle = BUSY, i.e. you're attending the event

Empty rectangle = FREE, i.e. you are not attending the event

Faded fill = OUT OF OFFICE

Diagonal line fill = TENTATIVE

Per the previous response, the color of the rectangle is a visual indication of which of your calendars the event is associated with.

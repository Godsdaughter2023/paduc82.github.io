You posted this in the Publisher 2007 forum, so I gave you 2007 answers.

If the page size is 5.5" x 8.5", you must be printing this image to a spread with a total size of 11" x 8.5". Go to Page Design>Size and set the paper size to 11 x 8.5. Ensure that you image overalps the paper edge by at least 0.125" on all sides.

  1. In Publisher, click on File, the Print.
  2. Under Settings, click on the page size dropdown and change Letter to Tabloid.

  4. Click on the Marks and Bleeds tab.
  5. Check Crop marks, Allow bleeds and Bleed marks. OK out.
  6. Print Preview will update showing the crop and bleed marks for your 11" x 8.5" spread.
  7. Proceed with printing the PDF.

This issue might occur due to the fact that alignment page continues to print due to the following factors:

  • The automatic alignment process is not complete.
  • The cartridges are not correctly installed.
  • One of the cartridges is defective.
  • One of the cartridges is low or out of ink. Black and all three colors must be available.
  • The new cartridge still has some of the protective tape on it.
  • The contacts on the cartridge do not correctly meet the contacts in the carriage.
  • The cartridge is remanufactured or refilled. Refilled or remanufactured ink cartridges might be damaged during the refilling process or have clogged ink nozzles.
  • The product needs to be serviced.

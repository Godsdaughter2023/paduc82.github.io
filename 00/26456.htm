PS has anybody at Microsoft/Live heard that the default port for SMTP has not been 25 for Years and some Major US based ISPs block it?


It is still the default port, and there are no end of mail servers that support only 25. I spend a great deal of my time telnetting to mail servers around the world to find out what ports are supported.

Those ISPs that block port 25 for SMTP do so only for users who aren't their own customers, or sometimes for those who don't authenticate themselves to their own SMTP server. Port 587 is the standard port for authenticated SMTP, and 465 for authenticated SMTP through SSL.

WMP will not tag correctly what it does not know.  Just because WMP will show all tracks in the same album does not mean the tracks are numbered sequentially or grouped by CD.

Naturally all the artist mp3 tags should be the same. it would mean more if the track name was the same as the track number.

All you want in settings, tags is ID3_V1 and ID3-V2.3. Set the bitrate in the encoder setting. Probably around 56 or 48 kbps for speech and will keep the tracks smaller.

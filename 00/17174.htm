jay freedman say:

You cannot do pleading-type line numbering with styles in Word. Instead, open the header pane and insert a tall, narrow text box in the left margin. Type the numbers into the text box, reposition the text box as needed to align with the body text, and then close the header pane.

Because the text box is anchored in the header, it will automatically repeat on each page.

It's important for alignment that none of your body text has any partial-line spacing (it must be either single-spaced or double-spaced, but not something like 1.08 lines) or any paragraph Space Before or Space After.

hansv mvp say:

Let's say the summary sheet has the word "Mapping" in A1.

And let's say the data are on a sheet named Data, also with "Mapping" in A1.

In B2 on the summary sheet:

=SUMPRODUCT(Data!$B$2:$AZ$1000, (Data!$A$2:$A$1000=$A2)*(Data!$B$1:$AZ$1=B$1))

Adjust the ranges as needed, then fill down and to the right (or vice versa).

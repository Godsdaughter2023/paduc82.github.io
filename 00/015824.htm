mike h.. say:

Hi,

 

I am assuming these are properly formatted dates and times.

 

dates and times in Excel are number formatted to look like the dates we recognise so we can use the MODE function to return what you want.

 

Most common weekday. This returns 1 thru 7 where 1 is Monday


=MODE(WEEKDAY(A1:A20,2))

 

Most common month. Returns 1 thru 12 where 1 is January

=MODE(MONTH(A1:A20))

 

Most common hour

=MODE(HOUR(A1:A20))

Hi Jeb:

There is a bug in Advanced Find/Replace and a fix is in testing now.  I am not sure whether it will enable you to search for ligatures, though.

I think you might have better luck inserting advanced typography using the Format>Font>Advanced menu.

Generally, ligatures are "decorations" of the core characters.  So you need to first insert the characters (e.g. f f l...) .

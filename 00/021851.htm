    In your case, weâ€™d like to confirm if you want to save one infopath form in a library and then publish it to another library.

     

    If so, you may try the steps below:

    1. Go to the library where the form was published.

    2. Go to Settings>Library settings>Advanced settings.

    3. In the Document Template section, click Edit Template. (The form template will open in Microsoft Office InforPath)

    4. In the Microsoft Office InfoPath dialog box, click Yes.

    5. On the File menu, click Save as.

    6. In the Microsoft Office InfoPath dialog box, click OK.

    7. In the Save As dialog box, browse to the location where you want to save a copy of the form template, and then click Save.

    8. Open the saved Infopath form via InfoPath Designer.

    9. Click File>Publish>Publish form to a SharePoint Library.

    10. Input the Site URL and follow the steps to complete the process.

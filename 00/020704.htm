alanwu_msft say:

Hi Sunil,

I know you want to stop the emails with the attachment patterns “.doc/docx?.xls/.xlsxt  from being sent out of your organization. It is hard to set up a rule to achieve this goal with the attachments’ pattern changed.

Given the situation, you can set up a rule to prevent all the attachments  being sent out of your organization as a workaround.

The detailed steps are:
1. Sign in to Exchange Admin Center (EAC) with an admin credential.
2. Click “compliance management”> “data loss prevention”> “+” (add New custom DLP policy)> Give a name of your DLP policy> “Save”> double click the DLP policy> “rules”> “Create a new rule”> “More options”> “Apply this rule if (The recipient is located outside your organization)”> “and (Any attachment size is greater or equal to)”> “specify attachment size limit (KB) (0)”> “Do the following (Block the message)”> Save.

Also, you can just set up a transport rule to  stop the emails with attachments  being sent out of your organization. The detailed steps are:
1. Sign in to EAC.
2. Click “mail flow”> “rules”> the  rest steps  are the same as those for the above DLP policy.

Regards,
Alan

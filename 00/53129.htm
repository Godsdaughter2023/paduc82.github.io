muralidhar_g say:

Hi,

 

Thank you for writing to Microsoft Community Forums.



We were able to reproduce the issue and yes, you’re right. When you are connected using Mouse without Borders, you may face issues in identifying the CAPS lock or indicator light state. When you move your mouse from the primary computer to secondary computer and try toggling the CAPS lock, you will not get any indication on the primary computer’s keyboard. You will see the indicator lights turning ON and OFF in the secondary computer’s keyboard.

When you move the mouse from secondary computer to primary computer, you will be able to type in the same state (Caps lock ON or OFF) which is indicating on the keyboard. Once you move it to the secondary computer, you will not see the change in your keyboard, however, you will be able to type in same state as you had left it earlier.

We value and appreciate your suggestions. I suggest you to send feedback to Microsoft on the Feedback Hub app.

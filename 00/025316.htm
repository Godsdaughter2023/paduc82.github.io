danida_u say:

Hi

You can set up Call forwarding to forward calls made to your Skype Number to another mobile number. This is a great option to not miss important calls if you are unable to answer it on Skype. Follow the steps below on how to enable call forwarding.

  • Sign in to My Account on the Skype website.
  • Scroll down to Manage Features and click Call forwarding and voicemail.
  • Toggle the button from  to  and select how many seconds before your call gets forwarded.
  • Choose mobile number and then select the country or region.
  • Enter your phone number and don't forget to click Confirm.

Note: You'll need a little Skype Credit or a calling subscription that covers the country you want to received the forwarded calls in. You can refer to this FAQ for additional details. How do I use Skype call forwarding?

Hope this information helps! If you need additional information or assistance, do not hesitate to post back so we can assist you.

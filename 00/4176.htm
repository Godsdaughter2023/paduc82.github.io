Footers - Microsoft Community

michael95472 say:

Footers

I have a document in Word 2010 that I want to put a page-of-page in the right corner of the footer, the date in the left corner and some custom text in the middle.  I would like the footer to be on every page but the header only on the first page.  I would also like the date and page number to automatically update.  The document does not have any section breaks.  I've been working on this problem on and off for days and can't seem to figure it out.
hansv mvp say:

Open the document.

Activate the Insert tab of the ribbon.

Click Footer > Edit Header.

This will automatically activate the Design tab of the ribbon.

 

Tick the check box 'Different first page' in the Options group.

 

Enter the header. This will be for page 1 only.

 

Click 'Go to Footer' in the Navigation group. This takes you to the footer for page 1.

 

Click 'Date & Time' in the Insert group.

Select a format and make sure that the 'Update automatically' check box is ticked, then click OK.

You now have the date on the left hand side.

Press the Tab key.

Type your custom footer text. It will appear in the center.

Press the Tab key again.

Click 'Page Number' in the 'Header & Footer' group, select 'Current Position', then scroll down and select 'Bold Numbers' under 'Page X of Y'.

Copy the entire footer, preferably without the final paragraph mark and copy it (Ctrl+C).

 

Click Next in the Navigation group. This takes you to the footer for page 2 and following.

Press Ctrl+V to paste.

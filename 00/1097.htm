julie fka profjulie say:
Ben Hulet said:

When you say Synch both ways.  I would assume that WMDC would be able to "combine" information from both PC and the Device.  Yet, I anly have a choice between "Replace items on Device" or "Replace items on desktop".  This is not true synching or "combining" (as the old ActiveSynch would do) data from both.  Are you saying a third option shows up with a new partnership set up that allows "combining"? 



When you create a new partnership between a computer and a pocket pc and if you already have data on the pocket pc, you are prompted for how you wish to proceed during the device's very 1st sync to the computer (with a new partnership), with 3 options:  1) Combine the data; 2) Replace the data on the pocket pc with data from the computer; 3) Do nothing.  These options are only available on your very 1st sync.

Thereafter, new or modified data on the pocket pc will be synced over to the computer and new or modified data on the computer will be synced over to the pocket pc whenever you sync the devices.  The "Replace items on Device" and "Replace items on Desktop" options only apply if the same records have been changed on both the computer and the pocket pc.  For instance, if you have a contact named John Doe in your contact listing and you make a change on the pocket pc to this record and make a change on the computer to the same record, WMDC will recognize a conflict on your next sync (changes were made to the same record on both the computer and the pocket pc, which one should be accepted and synced?).  If you've set the option to "Replace items on the Device", whenever WMDC recognizes that a conflict exists, it will replace the data on the pocket pc with the data from the computer.

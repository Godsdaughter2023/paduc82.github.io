ryan lv msft say:

Hi Cyrus,

I’d like to share some information here that may help you in your case.

Root cause:

Missing SSL certificate on ADFS server.

Details:

The issue is related to the certificates installed on their ADFS server (ADFS 3.0) and how the chosen CA provider delivers the certificates. They use SSL certificates provided by GoDaddy INC. They get the following two type of files (zip file) for SSL certificates from the provider.

1) The certificate itself with a .crt extension

2) A intermediate certificate usually named "gd-g2_iis_intermediates.p7b" (this file has inside two files 2 intermediate certificates named "Go Daddy Root Certificate Authority - G2" and "Go Daddy Secure Certificate Authority - G2")

They find that they had only one of the two intermediate certificates under the Intermediate Certification Authority, while using the MMC at the main principal ADFS server on the farm

Solution:

Reinstall .p7b file including two intermediate certificates in ADFS server farms (primary and other server farms) and each of the WAP servers. After restarting the ADFS, they are able to  login/authenticate in their mobile devices.

Suggestion:

If you experiencing a similar issue, please check if you have correctly installed required certificates. You may contact the CA provider’s support for detailed guidance of SSL installation.

If the above information doesn’t help, please post a new thread in our forum. We will provide assistance on the specific issue.

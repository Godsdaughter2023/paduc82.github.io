bob jones aka: cybertaz say:
Unfortunately we lost that capability in Excel 2011 -- In fact, IIRC we originally lost Pattern Fills altogether. Due to popular demand they were patched back in as part of an early update but in a limited manner. What we now have is that Pattern Fills consist of a Foreground color [the pattern itself] & a Background color [the otherwise white space between the components of the pattern].

I'm afraid the only option I can suggest (barring some VBA contrivance) is to select only cells that have the same Fill color when you apply the Pattern... i.e., do each like-colored group individually.

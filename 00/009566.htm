Solution -- The Requested Operation Failed on Office 365 Outlook - Microsoft Community

globetrotter1028 say:

Solution -- The Requested Operation Failed on Office 365 Outlook program when trying to add email accounts

I continually tried to add email accounts and kept getting the 

The Requested Operation Failed

message, which prevented me from finalizing the process. Extremely frustrating.

I spent hours with Microsoft trying to figure this out. After this exercise in futility, I stumbled upon the solution by chance:

Do NOT set up additional email accounts in Outlook from the Outlook program.

Instead, go to Control Panel-->Mail (Microsoft Outlook)--Profiles-->Highlight the Profile that you are using-->Properties-->Email Accounts-->New (be careful with the Port numbers and options re encrypted connection for incoming and outgoing on the Advanced tab; check these with your ISP. 

Somehow, this worked and I did not see the error message again!

Hope this message saves others from spinning their wheels like a rat on a treadmill!

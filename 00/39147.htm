junaid_a say:

Hi,

 

You may want to check if audio is enabled in BIOS.

 

Enable audio in BIOS:

  • Access the BIOS settings and go to the Advanced section.

  • Click on ‘Onboard’ or ‘Device Configuration’.

  • Enable Sound/Audio under Audio Controller or any other similar sound-related configuration.

 

Addressing Terratec Aureon 5.1 PCI audio output issue, you may try to install the Windows 8.1 drivers in compatibility mode and check if it works. If the issue persists, I suggest you to get in touch with the Terratec support, as there are no Windows 10 drivers.

 

Refer Make older programs compatible with this version of Windows to know how to install a software in compatibility mode.

 

Similarly, you may get in touch with the Digitus support if the issue persists despite of the audio being enabled in BIOS.

 

I suggest you to disable the Front Panel Jack Detection and then plug in the device. The audio works when the front panel jack detection is disabled because there is no interruption. Disabling front panel jack detection is recommended to get rid of the unnecessary interruption.

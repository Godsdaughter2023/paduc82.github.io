Hello,

Thank you for visiting Microsoft Community.

As I have understood, you want to know how to transfer photos from android phone to the computer.

I certainly understand your concern.

I would suggest you to try the following steps and check if that helps.

  1. Download and install the USB drivers for your smartphone on your computer.
  2. Insert one end of the mini USB synchronization cable into the mini USB connector on your smartphone, and the other end into a USB port on your computer.
  3. On your smartphone, hold and drag the notification bar, and tap the USB icon
  4. Tap Turn on USB Storage and tap OK.
  5. On the computer, click Start and then Computer. The micro-SD card of your smartphone is listed as Removable Drive.
  6. Double-click this removable disk to open it. Double-click the folder DCIM, and next the folder Camera.
  7. Copy the pictures and videos from this folder to any folder on your computer.

Important: If the removable drive does not show in (My) Computer, verify if the USB icon is showing on your smartphone. If the icon is not visible, try connecting the smartphone on a different USB port, and follow the above steps.

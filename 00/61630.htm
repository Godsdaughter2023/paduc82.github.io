Windows 10 Update restart loop [SOLVED]] - Microsoft Community

paul_w_chambers say:

Windows 10 Update restart loop [SOLVED]]

I constantly get a Windows Update notification that the machine will restart, even after I successfully updated Windows 10.

The fix I found was to go into Settings, Update and Security and then Advanced Options. 

Once in Advanced Options I turn on Pause Updates then I immediately unpause updates.

Windows 10 Updates then checks for updates and the warning and notice of a pending restart is gone.

I notice this only happens on my AMD machines, even running the current and latest drivers. I have 2 AMD desktops and 1 Intel based desktop.

I write this post because web searches, even to this very community, are so fragmented giving suggestions from Safeboot to Refresh to deleting profiles in the registry at Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows NT\CurrentVersion\ProfileList\.

I hope this works for you. Feel free to discuss.

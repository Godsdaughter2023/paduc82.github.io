I understand that the emails coming from the website where you registered your alias is going to your Junk folder instead. It's possible that the system might have recognized the email as junk since it is not yet in your account's safe senders list. I suggest you do it this way:

1. In your email page, go to the Junk folder and locate the email from that website.

Alternatively, you can add the website's email address to your Safe senders list. You can refer to the steps below on how to add it to this list:

1. From your email page, click on the gear icon next to your account name at the upper right side of the email page.
2. Select More mail settings.
3. Under Preventing junk email, click on Safe and blocked senders.
4. Click on Safe senders.
5. Enter the email address of the contact you want to add in the Sender or domain to mark as safe: box then click on the Add to list>> button.

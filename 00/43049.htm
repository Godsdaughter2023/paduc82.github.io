Windows Media Player Play button is too small

I have just recently purchased a computer with windows 8.1 and have discovered that the windows media player button is unusually small. This also occurs on youtube when using mozilla firefox. Any help would be greatly appreciated.
cmdrkeene say:

This occurs when you have a high resolution screen (Apple calls them "Retina Display").  The super HD graphics is because there's so many more pixel dots on the screen.  The play button on Media Player is really just a picture of a play graphic icon.  That picture is 20 pixels square.  But since your HD monitor has so many more pixels packed closer together, that 20 dots looks a lot smaller.

There's not a LOT you can do, but you can adjust the DPI (dots per inch) scaling, so that everything on your screen gets blown up 150% larger so that I seems "normal size".  Here's how:

  1. Right-click Desktop, choose Resolution
  2. Don't change resolution (a lot of people make this mistake). Leave it on the highest/recommended setting.
  3. Instead, click change the size of text and other items and then pick a new DPI scale level like 125 or 150%.

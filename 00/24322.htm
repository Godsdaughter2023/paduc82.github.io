In the site collection where has the library you want to enable IRM, you may create a custom permission level. To create this permission level, you can copy Edit permission, and then unselect Open items permission. Save this customized permission level.

5b. Search within /var for 'onenote'.

5c. Select all of the files (there should be around 6 to 8 of them)

5d. Delete them all. (BE CAREFUL TO ONLY DELETE ONENOTE-RELATED FILES!)

6. Empty the trash of everything that we've deleted. It may say some files are in use and cannot be deleted. Restart your computer and delete them. 

7. Reinstall OneNote from the Mac App Store. 

8. Enjoy OneNote!

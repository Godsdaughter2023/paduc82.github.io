kevin911 say:

[SOLVED} Dolby Digital Audio Issues in Windows 8 MCE

I just encountered some audio issues with my HTPC last night.  After the upgrade to W8 and MCE, all was well, but than a day later it had me set up my tuner cards again.  This is when the problems came...at first not all channels were scanning correctly, but I got it to work.  Now I am having issues with the Dolby Digital push for the audio.  My speakers never used to adjust to DD, but now it does for all channels.  Some channels now play audio and some play no audio.  All old TV Recordings do not play audio, presumably because it was recorded without Dolby Digital.

kevin911 say: This was due to my two audio devices conflicting with each other.  I had the realtek HD Audio and the HDMI Audio as enabled.  Disabling the Realtek Audio device solved this problem and the sound is perfect now.

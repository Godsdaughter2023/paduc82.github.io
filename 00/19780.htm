Remove Quarantined Threats History - Microsoft Community

waynemcdonald_842 say:

Remove Quarantined Threats History

Why can't I remove all quarantined threats and clear all history at once in windows 10?

[Original Title: Quarantined Threats]

rob koch say:

Why?

I never clear the quarantine and haven't for years.  It's always safer to leave these items for Windows Defender to empty itself, since if they were actually a false positive or other error, I might want to recover them later.  Since items in quarantine can't function, it makes no difference it they're deleted or not and Defender will delete any items it's certain are malware in any case, so I leave all of this to the automated functions Microsoft built in.

And there's absolutely never a good reason to delete the history, since this serves as a record if I'd ever want to know whether something was recurring.  Since these are simply tiny textual log entries, it would take literally thousands of them in order to use any significant disk space, so their cost is minimal for the information and record they provide.

Deleting such info only serves a purpose if there were a pathological situation such as malware that couldn't be properly removed, which caused hundreds or thousands of log entries, making them less valuable and more of an annoyance.  In such cases though, there's typically a good reason to consider resetting Windows altogether, which will usually flush all logs as a side effect including those in Defender.

Rob

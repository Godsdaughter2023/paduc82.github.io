cloudscout say:

I had the same issue and went through the following process with Microsoft Support to get it working again:

1) Open the Microsoft Health App.

2) Click on the Band icon at the top right corner.

3) Click on "Unregister".

4) Open up Bluetooth Settings on your phone.

5) Unpair the Band.

6) Shut down the Band by holding down both buttons.

7) If the Band doesn't restart on its own, plug it into the charging cable to wake it back up.

8) When the Band starts back up, it should say "Get Started".  Click on that.

9) Open the Microsoft Health app on the phone again.

10) There should be a "+" icon in the top right corner, tap that.

11) You'll get a dialog box or two... just tap OK.

12) On the pairing screen, click on "Go to Settings"

13) From Bluetooth Settings, pair the Band again.

14) Once paired, open the Microsoft Health app again.

15) It may say something about needing to update the Band's firmware.  Go ahead and do that.

16) Once finished updating, it should work correctly.  Mine did, anyway.

john korchok say:

Sorry, fillable forms in Word are strictly for entering information, that's why all formatting commands are disabled.

It is possible to create fillable and formattable text areas in Word 2010 or Word 2013 by using Review>Editing Restrictions, applying No changes (Read only), then adding exceptions for the areas that should remain editable. These forms will also work in Word 2011. You can't create them in Word 2011 because while it has read only protection, it lacks the ability to add exceptions.

Hello GibraltarPC,

Sorry for that inconvenience, hopefully this will shed some light on the situation. 

The reason that her account settings are not being backed up are because they are not managed by her Account on the PC.

Because your Account is the Administrator over her account which is a Standard account, her settings will be backed up along with your account due to the fact that she does have her own settings.

Things like her apps and other services which are linked directly to her Microsoft Account will be automatically backed up, but as far as her User Settings she would have to also have an administrator account for this to be possible.

I hope that this was able to help to answer your question.

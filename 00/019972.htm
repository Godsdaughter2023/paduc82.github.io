Hi, Keith

Adding a phone number as a contact works differently in Skype for Windows 10. In this version, you need to call the number first, before you can add it to your local address book to make any changes, including adding a name to the saved phone number.

  1. After the call, go to the chat window.
  2. Right click the number, and select View profile.
  3. Click Add to local address book which will show under the dialed number.
  4. Edit the details of your phone number contact.

Let me know if you have further questions.

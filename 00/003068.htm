Move information between columns without cut/paste - Microsoft Community

anne the saint say:

Move information between columns without cut/paste

Original title: Microsoft Excel

How do I move information from one column to another without having to cut and paste. e.g. from "Commercial real estate" to "Land" column in Excel.

hansv mvp say:

You can move a selected range to another location by dragging one of its borders.

By default, existing data in the destination range will be overwritten when you release the mouse button.

If you hold down Shift when you release the mouse button, you will insert the range.

If you hold down Ctrl when you release the mouse button, you will copy the range, instead of moving it.

If you hold down Ctrl+Shift when you release the mouse button, you will insert a copy of the range.

If you drag the range with the right mouse button, a menu will pop up when you release the mouse button giving you all of the above options, plus several more.

romankocherovsky say:

Just got it figured out. - MS Windows 10586.36, MS Office 365 2016 Business Edition, Current iCloud

1. Open iCloud for windows

2. Uncheck the mail, contacts, calendars check box and click apply

3. Close iCloud

4. Open outlook as admin

5. Go to File -> Account Settings -> Account Settings -> Data Files (tab)

6. in data files tab delete the iCloud, close Data Files

7. Go through your calendars, contacts, and tabs and delete the iCloud folder labels (don't worry your info is still on iCloud, you are just getting rid of the label in outlook)

8. Close all MS Office programs, including skype for business, and make sure they are not running in the back ground

9. Open iCloud for windows

10. Check the mail, contacts, calendars check box and click apply

11. Give it time to load, when the text box appears saying it is done click close

12. Open Outlook the standard way and your stuff should be there

Worked for me, hope it works for you

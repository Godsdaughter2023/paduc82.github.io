I want the result in hh:mm or minutes.

One way is to split the date and time into two columns and subtract them separately.

joeu2004 say: Bradex wrote;
I have two columns in dd-mm-yyyy hh:mm AM/PM format. When I try to subtract them in excel I am not able to do so.
I want the result in hh:mm or minutes.  One way is to split the date and time into two columns and subtract them separately.  Is there a simpler way without doing this?

Just the opposite.  Do not split them; the result might even be wrong if you did.  Simply use the formula =B1-A1, where B1 is the later date/time.  The key is to format the result using the Custom format [h]:mm .  Select the cell with the subtraction formula, right-click and click on Format Cells, then the Number tab, select Custom, and fill in the Type field.

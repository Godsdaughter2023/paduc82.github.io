Hi,

·        Which version of Internet Explorer is installed on the system?

·        What do you exactly mean by ‘Click off internet Explorer ’? Do you encounter the error when you close the Internet Explorer or when you browse websites?

·        Do you have any add-ons installed on the system?

If yes, then this error might be due to add-ons installed on your system. I would suggest you to enable/ disable add-ons and check whether the issue persists. Follow method 1:

 

Method 1:

Step 1:

a.      Start Internet Explorer.

b.     On theTools menu, click Manage Add-ons.

c.      Click the name of the add-on and click disable button.

d.     Disable all add-ons and close the Internet Explore.

e.     Restart the internet explorer and check whether the issue is resolved.

If it is resolved then the possibility is that the add-ons are causing the issue. Follow step 2.

Step 2:

a.      Start Internet Explorer.

b.     On theTools menu, click Manage Add-ons.

c.      Click the name of the add-on and disable one add-on at a time.

d.     Restart the internet explorer and check.

e.     Next time enable that add-on and disable a different add-on to find which add-on is causing this issue.

Follow step 2 for all the add-ons installed on the system and check whether the issue is resolved.

 

Method 2:

I would suggest you to perform an Internet Optimization and check whether you encounter this error again.

How to optimize Internet Explorer

http://support.microsoft.com/kb/936213

 

Method 3:

Reset Internet Explorer and check whether the issue is resolved.

How to Reset Internet Explorer Settings

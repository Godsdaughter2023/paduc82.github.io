¡firedog say:

Folders under Storage folders are quite separate from any others and have no link to any of your mail accounts. When you remove an account, all folders under the account heading will be permanently deleted along with their contents; this is why you should move any messages you want to keep out of account folders and into folders under storage folders.

It is quite possible to create folders under an account. With IMAP and HTTP accounts, such folders and their contents will synchronize with those on the mail server, which is why it's usually best to create account folders at the server. They can then be populated within Windows Live Mail. User-created folders will be removed along with the account.

Removing an account has no effect on storage folders, contacts or calendars.

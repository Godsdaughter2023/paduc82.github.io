Your AVG antivirus was probably installed with its default install option, which installs an email scanning module. Just disabling its email scanning
is not a sufficient remedy. Uninstall AVG, then reinstall, but select 'custom' install, which then allows you to unselect installing its troublesome email

Try temporarily unplugging all phones in your house and ensuring your DSL Modem goes into the correct slot of your DSL filter.

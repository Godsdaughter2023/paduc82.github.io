If "#prd del" is B2:B10 and "TTL" is C2:C10, the (weighted) average is about 400.662:

=SUMPRODUCT(B2:B10,C2:C10) / SUM(B2:B10)

[EDIT] I'm not sure what you expect when you say the solution is in "column D".  There is a single (weighted) average.  The formula above might be in D12, for example.  But if you are thinking there is a "weighted average" for each of D2:D10 (i.e. for each line item), that is incorrect.  If that is your expectation, perhaps you want something other than "weighted average" per se.  An example of the numbers that you expect in D2:D10 might be helpful in understanding your requirement.

I found the solution myself.

From the folder tab once you clicked on any folder under your email account

1. Click on IMAP Folders

2. Verify and make sure that the box "When displaying hierarchy [...] subscribed folders" is checked

3. Click Query

4. In the Subscribed tab, select and unsubscribe to every undesired folders

5. Click Apply

Now manually delete every folders from the Folder Pane.

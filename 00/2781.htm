alan henthorne say:

xbox live account associated with another user

I just got a Surface Pro 3 and am trying to use the Xbox video feature. However I keep getting a message that says I need parental permission to use this feature.

I log into Xbox live using my Windows ID and password. It seems that my email address is associated with another user's account, as a gamer tag name appears above the account. I then get another request for parental approval.

How can I make my Windows account work as an Xbox log in in this situation.

a. user say:

Hello Alan:

Thank you for posting to the forum regarding the issues you are having accessing your account.  Based on the information provided your account is currently set up as a child account.

To use services like Xbox Music, Video and games your Microsoft account will need to have a Xbox Live account associated with it.  This is where the Gamertag comes into play.  When an account is set up and the date of birth is under the age of 18 it is considered a child account and requires a parental account be associated with it. 

The first thing I would suggest you do is go to http://account.live.com .  Sign into this site with your Microsoft account.  Once you are signed in, go to the basic information section and make sure the name, date of birth and time zone are properly set.  Once this is done, go to the billing section and select billing info.  Here you will want to be sure the address is complete and up to date.

Once this is done, go to http://xbox.com .  Sign in with the same Microsoft account.  The nickname you see in the upper right corner is the Gamertag associated with this account.  Click on MY ACCOUNT > payment & billing > billing info.  If your account is indeed a child account you will be asked to enter a parental email here.  I would now try entering any parents emails, older siblings, significant others or secondary accounts you may have used.  If you get past this, you now know the parental email and can use this to access any apps or features that ask for the parental email.

If you are unable to get past the parental email you will need to determine what this is to continue to use this account.  You may be able to gather further options to find this information by starting a live chat session here, http://support.xbox.com/en-US/contact-us?xr=footnav .

Please let us know if you have any other questions or concerns.

Mark W.

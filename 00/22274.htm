bobgreenblatt say:

Looking at the actual sheet helps a lot. Thanks. I am not sure why this is working differently on different platforms. However, the times you have recorded are just text values. You should not put the apostrophe in the formula. The Mac includes the apostrophe as part of the text. (Windows Excel should also, I don't know why it is not.)

In B2 on the Momentum sheet, the formula as you entered it is:

=SUMIF('Team Edit Sheet'!$D:$D,"<='00:05:00:00'",'Team Edit Sheet'!A:A)

and it should be:

=SUMIF('Team Edit Sheet'!$D:$D,"<=00:05:00:00'",'Team Edit Sheet'!A:A)

Why did you think you needed the apostrophe before the time? Removing it on all the formulas should give you the correct values on both platforms.

Method 3: Delete all the temp files.

 

a)      Click on Start/Run. A box will open.

b)      Type %temp% and click Enter.

c)       A new window will open showing you all temporary files.

d)      Select all those files by pressing Ctrl + A on the keyboard, and after selecting delete them by pressing Delete button on the keyboard.

don cuthbert say:

The message about requiring further installation is likely just an interim message that was issued during the driver install and superseded by the driver install completion. I have seen that happen before.

This could be an issue with equipment owned by your ISP. Sometimes an ISP modem, somewhere in your neighborhood, could be starting to fail, or some physical wiring in your home or outside could have become compromised in some way. They should be able to detect what is happening and trace the problem to its source.

On the other hand, with Insider Builds, they have been making significant changes to Windows components, which have impacted networking in various ways. You should install the public build 14393 on a small partition and compare its performance on your same hardware (dual OS boot). This could eliminate any Insider build networking changes as being a source of this network performance degradation.

   · Click OK

4. Reboot

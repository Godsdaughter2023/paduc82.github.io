You don't have 70 in the history...

But...

 

Look in these two places in the Windows registry:

  • "HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\Map Network Drive MRU"
  • "HKEY_USERS\<UniqueToYouGUID>\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\Map Network Drive MRU"

vijay a. verma say:

hh:mm:ss formatting is allright. In formula bar, it will show as 12:01:30AM only which is the underlying value. But in the cell, it will show as 00:01:30

To convert into seconds (if time is in A2) -

=HOUR(A2)*3600+MINUTE(A2)*60+SECOND(A2)

Format the result cell as Number other wise it will show as 0:00:00 only.

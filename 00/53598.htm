    Notice: Bell Canada/Wholesale: Sagemcom connection issues - Microsoft Community

    jmjimmy1 say:

    Notice: Bell Canada/Wholesale: Sagemcom connection issues

    Those of you on VDSL with Bell branded Sagemcom modem/router combos (provided to Bell Canada customers & wholesalers like Teksavvy, Primus, Start.ca, etc)  please be aware that if you are experiencing connection issues with XBL this is due to two things:

    1) The Xbox dashboard no longer conforms to the minimum requirements that it claims.  The required minimum MTU for XBL is listed as 1364

    2) The Sagemcom has a "sync/no surf" issue due to the maximum MTU being 1434

    Whenever the Xbox Dashboard requires an MTU above 1434 (like when exiting an XBLA game into the My Games section) you will be disconnected from XBL.  There is no way to adjust the MTU in the Bell firmware and you will require many attempts to re-connect to XBL.  There is currently no resolution to this issue.  Bell does have plans to upgrade the firmware, however, this does not mean that the issue will be fixed when they do.  If you have the Bell branded Sagemcom and are experiencing random disconnects, specifically being disconnected upon exiting an XBLA game, or getting the MTU error message when you run the network diagnostic tool this is likely the reason why.  Some companies, like Teksavvy, will provide you with a different modem (ZyXel) but until either Xbox or Bell fixes the issue there's not much to be done.

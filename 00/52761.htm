Important!!!  There are options to change the way the tablet boots and so on....  This is sort of a bios, but tablets don't have a bios setup like a pc.  If you don't know what you're doing, don't select the option to mess with it.  You WILL turn your tablet into a really nice paperweight!!!  DO NOT setup Windows 10 on your flash drive as an upgrade!!!  Why?  Because the installation will not delete Windows 8.1, and you will have the same problem all over again.  When choosing how to download Windows 10, you will choose to install from a device (flash drive) as said before.  When Windows 10 installs you have the option of formatting or deleting drive partitions.  This is great!!!  You get your whole drive back, and Windows 10 gets installed.

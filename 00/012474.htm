     --> CTRL+A to highlight all items

     --> Other folder

How to divide fractions in Excel? - Microsoft Community

officeanswers say:

How to divide fractions in Excel?

Im having a hard time trying to divide fractions in excel because the divide sign is the same as the fraction sign, what do i do?
ronrosenfeld say: A fraction IS a division operation.  The value of the fraction 2/3  is the same as 2 divided by 3.

You can't display fractions in the formula bar, and if you have a formula that includes a fraction, it may get converted to a decimal.

For example, you can enter into some cell:

=(1/2) / (4 2/3)

But once you <Enter>  you will see:   =(1/2)/(4.66666666666667)

You could also enter   =1/2/4 2/3  for the same result.

You can also do:

A1:  0 1/2
A2:  4 2/3
A3:  = a1/a2

For A1 and A2, you will see the fractions in the cells, but the formula bar will still contain the underlying decimal values.

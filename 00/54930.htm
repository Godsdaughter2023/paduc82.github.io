kaptainkayos say:

My answer from another post -hope it helps:

I think I may have stumbled upon the solution from another post.  It appears that some hardware (ie USB 2.0, 3.0, ports etc) don't work when "fast startup" is enabled in the power options menu.  Uncheck this option and your PC may start up a tad slower, but I found that all my ports work fine from every boot no problem.   I don't know the reasoning, but I suspect that the drivers don't have time to get "registered" or something if fast startup is enabled. 
Be aware that if you fiddle with Hibernation and Sleep options you may enable/disable the fast startup default settings.  Obviously this is another fault in the operating system that needs attention Mr. Microsoft!!  Hope that this helps somebody else...  oh, and thanks to somebody else out there for their original solution!!

Cheers KaptainKayos.

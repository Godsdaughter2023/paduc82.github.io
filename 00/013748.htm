a. user say:

In-Place Archive is a hold on the mailbox. Meaning that no items can be deleted and remain eDiscoverable if the shared mailbox is deleted.

It does not mean you can turn off the In-Place Archive and get the mailbox back. If you turn off the In-Place Archive then the mailbox is permanently deleted and can't be recovered.

guntervspq say:

Too much space before endnotes in Word 2010

How can I remove undesired empty space between separator and the beginning of the endnotes on each page of a Word 2010 document?  (see image). If I try to erase the empty lines a pop-up message tells me that this is impossible with endnotes.
suzanne s. barnhill say:

It would appear that the empty paragraphs have been added to the Endnote Continuation Separator. Here's how to fix it:

  1. Switch to Draft view.
  2. Double-click on any endnote reference mark to open the Endnotes pane.
  3. On the toolbar of the Endnotes pane, in the dropdown list, choose Endnote Continuation Separator.
  4. Clicking Reset on the on the toolbar should suffice, but you can also manually remove the empty paragraphs.
  5. Switch back to Print Layout view.

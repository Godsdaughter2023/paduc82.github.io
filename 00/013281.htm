mark_n13 say:

Hi

Make sure that the Windows 10 privacy settings for your Microphone is enabled for Skype.

Here's how to check:

1. Click the Start menu and go to Settings(Gear icon)
2. Open Privacy
3. On the left pane, click on Microphone
4. Under "Choose apps that can use your Microphone", make sure Skype is switched

5. Restart your computer

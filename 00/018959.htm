charleskenyon (ia) say:
Use SEQ fields.
Captions are for use with items that are in a document such as Pictures, Tables, or Equations.

Create your SEQ field and save it as either an AutoText or AutoCorrect entry. Insert it where you want a number. If it will include the word Exhibit, include that in your entry.

Note that SEQ fields do not update automatically just by inserting or deleting one.  When you add one, no matter whether before or after others, it will be properly numbered but ones that already exist will not renumber just from the insertion. You can use print preview to update the fields or I can provide a macro that will update them.

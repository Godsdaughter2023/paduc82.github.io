suzanne s. barnhill say:

I'm unclear about why you are trying to remove the spaces, which are supposed to be there. In fact, they should be nonbreaking spaces. If you set the document language to French and type text with quotation marks, this is the result you'll see: guillemets separated from the quoted text by nonbreaking spaces. Changing the language to English will not change the quotation marks; for this you would need to use Replace.

 

What are you using as the text in the "Find what" and "Replace with" boxes? And are you actually trying to just remove the space or are you trying to replace with English-style quotation marks? In either case, instead of trying to use special codes, just copy/paste the ¬´ or ¬ª (with the space) into the "Find what" box.

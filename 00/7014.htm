Have read your feedback, Pieter. The new error that you received (Error: 0x8024401c) usually appears when there's an outdated driver especially for older peripheral devices (Ethernet card, printer, keyboard, etc.). We suggest that you update its drivers. Follow the steps below:

  1. Right-click the Windows key and click Device Manager.
  2. Expand the category of the device with a not-working driver.
  3. Right-click the device and click Properties.
  4. Select the Driver tab and click the Update Driver option.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions.

In case the issue persists after updating the drivers, then let's disable the IPv6 protocol in your network connection. Here's how:

  1. Right-click the Windows key and click Network Connections.
  2. Right-click the connection that you are currently using (either LAN or Wi-Fi) and click Properties.
  3. In the list of connection items, uncheck the box for Internet Protocol Version 6 (TCP/IPv6).
  4. Click OK.

Once done, try checking for updates again.

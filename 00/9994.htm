if you have a sharepoint online calendar and want to create a new sharepoint online calendar from it, you may try one of the two workarounds below:
1.if you just want to add some of the items in the previous calendar to a new calendar, you can copy the content and structure to a new calendar. the all day events and recurrence properties will also be copied to the new calendar.

  b.navigate to the site including the previous calendar and click the setting gear>site settings.

  d.choose the previous calendar in the left.
  e.choose the items you need and click actions>copy.

  g.click ok.

  a.navigate to the previous calendar and click the calendar tab.
  b.choose list settings and click save list as template under permission and management.
  c.fill in the blanks and choose include content, and then click ok.
note: the template name will be the app name for this template.
 
  d.click site contents>add an app.
  e.find the list template you saved with its name.
 
  f.click the app to create a new calendar.

note: you can manage the templates via list templates under web designer galleries in site settings.

Here's a couple of things about scheduled scans. To run, the PC must be on and connected to the internet (it cannot be shutdown, in sleep mode, in hybrid sleep mode, or in hibernation). The power saving settings must be such that the PC does not shut down or go asleep during the scan (or it will be as if it never happened). Scans do not begin precisely when scheduled - some can start an hour or so earlier or later than the officially set time (depending on Microsoft's servers and the loads at that time). While the computer attempts to start on time, the exact time is based in part on when Microsoft can fit it in.  With more users of MSE than ever before, the scheduled scans are diverging from the specified time a bit more than in the past.  For me, doing daily Quick Scans at 5AM, I've found they tend to start 15 minutes or so earlier than scheduled, but have also had them start up to an hour later than scheduled.  I guess the point becomes getting it done automatically even if the start time isn't precisely what you set.

If a scan is missed it does not start at the next available time, but waits for the next scheduled scan.

Assuming as you mentioned your Activity is in D column, Name is in E column and scroe is in J column, while range is from D1 to J21, the first row being header and from row 2 onwards you have the data.

Suppose your seprate result table (leaderboard) is in range K1 to M10, where header is in row 1. Column K is the No. of Top Scores, L contains the Name of the person and in M2 put this formula with Ctrl+SHift+Enter

=AVERAGE(LARGE((IF($E$2:$E$21=L2,$J$2:$J$21,0)),ROW(OFFSET($D$1,,,K2))))

in above formula L2 is the name of person for whom you want to calulate average and K2 is the no of top score (this is 4 as you mentioned, this is dyamic and you may change it)

crmango say:

I finally solved the issue for me by going here: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/software-download/windows10.

It's the Windows 10 download page, but there's an option there to check for updates. There is a fall creators update that's available. Installing the fall creators build re-installs the folder and the chances of it being corrupted a second time are small.

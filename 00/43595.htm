a. user say:

Welcome to Memory Lane. Those are midi files that were originally included in Windows 7.

Back in the day, when playing media on Windows was new and still a big deal, Microsoft included sample pictures, sample music and sample videos in Windows so users could try the media playing features.

Today, of course, playing media on a computer is commonplace. But Microsoft never removed some of the old sample media files, and you found them.

MIDI means Musical Instrument Digital Interface. It was one of the earliest forms of music playable on a computer, before computers were able to play all the formats that are ubiquitous today. They sound primitive now, but at one time they were, as Donald Trump would say, huge!

Hi,


I assume here the score for the first row is 3*2 + 3*4 + 4*5= 38


Let's say the first rows is in B3 to F3. Put this in H3 and drag down.


=SUMPRODUCT((LEN(B3:F3)-LEN(SUBSTITUTE(B3:F3,"x","")))*($B$2:$F$2))

=SUMPRODUCT((LEN(B3:F3)-LEN(SUBSTITUTE(LOWER(B3:F3),"x","")))*($B$2:$F$2))

iris he msft say:

Hi Lord,

It is not feasible to transfer the entire E3 account to the Action E3 subscription because we cannot merge two tenants into a one. And, the Enterprise E3 you are using and the Action Pack E3 subscription are different products which are provided by sellers. The former is from Microsoft, the latter is from the Microsoft Partner. We‚Äôd appreciate your understanding.

In this situation, you may consider setting up a new account for Action Pack E3 plan and migrating the data from E3 plan to Action Pack E3 plan manually.

Here are some references for you to do that.

To migrate SharePoint Online site and its data, consider saving the site as template including the contents then add it into the Action Pack E3 plan. Or using OneDrive for Business sync app to sync files, or using Open with Explorer command to bulk upload/download. Note: This command only works in IE browser

To migrate the contents in OneDrive for Business library, I suggest using OneDrive for Business sync tool or Open with Explorer command.

To migrate email, please refer to: How to migrate mailboxes from one Office 365 tenant to another.

If you encountered any problems when pulling out the data from Office 365 E3 plan, please let me know. However, if you encountered the issue when uploading the data to the Action Pack E3 plan, we‚Äôd suggest you contact the Partner support you were connecting with as they are specialist for that.

You also can monitor this thread. Hopefully other community members who have the same experience can share their ideas.

Please let me know if we can discuss further.

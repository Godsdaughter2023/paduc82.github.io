Hi Billy,
 
 
You do realize to update the USB drive as opposed to re-installing everything, you need to boot to it as if you were running the scan and at the beginning before it starts the scan it should ask you.  You can boot to a different computer to get the update and then run the scan by booting to the drive on the computer you're testing (so nothing on that computer affects the update or infects the drive).  You don't update it manually from the download site.  I have heard of some people who have problems with this and haven't seen a good solution (I suspect some hardware or BIOS problem but that's a guess) unless an error message is generated and then sometimes it can be fixed.
 
 
I hope this helps.  If not, submit an MS Support email support case starting here: https://support.microsoftsecurityessentials.com/ but since there's no category for WDO, follow the instructions as if you have MSE and then explain the problem and that it's WDO once you have a chance or reach somebody.  Getting support there for WDO isn't 100% certain - some say they helped and others said they didn't cover it.  If they refuse, ask for it to be escalated and post back here with the case number and we'll see if we can do anything (though it's doubtful but worth a try at that point).

Good Luck!
 
 
Kosh

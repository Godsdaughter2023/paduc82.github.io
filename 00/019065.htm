danida_u say:

Hi PanosH. and welcome to the Skype Community! 

The account you are trying to access might have two-step verification enabled. When two-step verification is enabled, the system will send you a security code to enter on the sign-in page if it detects that the device being used is a not trusted device. 

This is an extra layer of protection to make it more difficult for someone else to gain access to your account. 

If not two-step verification, the system may have detected an unusual sign in activity. This will prompt the system to send a code to the associated security info of the account to confirm that the account owner is the one accessing it. If you cannot receive the code, try using a different browser or clearing your browsing history and try to log in again. 

Double check on the Microsoft Accounts Diagnostic link that the security info on your account and the one that you are entering are the same. Enter the Skype Name of the account and click Continue. Choose your account and click Reset password. On the next page, choose I forgot my password and proceed with the steps until you reach your account's security info. 

Let us know what happens after.

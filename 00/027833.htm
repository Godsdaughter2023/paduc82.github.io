Based on your description. I understand that you received some email addresses form an email and you want to add them into contact list in Outlook for Mac.

For this need, we have no related option or feature to turn the email addresses in email into contact list directly. But there is a way to around it. Please refer to the following steps (use the .csv file):

  1. Download (http://download.microsoft.com/download/5/B/2/5B2108F8-112B-4913-A761-38AFF2FD8598/Sample%20CSV%20file%20for%20importing%20contacts.csv ) this sample .csv file to your computer and edit it to input email addresses. After that, export to a .csv file.

  3. Click File > Export to create a .vcf file.
  4. Drag and drop the .vcf file into the address book where you want them imported in the Outlook for Mac client (under “My Contacts”).

Add the option to Delete Message to your rule after copying to your folder under On My Computer.

You will still need to empty your account's Deleted Items folder periodically to eventually remove messages from the server. I do this by creating a schedule that empties the folder when I quit Outlook. You'll find schedules under Outlook menu -->  Preferences... --> Schedules.

Hi Susanna,

 

From your description, when you click on Save As in PowerPoint 2016 for Mac, the application crashes.

 

If yes, please try the following to see and let us know the result.

a. Close all the Office applications.

b. Enter Keychain Access.

c. In the search field, enter adal.

d. Select all items whose type is MicrosoftOffice15_2_Data:ADAL:<GUID>, and then press Delete.

e. In the search field, enter office.

f. Quit Keychain Access.

g. Navigate to <YourUserName>Library > Group Containers.

h. Find the folders below and move them to Trash.

UBF8T346G9.ms

UBF8T346G9.Office

UBF8T346G9.OfficeOsfWebHost

Warning: Outlook data will be removed when you move the three folders listed in this step to Trash. You should back up these folders before you delete them.

i. Restart the Mac.

j. Open PowerPoint 2016 for Mac and re-activate it.

k. Do a test again.

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

What you can do with Word OnLine is pretty limited.  However if you are talking about creating and editing documents in Word on your computer, and assuming that you are talking about the font in the Normal style,  right click on that Style in the Styles gallery and then select Modify and change the font to the one that you want to use and select the radio button for New documents based on this template.

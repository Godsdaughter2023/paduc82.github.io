Hi Sarah131415,

According to your description, you want to get the result of email searching in Outlook faster. suggest you try to open Outlook 2016 in safe mode by the following steps:

1. Open the Run command dialog (Simultaneously press Windows logo and R keys on your keyboard).
2. Type “Outlook.exe /safe” (be sure to add a space between “Outlook.exe” and” /safe”) and then click OK button to open Outlook 2016 in Safe mode.
3. On the Chose Profile dialog, choose a profile (by default, it choose “Outlook” and if you have multiple profiles, choose one) and click OK button.

If it works faster in safe mode, the cause of this issue might be related to add-ins in Outlook. In this way, we suggest you check the add-ins in Outlook 2016 and you can disable or reinstall them.

Besides, the large PST files may slow down Outlook. We suggest you compact the PST files by following steps:

1. Right-click your email account in the folder Pane.
2. Select Data file Properties > Click Advanced > Select Advanced tab.
3. Click Outlook Data File Settings > Click Compact Now.

Otherwise, as Outlook will not optimize folders in PST files and you have lots of older items which may affect the speed performance of Outlook client, it’s recommended to set the Outlook client to archive files automatically by using AutoArchive. You can follow the steps below:

The Intel SSD utility tells me that I should enable DIPM for my SSD, as opposed to HIPM.  This appears to take a bit of tinkering to do in Vista, and my situation is further complicated by the presence of my secondary (original) HDD, which would rather use HIPM.

carlos frederico jeunon say:

You will need two SATA controllers: either both onboard or one onboard and one on PCI-E. 

 

Configure the one that doens't need TRIM/DIPM (not using an SSD drive) as non-AHCI (IDE/ATA-6 compatible) in the BIOS and the other (where TRIM/DIPM will be used) as AHCI.

 

Plug the drive for which you need DIPM to the AHCI controller. See http://www.abxzone.com/forums/f155/tip-how-enable-dipm-ssds-windows-120968.html for more info.

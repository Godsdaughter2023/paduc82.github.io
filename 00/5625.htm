gil deg say:

The error message you are getting means that your distribution list is too big.
This message is actually sent by your ISP that you are using or the corporate mail administrator.
The sending limit of Outlook for outgoing email messages is three hundred messages per day. Maximum 100 per email.
The reason these limits are set is to protect against users sending spam e-mails. If you have too many names on a list, your e-mail may be marked as spam and treated as such. Since distribution lists are sending the exact same message to a large number of recipients, the spam filter will likely pick up on that message and mistake it for junk mail.

We suggest that you contact your mail admin or your ISP just to make sure about the sending limit.

Keep us posted.

Now once you open a program if it doesn't open and take up the entire screen,

Grab the very top bar of the window and drag it to the top left corner of your screen,

Then diagonally drag the window to fill the rest of the space, close the program and reopen it,

It should open in the same size,

Unfortunately you will have to repeat for all other windows you use,

Don't do this with multiple windows open, windows should remember the last closed window but not the first if you know what I mean,

The dock would depend on if your maintain aspect ratio is checked but also the optimize resolution notification popup will change your resolution and cause this type of problem, it's best to disable optimize resolution,

Right click your desktop again and point at graphic options,

Point at the notification listing and disable both options, it would only apply if you use a 52 inch monitor anyway,

Also point at panel fit, and make sure maintain aspect ratio is checked.

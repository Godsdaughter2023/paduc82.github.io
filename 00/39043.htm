First you have to add your email accounts. Press Ctrl-Shift-T and fill in the forms.

Then import messages from the old store folder. Press Alt-F I, select Windows Live Mail and press Next. Browse to your Windows Dead Mail folder and then leave the program to get on with importing your messages. This could take quite some time if there are a lot of them, so find something else to do meanwhile.

You'll probably find your messages in an Imported folder under Storage folders, so make sure that Storage folders is highlighted on the View tab. They may be several levels deep; use the little triangles to expand folders until you see the messages themselves.

When the import has quite finished and the program has quietened down, re-connect to the Internet and send/receive. Depending on the type of account, you may receive lots of messages that you'd already downloaded before, or you may only get a few new ones. Then you can decide which of the imported messages you need to keep, and where to put them. You can't move folders out of Storage folders, but you can move messages. You can move folders around within Storage folders, so it's as well to move everything out of Imported folder to a more sensible location, to keep file paths short.

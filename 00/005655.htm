c.      You can past it to any word processer and save it.

Method 2:

a.     Open the webpage that you would like to save.

b.     Press the ‘Alt’ key on the keyboard, this will bring up the file menu options.

c.      Click on ‘File’ and then ‘Save As’.

d.     Enter the name you would like to save the page with.

e.     Click on save.

The webpage will be saved as an offline page in your documents folder.

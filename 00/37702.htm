If you want, many people are manually installing the upgrade with success. If that is something you wish to do, below are a set of step by step directions on how to do this.

Please read thoroughly before starting.

Step 1. Go to Control Panel, click on Windows Update, select and install all available updates, both the important ones and the optional ones.

Step 2. Once you are finished with that, pull up your start menu and see if there is an icon on your shutdown/restart option. If so, restart your computer so that the updates can be configured with the system and vice versa. If you do not see that icon then proceed with step 3.

Step 4. Plug your laptop into its charger and leave plugged into a power source for the remainder of these steps (if you have a desktop, you can obviously ignore this step)

Step 5. Open Control Panel, Click on System, look to see if you are running a 32 bit or a 64 bit system. Remember this number accurately as you will need to know it for the next step.

Step 6. Click on the link at the bottom of this post, download the Media Creator Tool that is appropriate to the bits you are running with your system, either 32 bit or 64 bit.

Step 7. Once Media Creator Tool has downloaded completely, close all programs and run the Media Creator Tool program. Follow any prompts that you may come across. NOTE: This step here, may or may not take a while and it is completely normal and expected that your screen may flash and your laptop/desktop restarts some, those are normal.

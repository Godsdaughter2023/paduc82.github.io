In this case, I would suggest you to try these steps and check if it helps:

 

Method 1:

 

I would suggest you to check if you the forgotten network is listed in network profiles stored in your computer. Objective reviews are useful to other users who may want to install an app and to its publisher. Such reviews provide good feedback to publishers when something is not right with their app or game. Users can also read the reviews for a more in-depth picture of how an app works.

  • Reviewing an app in the Windows Store is relatively simple. To review an app, it has to be installed on your device.

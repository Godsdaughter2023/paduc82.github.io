Firstly, you will need to add the account to a Family with an adult if it is not already set up. Only a parent using the Microsoft family service can change Xbox privacy and online safety settings for a child account. Change privacy and safety settings for the child:

  1. Sign in to the adult's Microsoft account at Xbox.com.  
    • If you’ve forgotten your account email address or password, see the Lost Account Solutionor the Lost Password Solution. 
  2. Under My Xbox, select Profile. 
  3. Select Privacy settings or Online safety on Xbox One.  
    • Due to the secure nature of these settings, you may be asked to enter a security code that we’ll send to the alternate phone number or email address previously saved to your account. 
  4. Select the account you want to look at. 
  5. Review the settings currently in place and update those you want to change. 
  6. Save your changes on each tab before moving on to the next. 

Give this a try then have your son try to access the game in question.

Cell A1 i formula it as =today() for today's date.

C3 is Check in Date while D3 is Check out date. E3 will be the status (where it suppose to be "In", "Out" or "Yet)

"In" means that "Today's Date (A1)" is equal and in between C3 and D3.
"Out" means that "Today's Date (A1)" is greater than D3.
"Yet" means that "Today's Date (A1)" is greater than C3

7-May-18
Check in Check out Status
5-May-18 10-May-18
8-May-18 11-May-18

=IF(AND(C3>$A$1,D3>=$A$1),"Yet",IF(AND($A$1<=D3,D3>=C3),"In","Out"))

In Windows Live Mail, right click on the old account name in the folder pane.

Click Properties.

Uncheck 'Include this account when receiving mail or synchronizing.

Are there any messages in the Outbox?

For the folders that are not showing in the IMAP account, create new folders in the IMAP account.

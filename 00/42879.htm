arminatorx say:

Microsoft doesn't do currency conversion. 

If you add your US$15 to your account, you will have an US$ based account with 15US$ on it, that you can use on the US marketplace to buy something in US$.

If you currently use a Turkish account, then you will have 0TRY on a second billing account, even though you have 15US$ in your US based billing account. 

So no, you can't buy the DLC while you are set to Turkey. 

On the Xbox One, you can simply set the country and language to US English, and you will be able to redeem the 15US$, and if the DLC is available in the US, you can also buy that DLC with your US$ on your account. 

After the purchase, you can then switch back to the Turkish language and locale.

Is there a safe and foolproof way of making this message go away forever?  If so, what are the steps? Thank you!

stefan blom say:
I don't know of any other solution than making certain registry keys read-only (see https://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/msoffice/forum/all/disable-pick-up-where-you-left-off/d818eba4-af8f-4296-844f-abd540e41a0a). This may not be possible on all versions of Windows 10.

davem121 say:
Hi Doug

Editing the registry is the only way to stop this behavior, and once you follow the steps, it is easy and safe . . .

Click your Start Button, type regedit and hit Enter to open the Registry Editor

Navigate to (your Office Version No may be different to mine)

HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Office\16.0\Word\Reading Locations

Right click and delete all the sub Keys (folders) of Reading Locations

Then right click Reading Locations and choose Permissions

Set Permissions to 'Deny All' on your Account

Save that, close the Registry and restart your PC . . .

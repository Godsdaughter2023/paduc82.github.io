amandad6 say:

Conditional Formatting Based on Age

Hi,

I have a spreadsheet with Date of Birth Listed. The Dates begin in C3. I would like to use conditional formatting to highlight the date if it is more than 18 years from today's date. Basically, I want to highlight all of the people on the spreadsheet who are 18 and older. I cannot figure out how to do it. Please help.

Thanks

mike h.. say:

Hi,

Select C3 then drag to select as many cells as you want this to apply to. You can include some blank cells if you need for later additions, the formula will ignore them. Then:-

Home tab | conditional formatting | new rule | use a formula to decide which cells to format and enter this formula.

=AND(C3<>"",C3<=DATE(YEAR(TODAY())-18,MONTH(TODAY()),DAY(TODAY())))

click the format button | fill tab | choose a colour and OK out.

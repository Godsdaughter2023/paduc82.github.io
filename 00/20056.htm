Hi ipokhrel,

 

No, we cannot move items from the second-stage Recycle Bin to the first-stage Recycle Bin. Thanks for your understanding.

 

When we delete items from a SharePoint site, they are sent to the first-stage Recycle Bin. When we delete items from the first-stage Recycle Bin, they are sent to the second-stage Recycle Bin. 

In the second-stage Recycle Bin, we can either Delete or Restore the items. If an item is restored, it will go to its original location. If an item is deleted from the second-stage Recycle Bin, or it exceeds the retention time, it is permanently deleted.

hi CJaber,

One way would be to use a wildcard Find/Replace, where:
Find = ([!^13]{90}[! ]{1,9})([ ])

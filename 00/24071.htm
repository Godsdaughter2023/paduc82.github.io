"and stop processing rules" applies to the item being handled only - once a spam message has been moved to the Junk Mail folder, you don't want other rules to be applied to that message.

"and stop processing rules" does not mean that this rule won't be applied to further messages meeting the same criteria!

I think you miss the purpose of that action. It means that if that rules apply to a message, no further rules will be applied to that message. All other messages will of course still be checked against all your rules.

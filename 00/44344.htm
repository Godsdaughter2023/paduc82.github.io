davem121 say:
Hi Cottsnr

This blurred (acrylic) lock screen is a new 'Feature' of the 1903 update, it is definitely dividing opinion . ..

There is no setting to change that in Windows 10, there is however a registry fix, if you want to remove that . ..

Click your Start Button, type regedit and hit Enter to open the Registry Editor

Click View and make sure 'Address Bar' is turned on

Paste this into the Address Bar at the top and hit Enter

HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Policies\Microsoft\Windows\System

In the right hand pane, right click and choose New - DWORD

Paste this as the name of the new DWORD:

DisableAcrylicBackgroundOnLogon

Then double click that new DWORD and set its value to 1

Click OK and close the Registry

That's It!

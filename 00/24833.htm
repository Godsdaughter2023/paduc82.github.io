SOLVED

Good news is the problem is fixed now. The bad news is it does take a complete uninstall and reinstall of the Office Suite. No repairing efforts or downloading updates will work, you need to clean out the system and do a fresh install from scratch.

STEP ONE: uninstall Office and all of its subparts.

  • click start or the windows button
  • find the Office app
  • right click and then choose "uninstall"
  • wait for the uninstall to complete
  • NOTE: you should not need to uninstall the individual components (word, excel, and the like) - Just uninstall Office as a whole.

jay freedman say:

There is no way to get the navigation pane to do what you're asking under your constraints. It simply doesn't have the ability to differentiate parts of a single paragraph that way.

What you can do is to insert complete paragraphs, formatted with one of the built-in heading styles, to act as temporary tags while you're writing the thesis. When you're doing the final editing before submitting the thesis, make another copy of the document file. In that copy, open the Styles pane (Ctrl+Shift+Alt+S), click the down arrow of the style used for the tags, click the Select All x Instances item on the menu, and then press the Delete key. That will remove all the tags.

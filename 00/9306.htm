aaronzhu_msft say:

Hi Anthony,

The answer to your question is yes. You can create a custom Address List by filtering equipment mailboxes in Exchange Online to achieve your goal. Here are the detailed steps for your reference(Note: You need an administrator account for these steps):

1. Sign in to EAC(Exchange admin center).
2. Go to permissions > admin roles.
3. Double click Organization Management.
4. In the pop-up window, add Address Lists in the Roles(This step enables you to run some cmdlets about address lists in PowerShell).
5. Connect to Exchange Online using remote PowerShell.
6. Run the following cmdlet:

New-AddressList -Name "All equipments" -RecipientFilter { (RecipientTypedetails -eq 'equipmentMailbox') }

As a tip, after you run the cmdlet above, it will apply to newly created equipment mailboxes. For existing equipment mailboxes, we can temporary modify one of the equipment mailbox properties such as location in Exchange admin center, then change it back to manually update the mailbox.

Here is an article for your reference: Manage address lists in Exchange Online.

If you run into any errors when performing the steps above, feel free to let us know and we will further assist you.

david biddulph say:

D2 = 86%  

J3  = 62%

J4 = 73%

K3 = 72%

K4 = 77%

 

Hello ,

 

I am trying to create a formula for conditional formatting to have a cell turn Green, Yellow or Red.

 

Green D2 is greater than or equal to J3, but less than or equal to J4.

 

Yellow D2 is less than J3 or J4 greater than J4 but less than or equal to K4.

 

Red  D2 is greater than K4

 

I am not sure how to create the formula(s) that would set all the conditions so that the appropriate color will be applied.

 

I would really appreciate any help.


For your green condition the formula is =AND(D2>=J3,D2<=J4)

For your yellow condition the formula is =OR(D2<J3,AND(J4>J4,J4<=K4))  [but I don't think you really mean J4>J4, so perhaps you intended to say

=OR(D2<J3,AND(J4>K3,J4<=K4)) ]

For your red condition the formula is =D2>K4

David Biddulph

    Is there a way to remove non-breaking spaces and replace them with breaking spaces without having to specify each time through Search/Replace?     john korchok say:

    This is pretty easy to set up:

    1. In the navigation pane, select Find and Replace and type ^s into the Find field.
    2. Type a space character in the Replace field.
    3. Choose Tools>Macros>Record Macro
    4. Give the macro a name, then click on Keyboard to assign a keyboard shortcut. When you find one that's not already assigned to something else, click on the Assign button, then OK out. The macro is recording, so click on the Replace All button.
    5. Choose Tools>Macro>Stop Recording.

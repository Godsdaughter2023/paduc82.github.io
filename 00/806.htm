Check if the document is blocked. Follow the steps below:

Save the email attachment to the desktop.

Right click on the saved document and go to properties.

Check if you have an option to unblock. If you do, click on it and then try to open the file.

 

You may also Try Disabling Protected View for Outlook Attachments.

Open the relevant Office Program (I had to do it in Word and Excel)

Go to File > Options >Trust Center >Trust Center Settings > Protected View.

Deselect Enable Protected View for Outlook Attachments.

 

If the issue still persists, follow the step in the article below to set DCOM security settings and check if it helps:

http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2387587

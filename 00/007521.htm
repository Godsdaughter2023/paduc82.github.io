How to favorite a contact - Microsoft Community

jorge e. - wp7 support say:

How to favorite a contact

How to favorite a contact
jorge e. - wp7 support say:

From the Favorites Contacts screen

  1. Open the Favorite Contacts screen.
  2. Tap the plus sign (+) in an empty slot to open a list of all your contacts.
  3. To jump to a contact in the list, start typing the contact’s name.
  4. Tap the contact you want to add as a favorite.  The contact now appears on the Favorites Home screen.

Note: You can also add a contact to Favorites by opening their contact card and tapping More > Favorite.

From the All Contacts list

  1. Tap the contact you want to add as a favorite.
  2. Tap More.
  3. Tap Favorite. Your contact is added to Favorites.
Remove a single contact from Favorites
  1. From any contact screen, tap the contact you want to remove as a favorite.
  2. Tap More.
  3. Tap Unfavorite. Your contact is removed from Favorites.

Spammers cleverly use famous domain names to send spam email across the world which is indeed difficult to track and block/stop.

Here is a step which you can employ as a workaround to block such emails. You may try creating a rule to send all emails arriving with the Outlook.com domain name to the Spam folder, however, even genuine emails from Outlook.com users will be delivered to the Spam folder. For this, you can keep a track of such emails being delivered to the spam folder and add those genuine email senders to your Safe Senders list. This is of course going to be a tedious task and will involve your time and effort initially to check spam folder regularly and redirect genuine emails to Inbox and add those addressed to Safe Senders list. You may also add known Outlook.com users to the Safe Senders list beforehand to avoid missing emails from such senders being delivered to the Spam folder.

stefan blom say:

Your screen shots do not display, so I can't give specific advice.

However, note that indents (from the left) for bulleted and numbered items must be specified in the multilevel list dialog box, that is, you can't use the horizontal ruler or the Paragraph dialog. Click in the first level 1 list item and then click Home | Multilevel List | Define New Multilevel List. If necessary, click the More button to see all options.

"Aligned at" defines the position of the bullet or number symbol. "Text indent at" determines where the text starts after the bullet/number. And "Add tab stop at" defines where the second and subsequent text lines start. All measurements are from the left margin, which means that if you want a hanging indent, you would want "Text indent at" to be identical to "Add tab stop at" (and both of these values should be larger than "Aligned at" of course).

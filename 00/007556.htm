bernd p. say:


When I paste images, they are stored not in the notebook, but in the cache folder. 

No they are stored at first in the cache, then automatically (with more or less delay) in the notebook's master. This is called notebook synchronization. The master is the real notebook, on your computer, on a network share or on Onedrive.

But I must admit that there is one dangerous configuration: a notebook on Onedrive and no Internet connection. Then the synchronization is suspended until the next reconnection to the Internet.

During this time your picture lives only in the local cache.

And if you delete a page it is automatically deleted from the master (the original) and the cache.

What has to be protected is not the cache, it is the master ....

The cache can always be reconsructed from the master.

Bernd

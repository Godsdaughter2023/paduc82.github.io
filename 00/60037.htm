A: There are two purposes only, one is to help you add contacts easily and the second is to synchronize your contacts across all Microsoft services. 2) Beyond not "sharing, combining, collecting" my data (which is great), what do you do with it? (I am looking for the affirmative instead of the negative response, if you follow me.)

A: Yes, if you synchronize your contacts in Skype, the contacts will only be stored in Skype's directory. But as previously stated on the no. 1 item, your contacts will also appear on Microsoft's contact directory if you will associate /link your Skype account to a Microsoft account.

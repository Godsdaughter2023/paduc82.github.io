One can change the default Home Place of the Windows 8 Weather app by bringing down the App bar (right click keyboard or swipe from top edge using touch).   A bar will also appear at the bottom edge.  There will be buttons on the bottom edge Change Home (lower left), Current Location (lower right) along with others.    If you have already selected another Place, but not made it your new Home Place, the buttons are slightly different, and you would use the Set as Home button.

Thereafter, this will be your default Home Place until you change it.

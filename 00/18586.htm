qa guy commelec say:

Try this after creating a table NUMList with number field NUMList containing 1 through 3.   TESTED using your data.

TRANSFORM Sum(IIf([CountNUM]=1,PowerPurchase.[KWH],IIf([CountNUM]=2,[DEMAND],[ECA]))) AS SumOfKWH
SELECT Format([PPDATE],"mmm") AS [Month]
FROM PowerPurchase, CountNumber
WHERE (((CountNumber.CountNUM) Between 1 And 3))
GROUP BY Format([PPDATE],"mmm")
PIVOT IIf([CountNUM]=1,chr(32),IIf([CountNUM]=2,chr(127)&chr(160),chr(28))) & Format([PPDATE],"yyyy")  ;

Put your header of it.

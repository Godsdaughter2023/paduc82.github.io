The HP Solution Center requires the Active-X file, Flash9b.ocx, a component that is not included in Vista. Prior to Vista, Flash was included as a Windows component.

Unfortunately, the Solution Center programmers hard coded this specific version of Flash into the installation program. Updating, or removing, Adobe Flash Player will trigger the reinstallation of the Solution Center. A clean uninstall and reinstallation of the HP software will resolve the problem, that is until you update Adobe Flash Player.

Note: A reinstall requires stopping all programs running in the background, this includes Anti-Virus, Anti-Spyware, Firewall, Mail programs, and whatever else you have running at startup.

Nat Ray has included another suggestion which you can find from the link above.

When the Task Bar is at the bottom (or the top) of the screen, (instead of along the sides):

1.  From a blank space in task bar, move POINTER up or down, so it turns into an up & down ARROW. 

2.  HOLD and slide arrow up or down, to create the size Task Bar you want. 

The TIME, and DATE will remain in the single line Task Bar, but not the Day.

HI Danny,

There is an option where you can keep your files upon resetting Windows 10, but it will still delete the apps and drivers that are installed on your PC. Reinstalling Windows will fix your issue, yes, but we can recommend an alternative to get out of the automatic repair loop, by interrupting boot sequence. To do so, upon PC startup (usually when Windows logo appear or before automatic repair appears), force shutdown your PC by pressing and holding the power button. By doing this for 3 times, you will be able to access Windows 10 Advanced Startup Option. After doing so, simply click Continue (Exit and continue to Windows 10) and you will be able to turn your PC on normally.

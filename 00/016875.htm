doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

With a wildcard search, put the things that you do want to find inside [ ] followed by {1,}


For those that have a particular function in a Wildcard search, you will have to preface them with a \.


The following is a list of the characters that have a special meaning in wildcard searches ( [ ] { } < > ( ) - @ ? ! * \ ).

macropod say:

You could use:

Find = [!a-z0-9^s]{1,}
or, perhaps:
[!A-Za-z0-9^s]{1,}

Depending on your regional settings, you may need to use {1;} instead of {1,}

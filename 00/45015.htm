considerate_guy say:

When Microsoft released a version of Movie Maker for Windows 7, they called it "Windows Live Movie Maker" and included it as part of "Windows Live Essentials". The first version was a beta version called Windows Live Movie Maker 2009. They continued this naming until the 2011 version. Microsoft subsequently dropped the Live branding and now just calls the program Movie Maker 2012. So yes, we are talking about the same program, but it sounds like you just have an earlier version.

Windows Essentials download (Movie Maker 2012)

Movie Maker can edit videos longer than 1 hour. But if you are using the direct webcam capture feature, it will stop after 1 hour. The workaround is to just capture one hour (save that movie) and then capture another hour (save that movie).

Most capture devices come with separate software to handle the capture part. So if you don't want to deal with the 60 minute continuous capture limit in Movie Maker, you can use the software that comes with the device instead.

I figure you are going to be "babysitting" the capture anyway since you need to start the VCR playing and the device capturing and then you need to stop capturing when it is done. When I've done it, the software that came with the device was smart enough to notice that the video signal had stopped and to stop recording so I could just hit play on the VCR and record in the capture software, then walk away. It created a file and then I could edit it down as needed in something like Movie Maker.

considerate_guy say:

That device is not designed to defeat VHS copy protection (e.g. Macrovision or similar). Usually you'll either see a degradation in the image, squiggly lines, or even a message from the device saying it can't copy the tape.

The intent of those analog capture devices to take a clean composite signal (e.g. from home video VHS tapes) and turn it into a digital signal, but it probably will have issues with a commercial Hollywood VHS. You'll probably have better success just purchasing the DVD or digital download of the movie.

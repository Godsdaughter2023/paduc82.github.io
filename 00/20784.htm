hansv mvp say:

By placing InStr in the WHERE clause, you force Access to evaluate it for ALL records, even those with an empty (null) PrevTitle. This causes the error. You can use the Nz function to avoid this:

 

SELECT Mid(PrevTitle,InStr(PrevTitle,'/')+1,4)
FROM NewType5
WHERE PrevTitle Is Not Null And PrevTitle<>'' And PrevTitle Like '*/*' AND IsNumeric(Mid(Nz(PrevTitle),InStr(Nz(PrevTitle),'/')+1,4))

You are trying to publish to office online - not your sharepoint server. You can't use this method to publish to sharepoint. You either need to link the sharepoint calendar to outlook and copy the items you wish to publish into the sharepoint calendar 9or create them in it) or use a 3rd party utility.

The publish command in outlook uploads an ics to a webdav server - so it would be possible to publish it to a local server, just not to a sharepoint calendar.

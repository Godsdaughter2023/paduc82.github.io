brian tillman [outlook mvp 2007-2019] say:

I think I know what's happening, at least in part. I suspect you have an IMAP account, given the domain of your address. For IMAP, a move is a two-step process. The first step is a copy to the destination folder and the second step is a deletion of the original message. This latter step puts a copy of the message in Trash. I suspect that if you look, you'll see messages in the "Mail from Contacts" folder that duplicate the ones in the Trash folder. In other words, I think the rule may be working.

Note that for a rule that perform moves it's a good idea, in my opinion, to include the "stop processing more rules" action in the rule.

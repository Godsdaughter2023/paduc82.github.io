doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

The entries in a Table of Contents are always formatted with TOC # styles

and the number in the TOC # style corresponds to the TOC level

By default, paragraphs to which the Heading 1 style is assigned in the body of the document will appear as entries in the Table of Contents in the TOC 1 style.  Similarly, paragraphs in Heading 2 style will appear in TOC 2 style, and so on.

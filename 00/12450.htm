To get the hyperlinks fixed:
  • Click File > Options > Proofing > AutoCorrect Options.
  • In the dialog box, click the AutoFormat tab. (Note: Not the AutoFormat As You Type tab, which is similar.)
  • Clear all the check boxes except "Internet and network paths with hyperlinks".
  • Click OK.
  • Right-click the Quick Access Toolbar and choose Customize Quick Access Toolbar.
  • In the "Choose commands from" box, select "Commands Not in the Ribbon".
  • In the list below that, choose "AutoFormat Now" and then click the Add button between the lists.
  • Click OK.
  • On the Quick Access Toolbar, click the button you just added. That will convert all the addresses to hyperlinks.

  1. Press “Windows Logo” + “X” keys on the keyboard and select “Command Prompt (Admin)” from the list.
  2. Type “net user username password /add” and press “Enter”.

Note: Replace 'username' and 'password' with your desired username and password.

  1. Press “Windows Logo” + “X” keys together and select “Windows Explorer” from the list.
  2. Click on “View” tab and Click on “Options” at the top right corner.

  5. Click “Yes” to confirm, and then click on “OK”.

    • Ntuser.dat
    • Ntuser.dat.log
    • Ntuser.ini
  8. Right click and Select “Copy”.
  9. Locate the “C:\Users\New_Username” folder, where C is the drive that Windows is installed on, and “New_Username” is the name of the new user profile you have created.

nathan roberts (sunknath) say:

Hey there,

It looks like it is a guest account that you have enabled on your Xbox. To remove this, you must disable the guest account by clicking on disable guest through your settings. This is not an actual Xbox gamer profile, which is why you can not see it when you go into the sign in options or user accounts.

This also happens when you connect a second controller to your Xbox and sign in as guest on a game. Achievements can not be saved on a guest, and they will have all of the achievements you have.

Hope this helps,

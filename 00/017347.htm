bazdc say:

I had the same issue and did some research and found that the Service Pack 3 for Office 2003 breaks or disables handwriting recognition.

As I understand it the solution is to either copy and paste the penusa.dll file in C:\Program Files\Common Files\Microsoft Shared\INK from a machine without the SP3 update or to reinstall Office 2003 and copy the same file before updating, then paste it after the SP3 update.

See link below to similar question:

http://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/office/forum/office_2003-office_install/office-2003-sp3-update-for-windows-xp-sp3/be27abe8-d349-4eba-897b-189d6251a00b

I have tried this and it worked. I uninstalled and reinstalled Office 2003 on an old laptop I had then copied the early (2001) penusa.dll file onto my desktop, replacing the later version. My desktop now has fully functional handwriting recognition using a Wacom Graphire tablet.

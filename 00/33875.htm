Your compatibility details probably were not refreshed since you installed the new graphics adapter. To re-run the compatibility assessment:

1. On your keyboard, press the Windows Key + X, and click Command Prompt (Admin) from the options.

You'll need to wait up to 15 minutes for the assessment to run, then your new compatibility details will be available.

It is, unfortunately, a very old scam. They call up acting like they're from one company, such as Microsoft, and claiming to know a lot about something that you have and then they proved that in some way. All that advanced technology and better communications has done for the scam is make it more accessible and easy to pull off from anywhere.

The only defense is common sense. If you did not call them, assume it's a scam.  Safe bet.

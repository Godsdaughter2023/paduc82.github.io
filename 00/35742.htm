Eject the disc from the Xbox console and hold the power button for 10 seconds. Turn the console back on, and make sure it shows a green boot screen. 

Since this takes a while for the console to restart, take your disc to the kitchen. No matter how clean and pristine the disc looks, apply a small drop of mild dishwasher soap on the silver side of the disc. When the whole disc is covered in soap, make sure to hold the disc only with your index finger in the center hole and your thumb on the very edge of the disc from now on.

Rinse the disc under running water. Shake it several times to get the water off, and wipe the remaining drops with a soft, lint-free cloth. 

Now return to your console, which should have finished booting. 

Insert the disc and make sure the game shows up below the main game tile with a disc icon in the corner.

If you've set the Outlook as your default email and you're still having issues about the notifications, let's try another workaround. Try turning the notifications on for the Outlook app. Follow the steps below:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Click System then Notifications & actions.
  3. Click on Outlook 2016.
  4. Check if Notifications is On and set the Show in action center switch On.

If the issue still persists, try turning Mail app notifications off instead:

  1. Open Mail app then click Settings (gear icon on the bottom).
  2. Select Notifications.
  3. Turn off Show notifications in the Action Center to stop showing new emails in the Action Center.

Should you need further assistance, feel free to post your query anytime.

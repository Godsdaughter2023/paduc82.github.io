Changing Background - Microsoft Community

katiesharp1982 say:

Changing Background

Good morning

One of my colleagues is dyslexic and struggles with the white background on word, excel and outlook. How do I change this to grey for him.

Thanks in advance

roady [mvp] say:

File-> Office Account-> Office Theme

or
File-> Options-> Office Theme

The email background itself isn't affected since that is not part of the interface (unlike in Word when a document is sufficiently zoomed out).

You can set a background for the document or email itself but this will of course affect the email for the recipient and won't affect emails that are being received (unless he edits them first).

Not maximizing the application window and using a grey background as the Windows background might help with the "distraction" as well.

Also lower the screen's brightness itself if the bright white make the letters "dance" for him.

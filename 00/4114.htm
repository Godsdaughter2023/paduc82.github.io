a. user say:

Hi,

 

It is possible that your Calendar time zone and event time zone is not correctly configured on your account. Please refer to the steps below to know how to change your settings:

 

To check/update Calendar time zone:

 

1. In Windows Live Calendar, click Options

2. See Time Zone and update if necessary

3. Click Save.

 

 

To check/update Event time zone:

 

1. Click the Add more details link when creating a new event or click an already existing event to see its details.

2. Update as necessary

3. Click Save.

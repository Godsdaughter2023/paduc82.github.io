iii.       Use a different cable to connect your modem to the telephone outlet. If this solves the problem, you have a damaged cable. Replace the cable with a new cable.

iv.        Use your modem cable to connect a telephone that you know works. If this does not work, you have a damaged cable. Replace the cable with a new cable.

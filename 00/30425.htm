Method 1: Follow the steps below to know How to Enable Media Streaming with Windows Media Player 12.

1. Turn on the PS3 and PC, in addition to any necessary network devices. Highlight "Network Settings" from the "Settings" menu of the PS3 menu and press the "X" button.

3. Highlight "Search for Media Servers" from the media menus on the PS3 main menu and press "X." Once the PS3 has completed the scanning process, your Windows Media Player will be displayed on the PS3 menu.

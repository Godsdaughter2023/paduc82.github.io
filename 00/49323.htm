_elaine_ say:

Hi, jrt_q

Simple answer: No!  Skype does not charge for incoming calls to Skype Numbers.

If the people who call you using what you would select as a Skype Number incur international or toll call charges, they pay those charges -- not you.  Most people choose a Skype Number based as close as possible to where the majority of their callers are located, to avoid callers having to pay toll call fees.

A few things to remember ...

  1. Skype Numbers can not receive SMS replies: text message replies will be received by your mobile phone number.
  2. While you may have up to ten (10) Skype Number subscriptions active on a single account, for call forwarding purposes, it is the account itself that is forwarded, not individual numbers.
  3. If this is important to you, Skype no longer provides custom outgoing voice mail greetings.  Even if this was still available, it would have been one voice mail for the entire account, not individual numbers.  Likewise, the voice mailbox is for the entire account, and can not be assigned for each individual number.  If you have only one Skype Number subscription, then the previous sentence would not apply.

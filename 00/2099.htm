How to add domain/email address in blocked list.

1. Sign in to your  account.
2. In the upper right corner of the mailbox page, click on Gear icon, click on More mail settings.
3. Under Preventing junk email, click on Safe and blocked senders.
4. Click Blocked senders.
5. In the Blocked senders list, type the complete email address or the domain name of the account (the part of an e-mail address that follows the @ sign, such as hotmail/outlook.com) that you don’t want to receive mails from.
6. Then click Add to list >>.

How to create rules

1. Sign in to your account.
2. On the left side of your Inbox, click on Gear icon, then click More mail settings.
3. Under Customizing Hotmail, Click Rules for sorting new messages.
4. Click on New button to create rule
5. Under Step 1, Select from the options below to determine which messages are affected by the rule
6. Under Step 2, Select the action the rule applies
7. Click Save.

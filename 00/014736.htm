Via checking the NDR, we have found that the recipient (example@company.org) is not existed in an accepted domain for your Office 365 organization. For recipient (Office 365 user) was not found under the accepted domain, you can check if the accepted domain is authoritative firstly. Weird but true. You just unearthed another bug. The word Auto you refer to appears only if:

  • Deletions are set to display with Double Strikethrough and
  • Balloons are hidden

Solutions:

  • Set deletions to show as a single-line Overstrike and the intruder disappears, or
  • Enable Balloons

To change the display preference for deletions, select the Review tab in the Ribbon, then click and hold Show Markup to unfold the dropdown menu, choose Preferences  and change the attribute for Deletions.

To enable balloons, turn them on right there as well.

Note that deletions display correctly in the Review Pane.

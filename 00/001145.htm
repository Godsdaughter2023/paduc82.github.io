Actually, I think a better answer is that you don't really NEED any anti-virus software on a Windows Phone.  Because of the way the operating system is constructed, each app is 'sandboxed' in their own operating environment, with no access to other applications or other data (except for some specific exceptions).  So, apps installed on your phone can really only see themselves and the data they create.  There is no way for them to 'see' other data files or other applications and hence, so way to infect anything.

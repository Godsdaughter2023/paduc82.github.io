You should know that IE10 will be installed as an update, that’s why it doesn’t appears in the installed programs list shown by Programs and Features instead it appears in “Installed Updates” list.

Uninstalling – reinstall IE10 from Windows 7

a) Click on Start Menu>Control Panel>Programs>Programs and Features.

b) Click on “View installed updates” on the left pane.

c) Once installed updates are displayed on right-pane, select “Windows Internet Explorer 10 “update and click Uninstall button

d) Click Yes to proceed with the uninstall and further click on “restart now” button to uninstall IE10 and restore previous version of Internet Explorer you’re using earlier that could be IE9.

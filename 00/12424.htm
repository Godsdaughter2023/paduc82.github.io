what is the meaning of 24FS+3 days - Microsoft Community

geetanshupramod say:

what is the meaning of 24FS+3 days

Hi,

In one of the project plan, i could see predecessor as 24FS+3 days.
Could anyone, please help me to understand, what is this?

Thanks & Regards,
Geetanshu

andrew simpson uk say:
  • 24 is the ID of the task which is the predecessor.
  • FS means it's a finish to start dependency, so when the predecessor finishes the successor will start
  • + 3 days means there is 3 days lag between the two tasks. This means thet when the predecessor finishes, we will wait three days before the successof tasks starts. It's still driven by the predecessors finish date, but there's a wait (e.g. the predecessor task could be applying paint, and there's a 3 days lag while the paint dries before you can start the successor task).

I hope this helps,

Andrew

You cant technically delete the gamertag itself, but you can disassociate it from yourself completely. Please note: You will have a 30 day waiting period before you can move your gamertag to a new Microsoft Account if you fall into any of the below scenario's.

 

  • Your profile is less then 30 days old.
  • You have downloaded your profile from Xbox LIVE within the last 30 days.
  • You have already moved the profile to a different Microsoft Account within the past 30 days.

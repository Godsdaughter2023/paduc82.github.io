Getting the vendor names into a single cell is a bit messy but you can use the ARRAY formula below to get them in seperate cells.

 

For product 1

 

=IFERROR(INDEX($A$1:$A$5,SMALL(IF($B$1:$B$5=$B$6,ROW($A$1:$A$5)-ROW($A$1)+1),ROWS(A$1:A1))),"")

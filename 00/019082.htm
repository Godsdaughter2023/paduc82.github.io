steve rindsberg say:

You'll need to insert your placeholder on each layout rather than on the main slide master.

Try this:

View | Slide Master

Click on the first layout in the thumbnails pane on the left (this would be the SECOND thumbnail from the top ... the first is the slide master itself).

On the Slide Master tab, choose Insert Placeholder (it's in the Master Layout group) and insert a text placeholder.

Click and drag to set the size/location of the new placeholder.

Select and delete all the text in it, replace it with something more meaningful to your purposes;  "Slide#" or something like that.

Format it to suit, making sure to set Bullets to None on the Home tab.

Select it, press Ctrl+C to copy it.

Now Press Page Down then press Ctrl+V to paste it onto the next layout.

Repeat for each layout.  

Now add some new slides and verify that you can click in the new placeholder text box and type your slide numbers.

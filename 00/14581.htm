You need to put the code within  

Sub [macroname]()

With ActiveDocument.Tables(1)
    For i = .Rows.Count To 1 Step -1

In a Word TOC, you can't use bookmarks to add individual entries. (Instead, you make use of bookmarks when you want to limit the scope of a TOC.)

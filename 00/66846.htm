a. user say:

I suggest you leave the guest account disabled and create a standard user account for your guests either with no password or a simple one that you tell the guest when required.  Guest accounts can be more easily used by hackers to gain access to the system.  As a standard user account, it should have internet access unless you configired the system to restrict access to only administrators or certain users - and I suspect you didn't do that.  If the internet is not connected, you may have to do that yourself as you may not want to disclose the username and password to anyone else (and if it is set on automatic, it will use the guest account information which will be wrong). Once connected, they can just use Internet Explorer with no problems.  It should work with USB dialup or wireless - whichever network you choose to use.

I hope thi helps.

Good luck!

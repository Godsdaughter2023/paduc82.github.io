joanne li msft say:

Hi KohChi,

I'd like to explain that it is an expected behavior. When we press Tab key, it will automatically align to the texts above it. If the lengths of the word are not same, the distance between them will not be the same as well since it sets the distance between the first letter of each word to be the same when you use tab key, not the distance between words (the distance between the last letter of the last word and the first letter of the next word). And based on my test and research, no method is available It is not feasible to make it not align to texts above it automatically when we use Tab key.

As a workaround, you can try adjust the tab stops manually to make the distance between words to be the same.

Other community members who have experiences on this are welcome to share their insights here.

Regards,

Joanne

Oh might be.
I would remove the printer and readd it.
Right click your start button and select Run.
Type the following.

printmanagement.msc

Hit enter.
This will open the Print Management tool.
Expand Custom Filter.
Click Printers.
Right click the Canon and select Delete.

Reboot.

Log back in and reinstall the printer.

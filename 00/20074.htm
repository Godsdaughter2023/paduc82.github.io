easona say:

Hi, raysnlam. Are you encountering any error code or message? In the meantime, you may try performing the suggestions listed below:

1. Make sure that your phone is not in Battery Saver or Flight mode.
2. Make sure that the pairing devices are set to be visible to one another and there are no obstructions blocking the space between the devices.
3. If you cannot establish a connection or lose it soon after pairing, make sure that your phone book contacts do not have special characters or blanks before the name; rename or remove them if they do.
4. Clear the paired devices list on your phone's Bluetooth settings.
5. Reset the pairing Bluetooth device to clear information about the last Bluetooth devices and go through the pairing process (refer to the pairing device's user manual to find reset and pairing instructions).
6. Check if your phone's software and all of the applications are already up-to-date. For phone updates, go to Settings > phone update > check for updates. For app updates, go to Store > ... > settings > check for updates.

Your phone supports the following Bluetooth profiles:
 
1. Advanced Audio Distribution Profile (A2DP) 1.2
2. Audio/Video Remote Control Profile (AVRCP) 1.4
3. Generic Attribute Profile (GATT)
4. Hands-free profile (HFP) 1.6
5. Object Push profile (OPP) 1.1
6. Phone Book Access Profile (PBAP) 1.1

The compatibility with third party devices will still depend on the bluetooth standards and applications related by the device manufacturer. You may contact them for further assistance.

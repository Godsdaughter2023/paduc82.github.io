julie fka profjulie say:

There's no way to download any of the data that is stored on your My Phone web page directly to your computer, a CD or external drive.  Here's a way I worked out to do what you want to do, but it's a bit convoluted:

  • On your My Phone web page, select all the photos you want to download to your computer.
  • Then select "Share All" (you will need to have a site set up where your pictures can be shared - you can use a Windows Live website, but Flickr will work too - both are free)
  • Select the website where you want to share your pictures and select the "Share" button
  • Then go to Windows Live or Flickr and log on to your account.  The pictures will now be displayed there. 
  • For Windows Live, you can then select More > Download > Download as .zip file
  • After you've downloaded the pictures to your computer, you'll need to go back to your My Phone account and delete the pictures there to free up space

Both Windows Live and the free version of Flickr have some space limitations, so you may run up against those limitations when you attempt this.  If you do, you may wind up having to handle batches of pictures at a time (100 or 200 pictures at a time) until you've managed to download all of them to your computer.

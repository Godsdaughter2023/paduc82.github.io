You get one menu when you have one instance of Excel running and you are pasting.  These would be the regular and familiar paste special options.

 

You get the other menu when you have two instances of Excel running.  These would be the alternative paste special options.........Unicode Text and Text etc.

Several ideas:
1) Format first series as desired. Hit the up/down arrow, then F4. Repeat 99 more times.

Sub AllSeriesFormat()
Application.ScreenUpdating = False
'Loops through each series
For i = 1 To ActiveChart.SeriesCollection.Count
ActiveChart.SeriesCollection(i).Select
'Changes the format of series
With Selection
.Border.ColorIndex = 49 'xxx Line color
.MarkerBackgroundColorIndex = 53 'xxx
.MarkerForegroundColorIndex = 14 'xxx
.MarkerStyle = xlStar 'xxx
.MarkerSize = 5 'xxx
End With
Next i
Application.ScreenUpdating = True
End Sub

I've watched this myself and the audio worked fine, so I know it's not a content issue.

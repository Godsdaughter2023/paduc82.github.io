That D Drive is the Manufacturers Factory Recovery Image partition on your new PC.

You are not meant to access or even see that partition, that is a known bug in Windows 10 . .

Click your Start Button, type cmd, then right click Command Prompt and choose 'Run as Administrator'

diskpart

list volume

select volume D

remove letter=D

Close Command Prompt

That's It!

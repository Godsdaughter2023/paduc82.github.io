rapidscout say:

So I finally resolved the issued. date+time was the culprit, though not in the way I thought it would be.

 

To fix:

1. Go to settings>date+time

2. Turn off "Set date and time automatically"

3. Check your time zone - mine was blank.

4. Change your time zone to another zone. (didn't seem to matter which)

5. Back out of time zone settings and see if time zone is no longer blank. (should be displaying the chosen time zone)

6. Go back to home screen

7. Go to settings>date+time

8. Turn on "set date and time automatically"

9. Restart phone

10. Make sure app updates are set to update automatically

11. Within about 15 minutes your first "pending" app should start to update

 

I'm guessing that after the update the phone either didn't pull the right time zone or was confused on it. I had manually set it during one of my first attempts to fix the issue, but apparently manually setting it to the same one that it was on didn't fix it. I'm guessing it needed to pull it from the cell tower. I noticed after daylight savings kicked in earlier that my time was always off by an hour. It seemed like the phone was on Central Time (I'm in Eastern Time Zone USA) but when I manually set it to Eastern, even though it showed it in time zone settings, when I backed into date+time it would show the time zone field blank. I set it to Central Time, backed out then reset to automatically pull and within 15 minutes my phone was working right again.

 

This fixed my "pending" app download issue, my time issue, my data sense issue, pretty much everything is working as it should again. I'll update if I notice anything else wrong. But for now this resolved my issue.

 

Thanks for all the help everyone!

Put following formula in B2, C2 and D2 and drag down (If you are looking at only maximum, then don't drag down)

B2: =IFERROR(INDEX($B$9:$B$16,MATCH(SUMPRODUCT(LARGE((($C$9:$C$16>=$E$7)*($D$9:$D$16)),ROWS($1:1))),$D$9:$D$16,0)),"")

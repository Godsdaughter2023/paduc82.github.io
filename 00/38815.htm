http://www.nirsoft.net/utils/driverview.html

100% SURE FIX - INTERMITTENT WIRELESS DISCONNECTIONS

7. Locate the driver that you have downloaded and extracted

8. Windows will do the rest and this will fix the random wireless disconnections.

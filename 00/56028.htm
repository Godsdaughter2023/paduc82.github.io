joshh. say:

Well, this is embarrassing. Soon after posting about my issue, I found something else to try. I uninstalled my wireless adapter (Ralink RT5390R 802.11bgn), and rebooted. Once logged in, it had reinstalled the adapter, and it immediately allowed me to input my Wi-Fi password and access my wireless connection. 

So, for anybody with this issue, or a similar one, try this:

- Right click the Windows/Start Menu button

- Choose Device Manager

- Navigate to Network Adapters

- Find your Wireless Adapter

- Right click, and choose Uninstall (Do NOT click the "Delete Driver Software" check box!)

- Click Ok

- Reboot

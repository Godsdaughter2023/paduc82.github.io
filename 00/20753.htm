9.    After the alignment click on update labels.

10. Click on next preview labels.

11. Complete the merge.

12. Click on edit individual labels to select all the labels.

13. Now click on print.

Such is my scepticism that I am not at this stage prepared to tolerate the moderate inconvenience of a hard reset for an unlikely fix.

     

    I have noticed that:

     

    1) From an empty Inbox, the asterisk notification functions as it should. This can be achieved in 1 of two ways. Deleting all messages within the inbox (as I did). Or if your messages are stored on 'E:' (memory card), first remove the contact from the contact bar for which notifications are not working, within messaging (assuming no messages are stored on the phone) change message storage from E: to C: (phone). Then reinstate the contact & then return message storage to E:. The contacts bar will now show an empty message history for the contact even though messages are stored in the Inbox for that contact. Asterisk notification will now function for the contact in question (until, see 3).

     

    2) It has been suggested that merely removing & reinstating a contact on the contacts bar resolves the problem. In my experience it does not.

     

    3) **Notifications appear to work fine up until the 7th activity is logged for an individual contact. This is an interesting one as it would seem that the contacts bar log for an individual contact is capable of logging 6 activities (text/mms sent/received) but on receipt of a 7th message the asterisk notification no longer functions & messages only appear in the contacts log once it has been read from the Inbox.

     

    I can therefore only assume that there is some sort of bug when it comes to the 'handling of logged content' for a given contact once the log becomes full (after the 6th activity). It is at this point that the log should begin to start rolling/replacing logged content to show that which is most recent.

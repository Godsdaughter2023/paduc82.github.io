bob jones aka: cybertaz say:

Assuming you mean an x with overline [x̅]:

  1. Type the x then go to Insert> Symbol
  2. In the Character Viewer select Unicode from the left list [You may have to click the ✲ to Customize the List]
  3. Select Combining Diacritical Marks in the top middle pane
  4. Locate & double-click the Overline [U-0305] in the lower middle pane
  5. Close the Character Viewer

If you need to insert this frequently you may want to select the resulting character & create an AutoCorrect item so you don't have to keep repeating the process.

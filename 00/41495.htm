shenan stanley say:

You've installed software that has no uninstall facility (either through the "Programs and Features" "Control Panel" or through a removal process unique to the software itself?)

If you know the folder and its contents were put there by something that is either already been uninstalled using the manufacturer's suggested method and/or you manually removed the application in some other way and you don't need the stuff - delete away.

I would say the danger only comes in deleting the completely unknown.  If you do not *know* what created the folder and you do not *know* what uses it (if anything) - then you are taking a chance at randomly deleting such things.

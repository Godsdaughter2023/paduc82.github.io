rgharper911 say:

The best optimization for the pagefile (virtual memory swapfile) on your computer is to add enough memory to the computer that the swapfile is no longer needed.  Most programs will allocate a small amount of virtual memory when they start to ensure it's available if necessary, fiddling with that process usually causes crashes and difficult-to-diagnose problems.

Hi

The version of Skype app way back 2012 was the Skype Classic and that time, Skype Cloud was not launched yet to Skype users. All chat / conversation history are stored on your local device. The reason why the conversation did not appear on the new laptop bought by your husband is that the messages are saved in the old laptop. Same thing happened when you used a device from an internet café, your conversation is saved on the cafe's devices.

Last year, Skype cloud was fully launched. This means that your conversation will be stored in the cloud for two years. I apologize that it is not possible to retrieve your old messages as they are not stored on the cloud.

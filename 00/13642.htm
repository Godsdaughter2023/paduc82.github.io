Please follow the steps below to troubleshoot the issue:

1. Quit Excel.

2. Open Finder > Go > Go to Finder menu item, enter ~/Library/Containers/ and click Go. You will see all of the various “Containers” used on your Mac.

3. Go to com.microsoft.Excel folder, find the “Saved Application State” folder for Excel.( You will find it inside the Excel’s “Container” folder (above) → Data → Library → Saved Application State.)

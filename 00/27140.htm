I copied some mortgage data from fo banks web site to an Excel worksheet.  Most of the nimbers came in as a General format.   I can't figure out how to convert them to numbers.  Text to numbers - yes I can - but these came in as a General format and I need them as numbers.
joeu2004 say: Matthew wrote:
No luck - I cannot format them - so as you say they must be text - but even though error checking options are all set correctly - I do not have any error indicator in any of the cells so I cannot convert from text to numbers.

Your numbers probably have some sort of spaces around them.

First, try selecting a column of cells and use the Text To Column feature to convert in place.

If that does not work, the cells probably contain non-breaking spaces (HTML &nbsp), which is common when you copy-and-paste from web pages.

In that case, in a parallel column, put the following formula and copy down the column, assuming your data is in column A:

=--TRIM(SUBSTITUTE(A1,CHAR(160),""))

Then copy the new column and use Paste Special Value and Number Formats to paste over the original data.  You can delete the new column.

For what it's worth, I've found an acceptable (to me) workaround for this problem.

Close all instances of IE.  Open your "shortcut" to a specific site (other than your homepage).

Manually resize the IE window to cover the Full Screen.  Do this with the mouse, clicking and dragging the sides and corners to the size you want.  Use the formal exit command for good measure (Alt F, X).  Windows will remember the new size of this shortcut.

This will enable you to (set) open links in a new tab, instead of a new instance.

Frankly you would be better off with content controls. That way you could set a different first page header (which would be empty) and put your first page header content controls in the body of the first page and map them to the appropriate header of the subsequent pages. Then whatever you enter in any of the linked controls would be reproduced in the others and no macros are required.

http://www.gmayor.com/BookmarkandVariableEditor.htm provides a simple means of mapping content controls, and if you want to get more adventurous with content controls, Greg Maxey has a more complex version linked from the top of that page.

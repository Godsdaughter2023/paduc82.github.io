The error is trying to tell you that your table definition/field definitions require data, possibly of a certain type/format/size/..., and that you are missing that in your entry.

 

The error has nothing to do with having 20 fields in your statement.

 

You need to open your zip Codes table in design mode and review each field to understand which fields are required, what type of data each field is expecting...  But basically, you are not respecting the established table/field rules.

a. user say:

How to turn on microphone?


WSR, Windows Speech Recognition, requires a good microphone and a sound card that does not introduce electronic noise into the audio stream.

Confirm the microphone is working by making a recording in Windows Sound Recorder and playing it back. Go to Start, All Programs, Accessories to find Sound Recorder. Also, right click the speaker icon in the lower right Taskbar, then click Recording Devices. Make sure the sound card or USB audio device is the Default. You may have to go to Properties to increase the Levels.

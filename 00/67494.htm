rose cal say:

We appreciate your response. There are 4 methods on how you can adjust the screen brightness in Windows 10. To address your issue, we suggest that you follow these methods below which you're not done yet:

Method 1: Adjust screen brightness using hotkeys:

  1. Press Fn + F5/F6 can be used to adjust the screen brightness.
  2. Look at your keyboard to see brightness symbol on Function keys.

Method 2: Use Action Center to display brightness in Windows 10

  1. Click on the Action Center icon in the taskbar to reveal the Action Center pane.
  2. Click on the brightness tile to increase/reduce the display brightness. If you can’t see the brightness tile, click Expand button.
  3. Clicking on the brightness tile once will take the brightness to the next level. For instance, if you click on the tile when the brightness is at 25%, the brightness will be increased to 50%. Once the brightness is reaches 100%, click on the tile again to start from 0%.

Method 3: Use settings to adjust screen brightness in Windows 10

  1. Open Settings by clicking on its icon in the left side of the Start menu.
  2. At the home page of Settings, click System.
  3. You should now see Adjust brightness level option with a slider to adjust the display brightness. If you can’t see the option, click on Display (Settings > System > Display).

Method 4: Use Control Panel to adjust brightness in Windows 10

  1. Right-click on the battery icon in system tray of taskbar and then click Adjust screen brightness option to open Power Options window.
  2. At the bottom of the screen, you should see Screen brightness option with a slider. Move the slider towards the left of the screen to reduce the screen brightness and towards to the right to increase the brightness.

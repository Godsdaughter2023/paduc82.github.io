vanderghast say:

You are using a query? bring as many times the referenced table as you have fields, and JOIN each field to its OWN reference. As example, if you have a graph where the table Arcs represents the possible flight path between two nodes:

 

Arcs
ArcID,  FromNodeID, ToNodeID
1010            1                   2

 

and if Nodes is a table whcih can translate the nodeID into proper city names:

 

Nodes
NodeID  CityName
1             New York
2             Los Angeles

 

then in a query, bring Arcs once, and Nodes TWICE (one will get an _1 append to it, by default). JOIN FromNodeID to Nodes.NodeID  and JOIN ToNodeID to Nodes_1.NodeID.

The ribbon has never been movable in Word. In that respect, it is far less flexible than the toolbars in Word 2003 and older versions.

What you can do is minimize the ribbon tabs, so that you only see "File," "Insert," etc. when you are typing in the document. At the top right of the Word window, click the icon with the arrow and then choose the Show Tabs option.

I've added a video on my Sway, i want my video to play when my Sway is on autoloop. Is that possible? How do I make this happen? 

THanks,

jason (office engineer) say:

Video cards in Sway don't support autoplay.  However, if you use an embed card, the functionality will depend upon what you've embedded.  For instance, if you embed a YouTube video, that embed card will get converted into a video card automatically.  If you press undo when that happens, it will remain an embed card.  Since YouTube supports autoplay by including certain parameters in the embed URL, it will then work.  The same goes for any other embed source that supports autoplay.

Hope this helps!

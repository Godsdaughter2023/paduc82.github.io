Your formula requires a lookup in a table in a file that is not open.
When the formula needs to be recalculated Excel either needs a local copy of that table held within your workbook or it has to effectively open the workbook behind the scenes in order to determine the content of the table and evaluate the formula.  With such a large table Excel might decide not to bother.

Unless you're using a document template (such as in Publishing Layout View) Word documents don't have any "white box of pixels" for a picture to go behind unless you create one :-)

It sounds like what may be happening is that you do have a Text Box or Shape with white fill at the spot where the image is being inserted and that the image is being inserted as In Line with Text.

jon garnett-smith say:
Hi Robert,

Ok, so I have an Android and a Mac too, so I have experimented with trying to solve this problem myself and have found a solution that works for me. Please let me know if it works for you too.

Removing the autocomplete entry from Office 365:
1) Log into your Outlook account on Office 365 by visiting http://outlook.office.com
2) Start a new message, then begin typing the email address that you want to remove
3) When the desired email address appears, click on the X at the right hand side. You may need to use the arrow keys to select the address first in order for the X to appear.

Removing the autocomplete entry from Outlook for Android:
1) Open the Outlook for Android app
2) Click on the menu option in the top left (three lines)
3) Click on the Settings option in the bottom left (cogs)
4) Click on the account for which you have this autocomplete error Note: You may need to do this process on multiple accounts if the autocomplete error exists for more than one account
5) Click on RESET ACCOUNT. Don't worry, this is not resetting anything n terms of emails or important settings. It is just forcing the account to e-sync with Office 365.

Now check whether the autocomplete entry still exists when you start tping an email.

Let me know if that helps,
Jon

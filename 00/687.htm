jay freedman say:

"Properties" is the wrong button. You have two choices:

- Use the Customize Quick Access Toolbar dialog, select the All Commands category and add the Lock command to the toolbar. That button toggles forms locking on and off, just like the lock icon on the Forms toolbar of previous versions.

- Click the Protect Document button (on either the Developer tab or the Review tab) and choose Restrict Formatting and Editing to open the pane. Check the box under Editing Restrictions, and set the dropdown to Filling In Forms.

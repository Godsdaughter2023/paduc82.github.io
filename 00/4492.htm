Just copy and paste...

A macro usually begins with "Sub" and ends with "End Sub", e.g.:

Sub Macro1()
  'Whatever in here

Copy all this lines thereunder and rename the macro, e.g.:

Sub MyNewMacro1()
  'Whatever in here
End Sub

Andreas.

Select the entire macro from Sub macroname() up to and including End Sub.

Press Ctrl+C to copy the text.

Select Insert > Module to create a new module.

Press Ctrl+V to paste the text.

Change macroname to a different name.

Click your Start Button, type winver and hit Enter

Here is a registry fix, if you do not have that option . . .

Click your Start Button, type regedit and hit Enter to open the Registry Editor

In the left pane, navigate to:

HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Control Panel\Accessibility

In the right pane, double click MessageDuration

Set the Base to Decimal

Set the value date to the amount of seconds you want the notifications to last

Save that, close the registry Editor and restart your PC

a. user say:

Hi Séaghdha,

 

Welcome to Microsoft Community. We understand that some of your emails in your Inbox or other folders are ending up in your Deleted folder. In this case, there is a possibility that you have set a Scheduled Cleanup on your Microsoft account. In this case, it best that you check your Email Rules for it may be causing the issue. To do so, please refer to the steps below:

 

  1. Sign in to your Microsoft account.
  2. Navigate to your Inbox page.
  3. Click the cogwheel icon on the upper right corner of the page, then select More mail settings.
  4. Click Rules for sorting new messages under Customizing Outlook.
  5. See to it that there are no rules pertaining the email moving to your Deleted folder. (Note: You might as well delete all your rules then recreate them again).

In addition, if the Email Rule is set to move the emails to your Junk folder, please be reminded that all emails in the Junk folder will be deleted after ten days.

Please return back if the issue has been fixed. If not, we will further assist in you resolving the issue.

 

 

Thanks!

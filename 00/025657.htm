In the main window after Word 2013 starts up is the template view.

This brought up three (3) options for me.

 

- OneNote Linked Notes Add Ins (checked)

- OneNote Notes about Word Documents (unchecked)

- Send to Bluetooth (checked)

 

I unchecked the 'Send to Bluetooth' box and clicked Ok.

 

No more crashes.

 

Fixed.

scott .. b say:

Microsoft Word for Office 2003 and previous versions of Microsoft Word created documents in the .doc format. This changed at the premiere of the Microsoft Office 2007 version of Microsoft Word, where the native document format changed from .doc to .docx. Internet Explorer 11 will associate .doc with the native document format and try to open it as a .docx document.

While Microsoft Office 2007 can still open and save documents in the .doc format, they are not compatible with newer versions of Microsoft Word and thus must be changed or converted before they can be opened in Microsoft 2013 or later versions.  Have you tried opening the document from within Word 2013 (as opposed to double-clicking it in File Explorer)

Upgrade question about Office 365 Business Premium - Microsoft Community

davidkampert say:

Upgrade question about Office 365 Business Premium

Do I need to uninstall Office 365 Home version before upgrading to Office 365 Business Premium version?
vincentchoy say:

My apologies. 

O365 home and O365 Biz Premium are two radically different bundles. 

As a bundle it has many products. So it is not just a matter of removing one and installing the other.

Office365 Home is basically MS-Office (for home), Outlook.com consumer email, One Drive consumer 1TB storage, and Skype consumer giving you 60mins of credit a month

Office365 Biz Premium is different entirely. It has a different version of MS-Office (e.g. no publisher), it gives you a enterprise email account (based on Exchange Online), One Drive for Business, Skype for Business, Yammer, Teams, Planner.

So what you would need to do is

1. backup your emails using Outlook to a pst file. Also you may need to download your One Drive files as well.

2. Un-install your MS-Office (for home).

3. Setup your Office365 Biz Premium tenant(if not already done so by the company you work for)

4. Download and install MS-Office (for biz premium)

5. Download and install the other clients - One Drive 4 Biz Sync, Skype for Business, Yammer and Teams clients.

The setup for O365 biz premium is not straight forward if you are not technically inclined, as it involves domain name verification, DNS changes and MX record repointing, mail service configuration, mobile services configuration etc...

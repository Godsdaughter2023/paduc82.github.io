resourceful say: Hello. You are allowed two partnershps with Activesync/WMDC so that is no problem. I have 2 PCs syncing with my device. The sync is always two-way, you change something on the PC and it syncs to the device. You change something on the device, it syncs to the PC. The conflict (you change an item on the PC and device after the last sync) is where you can say to sync the PC info or device info. That is somewhat directional but you can't change it on the fly and you have to remember how you have it set. I try to avoid the conflict by making all my changes on the phone. You can definitely choose your sync options. You can only sync email for one partnership. Those are basically the ground rules.

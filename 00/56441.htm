xbf crystal p say:

Hi there Jamian,

Thanks for the reply! The Xbox console is trying to designate the connection through your network to the Xbox Server. The NAT type determines whether you can successfully use multiplayer gaming or party chat.

To let your Xbox One console communicate with Xbox Live, you might have to open or forward ports, which means you'll be making a configuration change to your firewall or network hardware, such as a router. I would recommend to change the configuration.  Port forwarding for Xbox One is done on your router or other network hardware, not on the Xbox console itself.

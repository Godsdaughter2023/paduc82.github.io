Default Font - Microsoft Community

Default Font

How do change the defaults in Word so that becomes the default for every new document?

jay freedman say:

In two steps:

  1. Click the little arrow in the bottom right corner of the Styles group to open the Styles pane. Then click the Manage Styles button (third from the left at the bottom of the pane). Click the Set Defaults tab of the dialog. Select the font, size, and paragraph settings you want for the default, and click the "New documents based on this template" option. Click OK.
  2. In the Styles pane, right-click the Normal style and set it to the same formatting as you chose in the Manage Styles dialog. Click the "New documents based on this template" option. Click OK.

When you close Word, if you're prompted to save changes to the Normal.dotm template, click Yes.

Hello catrinka1000,

Thank you for posting in the forum!

To delete content from your Zune device follow this steps:

1.  Start the Zune software and then connect your Zune player or phone to the computer. 
2.  In the destination selection, click device and then select music, videos, pictures, podcasts, friends, channels, or apps, as appropriate. 
3.  Locate and then right-click the file(s) that you want to delete. Then, click Delete from <device name> . 
4.  Click OK to confirm the deletion. 
5.  Disconnect your Zune player from the computer when the synchronization is complete. 

 

If you only want to prevent a file from syncing to the Zune player, please take a look into the following steps :
1.  Start the Zune software, and then connect your Zune player to the computer. 
2.  In the destination section of the Zune software, click collection , and then select music, videos, pictures, podcasts, channels, or apps, as appropriate. 
3.  Locate the file that you want to prevent from syncing to the device. 
4.  Right-click the file(s) that you want to prevent from syncing, and then click Never sync with <device name> . 
5.  Disconnect your Zune player from the computer when synchronization is complete. You can reverse this setting by locating the media in your collection, right-clicking the item, and then clicking Sync with <device name> .

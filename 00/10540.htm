teylyn say:
Hello,

some alternatives to the suggestion from Max:

=--(RIGHT(A1,4))

=Right(A1,4)*1

Also, you can use the mathematical operators on the text values without coercing them first. So if

B1 =Right(A1,4)
B2 =Right(A2,4)

you can use =B1+B2 and it will produce the correct result, but =Sum(B1:B2) will not because sum cannot handle text values.

neoncherry say:

There are lots of legitimate reasons why you'd hear a beep in the background during a call:

 

-Call diversion is switched on (for example it might be telling you it is diverting calls to your voicemail while you're talking to someone else)

-A new text message or e-mail has arrived

-A text message has been delivered (if you have delivery reports switched on)

-Clock or calendar alarm

-Battery low 

 

You can alter the alarm beeps from the Settings: Personal: Profiles: General (or whatever you use): Personalise: Warning Tones.

Without telling us what brand and model of webcam you have, or describing exactly how it's "not working," the best anyone can do is an educated guess.

Having an updated driver is not sufficient. For hardware to work on Windows 10, it needs to have a driver that is compatible with Windows 10.

If the manufacturer of your webcam is supporting the webcam on Windows 10, they will have a Windows 10-compatible driver. If the manufacturer is not supporting that webcam on Windows 10, you're on your own. There isn't anything Microsoft can do about hardware it didn't build.

In that case, go to the community forum for that webcam and ask if anyone who uses your model has had success with Windows 10. If so, ask them what driver they are using and for any other advice to get your webcam to work properly.

If no one else has had success with your webcam on Windows 10, it's time to buy a new webcam.

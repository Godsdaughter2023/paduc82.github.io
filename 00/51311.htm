mike hall... always say:

An analogue modem DIALS out like a regular land line telephone does, and in the old days was used to connect to DIAL UP Internet services.

A Data Fax Modem does exactly the same as the above, and it also has the ability to send and receive faxes. It is not an 'operating system dependent' device. It is dependent on a computer having a data fax modem included in the configuration. So, your XP machine may well have had an RJ11 port for connection to a telephone line, but it is doubtful that your Windows 8 computer has one..

What to get.. see here..

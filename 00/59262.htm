1. Press “Windows Key + W” from the keyboard.

2. Type “device Manager” without quotes in the search box and hit enter.

5. Now, without exiting out of any of the windows you just opened, restart your computer. (If you're using a USB mouse, right click on the windows button in the bottom left of your screen, click "Shut Down or Sign Out", then click restart.

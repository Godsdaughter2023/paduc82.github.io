The bottom right corner or the screen on a Windows 10 PC is the "Show Desktop" button. When you hover the mouse there, all windows become transparent. If you click it, it will minimize all windows.

Disable this in one of two ways: The first is to right-click the Show Desktop button at the far right end of the task bar and uncheck both "Show Desktop" and "Peek at desktop". The other is to right-click the Taskbar and choose "Taskbar Settings".. Turn off the switch for "Use Peek to preview the desktop when you move your mouse to the Show desktop button at the end of the taskbar". Either of these will prevent the windows from becoming transparent when you move the mouse to that corner. However, I don't believe if will disable Show Desktop of you actually click in the bottom right corner. If the mouse button is already depressed when you move the mouse there, however, it shouldn't activate Show Desktop.

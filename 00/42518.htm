sc tom say:

It depends on the resolution on the downloaded video and the native resolution of your monitor. If the movies are 320x240 or 640x480 (or similar smaller size), and your monitor is 1600x900 or similar larger size, then the full screen video is going to be blurry, no matter what you do. It's the same thing as taking a wallet-sized picture and blowing it up to 8x10. The pixels are stretched to the point of not being smooth any more.

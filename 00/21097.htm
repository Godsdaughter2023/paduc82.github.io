ron sommer mvp 2009-2017 say:

The part of an email address after the @ sign is called the domain.  The domain determines what server receives emails sent to that email address.  The part before the @ sign determines what account is used on the server.

You have a business email address that is ....... @mybusinessdomain.com.  Any emails will go to the mybusinessdomain.com server.

You created an outlook.com account using the @mybusinessdomain.com address.  The only way to get emails to that outlook.com account is to add an @outlook.com alias.  Forward the emails to the @outlook.com alias address.

Your @mybusinessdomain.com address is setup as Send only so you can send emails from the outlook.com account.

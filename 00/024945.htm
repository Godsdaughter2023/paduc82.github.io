Issues with synchronising SMS messages on Lumia phones

Hi All,

I'm hoping to get some advice on some difficulty I am having with sync'ing SMS messages and Lumia phones (apologies in advance for the length of this but I think it's better to give all the facts!)

The screen recently broke on my Lumia 925 so I purchased a Lumia 950 to replace it.  Started the setup and chose to restore most recent backup (which was from a few days before the 925 broke).  This finished and all the apps eventually installed after multiple restarts and messing around (I was happy to accept that there may be a few issues going from 8.1 to 10)

I then waited for my SMS messages to download.  They started doing so, and then stopped.  I found several people online saying the same thing had happened to them but that eventually they all came through.  Not so for me, multiple reboots and several weeks later I am still missing thousands of messages from 2013 onwards...

As a test, I hard reset a spare Lumia 535 we had at work and chose not to restore any backup.  I then added my live.com account and all my SMS messages downloaded fine EXCEPT for ones I had sent/received since I set up the 950!  So, it seems that at that point, not only did the old messages fail to download to the 950 but also anything new I am sending or receiving from it is not being synchronised with my live.com account either.

What I have done is to install contacts+messaging backup onto the 950 and the 535 and run a backup on both (doing so proved there was an issue as the 950 backed up 2,485 messages and the 535 backed up 8,835!) and copied these two files to my computer.

I now intend to copy what I need off the 950 (e.g. photos etc) and then hard reset it and set it up again in the hope that all the messages download this time (as they seem to have done on the 535).  If that is successful, my question then is - if I reinstall the contacts+messaging app and import the back up from the 950 (taken just before I reset it) - should this merge with the messages that have been downloaded and synchronise my recently sent messages with live.com or will I be left with thousands of duplicates from around 2013 (the point at which it stopped downloading) or some other bizarre scenario?

All I want is for all my text messages to appear on the new phone and to know that if it is lost or stolen I can download them all again onto another one!  If anyone is able to offer me any advice on what would happen if I attempt the above or suggest a better method to achieve what I want I’d greatly appreciate it.

Thanks very much for your time.

Hello SB_1982,

Thank you for posting on Microsoft Community.

We understand you really want to get your SMS messages. As for your question, it will create a duplicate of your old back ups.

With regards to your concern about syncing your SMS messages, you may follow the steps below:

  1. Reset your phone.
  2. Set up your live account again and choose not to restore.
  3. Check for updates with the phone, update if there is any.
  4. Go to settings and the restore back up.

Hope this helps.

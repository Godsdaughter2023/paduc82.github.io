a. user say:

Hello,

If an item has been accidentally deleted in Outlook.com or Outlook on the web, you may often recover it if it is still in your Deleted Items folder, and in some cases, you can recover items after the Deleted Items folder is emptied. If the item is still in the Deleted Items folder, you may recover it by following the steps below:

  1. In the left pane of the Outlook.com window, select Deleted Items.
  2. To restore an item, right-click the item and then select Move > Inbox.
  3. To restore a message to a different folder, right-click the item, and select Move > More. Select a folder location, and then select Move.

If the item is no longer in your Deleted Items folder, the next place to look for is the Recoverable Items folder. Here's how to recover items from the Recoverable Items folder:

  1. In the left pane of the Outlook.com window, select Deleted Items.
  2. At the top of your message list, select Recover deleted items.
  3. Select the items you want to recover, and select Recover.
  4. Select OK.

Note: Items removed from your Deleted Items folder are recoverable for 30 days. If you don't see the items that you want to recover, they have probably been permanently removed from the Deleted Items folder or Junk Email folder.

If you have further questions, feel free to ask us.

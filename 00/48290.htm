a. user say:

 Hi Jim,
Thank you posting the query on Microsoft Community.

From the description, it seems that you would like to know if you have entire 500 GB HDD for use.

When you open Windows Explorer it shows a 250 GB HDD installed. This means that you have formatted 250 GB for the C: drive.
When you are on Disk Management you see the entire space as 500 GB HDD.
Do you see 250 GB allotted to the C: drive and 250 GB as raw space which is not formatted?
This means that you have 250 GB in use for the C: drive and the remaining 250 GB is a raw space.
Hence, you do have the 500 GB HDD in your computer.
Hope this answers your question.

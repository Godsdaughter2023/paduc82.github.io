If; as it seems, the dates in Col B are in ascending order then you can do this:-

=LOOKUP(2,1/(userlog!$A$1:$A$17782=A3),userlog!$B$1:$B$17782)

If they aren't sorted use this ARRAY formula

=MAX(IF(userlog!$A$1:$A$17782=A3,userlog!$B$1:$B$17782))

yangzheng_msft6300 say:

Hi Ruben,

I understand that you want a set the Out of Office for all the members in your Office 365 organization at one time. There is now no such related option in Exchange Admin Center. However, there is a PowerShell command for an admin to achieve this. Please follow the steps below to do this:
1. Connect to Exchange Online with PowerShell.
2. Run the following command.
Get-Mailbox | Set-MailboxAutoReplyConfiguration -AutoReplyState Enabled -ExternalMessage "I am out of office" -InternalMessage "I am out of office"
Note: The ExternalMessage and InternalMessage are the detailed messages in the Out of Office. You can change them to what you want.

If you meet any error when running this PowerShell command, please post back with the error message.

Regards,
Yang

Hi Ruben,

After running this command, Out of Office will be enabled for everyone in your organization. It is not required to do this for each mailbox. Get-Mailbox | Set-MailboxAutoReplyConfiguration -AutoReplyState Enabled -ExternalMessage "I am out of office" -InternalMessage "I am out of office" –StartTime “10/1/2015 08:00:00” –EndTime “10/5/2015 17:00:00”

The option to automatically change the shape of the legend markers is not yet available in Office 2016. But if you really want to change the shape of the legend, we can do that manually by following this workaround:

5. Copy the shape and paste it, and then drag it again beside the legend.

6. If you want to change the color, click on the shape then click Shape fill.

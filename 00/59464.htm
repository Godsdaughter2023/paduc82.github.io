It's actually common practice to temporarily disable antivirus programs before installing software. Also before installing Windows Updates. AV software can sometimes interfere with the installer (by misinterpreting its actions as an attack by malware.)

Every antivirus program has its own way of being temporarily paused. You can find out how to temporarily disable yours in one of 4 ways:

  • from the Help file;
  • from the User Manual;
  • by asking the AV program's technical support; or
  • by asking your ISP.

Typically, your right-click on the icon in the notification area and make a selection from the menu.

Very important: Don't forget to re-enable your AV software after the installation has completed.

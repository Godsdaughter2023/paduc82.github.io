http://h30434.www3.hp.com/t5/Notebook-Operating-System-and-Recovery/Recovery-after-potential-trojan-on-recovery-partition/m-p/5487655/highlight/true

It’s really not that complicated, my friend – I’m not arguing against clean installs, recovery backups, or even removing the partition. After adding a domain in Windows Live Admin Center, put the correct MX record in Windows Live Admin Center. To do this, please follow these steps:

1. Get the MX record from your Windows Live Admin Center:

a. Go to http://domains.live.com and sign in with your administrator account.

a. Go to the registrar where you have purchased your domain.

a. Go to http://domains.live.com and sign in to your account.    

b. Click on the domain you would like to manage.

c. Click on the “Domain Settings” link at the left side of the page.

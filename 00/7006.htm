1. Do a factory reset. With the phone turned off press and hold the Volume DOWN key and then connect the charger. An exclamation mark should now appear. Now press the following buttons in the exact order: Volume UP, Volume DOWN, POWER then Volume DOWN. Step one: allow battery to die completely.
Step two: remove sim.
Step three: insert charging cable
Step four: while Nokia logo is displayed press VOL UP, VOL DOWN, POWER, VOL UP.
Step five: hold power, vol down and camera button until the phone resets.
Step six: the phone should go black, screen on, but nothing displayed. HOLD VOL DOWN AND POWER.

It should now reset and load up into your welcome screen.

As I said, method 1 worked. The battery was so far discharged, the phone simply shut off after the reset. An hour or so later, I quit looking at it and jumped in the shower. When I came back, it was sitting at the "Welcome" screen! I let it download the last backup and restore my apps.

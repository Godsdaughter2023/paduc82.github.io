roland hall ith say: Hopefully you got it worked out but for others, I will provide what I found to work.

1. Apple says you can use pxx-imap.mail.me.com (incoming) and pxx-smtp.mail.me.com (outgoing). xx = 01-99
2. Incoming port: 995 Encryption: SSL
3. Outgoing port: 587 Encryption: TTL

Now, if iCloud is installed, you CANNOT select the Outbox folder in Outlook or mail will not send. If you do, just close Outlook and reopen it. You can either wait for the scheduled Send/Receive, click Send/Receive and then click Send/Receive All or press F9.

Members

------------------

MemberID

MemberName

etc

Family

------------------

FamilyID

MemberID

FirstName

LastName

Status

Gender

etc etc

Members_Family

Member_FamilyID

MemberID

FamilyID

Relationship

You'd add Members to the Member table, and then add Family to the MemberFamily Table, and "relate" them in the Members_Family table.

Hello,

Keeping the connectors clean may prevent some issue to occur in your Surface Book. Kindly follow the steps below to clean the connectors properly. 

  1. Detach the Clipboard from the keyboard. 
  2. Wet a cotton swab with rubbing alcohol. (Use 95% isopropyl alcohol or higher if possible.)  
  3. Clean the silver pins on both faces of the three connectors located on the top middle surface of the keyboard hinge.  
  4. Rub the cotton swab back and forth vigorously to clean the pins. Use a fresh swab to clean each of the connectors. 
  5. Make sure the pins are dry and that there are no cotton fibers on the connector before reattaching the Clipboard to the keyboard. 

We also suggest that you install all the available updates on your Surface Book. In some cases, updating your device resolves the issue.

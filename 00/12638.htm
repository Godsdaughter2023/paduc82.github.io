A couple of alternatives...

=SUMPRODUCT(SUMIF(A4:A682,D11:D14,B4:B682))

Array entered version**:

=SUM(SUMIF(A4:A682,D11:D14,B4:B682))

=SUM(LOOKUP(D11:D14,A4:B682))

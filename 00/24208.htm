michael bauer (vboffice.net) say:
Either move manually the emails to the new file, or use the AutoArchive settings. For the first method click on a folder, switch to a list view, and sort the data descending, for instance by the ReceivedTime field. Then select all the items no want to move, and drag them with the mouse to the new folder. For the latter method right click on a folder, choose Properties, AutoArchive. There see which method serves you best.

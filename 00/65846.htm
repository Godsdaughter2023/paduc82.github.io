Skype paid features such as a Skype Number, as well as subscriptions, cannot be shared between accounts.

Can I transfer my current VoIP number? to be a Skype number?

Unfortunately, Skype does not have available what is called in the telephone industry as inbound number portability.  Therefore, we cannot transfer into Skype numbers from other carriers regardless of whether the numbers are landline, mobile, or other VoIP numbers.

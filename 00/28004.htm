Check if the following settings help:

  2. Enter your full name in Your Name field; Verizon email in Email Address field; and Verizon password in Password and Retype Password fields.
  3. Then, click Manually configure server settings or additional server types and Next.
  4. For Internet Email Settings, Your Name Field should be written how you want people to see your name.
  5. Email Address Field – type your full Verizon email; select POP3 for Account Type.

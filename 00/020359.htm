Disable synchronizing contacts Windows Live

How can I stop synchronizing my Windows Phone 7 contacts with Windows Live?

 

 

sara c. - microsoft support say:

Hi jan0610,

During setup of the phone you are asked to sign in with your Windows Live ID. This is considered the primary Windows Live ID, or phone account. Contact syncing for this account cannot be turned off. However you may have contacts that you do not want from this service on your phone. 

There are several options for managing and cleaning up contacts, just check how-to in the following link:

http://www.microsoft.com/windowsphone/en-us/howto/wp7/people/set-up-an-email-account.aspx

So as I said, contact syncing for the primary Windows Live ID cannot be turned off. For other services, contact syncing can be disabled. If you do not want to sync contacts for an account, you should:

1.On Start, flick left to the App list, tap Settings, and then tap Email & Accounts. 
2.Tap the account you want to change sync settings for. 
3.Under Content to sync, tap Contacts to remove the checkmark, and then tap done.

Note: This will stop syncing contacts for this account and the individuals will no longer show up in the contacts list in the People Hub. 

Hope this helps.

Going in to a Windows server, you may have better luck with SMB than AFP as your protocol.

However, the file that deadlocks can be "any one" of roughly 20 files Word holds open per document.

It would be so nice if we could get this data-loss bug cured.  But efforts to do so since 2001 have not succeeded.

There are two things that cross my mind about this.

When a user saves a Word document to a share location, Word creates a temporary copy of the document. Illegal characters such as slashes cause trouble even if they are in the name of the user's startup volume.

Hi Orion3k,

If you are using OneNote 2016, you can follow the steps below to move the notebook from personal OneDrive to OneDrive for business:

  1. From your personal OneDrive, open a notebook in OneNote 2016 client application.
  2. Go to File>Export, select Notebook>OneNotePackage (*.onepkg)>Export. Save the exported notebook on your Desktop.

  • Microsoft Office Activation Wizard Tech Support Scam

  • Windows has been blocked due to suspicious activity Tech Support Scam

  • Your Windows has been Banned Scam

Microsoft does not contact users via web page messages, phone or email and instruct them to call tech support to fix your computer.

Hi Mitchell,

Being cumulative wouldn't really matter...

Let's say the final (cumulative) value was 27.47  If your lookup value was either less than 0.58 (the Min from your list) or greater than 27.47 (the suggested Max), then the formula given in my last post would return an error, but there is no problem if you will always lookup within the range Min to Max.

However, if the lookup value could be outside this range then you could use:

 =IF(5<M10,L10,IF(5>M29,L29,IFERROR(INDEX($L10:M29,MATCH(5,M10:M29,0),2),INDEX($L10:M29,MATCH(5,M10:M29,1)+1,2))))

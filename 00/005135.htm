hansv mvp say:

What you want is impossible. A user-defined function cannot replace itself with a value. A user-defined function can only return a value that will be used in the cell's formula. Any other attempt to manipulate cells will fail.

 

As an alternative, you could write a procedure (sub) that either inserts a formula or a value in a cell, but such a procedure cannot be called in a user-defined function.

If you want to back up your data you must configure your account on a Mail Client. Follow the steps below to do this:

Windows Essentials

For more info about configuring your account

How do I add an account to Windows Live Mail

After doing this you have a copy of your emails on your hard drive that is synced with the outlook.com server.

a. user say:

Hi Ian,

For you to be able to move an existing Office installation to a new computer, all you need to do is to meet these 3 conditions:

  1. Have a license type that allows transfers, ie for consumers that is a Retail or FPP type license.
  2. You have your unique 25 character product key.
  3. A generic installation disk or file for the Office bundle that matches the product key.

If you want to know where to find your product key, you can check it from your email if you purchased it via an online store. The product key together with the email confirmation was sent through the account you used to purchase your Office. If you purchased it from a local store, then the product key is on the disk which the retailer has provided.

If you already have your product key, you need to uninstall your Office 2016 first from your old device so you can reinstall it on your new PC.

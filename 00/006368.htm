How can I set it up to automatically transfer my Outlook 2016 calendar to my outlook.com account? Without transferring mails? One or more times per day. I am using Win 10 and Android on the phone.

barry xie msft say:

Hi DonEgon,

 

It’s not feasible to automatically transfer the calendar events in your POP account to your Outlook.com account’s calendar. You can perform the following steps to manually transfer the calendar events:

1. Open Outlook client, navigate to calendar view, and only select your POP account’s calendar.

2. Click File > Save Calendar, Click on “More Options…” and choose the Date Range and Detail. I recommend that you set Detail as Full details.


3. Unselect your POP account’s calendar and select your Outlook.com’s calendar. Navigate to File > Open & Export > Import/Export > Import an iCalendar (.ics) or vCalendar file (.vcs) > select the calendar you just saved and choose Import.

4. Then you can see the calendar events in your POP account on your Android phone with Outlook.com account configured.

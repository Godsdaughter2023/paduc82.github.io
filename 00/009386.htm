Since you no longer have access to the email address associated with your account, your best option to recover it is by filling out the Account Recovery form.

Microsoft Word in Mac

I am attempting to convert footnotes to endnotes. These notes are in multiple chapters and each chapter is a new section so each footnote starts with 1.

On conversion to endnotes the numbers run onsecutively ignoring section chapter breaks. 

What do i need to do to maintain number formatting?

john korchok say:

Assuming each chapter has 1 section break separating it from the others:

  1. Click in each section of your document and choose Insert>Footnote...
  2. Under the Format section, change the Numbering dropdown from Continuous to Restart each section.

Changing the numbering between the 2 values can sometimes change the numbers displayed. If so, use the same dialog to set the Start at value to 1 for each section.

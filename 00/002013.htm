jay freedman say:
It isn't a bug, it's a side effect of a deliberate design decision that trailing spaces are normally treated as if they don't exist.

There are two possible solutions:

  • Replace the last trailing space with a nonbreaking space (Ctrl+Shift+spacebar).

  • Go to file > Options > Advanced, scroll to the bottom of the dialog and expand the Layout Options link, and check the box for "Draw underline on trailing spaces". (This may have an undesired side effect if you ever underline such a text instead of setting a box border.)

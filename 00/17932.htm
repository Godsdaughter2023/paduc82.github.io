hansv mvp say:

Let's say you have a time such as 09:47 in cell A1 (formatted as time).

In cell B1 (formatted as general), you enter a number such as 15.

In cell C1 (formatted as time), enter the formula =A1+TIME(0,B1,0)

The formula in C1 will add the number of minutes in B1 to the time in A1.

to overcome this, you can use: =A1+SIGN(B1)*TIME(0,ABS(B1),0)

or simply use: =A1+B1/1440

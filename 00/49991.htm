patrick m. martin say:

Racing Wheels

With Horizon 2 coming there will certainly be some new people in the market for racing wheels. On the Xbox One, there are currently two options. The Thrustmaster TX and the Mad Catz Pro Racing Force Feedback Wheel. Make sure you do your research before making a purchase.

From the reviews I've seen so far - the Thrustmaster is the far better option. It's also currently the cheaper option ($323 on Amazon, versus $399 for the Mad Catz wheel). The Thrustmaster is belt driven and very smooth. The Mad Catz is gear driven which some people have reported as making a loud grinding sound.  Another HUGE difference is that the Mad Catz wheel only supports 270 degrees of rotation and 900 degrees of rotation. The Thrustmaster wheel supports any degree of rotation between 180 and 900 by using a sliding scale.

If you haven't raced with a wheel before just know that it makes the game into an entirely different experience. Good luck to those of you in the market for a new wheel!! It's going to be great with Horizon 2!!!

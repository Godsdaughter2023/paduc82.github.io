SELECT Orders.OrderNumber, O1.OrderDate,
Seed.SeedType, OrderDtl.Quantity
FROM (Orders INNER JOIN OrderDtl

INNER JOIN Seed on Seed.SeedID = OrderDtl.SeedID

darleen pen say:

Hi Eddie,

Focused Inbox helps you focus on the emails that matter most to you. It separates your inbox into two tabs, Focused and Other. Since you've accidentally selected Always move to Other inbox, all of the emails that was sent by the sender of the email that you've selected were moved to Other inbox. To address your concern, you can go to Other inbox then right click the email, then select Always move to Focused inbox. This process is like undoing the changes that you made.

Once the emails are on Focused inbox, you can now choose the specific email that you want to move to Other inbox by selecting the email and choose Move once option.

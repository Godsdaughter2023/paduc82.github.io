Ok, I'm a little unsure what you have or have not done, so I'll describe how I play 360 games on my Xbox One and hopefully this will help you out.


I load up my Xbox One

Go to "My Games and App" (there is an icon on the dashboard)

Click on Xbox 360 Games (There should be a button at the top right labelled "Xbox One Games" click on it and one of the options will be Xbox 360 Games)

Then click on one of the games that you want to download


If its a game that you own on a disc, just put the disc into the Xbox One and it will download it onto the Xbox One.


That's it, now downloading of Xbox 360 profiles (your Xbox One profile is your Xbox 360 profile)

If it is MultiColor it's a system level issue.

Webcam in Word 2011 - Microsoft Community

andrewb13 say:

Webcam in Word 2011

How do I stop my webcam activating every time I enter Word on my mac? 
john mcghie say:

Word has no way to activate any kind of camera.

If you go into Notebook Layout View in Word, it offers you the option of taking Audio Notes.

If you click the "Record" button, Word activates the computer's microphone.  If you are using an external webcam, I guess it is conceivable that it's "Active" light might come on when the microphone is activated.  But Word has no way to connect to the camera.

If that is what is happening, go into Notebook Layout View and in Audio Notes click "Stop".

Then switch to Print Layout View.

If the light goes out, that was it.

For information on switching Views in Word, see the Word help topic "Views in Word".

Hope this helps

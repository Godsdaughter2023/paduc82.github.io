  1. When you say “to download my pics “, do you mean transfer the pictures from Note 4 to Windows 10 PC?

  2. If yes, did you try transferring the pictures to the PC form Note 4?

  3. Do you get any error message/error code on transferring the pictures from Note 4 to Windows 10 PC?

 

You will be able to transfer photos, videos, files and other data from the phone onto the computer using the data cable provided along with the mobile phone on purchase. The data cable also acts as an alternative charging chord when the mobile is connected to transfer the data to the computer.

 

Follow the steps below to transfer the photos and check if the issue is resolved.

 

  1. Connect the USB end of the data cable to the PC and attach your mobile phone to the other end of the chord.

  2. Wait for the device driver to get installed on the PC.

  3. After the driver of the device is installed, press Windows logo + E keys on the keyboard to open File explorer.

  4. Click on This PC form the left side panel of the window and check if the device is visible under Devices and drives.

  5. Double click on the visible mobile device and navigate to the folder in which the photos are located.

  6. Copy the photo files to transfer onto one of the system drives. To Copy the files, select the photos you want to transfer and press Ctrl + C keys on the keyboard.

  7. Press Windows logo + E keys on the keyboard to open another File explorer window, click on This PC and select the drive you to transfer the photo to.

  8. After navigating to the folder, press Ctrl + V keys on the keyboard to Paste the copied photo files.

  9. Wait for the photo files to be pasted in the selected folder and check if the all the copied files are transferred.

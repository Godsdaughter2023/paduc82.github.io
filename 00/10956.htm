ronaldoonenil say: As an example

AA1 to AA4 should be Food, Entertainment, Gas & Parking
AB1 to AB4 should be 12356, 22418, 33456 & 44556

A1 should be your data validation cell set to List with its source set to =AA1:AA4

B1 should be the formula =IFERROR(VLOOKUP(A1,$AA$1:$AB$4,2,0),"")

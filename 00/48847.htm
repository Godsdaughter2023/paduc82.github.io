a. user say:

My question: How do I restore my computer to the way it was before this update was automatically installed? 

There are two methods:

1- Restore an image backup that you made before the update was installed. This brings your computer back in time to exactly the way it was when the image was made. This method always works - even if your computer is completely useless.

2- Roll back to the pre-update state, if no more than 10 days have passed since your computer was updated. However, as a practical matter, the more you have used your computer since being updated, the less likely you are to have a successful rollback. Rolling back - unless you do it immediately after being updated - is not very reliable at best, and the longer you have used your computer since it was updated, the more that Windows has to try to 'undo.'

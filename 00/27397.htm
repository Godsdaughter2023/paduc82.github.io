coldwell shao msft say:

Hi Nvrpc,

According to my test, we can’t transfer contacts from outlook for windows to outlook for mac directly. However, to meet your goal, we can transport the contacts from outlook for windows to the Mac Contact application first, and then transfer the contacts from Mac Contact application to Outlook for Mac, the followings will show you the detailed steps to finish it:

Transport contacts from Outlook for windows to Mac Contact application:

  1. In Outlook for Windows, navigate to File> Open& Export> Import/Export
  2. Choose Export to a file, click Next
  3. Choose Comma Separated Values, click Next
  4. Then select the Contacts heading beneath of the account you want to export
  5. As the Wizard prompts, save the CSV file on your computer
  6. You can transfer the CSV file to your Mac via email or USB drive
  7. The last steps is opening the Mac Contacts application and click File> Import, and select the CSV file to import it to your Mac Contacts application

Transfer the contacts from Mac Contacts application to your Outlook for Mac:

  1. Select all the contacts (you can press Option key to select them) and drag and drop the contacts to your desktop of your Mac, the contacts will be created as .VCF file
  2. Then drag the .VCF file to your Outlook for Mac, you will see all the contacts are transfer to your Outlook for Mac successfully

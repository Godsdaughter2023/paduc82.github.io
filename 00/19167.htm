Prevent editing in parts of a shared Word document - Microsoft Community

aaron sweet say:

Prevent editing in parts of a shared Word document

Is there a way to prevent editing in parts of a shared Word document? For example, if I have a Word document that has a table. Can I lock the table and/or cells to prevent anyone else from editing it? I want other people to still be able to edit other parts of the document, just not parts that are already filled it or locked by another use.
jay freedman say:

You can do that. In typical Microsoft inverted logic, though, what you do is lock the entire document and then unlock the parts where you want to enable editing. The unlocked parts could be small pieces of a large document, or -- as in your case -- most of the document.

In the Restrict Editing pane, check the box under Editing restrictions, and keep the dropdown set to No changes (Read only). Next, select each part of the document that should enable editing, and check the Everyone box under Exceptions. Each part that's marked as an exception will be enclosed in a bookmark (shown a a thick gray square bracket if nonprinting characters are turned on).

When you start enforcing protection, the Restrict Editing pane shows buttons for finding the next region or all regions that are editable, and a check box for turning on or off highlighting of those regions.

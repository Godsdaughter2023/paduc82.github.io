scottgem say:

First, you don't have fields on a form. You have controls that may or may not be bound to fields in a table or query. This is a subtle but important distinction.

Second, as Marshall indicated, you may be working in Layout view. In Layout viewyou see data rather then the controls. This allows you to better align controls. Switching to Design view, however, may not alleviate the issue. The controls will still be grouped together. You have to remove that grouping in the Arrange tab when in Design view.

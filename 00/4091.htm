talking moose say:

Test your connection to your mail server outside of Outlook to determine whether the issue is with Outlook or your network.

Open the Terminal application found in /Applications/Utilities.

Use the nc command to test connectivity to your server address and port number. The format is:

nc -z server.domain.com port

To test my Outlook.com account’s incoming and outgoing mail servers, I would run these two commands and expect to receive “succeeded!” for both.

nc -z imap-mail.outlook.com 993

Connection to imap-mail.outlook.com port 993 [tcp/imaps] succeeded!

nc -z smtp-mail.outlook.com 587

Connection to smtp-mail.outlook.com port 587 [tcp/submission] succeeded!

If your tests are successful then possibly your saved incoming password is incorrect or you have mistyped some of your settings.

The spaces were bigger because I had accidentally increased the zoom level (the slider with the magnifying glass in the bottom right hand corner) to 105% rather than the standard 100% making everything look a bit different! Quite easy to change the zoom by accident using certain mouses or hotkey combinations so if anyone else has the same problem, check your zoom setting!

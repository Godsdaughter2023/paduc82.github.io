Hi Dennis,

As also described in the KB article you mentioned above, this issue actually comes down to a Loopback Protection that prevents this task from being carried out successfully. In an On-Premises environment, the Loopback Protection can be disabled, and this allows InfoPath forms to make these types of Web Service connections to SharePoint sites. However, for security purposes, in the online version of SharePoint, Loopback Protection is always on and cannot be turned off.

As far as I know, there is no out of box settings to bypass this in SharePoint online, I would suggest you post a new thread in our MSDN forum to see whether there is a workaround.

jay freedman say:

What you propose is not what Word considers to be a "booklet". If you use the booklet printing feature in Word as described at http://wordfaqs.ssbarnhill.com/2PagesPerSheet.htm#CreatingBooklet, you'll get pages that should be folded along the center line, NOT cut there. For an 8-page booklet (or an 8-page signature in a larger document), the layout will be

sheet one: recto => page 8 ------- page 1

                : verso =>page 2 ------- page 7

sheet two: recto => page 6 ------- page 3

                : verso =>page 4 ------- page 5

If you're using automatic duplexing in your printer, you should select the "Print on both sides - flip pages on short edge" setting. The "flip on long edge" setting produces the verso pages upside down and on the reverse of the wrong recto pages.

Sub Macro2()
    Dim v As Variant, vs As Variant
    vs = Array("xyz", "Abc", "mkn", "pqr")

              Application.CountIfs(.Range("A:A"), "Project", .Range("B:B"), "West", _

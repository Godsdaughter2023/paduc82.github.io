Word can't do that automatically. You can do it manually, at the cost of not being able to update the citation later.

Click in the citation, which causes the content control to display. Click the down arrow on the right side and choose "Convert citation to static text".

How do I use skype to place a conference call?

marites_p say:

Hi SenyoDewotor,

 

First, you and the participants should already have Skype account and using the latest version of Skype.

 

To make a call, find the person or group you want to call from your contact list, or use Search. Select the contact or contacts you want to call, and then... Select the new icon  to add participants, then select or video button. Select the group avatar to create a name for your group or to add a group picture. 

 

But if you wish to have a more comprehensive conference call like a meeting, you need to have a Skype for Business instead.

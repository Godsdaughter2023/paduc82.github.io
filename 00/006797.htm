In Unicode, the font of the character is not necessarily the same font as the surrounding text. Make sure you select ONLY the quote mark, and then have a look to see what the font is. In Unicode, the system runs down

You need to choose a font that is common in both MAC and WINDOWS.

julie fka profjulie say:

Unfortunately, since you cannot find the data in the My Phone archive, there is no way to restore that data.

When you delete data from My Phone and you have an active My Phone partnership, on next sync that data will also be deleted from your phone.  What you should do whenever you make radical deletions like what you described here is terminate the partnership between your phone and My Phone.  Then if you want to sync your phone top My Phone, you'll have to set up a new partnership.  When you sync with a new partnership, My Phone views the phone as a new device and only syncs what you tell it to sync and it won't delete anything from either the phone or the website.

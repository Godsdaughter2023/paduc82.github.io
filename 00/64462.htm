lemp say:

Unfortunately, no.

That COA (Certificate of Authenticity) is for Windows only.  The Product Key is needed if you ever want to reinstall Windows. 

Microsoft Office programs, such as Word and Excel, are different product that require a separate Product Key.  If a version of Microsoft Office came with your laptop (for which you likely paid an additional fee on top of the laptop's base price), you should ask your laptop manufacturer.  Perhaps -- only perhaps -- they will send you replacement software.

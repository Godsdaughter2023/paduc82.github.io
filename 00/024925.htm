Hi,

I think Bob assumed you were using Outlook, not Apple mail.

If you were opening and saving the Excel file while it was still attached to an email message, when you deleted the email message you also deleted the attached Excel file. That is the way most email programs work, including Apple Mail. 

For future reference, if you want to keep a copy of an attached file, use your email program to save the attachment somewhere, and then open and work on the saved copy.

If you have Time Machine running (you'd be crazy not to), then you can restore the email message, which would also restore the attachment and the workbook you were using.

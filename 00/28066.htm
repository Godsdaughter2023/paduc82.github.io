Joe Cool <joe.cool @ example.com>; Charlie Brown <charlie.brown @ example.com>; Sally Brown <sally.brown @ example.com>

Name                         Email Address
Joe Cool                    joe.cool @ example.com

After seeing "Unknown Artists" in Groove for years with MP3 music, I decided to do some testing and find out why. It turned out that for me, there's various causes:

Symptom

Solution

Avoidance Guidance

Track artists not appear in artist list even though COMPILATION is not present or set to "0"

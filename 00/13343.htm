hansv mvp say:

Select the range in column A that you want to format. In the following, I'll assume that the range begins in cell A2 and that A2 is the active cell within the selection.

On the Home tab of the ribbon, click Conditional Formatting > New Rule...

Select 'Use a formula t determine which cells to format'.

Enter the formula

=AND(A2<TODAY(),G2<>"Done",G2<>"N/A")

Click Format...

Activate the Fill tab.

Select red.

Click OK, then click OK again.

Repeat the above steps, but with the formula

=AND(A2>=TODAY(),A2<TODAY()+7,G2<>"Done",G2<>"N/A")

and with yellow instead of red.

ppf say:

Excel 2003 how do I shade alternate rows?

Excel 2003 how do I shade alternate rows (simply for easier reading)? Thank you.
jeeped say:
  1. Select a cell or range or the entire worksheet and then click Conditional Formatting on the Format menu
  2. The first drop-down list in the Conditional Formatting dialog box offers the Formula Is option. Select the Formula Is option from the first condition list, and then type the following formula into the edit box on the right:
    =MOD(ROW(),2)
  3. Click the Format button, select a color on the Patterns tab, and then click OK twice to close the two dialog boxes and apply the format.
The MOD formula applies your selected color to every other row. This formula shades every odd row. For every even row use,
=NOT(MOD(ROW(),2))

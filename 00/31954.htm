The WAN Miniport driver on your Windows XP system enables you to connect to Wide Area Networks (WAN) such as large-scale wireless networks.
 
Repair this item on your XP system by reinstalling the appropriate driver that shipped with your WAN Miniport network controller.
 
Visit the manufacturer's website to download and install the network adapter drivers for the same. You may also follow these steps to update the driver for the same.
 
a. Click "Start," right-click "My Computer" and click "Properties".
b. Click "Hardware" and "Device Manager".
c. Click and expand "Network Adapters" and click your "WAN Miniport".
d. Click "Driver", click on "Update" and select the drive containing the driver disk to reinstall the necessary file.

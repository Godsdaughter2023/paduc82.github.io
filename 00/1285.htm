2. Yes, it is possible. As a team site administrator, you can disable the sharing behavior for team members and let them send sharing request to you when they share the files via going to Microsoft Teams>going to the specific team>clicking on Open in SharePoint>going to the library>Settings>Library settings>selecting Permissions for this document library under Permissions and Management>Manage Parent>Access Request Settings>unticking “Allow members to share the site and individual files and folders.” and “Allow members to invite others to the site members group, Team2 Members.

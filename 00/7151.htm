ganesh achar b say:

Hi,

 

Thank you for posting your question in the Microsoft Community!

 

After going through your issue description, it appears that you need assistance regarding moving google toolbar.

 

I would suggets you to try these steps and check the issue status.

 

 

a.      Right-click on any blank part of the toolbar, and on the menu that appears, locate "Lock the Toolbar." If that option is not on the menu that popped up, then the mouse pointer is not on an entirely blank area of the taskbar. Reposition the pointer and try again. If there is a check mark next to that option, click on it once to unlock the toolbar. If there is no check, then the bar is already unlocked.

 

b.      Place the tip of the mouse pointer on any blank section of the toolbar, and hold down the left mouse button. Move the pointer to the top right-corner of the screen. The toolbar will jump to the top-right when the pointer is moved. Release the left mouse button.

 

c.       Right-click on a blank area again to bring up the options menu. Click on "Lock the Toolbar." This will prevent it from being accidentally moved in the future, since it will have to be manually unlocked first.

 

Hope this helps. Please do let us know if you need further help.

This means that the system will only grant access to the person who will be able to pass the verification process.

To verify you own this account, you will need to fill out as much as possible on this and the following forms. Even if your original information has changed, be sure to use the information you had associated with the account originally.

  • Fill out the form from a computer and from a location (i.e. home Wi-Fi or work network) where the previous successful sign-in occurred.
  • Make sure that you use an alternate email address that you have access with in order to receive the security code and not the email address of the account you want to recover.

Please be reminded that you just need to fill out information that you are just 100% sure and in the best of your knowledge.

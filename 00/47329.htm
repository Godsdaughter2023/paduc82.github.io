Pre-requisite Task

The Surface Pro and Surface China Edition offered in China do not implement a TPM Security Device Chip. However, you can use Software encryption:

To encrypt the Windows 8 Pro Chinese operating system:

1. Go to the Start Screen

2. Type: Group Policy

3. Choose Settings

5. Navigate to Computer Configuration/Administrative Templates/Windows Components/BitLocker Drive Encryption/Operating System Drives

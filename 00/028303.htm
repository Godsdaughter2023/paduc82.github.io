CONCATENATE is an Excel worksheet function, not a VBA function. Change the line

 

Cells(25, J + 4) = CONCATENATE(Cells(m, 5), " / 2011 R")

 

to

 

Cells(25, J + 4) = Cells(m, 5) & " / 2011 R"

 

The & is the concatenate operator for strings.

dale howard [mvp] say:

James --

Assuming you are using Project Online and not the Project Professional desktop app, do the following:

  1. Log into Project Web App with project manager permissions or higher.
  2. Navigate to the Project Center page and then click the name of a project.
  3. Near the top of the Quick Launch menu on the left side of the page, click the Schedule link.
  4. At the top of the page, click the Task tab to expand the Task ribbon.
  5. In the Data section of the Task ribbon, click the View pick list button and select either the Tasks Detail or Tasks Summary view.

scottgem say:

Access is a relational database. There are certain rules for the design of relational database. One of those rules is that we rarely store values that can be calculated. In your case, the number of media Queries is a calculated value so no need to be stored.

In addition, your tables are not designed properly. You need at least three tables here:

tblActivity: ActivityID (PK Autonumber), Activity, other info about activities

tblMediaTypes: MediaTypeID (PK Autonumber), MediaType

tblMediaQueries: MediaQueryID (PK Autonumber), ActivityID (FK), MediaTypeID (FK), QueryCount

Then you don't need tblCampaigns at all. You can create a crosstab query to sum the Count by Activity for each MediaType.

After renaming account, you will see a prompt “Great, you have a new Hotmail address". However, you will also need to remove the outlook.com alias for original account name from the alias list in your old account (else you will get an error “Name in Use”). Make sure you rename your account within 30 days of deleting the alias, or that email address will become available to anyone.

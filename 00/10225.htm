jp ronse say: Hi,

First of all, I made a mistake in the formula. It has to be in B2: = $A2 * B$1

Let me explain why to use semi-relative references to a cell.

In B2: you want the product of A2 & B1
In C2: A2 & C1
In B3: A3 & B1

Using A2 * B1 in B2 and dragging it to C1 will change the formula to B2 * C1, if I drag C2 down, it will become B3 * C2. So I have to refer the first part of the formula to column A and the row where the formula is entered. $A2 tells Excel that A is absolute (do not change it!) and 2 is relative. When dragging down, 2 becomes 3, etc.

Idem for B$1, dragging and dropping may change the column but not the row.

HTH

Hi MR_768,

Please note that the SharePoint mobile application is designed for viewing your SharePoint resources. To manage your document library, including download/upload your files, we recommend using the OneDrive mobile application instead.

In the OneDrive mobile application, you may click Libraries at the bottom of your iPhone screen, then select a SharePoint site > select the document library, then you may upload or download your files. 

However, there is a special approach that would enable downloading and uploading your files in the SharePoint mobile application, which is by adding a Document Library Web App on your SharePoint site. To add a Document Library Web App, you may refer to the following steps:

1. Open your SharePoint site in a browser > click the Edit button at the top right of your site;

2. In the Edit mode, move your cursor to the place where you want to add the web app;

3. Click the round "+" sign > select Document library from the list > select the document library that you want to connect to this web part;

4. Click Publish at the top right of the site page to save the settings.

After you added the Document Library Web App, you may go to the SharePoint mobile application and open the site, then browse to the web part to use the upload and download features.

- The Settings dialog should open and the “Display” tab should be showing on the left side.

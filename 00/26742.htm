I am on Outlook 2016 on a Mac with both the Mac and Outlook updated to the most recent version. 

asst_silentrlit say:

I, by myself, have discovered the solution to this issue.

If you are not saving a copy of sent emails, the button will not appear. To turn on sent saves, go to "Accounts" through preferences. Then click "Advanced.." There will be a tab for "Folders," click it. Then change your setting from "Don't store a copy of sent messages," to "Store sent messages in this folder." This will fix your issue.

I hope this helps.

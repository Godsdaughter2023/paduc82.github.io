Exel lookup or index function - Microsoft Community

mary wallace say:

Exel lookup or index function

I have an excel spreadsheet with numbers in Column A.  I need to find that same number in column B.  When I find the corresponding number in Column B, I need it and the rest of the data in that row (Column C and D) to go to another section of the spreadsheet.  Is this possible?

lars-Åke aspelin say:

Put this formula where you want the C-data corresponding to the number in cell A2 in column B:

=VLOOKUP(A2,B2:C100,2,FALSE)

Put this formula where you want the D-data corresponding to the number in cell A2 in column B:

=VLOOKUP(A2,B2:D100,3,FALSE)

Change the 100 to fit the size of your data in columns B to D.

Hope this helps / Lars-Åke

We apologize for the delayed response and the inconvenience this issue has caused you. I am happy to help you with your concern. I know that you're in the frustrating situation because of the lost emails on your Inbox after the upgrade.

 

The possible reasons why e-mails suddenly disappear are as follows. you can also refer to the information below on how you can avoid lost emails on your Inbox page. 

  • Messages were manually deleted either due to accidental deletion or were moved or deleted due to your account filter settings.
  • Messages were deleted due to account compromise issues where a hacker might have gained access to your account and deleted the emails.
  • You are using an e-mail client or a third party spam filtering software to access your account. This often happens when using the Pop3 protocol to retrieve your mail and the option “Always leave a copy of the message on the server” is not selected.
  • The account was marked as “Inactive." Windows Live Hotmail accounts become inactive if you don’t sign in for more than 365 days or within the first 10 days after signing up for an account. After an account becomes inactive, all messages, folders, and contacts are deleted.

Plrease be informed that when you upgrade your account to Outlook.com, you are only upgrading the user interface of Hotmail to the new user interface; hence all your emails should still stay in your Inbox..

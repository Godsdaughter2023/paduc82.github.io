window phone

how to copy contacts from sim to phone

a. user say:

Hello Wayne,

You can easily import or copy contacts from your SIM card to your phone using the People app. When you import contacts to your phone, it will also be automatically imported to your Microsoft account. To do this, follow the steps below:

  1. On Start , tap People.

  2. Tap ... (More), and then tap Settings.

  3. Tap Import from SIM. If you have more than one SIM card, select the one you want under Import contacts from.

  4. Under Save contacts to, select the account you'd like to store the contacts in, and then tap Next.

  5. Do one of the following:

    • To import all of the contacts on your SIM card, tap Import.
    • If you want to select contacts to import individually, tap Clear, select the contacts you want to import, and then tap Import.

If you think this was useful, you may "mark it as an answer" to help those who are facing the same problem.

Restore Factory Defaults

Through Firmware

  1. Open a web browser.
  2. Enter 192.168.1.1 in the address bar.

    (admin/password, admin/password1, admin/password on sticker, admin/serial number begining with CSJ).

  5. Click Yes on the warning screen.
  6. From the following menu click Restore Defaults.
  7. Click Restore Defaults.
  8. Click Ok on the following screen.

Through Hardware

  1. Plug the computer into a LAN port via an ethernet cable.
  2. Locate the reset button on the device.
  3. With paperclip/pen/toothpick hold down the reset button down for 30 seconds.
  4. Wait for lights to turn off then return to normal.

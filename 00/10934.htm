Each section break stores the formatting of the preceding section, which explains why you can't delete one without affecting the formatting of the document. What you can do is the following: Select the paragraph containing the section break causing the extra page, and then format it as hidden text and/or 1 pt font size, 1 pt line spacing, zero spacing before and after.

bkwsnx say:

Never mind, answered my own question. Remembered ampersands. For instance, to make each one of the actions have the output as bold, Arial and size 9 would be something like:

 

Sub AddLeftFooter()
   ActiveSheet.PageSetup.LeftFooter = "&""Arial""&B&9" & Range("A1").Value
End Sub

Sub AddCenterFooter()
   ActiveSheet.PageSetup.CenterFooter = "&""Arial""&B&9" & Range("A1").Value
End Sub

Sub AddRightFooter()
   ActiveSheet.PageSetup.RightFooter = "&""Arial""&B&9" & Range("A1").Value
End Sub

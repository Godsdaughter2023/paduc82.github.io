Kelly,

I hope you're not taking backup copies of individual files as that is the seed for corruption. It's okay to periodically save the whole folder as a backup, but saving individual files creates duplicate links to the dynamic master and that's when the trouble starts. The static master solves that problem but if you are using a separate resource pool, then you still have a linked structure and the same rules apply.

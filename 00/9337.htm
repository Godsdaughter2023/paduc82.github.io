macropod say:

Instead of using TIME, you probably should use DATE and the expression could be simplified to:

{IF{MERGEFIELD Start_date \@ M}< {DATE \@ M} "" "Retro-active payment will be made."}

However, that disregards the year and, so, will output records from former years as well, so you really need:

{IF{MERGEFIELD Start_date \@ YYYYMM}< {DATE \@ YYYYMM} "" "Retro-active payment will be made."}

Note that you must use 'M' for the month switch, not 'm' - which is for minutes...

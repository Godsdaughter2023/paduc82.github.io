Sway- Continuous play of videos

Greetings,

I want to insert several videos into my Sway and then have the videos autoplay one after another.  Now I click play, a video plays and then stops.  I would like it to automatically go to the next video.  Is this possible?   Thank you

jason (office engineer) say:

Hi Chuck,

There is not really a feature that will give you exactly what you want, but you might be able to fake it with the combination of a few things.

First of all, using the default method for inserting videos, you will not be able to set them to autoplay.  However, if you are using videos from a site that supports autoplay (like YouTube) you can embed them and get them to autoplay.  To do this, you will need to go directly to the video site, get a URL for a video that will autoplay on open, and paste this URL into an Embed card in Sway.  If you are doing this from a site that Sway natively supports (like YouTube), it will convert that Embed card into a Video card.  You then need to press the Undo button (or Ctrl+z) to bring it back to an Embed card.  Now, when you view your Sway, as soon as the video is ready to come on the screen, it will start playing.

The downside to this approach is that if you do this to multiple videos, each one will start playing as it is about to come on the screen without regard to whether another video is already playing and then you'll have multiple going at once.  To work around that, you need to choose the Slides layout for your Sway and only put one video on each slide.  It won't be automatic, but each video will play as the user navigates to the next slide.

Additionally, if you have a premium account, you can turn on autoplay for navigating the Sway, but that will navigate through the Sway without paying attention to playing videos so that might be tough to time just right.

Let me know if you have any other questions.

Jason

To enable windows defender via group policy, do the following:

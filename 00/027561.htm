pesach shelnitz say:

Hi,

Word is not interpreting 31/4/2012 as a date because April has only 30 days. Therefore, Word is taking this expression out of the list of dates and sorting the rest of the list, which contains expressions that Word recognizes as dates. Change 31/4/2012 to 30/4/2012, and you will see the sorting that you expect.

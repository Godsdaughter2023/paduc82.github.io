considerate_guy say:

My guess is you took the video vertically.  Movie Maker edits your video so that is can be viewed horizontally on an HDTV, on YouTube/Vimeo/etc., on a computer screen, etc.

I don't think you'd want Movie Maker to rotate your video so it fit horizontally (then you'd have to turn your head to watch it), or to stretch it horizontallyy (then everyone would be very short and fat), or to crop the top and bottom (then you would lose everyone's head and feet), so it does the best it can.  It shrinks the video and fits it all within a wide horizontal rectangle.  The rest of the area is just black.

There isn't much you can do with existing vertical video but I would highly recommend that you record future video with your phone turned so that you are filming horizontally.

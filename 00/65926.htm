As I work a little bit with LiPo batteries (RC hobby), I have done some research on how to maximise the performance and longevity of Lithium batteries (or batteries in general). And one thing that is true with all the battery chemistries is that they degrade when being charged and then discharged. Another fact with Lithium batteries is that they oxidise (lose capacity) extremely quickly when they are kept fully charged. And Lithium batteries do not tolerate deep discharging and overcharging well (lowers the service life: number of charging cycles). And there are many more characteristics of Lithium batteries, I have just emphasized the ones that are needed to supply my arguments why advanced battery options are necessary! (source: Battery University).

 

So my request is to implement advanced battery options for Surface: controlling when and if to charge, voltage levels (percentage of battery capacity tells almost nothing!!!), setting voltage levels (maximal charging voltage and minimal discharging voltage)...

hansv mvp say:

First, right-click the picture, select Text Wrapping from the popup menu and make sure that Inline with Text is selected.

Next, select the entire cell (move the mouse pointer near the left edge of the cell until it becomes a solid black arrow, then click).

Activate the Layout tab of the ribbon and click the Align Center button in the Alignment group.

Hi mB_803,

Even though you've unlocked your phone, there is a good possibility that the device's radio does not match the phone carrier on which you are trying to use the phone. Different phone carriers use different radio frequencies and looks as if you might be in such a predicament. You might have a case where the data radio matches up but the voice does not.

There is also the possibility that you might have damaged the device during the unlock process, unless the unlock was performed by Orange and not a 3rd party.

The only suggestion I have is to verify your APN, under Settings => Cellular (adding what you need to add), as well as verify it is showing Vodafone or Movistar, as well as look up the specs for your particular device on the Orange network and see if the radios are compatible with Vodafone or Movistar.

When you try this, you'll find that the AppData folder and everything under it is marked in Windows as Hidden. There are two ways to get to the desired folder:

stocktiki say:

Thanks for the tips. Happy to report it worked:

 

1. Sign into the outlook.com account with the address you want to merge into your primary Outlook.com account.

2. Add a new alias.

3. Change the address to the new alias.

4. Delete the old alias (changing to an alias before deleting holds the waiting period to 30 days).

5. Mark your calendar for exactly 30 days .

6. After 30 days have past -- log into the primary Outlook.com account.

7. Add the alias of the address you wanted to keep.

8. Change primary address to the alias you wanted.

 

Viola. Worked like a charm. It was a little nerve-racking waiting the 30 days -- hoping no one else grabbed the account name -- but if you mark your calendar and move fast -- should be ok unless you have a common name.

 

Problem solved!

Ideally, when you install a language pack or choose a different language other than your system language, most of the features are displayed in the modified language. c. Click on Add a Language and then select a desired language (Example: English) and click Open.

d. Now select a Language (Example: English (United States).

Note: Once you are done with setting the desired language, restart the computer and check.

Additional Information:

Language packs

anyone knows how to deal with it?

krzysztofjurkowski say: I think I found solution. When system will upgrade to 8.1 with installed language pack, will keep settings but delete language pack. Then new 8.1 showing in control panel that there is no available pack for download. If you can see your language on this list: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/language-packs#lptabs=win8 and your computer showing "language pack isn't available", then do this:

- go to control panel ->Clock,language and region ->language
- click on "advanced settings"
- In the option "Override for windows display language" change to "Use language list(recommended)"
- click "save" button
- Now you should see at your language is available for download :)

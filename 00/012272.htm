palcouk say:

A pop mail account when used on multiple devices will not be in sync across all devices, even if 'keep a copy on the server' is checked.

Received mail, if 'keep a copy.........' is selected will appear on all devices, but any mail sent will only appear on the device it was sent from.

And any tasks/Contacts etc will only show on the device on which they are added/edited.

There are third party paid for utilities that can be used to sync across devices.

If configured as an IMAP account (If your mail provider supports IMAP) the mail on all devices will be in sync

a. user say:

Hi,

Try this:



Hold down Ctrl and Shift with two fingers of your left hand.

While holding them down, press and release the semi-colon ;

Then release Ctrl and Shift.

At this point, it will seem as if nothing has happened.

Now press and release a vowel such as a, o or u.

You should see the vowel with an umlaut above it.



Another option is to activate the Insert tab of the ribbon and to click Symbol > More Symbols...

You can insert any character from the Symbol dialog. You can keep it open while typing in the document.

Let me know if that helps.

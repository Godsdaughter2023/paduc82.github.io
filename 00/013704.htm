However, when I did Right Click -> Properties -> Details Tab -> Comments Field and typed in the date it seems to have invalidated the signatures.

I did the same thing with my Excel and PowerPoint files and the digital signatures remained valid.

Could it be because I have the field linked to the doc property?

Thanks in advance!!

jay freedman say:

> Could it be because I have the field linked to the doc property?

I think that's correct. The digital signature is actually a cryptographic "hash" of the contents of the document. It's intended to signify that nothing in the document has been changed since the signature was applied. When the field's content changes, the hash in the signature no longer matches the actual hash of the document, so the signature is invalidated.

If you want to see the comments value within Word without inserting it into the document as a field, click File > Info and look at the Properties list on the right side. Alternatively, enter the date in the comments field (or in the Properties list in the Info page) before you apply the signature.

3D maps is not really designed to do that.

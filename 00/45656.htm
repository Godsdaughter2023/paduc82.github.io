    FIX - "Cant add account to this device" & "unable to - Microsoft Community

    leegplayer say:

    FIX - "Cant add account to this device" & "unable to change log in from local to Microsoft account."

    Hi All.

    I had the above problem and after a lot of head scratching and failed attempts to resolve it I finally stumbled on a solution.  I am posting this because there is no definitive solution on the forum.

    The reason I found for this issue is because after the upgrade to windows 10 there are references to the user accounts from the old install linked to the Microsoft accounts you are trying to connect with.

    I had two accounts that this issue affected, one being my own account which is fully functional on another machine and second account that existed only on this machine with the previous installation.

    The solution is to find and remove these reference accounts.  NOTE this process has no effect on your local and microsoft account so is safe to do.

    You need to be an administrator to do the following.

    Firstly right click on the start button and select Computer Management.

    Next click on Local Users and Groups and double click on Users.

    In the list of users you will see the names of the old user accounts which are truncated to 5 letters.  E.g. If the user account is William it will be displayed as Willi and will be disabled.

    Delete the respective 5 letter account that matches the account you are trying to activate.  I recommend only removing the account you are working on.

    Reboot your machine and then go to your local account settings and link your account.

    The linking should proceed as expected and your local account will become linked to your Microsoft account.

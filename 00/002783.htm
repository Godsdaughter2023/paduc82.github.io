Try this

1. Suppose in each tab, your data is  in range A2:E5 (headings are in A1:E1)

2. Please remove - from each of the tabs

3. On the Summary sheet, type the tab names in range J1:J3

=SUMPRODUCT(SUMIF(INDIRECT($J$1:$J$3&"!A2:A5"),A2,INDIRECT($J$1:$J$3&"!C2:C5")))

=SUMPRODUCT(SUMIF(INDIRECT($J$1:$J$3&"!A2:A5"),A2,INDIRECT($J$1:$J$3&"!D2:D5")))

Assuming you are asking about your Main contact list, not your group contact list.

 

How to access your main contact list:

Open outlook.com > top left next to the logo Outlook, there is a down arrow > click the arrow > click People > All your contacts are now listed on the left. Scroll down to see each contact.

How to add a new contact ;

In the same People window, toolbar at top, click New > enter info into required boxes > click Save when done.

 

To Edit your contact list :

Click at the contact's name ( NOT its check box ) > toolbar at top, click Edit.

Note :

1. Edit option will not appear until you have selected a contact.

2. Edit option will not appear if you click at the contact's checkbox.

To delete a contact from the list :

Just click at the contact > toolbar at top, click Delete.

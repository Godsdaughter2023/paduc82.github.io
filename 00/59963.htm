It the monitor is working correctly, don't bother.  Where are you getting this update?

It's rarely necessary to install or update a monitor "driver."  The "driver" is not like other device drivers. To create a regular playlist:

a.       Click the Start button, click All Programs, and then click Windows Media Player.

b.      If the Player is currently open and you’re in Now Playing mode, click the Switch to Library button in the upper-right corner of the Player.

c.       In the Player Library, click the Play tab, if necessary, to open the list pane.

d.      If you need to clear the list pane before creating your playlist, click Clear list.

e.      Drag items from the details pane to the list pane to add them to the new playlist.

f.        To select multiple adjacent items, press and hold the Shift key while selecting. To select non-adjacent items, press and hold the Ctrl key while selecting.

g.       To rearrange items, drag them up or down in the list pane.

h.      To save the list, click Save list at the top of the list pane, type a name for the playlist, and then press Enter.

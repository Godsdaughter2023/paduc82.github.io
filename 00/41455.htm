cmdrkeene say:

If the password reset is causing the wrong account to reset, it means your browser is logging you in and caching the wrong account while you're resetting. 

To make sure you are truly resetting the Outlook account and not the AOL one, do this:

1) Open the Private Mode feature of your web browser. For Chrome this is Ctrl+Shift+N to open an Incognito tab.  For Edge or IE it is Ctrl+Shift+P to open a Private browsing tab.

2) In that Private tab, go to bit.ly/MicrosoftAccountPasswordReset, use the "Forgot" option and put in your @AOL email address.

3) Go to your AOL email inbox and get the reset email message to finish the reset.

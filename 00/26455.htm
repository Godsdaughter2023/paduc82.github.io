scottgem say:

To reset the autonumbers you would have to delete all the records then Compact and Repair the database. But you may have a misunderstanding of what an autonumber is and how it should be used. An Autonumber is nothing more than a way to uniquely identify a record. It is NOT a sequential numbering system and should not be considered as one. It is simply a way to assign a primary key. 

As for the relation that fact that all you get is a line simply means that referential integrity cannot be applied because the data violates RI. It also means Access cannot detect the type of relationship, but it should have no affect on the issues you are experiencing. Except it could be why you can't enter into the subform.

I have to ask again whether you are using a subform or a subdatasheet. If you are clicking a plus sign in datasheet view that is a subdatasheet not a subform.

What are the ACTUAL parent and child links between the main form and the subform?  

I think the key here is that your PK-FK pairings may not be working properly. If child records are not being shown, check the tables directly to make sure that the FK is being populated properly.

Welcome to the Microsoft community. We're sorry for any inconvenience this may have caused as you cannot receive e-mails on your accounts. Let me see what I can do to help you. You can refer to the following to see if none of these settings is contributing to the problem:

You can check to make sure you haven't set up email forwarding on your account.


1. Sign in to your account.
2. Click Options and then More options.
3. Under Managing your account, click Email forwarding
4. On the Forwarding page, click Don't forward.
5. Click Save.


Note: If the radio button is on the Forward your mail to another email account and there's another address inside the box, we can conclude that there's another account receiving the messages that should be sent to you. However, the steps above will set your email address as the default receiver of your emails.

You might want to check the Blocked Senders, to see if those contacts are in the list, remove them and add to the Safe senders. Please refer to the following steps:

Check your Blocked sender list

 

1. Sign in to your account. Go to inbox.
2. Click Options located at the upper-right side of the page.
3. Select More Options.
4. Under Preventing junk email, click Safe and blocked senders.
5. Click Blocked senders.
6. In the Blocked senders list, select the name or domain (the part of an e-mail address that follows the @ sign, such as hotmail.com) that you now want to receive mail from, and then click<< Remove from list.

Add the email address to your Safe senders list

 

1. Sign in to your account. Go to inbox.
2. Click Options located at the upper-right side of the page.
3. Select More Options.
4. Under Preventing junk email, click Safe and blocked senders.
5. Click Safe Senders.
6. In the text box, type in the email address or domain you’d like to always receive email from, and then click Add to list >>.

Check E-mail rules

1. Sign in to your account

2. Click the gear icon at the top right portion

3. Select More mail settings

4. Under Customizing Outlook, click Rules for sorting new messages

daniel g. grau say:
Try this:

  • Edit > Find > Advanced Find and Replace
  • At the top of the dialog box, click on the Replace tab
  • In the Find field, type: ^p (the caret character is above the "6")
  • In the Replace field, type ^l (that's a lower case L)

You can access a list of placeholders as follows:

  • In the Find and Replace dialog box, click on the blue disclose to the left of the Cancel button, so it points up
  • Click on the Special button at the bottom right corner

You can do the same operation through the regular Find and Replace sidebar. The list of shortcuts is accessed by clicking on the gray disclose triangles.

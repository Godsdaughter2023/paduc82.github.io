Hi,

You are right. WMDC will not let you sync your WM2002 device, but you should be able to browse and use Desktop passthrough to access the internet.

Check that the valuue for EnableAutoDetect is set to 1 on your device.

Download a free registry editor for your device from http://www.phm.lu/Products/PocketPC/RegEdit/

Go to HKey_Current_User\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Internet Settings.

Set the value for EnableAutoDetect to 1.

If EnableAutoDetect does not exist, just create it as a new Dword value.

Reboot your device and connect again.

Assuming you're running the Anniversary Update, there is not an official option to disable Automatic Updates. However, you may unofficially disable automatic updates through the services console. Here is how:

1. In an administrator's account, open Start, type services.msc and hit enter.

2. Find the Windows Update service. Double-click it, and set the startup type to 'Disabled'.

3. Restart your PC.

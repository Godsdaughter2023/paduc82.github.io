Grey ADD button

I am new to word 2016 for Mac.

In spell check the ADD button (next to ignore and ignore all) is greyed out. I am unable to add new word in my document, such as Names and some words spelt differently to those in the custom dictionary. Why?

I MS word 2010, it was easy add new words to the spell check dictionary.

 
bob jones aka: cybertaz say:

The most common reason is that your Custom Dictionary is defined as being a specific Language, but the text you're attempting to Add is tagged as being in a Language that differs from that. IOW, you cannot add terms identified as French to a Custom Dictionary designated as German.

First, make certain that the text is tagged as the correct Language. That may make the Add command available even if your Custom Dictionary has a Language attribute set. However, you'll most likely also have to...

  1. Go to Word> Preferences> Spelling & Grammar
  2. Click the Dictionaries button
  3. If you have more than 1 Custom Dictionary, make sure the correct ones are checked

For each Custom Dictionary;

  1. Select the Custom Dictionary
  2. Change the Language if desired. However [especially if you have only 1 Custom Dictionary] it's generally best to set the Language to (none). That will avoid issues such as you're having now.

mister wizard say:

Yes, there is, via Notepad:

Open Windows Explorer and select the target folder in the left pane.
Press Ctrl + A to select all items in the right pane.
Press and hold the Shift key, then right click on the selection.
From the context menu, choose "Copy as Path".
Paste the list into Notepad or any word processor and print.

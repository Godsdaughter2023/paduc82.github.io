mlg63 say:

You don't put a shortcut to the scanner itself on the Desktop. Instead find the software program that came with the scanner such as Epson Scan or HP Scan etc. and make a shortcut to that. When you click on that, the program will open the scanner and be able to scan after you select the settings.

Installing the drivers and software correctly would have already put the shortcut on the Apps list. Find that in the apps list, right click and pin to Start and also pin to Taskbar. If you have not installed the software and drivers, go to the scanner's web site and download them.

paul van der heu say:

 

Yes (to all)

 

What the quote refers to is to not connect devices which will provide a signal or voltage on the jackplug as this may damage the phone. This refers to measurement 'dongles' such as used for sports/running/exercise apps which will measure heartbeat or other statistics and provide a signal on the audio out port. Likely for the 620 (being a budget device) this is not supported.

j.spraul say: Your clue regarding the outline/border changing is an indication that the developer tool has technically become the new foreground window. The most likely scenario is that it has a problem saving/restoring its position and is off-screen and/or an invalid size. Here is the solution whenever you need to grab hold of a window to bring it back into focus (use the keyboard!):

Immediately after IE8 loses focus to the tools window (right after pressing F12):

 1. Press ALT + Space to bring up the tools window system menu
 2. Choose 'Move...' (preferably by pressing 'M')
 3. Press any of the arrow/directional keys once (such as the down arrow key)
 4. The window will now be 'attached' to the mouse until you click, so you can drag it to the center of the screen and resize it as needed.

This solution is not specific to the IE8 developer tool; it works for example with programs that were left on a second monitor that is no longer connected.

a. user say:

Hello watpen,

Thank you for posting here.

I understand that you want to stop receiving junk mails from the blocked senders.

Please be informed that spammers use different email address that is why you are still receiving the same message. To stop them coming from your inbox, you may set your junk filter by doing the steps below:

1. Log in to Outlook.com.
2. Click the Gear icon beside your profile name then click More mail settings.

3. Under Preventing junk email, click Filters and reporting.
4. Choose Exclusive under the Choose a junk email filter, then click Save.

Note: Everything is sent to the junk email folder except messages from your contacts and safe senders, Outlook.com service announcements, and alerts that you signed up for.

bob jones aka: cybertaz say:

I'm not completely clear as to what steps you've taken, but perhaps it would help put things in perspective to think in terms of layers...

Anything in the Header/Footer layer will appear to be 'washed out' when working in the Text layer of a document. It has nothing to do with the transparency or formatting of the H/F content. Likewise, the Text layer content will appear faded while actively working in the H/F layer. Word is designed to work in that manner to help distinguish which content is where.

Typically, IOW, when you open a Word document the content of the Text layer will be displayed at full intensity & the content of the H/F layer will be dimmed. That behavior cannot be changed... essentially, it's at least partly due to Word documents not really being intended for electronic distribution.

When printed [or output as a PDF], however, all content will reproduce at its full intensity.

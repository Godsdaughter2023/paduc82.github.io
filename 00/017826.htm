Hey K_Gr, 

OneNote Product Manager here. Can you try and open the notebook from all your devices, and let them sync for as long as it takes to sync that notebook? On phones, you might have to plug your device into a wall and turn off autolock in order to get the content to sync quickly. 

What's happening here is that OneNote encrypts things at a section level locally and cannot upload parts of the encrypted content to the servers. So it might see that a section exists with protected pages and show you that in your OneNote clients, but unless all that data is uploaded to the server it cannot pull it down for you and display the contents.

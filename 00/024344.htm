  1. Select R2:S2 and then choose Format, Conditional Formatting.
  2. Change the first drop-box in Condition 1 to Formula Is and put the following into the formula text box to the right.
             =$R2="Yes"
  3. Click the Format button and select a green fill color. Click OK to save the format.
  4. Click Add>> and repeat the procedure using

  5. Click OK to save both rules.
  6. With R2:S2 still selected, press Ctrl+C (e.g. Copy).
  7. Expand the selection by selecting all of the entries in columns R and S.
  8. Right-click and Paste Special, Formats.
The cells in columns R and S should react to the value in column R.

roady [mvp] say:

OK, so you are indeed looking for the "Contact Group" functionality and not the "Group" mailbox functionality that is shown on the Home tab of the Ribbon.

The reason why the "Contact Group" functionality shows greyed out for you is probably because you are connected to your Outlook.com account via the Exchange ActiveSync (EAS) protocol. This protocol doesn't support creating and managing Contact Groups. This is one of the reasons why Microsoft is migrating all their Outlook.com accounts to their Exchange based Office 365 infrastructure. You can directly forget those details ;-)

When you logon to Outlook.com on the web, and look at the top left corner, does that state "Outlook Mail" or "Outlook.com"?

If it states "Outlook Mail", then your account has been migrated already and you can reconfigure your Outlook.com account as an actual Exchange account in Outlook. To do this, simply click File-> Add Account and fill out your name, @outlook.com address and password (or App Password when you have Two-Step Verification enabled) and it will configure the account as an Exchange account. You can then remove the account that was configured as an Exchange ActiveSync account. Exchange based accounts do have support for creating, managing and synching Contact Groups.

If it states "Outlook.com", then your account hasn't been migrated yet but most likely will be migrated before the end of summer.

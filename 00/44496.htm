To receive the product key, either you must have purchased the product key or already have the Windows 10 product key you bought from Microsoft or you already had previously upgraded to Windows 10 using windows update and your Windows 7 or Windows 8.1 product key has been now merged as a Windows 10 key and are simply reinstalling Windows 10 in your computer.

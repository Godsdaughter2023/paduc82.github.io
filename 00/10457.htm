All open workbooks are displayed within the Excel application window. hal hostetler - mvp-outlook say:
Those differences are usually due to the type of email account involved.  Exchange Server accounts including those from Outlook.com/Live mail/Hotmail have deleted item recovery capabilities.  IMAP accounts may or may not be able to do this, depending on how the email provider has their servers setup, and POP accounts normally cannot do this.  Your "Trash" folder is an indicator that this home account is either POP or IMAP.  You can check the account type by going to File > Account Settings > Account Settings.  The account Type is listed in the second column.

Not that I'm aware of. Have you considered changing to a For Each In loop?

Sub mcrSequenceVisible()
    COL_SID_CURRENT = 3
    COL_SID_CURRENT_STR = "c"
    ROW_LAST = ActiveSheet.UsedRange.Rows.Count
    ' Define aRangeStr
    aRangeStr = COL_SID_CURRENT_STR & "2"      ' -> "c2"
    aRangeStr = aRangeStr & ":" & COL_SID_CURRENT_STR & ROW_LAST      ' -> "c2:c24"

    Next c

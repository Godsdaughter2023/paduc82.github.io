andreas killer say:

You have to parse the text and the string patterns by yourself with VBA, then you can choose the appropriate replacement method.

With a Word document you can do something like this:

Sub Test()
  With ActiveDocument.Content
    With .Find
      .ClearFormatting
      .Text = "{\sub h2o}"
      With .Replacement
        .ClearFormatting
        .Text = "h2o"
        .Font.Subscript = True
      End With
      .Execute Replace:=wdReplaceAll
    End With
  End With
 
  With ActiveDocument.Content
    With .Find
      .ClearFormatting
      .Text = "{\sup h2o}"
      With .Replacement
        .ClearFormatting
        .Text = "h2o"
        .Font.Superscript = True
      End With
      .Execute Replace:=wdReplaceAll
    End With
  End With
End Sub

Anything else is up to you.

Andreas.

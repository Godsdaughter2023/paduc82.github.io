ken blake say:

It is not possible to change the "bitness" of any version of Windows from 32-bit to 64-bit, or vice versa. So you can *not* upgrade; you will have to do a clean installation.

So *nothing* will remain installed. You will have to reinstall all your software and all your drivers. And reload all your data from its backup.

j.e. mcgimpsey say: The regression method that Microsoft chose for calculating power trendlines (all versions) cannot handle zero or negative values.

The equivalent functions used are

y=a*x^b 

a: =EXP(INDEX(LINEST(LN(y-range),LN(x-range),,),1,2)) 

b: =INDEX(LINEST(LN(y-range),LN(x-range),,),1)


Since one can't take the logarithm of zero or a negative number, adding 0,0 as a data point makes the trendline indeterminate.

You'll also find that, while you can select "logarithmic" trendlines, they won't display with 0,0 data points.

techfreak_ say:

I currently have Skype 8.43.0.53 installed on my Android 6+ phone.

The capability exists in the settings. You will not see the video toggle until the automatic answer feature is enabled. The configuration is displayed below:

It is impossible to answer a phone call from any user with video enabled. Standard phone calls are incapable of supporting video since video is not a supported medium over the standard analog telephone. If the inbound caller is calling from their Skype account then video will be automatically enabled.

Skype has never exclusively supported a feature to answer calls automatically from specific contacts. To accomplish this you can limit your inbound Skype calls to contacts in general by enabling "Only allow Skype calls from contacts to ring on this device". After this restriction is enabled, you can temporarily block or remove all your other contacts and keep only the contacts you want to reach you, while you have automatic answering with video enabled.

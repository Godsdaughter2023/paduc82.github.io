  • Select all the lines of data.

Counifs Multiple Range & Multiple Criteria

Can multiple criteria arrays are possible in single countifs?

Like this:


Countifs(range,{criteria:criteria},range,{criteria:criteria},range,{criteria:criteria}, range,{criteria:criteria})

Range    Criteria

  A:A         E1:E5

  B:B         F1:F7

  C:C        G1:G10

  D:D        H1:H8

bernie deitrick say:

You can do that only if the criteria ranges have the same number of rows, and there are no 'cross-row' criteria sets.  Otherwise, you would need to use four columns of helper cells, along the lines of this in cell I1:

=IF(A1="",FALSE,NOT(ISERROR(MATCH(A1,E$1:E$10,False))))

copied across to L1, and then down to match your longest column.

Then your COUNTIFS becomes

=COUNTIFS(I:I,TRUE,J:J,TRUE,K:K,TRUE,L:L,TRUE)

amit tandon say:

Formula in cell I2:

=SUMPRODUCT(--ISNUMBER(MATCH(A1:A100, E1:E5,0)*MATCH(B1:B100, F1:F7,0)*MATCH(C1:C100, G1:G10,0)*MATCH(D1:D100, H1:H8,0)))

COUNTIFS doesn't works with multiple SUM range for multiple CRITERIA RANGE, therefore I would like to suggest few best alternatives.

{=COUNTIF($F$1:$F$5,$I$1:$I$2)*COUNTIF($G$1:$G$5,$J$1:$J$2)*COUNTIF($H$1:$H$5,$K$1:$K$1)}

{=SUM(COUNTIFS(F1:F5,{"A","B"}),COUNTIFS(G1:G5,{1}),COUNTIFS(H1:H5,{"Q1"}))}

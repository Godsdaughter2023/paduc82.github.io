Welcome to Microsoft Community.

 

I understand that you want to link your university account using Outlook Web Apps to Outlook.com. Currently, Outlook.com does not support importing emails from another web server to our server. As a workaround you can use upload your email to Outlook.com option using mail client via POP connection.

 

First, you need to access your account using Windows Live Mail then add your account using POP protocol. For correct POP and SMTP settings, I suggest that you contact your email service provider.

Once done, follow these steps below on how to upload emails to Outlook.com.

  1. Login to https://outlook.com.
  2. Click the cog icon.
  3. Select More mail settings.
  4. Under "Managing your account", click Your email accounts.
  5. Click Add send and receive account.
  6. Under "Upload an email account", click Upload your email to Outlook.com.
  7. You will be prompted to install the needed add-ons to perform the process.
  8. Select the account that you want to upload the emails from.
  9. Click Next.
  10. If Windows Live Mail is still open, you will be prompted to close it before uploading the emails. Once done, click OK.
  11. Click Upload.
  12. You will be asked if you want to receive new emails from the POP account or if you only want to upload old emails from your POP account.
  13. Click OK.

You will be notified if the emails were uploaded successfully or if the process failed. Once the upload process succeeded, you will receive an email confirmation that emails were uploaded in your Microsoft account.

How do I delete saves I no longer need?

You can manage your data for individual games through My games & apps on your Home screen.

From the Home screen, select My games & apps.

Highlight the game, press the Menu button on your controller, and then select Manage game.

Scroll to the right, highlight the saved data for your gamertag under Saved Data, and then press A on your controller.

You will be prompted to confirm deletion of this game save data:

Delete from console will remove the local copy of this save. You can retrieve it from the cloud the next time you play.

Delete everywhere will remove your saved data from this console, the cloud, and all other consoles you play on.

Cancel will leave your data intact.

Year date changing automatically - Microsoft Community

uldisuldis say:

Year date changing automatically

The year date on my computer keep changing to 16518 or 16599, I set the correct date and then after like an hour it changes again to 16518, please help, thanks.

andre for directly say:
Try resetting your BIOS defaults:

https://www.groovypost.com/howto/reset-pc-bios-...

If that does not work and you have a desktop computer...

Power down
Unplug it
Open the system unit

Remove the CMOS CR-2032 chip
Plug it in, start the machine, wait 10 seconds

Power down, unplug it, reinstall the CMOS CR-2032 chip
Plug it in again, power up

If it still occurs, you might need to replace your CMOS chip.

a. user say:

Hi,

 

We are glad to assist you with your concern. Based on your description, it seems that you wish to send an email to a particular sender. At this point, are you pertaining to Conversation threading when you say “chain of emails”? If yes, please follow the steps below for your guide to disable Conversation threading.

 

1.       Sign in to your account.

2.       Click the Gear icon beside your name on the top right part of the screen and then click on More mail settings.

3.       Under Reading email, click on “Group by conversation and pre-load messages”.

4.       Under Conversation Settings, select the Show messages individually  radio button.

5.       Click Save.


If the solution above does not answer your question, please provide us more details of your issue so we could give you an accurate solution.

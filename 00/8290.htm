Since day one, what became Office applications have had quality problems. So MS designed them to be “user friendly”. You can do a simple uninstall, from the Control Panel and the process leaves behind your configuration files, like Normal.DOTM for Word, and Registry Entries. The developers came up with a 3 step “fast and dirty” trouble shooting process. Step 1, start the application in Safe Mode to disable addins. This simple uninstall also plays it’s part in “upgrades”. To share an Outlook.com Calendar;

  1. Logon to https://calendar.live.com, click the Share button at the top and select which Calendar you would like to share.
  2. In the pane that pops-up, choose: Get a link.

jeeped say:

After you edit the contents of a cell, you can tap Enter or Tab to accept the changes or Esc to discard the changes.

Tab typically moves you to the next cell to the right or the next cell in the current selection if you have more than one cell selected.

Enter typically moves you down or to the next cell in a multiple cell selection but you can change this behavior within Excel Options (e.g. Alt+F,T).

Hi,

To assist you better we would like to know few details:

Most computers have a temperature sensor and a variable-speed fan. As your machine runs, it keeps an eye on how warm it is and decides at what speed to run the fan.

Reasons for Fan running faster:

  1. Sometimes, dust, dirt, and other debris block the air vents of your computer and it can’t create enough air flow to cool down efficiently.

  2. If the Processor is doing a lot of calculations; as video work, running a game or some other heavy-duty task. Your machine gets hot.

  3. Due to Malware the fan runs faster too.

These issues will also lead to excess Disk Utilization. Hence we would request you to try the below steps to run the System Maintenance Troubleshooter:

a) Press Windows and X key together and select Control Panel.

b) Click on Troubleshooting.

c) Click on View all on the left side panel.

c) Click on System Maintenance and follow the onscreen instructions.

    2 Sound, Video and Game Controllers > Realtek High Definition Audio

    4 Browse My Computer for Driver Software...

    5 Next window choose "Let me pick from a list of device ..."

    11 Adjust microphone settings to your needs and you are good to go.

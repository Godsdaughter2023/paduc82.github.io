The extracted driver folder should be named Win_B57_x64_v17.2.0.0

This folder should contain the following three files:

b57nd60a.cat

b57nd60a.inf

b57nd60a.sys

Make note of where this folder is located so you can point Windows 10 to it when updating the driver.

Now we need to get to your device manager.

From the Search Box on your Task Bar you can type in Device Manager and press the Enter Key

or from within your Control Panel you can double left mouse click the Device Manager icon

or from your desktop you can right mouse click the This PC icon and select Properties from the drop down menu.

Once your inside the Device Manager, find the Network Adapters entry and double left mouse click it.

You should now see the Broadcom Netlink (TM) Gigabit Ethernet entry. Right mouse click this entry and select Update Driver Software... from the drop down menu.

This will pull up a window asking how do you want to search for driver software.

Select the second option Browse my computer for driver software.

You will now have a window that says Browse for driver software on your computer. At the bottom of this screen select Let me pick from a list of device drivers on my computer.

You will now have a window that says Select the device driver you want to install for this hardware. At the bottom right hand corner of this windows left mouse click the Have Disk... button.

You will now see a Install From Disk window. At the bottom right select the Browse... button and point it to the folder Win_B57_x64_v17.2.0.0. Now double left click the file within this folder called b57nd60a

This will take you back to the Install from disk window, left click the OK button.

You will now be at the Select the driver you want to install for this hardware window with a list of Model entries beginning with 3Com Dual Port 10/100/1000 PCI-X Server NIC. Scroll down to the entry called Broadcom Netlink (TM) Gigabit Ethernet and double left click it. You may now see a Update Driver Warning window, left click the Yes button to continue installing the driver.

If all goes well you should now see Windows has successfully updated your driver software, click the Close button on the bottom right corner of this window.

Viewing your Xbox One friends on your Xbox 360 friends list

With Xbox One, a friend is anyone whom you add to your friends list. Adding new friends on Xbox One does not require that they send a friend request first.

When you sign in to an Xbox One console, your Xbox 360 friends will automatically appear in your friends list, as well as your followers list. If you accept a new friend request sent from an Xbox 360 console, you will see them as a friend AND a follower on Xbox One. However, friends who have been added on Xbox One will not automatically show up on your Xbox 360 list. If you plan to play with them on your Xbox 360, you’ll have to send a friend request through your Xbox 360 console. Once they accept, you’ll then see them in your friends list on both of your consoles.

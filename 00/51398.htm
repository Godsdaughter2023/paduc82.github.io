a. user say:

Hi Marcin,

Thank you for your response.

I understand that you would like the device to ask for password if the device goes to sleep on its own, or whenever you press the power button.

The "Password policy" in PC settings controls the time it takes for the device to ask for password, whether the device went to sleep on its own (time out) or by pressing the power button. Additionally, the device does not recognize how the device was put to sleep (whether naturally or physically).

Therefore, if you would like the device to ask for password every time the power button is pressed, my recommendation is to set "Always require a password" in PC settings. If it is set to "Require a password after the display has been off for 5 minutes," your device will not require a password for 5 minutes after the screen goes off, regardless of how it did.

Thanks for your question, and I hope this helps!

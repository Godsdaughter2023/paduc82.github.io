Select B2:B20 and apply conditional formatting, with the formula option, using the formula

=B2=C2-1

Then Select C2:U20, and use CF/formula with

=OR(C2=B2+1,C2=D2-1)

and then for v2:v20, the formula

=V2=U2+1

If your entries in A are numeric and not strings (hard to tell) you could use just one formula for all of B2:V20

=OR(B2=A2+1,B2=C2-1)

You can tell if they are matched by going to My Music and looking for a Cloud icon on the right side of the item in the list. Anything with a cloud icon has been matched to an album in the marketplace. If you add these albums to a playlist, you should be able to play them on a playlist on your second device. Similarly, those albums should appear on your second device with a streaming icon, to indicate they are being streamed from the cloud.

  3. Show the app bar by right clicking or swiping up from the bottom of the screen.
  4. Click the Match Album Info button on the app bar.

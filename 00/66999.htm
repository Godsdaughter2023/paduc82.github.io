j. wake say:

Hi All,

I know this isn't quite the correct place to post this message but I thought I'd include the solution in case others encounter this issue and come looking.

Problem was on the firewall with SSL Decryption. Our setup is the Palo firewall deals with all internet traffic (including SSL) and client use internal domain CA certificate to connect to it (similar to a man in the middle attack).

As the Surface Hub was not on our corporate domain, it did not have our trusted internal CA certificate installed so all traffic was considered untrusted by the Palo and was being dropped.

We made a special rule eliminating the SSL Decryption requirement for the IP of the Hub and updates ensued. All good now.

Hope this helps others.

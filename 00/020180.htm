Not printing envelopes with Word 365 subscription on iMac. - Microsoft Community

hoosiernorth say:

Not printing envelopes with Word 365 subscription on iMac.

After searching this forum for answers to my problem and several hours of trial and error on my iMac, I discovered that there are three places that need to have the correct information entered to print envelopes with Word 365 subscription on iMac.

1) Open a word document then go up to the Apple menu bar. Click on "Word" (next to Apple logo), then click preferences, then click the "Print" icon, then make sure the "Drawing Objects" box is checked. It probably is checked but if it is not, the following steps will not work.

2) Go to the word document: Mailings Tab-Envelopes icon. When the "Envelope Address" box opens do the following two items to allow the proper envelope to print.

3) Go to the bottom of the "Envelope Address" box and click on "Page Set Up" box and then in the "Paper Size" box select your envelope size; ie...Envelope #10. Click "OK".

4) Again. go to the bottom of the same "Envelope Address" box and check the "Custom..." box and make sure your envelope size, ie..Envelope #10, is in the "Envelope Size" box. You can also set your printer orientation here.

Hope this helps someone.

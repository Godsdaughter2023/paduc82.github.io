20% smaller than 11 point would be 8.8 point which would round to 9 point (I imagine)
20% smaller than 8 point would be 6.4 point which would round to 6 point (I imagine)

So the ratio of sizes will go from 11/8=1.39:1 to 9/6 =1.5:1

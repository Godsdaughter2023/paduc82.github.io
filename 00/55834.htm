Hi AbhinandanGhosh,

How is your Dolby Atmos connected to your computer? If it's connected via HDMI, then please follow these steps to properly configure spatial sound for your home theater:

  1. On the bottom right-hand corner, right-click the speaker icon and select Playback devices.
  2. Select your speaker on the list and then click Properties.
  3. Click the Spatial sound tab, then select the Dolby Atmos for headphones from the drop-down options.
  4. If you don't have the Dolby Access app yet, the Windows Store will open automatically to download the app.
  5. Once the app is installed, click the With my home theater option, then click Continue.
  6. Open the Sound settings again by performing Step 1.
  7. Under Playback, select your Dolby Atmos speaker, and set it as your default device.
  8. On the Dolby Access app, click Configure PC settings, then click the Spatial sound tab.
  9. Under the Spatial sound format, select the Dolby Atmos for home theater option, click Apply, then OK.

  6. After the uninstallation is complete, reboot your computer.
  7. After you have successfully uninstalled the driver, please go to the computer manufacturer’s website, download the Windows 10 Display driver for your computer and check.

  9. Click on the driver tab and update the driver and restart your computer.

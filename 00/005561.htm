Hi Divyang,

 

To use In-Place Archive, you need to enable it for the mailbox. Please refer to:

https://technet.microsoft.com/library/jj984357(v=exchg.150).aspx

 

After that, you can follow the steps below to move 6 month old messages to archive:

1. Create a default retention tag.

  • a. Sign in EAC(Exchange Admin Center).
  • b. Navigate to compliance management>retention tag.
  • c. Click + and choose applied automatically to entire mailbox(default)
  •  
  • d. Type the tag name. Choose Move to Archive under Retention Action. Enter the Retention period. Then save.


2. Create retention policy includes this retention tag.

  • a. Navigate to retention policies and click +.
  • b. Type the name and click + under Retention tags. Then add the retention tag created previously. Then click OK.
  • c. A retention policy as follow is created.


Note that if you only want to move 6 month old messages to archive, this retention policy is applicable. If you have any other requirements, you need to add other corresponding retention tags to this policy. For you reference:
Retention tags and retention policies: https://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/library/dd297955(v=exchg.150).aspx

3. Apply this retention policy to the user.

  • a.Navigate to recipients>mailboxes and double click the user.
  • b.Choose mailbox features column and change the Retention Policy to the one created previously.


4. The retention runs automatically one time every seven days for mailboxes that are larger than 10 MB. To force the retention policy takes effect immediately, you can use Managed Folder Assistant.
a. Connect to Exchange Online using remote PowerShell.

If you choose to perform a Cutover migration, you need to disable the DirSync, completely remove the existing users in EOP but you can keep the verified domain (This is because cutover migrate will create users in your online tenant during the process). Differences between Cutover and Hybrid? Cutover only suits for small organization like fewer than 150 mailboxes and may generate a little business downtime but is easier and faster. Hybrid provides more smooth process but you will have additional configurations. Please check following article:

A compact formula.

Enter below formula in cell H2 & copy down & to the right:

=SUBTOTAL(1,OFFSET($B$1,(ROW()-ROW($H$2))*3,COLUMN()-COLUMN($H$2),3))

Currently Outlook does not support email notifications.
For that you would need a 3rd party application.

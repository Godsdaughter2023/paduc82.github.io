Every one of the blue screen errors shows that the crash appeared to be initiated by the Driver Verifier.

The Drive Verifier should not be enabled under normal circumstances and being enabled can cause skewed results such as a driver causing a blue screen error only with the Driver Verifier being enabled.

So I suggest to turn off the Driver Verifier:

Windows Key + R > type verifier.exe in the "Open" box > OK > Delete existing settings > Finish

    kevin ipaq114 say:

    I have got to the bottom of why those recurrning appointments didn't sync properly. 

    The problem occurs if you don't set locale/timezone/date/time before you do the FIRST sync with the PC.   The sync copies across all the meetings and appointments already in the PC calendar, and you then set the timezone/locale.  You can now see two typical types of errors for meetings and appointments .  

    1. A weekly Wednesday meeting with one occurrence changed to Tuesday incorrectly shows that occurrence as Wednesday

    2. A multi-day appointment - Subject: Holiday (I reckon we all have some of those) Start: Friday End: Monday incorrectly starts Thursday, ends Sunday.

    The "workaround" is to set the locale/timezone/date/time on the iPAQ BEFORE you do the frst sync with the PC.  If you do this, all the meetings and appointments seem to be correct.

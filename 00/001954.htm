An IMAP account type is limited to only sync email. It does not support synching of Calendar, Contacts or any other folder types.

To sync those folder types, you either need an Exchange Account type (available as Exchange Online within Office 365 for Business) or an Outlook.com account (available for free). You can use an Outlook.com account also with your current email address. For details see:
http://www.howto-outlook.com/howto/outlookcompersonaladdress.htm

For more information about what syncs and what doesn't within Office 365 see:
Office 365 and cloud based sharing and synching

Private Sub Worksheet_Change(ByVal Target As Range)
If Not Intersect(Target, [B1]) Is Nothing Then

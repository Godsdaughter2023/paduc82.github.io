Hi K-Moth,

 

Thank you for posting your concern in Microsoft Community.

Please be informed that retrieving your folders in Windows Live Mail is not available. What you can do is to check your emails in web and try to recover them. However, based from what you have stated, it seems that you alreadry tried to recover your deleted emails from Outlook.com and nothing was there. Outlook.com will recover as many messages as possible and put them in your Deleted folder. If you don't see the deleted messages that you wanted, it means they are permanently lost.

 

If some of your messages are available only in Windows Live Mail, then there's no way for us to retrieve them. To prevent this from happening again, I suggest that you back up your files.See How do I import and export email, contacts, and calendars with Windows Live Mail?

 

Meanwhile, if you are using POP account, please check if the setting to leave a copy of the email on the server is selected. To do it, click on mail account in Windows Live Mail, select Properties, then Server, lastly Delivery. If the option, ‘should not be set to delete email from the server’ then there should still be a copy of the emails available on the server. If not, those emails are unfortunately gone.

 

You can refer to these steps on how to recover lost or deleted emails in Live Mail:

 

1. Remove the account from Windows Live Mail.
1.1 Right click on the account as displayed on the folder pane.

1.2 Click Remove account.
1.3 Confirm the action by clicking Yes.

2. Add the account again.
3. Right click on the account as displayed on the folder pane.
4. Click on Download all folders.
5. Wait for Live Mail to download all your folders and email headers.
6. Check if all your folders have been downloaded. If it does, proceed to the next step, if not go back to step 1.
7. Press on F5 on your keyboard or click on Send and Receive button to synch all your emails to Windows Live Mail.

 

Note: The option will not take effect to lost/deleted emails. This is a preventive measure to avoid losing emails again.

 

Should you have further questions, please let us know.

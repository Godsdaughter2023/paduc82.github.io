How do you remove the white (or whatever color) background - Microsoft Community

ao_5926 say:

How do you remove the white (or whatever color) background from a picture you have inserted into powerpoint or word so that the pic floats.

How do you remove the white (or whatever color) background from a picture you have inserted into powerpoint or word so that the pic floats.
steve rindsberg say:
Click the picture to select it.  The picture formatting toolbar should appear automatically.
If not, choose View, Toolbars and make the picture formatting toolbar visible.

On the toolbar there's a transparency tool.  Click it, then click any part of the picture that's the color you want to make "go away".

Everything in the picture that's *exactly* that color will disappear, become see-through.  

That applies to the background and anything else in the picture ... the red rose in the middle of the floral display in the picture will go away when you get rid of the red background.  !

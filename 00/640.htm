apollo p say:

While that is true TerFar, I've seen this issue message come up when an addin is not allowing changes made to the template in use (though typically it's the normal.dot) to be processed.  The most common addin to do this is Adobe Acrobat's PDFMaker (usually version 7 or 8).

If that is the case Greg, I suggest you first close Word, then disable the addin by going to the registry and disabling it.

You can get there, by clicking on the Windows button, then typing in the search field, "regedit" (without the quotes) and hit Enter.

Browse to this location in the registry:

HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Office\Word\Addins

Click on the addin (I'm using PDFMaker as an example as I believe it is the likely culprit) and then click on LoadBehavior in the pane on the right.

Change the LoadBehavior from 3 (default for start every time) to 0 (zero, which is do not load).

Bringing you more control over your Skype status

We have heard your feedback on the Skype 8 Presence model. We have seen two major feedback themes emerge – (a) My contacts don’t see me as Active/Online even though I am (b) Give me more control on my presence. We are actively working on addressing them and would love your feedback on some of the changes we are planning.

My contacts don’t see me as Active even though I am

This by far has been the biggest feedback. We are addressing it immediately. We are currently testing the change in Skype Insiders and hope to roll it out to everyone shortly. Earlier, we marked you Inactive when you minimized the app. Going forward, your contacts will see you as Skype Insiders and hope to roll it out to everyone shortly. Earlier, we marked you Inactive when you minimized the app. Going forward, your contacts will see you as Active if the Skype app is running on your computer and you are either using the mouse or the keyboard. This change also aligns us with the Skype 7 behavior you are familiar with.

Give me more control on my presence

How we share our availability and intent to communicate with others has evolved significantly over time, thanks to the ‘always on’ world we have come to live in where each of us is connected 24X7 through multiple devices. The simplified Skype 8 Presence model was a step in that direction. Your feedback makes it clear that there are times when you would like more control on how your presence is represented in the Skype ecosystem.

We spent a lot of time learning from users what aspects of the current model worked or did not work too well. The result is a streamlined model with four states – Active, Recently Active, Away and Not Active. The table below summarizes what each state means, how it’s represented, and how you would transition among these states.

It means You are in the app. On Desktop: you have the app running in the background and are either using mouse or keyboard You were active less than an hour ago You were active more than an hour ago You are signed out, not reachable or have not been active for an extended period
Can you set the status Yes No Yes No

 If you are wondering where are ‘Invisible’ and ‘Do Not Disturb’, we have you covered. 

Hide my Presence – You will be able to turn off your presence from Settings. Once you turn off your presence, you will be marked Not Active. We want to foster an environment of trust and openness. So, if you hide your presence, you will not be able to see others’ presence.

Quiet Mode – Many users shared that they found the Do Not Disturb status rude and preferred to sign out to minimize interruptions. We plan to have a new feature that we believe is a subtler but better way to handle unwanted interruptions.  Quiet mode will silence all incoming messages and calls. If you would like to let your contacts know that you will not be taking calls or responding to messages, you can select the ‘Do not Disturb’ custom status. Just like before, you will have the flexibility to decide if you want to be alerted to incoming calls or messages. 

When these states are just not enough - Many of us use the status message in addition to the presence states to share more about our availability. We plan to make status messages an integral part of your presence on Skype so that your contacts will easily know if you are unreachable because you are on a vacation or should expect a delay in response as you are attending a conference. We plan to make these status messages intelligent in future so that you don’t have to remember to remove an old status message.

john w. vinson say:

Well, a Form cannot be used as a Recordsource: a form is just a display tool, not a data repository. Recordsources must be either a Table or a Query (either a named query or a SQL string).

Could you please post the steps of your macro, and the Recordsource properties of your forms?

One thing to note is that a Subform is not included in the Forms collection: e.g. if frmGetNotes is being used as a Subform, the reference Forms!frmGetNotes!controlname will not exist. You must instead reference it via the mainform:

Forms!frmMainForm!subformcontrolname.Form!controlname

where subformcontrolname is the Name property of the Subform Control on the mainform; this will often be the same as the name of the Form object within that control, but not necessarily.

a. user say:

Hi Joe,

Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Community.

I understand that Windows 10 mobile mail app does not sync and I will be happy to answer your query.

I would request you to perform a soft reset of phone and check.

The soft reset is the same thing as pulling your battery (on devices where that is possible). This method will not remove anything at all from your phone and is completely safe to attempt in any problem situation.

You perform the soft reset by having your phone turned on. Then press and hold the volume down + power buttons until your phone vibrates and restarts. Note that you should not touch the device screen during the process (not even when the 'slide down to power off' prompt appears).

Example of situations where the soft reset should solve the problem:

  • An app that usually starts won't start
  • The camera won't open
  • The device starts using too much battery
  • The device starts behaving oddly

Hope this information is helpful. Please feel free to reply in case you face any other issues with Windows in future.

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

You could use a macro such as the following:

If Selection.ShapeRange.Count = 1 Then
    ActiveDocument.Unprotect
    With Selection.ShapeRange(1)
        If MsgBox("Do you want to change the fill foreground color", vbQuestion + vbYesNo) = vbYes Then
            .Fill.ForeColor = RGB(InputBox("Enter the Red value in the range 0-255", , 25), InputBox("Enter the Green value in the range 0-255", , 0), InputBox("Enter the Blue value in the range 0-255", , 0))
        End If
        If MsgBox("Do you want to change the fill background color", vbQuestion + vbYesNo) = vbYes Then
            .Fill.BackColor = RGB(InputBox("Enter the Red value in the range 0-255", , 25), InputBox("Enter the Green value in the range 0-255", , 0), InputBox("Enter the Blue value in the range 0-255", , 0))
        End If
        If MsgBox("Do you want to change the line foreground color", vbQuestion + vbYesNo) = vbYes Then
            .Line.ForeColor = RGB(InputBox("Enter the Red value in the range 0-255", , 25), InputBox("Enter the Green value in the range 0-255", , 0), InputBox("Enter the Blue value in the range 0-255", , 0))
        End If
        If MsgBox("Do you want to change the line background color", vbQuestion + vbYesNo) = vbYes Then
            .Line.BackColor = RGB(InputBox("Enter the Red value in the range 0-255", , 25), InputBox("Enter the Green value in the range 0-255", , 0), InputBox("Enter the Blue value in the range 0-255", , 0))
        End If
    End With
    ActiveDocument.Protect wdAllowOnlyReading
End If

To be able to run it with the document protected, you would need to either assign in to a keyboard shortcut or add a button to run it to the quick access toolbar.

jay freedman say:

You can create the URL of a hyperlink like this...

Start with the Web address of Google Maps, https://www.google.com/maps/place/ and then add the physical address of the location with plus signs instead of spaces:

https://www.google.com/maps/place/1600+Pennsylvania+Ave+NW,+Washington,+DC

In some cases you can use a description of a place instead of a street address:

https://www.google.com/maps/place/New+York+City+Hall

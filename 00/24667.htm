Most computers come from their major manufacturers with some sort of Office installation.  If Office 2007, it was either a Trial installation or an OEM licensed installation.  If you did not pay the computer manufactuer for an Office 2007 license, then you received a Trial.  If you paid the computer manufacturer extra at the time of purchase for an Office 2007 license, then it was an OEM license.  The product key for an OEM license for Office 2007 is printed on the Office 2007 Certificate of Authenticity that is affixed to the plain white thin carboard sleeve that has the Office reinstallation discs.  So the answer is, if you paid for an OEM license, you already have the reinstallation discs for Office.

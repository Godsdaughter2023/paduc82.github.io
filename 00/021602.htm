roady [mvp] say:

No, the old backup add-in for Outlook 2003 and Outlook 2007 doesn't work at all with Outlook 2013. The Registry fix that can be used for Outlook 2010 doesn't work for Outlook 2013 as the reason that it is broken for Outlook 2013 is a completely different one.


The CodePlex add-in can indeed be used as an alternative but you can also just copy the pst-file to your backup location when Outlook is closed. As the default now is to place the pst-file in the Documents folder of the user, it is automatically included when you backup that folder.


Note that currently the backup add-in has some detection, filtering and displaying issues for the pst-files when there are also other accounts configured (especially when also Exchange accounts exist in the mail profile).

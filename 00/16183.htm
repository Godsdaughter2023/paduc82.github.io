john korchok say:

There is no setting to turn off the page background when printing, but here's a workaround. The steps assume you have set the background of the page. If you have set this in the Master Page, open the Master first:

  1. Copy the picture to go in the background into the same folder as your document.
  2. In Publisher, open your document.
  3. Choose PAGE DESIGN>Background>More Backgrounds...
  4. Set the fill to No Fill and OK out, erasing the previous background.
  5. Again, select PAGE DESIGN>Background>More Backgrounds...
  6. Choose Picture or texture fill, then click on File.
  7. Navigate to the folder where your document and the picture are. Click on the picture once to select it.
  8. Click on the down-pointing arrowhead beside Insert and choose Link to File.
  9. OK out of Format Background.

You document should look the same as before.

Now close the document, move the picture to a different folder or your desktop. Open the document and print. Because the linked picture is not present, it prints the document without a background. After printing, close the document without saving and move the picture back into the same folder. Reopen the document and the background displays again.

bob jones aka: cybertaz say:
Re #1: I'm assuming that by "text boxes" you actually mean Text Box Placeholders -- Placeholders cannot be included in Grouped Objects, neither on Masters nor on slides.

Re #2: Nothing on a slide can be sent behind anything on the Layout Master. The stacking order pertains to objects on a slide, it doesn't include the underlying design of the Layout. Put another way, you cannot use a Picture Placeholder to create a Slide Background... Placeholders are exactly that -- they are controls which hold a place for content to be put on the slide. They aren't a magic portal giving access to the design of the Layout Master.

A slide Background must be added/changed in one of 2 ways:
  • By putting the Background image on the Layout Master itself, or
  • By using the Format> Slide Background command

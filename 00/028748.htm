david scammell say:
Hi RocaminSomar,

Sometimes, Facebook takes a bit of time to sync. However, you can perform a manual on-demand sync instead of playing the waiting game. Here's how:

  • From the Start screen, swipe over to the left to access the app list
  • Scroll down until you find Settings, tap on it.
  • Tap on email+accounts 
  • Tap & hold on Facebook
  • When the little menu pops up, tap on Sync. 

This will trigger manual on-demand sync for Facebook and all the changes you’ve made, added or removing people, will not reflect on your device.

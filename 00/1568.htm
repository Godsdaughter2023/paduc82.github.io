teylyn say:
Hello,

the following formulas will ignore zero values:

={QUARTILE(IF(YTD!AF3:AF215>0,YTD!AF3:AF215),1)}
={MIN(IF(YTD!AF3:AF215>0,YTD!AF3:AF215))}
=AVERAGEIF(YTD!AF3:AF215,">0")
={MEDIAN(IF(YTD!AF3:AF215>0,YTD!AF3:AF215),2)}
=MAX(YTD!AF3:AF215)
={QUARTILE(IF(YTD!AF3:AF215>0,YTD!AF3:AF215),3)}

Note that the curly brackets are not typed into the cell, but automatically added by Excel when you confirm the formulas with Ctrl-Shift-Enter. These are array formulas and will only work when you use Ctrl-Shift-Enter to confirm them.

AverageIF and Max are normal formulas.

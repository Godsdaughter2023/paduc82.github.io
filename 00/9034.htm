The phone is designed to sync with the cloud.  When it comes to calendars and contacts this especially the case.  The first thing you should try to get access to this calendar on your phone is to add your Office 365 account to the phone.  You would add this by going to settings > emails + accounts > add account > Exchange.  Once the account is added any cloud based contacts and calendars should be brought to the phone.  If you do not find the calendar after adding the account, please tap the account listed in emails + accounts and make sure that the calendar sync option is selected.

Short answer: Yes. Longer answer: There are 3 ways to send a fax: (1) Over the Internet using an online fax service; (2) using a fax modem connected to an ordinary telephone line; (3) using a fax modem connected to your Internet connection. The "fax modem" used in methods 2 and 3 above itself has 3 options: (1) an internal fax modem (these are less and less commonly found); (2) an external fax modem, usually connected to the computer via USB; (3) an "All-in-One" printer/fax/copier/scanner device. Very often, if you use the fax modem that is part of an All-in-One device, you have to fax from the device itself rather than from any software installed on your computer. Some printer manufacturers may bundle fax software that allows you to send a fax from your computer through the All-in-One product.

    drlxy say:

    Ok I found a way to fix this error, I hope others who have this problem will find this message.

    The error message says to log into your account via browser to verify your account. What it fails to mention is that you need to "approve" the account access from google/gmail in the Recent Activity settings.

    1. Log into your account via browser
    2. Go into ACCOUNT SETTINGS
    3. Go into RECENT ACTIVITY
    4. Find one of the recent "Security Challenge" entries, and look for one that doesn't have your current IP address (it will be labelled "This is your current session").  The one you are looking for will have "United States" in the location field and the IP address will be something like 205.85.xxx.xxx which is google's server.  When you expand on that entry there are two buttons "This wasn't me" and "This was me"
    5. Click on "This was me" and you'll get a message about fewer interruptions in the future.
    6. Go back into Gmail and add the account, or try to save the settings again (I just removed it and added it back manually) and you're done!  You should have access again.

    I wish they made the error message a bit more specific, but either way I hope this works for you too.

rblack40,

I think the picture you refer to is in the paragraph headed Data Form (?) and shows a form called Customers, with data from record 47 of 91. For more information on that, have a look at the Excel Help page Add, edit, find, and delete rows by using a data form - search Help under data form.

That's just a start ... Even though this is not VBA, books (and other media) on that subject will be a valuable source for learning about controls and forms because they are used so frequently with VBA (as opposed to without).

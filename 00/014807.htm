Perfect! Got it!

In your file Temp_Humid.xlsx you have column A formatted perfectly for the conditional formatting you want.

All you have to do is to click on the letter A at the top of the column. That selects the entire column. Use Edit > Copy (or press Command-C) and the whole column gets the dotted lines around it to signify it will be copied.

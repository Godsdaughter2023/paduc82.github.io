Set the default path in Options:

Moving old notebooks:

  1. In Onenote let your mouse hover over the notebook in the left pane to bring up the path to the notebook (its location) and remember it
  2. Close the notebook (not Onenote)

  5. In Onenote use File, Open to re-open the notebook from the new location

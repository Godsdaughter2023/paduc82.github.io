While routers will slow down to the slowest "connection" that means on the same subnet.  If you had a computer that could only connect @100Mb, that subnet would only be 100Mb.  It has zero to do with your WLAN (internet connection/speed)

You could plug the printer directly into the pc could you not?

The amount of data a printer is sent (from a pc) is infinitesimally small even if you are printing continuously.  A printed page uses ~2-4Kb per page.  If your printer can print 10 pages per min that mean only 20-40 Kb per second on a 1,000,000 Kb  (1Gb) connection.  So you can see it is not the printer that is causing you to be slow.

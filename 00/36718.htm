FSX MULTIPLAYER

How can I fly on multiplayer with other simmers without using game spy? is there any other way? and the error I get is that I don't have a internet connection.

pieloth say:

Although Gamespy is closed, you can still use other multiplayer services as replacement, they do maintain multiplayer sessions lists with IP addresses.

You connect to those sessions as a LAN session and not as a Gamespy session, you can read instructions for that in FSX Learning Center (Multiplayer sessions).

And you can also propose a multiplayer session, you just need to know your public IP address to create a multiplayer session.

For instance, you can go on this well known multiplayer site:

http://www.fsopen.co.uk/

Also, you can use VATSIM or IVAO to fly "as in the real life", but this requires to add a plug-in to FSX to connect to those multiplayer sessions.

Browse also the AvSim and flightsim forums, there are tons of good info on any topic.

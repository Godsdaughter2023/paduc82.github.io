    I’m not sure what you mean by a lock macro.

    The procedure for locking parts of a document is to put in continuous section breaks between the areas to be locked and not locked to segregate the areas.

    On the Developer ribbon, select Restrict Editing.

    Under Editing Restrictions, check Allow only this type of Editing.

    Choose Filling in Forms from the drop down. You will then be presented with a list of section numbers with check boxes.

    Check the sections that you want restricted.

    Finally click on Yes, Start Enforcing Protection.

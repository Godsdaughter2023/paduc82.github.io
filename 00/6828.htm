mike h.. say:

Hi,

If you're getting a #REF! error then the part of the formula giving the error is in bold.

=Sum('Start 13 14:End 13 14'!C8)

You need to check the names of the worksheets called

Start 13 14

End 13 14

They must be exactly the same as in the formula and a very common mistake is additional spaces either at the front or end of the name or in fact double spaces with in the name. I suggest you do this for eacj of those 2 worksheets.

Right click the worksheet tab - rename - and then re-type each name and at the enter tap enter.

When you've done that then select the formula cell and tap F2 and enter to make it recalculate.

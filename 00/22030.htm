psychomania---01 say: A-GPS is a separate feature to nokia maps so it is not controlled by the settings in that program.

A-GPS kicks in and connects to the server whenever any program that uses the GPS is started, even apps like google maps or nokia friend view.

To disable it:
Menu > Tools > Settings > General > Positioning > Positioning Methods > Highlight Assisted GPS > Options >Disable.

If you find that your phone no longer gets a quick satellite fix or the navigation becomes unreliable then you will need to turn AGPS back on.

If that is the case you may wish to buy a data bundle from your network to avoid the extra charges. Or you could even switch to a network with inclusive data.

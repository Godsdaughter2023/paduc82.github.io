How can I eject a cd photo disk - Microsoft Community

tmartink say:

How can I eject a cd photo disk

How can I eject a photo CD?
james vinyard say:

With the drive attached to the RT, there are two methods if the disc is not jammed:

 

1. hit the eject button on the faceplate of the CD drive.

2. click on the Desktop and open the File Manager and you will see the attached CD drive on the drive list.  Right-click on it and select 'Eject".  Touchscreen, press and hold down and you will see a square and release and the "mouse" menu will appear and select 'Eject'.

 

If the disc is jammed in the drive, un-jack the CD drive from the RT and get a paper clip.  Bend forward one end of the paperclip and locate the "emergency eject" on the CD drive.  That will be a small pinhole located just below the CD tray cover plate.  Insert the paperclip into that pinhole level with the drive until you feel resistance and then push forward.  The tray will pop open and you can remove the disc.

mac_freak say: Hello.

Bluetooth supports only one active connection. What it means is that even though you have paired and connected another Bluetooth device with your phone beforehand, only one of these devices are capable of sending data streams to the phone or vice-versa. That explains why after a call using the Bluetooth headset, the map returns to its original functionality. You can never have two simultaneous Bluetooth active connections at the same time.

Cheers.

tao980 say: Perhaps there is no data stream between the GPS receiver and the N80 while in a call using the bluetooth headset, however, and this is important, the connection is not lost, i.e. the blue led on the LD-3W is still blinking. If the connection would be lost, a message would pop up immediatly saying "no GPS". So the connections are simultaneous indeed, but not the data transfer.

tao980 say: @mac_freak:Look what I have just found in the FAQ section of the Nokia N80 support pages:

Q: On my device, can there be several active Bluetooth connections at a time?

A: Yes. For example, if you are connected to a headset, you can also transfer files to another compatible device at the same time.
Note: Some active connection might limit establishing another connection. E.g. if your phone has an active connection to headset you can not connect your phone simultaneously to car kit.

So it appears to be a problem with car kits only, this is why I have no trouble with headset and GPS receiver. I hope this sheds some light on this matter.

drejv say:

This is the problem.  you are trying to do a backup of an Orange to a Banana.  The  N97 data is in Symbian format (file structures, attributes,etc) and the N900 is in...maemo.   The only thing you can do is use phone switch.

 

PC suite 7.1.40.1 (parts of it) work wonderfully.

Content Copier - Does not work.  Use Backup on N900.

Sync works wonderfully

One touch access - yes in USB mode, no in bluetooth

Contacts- Works

Communication center - Does not work

Calendar- works wonderfully

File Manager - works

Image store - Does not work

Video Manager -  Not required do not need to convert video for N900

Application installer - not required

Software updater - Works

Map Loader- works

 

Updates to pc suite are comming

el_loco---01 say:

No way to boost power of FM transmit.

 

I guess you could hook up a wired headset and that may improve the antenna performance (maybe, not 100% sure).

 

Check that you use a frequency that has nothing being tranmitted. A white noise frequency many times is a sideband of a station. 

 

Example: If there's a strong radio station at 95.3 Mhz, then you can't use 95.2 or 95.4 even though when you set these frequencies they appear to be empty.

 

Try to use freqencies closer to the middle of the band to get the best tuning. So something close to 90 Mhz

 

Other than that there's nothing you can do. 

 

I've tested (and owned) at least 3 different FM TX accesories and they all have different problems. Overall they all sound really bad. This is also because FM is low quality audio anyways.

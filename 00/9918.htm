Hi Gavius,

As I described above, The primary data center is determined by your country or region which your admin inputs during the initial setup of the services. The place where they purchase licenses won't determine the destination where data is stored, and the place where the tenant registered does. In your customer's case, if you mean they would like to download and activate desktop Office 365 suite in China with their account whose region is in Singapore, it is available to do it.

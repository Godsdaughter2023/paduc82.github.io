tigerfan08 wrote:
Hi, I have an N95 8Gb, and a Motarola 820 Bluetooth wireless headset. I was wondering, is there any way of getting the radio to work with the bluetooth headset connected?


Not the traditional FM radio. The wire of the headset you'd plug into the phone acts as the FM antenna and without that there would be no reception. Also, the output of the tuner doesn't pass through any digital circuitry, it is sent straight to the audio amplifier and therefore no A/D conversion can take place in order to get the data to send via bluetooth.

On the other hand, if you have a data plan for the phone and/or a wireless LAN at home, you can listen to radio stations that stream over the Internet in RealAudio format. Since the audio is already in a digital form, sending it elsewhere over a bluetooth connection is not a problem.

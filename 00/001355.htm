hansv mvp say:

IFERROR, COUNTIFS and SUMIFS are new functions introduced in Excel 2007. They are not available in Excel 2003 or earlier versions.

 

You can replace IFERROR(some_expression, "") with IF(ISERROR(some_expression), "", some_expression)

You can replace an expression such as

COUNTIFS($F$2:$F$9999,$I7,$E$2:$E$9999,"<="&$J$2)

with

SUMPRODUCT(($F$2:$F$9999=$I7)*($E$2:$E$9999<=$J$2))

and an expression such as

SUMIFS($G$2:$G$9999,$F$2:$F$9999,$I7,$E$2:$E$9999,"<="&$J$2)

with

SUMPRODUCT($G$2:$G$9999*($F$2:$F$9999=$I7)*($E$2:$E$9999<=$J$2))

c. Click the Content tab.

d. Click Clear SSL state.

If you configure security settings to be too restrictive, you may prevent Internet Explorer from displaying certain Web sites. To determine whether an issue is caused by overly restrictive security settings, revert to default security settings. To do this, follow these steps:

c. Click the Security tab.

 a. Press Windows key + R and then type iexplore -extoff without quotes and press ENTER.

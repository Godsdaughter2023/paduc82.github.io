Hi Ken,

There are several ways you can add a photo to your email. However, the most reliable method is to save the photo to your PC's file storage then insert it to your email message. Should you be using Outlook.com, follow the steps below:

  1. Open the message or calendar event.
  2. Select the picture icon to insert a picture from your computer or OneDrive.
  3. Choose the picture you want to insert and select Open (if you chose a picture from your computer) or Insert (if you chose a picture from OneDrive).
  4. Drag the corner handles to resize the image. Note: Resizing an image doesn't reduce its file size. To learn how to reduce the file size of a picture, see Reduce the file size of a picture.
  5. Right-click the image to see formatting options.

If you're using an Outlook app, you can refer to this support article on how to insert photos to your email message. Follow the steps based on your Outlook version.

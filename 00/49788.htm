vanessa yar say:

Hi Max,

Your USB headset and your speaker need to be configured properly in order for you to use them at the same time. To do that, please follow the steps below:

  1. In the taskbar, right-click the Sound icon and click Sounds.
  2. Under the Playback tab, do a right-click on your speaker and click Set as Default Device.
  3. Click Apply, then OK.
  4. Open the Sound window again.
  5. Under the Playback tab, do a right-click on your headset and click Set as Default Communication Device.
  6. Move to the Recording tab.
  7. Right-click Microphone and click Properties.
  8. Click the Listen tab and check the box for Listen to this device.
  9. Under Playback through this device, select your headset from the list.
  10. Click Apply, then OK.

Once done, please check if both devices are working at the same time.

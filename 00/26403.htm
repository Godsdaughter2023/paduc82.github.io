For most Office 365 subscriptions we cannot install Office 2016 with Office 2013. When Office 2016 is installed Office 2013 will be removed. talking moose say:

Is it downloading mail data from your server? The nature of Outlook for Mac is to synchronize all mail with your server. If you have 10 GB of mail in your Exchange account then it will download 10 GB of mail.

You can put Outlook into headers only mode for the account. This should download the subject lines and dates but won’t download any mail until you actually go to view it. Choose Outlook menu >  Preferences > Accounts > your Exchange account > Advanced > Server tab and enable “Download Headers Only”.

Hope this helps!

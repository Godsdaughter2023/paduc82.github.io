You may be able to get into safe mode on a non booting computer by starting the boot and holding down the power button for 5 seconds to stop the boot and turn off the computer.  Do this two times and on the third attempt you will be given advanced options including safe mode

bcdedit /set {default} bootmenupolicy legacy>enter>reboot

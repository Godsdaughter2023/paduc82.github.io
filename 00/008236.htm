stefan blom say:
Actually, the easiest way to perform calculations in a table is to embed an Excel spreadsheet in your Word document.

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

If you put the selection is in the table, running a macro with the following code will insert the required product into the last cell in column E

Dim i As Long, j As Long
Dim Product As Single
Dim crange As Range
j = Selection.Tables(1).Rows.count
If j Mod 5 = 1 Then
    Product = 1
    With Selection.Tables(1).Columns(5)
        For i = 5 To j - 1 Step 5
            Set crange = .Cells(i).Range
            crange.End = crange.End - 1
            Product = Product * Val(crange)
        Next i
        .Cells(j).Range.Text = Product
    End With
Else
    MsgBox "The table does not contain a multiple of 5 rows plus one row."
    Exit Sub
End If

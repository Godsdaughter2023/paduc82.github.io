'mike p say:

Do you know why these processes show as suspended? Who or why are they suspended?.

Those suspended items, their configurations are locked in the background and memory/cpu usage is suspended to save power, ram, cpu cycles - this speeds up restarting those programs, speeds up going into sleep and waking, as there is no need to fully do all the checks and balances and configurations as needed when starting for the first time.   The suspended processes/programs open real quickly when needed - in the meantime, they save power and heat.  They are in-active yet still in the background.

The port settings are available if you wanted to add your Outlook.com accounts to a mail program. 

The port settings are available if you wanted to add your Outlook.com accounts to a mail program using pop3 protocol.  Pop3 protocol will only download Inbox emails.  There is no synchronization of folders and emails with the server.

Can anyone tell me what's going on here - why my tablet keeps resetting the page breaks from the laptop's version of the program?  Even more important, can anyone please provide a solution to this problem?  What do I need to do to the tablet version of Excel to avoid having to reset the column contents and page breaks every time I use it on the tablet?  I often take the tablet with me when I travel and update the spreadsheet during the trip.  As you can imagine, this is really annoying when I have to reset column contents and page breaks whenever I switch computers.  It is to the point where I am seriously considering replacing the tablet because the resetting takes a lot of time away from my actual work on the spreadsheet.  The concern with that option, however, is what if the new laptop does the same thing?

andreas killer say:
Don't use the Page Layout and Page Break Preview, this views are scaled using the printer driver of the OS.

That's the reason why different devices have different pages breaks.

If you use the Normal view you don't have this issue.

Andreas.

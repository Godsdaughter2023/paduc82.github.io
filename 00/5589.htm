If you're trying to acquire a reputation for your installer in order to distribute it to others, then the appropriate thing to do would be to purchase a certificate and digitally sign the installer package as well as the app itself.

It's preferable to purchase an Extended Validation Certificate, though these cost more, since Microsoft allows these to acquire the reputation of the earlier apps distributed by this same certificate, removing the delay normally required to distribute a significant number (typically in the thousands) of that specific download to gain a reputation.

Microsoft SmartScreen & Extended Validation (EV) Code Signing Certificates – IEBlog

Partnering with the industry to minimize false positives - Microsoft Security

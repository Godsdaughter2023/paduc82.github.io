Outlook CSV File by Category - Microsoft Community

david thornburg say:

Outlook CSV File by Category

Outlook used to export contacts by category.  Which means that if a person was assigned to more than one category they were entered twice.  Outlook 2016 no longer does this after a recent update.  

I am a general contractor and some contacts serve multiple areas.  It was nice to have it so that all I had to do was sort by the category.  Now, with the categories combined it has screwed up everything and now takes me much longer to sort the excel file. Because I have to go in a manually separate each contact that has multiple categories.

How do I get it to do that again?

david thornburg say:

In Outlook when you have the category view, you simply have to have the columns in the order you want them:

Name:    Company:    Address:    Etc:

Then, since Outlook displays the contacts multiple times for each category, its simply cut and paste into excel.

To do the whole list Ctrl+A, Ctrl+C and then paste into your excel sheet.

For just one category right click on the category header, select copy and then paste into your excel.

steve rindsberg say:

As Shyam says, there's no native shape/object locking feature in PowerPoint.  If you can be certain that your add-in will remain loaded and active, it can trap the selection change event and in the event handler, determine whether the current selection is a shape or slide and if so, is it one that you've deemed protected. 

Depending on what you're after, the simplest way to indicate that a slide/shape is protected is to add one or more Tags to it.

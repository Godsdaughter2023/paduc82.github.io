what does "Units" refer to in MS Project? - Microsoft Community

prasadnk say:

what does "Units" refer to in MS Project?

I have read about  the term "Fixed units" many a time.

Does unit refer to "time" ? 


Also, there is a formula -     Work= Duration *  Resources

Is there any formula, where the term "units" is involved ?
john - project say:
prasadnk,
In Project "units" is basically a warm body, or full-time equivalent person for a labor type resource or a quantity of material (e.g. boxes of nails) for a material type resource. For example, the Fixed Units task type means that the number of labor resources (people) or quantity of material assigned to the task should remain fixed while duration and work hours vary according to the work equation

Project's work equation is usually expressed as:
Duration (hours) = Work (hours) / Units (number of full time equivalent people or quantity of material)

Some of the non-Microsoft literature may use the term "resource" in  place of "units" but that can be confusing, as you can attest, because the terminology is not consistent with terms used in the application.

Hope this helps.

John

Editing the local group policy will fix this.

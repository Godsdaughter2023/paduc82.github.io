formula for loan interest - Microsoft Community

joe576 say:

formula for loan interest

Is there a formula that will give you total interest on a loan with the following details:

Borrow amount $1000

4% interest rate compounded monthly

Payments made once a month 12 times a year.

Loan duration is 60 months (5 years)

Thank you.

hansv mvp say:

You can use the CUMIPMT (CUMulative Interest PayMenT) function for this:

=CUMIPMT(monthly rate, number of periods, loan amount, start period, end period, type)

With your data:

=CUMIPMT(4%/12,60,1000,1,60,0)

This returns -$104.99 (what you receive is +; what you pay is -)

Alternatively: the monthly payment (principal + interest) is

=PMT(4%/12,60,1000)

Total payment is

=60*PMT(4%/12,60,1000)

This returns -$1,104.99

Of this amount, -$1,000.00 is the loan that you paid back, and -$104.99 is the interest.

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

You need to have the numbering for the second level set to restart after the first level numbering.

See the article "How to create numbered headings or outline numbering in your Microsoft Word document" on the following page of the Late Shauna Kelly's website:

stefan blom say:

Click in the first level 1 item. On the Home tab, click Multilevel List | Define New Multilevel List. In the multilevel list dialog box, you may have to click More to see all option.

The easiest fix is to recreate the numbering for level 2, by adding the current level number from the "Number style for this level" drop down and the level 1 number from the "Include level number from" drop down. Type in the period. Remember to restart after level 1.

For more, see http://shaunakelly.com/word/numbering/numbering20072010.html.

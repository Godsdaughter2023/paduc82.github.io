bernie deitrick say:

Use VLOOKUP on sheet 2 - in cell D2, enter the formula:

=VLOOKUP($A2,Sheet1!$A:$E,COLUMN(),False)

or, if the name really is Sheet 1 (with a space)

=VLOOKUP($A2,'Sheet 1'!$A:$E,COLUMN(),FALSE)

Copy that down and to the right, and you're done.

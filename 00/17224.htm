It seems that Excel does not like two values for the same cell(s) so I changed the original formula to display text either "OK" or "Too Low" and this works.  You learn something every day!  The formula I used is:

andreas killer say:
Yes, there are several ways to accomplish that, but what you say and what your picture shows is different.

If you write 50% into a cell, Excel automatically divide 50 by 100, means the cell value becomes 0.5

In general: If you want to have percent values in a formula write the % also. In some fields in excel the same happens as in the cell, after you click Ok Excel divides by 100 and shows the value.

That's the reason why the conditional formatting "Cell Value" shows 0.9 instead of 90%.

hi frank,

to share a specific worksheet, you can use the following workaround:

1. right-click on the tab of the worksheet you want to share. if you want to share more than one worksheet, hold down the ctrl key & click each one.

2. click on move or copy.

3. in the to book: select (new book) and check the create a copy. click ok.


the worksheet/s will now be opened in a separate workbook with a default name, like book1.

4. in this workbook, click on file > share > email > send as attachment.

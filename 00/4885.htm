This is the default for POP accounts. In Outlook 2016, click File>Account Settings>Account Settings. Double-click your account and choose "More Settings". Click the Advanced tab and check the box labeled "Leave a copy of messages on the server".

If you are using IMAP instead of POP, messages are always left on the server unless you choose to delete them in Outlook 2016, in which case they'll also be deleted on the server because that's how IMAP works.

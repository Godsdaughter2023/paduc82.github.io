jeeped say:
First, it would seem that the disk that was used to originally install your copy of Office 2007 was of the hybrid variety. Normally, a different disk is required to install the various flavors of MS Office (e.g. Home and Business, Professional, Volume License, etc). The hybrid disk allows installation of any of the flavors and the product key is used to determine which flavor is installed.

On the point of the message that it is now installing Office Hybrid, this can occur if some feature of the MS Office 2007 has been corrupted or partially updated. This can be as simple as a missing system font or the final process of an Office update. If the installation completes and you do not see it again, I believe you can reasonably assume that the MS Office 2007 installation has been repaired. If the message keeps reappearing whenever you start an Office 2007 application, try the Repair process on your Office 2007 installation from Control Panel, Programs and Features.

nsilmike say:

If I understood you correctly, then I cannot explain the 2.1GB of reserved space....IF you sync that playlist automatically, and not manually.  Here's what I'd do, if I were you, to make sure there is not some problem.

1) go into settings->device->sync options.  change the sync mode for music to 'items I choose'  and the sync mode for podcasts to 'all' or choose.  (all if you sync all of them, choose otherwise.)

2) connect your zune, and go into the software settings, and erase all.  

3) reboot your zune, restart the software and connect the zune.

4) Drag the playlist down to the zune icon in the lower left after the zune is recognized.  This makes it a sync group.  In this mode, any changes you make to that playlist on the PC will be synchronized to the device at the next sync.  this includes adding and removing music from the device to keep that playlist sync'd.

5) If you chose all for podcasts, you're all set with them, except...

5a) in the software, collection view for podcasts (collection... not device) look for episodes marked 'in collection' vs. automatic.  In collection means you've downloaded them manually and they will sit forever or until you delete them.  And, they will sync or not per your sync series settings, plus will categorize as guest or unmanaged (aka reserved space) content.

5b) if you chose 'choose' mode for the podcasts, right click the podcast icons and 'sync with zune' or drag them down to the zune icon to set them as sync groups.

Now, if you get a large reserved space, it would indicate some sort of problem, unless you have a bunch of 'in collection' podcast episodes.

Same answer as first attempt except use this macro to insert alternate columns.

Sub Insert_Columns()
Application.ScreenUpdating = False
 With ActiveSheet
        LastCol = .Cells(1, .Columns.Count).End(xlToLeft).Column
            For J = LastCol To 1 Step -1
                .Columns(J + 1).Insert

    End With
Application.ScreenUpdating = True
End Sub

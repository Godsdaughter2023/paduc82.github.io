arminatorx say:

As long as you make one of the account your home account, then it should work.

You can't make an "account" a home account.

An account can set a specific console as that account's Home Console. 

That way, this "Home Console" can share all games and memberships like Live Gold and Game Pass games with any account playing on that console. 

So it's not an account that is a home account, but a console that an account sets as a Home Console.

hillelswiller say:

Duplex Printing: Microsoft Word on Brother printer L2300D

I can print double sided pages from the web on my Brother L2300D but not from Microsoft Word.  There seems to be no Duplex print option in word.  Do you know how to do this?

Thanks

bob jones aka: cybertaz say:

Like most print functionality, duplex is a feature of the printer, not the program in which the document is created. IOW, Word doesn't make the determination as to whether the content is printed on 1 side of the paper or both.

You should find the necessary settings for duplex [or 'double-sided'] in the Print dialog... Usually on one of the panels in the Copies & Pages dropdown menu. It can vary depending on the specific printer driver involved. With my Epson, for example, Two-Sided Printing Options is an explicit selection in that menu... For your printer that may not be the case.

a. user say:

Hi countrydancer,

 

Welcome to Microsoft Community and we are happy to assist you with this. It seems that there's a social networking site connected to your account, and its profile picture is reflecting on your outgoing messages.

 

As for our initial troubleshooting step, we advise that you follow these:

  1. Sign in to Outlook.com.
  2. Click the cog wheel icon then select More mail settings.
  3. Under Reading email, click on Content from third-party networks.
  4. Select Don't show public content from third-party networks.
  5. Click the Save button.
  6. Try sending a test email and let us know the result.

 

If the issue still persists, try changing the profile picture associated with your account:

  1. Login to https://profile.live.com/details/Edit/Pic and type your email address/password.
  2. Once on the Profile page, click Change picture found below your profile picture.
  3. Click Choose file, select your desired picture, and then click Open.
  4. Click Save.

 

Let us know if the above steps worked for you. If it didn't, provide us the exact steps on how you're trying to remove the photo.

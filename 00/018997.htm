john guin say:

In OneNote 2010 (and I think 2007 but I don't have a machine handy to double check) you can right click on paragraph 5(a) and "Copy link to paragraph."

Then you can use that link for "larceny" back in 1(a).  Highlight the word "larceny," then hit CTRL+K to open the hyperlink dialog and paste the copied link into that dialog.

Now when you click "larceny" OneNote will navigate to paragraph 5(a).

Oh, and this tip works across sections or notebooks - no just on the one page.

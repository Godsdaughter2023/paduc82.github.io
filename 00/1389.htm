Yes, the tiles are ugly... and the reason why?  Because there is nothing cohesive about them.  A grid of icons aren't very cohesive either, but it's less "in your face" when it's not solid blocks of mismatched colors.  It gets even worse when you add full-tile graphics and the "flip" animations all over the place... It looks like you have banner ads peppered throughout your program launcher.

  • Ability to force tiles to a specific color (including transparent).  You could colorize all of your work applications blue and all of your games green, or just color everything orange.   • Adjust the spacing between tiles - it's still excessive for mouse use
  • Show the application name in small tiles
  • Create new sizes: small height with medium and large width options.  This would let the tiles flow more like a list rather than a grid.

To make the text on your screen larger or smaller

  1. Open Screen Resolution by clicking the Start button , clicking Control Panel, and then, under Appearance and Personalization, clicking Adjust screen resolution.

    • Smaller - 100% (default). This keeps text and other items at normal size.

    xircal say:

    When the machine boots to the desktop, hit CTRL+ALT+DEL and then click "Switch User" from the menu.

    If that doesn't work, do the following:

    1. Right click the Start button and click System.
    2. Click System Protection in the left hand menu and then click the System Restore button.
    3. Choose a Restore point prior to making the registry change and then click Next. If you can't see enough Restore points, checkmark the box to show more.
    4. Follow the prompts from there and the PC will reboot and wind back the clock to the time you chose to restore to i.e. before making the registry change.

    System Restore can take around 15 minutes or so to run during which time the screen will remain totally black.  It's important that you don't interrupt this process otherwise you risk corrupting the operating system.

    Eventually though, it'll boot to the familiar lock screen and you'll be able to login again normally.

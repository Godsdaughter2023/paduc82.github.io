Sync 3rd party downloads in video hub - Microsoft Community

vknbie say:

Sync 3rd party downloads in video hub

I 've downloaded videos from youtube using apps from marketplace. The videos are present in the videos hub but unable to sync with PC. Is there any way to sync the files which are downloaded by app
toddallcock say:

No, third party apps can't sync their files with Zune. it's a limitation of WP7's "sandbox" security model. (The data from one app can't play in another app's sandbox.)

 

Any syncing/sharing off the phone has to be handled by the app itself, usually via Dropbox, Skydrive or email.

 

In the case of Youtube videos, I find it much easier to just use a YouTube downloading app on my PC, save it in .mp4 format in a folder monitored by Zune (e.g. the PC's My Videos folder). Then you can just right-click it in Zune, select "Sync with (your device name)" and the video will sync the next time the device syncs via USB or WiFi.

One 365 we have 2 x connectors: inbound and outbound. The outbound one has successfully validated.

On-Premises we have 5 x receive connectors per sever: Client Frontend, Client Proxy, Default Frontend, Default, Outbound Proxy Frontend. All on different ports obviously. The one listening on 25 is "Default Frontend".

Usually the flow should be:

Exchange online > on-premises exchange > mailserver.recipient.com 

What is happening is:

Exchange online > on-premises exchange > CANNOT RELAY > Exchange online

...but not for all emails, and this is the thing that is causing the problems. Any thoughts on how we can troubleshoot this further?

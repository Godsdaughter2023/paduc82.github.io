lacrumb say:

This is something I tried and it worked for me.

To reinstall after recovery - Get earlier build

Go Settings, Updates, Recovery, Go back to earlier build.  This will take some time but should give you the earlier build.

When it is complete then right click Start, click on Run and enter regedit and hit enter.  This takes you to the registry.

Navigate to Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\WindowsSelfHost\Applicability

Under EnablePreviewBuild change the Dword to 1 or 2 now

Also if under Applicability there is RecoveredFrom.  You will find a build number.  Change the Dword to 0

Then get out and go to Settings, Updates, Check for updates.

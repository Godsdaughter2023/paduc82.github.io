stefan blom say:

If you are seeing entries such as "Normal + Arial 10 pt" in the Styles and Formatting task pane, note that these aren't really styles but a style + direct formatting applied to text.

To clear this formatting: Right-click the entry in the Styles and Formatting pane and use the Select All Instances command. You can then use Ctrl+Q (to reset paragraph formatting) and Ctrl+SpaceBar (to reset character formatting) from the selection.

If you are saying that new, blank documents have undesired formatting (and/or content) in them, see the article at http://word.mvps.org/FAQs/AppErrors/BlankDocNotBlank.htm.

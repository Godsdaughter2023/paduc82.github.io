christopher miller say:

Hi,

If you can't send a SMS message or make any calls, then the problem may be that the SIM card is not right or not configured correctly.  Can you take the phone to your mobile provider and have them put a new SIM card in and verify that it works?  They should also check the APNS and MMS APNS settings, if that is applicable for your phone and mobile operator.

The other alternative would be to do a hard reset on the phone and basically start over.  It's less drastic to do than it sounds, but I would have the SIm card and APN settings checked out first.

Another thing to try would be to temporarily swap SIM cards with your husband.  If your card works in  his phone, then the problem is not with the SIM card.  In that case, I would try the hard reset.  If that does not resolve the problem, then take the phone in to be checked for a hardware fault.

To reset your phone

  1. In the App list, tap Settings .

  2. Tap About, and then tap Reset your phone.

  3. You'll receive two warnings. If you're absolutely sure you want to restore your phone to its factory settings, tap Yes, and then tap Yes again.

Be careful. Resetting your phone will erase all content stored on your phone, including apps and games (along with app data and game progress), text messages, call history, music, photos, and more.

tedfritchlee say:

RESOLVED> I had the same issue. Modifying the HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\.html default value to htmlfile instead of FirefoxHTML fixed the issue.

Bad entry:

[HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\.html]
"Content Type"="text/html"
"PerceivedType"="text"

Corrected entry:

[HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\.html]
@="htmlfile"
"Content Type"="text/html"
"PerceivedType"="text"

1. Put the Windows 7 installation disc into the disc drive or boot from your USB thumb drive, and then start the computer.

chris cupler say:

ed, that's a feature that's been asked for for years.  Media Center has always functioned how you're describing here.
Will it ever be changed?  Who knows.  But, to be honest, if it ever is, it most likely won't be in this version of Windows.  Sorry.

The closest thing to a workaround that I can suggest:  (assuming, here, that you have at least two tuners in the PC) rather than change the channel, record the second channel.  That way, you can finish watching the first program, and then start watching the second (and, from its start) when you're ready.

Chris

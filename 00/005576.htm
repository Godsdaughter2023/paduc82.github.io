mdimport32 -g "/Applications/Microsoft Outlook.app/Contents/Library/Spotlight/Microsoft Outlook Spotlight Importer.mdimporter" -d1 /Users/<user_name>/Library/Group\ Containers/UBF8T346G9.Office/Outlook/Outlook\ 15\ Profiles/Main\ Profile

The first thing I would suggest you do is make sure your computer is fully up to date.  You can check for available updates here, http://update.microsoft.com/microsoftupdate.  Be sure you click the link that reads "check online for updates from Microsoft update". 

 

Next, review your computer storage for any files you no longer need.  We suggest you start with the temporary files, click start > type %temp% in the search box > press enter > delete all files in this folder that can be deleted.  You may also want to look for pictures, music, documents, etc.. that is no longer needed.  Also, connect your phone to the computer > go to the Zune software and click settings > phone > reserved space > set the slider to 0%.  Once you have done this, disconnect your phone and restart your computer.

bhringer say:
Malwarebytes has detected 2 registry keys with pup.optional in them. It says no action was taken. Why not? Can I go into the registry and delete those keys?

 No action was taken because default setting for detected PUPs is Warn user about detections.

You can change this in Settings>Detection and Protection>Non Malware Detections and set PUP (Potentially Unwanted Programs) Detections to Treat detections as malware.

You can then rescan and have Malwarebytes remove them or if you feel comfortable editing the registry go ahead and remove manually.

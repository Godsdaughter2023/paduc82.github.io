Microsoft Office 2013 Click-to-Run is a streaming and virtualization technology that greatly speeds up Office's download and installation. Using streaming technology, Click-to-Run lets you start using an Office 2013 app before the entire program or suite is downloaded. Click-to-Run does this by prioritizing which parts of the application get downloaded and installed first. Similar to how you can begin watching a video that is being streamed to your computer, Office 2013 streams the pieces that are used to launch Office first, so you can start to access your desired Office app while the remainder of its components continues to download.

 

Office setup interface continues with the initial streaming download. Priority is given to elements of Office that you use during this download; hence if you are trying to save the download file then you will see the size of the file as very small.

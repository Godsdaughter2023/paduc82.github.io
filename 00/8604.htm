jay freedman say:
The "Ctrl Click to Follow Hyperlinks" option only says whether or not you need to hold down Ctrl while clicking a hyperlink to make it go to the destination.

Instead, you need to click Tools > AutoCorrect Options > AutoFormat As You Type, and check the box for "Internet and network paths with hyperlinks". That's what controls whether typed-in URLS get converted to hyperlinks. And as Stefan said, that conversion doesn't happen on just a paste; you have to type a space or punctuation at the end of the URL to make the AutoFormat take effect.

macropod say:

In that case, it's not clear why you want the dropdown in the header, but the results in the footer. Unless you configure your document with a 'different first page' layout, whatever's in the first page header will appear on the other pages also.

 

That said, if you bookmark a content control dropdown or combobox in the page header, you can insert a cross-reference to the bookmark in the page footer. If you use a  'different first page' layout, you'll need to insert that cross-reference on both the first and second pages. The only thing to note about this is that changes to the content control selection won't automatically appear in the footer - you'll need to do a print preview (or a printout) to force the cross-reference to update.

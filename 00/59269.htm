How to: Import OE address book into Outlook
This procedure assumes you have already installed Outlook on your PC and have set up your e-mail account.
1.Choose File, Import and Export…
2.You will see a dialog box with several options,  select the last option in the list: "Import Internet Mail and Addresses"

A file explorer dialog will open. Navigate to where your backup of the .wab file is located and choose Open and your address book will be imported into Outlook.

A word on the terminology--an important point. You are not building
a new CPU. That's not possible. What you are building is a new
computer.

"CPU" stands for "Central Processing Unit." In modern personal
computers, the CPU is a relatively small electronic chip, an
integrated circuit. It sits on the motherboard, inside the computer's
case. It is not the case itself, nor is it the total computer, which
includes the case and everything within it.

computer the "CPU," but here's my guess: when you buy a computer, you
normally get the case and what's in it, along with a monitor,

It depends on how your cable modem and TV are wired...

If the cable modem and TV are in the same room and each get their signals from the same splitter then yes you will lose internet and TV when you unplug the cable between the wall outlet and the splitter. Here is an example of a 2-way cable splitter...

If the cable modem is in another room than the TV then no you will not lose internet when you unplug the TV from the wall outlet.

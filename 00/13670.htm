If you have a third party antivirus program on your PC, and you are at W10 v1803,

this is probably your problem.

Windows Defender has a Periodic Scanning button, that allows it to work in concert

with other security programs. If you have a third party antivirus, the button is located

down, on the "Virus & Threat Protection screen. When it is set to "ON",WD is in

"passive" mode.

On most PCs, that allows coexistence with WD and third party. However, some PCs

at v1803, require that the third party antivirus to be removed, in order for Windows

Defender to function. It appears that on some PCs, the Periodic Scanning button

can not do its job.

john w. vinson say:

As Tom says, a simple report expression will be difficult to do. You could create a calculated field in the Query upon which the report is based, and group by it:

 

SELECT (IIF(Product = "Orange" OR Product = "Hamburger", "Yummy", [Product]) AS Category, Expense1, Expense2 FROM yourtable;

This will produce a field containing "Yummy" for oranges and hamburgers (not to my taste, but de gustibus non disputandum est!) and the existing product name for everything else. You can use this field in the report's Sorting and Grouping.

I got this solved on my phone.

 

If you go into Settings > System > Notifications & Actions ... the following must be "On"

 

  • Show notifications in action center when my phone is locked
  • Show notifications on the lock screen
  • Show and sound alarms and reminders when my phone is locked

 

Then go to Settings and type in "Background" in the search bar and select: "Background apps"

Make sure "Block and Filter (glob..." is set to "On".

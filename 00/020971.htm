For “Having two profiles”, do you mean you have created a separate Outlook profile for your Google App account?

I did a lot of research and found that this is a known issue about Google Apps.

This issue occurs when a Google Apps Sync for Microsoft Outlook account and a Microsoft Exchange Server account are configured in the same Outlook profile. If the scenario above meets yours, I recommend you create separate Outlook profiles for them to check the result.

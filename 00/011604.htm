With a few seconds work (has to be done on a computer, not the iPad) you can make this happen.

Open the presentation, choose View | Slide Master

On the topmost master, put a rectangle on the left side of the slide master, give it an Action setting of Previous Slide.

Do the same on the right side but make the Action Setting = Next slide.

Make the rectangles as large as you like side to side; fill the slide top to bottom.

Give them both no line/no fill.

Now test your presentation on the computer in slide show view.  Clicking anywhere on the right will advance to the next slide, clicking on the left will go to the previous slide (except where the rectangle is covered by other content on the slides themselves.

Hello

Unfortunately there is no option for us to change Skype Name (username) but you can change your Skype display name as a workaround. You may follow the steps below:

On the new Skype app, select your Profile photo > click the pencil icon to change the Skype Name.  

For Skype for Windows 10 UWP, click your Profile photo > Manage account. You’ll then be routed to Skype website. Select your Skype display name once more > Edit profile and change the First name and Last name then hit Save.

Additional note: A Skype account which has a Skype Name that show as live:randomalphanumericnumber indicates that the account was created using a phone number. This is system=generated and cannot be changed.

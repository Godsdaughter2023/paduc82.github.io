ken blake - microsoft mvp say:

On Fri, 21 Sep 2012 20:31:58 +0000, lorrindab wrote:

how do i download info to a flash drive

First, please note that both "upload" and download" are the wrong
words. Neither one describes what you need to do to copy data to a
flash drive.

You simply need to copy the file(s) from wherever they are on your
hard disk to the flash drive. The process is exactly the same as
copying data from one hard drive to another or from one place on a
hard drive to another place.

There's more than one way to do this, but the two most common are
"copy and paste" and "drag and drop." If you don't know how to do
these, post back for more information.

Ken Blake, Microsoft MVP

hula_baloo say:

 You can use copy and paste or you can use Save As. 

For copy and paste

1) Highlight the text you want to copy

2) Right-click the highlighted text and click Copy

3) Click where you want to paste the text (a word processor - maybe Wordpad)

4) Right-click and click Paste.

 

 Once in the word processor you can then use Save As to save it on the flash drive.  It will have its own drive letter.

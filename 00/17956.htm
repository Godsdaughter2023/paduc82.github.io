jay freedman say:
It isn't possible to get rid of the bubbles altogether (except by accepting or rejecting the tracked changes). However, if you use the Document Inspector that you mentioned, and leave a check mark only for "Document Properties and Personal Information", then the names of all reviewers are replaced by "Author" and the times are removed.

To keep the tracked changes when running the Document Inspector, you must remove the check mark for "Comments, Revisions, Versions, and Annotations".

With Windows 8, Microsoft has changed from stickers that have the product key that the user has to type in when installing the operating system to new BIOS embedded product keys. What numbers can be transferred:
YOU CAN TRANSFER:

In general, you can transfer any phone number that is from a supported provider, including:

Caution: Transferring mobile numbers is only available in the U.S. and Puerto Rico.

-Toll phone numbers.

-Toll-free phone numbers.

-VoIP phone numbers from a phone provider such as Vonage or RingCentral.

YOU CANNOT TRANSFER:

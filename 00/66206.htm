In Windows 8, the location of iTunes Music folder is: “C:\Users\username\My Music\iTunes\”. Now, we just need to add this path of iTunes Music folder to the Windows Media Player Music Library Locations. For doing this you may follow these steps:

 

a)       Open Windows Media Player on your computer.

b)       Click on the option: “Organize”.

c)       Now, click on “Manage Libraries” and then select “Music”.

d)       This will open the Music Library Locations. Click on the “Add” on this window.

e)       Navigate to the location “C:\Users\username\My Music\iTunes\” and include the iTunes Music Folder in the Windows Media Player Music Library Locations.

f)        After adding the iTunes Music Folder, click on “Ok” and check if you are able to find the iTunes Music in Windows Media Player Library.

 

Hope this is helpful. If you need any further assistance, please do get back to us. We’ll be glad to assist you.

Hi Poppy G,

I see that you want to know about the caps lock indicator light on the Microsoft 800 wireless keyboard. I will help you with this issue.

 

1.Which operating system is installed on the computer?

 

Take a look at the keyboard carefully and you might find the caps lock indicator light. If you do not find the indicator light on the keyboard, then take a look at the wireless receiver.

 

The absence of lights on your keyboard could be to help preserve battery life. On some wireless keyboard or desktop sets, the status lights are on the wireless receiver. This is a by design issue on some wireless keyboards.

 

I would suggest you to refer to this article and check.

Troubleshoot status of NUM LOCK, SCROLL LOCK, F LOCK and CAPS LOCK keys: http://support.microsoft.com/kb/894037

 

If the caps lock indicator on the keyboard is not functioning then there is no way to view whether the caps lock is enabled or disabled without typing some text to see the result.

You will need to replace the keyboard to regain this functionality.

See: “How to replace Microsoft software or hardware, order service packs, and replace product manuals”:http://support.microsoft.com/kb/326246

Hope this helps.

Method 2: Get latest updates

1. Swipe in from the right edge of the screen, tap Settings, and then tap Change PC settings.

2. Enter Control Panel in the search box, and tap or click Control Panel.

3. Enter Recovery in the Control Panel search box, and then tap or click Recovery.

4. Tap or click Open System Restore, and then follow the instructions.

NOTE: Though it is said that System Restore does not affect your personal information such as email, documents, photos, videos, etc., but there is highly possible that some of your personal files are deleted while system restoring.

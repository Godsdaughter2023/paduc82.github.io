Please follow the steps below on how to resize a picture in Windows Live Photo Gallery 2009:

 1. Open Windows Live Photo Gallery.
 2. Select the picture, then click on Fix beside the File menu.
 3. On the right menu pane click on Crop photo.
 4. Adjust the cropping box by dragging the small boxes to your preferred size.
 5. Click on Apply if you are done adjusting.
 6. Proceed to email your pictures.

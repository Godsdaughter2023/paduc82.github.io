I think Microsoft have a hug opportunity to improve Excel by introducing an icon-based add-on to Excel. As in SD software, the add-on will collapse the time series for any variable into one icon, and will make use of arrows to show causal connections between variables (rather than using confusing and error-prone cell references). These icons will make an important visual distinction between inputs and outputs, as well as between stocks (accumulations, i.e. your staff) and flows (i.e. staff hired or fired per year). Additionally, the add-on can make use of sub-categories (known as arrays in system dynamics software) so that you can make detailed calculations without increasing the visual complexity of your model.

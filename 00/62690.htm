Hi Robin!

I can help you with this. It's not a hard process, but it's also not as obvious as it might be. It'll only take you a few minutes. Here's how to login without using your Microsoft Account, but still keep all your data, programs, and settings intact.

Save any documents that are open and close programs since this process will sign you out and back in.

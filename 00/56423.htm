No matter what console an account is signed into it will always be able to play its games and use its subscriptions.

 Each account can set One switch which console it has set as its home console 5 times a year.

So lets look at your situation. 

You have 

Console 1 = C1

Account 1 = A1 Your account

Console 2 = C2

Account 2 = A2 your sons account or any other account

Say A1 is the account that purchased the games and subscriptions.

In order for A2 to play any of those games and use the subscriptions whatever console that they want to play them on would have to be set as A1s home console.

So if A2 wants to play A1s games on C1 then C1 would have to be set as A1s Home console.

So you would want to set one console that the other players would use the most as A1s Home console.

I have all the game purchases and subscriptions on my account.

I have a Xbox One S in my living room and a OG Xbox One in my bedroom.

The Xbox One S I have set as my home Xbox and if my wife wants to play some games or use subscription in the bedroom she would need to have my account signed in at the same time as her account is signed in on the OG Xbox since it is not my Home Xbox.

So basically my Wife would go in and sign into my account then switch accounts to her account and she would then be able to play my games and use my subscription under her account on the our non Home Xbox.

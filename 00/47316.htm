smwutches say:

Yes. Suspension length is determined by the severity of the situation as well as any previous suspensions on your account. So you can get a longer ban or a permanent ban for something severe, but if you continue to do something smaller, like what you were suspended for previously, the length will increase with each suspension until it ultimately ends with a permanent ban.

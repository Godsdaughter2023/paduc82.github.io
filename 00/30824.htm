This is a most likely a permissions issue.  You can alter the permissions of the folder (or take ownership of the folder) as necessary to allow you to delete it without first deleting the files.  Unfortunately, this can sometimes take longer to accomplish than just deleting the files and then the folder - but if you do it at a high level, then it will apply to all the sub-folders (if you configure it to do so) and may saye you time the next time you need to delete a folder.

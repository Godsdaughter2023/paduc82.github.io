I was testing a lone heading style and forgot about the regular text after the heading.  Yeah, you could copy the whole paragraph as a way to  ensure copying the multilevel list number, but then you have to delete all the regular text.  So, instead just type "Section 1.1" (with the appropriate number) before the section title in your TC code.

 Type the headings and regular text  in seperate paragraphs. Apply the heading style to the first paragraph and your body text style (usually normal) to the second. pauli o say:

"Windows 10 Pro Insider on the Slow Ring is failing to Activate after a motherboard and CPU upgrade."

Windows 10 activation is based on PC's hardware ID and only minor changes are allowed. New motherboard is compared as new PC and it needs new license.

joenamco say: New Motherboard means a new activation key. only time a change in MB will get reactivated if you replace with the same version/model of MB.

cbarnhorst say:

Windows 10 Pro Insider on the Slow Ring is failing to Activate after a motherboard and CPU upgrade. I am receiving activation error code: 0xC004C003. I originally upgraded to Windows 10 Pro when it launched and have been an Insider ever since. Have tried Troubleshooting and selecting the 'changed hardware recently' but when I select the correct device I am trying to activate it says 'Unable to activate Windows' but provides no additional information or error codes.

This is by design and is expected.  Windows 10 is activated by the device.  If the device is a registered Windows 10 device for the edition of Windows you install then it will activate. 

The product key is only needed to register the device.  A product key does not activate it, it just registers it.  Since the device registration also requires the unique hardware IDs of the device, changing the mobo changes the IDs and the new IDs must be registered.  That requires a new Windows 10 license.

If you have replaced the old parts with the manufacturer's recommended replacement parts you can use the Activation Troubleshooter to avoid buying a new license.  If you have upgraded the old parts with retail upgrade replacements then you are required to purchase the license.

Go to Settings/Update and Security/Activation and click the link to Go to Store to purchase a license.  Be sure it matches the edition you have installed.

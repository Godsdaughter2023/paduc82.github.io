DLookup always works on the local database. To work on another database such as your BE, you need to open a database object, then a recordset on MSysObjects. Something like:

dim dbBE as dao.database

set dbBE = DBEngine.Workspaces(0).OpenDatabase("path-to-BE")

dim rs as dao.recordset

set rs = dbBE.OpenRecordset("select DateUpdate from MSysObjects where …", dbOpenSnapshot)

debug.print rs!DateUpdate

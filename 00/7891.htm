Remember: notebooks are folders (with the name of the notebook) containing .one files, the so called sections. To open a notebook double click on any of the sections.

And: the best place for the notebook folders is the default path

C:\Users\<user>\Documents\OneNote Notebooks

This path will be created during the installation of Onenote.

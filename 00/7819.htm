Access (all versions) are intrinsically shareable if you do it right. Even when you choose to delete your out-of-date security info, MS don't fully remove it for 30 days. This waiting period helps to deter hackers from updating your security info and gaining access to your account without your knowledge.

After 30 days, the user of the account will be able to access the security settings and update his/her preferred information.

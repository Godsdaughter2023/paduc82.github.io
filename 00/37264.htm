a. user say:

There is no free Vista download.

It is Toshiba's responsibility to supply you with a way to reinstall Vista:

Either a Vista Reovery DVD, or a Recovery Partition on your Hard Drive, from which you make Recovery Disk/s to reinstall Vista.

Microsoft is not involved in any of the above; it is between you and Toshiba.

Contact Toshiba and ask them to send you a set of Vista Recovery disk/s.

 

They normally do that for a small $ cost.

 

Also, ask them if you have a Recovery Partition on your Computer/Laptop to restore it to Factory settings.

Or borrow a proper Vista DVD(not Dell, HP, etc).
A proper Vista DVD contains all Vista versions.
The Product Key determines which Vista version gets installed.

There are 2 Vista disks: one for 32bit Operating System, and one for 64bit Operating System.

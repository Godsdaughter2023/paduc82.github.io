For your first question, the answer is 500. This is the maximum number of recipients allowed in the To:, Cc:, and Bcc: fields for a single email message. For your reference:
Recipient and sender limits in Exchange Online

john korchok say:

You don't need to reinstall. Here are the steps to create a new Normal.dotm file:

  1. Close Word.
  2. Hold down the Alt key while clicking on the Go menu and choosing Library. The hidden user Library folder opens.
  3. Open Application Support>Microsoft>Office>User Templates.
  4. Drag Normal.dotm to your desktop.
  5. Open Word, then close it. A new, blank Normal.dotm file is created.

If you had any custom macros or content in your old Normal template,  you can use Word's Tools>Templates and Add-ins command. Click on the Organizer button, click on Close File underneath the listing that is not Normal.dotm, open your old Normal.dotm on your desktop and copy the content to your new Normal template.

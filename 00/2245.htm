hi morri,

it’s controlled by the owa policy, rather than an rbac role. i’d like to share with you a way to use powershell to archive this as follows:
1.start powershell.
2.create a new mailbox policy.
new-owamailboxpolicy –name “<policy name>”
3.set the groupcreationenabled value to false.
set-owamailboxpolicy –identity “<policy name>” –groupcreationenabled $false
4.set the policy on the mailboxes of the user who isn’t allowed to create groups.
set-casmailbox –identity <user> -owamailboxpolicy “<policy name>”

If the phone has been unresponsive after the upgrade, it is best that you reset your phone using the hardware key buttons. Note that resetting the phone will remove all the files in your phone, including its apps, photos and other data.

To do this, follow the steps below:

  1. Plugged your phone into a power source. Make sure that the device is fully charged to avoid interruptions during the process.
  2. Press and hold the Volume down and Power buttons at the same time until you feel a vibration (about 10 to 15 seconds).
  3. When you feel the vibration, release the buttons, and then immediately press and hold the Volume down button until you see a large exclamation mark.
  4. Once the exclamation mark appears, press the following four buttons in this order: Volume up, Volume down, Power, Volume down. Your phone should now reset and restart itself.

The process may take a while to finish.

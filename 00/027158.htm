If you are enrolled in the Office Insider program, we have two different levels available.

  • Insider Fast puts out 1-2 updates a week most weeks. Fast will see some updates that contain no new features, just updates and fixes to existing features. Some of these will be visible to users, some won't. These updates have less testing done to them before release.

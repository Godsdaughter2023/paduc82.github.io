find the root of a number e.g. 2 is X root of 8 - Microsoft Community

amritlalvayda say:

find the root of a number e.g. 2 is X root of 8

Example

X = nth root of (Pn/P) - 1

where  the following are known

X is CAGR (compounded annual growth rate)

Pn is the amount after n years

P is the principal amount

I want to find out n which is number of years

 

Please assist.

 

joeu2004 say: AMRITLALVAYDA wrote:

Subject:  find the root of a number e.g. 2 is X root of 8

Example
X = nth root of (Pn/P) - 1
where  the following are known
X is CAGR (compounded annual growth rate)
Pn is the amount after n years
P is the principal amount

I want to find out n which is number of years


The formula for CAGR is:

x = (Pn/P)^(1/n) -1

You could solve for n algebraically and use either of the following equivalent formulas:

=LOG(A2/A3) / LOG(A1+1)
or
=(LOG(A2)-LOG(A3)) / LOG(A1+1)

where A1 is x in the form 5% or 0.05, A2 is Pn, and A3 is P.

But you could do it more simply as follows:

=NPER(A1,0,-A3,A2)

Note the use of minus A3 in the 3rd parameter v.

I would suggest you follow the steps to copy the content of the DVD to computer.

a)      Insert the DVD to disk drive.

c)       Right click on DVD drive and select explore.

d)      Right-click the file or folder you want to copy, and then click Copy.

e)      Open the location where you want to store the copy.

a)      Plug in the MP4 player.

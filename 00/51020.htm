If you are referring so, I would like to inform that Windows 10 is by design you will have to enter a password to log-in through Administrator profile using Microsoft account. This feature is being implemented to make more secure to the user profiles.

Also, if you don't wish to enter the password you may create the 4 digit PIN which will reduce your time to enter full password to log-in to the computer. Follow the below steps.

  1. Press Windows + I keys to open settings menu.
  2. Click on Accounts.
  3. Click on Sign in options.
  4. Click on Add under PIN.
  5. Follow the onscreen instructions to create Pin.
  6. Sign out and try to sign in again using PIN as your Sign in option.

If you wish to access the computer without password you can create local accounts.

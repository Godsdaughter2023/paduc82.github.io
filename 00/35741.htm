With regard to your query on how to turn on the laptop using the keyboard, this issue can be configured from the BIOS settings.

  1. Access the BIOS settings interface. When you first turn on your computer, the system BIOS can be accessed by pressing a special key. This will vary depending on the manufacturer, but it is most often the "DEL" or "F2" key. You should see a message on the screen while the computer is booting that tells you which key to use.   3. Turn off the PC and try to test your settings.

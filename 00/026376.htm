Good day, Lyn.

Let's work this out. Please provide us the following information:

  • When you say "phone wont knock off when you charge it", are you trying to turn off your phone when it's connected to a charger or your phone won't charge?
  • What's the OS build currently installed? To check, go to Settings > System > About > More Info > OS build.
  • When did the issue start?

Also, check if these settings are set on your phone to make sure you get message notifications:

  • Go to Settings > System > Notification & actions > Notification. Make sure that all options are turned "On." The same goes for the Messaging option under Get notification from these senders.
  • Then, make sure that System alerts has a check mark by going to Settings > Personalisation > Sounds. If System alerts doesn't have a check mark, then you might not get some of the alerts from your phone including Messaging notification.
  • Lastly, go to Settings > System > Battery. Make sure that Microsoft Messaging is Always allowed in background by clicking the Battery usage by app option.

After all settings are set, perform a soft reset to refresh your device. To perform a soft reset, press and hold Volume Down+Power button until your phone vibrates and restart. Then, lock your phone screen and ask a different person to send you a message to see if the message notifications work.

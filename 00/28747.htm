glenprouty say:

Hi Froggylu,

Controlled Folder Access is blocking your document because you have

Ransomware protection on and the document is not recognized as being

in the "whitelist".   (Allowed App)

You can find Ransomware protection at the bottom of your Windows

Defender "Virus & threat protection screen. Select "Manage Ransomware

Protection", and you are taken to the Ransomware protection screen.

You will see the CFA on/off switch. Turning this switch "off" will allow all

Apps to pass through.  (Not a good idea)!

You will lose the Ransomware protection that Windows Defender provides!

It is much better to add the Apps that you want to pass, to your "allowed" list.

In W10 v1809 that process has been greatly simplified. If you click on the

notification of a "blocked" App, or any of the same in the taskbar, you are

taken directly to the "Allow an App through CFA" screen. Click on the + sign

(Add an allowed App) and a popup shows "Recently blocked Apps". Select

that and you will see a list of the Apps that have been blocked by CFA on your

PC.

Select from that list any App that you want to pass through CFA by clicking

on its + sign, and it is added to the "allowed" list, immediately.

Use caution when selecting which Apps to allow. There may be some in the

list that you don't want.   (Ransomware at work)

Regards,  Glen

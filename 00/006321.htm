If your line calls for Signature <underscores> Date <underscores>, all on one line,   insert a four-cell, one-row table and turn off the borders except for the bottoms of the former underscore cells. 

If  your line calls for "Signature" and "Date" to be below the  "underscore, insert a three cell, two-row table.  the middle column is just there to show a gap between the two borders. Turn off all borders except for the bottom borders on the first and third cells on the first row.

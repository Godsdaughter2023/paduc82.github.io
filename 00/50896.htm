arminatorx say:

Try the following:

On the console, go to the network settings and choose to go offline. 

Then go to "My Games and Apps" and find the partially installed GTA V.

Press the Menu button to bring up the menu, and choose to manage the game. 

Uninstall everything from the game.

And VERY IMPORTANT: Delete the local savegame copy from the game! 

Your savegame is safely stored in the cloud, and will be downloaded later, when you start the game again, but if you keep a savegame on the console, the install will see that the savegame is from a newer version, and will try to download an update. 

So remove EVERY TRACE of the game: The main game, any DLCs, that might be already installed, and your LOCAL save games. 

DO NOT delete the savegame from all consoles in the cloud storage!

Now, with the game deleted, and the console in offline mode, eject the disc, and hold the power button on the console for about 10 seconds. 

After the console has turned off, take out the disc, and turn the console back on. 

The console will now do a startup animation on a green screen. During the startup of the console, take your disc to the kitchen. 

Apply a small drop of mild dishwasher soap on the silver side of the disc and wet your fingers. 

With wet fingers, "massage" the soap around the silver side of the disc in a spiraling motion.

When the disc is covered in soap, rinse the disc under running water. Then shake the disc several times, and wipe the remaining water drops with a soft, lint-free cloth, while holding the disc with your index finger in the center hole and your thumb on the edge of the disc ONLY!!!

Now, with the dried off disc, return to your console and insert the disc again. 

Make sure that the console is still in OFFLINE MODE by going to the network settings. 

With the disc in offline mode, let the game install from disc completely. 

After the game has finished installing from disc, turn the console online again, and try to start the game. 

The console will detect updates for the game and start to download them. 

Now you can either turn the console off (with the console being in INSTANT ON Mode in the settings, not Energy Saving mode), and the console will then download the update while you don't use the console. 

Or if you have particularly fast internet, you can play something else and then switch over to GTA when the download has finished.

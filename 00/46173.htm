ruz san say:

Hi Brian,

Your wireless connection may not be working very well because of the network adapter on your Surface Pro 3. We suggest checking for updates for the driver to get the most out of your wireless browsing. Follow these steps:

  1. Open Device Manager (Windows key + X > Device Manager).
  2. Expand Network adapters.
  3. Right-click on the adapter in use and choose Update Driver.
  4. Restart your Surface.

Let us know if you need more help.

Hi joey,

I understand that you wanted to have a shortcut provided for power plan so that you can easily switch them based upon there usage.

It is a great idea which will be very useful, you may create a shortcut for power plans by following the steps below:

1) Open a command prompt, type powercfg /list at the prompt, and press enter.

2) Make a note of Power Scheme GUID for each Power plan that you want shortcut to be created for.

3) Right click or press and hold on an empty area on your desktop, and click/tap on New and Shortcut.

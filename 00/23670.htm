jay freedman say:

For your first question: You may have inadvertently turned on "Keep together" in the paragraph formatting (either as direct formatting or as part of a style). If that's enabled, any paragraph that
doesn't fit entirely at the bottom of one page will be forced entirely to the next page.

For other possibilities, see http://wordfaqs.mvps.org/BottomLine.htm

hansv mvp say:

About your second question:

Click the Office button.

Click Word Options.

Click Advanced.

Scroll down to the "Ahow document content" section.

Clear the check box "Show text boundaries".

Click OK.

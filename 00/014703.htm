You want to test for the following conditions:
(B9 > 0) AND (B9 between 1 and 8) AND (B9+V9 > 8)

The first condition is superfluous. You would get the same result with

=IF(AND(B9>=1,B9<=8,(B9+V9)>8),1,0)

SUMPRODUCT appears to be not useful here. Why would you want to force its use?

=IF(AND(B9>=1,B9<=8,(B9+V9)>8,B9=INT(B9)),1,0)

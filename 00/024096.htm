chip pearson say:

I assume you are speaking about a "Worksheet". A Worksheet contains Cells that store data or have formulas. On occasion, a Worksheet is called a "tab" by novice users.  Worksheets are contained by a Workbook. A Workbook must have at least one sheet but can contain any practical number of Worksheets. A Workbook is contained in a file. A file contains exactly one Workbook. "Spreadsheet" is a generic term that is used to refer to both Worksheets and Workbooks, and thus should be avoided.

a. user say:

Firstly you don't "download" to a DVD - you COPY or BURN to a DVD. The term "Download" is used to refer to the process of transferring a file from a remote computer to yours, usually (but not necessarily) via the internet.

Save the Word document as normal, then use Windows Explorer to burn it to the writeable DVD.

You've recognized and stated the problem with any such suggestion within your own post, since any statement relating to which security (or in fact any) application is "best" is always inherently based on personal experience and preference.

That's not to say the choice is wrong, only that it's based on a single individual's personal experience and opinion, which may be different for another individual for the same reasons.

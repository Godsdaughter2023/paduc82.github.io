uberslackr say:

Windows 10 comes with "Mail" -- Not Outlook.

Unless you are referring to your login (which is your MS account.)

If you open the "Mail" application (Press Windows Key type Mail) then at the bottom there should be a gear/sprocket for settings. There in the settings a Notification option. Check "Apply to all accounts." If you have multiple setup in the mail app. Then UNCHECK "Show a Notification Banner." Then toggle the switch "Off" -- I had to uncheck show a notification banner, before toggling the switch off, so it would stop with the notifications.

You can also click "Manage Accounts." from in the settings of Mail. Click your outlook.com account, choose "Change mailbox sync settings." From there you can turn off syncing of your Email to the mail app -- Nothing Syncs = No pop ups / notifications.

alanwu_msft say:

hi ashok,

based on my experience, this situation is related to the cause that the “room finder” hasn’t been enabled. you can try enabling the room finder and check the result. to enable the room finder:
1. connect to exchange online powershell.
2. run the cmdlets
new-distributiongroup –name "room list name" –roomlist.
add-distributiongroupmember –identity "room list name" -member "room mailbox name".
the room list name is a name of your room list and you can name it as you want. room mailbox name refer to your room mailbox’s name.

after you perform these steps, you can choose the room: choose your newly created room list> “all attendees”> add your room mailbox.

note: the symbol “none” under choose an available room is by-design.

please let me know if you have any further concern.

regards,
alan

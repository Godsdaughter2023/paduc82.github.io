Why is it not possible to restrict styles but still use direct formatting in Word 2010?


Because styles restriction is intended to force the use of styles and prevent the use of direct formatting. What you are supposed to do is create character styles in case you want to format part of text as italic, bold, etc.

In the Styles pane (Ctrl+Alt+Shift+S), click the New Style button (on the bottom left). Choose "Character" for "Style type."

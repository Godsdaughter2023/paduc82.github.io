    neha singh msft say:

    Hi Brad,

     

    It is not feasible to remove the list of documents permanently, which you see under the apps (blue icon) in Office 365 home page, this list shows the recent opened online documents. However, if you want to remove any specific document from the list, then you may click on the ellipses and select “Remove from the list”, as shown in the following screenshot.

     

     

     

    If you want to remove the entire list of recently opened document temporarily, then you can follow the steps below:

     

    1. Go to the Office 365 home page and open any document in desktop application.
    2. Please make sure you are logged in with your work or school credentials in Office desktop application.
    3. Once you have opened the document from Office 365 portal, go to File>Open>Recent, you’ll see your recently opened document, right click on the document one by one, and select

       

      You must repeat these steps for all Office applications.

       

      Note: The above procedure will remove the document list temporarily only. If you open any document online, it will again start showing under “Your recent online documents”.

        

      Given the situation, I suggest you vote in Office 365 UserVoice to add the Ability to remove or hide "Your recent online documents".

when I try to reply to a craigslist ad by email it says there are errors and wont allow me to send a reply

foxfifth say: When you click on a craigslist add to respond, it uses your default email program.  It sounds like your computer may be set to Windows Live Mail but that is not the email program you are using.

The default can be set by going to the Control Panel and then entering "default programs" in the search box at the upper right.  In Default Programs click on "Set your default programs" and then select your email program and set it as default.

Note that if you are not using an email program on your computer (i.e., if you go directly to an email provider's website and send and receive your email just using your web browser) it may not be possible to have your computer automatically start an email just by clicking on an address in Craigslist or anywhere else.  If you are doing your email that way (webmail), reply with which email provider and which internet browser you are using.

Note that no matter what, you can reply to a Craigslist add by copying the email address (either right-click on it and select copy or drag your mouse over it to highlight it and then press Ctrl+C) and then open a new email yourself the way you normally do when you send an email and paste the address into the email.

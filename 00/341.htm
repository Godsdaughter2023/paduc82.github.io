umbrella guy say:

Excel - Cut out characters in a cell

I have too many characters in half of the 900 cells I am working with.  Is there a way to cut the length to 30 characters in all cells and delete all extra characters?
jim thomlinson say:

In an adjacent column add the formula

 

=left(A1, 30)

Where A1 is the target cell with the long text. Copy that formula down. Now just copy and paste Special Values the formula values over top of the original text. Or IMO better yet just hide the column with the long text just in case you ever need it back. If you paste over top of the long text  it will be permanently gone.

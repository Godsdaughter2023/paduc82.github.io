Skype for Business - how to disabled screen sharing - Microsoft Community

lup kong say:

Skype for Business - how to disabled screen sharing

We using skype for business in our organization and would like to know how to disable Screen share for all users. Is there a function in Office 365 menu where I can disable the screen sharing?
vasil l. michev say:

For the time being, you can disable it by assigning one of the predefined policies with sharing blocked. To get the list, use the following PowerShell cmdlet:

Get-CsConferencingPolicy -ApplicableTo *** Email address is removed for privacy ***  | ? {$_.EnableAppDesktopSharing -eq "None"} | ft Identity

Identity
--------
Tag:BposSIMPOnly
Tag:BposSIMPOnlyNoRec
Tag:BposSIMPOnlyNoDialout
Tag:BposSIMPOnlyNoRecNoDialout

To assign the policy to a user, use:

Grant-CsConferencingPolicy -PolicyName Tag:BposSIMPOnly -Identity *** Email address is removed for privacy ***

Do note that those policies block a bit more than just screen sharing. Until we get the functionality to create new policies, that's the only way though.

In Hybrid Environment, There are 2 ways to create Mailboxes in "Exchange Online" (EXO)

1. Migrate the On Premise Mailbox to Exchange Online.

If you are migrating on premise mailboxes to  EXO, then as soon as the migration is complete, on premise mailbox would get converted to "User Remote Mailbox" in Exchange 2010 and "Office Mailbox" in Exchange 2013 with all the "Target Address" stamping to route emails to Exchange Online as "*** Email address is removed for privacy ***". From Outlook.com Help:

I deleted, moved, or read an email in Outlook.com. Why don't I see the change in my connected account?

Syncing between your connected account and Outlook.com is one-way only. When you connect an account, Outlook.com syncs email from your connected account, but it doesn't sync any changes you make in Outlook.com back to your connected account. If you delete, move, or read an email from a connected account in Outlook.com, you won't see the change when you go to your connected account.

You need to login to her account directly.

If you are using Edge or Internet Explorer, Ctrl + Shift + P will open an InPrivate Browsing window.

You can have your account open in one browser window and her account open in an InPrivate Browsing window.

It is possible to use a different browser other than your default browser to login to her account.

steve rindsberg say:

Short of having some custom VBA code written for you, the best thing I can think of is something like this:

Create screenshots as you would normally, but instead of pasting them directly into your status presentation, save them to files.  Give the files names that you'll remember later.

Then in your status presentation, do Insert | Picture, select the picture to insert and then instead of clicking INSERT, click the downward pointing arrowhead/triangle next to it and choose INSERT AND LINK from the dropdown list.

Do this for each of the images you've saved to file.  Then add your hyperlinks to the inserted pictures.

So far, no time savings over what you're already doing, right?  Right.

But because you've linked and inserted the pictures, PPT will automatically update them whenever the original files change, so next month, you make and save your screenshots (to the SAME FILENAMES AS BEFORE!), open your status presentation and ... voila!  It's already updated.

Not totally automatic, but it might cut the amount of monthly work in half.

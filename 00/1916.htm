for example i've cells

A20    val=2
A23

A30

i need to fill cells A23 - A24 - A27 - A30 a one step starting from A20 val (2) ?
teylyn say:
To fill a series in Excel 2003, the cells must be contiguous, otherwise the fill series command does nothing. 

What you could do is enter a 2 into cell A20, then in A23 enter

=COUNT($A$20:A22)+2

Copy that formula. Then, one after the other, select the cells you want to increment and hit Ctrl-V to paste the formula.

Ctrl-click to select more than one cell will not work to copy a formula, though.

cheers, teylyn

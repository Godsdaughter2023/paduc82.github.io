As a work around...

1)  In Word, open the print menu (File > Print, or cmd-P).

3)  From the PDF drop down button (bottom-left), select "Open PDF in Preview".
4)  In Preview, open the print menu (File > Print, or cmd-P).
5)  Select the desired page range and print as usual.

Can't Delete Contacts in Win10 Mail app. - Microsoft Community

klaro-20 say:

Can't Delete Contacts in Win10 Mail app.

Hi All,

I had this problem, seen many posts and no real answers ... and I figured it out ... for anyone who has the same problem.

(at least it worked for me)

Here is a quick description of the problem again:

The contact list in Mail / People and in the actual Outlook.com account are supposed to be identical.

However - deleted contacts in Outlook were still present in Mail / People ... and the 'Delete' option was grayed out / no way to delete them there.

Solution:

Open Mail > go to Settings > go to Manage Accounts > choose Outlook > turn off syncing: contacts

close Mail > open again and check > ALL contacts are gone! > close Mail

Open Mail again > reverse above steps (turn syncing contacts on again)

Close Mail > open and check > now contacts in question should be gone

Cheers

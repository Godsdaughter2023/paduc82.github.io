My name is Daniel and I'm a developer on the Family Safety team.  We have found at least one case that can cause this to happen.  If your parent account is associated with a Microsoft Account, Windows will periodically ask you to re-enter your password for security reasons.  You may have seen this prompt already in your Action Center, or the Mail app, if you use those.  Unfortunately, this page of PC Settings doesn't have a specific message for this case.

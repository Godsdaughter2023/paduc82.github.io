The answer to this really depends on how your accounts are really set up. Let's assume the scenario is as described above: You have a Gamertag on your console that is NOT connected online to Xbox Live. And you have an online account on your PC, that you haven't played on the console.

You can't connect the console Xbox account to your Microsoft Account, since there is already the BurliestRelic82 account occupying your Microsoft Account email.

The solution for this depends on what you wish to keep:

If you are willing to NOT keep your Microsoft Account email you use on the PC currently, you can simply make a new Microsoft Account at http://www.outlook.com and then use that newly created account to Join Xbox Live on the Xbox console with your Gamertag.

If you WANT to keep your Microsoft Account email, you also have to make a new Microsoft Account Email at http://outlook.com and then download the BurliestRelic82 account to your console. DO NOT GO TO XBOX.COM WITH THAT NEW ACCOUNT!!!

Do this by pressing the Guide button, and X to sign out of any Gamertag if necessary. Now you should have a "Download Profile" option in the guide menu.

Choose that option, and enter the Microsoft Account email and password you used on this website to sign into Xbox.com

This will download the empty BurliestRelic82 Gamertag to your console.

Now go to the far right settings hub, there to the Account tile in the lower left, then to the "Your Information" and "Security Info" settings, to move the Gamertag to another Microsoft Account.

Provide the new Microsoft Email that you just created at Outlook.com to move the BurliestRelic82 Gamertag away from your desired Microsoft Account email.

Now you have to wait 30 days, as every Microsoft Account has a 30 day "cooldown" period after a Gamertag has been moved away from that account (this was a security measure when a lot of Gamertag accounts were hijacked by Fifa Ultimate Team thieves).

After 30 days, sign into your console Gamertag, choose "Join Xbox Live" in the Guide menu, and provide your Microsoft Account email you used here.

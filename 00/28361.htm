As explained:

Basically it is possible to keep autodiscover records pointing to Office 365 if all coworkers.contoso.com mailboxes are hosted online. In this case, if in the future you or anyone in your organization chooses to create coworkers.contoso.com mailboxes on-prem then autodiscover will not work.

Possible Fix for issues with OneNote draw with hand/hand with - Microsoft Community

wavyducky say:

Possible Fix for issues with OneNote draw with hand/hand with panning

So I was having issues with OneNote. Whenever I would use my hand to pan and zoom, it would draw instead. It was quite a hassle and I would manually have to switch between the pen mode and pan mode. I also saw many others were having a similar issue: 

https://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/msoffice/forum/all/problem-with-onenote-and-draw-with-handpanning/9a2045ee-6623-4875-a293-c1f19d0cdc18

The fix I found was restarting the setting that allowed for automatic switching. To do this go to File > Options > Advanced then from here scroll down to Pen options and check off "Automatically switch between inking, selecting, typing, and panning". (Image below) Click okay and close the app. After re-open OneNote and follow the steps again but this time check the "Automatically switch between inking, selecting, typing, and panning" setting on. Click okay and then close the app one more time. Restart your computer and VIOLA! It should fix. Hopefully you find this helpful and it issue fixes for you.

Sincerely, 

WavyDuck

Next on a hunch, I decided to disable the Norton Internet Security Anti Spam add in.   I closed Outlook and restarted it.    Email send functionality restored.

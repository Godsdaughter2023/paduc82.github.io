This is a bit of a trick question.

For consumer subscriptions, like 365 Home / Personal office does not "come with" an email account. It requires that you provide your existing personal email.

Business subscriptions work the other way. The company assigns you an email, then attaches the subscription to that email.

For consumer licenses, once you activate the subscription with an email account you are STUCK with it!  Sorry.

Hi Paul,

No, we cannot join the two accounts even if we have the same account name for both services.

These two services are kept separated by Microsoft since Office 365 work account is commercial service related, and Microsoft account provided by Microsoft services is for non-commercial use.

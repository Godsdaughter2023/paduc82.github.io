gerryh_1916 say:

recover a deleted contact in Outlook 2011 for Mac

How do I recover a deleted contact in Outlook 2011 for Mac? Thanks
talking moose say:
Unfortunately, Outlook for Mac is not designed to allow you to recover deleted contact records. You can restore your entire Outlook database from a backup but that would be overkill just to retrieve one item.

Do you possibly have this contact information elsewhere? Consider searching email signatures, paper records and even other electronic devices where you may have synced contact records at one time.

If you absolutely need to restore from backup then you'll want to recover this folder in your home folder (where " ~ " denotes your home folder):

~/Documents/Microsoft User Data/Office 2011 Identities/Main Identity.

Hope this helps!

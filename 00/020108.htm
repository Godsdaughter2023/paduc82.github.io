Have you by any chance received some files over Bluetooth? S60 phones like the E63 receive Bluetooth translissions as messages in  the inbox, many people forget to delete the messages once they've saved the files to memory card, so they're still taking up space.

You'll have to confirm the reset by selecting Yes then enter the lock code, which is 12345 if you have not set a personal code, then the phone will shut down and restart, after which it will be like a new phone and you'll be able to send again. You can then restore your data to the phone.

jay freedman say:

Here are the settings you want:

Besides setting the line spacing (within paragraphs) to Single and the Spacing After to 6 pt, you must uncheck the "Don't add space between paragraphs of the same style" box. This gives the spacing shown in the text behind the dialog.

You may think that the space between paragraphs here looks like 1 line (or 12 pt), but it isn't. The "leading" increases the distance between the base lines of the text in the single spaced paragraphs. If you want a smaller visual space between paragraphs, set the Space After to 3 pt or 4 pt (you'll have to type those in the box, because the up/down arrow buttons jump in increments of 6 pt).

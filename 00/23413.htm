charles kenyon say:

Each Word Section has three (3) headers. Some of them may not be displayed in some sections. Link to previous applies to the same kind of header:

  • Main (default) header - also the odd-page header if you have different odd and even.
  • First page (of the Section) header
  • Even page header if you have different odd and even

Linked / unlinked applies to each type of header in each section after the first one.

Header/Footer Settings Recap

Once you have a particular section unlinked, a change made in the unlinked header in a section will not transfer to a header in the section(s) before it and a change made to the section before it will not transfer to the following section.

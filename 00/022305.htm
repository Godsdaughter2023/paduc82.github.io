Thanks for the help!!

 

Ken K. - 2191

 

 

macropod say:

Hi Ken,

That's because a mailmerge gets the underlying data, not the control that produced it. What you need to do is to inser a field coded like the following into your mailmerge main document:

{SYMBOL {IF{MERGEFIELD CheckBox \* Upper}= "TRUE" 254 168} \a \f Wingdings}
where 'CheckBox' is the Access field's name.

Note: The field brace pairs (ie '{ }') for the above example are created via Ctrl-F9 - you can't simply type them or copy & paste them from this message.

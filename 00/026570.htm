jim gordon mvp say:

A Microsoft Identity (renamed Profile in 2016) is a folder containing everything relating to Entourage or Outlook. You can have more than one identity or profile.

A typical identity contains all email, attachments, calendars, tasks, category information, and account information for those email accounts that were put into the identity. Once you delete an identity everything contained within it is gone unless backed up somewhere. 

If you are comfortable that the 2008 identity has nothing in it you need (sure sounds that way) you will recover 56 gig of drive space by deleting it. Look for other identities in the same folder. Every time you rebuilt the database it made a new copy of the identity, so you can delete the old copies, as well.

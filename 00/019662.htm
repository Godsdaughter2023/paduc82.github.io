Hi Samuel,

 

It may take up to 72 hours for the DNS to take effect when you change your subscription from Godaddy to Hostgator.

 

Regarding “I cannot sign in…” do you mean that you cannot sign into Office 365 Web applications with your email address? If so, please do the following steps:

1. Use the inPrivate browse mode to log into Office 365 Web applications in Internet Explorer.

2. Clear your browser cache in your browser and then log in again.

3. Disable proxy. Click the Tools button in Internet Explorer and then select Internet Options -> Click the Connections tab and then select LAN settings -> Uncheck the check box for Use a proxy server for your LAN.

 

In addition, you can provide your tenant information through Private Message for us, and then we can help you check the DNS settings in your tenant. You can access the private message via the link below:

https://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/privatemessages/list

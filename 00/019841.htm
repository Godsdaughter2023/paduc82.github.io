melowin mar say:

Hi Richard,

OneNote automatically saves your notes while you work, but you can always back up your notebooks, just like the rest of your computer’s files and data. If you back up your notebooks, you can restore your notes if you ever need to.

To set automatic backups, you can choose a location where you can store your files. Here's how:

  1. Choose File >Options.
  2. In the OneNote Options dialog box, choose Save & Backup.
  3. On the right, under Save, choose Backup Folder > Modify.
  4. In the Select Folder dialog box, go to your backup folder. For example, you can use a private folder on your network or a USB drive.
  5. Choose Select to accept the new location, and then click OK.

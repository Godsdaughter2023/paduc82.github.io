2  It will not be an upgrade, it will be a complete installation.  There is no upgrade path except [if these are even available any more] upgrade from XP to Vista then upgrade from Vista to Win 7 then upgrade from Win 7 to Win 10.  And you'll be paying at each of those steps so the total might easily be £300.

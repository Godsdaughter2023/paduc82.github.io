page adjustment for printing to a specific size - Microsoft Community

wadda 65 say:

page adjustment for printing to a specific size

I have a doc on 8.5x11 page.  I would like to print it on to a 4x6 index card to print both sides so I could insert it into a book for reference
jay freedman say:
Word doesn't do scaled printing on custom page sizes, so you have to fake it. The idea you had of printing the smaller size on 8.5x11" paper and cutting the paper to size is correct. Here's how to get the text to print in the right place on the back of duplexed paper:

First, do some arithmetic (a drawing may help). Decide how big you want the margins on the final 7x9" sheet to be. Let's say you pick 0.75" on all four sides. So one of the side margins will be 0.75" and the other side margin will be 0.75 + 1.5 = 2.25". (That is, the "real" margin plus the amount you're going to cut off the paper.) Similarly, the top margin will be 0.75" and the bottom margin will be 0.75 + 2 = 2.75".

On the Page Layout tab, click the dialog launcher (the little arrow) in the bottom right corner of the Page Setup group. In the center of the dialog, set the Multiple Pages dropdown to "Mirror margins". This changes the labels of the side margins from Left and Right to Inside and Outside. Set the four margin measurements to the values you calculated. The "magic" of mirror margins is that it swaps the left and right margin sizes on the even and odd pages (the preview shows this as if in a book, but the right-hand page is the front of the sheet and the left-hand page is the back of the sheet).

If your document has headers and/or footers, also go into the header or footer pane and check the box for Different Odd & Even Pages. Then set up the even page header/footer in the correct location (and it could have completely different text from the odd-page header/footer).

jay freedman say:
In the lower right corner of the Print dialog, open the Scale to Paper Size dropdown and choose 4"x6".

If your printer doesn't do duplex (double-sided) printing, or if it doesn't want to do it for the 4"x6" size, check the Manual Duplex box in the upper right. You'll be prompted when it's time to turn the paper over and put it back into the feed tray.

If you have Facebook syncing with your Microsoft account, there is currently no way of disabling or activating specific birthdays.

To remove all Facebook birthdays from your Microsoft Account

- You'll need to remove your Facebook account from your Microsoft Account

- Then, completing your contacts' Birthday Field, will generate their birthday under Birthday Calendar

To keep Facebook synced with Microsoft Account but only display birthdays of desired contacts

- You'll need to hide Birthday Calendars in the devices you don't want to see. 

- You can then add the birthdays of the people you want to see by adding them as an appointment on your actual calendar (Either duration: all day, status: free or duration: (when you want to be reminded, status: free) also make sure that recurrence is set to Annually.

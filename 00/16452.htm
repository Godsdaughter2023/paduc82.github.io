If you have WinZip or 7Zip or a similar tool on your computer, try this.  Create a copy of your .xlsx file to play with (just in case).  Then change the filename from .xlsx (or .xlsm) to .zip.  Open it with WinZip or 7Zip.  Yes, it will!  You're going to find that at the top level it looks like this:

I think Avast has kind of alarmed you over something normal - especially if you know there are drawings in your workbook.

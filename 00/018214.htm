Hi Peter,

 

Welcome to the Microsoft Community and thanks for posting the question.  As I understand, you’re having trouble syncing the Outlook.com account in Outlook 2010 via the Outlook Hotmail Connector. Let me help you out here.

 

Outlook 2010 will use the Hotmail Connector to sync with the Outlook.com account; it will not use Exchange Active Sync.

 

From the sync error logs, it seems that Outlook Connector is facing issues while syncing the items in the Deleted Items and Junk Email folders. You may not be able to follow the steps in KB 2231628 since these folders are default folders in Outlook and they cannot be deleted. 

 

Instead, you may try the steps provided below and see if that helps:

a.       Empty the items in the Deleted Items and Junk Email folders in Outlook.

b.      Close Outlook.

c.       Login to Outlook.com and empty the same folders in the webmail.

d.      Now reopen Outlook and check if Outlook syncs fine.

 

You may have to sync multiple times before the error clears out. So if you get the same errors again, click on ‘Send/Receive All Folders’ 4 or 5 times (give an interval of few minutes between each sync) and check if the sync completes successfully.

 

Hope that helps. Let us know in case you need more information. Thank you.

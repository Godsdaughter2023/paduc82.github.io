philipradler say:

Assuming your overall page margins are already set at one inch all the way around, try this:

1. Double-click on the header to access it.

2. On the horizontal ruler (the one for the header you're now in), click and drag the left indent marker to the 0.5-inch point. (The left indent marker is that tiny rectangle under the indent arrows). All three markers will tag along, and your header margin ONLY will now be 0.5 inch from the left edge of the page. (The overall page margins won't change.)

3. Go to the "Right Indent" marker in the header (the up-pointing arrow at that's probably at the 6.5-inch point); click and drag it half an inch to the right.

4. Now that your header margins are set, insert your graphic.

 

It worked on my system; hope it helps on yours as well.

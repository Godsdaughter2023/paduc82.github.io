bernie deitrick say:

To round a time to the nearest 1/4 hour, first multiply the time by 24*4  - (96 - the number of 1/4 hour periods in one day), round it, then divide by 96.  For a time in cell A2

=ROUND(A2*96,0)/96

Or use

=MROUND(A2,"0:15")

And, of course, format the cell for time.

Using ROUND (or another variation, like ROUNDUP) gives you a little more control than MROUND.

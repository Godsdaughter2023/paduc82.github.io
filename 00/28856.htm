Since you just want a clickable placeholder, you can use a MACROBUTTON field that doesn't actually call a macro:

What is the formula to add and substract Acres and guntas - Microsoft Community

luther gwilson say:

What is the formula to add and substract Acres and guntas (Area)

I need formula for following calculation.

40 guntas = 1 acre.

Then how to add 2 acres 35 guntas + 2 acres 25 guntas ? ( Total is 5 acres 20 guntas ) Next, how to subtract 3 acres 20 guntas - 1 acre 30 guntas ?

amit tandon say:

Adding A2 & B2 with A3 & B3:

Formula in cell A5 (acres):

=A2+A3+INT((B2+B3)/40)

Formula in cell B5 (guntas):

=MOD(B2+B3,40)

Subtract: D2 & E2 minus D3 & E3:

Formula in cell D5 (acres):

=INT(((D2*40+E2)-(D3*40+E3))/40)

Formula in cell E5 (guntas):

=MOD((D2*40+E2)-(D3*40+E3),40)

roady [mvp] say:

When you configure an additional Outlook.com account, you can set that as the default data file while keeping the delivery of your POP3 account to a pst-file and keeping that as the default account. Outlook will then use Outlook.com mailbox to create its Calendar items in. However, this is a bit tricky to set up in Outlook as it will require mail rules. Outlook 2007 and later allows you to set it up without rules.

 

Another way to go would be to install a sync tool such as CodeTwo Folderync.

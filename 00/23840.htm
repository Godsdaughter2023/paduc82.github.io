hansv mvp say:

Because of the word "at", Excel sees the value as text, not as date/time.

Let's say the dates are in A2 and down.

If necessary, insert an empty column in column B.

Enter the following formula in B2:

=--SUBSTITUTE(A2,"at","")

You should see a number.

Format B2 with the desired standard format.

Fill down.

Hi YvonneJury,

To upload documents from iPhone to OneDrive for Business, the best practice is open the documents with Microsoft office application such as Microsoft Word that can be downloaded from APP store. Then save this local document to OneDrive for Business. To do that, please refer to the following steps:

1. Sign into the Microsoft Word application with your Work or School account on iPhone.
2. Click Open > iPhone > click the upload icon behind the document > Move to Cloud.
3. Click Add a Place > OneDrive for Business > sign into OneDrive for Business.
4. Select the OneDrive for Business place to save this document.

¡firedog say:

... attempting to scan photos via email ...

This indicates that the Microsoft Account you're signing in with has been migrated to the new platform, which means that the 'photo email' feature you've been using no longer works, not even with a single picture. It's the Send by email function of your scanner that invokes the photo email feature. The email may be sent, but the picture seems not to be uploaded to OneDrive, so the recipient will only have the thumbnail image in the message itself. 

You'll either have to send the pictures as attachments in the traditional way, in which case they will be at full size, or embed them in the message body where you can resize them and apply other effects.

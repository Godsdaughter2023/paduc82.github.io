wunders say: You probably don't want to disable one at a time.  Try groups of 20 or 30 at a time.  I'm not sure that you need to reboot between tries.  A few sample extensions that I tested were disabled immediately and right-clicking on a file did not show options for that extension.  It wouldn't surprise me that you might not be able to disable every extension.  Work with the ones that you can and see if anything helps.  Look in the "Filename" column and suspect any file that begins with "\\" or a drive letter that doesn't correspond to a hard drive on your system.

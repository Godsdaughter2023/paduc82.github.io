Need to add value from cell in previous sheet to following sheets in a - Microsoft Community

ajacneill say:

Need to add value from cell in previous sheet to following sheets in a series of 12 sheets.

Need to add valuse from cell in previous sheet to following sheet/s in a series of sheets.

Moved from: Office / Excel / Other/unknown / Office 2010

gord dibben say:

Since you have 12 sheets you may want to be doing other computations across
sheets.

This UDF can help with that.

Copy/paste this UDF to a general module in your workbook.

Function PrevSheet(Rg As Range)
'accounts for more than one workbook open
Dim n As Variant
    With Application.Caller.Parent
        n = .Index
        Do
            If n = 1 Then
                PrevSheet = CVErr(xlErrRef)
                Exit Do
            ElseIf TypeName(.Parent.Sheets(n - 1)) <> "Chart" And _
                .Parent.Sheets(n - 1).Visible = xlSheetVisible Then
                PrevSheet = .Parent.Sheets(n - 1).Range(Rg.Address).Value
                Exit Do
            End If
            n = n - 1
        Loop
    End With
End Function

Example of usage. . . . . .

Say you have 12 sheets,  sheet1 through sheet12. . . . . .sheet names don't
matter.

In sheet1 you have a formula in A10  =SUM(A1:A9)

Select second sheet and SHIFT + Click last sheet to group those sheets.

In active sheet A10 enter =SUM(PrevSheet(A10),A1:A9)

Ungroup the sheets.

Each A10 will have the sum of the previous sheet's A10 plus the sum of the
current sheet's A1:A9

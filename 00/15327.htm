Thank you for posting to the forum regarding the issue you are having with the Xbox Music application.  The first thing I would like to explore is whether this is happening with local files stored on the phone or streaming files from the cloud.  Below you will find a thing to try for each type of file.  If this happens with both types of files, please do the steps suggested for each.

If this is happening with the streaming files, those designated with the ((.)) symbol, I would suggest you remove the content from your cloud collection and then re-add the music.  The easiest way to do this would be to go to the following site on a computer, http://music.xbox.com .  Once you are signed into this site with the Microsoft account on your phone, go to your collection, locate the trouble files and delete them.  Once delete, go to the store and re-add the song files to your collection.  Once this is done, go to the phone > close the Xbox Music application > go to settings > emails + accounts > tap and hold the Microsoft account > select sync.  After the account syncs, launch the Xbox Music application and try the songs again.

If this is happening with local files on the phone please connect your phone to a computer and copy all the music files to the PC.  You will find the options to do this here, http://www.windowsphone.com/en-US/how-to/wp8/music-and-videos/sync-music-ringtones-and-podcasts-using-my-computer .  Once the files are copied, remove any SD card you might be using.  Be sure to follow the safe removal process here, http://www.windowsphone.com/en-us/how-to/wp8/phones-and-hardware/use-an-sd-card-to-add-space .  Copy the files back to the phone storage.  After the files are re-added to the phone launch Xbox Music and try playing the files again.  If you had been storing the files on the SD card, delete those files from the card and re-add the card to your phone.  Move the music to the SD card and verify if you have the same experience.

Hi,

 

Please be informed that the Subdomain addressing or Plus Addressing is currently not available in OUtlook.com. If you like you can visit this link and make a suggestion about it. On the other hand, creation of an alias that you can use for sending and receiving emails is available. If you want to create a new alias, please follow these steps:

 

  1. Sign in to your Microsoft account using this link: https://account.live.com/ManageAssocIds
  2. Click on Add an email address
  3. Verify that “Create a new Microsoft account to add to your account” is selected.
  4. Enter the desired user ID and then click "Add".
  5. A confirmation message that the alias was successfully added should appear on the Alias list page.

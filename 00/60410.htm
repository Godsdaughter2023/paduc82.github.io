Outdated sound driver is one of the usual reasons why you're experiencing this issue. We would appreciate if you could provide the answer to the following questions to further isolate the concern:

  • What specific troubleshooting steps did you perform when you received the error?
  • Did you perform any changes prior to the issue?
  • Are you using an external or internal speaker?
  • Is the issue happening on online movies, songs, and videos?

In the meantime, we suggest running the troubleshooter on this article: Fix sound problems.

This will help in identifying the root cause of the issue and provide appropriate fix for it.

However, if you've already tried the troubleshooter mentioned above, we recommend updating your drivers using the steps below:

  1.  Right-click on the Start menu.
  2.  Open Device Manager.
  3. UnderSound, video and game controllers, right-click on the driver for your sound card.
  4. Choose Properties, then click the Driver tab.
  5. Under the Driver tab, choose Update your driver.
  6. If Windows doesn't find a new driver, you can try looking for one on your device manufacturer's website and follow their instructions.

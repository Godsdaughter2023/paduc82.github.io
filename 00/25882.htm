Private Sub Workbook_Open()
    Dim Slr As SlicerCache

    Next Slr

Looking at your document, I see something similar. The document content appears to disappear and then the view returns to Draft view. At a guess, the document is damaged.

Do the following which seems to help here: In the existing document, turn off Track Changes mode. Create a new, blank document and make sure that Tracked Changes are off in the new document as well. Then copy the content of the damaged file, minus the final paragraph mark, into the new, blank document.

To show/hide paragraph marks (¶) as well as other nonprinting marks, click the ¶ icon on the Home tab.

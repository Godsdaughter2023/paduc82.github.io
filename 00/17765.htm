If the paragraphs are in the Normal style, and that style is defined (in the document) as single-spaced, then Ctrl+Q will reset them. basith m say:

Hi,

I tried to re-create the issue on my computer and I was able to recreate it. The fix for this issue is to enable the option "Allow the device to wake the computer" in Power Management for Network Adapter on your computer.

Follow the steps to enable the Power Management option:

a. Press Windows + X keys and click on Device Manager.

b. Search for Network Adapter.

c. Right click on the Network Adapter that you use and click Properties.

d. Click on the Power Management tab.

e. Check the option "Allow the device to wake the computer".

f. Click Apply and Ok to save the changes.

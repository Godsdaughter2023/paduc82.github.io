catherinekomly say:

what type USB Cable do I need for Espson Stylus 125 printer

Just installed a Epson Stylus NX125 printer but USB cable I had for old printer doesn't plug in to back of new printer. What type of USB cable do I need?

Cathy

 

jeeped say:

... What type of USB cable do I need?


The USB for an Epson Stylus NX125 is no different than any other standard USB printer cable.

                                  
You need a cable with a Type A on one end (computer) and a Type B on the other end (printer).
                                               
Try to get a cable that is rated as USB 2.0. You don't need to spend 20-25 bucks at Staples or Best Buy. Cables in large stores are sold at a huge markup. My local dollar store has 5ft A-B USB cables for a buck-and-a-half and I haven't had one fail yet.

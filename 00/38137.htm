The Xbox is connected to a  Yamaha RX-V379 and then to a TCL US5800, both of which are HDCP 2.2 compliant. They are connected using 2.0 HDMI cables.

bartmcq say:

Actually I figured out the problem this morning. It was simple and totally my own fault. The Yamaha RX-V379 is, in fact, HDCp 2.2 compliant, but only for HDMI in port #4. So the Xbox One X MUST be plugged into HDMI port #4 to play 4k apps. 


I hope that this helps anyone that was having the same problem as I.

Go to...Start / Computer...

Look at the drive letters and see if one of them
represents the camera. It may say Removable Disk

may be low after a long photo shoot could result in
corrupted photos.

Hi Robert,

To disable auto-forwarding of emails from your Gmail account to your Outlook.com inbox, you need to disable it from Gmail's settings. To verify if auto-forward is enabled in your Gmail account, follow the steps below.

  1. Go to your Inbox.
  2. Click on the Gearicon > select More Mail Settings.
  3. Under Managing your accounts, click Your email account.
  4. Locate the Gmail account > click the Details link next to it.
  5. Click Remove and then follow the prompt.

Use a custom flag field (Flag1) to tag yes/no on the tasks you want to select.

john - project say:

Trevor,

Slight correction. The percent complete for a group summary, (or any summary for that matter), is NOT the average of the percent complete of all subtasks. For a summary the percent complete is:

(Sum of actual durations of subtasks)/(sum of durations of subtasks)*100%

John

I agree that percent complete at summary level, and even at subtask level, is essentially meaningless since it is calculated from duration values only and generally has no bearing on the amount of work needed and accomplished. Rachel,

Okay, but did you read the part of my response to Trevor that noted the near uselessness of using percent complete as a valid metric. Nonetheless if you still have your heart set on tracking percent complete at a group summary level, then you will need multiple flag fields to isolate the tasks of interest in each department.

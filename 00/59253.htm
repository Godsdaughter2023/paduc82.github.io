Recover Win10 image to a smaller disk - Microsoft Community

brian4882 say:

Recover Win10 image to a smaller disk

By experimenting I have recovered  a Win10 image to a smaller disk than it came from. But have I done enough?

The original disc that failed was 680GB with about 70GB of data and the new disk is a 240GB SSD.

I used Win10 recovery USB to clean install Win10 on the SSD in the original laptop.
Then in my desktop I used Windows10 Disk Management to create VHDs of the two image files (.vhdx) 11GB and 250GB from the backup.
I reduced the size of the larger one to a size that will fit but still bigger than the 70GB of data
I then used MiniTool Partition Wizard Free to copy the 11GB to the SSD 2nd partition and the other to the 3rd partition.
I put the SSD back in the laptop, booted and let Windows do a repair.

Seems to be OK now, but it may not be ideal.

Hi Angus,

Let me help you.

All the notebooks are saved in your OneDrive and there is no export or import function in OneNote for Windows. Actually, you don't need to export or import, do following steps to move the sections to your personal account is enough.

1.Add your personal OneDrive as a location in OneNote client.

2.Create a new notebook in your personal OneDrive.

3.right click the sections you want to move, select move to.

4.Then go to https://www.onenote.com/hrd with your personal account and see if the new notebook is here.

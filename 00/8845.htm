jeeped say:
... Why doesn't C7 - B7 work in Mike H's post?

It doesn't work because you are using one formula with relative references for two cells. The formula as provided uses the references within the formula for the first cell. The second cell uses a relative adaptation of the formula just as if you copied the formula from one cell to another. C7-B7 would be interpreted as D7-C7 from the point of C7. By locking the row or columns with the absolute reference marker (e.g. $), it doesn't change relative to its location.

a. user say:

Hello,

 

No, we cannot merge old and brand new accounts. You can Auto-forward your Hotmail account to your new Outlook account. You’ll have all of your mail in one place, and can reply to it using your new address.

 

To auto-forward your email:

 

1.       Log in to your Hotmail account.

2.       Click Options, then click More options.

3.       Under Managing your account, click Email forwarding. You might have to sign in.

4.       Select the radio button for Forward your mail to another email account.

5.       Enter the email address for your new Outlook account.

6.       If you want, check the box for Keep a copy of forwarded messages in your Hotmail inbox.

7.       Click Save.

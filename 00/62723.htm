Thank you for reaching out to Skype Community.

Yes, you can have both a subscription and pay-as-you-go option at the same time. As these have different coverage, the system will choose the option more appropriate to cover a specific call.

For example, you have a subscription to call USA landlines and you also have Skype Credits (as a pay-as-you-go option). If you call USA mobiles, which are not covered by your active subscription, the system will use your Skype Credits.

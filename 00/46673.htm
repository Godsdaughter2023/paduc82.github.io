msauer002 say: Surface Pro stylus stopped working and screen would no longer rotate to portrait view.

This has happened to me twice since I got my device on launch day.  Both times this worked for me:

1. Shut down the device through the standard interface option (either using the touch screen or the mouse pointer if either are working.  Otherwise hold the power button for 10 seconds.
2. Completely power down the device by next holding the power button and volume up button together for 15 seconds minimum.  The Surface logo may appear a time or two, but keep holding the buttons for 15 seconds.  Release the buttons.
3. Hold the volume up button and press and release the power button.  When the Surface logo appears, release the volume up button.
4. You should be seeing the BIOS settings.  Test the stylus in this mode.  This has worked for me both times the main OS stopped responding to the stylus.
5.  If the stylus works in the BIOS screen, you should be good.  Select "Exit Setup" and the machine should boot normally.

This worked for me both times I had the issue.  It also resolved the rotating screen issue.

stefan blom say:

"Don't show the Backstage..." is misleading in the sense that it doesn't really disable the Backstage. At any time when you click the File tab, you explicitly enter the Backstage; that doesn't change even after you have selected the option.

The "Don't show the Backstage when opening or saving files" option, when selected, lets you use Ctrl+O as the shortcut to display the Open dialog box, instead of displaying the Open category of the Backstage. You can also use F12 to display the Save As dialog box.

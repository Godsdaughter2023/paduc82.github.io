Hi Chris,

You can't have a formfield in Header or Footer. See: http://support.microsoft.com/kb/110342

You should put the formfield into the body of the document and the cross-reference to it in the footer. You should also set the formfield's 'calculate on exit' property.

And all your old emails will be stored in this folder.

graham mayor say:
Protected forms have a deliberately limited functionality. They are intended only for the gathering of information. If you upgrade to Office 2007 or 2010, you will find that Word now includes content controls which do not require that the form be protected and thus allow spell check to work.

There will be no further development to the functionality of Word 2003.

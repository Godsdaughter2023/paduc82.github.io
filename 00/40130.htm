Hi,

There are several reasons that can cause a BIOS loop. Have you made any recent changes on your Surface? This screen normally appears after performing a recovery/reset on your Surface. It tells you that a Trusted Platform Module (TPM) configuration change was requested.

We recommend that you connect your Surface Typing Cover and once you get to the American Megatrends screen, press Fn+F12, and your Surface should continue to Windows.

  • If Windows starts: Install the latest Surface and Windows updates to help prevent future problems. For more info, see Install Surface and Windows updates.
  • If Windows doesn’t start: If the American Megatrends TPM screen appears again, contact our Answer Desk for Surface, or our Global Support for assistance.

Let us know if you have any additional concerns.

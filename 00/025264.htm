3. Then you can add product key for the Business Essential plans or Business Premium plans to your current tenant.
4. Finally, you can cancel the Office 365 Business Essentials or Business Premium plans (credit card).

johan_d. say:

Hi Samuel Rosenbaum,

 

Welcome to Microsoft Community. Let me assist you with setting up your third party email account to send and receive on Outlook.com.

 

Outlook.com currently does not have the option to support IMAP connection for send and receiving. However, you can setup your third party account as s POP3 account. To do this, you can follow the steps below:

  1. Login to https://mail.live.com.
  2. Click on the gear icon  on the upper right part of the mailbox page and choose More mail settings.
  3. Under Managing your account, click on Your email accounts.
  4. Under Add an email account, click on Add a send-and-receive account.
  5. Click on Advanced options.
  6. Enter your third party email account, password, POP3 and SMTP settings.
  7. Click on Next.

After adding your third party email account as a send and receive successfully on Outlook.com, you will be able to use the email account as a "send as" on Outlook.com by simply clicking on the dropdown menu next to your email address and choosing the third party email account.

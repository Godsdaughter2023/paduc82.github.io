    neo yu msft say:

    Hi amitbahl4589,

    The conversion steps are needed if we want to deploy DirSync/AAD Sync/AAD Connect with Office 365 after the cutover migration.

    If your mailbox is in the hosted Exchange and no Exchange related attributes are stored in the AD users, then we also can’t deploy DirSync with Office 365 since the on-premises settings will overwrite the attributes exists in Office 365. If that’s the case, it’s recommended that you first convert the mailboxes to mail-enabled users, export the user attributes from the hosted Exchange server and import them into the on-premises AD, then deploy DirSync with Office 365.

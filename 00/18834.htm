qa guy commelec say:

Try this --

SELECT Q02aSelectDataMerge.ID, Q02aSelectDataMerge.[Date Worked], Q02aSelectDataMerge.Time, Q02aSelectDataMerge_1.Time, Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.Lunch, Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.Location, Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.Details
FROM (Q02aSelectDataMerge INNER JOIN Q02aSelectDataMerge AS Q02aSelectDataMerge_1 ON (Q02aSelectDataMerge.[Date Worked] = Q02aSelectDataMerge_1.[Date Worked]) AND (Q02aSelectDataMerge.ID = Q02aSelectDataMerge_1.ID)) INNER JOIN Q02aSelectDataMerge AS Q02aSelectDataMerge_2 ON (Q02aSelectDataMerge.[Date Worked] = Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.[Date Worked]) AND (Q02aSelectDataMerge.ID = Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.ID)
GROUP BY Q02aSelectDataMerge.ID, Q02aSelectDataMerge.[Date Worked], Q02aSelectDataMerge.Time, Q02aSelectDataMerge_1.Time, Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.Lunch, Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.Location, Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.Details
HAVING (((Q02aSelectDataMerge_1.Time)>[Q02aSelectDataMerge].[Time]) AND ((Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.Lunch) Is Not Null)) OR (((Q02aSelectDataMerge_1.Time)>[Q02aSelectDataMerge].[Time]) AND ((Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.Location) Is Not Null)) OR (((Q02aSelectDataMerge_1.Time)>[Q02aSelectDataMerge].[Time]) AND ((Q02aSelectDataMerge_2.Details) Is Not Null));

Edited for records that might not have data in Location & Details.

bob jones aka: cybertaz say:
I believe what you're referring to is this:


Which indicates that a file cannot be found -- In all probability it has been deleted & the Trash has been emptied.

This is normal behavior. The Recent Documents list tracks all documents you've opened & saved but [IMO] it isn't as richly designed a it should have been. It does not remove files that are no longer available, nor is there any way to manually delete individual items. I'm afraid it's an 'all-or-nothing' situation.

You can go to Word> Preferences - General to clear the check on Track recently opened documents which clears the entire list. Go back to restore the check to turn the feature back on. It will then start a fresh list.

The list can also be cleared by just going to File> Open Recent - Clear Recent.

HI Barry. I'm Greg, an installation specialist and 9 year Windows MVP here to help you.

Contacts are stored in the People app, which like the Windows 10 Mail and Calendar apps is a sync service that syncs your web or Office mail accounts you add to any or all of those apps. More here:

When you upgrade to Windows 10, the previous Windows files and folders are automatically stored in C:\Windows.old folder. This folder is kept by Windows only for one month.

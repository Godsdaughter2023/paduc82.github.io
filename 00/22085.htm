Hi,

 

I assume that in the formula you want C7 to be 8 characters but you dont tell us what character you want if it's less than 8. This pads out with leading zeroes.

 

=REPT(0,8-LEN(C7))&C7

 

If it'spossible for C7 to be more than 8 characters and you want them all use this

 

=REPT(0,MAX(0,8-LEN(C7)))&C7

=REPT(" ",MAX(0,8-LEN(C7)))&C7

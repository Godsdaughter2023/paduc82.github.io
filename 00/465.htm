How do I copy a file on a disc to Word? - Microsoft Community

barbzeiders say:

How do I copy a file on a disc to Word?

I have a resume on a disc that I need to copy to Word.

a. user say:

Hi,

You actually need to drag the resume on the disk into your documents folder. To do this:
  1. Put the CD in the computer.
  2. Click on "Open folder to view files" in the box that pops up
  3. Make the box kind of small, and drag it over to the right-hand side of your screen.
  4. Open up your Documents folder, make it small, and drag it to the left-hand side of your screen.
  5. Drag your resume file into the Documents folder.
  6. Close both boxes, and take your disk out of the computer.
  7. Go to Word. Click on the File tab, and then click on Open.
  8. Navigate to your resume document, and double-click on it.
Your resume should now be open.

Hope that helps!
Kara

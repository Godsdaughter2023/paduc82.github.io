doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

The following code will add a dropdown formfield. populated with the entries Item 1 through Item 10 at the location of the selection.

Dim i As Long
Dim ff As FormField
With ActiveDocument
    Set ff = .FormFields.Add(Selection.Range, wdFieldFormDropDown)
    For i = 1 To 10
        ff.DropDown.ListEntries.Add "Item " & i
    Next i
    .Protect wdAllowOnlyFormFields, NoReset
End With

Before trying Harsha's suggested settings (which can open up security problems) I suggest doing this:

Save the email attachment to any convenient location on your hard drive (or do the same with downloaded files).

Right click the downloaded file's icon and choose Properties.

If the General tab of the properties dialog box includes an "UNBLOCK" button (and if you trust the sender) click it.

Then close the dialog box and doubleclick the file's icon to open it in PPT.

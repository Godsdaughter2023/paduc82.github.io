considerate_guy say:

You are correct.  There are no adjustments for contrast, tone or color in Movie Maker, only brightness.  You can change the overall tone to black & white, cyan or sepia tone, but I don't think that is what you are talking about.


The only suggestions I have are:

  1. Suggest this as feedback to the Movie Maker team for possible inclusion in a future release.
  2. Consider installing Windows Movie Maker 6.0 and custom effects. In particular, look at the Contrast effects and the Film Look effects. This version of WMM 6.0 can be installed side-by-side with the latest Movie Maker.  You could correct/alter a clip in WMM 6.0, save it and then make further edits in the newer version.
  3. Look into higher-end software that might include these adjustments natively such as Adobe Premiere (or Premiere Elements), Pinnacle Studio, Nero Video, etc.

If Value in Column B is "Win" , want user to have a drop down list of 3 options each with a different color code.

bernard liengme say:

Your first two specifications are fine.

We use a formula to get either NA (B =No bid or Lost) or a blank (B= Pending)

We use conditional formatting to get grey or yellow.

The formula for grey is =OR(B3="No bid", B3="Lost")

But rule 3 is a problem. We cannot type into the K cell without destroying the formula, so if we type (for example) X and then go back and type No bid, we will not get NA since the formula has gone.

Can we have Win1, Win2, and Win3 ?

AFTERTHOUGH: how about in K3:

=IF(OR(B3="No bid",B3="Lost"),"NA",IF(B3="Pending","", M3))

The M3 could "have a drop down list of 3 options " and we could extend the CF in the K cells.

Please ignore the dropdown on K3

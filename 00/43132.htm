anil kumar b say:

Hi Hitesh Aggarwal,

 

Welcome to Microsoft Community Forums.

 

Here is the difference between CAN (campus area network) and Lan (Local Area Network).

 

Campus Area Network: This applies to multiple small LANs that are connected across multiple buildings within a small area. It’s common to see CANs used to link up multiple buildings of a large company’s head office, a military base, or even a college campus.

 

Local Area Network: This is the simplest and most common type of network. Most LANS exist within a very small geographic area, or even within the same building. The wireless network in your home can be said to be a type of LAN.

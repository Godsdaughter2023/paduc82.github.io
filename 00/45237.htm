a. user say:

Hi Sam,

If the partition that you want to use is right next to your C drive, you can try to expand your C drive through Disk Management. The process involves the deletion of the volume of the partition that you're going to use to expand your system partition. We recommend that you back up your computer by following the steps in this article: Back up and restore your files. Once done with the backup process, kindly follow these steps using an administrator account to expand your C drive:

  1. Type computer management on the search bar.
  2. Click on Computer Management.
  3. On the left panel, click on Storage, then click on Disk Management.
  4. Right click on the partition that you want to use to expand your C drive, then click on Delete Volume.
  5. Right click on C drive, click on Extend Volume, then click Next.
  6. Specify the size that you want to add to your C drive, then click on Next.
  7. Click on Finish to extend your C drive.

Let us know if you need further assistance with your concern.

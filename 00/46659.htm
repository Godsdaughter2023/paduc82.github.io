Fix IDT High Definition Audio CODEC problem (no sound):

1. Open Device Manager.
2. Under Sound, video and game controllers select IDT High Definition Audio CODEC.
3. Right click then select Update Driver.

6. Erase any entry in the Search for drivers in this location: box.
7. Select High Definition Audio Device (Not  IDT High Def...  this is important.) and click Next.
    Reply Yes to Warning message.
8. Windows has successfully updated your driver software.

9. Restart your computer.

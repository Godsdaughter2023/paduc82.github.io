Please be informed that there are several ways on how you can block spam emails in your mailbox. Here are some options that you can set up in your account:

  • Junk mail filter - Selecting Exclusive option. This feature will let you receive emails from your contact list and safe senders list.

  • Email rules - You can set up email rules to filter incoming emails to automatically delete them and not getting them in your Inbox.

  • Blocked senders list - You can add email address/es or domain/s in this list to stop receiving emails from them. You also need to delete them from your contact list and safe senders list as well.

  • Sweep features - Sweeping emails for deletion.

    To set up a junk mail filter to Exclusive, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your Microsoft account .
  2. Click the gear icon located on the upper right panel of your mailbox, just beside your username.
  3. Select More mail settings.
  4. Under Preventing junk email, click on Filters and reporting option.
  5. Under Choose a junk email filter, select Exclusive.
  6. Click the Save button.

If you want to create an email rule to delete emails coming from an email address, kindly refer to these steps:

  1. Sign in to your Microsoft account .
  2. Click the gear icon located on the upper right panel of your mailbox, just beside your username.
  3. Select More mail settings.
  4. Under Customizing Outlook, click the Rules for sorting new messages option.
  5. Click the New button.
  6. Under Step 1: Which messages do you want this rule to apply to?, on the first box with drop down list, select the Sender's address and in the second box with drop down list, select yes.
  7. On the third box, type sender's email address. You may select any option that you want to use at first, second and third box depending on your preference.
  8. Under Step 2: What action do you want to apply?, select Delete these messages.
  9. Click the Save button.

You can also add the sender's email address to your Blocked sender list to stop receiving emails from that contact. Here's how: 

  1. Sign in to your Microsoft account.
  2. Click the gear icon located on the upper right panel of your mailbox, just beside your username.

  5. Click Blocked senders.

If you sign in with a MSA during OOBE it activates bitlocker apparently (?) and stores the key on your MSA. I don´t know if it´s supposed to be like this..

Sign in with your password and type in the following command:

"manage-bde -unlock C: -Recoverypassword ENTER-RECOVERY-KEY-HERE" and press enter.

You can check the status of the decryption by typing "manage-bde -status" and it will show the % of the process. As soon as it´s done it´s gone.

Change Wireless Channel

Login Information

  • Gateway - 192.168.0.1
  • Username - (blank)
  • Password - (blank)

Change Wireless Channel

  1. Open a web browser.
  2. Enter http://192.168.0.1 into the address bar. (Or click the link) Enter the username and password if the gateway asks. (Default is blank.)
  3. Click on Wireless Setup from the menu across the top.
  4. Click on Channel from the side menu.
  5. In the drop down box labeled Channel select the desired channel. (Channels 1,3,9,11)

At startup/power on you should see at the bottom of the screen either F2 or DELETE, to go into Bios/Setup, or F12 for the Boot Menu.

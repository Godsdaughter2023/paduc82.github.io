Going through the various responses in this thread, something seriously doesn't make sense specifically as it relates to the loss of emails

 

#1 - Unless you explicitly deleted the email account altogether at the server level, whatever emails existed prior to you doing anything related to the Outlook profile would continue to be there

#4 - There is nothing to prevent the same email account being set up as a POP account on one computer and as an IMAP account on another. What remains on the server depends on the settings related to the POP account.

 

First thing to do is search for all PST files on the primary computer in question (make sure that <hidden files> are included in the generic search for <*.pst> files.

john w. vinson say:

I'm surprised it works at all - the = in the middle of the expression is incorrect.

What you can do is use a sneaky trick: both + and & concatenate strings, but they handle NULL differently. If you concatenate two values with & a NULL will be treated as if it were an empty string; if you use + the entire expression will be NULL. So you can use:

=[FirstName] & (" and " + [Spouse]) & " " & [LastName]

If Spouse is null the entire parenthesized expression is NULL; if not it concatenates the word "and" so you will get either:

John Doe

or 

John and Jane Doe

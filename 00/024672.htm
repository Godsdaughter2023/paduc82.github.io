     ThisAddin.WordApp.Selection.Range.Text = oSlide.NotesPage.Shapes.Placeholders(2).TextFrame.TextRange.Text

rich michaels - mvp (greatcirclelearning.com) say:

In addition to what Jay has already mentioned about using DoEvents, the best thing you can do to help your processing of large PowerPoint slide decks is stay off the Clipboard as much as possible.

In my VSTO add-ins that use PowerPoint slides in Word documents, I export the slides to image files and then use an Insert Picture method to bring them into the Word document. If you do this I will bet that the clipboard issue you are having at times with Notes will also be resolved because the processing to and from the clipboard won't get overloaded as much. And, just as a precautionary measure, I advocate clearing the clipboard after each copy/paste sequence, which I see in your code that you are already doing.

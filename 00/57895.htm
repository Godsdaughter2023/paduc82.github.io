For the meantime, please do try these troubleshooting steps that may help in solving that issue.

  1. Right click on the speaker icon and select Recording Devices.
  2. Once open, right click on the default mic and select Properties.
  3. Click Listen tab and remove the check on "Listen to this device" if it has check on it.

anyway to completely disable IFrames..?? - Microsoft Community

mofukaz75 say:

anyway to completely disable IFrames..??

Is there anyway to completely disable IFrames from being rendered when viewing webpages in IE7 or IE8 at all.??
I just want to completely disable IFrames in IE altogether if possible for safety purposes..But i still wan JavaScript Enabled..

Is anything like this feasible at all..??
deepakmm say:

Hello MoFukAZ75

  1. Open Internet Explorer, select Tools | Internet Options
  2. Click the Security tab
  3. Choose the desired zone (Internet, Local intranet, Trusted sites, or Restricted Sites) and click Custom Level
  4. Scroll down to Launching programs and files in an IFRAME
  5. Select Disable to prevent iframes altogether or Prompt if you wish to decide on a site-by-site basis.
  6. Click OK
  7. Repeat for each of the desired security zones.
  8. Click OK to exit the Internet Options menu.

 

Nothing is impossible.. Deepak M

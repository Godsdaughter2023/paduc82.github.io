If the messages are arriving in your Junk Email folder, they are already considered blocked. You cannot prevent the delivery outright, only control the destination folder. If you have your junk filter set to "Exclusive", the Blocked Sender list is pointless. Only messages from your safe senders (your contacts and the addresses or domains in the Safe Senders list) will arrive in the Inbox. All other messages are already considered "blocked" (i.e., will arrive in the Junk Email folder).

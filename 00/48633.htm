zer0w1n say:

Only one account can be set as the home account for your Xbox One. Therefore, if you set up your account as the home account and there is an existing home account already set, his account will no longer be the home account. Your account will now be the home account, and only your games can be accessed by everyone using the same console. The previous home account's games will no longer be accessible to anyone but him.

hansv mvp say:

Values such as 2-102 are alphanumeric, not numeric, so Excel sorts them alphanumerically, not numerically.

Let's say your data are in column A, starting in A2.

If necessary, insert blank columns in B and C.

In B2, enter =1*LEFT(A2,FIND("-",A2)-1)

In C2, enter =1*MID(A2,FIND("-",A2)+1,100)

Fill B2:C2 down as far as needed.

Sort on C2, then on B2.

 

Alternatively, if the number after the - will never me more than 6 digits:

Enter =1000000*LEFT(A2,FIND("-",A2)-1)+MID(A2,FIND("-",A2)+1,100) in cell B2

Fill down as far as needed.

Sort on column B.

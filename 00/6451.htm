Thank you for posting to the forum regarding the update error you have received.  There are a couple of things that we have seen cause this error.  The most common is an issue with the firmware.  If this is the case then the mobile operator will have to be contacted to look into getting a firmware update or reinstall.  In some cases the phone must be replaced or repaired.

 

Before we settle on this there are a couple other things to try.   The first is related to the space on your phone and on your computer.  Please try the following steps and then attempt the update one more time.

 

Not enough free space on the phone:

To remove media from the phone:

  1. Start the Zune Music + Video software on your computer.
  2. Click Phone and then select music, videos, pictures, or podcasts as appropriate.
  3. Right-click the files that you want to delete. Then, click Delete from <device name>.

Not enough space on your hard disk.

There isn't enough available space on your computer's hard disk. Remove any nonessential programs, documents, or media files and try again. You can also free up some disk space by running the Disk Cleanup wizard.

To run Disk Cleanup:

  1. Click Start.
  2. Click All Programs.
  3. Click Accessories.
  4. Click System Tools.
  5. Click Disk Cleanup.


With the latest version of the Zune software, you can choose not to back up your phone's existing software and settings. This option appears in the Zune software when it detects that your computer doesn't have enough free space. Keep in mind that if you choose to skip the backup and the update doesn't complete successfully, you might not be able to restore your phone's software and settings to their previous states.

 

The final thing to try would be to connect your phone to another computer on another network.  We suggest you attempt this from a computer that has administrator rights on a network that is not restricted by any corporate networking rules.

 

Please let us know if you have any other questions or concerns we can assist you with.

 

Mark W.

van_m say:

Hi ChristinaHolloway,

 

Here are the steps below on how to unblock contacts on the available Skype application for Windows.

 

On Skype for Windows 10 UWP:

 

  1. Open Profile window by pressing the Profile icon shown at the top of the Recent list.
  2. In the Profile window select Settings.
  3. In the Settings window scroll down to the Contacts section.
  4. In this section select the option Manage blocked contacts.

 

On classic Skype for Desktop:

 

  1. In the main menu, select Tools > Options.
  2. In the Skype Options box, select Privacy > Blocked Contacts. A list of blocked contacts is displayed.
  3. Select the contact you'd like to unblock and click Unblock this person.

 

On the new Skype app for Desktop:

 

  1. Select your profile picture.
  2. Select Application Settings.
  3. Scroll down to Privacy and select Manage blocked users.
  4. Select the Unblock button next to the contact you wish to unblock.

 

Hope this information helps.

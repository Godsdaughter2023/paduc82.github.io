gza say:

Sounds like both the Arris and E900 by default use a LAN IP of 192.168.1.1.  You will need to change the LAN IP of one or the other from 192.168.1.1 to 192.168.1.X (where X makes an unused IP outside the range that the Arris is configured to hand out via DHCP).  To change it on the E900, you will probably need to temporarily unplug the Arris.  You may have to set your PC to a static IP of 192.168.1.X to reach the E900 but try without first.

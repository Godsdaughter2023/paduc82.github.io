Hi Jeff,

I have tested some similar solutions. If you have 3 mail accounts in one Outlook and redirecting rule applied on all accounts with the same trigger, it will create a loop. If we have email 1, 2 and 3 and the rule to forward to other 2 applied for all accounts, email sent to 1 will be automatically forwarded to 2 and 3 and when 2 and 3 receive the message, same trigger will be activated and the message will be redirected again from 2 and 3 and again... I believe that's why your rule doesn't work.

The possible solution is to select Forward as attachment instead of Forward message.

Also, if the other accounts are under same exchange server, you could try to create a shared folder and inbox rules for all accounts to move shipping information messages to the folder.

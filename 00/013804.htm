A real mouse?  Usually this is a symptom of using a Touch pad which has Touch screen emulation features built into it.   On a Touch screen there is no mouse pointer unless it is being driven by a real mouse.

However, since you have a full keyboard complete with a Numeric keypad, you can emulate a real mouse with Mousekeys.   If you don't have Mousekeys enabled, press and hold LeftAlt+LeftShift+NumLock until you see a prompt.  Then choose whether you are going to use Mousekeys with NumLock or without it.  Either way, with Mousekeys enabled, use one of its Cursor movement keys to move the pointer.  As soon as it is moved it will be shown.

ribastuka say:

Nokia 5800 has no integrated support for DLNA. I don't know about N8/E7 but C7-00 which is very similar doesn't seem to have either. However in previous E series models (E52, E72) DLNA is supported and actually works quite well (for pictures and music, video is useless). They can both act as a server (streaming to your PC, stereo, TV) or as a renderer (playing music from your PC, NAS, etc.) and you can choose between streaming realtime or copying a song/picture or album to your phone storage. My experience is very positive and far better than for example the DLNA clients I've found for the Apple iPad.

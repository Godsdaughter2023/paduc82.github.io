For Retention Tag:

 

Login to office 365 portal > go to Admin Center > in left side select Exchange > select Compliance Management > Select Retention Tag > Edit Retention Tag

 

For Retention Policy:

 

1. Go to Admin Center

2. Select Exchange in left side

3. Select Compliance Management

4. Select Retention policy

5. Select your Retention policy and Edit it.

6. Add (+) edited retention tag in that retention policy and Click on Save.

7. Click on ok.

 

You can also refer this article for add or remove retention tag from retention policy https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/exchange/security-and-compliance/messaging-records-management/add-or-remove-retention-tags

Can you explain the difference between a Course and a Module?

In my experience the attributes of a course are the ID, title and maybe a subject category (I.e. Math, Geology, etc.). Course may be given in different sessions (sememster?) with different sets of students. So a course record would not have the matericID or instructorID as FKs.

Generally there would be a junction table like this:

StudentCourse

StudentCourseID (PK Autonumber)

StudentID (FK)

CourseID (FK)

InstructorID (FK)

SemesterID (FK)

LocationID (FK)

etc.

You can trim this by having a separate table for session which includes the last 5 FKs and then just include SessionID in the above table.

Manual IP addressing will not help; that was the first step in setgting up Port Forwarding which is not a good solution for two Xbox consoles on one router.

In general, make sure UPnP is enabled, turn OFF any port forwarding (which keeps the second xbox from getting traffic from port 88 and port 3074) and instead set up Port Triggering on port 88 for UDP and port 3074 for UDP and TCP. Your ISP is likely the best point of contact for suppot in setting up the Port Triggering.

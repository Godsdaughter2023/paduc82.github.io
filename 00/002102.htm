You can't have more than 1 real-time scanning anti-virus product installed.

The in-built Win 8 anti-virus product Windows Defender is normally automatically disabled when another Anti-Virus product is installed.

You say that your anti-virus product of choice is Norton but you also mention McAfee.

To avoid conflicts, you need to uninstall McAfee and all of its possible remnants. Please use the McAfee removal tool from this list: List of anti-malware product removal tools - Microsoft Community

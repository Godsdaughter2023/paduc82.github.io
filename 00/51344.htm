"possible" that it is driver related (rare). Remove ALL power and reseat all cards, memory
and cables (on both ends) - actually remove and replace - do not just snug. Make sure

inspect the motherboard and other cards for damage. Pay attention to the fatal hardware
error implication.You may need a real computer shop (not leeks and peeks at a BestBuy
or other BigBox stores) or the System maker's Support.

Check the processor seatting, heat sink, and the compound between the processor
and the heat sink which are common causes.

There have been a few instances where Norton/Symantec and other antivirus/security
programs have caused this error.

rwjustus say:

This problem is caused because inept developers at Microsoft did not include Administrators in the DCOM (Distributed Communications) registry keys. Why on Earth they don't fix it is beyond me!

For the NT AUTHORITY\LOCAL SERVICE user to be able to run this application, the Administrators group must have full permissions. If you do not feel comfortable editing the registry yourself, ask an experienced friend for help. I went through these steps and no longer have problems with these issues.

These are step by step instructions. There may be some additional places where you have to approve as step but I do not feel comfortable clicking through to see what they are. Microsoft should have fixed this oversight years ago but apparently, Microsoft upper management doesn't think existing customers are worthy of support to correct mistakes made by their developers. Unfortunately, they rely heavily on users who may or may not be competent. Yes, that includes me!

  1. Find each of the keys below, right click and select Permissions.
    • {D63B10C5-BB46-4990-A94F-E40B9D520160}
    • {9CA88EE3-ACB7-47C8-AFC4-AB702511C276}
  2. Click the Advanced button.
  3. Click the Change link and change the owner to Administrators.
  4. Click the Add button and add the Administrators and give them full control.
  5. Highlight the Administrators permission entry.
  6. Check the Replace all child object permission entries with inheritable permission entries from this object check box.
  7. Click the Apply button.
  8. Click the Yes button.

You must do these steps for both keys.

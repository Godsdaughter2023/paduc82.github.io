Hi,

Attachments are too big. Windows Live Mail and Hotmail have 10 MB limits (20 meg for Hotmail Plus).

Error: "The file is too large to be attached"
http://windowslivehelp.com/solutions/cantattach/archive/2008/08/21/error-quot-the-file-is-too-large-to-be-attached-quot.aspx

There's a Size Limit When Sending Attachments with Windows Live Hotmail
http://email.about.com/od/windowslivehotmailtips/qt/Size_Limit_When_Sending_Attachments_in_Windows_Live_Hotmail.htm

There are a few limiting factors including the size limits imposed by your ISP. You could also
zip and then break it up into smaller pieces. Then send the pieces in separate emails along
with the instructions on how to put them back together.

Zip and Split - Free
http://zipandsplit.toolazy.me.uk/

Split and Zip - Free
http://www.jtoolgame.com/tools/splitzip.html

Akhir Split Zip - Free
http://www.mydigitallife.info/2009/04/24/akhir-split-zip-splits-files-into-smaller-zip-files/

Windows Live SkyDrive, Rapidshare, Megaupload, Yousendit, GigaSize and others allow that.

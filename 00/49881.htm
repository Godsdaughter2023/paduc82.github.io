This question is asked periodically and usually garners all sorts of
fervent responses, on both sides of the question. Some people never

The insider program does not provide a free license for Windows 10.  It doesn't provide anything that isn't already available to the general public.

Insiders are just users who have taken advantage of the free upgrade offer already and want to go the next mile by getting previews of changes Microsoft is proposing.  The idea is we try them out, find bugs or have comments, then Microsoft makes changes or not based on what we find before releasing the changes to the general public.

You see these happen where exactly?
You mean the dotted selection border?

These are 1 pixel in size and don't move. If they do, they aren't actually moving in Windows but could appear to be moving due to your screen setup. Common reasons are;

  • You have an analog connection to your monitor instead of a digital one. For instance, you are connected via VGA instead of DVI, HDMI, or a Display Port.
  • You have a switch box in between to switch screens between multiple computers.
  • You are not using the recommended display settings for your monitor; Screen resolution and DPI settings. This could result in the pixel becoming 1.5 pixel in size which could make it look blurry on your screen.

Your 8Gb is not a removable memory card, but built-in flash memory.

 

What you may do is connect to your PC using the PC Suite software and USB cable connection, once you have PC Suite installed it will recognise the phone being connected to the machine and Windows will beep to notify that a USB device has been connected.

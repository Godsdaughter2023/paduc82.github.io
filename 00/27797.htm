Hi Sean,

Thank you for posting in Microsoft Community.

Please be assured that we would do our best to assist you.

I suggest you to unlink your connected services with your account then remove the entire Birthday calendar and re-create it.

Here are the steps on how to remove the connected services.

  1. Sign in to https://account.live.com.

  2. Click Related accounts.

  3. Click Manage other accounts.

  4. Click Edit under the connected service that you want to remove your Microsoft account from. Ex. Facebook.

  5. Click Remove this connection completely.

  6. Click Remove.

To remove the whole Birthday calendar, you can follow the steps below.

  1.  Login to https://calendar.live.com.

  2. Click the Gear icon located at the upper-right side of the page and Options.

  3. Under Edit your reminder and calendar settings, click Birthday calendar.

  4. Click Delete located on the top part of the page.

To re-create it, click the arrow beside the New button and select Birthday Calendar.

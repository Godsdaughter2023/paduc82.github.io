marc a.. say:

Hi RickGarces,

 

If I understood you correctly you have entered your Google Account under "Outlook" on your Windows Phone and also have a separate Windows Live Account on your phone, is that correct?

 

Please note that the "Outlook" Option on your Windows Phone should only be used with an Exchange Server. That means any contacts you have on your phone that are coming from your Exchange Server/ Outlook will only be synced with your Google Account and not with your Windows Live Account.

 

Having said that, all contacts that you add to your phone and chose to sync with Windows Live will not sync with your Google Account. Otherwise you would have multiple entries on different accounts!


However, if you do feel that option should be available, please feel free to leave a suggestion in the following link:

Let's try to clear up a few things. First off the xbox is not designed to split the games audio of things happening in-game out to different audio ports. It will however accept a transient to be broadcasted via your microphone to others headsets. In other words as the xbox does not have a separate way to mix the signals of different elements and send them out to specific channels.  While playing games split screen, no matter how you set it up, you will always hear the same audio of  in-game play.

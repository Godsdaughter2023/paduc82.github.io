judith_c. say:

Hi R Parke,

 

Once you renamed your account, you can still receive incoming emails from your old Hotmail account. Your old Hotmail will be an Alias to your account where in you could also send messages.

 

Since you chose to rename your account to @outlook.com, a prompt would have asked you - "Where do you want to save your emails?” You will have the option to create a new folder for your old account or save the emails in the Inbox of your renamed account.

 

If you chose to create a new folder, the default name of the folder will be the name of your old account and the folder will appear in the left pane under "Folders". And all your old emails will be stored in this folder. Whichever folder you opt to use, incoming messages from the old Hotmail account will be directed to that folder.

 

Also, if you are using Windows phone 7 in accessing your Microsoft account, any name change will result in loss of data related to the account on the phone (hard reset). You will need to set up the phone using your new account.

armand cos say:

Hi Gordon,

Kindly check in safe mode, if renaming is working fine in safe mode. Follow the steps below:

  • Restart your computer and before Windows logon screen appears press F8 continuously which will bring “Advanced Boot” options.
  • Select “Safe mode” and hit enter to start your computer in Safe mode.
  • Once you have logged into safe mode, go to Control Panel -> User Accounts and create a new administrator user account.

If renaming your files is working fine in safe mode, perform clean boot to help resolve your issue. Here are the steps:

  • Click Start, type msconfig in the Start Search box, and then press ENTER.
  • If you are prompted for an administrator password or for a confirmation, type the password, or click Continue.
  • On the General tab, click Selective Startup.
  • Under Selective Startup, click to clear the Load Startup Items check box.
  • Click the Services tab, click to select the Hide All Microsoft Services check box, and then click Disable All.
  • Click OK.
  • When you are prompted, click Restart.

To undo the changes and start your computer normally, follow the steps below:

  • Click Start, type msconfig.exe in the Start Search box, and then press ENTER.
  • On the General tab, click the Normal Startup option, and then click OK.
  • When you are prompted to restart the computer, click Restart.

Hope this helps. Do get back to us should you need further assistance.

The GPS signal from the satellites is free.

 

A-GPS signal (faster location lookup/satellite lock) cost a bit (a few KB of data charges every time it is done).

 

The maps are free (and no data costs, if you use MapLoader on a PC to download them to your phone).

Searching/finding places is free (online searches might cost you depending on your data plan).

Additional services like city guides, etc., also cost something.

rick rogers say: Hi,

The message to press F1 to continue setup means that the CMOS battery on the motherboard is dead and needs to be replaced. Each time you shut down, the system is resetting to defaults and asking you to acknowledge this. Normally, this battery provides a trickle of power for the system BIOS to remember the hardware settings and system time (did you notice that your system time is probably wrong when initially powered on?). Once it is dead, the motherboard will always reset to defaults at power-on.

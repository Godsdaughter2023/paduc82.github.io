E1 and E3 refer to the Microsoft's online productivity suite of products covering Exchange, SharePoint, Skype, Teams, Yammer, Flow...etc.

However Azure Active Directory is not part of E1 or E3, When you sign up Office365 Microsoft provides you with Azure Active Directory Basic free of charge.

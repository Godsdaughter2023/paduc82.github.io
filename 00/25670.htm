david a matson say:

Greetings MaryLouV!  Welcome to the Windows Mobile Total Access forums!

To accomplish this process,

1.) You'll want to disconnect your device from your PC.
2.) Then open up the ActiveSync program on your PC.
3.) Click on the File Menu in the ActiveSync menu.
4.) Select the Partnership you want to delete by clicking on File >Mobile Device. There you will see a list of the partnerships on your PC.  If you only have the one partnership, then you can jump down to the next step.
5.) Click on File > Delete Partnership (or Delete Mobile Device) in order to delete the partnership you previously selected.

ActiveSync will now ask you to confirm if you are sure that you want to delete the partnership.  Doing so will not delete any of the items on your device, just the Sync items on your Host PC.

Click “Yes” if you want these files deleted on your PC.  I suggest saying "No", then rename the folder found in your My Documents folder (in case you want to keep the same device name).  You can always delete them later if you need to.

6.) Now go to your Mobile Device and start ActiveSync. Click Options, then select the Host you are syncing with, then select Delete.
7.) I highly recommend that you reboot your computer, and perform a soft-reset on the mobile device as well.
8.) When both devices are done restarting, then the next time you connect your device, ActiveSync will start a Connection Wizard to walk you through the new connection.

I hope this helps!

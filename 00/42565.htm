As already mentioned, when you buy a disc game, you still need to keep the disc inserted to play.  However, there are some exceptions to this.  For example, if you have the disc version, but your friend has the digital version - and your friend is using their account on your console - they won't need to insert the game disc, because they own the digital rights.

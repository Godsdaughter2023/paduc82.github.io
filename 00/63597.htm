    bill.p. say: Hi Liam, thanks for your question. I'm Graeme, a Microsoft Community expert and fellow user.

    Your Windows license would be OEM, as it came preinstalled on your computer with a sticker on the box.

    Unfortunately, these licenses are for one PC and are tied to your motherboard, and changing your motherboard is considered a new PC in terms of Windows licensing. That's why your Windows isn't activating - your license is tied to your old motherboard which isn't working anymore. You may need to purchase a new license.

    You can find more information about hardware changes and activation here:
    https://support.microsoft.com/en-au/help/20530/...

    If you'd like to see if Microsoft Support can help you with your licensing, you can contact them here:
    https://partner.support.services.microsoft.com/...

hansv mvp say:

Access tends to save the filter and sort order silently, without asking.

One workaround is to put a command button on the form that closes the form without saving its design:

 

Private Sub cmdClose_Click()
    DoCmd.Close acForm, Me.Name, acSaveNo
End Sub

 

(Please note that if the current record has been modified, it will still be saved - the acSaveNo applies to the form design only)

 

You could even prevent the user from closing the form a different way.

 

Another option would be to reset the filter and sort order in the On Close or On Unload event of the form.

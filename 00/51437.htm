davem121 say:

nVidia cards use a technology called Optimus to switch between the Intel HD graphics and the nVidia card depending upon the task you are performing - this is the default

You can turn this off and just use the nVidia Card if you wish.

In general on Dell systems this is done in the BIOS. When your system starts, repeatedly press the F2 key until you see text on the top right of your screen - 'Entering Setup'

When you get into BIOS, scroll down the list on the left to the Video section, then uncheck the Optimus option.

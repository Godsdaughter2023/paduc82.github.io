aljon_t say:

Hi Amande,

Your contacts and other information are tied to your Skype account and can be accessed across different devices as long as signed in to the correct account. And based on your post, the account you have signed into seems to be a new account as there are no messages and contacts shown. Probably, you are logged in to a different Skype account.

And for us to check, sign out and sign in again using your Skype name.

Note: On the log in page, if there is an option "Sign in using a different account", click it before signing in.

For instance, that you were unable to sign in due to password, try to reset it again by following the instructions below.

  1. Go to our Problems signing in page.
  2. Enter your Skype name and select Continue.
  3. Click Reset password.
  4. Choose "I forgot my password".
  5. Enter the character you see on the field provided.
  6. After that, it will show the security info stored on your account. Choose one that you have access to.
  7. Then you will receive a verification code and instructions on how to reset the password.

See this link for more information. I've forgotten my Skype name or password

Hope this information helps. Feel free to post back if you have questions and clarifications.

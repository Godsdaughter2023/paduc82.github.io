How to Fix a MTP USB Device Driver Problem (Music-related) - Microsoft Community

david scammell say:

How to Fix a MTP USB Device Driver Problem (Music-related)

Summary
Sometimes, when trying to move music to a Windows Phone device, you might receive a message from your Windows-based computer that the MTP USB Device is not installed correctly. If this describes you, then this Wiki is for you!
Details
Here's two different methods for fixing your MTP USB Device issue on your Windows-based PC:

Fix MTP USB Device Driver Problem - Option 1
  • Download MPT (Media Transfer Protocol) Porting Kit from Microsoft's official website.
  • Install it to your computer.
  • Reboot your computer.
  • Reconnect your mobile phone to your computer by using the USB cable.

Fix MTP USB Device Driver Problem - Option 2
  • Go to Device Manager, under Portable Devices, right-click on MTP USB Device, and then click on Update Driver Software to update the MTP USB device driver.
  • Select “Let me pick from a List of device drivers on your computer”. The list will show installed driver software compatible with the device. 
  • Select the driver you want to install and then click “Next”. Reconnect your mobile phone to your computer.

If neither of the ways above can solve the MTP USB device driver problems, you'' probably need to look for the MTP USB driver on Nokia's Support Site.

I hope this helps!

Thanks for letting us know about the cross posts.

Your macro is slow because the workbook is slow. And why the workbook is slow depends wholely on its content.

Excel 2010 (and 2007) have a much, much larger grid, hence sometimes calc is slower. Given that you have a 2003 format workbook, this shouldn't make a difference.

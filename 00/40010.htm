Performance issues always depends on how you operate your computer. There are several reasons that it will operate in an unusual behavior. We can give you these best practices to make your computer operating normally again:

  • Keep at least Windows Defender only.
  • Unplug all unnecessary external devices that is not currently in used (External hard disk, webcams, etc.).

  • Have a stable Internet connection.

  • Make a regular Disk Cleanup.

peter atherton say:

Try these

 

=AVERAGE(IF(A1:F1>0,A1:F1))

The above is an array formula and must be entered with CTL + SHIFT + ENTER. Excel will insert curly brackets around the formula

or an ordinary formula

=SUM(A1:F1)/COUNTIF(A1:F1,">0")

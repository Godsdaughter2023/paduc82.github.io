=VLOOKUP(B3,ID_NUMBERS,2,FALSE)

bernie deitrick say:

Because your names are in the column to the left of your ID numbers, you cannot use VLOOKUP. Instead, use INDEX / MATCH like so

=INDEX(Sheet2!A:A,MATCH(B3,Sheet2!B:B,False))

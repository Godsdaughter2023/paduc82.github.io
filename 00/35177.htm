monica cam say:

Hi Antonio,

As for your concern, what battery calibration are you referring to?

It is normal for a battery to lose its original design capacity as time goes by with the charge and discharge cycle. Using any kind of software to do a battery calibration will not help you regain the lost battery capacity. However, you can run the built-in power troubleshooter. To do this, follow the steps below:

  1. On the Start menu, type Troubleshooting.
  2. Click View all.
  3. Select Power and run the troubleshooter.

Should you need further assistance, feel free to get back to us.

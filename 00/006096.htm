dalekjen say:

I just solved this issue with an Outlook account using one IMAP and one Exchange account.

For me, the solution was to manually configure the Exchange account rather than allow it to autodiscover. So, go to File, Account Settings, Remove the Exchange Account. Then, hit "new," select "Manual Setup" at the bottom, select Exchange ActiveSync, enter your name and email address. The mail server is eas.outlook.com

Then enter your username (your email address again) and your email password. It should complete and be good to go!

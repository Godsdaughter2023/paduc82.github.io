No, it is not possible unless you have some more information.

You have 5 data that are calculated from 7 inputs, that means that you can have infinitely many solutions.

If the November number was x and the December number was y, then the following are the expressions for the numbers of all seven months:

Nov: x
Dec: y
Jan: 45 - x - y
Feb: 9 + x
March: 3 + y
April: 36 - x - y
May: 6 + x

As you see, you can put in any x and y and still get your 5 averages as stated.

Get-AppxPackage Microsoft.WindowsFeedbackHub | Remove-AppxPackage

Get-AppxPackage -allusers Microsoft.WindowsFeedbackHub | Foreach {Add-AppxPackage -DisableDevelopmentMode -Register “$($_.InstallLocation)\AppXManifest.xml”}

ranmo_msft say:

Hi James,

Here are the answers of the questions.
1. We don’t have official articles specifying this data. Generally, you can keep the MX record pointing to on-premises as long as you wish. But we suggest migration administrators delete their migration batches only after they have verified that they no longer need any incremental syncs.
2. I’m afraid this is not feasible in cutover migration. A batch will migrate all of the user accounts of the on-premises Exchange to Office 365 at one time.

Mirror margins not working on landscape pages - Microsoft Community

butterflyfish_14 say:

Mirror margins not working on landscape pages

Hi,

I am using Office 2016 on a Mac. Most of my document is in portrait layout, with mirror margins to allow for binding. The margins are: top, bottom and outside: 2cm; inside: 4cm.

I have a few pages (mostly tables) in landscape layout, and I want the margins to continue as if they were on a portrait layout. At the moment it doesn't work and all the landscape pages have 4 cm on the left, whether odd or even. How can I fix this?

Thanks a lot for any advice!

john korchok say:

To understand how Word works, we have to back up a step and talk about what mirror margins were originally designed for: to accommodate a binding along on edge of the page. The inside margin is usually made larger to allow for some type of binding that effectively makes part of the page under the binding unusable.

When you have a portrait page, that edge is the right or left edge of the page, depending on whether the page is odd or even. When you introduce landscape pages into this scenario, you don't bind them on the left or right edges, because the pages would stick out from the rest of the document. Landscape pages get bound top and bottom. 

In the Margin dialog of your landscape  pages, take a look at the headings when you change Mirror margins. They are Inside, Outside, Left and Right. Inside and Outside have replaced Top and Bottom, not Left and Right as they would on a Portrait page. This is by design, it's not a bug. Word 2008 and 2011 work the same way.

Option Explicit

Private Sub CommandButton1_Click()
LoadPik ("A41")

Public Sub LoadPik(picRange As String)

    Dim PicLocation As String
    Dim MyRange As String
   
ActiveWorkbook.Unprotect Password:="xxx"
ActiveSheet.Unprotect Password:="xxx"

Range(picRange).Select
MyRange = Selection.Address
PicLocation = Application.GetOpenFilename("Image Files (*.jpg),*.jpg", , "Select Image File", , "False")

If PicLocation <> "False" Then
    ActiveSheet.Pictures.Insert(PicLocation).Select
    Else
    ActiveSheet.Protect Password:="xxx"
    ActiveWorkbook.Protect Password:="xxx", Structure:=True, Windows:=False
    Exit Sub
End If

With Selection.ShapeRange
    '
    'inserted images in '07 are no longer placed in relation to active cell
    'must also specify top and left proerties as well
    'This is not necessary for '03 version but does not impair functionality
    '
    .Top = Range(MyRange).Top
    .Left = Range(MyRange).Left
    .LockAspectRatio = msoTrue
    If .Width > .Height Then
        .Width = Range(MyRange).Width
    If .Height > Range(MyRange).Height Then .Height = Range(MyRange).Height
    Else
        .Height = Range(MyRange).Height
        If .Width > Range(MyRange).Width Then .Width = Range(MyRange).Width
    End If
End With

With Selection
    .Placement = xlMoveAndSize
    .PrintObject = True
   
End With


With Selection
        Select Case Application.Version
            Case "11.0"
                Compress2003
            Case "12.0"
                Compress2007
            Case Else
                Compress2003
        End Select
End With

'remove border when inserting images

    Selection.Borders(xlEdgeLeft).LineStyle = xlNone

    ActiveSheet.Protect Password:="xxx"

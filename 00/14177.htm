You use following formula (replace 20% as your requirement)

B2: =INT((B2&"."&C2)*20%)

C2: =MOD((B2&"."&C2)*20%,1)*10^LEN(C2)

B3: =INT((B2&"."&C2)-(B2&"."&C2)*20%)

C3: =MOD((B2&"."&C2)-(B2&"."&C2)*20%,1)*10^LEN(C2)

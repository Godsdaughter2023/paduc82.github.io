barry xie msft say:

Hi Spunkybd,

 

First, I’d like to tell you that the retention age is calculated from the last modified date.

 

To force the retention policy takes effect immediately, you can use Managed Folder Assistant.
a. Connect to Exchange Online using remote PowerShell.
b. Run the cmdlet: Start-ManagedFolderAssistant -Identity user@contoso.com (Replace with the user’s address).

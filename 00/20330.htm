I'm not sure what you did to "convert" the tool to Word, but that's probably not the way to get what you want. Instead:

  • Go back to Excel, select the audit tool, and copy it to the clipboard.
  • Open the Word document and click on the page where you want the audit tool to appear. You can make a new page if needed by pressing Ctrl+Enter to insert a page break; the new page will still be in portrait orientation.
  • On the Home ribbon tab, in the Clipboard group at the left, click the down-arrow of the Paste button and click Paste Special in the menu.

  • Right-click the picture, click Text Wrapping in the menu, and click Square.
  • On the ribbon, go to the Picture Tools | Format tab. In the Arrange group, click the Rotate button and choose either Rotate Right 90° or Rotate Left 90°.

Try this.

=MROUND(A1,0.25)-(0.01*ISEVEN(MROUND(A1,0.25)*100))

How do I transfer word and excel files from Office 2010 to office 365 - Microsoft Community

betsy buechner say:

How do I transfer word and excel files from Office 2010 to office 365

There is Office 2010 and 365 on one of our office computers.  How do I get the word and excel files from those saved on the 2010 version to the 365 version?  There appears to be a conflict that both of these are on the computer.

jay freedman say:

Either version of Office can open documents from the other version. A "transfer" is not required. The problem is that only one version is named in the Windows Registry to respond when you double-click a document file in File Explorer or elsewhere. Apparently, Word 2010 is the one that's still responsible for that method of opening.

One way to be sure Word 2016 from Office 365 will handle a particular document is to open that program first, and then use the Open command to call up the document.

For a more permanent solution, ask the Windows search/Cortana for "Apps & features". In that page of the Settings, click "Default apps" on the left side. Scroll the right side list to the bottom and click "Choose default apps by file type". For each of the file extensions you use (.doc, .docx, .dotm and so forth), click the icon in the right-hand column and choose Word 2016.

If that doesn't work for you, it's possible to change the registry directly, but I won't recommend it.

joe men say:

Hello, Abhijit.

Thank you for posting your query to Microsoft Community. We understand that you wanted to type texts using Marathi characters and facing flickering issues on the Edge browser while scrolling, when 3-4 tabs are open. We're here to help.

There are two ways you can type texts on the Marathi characters. By adding the Marathi keyboard or using an app.

Follow the steps below to add a Marathi keyboard:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Tap Time & language.
  3. Tap Keyboard.
  4. Tap Add keyboards.
  5. Scroll from the list of keyboard languages you can install and put a check mark on the Marathi check box.
  6. Tap on the Check button.
  • Once the Marathi character keyboard is installed, you can change keyboard language by swiping left or right on the space bar key.

For the app method, you can download the Type Marathi+ app which is free. With this app, you can type in Marathi and share it to the world via social apps, SMS and Mail with a click of a button.

Regarding your concern about the screen flickering when you scroll using the Edge browser, please clear your Internet cache by following the steps below:

  1. Tap on the ellipsis button [. . .] on the lower right portion of the Edge browser screen.
  2. Tap Settings.
  3. Tap Choose what to clear button under the Clear browsing data.
  4. Put a check mark on the Cached data and files, Cookies and saved website data, and Form data.
  5. Tap the Clear button.

Hope this helps.

Feel free to post back if you need further assistance.

We have a Microsoft Volume Licence (VL) Agreement
If we upgrade from Windows 7 Pro VL, to Windows 10 Pro VL, does this claim our Windows 10 OEM licence for that machine too?   We'd be using VL media, internal KMS server and VL keys before July 29.
If we wipe and want to resell the machines in the future, does the machine know it is entitled to Windows 10?

a. user say:

Maybe it would be easier to look at it this way.

Take the hardware out of the equation.

Consumer (OEM) and Volume licenses are completely separate. You can't upgrade one with the other.

So if the Consumer (OEM) version hasn't been upgraded to Windows 10 Consumer, then that license won't be upgraded. It can't be upgraded by the volume license. So if you don't upgrade your Consumer (OEM) licenses before July 29, then they won't be considered upgraded under the free program.

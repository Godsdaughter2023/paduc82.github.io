Planner icon - Microsoft Community

rpschmitt say:

Planner icon

When I copy a plan and rename it, the 2 letters on the icon remains unchanged and uses the same ones as the original plan.  Is there a way to change the 2 letters?
alex chen msft say:

Hi rpschmitt,

 

Thanks for your update.

As a workaround you could take my method about copy plan and then rename the plan.

 

But it's feasible to achieve your request if you have Office 365 admin permission. Please check my explanation below:

The reason why plan icon won't be changed even after you rename the plan name is because, when you create a new plan, an Office 365 Group is added automatically, and the plan icon is the directly linked to that group icon, so no matter how you change the plan name, it won't have any influence on the icon.

 

In simple: You will need to rename the Office 365 Group name to change how the icon looks like. Thus, you will need at least Office 365 user administrator role. 

Office 365 Group Name Change

 

If you are not the admin in your tenant, just use copy and rename method as it's a more efficient way.

Your problems result from storing a new notebook directly into the local Onedrive folder.

Onenote notebooks on Onedrive (those with the Internet shortcut) have another format than the local notebooks. The necessary format conversion is done by using the File, Share feature.

  2. then move it to Onedrive using File, Share

Based on your description, your emails haven't been delivered by Outlook and stuck in Outbox folder. Generally, this is because Outlook client failed to connect to your email server and the mail flow is broken. To troubleshooting this issue, you can check if the Outgoing SMTP server setting is configured correctly for your account via File > Account Settings > Account Settings. For your reference, see POP and IMAP email settings for Outlook.

And if the port, server address and encryption method of Outgoing SMTP server are configured correctly, please check if your account is connected to email server. The connected status appears at the bottom of Outlook.

Meanwhile, you can navigate to Send / Receive tab and click Send/Receive All Folders button to check if it helps. While if the issue still exists, please try to Cancel All sending requests to clear the stuck messages in Outbox folder. Then make a new attempt to check if it's available to send emails out.

For the further investigation, I'd like to collect the following information.

1. What's your email account type, POP, IMAP or Exchange?

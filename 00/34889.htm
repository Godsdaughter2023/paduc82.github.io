Seeng a pink cast aross all of the screen is normally a sign of a faulty 15 pin sub-D monitor cable - missing one of the RGB colours. I would suspect that the cable between PC to projector is faulty. This is a common fault I see as an AV engineer


Two tests:
1/ Do you get the same colour cast on the NEC builtin menu - I would suspect not, and this would confirm that the projector internal chips are displaying colour correctly (if the menu is missing colours however, we have a different issue)
2/ Can you test another XGA lead (15pin sub male to male) via laptop and projector. This wil help confirm that the lead is at fault.

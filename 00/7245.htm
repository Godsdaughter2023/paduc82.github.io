Getting Rid of Crop Marks on my Word Docs

swischner say:
It's not in the Word ribbon menu, but on the MacOS menu. So with Word open, click on "Word" on the top left of your screen (next to the Apple symbol), then choose "Preferences...". In the next windows click on "View" (the second option). Next window contains some view related settings. In the upper area (labeled "Show in Document") look for "Crop Marks" and uncheck it.

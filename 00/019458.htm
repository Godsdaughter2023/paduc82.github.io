Outlook 2010 with multiple data files should synchronize with your Windows Phone 6.5 or prior just fine.  This presumes that you have all of your appointments, contacts, etc under one primary data file (usually the default).  If you have separate profiles, or have your appointments, contacts, etc separated into different data files, then this will not work.  Windows Phone does not know how to separate these contacts, so they are all synchronized with the main account.

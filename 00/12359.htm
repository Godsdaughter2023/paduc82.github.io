brad vogt say:

Probably the easiest way would be to use the formula =Master!A1 as the formula in cell A1 on all of the sheets that are not the master, then fill that formula down and across to pull the data to the other sheets without duplicating the data (ie. without copying and pasting).  This is assuming that the first tab is labeled "Master".  After doing this, click on the data tab, then Filter in the Sort and Filter group.  On each tab, Filter by the year that the tab is supposed to display.

You may want to do this to one sheet and get all of the data organized the way that you want, then hold CTRL and drag that sheet tab to the right slightly to copy the sheet.  If you want to have summary information at the bottom, you will need to use the sumif or averageif function.

I wouldn't consider that a bug, but Excel adapting to a changing condition. Since you didn't explicitly format A2 as $#,##0_);[Red]($#,##0) prior to adding the formula, when the formula triggers a non-integer result, Excel responds by adding the .00. If your formula had resulted in an integer, the format wouldn't have changed.

When you explicitly change the format after the first calc, it won't change on subsequent calcs. It's just that first time on an unformatted cell.

Make sense?

obsessive power say:

Hi,

Sorry but the Acer XF240H only supports FreeSync over display Display port, not HDMI.  This information was obtained from the online manual for that monitor.

While it's possible to buy HDMI to Display port adaptors, these devices will not give you FreeSync support to your display either.  Sorry.

As M3tal Daz3 has already stated.  To get this to work, your display must support 120Hz over HDMI.

Here are the steps on how to add a contact to your safe sender's list.

 

1. Sign in to your account and navigate to your Hotmail Inbox. 
2. Click Options located at the upper-right side of the page. 
3. Select More Options.

nemanja pantelic msft say:

Hi Matt,

 

Thanks for the header and answers you sent me as a private message. Based on the message header analysis, reason of SCL -1 score is internal address. If the forged address has your company domain, mail server will recognized the sender as safe one.

 

For the further protection, I could suggest you some mail flow options which could redirect this or similar senders to Junk folder, Quarantine, etc...

 

For the start, if you check the mail header you have sent me, you can notice that the values of SPF, DMARC and DKIM are =fail or =none. These missing or corrupt record suggest us that the message is probably a phishing one. But Exchange Online center provide us with a way to prevent this kind of mail finish in our inbox. To set this option, please follow the steps:

 

·         Go to

·         Go to Admin> Admin centers> Exchange> Mail flow> Rules and click on +

·         Select Apply Office 365 message encryption and rights protection…

·         Name the rule.

·         In Apply this rule if… select A message header> Includes any of these words.

·         Add spf=none

·         Dkim=none

·         Dmarc=none

·         Spf=fail

·         Dkim=fail

·         Dmarc=fail

·         In Do the following, choose the action you want.

 

Please test if the rule resolve the problem and feedback me.

 

Regards,

Nemanja

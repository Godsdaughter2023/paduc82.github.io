Hi,

The Intel programs are for the Video and the IDT PC Audio is the sound and you probably
do not want to disable those.

Actually none or the StartUp Programs are absolutely necessary however you probably will
find that utilities put there by the System Maker are good to keep. Ones from Adobe, WinAMP
and simitar programs that only allow those to start up a tiny bit faster are not required.

Be careful as a lot of these programs and services actually need to run and often removing a
few does little to speed the machine while making the machine less friendly to use.

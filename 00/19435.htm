teylyn say:
Oh, I see,

So you have very tall cells and would like to scroll so that only the lower part of the cell shows. I'm afraid that this is not how Excel works. The anchor point is always the top left corner of a cell. If the cell is too high or too wide to fit the window, the content will be cut off at the bottom and the right, but you cannot align a sheet so only part of a cell shows at the top left of the window.

You could try to reduce the zoom of the sheet. Hold the Ctrl key and scroll the mouse wheel to zoom in and out or use the zoom slider at the bottom right of the window.

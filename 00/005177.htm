You've asked a very difficult question as there can be so many answers as to why this is happening. Just to name a few of the top of my head:

  • Your device might be faulty. Return it to the service provider and see if they'll switch it out.

Volume Down + Power Buttons, hold together for about 15 seconds

 

When the phone turns back on go to Settings > Date + Time, disable automatic and manually set the correct Date and Time.

After that, go to Settings > Email + Accounts, over there tap and hold your finger on your Microsoft Account and Select Sync.

Try to download the app through the Store again.

waseemulla shariff say:


Hi PEYTON,

Try the following steps and check the result.
a. Plug in your Sansa clip to the USB port on your computer.
b. Open your iTunes program. If your Sansa clip is not iTunes compatible it will not sync with iTunes and it will not show up as a device on the iTunes program.
c. On your iTunes program click on the Podcasts icon to show the files that you want to download to your Sansa clip.
d. Right Click on the first file, then click on Show in Window Explorer. This will open up the iTunes folder that contains all the podcasts.
e. Copy all the podcasts and load it to Sansa clip by opening its drive in Windows Explorer.

If your disk shown as removable, it means the disk interface is hot-swappable SATA that is is good to have. You can use that to good advantage for backup etc by extending the interface to a front rack where you can plug in and plug out your hard disk just like USB flash memory. I do my backup this way because the transfer rate is the fastest you can get compared with network backup.

On your other point, I don't think you can selectively hide the status notification only for one device; You may hide the "Safely Remove ..." which applies to all, but get to whenever you need by navigating to. Remember that the status notification is doing its job as expected.

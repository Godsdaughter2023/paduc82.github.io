jomel ber say:

Hi Liz and Roger,

When you start an Office app, such as Word or Excel, your app may close unexpectedly or you may see the error message you posted above. This issue occurs with applications that depend on Windows Task Scheduler. The issue is intermittent, and occurs only on computers that are protected by the 1.1.15700.X version of Microsoft Malware Protection Engine. 

To resolve this issue, update the threat definition files to version 1.1.287.94.0 or higher. For information about how to check and update these files, go to Definition updates for Windows Defender Antivirus and other Microsoft antimalware and then restart your PC after installing these updates.

Had this issue several times after upgrading to a new build, again with 1607.


What worked for me was ressetting Edge.

Open in File Explorer:

%userprofile%\appdata\Local\Packages\Microsoft.MicrosoftEdge_8wekyb3d8bbwe

delete everything inside

Open powershell (run as administrator)

paste:

Get-AppXPackage -AllUsers -Name Microsoft.MicrosoftEdge | Foreach {Add-AppxPackage -DisableDevelopmentMode -Register “$($_.InstallLocation)\AppXManifest.xml” -Verbose}

After that I was able to open Edge without crashing and I was able re reconfigure Edge.

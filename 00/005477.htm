vijay a. verma say:

In short, it is not possible. If you show data tables, it will be shown for all series.

But there are work around available to handle the situation.

1. Make sure that your target line is last column in your table. Hence, it will appear at the bottom in your data table.

2. Draw a rectangular shape over last row in your data table. Format this shape with White Solid Fill and No line. Adjust its width, length etc. so that your other rows in the table are visible and you get desired look and feel.

3. Group chart and this shape together.

Using this, I have drawn the chart. See the green line with a target of 10.

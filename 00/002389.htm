Hi Stuart,

I'd like to explain that when user signs in with his/her account on a computer that has shared computer activation enabled, Office 365 ProPlus contacts the Office Licensing Service on the Internet to obtain a licensing token for the user. Note: After your user signs in on one server in your farm, the licensing token will be created and stored on that server. If this user uses other servers, he/she need to sign in again to let Office 365 ProPlus contacts the Office Licensing Service to create related licensing token.

It is not feasible to use all shared computer activation enabled servers without sign in. Thanks for your understanding. You can check this article to get more info about it: Overview of shared computer activation for Office 365 ProPlus.

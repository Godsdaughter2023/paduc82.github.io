'Oct 26, 2014

Const colB  As Long = 14 '<<  search for text CLOSED in column 14
Const N As Long = 9 '<<< data from row 9

End If

Hi 911bob,

We can never say the sender is detected as spam sender for using Office 365 or any other email service provider. Most of time, an email is regarded as spam, the main factor should be related to the recipient’s email service mechanism.

So, for your situation, I recommend you try sending from the “***.onmicrosoft.com” domain and see whether the issue persists. This step is to identify whether the issue is related to the blocked domains (your Office 365 custom domains) by your recipients. This time, you can do: Email subject “test email” and email content “abc 123” (as simple as possible). Or, you can contact the recipients side (via phone or any other method reaching to them) to add these blocked domains to be their allowed senders.

melchizedek qui say:

Hello Shiela,

To help you enable cookies in Internet Explorer 11 and Microsoft Edge, I suggest applying the settings below:

For Internet Explorer:

1. In Internet Explorer, select the Tools button, and then select Internet options.

2. Select the Privacy tab, and under Settings, select Advanced and choose if you want to allow, block or be prompted for first and third party cookies.

For Microsoft Edge:

1. Launch Microsoft Edge.

2. Click on the three-doted button, select Settings.

3. Under Advanced settings, click on view advanced settings.

4. Under Cookies, select Don't block cookies from the drop-down menu.

5. Restart Microsoft Edge.

Let us know if you have other concerns.

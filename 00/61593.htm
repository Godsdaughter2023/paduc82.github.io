cmdrkeene say:

Are you referring to the true screensaver, or just the photo lock screen slideshow?

If it's the real screensaver, here's how to see where the pictures come from.

Go to your screensaver options and click Settings, then Browse.  There you can see what's highlighted as the source folder or library for the pictures. That's where they are coming from.  For example, mine shows the Pictures library:

It's important to understand the libraries are not folders, they are more like search results.  For example, my Pictures library automatically shows all pictures it can find on all the computers in my house (7 different computers).

If you want to see where your library's search results come from, go to File Explorer.  In the left side navigation pane, right-click a blank place and choose "Show libraries" to see them.  Then you can right-click the Pictures library and go to properties to see what places it searches to find pictures.

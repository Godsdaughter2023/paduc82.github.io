Yes, you can, if you use the modification from my previous reply:

 

    iRow = ws.Range("1:50").Find(What:="*", SearchOrder:=xlRows, _
        SearchDirection:=xlPrevious, LookIn:=xlValues).Row + 1

 

for the userform that manages rows 1:50, and

 

    iRow = ws.Range("52:102").Find(What:="*", SearchOrder:=xlRows, _
        SearchDirection:=xlPrevious, LookIn:=xlValues).Row + 1

 

for the userform that managers rows 52:102.

Your formula assignment for H1 would be better written as,

Cells(1, 8).Formula = "=SUMIF(A5:A" & LastRow & " , ""x"",  E5:E" & LastRow & ")"

Since this is a single cell and not a long column of SUMIF() formulas, I'm curious as to why it requires rewriting to specify lastrow at all. You should be able to leave it as one of these,

=SUMIF(A:A , "x",  E:E)
=SUMIF(A5:A9999 , "x",  E5:E9999)

Even that last (most convoluted and volatile) formula should not cause undue calculation lag for a single cell.

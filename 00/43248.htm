darian tab say:

Hi Robert,

There are a few steps to change the time format from 24 hour to 12 hour.. Let's follow these steps on how to do so:

  1. Click on the Start and select Settings.
  2. Click on Time and Language.
  3. Click on Change date and time formats link.
  4. On the next screen, click on Short Time and pick h:mm:tt from the drop-down.
  5. Click on Long Time and choose h:mm:ss tt from the drop-down.
  6. Once done, just close the settings window. The change will be reflected immediately on both Long and Short time locations of your computer.

Special notes:  The structured type value consists of a sequence of

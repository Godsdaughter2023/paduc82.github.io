If nothing shows up there, then click on See full history and check the Full history page, which corresponds to the All detected items page in the classic Windows Defender interface. 1116 – Threat detection

1117 – Threat remediation

1119 – Remediation failure

Hi Jeff,

As you know, when you add a email address to Outlook contact, this contact will show as your personal Outlook contacts, other people in your organization can't see this contact from the address book.

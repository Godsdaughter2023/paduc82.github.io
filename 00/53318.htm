Method 1: DISM TOOL:

  1. On the Search bar type “cmd”. Right Click on the Command Prompt option and select Run as Administrator. (If prompted for a password, type the password and click Allow).

  3. After the scan is complete, type “exit” and press Enter.

For more information about SFC scan, click on the link below:

https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/kb/929833

Hi,

What I suggest you do here is the following.

Make a note of the exact time the game minimizes the next time. After you finish playing, hit "Windows key + X" to open the power users menu and click Event Viewer.

Expand the tree to Windows Logs and then click Application. Wait until the center pane populates and then right click the same Application submenu and go to Filter Current Log.

In the menu which opens, change "Anytime" at the top to "Last hour" (assuming you finish your game within one hour of it minimizing suddenly), or "Last 12 hours" if it's longer and then click OK.

Find the log file(s) which coincide(s) with the time the game minimized, right click it and go to "Copy" ---> "Copy Details as Text". This will copy the data to the Windows Clipboard in a readable format.

Next, open Notepad, right click and select Paste.

You can also copy the contents of the log file here if you wish and I'll take a look at it. It'll stay on the Windows Clipboard until you either copy something else, or shutdown or reboot.

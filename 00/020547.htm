Regardless of what colour your cat is, you have either subscribed to a full version that supports local install, or you haven't.

Microsoft have released the new Build 10074. I would suggest you to upgrade to the latest build and check if the issue persists.

Method 1: If you have any proxy settings enabled on your computer, I would suggest you to disable proxy settings and see if that helps.

a.     Click the Gear box at the top right corner in Internet Explorer 10 desktop version.
b.     Click the Tools button, and then click Internet Options.
c.      Click the Connections tab, and then click LAN Settings.
d.     Uncheck the option “Use a Proxy Server for your LAN”.
e.     Click Apply and OK to save changes.

Method 2: The issue could also be due to video card drivers not updated. I would suggest you to update video card drivers from the manufacturer’s website and see if that helps.

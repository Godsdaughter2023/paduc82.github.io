Excel stores numbers in binary format with a finite precision, and this may cause v minor or tiny rounding errors.

Using below will return 3:

=MATCH(ROUND(C15,3),B3:B13,0)

Or else you may Round off the SUM viz:

=ROUND(SUM(C4:C13),3)

and then do the Match: =MATCH(C15,B3:B13,0)

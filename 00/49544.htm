Hi,

 

We don't have the feature or an option to send desctructive virus to a spammer. Once the system detected that your account is sending bulk emails it will automatically block your account.

1. Sign in to the Microsoft Hotmail website with your Microsoft account
2. On the Action bar, click “Options”, and then click “More options”.
3. Under Junk e-mail, click “Safe and blocked senders”.
4. Click "Blocked senders". Enter an e-mail address or domain, and then click “Add to List”.

 

With regard to phishing, it is a method of online identity theft designed to steal personal and financial data. To learn how Internet explorer can help you block such email please check this link:  How to turn on or turn off the Phishing Filter in Windows Internet Explorer

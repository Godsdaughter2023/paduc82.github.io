    I bought a used HP laptop running ubuntu and with a COA of windows vista business at the bottom.

    Now, Legally can I use it on my laptop? what if the previous owner still use it on other PC?

    tricky300 say:

    I am not an expert in the legalities of licence rights after resale. However, I can say this...

    • COA stickers only come with OEM versions and do not necessarily have 'OEM' on the sticker.
    • OEM licences are tied to the PC that they were first installed on and there are no rights to install using that key on any other PC.

    The logical conclusions to the above, IMO, are...

    • If the original owner has used the key on your COA on another PC, he/she has done so illegally.
    • You can legally install Vista Business using the key on your COA.

    If the original owner has re-installed using the key on your COA many times, you may have trouble activating Windows after installation and you may have to use telephone activation (instead of on-line).

     

    If in doubt, I would suggest contacting Microsoft licencing for your region.

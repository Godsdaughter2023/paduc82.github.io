burrwalnut say:

You have to change the path to C:\Users\YourUserName\Documents. My Documents will still be valid on the M drive.

This should work:

Rem All "Document file" data on C Drive will be incrementally
Rem backed up to Removable Drive M:
xcopy C:\Users\YourUserName\Docume~1\*.* M:\MyDocu~1 /S /D /Y
Pause c: drive backup complete

 

 

david. f say:

You can also use quotes around the full name for better readability. Also, assuming your backed up files are on "M:\My Documents", you will want to reverse the command:

xcopy "M:\My Documents\*" "C:\Users\YourUserName\Documents" /S /D /Y

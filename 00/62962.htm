But in your case, it's entirely different.  Getting a message that an administrator has blocked you from a regular windows utility like MMC is almost a sure-fire indication that you have been infected with a virus or malware.  Especially when your computer isn't part of a company that has a system administrator that sets the policies and blocks things against you.

Don't just power down the modem/switch/router. 

1.  turn off all computers/game systems attached to the modem/switch/router

2. unplug all switches (if separate from modem)

3. unplug modem for at least 10 seconds.  If it has a battery back up, skip that and hold the tiny reset button in the back for at least 10 seconds and release.  It will reboot and reload fresh settings.

5. turn on 1 switch at a time and let it complete the self test and wait at least a minute before proceeding to the next step.

6.  Turn on 1 computer at a time and let it load, then turn on the other computers 1 at a time.

This should (possibly) solve your problem.

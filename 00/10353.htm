1. Sign into Outlook Web App with the affected Office 365 account.

2. Check if the emails are in the Junk Email folder or Deleted Items.

3. Click on Options > Mail > Accounts > Block or allow and check if the email address is blocked.

Hard reset your phone using the method below:

In Sheet2's B2 (where B1 is Contract#) use this formula,

=VLOOKUP($A2, Sheet1!$A:$E, COLUMN(B:B), FALSE)

... then fill both right to column E and down as far as necessary.

The Software Licensing Terms for Office 2013 say that the product is non-transferable, which means it is only ever properly licensed and activated on the computer on which it was first activated and cannot be moved to any other computer for any reason.

 

So if you replace a computer with your Office 2013 activated on it, the Office 2013 activation stays with the computer.  You will have to buy a new license for any new or different computer.

When tables are wrapped (on the Table tab of the Table Properties dialog, select Around for "Text wrapping"), the Positioning button becomes active.

You must have RegConverter V1,0 from Sordum installed on your system for this to be popping up!

This is not a Windows 10 error, if you do not need this software, remove it and the error will go away . . .

However, if you do need this app, sign into the Administrator account, open a command prompt as administrator (Right click the start menu button and select Command Prompt (admin)) and enter the following command (replacing username with the name of your user account) and hit enter:

net localgroup administrators username /add

This will add your account to the administrator accounts and should stop this error from appearing . . .

I would delete it from Windows 10 startup:

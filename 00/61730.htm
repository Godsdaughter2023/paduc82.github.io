Certainly you can make the laptop a lot faster by uninstalling all the Dell Bloatware - I doubt you have ever used it

Whilst in Control Panel - Programs and Features, uninstall anything you no longer use, like old printers, cameras phones and indeed any software that you do not need

Then you can remove all things from the startup that you do not need or use:

  1. Press Ctrl + Shift + Esc to open Tack Manager
  2. Click on the Startup Tab
  3. Select all the items you do not need like software updaters, OneDrive if you do not use it . . .
  4. Close Task Manager
  5. Press Windows + R Keys, type msconfig into the run dialog and hit enter
  6. Click on the Services Tab and check the box marked 'Hide all Microsoft Services'
  7. Check the list for anything like Software updaters and other services that you know you do not need
  8. Click Apply and reboot

I am sure the laptop would be much faster after performing these steps!

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If however, you want to reset Windows and keep all your files and applications, I would backup the data first just in case, then Reset the PC (Step 4 is important!)

  1. Press Shift whilst clicking the Restart Button
  2. The laptop will boot into the Windows 10 Recovery Environment
  3. Go to Troubleshoot - Advanced Options - Reset this PC
  4. Choose to keep Files and Apps

eggsboxjuan say:

If you’re seeing this error, no storage device was found during the software update. As you're using the 4GB S console you can't remove your storage device, your profiles going missing isn't a good sign sorry to say. You could try formatting your memory unit, that may then enable you to update your console but would of course result in the total loss of all data.

-

I would suggest if possible that you copy/move any game saves you can to a USB flash drive. Link below describes how to use a USB flash drive on the console.

-

www.xbox.com/.../storage

-

Formatting your memory unit is easy enough to do. Go to settings/ system/ storage. Highlight your memory unit and press Y on the controller, you will see the format option there..

-

Having formatted the storage device you can download any items you have purchased by going to settings/ account/ download history, highlight the item in the list and download again at no charge.

-

If the format does indeed then enable you to update the console you will need to download your profiles again.

-

support.xbox.com/.../profile-move-delete

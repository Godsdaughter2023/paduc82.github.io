a. user say:

When windows updates from windows 7 or 8 to 10 it uses 3 folders

1. C:\Windows\SoftwareDistribution\Download

2. Renames C:\windows to C:\windows.old and installs a new c:\windows

3. Creates a hidden folder C:\$Windows.~BT and installs windows 10 using the files in C:\$Windows.~BT\source
To view this folder click , files explorer, view - tick [/] Hidden items

If you want to restore back to windows 7 or 8 you should rename the two folders to something else straight away
or after one month windows.old will be deleted and $Windows.~BT will be deleted and updated with the
next build information.

example

C:\windows.old to c:\winsave_windows.old
C:\$Windows.~BT to c:\winsave_$Windows.~BT

Then rename them back before your want to restore back to windows 7 or 8.

You cannot mix 32 and 64 bit installations in the same Windows session.

Unfortunately that blank message is a 'known feature' in MS Speak ("BUG" to the rest of the world). The installer can identify that there is something 32-bit Officish in the installation folder, but it just can't figure out what it is.

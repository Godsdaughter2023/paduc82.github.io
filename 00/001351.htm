jim gordon mvp say:

Hi David,

You should not have uninstalled Office 2016 from your old Mac. So where do we go from here?

It depends on whether you have an Office 365 subscription or a perpetual license.

If you have an Office 365 subscription, then no harm done. On your new Mac, simply log into the portal site where you can de-activate the old user and re-download the Office suite on your new Mac and then sign in using your new credentials. Having a subscription is a big plus when it comes to upgrading your hardware.

If you have a perpetual license, then you have to reinstall Microsoft Office 2016 on your old Mac. Then you can use Apple's migration assistant to bring the installation forward to your new Mac. It's the only way, as perpetual licenses are not transferable from one Mac to another.

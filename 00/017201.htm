You could use a public variable in a General module (don't put the declaration
in either of the userform modules).

Public MyVar as String

Then you could just populate that variable when you want to in the first
userform:

myVar = me.tbmyInputfield.value

and read from it in the second userform:

me.tbsomeotherinputfield.value = myVar

lars-Åke aspelin say:

I have 4 sites

I have current IT and Operations costs per site

I have future IT and Operations costs per site

I would like to create a bar chart that displays the data using only  two bars per site.  The first bar will indicate the current costs distinguishing IT from Operations. The second bar will indicate the future costs distinguishing IT from Operations.


Try this data structure:

Cell A1: Site
Cell A2: Site A
Cell A4: Site B
Cell A6: Site C
Cell A8: Site D
Cell B2, B4, B6, and B8: Current
Cell B3, B5, B7, and B9: Future
Cell C1: IT
Cell D1: Op
Cells C2:D9: The respective cost values

Then select cells A1:D9 and click on Column -> 2-D Column/Stacked Column in the Charts section of the Insert Tab.

This gives two bars per site The left one shows, in a stacked fashion, the current IT cost and Operations cost and the right one shows the same for the future costs.

    a. user say:

    Hello Mulberry04:

     

    Thank you for posting to the forum about your issue opening photos sent to you via text message.  MMS is something that is tied to the carry and issues with sending and receiving MMS typically relate to some carrier/data settings.

     

    There are a couple of things we would like to have you check.

    • Please verify that your data connection is currently on.  Tap settings > cellular/mobile > ON.
    • Please go to the marketplace and into your manufacturer applications.  Look for an application that is titled something along the lines of "Connection set-up".  After you download this application, run it.  You may have to select some settings like your country and carrier.

    After you have done the above, try to open a send picture message.  If you continue to have issues receiving these pictures we would like the following details to try and find additional solutions.

    1. Who is your mobile operator?
    2. What country are you located in?
    3. Are you able to send picture or video messages?
    4. Have you ever successfully opened a picture message?
    5. What is the make and model of your phone?

    Please let us know if you have any additional questions or concerns.

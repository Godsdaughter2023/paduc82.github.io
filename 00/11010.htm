N8 cover is not unscratchable. The body is made with aluminium. Aluminium is not unscratchable, but it needs really strong pressure and hard things to scratch it. Normally you then would see the aluminium color (silver) within these scratches.

So if you are rough enough, you will scratch it. But not with normal, even unwary handling.

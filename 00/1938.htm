a. user say:

Hi Eve,

This happens if the email forwarding is enabled or if there are any rules setup. Please follow the methods below:

Method 1:

Please check if your Outlook.com email account is creating a copy of your emails.

 

  1. Go to www.outlook.com and sign in with your Microsoft account.
  2. Go to your Inbox.
  3. Click on the Gear icon and select Options from the dropdown list.
  4. Under Managing your account category, select Email forwarding.
  5. Uncheck Keep a copy of forwarded messagesin your Outlook inbox.
  6. Click Done to confirm changes.

 

Method 2:

Please check if there are existing rules on your Outlook.com account.

  1. 1.      Sign in to your Microsoft Outlook.com account.
  2. Click Gear icon, and then select Options from the dropdown list.
  3. Under Customizing Outlook category, click Rules for sorting new messages. Check if the rules are set and remove them.

 

Let us know about the outcome.

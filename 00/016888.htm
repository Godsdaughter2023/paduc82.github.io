brian tillman [outlook mvp 2007-2019] say:

Using rules to control spam is nearly a hopeless endeavor because spammers change their sending address all the time.  Using and exact match as described by SnipperZZ is less effective than using "Sender's address" and "contains" or "contains word" and then using a portion or all of the domain.

 

Setting your junk mail filter to "Exclusive" is the most effective.  You then add to your People list or your Safe Sender list those addresses you wish to reach you.  Anything else will be considered junk and put in your Junk folder.  You can then periodically review the messages in Junk to make sure messages you really want aren't misfiled there, adding the senders to your protected lists (i.e., People or Safe Senders).

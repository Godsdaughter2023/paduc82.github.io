Hi Nick,

Thanks for reaching out to us in forums for this issue. Insofar as placing limits on the gamings/download speeds with the console, there is nothing that it contains to limit the speeds. Speed is usually determined by the ISP in conjunction with the amount of devices or traffic on the network and can vary from device to device.

We recommend that to improve speeds a wired connection is set for the console rather than wireless connection to ensure that the minimum speeds are met: for gaming, 3 Mbps for download & 0.5 Mbps for upload. If you're getting slow performance while gaming, then its recommended that a speed test be performed using our Slow Performance solution. Another way to increase speeds is to port forward the console to give it a more stable connection as well.  There is a wealth of information available on our support.xbox.com site that can help with any difficulties that one may experience due to vacillating speeds with Xbox consoles as well.

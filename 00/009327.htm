i tried to embedded the album cover jpeg image within the mp3 idv2 tag through itunes and window media player but they don't show in my N95-2 music player while playing. Not supported? any solutions?

el_loco---01 say: Several things that can be happening.

Check the resolution of the picture. Try something in VGA range 640x480. I usually use 300x300

Check the file size of the file. It should be less than 200 KB.

Check what it was set to. Only ALBUM COVER FRONT is recognized by the phone. By default many of these tag editors will put the picture as "User Defined" and that won't work.

Check that only 1 picture is attached to the metadata. If there are more than 1 pictures (like cover front and cover back) then the phone seems to get confused and not display anything.

Check that the format of the picture is actually JPEG and not BMP or GIF as these may not be supported (they didn't work in N91 days).

Drag and Drop the files to the phone but don't use Windows Media Player to synchronize them to the phone. There's a bug in WMP that when it Converts before transferring it deletes the Album Art.

that's all I can think of at the moment.

4. Set up microphone

5. Take actions related to Audio Driver
Windows Key+X > Device Manager > Sound, Video & game controllers > Biometric Devices > Right Click on the entry below > Properties > Driver Tab >

A. Roll Back - If Roll back driver is enabled, roll it back

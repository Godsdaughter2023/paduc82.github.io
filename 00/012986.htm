Delete Birthdays from Outlook.com Calendar - Microsoft Community

vitaminb say:

Delete Birthdays from Outlook.com Calendar

How do I delete birthdays from Outlook.com calendar?

Especially all Facebook birthdays since the Facebook link is no longer supported?

Really need better support for managing birthdays right in the calendar and in contacts, should be able to choose whether to add/show birthday for the contact.

Thank you.

kurt s. say:

Vitaminb,

There should be 2 options to delete unwanted entries from the birthday calendar on Outlook.com:

Option A:

Recreate the birthday calendar completely new from the birthday information in your contact details.

1. In outlook.com click on the "gear wheel" -> Options -> Calendar -> "additional Calendars" -> Birthday Calendar

2. Select "deactivate birthday calendar"

3. Click on "Save"

Now your birthday calendar will be deleted on outlook.com and also in the Windows apps on your PC/Smartphone.

Go back to the same place described under 1. and select "activate birthday calendar". Again click on "Save".

Now your birthday calendar will be recreated from the birthday details of your outlook.com contacts only. Please be patient this recreation can take a couple of hours depending on the "mood" of the outlook.com servers.

Option B:

1. On outlook.com go to your Calendar.

2. Double-click on the unwanted birthday entry to open the details.

3. Now click "remove birthday" (top right corner).

4. Click again "remove" to confirm.

The birthday entry will be deleted from the calendar, but the date itself will stay in your contact details.

I couldn't test it, but assume this should work for any birthday entries from other sources too.

Note: Above terms might read different on your outlook.com. I just roughly translated those from my local GUI into English.

Private Sub Worksheet_Change(ByVal Target As Range)

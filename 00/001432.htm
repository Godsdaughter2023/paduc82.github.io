You may have inadvertently pressed Ctrl+T which applies a hanging indent. Ctrl+Shift+T should clear it. Or you could select the text, right-click and choose Paragraph from the context menu. In the dialog box, make sure that "Special" is set to "(none)." Click OK.

A third option is to press Ctrl+Q, which reverts the paragraph formatting of the current selection to that of the underlying style.

What I wish to be able to do, and cannot find out how to do it, is to have a total of the hours worked,   Placed underneath the column which provides the hours worked.

j.e. mcgimpsey say: One way:

XL stores times as fractions of days (e.g., 6:00 AM = 0.25, 6:00 PM = 0.75), so instead of 

   =TEXT(F21-E21,"h:mm")

which produces a Text value, use

   =(F21 < E21 ) + F21 - E21

(the F21<E21 term allows the times to span midnight) and format the cell using Format/Cells/Number/Custom  [h]:mm

Then you can simply SUM() the range of cells that hold the individual net times.

azeez n say:
Hi Allen Furfine,

You may check the Logitech QuickCam Communicate STX's compatibility with Windows 7here.

To uninstall the existing webcam drivers from Device Manager:
 
1. Click Start, type Device Manager in the Start search box and hit Enter.
2. On the menu select View>>Show Hidden Devices
3. Under Imaging Devices you will find the webcam.
4. Select your webcam, right-click and select Uninstall.
5. Restart the computer, if prompted.
Drivers should get installed automatically after restarting the computer.

Do not connect your webcam while you are uninstalling the webcam drivers.

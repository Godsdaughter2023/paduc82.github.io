rich michaels - mvp (greatcirclelearning.com) say:

If you notice from your screen shots, those tabs "Format" and "Format Picture" are a different color than the others. That's Word's indicator they are contextual, meaning they will only appear when a shape or photo is selected in the document. Sometimes you have to click that contextual tab before it will open. In other words, it is not an auto-open tab.

So my first thought is, are you selecting the shape or picture? My second thought is, are you selecting the contextual tab. And my third thought is, in Word > Preferences > Ribbon is the "Hide Group Titles" option checked. If that is checked the area of the tab that contains the Styles will not be labeled and thus may not be as evident with a quick glance.

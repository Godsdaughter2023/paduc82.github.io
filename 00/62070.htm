Hi,

What sometimes happens is that Windows 10 occasionally checks with Microsoft Servers to verify it's license... If you do not have a internet connection at the time, you may get the Activation message until the machine can connect...

At times Microsoft has issues with their Activation Servers, and it just takes awhile...

It is certainly possible that the downloading\installation of a Windows Update may temporarily cause a connection problem to the server...

There have been a couple of times that a Windows Update caused an error in which a large number of machines started getting the Activation message...

If your device's storage capacity is more than 4 gigabytes (GB), and your entire Player Library can fit on the device, Windows Media Player will automatically sync unless you choose to manually sync.

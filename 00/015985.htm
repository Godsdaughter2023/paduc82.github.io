smittypro1 say:

If you enter a value in a cell that has a formula in it, you'll wipe out the formula. If possible, separate your data entry from your formulas and reference the entry cell. So instead of =L17*25.4, put 25.4 in another cell and use =L17*L18.

That's much more transparent, and you can use worksheet protection to keep formula cells from being overwritten. I find it's really easy to use Input & Calculation styles to point people in the right direction.

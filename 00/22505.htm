hansv mvp say:

Let's say the names are in Sheet1!A2:A500 and the dates in Sheet1!B2:B500.

On another sheet, you have the unique names in E2 and down.

In F2, enter the array formula (confirmed with Ctrl+Shift+Enter)

=MIN(IF(Sheet1!$A$2:$A$500=E2,Sheet1!$B$2:$B$500))

In G2, enter the array formula (confirmed with Ctrl+Shift+Enter)

=MAX(IF(Sheet1!$A$2:$A$500=E2,Sheet1!$B$2:$B$500))

Select F2:G2 and format the cells as a date.

Then fill down as far as needed.

We can try the following steps to adjust the time interval (It is 3 hours by default):

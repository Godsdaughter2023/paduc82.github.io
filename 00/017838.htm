Private Sub movetoWeedFrm_GotFocus()

Think that the team's use of "canary" is some sort of office jargon?  It isn't.  "Canary" is the term adopted by the software development community for a just compiled new version of a software that has not yet been thoroughly tested and evaluated for distribution to the next "ring."  In this methodology, canaries are submitted to local self-host testing and then evaluated for candidacy by a small, inner group for flighting to the next, larger ring.  Once tested and evaluated it is no longer a canary but is either a rejected build or a candidate build.  Whether or not a candidate ever flights depends on need for a new flight, of course.  "Canary" is meant to conjure an image of the use of birds to give early warning of toxic mine gasses as everyone knows.  I suppose if the build dies, get out fast.  :)

Option #2 (not my first choice)

- open the PST file in the profile

Make a new file
Write the workdays in your local language into column A

In group General click "Edit Custom Lists..."

Click Import
Click Ok

That's it. You can do the same for e.g. month or other lists that you want to fill.

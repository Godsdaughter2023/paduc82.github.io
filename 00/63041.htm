Move your cursor to the taskbar. Right click on it and make sure that 'Lock the Taskbar' is not ticked. If it is, click on it to change. Once the taskbar is unlocked all you have to do is to have your cursor on the taskbar, click and hold left mouse button, then drag the the taskbar towards the bottom of your monitor, (screen).

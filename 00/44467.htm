aakkam22 say:

You can disable your smart card using Device Manager to remove it from UAC prompts. To do this:

1. Click Start, and in the search box, type Device Manager. Click to open it.

2. In the device manager, find and expand Smart Card Readers category

3. Right-click on the smart card and select disable the device. Confirm that you want to do this.

That's it. The smart card prompt should now be removed.

Pressing Enter in Word drops the cursor by 6 lines - Microsoft Community

gracelipski say:

Pressing Enter in Word drops the cursor by 6 lines

Original Title: Microsoft Word problem in line spacing

In Microsoft Work after typing and clicking "enter" the cursor moves about 6 lines below.  How can I single space?

suzanne s. barnhill say:
For six lines, I'm guessing the problem is not the paragraph spacing but the vertical alignment of the page. Go to Page Setup (the easiest way to do this in any version of Word is to double-click at the top of the horizontal ruler) and select the Layout tab. Make sure that "Vertical alignment" is set to Top and not Justified.

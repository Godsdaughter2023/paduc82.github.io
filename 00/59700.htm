tom.garrett say:

Hi David,

I just had this problem as well.  I found the problem is that the microphone volume is down too low.  It seems that when you use Microsoft's Speech setup wizard it will turn down the microphone volume way to low.  To correct this manually right click on the speaker icon by the date/time on the taskbar.   Select recording devices.  Select your microphone from the list of recording devices and select properties on the bottom left.  Select the levels tab and increase the microphone volume.  If it has a microphone boost you may want to up that as well.  Select the OK and you will be taken back sound recording tab.  Talk normally into your microphone and you will see the meter to the right of the microphone fluctuate.  If it goes up and down while talking it should be fine and Cortana will work.  if it is still low or is pegged to the max while talking you will need to readjust the microphone volume accordingly.  I hope this helps.

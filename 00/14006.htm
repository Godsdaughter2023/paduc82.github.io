talking moose say:

It’s possible to add attendees as optional in Outlook for Mac.

  1. While in Outlook’s Calendar, choose File > New > Appointment to open an event in a new window.
  2. Click the Invite button.
  3. Click the Scheduling Assistant tab in the middle of the window.
  4. Click the plus button ( + ) to add a new invitee and search for someone in your Contacts list.
  5. Select the contact and click the Optional button at the bottom of the window.

Alternatively, you can enter all your invitees in the To field and then change each invitee's status under the Scheduling Assistant tab.

Difference between Skype name and account name - Microsoft Community

lizzabellaortiz say:

Difference between Skype name and account name

Which one is the Skype name and the account name.  For me, it appears as though both are the same but just now when I signed up to ask a question, I had to change my profile name.  So is my Skype name my profile name?

Thank you.

jomel_g say:

Hello

Welcome to Skype Community!

A Skype Name is a unique user name that many people use to sign in to Skype. A Skype Name is created when you first signed up for Skype, and it cannot be changed. Skype previously offer an option to create your preferred Skype Name upon the sign up process. But since the migration of Skype to Microsoft, this has been removed. In an effort to improve the sign-up experience, Microsoft now offers a new simplified method for creating an account. You will no longer have an option to create your Skype name because the new simplified account sign-up method only requires an email or a phone number. This new method aims to reduce the sign-up effort by presenting a faster and more flexible experience for new users.

If you created your Skype account using a phone number or email address, checking of Skype Name requires you to log in to the Skype website and follow the steps below:

  1. Scroll down and look for Purchase history.
  2. Your Skype Name will be shown at the upper right side of the Purchase history page, before the Sign Out button.

On the other hand, Display Name is the full name on your user profile. This one can be modified by signing in to Skype online or through the application. Display Name is what your contacts see when they add you as their contact.

When someone searches you on Skype, they can either use your Skype Name or Display Name to locate your account.

These two can be compared to an identification card. Your Skype Name is the identification card number, while your Display Name is your name on the card.

I hope the information above explains the difference between the two.

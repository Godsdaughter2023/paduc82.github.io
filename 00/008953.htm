Connect-SPOService -Url https://contoso-admin.sharepoint.com

(replace contoso with your tenant domain)

To see all the deleted sites, you can run this command:

Get-SPODeletedSite | fl

If the deleted site is listed and DaysRemaining is NOT 0, we can restore the deleted site by running 

Restore-SPODeletedSite -Identity siteURL

I understand how convenient it is to be able to send emails as attachments. However, that feature remains unavailable in Outlook.com.

 

You may send a feedback Gear Icon at the upper-right corner of the page. Then click Feedback and follow the on screen instructions.

 

Windows Live Mail is one of the programs that has the option to attach emails to your outgoing messages. It will also keep everything in sync with your Outlook.com account. Whatever you do in Windows Live Mail will be reflected on the web and vice-versa. I have provided steps below on how to forward emails as attachments in Windows Live Mail:

 

1. Right click on the email that you want to attach

2. Choose Forward as attachment

3. A window will pop up and the email is already attached

 

Here are the steps on how to attach several emails to one mail:

 

1. Select the emails that you want to attach by pressing Ctrl + clicking the emails one by one

2. Right click and choose Forward as attachment

3. A window will pop up and the emails are already attached

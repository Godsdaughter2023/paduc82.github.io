    Can't use ipod/itunes because of Bitlocker to Go - Microsoft Community

    ldu97177 say:

    Can't use ipod/itunes because of Bitlocker to Go

    Every time I connect my ipod to my laptop it asks me to encrypt with Bitlocker to go. If I don't encrypt itunes says it's corrupt, and if I do I can't manage my music on it. Is there anyway around this?
    cityjim say:
    Every time I connect my ipod to my laptop it asks me to encrypt with Bitlocker to go. If I don't encrypt itunes says it's corrupt, and if I do I can't manage my music on it. Is there anyway around this?


    After doing a lot of searching I have the solution. The good news is, it's easy! I had exactly the same issue. It is caused by the configuration settings of BitLocker on the computer, to fix simply do the following:


    1. Open Group Policy Editor - From Windows Start Menu, type in: gpedit.msc

     

    2. When the Group Policy Editor window is open, then follow:
    Computer Configuration\Administrative Templates\Windows Components\BitLocker Drive Encryption\Removable Data Drives\Deny write access to removable drives not protected by BitLocker

     

    3. Select the "Disabled" radio button and click OK.

     

    That's it! Done.

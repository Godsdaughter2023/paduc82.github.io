Project resource leveling stops at the first over allocated resource and gives me the message to either increase the allocation from 50% on the project to 100% on the project or to cut the hours.  I have all hours loaded as 'work' for each task.

trevor rabey say:

"Effort driven" has no relevance to this.

Max units is the maximum availability of the resource.

The default is 100%.

Over-allocation occurs when the assignment exceeds the max units.

You can test this by just using one task.

Have you set the max units of your resources to 50%?

Have you assigned them at 100%?

If so, then your resource will be over-allocated, obviously (although nothing is ever necessarily obvious).

To see more clearly what is happening, try just using two tasks to test the concept and the mechanics of leveling.

You say " I expect project to reschedule these resources and push out all of the dates", but you must mean re-schedule the tasks. That's what leveling does. IT delays tasks until the resource over-allocation no longer exceeds the resource max units.

When leveling, make sure that you check the leveling options first. Usually, it is better to uncheck the fine tuning options checkboxes and find a raw solution, then refine it.

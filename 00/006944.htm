Hello again !

 

I figured out what's the problem ;) There is nothing wrong in your C7 at all ! The thing is : your nokia is saving contacts in your SIM card , which restricts all details what so ever ! In order to be able to save other details , you will have to save your contacts in your phone memory ! More space , more details , almost a unlimited number of contacts :D unlike in SIM card !

 

I advice you to save all your contacts in your phone memory and delete all your contacts from your SIM , keep it empty ( if you want ) , and occasionly backup your contacts in order to be on the safe side :D !

 

To do that :

 

1/ We want to make sure you are seeing contacts in the SIM card only :

* Open the contacts list .

* Go to "Options" --> "Settings" .

* Select "Contacts to display" and make sure ONLY "Sim memory" is checked, then press OK and then BACK .

 

2/ We want to copy all contacts from the SIM card to the memory card :

* Go to "Options" --> "Mark / Unmakr" --> "Mark all" .

* Go to "Options" --> "Copy" , and then select "Phone memory" as it appears .

 

3/ Now that the contacts have been saved in the phone memory , no need to keep them in the SIM card , so let's delete them from the SIM card [ /!\ SKIP STEP 3 IF YOU DONT WANT TO DELETE THE CONTACTS FROM THE SIM CARD , however I advice you to do so :D ] :

* Go to "Options" --> "Mark / Unmakr" --> "Mark all" .

* Go to "Options" --> "Delete" , and then select YES .

 

4/ Now we must select the phone memory as default for contacts :

* Go to "Options" --> "Settings" .

* Select "Contacts to display" and make sure ONLY "Phone memory" is checked, then press OK and then BACK . [ Usually after this step , the phone will automatically do the following step , but here it is just to make sure : ]

* Select "Default saving memory" and make sure ONLY "Phone memory" is checked, then press OK and then BACK .

 

And that's it :D you can from now on , save in one contact more than one number ( unlike in SIM ) , you can add as much detail as you like ( unline in SIM ) , you can add a picture to each contact ( unlike in SIM ) , and you have almost a unlimited number of contacts to save ( unlike in SIM ) !

 

If you have previously saved two numbers for one contacts seperatly ( which would be the case because you have been saving contacts in SIM card ) , you can merge them under one contact by going to "Options" --> "Merge contacts" and selecting what numbers you wanna merge under one contact only .

ray gao msft say:

Hi Linda,

 

Many SharePoint permissions depend on other SharePoint permissions. For example, you must be able to open an item to view it. In this way, View Items permission depends on Open permission. The View Application Pages permission refers to the permission for users to view documents and views in a list or document library. View pages and Open items permissions are basically the lowest permissions. Most of the default permission levels have these two permissions. And many other permissions depend on these two permissions. For more information, please refer to the following article.

Understanding permission levels in SharePoint

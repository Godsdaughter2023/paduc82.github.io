jason (office engineer) say:

Hi Mary,

When you share something on Facebook, other social sites, or even sharing engines, those sites peek at what's in the URL and extract the content themselves.  Even though Sway has updated the title, picture, etc. those sites have to grab the new version.  Once they've indexed a URL like this, it's up to them when they index again.  For many sites, this is every seven days.  Unfortunately, even sharing the same URL again won't help because the site will have stored its index results for the same URL.

The best workaround for this is to actually use a different URL.  I know this can be a bummer if you've already disseminated that URL to people, but if you choose to do this route, you could keep both URLs up.  The way to do this is to go into Sway and use the duplicate functionality.  This will make a copy of your Sway and give it a new URL.  If you keep both copies around, it may be a little confusing to manage them both, but at least you'll get the new indexing results on Facebook.  If you do this and you decide to delete the original, make sure you're very careful to check the URL of the one you are deleting first as we've had many reports from people that they deleted the wrong copy of their Sway.

Good luck and I hope this helps!

Jason

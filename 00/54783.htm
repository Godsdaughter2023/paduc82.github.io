patrick barker say: Hi Paul,

The best way to keep all of your drivers up to date is to stay away from 3rd party driver updating programs and to somewhat regularly visit the manufacturers website for your devices to see if updates are available.

For example, if you have an AMD video card, you'd navigate to AMD's Support & Drivers website and see what the latest driver is and if you need to update. Do the same for all of your devices in regards to their manufacturer.

If you have a pre-built OEM computer like HP for example, or Dell, you instead navigate to HP or Dell's driver website.

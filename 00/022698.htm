Adobe fonts add extra space after an apostrophe - Microsoft Community

bl5 say:

Adobe fonts add extra space after an apostrophe

Several of the Adobe fonts (Adobe Fan Heiti, Adobe Fangsong, Adobe Gothic, Adobe Heiti, Adobe Kaiti, Adobe Ming, Adobe Myungjo) add an extra space when you type an apostrophe.

So instead of:

John's

You automatically get:

John' s

Same thing happens with double quotations marks as well: ".

You can't simply go back and delete the extra space. When you change it to another font the space disappears. 

The only workaround I've found so far is to manually insert an apostrophe from the Advanced Symbold drop-down.

Tried the solutions here, but no luck: http://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/office/forum/office_2007-word/extra-space-after-apostrophe/13edb10b-6a5f-464e-8f33-0b41c63c4680

thg250 say: Those are all fonts intended only for Chinese, which sometimes have double width punctuation.   The fix is to not use them for English or other Latin script languages.

hpholm say:

Hi Rui,

Nokia 515 is designed to sync with Mail for Exchange e.g. a Windows Live account.

Moving the contacts from PC Outlook to outlook.com is relatively painless, but a three-way sync of PC Outlook <-> outlook.com <-> phone is likely to end in tears.

One user reported success using an old version of Nokia Suite (3.6.36) and the phone in USB modem mode to sync with PC Outlook the old-fashioned way, but this is not officially supported:

http://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/mobiledevices/forum/mdnokian/nokia-515-nokia-301-and-pc-suite-connectivity/a6e52bc5-c62a-463c-929a-425719923f7b?page=4

The posts were moved into here from Nokia Discussions so some links are missing.

I suggest go for a Live account, unless you need PC Outlook for business use, in which case I would replace the phone, with a older S60 or Symbian, or a phone from another manufacturer that sync's with the PC out of the box.

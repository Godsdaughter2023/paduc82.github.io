Regarding the error message, I suggest you follow steps below:

 a. In the upper-right corner of the Office 2013 (2016) app, click your name, and then click Switch Account.

 b. On the Accounts screen, click Sign out.

 c. Locate the account that you want to remove, and then click Sign out.

 a. Edit the registry to remove cached credentials. To do this, follow these steps:

  2). In Registry Editor, locate the following registry subkey:

  4). In the Identity subkey, locate Profiles, right-click the same Office account that you deleted in Step 3) of this procedure, and then click Delete.

  5). Exit Registry Editor.

 b. Remove the cached credentials in Credentials Manager. To do this, follow these steps:

  1). Open Control Panel, and then click Credentials Manager.

  2). Under Generic Credentials, locate the account that you want to remove, and then click Remove.

 c. Log off, and then log on to the computer.

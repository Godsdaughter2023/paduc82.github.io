julie fka profjulie say: There are four ways you might be able get your data over to the HTC Hero, which runs the Android OS:

  1. Copy all your contacts to your SIM card, then insert the SIM card into your new device (assumes you can use the same SIM card in both devices) - of course this will only copy your contact information and you still won't have tasks, or appointments there.
  2. Sync the Windows Phone to Outlook, then upload this data to google, using software like Google Apps Sync , then sync the data to your android phone . 
  3. Sync the Windows Phone to Outlook, then using software like Companion Link sync that data to your android phone.
  4. Sync the Windows Phone to Google, using software like Google Sync .  Once the data is on Google, you should be able to sync this data to your android phone

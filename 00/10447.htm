First, let me thank you for the many high quality answers you've been providing here in the forums. Your assistance is appreciated by me, at least, but I am confident the appreciation level is much higher for those whom you have helped.

Where Excel decides to make the page break depends on at least two factors:

  • The current default printer and its driver version
  • Fonts

For the record, this problem has nothing to do with whether the worksheet is protected or not. As you discovered, uninstall and reinstall has no effect.

jeromestack say: There may be a simple solution to the problem, which lies with the video driver. Mine was not updated and had been replaced with a generic driver.

 Windows 8.1 has a default setting that prevents it from downloading updates to drivers over a "metered connection." This is nice feature for those who are connecting to the internet with a device that uses a metered connection, of course, but it prevents Windows from updating drivers properly. 

Changing the setting from Off to On did the trick and the drivers updated automatically and quickly. 

To find this setting you need to go to Settings -- Change PC Settings -- PC and Devices -- Devices. You may see notices after the devices indicating that they have not been updated. Scroll down a bit and find the Download Over Metered Connection item and change the setting to ON.

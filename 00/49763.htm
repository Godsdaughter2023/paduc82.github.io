    Recovery Disk D is Full - Microsoft Community

    julia jacob say:

    Recovery Disk D is Full

    my recovery disk driver (D) gives a message that its full.. how can I empty it.. when am doing disk cleanup it doesn't help! please advise

    a. user say:





    Your D: Drive normally is the Recovery Partition and is about 5Gbs-19GBs in size, that the Manufacturer put there, for your computer to put it back to factory settings in case of System Crash.

     

    Contact your Computer Manufacturer on how to remove anything you have saved to the Recovery Drive, as it is their Recovery Partition, not Microsoft’s.

    It is not for you to save anything to; and it is not to compress and/or Index,, Disk Cleanup, use for Backup, Defrag or have System Restore turned on for it.

    You are supposed to make Recovery disks from D:, if possible, in case of Hard Drive failure.

    Ask the Manufacturer if you can make them, and how to delete/fix what has been done there.


    Cheers.

diane poremsky mvp say:
Do you have more than one computer or device accessing this mailbox? (Whether or not the other device is trying to check mail)

You'll get this error with pop3 accounts if two devices try to access it at once -- or if the lock was not removed.

Locks should be removed when the mail client is done checking mail but if you are checking mail too frequently (less than every 5 min) it could be interrupted and not delete the lock. They should expire on their own after about 10 min too.

If you are sure there is no a second computer or phone trying to check mail, your mail host will need to remove the lock from the mailbox.

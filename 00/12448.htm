The Different Odd and Even option doesn't allow you to have different header/footer margins, just different headers and footers. But, by using those different headers and footers, you can fake different margins. Set the top/bottom margin to the smaller amount, and then use Spacing After in the header and Spacing Before in the footer to increase the effective margin.                         net start cryptSvc

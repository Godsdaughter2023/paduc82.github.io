 4. Click on System info under Related settings (a new window will open)

 5. Click on System protection on the left (another new box will open)

 6. Click the system restore button. (another window will open that says Restore System Files and Settings)

 7. Click next

 8. Click the show more restore points box (on the lower left)

 9. That is how you will see all available restore points. If you have one prior to August 28 click it to highlight it.

10. Click next

If you are running windows 7 the step by step is completely different but still achievable.

The only way to return your computer completely intact to an earlier date is by a system image you created yourself at a particular date. It is like a photograph of your drive. If you don't have one you can't accomplish what you want to do. Your only choice is to reinstall what you lost.

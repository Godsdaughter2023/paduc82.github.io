Hello,

We understand that you want to delete the duplicate Administrator account on your computer. To address your concern, you may refer to the steps below on how to delete a user account using Control Panel:

  1. Right-click on the Start/Windows symbol on your computer.
  2. Click on Control Panel.
  3. On the Control Panel screen, make sure that you are in Category view.
  4. Click on User Accounts.
  5. Click on Remove User Accounts.
  6. Click on the user account that you would like to delete.
  7. Once you click on the account that you want to delete, you will see a screen providing you the options to Keep Files or Delete Files. You can choose the option that you want.
  8. If you click the Keep Files button, Windows 10 will save the files from the user's desktop and will also save files found in the folders of the user.
  9. After clicking Keep Files, click on the Delete Account button to delete the selected user account from your computer

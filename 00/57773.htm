I want control of Windows 10's appearance - Microsoft Community

david dickinson say:

I want control of Windows 10's appearance

Microsoft used to give us control over the colors and sizes of almost any display element in Windows, from the fonts and color gradients used in title bars to the sizes, colors, and fonts used in toolbars, window frames, workspaces, and other window elements. I think the easiest and most flexible control we had was under Windows XP.

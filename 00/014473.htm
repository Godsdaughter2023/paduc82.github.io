﻿If videos you’re watching over the internet are buffering (stopping and starting) that means the video isn't being sent to your computer fast enough.     how do i remove write protection from my floppy - Microsoft Community

    digger63 say:

    how do i remove write protection from my floppy

     I created a resume on power point 2007 at my home down loaded it to a floppy.  came up here to the library to down load it to a company web site and can not says its got write protection how do i remove
    ruchi bisht say:

    Step1:

    If you are able to access the floppy follow the below steps:

    1.       Copy the document and save it on the system.

    2.       Right click on it-> Properties-> uncheck Read only-> ok.

    3.       Try loading the document to your website.

    If the floppy is write protected follow the instructions in step2:

    Step2:

    1.       Pop the floppy out of your computer and take a look at the top of the floppy disk.

    2.        In the upper left hand corner you should see a square with a slider; it is easy to tell if a disk is write-protected, it will have a space in the middle.

    3.        Locate the slider tab and make sure it is in unprotected mode; if the space is uncovered, it's in write-protection mode.

    4.        Removing write-protection is easy; all you need to do is simply move the slider up and "cover" the space with the tab.

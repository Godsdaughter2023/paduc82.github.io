m joseph say:

Hi,

Media information about music files in your library, including files that were created when ripped from CDs, is displayed in the Player. The existing media information cannot be removed, but you can prevent new media information from being retrieved and displayed. However, doing so will cause any files created during ripping to be displayed in your library as "Unknown."

 

1. Click the Start button, click All Programs, and then click Windows Media Player.

 

If the Player is currently open and you’re in Now Playing mode, click the Switch to Library button in the upper-right corner of the Player.

 

2. Click the arrow below the Organize, click Options, and then click Library.

 

3. In the Automatic media information updates for files area, clear the Retrieve additional information from the Internet check box, and then click OK.

 

This also clears the Update music files by retrieving media info from the Internet check box, located on the Privacy tab.

Remove information about what you have played in Windows Media Player

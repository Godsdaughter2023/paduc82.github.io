Hi Dana.D

I figured it out by using the following:

If the two trend line equations are the following:

65,884x -1y = -150,03

5,4878x - 1y = -465,37

Then put the values into the spread sheet like this

                                                                                                    

A B C
1 65.884 -1 -150.03
2 5.4878 -1 -465.37

Then select two cells in a column and press F2.

Wright the following formula: =(MMUTL(MINVERSE(A1:B2),C1:C2)

Press ctrl+shift+enter.

This will give you the intersection of the two trend lines.

Top cell is x.

Bottom cell is y.

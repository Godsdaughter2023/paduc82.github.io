Re-Sharing a Shared Document - Microsoft Community

nolan fenwick say:

Re-Sharing a Shared Document

If a I receive a link for a shared document can I then turn around and share that document with someone else? Is this different depending on if I am internal or external to my company?

Nolan

tim liu msft say:

Hi Nolan,

If you get a link which can be accessed via Anyone, you are able to share it with others as the link is for Anyone. However, it's better to contact the owner if you could re-share it to others.

If you get a link which needs you sign in, that link might not be accessed by other users. This kind of link needs to be signed in with related accounts. However, after you sign in, if you have proper permission, you are able to find the Share button and share it with others no matter you are external or internal user .

If you get a invitation email which show the item in the mail, you could click it to access the library. When you have proper permission, you are able to find Share button and share it with others no matter you are external or internal user. 

Note: For all scenarios, I suggest you firstly contact the document owner. Check if you could re-share the document to others.

Tim

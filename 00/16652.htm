jllatham say:

Excellent presentation of your data and formulas.  Now, if I offer just one hint, I'm thinking you can get the job done.

 

Hint:  "Wrap" your formulas inside of the very same formula you used to get conditional formatting accomplished!  Example:

=IF(WEEKDAY($A2,1)>5,"",your formula for weekdays here)

The ,"", part will simply return an empty string when WEEKDAY() returns 6 or 7.

 

 

By the way, (and since it's working, why fix something that's not broken) - but for the Conditional Formatting formula, you could simply shorten it to

=WEEKDAY($A2,1)>5

It gets interpreted as either TRUE (1) or FALSE(0) automatically when used for conditional formatting.  But again, as long as it's working, why risk breaking it - just food for future thought.

This error occurs when you have a dedicated graphics card on your computer. It disables the use of the dedicated card and uses the integrated card instead. To have the said error fixed, kindly follow the steps below:

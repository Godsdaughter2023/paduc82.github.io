    Hi Alex,

    UserNotPresentSession captures Power related data (Sleepstudy, AdaptiveStandby) when no user is actively using the device. We suggest that you set "UserNotPresentSession.etl" to Read-only. See steps below:

    1. Go to your C: drive, then click on the Windows folder > System32 > SleepStudy.
    2. Right-click on the file: UserNotPresentSession.etl and then Properties from the list.
    3. Put a check mark on the Read-only checkbox and click on OK, then Apply.
    4. Restart your computer.

Hi Nsgma,

If you want to manually manage the site storage quota, as a SharePoint administrator, you need to choose Manual to Site Collection Storage Management settings under the SharePoint admin center settings page. Regarding “the personal site ... is feasible to share a folder...”, end users are the site collection owners of their personal site, and they can share personal site contents by default. If users want to share folders, they need to share a folder via the email invitation.

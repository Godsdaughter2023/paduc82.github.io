speck73 say:

There are two locations where your 5800 might store all of its text messages and emails. One is on your C: drive and the other is on the memory card. Often, 5800 and N97 users get into a bit of a pickle and end up with two message stores, one in each location and each representing various points in time in relation to their messages.

 

Switch between these two possible message stores by going into the Messaging application and choosing Options - Settings - Other. You might find that all of your 'lost' messages magically appear when you choose the alternative message store location.

 

I know that on the N97, the default message store location defaults back to the C: drive when you attach the device to a computer in 'Mass Storage Mode'. This irritating tendency might also exist on the 5800 although I do not have one to test at this moment.

 

You should also be aware that the 5800 is currently up to firmware version 52.0.007 and a freshly hard reset device running this firmware is well sorted and very reliable indeed.

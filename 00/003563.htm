If you're receiving this error, it means that your email message was sent from a server or domain that is currently listed as a high volume spam source by a reputable global black list. psychomania---01 say:

 

a_petrov303 wrote:

I have inserted another tesco sim and it works...so not sure if it is sim locked or what else...

 

at the same time a giffgaff sim (also uses O2 network just as tesco mobile) does not work, therefore I presume we are not dealing with a network lock here, because the network is the same.

 

That is perfectly normal behaviour for a locked phone from an MVNO.  It will accept any sim that uses the same MVNO network (tesco) but won't accept a sim from the actual network it uses (O2) or another MNVO such as giffgaff.

 

Phones that are actually locked to O2 may accept sims from MVNO's such as tesco or giff gaff, but it isn't guaranteed. 

 

As an example from my own personal experience: A phone locked to T-mobile will accept a sim from Virgin mobile as they are an MVNO that uses the T-mobile network.  A phone that is locked to Virgin will not accept a T-mobile sim.

 

Good luck getting this sorted with tesco! Every little helps ;)

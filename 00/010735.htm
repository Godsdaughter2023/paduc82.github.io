semian m. s. kanimarani say:

How do I force Windows Defender to take over security? Previous AV has already been removed but WD refuses to believe it.

When you uninstall your previous antivirus, Windows Defender service turns itself on. This is the normal behavior. To check this, go to:
        Settings -> Update & security -> Windows Defender

In this window, if "Real-time protection" option is "On" then Windows Defender is on and it is working. In this case it may needs to be updated.

If the "Real-time protection" option is "Off" and it is disabled (you cannot turn it on) then your previous antivirus service(s) is still running behind the scenes. In this case use your previous antivirus's "uninstaller" program to completely uninstall the antivirus. For this purpose refer to the website of the antivirus you want to remove.


Hope this answers your question.

ericyang_msft6286 say:

Hi Robert,

Most of domain hosting providers provide a feature called URL forwarding/redirection. To make it possible to visit your SharePoint Online site using a custom domain, you can use this feature to forward the custom domain to the URL of your SharePoint Online site. However, the custom domain cannot be changed directly from SharePoint Online side. this is because that SharePoint Online is designed for internal use and many features in SharePoint Online is based on the original URL of the sites. Changing the domain in the URL may cause various potential conflicts.

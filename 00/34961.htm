No.

If XP came preinstalled on your HP Computer, it is classified as an OEM licence, and is non-transferable.


The OEM XP Licence is tied to the original Computer it is installed on, and dies with the Computer.
You can NOT transfer it to another Computer/Laptop

If a Retail XP Licence, you may transfer it to another Computer/Laptop.
But, it may only be installed on one Computer at a time.

 

One Licence = One Installation.

Follow these steps on how to turn high contrast mode on or off in Windows:

  1. Type "high contrast settings" in the search box, and then select High contrast settings from the list of results.
  2. Under Choose a theme, select a high contrast theme from the drop-down menu, and then select Apply.
  3. To turn off high contrast mode, select None from the drop-down menu, and then select Apply.
  4. To turn high contrast mode on or off from the sign-in screen, select the Ease of Access button in the lower-right corner and choose High Contrast.

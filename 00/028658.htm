mukherjee ashutosh say:

This error could occur if in your account settings, you have set your SMTP e-mail server to use a secure connection, and your server does not support secure connections.

 

To turn off secure connections by your SMTP e-mail server:

  1. On the Tools menu, click Email Accounts. > View or change existing email accounts
  2. Click to select the account listed in the error message, and then click Change
  3. In email account settings window select More Settings.
  4. On the Advanced tab, for your Outgoing mail (SMTP), click to clear This server requires a secure connection (SSL) check box.
  5. Click OK, and Close.

 

For more information on how to troubleshoot error messages that you receive when you try to send and receive e-mail in Outlook you can check the following Microsoft Kb article:

http://support.microsoft.com/kb/813514

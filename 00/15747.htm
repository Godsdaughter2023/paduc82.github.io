Actually, Bernd has mentioned the answer. "The Ordering".

Think of this simple example in real life.

1. You have a list.
Book, in the drawer
Pen, on the table
Eraser, in the bag

or,

2. You put a label for each item.
put a label on the Book: "in the drawer"
put a label on the Pen: "on the table"

jay freedman say:
You didn't say, but I assume you're talking about the dictionary opened by the Define button on the Review tab, which is also one of the apps you can get on the Apps for Office button on the Insert tab.

There is no way to install these apps "as part of the installation package." If you can't go to the Office App store, you can't get apps. (If you do get apps at the App Store, they aren't "installed" on your computer anyway -- they're web apps that are downloaded from a server each time you access one.)

The Encarta Dictionary is installed with Office, though. Hold the Alt key while you click on the word you want to define, and the Research pane will open. If the Encarta Dictionary isn't selected, click the dropdown (the second box in the pane) and select it. If it isn't in that list, click the Research Options link at the bottom of the pane and check it in the Options dialog.

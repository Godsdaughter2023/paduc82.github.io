stefan blom say:

Since a table of contents, by default, is created based on heading styles, you will have to re-format a paragraph which should not be in the TOC by applying a different style. Just modifying the direct formatting (such as font and size) won't suffice, as you may have noticed.

Also, note that if you are depending a lot on text boxes in your documents, I recommend that you reconsider that design. Any document which contains a large number of objects in the drawing layer is more likely to become corrupt.

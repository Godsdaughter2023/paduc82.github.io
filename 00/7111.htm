kathleen orland say:

If Outlook isn't closing, there are several reasons why. Programs that tend to keep Outlook open are:

 

FAX software (example: WinFax Pro)

Virus scanner (there is no need to actively scan your incoming and outgoing emails, your real time scanner will catch everything it's updated for)

Antispam filter

Synch software (iTunes, Blackberry, ActiveSync) - removing the handheld from the cradle

Vista and Windows 7 sidebar gadgets displaying Outlook info

Skype

 

More information and troubleshooting steps are located here: http://www.howto-outlook.com/faq/outlookdoesntclose.htm

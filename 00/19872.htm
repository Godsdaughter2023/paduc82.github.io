If the document is opened in Word 2013, it will still show this behavior, until saved in Word 2013 native mode rather than compatibility mode.

The voice on HERE Drive navigation on my Nokia Lumia phone stopped working.  The maps portion still works though.
How do I get the voice back ?
Mark 

joe men say:

Hello Mark,

To enable the voice navigation of the Here Drive app, check that you have downloaded the navigation voice file and the speech language files in the Windows Phone settings.

Here's how:

  • In Here Drive, go to Settings > Navigation voice and check that a voice file has been downloaded and is ready to use.
  • Go to phone Settings > Speech and make sure you've chosen a speech language and that the speech file is downloaded. Tap the Speech language settings to confirm that the speech file has been properly downloaded. You may need to download the speech file again if you've recently updated your phone software or reset your phone.

You should also check if the phone's volume is turned up.

Feel free to post back if you need further assistance.

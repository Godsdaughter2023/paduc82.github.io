from the initial post, it sounded like you needed to learn how to pull values in from the GAL That sample is just to show how to read the gal, it does a lookup based on a value - in the sample, the value is an entry in a contact group. Set olEntry = olAL.AddressEntries("Diane Poremsky")
strEntry = olEntry.GetExchangeUser.FirstName & " " & _
olEntry.GetExchangeUser.LastName & " " & _
olEntry.GetExchangeUser.BusinessTelephoneNumber & " " & _
olEntry.GetExchangeUser.PrimarySmtpAddress & " " & _
olEntry.GetExchangeUser.JobTitle & " " & _
olEntry.GetExchangeUser.Department
MsgBox strEntry

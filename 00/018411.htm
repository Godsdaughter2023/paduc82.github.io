vqsinc. say:

how to highlight duplicate values on the same row?

Hello,

Does anyone knows how to highlight duplicate values on the same row? 

vijay a. verma say:

1. Select your range. Let's assume that your range is A3:E10.

2. Home tab > Conditional Formatting > New Rule > Use a formula to determine which cells to format and put following formula and choose appropriate Fill Colour

=COUNTIF($A3:$E3,A3)>1

(Note the formula is dependent upon the your starting cell in the range. If A3 is not the starting cell, then you will need to replace $A3 and A3 appropriately)

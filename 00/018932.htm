Taking this one stage further, to get a listing of the names and email addresses of the group members,

  1. Click Add a subject and type the name of the group.
  2. Click Save draft.
  3. Click Outlook in the top left-hand corner.
  4. Click Drafts in the left-hand pane, then select the message you just saved.
  5. Use your mouse to mark the name+address entries in the To area of the message, then press Ctrl-C.
  6. Open a new document in your favourite word processor and press Ctrl-V
  7. Use Find and replace (in Word, that's Ctrl-H) to find '; ' (semi-colon space) and replace with newline (in Word, that's ^p).
    Find '(' (opening parenthesis) and replace with tab (in Word, that's ^t).
    Find ')' (closing parenthesis) and replace with nothing.
  8. Save and/or print.

If your Lumia 900 does not come on when connected to the charger please perform a reset first. This will not delete any personal data;

 

  • Press and hold the power button for 10 seconds
  • Release the power button
  • Press and hold the volume down and power button until the phone vibrates
  • Release the power button
  • Release the volume button after 10 seconds

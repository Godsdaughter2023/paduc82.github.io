david_dave say:

You can’t do that with IMAP email.  The only way is to copy the email to a separate folder (PST would work) within Outlook.

 

IMAP by design syncs your email up and down.  So if you delete locally it will delete from the server.  If you really are opposed to copying emails (could even setup a rule to do it for you automatically) you should see if your email provider uses POP instead.

You may move an email from your inbox to junk by clicking Junk button on the toolbar after viewing or selecting the said email. This will also move incoming emails from the sender. While blocked senders list will automatically block the emails from the sender and permanently delete them.

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. Open or view the email you want to be blocked.
  3. Click Sweep on the toolbar.
  4. Select Delete all from…
  5. Tick Also block future messages.
  6. Click Delete all.

tblTools

PCU Avg. Temp.? - Microsoft Community

robertrindone say:

PCU Avg. Temp.?

What is a healthy temp for an Intel I7(4700MQ) processor to run ?
patrick barker say:

Hi,

It depends whether you're speaking of IDLE or LOAD temperatures. If IDLE, it depends on the ambient environment, etc. If you're in a semi-warm location, I would say 30-40 (and mid 40) C is okay.

However, as noted above, LOAD is what's really important. LOAD temperatures vary greatly depending on what application you're running, etc. However, you never want to go above 70 (or very low 80) in extremely intensive applications.

If you're any higher than that, considering lowering the graphical settings of the application, the resolution, and also cleaning the system of any dust (heat sinks, etc).

f) Right click your mouse to show the App bar (If you are using a touch screen: Touch your finger to the bottom of the screen and slide up with your finger)

g) Click Set as default which will be displayed in the lower left corner of your screen

Your charger charges the laptop battery even while the machine is turned off.

While the machine is turned off, Windows is not active in any way.

If our charger fails to charge the battery while the machine is turned off, Windows 10 cannot be the culprit.

Here are a few possible reasons why the battery is not charging. Windows is not one of them:

  • Faulty power cable.
  • The power cable is not plugged in properly.
  • The charger is defective.
  • The power socket in your laptop is defective.
  • The battery is defective.

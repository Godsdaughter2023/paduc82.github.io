Yes. We can use the in-place hold or litigation hold to back up our mailbox data. Once we enable either one, our mailbox data will be restored in the Office 365 Exchange server. This includes deleted items.

They are expecting the Ribbon will be customisable to the extent that you can add or remove controls that already exist within the application (same as the PC version's ability to add or remove the Developer tab, but for a much wider range of controls).

That said, I am not expecting RibbonX in this version, so no adding our own controls.  We might get lucky, but I wouldn't bet on it.

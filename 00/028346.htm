ashish mathur say:

Hi,

 

If you want to search for "Part# 1234" in the cell, then try this

 

=sumif($B$2:$B$1000,"*"&B1002&"*",$C$2:$C$1000)

 

B1002 has Part# 1234.  B2:B1000 is the list of all part numbers.  C2:C1000 has corresponding quantities.

With source data as-is (without concatenating),

=sumproduct((Cond1)*(Cond2),SumRange)

=SUMPRODUCT((Sheet1!$A$2:$A$25="xx")*(Sheet1!$B$2:$B$25="yy"),Sheet1!$C$2:$C$25)

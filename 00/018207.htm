Hello Peter14pa:

 

Thank you for posting to the forum regarding your question about changing the Xbox account on your phone.  LCRT is correct that if you want to use another account on the phone you will need to perform a factory reset of the phone.  I just wanted to clarify what that means.

 

That means that you will be erasing the current contents of your phone.  You can back up the media on your phone and then sync back to the phone via the Zune software.  The process of backing up your media is outlined here, http://support.xbox.com/en-US/zune/software/syncing-media.

 

Since you will be putting a new account on your phone, the contacts and calendars will not automatically be brought to the phone.  You can get this information to sync to the phone by adding the current account as a secondary on your phone.  To do this tap settings > emails + accounts > add an account > enter the details associated with the previous account.

 

Finally, text message history and game progress cannot be backed up.  This will be reset and you will start over from scratch.  If you have important information in the form of a text, we suggest you copy and paste this into an email and send to yourself. 

 

Once you are ready to reset the phone you can follow the steps in this article,  http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2430020?wa=wsignin1.0.  The steps to reset are tap settings > about > reset phone.  Once you have this initiated, follow the on screen prompts.

 

Please let us know if you have any additional questions or concerns.

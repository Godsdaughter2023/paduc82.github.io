ron coderre say:

Obviously, Microsoft has confusion about what to call the [+] and [-] buttons.
• If you select several contiguous rows that you want controlled by a the buttons (regardless of subtotals),
the command is Data.Group_and_Outline.Group

• AND...if you select some of the rows within a Group, they can be UNgrouped via: Data.Group_and_Outline.Ungroup
to roll back one layer of grouping at a time.

• However, to clear all groups at one time, the command is Data.Group_and_Outline.Clear_Outline

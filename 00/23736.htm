slance310 say:

Hi,

This may not be related to your problem, but I thought I would mention it...

I have a program called SpeedFan that monitors temps among other things...

After a couple of my most recent upgrades I have a HDD that causes SpeedFan to take 5-8 minutes to initialize after a cold boot... In addition, the HDD is not accessible... just a green scroll bar slowly moving to the right... Turning off Fast Startup resolves the problem.

It turns out that this HDD is hooked to a 3rd party Asmedia SATA Controller... After updating the driver with one from the motherboard manufacturer (Asrock) I was able to turn Fast Startup back on, and everything is good..

I have also had issues in the last few builds with the Microsoft furnished Intel driver for my Intel Chipset... The USB 3.0 in particular, which maxes out at 20mbps... A slow USB 2.0 speed...

Updating the Chipset (INF) driver from either the motherboard manufacturer or Intel resolves the problem...

Both of these issues are unrelated to the recent Meltdown and Spectre patches, as they both existed prior to that being released..

Content disappears after clicking Enable Editing on an Office document - Microsoft Community

a. user say:

Content disappears after clicking Enable Editing on an Office document opened in Protected View from the internet

Problem:

In certain situations, MS Office appears to re-download content from the internet when clicking "Enable editing" on a document in protected view. Since the connection initiated by Office does not share the same session with IE, the user may lose the content of the document.

 

Question: How to make Microsoft aware of this issue so that it can be fixed?

Steps to reproduce:

Prerequisites: A link to an Excel document within a website that requires authentication. If attempting to access the document via the specified URL when not logged in, the website should redirect to another page on the site, such as a login page. The site should not be in the list of trusted sites. MS Office should be configured to Enable Protected View for files originating from the internet.

1. Using IE (tested on version 9 and 11 so far), click on the link to open the MS Office document.

2. When prompted to Open or Save, choose Open.

3. When the document opens, click on “Enable Editing” in the protected view message bar.

4. Excel will replace the content of the spreadsheet with the content of the login screen OR a blank screen.

Versions tested: MS Excel with IE 9 and IE 11.

Note: MS Word appears to be affected by this problem as well.

_ellen_ say:

How do I divide a word document into smaller parts and keep editing content

How can I split this into smaller parts that are

1) connected (format, page number, list of contents)

2) editable


Thank you!


phillip m jones say:

The images are what is affecting your document.

To split the document into to parts :

Try:

  1. Open document that is acting funny.
  2. Open one Blank Document.
  3. Go back to Main document.
  4. Click at beginning of document and scroll to about half way
  5. Click the ¶ symbol on Standard Tool Bar
  6. Now hold shift key down and click just to right of the last ¶ on that Page
  7. Hold down ⌘ key and type C to Copy.
  8. Switch to Blank document.
  9. Now hold down ⌘ key and type V (to paste).
  10. Now immediately save this file using original name and add part A to name.
  11. Next switch back to original document
  12. This time if text is still selected hit delete.
  13. Then immediately save using original name wit part b appended to name.
    Example:
    My Paper (is Original File)
    My Paper Part A (is first half of original Document
    My Paper Part B (is second half of original Document
  14. Now Try working on Individual parts. See if you can edit. If so continue on and when finished editing you can rejoin the pieces by opening both part A and B 
  15. Copy all of B
  16. Then switch toA go to end.
  17. Paste B into A and Re-save as Final.

hansv mvp say:

In an outgoing e-mail:

  • In the e-mail window (not in the main Outlook window!), activate the Options tab of the ribbon.
  • Click to highlight the Bcc button in the Show Fields group.

In an incoming e-mail, you cannot view Bcc recipients (by definition). The Bcc recipients were stripped from the message before it was delivered to you.

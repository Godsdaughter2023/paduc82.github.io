stefan blom say:

Click in a paragraph formatted in Normal style. Press Ctrl+Shift+S to display the Apply Styles pane. Click the Modify button. In the dialog box, change "Style for following paragraph" so that it says "Normal." Select "New documents based on this template" and click OK. Later, if/when you are prompted to save the template, choose to do so.

Doing that will prevent Word from changing the style in the future, as you press Enter. For existing text, though, you will have to reapply Normal style, which you can do easily by selecting text and pressing Ctrl+Shift+N.

The indentation in the Navigation Pane is based on the Outline Level "assigned" to the paragraph  in the Paragraph dialog.

This grabs the worksheet tab name from it's index and uses the tab names held in the variable Ws1 and ws2. It displays the message boxes at the end of the code.

 

 

Sub nn()

How to add ringtones to your smartphone - Microsoft Community

jack cook - aka help_line say:

How to add ringtones to your smartphone

So you found a terrific ringtone and have decided that you want to have it on your device but now that you have it, how do you place it on your phone?

 

It's simple and if you follow these steps, you will be listening to that new tone in a flash!

 

1.       1.       Connect your smartphone to your PC with the USB cable.

2.       2.       Open ActiveSync on your PC.

3.       3.       Click the Explore button.

4.       4.       Double-click My Windows Mobile-Based Device.

5.       5.       Double-click Application Data. You will see a list of folders, one of which is Sounds.

6.       6.       Using Windows Explorer on your PC, find the ringtones you downloaded and drag them into the Sounds folder in ActiveSync.

7.       7.       Disconnect your smartphone.

8.       8.       On your smartphone, press Start > Settings.

9.       9.       Select Sounds.

10.   10.   Under Ringtone, use the left and right navigation keys (next to the Center Joystick) to scroll to the ringtone you want. If you wait a second or two after scrolling to each choice, you'll hear what it sounds like.

When you've found the ringtone you want, press the Done soft key

toneml say: You would usually be aware what version of Office you purchased or downloaded and installed after you purchased the machine.

jan de messemaeker - project mvp say:

Hi,

Please be aware of the definition Project handles for %Complete:

%Complete=100*Actual Duration/Duration.

Now that is a definition, there is no workaround. That is what it is.

So in Project you cannot have an actual duration of 2 days, and you want the duration to remain 5 days, %Complete will be 40%. Full Stop.

Now let's be positive.

First, if you simply set the task to 50%complete (via Task information or Update tasks) the finish date will NOT change - instead Project will mark the task as finished till Wednesday noon.

Second, if (which I understand) your definition of %Complete is different, don't use Project's way of calculating: enter actual duration and remaining duration (both available through Update Tasks) and to show that the task is half the way in your terms, enter that value in Physical % Complete: Duration will not change.

suzanne s. barnhill say:

Once you've corrected the pagination issues, you can reinsert the section breaks as needed, and each new section will follow the formatting of the previous one. Another approach, when you need to handle pagination in "landlocked" sections, is this:

  1. Place the insertion point in the section in the middle of the page (for which you can't access the header or footer).
  2. On the Insert tab, in the Header & Footer group, click Page Number and choose Format Page Numbers... on the menu.
  3. In the Page Number Format dialog, set the number to "Continue from previous section."
  4. Repeat as needed for additional sections.

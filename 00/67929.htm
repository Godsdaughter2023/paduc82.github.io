Device Manager does not display all devices by default. Non–Plug and Play devices and certain other devices are hidden. At times, you might need to view these hidden devices for troubleshooting problems with devices installed on your computer.

 

 

Delete hidden devices:

a.    Click show hidden devices under View

b.    Expand all the nods under device manager

c.    There would be many grayed out entries in device manager uninstall all the grayed out entries under all the nods and then restart the computer

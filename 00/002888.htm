bob jones aka: cybertaz say:

I'd suggest that you first make sure you have Office 2011 fully updated - 14.6.2 is the current version. Restart after applying any updates, then see if things are any different.

charles kenyon say:
First, not in answer to your question, do NOT directly open documents sent by email by double-clicking on them in the email. Instead, save them to your hard drive, to a known location, and open them there. Failure to do this will result in lost work, sooner or later.

To have a double-click on a .doc or .docx file automatically open in Word you can re-register Word. Under the Start menu in the search box, type the following:
winword.exe /r
and press Enter. Note the space before the slash.

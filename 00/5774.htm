a. user say:

Hi Robin,

Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Office Community.

Let's check whether Office applications are running in Compatible Mode. If yes, disable the same and verify the result.

1) Browse to the location C:\Program Files\Microsoft Office 16\root\Office16 or  C:\Program Files(x86)\Microsoft Office 16\root\Office16

2)  Right click on Office application like Excel  and then click in Properties.

3) In the Properties window click on Compatibility tab.

4) Uncheck ‘Run this program in compatibility mode for’ and then click on Apply and OK.

Keep us posted on the status of the issue and we will be glad to offer our assistance.

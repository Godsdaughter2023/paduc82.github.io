capicoffice say:

a. user say:

Hi,

Thank you for posting in Microsoft Communities.

I understand the inconvenience caused, but rest assured, I will be assisting you with the issue.

In this scenario, we can add the @Hotmail.com as a secondary alias and then convert it to primary alias.

I suggest you to follow the steps as given below:

Add Hotmail account as a secondary alias:

  1. Log into http://account.live.com using your existing e-mail address.
  2. Under Basic Info, select Manage aliases.
  3. Towards the bottom of the page, select Add alias.
  4. Choose Add an existing e-mail address as a Microsoft alias, and enter the Hotmail e-mail address that was removed.
  5. Click on Create Alias.

 

Now that we have added the Hotmail account as a secondary alias, let us convert it to primary alias. But before that, we have to verify the account.

 

Verify Secondary e-mail address.

  1. Log into http://account.live.com using your existing e-mail address.
  2. Under Basic Info, select Manage aliases.
  3. Under Account aliases, click on verify next to the secondary e-mail address.
  4. Click on send e-mail.
  5. Log into http://Outlook.com using the secondary e-mail address that we have added.
  6. Open the verification e-mail that is sent to you for verification.
  7. Follow the instruction as instructed in the e-mail.
  8. Log out from the account.

 

Convert the alias to Primary alias.

  1. Log into http://account.live.com using your existing e-mail address.
  2. Under Basic Info, select Manage aliases.
  3. Under Account aliases, change the Secondary alias to Primary alias.

 

I hope the above information helps you. Please do reply with the status.

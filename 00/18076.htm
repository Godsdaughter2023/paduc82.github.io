Word templates are stored in your user Library folder, which is hidden by default. To open it, hold down the Option/Alt key while clicking on the Go menu and choosing Library.

Word 2019 templates are stored in ~/Library/Group Containers/UBF8T346G9.Office/User Content/Templates.

Please do not replace your Normal.dotm template. Instead:

  1. Open the template with the styles you like in Word.

  5. Close the dialog, then close Word to save the changes to Normal.

The primary component of all Anti Virus/Malware applications is the resident file system scanner.  Its purpose is to identify and kill anything bad before it gets written into either memory or the hard drive.  The reason this works for email (and why you don't need a separate email scanner) is because of the way attachments are handled by the email system.  One of the primary requirements of all email systems is that anything sent can ONLY be composed of printable characters...this means virtually ALL binary CANNOT be executed as a program until it's converted back into binary data, so it is harmless until the conversion happens.  That's where the resident scanner comes into play.  As soon as your email program (any email program, not just Outlook) begins the conversion process, this scanner goes to work.

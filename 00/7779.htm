How do I Remove a Domain from Live Domains? - Microsoft Community

t. baxter say:

How do I Remove a Domain from Live Domains?

It seems I am only able to add more domains to Live Domains. I want to remove a domain! How can I do this? No one ever though of a Remove functionality? THis is crazy.

cherry anne m. say:

Hi T. Baxter,

 

Thank you for posting your questions in Microsoft Community Forums.

 

From my understanding, you want to cancel service for a domain. If this is correct, a customer can cancel service for a domain at any time. However, if the customer uses Windows Live Hotmail as the e-mail service and he cancels the Windows Live Admin Center service, the users’ e-mail and mailboxes will be deleted. The deleted e-mail cannot be recovered.

 

To cancel the service for a domain, you need to perform the following steps:

1. Sign in to Windows Live Admin Center by using your administrator Windows Live ID.

2. Under Your domains, click the appropriate domain.

3. In the navigation pane, click Domain Settings.

4. Click Cancel Service, and then click Yes.

 

Notes: When a service is canceled, Windows Live accounts are maintained so that users can still sign in to all other Windows Live services. However, Hotmail subscriptions will be canceled. This includes any existing Hotmail Plus subscriptions.

 

 

Hope this helps. Let us know if you need further assistance.

 

Thanks,

Cherry

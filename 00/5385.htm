Hi karlo1020lumia,

 

Alas the issue isn't with your Lumia, it's with the website (and other websites) itself.

 

Without going too much into it, historically, drop-down menus use a CSS Hover state, this means that a user would hover a mouse over an item and the drop-down would appear. When it comes to touchscreen devices, a user obviously can't 'hover' their finger over the item (well they could but nothing would obviously happen lol). There are some pretty straight forward css techniques to make drop-downs work correctly, but I'll not bore you with the details.

 

For this reason a good web designer should either code drop-downs with this in mind (all should nowadays and anyone not is failing their customers & users), or use a js library/plugin to help.

 

Hope this answers your question.

Hi Sad,

We're sorry for the inconvenience caused. And we really appreciate all your effort and time for reporting this issue. We are aware of the issue now. Here we'd like to share a summary for the issue here which will benefit other community members.

We have been receiving multiple threads having similar issues caused by sign in issue on Office for Mac.

Users may not be able to sign in to Office applications, or meet error message when you want to save changes for files stored in OneDrive for SharePoint Online locations within from Office apps.

The possible scenarios:

1.    You may get error message that “no network connection. Please check your network settings and try again”.

 

2.    You may see a spinner forever saying “loading” when you try to sign in.

Issue Environment:

The issue happens to both the latest 16.16.8 and 16.23.

Workaround:

Several community members mentioned that Installing 15.41 can get rid of this issue. Affected users can choose to install this previous version or use Office Online temporarily before the final fix released. We will keep working on the issue.

Assessment:

As the issue only happens to specific users, and we can't reproduce the issue, we just got issue log provided by one of the affected users, we are reporting this issue with the log for further investigation. 

For affected customers to easily identify the information here, this thread will be closed. Affected users can keep monitoring the updates in this primary thread.

We appreciate your patience and understanding.

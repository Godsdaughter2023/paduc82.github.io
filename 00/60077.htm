davem121 say:

When you say you moved programs from your old PC, do you mean you copied them to the new laptop?

If so, they will never work, Applications install files into C:\Program Files or C:\Program Files(x86) and also the your ProgramData Folder and into your user/AppData folder. Registry entries are also written for most applications. 

Applications need to be installed onto a new system rather than being copied from an old system in order for them to work and when you are installing them you can install them onto your D drive, but this sometimes causes issues as some applications need to be on the same drive as the OS if badly written . . .

With regard to your Data, you can right click any of your library folders (Documents, Pictures . . . etc.) and click on the location tab to set their location to a folder on your D Drive, in fact this is the best option. Just remember to set the new location to a folder on your D Drive and not the Root as a lot of users have made this error and their files system has become unmanageable . . .

I Hope this Helps!

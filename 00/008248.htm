There are 2 possibilities:

First is that the message is correct. Did you get it at a really good discount compared to the MS store price? That is a strong possibility since the company you bought it from has evaporated. What happens is that someone buys a "Volume license" with multiple product keys. Then they resell those keys to the unsuspecting public. The problem is that the license terms do NOT allow the original buyer to sell the key to anyone outside of their company. They do this, then close up shop and keep the money. At some point MS recognizes the keys have been distributed improperly and marks the keys invalid. They poor suckers like you get stuck holding the bag. MS is happy to sell you another key.

The second possibility is that the message is wrong. Do a phone activation, but this time do not reply to they prompts until they offer you the option of talking to a live service rep.

As of Thursday there are two flavors of Fast Ring previews, rs3_release and rs_prerelease.  Please check for a dropdown in Settings/Update and Security/Windows Insider Program that lets you select between two choices, one of which is the skip ahead choice.  The meaning of "skip-ahead" is that you would be bypassing the remaining few months of Redstone 3 (Fall Creators Update) development and moving to the next version of Windows 10 after that (as yet un-named) scheduled for release in March, 2018.

Do NOT use skip ahead unless you are willing to risk a lot of bugs and possible forced re-installations of Windows.  The rs3_release is the Fall Creators Update, to be released in September, 2017.  The rs_prerelease is the version that will be released in March, 2018.  Both branches are part of the Fast Ring and are updated once a week or so, depending on qualitative decisions by the dev team.  The build numbers are nearly the same right now so it is a little confusing.  

If you just want to see what's up with the September release of the Fall Creators update a much safer choice is to set the ring to Slow.  That will give you build 16232.  A relatively stable version of the Fall Creators Update.  Slow Ring receives updates once a month.

Also, as a new Insider you should know that no insider preview builds are suitable for your primary or production computer.  You risk your important data if you do that.  Insider previews are NOT supported by Microsoft.  If you get in trouble you are on your own to get out of trouble.

Use only a spare device or a separate partition for preview builds.  If you are not in a position to do one or the other then simply continue on the Creators Update and you will be upgraded in September.  As always, back up your entire system before starting a major change to a system.

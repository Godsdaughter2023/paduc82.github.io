danida_u say:

Hi

Skype purchases are delivered on the account that was used for the initial purchase. For you to receive calls to your Skype Number, please make sure that:

  • You are signed in to the correct account. Although this seems obvious, most Skype users tend to overlook this. If you have multiple accounts, make sure that you sign in using the credentials of the account you used to make the initial purchase. You can use the Microsoft Accounts Diagnostics link to determine the accounts under your email address.

  • You are signed in to one device at a time only. We do not recommend being signed in to multiple accounts at once as there may be a possibility that your calls, messages or notifications get misrouted.

  • Your privacy settings are set to accept calls from anyone.

If the issue persists after doing the above suggestions, do not hesitate to post back so we can send you a follow-up message.

suzanne s. barnhill say:

This is not a "problem" to be "fixed." When you click on the Numbering button, you get indented numbering presumably because the Office team, from customer feedback, has determined that most users want numbered paragraphs to be indented. For numbering that has the indents you prefer, you need to apply a customized numbered style.

The "bible" on autonumbering is http://www.shaunakelly.com/word/numbering/numbering20072010.html. Although you may not think you need a "multilevel list," in recent versions of Word all lists are actually multilevel lists. If you set your desired paragraph formatting in the Define New Multilevel List dialog and link the level to a specific style, you will always get the numbering and indents you want when you apply that style. What you get when you just click the Numbering button, or allow Word to apply numbering for you through AutoFormat As You Type, is a gamble.

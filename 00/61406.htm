considerate_guy say:

First make sure you have selected a video clip, the one you want to speed up or slow down.  The speed control will only affect the speed of a video clip (not a photo) and you must select the clip first.

If you want to change the "speed" of a photo (or group of photos), you do that by changing their *durations*.  A shorter duration means the pictures will place faster. A longer duration means they will play slower.

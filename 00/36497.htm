hairyfool say:

I absolutely agree with you, the solution was just trying to keep it within the functionality of Windows Media Centre. How WMC captures the TV signal to generate the recording is specific. You would need to ensure an alternative capture file is compatible with WMC playback

Then you would need an HDMI capable Video capture device but bear in mind that HDMI supports High-Bandwidth Digital Content Protection and there is a distinct possibility the STB may be looking for a HDCP compatible device on the output. How the output device responds if not connected with HDCP active varies with Snow, an error message or just a blank screen being used or the signal is reduced to low res mode.

StarTech make both USB 3 and internal PCI-e cards but there is no mention of HDCP in the specs and they are not cheap. There are low cost "game capture" devices but active content does not need HDCP so these almost certainly do not support it.

c)       Click on the driver tab and click on “Uninstall” to uninstall the driver.

d)      Now, click on “Update driver” and then click on “Browse my computer for a driver software” to browse the location where you have saved the driver you downloaded from the manufacturer’s website.

e)      Follow the prompts to update. Restart and check if the device is recognized.

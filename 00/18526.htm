So if Word runs OK with a Safe Start, you have three candidates to blame. Easiest course of action is to try moving EndNote to the Desktop, away from its autoload folder (presumably Applications > Microsoft Office 2011 > Office > Startup > Word).  

 

The latest version is X7.5, released last February.

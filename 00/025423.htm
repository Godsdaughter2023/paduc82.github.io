Sub Macro1()
  Dim WS As Worksheet

      For Each WS In ActiveWorkbook.Worksheets

Follow the steps below to cancel your subscription.

  • Sign in to your account on the Skype website.
  • Under your profile picture, select the subscription you want to cancel.
  • Select Cancel subscription. 
  • Select the radio button next to your reason for cancelling,
  • Click Cancel subscription to confirm your cancellation. 

Note that even if you cancel the subscription, it will be active on your account up until its expiration date.

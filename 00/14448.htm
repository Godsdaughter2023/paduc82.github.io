suzanne s. barnhill say:
Headers and footers (including page numbers) do not print when "Selection"
is selected in the Print dialog. That's because the pagination will not
necessarily match that in your document (if the selection begins in the
middle of a page but printing begins at the top of the sheet).
 
To start new sections on a new sheet when duplexing, you must begin each
section with an Odd Page section break. Word will then insert a totally
blank even page, when required, to finish out the previous section. You
won't see this blank page in Print Layout view or even in Print Preview
unless you (a) have "Mirror margins" or "Different odd and even" enabled and
(b) are viewing a two-page spread, but it will "print" when you print the
document.

fetaa say:

hi michael,

to move the files (more than 20,000 files) to a sharepoint online site, you may try creating five or more document libraries in your team site and make sure that every library has less than 5,000 files. to create a document library:
log into the sharepoint online site> click the gear on the upper right corner> click add an app > click document library to create it.
note: every file size limitation is 2gb when uploading files. 

to share the files with external users, you may try sharing the libraries you created and grant the external users contribute permission(users could read and upload and delete files). to do that:
open the library>under library button, click library settings> under permissions and management, click permissions for this document library> under permissions, click stop inheriting permissions> click grant permissions, input the external users' email addresses and select contribute permission, and then click share.
note: after clicking stop inheriting permissions, it would create a unique permissions for this document library. changes made to the parent site permissions will no longer affect this document library.

for the onenote notebooks, they have their own sync mechanism, they aren't synced by the onedrive for business sync client. you can upload onenote notebooks to a sharepoint online page. however, they won't sync with through the onedrive for business sync client application. additionally, if you add a onenote notebook to a local folder that syncs with sharepoint online, the notebook won't sync with the sharepoint site and may cause other sync errors within the local folder. for the detailes, please refer to https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/kb/2933738 .

if anything is unclear, please let us know.

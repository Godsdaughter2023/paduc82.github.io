talking moose say:

As you’ve already learned, Outlook for Mac uses Spotlight. Therefore, troubleshooting Spotlight is the correct way to approach this.

One thing I didn’t see in your research was completely resetting the Spotlight index. You’ve correctly tried temporarily adding and them removing the Microsoft User Data folder to Spotlight to kickstart the indexing for this folder. But if that doesn’t work then completely rebuilding the entire Spotlight index makes sense.

  1. Open the Terminal application found in Applications > Utilities.
  2. Type this: sudo mdutil -E /
  3. Press the return or enter key and you’ll be prompted to enter your password. Type it and press return or enter again.

This effectively deletes the entire Spotlight index for your computer and builds a new one. Depending on the amount of data on your computer this could take a few hours to complete. You can click the Spotlight menu icon on the upper right corner of your monitor to monitor progress.

Hope this helps!

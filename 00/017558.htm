count number of years to one decimal between two date - Microsoft Community

excelaid say:

count number of years to one decimal between two date

Hi,

 

I have a curious problem.

 

I need to find the number of years between two sets of date down to the 0.0 range

 

for example 7/7/2009 - 9/14/2012 = 3 yrs , 2 month , 1 week: or (3.2) years

 

I want it to display 3.2 somehow, is there a solution in excel for this? thanks!

 

EA

 

 

 

hansv mvp say:

Let's say the dates are in A1 and B1.

A good approximation is =(B1-A1)/365.25

A somewhat more accurate formula is

 

=DATEDIF(A1,B1,"y")+DATEDIF(A1,B1,"yd")/DATEDIF(EDATE(B1,-12),B1,"d")

 

Excel tends to format the result of this formula as a date. Format it as Number with 1 decimal place instead.

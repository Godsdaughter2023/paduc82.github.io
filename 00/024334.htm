puigchild say:

Hi makememad,

 

Thank you for your post and welcome to the forums!

 

When you first start the phone, you can select to copy contacts from the SIM card to your Windows Live ID.  After this Windows Phone automatically stores all your contacts to the main Windows Live account.  

 

Unfortunately this is by design and cannot be changed, you can only import existing SIM card contacts to your phone, and when you create a new contact it will be automatically added to your Windows Live ID/Hotmail account.

john korchok say:

To have each chapter show a different chapter name in the header, you must unlink each section as you create it.

  1. If you haven't already made the non-printing characters visible, please choose Word>Preferences>View and check All, then OK out. Now you can see the section breaks.
  2. Go to the first page of the new section and double-click on the header to open it.
  3. Click on the Header and Footer tab of the Ribbon.
  4. Uncheck Link to Previous.
  5. Move to the next page, which has either an even or odd header.
  6. Uncheck Link to Previous.
  7. Move to the third page.
  8. Uncheck Link to Previous.
  9. Now enter the chapter title for the new section. Since the headers have been unlinked from the previous section, the first section retains its original chapter title.

You'll have to repeat the above steps for each new chapter.

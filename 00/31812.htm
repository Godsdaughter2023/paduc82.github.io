SettingSyncHost.exe causing apps to randomly lose focus - Microsoft Community

1corvin say:

SettingSyncHost.exe causing apps to randomly lose focus

So I ran into this weird issue recently - in Outlook the cursor would sporadically change to spinning wheel and back. This was annoying, but not deal breaking. Then I noticed that hovering over tabs in taskbar (e.g. explorer or Chrome), which would normally display a preview of app window, would have preview show up momentarily and disappear. Annoying, but possible to live with. And lastly, I noticed that in many Windows Forms apps (HxD for me) dropdowns would close on their own and autocomplete would reset itself constantly, I also noticed that these apps and controls would flash subtly, as if something was refreshing the windows all the time. This was really bad and prompted me to investigate. I combed through my startup apps, video and audio drivers - nothing. I killed all 3rd party tray apps - nothing. Eventually (took me a few hours, mind you!) I created another user account and logged into it while having my primary account running - bingo, no issue in the other user account. 

At this point I just opened Process Explorer and started killing off every process running under my primary user account. Issue stopped occurring after I killed SettingSyncHost.exe, a ***WINDOWS process***. From here on, it was easy to figure out that this process was spawned whenever "Sync Your Settings" feature was enabled. I had been working from my laptop and had my account logged in on a recently procured desktop. The lil bastard was, probably being extremely aggressive syncing those settings and updating window styles all the time. Issue disappeared as soon as I disabled the settings sync. 

Looks like someone in Windows team deserves a good spanking for this. I wonder if anyone else hit this problem.

graham r seach say:
Firstly, what you're asking the wizard to do is create code to open a form - not display or filter a subform.
 
Secondly, the left-hand pane shows the fields in the form on which the button is being placed. If you don't have a list of fields, then my guess is your form is unbound. Open that form in design view, select the Data tab of the Properties dialog, and look at the RecordSource property. If it's blank or invalid, then your form is unbound. If you want the left pane to show a list, the form's RecordSource must be the name of a table or query, or be a valid SQL statement.

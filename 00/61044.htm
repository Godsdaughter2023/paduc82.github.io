The Shift + Delete option is used when you want to delete files completely without them first going to the recycle bin. This option is only used when you are absolutely sure that you will not need to recover the file.

You could try free application called Recuva: https://www.piriform.com/recuva this may be able to recover some of the files you have deleted.

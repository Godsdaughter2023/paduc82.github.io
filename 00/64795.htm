http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-8/restore-refresh-reset-pc

Disclaimer: Apps you installed from websites and DVDs will be removed. Apps that came with your PC and apps you installed from the Windows  Store will be reinstalled. Windows puts a list of removed apps on your desktop after refreshing your PC.

If you do not have an Installation Media, then refer to the following Microsoft Help article to create one.

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-IN/windows-8/create-reset-refresh-media

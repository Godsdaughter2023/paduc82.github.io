cager say: The stop code 0X0000007B is an Inaccessible Boot Device - which could be due to errors on your hard disk or with your hard disk controller driver.

Go into your pc's BIOS and change the boot priority so that the pc boots from the CD drive.
Insert the XP CD & boot from it.
When Setup has loaded the hardware drivers it will present you with a list of 3 options - press R to pick the second option and start the Windows Recovery Console.
When the WRC starts it will detect your Windows installation & ask you which installation you want to log onto - the answer is normally 1.
You will then need to log-on using your Administrator password and you will be presented with a prompt, such as C:\Windows >
At the prompt type chkdsk /r and then chkdsk will check and repair the volume on which Windows is installed.

Depending on what chkdsk finds, you may need to reinstall XP again.

To reset your console from the Xbox Startup Troubleshooter, use the D-pad  and A button  on your controller to select Reset this Xbox. When prompted, select Keep games and apps.

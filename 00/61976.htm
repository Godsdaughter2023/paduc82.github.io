visageofanangel say:

In regards to San Andreas, Xbox Originals run emulation software to function on the 360 and that emulation can be buggy.  For example, Halo 2 suffered a ghosting effect and Fable could confuse the texture for your clothing with the ground.

 

I've not played GTA IV or Borderlands 2 so I cannot speak about specifics for those games.  I do believe that Borderlands 2 runs on the Unreal Engine which is prone to texture issues.  Technically speaking, texture pop-in is generally a result of insufficient video RAM to handle everything on the screen.

 

You might try clearing your system caches and seeing if that helps.  Also, if these are digital copies you could attempt to re-download them, sometimes files get messed up during download.

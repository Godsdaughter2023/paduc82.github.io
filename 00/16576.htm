a. user say:

Hi Alexander Korabelnikov,

 

I appreciate you for providing the additional information in the private message area. Since you mentioned that the LinkedIn contacts are syncing fine in Outlook.com, I advise that you remove LinkedIn from your connections and try adding it again. When using a computer browser, follow these steps:

 

Removing the connection to LinkedIn:

  1. Login to the Microsoft account page.
  2. On the left panel, click Permissions, then click Manage accounts.
  3. If you see your LinkedIn account connected, click on Edit.
  4. On the next page, click Remove this connection completely.
  5. A pop-up window will appear confirming if you want to remove the account, then select Remove.
  6. Log out and log back in for the changes to take effect.

 

Adding connection to LinkedIn:

  1. Login to the Microsoft account page.
  2. On the left panel, click Permissions, then click Add accounts.
  3. Look for LinkedIn's logo and click click See your LinkedIn contacts and their updates link.
  4. On the next page, click Connect. You will be prompted to the LinkedIn login page and enter your credentials.
  5. A notification page will load stating that you have connected your LinkedIn account to Outlook, then click Done.
  6. Log out and log back in for the changes to take effect.

 

Please confirm if the LinkedIn contacts reappeared in Outlook.com's People page. If yes, then you need to remove and re-add your account from the phone. However, doing this will reset the phone to its factory settings, and I advise that you backup the necessary information stored on your phone to avoid any loss.

 

Thank you.

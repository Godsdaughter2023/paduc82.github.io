Well, change the formula to =21-COUNTIF(A$1:A2,A2) for those that start at 20 classes and =11-COUNTIF(A$1:A2,A2) for those that start with 10 classes.

Better yet, add column C: Starting No of Classes

=SUMIF(A$2:A2,A2,C$2:C2)-COUNTIF(A$1:A2,A2)+1

Enter 20 in cell C2 (for John Smith)
Enter 10 in cell C3 (for Jane Doe)

a. user say:

Hello Jerry,

Thank you for keeping us updated on the issue.

The drive may have stopped working because something on your computer changed.

Installing or uninstalling software on your computer, or upgrading your Windows operating system, can sometimes cause the CD or DVD drive to stop working. This is usually because something happened during the installation that caused a problem with the CD or DVD filter drivers.

If the drive stopped working after you installed new software or made other changes to your computer, you can try turning it on again.

To turn on your drive:

  1. Press Windows key + E
  2. Right-click the DVD drive, and then click Properties.

  3. Click the Hardware tab, and then click the DVD drive (select the device for DVD-CD ROM Drives).

  4. Click Properties

  5. From General tab, click Change Settings

  6. Under Driver tab, Enable the driver if it is disabled and click OK.

If it is already enabled, click on Update Driver.

You may also try Roll Back driver and check if the issue still persists.

Hope this information is helpful. Please do let us know the result, we’ll be glad to assist you.

The OneNote app on iOS always asks for password. But the login screen does not appear when clicking “Tap to enter password”.

 

Environment:

OneNote app version 16.0 (170508).
iOS version 10.3.2.

 

Current Status:

We have submitted the issue to our related team for further investigation. If there is any update, I will update in this post.

 

Workaround:

Follow the steps below to clear the credentials and re-sign in.

  1. Close all the Office for iOS apps. To do this, press the Home button on your iPhone twice, swipe to find each Office for iOS app, and then swipe the preview screen up to close each app.
  2. Go to iPhone Settings > select OneNote app > Reset OneNote > Delete Login Credentials.
  3. Start OneNote app and sign in again.

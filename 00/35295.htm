If you are trying to make calls to mobile phones in Sweden, then note that these phone numbers are not covered by the Office 365 subscription.

Canada, China, Guam, Hong Kong SAR, Japan, Puerto Rico, Singapore, Thailand, and the United States.

Is the number/area code you are dialing a special (like shared cost), premium, or non-geographic? If that is the case, those calls are not covered by any subscription and will require credit.

Note that special, premium, and non-geographic numbers are not covered by Skype minutes. Additionally, it isn’t possible to place emergency calls.

Hello!

To prevent automatic login when you start Skype, just close your session, so when Skype is started the next time, you'll have to enter your credentials to log in. You can do that in File > Sign out. Please note that if you close Skype, but do not sign out your session, you will be active if you have selected "On close, keep Skype running".

If you do not want Skype to run automatically when you start your Mac, you can do it from the System Preferences in your computer. This setting is not located inside Skype preferences as it is managed from the MacOS system. Just open your System Preferences, go to Users and groups and click on your user. You'll see a tab for startup items. From there you can remove Skype so it does not start at login. If you wish, you can manage this from the Skype dock icon using the right-click in your mouse. It will bring the contextual menu to start Skype when you start your Mac.

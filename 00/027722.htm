suzanne s. barnhill say:

To crop the picture, you need to click on the Crop button, which will change the display of the picture so that it has cropping handles as shown.

You push on any one of the handles to crop.  If you can't see the entire picture on the screen, either use the scroll bar(s) to bring the other sides in view or reduce the Zoom until you can see it all. Don't press Escape until you're done cropping (you can also just click away from the picture to finish).

As Doug says, most photo editing is better done before inserting the picture in Word, using software designed for the purpose.

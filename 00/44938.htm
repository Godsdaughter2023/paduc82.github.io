You should know that to calculate the measurement in centimeters is the size in pixels of the image and the density of pixels that the image has, which is measured in points per inch (PPP).

Hi Susan,

Start your PC, just as the manufacturers logo disappears and Windows attempts to load, press and hold Power Button for 5 - 10 seconds to perform a Hard Shut Down

Do this twice

On the third start Windows will boot into the Recovery Environment and from there you can access System Repair, Safe Mode, Command Prompt . . . etc.

Ggo to Troubleshoot - Advanced Option - Startup Settings and click Restart
Upon restart, press 4 to enter Safe Mode

That's It!

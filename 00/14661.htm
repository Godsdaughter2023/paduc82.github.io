Hi Lauren:

That crash has nothing to do with the "Documents".  If you inspect the lines following "Dyld Error Message" it tells you what is wrong: part of the application (a Dynamic Load Library) was not loaded because the system couldn't find it.

It is supposed to be in /Applications/Microsoft Office 2008/Office/MicrosoftComponentPlugin.framework/Versions/12, but it isn't.

This means that the content of your /Applications/Microsoft Office 2008/Office has been damaged.

The fastest cure is to re-install (this is one of the very few times when I would recommend a remove-and-reinstall on the Mac). Make SURE you have your installation DVD AND your product key, then:

1)  Delete the entire /Applications/Microsoft Office 2008 folder.  If you don't do this, then there's no point in re-installing, because nothing will get changed.

2)  Now re-install Office.  Insert your product key if prompted (you might not be).

3) Re-apply the Office 2008 updates (all of them, when you re-install you go all the way back to Version 0 of the application.

You can get the updates from here:

http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/search.aspx?q=microsoft%20office%202008%20for%20mac&p=0&r=10&t=&s=availabledate~Descending

http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=35381

http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=7799

http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=36897

If you put in the top one (the Autoupdate update) then go to Help>Check for Updates in Word, hopefully AutoUpdate will suck the rest in for you.

The master page will not help in this situation. Just get rid of it.

 

If you create a 4 X 10 text box on the first page and import/insert a multipage Word doc, then Publisher 2010 will autoflow the extra content into other pages with full width text boxes based on the margins set for the publication. The default is .5 inch.

 

Maybe this will get you where you want to go:

 

Go to Page Design > Margins > custom margins > margin guides and set the left margin for .5 inch and the right margin for 4.0 inch and the top and bottom .75 inch.

 

Draw a 4x10 text box on the first page along the margin guides. By the way if you did not do it before go to View > and check 'boundaries, guides, rulers and page navigation. It will make it easier to see what you are doing.

 

Now click in the text box > Insert > insert file and browse to your Word file. Publisher will automatically produce new pages with a 4X10 text box.

 

Is that what you wanted?

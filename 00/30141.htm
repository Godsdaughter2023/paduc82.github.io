Hi,

It might be related to a task in Task Scheduler. It might also be something in your startup programs.

First you may check Task Scheduler. Press Win+R on your keyboard to open up Run and type in taskschd.msc, hit Enter. From the left hand pane, click on Task Scheduler Library and then you should be greeted with all sorts of tasks that have been assigned for your computer. Take a look at those and click on things you might suspect, check the description and disable if needed.

Secondly, try disabling startup programs. Again, press Win+R on your keyboard to open up Run and this time type in msconfig, hit Enter. Click on Startup and disable the programs that start with your computer. Restart and check if the issue is fixed.

Hope it does the trick. In case of any issues, I'm happy to help you more when needed. If it worked, please mark the answer as helpful.

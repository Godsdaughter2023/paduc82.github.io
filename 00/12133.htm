Sub Hide_Two()
Dim LastRow As Long, x As Long
LastRow = Cells(Cells.Rows.Count, "B").End(xlUp).Row
For x = LastRow To 1 Step -1

Hello Maria,

Your phone contacts are automatically saved and should be available online by signing in here your Microsoft account that's associated on your Windows Phone. If you upgraded to Windows 10 Mobile and are missing contacts, email, or calendar items, make sure your phone is updated to the latest version, and re-add your Microsoft account.

  1. On Start , swipe over to the All apps list, and then select Settings  > Update & security > Phone update.

  2. Select Check for updates and follow the instructions to update your phone.

  3. Go back to Settings , and select Accounts > Your email and accounts.

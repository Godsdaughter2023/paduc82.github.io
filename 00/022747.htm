_($* #,##0.0"0"_);_($* (#,##0.0"0");_($* "-"?"0"_);_(@_)

which you would enter by choosing Custom on the Number tab (see earlier post) and typing this format into the Type line.

Maybe I misunderstand your questions. But why show 2 decimal places if you don't want to see anything in the second decimal place other than 0?

2nd question: Select all the cells you want to look this way and then apply the format as suggested.

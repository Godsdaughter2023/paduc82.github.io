Quick Scan vs Full Scan - Microsoft Community

azmoman say:

Quick Scan vs Full Scan

I suspect a virus, and run a quick scan, and get nothing, but when I run the full scan, it detects the virus.  What can I do to get quick scan for it to be more effective
stephen boots say:

A quick scan only scans common locations for a virus to reside as well as checking the current memory usage. A full scan will deep scan all files on the PC. An infected file stored on the hard drive can be dormant. If you open or execute the file, real time protection should detect it and take action. There's nothing you can do to change what a quick scan is doing.

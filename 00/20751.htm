The first is to verify your phone is listed to your account.  Go to the phone and tap settings > emails + accounts > verify the first email listed as Windows Live.  Now, go to http://windowsphone.com and sign into that account.  Once signed in, go to MY PHONE.  On this page you should see the name you gave your phone and the make and model of the phone.

If the phone is not associated with your account you have a few things to consider:

  • It may take 24-72 hours for the phone to be associated with your account.  If you just recently set this phone up, wait a bit longer.

Please let us know if you have any other questions or concerns.

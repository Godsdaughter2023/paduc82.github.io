Q: Is there an option to have Skype trigger an email every time I receive a phone call (or for each call that I've missed)?

A: Skype does not have a feature wherein it sends email notification for every missed call  You will be notified for any recent activities via the Skype application. If you do not want to miss any call, you can either set up voice messaging so your contacts can leave you a message when you are offline or set up call forwarding and forward your calls to a mobile number. 

To enable call forwarding: 

  • Sign in to your account on the Skype website.
  • Click Call forwarding under Manage Features.
  • Toggle the button to enable call forwarding and choose whether you will be forwarding your calls to another Skype account, to a mobile number or activate voice messaging. 
  • Set up how many seconds before your calls are forwarded. 
  • Select Confirm to save your changes.

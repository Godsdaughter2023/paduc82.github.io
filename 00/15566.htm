Whilst Word does allow you to set a given colour as transparent, doing this with JPG files, in particular, is problematic as what may appear to the eye as a single colour can in fact consist of multiple colours, especially in areas near colour transitions. You'd have much better results if you used, say, a GIF image of your signature on a plain background and made the background to that transparent. The alternative is to combine your signature & logo in a graphics editor, change the layer transparency there to what you require, then flatten & export the image.

According to this page on Canon's website:  "Canon will not issue drivers for this model to support the Windows 10 operating system. The Windows 7 drivers should function in the Windows 10 environment with some limitations which are currently unknown to Canon."

Canon doesn't support your scanner on Windows 10. That means Canon hasn't tested your scanner on Windows 10 and hasn't updated the driver to keep up with the changes to Windows.

Therefore, Canon doesn't really know whether your scanner will work on Windows 10  and can't help you if you run into problems on Windows 10. Canon thinks that the Windows 7 driver will work, but they can't be sure.

So your scanner may work on Windows 10, or it may not. If it works currently, it may stop working after the next update to Windows. Without support from Canon, you're on your own.

The best thing to do in this situation is to post a question in Canon's own Community Help Forum and find out if anyone else is successfully using your scanner on Windows 10 and, if so, how they were able to make it work.

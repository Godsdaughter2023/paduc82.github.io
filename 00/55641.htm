wunders say:

When you have the window up, select 

   View -> Details

This will show your drives with headers "Name", "Type", Total Size", "Free Space" and "Comments".  Click on the header "Name".  This should sort them alphabetically. If it is sorted in reverse alphabetical order, then click on "Name" again.  Once it is sorted like you like it, select:

    View -> Tiles

To get it back to the original look.

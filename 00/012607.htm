Hello Aaron,

Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Office Community.

There is no option in Outlook to schedule multiple re-occurring appointments within a single instance of the appointment. This has to be done individually.

Also the third occurrence of a particular day in a month may not be the third of later months.


You may check if this can be done using macros. For assistance in creating macro's post in following section of Microsoft Office Community:

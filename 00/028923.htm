schmak say:

I think I fixed it on my phone.

I was playing around with other settings because my continuum won't display video, when I came across a weird section under Privacy -> Other Devices.

In here, I had THREE bands listed and active.  different keys for them as I have had three different bands (last two replaced due to rubber breaking).  Only one was actually paired with the app, but all three were still showing live and active here.

I disabled all but the one that matches the current one I am using, and over the last 24 hours by usage by app for the band has dropped dramatically, to 9% from the 35+% it was before.   So it looks like the Band app doesn't care if a device is paired, it keeps trying to connect to ALL the previously attached bands until you disable them.  There is NO option to remove items from this other apps list, so you can only disable. 

This might not help folks who haven't had more than one band paired, but it might be worth a shot to take a look, and also disable any other devices that you no longer use that are in there, with apps trying to communicate to them.

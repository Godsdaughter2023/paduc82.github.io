Ok, I finally found a solution.

In the above mentioned registry key "HKEY_CURRENT_USER\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\OneDrive\Accounts\Business1" there's an entry called "NamespaceRootId" containing some GUID-string.

I searched the entire registry for this value and fund two more entries and deleted them, too. After a restart I re-entered my credentials in OneDrive. this time it found the correct name of the company.

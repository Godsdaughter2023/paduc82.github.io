ou get the error "Why Outlook can not synchronize subscribed folders" with error 0x800CCC0E
description of the problem

After configuring Outlook to access your mail, it says that 3 of the 4 tasks have been successfully completed with an error stating "Synchronization of subscribed folders for [name] notified error 0x800CCC0E: 'Outlook can not synchronize the subscribed folders'"

This means that Outlook has not been configured to recognize that the server requires an advanced IMAP configuration parameter to read correctly from the folder structure. This is called IMAP route prefix.

Resolution of the problem

Note: This solution only works if the server you are connecting to uses Courier-IMAP as your mail server.

To solve this problem, open your account settings and go to the Advanced tab. At the bottom of the window, you will see an option titled "Root folder path". Put it in uppercase INBOX. Click on OK to apply the configuration. Now you must completely close Outlook and then reopen the software for the change to take effect.

If you have not been able to find this configuration, see our Outlook guide for more details.

Now you should find your connection to the server working normally.

Resolution of the problem

If you discover that your mail folders have disappeared after making this change, simply revert the change and restart Outlook and your folders will come back. You may have to restart your computer, because sometimes Outlook does not shut down completely until it does.

Hi,

Try this formula.

=SUMPRODUCT(SUBTOTAL(3,OFFSET(Sheet1!$C$1:$C$1000,ROW($C$1:$C$1000)-MIN(ROW($C$1:$C$1000)),,1))*(Sheet1!$C$1:$C$1000="S")*(Sheet1!$D$1:$D$1000=A3)*(Sheet1!$I$1:$I$1000="Y"))

Note here 2 things.

1. I cut down the range from full columns to 1000 rows. If this isn't enough then increase it but avoid full, it slows things down.

2. You have apostrophes around the worksheet name and these aren't needed or allowed if there's no space in the worksheet name.

bossdweeb say: Sometimes when older games that use a resolution lower than your desktop resolution crash they cause the desktop to stick on that low res.
Rebooting or logging out and back on should re-set the desktop.

What may be happening with -  "only 1 instance of this application can run"
is the game is not loading fully, but not closing (the process not
ending) fully either  before you try to launch the game again.
When this happens look in Task Manager > Processes for the games executable. and end the
process. Sometimes both the current attempt and original attempt will be listed.
The game should launch after you end the extra process.

It could also be due to a recent update on your Anti-Virus, or the game files have somehow
become corrupted. You could try a re-install (backup your saves first), or try restoring the game
to a previous version (if one is available). Previous versions are found by R/clicking on the games
install folder, and clicking Properties.

john korchok say:

The greyscale icon just gives you a print preview. It doesn't make any changes to the file. If you like the look of the picture in that mode and want to make that the default appearance, you can make a screen capture of the preview and replace the presentation image with the screen capture.

To make a screen capture of just the active windows, hold down the Alt key and press PRINT SCREEN on your keyboard (It may read PRTSC or PRTSCN). Then open Windows Paint and paste the image. Crop out the PowerPoint part of the image, leaving only the picture. Save as .PNG and replace the image in your PowerPoint.

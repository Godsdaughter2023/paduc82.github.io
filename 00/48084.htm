I have spent a long time with this issue and have worked out a way which will help users who have a built-in graphics but still want to use their Nvidia card. However, it means that GeForce will not be on when playing your game, meaning you won't have customised settings.

  1. Disable your graphics driver (Search "computer management" and open it. On the left sidebar, you will see "Device manager". Go to that. Under display adapters, disable the Nvidia driver

  3. Re-enable it
  4. Start Minecraft

This works because Nvidia GeForce only starts up if the graphics card is enabled. Since it's disabled, it will not start. The game then launches fine. 

However, you would have to diasble it before restarting / shutting down you device because Nvidia GeForce will start if the driver is enabled.

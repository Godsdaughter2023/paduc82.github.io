sreejith_n say:

Hello Quinton,

You may want to re-think your setup.

So, what happens is if you use Office 365 for other platforms in your case Skype for Business (NOT for Yammer) and if you add your Yammer domain to Office 365, Office 365 automatically will map your Yammer Basic and will make it to Yammer Enterprise. Which for some reasons are not communicated to the customers.

So for you it will be Yammer Basic, however in our systems it will show as Yammer Enterprise. And the moment your Office 365 expires your Yammer too will expire cause it has been mapped in Office 365.

Now, you won't be able to manage users from AAD for Yammer cause you have not provided them a valid O365 license for Yammer. Also Yammer SSO has been deprecated. Below article can give you a better understanding. Do read the section of Enforce Office 365 identity.

https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Plan-for-Yammer-SSO-and-DSync-deprecation-847366a4-30c8-49d1-94cc-9d2452f6d43a?ui=en-US&rs=en-US&ad=US&fromAR=1

So either you can remove the domain form Office 365 and use Yammer as a Basic Free Service or upgrade it to Yammer Enterprise and achieve your goal of managing users from AAD. 

And Yes you can authenticate against AAD for non Microsoft products, however I do not have any expertise over that :( 

Regards

Sreejith

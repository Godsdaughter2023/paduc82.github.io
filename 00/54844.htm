And more importantly will it work?

a. user say:

Hi Thomas,

Thank you for posting in Microsoft Community.

1) Are you trying to create a system image backup of Windows 7 or Windows 10?

Ideally it should work to take the system image backup from Windows 7 backup app. You can perform these below steps to take system image backup and restore.

1. Open Control Panel and go to File History.

2. At the bottom of the left pane, you should see a link to System Image Backup, under "See also." Click this link.

3. The System Image Backup utility will open. Pick a place to save your system image backup (on a hard disk, on one or more DVDs, or on a network location), and click Next. Confirm your settings and click Start backup.

4) To use your system image to restore your PC, open up the new Windows 10 Settings menu and go to Update & recovery. Under Recovery, find the Advanced startup section, and click Restart now. When your PC restarts, go to Troubleshoot, Advanced Options, and then choose System image recovery.

Hope it helps. Please reply to us with the status of the issue.

Thanks.

You have a simple choice to make on 1 of your 2 options.  Either choice is going to burn up a W7 license.

On the dual boot, on July 29th, you can quit Insiders and W10 RTM will activate and your W7 will become toast.  You are now a W10 activated use with no W7 license. 

Since you say that you have W7 on a SDD and Reserved for W10, then it  appears to be a valid license.  If you take this route, the W10 RTM is not being released on July 29th to non-Insiders.  It would appear that the W7 on SDD would activate when W10 RTM is released to the masses.

If you have just 1 W7 license, then I doubt that they are both activated now.  Your choice.  There is no free ride here.  It is 1 W10 license in and 1 W7 (or W8.1) license out.

This might be a better plan since you really want to end up with W10 on the SDD.  Take the SDD and d/l 10162.  This should then make the SDD eligible for W10 RTM on July 29th.  On the dual boot, just leave alone and do nothing.  It can continue in Insiders if you wish but it will dead end.  I doubt your W7 on the dual boot will be activated unless you have 2 licenses.

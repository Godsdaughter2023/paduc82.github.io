Good news!  Alaskachu has figured out a solution to our problem.  I tried it today and I now have gestures.  Here is his fix:

Download the elan driver from Samsung.  You want version 2.8.0.0 from the QX411 page.  You are looking for "Touchpad (Driver) ver.2.8.0.0 - Windows 7(32bit,64bit) (MULTI LANGUAGE, 140.18 MB)"

Download the Microsoft tool to prevent driver updates from here.

Run it and tell it to not install the two pending elan drivers from windows update.

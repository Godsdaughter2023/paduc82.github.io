sanmarz say: The same thing happened to me -I exported my Contacts from Outlook 2003 to  csv file, then I imported them to Outlook 2013.  My People (Contacts) listed the names, but they do not show up in my Address Book when composing a new email.  I found the answer on another web site which I can't find to give credit to the person.  He found that when the Contacts were imported, all the Contact information was imported except the Email Address field was not passed across to Outlook 2013.  When you import the Contacts into Outlook 2013, select "Map Custom Fields" and then drag the Email Address field on the left to Email Address field on the right (Outlook 2013).  Then, complete the import.  Now, your email addresses from your Contacts will show up in your Address Book.

 Important - You should delete all your Contacts prior to re-importing your Contacts as described above, otherwise, you will have duplicate Contacts for each and every one of your Contacts.  It will be more difficult to delete each duplicate one-by-one which I ended up having to do.

rich007a say:

I know, it's a pain.

Your question implies that you want all your pasted slides to have the same Custom Layout applied.   You could simply copy the slides from the Thumbnail pane or Slide Sorter into the new presentation and select the "Keep source formatting" option.  Then, in the Slide Sorter, select all your pasted slides and do Home tab > Slides group > Layout... and apply the required Layout (from the "good" Master) to all the new slides in one go.

If the Slide Master that was carried across was not "preserved" in the source file, it will be automatically deleted, along with its child Layouts.  If it is preserved, you have to go into the Slide Master view and delete the bad Slide Master, and all the bad Layouts will be blow away with it.

But if it's a solution you need that all users will use, I'm afraid it's down to good old fashioned user training :o(

 

One last option.  In 2007 you can do Design tab > Themes group > then click the down arrow in the bottom right corner of the themes gallery (to expand it), then do Browse for themes.  You can then apply your new template to the OLD presentations...  but for some reason with our templates this causes PowerPoint 2007 to crash and burn so I tend to keep this functionality a secret!

rich michaels - mvp (greatcirclelearning.com) say:

From the character viewer you need to drag what you've selected to your document. Then leaving the fraction you dragged in highlighted/selected, open the AutoCorrect dialog. (Tools > AutoCorrect ...)

The selected item will appear in the "With" box and you type in the "Replace" box the string you will use for correcting to the new fraction.

Before clicking OK make sure the checkbox to" Replace text as you type" is marked.

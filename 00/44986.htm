the reason it does not play thru the rear speakers on 5.1 is that the song does not have any rear channels set to it.

you will notice when your playing a game in 5.1 you will get most of your sound from the front 3 speakers - when you have sound behind you your back 2 speakers will produce sound depending on where its coming from.

with the settings set to Digital Stereo - it will play thru all speakers at a certain level - usually all speakers the same from each.

you should be able to leave the XBOX set to 5.1 - and on your Panasonic Receiver change the sound setting from 5.1 to one of the other settings- just switch thru them for the best option for when you have music playing.

then when you game / watch movie - switch the receiver back to 5.1

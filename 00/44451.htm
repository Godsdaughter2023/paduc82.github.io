HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\.exe

HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\exefile

f.     With exefileselected, right-click (Default) and click Modify…

g.    Change the Value data: to "%1" %*

h.    Browse to and then click on the following registry key:

KEY_CLASSES_ROOT\exefile\shell\open

i.      With openselected, right-click (Default) and click Modify…

j.     Change the Value data: to "%1" %*

k.    Close the Registry Editor and restart your PC

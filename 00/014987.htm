Sorry, I misunderstood your mention that the rogue picture is "in the document elsewhere".

I assume the rogue doesn't appear in the Print Preview before you click the Print button. If it does appear there, the picture could be anchored in a header or footer, as a watermark would be.

So if I understand correctly, the hard disk is a stand-alone device connected to the network with a LAN cable (e.g. to a switch or a router).

 

Then you would need to investigate which options your "mobile LAN disk" offers.

 

In general, if your E72 is to act as a client, the HD has to act as a server (e.g. one or more of: webdav, http, ftp, smb).

 

Once you figure out which protocols your HD offers, you will know what sort of client(s) you need to run on your phone.

If SMB, you'd need SymSMB (or SymNC or whatever it's called)

If FTP, you need an FTP client.

etc.

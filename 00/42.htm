On Windows, "Office 365 ???" installs in a separate "virtual computer" which isolates it completely from previously installed copies of Office. Unlike the older "traditional" installations which by default do uninstall your older version. Office 365 does not uninstall existing copies of Office.  Sorry, I do not know the specific details of how it is installed on a Mac.

On Windows only sometimes do you have to turn off Antivirus, and only some specific products (sorry I don't know which ones). Usually you would leave the AV program running until you have a specific problem.

I do know for sure, "re-downloading" / installing / activating on the SAME computer will NOT "use-up" another installation count.  The activation system is supposed to be smart enough to recognize it is on the same computer and to re-use the count.  There have been reported problems where the activation system did NOT reuse the count.  But that is still NOT a problem.  You can identify the problem in the MYACCOUNTS page. It will show 2 activations for the same computer ID.  Office 365 has introduced the ability for the "account owner" to control their allocation of 5 concurrent activations. The account owner can "deactivate" these errant counts.

First of all, there is no way this is a bug, and in any case it is far too early in your investigation to jump to that conclusion. This is how it works.

Where you say "It looks like when you..." the first part of your hypothesis is wrong because MSP does not use max units to calculate work, and you are correct where you are sure that it should not. Although you say that you start with the same starting point in each case, it is clear that you did not start with a duration of 1.5 days and no resource assignment and no work in case 3. Your current settings are task type = fixed units and effort driven = off, same as mine, and that's the default for MSP2010, and I got the same result as you did.

MSP has three task types and effort driven can be on or off for two of them and on only for one of them, making 5 possible responses to a change in duration or a change in work or a change in resource units.

Max units is just a set point to decide if a resource is over-allocated.

The reason is that later when you are leveling you will have more control, more options etc.

luee say:

downloading contacts to W10 lumia 640

a. user say:

Lost all contacts in update from W8 to W10. Found previous android but I am having difficulty putting contacts on W10 through bluetooth.

Hi, luee.

We're glad to help you back on track. To transfer the contacts from your Android phone to Windows phone, please follow the steps below:

  1. Connect your Android phone and Windows phone to a Wi-Fi network.
  2. Sign in your Gmail/Microsoft Account in your Android phone. Go to Settings > Account > Google.
  3. Click your account and check Sync Contacts.
  4. In your Windows phone, go to Settings > Accounts > Email & app accounts> Add an account and choose Google.
  5. When prompt, choose Allow.
  6. Please make sure that the Sync option is On. Wait a few minutes to sync your contacts.

Please let us know if you needs more assistance and we'll be glad to help.

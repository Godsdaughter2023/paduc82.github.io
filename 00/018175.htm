danida_u say:

Hi

Skype deeply relies on the connection used by the consumer. There is a difference between calls covered by calling subscriptions and Skype-to-Skype calls.

Although both need to be connected to the Internet, calls covered by subscriptions are for non-Skype users, meaning they do not have Skype accounts and you'll be calling their mobile and/or landline number (depending on your subscription) directly from your Skype application. The number you will call will not be required to have Internet access because the call will go through as a normal phone call while Skype-to-Skype calls are calls made from one Skype account to another Skype account. These calls are free anywhere in the world.

  • Skype to Skype calls are free anywhere in the world

It would appear that your Building Blocks.dotx template may be missing or damaged. Word will rebuild the file.

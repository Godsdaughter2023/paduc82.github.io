jeeped say:

,,, Lately thought, I have been getting the formula displayed (ie:='2010-oct'!A147) rather than the actual value?!


Here are a few possibilities:
  1. You could be in Display Formulas mode rather than Display Values mode.Tap Ctrl+` to toggle between the two display modes. The ` or accent grave is the lower case tilde just to the left of the 1 on a standard US keyboard.
  2. You could have a prefixed or suffixed <space> which is forcing the formula to be interpreted as Text. Manually edit the cell's content to remove any leading and/or trailing spaces from the formula.
  3. The cell's format could have been inadvertently set as Text. Change it to General then press F2 then Enter↵ to re-evaluate the cell's contents.

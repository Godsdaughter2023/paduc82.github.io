file as a 'recipe' for a video)

For uploading and sharing you need to save the project in the
.WMV movie format.

(Publish to YouTube)

If the file size of your .wmv video clips exceed the limitations
of your email provider...it may be worth a try to upload your
video to a site like the following and send your recipients the
download link:

"SendSpace"
http://www.sendspace.com/

It's very simple...on the SendSpace site the following
steps are all you have to do.

Left click the Browse button and it will open a window
that allows you to access the files on your hard drive.

Navigate to the folder your video is in and select it.

Left click the Upload button.

When the Download Link appears...left click the Copy
button and then right click/paste the link into an email
message to your recipients.

Recipients may download the video file as follows.

Left click link in the email message.

When the SendSpace site loads.

***Scroll down slightly and left click the blue text***

"Click here to start download from sendspace".

Save the file to your desktop and view in Windows Media Player.

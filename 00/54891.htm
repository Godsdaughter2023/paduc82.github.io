cmdrkeene say:
It could be the ease of access center.  If you type mouse into your Start menu, then choose Change how the mouse works, you'll be presented with the basic cursors (plain white, no effect).  These override the choices in the mouse control panel.

However, if you have selected the Aero cursors from the mouse control panel and applied it, and the cursor doesn't change accordingly, it's actually most likely that the aero cursor files were deleted.  Why?  Because they are raw images, not an effect being applied on top of the standard pointer. You have to cancel the reservation and reserve Windows 10 again if you want it to a different email address.

Hi Utilitarian,

 

You may try the following steps and check.

Step 1:

Make sure that you don't have the mouse pointer set to hide when you are typing.

a. Open Mouse by clicking the Start button, clicking Control Panel, clicking Hardware, and then clicking Mouse.

b. Click the Pointer Options tab, and uncheck the Hide pointer while typing box.

 

Step 2:

You may try to uninstall the touchpad drivers and try installing the latest drivers from the manufacturer's website and check.

a. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

b. Double-click System, click Hardware, and then click Device Manager.

c. Click the PLUS SIGN to expand the Device

d. Double-click the device driver.

e. On the Driver tab, click on Uninstall

f. Restart the computer and check.

alancitoxv say:
I can't even find out how to change the title of a Notebook or if it's even possible to do that.

As far as I can tell, the title/name of a Notebook can currently only be changed via OneDrive — not OneNote for Mac. I gleaned the following from various Windows-related documentation and it has worked for me. But it's unofficial, so use at your own risk...

1. In OneNote for Mac: File > Close This Notebook.

This removes the Notebook's contents from the cache on your Mac's drive. The master copy of that Notebook will remain unaffected on OneDrive.

2. Sign-in to OneNote Online via:

https://www.onenote.com/notebooks

3. Click on: Manage and Delete Notebooks.

This takes you to OneDrive — which you could have gone to directly via:

https://onedrive.live.com/?gologin=1

(I just wanted to show that OneDrive is the place to manage Notebooks.)

4. In OneDrive, open the Documents folder which contains your Notebooks.

5. Right-click a Notebook and choose Rename.

6. In OneNote for Mac: File > Open Notebook...

This syncs your renamed Notebook from OneDrive back to the cache on your Mac's drive.

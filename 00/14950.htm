    tisky zheng msft say:

    Hi Doyle,

     

    Based on your description, I suppose you want to open Excel files in a new instance rather than open files in the same Excel window. You may follow the steps below:

     

    1. Click Start, type regedit in the Search box, and then select regedit.exe in the search results.

     

    2. Locate and select the following registry subkey:

    HKCU\Software\Microsoft\Office\16.0\Excel\Options

     

    3. On the Edit menu, point to New, and then select DWORD Value.

     

    4. Enter DisableMergeInstance, and then press Enter.

     

    5. In the Details pane, press and hold (or right-click) Modify.

     

    6. In the Value data box, enter 1, and then click OK.

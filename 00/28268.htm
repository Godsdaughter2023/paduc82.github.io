Do a search for Application.Screenupdating and Application.Calculation (the latter probably isn't affecting you, but I tend to use both together)

Top of Sub:

Application.Screenupdating=False

Application.Calculation=xlCalculationManual

Then reset them at the end of your sub

Application.Screenupdating=True

Application.Calculation=xlCalculationAutomatic

Warning: If your sub errors out, screenupdating never gets turned back on, and you will think that Excel crashed. Best option is to avoid programming that can error out, second best is to trap errors and turn screenupdating back on, third option is to write a quick macro in Word that reaches out to the Excel instance and turns screenupdating back on. Otherwise, you have to use task manager to kill excel and then re-open it, which is a drag and you might lose work.

A. Avoiding Spam.

1. Make sure you have adequate personal Security Software that is regularly updated.
2. Stop posting your e-mail address on a public forum or website.

It is estimated that 95% of all junk e-mail is caused by a person publicly disclosing his or her e-mail address.
3. Avoid: Popular newsgroups - robots often scour these lists for addresses.
Chat rooms like Yahoo Chat etc- spammers or spambots are actively mining in popular chat rooms.
4. Use disposable addresses to identify and shake off sources of spam.
Suggest you have one main account, and be wary who you give it to - even your friends.

All they need to do is send you an innocent e-card to get you back on a spam mailing list.

B.To report spam or unsolicited emails:

In Outlook, select the offending message and click the Junk button to report the message in one action; or you may click the arrow next to the Junk button and select:

  • Junk for routine junk email.
  • Phishing scam for email that is trying to trick you into giving out sensitive personal information like passwords, bank accounts, or your Social Security number.

For non- Outlook or Hotmail customers should address an email to report_spam@outlook.com. Attach a copy of the spam email to this email and send it. If we do not receive a copy of the email as an attachment our ability to take action against the spamming account is severely impacted.

jay freedman say:

If the cursor is in an otherwise empty paragraph when you start to enter an equation, it will be in display mode. If there is even one other character -- a tab, a space, a letter, anything other than the invisible paragraph mark -- then the equation will start in inline mode.

It helps to click the ¶ button on the Home tab (or press Ctrl+Shift+8) to display nonprinting characters, so you can see spaces and tabs.

You have two email accounts.

A Roadrunner account and an Outlook.com/Microsoft account.

The Outlook.com account means that you have OneDrive, Calendar, People, ............

Outlook 2016 recognized your Roadrunner address as a Roadrunner account.

It did not recognize the Roadrunner address as an Outlook.com account.

Add an @outlook.com address to your Outlook.com account.

Setup the outlook.com account in Outlook 2016 using the @outlook.com address.

Adding an @outlook.com address to your existing Outlook.com account does not change the primary address or From address of the Outlook.com account.

If you sent an email using the @outlook.com address in Outlook 2016, the Roadrunner address would be seen by the recipient.

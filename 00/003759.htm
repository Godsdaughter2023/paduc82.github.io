Check out the COUNTIFS function. You can either add an additional condition of onlying counting things less than 2 (aka, 200%, but this assume you always work more than 2 hrs), or you cuold use a helper row marked with x's on the columns you want to count, and have that become the critiera.

=COUNTIFS(C3:H3,">=100%",C3:H3,"<2")

=COUNTIFS(C3:H3,">=100%",C1:H1,"X")

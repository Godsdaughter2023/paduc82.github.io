You will have to use a helper column for this. Assuming your data starts in row 2, put following formula in B2 and drag down -

=--RIGHT(SUBSTITUTE(A2," ",REPT(" ",LEN(A2))),LEN(A2))

Now, select columns A& B and sort on second column.

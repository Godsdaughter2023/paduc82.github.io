    Sequence for the maze levers in Buried in 5 tries or less - Microsoft Community

    team klepek say:

    Sequence for the maze levers in Buried in 5 tries or less

    Last night playing with a group on Buried, we attempted the Easter egg, but got knocked out of the game by a disconnect. Anyway, we were stuck on the maze for quite a while. Here are some things to note for anyone trying the egg, and helpful hints we didn't know at first.

    .

    The levers do not spark until all 4 are pulled. If you pulled a lever in the correct sequence, it will spark after the last lever is pulled. If you pulled it out of sequence it will not. So it is important to have all 4 players in the maze looking at the levers. We started out running as a group to find them and then nothing happened as some sparked but we were not there, only at the last lever.

    .

    Second, systematically, you can get the right combination in 5 tries or less. Pull the first combination of levers and rotate the colors for each consecutive pull.

    Red, Blue, Green Yellow

    Blue, Green, Yellow, Red

    Green, Yellow, Red, Blue

    Yellow, Red, Blue, Green

    .

    If you are watching all 4 levers each time, all 4 will have sparked at some point during the last 4 pulls. Remember which order the lever was pulled when it sparked after you pulled the last lever and write it down.

    .

    Spelling this out, it means, If you pulled Red, Blue, Green, then Yellow and the green sparked at the end, then Green is in the correct location but the rest are not. So try again after exiting the maze and coming back through the ghost house. Then keep green at the third pull, and try another combination like Blue Yellow, Green, Red. If both Red and Green spark then it leaves you with only two to figure out, So switch Blue and Yellow and you have your order.

    .

    Most of the tutorials online are not helpful. Understanding that the switches spark at the end is much better than trying the 26 combinations possible and hoping you didn't forget one.

    .

    As well, at points a lever will not spawn, and you need to go back through the house again to get it to respawn in, sometimes it takes more than one try. Yellow is most common for this but it also happened once with green.

    .

    Last, buy some Galvaknuckles and just hit the witches or ghosts with them. Saves a ton of ammo and you are never caught in a reload. Just bank the points first.

    .

    Hope this helps. Now to take on the targets.....

So when you are in a call, if you hold the phone next to your face the proximity senor senses that you are close to your face and turns off the screen (to save battery power).

 

What is probably happening since you indicated that it is intermittent is that you are somehow covering the proximity sensor with your hand (fingers).  Try this to see if it resolves the issue and then you will just have to learn to NOT touch that sensor.  Lay the phone flat on a table during a call.  The screen should appear and then you can touch the screen button to show the dial pad.  Then you can press the numbers.

 

Some folks have put a screen protector on their device and that can have an effect on the sensor.  With the new gorilla glass screens, I don't recommend a screen protector any more.

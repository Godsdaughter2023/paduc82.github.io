xbf diego m say:

Hi Bigsease30,

Thank you for posting on the Xbox forums, I'd be happy to answer that.

Unfortunately, you cannot merge or transfer a gamertag with it's contents to another email address. If you have full access to the older Hotmail email with the gamertag (ex. you know the password), then you simply just need to add that account to your new Xbox. You will need to manage your Xbox profile using the older Microsoft Account email. You can continue using your primary email for other services, but your Xbox profile is tied to that older email and can only be changed to another email as a sign in option using an email that is not currently in use by an active Microsoft Account. Keep in mind that removing any Microsoft related emails (alias) from a Microsoft account and changing to another one, will permanently delete the removed email account and any associated messages or associations.

If your primary email is not set up to a Microsoft account, then technically you can add that email as an alternate email alias to your older Microsoft account and set it as the primary sign in option. You could then choose to leave the older email on the account as a secondary sign in option or remove it and permanently delete the older email account. If your primary email is already being used by a Microsoft account, then this will not work and since they're both Hotmail, your primary email more than likely already has a Microsoft account.

I hope this answers your question!

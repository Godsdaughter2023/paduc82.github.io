Here is some clarification about these two services:

    There is no way to download any of the data that is stored in My Phone to a WP7 device

    There is no way to export any of the data that is stored in My Phone to Outlook or to the new Windows Live Phone service

    • There is no way to backup text messages on a WP7 device at this time,
    • There is no way to restore text messages that are backed up on a My Phone account to a WP7 device
    The Windows Live Phone service does provide some services that are similar to My Phone's Premium services (such as Find my Lost Phone/ Lock my Phone / Wipe my Phone)

Hello EmSos

There are two likely scenarios

#1 - Your account has been compromised and someone is sending emails from your account in which case those emails would be in your <Sent Items> folder. If that's the case, you should certainly change your password and for better protection, enable 2 factor authentication. If I were to guess, this is not likely the case.

#2 - A more probable scenario is that your email address has been hijacked which means that spammers are sending our emails with a spoofed <From> email address (as in yours) and you're getting bounce backs.

In this scenario, there's nothing you can do about it other then wait it out. The spammers will move on to using another "hijacked" email address.

Windows Live Mail does not block senders - Microsoft Community

slacks35 say:

Windows Live Mail does not block senders

I have Microsoft 7 Ultimate. I use Windows Live Mail. I cannot block senders.

christian_n. say:

Hi slacks35,

 

I'm happy to assist you with your concern that you could not block senders using your Windows Live Mail.

 

Please be informed that for setting up your safe and blocked senders list you need to log in to your webmail account and enble it from there.

 

For more information about the issue, please refer to the solution article below.

 

Check your Blocked sender list
1. From the Hotmail Options screen under Preventing junk email, click Safe and blocked senders.
2. Click Blocked senders.
3. In the Blocked senders list, select the name or domain (The part of an e-mail address that follows the @ sign, such as msn or Hotmail.) that you now want to receive mail from, and then click << Remove from list.
4. Click on Options in the upper left to go back to the Hotmail Options screen.
 
Add the email address to your Safe senders list
1. From the Hotmail Options screens under Preventing junk email, click Safe and blocked senders.
2. Click Safe Senders.
3. In the text box, type in the email address or domain you’d like to always receive email from, and then click Add to list >>. 
4. Click on Options in the upper left to go back to the Hotmail Options screen.

 

I hope this will help you with your concern and feel free to post back.

Hi there Mark! I'm a microsoft community member here to help. Currently there isn't an easy way to make every value in the document round, but you could try this: Click a blank cell C2 and input the formula: =ROUND (A2, 2),

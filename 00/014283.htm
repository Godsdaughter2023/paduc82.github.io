Contacts from my address book were added to Skype, however I'd like to delete several contacts and there's no option for that.  I am using a Note 8.  I saw someone had this same inquiry in July and a response said it should be fixed soon.  That was over 5 months ago.
jannine_p say:

Hello

To delete a contact on the new Skype application:

  • Tap the Contacts icon located on the upper right side corner beside the Calls tab.
  • Locate the name of the contact you wish to delete from your list.
  • Press and hold the name of the contact then choose View Profile.
  • Scroll down and tap Delete contact.

Please be advised that we can only delete Skype contacts. Other contacts sync from your phonebook cannot be deleted from the Skype application unless, you delete the contact itself from your phonebook.

If the above information work, please do not hesitate to reply to this post.

Thank you!

jannine_p say:

Yes, that's correct. That's the only way we can remove an address book contact from Skype, is to remove them directly from your address book. We'll take note of your feedback and hopefully, in the future, this will be an added feature of Skype.

Should you have other questions or concerns, please feel free to post again in our Community forum.

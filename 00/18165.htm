There are no version upgrade licenses for Office 2010, Microsoft discontinued the version upgrade license when it released Office 2010.

Office 2010 still has three license types, designated FPP, PKC and OEM.

 

FPP licenses permit you to concurrently activate the Office Home and Business 2010 and Office Professional 2010 product on two of your computers, and permits three concurrent activations of the Office Home and Student 2010 product; FPP licenses permit you to move the allowed activations from computer to computer to accommodate, for example, an old computer being replaced by a new computer.

 

PKC and OEM licenses only permit one activation at a time of the product on one computer, and are further limited in that the activation is not permitted to be moved to any other computer.  Essentially, the license lives and dies with the computer on which the license is first activated.

By default, when you print from Final Showing Markup view, the document is printed reduced to allow marginal space for the balloons.

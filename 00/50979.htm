  1. There are lots of ways to re-install.  Without doubt your hard disk will have a recovery partition which can be used to return your computer to its as-supplied (factory) state.  You can use that unless your hard drive fails and has to be replaced.  That will re-install Windows 10and any software Dell bundled with your computer.  Your recovery disc will probably do the same and could be used in the event of a hard drive failure.  Or you can create recovery media as advised.

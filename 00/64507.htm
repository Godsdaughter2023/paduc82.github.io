obsessive power say:

Due to the copy protection for the game, you can't just copy the install from your old PC to your new one and expect it to work.  Halo 2 on the PC still requires the original game disc to be verified from time to time.

Since your computer doesn't have a DVD drive, you have two possible options:

1. An external DVD drive connected to your computer via USB.
2. A DVD drive on another PC, shared through your local network to your computer.

With either of those above options you would need to install the game to your computer as per the instructions in the game manual.  It probably won't work with your current method.

thrashzone say:

If you have never opened office picture manager you will need to do this,

Click the start button,

Then click on all programs listed at the bottom,

Scroll down until you see your office folder, click on it and another folder called office tools,

Office picture manager will be listed inside this folder along with all of the other office features.

Right click it to open,

Also to make it your default you will need to,

Click the start button,

Then click on the default programs listing,

Then click on associate a file type and protocol with a program,

This page will have all file types listed here,

If you know what raw file type your camera produces, along with jpg/ jpeg/.....click those boxes one at a time, and select change,

Browse for the office picture manager feature, it should show up in recommended programs you can use.

For future reference, to clear the calendars cache do the following (it is very easy):

- Launch Outlook.
- In the Navigation Pane, click Calendar.
- At the top of the Calendar Navigation Pane, right-click (or ctrl-click) Calendar, then select Folder Properties. A Folder Properties: Calendar dialog box will open.
- If the General tab is not already selected, click General.
- Under Empty Cache, click Empty.
- Quit Outlook.
- Restart Outlook.

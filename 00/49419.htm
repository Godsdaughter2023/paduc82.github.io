2. In the command prompt window, copy/paste the following command.

 

powercfg -energy -output %userprofile%\desktop\energy-report.html

 

Press ENTER.

3. Go to the Desktop and double click the file to open.

Design Capacity xxxxx

andre da costa say:

The retail and OEM setup disc are identifical, it is the licensing that differentiates them.

OEM - tied to the first computer it is installed on and cannot be moved to another computer.

Retail - can be transferred to another computer as long as it is removed from the current computer.

To determine if you have an OEM license installed.

Click Start, right click Computer

Click Properties

Scroll down to Windows Activation

Where your Product ID is displayed, look at the PID It will look something like this

XXXXX-XXX-XXXXXXX-XXXXX, the portion you are interested in is the three X's section, if it says OEM, then its an OEM license. Example XXXXX-OEM-XXXXXXX-XXXXX

By default, Excel uses the unit specified in your Windows settings (metric vs US).

You can deviate from this in Excel:

  • Select File > Options.
  • Click Advanced in the navigation pane on the left.
  • Scroll down to the Display section.
  • Select your preference in the 'Ruler units' dropdown.

  • Click OK.

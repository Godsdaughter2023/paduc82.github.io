timliu_msft1261 say:

Hi Robert,

Are the unlicensed users who don't have SharePoint Online license? If yes, they may be able to access the SharePoint Online team site when you share the site to the users, no matter they have the SharePoint Online license. Some groups such as Everyone group already include all the users in your tenant.

So, in this situation, we need to separate the licensed users and unlicensed users. Make sure only the licensed users have the permission for the team site, libraries and items (avoid to share items to a group include all the users).

Moreover, to get a list of all the users and groups underneath the site, click setting gear> Site settings > People and groups > check the URL of the page, change "MembershipGroupId=x" to "MembershipGroupId=0".
NOTE: some special groups in the list, such as "Everyone" or "Everyone except external users", may already include all users (internal or external) underneath your Office 365 tenant so some users won't be listed individually.

You need to anchor the logo to the First Page Header and the text box to the First Page Footer. The Drawing Tools and Text Box formatting tools allow you to specify the exact position of each object.

As far as I'm aware, the only standard Windows 7 fonts that have old-style number formats as their default are Constantia (a serif font), Candara (a sans serif font), and Corbel (also sans serif).

Calibri, Cambria, and Consolas include old-style formats, but their default (the only format you can get in Word 2007 or earlier) is the lining format. Word 2010 was the first version that offered control of whether to display old-style or lining numbers in fonts that offer both formats.

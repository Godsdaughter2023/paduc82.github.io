  • The 'Game vs. Music' volume slider has a fundamental flaw in that the sum of both ends should always be 100, but the centre position is summing two signals at "full blast" to make 200.
  • In my 4 examples above the total volume went from 200%->175%->150%>100%, hence getting cleaner and cleaner.

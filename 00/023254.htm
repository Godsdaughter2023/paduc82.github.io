amit tandon say:

To co-relate my earlier explanation:

Formula in cell U14:

=IF($P14<$O14,COUNTIF($M$14:$M$131,">="&$O14)+COUNTIF($M$14:$M$131,"<="&$P14),COUNTIFS($M$14:$M$131,">="&$O14,$M$14:$M$131,"<="&$P14))

The IF statement (IF($P14<$O14) has been used to determine if the second time is less than the first time in which case it indicates that the day has changed.

That is why we use the + to add the 2 times: first time to 12 midnight COUNTIF($M$14:$M$131,">="&$O14) + midnight to second time COUNTIF($M$14:$M$131,"<="&$P14).

If the second time is greater than the first time we simply use COUNTIFS COUNTIFS($M$14:$M$131,">="&$O14,$M$14:$M$131,"<="&$P14)

Hope the above explains clearly.

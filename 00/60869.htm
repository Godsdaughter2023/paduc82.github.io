davem121 say:

If you can run your laptop on battery, your battery is OK. If you pull out the battery and plug in the charger and your laptop works, your charger is OK.

Try both of the above to see if either is not working . . .

If your laptop is working in both cases, then it is a driver issue!

  1. Go to Control Panel - Device Manager (Accessible by right clicking your Start Menu
  2. Expand the Batteries section and uninstall the 'Microsoft ACPI-Compliant Control Method Battery' driver
  3. Reboot and the driver should reload, check to see if your battery is now charging

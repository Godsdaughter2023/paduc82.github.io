Two issues here
1. The key should work for both 32 bit and 64 bit installations - and I understand that the 64 bit and 32 bit installers are both on the DVD. Home and Business may be activated only 2 PCs. The product should activate automatically - check Word > File > Help, which will show the activation status. You may not be able to activate if you have a pre-installed trial of Office 2007 on the PC. Remove that first.
2. Unless you have a need to work with very complex Excel worksheets, you would be better with the 32 bit version. The 64 bit version creates more problems than it solves. In particular there will be compatibility problems with a raft of third party software add-ins.

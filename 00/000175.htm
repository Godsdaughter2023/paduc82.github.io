In order to re-install and re-activate Office Home and Student 2013 you will have to login to your Microsoft Account with your Microsoft Account credentials and then download and install Office 2013. Let’s follow these steps.

Login to your Microsoft Account with your Microsoft Account Credentials and check if your Office 2013 edition reflects on the My Account page. The URL for the Microsoft Account webpage is: http://office.com/myaccount

If your My Account page reflects your version of Office 2013 Office Home and Student 2013 - then follow these steps to remove the remnants of the previous installation of Office and re-install Office Home and Student 2013. Un-installing the remnants of Office is a necessary as this resolves most Office installation issues.

  • In order to un-install Office remnants from your computer, you will have to install and run the Office 2013 FIXIT tool. Please run the tool even if you do not have Office 2013 showing in your list of programs at present – as there may be remnants of the application on your computer.
  • Download / Install and run the Office 2013 FIXIT tool from the following link: http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2739501

Once you have completed running the FIXIT follow these steps to delete/rename the Office 15 folder.

  • From Windows Explorer please navigate to the following location.
  • C:\Propgram Files\ or C:\Program Files (x86)\
  • Make sure to delete the Microsoft Office 15 folder. In case you are able to delete the folder then right-click on it and click on Rename. Rename the folder as Microsoft Office 15 old
  • Once you have completed running the FIXIT be sure to restart your computer.
  • Now try logging into the My Account page and re-install Office Home and Student 2013.

Check if you were able to download and install Office Home and Student 2013 successfully.

In case the above steps do not work then please follow the steps from this link to perform general troubleshooting for Office 2013.

http://support.microsoft.com/kb/2822317

Try the above steps and please let us know if you need further assistance.

Thank you.

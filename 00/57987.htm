Hi LinnLiljedahl,

To change an administrator account to a standard account, please follow the steps below:

  1. Press the Windows and I keys simultaneously to open Settings.
  2. Click on Accounts.
  3. Click on Family & other people.
  4. Click on the profile that you wish to change to a standard account and select Change account type.
  5. Under Account type, select Administrator 
  6. Click OK.

In regard to your question on how to duplicate the settings of the accounts, there is no option to "copy and paste" the setting from one account to another. You need to manually change the settings for both profiles to be identical. For the apps, you don't need to install them on each of the user profiles, the apps will be available for both profiles.

Are videogames art? This question is often posed to and by gamers. Society has always  typified art as a recorded emotional reaction. This definition is best reflected in painting, poetry and live music. Other works -- paintings and plays; poems and movies -- can stand alone. The pages of shakespeare do not disappear when we look away, nor do the words of Yates, or the scenes of Scorsese. It is only in video games that the narrative is incomplete without the player, who is also the audience.

Windows Update: Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up Wi-Fi wireless password? - Microsoft Community

geoffmarshall19 say:

How do I set up Wi-Fi wireless password?

Hi,

Just want to use my mobile to make telephone calls through my laptop.

anil kumar b say:

Hi,

 

 

To set up Wi-Fi wireless password, refer the steps.

 

 

a)      Open up a web browser and type in your routers address and then click enter.

 

b)      Once you hit enter you should get a connection box that will request a user name and password from you. Use the default as stated above unless you have changed this in the past.

 

c)      Once you get in the setup click on the WIRELESS TAB and then click on WIRELESS SECURITY. Choose a passphrase anything will do. Then click GENERATE, and will you have your keys. Make sure you save your settings. Save them in safe place.

 

d)      Now to check if your password keys work. Fire up your laptop and right click on the wireless icon, located on the bottom right hand corner of your task bar. Then Click VIEW AVAILABLE WIRELESS NETWORKS.

 

e)       If you did it right you should see your network show up with a lock stating that it is a SECURITY ENABLED WIRELESS NETWORK.

 

f)      If you click on the network a WIRELESS CONNECTION PROMPT will appear.
This is where you type in the keys you've generated. Confirm your key and that's it you'll be connected each and every time no retyping the key in over and over again. If you have issues email me and I am more than willing to help.

 

 

Hope it helps.

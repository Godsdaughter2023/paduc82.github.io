How did you enter your milestone? If you entered a date directly into the Start or FInish fields, you automatically set a constraint. bernie deitrick say:

Basically, you show 4 cells that need to add up to the value in F.  That is 4 degrees of freedom, which means that you could have uncountable solutions that work, and need to reduce the degrees of freedom to 1: which mean entering 3 constant values.

If you are always entering values into B, C, and D, then in E2 you could use the formula

=IF(SUM(B2:D2)=F2,"",F2-SUM(B2:D2))

If you were always entering values into B and C, and needed D to make up the difference (2 constants and 1 degree of freedom), then in D2 you could use

=IF(B2+C2=F2,"",F2-B2-C2)

Note that you cannot have two such formulas on any one line.

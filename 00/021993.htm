No.  Lithuanian is not an available language in Mac Office.  The available languages are listed here:
http://mac2.microsoft.com/help/office/14/en-us/word/item/6474a4a6-9d3e-4bde-a255-fb9286ef5d10

We have no idea when or if other languages will appear.  My guess would be "not in the next five to ten years, if ever".  But I don't know, and Microsoft will never tell you (a public corporation in the USA cannot tell you what they will do in the future, it is against the law).

You can use Parallels to run Microsoft Office for PC on your Mac.  That has a Lithuanian version available, with a Lithuanian spell-checker.

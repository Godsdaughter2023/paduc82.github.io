Think of a Local Account as the same as an account you would have had on previous versions of Windows.

It is a user account that is not tied to your Microsoft Account and which exists just on your PC.

I use a Local Account, because I have seen too many problems from users that cannot log into their PC because of problems with their Microsoft Account

peter and christinamanousos say:

This was an invasive fix, but it did work.  After verifying my account was viable on mail.live.com (logged in, sent a test message to a different account, saw that it was indeed sent and received, then logged out of that account via the online interface), I then in Windows Live Mail 2012 deleted my non functioning account and reestablished it in Win Live Mail 2012.  After about 5 minutes it seemed to sync and access the server properly through Windows live mail 2012 -- it did not work right away though - still gave me the same errors. 

Caution - the account that was not functioning for me (not accessing the server in Win Live Mail 2012) had very little history and no sub folders in Win Live Mail 2012.  Before blowing it away, you may want to temporarily move any folders you have in Windows Live Mail 2012 for that non-functioning account to a different working account.  And then when the account becomes viable again move them back.

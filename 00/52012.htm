TouchPad makers can integrate their controls/options directly into the mouse control panel, or sometimes they put it in another entirely.  It's really all over the place because whomever built the touchpad, or the company that bundled it into the laptop hardware, gets to decide where to put the options.  Since it's not built right into the OS, we can't really tell you exactly where or how to adjust the sensitivity or special features (like what Synaptics calls PalmCheck or other accidental-touch-blocking-tricks) that your touchpad might have with it.  Best bet is to ask LG directly or consult your owner's manual.

I believe Excel does the same thing.  The real issue is how the text between the double-quotes is interpreted.

IMHO, the double-quotes should mean that the text should not be changed.  For Excel, that would mean that for "00012.0000", either it should be entered as a number with an appropriate Custom format to preserve the leading or trailing zeros, or (more likely) it should be entered as text.

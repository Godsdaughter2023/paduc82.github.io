The only way you can do what you are describing would be to manually drag the window until it is split between the 2 monitors. You would then need to manually drag each window border until it was position to fit your needs.

The keyboard shortcuts (WinKey+ left/right Arrow Keys will only snap the window to the side of each monitor.

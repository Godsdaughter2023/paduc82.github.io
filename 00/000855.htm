Hi,

I had a similar issue with my Nokia 735.

I resolved the issue by de-assembling the phone, then

1. carefully removing the camera (connected by a cable), and

2. popping out the lens cover from the inside face (the lens cover is glued to the outside face), finally

I then put a bit of "crystal clear sellotape" onto the phone back cover - 1 bit either side of the lens hole in the cover to protect the lens itself from dirt.

Worked a treat and cost nothing....

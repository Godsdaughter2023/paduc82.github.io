    A custom number format of

    0"lbs" ?/16"oz"
    gets close but includes an unwanted "/16"

    I can't think of anything better without using formulas

    teylyn say:
    Excel has the basics of that format as a built-in fraction format


    but, as Bill says, the "/16" will always show. So, just formatting the value cell won't get the desired result. 

    You can use a formula to show the desired representation in a different cell:

    =INT(A1)&" lbs "&MOD(A1,1)*16&" oz"

As long as the voltage is correct (and it should be, if it conforms to the micro-USB standard), then you should be fine.  Power supplies don't 'put out' current, they are rated to a certain maximum 'draw' from the device.  So, buying a bigger one won't necessarily make the phone charge any faster.  And no, it won't deteriorate the battery life.

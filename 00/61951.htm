If you are importing a single video, then it appears in your storyboard / timeline area as a thumbnail of the first frame.  You can expand that view slightly by using the zoom slider in the lower right of the window.  That will expand the view of the clip into a sequence of frames.  However, it won't show you different frames, just the first frame repeated.

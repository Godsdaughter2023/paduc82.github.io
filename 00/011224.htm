hal hostetler - mvp-outlook say:
An .OST file is an offline copy of her server mailbox, it is volatile, and cannot be used as a backup.  What kind of email account does she have?  If the account is Hotmail/Live mail/Outlook.com or Office 365 online, its an Exchange Server account, everything is on the server, adding her account to Outlook on a new laptop will restore everything into a new .OST file.  If the account is IMAP, all her email and folder structure is on the server and will be restored to a new .OST file.  If there were any "This Computer Only" folders, the contents are lost.  If the account is POP, you would see .PST files, not .OST files on the old hard drive.

ralf_g say:

"when I initially set up the headphones with a Bluetooth connection my laptop finds them and says "connected" then after about 5 seconds the text changes to "paired"."

That means the computer tried to connect to the headphones but the connection failed. Most likely what has happened is that on the headphone side either the pairing with the computer is no longer valid or else the headphones have already automatically connected to some other Bluetooth device that you've previously paired them with. Having looked it up, those headphones apparently can store up to 8 pairings, but you can only connect then with one device at a time. That holds true for the computer Bluetooth connections as well.

" when I highlight the headphones it will not allow me to connect or make them the default device."

It happens because there is no Bluetooth connection established between the headphones and the computer.  If you have other nearby Bluetooth devices (phone, ipod etc) turned on while you are trying to connect the headphones to the computer, turn off Bluetooth in those other devices and try to connect the headphones with the computer again.

Yours will say Sony obviously and the icon may be different.

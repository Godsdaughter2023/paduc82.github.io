a. user say:

Hello,

Thank you for posting your query in Microsoft Community.

As per your description, I understand that you wish to transfer your desktop icons from Local to Microsoft Account on the same computer.

To do so, you might need to follow the below provided steps. Also, you need to have administrative privileges on both the accounts.

  1. Login to your Microsoft Account.
  2. Press WinKey + R, type C:\Users and hit ENTER.
    (You will see both the accounts mentioned in the list of Users.)
  3. Open the local account.
  4. Go to the Desktop folder.
  5. Copy all the icons and go back to the Users folder.
  6. Open your Microsoft account.
  7. Go to the Desktop folder.
  8. Paste the contents into this folder.
    (The icons will be exported to the Desktop of Microsoft Account.)

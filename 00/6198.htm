amit tandon say:

Enter below formula as an array formula (Ctrl+Shift+Enter) in cell B2:

=SUM(--ISNUMBER(--SUBSTITUTE(IF(LEFT(A2:A10,1)="<",A2:A10),"<","",1)))

This will count precisely for cells containing ONLY ONE "<" symbol, followed by a NUMBER ONLY.

So "<7R" or "<T" or "<<5" are not counted.

Hi Scott,

An Office 365 subscription is needed to ensure that you can use the Office suite and all its features. Once you cancel your subscription, Office will not run after your next due date. Nothing to worry. It's simple, and easy to get back those free hdd space occupied by system resources, or third applications.

  1. Press Windows button to open Start menu, in the search box, type Advanced System Settings, and then click View advanced system settings from the list.
  2. Select the C drive in list and then choose System Protection tab.
  3. Click Configure.

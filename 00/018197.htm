'32-bit API declarations
Declare PtrSafe Function SHGetPathFromIDList Lib "shell32.dll" _
    Alias "SHGetPathFromIDListA" (ByVal pidl As Long, ByVal pszPath As String) As Long

Declare PtrSafe Function SHBrowseForFolder Lib "shell32.dll" _

techbuzzireland say: From the FAQ

There are two alternative ways to send a text message to multiple recipients:

Open up your Messaging and create a new message, select Start > Messaging > New Choose a message recipient by tapping the plus (+) sign at the top of the screen, once contact name was added, just tap the plus (+) again to add new contacts, repeat with the each recipients.

If you have a regular set of people you send messages, you could assign contacts as a group.Open up your People Hub and create a new group, select Start > People > New group Enter a Group name, then select Add a contact, to add another contact, select Add a contact again, after you have finished press Save.Once you have created a group, open up your Messaging and choose a group by tapping the plus (+) sign.

Note: The phone sends the message separately to each recipient in the list. Therefore, sending a message to multiple recipients may cost more than sending a message to one recipient.

Tip: Pin your most important contacts or contact groups to the start screen (To remove a Tile from Start, Tap and hold the Tile and then press Unpin at the top right corner).

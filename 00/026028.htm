brian tillman [outlook mvp 2007-2019] say:

Hotmail addresses ARE Microsoft accounts, provided they aren't aliases of another account.  Log into that account by logging off of the "primary" account and then connecting to http://mail.live.com/.  Enter the "old" Hotmail address as the username and whatever password you gave it when you last updated the security information for that account.

 

If the Hotmail address is an alias of another account, you can sign in with the alias address as the username and the primary account password as the password or you can simply use the primary address as the username.

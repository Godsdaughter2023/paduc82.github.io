Please be informed that by upgrading to Outlook.com, you are only upgrading the user interface of Hotmail to the new user interface; hence all your emails should still stay in your Inbox. Here are the possible reasons why some emails are missing from one’s account:

 

A. Messages were manually deleted either due to accidental deletion or they were moved to a folder. Please check if emails are being moved to a POP folder, and/or other personal folders that can be due to your Email rules.

It is also possible that they were deleted due to your account filter settings. Kindly check if Schedule Cleanup is set to automatically delete emails. You may refer to the link below for more information about the feature:

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/hotmail/conquergraymail/automate-your-inbox/scheduled-cleanup

B. Messages can also be deleted if you are using a mail client or a third party spam filtering software to access your account. This often happens when using the Pop3 protocol to retrieve your mail and the option Always leave a copy of the message on the server is not selected.

C. Emails got deleted due to Inactivity.
When users don't sign in for more than 365 days or within the first 10 days after signing up for an account, the system will flag it as Inactive. After an account becomes inactive, all messages, folders, and contacts are deleted. Incoming messages will be sent back to the sender as undeliverable.

Also, if you were accessing your emails on a mail client such as Windows Live Mail or through your mobile device, but didn’t log into your web account for over 365 days, you may lose emails due to inactivity in web account.

D. Account has been hacked or compromised.
You may lose emails if your account was hacked and the hacker had already deleted emails in your account.

E. Conversation threading is enabled.
If the feature Conversation threading is turned on wherein all emails with the same subject line are grouped together. You can visit the link below to know more about this feature:

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/outlook/auto-upgrade-outlook-faq

Please be informed that you can use the recover deleted or lost emails feature in Outlook.com to bring back your lost emails. However, when Outlook.com recovers missing or deleted emails, our system restores as many emails as it could regardless of its dates. There is also no guarantee that the messages that you are looking for will all be recovered. You can refer to the Solution Article below for the steps to retrieve your lost or deleted emails:

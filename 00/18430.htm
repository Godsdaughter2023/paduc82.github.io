alexsun_2012 say:

Hi Dracoman,

The emails from the sub domain info.ourdomain.edu will be exempted after you block ourdomain.com. Based on my test, when we block the domain using a spam filter policy, the SCL value will be set to 9 and the emails will be marked as spam and moved to Junk or Quanratine. However, the emails from the sub domain will not be affected as the SCL value is set to -1 to 1. Moreover, since you have several sites and servers to send emails from your domain, it is suggested you add the FQDNs or the IP addresses of the sites to the SPF record of your domain.

Please let us know if anything is unclear or you need further assistance.

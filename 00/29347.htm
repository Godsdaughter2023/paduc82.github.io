  1. Right click Start

pcr just pcr say:

Speakers normally are found under the "Audio inputs & outputs" category. However - in my case anyway - they came in with the device under "Sound, video & game controllers". So, update that one.

But if you have an "Other" category, that means something didn't install properly. Open it, & update the device under it. If it's the audio or the speakers & things go well - after reboot, "Other" will disappear, & the device(s) will go to where they should be.

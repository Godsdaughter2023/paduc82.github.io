      978976 Error: Can't obtain an IP address from your router or modem

      If you select Automatic, the Xbox 360 console tries to automatically determine your DNS settings from your router, modem, or Internet service provider. We recommend using automatic DNS settings.

      978847 Error: Your DNS server can't resolve Xbox LIVE server names

There is a file zune software uses to keep all your settings, your podcast subscriptions, etc.  It can get confused occasionally, and when that happens you typically see music refuse to sync, or getting duped on the device or phone.
It's worth seeing if this is the source of your problem.  To reset this file (which is not reset by a zune software reinstall...and that's usually a good thing) run this tool:
Zune media collection reset tool
Now, after you run this, start the software and wait a bit.  Your collection will appear empty, but the software will find it again (unless your settings are for a non-standard location) in a few seconds.
Anyway, this wipes out everything zune knows about your stuff, so unfortunately, you will have to re-do any custom settings you've made, and will have to resubscribe to your podcasts.  It will not touch any content at all.  So, any podcasts you do have downloaded will change from 'automatic' to 'in collection.'  To fix this, set the series settings for them again, then right click -> delete the episodes you want converted back to automatic.  They will redownload if the series settings say they should.
Also, your music playcounts will be wiped, so any autoplaylists that depend on playcounts will reset. Bummer...

I'll try again. merging cells is something that will almost always end up giving problems. don't do it, unmerge the cells and the problem goes away. If you must keep them you can get around your issue like this:-



Note you must do the copy after un-merging the cells.


Sub Paste_Into_Merged_Cells()
Dim r As String
r = Selection.Address
Range(r).MergeCells = False
Range("B2").Copy
Selection.Cells(1).Select
Selection.PasteSpecial Paste:=xlPasteValues, Operation:=xlNone, SkipBlanks _
     :=False, Transpose:=False
Range(r).MergeCells = True
 End Sub

No,

Chromecast works best devices that have the Chrome browser (Android, desktops) or a dedicated mobile app (Android, iOS).

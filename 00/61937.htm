This is a keyboard language issue . . .

Right click your Start Button, choose Powershell (admin)


If the @ symbol on your keyboard is above the 2, you have a US English Keyboard

Paste this commend into Powershell and hit Enter:

$1 = New-WinUserLanguageList en-US


If the " symbol on your keyboard is above the 2, you have a UK English Keyboard

Paste this commend into Powershell and hit Enter:

$1 = New-WinUserLanguageList en-GB


Then, Paste this commend into Powershell and hit Enter:

Set-WinUserLanguageList $1


Close Powershell and restart your PC

That's It!

andre da costa say:

Yes, the license is transferable, just use the product key to upgrade then use chat support to reactivate after, since it was previously in use.

Click Start > Settings > Update & security > Activation > Change the product key then enter the Windows 10 product key then click Next to Activate

Your computer will be restarted and upgraded to Pro.

Learn more:

How to Perform an Easy Upgrade in Windows 10

As for reactivating:

Nikolaos,

Best to run the CHKDSK /R overnight while sleeping.  Turn off sleep mode or it may stop running.  My 2TB HDD takes over five hours to complete.  The /R stipulation examines all clusters/sectors and repairs damaged areas when possible.  It takes a long time.

The /F stipulation is good to run before  running the  /R stipulation.  It will mark areas of concern on the HDD that the /R stipulation will later correct 

The CHKDSK /F takes only minutes to complete.  Run it before the comprehensive and thorough CHKDSK /R.  

If you force a shut down during a CHKDSK /R  it may resume where it left off when you reboot.  You will simply have to wait for it to finish then.  If it doesn't resume where it left off, then try running the /F stipulation first without the /R stipulation to prime the system.

Are you having problems with your HDD from power outages, etc?  The CHKDSK /R can certainly repair it.  That is the good news.

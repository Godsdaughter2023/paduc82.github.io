    So what I have done :

    Manually clean the value on all attribute used by Lync/Skype for business before being able to enable them for Skype for Business Online

    1 unassigned the Sfb license on Office 365

    2 synch with AD

    3 clean the attributes

    * msRTCSIP-DeploymentLocator

    * msRTCSIP-FederationEnabled

    * msRTCSIP-InternetAccessEnabled

    * msRTCSIP-OptionFlags

    * msRTCSIP-PrimaryHomeServer

    * msRTCSIP-PrimaryUserAddress

    * msRTCSIP-UserEnabled

    * msRTCSIP-UserPolicies

    * msRTCSIP-UserRoutingGroupId

    4 synch with Office 365

    5 enable the Sfb license

    Wait a bit, and it works !

hansv mvp say:

If you want the two-digit day in a separate column in a query, use

TwoDigitDay: Format([DateField], "dd")

where Datefield is the name of yourt date/time field.

Similarly for the month:

TwoDigitMonth: Format([DateField], "mm")

Or in the control source of a text box on a form or report:

=Format([DateField], "dd")

=Format([DateField], "mm")

qa guy commelec say: Access stores dates as decinal numbers and should not be viewed there.   Do not apply the formatting to the table but to the query, form, or report for display.

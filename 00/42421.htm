internet expert say:

Those sites are pretty "gray market". For the overall security of your account, I wouldn't recommend it.


Those sites are comprised of individual resellers who have a bunch of unused Xbox codes on hand to resell. The real risk comes from how they acquired those codes, because a few resellers out there bought the codes with stolen credit card info. When that happens, the actual owner of the credit card will eventually see those fraudulent charges on their bank statement, call the bank to dispute charges, and initiate a chargeback. When a chargeback reverses the payment made to Microsoft, Microsoft checks for any and all Xbox accounts associated with the code: likely who bought it and who redeemed it - and bans those accounts permanently for "Marketplace Theft". Granted, those CD Key sites remain in business because the risk is small, but the risk is still there.<

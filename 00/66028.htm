Having the USB in the internal boot order doesn't always work because some Flash Drives can be identified as two different types depending upon how it was made...

If you enter Boot Options at startup by hitting a F2, F9, F11, F12, ESC, or DEL at the manufacturers screen you will probably see the flash twice... Once as normal and again as UEFI... You probably need to select UEFI...

I can't say what key will work for your machine... It varies widely by manufacturer and model...

WORD 2013 MACROS DISAPPEAR - Microsoft Community

pedromperezs say:

WORD 2013 MACROS DISAPPEAR

I work on Word 2013 (Windows 7). Every time I rec ord a macro it works well until I close Word. When I reopen it, all recorded macros have dissapeared. I have tried changing the Normal.dotm file name, reconfiguring the Complements, etc. and nothing seems to work. Please help.
jay freedman say:

This happens because changes to the Normal.dotm template are not being saved. That's usually because some add-in is interfering with the saving process.

Try disabling or removing each of the add-ins listed as active in the File > Options >Add-Ins dialog, one at a time, and testing whether a recorded macro stays in place during a shut-down and restart of Word. If you find one whose removal allows the macro to stay in place, you must either find an updated version of that add-in or decide to leave it disabled.

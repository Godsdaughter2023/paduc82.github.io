There may also be hidden files on your phone’s storage or SD card that consume the storage of your device. In this case, we suggest connecting your device to a PC or laptop and check if there are hidden files (recent file). From there, you have the option to delete or move it temporarily outside your device or SD card. Then, kindly observe if the issue will persist.

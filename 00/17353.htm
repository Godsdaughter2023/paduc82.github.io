    jay freedman say:

    I suspect you used the wrong kind of text fields. Word 2007 has a new kind of control, "content controls", and those are the ones prominently displayed in the Developer tab's Controls group. The kind
    of form fields that were available in Word 2003 are now on the single Legacy Tools button in that group. If you use forms protection, you must use the text form field from the Legacy Controls and not
    the (rich text or plain text) content control. If you mix the two types, you get the kind of problem you're seeing.

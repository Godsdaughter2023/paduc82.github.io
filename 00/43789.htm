How was Windows 10 originally Activated on the PC? Did it come preinstalled from Factory, was it Upgraded with free or paid Upgrade, or was it a retail copy and license?

For Upgrade, when you first upgraded and it activated it assigned a permanent Digital License to the PC in Microsoft Activation Servers so you can reinstall any time and in any way you want as long as you reinstall the licensed version. It should reactivate soon after going online.

Retail keys must be reinserted for reinstalls during install or in Settings.

What is the Status and Edition shown at the top of the page at Settings > Update & Security > Activation?

Hi lance

 

Yes, the apps must be installed in each users account. Any Store Apps can be installed on up to 5 computers and an unlimited number of users on those PCs. This includes store apps that you have purchased.

 

Log on to the computer with one of the other accounts.

 

Open the Settings.

 

Select Your Account in the settings menu and log into the Store with the same store account you used to install the apps for your Administrator account.

 

Right click in the Store window and select Your Apps on the top toolbar.

 

Select All Apps in the drop down menu.

 

Select all of the apps you want to install to the account and click the Install Icon on the

 

Repeat this process for each of the accounts.

 

This process will be changed when the Windows 8.1 update is released later this year. You will be able to install the apps to the other accounts all at once using your administrator account.

john w. vinson say:

You're not getting any error messages? Perhaps not: you have a line On Error Resume Next which will suppress displaying them to you. I'd suggest changing that line to 

On Error GoTo Proc_Error

and adding a few lines before the End Sub:

Proc_Exit: 

  Exit Sub

Proc_Error:

  MsgBox "Error " & Err.Number & " in cmdImport.Click" & vbCrLf & Err.Description

  Resume Proc_Exit

How big is your database (in the Windows Explorer properties)? One problem with storing attachments is that they can be pretty big, and an Access .accdb or .mdb file is limited to 2GByte. It's often better to store just the path and filename to the external file in your table.

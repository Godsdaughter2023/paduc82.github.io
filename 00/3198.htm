The percentage value that is being displayed is the total battery usages by each app, not just the background usage.  Below each app name, you will see two bars.  The top bar represents the usage when app is in the foreground, the bar below (if enabled) shows the background usage.

Hello,

If you're receiving unwanted emails, you can block the email addresses and domains you don't want to receive email messages from. Add any email addresses or domain to your blocked senders list to send these emails directly to your Junk Email folder:

  1. Select Settings > Mail > Accounts > Block or allow.
  2. Under Block or allow, select Automatically filter junk email.
  3. Under Blocked senders, type the email address or domain that you want to block and select Add.
  4. Select Save.

To have email that doesn't come from the Safe senders list go directly to the Junk folder, select the Don't trust email unless it comes from someone in my Safe Senders list.

shyamal debnath msft support say:

Hi KarenWeise,

If a hold placed on her mailbox before it's deleted, the contents of the mailbox preserved or discoverable.

However, if a hold isn't placed on her mailbox before it's deleted, the contents of the mailbox won't be preserved or discoverable. The deleted mailbox can be recovered within 30 days of deletion, but the mailbox and its contents will be permanently deleted after 30 days if it isn't recovered.

Delete or Restore User Mailboxes in Exchange Online

-HiddenFromAddressListsEnabled $false (Currently the default)

toby tu msft support say:

Hi timmar10,

It is not feasible to change the default value of "HiddenFromAddressListsEnabled" for unified groups at a Global level in Office 365.

You only can bulk hide the Unified Groups from the GAL by running the following commands.

1. Connect to Exchange Online PowerShell

2.Get-UnifiedGroup| Set-UnifiedGroup -HiddenFromAddressListsEnabled $true

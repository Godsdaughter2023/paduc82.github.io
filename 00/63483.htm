Well, first of all I suggest you to uninstall all the third part antivirus and use Windows Defender instead (third part antivirus usually use a great amount of resources, while booting and sometimes also while running).
Then I suggest you to set your system to better performance settings:

1. Type "control panel" in windows search bar
2. Click on "Control Panel" from the results
3. Click on "System and Security"
4. Click on "System"
5. Click on "Advanced System Settings"
6. Click on "Advanced"
7. Click on "Settings" under "Performance"
8. Click on "Set to get best performance"
9. Click on "Apply"

Once done, reboot your device (I assume you have yet performed the clean boot)

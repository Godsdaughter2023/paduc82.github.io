Ryan Chamberlain

hansv mvp say:

Place the insertion point at the top of page 3. On the Layout tab of the ribbon, click Breaks > Next Page.

Do the same at the top of page 8. Your document now consists of 3 sections:

Section 1: page 1 and 2

Section 2: page 3 through 7

Section 3: page 8 and beyond

Click anywhere in section 3.

Activate the header. You should see "Header -Section 3-" and "Same as Previous".

In the Navigation group of the Design tab of the ribbon, click to unhighlight the "Link to Previous" button.

"Same as Previous" should disappear.

Click Previous, and do the same in section 2.

You can now enter text (and/or graphics) for a header in section 2 while leaving the headers of sections 1 and 3 unchanged.

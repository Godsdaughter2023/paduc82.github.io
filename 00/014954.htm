This is not something which you can control... at least not by any means considerably more invasive than having the other users install the font. If they don't want to simply install a font the most certainly would strenuously object to an outsider programmatically controlling their computers.

It won't continue while the computer is asleep (nothing can actually process during sleep, all activity gets paused).

That should be fine.  Most devices built in the last roughly 5-7 years have more modern capabilities with power management and will prevent a battery from overcharging or decaying performance from being plugged in too much.

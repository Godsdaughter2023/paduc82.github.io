¡firedog say:

Please see this page: What happened to contact groups?

I find it useful to give categories names starting with a number. Then, if you do click the To... button, categories will be at the top of the list. To address a message to everyone in a category, just type its number in the To... box. Numbered categories will also sort neatly in the left-hand pane of the Contacts window.

You might find sub-categories useful, too. A category called '1 Hockey players' could include all players, but '11 Hockey A Team' only those in that team. Type 1 in the To... box, and you'll include all; type 11 and you'll only get the A team members.

If you want to exclude a member of a category, click the '+' sign to reveal all the names, then click on the one to be excluded and press DEL.

To address a message to only a few members of a category, it's best to start in the Contacts window. Select the category in the left-hand pane, then hold Ctrl down while clicking on the contacts you want. Then click the Email button on the ribbon.

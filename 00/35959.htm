Where to find the product key depends on how the copy of Windows was obtained:

1. Through an authorized distributor. The product key is found on a label or card that is inside the box in which Windows came. For more information, contact the hardware manufacturer.

2. A new PC with Windows. The product key is pre-installed on the PC, included with the package in which the device was sent or in the Certificate of Authenticity attached to the PC. For more information, contact the hardware manufacturer.

3. A digital copy of a Microsoft website. The product key is found in the confirmation email that was sent after purchasing your digital copy of Windows. If you have purchased Windows 10, you will receive a digital license instead of a product key. You can use the digital license for activation. For more information, see Activation in Windows 10 or contact Microsoft Technical Support.

4 The free version of Windows 10. If you upgrade for free from Windows 10 from Windows 7 or Windows 8.1, you should have a digital license instead of a product key. For more information about digital licenses in Windows 10, see Activation in Windows 10 or contact Microsoft Technical Support.


NOTE: If the product key is scratched, appears blurred or can not be used for any other reason, please contact the distributor that sold the product. If you can not get help from your reseller or if you've tried to get a key that works, but has not succeeded, call Microsoft Technical Support.

brian tillman [outlook mvp 2007-2019] say:

I don't believe you need to uninstall your security software first before installing Office Home and Student.  Keep in mind, though, that for any mail client you use (Office Home and Student does not contain a mail client like Outlook), your security software can interfere with its communications with your mail server.  That's unrelated to your installation of Home and Student, but I thought I'd mention it.

Any Word or Excel documents you created with Office Starter 2010 will be available within whatever other version of Office you decide to install.  They'll be saved because user-created data does not reside in the same folders where Office files are installed and so won't be touched when you install any Office, but they won't be "sent to" anything.  They'll just be in whatever folder they were in when you created them, which is usually the Documents folder in your Windows profile, and hence they'll still be there and available when you install any other version of Office.

Hi,

 

It is a pleasure to help you with your issue in putting a link on your photos in your messages.

 

To put a link on your photo, the photo should be accessible to the public by saving it in a cloud storage such as Microsoft SkyDrive and the photo should not be sent as an attachment.

 

To upload photos in SkyDrive, please refer to this article for more information. You may also ask for SkyDrive moderators' help for further assistance.

 

I took screenshots of the steps for your convenience.

 

1. Once you successfully uploaded your photo, open the photo in SkyDrive and click View original.

 

 

2. Another page will appear. Right click on the photo and select Copy

3. Go to Outlook.com compose an email page. Right click the blank space and select Paste.

 

 

 

4. Click on Options and select Edit in HTML.

 

 

5. You will see the HTML codes on the message area. Your screen should look like this:

 

 

6. Put the following codes on the message area. The codes are in bold letters. Sample codes:

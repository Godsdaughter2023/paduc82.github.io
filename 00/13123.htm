Try below:

=SUMPRODUCT(COUNTIF(INDIRECT("'"&TabList&"'!"&"B"&ROW(16:16)&":C"&ROW(16:16)),B$15))

=SUMPRODUCT(COUNTIF(INDIRECT("'"&TabList&"'!"&CELL("address",B16)&":"&CELL("address",C16)),B$15))

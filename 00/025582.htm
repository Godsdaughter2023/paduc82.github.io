Although it seems to give a 3D impression this really is not an effect - it's an inherent part of the design of the shapes used for the SmartArt Object explicitly named Hierarchy. AFAIK, you can change certain attributes of the [blue] background components but they cannot be removed.

Trying to fix that will be just about impossible, & each structural modification you make will create another obstacle to overcome. SmartArt Objects are "canned" for user convenience, they aren't starting points for custom creations. There is a certain amount of flexibility offered on the SmartArt & Format tabs, but anything beyond that is problematic.

IMO, the most appropriate approach would be to use the SmartArt Object called Organization Chart, which is the same type of hierarchy chart but has a plain design.

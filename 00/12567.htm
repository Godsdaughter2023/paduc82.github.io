scottgem say:

Why do you need code? If you select a control on a form (you don't have fields on a form, you have controls that may or may not be bound to fields in a table. This is a subtle but important distinction) You can then press the Sort button on the ribbon to sort by that field. 

You can, however, use generic code:

Me.OrderBy = Screen.ActiveControl.Controlsource

Me.OrderByOn = True

I would use the On Double Click event for that code, you don't want it resorting whenever you enter a control.

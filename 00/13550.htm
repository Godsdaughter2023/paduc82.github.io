a. user say:

Hello G_I_U_2,

 

Greetings!  Welcome to the forum and thank you for posting your issue.  I understand that you want to create a group wherein the member's email address is not shown to other members of the group.  I know how this concerns you, allow me to assist.

 

When you create a group, you need to put in each member's email address.  Note that the email address of each member may or may not be seen by other members of the group.  It depends if a member is included on another member's People contacts list; in this case, the email address of the member that is in the People contacts list is visible on the other member.  On the other hand, the email address will not be visible in the group only if a member is not listed in the other member's People contacts list.

 

To explain further, let us make an example.  Let's say you created a group which includes 3 members: you, Jack and Jill.  If members Jack and Jill are in your People contacts list, you can view Jack and Jill's email address in the group.  Otherwise, if only Jill is listed in your People contacts list, you can only view Jill's address in the group, and you cannot view Jack's email address.

 

I hope I was able to provide the information you need.  If you have other clarifications, please don't hesitate to post.  I'll try my best to help you with it.

 

Have a pleasant day!

For you to delete all the contacts saved in your simcard, we have to change the settings of your contacts first. 

 

Here are the steps on how to change the settings of your contacts list:

 

  1. Go to Menu.
  2. Select Contacts.
  3. Scroll down to More.
  4. Choose Settings.
  5. Open Memory in Use.
  6. Select SIM card only.

If you want to delete all your contacts save in your SIM card, here are the steps:

 

  1. Go to Menu.
  2. Open Contacts.
  3. Open Names.
  4. Select options.
  5. Scroll down to More.
  6. Select Marking options.
  7. Choose Mark all.
  8. Click on options again and then choose delete marked.

rowena cam say:

Hi, mohitchimanlalmohit.

Thank you for posting on Microsoft Community.

You perform the soft reset by having your phone turned on. Then press and hold the volume down and Power buttons until your phone vibrates and restarts. Note that you should not touch the device screen during the process (not even when the 'slide down to power off' prompt appears).

After soft reset do the steps again to blocked calls.

How to Block Calls and Messages:

1. To block a call, Open Phone> Tap and hold a number> Block. That’s it! Now, when you get calls from that particular number, it gets rejected automatically.

2. To block a message, Open Messaging> Tap and hold a message> Block. Now, you will get rid of the messages you don’t want to see/read.
3. Apart from blocking calls, and messages separately, you can block a contact, so that you can neither get calls nor messages from that particular contact. To do this, open Block and Filter using any of the method given above. Tap on the Hamburger Menu> Select Block List> Tap the ‘+’> Enter the number you want to block.

How to manage Blocked Calls and Messages:

1. You can unblock the blocked calls anytime, just follow the above step till you reach the Blocked Calls menu, tap, and hold the number you want to unblock, Click on Unblock.
2. Similar to unblocking calls, messages can also be unblocked by tapping and holding the blocked message in the Blocked Messages menu.
3. Numbers with no caller ID can also be blocked under Settings of the app.
4. You can also get notified when you get a message or call from the blocked contact, just by switching on the option under Settings.

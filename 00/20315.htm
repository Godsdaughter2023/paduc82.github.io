Do you need to insert text in a paragraph between the table and heading, or are you just trying to create more space? If the latter, try adding more Spacing Before to the heading.

When you insert a cross-reference to a heading, a "hidden bookmark" is generated. If you haven't inserted a cross-reference, then there won't be a bookmark, and so the issue is moot.

srimadhwa b say:

Hi,

Thank you for posting your question in the Microsoft Community Forums.

The maximum number of entries allowed in the account's block domain and sender list is 500. Which means that you have 1000 entries for your blocked senders list. Once you have maxed out your limit, there is no way to have it increase.

As a work around, you can create an email rule to your account for each senders. You can block either their domain or email address. You can create up to 500 email rules for your account.

The difference between “sender contains” and “recipient contains” is same as the “To address” and “From address”.

When using "Keyword contains" the Keyword applies to the entire email. When the criteria matches the mail is moved to the “Deleted Folder”. If you have your email id in the CC or BCC section with an attachment then the mail will land in the junk folder. There could also be some settings or rules which might have been set incorrectly.

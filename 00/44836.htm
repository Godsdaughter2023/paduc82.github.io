In the chkdsk command

/F              Fixes errors on the disk.

 /R              Locates bad sectors and recovers readable information

 And hence the delay

Ideally, Its not suggested to stop the drive error checking process before completion. In this case, this seems to be the only option if not wait for the scan to complete.

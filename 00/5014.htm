-few thousand rows

-no addons

-turned the calculation from auto to manual (link above suggested)

.... Still same issues.

I figured out what was wrong with mine. 

My setup:

Excel 2013

Symptom, click on link formatted =HYPERLINK("http://www.google.com")

and every other link would freeze my excel for about 2 minutes. Then I could click on the link. Then next Link would work, then the following link after would freeze Excel for 2 minutes.. rinse and repeat.

Browser: Chrome

I realized that my hyperlink was set to =HYPERLINK("http://www.google.com/")

so I changed the link to a secured http connection

=HYPERLINK("https://www.google.com/")

IT NOW WORKS WITHOUT FREEZING!

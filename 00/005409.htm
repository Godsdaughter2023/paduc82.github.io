No!! Don't delete all your fonts!  That will create a catastrophe (personal experience here).


Fonts come with your system. Fonts come with applications such as Microsoft Office. Fonts can be purchased or acquired for free from many sources. Use a font management tool such as Font Book application to install, delete, and manage your fonts. When you have duplicate fonts, keep the one with the most glyphs (which is usually the newest version of the font).


Be careful when deleting fonts. Some of those oddball fonts you never use are used by the operating system.



jim gordon mvp say:

Hi Nick,

Here are the answers to your questions:

if I just cancel the second subscription will we just be able to renew our main licences and add another licence post migration or do I need to do something further.

Do you mean to renew your first subscription and add another license to it after cancelling the second one? If yes, the answer is yes. Here are the detailed steps to add more licenses for your Office 365 for business subscription:

The answer is no.

As a tip, if you want to continue to use the account info@ xxx in your second plan, you should buy and assign another license for the account before cancelling the second plan to avoid any data loss in the account.

suzanne s. barnhill say:

You can add the Lock button to the Quick Access Toolbar. Find it in the Commands Not in the Ribbon category in the Customize the Quick Access Toolbar dialog.

But the directions you were given, though admittedly more cumbersome than having a Lock toggle button, do work:

  1. On the Review or Developer tab, in the Protect group, click Protect Document and choose Restrict Formatting and Editing. This opens the Restrict Formatting and Editing pane.
  2. In the pane, if the document is protected, there will be only a Stop Protection button. This unlocks the form.
  3. To relock it, assuming that "Allow only this type of editing" is still checked and "Filling in forms" is still selected, you click Yes, Start Enforcing Protection.

Auto Correct Does not work for existing documents or when I paste a content.

Hi,

 I have a set of autocorrect entries which I will be using for my documents. This works fine when i type, however, it is not the case when i paste a paragraph or open an existing document. I need this facility as I will be doing this lot many documents.   I thought autocorrect is my best option. I have not tried any other options.

stefan blom say:

Each AutoCorrect entry is triggered/inserted by a the typing of a specific string of characters. For example, typing (c) in Word creates the copyright sign. Pasting content won't trigger AutoCorrect, as you have noticed.

Note that if you often insert large "chunks" of predefined content, AutoText may be a better choice. You can insert AutoText entries during typing.

Windows 8.1 - updates not working - finally fixed !!!

After several months and many hours of trying to update one of my Windows 8.1 (32-bit) desktops, today I found a solution.  May not work in other cases, however, this is what worked for me:

First, change Windows Update settings to "Never check for updates"

Then, shut off and re-boot the computer

Next: right click Start > Run command> type "Services.msc" (without quotation marks)

Navigate to and click on "Log On" > Local System Account

Check the box marked "allow service to interact with desktop"

Go back to PC Settings > Update & Recovery > Check for Updates

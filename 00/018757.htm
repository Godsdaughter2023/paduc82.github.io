Let me recommend you and your friends investigate GroupMe. It is an app available on the major smartphone platforms, WP, Android, iOS. It is pretty consistent that when you get a text, it comes in from the sender, but there is generally no way to see that it was sent to a group, and no consistent action when you reply. the iPhone has an option buried in the settings to make that decision for you. GroupMe takes care of that issue.

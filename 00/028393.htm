Use a macro containing the following code:

 

Selection.HomeKey wdStory

    Loop

Presume you mean either the exact date or the next larger date - this is given in column D:

=IFERROR(VLOOKUP($B10,$A$3:$D$5,IFERROR(MATCH($A10,$A$2:$D$2,0),MATCH($A10,$A$2:$D$2,1)+1),FALSE),"NA")

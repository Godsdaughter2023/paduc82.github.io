Here's what you can do:

1. Delete the association between My Phone and your device. To do that go to the web portal, select "Connected Phones", select your phone and hit "Delete".

2. After you've done that, delete the songs that you want to free up space. You'll still get the message "Delete selected item(s)? These item(s) will also be deleted from your phone" but you can safely ignore it because you no longer have your device associated with My Phone.

3. Go to your My Phone client application, unselect Music and resync your device. Because you've broken the association, everything will be resynced. You may want to use a wi-fi connection for this. This process will not generate any dupes.

4. After the sync has finished, go to the web portal and verify that nothing has changed, i.e. all your information is there (minus the music).

5. Check that your music files are still safe on the device.

6. Finally, go to the "Deleted Items" and permanently delete your music files to free up space.

how do I copy autotext enties in word for mac 2011 to another mac? - Microsoft Community

rosalindmathias say:

how do I copy autotext enties in word for mac 2011 to another mac?

how do I copy autotext enties in  word for mac 2011 to another mac?
rich michaels - mvp (greatcirclelearning.com) say:

A simple way is to make a copy of your Normal.dotm template, save it to your Desktop and give it a new name, something like Transfer.dotm will work nicely. Take Transfer.dotm over to the other machine.  Double click on the template to open it.

  • What opens is a blank new document with Transfer.dotm attached. Go to Tools > Templates and Add-ins. On the Templates and Add-ins dialog you'll see an Organizer button, click it.
  • What opens next is a kind of like a two column dialog with your current document and attached Transfer template on the left and on the right the Normal template for this current machine.
  • You want to choose the AutoText tab and on the left side make sure the pull down box shows the name Transfer.
  • Use the controls in the middle to copy all of the autotext entries from the left box to the right box. The Shift Key allows for multiple selections to be made.

When all done make sure you quit Word completely and restart it. If prompted to save the Normal template, do so. At that time you can also delete Transfer.dotm.

Hope this helps

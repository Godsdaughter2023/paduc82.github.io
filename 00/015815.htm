dale howard [mvp] say:

GKB -

Sure, the simplest way to do this is to change the formatting of the timescale bar at the top of the Gantt Chart pane.  Do the following:

  1. Double-click the timescale bar.
  2. On the Middle Tier page of the Timescale dialog, click the Label pick list and select the W4, W3, W2, W1, ... (From End) label format.
  3. Click the OK button.

When you look at the Gantt Chart view, the timescale bar will show you many weeks away the Finish date of the project is.  Hope this helps.

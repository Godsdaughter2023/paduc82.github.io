toby_727 say:

Skype profile photo changed.

Original Title: Stealing Skype profile photo.

The picture which is officially my profile picture is not the one being sent my emails. Instead it is using an old Skype photo which I never gave permission for, and which I have unlinked with my Hotmail account. I would like to change this immediately.

a. user say:

Hi,

   

  • How are you trying to access Outlook.com?  Is it through Web or App? 

Yes, off course, the picture that shows on the sent messages is nothing but your Skype profile picture which might be still linked with your Microsoft account.

If you are trying to access your Outlook.com account via web browser, then I suggest you change the profile picture first in Skype and then unlink your Skype once again from your Microsoft account.

Please try the methods provided below:

Method 1

change Skype profile picture.

  • Open any email in your inbox or sent folder.

  • Place the mouse cursor on your profile name (In the top left corner of the email)

  • When you place the cursor, you will see a drop down, just click on edit profile details.

  • Under My profiles, click on Skype profile.

  • Click on the Skype icon (Located exactly on top of the page and the first icon/option)

  • On the left panel, click on your profile picture.

  • Click on the Profile picture again to upload a new one.

  • Select a picture and click on Open.

Method 2

unlink Skype frompeople/Microsoft account.

  •          Access you Outlook.com via web browser.
  •          Click on your Account Name.
  •          Click on Account Settings.
  •          Click on Your info tab.
  •          Click on Go to your Skype profile.
  •          If you are not signed in, then please sign in with your Microsoft account.
  •          Click on your Skype profile name.
  •          Scroll down and click on Account settings.
  •          Under Account settings, you will see Microsoft account as linked, so just click on Unlink.

If you unlink this Microsoft account, you will be signed out of Skype and your Skype account will be closed. In order to use Skype again, you will need to go back to the sign-in process. Your current Skype contacts will be deleted but any Messenger contacts from your Microsoft account will appear the next time you sign into Skype with a Microsoft account.

Hope this helps in resolving the issue. If the issue persists, do get back to us. We will be happy to assist you.

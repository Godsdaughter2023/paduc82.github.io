As mentioned, it's up to the web developers of each company to update their website to be compatible with the latest browsers. There is a way around it though, it's called Compatibility View (I'm not sure why you said F12 as that brings up the Developer Tools).


To put a website in compatibility view:

  1. Go to the website you are having issues viewing.
  2. Select the Gear icon (Tools) and select Compatibility View Settings (you can also do this through the menu bar under Tools.  If the menu bar is not active, press Alt to temporarily enable it).
  3. You'll see the website listed under Add Website so click on Add.
  4. When you click on Close, the website will automatically refresh into compatibility view. This setting is saved so you will continue to view this website in compatibility view until you remove the website from the list.

Yes, Microsoft is supporting Bing but that doesn't mean you can't use Google as your default search engine.


Here's how to setup Google as your default search provider in Internet Explorer 11:

  1. Select the Gear icon (Tools) and select "Manage Add-ons"
  2. At the bottom of the windows, click on a link called "Find more toolbars and extensions..." This will take you to this website: http://www.iegallery.com/PinnedSites
  3. Go to the Add-ons section and select All.
  4. You'll see Google as the second option from the left on the top row so select that. That takes you to this website: http://www.iegallery.com/Addons/Details/14083
  5. Click on the "Add to Internet Explorer" button.
  6. Back in the Manage Add-ons window, select Search Providers, select Google, click on the "Set as default" button at the bottom, and close the window. Viola, Google is now your default search provider. You can test this by typing a random word into your address bar, pressing Enter and Google will show you the results for that word.

Hi Kelly,

There are two types of groupings in Sway:  sections and groups.  Sections appear anytime you insert a heading; everything after a heading up until the next heading is automatically put into a section.  Content before the first heading is special in that it doesn't show as in a section, but it is really the first section in the your Sway.  The only way to not have everything appear as if it's in a section is by removing all headings, but in reality, you'd just be leaving all your content in one section right after the title.  As for groups, there a many different types, but they behave similarly when it comes to containing other cards.  As you stated, the ungroup button removes the group and leaves all the cards that were in it behind.

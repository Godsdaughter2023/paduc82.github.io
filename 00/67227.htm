    Hello SueMG224810,

     

    You'll want to check that the app hasn't been unpinned. At your Start screen sweep up with your finger from the bottom of the screen slightly, that should bring up a bar at the bottom with All apps in the right corner, select it.     Beginning the system scan. This process will take some time.

    Beginning verification phase of system scan.

    Verification % complete.

    …found no integrity violations

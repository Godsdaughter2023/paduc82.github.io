Try recreating the hosts file.

::1 localhost

 

The "1" in the "127.0.0.1" must be at the first column of the line and there must be at least one space between "127.0.0.1" and "localhost". In the second line, there must be at least one space between "::1" and "localhost".

SOME SIMPLE HELP WOULD HAVE BEEN GREAT


The key to getting simple help is asking simple questions. I'm sure that most of those who read your question thought that you knew that Alt-0176 would produce a degree sign, but that you wanted to program a key on your keyboard to produce the sign. If you had asked 'how can I insert a degree sign into a mail message in Windows Live Mail', we'd have been falling over ourselves to tell you that all you have to do is to hold down the Alt key while typing 0176 on the numerical keypad. You can read more on this page: Insert a special character into a document.

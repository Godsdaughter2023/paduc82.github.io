Typically that error in such a situation indicates that the worksheet you think is named

radar2013-11-06

really doesn't have that name on its tab. Often people have typed in an extra space character either before or after the visible characters on the tab. So that's the first place to look.

Also, if you have more than one workbook open, make sure the one with that sheet in it is the one selected for use at the time. If that sheet is in the same workbook with this code, then change all of the "ActiveWorkbook" entries to become "ThisWorkbook" (without the quote marks).

ActiveSheet.Sort.SortFields.Clear

ActiveSheet.Sort.SortFields.Add Key:=Range("F2:F100"), _

 SortOn:=xlSortOnValues, Order:=xlAscending, DataOption:=xlSortNormal

With ActiveSheet.Sort

  .SetRange Range("A1:AI100)

  .Orientation = xlTopToBottom

  .SortMethod = xlPinYin

  .Apply

End With

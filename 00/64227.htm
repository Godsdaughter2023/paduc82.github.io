Hi.

It is not good to configure the PC to boot from the USB stick. Once you do that, your PC will always try to boot from the USB stick and Windows installation will not be finished unless you interrupt booting from the USB stick.

You should insert the USB stick and then try to boot from the USB stick only once. Then on next restart, booting device will revert to HDD and installation of Windows will continue from HDD.

Hope this helps.

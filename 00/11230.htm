joenamco say: As an update is updating files it has to write to the HD and then delete the old file and replace it with what was written to the hard drive and this takes time. Also suggest Google or Bing and ask how does updating a PC work.

hula_baloo say:  If you have a disk activity light on your PC, you can see it feverously blinking, indicating a great deal of hard drive activity during these apparent slow progression stages.  Some parts of the upgrade are more CPU bound so the percentages go faster when less I/O is involved.  This of course is because hard drives and SSDs operate in milliseconds while the CPU operates in nanoseconds, the difference being a factor of a million.  Computer science 101.

    bingadselyse say:


    Hi Rigem,

     

    Thank you for reaching out with your inquiry, I’m happy to assist!

     

    On the Bing Ads platform, there a few different reasons as to why the system would automatically pause your campaigns. These reasons are as listed:

     

    • Limited by budget
    • Campaign out of budget

     

    Limited by budget:

    • Limited by budget simply means that your ads are running, but to avoid going over your daily budget, Bing Ads occasionally limits the number of times your ad is shown. If your campaign has this status, it can still be successful.

    Campaign out of budget:

    • Campaign out of budget is when no ads are running for your campaign, ad group, ad, or keyword because your campaign budget has been used up for the day.

    The issue that you seem to be facing may be due to the budget type you have your account set to.  Bing Ads offers two different types of budget options which are:

     

    • Daily – Standard: This option is best if displaying your ads throughout the entire day is important and you are willing to monitor your budget daily so you can adjust if necessary.

    • Daily – Accelerated: This option is best if you want to maximize impressions but are also are willing to monitor your budget daily so you can make adjustments if necessary.
    • Shared – Accelerated: Choose this budget type if you want to maximize impressions across multiple campaigns but are also are willing to monitor your budget daily so you can adjust if necessary.

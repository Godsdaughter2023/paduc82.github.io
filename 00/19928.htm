Disable ability for users to share folders/documents in Document Library

We have a document library that contains sensitive personal information for employees. While we can restrict the permissions appropriately as an admin, the employees are also able to share the folders and documents that they have access to. How can we prevent people from sharing things they have access to view and edit within a document library?

reneyu_msft say:

Hi Scavok,

We are able to turn off the sharing ability at site level to disable sharing the contents underneath the site for the members.

1. Go to the site > Site settings > under Users and Permissions, Site permissions.

2. On the PERMISSIONS ribbon > Access Request Settings. You may need to click Stop Inheriting Permissions first if you are working on a subsite.

3. Uncheck Allow members to share the site and individual files and folders and Allow access requests.

4. OK.

Best Regards,

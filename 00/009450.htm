Hello, DG3149.

We understand that your Lumia 720 keeps on showing the "Slide down to power off" message. I'm here to help.

The "Slide down to power off" message will only show if if you have pressed the Power button for 3 seconds.

Here are some of the possible reasons as to why the said message appears:

  1. You are pressing the Power button accidentally.
  2. If you're phone is encased in a protective case, it is possible that the case is tight and it's accidentally pressing the Power button.
  3. You're charging the phone while using it.
  4. The Power button's membrane is damaged and need to be replaced.

If the above reasons are not a factor on what's going on with your phone, we recommend that you take it a Lumia Care Point facility to be checked.

Use the Lumia Care Point Locations form found here, to search for a facility near you.

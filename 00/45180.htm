sandeep ghatuary say:

Hi ArnoldAubee,

 

Follow these steps:

a)    Launch Windows Media Player and navigate to the main "Library" screen. Choose "Burn List" from the "List" panel to begin editing the burn list.

b)    Drag and drop the WAV files from the library directly into the "Burn List" panel.

c)    Insert a blank CD into the computer and click "Start Burn." Wait for Windows Media Player to finish burning the CD.

d)    Select "Options" from the "Tools" menu and click on the "Rip" tab. Select "MP3" from the drop-down menu of rip setting formats and click "OK."

e)    Click the "Rip" tab and import the newly created CD back into WMP. The files will automatically convert to MP3 when they are copied into the Windows Media Player library.

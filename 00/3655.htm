macropod say:

How would I select say headings 1 to 3 and 9? ...don't ask why - it is to do with Appendix and with chapter numbers in labels ... a tad complex but the net affect is the need to select these heading styles with Heading 1 and 9 as the TOC1 entries

What are the other \h, \z, \t for???

You can specify the particular heading Styles that are to be included via the 'Tables of Contents Options' pane shown in your earlier post. Simply assign a suitable TOC level for each of the Styles you want to include.

To edit an existing TOC that appears as, say, {TOC \o "1-3" \h \z \u} (meaning it displays headings 1-3 and hyperlinks to them) so as to include Heading 9, you might edit the field code to display {TOC \o "1-3" \h \z \u \t "Heading 9,4"}, which tells Word Heading 9 is to be added and displayed at TOC level 4. To edit the field code, either press Alt-F9 to expose all field codes in the document, or selected the entire TOC and press Shift-F9 to expose just the TOC field code. When you're done editing, click anywhere in the field and press F9 to update and, if you used Alt-F9 to expose all field codes, use it again to return the field code display to normal.

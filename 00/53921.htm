Yes, you can use any USB Flash Drive which has minimum 1Gb on it ;).

 

On your Dashboard, go to System Settings, then go to Memory. Plug in your 1Gb (or more) USB Flash Drive. Now, you should see it on the list. Select it and press A. Click on Configure Now. It will erease everything on the USB Flash Drive, but you will be able to use it as a memory storage option for your Xbox 360. Don't worry, you can revert the USB Flash Drive Storage Device to a normal USB Flash Drive.

 

Warning, you can only use two USB Flash Drives and use a maximum of 16Gb of them (E.G, you have an external Hard Drive of 250Gb, it will only use 16Gb of it). It means 32Go of total USB Flash Drive storage :P.

 

I don't know if this works as you just bought the adapter, so if you didn't connect to LIVE before, I'm not sure if you have the right Dashboard to use USB Flash drives, but you can give it a try.

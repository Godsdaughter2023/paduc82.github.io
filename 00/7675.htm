Right now round number 0 and 3 have a status of not complete

Pat_ID      Round Number    Next Round  Status

ken sheridan say: Try:

SELECT Pat_ID, [Round Number], [Next Round],
IIF(EXISTS
    (SELECT *
     FROM [YourQuery] AS Q2
     WHERE Q2.Pat_ID = Q1.Pat_ID
     AND Q2.[Round Number] = Q2.[Next Round]),
"Complete", "Not complete") AS Status
FROM [YourQuery] AS Q1
ORDER BY Pat_ID, [Round Number];

waynerupp say:

Hey MrMike3,

Your computer device acquired a Windows 10 digital license when it was upgraded from 8.1 to 10.  That is a permanent license for your device's entire life that is stored on Windows Activation Servers.

If and when you ISO install Windows 10 again for any reason, you simply skip any inquiries for a product code during installation by answering (checking box) in the following manner.

1. I don't have a product code

2. Skip this step until later

After the Windows 10 installation completes on your device, it will self activate the digital license that is stored on Windows activation servers.

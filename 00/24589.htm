OpenCL.dll comes with your video driver's install. The generic OS openCL client library is OpenCl32.dll and is stored in windows\system32.

In general, the driver manufacturers store their OPGL libraries in a folder under Program Files or Program Files (86), is this where you where looking?

But whatever their location, OpenCl.dll is not used by Windows or any of the applications that comes with it, only some third party (graphics) applications may need OpenCL.

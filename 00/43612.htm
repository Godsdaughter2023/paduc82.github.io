A microphone is a fairly basic device and as such does not require a driver. It should work on any standard Microphone input.

Check the specifications of the audio interface for socket assignment as they often reallocate the microphone input to provide surround sound output for centre/sub or rear speaker if there are only 3 jack sockets. If possible try the Microphone in another device but remember that Apple use different ring assignments on the jack.

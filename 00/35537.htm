a. user say:

This would be based on who you ask. However, I would myself say passwords are more secure because they are longer and also have more characters (A through Z and 0 through 9). However, if you do not mind having a less secure system a pin is good if you like convenience. Pins can also be secure. As most pins require the user to have minimum 4 characters and those characters are 0 through 9.  This leaves around 9999 possible choices for just a 4 digit pin. You can see all of them here:  https://www.deviantart.com/l33tn3rdz/art/All-possible-4-digit-PIN-Numbers-0000-9999-hax-436606629.    And if you use a longer pin it will also be as strong as a password.
So basically both are secure. Just one is less secure (Pin). But keep in mind that pins can be super secure if you use more than the standard 4 character pin and go for an 8 digit.

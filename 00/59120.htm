Hi Joe

1
Dell laptops are particularly fussy about the Battery and Power Adapter used . .

Boot up your laptop, repeatedly press F12 on the Dell logo screen

Then choose Diagnostics form the resulting menu

Run Dell Diagnostics, when asked if you want to perform the extended Memory Test, choose No

Does your Battery and Power adapter pass the test?

2
There is a known issue in Windows 10, whereby it will charge laptop batteries to 92% and then stop, this is Reported on Dell, Lenovo and especially on HP laptops.

And there is no known fix for this bug, though it does deem to disappear after a major Windows Update

david scammell say:

Hi Belthazor,

I just tested his on my own device to insure it works and it worked flawlessly!

  • Connect your Windows Phone device to your computer and open the Zune Desktop client.
  • Select Collection.
  • Select Pictures
  • Select "From <device name>"
  • Select "Camera Roll"
  • Select the picture or pictures (use CTRL key to select multiple pictures)
  • Drag down to icon of phone at bottom left-hand corner of Zune Desktop.
  • Allow it to sync.

Now, when you disconnect your phone and look at your Pictures Hub and selecting Camera Roll, you should now see the pics you selected on your device.

Ideally though, you'd create a seperate folder and copy pictures to that folder, either on your device, Facebook ro SkyDrive.

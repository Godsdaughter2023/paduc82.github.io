Here's how you can do it without Pivot. As far as I know, you can't use wildcards in arrays so you'll still need the =Left function in an additional column to extract the first 3 values from the scannable ids. 

Example: 

A B C
1 Shipment Scannable ID Location
2 Value Value =LEFT(B2,3)

In this example, let's say you data goes down to row 16 and the values start in A2...The formula to count unique chR would be (mind you its caps sensitive):

=SUM(--(FREQUENCY(IF(C2:C16="chR",MATCH(A2:A16,A2:A16,0)),ROW(A2:A16)-ROW(A2)+1)>0))

Press Ctr+Shift+Enter once you finish typing in the formula (this is an array formula so you can't just press enter with it).

Hello Jags,

Call history and "Recent" tab in People hub is slightly different.

The call history will open every time you tap on phone icon from the home screen. The list will show the history of all incoming/outgoing calls; it will also contain just phone numbers for the calls that you do not have the number stored. "Recent" tab in People hub (Home screen > People > All contacts > What's new > Recent) keeps a log of your recent activity not limited to calls only.

macropod say:

You can use:

Find = ^39

for straight single quotes;

Find = ^0145

for opening single quotes; and

Find = ^0146

for closing single quotes.

Similarly, you can use:

Find = ^34

for straight double quotes;

Find = ^0147

for opening double quotes; and

Find = ^0148

for closing double quotes.

I don't know why you believe 'Replace All' is "too unsafe" - it's only liable to get something wrong if your quote marks are poorly positioned, which is hardly Word's fault.

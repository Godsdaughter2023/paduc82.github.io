Libraries are 'virtual folders'. They do not physically duplicate folders or files, they simply 'mirror' the data that is in the actual folders. They also do not take up any space on

1. Open regedit

2. Navigate to HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Class\{4d36e972-e325-11ce-bfc1-08002be10318}\

3. Determine the device number for your adapter. (One way to do this is to click on the 'DriverDesc' for each device until you find one that matches your Wifi adapter name. Then you know this is the device you want to modify. For me it was '0001')

4. Locate the value 'PowerSaveMode' and change the value data from '2' to '0'

I think this changes the power consumption mode for the device from Max PSP to CAM (Constantly Awake Mode). 

Anyway, I'm not an expert so I don't recommend mucking around in the registry unless you're ok with the risk of permanently breaking something.

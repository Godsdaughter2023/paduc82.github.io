Why spend your time trying to do something that is not recommended? Why not spend your time fixing the problem. the problem is that you are not using your autonumber ContactID as a foreign key in all your tables. This is where you should focus your efforts.

bernd p. say:

If you really moved (not copied) them to Onedrive then you have to move them back and Share.

Remember: you have to recreate local notebooks, that means creating a folder in explorer with the notebook's name and filling them with the section files of type .one.

Correct created notebooks on Onedrive show no section files (*.one), they look like one file with the name of the notebook. Use your browser and look at https://onedrive.live.com

And another tip:

The Share feature leaves behind the "moved" local notebook. So in reality the move to Onedrive is just a copy. But the left behind notebook is now an orphaned child. It never gets updated and should have been deleted at the end of the Share process. Use it as a temporary backup until all is well.

jim gordon mvp say:

The formal name of the "slider" control is "scroll bar." To be able to insert a scroll bar, you must first turn on the hidden Developer tab of the Ribbon. To turn on the hidden Developer tab of the Ribbon, go to the Excel menu and choose Preferences. In the View preferences, click the box for In the Ribbon Show > Developer Tab.

Now click on the Developer tab, click on the Scroll Box control and drag across a worksheet to add your slider control. Right-click on the control and choose Format Control to set its properties.

Hi,

 

Thank you for giving us more information about your issue.

 

Based on the screenshot you provided, it appears that you are referring to instant action icons. You can remove it by following these steps:

 

1. Go to www.outlook.com and sign in with your Microsoft account

2. Click on the Gear icon and select More mail settings

3. Under Customizing Outlook, select Instant actions

4. Uncheck Show instant actions

5. Click Remove from list until the column is empty

6. Click Save to confirm changes

 

Let us know if you need more help.

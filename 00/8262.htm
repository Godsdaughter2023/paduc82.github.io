Hi Tsavo,
You need to go to vineyard vines and resubscribe to their newsletters.

A subscription is a newsletter or mailing list you signed up to receive... outlook.com picks up subscription information from the message header and adds it to the list of subscriptions. But some spammers will include subscription information to help get past spam filters - your list looks spam-free - but if it has spammers on it, you shouldn't try to unsubscribe as it just confirms you have a valid address and they'll put you on more spam lists.

tsavocole14 say: Oh that makes more sense. Is there a way that I can get my subscriptions with those two emails back? And what's the difference between unsubscribing and blocking?

diane poremsky mvp say:
Blocking sends all mail from that address to your junk email folder. To unblock, go into Settings > View all Outlook Settings > Junk email and remove the address or domain from the blocked list.

Unsubscribe sends a message to the sender asking them to stop sending you mail. In most causes, its fully automatic - a special unsubscribe message is sent to the mail server and it removes your address. You need to go back to the sender's website and subscribe if you want to get their mail again.

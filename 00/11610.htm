Lumia 920 has the required hardware for 4G, but since LTE were not an option in many countries at the time of manufacture and sale, a software update is required to enable it for each country or operator.
This is explained on

http://www.microsoft.com/en-in/mobile/support/product/lumia-920/userguidance/?action=singleTopic&topic=S-NETWORK-SERVICES

"Support for LTE networks may have been disabled on your device depending on your country or service provider."

2300 MHz is not in the list of supported frequencies for a Lumia 920, unfortunately.

  'Store Folio? controls (Tag 23..50) into Folio array
  Dim Ctrl As Control
  Dim i As Integer, j As Integer
  For Each Ctrl In Me.Controls
    If IsNumeric(Ctrl.Tag) Then
      Select Case Ctrl.Tag
        Case 23 To 50
          'Calculate indices
          i = (Ctrl.Tag - 23) \ 4 + 1    '1 to 7

Hi PattiEdises,

 

I understand that you want to block junk email. Let me assist you on this.

 

If you access your account using the mail application in Windows 8, you need to post your concern using this link: Windows 8 Email and Communications

 

If you access your account using a web browser, here are the steps on how you can add entry on your blocked senders list:

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. In the upper-right corner of the page, click wheel/cog icon, and then click More mail settings.
  3. Under Preventing junk email, click Safe and blocked senders.
  4. Click Blocked senders.
  5. Under Blocked email address or domain:, type the email address where these emails came from,
  6. Click Save.

You may also create rules that will automatically delete emails. Here’s how:

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. In the upper-right corner of the page, click wheel/cog icon, and then click More mail settings.
  3. Under Customizing Outlook, click Rules for sorting new messages.
  4. Click New.
  5. Select an option how to determine messages you want to be deleted.
  6. Tick Delete these messages—don't even put them in my Deleted folder.
  7. Click Save.

 

I hope these help. Should you have further questions, feel free to reply to this thread.

In Word 2003, the option you are referring to is at Tools | Options | View: "Windows in Taskbar."

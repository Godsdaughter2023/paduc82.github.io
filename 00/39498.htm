What is the difference hiding or deleting fonts - Microsoft Community

rayhackart say:

What is the difference hiding or deleting fonts

Which fonts can deleted. What is the difference hiding or deleting fonts
ruz san say:

Hi Ray,

Some fonts that you have on the computer are system-protected. These fonts are the ones that you cannot delete. Removing system-protected fonts may result to incorrect text display when opening certain pages. They can be hidden, though. Hiding fonts keeps the fonts stored on your computer, but makes them unavailable as font options when selecting the font style for workbooks or pages. You can show hidden fonts to make them available again.

On the other hand, deleting fonts completely removes fonts from your computer. Fonts that can be deleted are usually those that do not come pre-installed in Windows.

You can read about installing or removing fonts on this page.

Let us know if you have other concerns.

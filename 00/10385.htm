Count the number of times a specific word is used in a MS Word document?

I would like to be able to count the number of times a word or certain sequence of characters is used in a word document. Is there a way that I can count the number of times a specific word is used in a MS Word document?
bob jones aka: cybertaz say:
The Find feature displays that information in the Sidebar. If you type what you're looking for into the Search in Document field you can either click the field's magnifying glass or go to View> Sidebar> Search Pane if the Sidebar isn't already displayed. The number of Matches is displayed at the top of the listing of found instances.

Alternatively, you can either;

  • Go directly to View> Sidebar> Search Pane & enter the string in the Search Document field, or
  • Go to Edit> Find> Advanced Find & Replace, enter the string in the Find what field & confirm that Highlight all items... is checked & refers to Main Document, then click Find All.

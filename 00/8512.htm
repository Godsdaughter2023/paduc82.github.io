robert pc help say:
Hi

An online, web-based email client (webmail) does not have a refresh or get mail buttons, since you're technically connected to your mailbox, so if anything arrives it's there immediately.

Get Mail/Sync is only seen in client applications for computers and other devices, since they rely on a connection that can't be on all the time, and only checks emails periodically or by using the 'push' function. Hence the 'get mail' button if the user wants to refresh by himself.

But webmail has no need since you are connected to your live mailbox.

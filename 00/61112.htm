Why light versions of default fonts (Arial, Times) not available on Windows?

On Mac OS X, iOS and Linux, fonts come with lighter (thinner) versions.

If you know HTML+CSS, you can figure out what font-weight:100 or font-weight:200 does. It renders font very thin, if possible. On OS X, many fonts come with thin versions. Here's an example from OS X:

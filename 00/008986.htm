To create a text message:

1. Press 'Start' and select 'Messaging' > 'Text Messages'.

2. Click 'New' then type your message.

3. In the 'To' field, enter the recipient's mobile phone number. Use a semicolon [;] between numbers if you’re sending the same message to more than one person.

4. Click 'Send' and your text message will be sent immediately.

5. To cancel a message, click 'Menu' > 'Cancel Message'.

1. Click 'Menu' > 'Message Options'.

2. Select 'Request Message Delivery Notification'.

3. Click 'Done' to receive notification that your message was delivered.

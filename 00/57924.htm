jzhang.opt say:

1. Locate the registry entry:

HKCU\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\PushNotifications\AppDB

2. Delete the AppDB entry and you will notice the notification center is initialized.

3. Start an App that can send notifications, and it will be stored in the notification center and the "Notifications & actions". For example, start Outlook or MS Mail and send an email to your account, check the "Notifications & actions" after the notification.

It is possible to contact a Skype for Business user using a regular Skype account. To connect with a people who are using Skype for Business, you need to have the following: 

  • The latest Skype version. 
  • Your Skype account to be linked to a Microsoft account. 
  • The email address of the Skype for Business user you wish to contact.

First thing you need to do is add the Skype for Business user as a contact. To add a new contact:

jim gordon mvp say:

You can't add rings of circles to an existing SmartArt object, but you can get the visual effect you want.


Remember, everything in PowerPoint is in a layer. You can take advantage of this by making two SmartArt objects and placing them on top of each other.


You can see the name of each SmartArt object by holding the mouse over it in the Ribbon.Start with your Radial Cycle SmartArt. Size it small enough so you can add another ring of circles. Then right-click on the border of the Radial Cycle Smart Art, and choose Copy. Then click elsewhere on the slide and paste. The new object is now on top. Select the center circle of the new SmartArt object. Format that center circle with no fill, no line and no text, making the circle  invisible. Then reposition the new object on top of the original SmartArt object. Enlarge and center the new object over the original object and then it will look like what you are asking for,

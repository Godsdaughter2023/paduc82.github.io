Hi UserSI1983,

 

In this situation, it's not feasible to remove/change the "My Name" part form the synced folder name, it will always display the owner's name.

 

Since you're syncing the folder with everyone in your company, I suggest you create a folder in a document library in your team site (https://contoso.sharepoint.com) and share it with the "People in [your organization]" group (select "Allow Edit").

jeeped say:
... Range ("A" & x":B" & y).select

You are missing an ampersand to concatenate the first cell's row and the colon. Try,
Range ("A" & x & ":B" & y).Select
... or,
Range ("A" & x , "B" & y).Select

With 1 as x and 9 as y, both examples reference A1:B9.

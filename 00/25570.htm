I should probably note that following the upgrade to 10166 I was still being constantly prompted (by the store app) to download an update to "touch" however this update would never install and I eventually uninstalled the original instead. I suspect this was a mistake as on 8.1 this is where you can enable/disable the double tap and having uninstalled it I could find no way to reinstall it.

Problem #2: Battery life; from a full charge (100% indicated on screen) it took just shy of 6 hours to completely drain the battery. I have had problems with battery life on this phone ever since I started installing previews for 8.1 but the phone would always last at least a day (12-18 hours), 6 hours is totally useless. Again I will point out that at my location during the testing I had very poor 3G and Wi-Fi so it is possible that some of the power was used hunting for signals but even so this remains unacceptable.

Problem #3: Heat; again I have had this issue since I first started installing preview updates for 8.1, but the phone runs incredibly hot. This is also true when installing the updates themselves which I actually have to do whilst the device is physically suspended on the front of a pedestal fan in order to prevent it from shutting down (heat overload) during the upgrade process.

Problem #4: Keyboard; I found that when using the keyboard there was always a lag between key press and the corresponding character appearing on screen. This seemed to be pretty much across the board wherever I was typing and not limited to any specific application(s).

Problem #5: People hub; this is probably more of a personal preference, but I absolutely hate the round photo icons assigned to people and the new animation on the start screen (which incidentally only appears to work for certain tile sizes) is hideous. Before anybody starts shouting at me about pre-release software, I am aware that I should expect problems, this is why I tried using the device during a time where it was unimportant whether I was contactable or not and also why I moved my SIM from the active 930 back to the 920 for testing and did not instead update the 930. The objective of this message is to provide feedback as to the experience in the hope that it will go toward making the final RTM more reliable.

If your microphone is muted, you can follow the steps below to unmute it:

  1. Right-click the speaker icon on the system tray of your Windows 10.

  2. Click Recording devices.

  3. On the Sound box, ensure that the Recording tab is selected.

  4. From the recording devices' list, double-click your microphone.

  5. Locate and click the Levels tab.

  6. Increase the Microphone slider and click the speaker icon next to it to unmute the microphone if set to mute.

First off, you need to access the Library folder for your User Account, which Apple has seen fit to make a Hidden folder. Search for highlighted text

suzanne s. barnhill say:
  1. Press Ctrl+H to open the Replace dialog.
  2. Select the Find tab.
  3. Click More>> to expand the dialog.
  4. Click Format, then Highlight.
  5. You can use Find Next to step through the document or Find In to select all the highlighted text.

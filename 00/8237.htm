WARNING:

When clean installing  Windows 10, do not create a recovery partition on the Hard Drive. Doing so will cause Windows 10 Operating System to display "Windows is not Activated" in Computer Properties, forcing you to go into Disk Management, and delete the Recovery Partition, and Extend it to your C:.

Luckily, do this allows the Operating System to activate, and afterwards you are free to shrink your volume, and do what you will.

wv emt say:

Xbox One headset issue plus quick fix

The problem:

I ran into an issue yesterday with my xbox one headset. Apparently when you power down your xbox one with the headset plugged in and the mute button is active the next time you boot up your Xbox One the mic will appear to be dead (i.e. completely unresponsive). I tried using a different mic which I had purchased with no luck.

wv emt say:

Quick fix:

Unplug your Xbox One from the power outlet, wait 30-35 secondst and plug it back in. This forces the console to do a cold boot (I imagine powering it down only sends it into a standby/hibernation mode). Once the Xbox One boots up full functionality is restored.

Happy gaming

Hi Ann, I'm an independent advisor and will try to help.

Its really hard to help you without knowing what is happening and what you have tried. so all I can do at this point is give you some general tips.

First, as a general rule we don't store calculated values. These should be calculated on the fly as needed. In your Subform, you should have a footer with a textbox control using a Controlsource of:
=Sum([totalcolumn])

If you want to reference that value on the mainform add another text box on the main form with a Controlsource of:

=Forms!formname!subformname.Form.controlname
where formname is the name of the main form, subformname the name of the subform control and controlname the name of the control I just told you to add.

As for Balance, you need to define further what you are trying to get a balance for. Is it a total of unpaid invoices for the customer or what? Balance is general calculated in a Group By query that totals debits and credits by customer.

If you need further clarification on this please feel free to ask. But you will need to provide further details for me to help further.

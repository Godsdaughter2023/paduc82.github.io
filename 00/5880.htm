    alan wu msft say:

    Hi Jakob,

    Please be noted that a room resource calendar is used for recording the room resource's free/busy information during the specific period (time) but not for the meeting attendees' statement. As a result, it won't get updated when the attendee is added or deleted. And, the attendee's update can be viewed in each of the attendee's calendar itself.

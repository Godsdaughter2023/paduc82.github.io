jameswhittington say:

Okay after reading some other Office 2016 shared folder issues with Office 365 our issue the fact the shared inbox was associated with a domain other that our primary domain.  

The domain that the shared inbox was associated with did not have autodiscover records.

Added the following records to our domain fixed things for us:

autodiscover            IN      CNAME   autodiscover.outlook.com.

msoid                   IN      CNAME   clientconfig.microsoftonline-p.net.

This wasn't at issue with previous versions of outlook but it seems to matter with Outlook 2016.  

Hope this helps out someone else with the same sort of problem.

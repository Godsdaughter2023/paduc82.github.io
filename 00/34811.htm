There was no deposit scheduled for the 30th. The last deposit for November took place on the 24th. Your credits would be scheduled for the deposit on the 15th.

When credits are deposited is based on when you get to 5,000 pending credits. Generally if you get to 5,000 between the 1st and 15th, they are deposited by the 30th.

remittingfire58 say:

Any TV and device combo is going to only be as good as the best the lesser item can handle. 4k TV is nice and future proof but it's not going to make a device better then it inherently is. If a game is only built to be 720p the best 1080p TV isn't going to matter for the most part. Perhaps 4k support and development will come in the future, but right now I'm unaware of anything that your setup can take advantage of.

If the image is bad though perhaps you can make sure your settings are running at their best. To into settings and choose the proper settings for your TV instead of using auto-select. You can also go into the TV configuration and follow the steps to establish your tv's settings. Good luck.

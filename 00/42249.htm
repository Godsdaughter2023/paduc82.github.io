3.) Left Click your audio device; for instance mine is Conexant Pebble High Definition SmartAudio, as I am assuming yours to be as well.

4.) You should see 'Configure' and 'Properties' light up on the bottom. Left click "Properties"

5.) Left click on the "Enhancements" tab

6.) Left click the "Disable All Enhancements" box, left click apply, and you should have sound.

The sound will only be 16 bit sound. It will sound bad, but it will work.

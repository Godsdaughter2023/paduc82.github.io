rdrewd2 say:

TR,

Regarding the "laptop open" problem, two things:  first, go to settings, system, display, and in the "multiple displays" box make sure "duplicate these displays" is selected.  If not, select "duplicate" and hit apply.  If you're still getting a partial display, then right under that click "advanced display settings", go to the Resolution box, and change the resolution to match your external monitor's resolution and click apply (if you don't know your resolution,  just try a couple of the settings and click apply until your external screen matches your laptop screen).  To fix the "laptop closed" problem, go to control panel, system and security, power options, and over on the left click "choose what closing the lid does".  In the row marked "When I close the lid:", change both the battery and plugged in settings to "Do nothing".  Now your external monitor will work regardless if your laptop is open or closed.  Hope that helps.  Cheers.

graye say:

Why is this so difficult....I just want my favorite bar back at the top...internet edge is not easy friendly

So, I assume you're talking about Windows 10 and Microsoft Edge?

If so, there are two steps to get things to show up in the horizontal Favorites Bar

  • Enable the Favorites BAR feature
  • Move some favorites in the Favorites BAR

Most people just use the horizontal Favorites BAR for their frequently used favorites... and do not attempt to put their entire collection of favorites there.  Instead, the "big" list of favorites remains in the vertical Favorites PANE.

So, open the vertical Favorites PANE the normal way (via the "hub" icon... the 3 little lines), expand the FOLDER called "Favorites Bar", and move a few favorites there.  This is a special folder that mirrors the horizontal Favorites BAR.  So, anything you put there will show up both in the vertical Favorites PANE and in the horizontal Favorites BAR.

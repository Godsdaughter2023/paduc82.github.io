mp3 to ring tones - Microsoft Community

victor13 say:

mp3 to ring tones

i have a 8125 smart phone i want to add ring tones . but they allways go to windows medea player. can u help

jack cook - aka help_line say:

Follow these steps:

1.       Connect your smartphone to your PC with the USB cable.

2.       Open ActiveSync on your PC.

3.       Click the Explore button.

4.       Double-click My Windows Mobile-Based Device.

5.       Double-click Application Data. You will see a list of folders, one of which is Sounds.

6.       Using Windows Explorer on your PC, find the ringtones you downloaded and drag them into the Sounds folder in ActiveSync.

7.       Disconnect your smartphone.

8.       On your smartphone, press Start > Settings.

9.       Select Sounds.

10.   Under Ringtone, use the left and right navigation keys (next to the Center Joystick) to scroll to the ringtone you want. If you wait a second or two after scrolling to each choice, you'll hear what it sounds like.

When you've found the ringtone you want, press the Done soft key

richard diver say:

Hi,

it sounds like a mis-match in screen DPI, whilst connected to the TV you can use WIN + P to select between "Extend" and "Duplicate" - I would suggest "Extend" as this will allow for different resolutions on the Surface and TV, and choose "PC Screen Only" before you disconnect the cable.

If you still get the issue you can right click on the desktop and choose "Personlize", then choose "Display on the bottom left. In this screen you can  see the slider that allows you to enlarge the text and other screen items with the magnifier, or just use the text size only option at the bottom.

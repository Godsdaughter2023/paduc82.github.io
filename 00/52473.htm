theplectrum say:

You can't.

You can exchange midi data between the keyboard and a PC, via the USB port . But not sound as the keyboard is not an audio device and therefore has no appropriate drivers.

Midi is not sound per se, its just a series of electonic instructions sent to a module that transforms those 0's and 1's into sound samples. This isn't the same principle as a sound card/device.

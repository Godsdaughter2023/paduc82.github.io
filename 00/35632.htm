Exclusive volume lock levels - Microsoft Community

cheetahrus say:

Exclusive volume lock levels

Windows 95 (maybe the others too) provides 4 levels of exclusive volume locking.

Level 0 for format disk programs
Level 1 for defrag programs (read/write allowed)

Level 2 (read allowed, write is not allowed)

Level 3 (write/read is not allowed)
Level 0 is alone but Levels 1-3 form a hierarchy of levels with stronger rights(from 1st to 3rd).

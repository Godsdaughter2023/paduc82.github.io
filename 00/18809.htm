The numbering and border are probably part of a text box that extends into your document left margin. The indents work from that margin, not the pseudo margin created by the text box.

If the rule and numbers are on every page, change your page margins so that the entire textbox is outside the left margin.

Note, be very careful with numbering in documents that will be edited extensively or you will end up with what is technically called "spaghetti numbering."

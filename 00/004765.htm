Hi Lloyd,

 

To resolve the destination folder for the connected account on your Outlook.com, please follow the steps below to add or edit Inbox rules:

 

  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Under Mail, click Inbox and sweep rules.
  4. Click the plus sign to add a rule or the pencil icon to edit a rule.
  5. From the drop-down list of When the message arrives, and it matches all condition, click It was sent or received.
  6. Select Sent to.
  7. Add the email address, then click OK.
  8. From the drop-down list under Do all of the following, click Move, copy or delete.
  9. Select Move the message to folder.
  10. Choose the destination folder and click OK.
  11. Select OK to save the new Inbox rule.

deox j say:

Cannot cancel subscription?

So a month back I bought a months worth of xbox live; I have now just received ANOTHER email from paypal saying I have just paid out another £6 to xbox... I only wanted one month.

So how do I cancel it from taking money out of my account every month?... I tried removing the payment option but it wont let me.

klokkwork say:

To cancel an auto-renewal, go to www.xbox.com/account, look for your 1-Month Gold Membership, click the link to cancel auto payment, then follow on screen directions.  When finished, you will be returned to the My Account page, and it will say "Expires on DD-MM-YYYY" instead of "Renews on DD-MM-YYYY", and the cancel auto payment link will be gone.  You'll also receive an email notification stating that your membership is scheduled to cancel.  If you want to remove the payment option, you'll be able to do that once the Gold expires, as long as it's not attached to any other memberships on that account, or still has any kind of pending charge or anything.  Hope this info helps.

yoga yao msft say:

Hi Steve,

If you would like to keep one cell being linked to Tue when you resort the Column A, just as Andreas, you need to use a formula to achieve it. You can refer to my example below:
    1. Take an example, "Tue" refers to value 20, and we need to keep Cell D2 being linked to "Tue".
    2. Use this formula to link Cell D2 to "Tue": =VLOOKUP("Tue",A1:B5,2,FALSE)

  3. In this case, even though I resort the Column A, the Cell D2 will keep being linked with the value.

Hi Edward,

Firstly, you need to add all staffs to your staff list, you need to assign admin role to the staff member who is designated to handle bookings. For your reference:

https://support.office.com/en-us/article/create-your-staff-list-298c529b-407b-4a2b-b2c5-6e77a9d1f07f?ui=en-US&rs=en-US&ad=US

Then the admin can set employee workings hours and schedule business closures, time off, and vacation time. 

https://support.office.com/en-us/article/set-employee-working-hours-0968717e-b61f-4b06-987c-6c1464541782?ui=en-US&rs=en-US&ad=US

Admin can create and staff your bookings. As far as I know, if you assign a booking to a staff, the staff is able to receive a meeting invitation so the staff can add it to his personal calendar. 

https://support.office.com/en-us/article/create-and-staff-your-bookings-03a9acc9-f29c-456b-9fb2-0f49474b2708

In booking page, you need to tick the Notify the business via email when a booking is created or changed. In this situation, if you cancel or change a booking, both staff and customer will receive a notification of cancellation or change.

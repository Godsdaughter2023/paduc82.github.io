Private Function SortArray(Ar As Variant) As Variant
    Dim i As Integer, j As Integer
    Dim Temp As Double
    For i = LBound(Ar) To UBound(Ar) - 1
        For j = (i + 1) To UBound(Ar)
            If Ar(i) > Ar(j) And Ar(i) <> 0 Then
                Temp = Ar(j)
                Ar(j) = Ar(i)
                Ar(i) = Temp
            End If
        Next j
    Next i
    SortArray = Ar
End Function

christopher miller say:

I'm sorry, but that's a limitation of Windows Phone.  

One thing that you can do is to vote for the UserVoice item that matches your request the closest.  Like this one.  Microsoft and other companies use the Uservoice site to solicit feedback and product suggestions.  The issues logged on the Uservoice site are weighted by the number of votes they receive.  If you haven't voted for this issue already, add your votes to the item that has the highest number of votes that applies to this issue.

There are a few work arounds, all of them annoying.  From Reddit, I found a few suggestions.  They may not be useful with your phone and data plan, but people are reporting that they work:

  1. Download UC browser, log in to live.com. Search for the app you want to download on google or in the store. Turn on speed mode before going to the app's page (this is important because without speed mode it will just open the store app). On the apps page, if you scroll down a bit, there is an option to "download and install manually". That will download an .xap file and won't be subject to the limit. The tricky part is getting it to a location where your phone will find it, because UC Browser is limited when it comes to file management. There's an export to SD card option that works well, but obviously not on a 930, so I think your only option is to use bluetooth to send it to another device and then back to your download or documents folder or wherever. Once that's done, when you open up Store, there will be an option in the bottom menu that says Install local apps. (you might have to wait a few minutes for that to appear)
  2. Place your SIM another phone and make that phone a Wi-Fi hotspot and connect your phone to it.

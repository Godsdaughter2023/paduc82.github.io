a. user say:

Hi,

 

To create a group of contacts, please follow the steps below:

  1. Sign in to https://people.live.com.
  2. Click on Manage located above the page then choose Manage groups.
  3. Click on Group (which has an encircled plus sign beside it) then type the name of the Group that you want to create.
  4. Click Apply.
  5. On the left side pane, tick the checkboxes for the contacts that you want to be included in that group.
  6. Click on Groups located above the page then tick the checkbox of the group that you have created.
  7. Click Apply.

 

After doing the steps above, the contacts that you have chosen will then be included in that group. When sending an email, just type in the name of that group inside the "To:" field and the name will then appear.

mccbleue say:

There are a couple of things you can do with the "Communication Centre" in PC Suite. Firstly, you could select all messages and copy/paste them onto a folder in your PC, they will be saved as individual files with the VMG extension. By default, these would be opened in a Communication Centre - I'm not sure what other software would open them.

 

You can also select all messages, then go to File>Export and the whole selection will be saved as a CSV file that can be opened in programs like Excel.

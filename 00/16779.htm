That's not the formula I posted, mine had -2 at the end, you changed it. So with this in a1

aaa bbbb cc dddd ee

This returns 2 which is the third space from the right.

=(LEN(A1)-LEN(SUBSTITUTE(A1," ","")))-2

If we put that in a formula this returns everything after the 3rd space from the right.

=TRIM(MID(A1,FIND(CHAR(7),SUBSTITUTE(A1," ",CHAR(7),(LEN(A1)-LEN(SUBSTITUTE(A1," ","")))-2)),LEN(A1)))

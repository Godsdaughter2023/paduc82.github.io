PFTUSMAND --

Text can only be wrapped in a field when there are spaces present in the text string.  That requirement eliminates wrapping the contents of the Predecessors and Successors columns, unfortunately.  Hope this helps.

tom_bpc say:

Adding to Dale's response, you can't add spaces to the predecessors or successors columns because they are linked into the guts of the scheduling engine.

What you CAN do is make a custom text field with a formula that copies and inserts spaces into the predecessor and successor strings so they can be wrapped.  This one copies the first 100 characters of the predecessor string and inserts a space (i.e. Chr(32)) every 20 characters.  There's probably a more efficient way to do it, but this is the two minute version.

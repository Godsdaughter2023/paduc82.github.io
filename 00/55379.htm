Here's a quick solution that worked for me. Browse to the drive of the folder you want to map.  Right click and choose "share with" You can choose your homegroup or specific people. Be sure to choose read/write options as appropriate.

 

Now browse the network to your computer name. When you double click the computer name, you'll see a list of shares including the folder you just shared. Right click and choose Map network Drive and select the drive letter you want to use. Click Finish and you are good to go!

 

If you use the subst command other recommend, you will have to enter every time your computer is rebooted or you will have to create a batch file that auto runs when the computer boots.

OK. As you see, that is a temporary system error at the server, so there's probably not much you can do about it. However, BT accounts are notoriously temperamental when accessed from a mail client as opposed to via webmail. You may, though, be able to take a few steps to make this sort of fault less likely.

The first is to sign in to bt.com and change your password. Once you've done so, enter the new password on the Servers tab of the account's properties dialogue.

Repeat the process, taking half of the messages each time until the dodgy message can be identified and dealt with in webmail.

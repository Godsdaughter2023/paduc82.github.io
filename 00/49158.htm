Removing write protection from a SD card. - Microsoft Community

dkeefer5 say:

Removing write protection from a SD card.

I have a scan disk SD card that goes into my phone. I am trying to copy music from my computer to it. However, it says that it is write protected. I have used it before to put media on it. It is not working now. Is there a way to remove the write protection from the disk? I have windows vista.
santosh y say:

Hi dkeefer5,

Try the following steps:

Step 1:

The SD card has a write-protection tab on the side that has slid down. This can happen when the card is being inserted into the computer. Try removing the card, push the tab back into place and try inserting it into the computer again. Read the manual that came with the SD card for help.

Step 2:

The SD card may be infected with a virus. Run a complete scan on the SD card to make sure that it is free from virus infection.

Step 3:

Try formatting the card using low level formatting and try again. Make sure you have a backup of the data on the SD card.

Step 3:

The phone might have formatted the SD card in a way that it is now inaccessible on your computer. In this case you would need to contact either the SD card manufacturer or the support for your phone.


Hope this help.

Thanks and Regards,

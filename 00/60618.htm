System Restore can be invoked in many ways, e.g. like so:

  1. Ask a friend to create a bootable Windows Repair flash disk on his Windows 10 PC, using the command RecoveryDrive.exe. Do NOT copy the system files.
  2. Plug this disk into your PC.
  3. Turn on your PC, then turn it off as soon as the spinning circle appears.
  4. Repeat Step 3 three times.
  5. Select "System Restore" from one of the menus that will appear. You do not need a password.
  6. When finished, plan ahead a little and create, test and document a spare admin account. Having a single admin account is high-risk stuff, same as having just one house key.

Since your one and only admin account is compromised, I can only think of three alternatives:

The first two methods may or may not work, depending on the available restore points.

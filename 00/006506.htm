todtown say:

Here's a quick and dirty way. Maybe not what you wanted, but..

 

Assuming your True/False column is Column A, beginning with Cell A2. Choose a blank column. In row 2 of that column put this formula:

 

=If(A2="True","a","")

 

This will insert a letter a if the corrosponding value in Column A is True. Otherwise it will put a blank. Copy this range, right click in A2 and chooose PasteSpecial and choose Paste Values. Now hightlight the range you just copied. Click Ctrl+1 to bring up the Format Cells dialog. Go to the Font tab. In the Font list choose WebDings. (It's 5th up from the bottom of the list). This will format the letter a to look like a check mark.

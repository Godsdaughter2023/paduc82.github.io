You could use this approach:

=B1 & TEXT(A1,"0000")

where B1 contains your 555 and A1 contains 44 formatted to show as 0044. If B1 might also be formatted to show up to 3 digits with leading zeros, then you could have this:

=TEXT(B1,"000") & TEXT(A1,"0000")

If the subscription is a renewal you are billed up to 3 days in advance of the end of your monthly billing cycle (based off when you signed up) to avoid billing issues. This ensures minutes are allotted on time. Minutes do not get awarded early. That additional subscription would also have its own 30 day cycle.

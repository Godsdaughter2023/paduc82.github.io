Sounds like there is a mismatch. Somewhere along the line something thinks it is printing on letter size paper.

Are you inserting the paper in a legal size paper tray, or feeding it through the manual paper feed.

In Word, check in the Page Layout tab > Page Setup group > Size dropdown.  Confirm that you have legal selected.  In the same group, check the margins. Make sure that the top and bottom margin size is reasonable.

Still in Word, switch to Print layout mode. Does the paper size look about right? Does the text display all the way down the page like you expect.  You can turn on the ruler to see if the size is displayed right: View tab > Show group > Ruler check box.

Go into File menu > Print command. Make sure the preview image looks good, that the right paper size is selected etc.

If everything looks good in Word, then your problem is with the Printer.

joe men say:

Hello, Lendsay.

Thank you for posting your query to Microsoft Community. We understand that you want to your contacts from your Android Phone to your Microsoft Lumia 540 phone. I'm here to help.

An internet connection is required to accomplish this. Please follow the steps below:

On your Android Phone:

  1. Go to Settings > Accounts > Google.
  2. Tap on your Google email account.
  3. Put a check mark on Sync Contacts.
    • Once you do this, wait for a few minutes and your contacts will sync to your Google email account.

On your Lumia 540:

  1. Go to Settings > email+accounts.
  2. Tap on add an account.
  3. Tap on Google.
  4. Enter your Google email account credentials.
    • Your Google email account will be added to your Lumia 540.
      1. Tap on your Google account in the email+accounts menu.
      2. Put a check mark on the Contacts under the Contacts to sync.
      3. Tap on the check icon.

Your contacts are now synced on your Lumia 540.

Hope this helps.

Since you're creating these fields manually and they're all in the same table row, in your DESCRIPTION column you could use a construction like:
{IF{MERGEFIELD jobMaterial.item_number}= "LABOUR*" "{MERGEFIELD jobMaterial.name}¶

"}{IF{MERGEFIELD jobMaterial.item_number}= "RUBBISH*" "{MERGEFIELD jobMaterial.name}¶

2. Use OpenOffice

Please follow the instruction below to disable sending Read Receipts in Outlook for Android:

1 . On a PC or Mac, sign in Outlook Web App(OWA).

2 . Click the gear icon and choose Mail.

3 . Go to General->Mobile devices.

4 . Check Don’t send read receipts for messages read on devices that use Exchange ActiveSync.

 

Then your Outlook for Android will not send Read Receipts anymore.

bill manville say:

That is an unfortunate feature of data validation - it can be wiped out by Pasting a different cell.

 

The options open to you are:
- disable all means of pasting other than Paste Values
OR
- a Worksheet_Change procedure in which you detect that the validation has gone from a cell you expected to have it, you put the validation criteria back and you check in code whether the criteria are satisfied by the value entered

OR
- (simpler than above) use a Worksheet_Change procedure instead of data validation (so you just have one means of performing the test and it is not nullified by pasting.

 

If you want to proceed down one of these routes and need some help getting started, come back.

  • Endnote Separators appear above the endnotes, not below them. The separators separate the endnotes from the text that precedes them.
  • Standard Endnote separators are rather short [approx. 2" in length] starting at the left margin. They don't go "across the top of the page".
  • Endnotes can be specified to be placed at the end of Sections, but they only appear for Sections which do contain endnotes, not on every page of the Section.
  • Endnote separators typically do not appear at the top of a page... That's why they're calledendnote separators :-) They always & only appear at the end of the content on the last page of the document or Section.
  • If there isn't enough room the separator will get shifted to the next page, but it will be followed only by the endnotes pertaining to that Section.

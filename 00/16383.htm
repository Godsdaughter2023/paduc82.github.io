You may have to forcibly set your domain IP addressing for your LAN adapter.What I mean is:

From "Network and Sharing Center" -

Left side select "Change adapter settings"

Right click on the Ethernet adapter and select "Properties"

Click on the lower right "Properties" button

Change the lower radio button to :Use the following DNS server addresses:"

Enter in your preferred DNS server

Click OK

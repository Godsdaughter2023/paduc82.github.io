    Run as administrator

  6. Change the Value data: to exefile
  7. Browse to and then click on the following registry key:
    exefile
  8. Browse to and then click on the following registry key:

    HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\exefile
  9. With exefileselected, right-click (Default) and click Modify…
  10. Change the Value data: to "%1" %*
  11. Browse to and then click on the following registry key:
    "%1" %*
  12. Browse to and then click on the following registry key:

    KEY_CLASSES_ROOT\exefile\shell\open

You can reinstall as many times as you like as long as you reinstall the exact edition.

  1. Type troubleshooting in the search bar. 
  2. Select Troubleshooting.
  3. Select View all on the top left corner.
  4. Click on Hardware and Devices.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to run the troubleshooter.

Method 2: I suggest you to Update display drivers and check if it helps.

To Update display driver, please follow these steps:

  1. Press Windows + X keys and click Device manager.
  2. Click to expand Display adapters, right click the adapter and click Update.
  3. Restart the computer and the basic driver will be updated automatically.

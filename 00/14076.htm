MissLilu,

After posting my above reply saying I had a similar issue I ended up posting a separate thread about it. And, I got help and an answer: Link to thread with Answer

The quick and dirty of it is below:

First (and not included in the answer thread) is that the "Restrict Editing" except for "Filling in Forms" was apparently designed only for use with the Legacy Form tools and it breaks the newer Content Controls (see here for a comprehensive explanation about this by Greg Maxey).

Second, (and more to the point) the Repeating Section Content Controls are not strictly a container for other content controls, they will repeat anything that is contained within. And, more importantly (for your specific form), they are a content control themselves. So, locking for editing (either way) still allows content controls to be edited, including the Repeating Section Content Controls. Therefore, in order to not be able to change any of your static text under "Background", you need to check the "Contents cannot be edited" property under the Repeating Section Content Control - it will still allow you to fill in the controls nested within.

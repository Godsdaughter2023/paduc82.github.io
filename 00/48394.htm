Hi Gracie,

Since you've mentioned that you would like to resize the text, apps, and other items in Windows 10, follow these steps to resize everything:

  1. Right-click on desktop and select Display settings.
  2.  Under Change the size of text, apps, and other items: 100% (Recommended) use and drag the slider to the right to increase the size while dragging it to the left decreases the size.
  3. Click Apply.

Note: You may see a notification Some apps won't respond to scaling changes until you sign out. Just click on Sign out now then sign back in.

If you just prefer to change the text size,

  1. Right-click on desktop and select Display settings.
  2. Tap Advanced display settings.
  3. Scroll down and click on Advanced sizing of text and other items.
  4. Click on the drop-down menu under Change only the text size Choose the item you prefer to change then choose the text size. You also have the option to make it bold by checking the box beside Bold.

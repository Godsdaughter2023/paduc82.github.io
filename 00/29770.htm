vaseem ahmed say:

Hi,

The issue here can be one of several issues, the following may apply:

·         The HDMI graphics card may support HDMI Video, but may not support HDMI Audio

·         You may need to set the HDMI audio device as the default sound device

·         You may need to update your HDMI video card drivers for your version of Windows

·         If your HDMI video card does not support audio, you may need to connect additional audio cables between the PC and TV

 Step 1:

For the steps for some of these issues see the following No sound tutorial (look on the Check your Hardware tab under HDMI cables).

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/help/no-sound-in-windows

Step 2:

Follow these steps and check if it helps:

a.       Install the latest drivers for your sound card.

b.       Connect the HDMI cable from PC to the assigned TV HDMI PC output.

c.       With the TV off, restart your PC.

d.       Go to Control Panel, Sound and then Playback. HDMI should be available.

e.       Right click the HDMI output and select Test; the sound bar should move. Classify the HDMI as Default Device.

f.        Turn on the TV and select the assigned HDMI PC jack for the input.

The following article could be helpful.

“Connect your computer to a TV”

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows7/Connect-your-computer-to-a-TV

No one seems to have addressed the issue of your keyboard shortcut not working; I personally would not want to use any of the suggested alternatives, which are much more trouble. Here's what I'd do:

  1. Go to File | Options | Customize Ribbon.
  2. Beside Keyboard shortcuts, click Customize...
  3. In the Customize Keyboard dialog, select any command (the one that's already selected will do) and press Ctrl+Shift+Hyphen in the Press new shortcut key box (it will be displayed as "Ctrl+Shift+_").

  6. To reassign the shortcut, select Common Symbols at the bottom of the Categories list.
  7. Scroll down to Nonbreaking Hyphen, press Ctrl+Shift+Hyphen, and click Assign.

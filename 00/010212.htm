mscher say:

Also have this problem with Outlook 2016 but I just figured it out.

There are two options to the answer:

NUMBER 1

By default Outlook shows email messages marked for deletion with a strikeout line through the messages. You may have set up your Outlook to hide such messages. Please check your Outlook settings via the View menu > Current View (Change View), as you may have to turn off the "Hide Messages Marked for Deletion" feature and just select "IMAP Messages".

NUMBER 2 (my preference)

I went into the IMAP settings:

1. Go to File-> Account Settings-> Account Settings…
2. Double click on your IMAP account.
3. Click on More Options… and then open the Advanced tab. 

and "UNCHECKED" under "Deleted Items" Mark items for deletion but do not move them automatically. Items marked for deletion will be permanently deleted when the items in the mailbox are purged.

charles kenyon say:

First, "Enable all macros" is fine for diagnostic work but should not be your working state.

Where are your macros being stored? By default, they would be stored in the NewMacros module of the Normal.dotm template. When you record the macro, you are given the choice of storing in normal.dotm, the document's template (often normal.dotm) or in the document.

If they are being stored in your normal.dotm file, something must be preventing saving changes to the normal template. The culprit here is often a poorly-written Add-In file.

See Add-Ins in Microsoft Word

You could put an autoopen macro in the Normal.dot template that contained the following commands

Application.PrintPreview = True
ActiveWindow.View.Zoom.PageFit = wdPageFitBestFit
ActiveWindow.View.Magnifier = False

but why not just use Print Layout view as that gives you access to all of Word's functions without first having to Exit from Print Preview for many of them.

jay freedman say:

Canceling an Office 365 account won't do anything to the documents, and you should still be able to open them in Office Online.

There are two points to consider, though:

  • The Word Online app is still significantly limited in some respects compared to the full Word 2013 that comes with Office 365. You should try out Word Online to be sure it will do everything you want to do, before you decide to rely on it.
  • If you do start with Office 365 and then decide to cancel it, first be sure to move all your documents off the OneDrive storage that's part of 365 and into the (smaller) storage that comes for free with a Microsoft account. Or store it on your computer, and upload it only when you need to edit it in Word Online.

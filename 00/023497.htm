Andreas.

Private Sub CommandButton1_Click()
  ChangeLabel Label1
End Sub

Private Sub ChangeLabel(Label As MSForms.Label)
  With Label
    Select Case .Caption
      Case "Warning"
        .BackColor = 6 'Yellow
      Case "Alert"
        .BackColor = 3 'Red
      Case Else
        .BackColor = xlNone
    End Select
  End With
End Sub

Hi Timex14

Your Skype account would expire after 5 years of inactivity.

Since October 2016, Skype accounts are now linked to Microsoft Accounts just like other Microsoft Services such as OneDrive. Due to this change, it is no longer possible to delete your Skype account without also closing your Microsoft Account.

The best thing you can do is no longer sign into that account and use the available options of transferring your contacts and credits to the new account.

Before you close your Microsoft account, let's make sure you aren't leaving anything important relating to Skype behind:

Sign in to Skype.

https://support.microsoft.com/help/12412

Important information such as files and contacts are saved.
Your Skype Credit balance has been used. Once your account is closed, you can't get it back.
Active subscriptions have been cancelled so you don’t continue to get charged. If you have Auto-recharge enabled and cancel your account without first cancelling your subscription, you will continue to be charged until your account has been permanently closed in 60 days. https://support.skype.com/faq/FA633

You can close your Microsoft account by following these steps:

Go to our Microsoft account closure page (http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkId=523898). You may be prompted to sign in or verify your password.
Make sure you are signed in to the Microsoft account you want to close and select Next.
In the Select a reason drop-down list, choose the reason you're closing the account.
Select Mark account for closure.
Your Microsoft account will be closed in 60 days. We wait 60 days before permanently deleting accounts in case you change your mind or need to access something before it's gone. You can change your mind any time during the 60 days, just sign in (https://go.skype.com/myaccount) to cancel the closure.

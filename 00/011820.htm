You have been using the wrong approach to this, I am almost certain.

Headers is a shorthand way of saying "running headers." The idea is to have something at the top of the page to help you figure out quickly what it is you are reading.

A number of people have mentioned the StyleRef Field. Take a look at the link and possibly Useful StyleRef tricks by Suzanne Barnhill. To someone with a good understanding of how headers and footers tie into sections, headers and footers make sense. However, few users have that understanding. Using Headers and Footers by Suzanne Barnhill

Generally, again, the StyleRef field is the way to get unique headers. It doesn't handle everything, but much more than you might expect.

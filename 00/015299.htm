john w. vinson say:

Sure. Just open the Report in design mode and drag the right edge of the report detail to the left until it snugs up against the rightmost actual textbox/label/line/other control. The header section should follow, if not, do the same there. 

Access will often make the "used" portion of a report section a lot bigger than needed and cause this kind of problem. Even if it's just whitespace, it is seen as being outside the margin, and it will go to a (blank) second page.

Sometimes there's an annoying, automatically generated Line or Rectangle object inserted by the report wizard. Just give the wizard a brisk smack on the mazzard and resize the offending object if so.

What you're referring to [ ¶ ] is one of the nonprinting characters, display of which can toggled on/off in several different ways:

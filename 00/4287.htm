Here's a cut and paste from my previous post on this subject:

"Open Regedit and navigate to HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Internet Explorer\Zoom. In the right-hand pane is a listing for ZoomFactor. Double-click on it and click the button next to Decimal.

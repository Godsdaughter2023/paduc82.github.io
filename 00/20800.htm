How do I calculate the number of days worked between two dates for part time workers?

hansv mvp say:

Excel 2010 has a new function NETWORKDAYS.INTL that lets you specify "weekend days".

For example, with a start date in cell A1 and an end date in cell B1, the number of days worked in between for an employee who works on Monday, Thursday and Friday would be

=NETWORKDAYS.INTL(A1, B1, "0110011")

The string "0110011" is 7 characters long. For each day of the week, starting with Monday, 0 indicates a working day and 1 a non-working day.

See the Excel help, or http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/excel-help/networkdays-intl-function-HA010354379.aspx

a. user say:

Hi Dave,

Thank you for choosing Windows Insider Program.

I can imagine the inconvenience you are experiencing and will certainly help you in the right direction to fix the issue. The alarm clock is set to 7 AM by default. I tried to set it to different time frame on my machine but could not reproduce any issue setting the alarm clock on either of the build versions.

So, I would suggest you to try the following diagnostic steps to troubleshoot this issue on your system.

Method 1:

Remove the 7 AM time and set a new alarm with your desired time.

  1. Click Start button.
  2. Type Alarms in the Search bar and select Alarms & Clock.
  3. in the Alarms & Clock window, click Manage button (Pencil icon).
  4. Click on the X near 7 AM time.
  5. Now, click on the New (+) button.
  6. Set the new alarm and click Save .

Restart the Alarms and clock window and check if the issue persists.

Method 2:

Check if the Date and Time settings on your system are correctly configured.

  1. Right click on the date & time in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
  2. Click Adjust date/time.
  3. Then click Change date and time settings and the set the clock accordingly.
  4. Click the Change time zone button.
  5. Select your time zone and tick automatically adjust clock for daylight saving time and press OK.
  6. Press Apply, and OK.

In addition to that, I would also suggest you to set multiple alarms and check if they notify at the set time.

Hope this information is helpful. Please write back to us if you need further assistance, we'll be glad to assist you.

Hi Carla, this is a known issue in the 1803 Update,

It is the recovery drive, you are not meant to see it, it is not meant to have a drive letter, remove the drive letter and everything will return to normal:

Click your Start Button, type cmd, then right click Command Prompt and choose 'Run as Administrator'

Run this command and hit Enter

diskpart

In the second command window, run this command and hit Enter

select volume F

Run this command and hit Enter

remove letter=F

Close Command Prompt and restart your PC

That's It!

xircal say:

If you're using Firefox as your browser, it makes five daily backup of your bookmarks. To restore them, do the following.

  1. Launch Firefox and then hit CTRL+SHIFT+B
  2. At the top of the menu, click Import and Backup followed by Restore. You'll get a list of dates you can restore to.

In Internet Explorer.

  1. Launch IE and then click the star on the right.
  2. Click the down arrow to the right of "Add to favourites" and choose "Import and Export".
  3. "Import" is selected by default so click Next and then follow the prompts.

I don't know how Google Chrome or any of the other browsers works though I'm afraid.

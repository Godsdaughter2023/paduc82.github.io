Change the frequency of checking mail?

Is there a way to change the frequency with which Outlook 2011 for Mac checks for mail?  I'm not even sure what the default frequency is!

Thanks.
talking moose say:
For POP accounts you can go to  Outlook menu -->  Preferences... --> Schedules and edit the Send & Receive All schedule, which is to check every 10 minutes.

I don't suggest lowering this from 10 minutes since many services providers limit your number of connections per hour. The POP protocol was not intended to be an immediate delivery service.

If you're using IMAP accounts then go to  Outlook menu -->  Preferences... --> Accounts and select your IMAP account. Click the Advanced... button and select the Use IMAP IDLE option. If your IMAP account supports IDLE then you will receive mail as soon as it arrives at your server.

Exchange accounts will check mail once per minute. This schedule cannot be changed.

Hope this helps!

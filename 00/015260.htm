In Outlook.com, it is possible that your alias  *** Email address is removed for privacy *** emails will go to your Inbox and your original account *** Email address is removed for privacy ***'s email will go to a separate folder. To do this, we need to create an email rule. You can follow the steps below:

1. Login to Outlook.com.

2. Click the gear icon at the upper right and click Options.

3. Under "Customizing Outlook.com", click Rules for sorting new messages.

4. Check your email rules first and if you see any rule for you alias account, delete it first.

5. Once done, create a new email rule the same as: "Sender's address is - *** Email address is removed for privacy *** - Move to Inbox".

6. You can also create an email rule to your original account the same as: "Sender's address is - *** Email address is removed for privacy *** - Move to a new folder".

jamesjoey say:

Access Printing out 2 pages of Report When There is Only 1

I have a report which Access seems to think there are 2 pages but there is only 1. The first page prints out and ends well before the bottom of the page. The second page seems to be identical to the first page but with no data.

Any ideas??

James

hansv mvp say:

Check whether the report fits within the page margins. For example, if your report has portrait orientation, a left and right margin of 1" and is printed on Letter paper, the maximum available width is 8.5" - 2x1" = 6.5". If the report is wider than that, you'll get an extra page for each page with data.

If necessary, reduce the width of the report, or decrease the left or right margin.

Hi Brage Salhus Bunk,

Welcome to Skype Community forum!

By default, Skype will use the default audio device you have on your computer. If it is not automatically selected, you may check the Skype settings and the default audio device on your computer. 

Skype

1. Log in to Skype application.

2. Click Tools > Options > Audio settings.

3. Select your audio device then click Move up, that device should have the green check mark as well. Click Save.

On your Computer

1. Right-click the volume icon on your taskbar.

2. Select Playback device, then check if there's green check mark and  Default Device on the audio device you wanted to use.

3. After that click the Recording tab and check if there's green check mark and Default Device on the audio device you wanted to use.

Once you were able to select the audio device as default, make a test call in Skype and let me know if it will work or if the issue persist.

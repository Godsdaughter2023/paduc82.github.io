jay freedman say:

This isn't easy to do in Word.

First, to set up the kind of multilevel numbering you have, you should use the procedure in https://shaunakelly.com/word/numbering/numbering20072010.html. In part 4 "Create a list style", step 5 "Set up the indents" describes how to set the location of the start of the text after the number.

The difficulty is that, as Shauna says there, "they're all measured from the left margin." That doesn't take into account the width of the number(s) at the start of the paragraph. You can adjust the indent of each level to bring the text to the position you want, by trial and error. It works reasonably well when all the levels have single-digit numbers, even in a proportional font, but you're in trouble if any of the numbers reach 10 or more.

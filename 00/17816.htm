Contact Group can be accessed only from within the contacts folder, not as a separate folder under contacts.

Hence you must open the group contact and click on add member and select the members from main list to add the contact to the group.

You may create a new contact folder and drag and drop the contacts into the new folder and see if it helps.

1) Right click on the contact folder and select New folder.
2) Ensure that Contacts folder is selected, enter a name and click on OK.
3) Now drag and drop the contacts.
4) Right click on the newly created contact folder and go to Properties, Outlook Address Book and check 'Show this folder as an e-mail address book'.
5) To use the contacts from new contact folder, open the address book (directly or through 'To'>address book and click on the drop down under Address Book) abd select the folder.

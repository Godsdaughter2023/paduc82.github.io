To be able to use the Skype translator on Skype for Web, please follow the steps below:

1. Select your contact.

2. Select the Translator icon:

  • on Windows Desktop or Skype for Web: The Globe icon .
  • on Skype for Windows 10: The translator icon  in the message area. 

3. Turn Translator On. 

4. Use the drop-down next to your contact's name to select the language for translation while in a call or instant messaging.

5. While on a call:

  • Wearing headphones is the best way to understand the translations.
  • Make sure you speak clearly and wait for translations to appear.
  • As you or your contact speaks, it will be translated in your headphones and the translations will also appear on your screen.

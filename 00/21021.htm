Any time you use aggregate functions such as Sum, all other fields in the SELECT clause must appear in an GROUP BY clause:

SELECT InvoiceDetail.[Rpt Date], InvoiceDetail.[RMS Placement Date], InvoiceDetail.[Inv #], InvoiceDetail.[Cust #], InvoiceDetail.[Agent/UPN], InvoiceDetail.[$ Coll RMS], InvoiceDetail.[$ Coll Farmers], (Sum([InvoiceDetail]![$ Coll RMS]+[InvoiceDetail]![$ Coll Farmers])*0.115) AS [Calc Fee]

GROUP BY InvoiceDetail.[Rpt Date], InvoiceDetail.[RMS Placement Date], InvoiceDetail.[Inv #], InvoiceDetail.[Cust #], InvoiceDetail.[Agent/UPN], InvoiceDetail.[$ Coll RMS], InvoiceDetail.[$ Coll Farmers]

=CountIf(A1:A12,”Focus”)+ContIf(B13:B24,”Focus”)

One thing to check if you have wireless connection to your router--left click on the wireless icon in the lower right and select your wireless network and see if Connect Automatically is On for your network. If there are lots of wireless networks in the area, the computer might have to go through each one to find yours. Check the other networks in the area that belong to your neighbors. Make sure your wireless does not try to connect to any of those and connect automatically is NOT selected. Some folks don't protect their wireless and allow anyone to connect.

To test, use a cable to connect your computer to an ethernet port on your router and see if there is any problem when you start the computer. There should not be.

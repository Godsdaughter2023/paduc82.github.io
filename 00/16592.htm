CALLOUTS - Microsoft Community

williamgrumitt say:

CALLOUTS

Just upgraded to Office 2016.  The callouts to demonstrate parts of a photo I am inserting to word have changed from the old neat black and white with times new roman to an ugly affair with bold blue lines and a fill.  Is there an easy way to get back?

jay freedman say:

In the Drawing Tools | Format tab that appears when a callout is selected, open the gallery in the Shape Styles group and click the top left icon, the one with a black outline and white fill.

You can use the Shape Outline button to the right of the gallery to change the weight of the outline if desired.

Once the callout looks the way you want it, right-click it and click Set as Default Shape. New callouts that you add to the document will have the same formatting.

Unfortunately, this default persists only in the current document during the current session. You'll have to reset it each time you open this or another document, or restart Word.

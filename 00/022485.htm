amit tandon say:

To give an example:

=(NOT(ISNA(MATCH($A$1:$A$10,$B$2:$B$5,0))))

This will return TRUE / FALSE for each entry in the range $A$1:$A$10 for which there is a match in the range $B$2:$B$5.

And using below will return the SUM of all values in the range ($C$1:$C$10) where the above is TRUE.

=SUMPRODUCT(($C$1:$C$10)*(NOT(ISNA(MATCH($A$1:$A$10,$B$2:$B$5,0)))))

Hope this helps.

  1. From Settings, tap Email+Accounts, and then tap Add an account.
  2. On the Add an account screen, tap Google.
  3. Enter your email address and password, and then tap Next.

  1. On the Google Settings screen under content to sync, tap Contacts and Calendar.
  2. On the Change Google settings screen, tap Continue.

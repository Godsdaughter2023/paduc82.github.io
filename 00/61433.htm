Hello Bill, 

Please ignore Frederik's rude responses.  You are not alone in receiving this message.  

You were correct. This message is from a Samsung application installed on our laptops. The application creating the message is "Fast Start".   "C:\Program Files (x86)\Samsung\Easy Display Manager\SmartSetting.exe"

The only way I have found to get rid of these messages is to disable Fast Start. You can do this by hitting FN-F1 and then flipping the switch on Fast Start.  Sometimes renaming the exe, after disabling the feature, is necessary.

If you disable this feature, your laptop will not automatically power on when you open the lid of your laptop. You will need to press the power button after you open the lid.  The laptop may also use more power while in sleep mode, but I have not tested this.

I have not yet found a way to keep the benefits of fast boot, but disable the annoying alert.  There are a couple XML files which correspond to Fast Start in the program directory. I am still experimenting, but progress is slow since I need to wait a week between each change. Haha. 

I hope this helps.

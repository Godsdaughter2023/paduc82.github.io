john mcghie say:

Window/Orphan and "Keep Lines Together" are mutually exclusive: decide which one you want to use and turn the other off.  Keep Lines will throw the entire paragraph to the next page if any part of it won't fit on the previous page.

It sounds to me as though you might have deleted a section break, and therefore altered your pagination.

As Phillip suggests, turn on you "Show non-printing characters" so you can see what you are doing.  But be aware that will the non-printing characters shown, the pagination of the entire document becomes inaccurate (because display room is taken by the non-printing characters).  For exact pagination, you need to hide those.

Sub AddDeleteColumns()
x = InputBox("Add: select a or A" & vbCr & "Delete: select d or D", "Add-Delete Columns", "")
x = UCase(x)
Select Case x
Case Is = "A"
Selection.EntireColumn.Insert '<<< add on the left side
Case Is = "D"
If MsgBox("delete entire column ?", vbOKCancel + vbExclamation) = vbCancel Then Exit Sub
Selection.EntireColumn.Delete
Case Else
Exit Sub
End Select
End Sub

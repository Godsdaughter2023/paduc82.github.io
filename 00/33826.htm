ken onweller say:

I have found the green video screen (Windows 10 Netflix and Hulu apps) only happens when the Hypervisor is turned ON.

To turn it off open an Administrative Dos Prompt:

WIN-X and then hit A.  You should see "Administrator:  Command Prompt" on title.

Type:  bcdedit

The very last line should say "hypervisorlaunchtype  Auto" if it is on.

Type:  bcdedit /set hypervisorlaunchtype off

reboot your pc.

when it resumes, open another Administrative Dos Prompt and again type:

bcdedit

Verify the last line now says "hypervisorlaunchtype Off".

On my particular PC this is all that is necessary to allow the Windows 10 Netflix and Hulu apps to work again with no green video.

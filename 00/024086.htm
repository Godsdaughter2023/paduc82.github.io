In Outlook 2016 some of you may know that Control + 2 shortcut brings up Calendar in full screen. This is bypasses a problem in Outlook 2016 where the small Calendar on the right does not show events.

Click "New Group" into Home (mail) ribbon .Right Click and rename to "Calendar". Select from dropdown "Commands not in Ribbon", select Calendar Click "Add >>" to move it into the new ribbon item Calendar Click "Ok" and a Calendar ribbon command appears on the right most side of Mail ribbon.

Now when the Calendar icon is clicked the full screen Calendar is brought up bypassing and problems with the little calendar on the right which does not work as well as Outlook 2010 version.

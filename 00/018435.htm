The Report Header is the one at the beginning of the report, it is not a group header.  The ForceNew Page option does not apply to group headers.

You can suppress the page header by making it invisible in the report header and the group footer sections' Print event

   Me.Section(2).Visible = False

and make it visible in the group header's Print event.

   Me.Section(2).Visible = True

On the Developer tab of the ribbon, click to turn on Design Mode.

Double-click the Date and Time picker.

This will create a CallbackKeyDown event procedure for the date and time picker.

Ex  : (first letter in the word not capitalized) : india, japan,china  etc

Ex : ( capitalized automatically) : Germany,Singapore, Russia .. etc..

Please explain me about this...

Thanks in advance,,

Regards

Naveeen Bathina 

jay freedman say:

The automatic capitalizations occur because an option (File > Options > Proofing > AutoCorrect Options > Automatically use suggestions from the spelling checker) is turned on, and the examples you listed have exactly one suggestion in Word's spelling dictionary.

The country names that are not automatically capitalized have other meanings in English when they are not capitalized: "india" may or may not be capitalized in the term "india ink"; "japan" refers to a hard black finish applied to metal objects; and "china" refers to a type of porcelain used for dishes and other food-related objects. Word will not automatically replace an item based only on the spelling dictionary if its dictionary entry contains more than one suggestion.

If you want these and other country names to be capitalized automatically, add them to the list in the AutoCorrect Options dialog.

Information with regard to your concern is still unavailable, it is not possible to go back to the previous build. We suggest that you use our Feedback Hub for comments and suggestions.

For the issue that you are facing, we suggest to perform a soft reset. This method will not remove anything at all from your phone, and it is completely safe to attempt in any situation.

To perform a soft reset:
1. Make sure your phone is turned on.
2. Press and hold the volume down and Power button until your phone vibrates and restarts.

Take note that you should not touch the device screen during the process (not even when the 'Slide down to power off' prompt appears).

After performing a soft reset, try to check if the issue persists.

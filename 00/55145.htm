Hi, 

 

Thank you for posting you concern here in Microsoft Community. 

 

Follow the steps below on how to setup Family Safety on your PC: 

 

  1. Download Windows Essentials from Microsoft : Windows Essentials

  2. Choose Family Safety from the list, then click Install. 

  3. Once Windows Essentials is installed, click Close. 

  4. Open Control Panel and it will open the Windows Essentials end user license agreement which you can read, then click Agree when you're ready to proceed. 

  5. You can now Sign In to Family Safety with Microsoft Account. ( If you don't have a Microsoft, you need to create one.)  

  6. Once you're signed in to Family Safety, you can choose which account you want to monitor, then click Next. 

  7. Match your Windows account with a Family Safety member or add the account to it.

Hello Susan. Please follow these steps to re-enable Windows Defender for Windows 8.1.

  • Open the Start screen.
  • Search for "Control Panel".
  • In the search box, type "Action Center" & press enter.
  • Click on "Action Center" with the blue flag icon.
  • Drop down the "Security" tab.
  • Click on turn on messages about virus protection, if you do not see a red popup.
  • At the red popup, at the bottom right corner, click 'View installed antivirus apps."
  • If you see another antivirus other than Windows Defender, quit/exit the antivirus.
  • Now, click on Windows Defender & press Turn On.

Please note if you notice a performance change, you may want to disable Windows Defender.

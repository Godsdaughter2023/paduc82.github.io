brian tillman [outlook mvp 2007-2019] say:
The way I did it was to click File>Options>Proofing>AutoCorrect Options, then on the AutoCorrect tab, in the "Replace" field, I entered "m2" and in the "With" field, I typed "M", then went to Character Map, located the superscript 2 character, selected it, clicked Copy, then pasted it in the "With" field right behind the "M".  I then clicked Add and then OK, then OK.

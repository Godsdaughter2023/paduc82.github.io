a. user say:

Hi Santosh,

  • What is the make and model of your Phone?

Please be informed, you cannot transfer/import the contacts in your email account to your phone. Instead, you can sync those contacts in an app or access directly via web browser. When you email a contact from Hotmail/Outlook.com email account, it picks up that person's email address from the People address book (which is where their contact info lives).

To sync or view your people address book contacts in your phone, there are three ways :

  • If it is a Windows phone, you will get a default app called 'People app', in which you can access the same people address book contacts.
  • If it is an iPhone or android you can download the respective Outlook app for that particular phone and sync those contacts, with the help of Exchange Active Sync (EAS) protocol.
  • Directly access https://people.live.com via web browser.

In future, if you have any issues related to Outlook.com, do get back to us. We will be happy to assist you.

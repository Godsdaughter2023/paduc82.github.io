wenbins say:

This is caused by a process called toaster.exe. This process belongs to Dell DataSafe Local Backup. One simple way is to uninstall this software. But it seems that this software is integrated into Dell factory image restore, I am not sure it is safe to uninstall. I checked the startup service shown in the msconfig, and found a familiar name, which is SoftThinks Agent Service.SoftThinks is the software vendor who provides this problematic software. So here is the way to get rid of the process toaster.exe:

  1. Open control panel
  2. Double click on the ‘Administrative Tools’
  3. Double clikc on the ‘Services’
  4. Scroll down and find the ‘SoftThinks Agent Service’, change the startup type to ‘Disabled’, and then click ‘OK’.
  5. Restart your computer, problem solved ;).

a. user say:

Hi ANil,

 

Yes, you can upload the photos to your SkyDrive along with the people tag. Here are the detailed steps:

 

1. Open the folder the contained the photos to be uploaded in SkyDrive.

 

2. Make sure that People tags can be viewed in the photo:

 

    a. Right-click the photo.

    b. Select Properties.

    c. Switch to Details tab and check the Tags.

 

3. If photos are all set for uploading, you can refer to the steps below for ways on how to upload them to your SkyDrive account.

 

Using web browser:

 

  a. Login your account at http://skydrive.live.com.

 

  b. Create a new folder by clicking Create then Folder.

 

  c. Open the folder and click Upload in the upper part of the page.

 

  d. Upload the files from your computer.

 

Using SkyDrive application:

 

   a. Download SkyDrive application installer from the link below:

 

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/skydrive/download

 

   b. After successful installation, drag and drop your files into the SkyDrive folder in your computer.

 

Files will be automatically uploaded to your cloud storage. Time of uploading depend on your internet connection. Login your account at http://skydrive.live.com and double-check the people tags.

 

For more information about SkyDrive, check the link below:

 

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows-8/cloud-connected#1TC=t1

 

If you have further questions, please let us know.

 

Thanks!

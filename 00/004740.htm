massive timesink say: If any of you other five are still following this thread, or for anyone else dismayed by the inability to resize chart margins by click and drag, here is a kludge that I am using. It involves some trial and error, but does provide the functionality that MS didn't bother to include. 

PAGE LAYOUT>MARGINS 
Set all margins and headers to zero 

VIEW>ZOOM>CUSTOM 
Enlarge until horizontal margins are filled 

PAGE LAYOUT>MARGINS 
Bump up bottom margin until chart is right sized vertically

For final fine tuning you may need to use the scroll arrows to get everything properly aligned. 

SAVE the chart 

This will save all your work, including the scrolled position of the chart.
The chart should then reopen looking as you left it.

a. user say:

Hi,

Unfortunately, unlimited subscriptions are not available in Skype Connect. For all outgoing calls through Skype Connect to mobiles and landlines, Skype charges a per minute charge. This is taken from the Skype Credit that you allocate to the SIP Profile involved. Calls are rounded up to the first 30 seconds and then in 6 second intervals thereafter.

If you call the US a lot, you could save on your calling with US minute bundles. Each bundle includes a monthly allowance of 5,000 minutes to call mobiles and landlines in the US. The number of US minute bundles you enter cannot exceed the number of channels you have entered. If the bundle’s minutes run out before the next renewal date, calls to the US will be charged from your Skype Manager's available Skype Credit balance at Skype Connect standard call rates.

For more information on Skype Connect, you can refer to our PDF User Guides here.

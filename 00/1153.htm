If you continue to be met with the message that you are not able to get signed in due to being offline the next thing I would suggest you do is a long power cycle of your network.  This would entail you disconnecting your router and modem for 5 minutes and then reconnecting and rebooting your system.  Upon doing this, launch Xbox Music and monitor for sign in.

Delete all traces of your phone on the iDrive and remove all traces of your car ( should be named BMW xxxxx ). Install the N810 application on your phone and make sure it is running before attempting to pair you phone to the car again.

Follow the usual Bluetooth pairing procedure to pair up with your iDrive and be patient when you see "Please wait..." on your iDrive. Depending on the volume of contacts on your phone, it will take a number of minutes to upload the contacts initially. Once done, you should see your complete phonebook and be able to match caller id to your phonebook as well. If you have contacts with multiple phone numbers, they will show in parentheses. For example, John (M) and John (W) indicating John's Mobile and John's work number respectively.

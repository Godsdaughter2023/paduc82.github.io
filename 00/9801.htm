doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

This may be the cause of the behavior:

 

See http://support.microsoft.com/kb/832897

While if the option to Update fields before printing is set, the filename field will be updated for the printing of the document, that action is not then saved with the document, even by manually saving the document IF the updating of that field is the only change that has been made to the document.

The reason for that is, the updating of the filename field does not render the document as "dirty" from the point of view of it having been changed and possibly needing to be saved.

That was done intentionally, to avoid the case where you open a file, print it, and close only to get the “do you want to save your changes” dialog box, which was an oft-reported “bug”.

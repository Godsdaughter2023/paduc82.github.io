When typing a date in a Date/Time field - Microsoft Community

ivan hartman say:

When typing a date in a Date/Time field "20/11/2011" it changes automatically to "20/11/11324" What can be wrong?

When typing a date in a Date/Time field "20/11/2011" it changes automatically to "20/11/11324" Or if i select a date from the Date picker it comes out funny and when pressing enter message not valid date appers. What can be wrong?
john w. vinson say:

That's the problem: the dd/MM/yyy format in the settings. "yy" means a two digit year (e.g. 11); "yyyy" means a four digit year (2011); but yyy combines a two digit year with the sequential day of the year. E.g. today is the 283rd day of the year so:

?format(date,"dd/mm/yyy")
10/10/11283

Change the computer's format to use either yy or yyyy and that problem should go away.

The excel problem appears to be unrelated and refers to what would seem to be a damaged workbook.

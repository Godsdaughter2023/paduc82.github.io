The issue isn't your Axim, it's that WMDC can't "see" Outlook 2013.  As far as WMDC is concerned, you don't have Outlook installed on your computer, so there's nothing to sync with.

Outlook 2013 supports OTA sync of email, contacts, calendar and tasks via a Live account.

Rose-DC,

What you should have done is to set a baseline right after the plan was developed but before any work was done.

In Project the Start and Finish fields are the estimated schedule. They will change based on schedule dynamics (i.e changes as the plan is executed).

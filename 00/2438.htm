stefan blom say:

To force Word to show a single page even with smaller zoom ratios, you have to make use of a macro such as the following:

Sub SetCustomZoomForSinglePage()
With ActiveWindow.View
   .Type = 3  'Print Layout view
   .Zoom.PageColumns = 1
   .Zoom.Percentage = 80  'set the zoom to 80%
End With

End Sub

The above macro sets the zoom to 80% and specifies that a single page should be displayed. You can assign the macro to a keyboard shortcut and/or to a button on the Quick Access Toolbar. See http://www.gmayor.com/installing_macro.htm.

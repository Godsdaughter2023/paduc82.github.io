To backup your favorites

  1. Open Internet Explorer by clicking the Internet Explorer icon on the taskbar.
  2.  Click the Favorites button .      
  3. Click the down-arrow next to Add to favorites, and then select Import and export.

  5. Select Favorites (and any other settings) that you'd like to export, and then click Next.
  6. Click Browse, select a file or drive to export your favorites to, and enter a name for your bookmarks file. Click Save when you’re done.

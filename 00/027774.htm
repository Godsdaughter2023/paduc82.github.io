doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

Do not use Filling in Forms restriction for a document that contains Content Controls.

To achieve what you are after, insert a centre-aligned tab stop in the cell where you want the centered Content Control and then press CTRL+Tab in that cell to move the insertion point to the tab stop.  Then insert your Content Control.  When you have all of the Content Controls in your form in place, select the text in the document, or at least the part of it that you want to protect, and then use the Group facility in the Controls section of the Developer tab of the ribbon to apply the "Group Text Range" facility.

Pressing the Backspace key will then have no effect on the alignment of the Content Control.

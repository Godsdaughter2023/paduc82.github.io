Let's say the first table is on Sheet1 in A1:Y32.

On Sheet 2, enter the following formulas in A2, B2 and C2, respectively:

Shut Down After Scan - Microsoft Community

harrybear say:

Shut Down After Scan

Is there a way the MSE can be programmed to shut down my computer after it has finished a scan.  I like to scan in 
the evening when I go to sleep.
Thanks
Harry
kosh vorlon say:

Hi Harry,

 

Nope.  There isn't an option within MSE to shutdown (or put the computer into hibernation or sleep) when the scan is complete. 

 

Based on historical estimates of how long it takes to complete scans, you can change the power options to shutdown the computer after it has been idle for that amount of time plus an additional 2 hours just to be on the safe side.  I believe although the scan is running, the PC will consider itself idle during that time if you do nothing else like move the mouse or strike the keyboard.  It may be on longer than you wanted, but at least you can use this method to stop it.  Note that if the scan is somehow not done when the PC shuts down it will be as if had never run and will need to be done again - so give yourself enough leeway to be sure it will finish.  This means constantly changing the power settings from normal to this variation and then back again each time, but it's the only workaround I can think of.

 

I hope this helps.

 

Good luck!

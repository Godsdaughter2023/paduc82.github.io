bob jones aka: cybertaz say:
Two points of clarification :-)
  • Mac Word's Publishing Layout View is not Microsoft Publisher... essentially or otherwise. It's merely a mode that is nothing more than a veneer which gives the impression that Word can be used for page layout work. "Under the hood" it's still Word. MS Publisher is a completely different program expressly designed for page layout & is not available for Mac.

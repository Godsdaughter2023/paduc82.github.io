doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

Assuming that Receipt# is in cell B1, use the following code:

Dim i As Long
Dim lngReceipt As Long
With Sheets(1).Range("A1")
    lngReceipt = .Offset(1, 1)
    For i = 2 To .CurrentRegion.Rows.Count - 1
        If .Offset(i, 1) = "" Then
            .Offset(i, 1) = lngReceipt
        Else
            lngReceipt = .Offset(i, 1)
        End If
    Next i
End With

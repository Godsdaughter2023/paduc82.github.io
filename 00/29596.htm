scottfguinn say: Click "Start"

In the search bar, type "Computer Management" (without quotation marks). Click on the icon for the program that shows up (should be at or near top of search results).

Look for "Disk Management" on the left pane of the window. If you don't see it, click the arrow to the left of "Storage"

Look for a drive that is titled "Unallocated." If you see one, check to see if it is the same size as your Media Drive. ( A 1TB drive will show as 931.51 GB, etc.)

Right-click on the unallocated drive and then click on "Create Simple Drive"

Go through the wizard using the default settings. When the screen where you can name the drive comes up, name it "HP Media Drive" (or whatever you'd like).

After the wizard finishes, wait a couple of minutes while the drive formats. Then open "My Computer" and the drive will show up. 

Right-click on the icon and click "Create Shortcut" if you'd like to have a desktop icon to access the drive.

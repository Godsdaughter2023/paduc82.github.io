Excel is changing my legend - Microsoft Community

la__ say:

Excel is changing my legend

When I want to delete a section of a line on my graph, the legend changes from the 'Series' name to all of the horizontal axis' data point names.  For example, instead of having the one line called "% of Independent Trials," the legend has a plot for "Week 1," "Week 2," etc.  How do I keep my legend the same?
andy pope say: When you have a single series and you modify a point within the series the legend will automatically create a legend entry for each data point.

Simplest way to keep the legend as intended is to add another series and then delete the second legend entry. This should then keep series 1 legend entry intact even though the data points are individually formatted.

The additional data series does not require any data.

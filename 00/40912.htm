Hardware and Devices troubleshooter checks for common problems and makes sure that any new device or hardware connected to your PC is installed correctly.

1.    Press Windows key from the keyboard, type Troubleshooting and select it.

2.    Click on View all option on the upper left corner of Troubleshooting window.

Step 2: Update the driver.

1.    Press Windows key + X key when you are at desktop.

2.    Select Device Manager.

3.    Expand Keyboard.

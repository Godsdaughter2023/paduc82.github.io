Hi CAnonymous,

This indeed, can be fixed by restarting the Print Spooler service. To do so, follow these steps:

  1. Right-click on the Start menu button, and select Computer Management.
  2. Click on Services and Applications on the left pane.
  3. Double-click on Services.
  4. Scroll down and look for Print Spooler, right-click on it, and select Properties.
  5. Click Stop to stop the Print Spooler service.
  6. Click Start to restart the service.

Converting Vacation days into hours - Microsoft Community

charleneritchie say:

Converting Vacation days into hours

how do i convert 10 vacation days to hours if i worked 31.5 hours a week.
joeu2004 say: Charlene wrote:
how do i convert 10 vacation days to hours if i worked 31.5 hours a week.

Please note that the correct answer might depend on labor laws that apply to where you work.  For example, labor laws might state that vacation days are always full calendar days, even if you only work part-time [1].

Be that as it may, I suspect you want to prorate the 10 vacation days in hours.  First, let's do it manually.  If you work 31.5 hours a week, and "a week" is 5 days, you work 6.3 hours per day (31.5 / 5).  So 10 vacation days might be construed to be 63 hours (10 * 6.3).

In Excel, if A1 contains 31.5, A2 contains 5, and A3 contains 10, the vacation hours might be:

=A3 * A2 / A1

  • http://blogs.msdn.com/microsoft_office_word/archive/2007/12/14/bibliography-citations-1011.aspx

Even if you don't want to use the template, the discussion forum can still be of interest to you as it contains several threads going into the details of the format that is fed to the xslt.

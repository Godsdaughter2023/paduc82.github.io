Although all other aspects of file management are restricted as John indicates, you can create new folders & sub-folders while in the Save As dialog window presented to programs. steve rindsberg say:

An alternative approach: one of the gadgets in my free PPTools StarterSet addin (http://www.pptools.com/starterset/) lets you memorize the size/location of any shape on your slide then "hammer" any selected shape into it.

If you memorize with nothing selected, it'll memorize the size of the slide.

Then all you need to do is select something and click the "hammer" icon to size the selected shape to match the slide.  You have the option of forcing the size (ie, distorting if need be) or maintaining aspect ratio, in which case the shape is made as large as possible w/o distortion.

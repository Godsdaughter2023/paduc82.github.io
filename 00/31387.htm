Hi,

I can only think of two possible reasons for this, both of which suggest a fault with the drive.  The noise is either coming from the drive motor, which indicates its on its last legs, or the noise is coming from discs being in physical contact with the laser diode.  If its the latter, each one of your game discs will typically have a perfect circle scratch around the disc.

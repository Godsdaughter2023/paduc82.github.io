Generally, the license of the user will be released after we delete the user in Office 365 admin center.

Open a new email message and the click on the Format Text tab of the Ribbon and then on the pulldown arrow thing in the title bar of the Paragraph section of the ribbon and in the Paragraph dialog that appears, go to the Indents and Spacing tab and set the Spacing Before and After paragraphs to 0 and then click on Set As Default.

Hi sophor,

The only options for the screensaver speed are slow, medium and fast with the registry settings as 0,1 and 2 respectively. There is no setting lower than ‘0’.
 
There is a workaround which may or may not work, but you could try nonetheless.
 
Reduce the hardware acceleration and check.
 
a. Click on start button and control panel.

e. Now click on Troubleshoot tab and select Change settings button.
f. On the security dialog, click Continue.
g. Move the Hardware Acceleration slider to the third notch.
h. Click OK.
i.  Restart Windows.

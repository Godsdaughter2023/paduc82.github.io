Could you try clearing your local database cache, re-download the app, and tell me what you see now?

Follow these steps:

- Navigate to your site in your web browser so you have it available
- Close all instances of Access client
- Navigate to this hidden folder on your computer/device:

- Delete that entry to send it to the Recycle Bin
- From your browser, click the Customize in Access option and re-download the web app which will open up Access again

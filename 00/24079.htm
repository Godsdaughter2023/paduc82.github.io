    Need help with graphical indicator for finish date compared to - Microsoft Community

    leah42 say:

    Need help with graphical indicator for finish date compared to deadline

    All,

    I am using MS Project 2007 non server.

    I need to set up a graphical indicator for the following:

    If the Finish Date = Deadline    GREEN

    If the Finish Date < Deadline     Orange

    If the Finish Date > Deadline    Red

    Thanks for your help.

    Leah

    dale howard [mvp] say:

    Leah --

    Your formula does not test for tasks where there is no Deadline date specified.  Therefore, I have rewritten your formula in a custom Number field as follows:

    Switch([Deadline]=ProjDateValue("NA"),0,DateValue([Deadline])>DateVAlue([Finish]),1,DateValue([Deadline])=DateValue([Finish]),2,DateValue([Deadline])<DateValue([Finish]),3)

    I use the Switch function to test for four different conditions.  I am using the DateValue function for every date in the formula to compare only the date, but not the time, in the Deadline and Finish fields.  Remember that every date in your project includes both the date AND the time.

    The formula results in a 0 value if there is no date in the Deadline field.  The formula returns a 1 value if the Deadline date is greater than the Finish date.  The formula returns a 2 value if the Deadline date and the Finish date are the same.  The formula returns a 3 value if the Finish date has slipped past the Deadline date.

    Based on this formula, I am recommending you use the following graphical indicator criteria:

    • Equals 0     No indicator shown
    • Equals 1     Green stoplight
    • Equals 2     Yellow stoplight
    • Equals 3     Red stoplight

    I believe you want to see a green stoplight if the Deadline date is later than the Finish date of the task, as this means there is time left to meet the Deadline date.  I believe you want to see a Yellow stoplight when the Deadline date and Finish date are the same, as you have run out of time to meet the Deadline date, so the task MUST finish as scheduled.  You obviously want to see a red stoplight when the Finish date has slipped past the Deadline date, as this indicates the task has missed its Deadline date.

    I have tested the formula and the graphical indicator criteria, and they perform as expected.  Hope this helps.

    So the possibility of you having a virus is slim to none. Windows Phone is incredibly secure and it is virtually impossible for it to get a virus. That said, websites you may visit can get a virus and can then redirect to inappropriate websites.

    On your device, open IE. Press the tabs button (lower left next to the URL bar). You will see all open tabs at that time. Press the "X" on each tab or if a lot are open, press the ellipses (...) in the lower right then press "close all".

    Now you will be looking at a blank page. Press the ellipses, scroll down to settings and press it. Now press the delete history option and then press the confirmation to delete.

    Now Open IE and press a favorite or enter a URL in the address bar and you should be good to go.

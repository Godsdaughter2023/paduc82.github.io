Hello Professor X:

Thank you for posting to the forum regarding the issue you are having adding a Nokia account to your phone's.  At this point it looks like the issue is related to the specific account you are trying to add or your network connection.  I personally have added Nokia accounts to a Lumia 1520, a Lumia 1020 and a Lumia 920 without issue.  The 1520 is running Cyan, while the others are running the developer preview and black. 

The one thing I see that looks odd is that you are mentioning an application.  I have always added my Nokia account to the phone settings by going to settings > emails + accounts > add account > Nokia account.  I would like to start by verifying your experience when trying to add the account in this way.  While you are here, please tap and hold any other accounts listed and sync them.  Let us know if any give you an "attention required" or "not up to date" error.  If you only have this issue when trying to sign into an app, please provide the exact name of the app you are trying to sign into.  I am unable to find any application titled "Nokia account app".

As for troubleshooting there are two things I might suggest you try.

  • Go to settings > WiFi > tap and hold the current WiFi network > delete.  Once this is done, tap the manage button followed by the + icon.  Manually re-add your network details to the phone.  Upon connecting to the network, try adding the account again.
  • Try creating another Nokia account as a test.  From there attempt to add this to at least one of the phones to see if you have the same issue.

Please let us know if you have any other questions or concerns we can assist you with.

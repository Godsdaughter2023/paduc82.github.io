jim gordon mvp say:
A line can be made a variety of ways. Before proceeding, make sure the document is not protected by going to the Tools menu and making sure the Protect Document option is displayed. If it says Unprotect Document, then choose that option to unprotect the document.

If the line is a shape, then when you click on the line it will become selected and you can press the delete key to delete the line.

If the line is part of a form field, when you click the line it should select the form field. Press the delete key to delete the form field.

If the line is part of a table, when you click the line you should see the table outline. Click on the outline of the table to select the table and then press the delete key on the keyboard.

If the line is an underline made by pressing the space bar a bunch of times and then formatting the spaces as underlline, then select the spaces and toggle the underline off.

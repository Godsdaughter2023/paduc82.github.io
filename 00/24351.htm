oliver l from de say:

I've found it: the reason was obviously that I had a former 64 bit Office 2010 installation.

 

Go to Options - Security Center settings - Trusted Locations

 

You'll find there the 64 Bit Office 2010 directory:

C:\Program Files\Microsoft Office\Office14\ACCWIZ\

 

Just add the 32 Bit version:

C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Office\Office14\ACCWIZ\

 

and restart Access 2010.
Now Shift-F2 should open the zoom window as usual.

 

Good luck. Did it help?

timothyboyle1947 say:

country code has changed in my contacts

All the Skype numbers I have for people in the US have changed to the Japan country code.  In other words, "+1" and number has become "+81" and number.  So to call, I have to manually enter the number.  I'd also like to know how to delete some old contacts.  It says I must delete from "Mac Contacts" but I don't know how.

susan_s. say:

Hello

ISSUE: Country code has changed

ANSWER: A possible reason for this was your Skype app was set to the last country code you call on Skype. To change it to US country code, please find below information, depending on the Skype version installed from your device:

Classic Skype for Mac v7.59

Click on dial pad > From the drop-down arrow Choose Country or Region, select United States (+1)

New Skype for Desktop (Windows, Mac and Linux) v8.15

Click on Call icon, then DialPad > From the drop-down arrow, choose United States (+1)

ISSUE: DELETE CONTACT

ANSWER: Please see below information for this concern.

Classic Skype for Mac v7.59

  1. Sign in to Skype.
  2. Find the person in your contact list. Go to the sidebar and click Contacts, then to the right, click Skype. You may have to click >> to see all of your lists.
  3. Right-click the contact you’d like to delete and select Delete Contact....
  4. A message box opens with the prompt: “Are you sure you want to remove [name of contact] from your contact?” Click Remove.

For more information, you can check this link, click here.

New Skype for Desktop (Windows, Mac and Linux) v8.15

  1. Sign in to Skype.
  2. Click on the Contact icon to show your contacts.  Find the person in your contact list, then Ctrl + Click > View Profile > Scroll down and choose the option Delete contact.

Hope this helps! Let us know if you need further assistance.

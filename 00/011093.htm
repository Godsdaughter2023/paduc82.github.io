I don't know how long an action is.  However, it's easy enough to substract from a date.  So suppose you enter a date in a cell, let's say A1.  Assuming each "action" takes a day, you can just enter =A1-12, which would give you the date 12 days earlier.

bernie deitrick say:
If each of the 12 steps takes 2 work days:

=WORKDAY(DATE(2014,4,22),-24)

Change the -24 to the negative of the total number of days.

If you don't want to account for weekends, then just subtract:

=DATE(2014,4,22)-24

In either case, format the cell as a date.

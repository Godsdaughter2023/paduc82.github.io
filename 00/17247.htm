Good Morning,

Thank you for your question. the number of keyword recommended per adgroup depends on many factors, your bid to budget ratio, yoru goals for that adgroup. One thing that I see happen frequently is that the total bids on keywords can be close to or higher than the budget and this will cause the system to slow down or even stop serving to avoid going over  you budget.

Strictly from a technical perspective the limit is 20,000 keywords per adgroup and 20,000 negative keywords per adgroup.

Consider your ad when choosing the keywords for you ad group. Are the words included in the ad? Or are similar word in the ad?

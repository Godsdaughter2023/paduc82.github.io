ariel acu say:

Hi Nikhil,

There are a number of reasons why you encountered an encryption error when encrypting an email. This mostly due to a certificate conflict or a corrupt certificates in Outlook. Let's check the Trust Center Settings in your Outlook by performing the following steps:

  1. In Outlook, Click File.
  2. Choose Options.
  3. Select Trust Center, then click Trust Center Settings.
  4. Go to Email Security. Check if the following options are unchecked:
    • Encrypt content and attachments for outgoing messages.
    • Add digital signatures to outgoing messages
  5. Go to Trusted Publisher, then delete the digital signatures that may appear in the list.
  6. Relaunch Outlook.

Once done, please check if you can now encrypt and open the email.

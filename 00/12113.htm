yasser m.abdel moneim say:

Office 365 as a mail relay

Hi

I have to use Office 365 as mail relay to my applications .

is there any guide to explain how we can use office 365 as a relay for my applications to send emails and using office 365 as a relay .

violetchen_msft say:

Hi Yasser,

There are 3 methods:

a. SMTP client submission, If your device or application can authenticate and send email using an Office 365 mailbox account, this is the recommended method.

b. Direct send, you can configure your device to send email direct to Office 365. However, in this case, Office 365 does not relay messages for external recipients and will only deliver to your hosted mailboxes. If your device sends an email to Office 365 that is for a recipient outside your organization, the email will be rejected.

c. SMTP relay, Office 365 SMTP relay uses a connector to authenticate the mail sent from your device or application. This allows Office 365 to relay those messages to your own mailboxes as well as external recipients.

Please refer to this article and choose the best way according to your requirements.

If you can’t decide which way is more appropriate, please share with us your detailed requirements and we will help you choose one.

Violet

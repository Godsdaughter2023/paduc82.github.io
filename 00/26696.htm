a. close outlook client, sign in to outlook web app.
b. click settings>option in the right upper corner.
c. go to mail>automatic processing>retention policies.
d. click the plus button to add the retention tags.
e. open outlook client, click assign policy>set folder policy to apply retention policy.


5. login to office 365 owa > right click "deleted item", and then click "assign policy" > select retention tags to applied it to the "deleted item" folder.

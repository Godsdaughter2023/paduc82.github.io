*This was given to me by a MS MVP Andre Da Costa

Its a known issue, you can easily restore it:

Press Windows key + X

Click Command Prompt (Admin)

At the command prompt, enter the following command:

bcdedit /set {bootmgr} displaybootmenu yes

Hit Enter

Type: exit

 Restart


*What it will do is give you the old style boot menu, not the new one.

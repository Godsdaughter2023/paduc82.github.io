To answer your questions, no, you do not necessarily need to add the resources in your project to the Enterprise Resource Pool.  If the resources are not in the pool, however, the information about them is basically NOT reportable in Project Web App views and reports.  The question you need to answer is whether you want to report on these resources.  If yes, add them to your Enterprise Resource Pool and them map each local resource with a corresponding enterprise resource when importing the project.

Thank you for the response. We suggest doing hard reset on your phone. Take note that this method will erase all content stored on your phone, including apps and games (along with app data and game progress), text messages, call history, music, photos, and more.

To perform hard reset:

  1. On Start , swipe over to the All apps list, then select Settings .
  2. Select System > About > Reset your phone.
  3. You'll receive two warnings. If you're absolutely sure you want to restore your phone to its factory settings, select Yes, and then select Yes again. It might take a little while for the process to complete.

Don't forget to back up the files stored on your phone and check if you have turned off the Reset Protection.

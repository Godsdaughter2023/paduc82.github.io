djsteele, mvp say:

Alphanumeric characters are represented by Ascii values. As I mentioned earlier, lower case letters have values between 97 and 122, while upper case letters have values between 65 and 90.  The Chr function will returns a string containing the character associated with the specified value. In other words, Chr(97) returns a, Chr(98) returns b, Chr(99) returns c and so on to Chr(122), which returns z, while Chr(65) returns A, Chr(66) returns B and so on to Chr(90), which returns Z.

If you want your scores per columns, taken from column F, below each column try this formula in cell A7 (i.e. somewhere below your data in column A)

=INDEX($F2:$F5,MATCH(MAX(A2:A5),A2:A5,0))

Copy cell A7 to the right until column E.

Deleted all contacts at My Phone website and on the phone. - Microsoft Community

gretes say:

Deleted all contacts at My Phone website and on the phone.

Hi!  I have deleted my contacts on the Windows Live My Phone website and on the phone by mistake. Is it possible to get them back???
julie fka profjulie say:

Log in to your My Phone account from a computer and then look for the contacts in the archives there.  If you find them there, you can move them back to the contacts area.

Then stop the partnership between your phone and My Phone and set the partnership up again.  My Phone will then see your phone as a new device and sync your contacts to your phone.

If you don't find your contacts in the Archive, then they are lost and there won't be a way to recover them.

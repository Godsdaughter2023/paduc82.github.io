You can't tell which USB connector is on which hub unless you have the circuit diagram of the motherboard.

Well, that article focuses on installing or uninstalling a program. But the Safe Mode result tells you that a startup program is causing the problem.

You will have to do more hands-on work. From Start> Run type in MSCONFIG then hit Enter.

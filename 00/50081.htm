peterjchandler say:

The only way to create a call record or history is to screenshot every part of your timeline (or just the calls if you want to exclude the chat messages) and then assemble those screenshots into a single document.  MS Office will not do this as it does not take many photos for MS Word to give up and display the inserted photos at random, with the original formatting irretrievably lost.  Had a colleague do this for about 18 months of regular Skype contact, it took him about a month (using Libre Office, the free office software), fortunately at the time he did not need to work so he was able to spend far more time on this than any full time employee would work, it still took him about a month though.  That was with earlier versions of Skype with far far less wasted space!

In my case. looking at around 10-15 thousand screenshots or more of call history across a few devices to create a call history soon!

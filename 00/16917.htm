To change the doughnut chart’s hole size in Excel, please follow the steps below:

1. Right click at one of the data series in the doughnut chart, and select Format Data Series option in the context menu.

2. Then in the Format Data Series dialog, you can drag the slider to adjust the hole size you need in the Doughnut Hole size section, also you can type the percentage into the text box to get the exact size you want.

Note: In Excel 2013, go to the Series Options tab of Format Data Series pane, and move the slider of Doughnut Hole Size or enter proper percentage in the following box.

3. Click Close to exit the dialog, now the hole size of the doughnut chart is changed.

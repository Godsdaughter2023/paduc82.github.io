Just to reiterate Ratandeep's post above, this issue occurs if the following scenarios are true:

  • Windows Update or Microsoft Update tries to install a driver a second time for an additional piece of identical hardware such as a graphics adapter.
  • Windows Update or Microsoft Update tries to update a currently installed driver to an unsuitable version of that driver.

For the most recent available driver, contact the device manufacturer. This issue can be safely ignored if this applies to a driver.   2. After the scan is complete, tap or click the Optional updates are available link.
  3. Tap and hold or Right click the update you want to hide, tap or click Hide update, and then tap or click OK.
  4. If any other updates are available, save all your work in other programs, then tap or click Restart now to install updates, otherwise, close the Windows Update window.

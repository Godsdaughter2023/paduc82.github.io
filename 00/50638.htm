a. user say:

What is your XP version and Service Pack?

There are several VSC fixes in SP3, so if you don't have SP3, you might want to get it, then troubleshoot any remaining issues.

There are many valid reasons why you can go days and days without an automatic System Checkpoint being created.  XP would certainly like to create one at least every 86,400 seconds as a rule (24 hours), but it may not find that the conditions are favorable at the time and will wait until the next cycle if it misses the window of opportunity.  Not seeing a RP every day does not necessarily indicate a problem.

For example, if there was already a Restore Point created in the last 24 hours for any other reason, the automatic 24 hour Checkpoint XP would normally create will be skipped since the "create at least one every 24 hours" requirement has been satisfied with another prior Restore Point.  There are lots of valid reasons not to see one every day...

Did your SR stuff work as desired before installing Carbonite?

If you think the symptoms are linked to the Carbonite installation, have you checked their WWW support page for your symptoms?

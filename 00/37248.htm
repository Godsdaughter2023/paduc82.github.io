    Press Windows key + R to open the Run dialog. Type services.msc and press Enter

    Select the service named Windows Presentation Foundation Font Cache 3.0.0.0 and click on the stop button in the toolbar. (If this service is not running, then there is no need to stop the service).

Sometimes you may need to remove the account before re-configuring it, make sure you follow the instructions to remove the account given at the bottom of the article, if applicable.

For Windows Live Hotmail account, you can refer to the article Send and Receive Windows Live Hotmail emails from a Mail Client for instructions on how you can set up your account as POP3.

     

    If the above steps didn’t work for you, these errors may be occurring due to three different kinds of issues: Network Issues, Machine Configuration Issues or Software Configuration Issues. Given below is a list of the issues and you may click on the links below to read articles about how to resolve each of these categories of issues.

     

    Network Issues:

     

    1.       You were not connected to the Internet when you tried to synchronize your account

     

    2.       The mail server that you are attempting to send or receive data from may not be available

    the F: drive is a non-issue. A mistake that came with 1803
    WIN 10 CREATES A NEW, SMALL DRIVE / PARTITION
    Version 1803 creates a small (around 500mb) “recovery drive” partition on your HD. Mistakenly it gives it a drive letter that shows up in File Explorer. This drive is just big enough for it’s purpose, with very little free space on it. This triggers an inappropriate low space warning Windows. There are two possible fixes. Best is to remove the drive letter mapping from it so that it is no longer visble to you or Windows. So that you don’t accidentally delete the contents or try to use it for something else. Your other option is to use disk management to slightly increase the size of the partition to stop the message.

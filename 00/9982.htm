Note that he screen shot shows the dialog box with levels associated with heading styles; you'll see something else in your document. Also, in your case you will only be interested in level 1. Just ignore the others.

In the dialog box, use "Aligned at" to specify the position of the list item; leave it at zero for no indent at all. "Add tab stop at" determines where the first line of text starts. This is the one you need to adjust. "Text indent at" determines the position for the second and subsequent text lines. Its value should match "Add tab stop at," assuming that you want a hanging indent.

Note that all measurements are from the left margin. To find the appropriate values, you may have to experiment a little.

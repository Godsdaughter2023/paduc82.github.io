      ken isaacson say:

      FIXED!

      I noticed that in Taskbar Properties, although the "Use Peek to preview" box was checked, that option was greyed out (couldn't even uncheck it if I wanted."

      Googled "use aero peek to preview the desktop grayed out" and found this solution: http://bit.ly/1AneqcG

      Basically, had to go into System Properties|Advanced|Performance settings. In the Visual Effects tab, "Enable Peek" was not checked.

      Checked that, and now it works.

Some of my contacts on Outlook.com (or windows live?) - Microsoft Community

linjill say:

Some of my contacts on Outlook.com (or windows live?) don't sync to my Lumia 900, what to do?

I've just noticed that there many contacts missing on my Lumia 900 phone.

My "People" on Lumia is set up to sycn with Windows Live and Facebook.

All my contacts from Facebook sync to my phone with no problems, but there are only few from Windows Live actually made it to my phone.

How to solve this problems?

 

Thanks

 

Jill

 

a. user say:

Hello LinJill:

 

Thank you for posting to the forum.  There are a couple of things you may want to look at when it comes to contacts on contacts.live.com not showing on your phone.

 

The first thing to do is verify that the missing contacts are not added as favorites.  If you have them listed as favorites, move them out of favorites and go back to the phone and sync the phone, tap settings > emails + accounts > tap and hold the first account listed as Microsoft account.

 

The next thing to look at is the nature of the contact information.  Make sure that the information in the contact is information supported on the phone.  An example of unsupported information is a mobile 2 number.  If you find that the missing contacts include this information, remove it or recategorize it.

 

Another thing to try and do with the contacts is to group them on contacts.live.com.  Once you have added them to a group, sync the phone and look for the group.  If the group syncs, you can then unlink those contacts.

 

Finally, look on contacts.live.com to verify where the contact has originated.  Contacts can link to your Microsoft account from Skype, LinkedIn and other accounts.  There may be an issue with the linkage between the two accounts.  If you find that the contacts are coming from another source, go to accounts.live.com and then to account settings.  Unlink and relink the two accounts.

 

Please let us know if you have any other questions or concerns.

 

Mark W.

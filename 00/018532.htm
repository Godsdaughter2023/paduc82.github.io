Importing your Google Calendar into Outlook.com

michael. o say:

If you have an Outlook.com account, you can Import or Sync your Google calendar events into your Outlook.com calendar.

If you don't have an Outlook.com account, you can sign up for one.

 

Import your Google calendar events into Outlook.com

 

Note: This is the recommended method for getting your Google Calendar into your Outlook.com account because you’ll have full edit capabilities.

You can find detailed steps for moving your Google Calendar events to Outlook.com by clicking the link.

 

 

Subscribe to your Google Calendar from Outlook.com


Note: This is not the recommended method because the linked Calendar events will be Read Only and could take up to 48 hours to appear in your Outlook.com Calendar.

  1. Sign in to your Google account.
  2. Tap or click Calendar, tap or click the settings icon, and then tap or click Settings.
  3. Tap or click on the calendar you want to subscribe to and next to the Private address, tap or click ICAL.
  4. Copy the URL under Private address.
  5. Sign in to Outlook.com.
  6. Tap or click Subscribe.
  7. Paste the URL you’d copied earlier in Calendar URL, add a name for this calendar, and tap or click Subscribe to calendar.

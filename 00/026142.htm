How do I set my home/default location or favorites for MSN - Microsoft Community

sarah p.b. say:

How do I set my home/default location or favorites for MSN Weather?

There are a couple ways to set your home location for Msn Weather. Once set, this location should be the default location shown for weather data.

Option 1: Update your site-wide preference for a signed in user:

  1. Sign in to your Hotmail or Outlook.com account on www.msn.com.

  2. From the upper right hand corner, click on the Settings gear.

  3. Select “Settings” from the dropdown menu.

  4. Under the “Home Location” section, enter the location you want set as your home or default location into the text box and hit enter or click the magnifying glass icon. Then select the correct location. You can also click on the  icon to detect your current location.

     icon to detect your current location.

  5. Go back to the previous page.

Option 2: Update your non-signed in preference on the www.msn.com homepage.

  1. To the right of the weather module in the upper left hand corner of the page, click on the  icon. This will open a box asking you to set your home location. Enter the location you want set as your home or default location into the text box and hit enter or click the magnifying  icon. This will open a box asking you to set your home location. Enter the location you want set as your home or default location into the text box and hit enter or click the magnifying glass icon. Then select the correct location. You can also click on the  icon to detect your current location.

  1. Click Done.

Option 3: Update your non-signed in preference on the weather homepage.

  1. From the weather homepage, click on the  icon which will detect current location. If this is the location you wish to set as your home or default location, expand the location dropdown.

  2. Near the bottom of the expanded list, select “Make This Place My Home.”

  3. Alternatively, if the populated location is not your correct location or you wish to set another location as your default, you can choose to search for that location in the search box at the top of the page or in the search box located in the expanded location dropdown.

  4. Once you find the location you wish to set as your default and select it, expand the location dropdown and select “Make This Place My Home.”

Option 4: Add a previously viewed location as your home on the “My Places” page.

  1. Go to the “My Places” link in the header, click the  icon to the right of the “Favorites” section header, and click the white house icon in the upper right hand corner of each tile to add a location as your default location.

If you wish to add any location you have searched for as a favorite, one you wish to view details for on a regular basis, you must sign in to your Hotmail or Outlook.com account to add that location as a favorite. This can be done a few ways.

  1. From the weather homepage after sign in, search for a location you wish to add as a favorite.

  2. Click on the  icon to the right of the location dropdown menu. This will add this location as a favorite location.

  3. Alternatively, you can expand the location dropdown and select “Add To My Favorites.”

If you wish to remove any location from your favorites list, you can accomplish this in the following ways.

  1. Search for the location you wish to remove as a favorite.

  2. Click on the  icon to the right of the location dropdown menu. This will remove this location as a favorite location.

  3. Alternatively, you can expand the location dropdown and select “Remove From My Favorites.”

  4. As another option, you can go to the “My Places” link in the header, click the  icon, and use the white “X” in the upper right hand corner of each tile to remove the location as a favorite.

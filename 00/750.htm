acslaeu.exe

asscrollb.exe
acscore.exe
acslang.exe


If a binary editor is used on these files to change the "acs" sequence to something else, the hang goes away.

If the interference occurs on Skype to Skype calls, it can be due to interference being picked up by local equipment or a participant's computer. When you dial phone numbers you connect to an analog network that is open to more crosstalk created by shielding issues, miswiring, and other factors along the line with any participant. If this occurs with specific numbers you dial, you can report the issue to Skype support or the phone company/partner facilitating the call. This should allow the problem to be investigated. Somewhere along the POTS connection or destination's phone equipment, interference is being picked up. If the problem only occurs with a specific participant present (other than yourself),in a group call, that participant could be introducing the interference with their call.

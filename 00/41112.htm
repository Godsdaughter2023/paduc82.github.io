You may follow the steps listed below to To add a missing broadcast TV channel

1) On the start screen, scroll to Tasks, click settings, click TV, click Guide, and then click Add Missing Channels.
2) Click Add Channel.
3) Type the name of the missing channel, and then click Next.
4) Type the number of the missing channel, and then click Next.
5) If prompted, type the physical number of the missing channel, and then click Add.
6) Click Done.


To edit channel numbers as they appear in the Guide

1) On the start screen, scroll to Tasks, click settings, click TV, click Guide, and then click Edit Channels.
2) Click Edit Numbers, and then make the changes you want.
3) Click Save.

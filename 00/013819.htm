hansv mvp say:

Activate the 'Page Layout' tab of the ribbon.

Click the little arrow in the lower right corner of the 'Page Setup' group.

Activate the Layout tab of the 'Page Setup' dialog.

Select Top from the 'Vertical alignment' dropdown.

Click 'Set as Default'.

Click Yes, then click OK.

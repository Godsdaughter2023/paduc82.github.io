Moving the contents of one Site to another Site in SharePoint - Microsoft Community

katymurray- [o365] say:

Moving the contents of one Site to another Site in SharePoint

1.  Is it possible to move the contents (folders and files) from one site to another?  (Or to copy from one site and then delete the original to achieve the same goal?)    

2.  Is it possible to move the contents from a team site, to a sub-site within the team site, or copy and delete as above?  

I have no problem moving files and folders around within a site, but have a need to do this between sites.  

william zeng msft say:

Hi Katy,

 1. Yes, it is feasible to copy or move contents from one site to another one.

  • Click Site settings > Content and structure (if you can’t find Content and structure under Site Administration, you may need to Activate SharePoint Server Publishing Infrastructure)
         Also, you can visit http://yoursite/_layouts/15/sitemanager.aspx directly to step into site manager.
  • Select the site of you want to move in the left navigation pane.
  • Check the box next to the subsite you want to move in the right pane.
  • Click Actions drop-down and click Move.
  • Select Destination of the subsite selected in the next dialog box.

2. Yes, it’s feasible to move the contents from a team site to a sub-site within the team site.

Regards,

William

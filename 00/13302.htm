Footnote Format - Microsoft Community

cassidy721 say:

Footnote Format

I want the footnote numbers to be superscript in the document but not to be superscript in the footnote reference. I want the footnote numbers to be superscript in the document but not to be superscript in the footnote reference.

suzanne s. barnhill say:
See https://wordmvp.com/FAQs/MacrosVBA/UnSuperscptFnotes.htm

cassidy721 say: I tried this earlier, but it didn't change anything. Is there another macro code I can use for Word 2010?

stefan blom say:

The following macro (from the article):

Sub InsertFootnote()
'Macro from https://wordmvp.com/FAQs/MacrosVBA/UnSuperscptFnotes.htm
'Modified by Stefan Blom, MVP, July 2019

  ActiveDocument.Footnotes.Add Range:=Selection.Range

   .Paragraphs(1).Range.Font.Reset
   .Paragraphs(1).Range.Characters(2) = ""
   .InsertAfter "." & vbTab
   .Collapse wdCollapseEnd

must be combined with another macro:

Sub InsertFootnoteNow()
'Macro from https://wordmvp.com/FAQs/MacrosVBA/UnSuperscptFnotes.htm
'Modified by Stefan Blom, MVP, July 2019

  ActiveDocument.Footnotes.Add Range:=Selection.Range

   .Paragraphs(1).Range.Font.Reset
   .Paragraphs(1).Range.Characters(2) = ""
   .InsertAfter "." & vbTab
   .Collapse wdCollapseEnd

and both of these must be added to the Normal template.

Also, remember that you must modify the Footnote Text style (as noted in the article).

If you have already inserted footnotes in your document, the macros won't work; they will only work for future footnotes that you insert.

doug robbins - mvp office apps & services (word) say:

For a document in which you have already inserted the footnotes, switch to Draft view and then in the Footnotes section of the Reference tab of the ribbon, click on Show Notes.

Then select all of the footnotes and in the Find and Replace dialog, click on the More>> button and then with the Selection in the Find what control, click on Format>Font and click on the Superscript box in the Font dialog until it is checked.  Then move to the Replace with Control and access the Font dialog again and click on the Superscript box until it is unchecked.  Then click on Replace All and when you are asked if you want to search the rest of the document, click on the No button.

It would be best to wait until you have completed the document before performing this operation.

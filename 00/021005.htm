roady [mvp] say:

Then you'll first need to copy over your pst-file from the old computer to the new computer (don't overwrite your existing pst-file).

 

Once you've done that, go into your Account Settings (File-> Account Settings-> Account Settings) and select to change your delivery location for your account (button: Change Folder).

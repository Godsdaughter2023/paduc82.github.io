ken blake say:

That depends on what you mean by "everything."

You can transfer data files (word processing documents, photos, videos, music, etc.) but you can *not* transfer programs. Programs will have t be reinstalled form their original media.

There are several approaches to transferring data files. Which is best for you mostly depends on how much data you have to transfer. Here are some choices:

1. Transfer them on CDRs or DVDs.
2. E-mail them from the old machine and read the E-mail on the new one.
3. Network the two computers, and just copy the files over the network.
4. Temporarily (or even permanently) install the old computer's hard drive in the new computer and just copy them over.
5. Use a USB thumb drive.
6. Use an External hard drive.

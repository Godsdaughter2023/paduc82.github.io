As far as I know there are no known issues with Use Tighter Spacing being missing.  It is intentional that Coming Soon is currently not visible.  Coming Soon is used by the Outlook Team to help preview new functionality.  The last major update was the Simplified User Experience as documented here, https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/Outlook-Blog/Outlook-for-Windows-rolls-out-its-simplified-user-experience/ba-p/653734.  That update released as Version 1905 (Build 11629.20196).  Now that it rolled out Coming Soon was turned off.  When the next major feature is in preview you will see it be re-enabled.

If Use Tighter Spacing is missing from the View tab make sure that Office is on Monthly Channel and updated to the latest version, 1906 (Build 11727.20244).  This page shows the latest versions by channel.  You can check the version and channel by clicking File, Office Account under About Outlook.  

If you confirm that you are on the updated version and channel and still seeing the issue submit an In App ticket by clicking Help, Contact Support.  Contact Support is being rolled out slowly so not everyone will have it but worth checking.

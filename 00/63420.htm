The first thing to do is check the bios  when the errors or codes are generated.

Some bios have a fallback setting that will kick in if it can not find or load a device.

or dvd.

But for each device chipset on the board I have to tell the bios what the order is because it will  

just add the next connected device as the first one. 

abut it does not just place the previous first device as the second one, it can place it anywhere in the chain.

jneubrander say:

You're going to hate yourself for the answer. I had the same problem... here's the fix.

You need to change the Backup Folder path (located at File --> Options --> Save & Backup. On the right side, under Save, double click "Backup Folder" or select it and then hit modify. Choose a new path to save to, even a USB (works just fine for me).

After this, you must select the OK button at the bottom right of the window. If you don't, and immediately try to manually back up, it will give you the error that it can't find that file. However, once you do that, it may ask you to restart OneNote. Then you can restart it, go back into the options, and manually back up your notes by clicking the "Back up all notebooks now" button.

That's it. Simple. Irritatingly simple.

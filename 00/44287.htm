a. user say:

Hi Laine,

There are several ways to troubleshoot issues with the Photo App in Windows 10. If you cannot view photos with the Photo App, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open File Explorer.
  2. Click on View tab.
  3. Click on Navigation pane and choose Show libraries.
  4. In the left pane right-click on Libraries and click on Restore default libraries.
  5. Open your Photo app again, and you should be able to see images.

Should you have further questions, please let us know.

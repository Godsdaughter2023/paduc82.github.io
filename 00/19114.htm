ousmane c. say:

Hello TomSpalding,

 

Thanks for your post on the forum.

This error code C00D135C occurs because a communication error with the Zune service is preventing Zune from verifying your media usage rights. This is probably the result of a firewall or proxy on your computer. Also, the Zune service could be down.

In other cases, this error results when you're trying to download something when no download is possible.

Songs downloaded at Zune Marketplace using your Zune Pass can be re-downloaded up to four times. Trying to re-download a Zune Pass song a fifth time will produce this error (C00D135C).

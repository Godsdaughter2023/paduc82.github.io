Hi Helen:

You need to convert the picture to "Inline with text" layout, otherwise it cannot go into a table.

You can do that after putting the picture into the document.

Format>Picture>Layout and set it to "Inline with text".

The reason is that a table is a special case of "paragraph", it is a text object in the text layer of the document.  A picture is a graphics object, and exists only in the graphics layer -- either in front of, or behind, the text.  Unless you say otherwise, but converting it to "Inline with text" which makes it behave like a very large character in the text.

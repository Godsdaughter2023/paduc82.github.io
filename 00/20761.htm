bernie deitrick say:

For a date time in B2:

(24 hours)

=WORKDAY(B2,1) + MOD(B2,1)

(72 hours)

=WORKDAY(B2,3) + MOD(B2,1)

If you have a list of Holidays, WORKDAY will accept those as its third argument and adjust the result as appropriate

=WORKDAY(B2,1, HolidaysList) + MOD(B2,1)

You need to include an example of what you want for the 9 hours / 27 hours

Maybe:

=WORKDAY(B2,1) + MIN(MOD(B2,1),0.75)

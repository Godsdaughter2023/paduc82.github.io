taidara say:

[quote user="TheEternal170"]tri-band 2.4Ghz, 5GHz, 5Ghz if that's wrong terminology then 3 WiFi connections.[/quote]This feature is not referred to as "tri-band". It is simply referred to as having two concurrent 5 GHz radios enabled on a single AP. Sure, throughput may increase by as much as 25-30%, by having two concurrent 5 GHz radios enabled. But, and here's the big BUT. The data retry percentages will increase dramatically, and can in fact jump as high (skyhigh) as 30% on the uplink and 40% on the downlink. Having two concurrent radios that close to each other... Sorry! Physics won't play nice on those two radios.

I think the problem is that you are in "Documents Library" view, rather than the actual "Folder Location"


1. Right-click the folder you want to change the icon of

Thanks for your reply. If the emails this user deleted are past the deleted item retention period, you can’t, and they will be deleted from server. If you want to retain files for this user for future search, you are not suggested to use retention hold, instead, I suggest you place a litigation hold or in place hold. To preserve user mailbox items, you should place user mailbox in legal hold.

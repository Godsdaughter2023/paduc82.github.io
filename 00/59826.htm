Factory Restore returns the computer to its original factory-installed software configuration. This option reformats the hard drive and removes all data from the computer.

To complete a Factory Restore on your computer, perform the following steps:
  1. Insert the Compaq Recovery CD into the CD-ROM, CD-RW, or DVD-ROM drive.
  2. Turn off the computer.
  3. Turn on the PC by pressing the Power button on the front of the PC. The system restarts and the Compaq Recovery CD Menu displays.

  4. A Window appears warning that all data will be erased. Press Enter if you wish to continue. The Factory Restore process lasts several minutes.
  5. The CD tray opens automatically, and you are prompted to remove the Compaq Recovery CD. Remove the CD from the CD-ROM, CD-RW, or DVD-ROM drive.

  7. Once the Factory Restore is complete, you will need to set up Windows and reinstall software.
Exiting the Compaq Recovery CD
If you choose not to use recovery, restart the PC. Perform the following steps to exit the Recovery CD.

    fat_bob say: Thanks cricfancy, that got me to where I needed to.

    the main issue is the encryption. My company encrypts the Local copy. I suppose that means no one can look through my email if my laptop gets stolen.

    So what I did was create a second replica of the main database (Mail database) and only included the Calendar Items.

    To do this:

    Goto "Workspace", right-click on the Mail icon, replication->New Replica

    change the name in the file path, e.g. from MAIL\{username}.NSF to MAIL\COPY.NSF

    Click Replica Settings, uncheck "Encrypt the replica...."
    Click on More Settings then the Advanced tab
    Check "Documents in Specified Views and Folders", and highlight "Calendar"

    Click OK then OK again (or play with other settings if you need to).

    check the folder:
    C:\Documents and Settings\{username}\Notesdata\mail\ and there should be a file called "COPY.NSF"

    That's the file you want your Nokia PC software to look at...and Fat_Bob's your uncle!

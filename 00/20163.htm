I have several email accounts that I use with the Windows 10 Mail app, and somehow the name from one email account was copied over to another email account's settings.  I finally fixed the issue by reviewing all my email account settings using the steps below.

  1. Click on the settings icon/button in the Mail app.
  2. Click "Manage Accounts".
  3. Select an account to review/edit.

  5. Scroll down to "Your name" and edit as necessary.
  6. Repeat for all accounts.

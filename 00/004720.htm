Here is How:

If you did not find a suitable match, you can edit the data your self.

 

Here is how:

 

1. Start the Zune Software

 

2. Go to your collection.

 

2. Find the album that you wish to edit and "Right Click" This time, click "Edit" You will be taken to an "Edit Album Info" Page. Here, you can manually edit any piece of data listed in the boxes. I like to use the Find Album Info feature, but sometimes, you just need to fix something your self.

 

I hope that this helps you out. The Zune software gives great and easy to use tools to edit a music collection!

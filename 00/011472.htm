ed ferrero say:

Instead of using =TEXT(D1-C1,"h:mm") to calculate the elapsed time, just use =D1-C1 and format the cell as [h]:mm

Then select a cell in your range and use the Ribbon item Insert -> Table

In Table design, check Total Row. Click on the total and change it to Sum. Format as [h]:mm

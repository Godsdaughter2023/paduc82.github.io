john korchok say:

Typically, language changes in a master do NOT ripple through to the slides. However, setting the language on the master may make your editing a little easier. To convert the deck to English:

  1. Choose View>Slide Master to open the slide master view. Scroll up the the larger slide at the top, which is the master.
  2. Click and drag over the slide to select all placeholders.
  3. Choose Review>Language>Set proofing language, then select your preferred dialect of English.
  4. Select each slide layout below the master, click and drag and set the language.
  5. Choose Slide Master>Close Master View to return to viewing the slides.
  6. Select each slide, click and drag to select all content, then choose Review>Language>Set proofing language and choose English.
  7. Save the file and you should have no further spellcheck issues.

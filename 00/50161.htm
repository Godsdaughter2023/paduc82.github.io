Your Windows 8 PC goes to sleep after a certain time after you step  away from it. When you come it is in sleep mode and locked. So, you have to reenter your password. However if you want to increase the time after which your computer goes to sleep then you can follow the following steps :-

This would be a use for the My Home Xbox feature. https://support.xbox.com/xbox-one/games/my-home-xbox

Although it's meant to be on one console with multiple users, it can technically be used in this situation in one house with 2 xboxes.

Here's how it works (this can get confusing because of the way you want to use this feature): There's account A and console A with the gold and games and account B and console B without gold and without the games.

1. You log in on the account A with the gold and games you want to share on console B.

2. Set that console as the "my home xbox" account (see link above).

3. Log in on the account A on console A with that account that owns the gold and games and also log in on console B with account B and that account now has access to gold without having to pay for it again - being shared from account A's my home xbox.

4. Some games may not be able to be played two times at a time with this feature, and you may need to purchase the game again on account B but the gold will share for sure with no problems.

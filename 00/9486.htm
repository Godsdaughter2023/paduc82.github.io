generally, the total storage in sharepoint online you can get is based on the calculation 10 gb + 500 mb * number of users. in your scenario you can approximately get 17.5 gb. but in fact, some subscribers can get some additional storage. so you may get the storage more than 17.5gb. please check the pooled storage in sharepoint online.

if you want to use a shared site for all users in your organization, i suggest you use the onedrive for business library. its storage is 1tb and far exceed 100 gb. you can share the onedrive for business library with each user in your organization to achieve your requirement.

1. click the onedrive icon to access the onedrive for business library.
2. click return to classic onedrive.
3. if there is no library ribbon in the classic onedrive mode, click ribbon to enable it.

4. click the share icon to share the library with users.

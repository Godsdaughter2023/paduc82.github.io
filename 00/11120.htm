Hi

Chances are that the dates are text. Use the formula =TYPE(A1), where A1 is a cell containing a "date". If the formula returns a 2 then the date is actually text (The max or min of text = 0).

If this is the case then you need to convert the text to a date.  The easiest way to do this is to insert a column to the right of the current Date column.  Then add the formula =DATEVALUE(B2) and fill this formula down the new column, by double clicking the fill handle (small square bottom right corner of cell) on the cell containing the formula.

You can the use this new column in your PivotTable.

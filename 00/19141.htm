hansv mvp say:

Do not enter the formula in the text box itself. Instead, while the text box is selected, enter the formula in the formula bar.

Remark: you must use a formula that refers only to a cell address or to a named cell. You cannot enter an expression such as =B3+6, or a function such as =CONCATENATE(A1,B1).

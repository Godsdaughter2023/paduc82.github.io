Making this change to the format does not make sense if Microsoft wants to make it easier for enterprises to adopt Yammer.  Using email notifications is a common method to easily move users into the Yammer conversations.  I have used Outlook as my "Yammer monitoring" tool for years, writing rules to manage the many posts I routinely read and multiple groups I monitor.  Replying was easy and quick.  Now the posts are all mucked up with the graphical images.  Forcing people to post in Yammer is just not making it easier for adoption of the tool.

I have to switch a mail account from POP to IMAP. How do I avoid - Microsoft Community

robertmacleay say:

I have to switch a mail account from POP to IMAP. How do I avoid losing all of my Rules?

I have to switch a mail account from POP to IMAP. 

How do I convert my dozens of complicated POP rules for sorting incoming mail to IMAP rules without spending weeks rebuilding them from scratch?
dianeoforegon say: Sorry but there is no way to move rules from POP to IMAP. I suggest you take screen shots of your current rules to help re-creating.

Most likely you can simply your rules if you use categories. See some suggestions here:

Get Started Lesson: Learn to Setup Rules

I use a combination of  Mail Views (Smart Folders) and Rules to read and sort my messages.

Search in Entourage 2008 is so improved that having multiple folders and subfolders is no longer needed to find mail.

Tip: IMAP Inbox needs to be kept clean since it's polled by the server where POP just downloaded what was on the server.


  • Do Not Rebuild Identities with Exchange and IMAP accounts. Delete the accounts (this deletes the cache files in Entourage). Do the rebuild then add back accounts. Better yet start over in a new Identity.
  • Make sure you have plenty of free space on your drive. More info for Entourage rebuild
  • Rebuilding does not fix all issues. It can make some problems worse. Even though the Database Utility will make a backup, I advise making a copy in the Finder first.
  • You can delete the rebuild version after you are satisfied with the results. This copy will be named like this: Main Identity [Backed up 1-21-2012 13.35]  

Tip: If your Identity will open, before you rebuild, check "On My Computer" Inbox and Sent folders to be sure they are under the 2GB limit. Generally this is less than 10,000 messages but with large attachments could be less. Make subfolders e.g. Inbox 2011, Inbox 2010, Sent 2011, Sent 2010 etc to help reduce the number of messages in a folder. (Check for any large archive folders you might have in addition to Inbox and Sent).

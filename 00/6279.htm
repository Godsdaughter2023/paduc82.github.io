Have a try with this code.

 

Will take each row of 20 cells and split into two rows of 10 cells.

 

Sub WrapUnder()
'assumes 20 columns originally

'anything else, user does the math to adjust K1:T1 range<g>
Dim i As Long
    For i = Range("A1").End(xlDown).Row To Range("A1").Row Step -1
        Cells(i + 1, 1).EntireRow.Insert
        Cells(i, 1).Range("K1:T1").Cut Cells(i + 1, 1)
    Next i
End Sub

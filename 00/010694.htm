k.c.s. say:

If you do not see the "DATE ADDED" column in the Zune software (I didn't) then you need to right-click your mouse on the header row word at the top of the list you are viewing, the word "Song" in my case, and pick "Choose Columns" from the box that pops up.  A window will open with all of the columns you can view.  Put a checkmark next to "Date added" if one is not already there.  This will allow you to view and click on the "DATE ADDED" column to sort by newest/oldest by the date you added the item to your collection.

 

There is also a "Choose Columns" button in the bottom-right corner of the window that I never noticed until now that should allow you to do the same thing.

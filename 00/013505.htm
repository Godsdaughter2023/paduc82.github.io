We currently don't have the option to mass delete my email messages by date. I will forward the suggestion over to our product team as consideration for a future release.

 

The workaround that I can think of is to sort the messages accroding to date (from latest to oldest), select the latest emails and move it to a newly created folder and then delete the rest of the emails in your inbox. Here's how to do it:

 

1. Sign in to your Microsoft account

2. Go to your Inbox

3. Click View:All which is located at the upper left portion of your screen and select All

4. Click Arrange by which is located at the upper right portion of your screen and then select Date

5. Create a new folder by clicking New folder

6. Select all the latest emails by putting a check mark on it and move it to the newly created folder

7. Empty your inbox click clicking Inbox, right click it, and select Empty folder

 

You can also use Sweep function of Hotmail. Here's how:

 

1. Sign in to your Microsoft account

2. Go to your Inbox

3. Select the emails and click Sweep and select Schedule cleanup

4. You can either choose Move or Delete emails. The oldest email that you can move/delete is not older than 60 days

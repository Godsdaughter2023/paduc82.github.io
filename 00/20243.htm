You can use one same domain for your public site hosted on a third-party service provider and Office 365 Exchange Online email service at the same time, and these two services won’t affect each other. You need to add the following DNS records to achieve this:

1. For Exchange Online email service, you need to add an MX record, a CNAME record, record and a TXT record. Office 365 will guide you through this when you add the domain to Office 365.

About how to add the DNS records for your domain, we suggest you turn to your domain hosting provider for more help.

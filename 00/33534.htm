greg carmack - mvp 2010-2020 say:
I'm Greg, an installation specialist and 8 year Windows MVP, here to help you.

The Digital License is tied to the PC and not your Microsoft Account, which only associates it for ease of reactivation using the Activation Troubleshooter.

A way to remove it from your Account is to Reset the PC: http://www.laptopmag.com/articles/reset-windows....

It may then associate with a new MS account that signs in on that PC, but if not it doesn't matter because the DL is permanently assigned to that PC and will not go away unless the motherboard is changed - steps to handle this scenario here: https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/help/20530/...

I hope this helps. Feel free to ask back any questions and let us know how it goes. I will keep working with you until it's resolved.

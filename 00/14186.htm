jay freedman say:

Alex, I have the same version/build of Office, although it isn't Office for Business and isn't connected to a domain. On this (WiFi-connected) Canon printer, I can't reproduce the problem. Maybe Kristine can come up with a solution or confirm that it's caused by a recent update.

In the meantime, here's a workaround that may help you: Create a second printer definition in the Windows settings dialog, identical to the current one except that you set it to single-sided instead of duplex. Name it so that its purpose is clear. Instruct users to choose that "printer" in Word when they want a single-sided printer. Each time Word is restarted, it will go back to the duplex "printer" (assuming that's selected as the Default printer in Windows).

arminatorx say:

Since you say you "reinstall" the games, you probably have a savegame for that game probably, right?

Often those "Installation Stopped" problems come from a version difference between the game you try to install and already existing savegames or DLC add ons. 

So go to My Games and Apps, and highlight the game you try to reinstall. 

Press the Menu button on the controller and choose to Manage your game. 

On the left, remove the LOCAL savegame copy. DO NOT delete the save from all consoles!!!

Then make sure you also uninstall any add on and DLC content that is already on your console's storage. 

And also make sure you have enough space on the drive you're installing to. 

If you have multiple drives and just check the free space in "My Games and Apps", then this shows the free space across all drives. 

So for example, if you try to install a 4 GB game, and you have 2GB on the internal drive free and 3 GB on an external USB drive, then the console will show 5GB free, but will be unable to install the game on any drive, as there is no 4 GB free on any drive.

Also, just to add another tip, I would also check the internet connection. Make sure that if you're using WiFi on your Xbox, that the signal is good through the router and it's not losing signal.

Here are the solutions you can perform:

Solution 1: Check for updates

Make sure you have the latest updates on your Windows 10 PC. From Start, choose Settings. Go to Update & security, and then select Check for updates.

Solution 2: Check Game DVR Settings

In the Xbox App, select Settings, and then Game DVR. Make sure that the Record game clips and screenshots using Game DVR setting is On.

Solution 3: Verify the hardware requirements

To record game clips with Game DVR on Windows 10, you need one of these video cards:

  • AMD: AMD Radeon HD 7700 series, HD 7700M series, HD 8500 series, HD 8500M series, R9 series, and R7 series or later.
  • NVIDIA: GeForce 600 series or later, GeForce 800M series or later, Quadro Kxxx series or later.
  • Intel: Intel HD graphics 4200 or later, Intel Iris Graphics 5100 or later.

To check the video card on your PC, go to the search box on the taskbar and search for “Device Manager.” In Device Manager, expand Display adapters.

Solution 4: Restart Game DVR

Sometimes, a recording doesn't end correctly. When that happens, you'll need to restart Game DVR to fix the problem:

  1. Close the game.
  2. In the search box on the Windows taskbar, enter Task manager, and then select that desktop app.
  3. In Task Manager, select More details.
  4. In the list of running processes, select the Broadcast DVR server (bcastdvr.exe) process (if it's still there), and then select End task.
  5. Restart the game and try recording again

Keep us posted.

techfreak_ say:
Cancellation is not an option when a renewal is being processed. A payment method cannot be deleted if it is currently in use by a subscription or auto-recharge. To ensure your card does not get billed, you should cancel your subscription 3+ days prior to its expiration. This ensures the auto renewal does not take place, or reverses if it does. If you missed that window, it will generally attempt to charge the card for the next 3 days until your subscription expires. Once the subscription expires without payment, the subscription will auto cancel. You should no longer be billed to the card. At that point, the card can be deleted from the system.

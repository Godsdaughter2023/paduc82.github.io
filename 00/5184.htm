suzanne s. barnhill say:

Are you wanting to add a page at the end? It must contain content (an empty paragraph). At the end of the last page, press Enter to create a new, empty paragraph, then press Ctrl+Enter to insert a page break; repeat to leave a blank page.

A better way, if you are wanting a blank page between chapters, is to use an Odd Page or Even Page section break. Word will then insert a totally blank page, as needed, to force the next chapter to start on an odd or even page.

If you're wanting a blank page at the beginning of the document, press Ctrl+Home to get to the beginning, then Ctrl+Enter to insert a page break.

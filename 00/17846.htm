Ecxel 2010

How do i get my formulas NOT to change and keep the reference when I copy a cell or range of cells?
myall_blues say:

You can change cell references in formulas from relative ('unlocked') to absolute ('locked) by addition of the $ symbol.

So for example:

A1 - row and column both change when copied/pasted

$A1 - row number will change but column does not

A$2 - column changes but row does not

$A$2 - neither the row nor column will change

Use a mixture in your formula according to your requirements.

How do I pause a sound during a slide and hide the music. - Microsoft Community

jack_76 say:

How do I pause a sound during a slide and hide the music.

Pause a sound

Hi I have inserted a sound file mp3 to play across slide, how do work round it for pause whenever I want it to be pause if the sound is set to be hidden when playing.

thanks a lot for reading.

heather ackmann say:

You will need something (a shape, object, text, etc.) to click to act as the trigger.

1. Go to your Animations tab (yes, even though you are working with sound), and click on Animation Pane to open the Animation Pane.
You should see your sound located in the animation pane.

2. Click Add Animation-->Pause. This will add a pause animation in your animation pane. By default, the pause action will be set to On Click, meaning you will be able to pause the audio with a click of the mouse button; however, what I find especially useful is to trigger the animation on click of a particular slide object.

 

To set a trigger

1. click the drop arrow to the right of the pause animation (in the animation pane), and select Timing.

2. On the Timing tab for the Pause Audio animation, click the Triggers button at the bottom of the pane.

3. Select the option "Start effect on click of" and select an object from the drop menu.

 

That way, that one object will become your pause/play button for your presentation audio.

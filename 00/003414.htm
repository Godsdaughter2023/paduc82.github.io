How do I change my degrees from Celsius to Fahrenheit (or Fahrenheit to Celsius) on MSN Weather?

There are three relatively simple ways to change your default temperature settings.

Option 1: Update your site-wide preference for a signed in user:

  1. Sign in to your Hotmail or Outlook.com account on www.msn.com.

  2. From the upper right hand corner, click on the Settings gear.

  3. Select “Settings” from the dropdown menu.

  4. Under “Market and Language” section, select either Fahrenheit or Celsius under “Display Temperature In.”

  5. Go back to the previous page.

Option 2: Update your non-signed in preference on the www.msn.com homepage.

  1. To the right of the weather module in the upper left hand corner of the page, click on the  icon. This will open up a box asking you to set your home location. Select either Fahrenheit or Celsius under “Display Temperature In.”

  2. Click Done.

Option 3: Update your non-signed in preference on the weather homepage.

From the weather homepage, click on the temperature units marker (C or F) to the right of the current temperature.

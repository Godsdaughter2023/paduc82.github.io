lenoredavis say:

email delete on phone dont want to delete from computer

in my blackberry, if i delete an email from my phone, it leaves the email on my computer email server.  i have an htc arrive, is there a way for me to delete messages from the phone without it affecting the email on my computer server?
steven _fyiguy_ hughes say: It all depends on how you have your email accounts setup on the phone.

  • POP mail downloads the message from the server to the device and it no longer resides on your server
  • IMAP mail leaves the email on the server until a delete request is made and then it is moved to the deleted email folder.
  • Exchange email will do the same as IMAP, but has the ability to store emails/remove from the server and store locally in folders.
How email is handled by your mail server may be configured by your mail provider. So you may want to check account settings with your provider.

To check your email settings on your device go to settings->system->email & accounts-> select an email account

    1. Go to the Search box on the taskbar, then type in Device Manager and click on it
    2. Under Device Manager, double click the Batteries category
    3. Double click Microsoft Surface ACPI-Compliant Control Method Battery, then select the Driver tab, and click on Uninstall - OK. (The driver will be automatically reinstalled when you start your computer.
    4. Turn off your pc.
    5. If you have a Removable battery - remove it and then insert it back
    6. if you don’t have a Removable battery - skip this step
    7. Turn on your computer
    The steps to turn off the Battery saver:
    1. Go to the search box on the taskbar to type Settings and click on it
    1. Click on System icon
    2. Find Battery Saver on the left-pane and click on it to see overview of battery saver feature
    3. Tap on Battery saver settings, then uncheck the option Turn battery saver on automatically if my battery falls below to disable Battery Saver feature.

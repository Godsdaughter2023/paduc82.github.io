A site's certificate allows Internet Explorer to establish a secure connection with the site. To install a new certificate on your Internet Explorer 11, you may follow the steps below:

  1. Open the website with https.
  2. You will receive "There is a problem with this website's security certificate". Click Continue to this website (not recommended).
  3. In the Certificate Invalid popup, click View certifcates.
  4. In the Certificate information, click Install Certificate.
  5. In the Certificate store, check Place all certificates in the following store and then Browse.
  6. Check Show physical stores.
  7. Highlight the Trusted Root Certification Authentications, and then click Next to continue.
  8. Click Finish to complete the certificate import.
  9. You will see the import was successfully.
  10. In do you want to install this certificate, click Yes to confirm it.
  11. After the certification installation, you can run MMC and Certificate to confirm the installation.

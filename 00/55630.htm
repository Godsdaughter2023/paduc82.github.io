Is %SystemRoot%\MyPrograms\PFE\pfe.exe % 1, really the location of your program? I used the MyPrograms folder as an example, also there shouldn’t be a space between the % and 1.

 

Associations are shown in the registry (and can be changed rather than using assoc and ftype) as follows:

1. HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT. In the left pane, look for the extension, for example .txt. The (Default) field in the right pane contains the filetype, e.g. txtfile.

 

2. HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT. In the left pane, scroll down beyond the extensions and look for the filetype, for example txtfile then click shell\open\command. In the right pane, the (Default) field shows the full path of the program that opens it, e.g. notepad.exe.

Incoming voicemail calls not logged in call History

There are 4 possible types of phone calls:

1) Incoming, answered

2) Outgoing, dialed

3) Incoming, missed (incoming call, not answered, caller hung up, no voicemail)

4) Incoming, voicemail (missed call, not answered, caller left a voicemail)

Call types 1, 2, and 3 are shown in the History tab:

"Phone #

Day Time, Incoming/Outgoing/Missed" <- types 1, 2, 3 all show up properly in History

But type 4 (incoming->voicemail) is not. If you miss an incoming call and the caller leaves a voicemail that call is not shown in history at all. You get a voicemail alert of course, but because the call is not logged in history the only way to figure out who called is to call into voicemail and check messages.

I think that 4th type of call should be logged in History as well:

"Phone #

Day Time, Voicemail"

You'll need to put this together:

A 16:9 display attached to the PC (one that the display driver on the PC recognizes as a 16:9 display) and a display board that supports the display at 16:9 resolutions.

You'll need to tell PowerPoint to display the presentation on the external display; then it will recognize that it has a 16:9 display to work with, so it'll scale your 16:9 presentation to fill the whole display, rather than trying to fit it to 4:3.

How to parse data using the Text To Columns command in Excel

This protocol acts as a mirror of your Microsoft account on your Windows Live Mail or vice versa. This means, whatever activity that you do on your web-based Outlook.com, this will automatically sync on your Live Mail.

1. Open Windows Live Mail.

2. Sign in to the account that you want to have the file.

3. Click Contacts.

4. Click Import then select Comma separated values (.CSV).

5. Click Browse then navigate to where the file is stored in your computer and double click the file.

6. Click Next.

7. Click Finish.

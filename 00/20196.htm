RustyDargel replied on

I have read that "read receipts" cannot be sent in your emails in Outlook.com that replaced Hotmail.com in Microsoft internet explorer. However, I have found a way to send "read receipts" using Outlook.com email which has been working for me. I have Microsoft Windows 7 with Window's Live Mail.  You first have to add Your Outlook.com email program to Window's Live Mail.  Go to Window's Live Mail and select "Accounts"  at the top.

amit tandon say:

Refer below image:

Column D illustrates how to search for specific text only ie. "apple". Enter below Formula in cell D2 & copy down:

=ISNUMBER(SEARCH(","&"apple"&",",SUBSTITUTE(","&A2&","," ",",")))

This formula adds commas before and after the string in cell A2 & also inserts a comma in lieu of blank spaces, and then searches for ",apple,". This will ignore Pineapple or any letters preceding or succeeding "apple".

In your formula, replace the first OR option with the above, so that the updated formula in cell B2 is as below:

=IF(OR(ISNUMBER(SEARCH(","&"apple"&",",SUBSTITUTE(","&A2&","," ",","))),ISNUMBER(SEARCH("Banana",A2)),ISNUMBER(SEARCH("Grape",A2)),ISNUMBER(SEARCH("Pear",A2))),"MyFruit","-")

lingyusun say:

hi c. michaelbaker,

good to know you can successfully import the data from your local drive to sharepoint online now. thanks for your time and cooperation and i’d like to summarize here for benefiting other community members who runs into the similar issue:

issue description
============
failed to migrate local fileshare to sharepoint online via a network import according to https://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/library/mt210445.aspx. (about 700 mb data)

root cause
===========
there are some issues in the csv file which is submitted via the office 365 portal > import.

assessment
===========
use the command submit-spomigrationjob to submit the migration job for avoiding upload the csv file.

for example:

$azurelocations = set-spomigrationpackageazuresource -sourcefilespath 'd:\contoso\data' -sourcepackagepath 'd:\contoso\package' -accountname <account name> -accountkey <account key> -filecontainername sharedfiles -packagecontainername sharedfilespackage

the account name and the account key can be found at office 365 admin center > import > + > upload files over the network:

#instead of importing the csv

submit-spomigrationjob -targetweburl 'contoso.sharepoint.com' -migrationpackageazurelocations $azurelocations

again, appreciate your working with us.

if this helps you, please let us know by marking "yes" the post which was helpful, or answered the question.

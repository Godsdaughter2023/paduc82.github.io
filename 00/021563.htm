For me it sounds like you wanted to create the new notebook within an existing one. Could happen by navigating to the wrong location.

If you renamed your "old" notebook before trying to create the new one and now tried to create the new one with the"old" name, this will not work, because the renaming of a notebook in Onenote doesn't rename the corresponding Windows folder. The result would be overwriting your old notebook.

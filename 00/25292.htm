jay freedman say:
I think you can replace the entire Document_Open procedure with this:

Private Sub Document_Open()
    Dim ils As InlineShape
    For Each ils In ActiveDocument.InlineShapes
        If ils.OLEFormat.ClassType = "Forms.ComboBox.1" Then
            With ils.OLEFormat.Object
                .Clear
                .Font.Size = 9
                .TextAlign = 1 ' 1 = Left 2 = Center 3 = Right
                .AddItem "Select One"
                .AddItem "On Target"
                .AddItem "Behind Schedule"
            End With
        End If
    Next
End Sub

This depends on two assumptions: (1) All the combo boxes are InlineShapes (not floating Shapes) in the body of the document (not in headers/footers/text boxes/footnotes/endnotes), and (2) you want the same items added to every combo box.

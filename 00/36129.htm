CTF Loader (ctfmon.exe) handles things like speech and handwriting recognition.

If you don't want to use it, do the following.
  1. Go to Window Control Panel and double click Regional and Language Options.
  2. Click the Languages tab and then click the Details button at the top.
  3. In the Text Services and Input Languages menu which appears, click the Advanced tab.
  4. Remove the checkmark from both boxes and then click Apply/OK.

suzanne s. barnhill say:

There are no .dic (dictionary) files in the proof folder (\Users\user_name\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Proof).

The only .dic file you would see in that folder is CUSTOM.DIC, the user dictionary, and that would likely be from earlier versions. The Word 2013 CUSTOM.DIC is in C:\Users\<username>\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\UProof. This is also the folder where you will find exclusion dictionaries for your installed languages.

The .lex and .dll files that power the spelling, grammar, hyphenation, and thesaurus functions are at C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Office\Office15\PROOF for locally installed 32-bit Office 2013; the location may be different for Office 2013 in Office 365 (subscription version), but the file you would be looking for for the U.S. English spelling lexicon would be MSSP7EN.LEX.

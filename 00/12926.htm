You really need to provide a little more information griffinterrence but I'll take a stab assuming that you have a recent Windows Mobile device and that you have Outlook installed on your computer.

So, let's try this first:

With your device connected, open Device Manager and locate your device.  Remove it from Device Manager and then then disconnect from your PC.  Reboot the PC and soft reset your device.

Now, on your device, go to  Start - Settings - Connection - USB to PC. and uncheck Advanced network functionality.  Now reconnect your device and it should discover it and load the correct drivers.  Assuming you have WMDC (or Activesync if using XP) installed, it will automatically start ... itt takes a few (long) seconds but it will start.

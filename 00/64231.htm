vijay b say:

Hi, Mike,

I would suggest you to manually remove your account and sync it back.

a) Open the Mail app.
b) Press Windows Key + C, click on settings.
c) Click on Account, Click on your synced account.
d) Scroll down and Click on Remove Account.

You will be prompted again, click on Remove Account once again if asked.

After you have successful un-synced your account try to sync it back once more.


a) Open the Mail app.
b) Press Windows Key + C, click on settings.
c) Click on Account, Click on Add an account.
d) Follow the steps which will be prompted to you

Refer the following links.

http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-8/mail-app-faq

How do i turn off security essentials temporarily so i can upgrade a - Microsoft Community

ecmiller say:

How do i turn off security essentials temporarily so i can upgrade a program?

I need to upgrade the firmware to my backup drive and the website tells me to disable the virus protection program before doing this.  How do i turn off the Security Essentials so I can perform the upgrade?
kosh vorlon say:

Hi ECMiller,

 

If you can disconnect from the internet before doing it (and it isn't required for the installation) that would be best. If required, I'd shut down everything else using the internet (email, IE,...) to reduce your risk while not protected.

 

Then open MSE and go to the Settings Tab and click on Real Time Protection and uncheck the box to Turn on Real Time Protection and click Save Changes. The border should change from green to red, the MSE icon should change from green to red, and the Home Tab in MSE should say that your computer is not protected or at risk or something like that. At that time it will essentially be deactivated and should not interfere with your installation.

 

Once the installation is done, please return to MSE and recheck the box to Turn on Real Time Protection and Save Changes. Everything should change from red to green. The Home page should say your system is protected. You'll be back in business and the program will be installed.

 

That's it. If it were me, just to be on the safe side since you were without protection for a period of time (especially if you needed to remain connected to the internet), I'd update MSE and then run a full scan just to be sure nothing snuck in while it was off - but that's up to you.

 

I hope this helps.

 

Good luck!

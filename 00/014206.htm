    Dim objPara As Paragraph

jllatham say:

This will let you specify the columns to fill, with defaults of A and AZ:

Sub GetAddressLetters()
  'will put the letter portion of
  'a cell's address into specified
  'cells in row 1 of the sheet
  Dim startColumn As String
  Dim endColumn As String
  Dim CLC As Integer ' column loop counter
 
  startColumn = InputBox("Enter first column letter:", "Start Column", "A")
  endColumn = InputBox("Enter last column letter:", "End Column", "AZ")
  For CLC = Range(startColumn & 1).Column To Range(endColumn & 1).Column
    Cells(1, CLC) = Mid(Cells(1, CLC).Address, 2, _
     InStrRev(Cells(1, CLC).Address, "$") - 2)
  Next
End Sub

If you always want columns A:AZ without the user having to enter them:

elbab say: Hi there!

Please be informed that unlinking account is no longer supported. While it's not possible to unlink your Skype and Microsoft accounts, you can move your Skype account to a different Microsoft account.

Note that moving your Skype account can only be done once, so it is a must that the Microsoft account you want to use is correct and verified.

To delete a Skype account without affecting your Microsoft account, move your Skype account to a different Microsoft account, it can be a dummy or an old account.
Follow these steps to move your Skype account...
 
1. Sign in to the Microsoft account that your Skype account is currently linked to. 
2. Select the Your info tab.

3. Select Manage your sign-in email or phone number.
4. Next to your Skype name, select Move to another Microsoft account. 
5. Double-check that you know the correct Microsoft account you want to use, and then select Next to continue.

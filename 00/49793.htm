Hi Tom,

Click OK to open the Services (Local).

Hi Bernd Gramberg and welcome to the Skype Community! 

All Skype accounts were migrated to basic Microsoft accounts last October 2016, but it is still possible to sign in to Skype using a legacy account (Skype Name-created accounts). You just need to make sure to log in using the credentials you used to initially create the account (If you created it using a Skype Name, sign in using a Skype Name, same goes with accounts created via email addresses.)

If you are automatically signed in, sign out from the app and make sure to sign in using your Skype Name. If the issue persists, try to Force stop the app and then Clear data. Re-launch and then log back in. 

Updating your Skype and Microsoft accounts is one good way to unify your sign in across all Microsoft services. It is very important to be very careful when you link your accounts, because once an account has been linked, it is not possible to unlink them.

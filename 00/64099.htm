Hi,

2.       What tools have you used to disable the program?

Method 1: Try to disable Windows Live Messenger from the Tools menu and check if that helps. To do this, follow these steps:

1.       Open Windows Live Messenger

2.       Click Tools on the menu bar. (If you do not see the menu bar, press Alt on the keyboard).

3.       In the drop down menu click Options.

4.       From the options available on the screen click Sign In on the left of the screen.

5.       Now on the right side of the screen, under General, click to Clear the box which says Automatically run Windows Live Messenger when I log on to Windows.

6.       Click Apply and OK.

netsh winsock reset catalog

netsh int ip reset

c. Reboot your computer and check internet connection

Step 2 - If you still have no connection (flush your DNS cache)

a. Press windows key + X together and select Command Prompt (admin)

b. Type the following command and hit enter after each line:

ipconfig /flushdns

ipconfig /registerdns

ipconfig /release

ipconfig /renew

c. Reboot your computer and check internet connection

suzanne s. barnhill say:

Okay, in the first place, you don't want an Even Page break, because that makes the next page begin on an even page, but if you are restarting numbering at 1 in the next section, then Word fill force an odd page; ordinarily I would expect it to just change the Even Page break to Odd Page. You'll generally find it easier to see section breaks in Draft view, and they will be clearly labeled as to type. Just make sure that your second odd page starts with an Odd Page break, and Word will insert a totally blank page (no header, footer, or page number) after the previous odd page (make sure that that odd page doesn't end with a page break).

Since you've obviously got front matter numbered separately, you might want to look at http://www.word.mvps.org/FAQs/Formatting/front_matter_2007.htm.

jeeped say:

Subject to my previous post, here is a first draft at a UDF for your problem.

Tap Alt+F11 and when the VBE opens immediately use the pull-down menus to Insert, Module (e.g. Alt+I, M). Paste the following into the new pane titled something like Book1 - Module1 (Code).

Public Function udf_Count_Specific_Ages(rR1 As Range, rC1 As String, _
  rR2 As Range, rC2 As String) As Long
    Dim o As Long, v As Long, w As Long, ttl As Long
    Dim vV1 As Variant, vV2 As Variant, rQ As Range
    For Each rQ In rR1
        o = (rR2.Column - rR1.Column)
        vV1 = Split(rQ, Chr(10))
        vV2 = Split(rQ.Offset(0, o), Chr(10))
        If UBound(vV1) = UBound(vV2) Then
            For v = LBound(vV1) To UBound(vV1)
                ttl = ttl + Abs(LCase(CStr(vV1(v))) = _
                  LCase(rC1) And LCase(CStr(vV2(v))) = LCase(rC2))
            Next v
        End If
    Next rQ
    udf_Count_Specific_Ages = ttl
End Function

Tap Alt+Q to return to your worksheet. Use like any other function. In my example below, I am looking for two year old males.

=udf_Count_Specific_Ages(<first criteria range>, <first criteria>, <second criteria range>, <second criteria>)

=udf_Count_Specific_Ages(C2:C7,"Male",E2:E7,2)

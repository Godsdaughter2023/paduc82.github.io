max, singapore say:

Assuming your source table as posted is in A1:D4
this simple formulas set-up will deliver the exact stacked data set that you desire 
In F2: =INDEX($B$1:$D$1,,INT((ROWS($1:1)-1)/3)+1)
In G2: =OFFSET(A$2,MOD(ROWS($1:1)-1,3),)
In H2: =OFFSET(B$2,MOD(ROWS($1:1)-1,3),INT((ROWS($1:1)-1)/3))
Copy F2:H2 down to H10

Above tested fine in xl03, and it should work just as well in xl07 or higher

don guillett excel developer say: Sub lineemupSAS()
Dim i As Long
Dim dr As Long
For i = 2 To Cells(1, Columns.Count).End(xlToLeft).Column
dr = Cells(Rows.Count, 1).End(xlUp).Row + 1
Cells(1, i).Copy Cells(dr, 1).Resize(3)
Cells(2, 1).Resize(3).Copy Cells(dr, 2)
Cells(2, i).Resize(3).Copy Cells(dr, 3)
Next i
End Sub

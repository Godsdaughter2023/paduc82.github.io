According to this article, the UNEXPECTED_KERNEL_MODE_TRAP bug check has a value of 0x0000007F. This bug check indicates that the Intel CPU generated a trap and the kernel failed to catch this trap.

Bug check 0x7F typically occurs after you install a faulty or mismatched hardware (especially memory) or if installed hardware fails.

click a second time and it goes to Favourites..same with the CC and BCC (IF YOU CAN FIGURE OUT WHERE BCC WENT AFTER YOU CLICK IT (ONE FOOLISHNESS)

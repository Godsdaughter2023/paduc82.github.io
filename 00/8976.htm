1. reset ie to clear the cache data and cookies by referring to this link https://support.microsoft.com/kb/923737.
2. delete the previous credentials via control panel > credential manager > windows credentials and remove the credential one by one.

3. after that, right click onedrive for business app and choose "stop syncing a folder" to stop syncing the previous libraries, restart the onedrive for business app (right click app icon and choose "exit").
4. then you can sync your new office 365 libraries via right click > sync a new library and type in the library url you want to sync. you can also click sync on the portal and click change to change a different local folder to sync the library.

note: there is a login box will pop up and prompt you to input your office 365 account when resyncing the library. then use “tab” + ”enter” via keyboard to proceed.

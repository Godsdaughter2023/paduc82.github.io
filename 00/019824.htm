mandyshaw say:
In addition to Andreia's mention of text messages, you will lose your 'phone settings (e.g. email accounts, wifi settings, internet sharing security key), application settings, and the data in any application that doesn't have an upload/sync/backup mechanism built into it (e.g. any PDFs you have stored in Adobe Reader). Therefore, you need to make a note of as many of these things as possible. Also, although this is unusual, any app that is no longer listed on the Marketplace will not be re-installed.

One final point, you may find the free Reinstaller app useful- if you install this from the Marketplace after the hard reset, it will lead you through re-installing your apps.

In that case I would use a slightly modified version of Max's suggestion:

=SUMPRODUCT(--(TEXT(A1:A100,"mmmyyyy")=TEXT(NOW(),"mmmyyyy")),B1:B100)

Or:

=SUMPRODUCT(--(TEXT(A1:A100,"myyyy")=TEXT(NOW(),"myyyy")),B1:B100)

However, these depend on your location. In some international locations the date format codes used in the TEXT function may be different.

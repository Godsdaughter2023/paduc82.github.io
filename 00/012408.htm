You can create shapes in an illustration program such as Corel Draw, Adobe Illustrator or the free InkScape.

Here is a workaround about Outlook 2016 missing ‘start application’ and ‘run a script’ actions in Rules.

 

Please do the following steps to add a new value in your computer’s registry:

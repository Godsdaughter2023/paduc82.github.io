64 bit Download/USB Installation Procedure:


 1.  Connect the USB cable and verify that the unit is powered on.


 2.  Click on Start, right mouse click on Computer and then left click on Properties.


 3.  Left click on Device Manager on the left hand side.

 5.  Double-click the first one, then click on the Driver tab and then Update Driver.


 6.  Click on Browse your computer and then Browse again.


 7.  Select the Desktop from the list and then the Canon folder.

 8.  Click Next and then OK and this will install the driver.


 9.  Close the Properties box.


10.  Perform the same steps for the remaining Canon entries listed in Device Manager.

radfordhound say:

Hi, all. It seems several people are having this issue. I too downloaded the update and was surprised to see that I was suddenly unable to play split screen with my sister. However, after many desperate attempts, I found a solution that seems to be working and posted it on this thread at the Terraria forums: www.terrariaonline.com/.../second-player-cannot-join-split-screen.119896

I'll re-post the solution here for convenience. Hope it works for you as well. ;)



Start the game as normal using your Xbox Live Gold account with your previously working world and character, but choosing to "play online."


Connect another controller, and press Start on it. A dialog should pop up allowing you to choose a Xbox Live account. Choose the Guest account. Then make a new character, doesn't matter what he/she looks like or is called, just accept the default.


If all goes well, the game should properly let you play split screen with this guest account.


Now, pause the game from the 1st controller and choose to return to the main menu.


On the 2nd controller, sign out. Then start the game as regular with your first controller, but this time with "play online" off.


Now press start on the 2nd controller. This should bring up the sign in dialog. Choose any account, even non-xbox live accounts work.


Choose your storage device and character.


TADA! At least for me, the game worked perfectly after that. Hope fully it'll work for you.


Sorry for the extra long post. Just wanted to explain the solution in detail.



Thanks for reading, and I hope this solution fixes your problem as well. :)

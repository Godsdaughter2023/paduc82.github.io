jay freedman say:

The arrow buttons you referred to were removed in Word 2013 and 2016. There is no direct replacement for jumping by section. The Navigation pane lets you choose to jump by Graphics, Tables, Equations, Footnotes/Endnotes, or Comments, but not sections.

As a workaround, you can assign keyboard shortcuts to the commands, which don't have shortcuts by default.

  • Open the Options dialog and go to the Customize Ribbon page.
  • Click the Keyboard Shortcuts Customize button.
  • Scroll the Categories list and click on All Commands.
  • Scroll the Commands list and click on GoToNextSection.
  • Click in the "Press new shortcut key" and press the keys you want to use for a shortcut. Then click the Assign button.
  • Repeat the previous two steps for the GoToPreviousSection command.
  • Close both dialog boxes.

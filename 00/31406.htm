Here's what people don't 'get' about the updates: 

Windows 10 is fundamentally different from every previous version of Windows. With previous versions of Windows, you got a complete operating system. Microsoft can no longer afford to wait years to issue a new version of Windows. Technology changes too quickly. They'll get buried.

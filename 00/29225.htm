bill smithers say:

Hi,

It depends on the type of License you have, as to whether you can transfer it or not.

Preinstalled Windows OEM Licenses (HP, Acer, etc) and Microsoft System Builder OEM Licenses are tied to the first machine that they are installed on and the License dies when that machine dies.

OEM Licenses are non-transferable.

 

If you bought your own Windows 7 Ultimate Retail License and installed it yourself, yes you may transfer it to another Computer.

Retail Licenses are transferable.

 

Just be sure that you only have it installed on one machine at a time.

One License means One Installation.

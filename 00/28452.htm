Stacking Fractions

How do I stack a fractions like 3/8 or 7/8?  Word will only do it to 1/4, 1/2 or 3/4 fractions

hansv mvp say:

¼, ½, and ¾ are built into all fonts as separate characters.

Unicode fonts may have some more fraction characters, but it's obviously impossible to include all possible fractions as individual characters in a font.

You can use Insert > Equation, and select Fraction to insert any kind of fraction into a document.

kholt say:

I've solved my issue.  In short, I needed to load all the root certificates in the certification path for my OWA certificate.  As I suspected, the primary problem was not having the correct certification(s) loaded on my Q.

 

Here are detailed instructions for the benefit of others who have the same problem.

 

As mentioned above, I couldn't load the OWA certificate (named "mail.mycompany.com") onto my Q because it's not a root certificate.  However, when looking at the OWA certificate by doubleclicking the status bar in IE, I selected the "Certification Path" tab.  In my case, there were two other certificates above the OWA certificate.

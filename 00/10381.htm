suzanne s. barnhill say:

You can replace styles.

  1. Ctrl+H to open the Replace dialog.
  2. Click More to expand the dialog.
  3. With the insertion point in the (empty) "Find what" box, click Format | Style, and select Heading 3.
  4. Place the insertion point in the (empty) "Replace with" box, click Format | Style, and select Heading 4.
  5. Replace All.

Repeat to replace Heading 2 with Heading 3 and Heading 1 with Heading 2, then replace Heading 4 with Heading 1.

Another way to accomplish the same thing is to open the Styles pane, make sure Headings 1-4 are displayed, click in a Heading 3 paragraph, then right-click Heading 3 in the Styles pane and choose "Select all x instances of Heading 3." When they are selected, click Heading 4 to apply that style. Repeat for the rest as above.

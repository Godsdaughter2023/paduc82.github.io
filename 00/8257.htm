suzanne s. barnhill say:
Considering just the entries that are missing the period leaders, are those entries much shorter or longer than the other entries? Often this problem is a result of placement of tab stops in the relevant TOC style; in that case, however, the problem also results in page numbers being in the wrong place as well as lacking leaders. The situation arises when there is a tab stop (or hanging indent) fairly far to the right in the TOC style. A very short entry will stop short of this tab stop, and so the tab that should go to the right-aligned tab stop at the right margin (for the page numbers, with leader) instead stops at the first tab stop/hanging indent.

lex del say:

Hi soohoahn,

If you want to disable the reset protection on Lumia 640, you may follow the steps below:

  1. If you want to carry over things to your next phone, back up your stuff.

  2. Select Settings > System > About > Reset your phone.

  3. If you're ready to erase all of your personal content on your phone, select Yes, and then select Yes again to verify.

  4. Type your Microsoft account password, and after a few moments Reset Protection will turn off.

You can also turn off the Reset protection by going to account.microsoft.com/devices > Next to the phone you no longer own, select Remove phone > I'm ready to remove my phone check box, note the recovery key > Remove (The recovery key will display on the page and will also be emailed to you) > When you're certain that you have your recovery key saved, select the I've kept a copy of the recovery key check box, and then select Okay. Then, forward the recovery email to your phone recipient.

You may find more details by visiting this article: Protect my phone.

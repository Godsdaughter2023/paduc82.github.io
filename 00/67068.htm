richie m. say:

You may want to uninstall and reinstall the Mail App. 

Click on Start and search for PowerShell and choose run as administrator. 

Copy and paste this command to the PowerShell window. 

get-appxpackage *microsoft.windowscommunicationsapps* | remove-appxpackage

Mail App should be gone if the uninstallation is successful. 

Open Window Store and search for Mail & Calendar and download the app again or use this link: https://www.microsoft.com/en-hk/store/p/mail-and-calendar/9wzdncrfhvqm?rtc=1

xbf diego m say:

Hi there,

Was Dolby Vision working for you before you sent it in for repair? What TV model and brand do you have?

I recommend going to Settings>Display & sound>Video output>4K TV details and make sure that your TV setup supports Dolby Vision which will be represented by a green checkmark. Then go to Video modes and make sure Allow 4K, HDR10, and Dolby Vision is checked.

Keep your color depth at 8-bit and refresh rate at 60Hz at 4K UHD as your resolution.

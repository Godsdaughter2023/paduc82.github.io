mike h.. say:

Hi,

Your formula is recognising zero as a value but i'm afraid it's not a good formula and likely to give unpredictable results'

IF(D6>0,0-D6,IF(D6+E6=0,"",E6))

Let's say D6 =10 the formula will evaluate 0-10 which equals -10

But at the same time E6 could be -10 so the second part of the formula IF(D6+E6=0 is also TRUE but won't evaluate.

You will have to decide what order you want the formula to evaluate in or explain; in words if you want, what your wanting the formula to do because if it was meeting your requirements then you wouldn't be asking the question.

Or maybe the answer you looking for is this

IF(D6>=0,0-D6,IF(D6+E6=0,"",E6))

Now if D6 is > or equal to zero the first part of the formula evaluates.

The upgrade has not yet been staged to all users. Why it hung when you checked, I can't say. It may be due to a server busy situation.

Your second issue, with the install itself not proceeding, I can't tell you why that happened, either. It was probably attempting to stop the services and uninstall v1, but encountered some issues. A reboot is the first thing I'd suggest. Had that not worked, a manual uninstall.

The new option allows you to throttle the usage of the CPU by MSMPENG when scanning. Without throttling an underpowered machine is rendered useless for all other activity. A high powered machine would typically not need throttling, though overall performance while scanning will be diminished. Scanning by any antivirus program uses quite a bit of CPU unless the vendor has already throttled their scanner by default. Scanning is very resource intensive. Yes, throttling to a certain level frees up the rest of the power for other services/tasks/programs/etc.

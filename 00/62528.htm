Hello Chrispy19,

Thank you for writing to Microsoft Community Forums.

In the latest versions of Windows, Microsoft has a feature called adaptive brightness which utilizes ambient light sensors to detect surrounding levels of light. Adaptive brightness will conserve battery life since the display is one of the power component. You need to disable adaptive brightness to get ambient sensor to work perfectly. To do that, you may follow the below steps:

Step 1: Disable Adaptive brightness.

  • Click on the Start button and search for Power Options to launch the corresponding Control Panel section.

  • Click on Change plan settings on whichever power plan you’re on or want to change.

  • Select Change advanced power settings to launch the Power Options Advanced Settings.

  • Scroll down to Display section and click on the plus sign to show the submenus. Now click on the plus sign next to disable adaptive brightness to expose its settings.

  • Change the settings as you wish, then click on OK to Save.

Step 2: Update display drivers.

Driver updates for Windows 10 and many devices (such as network adapters, displays, printers, and video cards) are automatically downloaded and installed through Windows Update. If you are having trouble with a device, you can try fixing it by updating or reinstalling the driver. To do that you may follow the article on how to update drivers in Windows 10.

tricky300 say:

Have you disconnected the drive's data cable for some reason recently?

This can be caused by (re)attaching the cable upside down, either at the controller/motherboard end or at the floppy drive end.

The cable should have only one coloured edge to denote where pin 1 is (normally red but I have seen blue too). Your controller/motherboard should have pin 1 marked (sometimes with a triangle, sometimes with the digit 1). The floppy drive should also be similarly marked. Make sure the cable has the correct orientation at both ends. Also, the cable will have a twist on some strands, this should be at the floppy drive end.

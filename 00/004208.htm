cortig say:

Yeah, Outlook is not very good at importing (and horrible at exporting).

Here's what I would do:

Import the contacts in the OS X Contacts app. It should gracefully deal with the CSV (or whatever other format you throw at it…).

Once imported, select all the contacts and drag them to the desktop to generate a .vcf.

You should be able to drag and drop the .vcf to the Contact list in Outlook to import them all.

Be aware though that this is in no way an option to SYNC the contacts. It's a one-way street.

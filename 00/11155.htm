john korchok say:

You can work around this by clicking in the second column where the numbers continue from the previous cell instead of restarting. Click on the first entry in the second column. Click on the HOME>Numbering button and change the numbering style to a different style. Then change it back again. The numbering should restart at 1.

Please note, this only works for 2 columns. Editing the text will make the numbers continue again, so do this change as a final step after you've entered all text.

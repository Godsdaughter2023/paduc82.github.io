jetta48 say:

The circle indicates that you PC is working on something - this used to be a hour-glass on previous versions of Windows (you can select what the pointer looks like in Control Panel->Mouse.

The fact that your page is moving up and down (and sometimes it can be other things too) is just a symptom of your PC lagging behind on previous instructions you have given to it via or mouse or keyboard.

Basically when this happens, just wait a moment without touching either mouse or keyboard for your PC to catch-up with itself - patience is a virtue.

If the cursor stays busy for a protracted period of time then either the program or Windows may have crashed. Press CTRL + ALT + DEL and try ending the offending program. NOTE: If you end the program this way you will lose any unsaved data in that program. If your system is completely unresponsive, even to a CTRL + ALT + DEL then you will have to power down and reboot your PC. NOTE: If you do this you will lose all unsaved data in all programs. Hopefully your PC will reboot - if it doesn't then you will be into Startup Repair territory.

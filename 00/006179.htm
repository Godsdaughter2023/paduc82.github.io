    In Excel, time is held internally as a fraction of a day. So to subtract 12 hours from a cell, you simply subtract 0.5.

    So if you wish to subtract 5 minutes from cell A2 you use a formula like this:

    =A2-5/60/24

    If you copy that formula down, it will auto-adjust the row number and A2 will become A3, A4, A5, ...

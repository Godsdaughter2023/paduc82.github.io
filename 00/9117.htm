hansv mvp say:

The maximum number of significant digits of a number in Excel is 15. That's why you get all those zeros after ...115.

To keep all the digits, you can enter the number as text. Either format the cell as Text before entering the number, or prefix the value with an apostrophe '. If you enter '800220180327115726099, Excel will display 800220180327115726099 and treat it as text.

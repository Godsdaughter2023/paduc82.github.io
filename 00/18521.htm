cody c. say: Hey PoloUW,

Outlook 2011 doesn't support Read Receipts, so there's not an option to enable or disable this feature.

In most scenarios like this, the only option is to change the setting in Entourage and then reimport your settings into Outlook.

Once you've disabled read receipts in Entourage, you'll want to remove the existing email accounts in Outlook by choosing Tools > Accounts from the Menu Bar. Once there, you can press the Minus button in the bottom-left corner to remove accounts.

When you've removed the accounts, follow the steps below to import the correct settings and accounts into Entourage:

  1. With Entourage closed and Outlook open, choose File > Import... from the Menu Bar
  2. Choose Entourage information from an archive or earlier version and click the Next arrow
  3. Choose your version of Entourage and click the Next arrow
  4. Only check the box for Preferences and Accounts and click the Next arrow
  5. Finish the wizard to finish importing the settings

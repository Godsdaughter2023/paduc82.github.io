Well, I'm replying to my own post, since no help so far. Someone else might find it useful to read what I discovered about synching Outlook with Google mail:

Go to Google calendar, in the left pane under the heading Calendar, hover over name of the calendar you want to subscribe to, and click the little arrow on right. Click Calendar settings, then the Private Address ICAL button. Highlight and copy the long address string.

Log in to www.live.com and select Calendar, click Import, Subscribe. Paste the Calendar URL into the entry box and type a name for Calendar Name. Click subscribe. After a little while it should sync with your Google calendar, and you'll see the top calendar entry for Today displayed in the live Start Page tile, plus an indication of how many other appointments there are for Today. You'll also see the top calendar entry for Tomorrow, plus the number of other appointments.

Outlook only seems to check every few hours, but that's ok for most purposes.

It's a shame that the live tile doesn't just scroll through the appointments for Today, but at least you will now have updated Google calendar appointments in your Outlook calendar.

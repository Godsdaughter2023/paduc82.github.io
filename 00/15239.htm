Here's how to resize text in 10074...note that this behavior seems buggy to me because of what I had to do to get it to work...without further ado...

1) Right click on your desktop to bring up the context menu

2) Select 'Display Settings'

3)Move the slider under 'Change the size of text, apps, and other items' to the 125% position

4) Hit 'Apply' and then Sign out and back in as directed

5)repeat 1 & 2 above

6)Select 'Advance Display Settings'

7)From the 'Customize your Display' screen select 'Advanced sizing of text and other items'

EDIT: OK, I found out how to change only font sizes under 10074.  It *is* possible to adjust the Windows screen fonts independently of any screen magnification--this also solves the "blurry font" issue people have had:

1) Right click on your desktop to bring up the context menu

2) Select 'Display Settings'

3)Move the slider under 'Change the size of text, apps, and other items' to the 100% position

4) Hit 'Apply' if necessary and then Sign out and back in as directed

5)repeat 1 & 2 above

6)Select 'Advance Display Settings'

7)From the 'Customize your Display' screen select 'Advanced sizing of text and other items'

8) Under 'Change the size of items' select 'set a custom scaling level' to 100% also

9) Hit 'Apply' and if necessary sign out and back in

10)If you signed out & in repeat 1,2,6,& 7 above, and finally...

11)Notice that 'Change only the text size' is no longer grayed out and you can change it at will (I set all of mine to 13 points.)

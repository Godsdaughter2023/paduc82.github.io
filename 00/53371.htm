I have imaged and backed up both drives.  I also understand it will not restore data after the restore point.  My question is, since I have system protection on both drives turned on, and I want to try to do a System restore to a previous time rather than a complete backup restore of everything, which drive is restored? A System Restore will work only on your boot drive. However having System Protection enabled on your data drive will allow you to restore previous versions of changed files. See this Microsoft article for a more detailed explanation and System Protection FAQs - http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows7/What-is-system-protection

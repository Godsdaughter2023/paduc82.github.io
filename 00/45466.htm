    Can I transfer files from one user account to another on the same - Microsoft Community

    kateschnell say:

    Can I transfer files from one user account to another on the same Windows 7 computer?

    Can i transfer or copy certian files that i choose from 1 windows user account to another from the same computer? If so how do i do it or where do i go to find out how?
    foxfifth say: Log in with Admin privileges or as the user that currently has the files and go to Start > Computer (in the Start menu list at the right under Documents, Pictures, etc)  (or do Winkey +E)  and in Computer double click on the drive where the files are located and then double click on the "Users" folder and then double click on the original user's folder and work your way to the files you want to move.  Leave that window open (and optionally drag it to the right side of your screen until the mouse hits the edge and it will automatically resize to fit the right half of your screen or you can resize it manually).  Then start the same instructions over again (Start > Computer etc) but the second time when you open the "Users" folder, navigate to the new user.  With both windows open you can now drag files from one window to the other.

    An option other than copying the files is to share them as described here: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows7/Share-files-with-someone

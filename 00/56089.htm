Usually if the brightness adjustment isn't working, forcing the computer to redetect your monitor will fix it up.  Here's how to do it quickly:

  1. Right-click your Start button, choose Device Manager
  2. In the list, find the "Monitor" section and open it.
  3. Right-click your monitor and choose "Uninstall", then click OK.
  4. On the toolbar at the top, click the "scan" button (has a magnifying glass icon on it)
  5. Right-click the Start button again, choose "Mobility Center"
  6. Drag the brightness option here to see if it is working now

Hi Kkkk2343245,

 

Here are some answers to your questions.

  1. When the value of OptOutOfGrooveBlock is True, users is allowed to sync OneDrive for Business libraries with the old OneDrive for Business sync client. When the value is changed to False, the Groove sync is blocked.

And while it may not be a problem for many users now, it will definitely be for all the schools, teachers, offices, engineers, etc. that do rely on MathType for their equation editing.

Does anyone know of any workarounds or even where the problem might be?  DesignScience is looking into it, but it's a new one for them and so far they have found no underlying problem with the product nor have they any history of an issue like this in the couple of dozen years this product has been on the market.

Anyone who thinks MS has not had a revelation and that a change of behavior & attitude into the future has not, ALSO, come along w/ the Windows 10 Platform is in denial...

1. Hold times no longer insanity!
2. No jungle of options to wade through (phone buttons to push)
3. People answering w/ whom language is not a problem
4. Phone connections that are not terrible.

Have been trying to express for about 6+ months there is more worthwhile change coming to the future from Microsoft than just an OS.  The positive differences from the past that accompany the Windows 10 Platform are huge.  It all may touch different Users in different ways, to differing degrees, some soon, some later.  The perks & benefits will be noticed and appreciated, all in good & due time, maybe overdue time but, better now than never.

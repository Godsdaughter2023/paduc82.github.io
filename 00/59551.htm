internet expert say:

Your Dell monitor's manual suggests that it is only using HDMI 1.4, which is only sufficient for 4K@30Hz. It seems 4K@60Hz is only feasible when connected with DisplayPort, which is not supported by the Xbox.


Xbox One S requires that the 4K display has at least HDMI 2.0, which has the necessary bandwidth to display 4K@60Hz. 


4K TV sets are pretty much expected to carry this HDMI specification, but 4K computer monitors are more of a mixed bag.

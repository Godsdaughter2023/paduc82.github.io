hal hostetler - mvp-outlook say:

There is no way with import or export from either application to move email from Outlook to WLM. I've come up with a safe and easy way to do this by employing a free Hotmail/Live Mail account:

1. First, get yourself a free Hotmail/Live Mail account.
2. Download and install the Outlook Hotmail Connector .
3. With this in place, connect to the Htomail/Live mail account with Outlook. You can now drag and drop whole folders full of email from Outlook to Hotmail/Live Mail.
4. Close Outlook and connect Windows Live Mail to the Hotmail/Live Mail account.
5. WLM will not allow you to drag whole folders from Hotmail/Live Mail to local storage, but you can create local folders of the same name and drag the contents of the moved folders from Hotmail/Live Mail to these local folders.

This process is bi-directional, you can just as easily use it to move email from WLM to Outlook.

To backup Outlook, merely close Outlook and then make a copy of your .PST file. Should you ever need to restore anything, you can open the backup copy by using 'File|Open|Outlook Data File' and drag anything you need from the backup copy to your primary .PST file.

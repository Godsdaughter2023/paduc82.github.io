jay freedman say:

The merged document contains a table, where each card is in a cell. When you click in any cell, you'll see the Table Tools tab on the ribbon; under that tab, click the Layout tab.

On that ribbon, in the Table group at the left, click the Properties button. In the dialog, under the Alignment title, change the value in the "Indent from left" box to something slightly larger, maybe 0.1". That will move everything to the right by that amount. Be careful that you don't push it too far, or the right end of any long line may print onto the wrong part of the label sheet.

As for the step 6, since the users are synced from the on-premises server, the mailbox attributes are managed by the on-premises Exchange server. If you decommission the on-premises Exchange server, you can only manage these attributes via third party tools, such as ADSI edit which is not supported by Microsoft after extending the AD schema.

echo4ent say:

It appears that the Sidewinder "92626" is not a HID compliant Joystick.  It was an update to an old Gameport standard joystick, and the USB was just a conversion from the gameport  protocol, which required specific drivers.

The XAC requires HID compliant joysticks, I am sorry.

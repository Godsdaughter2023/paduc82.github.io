  2. Type the range to be selected [e.g., A50:K297]
  3. Press return

  • If you aren't sure of the starting & ending cell references, another option is;
  1. Click the starting cell
  2. Scroll to reveal the ending cell
  3. Hold Shift while you click the ending cell

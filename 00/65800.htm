¡firedog say:

Server Error: 4102

The usual cure for this problem is to remove the account, then add it back again:

  1. Right-click on the account name in the folder pane and select Remove account.
     
  2. Close down and wait five minutes while the program adjusts to the change.
     
  3. Re-launch Windows Live Mail, then press Ctrl-Shift-T to start the Add your email accounts wizard.
     
  4. Fill in your email address, password and display name, click Next and Finish.
     
  5. Wait for the program to re-download your folders and messages. This might take some time (minutes or hours, depending on the number of messages involved), so leave it alone to get on with it.

frankpo1 say:

I solved this problem for my Acer E5 notebook with Intel 620 graphics. Running Win 10 64 bit, update 1709.

After loading update 1803 could not use second LCD screen, Win said there was no second screen.

Tried uninstall /updated video drivers, both for Intel video card, and Acer Monitor. Did not work, problem remained.

Rolled my system back to 1709 update. All screens worked fine.

Then copied Mstsc.exe and mstscax.dll from Syswow64 subdir to USB. These 2 files are the 1709 version for Remote Desktop operations.

Reloaded Win 10 upd 1803.

Renamed these two files now part of 1803 to Mstsc_old and Mstscax_old. (you have to change ownership from trustedinstaller to Admistrator, under Security Tab, and allow full control,  to do this or you will get 'access denied' message)

Copied 2 files (1709 version) from USB to syswow64 subdirectory.

Then updated Intel drivers via device list.

All screens worked fine when done.

In summary, there is a problem with the 2 files under 1803, replace them w older version and remote monitors will work.

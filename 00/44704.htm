Here is how you can find out what it is:

  1. Click the Start globe
  2. Type the word msconfig in the Search box and press Enter
  3. Untick every tick mark under the Startup tab. Windows will start regardless.

Can I bring the xbox to Aisa? My concern is the power voltage - Microsoft Community

carollau1 say:

Can I bring the xbox to Aisa? My concern is the power voltage different.

Anyone knows the answer??
arminatorx say:

If you have a Xbox One X or Xbox One S, then you can take the console anywhere. You'll just need an adapter for the wall plug. 

If you have a Xbox One (without S or X, the one with external power supply) or a Xbox 360, then you will need to know what voltage your country uses, and what voltage the other country uses (if you simply mistyped Asia, it would help WHICH Asian country you exactly mean, as different Asian countries have different voltages).

If you have a Xbox 360 or Xbox One with external power supply, you would need a voltage converter that can sustain at least 300 Watts of power.

Try it this way. Note I reduced the formula from full columns to 1000 rows. Full columns makes the workbook very slow and should be avoided if possible. Increase the 1000 rows if you have to to catch all your data.



=SUM(Data!U2:U1000)-SUMPRODUCT((ISNUMBER(MATCH(Data!W2:W1000,{7016,7017},0)))*(Data!U2:U1000))

=SUM(Data!U1:U1000)-(SUMIF(Data!W1:W1000,7016,Data!U1:U1000)+SUMIF(Data!W1:W1000,7017,Data!U1:U1000))

sandy johnson say:

1) Pick the slide that you want to use as the "slide master."

2) Assuming that everything you want on your master is on this particular slide, Select everything on that slide expect the text boxes.

3) Hit Ctrl-X to cut everything from the slide.

4) Don't panic.

5. Go to View > Master > Slide Master

6. A blank slide master for "Title and Text" layouts is available

7. Hit Ctrl-C to paste every thing on the Master.

8. With everything still selected, right click and choose send to back.

9. Reformat the text.

10. Close slide master view.

Now each time you Insert > New slide your slide will use the master layout you just designed.

But like Rick says, there's  a whole lot more to it, but this will get you started.

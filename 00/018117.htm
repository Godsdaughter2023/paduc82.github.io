    With ActiveChart.Axes(xlValue)

An update after a Windows 10 installation change the windows.old - Microsoft Community

rudolfaigner say:

An update after a Windows 10 installation change the windows.old content

Hi,

a Windows 10 preview installation saves all programs and data in a folder called windows.old.

That's very helpful to rebuild the system after the installation. Unfortunately if there's an automatic update after the installation it wipes out all the content in this folder with a new content. All in a sudden all the information and content of previously installed apps/programs are gone.

That's really disturbing and gives a lot of additional work to rebuild a system.

Regards

Rudolf Aigner

Good night, I have Onenote for windows 10 and I want to keep a notebook saved in a pen drive because in my house I have measured connection for internet and I don't want Onenote being sincronized in any moment, it will be destroying my internet connection, is that possible?

desiree yau say:

Hello Valery,

There's an option in OneNote that you can manually sync your notebooks so it won't use your internet data. Here's how:

  1. Start OneNote.
  2. Click on the ellipsis (Settings and More).
  3. Select Settings.
  4. Choose Options.
  5. Turn off Sync notebooks automatically.

Now, if you wish to save your notebook to a pen drive, you can simply copy and paste it into the drive. Doing so you'll be able to open your notebook to a different device that has the OneNote app.

Hope this information helps you. In case you need further assistance, don't hesitate to post back.

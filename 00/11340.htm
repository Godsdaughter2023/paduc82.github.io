jeffonethebeach say:

I had this problem and is now fixed. Turns out, it is a common issue with the Nokia flash unit.  After doing all the useless things I found on forums, like uninstalling software, resetting factory, etc., I finally took it to a terrific repair place (Technibility in Glendale, CA), and the guy told me that the flash unit was bad and needed to be replaced.  He showed me that it was actually physically, broken, and told me that this was common.  The part was $20 and he charged me $20 labor.  I guess you can do it yourself if you want to.  The key, was that after installing the new flash unit, you need to do a soft reset on the phone (power down won't help) for the Nokia to recognize the new part.  Once Brian did that, it worked like a charm.

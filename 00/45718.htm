cmdrkeene say:
Well you have certainly seemed to demonstrate that some process, intermittent at best, is changing what is being sent to the clipboard.  My suspects are Microsoft Office Clipboard or Microsoft Office OneNote, both of which would show in the Notification Area by the clock by default, if they were running. (Do make sure they're gone from MSCONFIG's Start up tab, at least).

If that fails, since you've already tried the clipboard monitor, the only other thing I can think of to track it down would be going into MSCONFIG on the Startup/Services tab and disable everything (except required services), and add them back in batches, rebooting in between, to find which one causes the issue.  Not a very elegant exercise for sure.

On a related note:  I've noticed that some Microsoft Office plugins, COM add-ins, and some Excel "tool paks", will cause the clipboard to clear when initialized.  Drove me nuts:  I'd copy something, open Word, and then be unable to paste until I copied it again.

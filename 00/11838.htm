jllatham say:

A possibly much easier solution.

IF you want to ONLY keep the text to the left of the SMTP entry, then

=TRIM(LEFT(A2,FIND("SMTP",A2)-1))

and to prevent #VALUE! errors from showing up

=IF(ISERR(TRIM(LEFT(A2,FIND("SMTP",A2)-1))),"",TRIM(LEFT(A2,FIND("SMTP",A2)-1)))

**********************

and if you want to actually turn the results into an email link that you could click on to open up your email application and start an email, try this:

=HYPERLINK("mailto:" & IF(ISERR(TRIM(LEFT(A2,FIND("SMTP",A2)-1))),"",TRIM(LEFT(A2,FIND("SMTP",A2)-1))),IF(ISERR(TRIM(LEFT(A2,FIND("SMTP",A2)-1))),"",TRIM(LEFT(A2,FIND("SMTP",A2)-1))))

and you can hide column A from view to help cut down clutter and/or confusion.

a. user say:

in 2016 when a PC upgrade from win 8 to win 10 ?? what kind of license it is ? OEM-DM or OEM-COA-NSLP?

Neither, it's a digital license, but that's only a name. A digital license is actually no license at all. I'll explain:

To install Windows 10, you must have a license. But during the first year after Windows 10 was released, if you upgraded a copy of Windows 7 SP1 or Windows 8.1 to Windows 10, Microsoft let you upgrade without a license. Microsoft called that a digital license, but really, it's no license at all, even though it has a number attached to it. It's a permission slip to install Windows 10 without a license.

For that reason, you can't transfer your 'digital license' to another computer. Can't transfer a license you don't have in the first place.

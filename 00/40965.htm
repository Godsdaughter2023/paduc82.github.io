    Where does Windows store passwords? - Microsoft Community

    qaziqaisir say:

    Where does Windows store passwords?


    Where Windows save Administrative password.

    Suppose i am login in a domain base network, i  as a administrator loss my pass word how do i find it. Where windows store his pass word.

    Tell me both ways, if i am connect as administrator or as a limited user on domain base networking.


    wunders say: Passwords are not saved anywhere on your computer.  Only a cryptographic hash of the password is stored.

    If you are on a domain and you are a local administrator on your machine, then if you forget your password you need to connect your machine to the domain network and contact one of your domain administrators who can then reset your password on the domain.  In a domain, your logon credentials exist on the network, not on your local computer.  If permitted by the domain, a cached credential may exist on your local machine for use when not connected to the network.  Changing your computer from Domain to Workgroup will invalidate this domain credential and require a Domain Admin to re-add your computer to the domain.

    Change all options you find there to D

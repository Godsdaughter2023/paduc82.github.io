matthew horsley say:
Just make a shared folder on a server (I would not use the NETLOGON folder for drive mappings). Once you create a shared folder put bginfo.exe and bginfo.bgi in that new folder. Then add a drive mapping to your script.

Example. Say on your domain controller you create a new folder on C drive called scripts and share it its UNC path will be \\sm.ad\scripts and your script will look like this.

[script] @echo off
net use S: \\sm.ad\scripts
S:\bginfo.exe S:\bginfo.bgi /timer:0 /NOLICPROMPT

When your device goes in "Battery Saver Mode", it extends the battery life by limiting background activity and push notifications when your devices is low on battery which you've set to "30%".

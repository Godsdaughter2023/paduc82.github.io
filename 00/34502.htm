Hi ScallywaG! This is most likely an issue related to the surround sound setting of your console. For the games you've indicated, I have heard this issue in the past, and it has boiled down to the console trying to output in a format of surround sound which your headset or surround system does not support.

For example, if you're using a stereo headset, but your console is outputting 5.1 Dolby Digital, you may hear some sound (basically, the side channels mixed together) but the dialogue, which usually comes from the center channel, will be very dim or completely mute. (You may hear dialogue in some games, and that's because how dialogue is handled is up to the game developer - some may make dialogue come from all channels, others like Ubi or EA tend to limit it to the center channel, I've experienced this in the past.)

Can you verify, what type of headset or sound system are you using? And also, try going to "Settings -> Display & Sound" and see what it says under the "Digital Audio" section. It will depend on if you're using HDMI or Optical as to which item you need to look at.

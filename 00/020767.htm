Try this...

=MID(CELL("filename",A1),FIND("]",CELL("filename",A1))+1,255)

The formula can be entered in any cell.

The file must have been saved at least once and given a name for this to work.

DATE(Year,Month,1+N*7)-WEEKDAY(DATE(Year,Month,8-Day Of Week))

Where N = the nth instance. In your case N=4 (for the 4th Wed)

Day Of Week is a number from 1 to 7 for the day of the week. Where:

  1. Sun
  2. Mon
  3. Tue
  4. Wed
  5. Thu
  6. Fri
  7. Sat

In your case Day Of Week = 4 (for Wed)

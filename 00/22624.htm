cliffgu_msft5984 say:

Hi Rizni,

The total storage of the SharePoint sites( the pooled storage) is based on 10 GB + 500 MB * number of users. It is the storage you can use in SharePoint Online. In your scenario, the total storage you can use is 24.5 GB. Even if you select to manage storage limits automatically and each site is set to a maximum of 1TB, the pooled storage is far smaller than 1TB and you in fact only can use a maximum of 24.5 GB per site. But when you use 24.5 GB in a site, you will be unable to use other sites because the pooled storage has been used up. If you want to use other sites, you need to buy more storage. Alternatively, you can control the storage as much as you can use per site by selecting setting the storage of the sites manually.

So, Self Home remedy ... :)

Battery was draining instead charging when you connect to charger, due to short circuit caused by water inside changing terminal (My observation).

Follow the steps.

  • Leave the band2 to dry for few hours/days. 
  • Remove the charging terminal plate by removing 2 screws by T3 Trox type driver
  • Clean the terminal with dry cloth
  • Apply very little sealant (Silicon / Adhesive) all round where plate sit.
  • Tighten the screws
  • Charge the Band 2 for long time. Small cursor blinks first and then band2 wakes up.
  • If you reset the band2 then blue line progresses left to right.
  • Enjoy the Band2.

As far as I know, MSE does not scan mail, so I don't think that's the problem.  However, the symtoms are indicative of an add-in integrated with Outlook that is modifying the incoming data stream.  Try starting Outlook once in safe mode.  Hold Ctrl while you start it or press WinKey+R and in the Open field that appears type

outlook.exe /safe

Hello

The steps to delete a Skype contact depends on the Skype application that you're using on your Windows device. Please check and follow the steps that's applicable on your app.

If you're using Classic version of Skype:

To remove a contact from your contact list on the classic version of Skype:

  1. Sign into Skype.
  2. In your contact list, find the contact you want to remove. Right-click the contact name and select Remove from Contacts. Then confirm by clicking Remove.
  3. The contact is removed from your contact list but will still appear in the Recent tab with a question mark. Right-click the contact name to see the options: Add to Contacts or Block This Person.

If you're using Skype for Windows 10 (UWP):

  1. Sign in to Skype for Windows 10.
  2. In your contact list, find the contact you want to remove. Right-click the contact name and select Remove from Contacts.
  3. The contact is removed from your contact list but will still appear in the Recent tab with a question mark. Right-click the contact name to see the options: Add to Contacts or Block This Person.

The solution did not lie, apparently in anything in the forum or on the linked Microsoft page.

When trying to run Outlook as an administrator from the Outlook-not-working user account, I opened Outlook properties by right-clicking on the start menu. On the Security tab, I edited the account permissions to full control and clicked apply.

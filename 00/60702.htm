charline gal say:

Hi Hiroki,


Let's work on this together. Try uninstalling the Batteries driver from the Device Manager. Here's how:

  • Open Device Manager by simultaneously pressing Windows and R keys, type devmgmt.msc then press Enter
  • In the Device Manager, expand Batteries
  • Right-click on Microsoft AC Adapter and click Uninstall
  • Click OK when you see the warning message to continue uninstalling the driver
  • Click on Action menu then click on Scan for hardware changes option to restore the missing battery icon

Let us know if it works.

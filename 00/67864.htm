I'm assuming that your TV is too far from your router to easily run an Ethernet cable.


Open Control Panel> Network and Sharing Center and click Change adapter settings.


Shift-click to select both your WiFi and Ethernet adapters. Release Shift and right-click on either adapter and choose Bridge Connections. You should end up with a new icon labeled Network Bridge.


Connect your TV to the laptop using a Crossover Ethernet cable. A standard Ethernet cable will not work.

Word 2003 and 2007 flag "nosie" as an error (as does IE 11), but Word 2010 and above do not. That being the case, I guess you have no recourse but to add it to an exclusion dictionary. See http://wordfaqs.mvps.org/ExcludeWordFromDic.htm for instructions.

hansv mvp say:

Try a dynamic range. For example, a named range with formula

=OFFSET(Sheet1!$K$1,0,0,COUNTA(Sheet1!$K:$K),1)

will refer to the range from Sheet1!K1 down to the last filled cell in column K (assuming there are no blanks in between).

The named range will adjust itself automatically as values are added or cleared.

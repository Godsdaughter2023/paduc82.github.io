    Well actually with a 6 day workweek and running from 3 Dec 2016 through 24 Dec 2016 gives 19 work days, not 18.

    The usual reason for discrepancy is the setting for hours per day. The default is 8 and with that default, Project takes the total difference in working time between the start and finish and divides by 8. If you have changed the definition of a day or you have changed the working hours (i.e. the default is 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 1:00 PM through 5:00 PM), what you see in the Duration field will probably not be what you expect.

    What is your definition for a day (File/Options/Schedule group)?

    What are your shift start and stop times for your 6 working day calendar?

    Are your tasks manually scheduled or auto-scheduled?

    One thing that can be very helpful in figuring out time spans is to display the time along with the date in the Start and Finish fields. To do that, go to File/Options/Display group and select a date format that includes the time.

  1. Pasted bcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz into cell A1

  3. Pressed Control-D to fill the entire column (that's over a million rows of data)

jay freedman say:
The gray background in legacy form fields is controlled by the Form Field
Shading button, which is the second-to-last button on the Legacy Forms row
of the Legacy Tools button in the Developer tab (that is, just a few buttons
over from the one that inserts text fields). It is completely unaffected by
any of the other "shading" or "background" or "highlight" buttons. The only
options for that shading are on or off, no other colors.

Greg Maxey has a workaround for tabbing through rich text content controls
in http://gregmaxey.mvps.org/ContentControl_Grouping_And_Navigation.htm, but
it may be too limiting for your purposes.

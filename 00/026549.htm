Hi,

To assist you with the issue, I need more information:

How is the Gmail account configured in Outlook, is it as POP or IMAP type?



This issue is due to the fact that Gmail also has a special 'All Mail' and 'Starred' folder in addition to your Inbox folder. When you connect to Gmail via an IMAP account, you can either unsubscribe from these folders or apply a filter to your To-Do List view.



Note: POP3 connected Gmail account do not have this issue.



In order to troubleshoot the issue related to Outlook, try the steps below:

Step 1:

Unsubscribe from the ‘All Mail’ and ‘Starred folder’ for Gmail account:

You can unsubscribe from the ‘All mail’ folder by following the steps below;

Right click on the top of your Gmail mailbox (so one level above the [Gmail] folder itself) and choose IMAP Folders…
In the IMAP Folders dialog click on the Query button.
From the folder list that is being retrieved select; [Gmail]/All Mail
Press the Unsubscribe button.
Press OK to close the IMAP Folders dialog.
Step 2: Apply a filter to the To-Do List

If you’d like to keep those folders in Outlook, then you can also apply a filter to the To-Do List to hide the duplicate items. The filters that you need to add are;

Field: In Folder
Condition: doesn’t contain
Value: All Mail
and;

Field: In Folder
Condition: doesn’t contain §Value: Starred.

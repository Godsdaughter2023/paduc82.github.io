·         Go to

·         Go to Admin.

·         In Fall back action, select the action you want to apply if the disclaimer can’t be inserted.

·         Save the rule.

 

To modify message subject please follow the steps:

 

·         Go to

·         Go to Admin.

·         Under Admin Centers, click on Exchange.

·         Click on Mail Flow.

·         Select Rules.

    Transfering Gmail/Yahoo Contacts to Outlook.com - Microsoft Community

    arkengel say:

    Transfering Gmail/Yahoo Contacts to Outlook.com

    Technical Level : Basic

    Summary

    This article will go through the steps needed to migrate Gmail or Yahoo contacts to an Outlook.com account.

    Part 1 - Exporting Contacts from Gmail

    Part 2 - Exporting Contacts from Yahoo

    Part 3 - Importing Contacts to Outlook.com


    Details

    Part 1 - Exporting Contacts (Gmail)

    1. Log in to Google Contacts (or Gmail and then go to Contacts)
    2. In Contacts, click on More button under the search field, then select Export
    3. Choose All Contacts and Google CSV format



    4. Click Export and save it to a location of your choice.

    Part 2 - Exporting Contacts (Yahoo)

    1. Log in to Yahoo Mail and then go to Contacts
    2. In Contacts, click on actions and then select Export


    3. Choose Yahoo CSV format and click Export to save it to a location of your choice.

    Part 3 - Importing Contacts

    1. Log in to People hub in Outlook.com.
    2. On the right screen towards the bottom there is a button Start Import, under Import contacts. Click it!
    3. Select Google
    4. Click Browse and locate the file you created
    5. Click Upload



    6. You'll be asked to confirm if imported information is correct.
    7. Press Import to finalize the process.

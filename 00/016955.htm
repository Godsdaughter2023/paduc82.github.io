To avoid this, backup these items on the computer, then delete your existing ActiveSync partnership.  When you are asked if you wish to delete your My Documents folder, say NO.

Then set up a new partnership.  Upon sync, ActiveSync may ask you how you wish to proceed (1) Combine the data; (2) Replace the data on the pocket pc with data from the computer; or (3) do nothing.  If you get that message, choose REPLACE.  Otherwise ActiveSync should just sync all that data back to your pocket pc.  If something goes wrong and the data gets deleted from your computer (which it shouldn't), you'll have that stuff safely backed up.

jeeped say:

If you are absolutely sure that DE3600 is the last cell will data in it, walk through these steps,

  1. Press F5, type in DF3601 and hit <enter>.
  2. Press CTRL+Shift+<right arrow>. Release and press CTRL+<spacebar>. Release and press ALT+E then D or right click the highlighted columns and select Delete.
  3. Press Home, then press CTRL+Shift+<down arrow>. Release and press Shift+<spacebar>. Release and press ALT+E then D or right click the highlighted rows and select Delete.
  4. Press CTRL+Home to return to A1. Immediately Save or Save As if you don't trust your keystrokes.
  5. Press CTRL+End and you should be in the Last_Cell (e.g. DE3600).

It might seem like a lot, but it takes a lot more time to type it than it does to actually do it.

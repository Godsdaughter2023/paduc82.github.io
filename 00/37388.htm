For years now I've worked in the gaming retail industry.  I talk to gaming fanatics across all systems about many different topics, but one keeps coming up...

"Sometimes my graphics look like they didn't load properly, if at all."

"Can I fix that?!"

Yes, you certainly can!  The XBOX 360 offers a great solution.  Simply installing your game to the hard drive!  When a game is installed, it will allow content to load much faster, graphics will sharpen almost instantly and the overall flow of game play is seamless.  It also causes less wear and tear on your disc drive, which is something I think we can all appreciate!  This is how you install a game to your hard drive!

Step 1: Insert the game you wish you install into your disc drive

Step 2: Go into the game details.  It should be the "Y" button for pre-Metro dashboard update, and the "X" button for post-Metro dashboard update.

Step 3: Once you into the games details, there should be an option that states "Install game to hard drive".  Click that!

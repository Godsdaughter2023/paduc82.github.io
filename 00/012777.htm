grschinon say: You first need to know what number is used as an entry point to your voicemail. It isn't necessarily the number you dial to pick up your voicemail.

Dial *#61# and request the details for voice calls. You'll see a number and a timeout. Let's assume the number is 0123456789.

Next, if you want to stretch the timeout to 30 seconds (it can be anything from 5 to 30 seconds in increments of 5 seconds), dial this code:

**61*0123456789*30#

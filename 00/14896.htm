suzanne s. barnhill say:

Many of the replies that have been posted to this question through the NNTP Bridge are not being displayed, but it has been pointed out that Envelope Address is the style used for delivery addresses. This style can be modified. It can be changed to single spacing, for example, or, if the problem is Spacing After, that can be removed.

The real issue here, however, is that the Envelope Address style also includes a frame of defined size, and that size accommodates a fixed number of lines of text. The size of the frame may also be modified, however, through the Modify Style dialog, though of course the size of the envelope and positioning of the frame on the envelope will limit the usable size. The position of the frame can also be modified through the Format Frame dialog.

An easy way to access the Envelope Address style for formatting is to click the Add to Document button in the Envelopes dialog. This will give you an envelope that you can see on the screen. When you click in the delivery address, you should see the hashed frame border around it. If you right-click in the address paragraph, Style... should be one of the entries on the context menu. Click on that, then Modify, then Format, then Paragraph or Frame as desired.

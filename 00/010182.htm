Your issue is being caused by also having your account configured as a POP3 account. When a POP3 account checks for email (by default every 30 minutes), all emails that are still on the server will be marked as read.

You really only need to configure your account as an Exchange account (automatically done via Auto Account Setup) the POP3 account isn't needed at all in Outlook. On you Android smartphone, you can either configure the account as an EAS account or use the Office 365 app.

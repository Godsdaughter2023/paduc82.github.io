An organization is leveraging Microsoft Office Business Premium Open Volume Licensing and recently moved from hosted Exchange Mail to Office 365 mail. If the organization and its employees are using a Microsoft Exchange Account through this service offering for Office 365, this would likely work, but I wanted to ask the experts out there the following questions.

1. After transitioning from Exchange mail, does Office 365 Mail still run through an Exchange server since it is cloud-based?

2. Would a 3rd party "Exchange Online Backup" such as SkyKick for Office 365 still backup Office 365 Mail even if they are using Outlook Client or Outlook Web App? 

OR

3. Will the organization have to use the Exchange Account in order to leverage a 3rd party backup like SkyKick?

4. Is an Exchange Account through Microsoft Office Business Premium included or an add on?

anna ma msft say:

Hi Braun1CS,

 

Thanks for your posting. It seems like your organization have recently performed a migration from Exchange on-premises to Office 365. To better know about your situation, I’d like to confirm whether your organization are currently in Office 365 pure online, or in Exchange Hybrid environment?

 

If all users in your organization have fully moved to online, and if “Microsoft Office Business Premium Open Volume Licensing” you mentioned is Office 365 Business Premium, as this subscription including Exchange online service, user can now access Exchange online service to send/receive and manage their emails.

 

#1. Yes, Office 365 mail service still running on Exchange server and it is cloud-based.

 

#2 & #3.  In my view, the third party will back up Office 365 mail no matter whether user are using Outlook Client or Outlook Web App since user’s mailbox is hosted in online Exchange server. To obtain more detailed information, I suggest that you may contact the third-party provider.

 

#4. It is included and not need an add-on. When admin create an account in Office 365 tenant, this account should be Office 365 for Business online account. Office 365 services and features are account-based, from Exchange service aspect, the account is also an Exchange online account.

Anna

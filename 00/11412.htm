Sub doit1()
Range("FullData").SpecialCells(xlCellTypeBlanks).Formula = "="""""

Sub doit2()
Range("FullData").SpecialCells(xlCellTypeBlanks) = "'"

Note that "=""""" contains 5 double-quotes at the end.  Alternatively, write "=" & Chr(34) & Chr(34).

Dim c As Range

Transfering music - Microsoft Community

peterlaflamme1 say:

Transfering music

How can I transfer music from my phone to my Media Player

cmdrkeene say:

Well you wouldn't really transfer it to your Windows Media Player directly, you would transfer it to your computer.  Then you could use Windows Media Player to open and listen to those music files.

It's like how you might transfer a Word document to your computer and then use Microsoft Word to open and read those files.  The files are not transferred into Word (Word doesn't "store" anything, it just reads files stored somewhere else).  Media Player is the same - it doesn't store music, it just plays music files.

In any case, it really comes down to what kind of phone you have, where you purchased the music and how you downloaded it to your phone originally.  If the files are just regular music files on your phone, you may be able to just plug your phone into your computer with a USB cable, and open it like it was a USB thumb drive to drag-and-drop the music files to your computer desktop.

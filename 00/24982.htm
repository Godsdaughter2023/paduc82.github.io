Hello,

 

There are three ways on how you can sort your messages or delete the duplicate emails.

 

1. Manually delete or move each messages.

2. Use the Sweep feature of Outlook.

3. Sort the emails according to conversation.

 

First, manually deleting the messages requires you to select a message and click Delete on the upper portion of Outlook.

Your licensing was tied to the motherboard by a technology called "digital entitlement."  That's why there is no product key.  You do need a retail license (product key) to install Windows 10 on a new motherboard so that the computer can be registered as a Windows 10 device.  After that, of course, you will not need a product key for re-installations.

rohn007 say:

If you already have Office 365 Personal activated, submitting another product key will EXTEND the the expire data. No harm will be done. You will not "lose" anything. You will NOT have 2 copies running parallel.

You have 2 options.

One is to make sure the "autorenew" option is turned off on the current subscription. Then just wait for it to expire.  If you are lucky, there will be a "grace period" after the expire date that it continues to work. Reports of how long that grace period is varies. As soon as it goes into "limited function mode", where you can no longer save changes to existing files or create new ones you can submit the new key to activate the subscription again.  If you go for this option, make sure to keep your key safe. If you lose it, you are out of luck.

The other is to just submit the key now. It will extend your current expire date by a full year.  So if you have 6 months left right now, afterward you will have 18 months.

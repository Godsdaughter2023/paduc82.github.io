phillip m jones say:

They changed the measurement of an inch in 2016.

In 2011 and below the Fine adjustments of an inch is based on 16's of an inch like in a Standard ruler.

In 2016 they changed to 10ths of an inch.

For example in 2011 and below you can a margin as close of .250 (or 1/4 of an inch).

In 2016 you can not have margin narrower than .300 inch and increments above change by a 1/10 of an inch.  A 2011 Document will convert from 1/16th of an inch to a 1/10th of an inch in 2016 thus throwing all documents off. 

Most of the time because of the subtlety there should not be too much of a change. But once in a while depending on font style of font and point size of the font and really throw off formatting. 

Why they changed it I don't know.

lemp say:

You can't put 10 pounds of sand in a 5 pound bag.

To increase the number of columns of a spreadsheet that will fit in a Word document, you can ...

  • Set the page orientation to Landscape
  • Decrease the page margins to as small as allowed by your printer (or zero if you're not going to want to print the document)
  • Decrease the font size to the smallest font you can read
  • Select the entire table, right-click, select Autofit > to Window (this will result in narrow but high cells; you probably won't like how it looks)

This screenshot of a Word doc shows two copies of the same table.  The page orientation is Landscape and the left/right margins are set to 0.5 inch.  The top copy shows what happens when you "Autofit to Window."  The entire table fits within the margins (note the 4 columns at the right that don't show in the lower copy), but the contents of the columns have been squeezed to fit.

ashish mathur say:

Hi,

Here is a workaround.

1. Edit your formula to force an error in the cell instead of blank.  So in your base data, if your formula is =IF(B5=2,"",10), then change it to =IF(B5=2,NA(),10)

2. Refresh the Pivot Table and right click > Pivot Table Options > Check the box for "For Error Values show" > OK

Hope this helps.

I've probably missunderstood this problem, particularly the part about "50 equally spaced point", but "need to identify all points manually", seems contrdictory to me?

Suppose you have the following data starting in A1:C2500:

              x              y       Y1 Mark
61 54 #N/A
72 59 #N/A
21 61 #N/A
59 3 #N/A
69 19 19     x
1 22 #N/A
86 31 #N/A
95 64 #N/A
57 11 #N/A  

 

Your X's in A, your Y's in B and the formula

=IF(D2="x",B2,NA())

In C2.  Plotting A1:C2500

Now if you are manually deciding one which point are markers you just enter an X in column D.

If on the other hand you want 50 equally spaced entries marked

Then enter the above formula from C2:C51 and then in C52 enter =C2 and fill it down the rest of the way.

You mark the first point by entering an x in column d and every 50th number from there down is marked.  And you chart is plotted.

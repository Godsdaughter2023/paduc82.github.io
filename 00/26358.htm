eric hicks say:

It could also be something in your exchange account that's causing problems.  A good test to try if the one of the other two people with the same phone is willing to help would be to sync their device to your exchange profile and see if the problem happens again.  They would need to erase their information from the device first (so their information doesn't get synced to your account and vise versa) which can be done by removing all the exchange information from activesync on the device.  You will be prompted that all the contacts, e-mail, tasks and calendar items will be erased from the device.  Then sync that device with your exchange account.  If the problem goes away then something is wrong with your device, if the problem still happens then something is wrong with your exchange account.  It may need to be purged then recreated.

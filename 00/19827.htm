Check the wrapping style on the picture. If it is In Line with Text, you can move the picture only the way you would selected text (it acts just like a giant font character). If you want to be able to move it freely (including outside the margins), you will need to change the wrapping style to, say, Square. Dave's suggestion of Behind Text will not be helpful because, once the picture is behind the text, you won't be able to access it until you click on the Select Objects tool at Home | Editing | Select.

When you change the wrapping style, the wrapped picture will be anchored to a specific paragraph. As you drag the picture, the anchor may go with it, but if the anchor is locked, you won't be able to move the picture to a different page because it will have to stay on the same page with the paragraph it's anchored to.

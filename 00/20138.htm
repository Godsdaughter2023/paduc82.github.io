joe men say:

Hello Davis,

You can free up some space on your Lumia 640 XL by removing the temporary files, moving some of the content to the extended storage device and moving your apps.

To remove the temporary files go to Settings > System > Storage> This Device > Temporary files >Temporary files, then tap Remove files.

To move the new content on your extended storage go to Settings > System > Storage > Change where new content is saved then, change the storage location of your apps, documents, music, photos, movies and TV shows, and offline maps to the SD card.

To move your apps, go to Settings > Apps > Apps & features, select the app that you wanted to move and tap the Move button.

After doing the mentioned steps, restart your phone and try to update it again.

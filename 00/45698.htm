gerald vit say:

Hi HeadDog1,

The folders Deleted Items and Trash are created from the folders that are synced to your email account. For example Outlook uses Deleted Items folder for items that you delete and is visible in the Mail app once you sync it to the Mail app. If your email account uses another email client such as those on mobile devices they might be using a Trash folder for their deleted emails.

For more information, you may read the discussion between Brian Tillman [MVP-Outlook] and ac603xb on this link regarding Deleted Items vs Trash folders.

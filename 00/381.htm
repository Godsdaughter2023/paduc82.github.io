        ActiveSheet.Shapes("Check Box " & x).ControlFormat.Value = 1
    Next x
Else
    For x = 1 To 26
        ActiveSheet.Shapes("Check Box " & x).ControlFormat.Value = 0

End If

i have accidently deleted cookies from my browser and cannot log into - Microsoft Community

hay242 say:

i have accidently deleted cookies from my browser and cannot log into sites

original title: i have accidently delted cookies from my browser and cannot log into sites

email

a. user say:

Cookies don't typically prevent you from logging into sites (as long as they are activated - some sites require them to be enabled).  What happens is that the automatic logon features no longer may function and the sites may no longer recognize you (so you may need to re-register or register under a different identity or login using some other method if it won't let you use the old one because it thinks it is already taken - login information is often stored in cookies and if the cookies are gone you may have some trouble on some sites and there really isn't much you can do about it).  In some cases, you may need to contact the site technical support to regain access under your old account (they will reset your account and create a new cookie - and this may occur on sites where you didn't actually setup an account as well as those where you did but not many of those).  Unfortunately, you'll have to live with this until you've resolved all the sites where cookies were required or used for logon or access to account information or historical information.  This problem is especially true for e-mail sites where the cookies store a lot of information - you may need to contact the e-mail provider to reset the account (or there may be some way to do this on their web site yourself since people change computers and use other computers all the time where cookies haven't been created and they don't seem to have this problem or are able to figure out a way around it).  I've never deleted my cookies so I don't fully appreciate the consequences or how it affects the system.  I suspect this is happening with only a few sites and not the majority of them so resolving the problems shouldn't take too long.

There's no way to recover the cookies unless you have them on a backup - they aren't stored anywhere else and once deleted they are gone for good.

I hope this helps.

Good luck!

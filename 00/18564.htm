To ensure you're not syncing and to maintain your privacy alter the server address of your microsoft account to 127.0.0.1.

On first setup of your phone or after a reset you'll need to allow your phone to communicate with microsoft (for Store purposes, also manually installing apps) so ensure you have NOT added any contacts before creating or signing into a microsoft account because it WILL sync them in this situation.

I've had no problem installing apps from the store, these are freebie apps i have no idea how it will behave for paid apps with an altered server address.

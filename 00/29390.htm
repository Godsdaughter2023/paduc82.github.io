Hello, friends

I tried some more ways.
The problem with the taskbar only appears when the SP4 is running on AC, NOT when running on battery!
Keyboard attached or not does not help.

But now, in Device Manager, I deinstallated the Akku Driver (Device Manger item in German Akku, might be battery); when you doublecklick, you get two subitems. I deinstalled the Microsoft Surface ACPI-compliant Control method battery. And the problem was solved!!! Didn't need a restart. Clicking ok and ok.
Ok, after next restart, the driver was reinstalled and the problem reappeared...
So, the problem might be a faulty driver!?

There are the 2 files battc.sys and CmBatt.sys, Version 10.0.586 (th2_release 151029-1700) from Oct 30, 2015, 08:17

steveg47 say:
Deinstalling that driver may result in very poor battery performance.  Don't assume that the problem is definitely a driver problem.  You may have simply bypassed a hardware issue with the SP4 or the AC charger by DE installing the driver..  I would still try a different AC charger and/or replace the SP4.  Incidentally the driver I am using is 1.2.0.2 dated 11/13/2015 for the Microsoft Surface ACPI-compliant Control method battery.

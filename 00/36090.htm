jomel_g say:

Hello

Welcome to Skype Community!

That seems to be unusual that your contacts are not showing whilst signed in via the email address linked to your Skype account.

In this case, I suggest that you sign out of the application, and sign back in using your Skype Name instead. If your contacts show up after this, it means that your Skype account is not yet completely linked to your Microsoft email account.

You may refer to the steps below on how you can link a Skype account to an email address:

  1. Login to the Microsoft website using your Skype Name.
  2. You will see a message "Your Skype account is now a Microsoft account", click Next.
  3. You will be asked for the email you want to link your Skype account with, please type your Microsoft email address and click Next.
  4. Follow the steps.

Note: I strongly suggest that you try the steps above using a private browser.

I hope the information above helps.

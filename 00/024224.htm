There are a few things that can cause a scan to take a long time:

 

1. Slow PC - If you are on an older machine, it will take some time to process all of the files. Recommendation: Let the scan complete.

2. Degraded Hard drive - If you're hard-drive is not performing properly, it can take a very long time to scan.  Generally, you will experience slow down when you run other programs. However, if there is damage to a specific part of the disk, then you may only experience the problem during scans.  Recommendation: Run chkdisk on all of your drives.  If there are any errors, you can either replace the drive, or exclude the files affected by the damage in the MSE settings.

3. Infection - Some malware will intentionally cause scans to take longer, to avoid detection.  Recommendation: Contact support (https://support.microsoftsecurityessentials.com/) or try the beta for Microsoft's Standalone Offline System Sweeper (https://connect.microsoft.com/systemsweeper)

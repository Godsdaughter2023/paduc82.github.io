jeeped say:
Your problem could be that a) the document is a PDF and not a DOC or DOCX at all, or b) the document has been blocked due to your security settings and the fact that you got the file from the internet. Possibly a combination of both.

Using Explorer, locate the document in your Downloads folder or wherever you have secured a copy and right-click Properties. On the General property page, is the document of the PDf or DOC/DOCX variety? Look at the bottom below Attributes. Is it Blocked?

If it is a PDF, it will not open in Word. Get yourself a free PDF reader like Adobe or Foxit to view PDF files. These are available completely free of charge for download, installation and use.

 

Adobe Reader

Foxit Reader


If the document is a DOC or DOCX and has simply been blocked, unblock it and then open it in Word.

rangarajvidyakar say:

Sway - Content not updating to shared users

Regards

jason (office engineer) say:

Hi Vidhu,

Every time a user opens a Sway, they should be getting the most recent version of it.  If they didn't refresh their page, that would be one reason it didn't update.  Another possibility is that maybe you duplicated the Sway and they're looking at a different version.  If it's neither of these issues, let me know and we can dig deeper.

Jason

rangarajvidyakar say:

Hi Jason

Thanks for your reply

I am pretty sure that it's not duplicated. Also , Yes. Refreshing the page works but my question is do they have to refresh the page every time? cos I have a stats report and I change it often and expect to reflect the changes straight away to my team without having to pass on a message and asking them to refresh the page constantly.

Regards

Vidhu

 

jason (office engineer) say:

Vidhu, when a Sway is opened by someone who is not an author, the content will not update unless they refresh the window or resize/rotate the window.  The reason for this is it could be very disorienting for it to be constantly updating if a user was trying to read a Sway while the author was editing it.

When an author opens a Sway for editing and then taps Play, the Sway will update in that mode because the thought is that you are actively authoring it and wanting to view updates.  You could add people as co-authors, but I wouldn't suggest that because having many authors in the Sway leaving it constantly open will be quite cumbersome.

Hope this helps!

Jason

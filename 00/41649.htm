The power of Libraries is that they can combine the contents of multiple folders is a single 'logical' folder and the data displayed in different ways.

For instance, because I create and save a lot on my Desktop, I've added the Desktop folder to my Documents library. Then, when searching, I don't have to worry about whether it's in Documents or Desktop, I just search the Documents library.

The other useful feature is Stacked views. Right-click in the background of a Library folder and hover over 'Arrange by...'

You will see various options, and these option differ based on the Library type (Documents, Music, Pictures)

Hello John,

Thank you for patiently waiting. We sincerely apologize for not being able to answer your concern in a timely manner. For your concern, Digitally signing a message applies the sender's certificate and public key to the message. Your certificate is sent with the message to help authenticate you to the recipient.

Sending and viewing encrypted e-mail messages requires both sender and recipient to share their digital ID, or public key certificate. This means that you and the recipient must send the other a digitally signed message, which enables you to add the other person's certificate to your Contacts. Once both parties have shared certificates, sending and viewing encrypted e-mail messages between them is the same as with any other e-mail messages.

      Hi DisriNieuwoudt

      Put 10 in a blank cell
      Do CTRL+C of this cell where you put 10
      Select your data range
      Home tab > Paste > Paste Special > Multiply > OK

      Do let me know if you have any more question or require further help.

      davem121 say:
      Hi DisriNieuwoudt

      Enter the number 10 in any spare cell on your spreadsheet and click Enter

      Select that cell and choose Copy (or press Ctrl + C)

      Select all those cells you want to multiply

      Then choose, Paste - Paste Special

      On the resulting dialog, in the Paste section, choose 'All' and in the Operation section, choose 'Multiply'

      Click OK

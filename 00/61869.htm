Ok so after much trial and error I found there was a setting in my Ethernet device properties that "allows this device to transmit and receive packets with minimal bursts of energy and enter a low power state when idle". For anyone else that may stumble across this thread with the same issue this worked for me:

Keep in mind some things may be different on your own PC, such as names and devices but the basic principle is to disable any power saving features that may be active on the device you're having trouble with.

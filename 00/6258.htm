dianeoforegon say: Have you upgraded to 14.2.1? If no, then I suggest you update first.
Microsoft Office 2011 14.2.1 Update

Rename and move your old Identity back to the correct location:

 ~/Documents/Microsoft User Data/Office 2011 Identities/your identity

  • Select this Identity as default in Database Utility.
  • Open Outlook and put it into work offline mode.
  • Delete your Exchange account and any IMAP accounts in the Identity. This will leave only "On My Computer" data.

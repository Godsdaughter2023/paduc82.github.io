I would like to help you.

If the personal subscription of Office 365 is contracted, you can install the suite on a single PC or Mac, while if the Office 365 Home subscription is made, the license will allow you to install the suite on up to 5 computers.

If you want to use Office 365 on a different computer or Mac then you are using, you must transfer the license from one computer to another in order to continue using Office 365.

The first thing you have to do it from the computer where you want to use the license, open the Office page in a browser and log in with your account. Once you are logged in, on the home page, click on the user photo in the upper right corner of the window and then select the My Account option.

Next, press the Install button, which will show the information of the device or devices where you have the suite installed and the Office 365 license activated. Therefore, first of all, you must proceed to deactivate the current installation and make a new one in the new equipment. The next thing you must do then is to click on the Disable installation of the equipment you have been using so far option and confirm the warning that will come out for the process to be performed.

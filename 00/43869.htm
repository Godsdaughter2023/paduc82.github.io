cambunctious replied on 

In my case, the internet sharing was incorrectly linked with my Wi-Fi adapter instead of the hotspot adapter. Here's how I fixed it.

  1. Open Mobile hotspot settings (Settings > Network & Internet > Mobile hotspot)
  2. Turn on mobile hotspot
  3. Under "Related Settings", click Change adapter options
  4. Take note of which adapter is connected to the internet and which adapter is your mobile hotspot (typically named "Local Area Connection* [#]")
  5. Right click the adapter that is connected to the internet and click Properties
  6. Go to the Sharing tab
  7. Uncheck "Allow other network users to connect through this computer's Internet connection". If it is already unchecked, skip this step.
  8. Click OK
  9. Again, open Properties > Sharing for the same adapter.
  10. Check "Allow other network users to connect through this computer's Internet connection"
    (If you get a popup that says "Internet Connection Sharing is currently enabled...", click OK.)
  11. Under "Home networking connection:", select the mobile hotspot adapter
  12. Click OK

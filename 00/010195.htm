ron coderre say:

To calculate the hourly rate, you'd need to divide the salary by the appropriate number of hours.

 

Typically, the salary amount is converted to an annual amount and divided by the annual number of hours.

Example:

Monthly Salary: $4,000.00

Annual Salary: $48,000.00

 

Annual Hours: 40 hrs/wk x 52 wks = 2,080

 

Hourly rate: $48,000 / 2,080 = 23.0769230769231

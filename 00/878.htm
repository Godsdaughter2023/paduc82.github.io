Most of this arises as you have not checked the toner and the ink cartridge. The toner and the ink cartridge are designed to have a certain capacity. If the printer toner and cartridges are empty it will cause to turn your wireless printer offline. Incorrect installation of your printer can also make your printer turn offline.
 
Wireless Printer Hanging Issue: The turn offline problem arises due to some hanging problems of network or other reasons. Sometimes your wireless printer will hang due to problems like sticky label residue, paper clips or torn paper that might be left in your wireless printer. , only the donor name is recorded in donations (a foreign key from the DONORS table in the Donations table).

First names are not good for a key field as you can have duplicate names. Your tablers should look like this:

tblDonors

DonorID PK Autonumber

DonorFirstname

other into about donors

tblCampaignsandEvents

CampEventID (PK Autonumber)

CampEventTitle

other info (You might need separate tables for info unique to campaigns or events.)

tblDonations

DonationID (PK Autonumber)

DonorID (FK)

CampEventID (FK)

DonateDate

Donation

other info

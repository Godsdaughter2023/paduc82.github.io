bob jones aka: cybertaz say:
Most importantly, "delete and install" is not a recommended solution on a Mac except in certain extreme situations. That's a Windows "fix" which can do more harm than good, especially if a proper Remove of Office isn't done first. Deleting Office does not even touch the files that might be responsible -- assuming something is wrong to begin with.

As for the reordering of pages, keep in mind that Publishing Layout view is just a veneer. You're still dealing with the structure of a standard Word document 'behind the scenes', so there are no pages involved. What you actually have are collections of objects contained in Sections represented by Master Pages. The veneer just makes it look like physical pages exist.

Generally, content can be moved within a Section by dragging the Thumbnail, but it's the content that's actually being moved to a different location in the Section, not the metaphorical page. It's as though you selected all of the objects on that 'page' & dragged or cut/pasted to a new position in the flow. The difference is that the underlying Master Page & its properties are included.

However, each Section can have its own distinct components based on the Master Page for that Section -- you may see a blue line separating one group of Thumbnails from the next -- which are incompatible with other Sections. Depending on what those distinctions are it's entirely possible that any given page cannot arbitrarily be dropped into another Section. There could be a variety of factors involved so without seeing the document it's impossible to tell what specific roadblocks you may be running into.

Suffice it to say that although Microsoft has gone to a great deal of trouble & expense to make it seem otherwise, Word just isn't the same as a desktop publishing / page layout program.

Hi mikeymike74,

In fact once you change your Microsoft account security information there is no particular steps that you need to perform on your Windows Phone device to gain full access to your Microsoft account services through your Windows Phone. 

The Next greyed button is only enabled I case your Windows Phone need to get your Microsoft account password information updated.

You can try to soft reset your Windows phone and manually try to sync your Windows Phone account.

soft reset: Press and hold simultaneously on power button + volume down for  at least 15 seconds 

Manually Sync: Press and hold on Microsoft account for at least 5 seconds then press sync.

greg maxey say:

Eventually yes ;-). 

 

You can do this.  Again the best (while perhaps not easiest) is to use a mapped content control.  Dropdown and combbox dropdowns have a display name e.g., AL, OH, TX and value e.g., Alabama, Ohio, Texas.  The user sees the display name and the value is stored in an XML data node (if you map to one).

 

You could then create and add a plain text control and map it to that same node.  That control will display the dropdown value.  If the user selects TX in the dropdown then Texas is displayed in the text field.  I would set the text field attribute "can not be edited" to keep the user from manually entering invalid text.

 

Another alternative it is to manually link two content controls http://gregmaxey.mvps.org/word_tip_pages/linked_content_control_dropdown_list.html
